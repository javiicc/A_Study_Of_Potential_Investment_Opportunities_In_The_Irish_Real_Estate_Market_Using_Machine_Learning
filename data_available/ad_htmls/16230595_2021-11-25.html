    Dun Aitinn or Fort of The Furze is a stunning 4 bedroom bungalow presented in turn key condition and set on a superb elevated site. This hugely impressive property which is set behind electronic gates enjoys amazing views over Raheenmore Bog, Croghan Hill and the surrounding countryside. The property is located approx. 10km from Tullamore Town, 5km from the N6 in Tyrellspass and Kilclonfert is 3km from the property. The property comes with 5.2 acres of grazing land.\nThe interior and exterior of this beautiful home are finished to exacting standards. The property offers further scope for additional accommodation as it has a floored attic space ready for conversion.\nThe accommodation briefly comprises entrance porch/sun room, generous sitting room wtih front aspect, living room and kitchen have a lovely open plan design. The kitchen has extensive solid oak units with granite work surfaces and double doors open to the back garden. There are three double bedrooms one which has an en suite and the superbly appointed bathroom. The fourth double bedroom which also has an en suite is located off the mini kitchen/utility room and hence would make an ideal independent living area for guests/inlaws.\nOutside you will find landscaped front and rear gardens with extensive deck, gazebo, lawned area and shrubbery and plenty of outside lighting. The back garden is ideal for outdoor entertaining. There is also a double garage which is fully wired and loft area to rear of garage ideal for conversion to gym or home office. Overall a stunning home with land which must be viewed to be fully appreciated.\n\n\nEntrance Porch/Sunroom 2.08m x 2.94m. Open plan from the kitchen featuring Stanley oil stove with brick surround and over mantle railway sleeper.\n\nKitchen/Dining Room 3.70m x 6.11m. The kitchen is equipped with a super range of solid oak kitchen units with granite worktops, beautiful cream tile splashback, whirlpool double oven, ceramic hob, integrated dishwasher and fridge freezer. Lovely dining space with bay window and also french doors to the back garden.\n\nUtility Room 2.49m x 3.72m. With fridge freezer, granite worktops, sink, gas hob, extractor fan, washing machine, dryer, storage presses, tiled floor and tiled splashback.\n\nMaster Bedroom 2.87 x 4.52.
