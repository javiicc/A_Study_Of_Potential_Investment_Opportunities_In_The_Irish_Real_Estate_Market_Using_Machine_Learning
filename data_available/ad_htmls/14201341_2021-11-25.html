    DNG is delighted to present 100 Blacklion to the market. This charming cottage is very deceptive in terms of the size and the space that it offers. The property accommodation of approx. 201 Sq M is divided between a 5-bed semi-detached cottage of approx. 140 Sq M and a 2-bedroom granny flat of approx. 61 Sq M. \r\n\r\n\r\nPlease copy and past the following link into a web browser to see the virtual tour for the granny flat.\r\n\r\nhttps://my.matterport.com/show/?m=Uk7oRnQGMdR\r\n\r\nClick the video link for the tour of the main house.\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nThis rather unique layout is sure to appeal to a variety of home buyers whether they are buying as a single-family unit or those looking to buy with extended family relations. The granny flat may also suit as a home office or consultation suite if required (subject to P.P.)\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nThe accommodation to the main house comprises an entrance porch with views over the garden and a pleasant sea view over the rooftops of the neighbouring homes. A large open plan living dining room enjoys a similar view of the front garden and features a large solid fuel stove. Off the entrance hall there is a single bedroom and a small study. an inner hall leads you to a shower room, a second bathroom and the Kitchen. Off this hall there are 4 double bedrooms. The kitchen has a large breakfast bar, a good selection of cabinets and windows facing south and west.\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nThe granny flat accommodation comprises hall, living room, generously proportioned kitchen dining room, a family bathroom and two double bedrooms, one of which is en-suite.\r\n\r\n\r\nThe property has gated off street parking to the side and a well-maintained lawn to the front which is bordered by a mature hedge on two sides. To the rear there is a large deck area and a second lawn. Behind the granny flat there is a tiered lawn with a steel shed for additional garden storage. The gardens and patio area are almost due west in orientation so enjoy afternoon and evening sunshine.\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nThe location is excellent for families of all ages. For the younger ages you are just a few minutes' walk to a selection of primary and secondary schools. For older college going students there are Dublin Bus stops to Dublin City practically on your doorstep and the Dart is just a 1.7km or a 20-minute walk from the house. Close by there is a selection of shops and cafes to choose from. The bustling village of Greystones and all the amazing amenities it has to offer are all within a 2km radius of the house and so are really accessible by foot, bike or car if needed.\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nFeatures of note\r\nRather unique 7 bed property of approx. 201 Sq M\r\n\r\n5 Bed semi-detached bungalow of approx. 140 Sq M \r\n\r\nBuilt c.1930 and extended c.1970\r\n\r\nInsulated, rewired and replumbed c.2005, New boiler c.2015\r\n\r\n2 Bed granny flat of approx. 61 Sq M \r\n\r\nOriginally used as playschool c.1976 \r\n\r\nInsulated, rewired and replumbed c.2005, New boiler c.2019 \r\n\r\nGas fired central heating to both properties\r\n\r\nDouble glazed windows throughout\r\n\r\nOff street parking\r\n\r\nShort stroll to shops and schools\r\n\r\nJust 1.7km or a 20-minute walk to Greystones Dart Station\r\n\r\nOnly 1.2km to the Harbour and Marina\r\n\r\nWest facing rear garden \r\n\r\nLarge steel shed for additional garden storage\r\n\r\nLarge deck area that enjoys all day sunshine\r\n\r\n\r\nHouse Accommodation\r\n\r\nPorch 2.92m x 1.46m.\r\n\r\nHall 2.06m x 1.35m.\r\n\r\nStudy 2.61m x 2.02m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 3.95m x 2.42m.\r\n\r\nLiving Dining Room 6.28m x 4.55m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 3.71m x 3.09m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 3 4.24m x 3.48m.\r\n\r\nShower Room 3.7m x 0.94m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 4 3.78m x 3.06m.\r\n\r\nBathroom 4.04m x 1.55m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 5 3.44m x 2.67m.\r\n\r\nKitchen 4.78m x 3.33m.\r\n\r\nGranny Flat Accommodation\r\n\r\nHall 5.89m x 1.17m.\r\n\r\nLiving Room 3.63m x 3.27m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 3.62m x 3.02.\r\n\r\nShower Room 2.42m x 1.76m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 3.14m x 2.91m.\r\n\r\nEn-suite 2.09m x 0.89m.\r\n\r\nKitchen Dining Room 4.45m x 4.27m.
