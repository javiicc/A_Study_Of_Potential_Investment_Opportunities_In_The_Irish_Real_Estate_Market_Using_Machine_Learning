    The definition of an Irish Cottage!\n\nIn a most scenic and tranquil location this wonderful thatched cottage has been extensively restored and retains all of its traditional charm and beauty. \n\nA beautiful, stone-built 19th century, traditional Irish thatched cottage, surrounded by farmland, situated just 5 miles from the heritage town of Lismore, County Waterford. \n\nBoasting an abundance of character, including exposed beams and whitewashed original stone walls, this cosy Lismore cottage represents the perfect getaway from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Bramble Cottage is split, with the living quarters and main bedroom located in the original thatched building and extra living space is found in a converted barn just across a small courtyard, perfect for visitors looking for extra privacy or possibly for use as a studio/office. \n\nSurrounded by mature lawned gardens, with a large adjoining paddock, the property is accessed via a private gated lane. It s west facing patio is perfect for alfresco dining with panoramic views across the countryside. \n\nSet in an expansive rural setting and in a desirable elevated position, this Lismore cottage offers sweeping mountain views as far as the eye can see. \n\nComplete privacy is at your fingertips in this location. Within easy commuting distance of Dungarvan, Lismore, Clonmel and Cork. \n \nAcc: Living, kit/din., utility, 3 beds, bath, shower room, studio. OFCH. 224 sq.m.
