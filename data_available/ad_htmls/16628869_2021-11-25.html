    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to welcome to the market 9 B Metropolitan Apartments. This stunning second floor, two bedroom apartment located in this contemporary apartment complex at The Metropolitan in Kilmainham. \nSet in a former four-storey 19th century mill building this is one of Dublin 8 s finest developments. The original structure is incorporated into the modern design of the new buildings, using sympathetic materials and large glazed areas. \n\nThe entrance hall leads to an open plan living / dining and kitchen which opens onto the balcony, the main bedroom comes complete with an ensuite bathroom, the second bedroom is a good sized double bedroom and the accommodation is completed by a family bathroom and a storage area off the hall. No.9 B also comes with an allocated underground parking space. The development has the added benefit of a gym located on the lower levels ideal for those keen fitness enthusiasts! \n\nThe Metropolitan Apartments are ideally located in the heart of Kilmainham, a cultural location within Dublin city within distance of a host of a local amenities and public transport. Local attractions include the Museum of Modern Art, Kilmainham Gaol Museum., The Royal Kilmainham Hospital and Heuston Station.
