    Sherry FitzGerald Geraldine O Reilly is delighted to bring to the market this stunning 2 Storey Red Brick, Georgian style 5 bedroom property with home office located only 4.4 km from Virginia Town and 8.9km from Oldcastle Town. Also within walking distance of Deerpark Forest. circa 4000 sq ft.\n\nThe property ( built 2004) is warm ( B3 Rating) ,spacious and no room encapsulates this better than the kitchen/diningroom with plenty of storage space that opens into the sunroom. The living room has a beautiful cream marble fireplace over a solid fuel stove. Another reception room has an attractive Black marble fireplace for an open fire leading to the large room suitable for entertaining.\n\nUpstairs each room is bright and has plenty of storage space in the form of wardrobes and the master bedroom is very spacious with double doors to the rear for a balcony. The attic of the property also has ample storage capacity with access via a full staircase.\n\nThe large extension on the property has a multitude of options such as a gym, an office, a bar or a games room. \n\nThe outside of the property is beautifully landscaped with electric cast iron gates, a cobblelocked driveway and a large front lawn with plenty of greenery. There is a large garage to the side of the property which has planning for a one bedroom annex.\n\nThe town of Virginia is only 4.4 km away and Deerpark Forest is within walking distance. Amenities include Lough Ramor, Ramor United GAA grounds, Virginia Rugby Club, St Mary s National School and Virginia College.
