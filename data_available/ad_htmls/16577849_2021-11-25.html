    A most appealing detached double bay windowed bungalow ideally located on this highly favoured mature road with a delightful private rear garden and a highly convenient location a short walk to the Luas, Churchtown and Dundrum Villages and Dundrum Town Centre. The property offers a bright and spacious interior which was extended to the rear to provide a wonderful sunroom approximately 15 years ago. \n\nThe property was built in 1950 and presently extends to approximately 120sq.m (1,292q.ft), it has been well maintained over the years and offers a well-proportioned interior which benefits from an abundance of natural light filtering throughout. The accommodation comprises a large entrance hall which leads to the two reception rooms to the front of the property. The kitchen / breakfast room is also accessed form the hallway and provides a second entrance to the property via a side porch. The main bedroom to the rear leads directly into the extended sunroom accessing the private rear garden. There are two further bedrooms and a family bathroom. The property also has a garage to the side and offer obvious potential to be further extended subject to acquiring the necessary planning permission. \n\nWoodlawn Park is a highly prestigious setting within a short walk of a host of amenities. There is a wide choice of both primary and secondary schools in the immediate area and the property is also conveniently located within minutes of the Luas Stations at both Windy Arbour and Dundrum Village.
