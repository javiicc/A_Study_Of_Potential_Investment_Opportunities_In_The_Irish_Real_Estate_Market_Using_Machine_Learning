It is traditional but has a new kitchen with all appliances and electric storage heating.\n\nThe property has a 2ft thick wall and is well insulated making this an easy home to heat.\n\nThe property is comfortable, easy to run and very close to the town centre in a quiet street.\n\nThere is ample private within the gated parking and a large back garden and patio that gets the sun all day as it is south facing.\n\nBLIND GATE HISTORY: As part of one of 3 gates into the barony of Kinsale, there was Cork Gate, Water Gate and Nicholas gate.
