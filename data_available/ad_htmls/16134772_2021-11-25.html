    We are delighted to present this charming 3 bedroom home offering superb potential and a most private 30ft garden to the rear. Well maintained over the years it now offers its new owners a superb opportunity to put their own stamp on it. \r\n\r\nAccommodation comprises porch, living room, dining room, kitchen, downstairs wc. Upstairs there are 2 bedrooms and the main bathroom.\r\n\r\nMount Tallant Avenue is a highly desirable location due to its easy access Terenure village, Rathgar and the city centre. This green and leafy neighbourhood offers exceptional suburban living that is within a mere 4 km commute to the city. It has a number of well serviced bus routes alongside cycle tracks directly into the heart of Dublin. Schools within the Terenure area include St. Joseph's BNS, Presentation Primary School, Presentation College, Terenure College and Our Lady's Secondary school. The superb amenities of Bushy park are only minutes away offering a variety of sporting facilities, river walks and of course its famous Saturday Market!
