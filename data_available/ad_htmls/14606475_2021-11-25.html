    RAY COOKE AUCTIONEERS are delighted to present this detached three bedroom bungalow to the market nicely tucked away off the Kylemore Road, Dublin 12. Bluebell is well serviced with excellent public transport links including the 13, 68, 69, 151 & 18 bus routes and the LUAS red line. The N7 & M50 motorway are both very easily accessed and Liffey Valley Shopping Centre is within arm's reach.
