    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    This is a superb four-bedroom family home located in the extremely popular Donnybrook Castle development, situated in the heart of Donnybrook. Built by Shannon Homes in 1997 number 10 has been meticulously maintained by its current owners and comes to the market in turn-key condition. \n\n\nThe accommodation briefly comprises an entrance hall with guest w/c off, living room with interconnecting double doors to the dining room, separate kitchen featuring all mod cons and a sunroom. A separate utility room completes the ground floor accommodation. On the first floor there three double bedrooms and one single bedroom with the master en-suite and a main bathroom. The attic is part floored and accessed by a folding stairs. \n\nThis exclusive gated development is located off the Stillorgan Road, close to the junction of Eglington Road and convenient to a host of nearby amenities and facilities. Donnybrook Village is only a short distance away offering numerous cafes, food stores, bars and restaurants including Arthur Mayne' s, Donnybrook Fair and Marco Pierre White' s while the ever-popular Herbert Park is just a gentle stroll.\n\nThe location also boasts many excellent schools including St. Michaels College, Blackrock College, The Teresian, Muckross Park and Mount Anville to name but a few. St Vincent' s University Hospital and Blackrock Clinic are both nearby while Irelands largest University, UCD is within walking distance. The property is well served by various bus routes in and out of the city center.\n\nA brief description does not do justice to this fine family home which has much to offer and must be viewed to be fully appreciated...\nACCOMMODATION\n\n\nEntrance Hall: Wood floor, burglar alarm control panel, intercom to hall door, recessed lights.\n\n \nGuest Cloakroom: Tiled floor, part tiled walls, w/c, w.h.b. with fitted presses beneath and mirror over.
