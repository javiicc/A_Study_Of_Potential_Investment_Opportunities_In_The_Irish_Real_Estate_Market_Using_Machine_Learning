    Woodlawn is a mature, private development ideally located on the popular Lahinch Road within walking distance of Ennis town centre and all amenities afforded to the town. Villa Nova is located within a minutes drive of the link road to the M17 & M18 for those commuting to Shannon, Limerick & Galway. Villa Nova was originally built in 1983 and was formally a bed and breakfast. The property stands on a large private landscaped site of over an acre with mature gardens and an abundance of trees, shrubs, double driveway and car port. The accommodation has been extensively extended over the years and is bright and very spacious with extensive living accommodation which includes, 2 living rooms, dining room, kitchen/ breakfast room, seven bedrooms with five bathrooms.
