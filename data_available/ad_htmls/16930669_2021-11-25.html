    Located within the ever-popular residential development of Belarmine, 1 Littlewood is a beautifully presented and spacious two-bedroom apartment with a most generous south facing patio with direct access to the communal gardens.\r\n\r\nAccommodation comprises of a hallway, tri aspect open plan kitchen / living / dining room with patio access and second balcony access from the living area, two double bedrooms (master bedroom with patio access and ensuite), storage closet, hot press and family bathroom. Outside the manicured communal grounds are presented in lawn with colourful planting with generous parking.\r\n\r\nNestled in the foothills of the Dublin Mountains, Belarmine is beautifully maintained and boasts plentiful manicured green open spaces. The location is ideal and within a gentle walk to the bustling village of Stepaside with its generous range of shopping and dining amenities. There are also a range of convenient stores and eateries within the Belarmine development itself. Also nearby are Dundrum Town Centre, Sandyford Business Park, Beacon South Quarter and Leopardstown Racecourse.\r\n\r\nThere are a wide range of activities nearby including Fernhill Gardens, Westwood gym, golf clubs, equestrian centres, not to mention an abundance of nearby hill walking and mountain bike routes. There is an excellent selection of local schools including The Gallops Montessori Preschool and Glencairn Preschool, Rosemont, Holy Trinity NS and St Olaf\xe2\x82\xacTM NS to name just a few. Public transportation is well catered for including the Luas Green Line and several Bus routes. Dublin City Centre is approximately 25 minutes by car. The M50 and N11 road networks are both just minutes from the home also.\r\n\r\nPublic transportation is plentiful including the Luas Green Line, Dublin Bus No.44, 47 and 118. Access to both M50 and N11 is close by. With that, 1 Littlewood will no doubt appeal to both First time buyers, investors, and those trading down.\r\n\r\nEntrance Hall\r\nTiled floor, recessed lighting, radiator cover, storage closet and shelved hot-press.\r\n\r\nLiving / Dining Room\r\nTri-aspect light filled room with tiled floor, dual balcony access and television point.\r\n\r\nKitchen\r\nTiled floor, fitted floor and wall units, granite worktop, tiled splashback, Indesit oven and 4 plate hob, extractor fan, dishwasher, washing machine and spot lighting.\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 (Master) \r\nDouble bedroom with fitted carpet, storage, television point and balcony access.\r\n\r\nEnsuite\r\nTiled floor, partially tiled walls, step in shower, wc, whb, wall mounted vanity unit and heated chrome towel rail.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2\r\nDouble bedroom with fitted carpet and storage.\r\n\r\nFamily Bathroom\r\nTiled floor, partially tiled walls, wc, whb, bath with shower attachment, wall mounted mirror and recessed lighting.\r\n\r\nNote: Brady & McCarthy and the vendor/lessor give note that the information contained in this brochure do not form any part of any offer or contract, are provided in good faith, and are for guidance only.
