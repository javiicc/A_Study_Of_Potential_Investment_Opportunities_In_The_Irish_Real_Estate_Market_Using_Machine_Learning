    Welcome to 62 Wendell Avenue, a property that presents a wonderful opportunity to acquire a well proportioned 4 bedroom semi-detached family home which is ideally located on a quiet cul-de-sac over looking a green area. Upon entering viewers will be impressed by the spacious living accommodation which comprises of porch entrance, reception hall with storage press, a large lounge which runs the width of the house and an open plan kitchen/dining area to the back completes the ground floor area. There are four generous bedrooms and a bathroom at first floor level. Further features include PVC double glazed windows, side entrance and off street driveway parking. Perfectly positioned the house is within a short walk of Portmarnock Beach, the Coast Road, local shops, primary and secondary schools and Portmarnock Leisure Centre with a host of sporting activities. Viewing comes highly recommended.
