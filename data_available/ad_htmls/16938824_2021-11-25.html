    Gallagher Auctioneers are delighted to bring to the market this spacious four bedroom bungalow on an elevated site overlooking St.John's Lough. The accommodation comprises of entrance porch, entrance hall, fully fitted kitchen with range of wall and base units, dining area, utility room, reception room one with feature fireplace, double doors leading to reception room two, reception room two also has double doors leading out onto a raised patio area with incorporates some wonderful views of St John's Lough, four double bedrooms ( two with ensuite bathrooms) the master bedroom benefits from a walk in dressing area and large ensuite bathroom. The gardens are well looked after and there is off street parking for a number of cars. Benefits from oil fired central heating, double showers in both main bathroom & ensuite bathroom and spacious reception rooms. Located only a short drive to the town of Ballinamore and a great selection of fishing lakes within the vicinity.
