    Hunters Estate Agent is delighted to present 20 Brabazon Square to the market. This is an excellent opportunity to acquire a two-bedroom, mid terrace cottage with an opportunity to modernise to taste, extending to approx. 35 sqm / 376 sq. ft. Located within this exceptionally convenient area. \r\nAccommodation briefly comprises a living room, kitchen/breakfast room, main bedroom, second bedroom, shower room and rear courtyard.\r\n\r\nThe convenience of the location cannot be overstated having every conceivable amenity within walking distance. \r\n\r\nSituated within a short walk of Dublin city centre, 20 Brabazon Square is ideal for people seeking their first home or someone seeking an investment opportunity. Brabazon Square is a pleasant and peaceful square containing trees, shrubs and flowers, offering tranquillity and quiet, and yet in the heart of the city centre. The cottages are charming, character-filled artisan dwellings, built by the Guinness family and dating from the late 19th century with a period charm. The square is picturesque, peaceful and calm which belies its city centre location. It has ample parking and plenty of light.\r\nDublin city centre is easily accessible to this property with St. Stephens Green within a 10-minute walk. Regular bus routes service the area and the LUAS and Dublin Bikes are also nearby. There are a fine selection of restaurants, bars and caf\xc3\xa9s in the locality. Other services and amenities that are easily accessible include Heuston Station, The Guinness Storehouse, The Phoenix Park, Royal College of Surgeons Ireland, Trinity College Dublin, St. James' and The Coombe Hospital.\r\n\r\nViewing is highly recommended. \r\n\r\nSPECIAL FEATURES\r\n- 2-bedroom mid terrace period cottage\r\n- 35 sqm / 376 sq.ft\r\n- Excellent first home or investment opportunity \r\n- Located near wide-ranging public transport network\r\n- Within walking distance of many amenities \r\n- Ample Parking in the square\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nACCOMMODATION\r\nENTRANCE PORCH\r\n(0.983m x 1.456m)\r\nEntrance porch with tiled floor.\r\n\r\nLIVING ROOM\r\n(4.081m x 3.504m)\r\nThe living room features a picture rail and fireplace.
