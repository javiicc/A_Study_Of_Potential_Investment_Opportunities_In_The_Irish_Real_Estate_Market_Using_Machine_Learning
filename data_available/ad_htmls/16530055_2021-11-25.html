    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    New to the market, this delightful 4 bed semi-detached home in a small and superbly maintained development in close proximity to Ballyvary village. All local amenities are on hand such as the local school and shops. Situated just off the main N5 with strong public transport links, Castlebar is a short distance (10 minutes) and Ballina and Knock airport are also easily accessible. This excellent property is wonderfully spacious with large living room leading to open plan kitchen/dining room with access to the rear garden, On the ground floor there is also a very useable utility space and a W.C. On the first floor there are 4 bedrooms, one ensuite and the main bathroom. All rooms are spacious and very bright. \r\nExternally there is a tarmac front drive and and enclosed rear garden which is fenced and has a small paved patio area. There is also a garden shed. \r\nThe property is fully furnished and can be sold as seen.
