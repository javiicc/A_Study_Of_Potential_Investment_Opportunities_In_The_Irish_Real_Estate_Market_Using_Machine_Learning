    Coleman Property are thrilled to present 13 Rosewood to the market for sale. This beautifully presented 5 bed detached property is situated in the much sought after Rosewood Estate, located in the heart of Whitechurch village. This exquisite property offers spacious living in a quiet cul-de-sac, while being a mere 11km from Cork City Centre. 13 Rosewood ticks all the boxes for those seeking the benefits of countryside living while being in the Centre of a vibrant community. \r\n\r\nThis property benefits from a B3 BER rating, a large south facing garden and patio area, oil fired central heating with a newly replaced A rated boiler & Fibre broadband. The light filled property has double doors linking the sitting room and kitchen allowing optional open plan living.There is generous car carparking onsite. \r\n\r\nInternally, this most impressive five bed property comprises of an entrance hallway a kitchen, dining room, two large sitting rooms, utility room, guest bathroom, three doubles rooms, two of which are en-suite, two single bedrooms/ multi-use rooms and a bathroom. \r\n\r\nRoom sizes: \r\n\r\nEntrance hallway 4.93m x 3.54m \r\nSitting room 4.45m x 3.44m \r\nKitchen 5.8m x 3.94m \r\nUtility room 2.92m x 2.6m\r\nGuest Bathroom 1.8m x 0.87m\r\nDining room 3.9m x 4.87m\r\nLiving room 3.9m x 5.86m\r\nBedroom 1 3.9m x 4.7m En suite 2.3m x 1.1m \r\nBedroom 2 3.9m x 4.26m En suite 2.2m x 1.25m\r\nBedroom 3 4.45m x 3.43m \r\nBedroom 4 2.69m x 2.7m\r\nBedroom 5 2.69m x 2.7m \r\nBathroom 2.46m x 3.78m \r\n\r\nLocation: 750m from Whitechurch National School, 750m from Whitechurch Community centre, Sports facilities & playground, 7.5km Blarney Village, 9km to Blackpool shopping centre and 11km to Cork city centre. \r\n\r\nViewing strictly by appointment only.
