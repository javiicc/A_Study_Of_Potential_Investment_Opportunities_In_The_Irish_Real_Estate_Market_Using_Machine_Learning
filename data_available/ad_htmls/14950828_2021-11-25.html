    We are delighted to bring to the market this substantial detached property. This 10 bedroom (10 en-suite) detached property sits on c. 3 acre site (approximately) and is located on the outskirts of Kilkee Town approximately 10 minutes walk to its beautiful horse shoe shaped beach, pollock holes located at the western edge of Kilkee Bay, cliff walks, bars, restaurants, shops and all local amenities. This bright and spacious property extends to 4,700 sq ft. The property comprises entrance porch, hallway, 2 reception rooms, 10 bedrooms (10 en-suite) kitchen, dining/family, sunroom, main bathroom, laundry room and garage. This substantial property would be ideal as a Bed and Breakfast, there is ample space for a home gym or office space. Externally the property sits on c. 3 acre site with beautiful countryside views, stone wall to the front with double entrance, gravel driveway with lawned garden and mature shrubs and hedging. Beautiful Indian sand stone patio area ideal for dining alfresco.
