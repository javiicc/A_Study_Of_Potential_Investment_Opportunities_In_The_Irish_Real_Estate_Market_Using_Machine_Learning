    Sherry FitzGerald is proud to present to the market San Quirico, a most exquisite, detached residence located in the highly regarded location that is The Coppins, Brighton Road, just moments from Foxrock village. This home has been very tastefully renovated by the current owners to include the addition of a large attic conversion which extends to approximately 900 sq.ft adding greatly to the property. The overall site extends to approximately 0.2 acres and offers the discerning purchaser a home of utter privacy both front and rear. Entering via newly fitted bespoke electric wooden gates, 8 The Coppins forecourt has been laid in gravel and bordered with gold granite cobblestones. This allows for multiple car parking with beautifully landscaped garden to one side, gold granite footpath and mature hedging that offers total privacy. The property has been subject to many upgrades to include external and internal insulation coupled with the addition of solar and pv panels, a newly fitted gas boiler and newly fitted radiators throughout which have attributed to the B3 energy rating. \n\nInternally the property is beautifully presented offering well laid out, spacious accommodation. The hallway is bright and airy with a contemporary fireplace. The drawing room which is incredibly bright to the front of the property has newly fitted custom made entertainment units with lighting. The impressive country style kitchen and dining room certainly has the wow factor being spacious with feature high ceilings and a newly fitted utility room with Belfast sink in both. Access via double doors the rear garden which is completely private. The family room is located beside the kitchen to the front of the property which also benefits from custom made entertainment units, library shelving with downlighting and newly fitted carpet. \n\nOff the hallway there are three bedrooms. Bedroom 1 and 2 with ensuite and bedroom 2 with walk in wardrobe. Bedroom 3 currently being used as a gym. The family bathroom has a walk in shower and Villeroy & Bosch sanitary ware. The office area with a newly fitted staircase with glass balustrades leads to the attic conversion. The converted attic measuring some 900sq ft offers a luxury peaceful getaway with stunning views of the rear landscaped gardens via a large custom made circular window (1.5m Diameter). This area is divided generously into three extremely spacious but adjoining areas equipped with an internal speaker system. The main area accommodates a Queen size bed comfortably. Separately via a clouded glass door is a deluxe ensuite with Devi underfloor heating, glass walled shower with tiled seating, slipper bath, vintage chrome rads, taps, shower heads, Roca sink unit with black marble top and electronic Vitra toilet/bidet. Through 2nd clouded glass door lies a sumptuous bespoke walkthrough wardrobe running the entire length of the house. Custom designed and fitted by Ropeplan are extensive hanging rails, soft closing drawers and shelving in Robinson oak concluding in generous dressing area.\n\nThere is a large lawn area with a meandering pathway which leads to the first of two patio areas bordered by colourful planting. A second patio provides another area to relax, dine al fresco and enjoy the peaceful surrounds. Bordered on all sides with fencing and mature hedging giving a wonderful degree of privacy.\n\nThere are outdoor power points and Abwood custom built wooden storage for household bins. Two concrete storage sheds and wooden shed to the side offer extra storage. Dual access to rear garden from each side of the property.\n\nThere are excellent leisure facilities nearby including Foxrock & Carrickmines Golf Clubs, Carrickmines Lawn Tennis & Croquet Club, Westwood Leisure Centre and Leopardstown Race Course and Driving Range. Other amenities include shops, boutiques, patisserie and restaurants. Extensive shopping facilities are nearby at Cornelscourt, Stillorgan, Blackrock, Dundrum and Carrickmines Retail Park. There are a host of very reputable primary and secondary schools in the immediate area and the property is located close to the LUAS, and the QBC on the N11 with the M50 within a short distance, this property offers easy access to main arterial routes.\n\n \n\n\nEntrance Hall Large, imposing entrance hall with feature curved wall, ceiling coving, recessed lighting, Velux window, contemporary
