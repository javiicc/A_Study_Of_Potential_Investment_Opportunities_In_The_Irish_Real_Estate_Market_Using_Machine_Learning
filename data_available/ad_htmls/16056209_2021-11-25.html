    Setting Sun is an outstanding detached residence nestled in the centre of the charming and picturesque fishing village of Ballyhack overlooking the Barrow Estuary. The property which was been extended and exceptionally well maintained by the current owners offers potential buyers a rare opportunity to acquire a home in this historic village. Arranged over two floors the bright and flexible living accommodation of 135sq m comprises of a sitting room, dining room, kitchen, living room on the ground floor. Upstairs there are 3 bedrooms, main bedroom with en-suite shower room and walk in wardrobe and main bathroom. Outside there is a beautiful south westerly facing patio areas ideal for enjoying the stunning views. Ballyhack is home to the historic Knights of Templar tower, Arthurstown, Duncannon and all the amenities and attractions of the Hook Peninsula are on your doorstep. Waterford City is also easily accessed via the Ballyhack Car Ferry.
