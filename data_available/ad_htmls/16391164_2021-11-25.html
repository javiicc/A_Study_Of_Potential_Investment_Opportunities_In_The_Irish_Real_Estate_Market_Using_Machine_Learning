    **** LARGE 1 BEDROOM APARTMENT FOR SALE IN THIS MUCH SOUGHT AFTER DEVELOPMENT ****\n\nDillon Marshall are proud to present Apartment 3 The Atrium to the market, a large one bedroom apartment which has the added advantage of large balcony. This high specification development is well positioned close to the IFSC and would make an astute investment. It would also be very suitable for a First Time Buyer looking for a city centre residential home.\n\nA number of features that this property exudes includes floor to ceiling glass in the living area, sun drenched balcony and stunning views, efficient boiler and recessed lighting not to mention the unique Atrium which is provided in the common area of this building.\n\nAccommodation comprises:\n\nEntrance Hall : Wooden floors throughout and access to large storage area.\n\nKitchen/Living Area : Wooden floors, floor to ceiling glass, built in kitchen units & recessed lighting.\n\nBedroom : Fitted Wardrobes and newly fitted carpet.\n\nBathroom : Recently modernized with bath & shower facility, wc and whb.\n\nThe Steelworks is ideally positioned close to Connolly Station and Busarus Central Station. The LUAS line is also within close proximity to this much sought after development. There are an abundance of amenities within easy reach to include the IFSC, Henry Street, Jervis Shopping Centre and a range of bars, shops, cafes and restaurants. The Convention Centre and the Bord Gais Energy Theatre are also popular among residents in the locality.
