    Allen & Jacobs bring number 16 Railway Cottages to market. This remarkable architecturally designed property is presented in excellent condition throughout; with creative vision and refined craftsmanship no. 16 extends to 206 sqm of a most stunning city residence. Using exquisite materials and fittings, married with great taste and modern technology, to create an inspiring grand scale living space. The property provides a generous mix of family living and entertaining space, spacious bedrooms & bathrooms and a striking, tiered and private sunny courtyard. The property comprises entrance hall, living room/dining room, kitchen, guest wc at hall level. The master bedroom with ensuite is at 1st floor while 2 further bedrooms (one ensuite), a family bathroom and a large utility/laundry room are at the lower floor level.
