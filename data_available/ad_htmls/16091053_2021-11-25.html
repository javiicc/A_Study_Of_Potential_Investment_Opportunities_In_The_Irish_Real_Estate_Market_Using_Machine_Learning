    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 93 m\xc2\xb2
    New to the market is this fine holding of 32.15 Acres all in one lot. The Lands are conveniently located on the Ballyhaunis town boundary and command an elevated position which offers extensive views overlooking the Town and surrounding countryside. The Lands include a two storey Farmhouse in need of modernisation throughout. The Farmhouse is accessed by means of a long driveway up through the Lands and offers the potential to be easily refurbished into a modern family home. The Lands would be considered to be elevated good and mixed quality grazing Lands with Water laid on. All parts of the Land are easily accessible given the fact they are set just off the main Ballyhaunis/Knock Relief Road R323. This holding is taken from Folio MY8277 comprising 27.41 Acres and Folio MY50728F comprising 4.74 Acres. The Lands and Farmhouse will be sold in one lot. For further particulars to include maps or to arrange a viewing please contact the sole agent John Higgins Auctioneer on 094 9631372.
