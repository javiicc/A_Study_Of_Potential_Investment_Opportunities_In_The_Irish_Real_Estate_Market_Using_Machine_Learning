    Number 26 Goldsmith Hall is an excellent two-bedroom apartment, located on the second floor of this quiet and mature residential development of Prospect Wood, Longford. Offering rooms of generous proportions and dimensions, this light-filled and roomy apartment is sure to appeal to a choice of potential purchasers from the investor to owner occupiers alike. The property comes as vacant possession. The accommodation comprises an entrance hallway with hot press, a living room/dining room/kitchen with wooden laminate floor and tiles. The room is bright with two north/east facing windows overlooking Prospect Wood. The apartment has a large master bedroom with fitted wardrobes and ensuite shower room. The second bedroom also has a fitted wardrobe. The property is within an estate that is further enhanced by its green areas, communal gardens and ample surface parking. This apartment is in a highly convenient location, positioned just off the N63, N5, the Royal Canal and Slashers Football pitch. It is walking distance to Longford town centre shops and restaurants and the train/bus station. \nEstimated rental income 850 pcm
