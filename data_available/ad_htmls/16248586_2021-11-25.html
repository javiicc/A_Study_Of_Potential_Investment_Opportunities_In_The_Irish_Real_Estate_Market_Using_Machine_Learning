    Branagan Estates are delighted to bring to the market this exceptional architect designed 4 bedroom detached property on just over one third of an acre. The accommodation includes an entrance lobby, hall, double height living room, open plan kitchen/dining room, utility, family room, 4 bedrooms (one en-suite), 2 bathrooms, games room and a mezzanine level. There is off road parking to the front and side for a number of cars and it is surrounded by tree lined gardens. This is a very desirable location adjacent to Dunboyne Village and its many amenities including schools, shops, restaurants, pubs, sports clubs, bus routes and Dunboyne train station to name a few. All major road networks, the Blanchardstown Shopping Centre and Dublin Airport are all easily accessible via the nearby M3.
