    Home and Away Real Estate is delighted to present, 3 Orrery Road, Gurranabraher. This 3 bedroom townhouse property comes to the market in excellent condition, perfect as a new home for a family. Ideally located, Gurranabraher is a suburb of Cork City. It is located on the northern side of the city and borders onto Fairhill, Shandon, Churchfield, Blackpool and Sunday's Well. Local amenities include selection of schools, pubs, shops and restaurants. The area is also well serviced by buses. This property is in turn key condition. The ground floor of the property is comprised of kitchen to the front of the property with an open plan feel to the living / dining room. This room is flooded by natural light with patio doors out to the south facing patio area and garden. Downstairs there is also hallway and utility found under the stairs. Upstairs comprises of 3 bedrooms and a master bathroom, all of which boast ample storage space and enjoy plenty of sunlight during the day. There is a well-maintained garden at the back of the property, with a patio area to enjoy the stunning city views. To the front there is room for off street parking. Only a short walking distance from numerous schools, a church, shops restaurants and pubs, this location is truly second to none. This property offers a great opportunity to acquire a quality home in a great location.
