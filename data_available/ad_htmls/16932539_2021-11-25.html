    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    LOCATION LOCATION ... this family home is in a superb location. 2 minutes from the M1 motorway which has you at Dublin airport in 20 minutes and Belfast in an hour. It is a 10 minute walk to one of the wildest and most beautiful beaches in the country. This home is enveloped with the countryside but still allows you reach the seaside town of Balbriggan in 5 minutes and the historic town of Drogheda in 10 minutes. It has an excellent bus service into Dublin City centre with the added option of taking the train from Gormanston station. There are numerous school's in the area which are diverse and traditional depending on your needs. St Patricks GAA club is nearby where budding footballers can dream of playing for Meath - or if Dublin is more appealing, then a quick trip into Balbriggan will give you access to O' Dwyers GAA club, cricket and rugby options. \n \nThe large and gorgeous garden is spectacular in all seasons. The periphery has mature bushes fully surrounding it, which are golden orange in autumn and bright green and luscious in the spring and summer. Wintertime in the garden brings its own unique beauty. Apple, pear and plum trees grow there and it is an ideal garden to grow your own vegetables and really embrace 'the good life' - whilst still enjoying a stroll to Gibneys thatched pub which serves delicious food as well as a good pint of Guinness. The back part of the garden will tire any children and pets out as they kick a ball with their friends. There is a large outbuilding which can be used as a stable if required. \n\nUniquely to this property is the potential to build a large house to the side if someone wishes to. Planning permission for this is currently being sought and if and when granted, will be part of the sale - this can be used for extended family or maybe built on and rented out - the choice is yours. \n\nInside this home are 3 large reception rooms. One of these has an en suite bathroom which make it an ideal 'other' bedroom - or an amazing self-contained playroom for groups of small pals. A lovely open fireplace is in the other room which makes it a cosy winter place to relax and a wood burning stove is in the 3rd reception area which makes it a bright and cheerful room to read in. The kitchen is a very good size and has a seating area opening onto a patio and sheltered side garden. Upstairs there are 4 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms- one which is jack and Jill style linking two of the bedrooms. There is also an office with great light from large velux window. The views over the countryside from the large back double room would lift your heart \n\nThis is truly a mix of rural and urban life. Viewing is essential to fully capture the beauty of this home.
