    Sherry FitzGerald are pleased to present to market this spacious 2-bedroomed apartment in the Poplar Grove development. Number 8 is a first floor apartment, it has been well maintained and is presented in excellent condition.\nAccommodation comprises entrance hall, Living/dining room, kitchen, 2 double bedrooms and bathroom.\n\n\nEntrance Hall The hallway has a laminate oak floor, with large hotpress off.\n\nLicing Room 5.8m x 3.52m.
