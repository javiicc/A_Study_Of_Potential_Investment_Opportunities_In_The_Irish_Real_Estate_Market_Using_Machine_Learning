    Register Now: www.laurelhouse122ranelagh.com\n\nBehind the original stone cut wall and wrought iron gate lies Laurel House 122 Ranelagh, well set back from the vibrant village, a most gracious end of terrace Victorian property of immense character, enjoying period features such as coving, sash style windows and high ceilings. Superbly located within in the village of Ranelagh, the long garden path leads to a short flight of granite steps and main entrance hallway. Once beyond the threshold, a wonderful opportunity awaits the discerning purchaser. Currently in use as office rooms, the very spacious accommodation could be reconfigured to create a delightful family home over three levels. If explored, a further option of a self- contained apartment at garden level could also supply a possible additional income. As the current layout presents, there are 10 office rooms and associated services such as accompanying kitchens, shower room and guest wc facilities. An Architect s feasibility study is available, which suggests that without any expansion, the house could be reconfigured comfortably to a 5 bedroomed property with versatile accommodation (subject to pp). \n\nA remarkable feature of Laurel House is its setting, with fantastic gardens to front and rear. The front garden is laid in lawn and well stocked flowering planting beds. The rear, with valuable off street parking offers 12 car parking spaces currently, however this car parking area could be converted to a traditional lawn with off street parking set behind electric gates. Alternatively, there is an opportunity for a 3 bedroom mews property subject to PP, if desired. Please see Architects feasibility study.\n\nLaurel House is wonderfully positioned in Ranelagh Village offering wide range of amenities and services both social and essential on its doorstep. There are also numerous schools close by including Gonzaga College, Sandford Park, Alexandra College and Muckross Park College. U.C.D. s Belfield Campus is a few minutes drive. The villages of Rathmines and Donnybrook are both within gentle strolling distance, while nearby Luas stops ease access to the city centre.\n\nFloor Accommodation 254sqm / 2,730sqft\n\nPlease note virtual staging has been used in some of the internal photography.\n\n\nHall Level off a short flight of granite steps, wide hall door with fanlight over head, spacious internal hall with wood style laminate flooring and high ceilings.
