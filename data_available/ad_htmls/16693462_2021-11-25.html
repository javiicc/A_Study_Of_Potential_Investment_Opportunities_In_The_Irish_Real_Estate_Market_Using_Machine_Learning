    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    This one bedroom apartment is a bright and modern property which would be a great starter home or retirement home. There is a balcony off the spacious living room which gives the occupants the option of outdoor dining, when the weather permits. This apartment has a special warm welcoming atmosphere, which is not always apparent in apartment living, therefore viewing is highly recommended.\r\nThis apartment complex is very quiet and is located a few minutes walk Clonee village with its many shops and restaurants including Lidl and Aldi. Dunboyne tennis club is located to the rear of the complex and within the block there are services including dry cleaners, fitness club and small supermarket. The apartment complex is within a couple of metres of the Dublin border and is minutes drive to the N3 with easy access of the M50 motorway, giving residents access to all local and national routes. Under 15 minutes drive to Dublin airport and 10 minutes drive to the National Aquatic centre, Clonee gives residents that wonderful experience of village life but has the benefit of having easy access and short driving distances to services within County Dublin.\r\n\r\nSpecial areas of interest within 10km include Fairyhouse Racecourse, Tolka Valley Park, Tayto Park, Newbarn Farm, Hamwood House and Gardens, Ark Puddenhill Activity Centre, Rathbeggan Lakes, the Royal Meath Pitch and Putt club along with the local GAA clubs Erin Go Bragh and Clonee GAA, So plenty of opportunity for adventure close to home.
