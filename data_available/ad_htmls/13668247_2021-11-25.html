    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 0.49 ac
    This is a rare opportunity to acquire a plot of land which would be suitable for a house site (SPP). The plot is half an acre in an area which is fully enclosed by stone walls. The site is been offered for sale subject to planning permission. Local housing need criteria will apply to anyone looking to buy the property.\r\n\r\nThe site has a south facing rear aspect with lovely countryside views. The property is an ideal opportunity for those looking to buy into this very scenic and tranquil area of Connemara and yet be within easy commuting distance of the city.\r\n\r\nThe area has many amenities with a shop and pub only minutes walking distance from the property while Rosaveal Harbour, TG4, Radio na Gaeltachta are no more than a few minutes drive. Other amenities included schools, churches, beaches, supermarkets are all within a 10 minute drive.\r\n\r\nFeatures\r\nSouth facing rear to site\r\nCentral and convenient location\r\nSet back from roadway\r\nLots of local amenities\r\nCommuting distance to Galway city\r\nMany beaches locally\r\nFerry to Aran Islands nearby\r\nRadio Na Gaeltachta and TG4 nearby\r\n\r\nBER Details\r\nBER: Exempt\r\n\r\nDirections\r\nFrom Galway take the coast road and go through Spiddal onto Inverin. At Tully (TG4) watch out for the shop (Costcutters) on the left hand side. Just past this the road goes in three directions. Take the centre road towards Rosaveal. Drive along this road and take the second left turning. Drive along this road and take the first left. The site is at the end of this road on the right hand side. It is also identified by a DNG Martin O'Connor For Sale sign.
