    Mount Charles Terrace is delightfully set just off the Miltown Malbay Road on the outskirts of Kilkee town on the west coast of County Clare. The property is within walking distance of all services and amenities of the local town.\r\n\r\nKilkee is a quaint coastal town set on the Wild Atlantic Way with a beautiful horse-shoe shaped sandy beach dominating the centre of this attractive coastal town. \r\n\r\nKilkee offers a huge selection of services that includes supermarkets, pubs, restaurants, cafes, golf, hotels, guesthouses, fishing, water sports, various scenic walks including the famous Cliff Walk with the famous breadth taking views of the Atlantic Ocean. \r\n\r\nThe property is a three-bed mid-terrace dwelling house that was constructed in the 1970's. The property comprises of an entrance hall, kitchen, living room, dining room, sun lounge, first floor lounge/TV room, bathroom, three bedrooms and one en-suite.\r\n\r\nThe residence is in good condition throughout and enjoys beautiful sea views from the front garden and front lounge.
