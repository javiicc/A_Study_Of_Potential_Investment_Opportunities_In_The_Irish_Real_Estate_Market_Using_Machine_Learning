    Murphy Mullan Estate Agents are delighted to bring to the market this superb 2 bedroom apartment at 18/21 Lower Mount Street one of Dublin\\'s most sought after and impressive apartment developments. No.15 is located on the first floor of Block B a contemporary designed block overlooking a courtyard communal garden, built to the rear of the converted Georgian houses to the front of the development.\r\n\r\nThe generously proportioned accommodation stretches to 94sqm and boasts every modern convenience including a contemporary fitted kitchen with Miele appliances, high quality sanitary ware in bathrooms, gas fired central heating, secure undergound carpark with lift access and a large balcony overlooking the carefully maintained gardens. The accommodation briefly comprises large open hallway with utility/storage room, two beautifully laid out bedrooms (master en-suite) and main bathroom, a wonderful living space that incorporates the kitchen, dining area and living room which leads out onto the balcony. The underground car park provides one parking space and and a communal bin room. The apartment also comes with its own storage room located off the car park.\r\n\r\nThe location of this apartment could not be more enviable given its proximity to the best amenities Dublin City has to offer, including Trinity College and the Royal College of Surgeons along with Merrion Square and the National Maternity Hospital at Holles Street within the immediate vicinity. Recreational amenities includes the National Gallery of Ireland and National Concert Hall as well as the Bord Gais Theatre at nearby Grand Canal Dock, which also provides an abundance of quality restaurants as well as the nearest Luas Stop to take you all around the City environs. Grafton Street, located just off St. Stephens Green is one of the prime retail streets for shopping, again within easy reach. \r\n\r\nNo.15 will prove very popular with those looking for a luxurious living space in a beautiful area of Dublin City which is accessible to every conceivable amenity. \r\n\r\nMust be seen to be appreciated.
