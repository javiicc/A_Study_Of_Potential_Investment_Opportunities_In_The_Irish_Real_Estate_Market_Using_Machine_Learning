    DNG are delighted to present this truly suburb 1 bedroom duplex apartment to the market. Situated in the quiet, secure and secluded development of Gracepark Manor number 123 boasts many unique features. Set out over two floors and extending to a generous 78 sqm, its double heighted south facing window allows for an abundance of natural flight to flood both the ground floor and mezzanine level above. The property has been well maintained over the years and comes to market in excellent condition throughout.\r\nThe accommodation is well proportioned and briefly comprises a welcoming entrance hall with staircase to upper floor, access to understairs storage as well as the conveniently located first floor bathroom. The ground floor also boasts a generous living/dining area which is partially double heighted, wooden floored and off of which is the gally style kitchen. On the first floor there is a second bathroom with w.c, w.b and bath shower combination as well as the spacious double bedroom with built in wardrobes. \r\nGracepark Manor is ideally situated immediately off the Swords Road, adjacent to Griffith Avenue and less then a kilometre from Drumcondra main street with its host of local shops and amenities. There are excellent transport links close by with an abundance of bus routes which provide easy access to the city centre and beyond. The M1, M50 and Dublin Airport are all within easy access of the property. \r\n\r\n\r\nEntrance Hall 1.8m x 2.7m. Solid wood flooring with access to understairs storage.\r\n\r\nBathroom 1.9m x 1.1m.
