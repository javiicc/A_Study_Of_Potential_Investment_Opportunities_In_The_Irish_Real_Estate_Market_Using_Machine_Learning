    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 180 m\xc2\xb2
    Savills is delighted to introduce " The Folding House" , a beautiful architecturally designed award-winning family home in the heart of Cork City.\n\nThis spacious three-bedroom detached futuristic design has an abundance light and space. It is undeniably private and peaceful within its walls and yet close to all imaginable amenities.\n\nThis unique home was built in 2012 and is decorated to the highest standards.\n\nUpon arriving at the property you are greeted by a private automated gate that allows access into the parking area. Entering and walking along the sheltered path to the front door you will see the large windows that illuminate the interior and yet the space inside is truly private.\n\nEntering the central double height roof light entrance hall you will look up and marvel at the abundance of natural light that illuminates the house, by design. There is a spacious kitchen/ dining/ family room to the left, that has a bespoke kitchen with a great selection of appliances and direct access to the south facing designer garden to the rear.\n\nJust across the hall is the guest WC and cloakroom. Beyond is the master bedroom with its large windows, lovely proportions, and light. This room is serviced by an en-suite with a designer finish and walk-in wardrobe.\n\nGoing to first floor level up the minimalistic, yet beautiful staircase you arrive at the main living room for the house. This magnificent space is very generous and has superb views overlooking the Sunday' s Well area across the river in the distance. This room is ideal for relaxing and entertaining. The large sliding glazed doors really opens the house to the huge private balcony from which to watch the world go by.\n\nAlso, on this floor are two other double bedrooms and a master bathroom, again finished to the highest standard. \n\n\nTo the rear is the landscaped designer garden, which is not overlooked and provides total seclusion, relaxing green space, ideal for those who love gardening, barbequing and entertaining! There is an exceptional selection of ferns, Japanese acers, miniature bamboo and more. The patio has been paved with both polished concrete and granite, giving a civilised finish that is low maintenance.\n\nThe local area is one of the most sought-after residential neighbourhood in Cork city. It is only a short walk to any amenities one may need, including the UCC sport arena and gymnasium, primary and secondary schools, UCC, CUH, shops, restaurants, bus and train service and the best that Cork city has to offer!\n\nBER No: B3\nBER Number: 114301245\nEnergy Performance Indicator: 138.75 kWh/m2/yr\n\nVIEWING\nBy appointment with Savills Cork\n(021) 427 1371 or email:cork@savills.ie\n\nFitzgerald Park - 40m\nMardyke Sports Ground Arena (UCC) - 70m\nNearest Bus Stop - 120m\nCork County Cricket Club - 400m\nUCC - 400m\nThe Kingsley Hotel - 950m\nBons Secour Hospital - 950m\nRiver Lee Hotel - 1.1km\nCUH - 1.4km\nWashington Street - 1.4km\nApple Holyhill - 2.3km\nKent Train Station - 3.2km\nCork International Airport - 7.2km\n\nGround Floor \n\nEntrance Hallway A very welcoming large area with double height open ceiling and large windows allowing in an abundance of natural light. Solid maple timber flooring, light fitting on rail, sliding glass door to entrance, beautiful stairs to first floor and cloakroom.\n\nOpen Plan Kitchen / Dining / Sittingroom A beautiful large space with direct access to rear garden and surrounded by glass, maximising light.\nThis lovely room has an ultra-modern fully fitted bespoke designer kitchen, with a selection of appliances including, dishwasher, oven/grill, 5 ring electric hob, fridge, freezer, extractor, along with two sinks. There is a beautiful large island providing a focal point for the kitchen area, with additional storage and a Carrara marble worktop. The kitchen also has a beautifully tiled splashback, light fittings on rail, TV point and telephone point.\nAdjacent to the kitchen is the dining and sitting area which are ideal for the whole family to congregate for breakfast, lunch or dinner.\nThere is a large sliding door providing access to the rear garden, giving a feeling that the room can extended if needed, especially in the finer months.\n\nUtility/Pantry Located just off the kitchen this room has a tiled floor, a washing machine, dryer, and additional sink.\n\nGuest WC Tiled floor, WC, WHB, recess light fittings, and mirror.\n\nBedroom One (Master) A beautiful large double bedroom located to the front of the house with multiple windows allowing in light and giving beautiful garden views. This well-proportioned room has a solid timber maple floor, a sliding door to the garden, blinds, TV point and telephone point.\n\nEn-suite A large room that is fully tiled, WC, WHB, a Vola Shower, cabinets, mirror with strip light, recess light fittings.\n\nWalk-in Wardrobe A generous room with natural light, solid maple timber flooring, recess light fittings and plenty of clothes hanging facilities.\n\nFirst Floor \n\nLivingroom This is by far the largest room in the house and a wonderful bright space. This magnificent open plan space has a solid maple timber floor, rail light fittings, TV point, telephone point, 1/3 sliding door opening the large, glazed window overlooking Mardyke Walk, and two double embedded power point in the floor.\n\nBalcony from Livingroom From the livingroom sliding door is a very large triangular balcony, that overlooks Mardyke Walk, The UCC Sports Arena, ' The Shakey Bridge' and beyond Sundays Well. The special balcony is very spacious, private and ideal for watching the sunset on warm summer evenings. There are external power points and lighting here.\n\nBedroom Two Located at the rear of the house, this good-sized double room has a private hidden balcony that gets the southern sunshine, the balcony has external power points and lighting. This lovely room has solid maple timber flooring and recess light fittings.\n\nBedroom Three Located to the side of the house overlooking the approach, this lovely double room has solid maple timber flooring and recess light fittings.\n\nBathroom A bright room with a large skylight overhead, this room has a tiled floor, a Vola shower, WV, WHB, recess lighting and a strip light over sink.\n\nOutside Entering the automated sliding gate to the front of the property you have a gravel set down area, perfect for two cars and a selection of plants and shrubs. \nPassing along the polished concrete path to the rear you are greeted by a good-sized garden/patio area that is private and south facing, ensuring all day sunshine. There is a great selection of ferns, Japanese acers, miniature bamboo and more.\nThere are external light fittings and power points surrounding the house, an external tap and a video intercom to main gate.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom Cork City centre, Washington Street proceed down Western Road, passing the River Lee Hotel and at 725m (at the front gate of UCC) turn right and then immediately left onto Mardyke walk. Continue for 600m and ' The Folding House' is on your left. Alternatively, for Satnav or Google Maps input: T12 CDF3
