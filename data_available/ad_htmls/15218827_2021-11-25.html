    SALE ARRANGED ** SALE ARRANGED ** SALE ARRANGED A unique opportunity to acquire a substantial 5 bedroom (3 en suite) residence on a site of approx 0.8 acres situated in the foothills of the McGillycuddy Reeks. It is located near Cronin's Yard, the traditional starting point for ascents of Ireland's highest mountain, Carrauntouhil. Originally built in 2004, the house itself has approx 2,980 sq ft of floor space over the ground and first floors. It features a split level living area which includes a kitchen / dining area that has a central island containing the gas powered range style cooker on the kitchen side and an integrated solid fuel stove on the dining room side. The kitchen includes granite worktops and an island breakfast counter. Extending off this area is the raised sun room which has a vaulted ceiling. This area captures the spectacular mountain scenery very well to the south and west. In addition, it has been finished to a high standard internally with solid walnut floors and pitch pine doors throughout. The property offers a turn key solution to those looking for a new family home or those looking for a home / an upmarket accommodation business. This impressive property has served both purposes in the recent past. It has immediate access to several established hill walking routes and is only a short drive from the Gap of Dunloe and the Killarney National Park.
