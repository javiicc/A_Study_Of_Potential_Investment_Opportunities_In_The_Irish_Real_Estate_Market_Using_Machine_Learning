There is a particularly large garage with adjoining utility space which would be ideal for conversion, subject to relevant planning permission. Whilst the property would benefit from some decorative changes it affords the opportunity to create the home of your dreams however it has been upgraded since original construction to include a modern oil fired heating system, re-wiring and pvc double glazed windows. This home oozes potential and is an ideal home for first time buyers or families. Set on c. .5 of an acre with ample parking and lawns to front and a large area to the rear. The village of Adare offers various amenities including four national schools, Adare Manor & Golf Course and shops and restaurants.
