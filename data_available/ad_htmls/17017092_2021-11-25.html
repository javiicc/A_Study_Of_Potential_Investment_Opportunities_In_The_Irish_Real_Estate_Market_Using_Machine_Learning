    Gallagher Auctioneers are delighted to bring to the market this two bedroom cottage located in the much sought after residential area of Cortober, Carrick on Shannon. Located within a 5 minute walk to Carrick on Shannon train station which serves the Dublin/Sligo Line (approximately 5-7 trains daily) The accommodation comprises of entrance hall, living room with open fireplace, kitchen/dining area, two bedrooms ( one single and one double), fully tiled shower room. The property also benefits from two external sheds both with electricity. This property has an exceptionally large garden to the rear of the property which is ideal for the enthusiastic garden or it lends to potential for an extension to the main residence. Within walking distance of all town centre amenities such as shops, restaurants, hotels, Leisure Centre, Cinema, The River Shannon Boardwalk & The Dock Theatre.
