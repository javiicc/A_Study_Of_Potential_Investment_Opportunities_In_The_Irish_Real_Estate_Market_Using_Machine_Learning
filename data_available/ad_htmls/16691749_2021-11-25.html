    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to present 153 Pugin House to the market. Set in the modern Rathfarnham development, this bright and spacious one bedroom apartment is located on the third floor of the mature residential development overlooking manicured communal gardens and the historic Loretto Abbey. Benefitting from an abundance of natural light and well-proportioned living space, this property combines all the ingredients for modern everyday living.\n \nThe well-appointed apartment includes a welcoming entrance hall leading to the bright living/dining room with stunning views of Loreto Abbey. There is a modern kitchen with ample storage space and all the integrated appliances needed for modern living. Completing the accommodation is the large double bedroom, a generous sized modern bathroom, and a utility closet/ hot press. A superb west facing balcony provides space to relax and enjoy the summer sunshine while enjoying beautiful views of the communal gardens. \n\nA perfect setting coupled with a central location, Loreto Abbey is serviced by direct buses to the city centre and is within easy reach of popular shopping centres like Nutgrove and Dundrum. This desirable location boasts proximity to a wealth of recreational facilities with St Enda s, Marley and Bushy parks close by as well as an excellent selection of fine bars and eateries.\n\nThere is secure underground parking. One designated space and ample visitor parking. \n\n\nHall The welcoming entrance has a hotpress and storage press which is plumbed for the washing machine\n\nBathroom Tiled flooring, partially tiled wall, feature wall mounted mirror with recessed lighting above and solid granite shelving below, bath with shower, whb and wc.\n\nBedroom Large bright double room with laminated flooring, built in wardrobes and door opening out to the beautiful west facing balcony overlooking communal gardens.\n\nKitchen Floor tiling, fully fitted kitchen with wall and floor level units with granite countertop, large extractor fan, integrated fridge freezer, integrated dishwasher, Electrolux oven and ceramic hob, and recess lighting.\n\nLiving/ Dining Room Stylish laminated flooring, floor to ceiling windows overlooking Loreto Abbey, tv point, telephone point and access to west facing balcony.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nComing from the city centre continue past Rathfarnham Village and the Yellow House pub on your right hand side. Continue straight through this set of traffic lights and take a right hand turn at the next set of traffic lights onto the Grange Road. Continue up this road take a left turn at the traffic lights into Loreto Abbey.
