    Positioned on a hillside sloping to the south, the elevated setting affords the property a bird's eye view of the surrounding countryside and the rolling hills towards the sea, at Dooneen, The Old Head and Garrettstown. Most of the front elevation of the property is glazed ensuring it takes full advantage of its special setting.\r\n\r\nExternally the property is finished in stone and sits very well into the natural landscape. A large deck runs along the front of the house with the main living areas opening to it, and steps lead down to the lawn beyond. A decked path leads to the front door which is neatly tucked around at the rear of the property, ensuring shelter from prevailing winds.\r\n\r\nThe accommodation is spacious and very well laid out, with a very welcoming entrance hall with a beautiful stairs case opening to the livingroom to the front, and kitchen / dining area to the left. The kitchen is a large room and very well fitted, with granite worktop and large breakfast bar. Doors open to the deck and garden from here and of course the stunning view! To the rear of the kitchen is a useful utility room. The fine sized livingroom is double height and has impressive floor to ceiling windows to enjoy the spectacular and ever changing vista, as well as a mezzanine area from the first floor. There are four well-appointed bedrooms on the ground floor including a very spacious master suite with double doors to garden and views, and a large bathroom complete with a sauna. A spiral staircase leads from the master suite to a very large dressing room and further storage, with Velux window. The study, bedroom with en suite shower-room and mezzanine complete the first floor accommodation. The guest bedroom on the ground floor also has ensuite facilities.\r\n\r\nThe property is a quality build and is beautifully appointed, both internally and externally with superb attention to detail throughout.\r\n\r\nEntrance Hall -
