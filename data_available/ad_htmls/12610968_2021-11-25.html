    Fantastic potential - A unique opportunity to purchase a historic building in a peaceful woodland setting with onsite natural flowing stream.\n\nBelieved to be the oldest existing school building in Waterford, Mount Stuart School has a most scenic setting. Standing on 0.44 of an acre (approx.), this stone building is nestled on top of a woodland glen with direct access down to a natural running fresh water stream which bounds the site on one side. \n\nInside, the school house itself has a world of potential with 612 sq.ft (approx.) of internal floor space. The exposed stone and 10ft high antique beams are amazing original features and with 3 large windows on both sides it creates an architect s dream.\n\nExternally, the property has an separate 11 x 5 stone outhouse which was the original bathroom for the school in its time of operation. To the front of the property, on the upper level, there is ample space to create a front garden/driveway. \n\nLocated just 15 minutes from Dungarvan town centre and 10 minutes from the N25 Cork-Waterford route. This property has massive potential in the rights hands.\n\nBER: Exempt\n\n\nInternal Dimensions 10.36m x 5.49m.\n\nOuthouse Dimensions 3.35m x 1.52m.
