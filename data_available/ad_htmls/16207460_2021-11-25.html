    13 Carysfort Avenue is a most impressive double fronted Victorian period property. Built circa 1840 and superbly located in the heart of Blackrock, the property enjoys stunning period features and a private rear garden extending to approximately 20m in length.\n\nThe property is accessed via a pedestrian gate and manicured front garden. The hardwood front door has a striking ornate timber surround that leads into the magnificent front hall with stunning period features that are a common occurrence throughout the property. There are two well-proportioned reception rooms to the front of the property that enjoy wonderful ceiling height with beautiful ceiling coving, tall sliding timber sash windows with timber shutters and open fireplaces. On the garden level there is also a family room, kitchen, utility room, family bathroom and guest shower room. Upstairs, on the first return there is a large double bedroom complemented by three further rooms on the first floor. The bedrooms on the first floor enjoy outstanding ceiling height of 3.7m and there is a fifth bedroom on the first-floor return.\n\nTo the rear of the property there is a private, granite walled garden laid out in lawn with mature planting. The property has been lovingly maintained by the current owners through the years and will appeal to a wide range of people given its highly convenient location and period features.\n\nCarysfort Avenue enjoys a location of unparalleled convenience being only a gentle stroll from all the amenities of Blackrock village, including specialist shops, restaurants, pubs, two shopping centres including the renovated Frascati Shopping Centre. The property also enjoys excellent public transport, with a host of bus routes on the Frascati Road, and the Blackrock DART station just off the main street. In the locality there are some of Dublin\xe2\x80\x99s best-known primary and secondary schools, including Willow Park, Blackrock College, Carysfort N.S., and Col\xc3\x83\xc2\xa1iste Eoin.
