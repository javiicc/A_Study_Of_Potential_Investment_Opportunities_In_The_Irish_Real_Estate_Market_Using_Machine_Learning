    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Set in an exclusive development of only seven properties, No. 2 The Cedars is a spacious five-bedroom detached property which is well-finished internally, with an impressive Georgian-style exterior. Measuring approximately 2500 square feet, the property is located just a short distance to Ballybofey town and all local amenities including supermarkets, a good choice of primary and secondary schools, hotels / restaurants, Drumboe Woods walking route and the Finn Valley Leisure Centre. Internal accommodation comprises of entrance hall, living room, kitchen, utility, family den, sunroom and ground floor bedroom/study, while upstairs there are four bedrooms and a family bathroom. This property is complemented with a garden to the rear with a view of the River Finn and a very useful detached garage.
