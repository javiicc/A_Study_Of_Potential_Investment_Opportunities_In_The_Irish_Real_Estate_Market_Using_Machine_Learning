    James L. Murtagh Auctioneers are delighted to offer for sale by public auction Farra Cottage on c.9.8 acres at Rathaniska, Mullingar, Co. Westmeath.\r\n\r\nFarra Cottage comprises a period style residence which dates back to c.1865. The property is ideally set back from the main Dublin to Sligo road (N4) and is accessed via a rough hardcore roadway which is in charge of Westmeath County Council. \r\n\r\nNestled on a private site, this attractive cut stone house retains much of its early character and form such as the cut stone facade, 4 large sash windows to the front of the property offering dual aspect views, 2 beautiful marble fireplaces, wood panelling, high ceilings (with ceiling roses) cornicing and original internal shutters. An orchard garden, paddock to the rear, and various cut stone outbuildings ( stable block and garage) surround the property. \r\n\r\nEntering the house, the dining room and drawing room lead off from the hallway to left and right, moving down the house, there are 4 bedrooms, a farmhouse kitchen (including a range and beautiful original flagstone floor), a bathroom and separate toilet.
