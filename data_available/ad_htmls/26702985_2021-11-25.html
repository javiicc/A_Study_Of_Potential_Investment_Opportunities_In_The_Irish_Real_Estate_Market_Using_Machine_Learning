      One of Dublin's most impressive period mid terraced red brick residences to come on the rental market this Autumn. One of Irelands leading health clubs Fitzwilliam Lawn Tennis Club is within close proximity of no 7. Its such a benefit to have a sought after club on your door step. \r\n\r\nThis property is being offered unfurnished. Understairs storage. Ideal family room/cosy sitting room with feature fireplace off the entrance hall. Guest WC. Splendid open plan flood lit white kitchen, dining/family area, with aga, hob. All good working appliances. Larder ideal for storage. \r\n\r\nOutside: To the front there is parking for 2 cars.
