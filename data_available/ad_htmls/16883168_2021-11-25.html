    DNG are delighted to represent the sale of 64 Shantalla Road, Beaumont, a spacious 3 bedroom mid-terraced family home just a stone's throw from local shops, cafes, shopping centres and parks. \n\nRequiring some modernisation, the property enters the market in its original footprint and offers well proportioned rooms, two bathrooms, a large driveway for off street parking and a South facing garden with room to extend. There is wide and convenient rear access to the property via a laneway allowing for future renovation works to complete with ease. With a host of local amenities, primary and secondary schools and excellent public transport links running directly by, this property truly makes for a fantastic family home in a secure and well established area. \n\nThe accommodation extends to a total floor area of approx. 92 SqM / 993 sq.ft and comprises hallway with downstairs WC off, sitting room, living room and kitchen. Completing the accommodation upstairs are three bedrooms to include two doubles and one single and a fully tiled family bathroom. \n\nThere is a host of amenities close by including DCU, Beaumont Hospital, sporting clubs and excellent shopping at Artane Castle and OMNI Shopping Centre with new Lidl and Marks and Spencer supermarkets.
