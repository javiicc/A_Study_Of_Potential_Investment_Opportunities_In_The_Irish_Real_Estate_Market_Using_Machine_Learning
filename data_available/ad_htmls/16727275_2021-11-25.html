    Situated in a quiet cul-de-sac, 20 Hyde Park is a semi-detached two-story property enjoying a superb setting. Positioned on the sunny side of Hyde Park, No.20 offers a private south/west facing lawned garden to the rear. \n\nInternally the well-appointed living accommodation briefly comprises; bright entrance hall leading through to a spacious kitchen overlooking the rear garden, living room with feature open fireplace and a dining room with access onto a paved outdoor garden terrace. A useful utility room and downstairs w.c complete the accommodation at this level. Upstairs there are four generous bedrooms benefitting from lots of natural light and a good-sized family bathroom. There is opportunity to further extend the property subject to the relevant PP. \n\nThe rear garden is south / west facing and very private, approx. 87ft long with mature plants, shrubs, a large lawned area and a stone cut patio area bordering the rear of the property, an ideal outdoor space for family BBQ s and entertaining. Off street car parking is provided to the front of the property. \n\n20 Hyde Park is convenient to a myriad of specialty shops, restaurants, bars and the wonderful scenic walks afforded by Dalkey and Killiney Hills. The Dart station and bus service at Dalkey is close by, and there is excellent shopping at nearby Sandycove, Glasthule and Dun Laoghaire. There is also a selection of well-regarded schools within walking distance including Loreto NS, Loreto Abbey, Harold s Boys, Castlepark School, Rathdown, Holy Child Killiney and St. Joseph of Cluny National School, St Josephs of Cluny and Rathdown Secondary School in locality. Tennis, rugby, GAA, Football, Golf and sailing clubs are all within easy reach.\n\n\nGround Floor\n\nEntrance Hall Spacious entrance hall with wooden flooring, window to the side, picture rail.\n\nLiving Room With a large picture window overlooking the front garden, feature fireplace, solid oak doors opening through to the dining room, tv point and, picture rail.\n\nDining Room Feature fireplace, centre light, picture rail and French doors opening onto to rear garden.\n\nKitchen A range of floor and eye level kitchen units, four-ring gas hob with double oven. Large window to back garden and window to the side and breakfast bar with built in storage.\n\nUtility Room With storage, gas boiler and access to the rear garden and side of the property.\n\nDownstairs w.c. With tiled flooring, wash hand basin and w.c.\n\nFirst Floor\n\nLanding Centre light, built in bookshelves, hot press and carpet flooring.\n\nBedroom 1 Large double room overlooking the rear garden, carpeted floor and fitted wardrobes.\n\nBedroom 2 A well-proportioned double room with window to the front of house, carpeted floor, built in wardrobes, built in study desk and shelving and picture rail.\n\nBedroom 3 Positioned to the front of the property, picture rail, built in wardrobe, desk with shelving.\n\nBedroom 4 With a lovely outlook over the rear garden, built in wardrobes, desk with shelving.\n\nBathroom With tiled floor and walls, shower stall with Triton shower, window to side, wash hand basin and w.c.
