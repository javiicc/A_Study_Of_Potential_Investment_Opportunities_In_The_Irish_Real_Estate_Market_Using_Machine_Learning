    Sherry FitzGerald O Reilly are delighted to present to market number 51 Morell Drive, a spacious three-bedroom semi-detached home perfect for a growing family. \nThis is a light filled family home, presented in excellent condition, with a wonderful landscaped garden to rear. This spacious rear garden plus a large side entrance offer great potential for a home extension (subject to pp)\nNumber 51 is in a prime location. It is just a short walk to the Monread Park, Monread Shopping Centre, local primary school, leisure centre, sporting facilities and cr che. \nThis home is perfectly located for the commuter as it is less than 5 minutes drive to the M7/N7 interchange and the Arrow rail link at Sallins is a 20 minute walk away. \nAccommodation in this fine property briefly comprises - downstairs entrance hall, sitting room, dining room, kitchen, utility room, guest wc. Upstairs- 3 bedrooms ,one en-suite and a family bathroom.\n\n\nEntrance Hallway 5.36m x 1.81m. The bright entrance hallway has a composite front door and a laminate oak floor.\n\nSitting Room 5m x 3.31m. The stylish sitting room is a generous room featuring a marble fireplace with solid fuel stove. It boasts custom built cabinets and shelving. An archway brings you to the Dining Room.\n\nDining Room 3.3m x 2.6m. The dining room has a wooden floor and both French doors to the decking outside and door to kitchen.\n\nKitchen 5.84m x 2.6m. The kitchen is fitted with a range of units in an attractive sage green. It includes a Smeg cooker with gas hob, fridge freezer and dishwasher. The floor is tiled in classic black and white. With back door to deck.\n\nUtility room 1.42m x 1.41m. The utility room is plumbed for washing machine dryer. With tiled floor, worktop and shelving.\n\nGuest WC 1.5m x 0.81m. With wc, whb and linoleum floor.\n\nUpstairs\n\nLanding 2.5m x 2.42m. The landing is light filled due to the large window. It has a carpet floor and a hotpress off. With attic access.\n\nBedroom 1 4.15m x 3.07m. This is a large double bedroom to rear with built-in wardrobes and a new washed oak floor.\n\nEn-Suite 2.23m x 1.19m. The en-suite has been recently refurbished and now includes modern wall hung wc, whb and wet room style shower, all enhanced by the encaustic effect floor tiles and matt finish wall tiling.\n\nBedroom 2 2 3.64 x 2.88m. This is a spacious double room to front with a fitted wardrobe and carpet floor.\n\nBedroom 3 2.7m x2.4m. With front view, this is a single bedroom with a laminate oak floor.\n\nFamily Bathroom 2.18m x 1.63m. The family bathroom has been renovated recently. It boasts a contemporary suite of wall mounted wc and whb with storage. It includes a shower-bath with rainfall shower and superb mosaic tiling. The walls and floor are tastefully tiled.\n\nOutside To the front the garden is in lawn with hedging marking the boundaries, with off street parking for two cars. There is a large, gated side entrance to the rear garden.\nThe stunning rear garden is packed with detail. It features twin circular lawns divided by bamboo and a stone slabbed patio which fronts the pretty summer house (2.5m x 2.5m). Outside the back door is a large deck providing seating. There is an abundance of planting with a wide variety of trees such as willow and ash, with climbers, flowers and shrubs filling the flower beds. With wooden shed to rear (3m x 2m).\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom Naas town, take the Sallins road. At the roundabout take the third exit onto Monread road. Take the next right turn onto Morell Road.
