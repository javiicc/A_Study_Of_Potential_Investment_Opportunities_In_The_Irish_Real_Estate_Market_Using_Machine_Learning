    Eircode: H91 DDP6\r\n\r\nProperty Partners Emma Gill take great pleasure in presenting to market this stylish semi-detached home which is presented in move in condition. It is located in the popular Ashthorn residential estate on the outskirts of Headford town in a cul de sac location. \r\n\r\nAccommodation extends to c. 159 m\xc2\xb2. (1,712 sq. ft.) and is spread out over 3 floors with excellent quality finishes.\r\n\r\nUpon entering the property you are greeted by a welcoming entrance hall. The living room features a feature solid fuel fire insert which benefits from a back boiler. The open plan kitchen / dining room is finished with a newly fitted modern kitchen with stylish backsplash. The dining area is filled with light and benefits from double doors leading to a garden at the rear. In addition there is a utility room, guest WC & storage room. On the First floor you will find 3 bedrooms, 1 of which is en-suite, as well as a main bathroom and hot press. The top floor offers a cosy rooftop double bedroom with its own en-suite which is fully tiled and a further storage room. \r\n\r\nTo the front of there is a paved driveway with off street parking for 2 cars. The enclosed garden has side access from the front of the property. There is Gas fired central heating with a solid fuel stove and back boiler making for a very cosy and energy efficient home. See attached floor plan for individual room specifications. \r\n\r\nAshthorn Avenue is a sought after development which has been designed to take advantage of the elevated site with excellent views over the countryside. Headford is a thriving village with a vast array of amenities available, including shops, supermarkets, pubs, hotel, banks, public transport, restaurants, schools, childcare facilities and various sports facilities. \r\nIt is a mere 3 minutes to Headford from the property and within easy reach of Castlebar, Galway City, Claregalway and Tuam. Lough Corrib, a world renowned fishing area is also located close to the property. \r\n\r\nThis property is likely to appeal to a wide variety of purchasers and truly has to be viewed in order to fully appreciate it.
