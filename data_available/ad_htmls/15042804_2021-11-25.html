    This spacious four bedroom residence enjoys a magnificent position on the Wild Atlantic Way surrounded by mountain ranges and overlooking Killary Fjord. \n\nThe elevated 3.5 acres site offers privacy and breathtaking panoramic views of Mweelree, The Maamturks and Twelve Ben Mountains. \n\nThe generous floor area extends to approximately 255 sq m/ 2,745 sq ft of well laid out rooms that take full advantage of the exceptional site. Completed in 2011 to a very high standard, the house s features include a solid frame cherry wood kitchen, window seat box-bay windows in the first floor bedrooms, underfloor heating, hollow core floors for soundproofing and five en-suite wetrooms. \n\nGround floor accommodation comprises of the entrance hall, a spacious living dining room with picture windows giving Fjord and mountain views, a well appointed kitchen with steps down to a suntrap sitting room with triple aspect windows offering 180 degree sea and mountain views, utility room, guest WC, two double en-suite bedrooms and bathroom. On the first floor there are two large double bedrooms, both en-suite and one with walk-in wardrobe, and an en-suite office/study. \n\nThe secluded site is set back from the Leenane to Clifden Road. There is a large family friendly lawn, ample parking, a detached garage (requiring some completion work) and timber shed. Ducting is in place for the installation of external lighting and electric gates. The main area of the site has been left in its natural state. \n\nDerrynacleigh is situated in one of the most scenic areas in the West of Ireland and is just a five minute drive from Leenane village. The village gained an international profile as the setting for the acclaimed film The Field. Its amenities include restaurant, cafes, restaurant and seaweed baths. The area is a haven for outdoor lovers with a choice of walking and hiking trails nearby including the Western Way which is just a five minute walk from the house. A swimming spot in Killary Fjord is a ten minute walk and Glassilaun beach, a stunning white sand cove, is a ten minute drive. The property is located between Clifden, 28 km distance, and Westport, 37 km distance.
