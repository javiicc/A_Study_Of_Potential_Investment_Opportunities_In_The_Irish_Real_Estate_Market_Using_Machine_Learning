    Tarpey Maloney Properties are delighted to present to market this first floor 3 Bed duplex Apartment in Sli Gheal. \r\n\r\nThe property consists of a large dining/living area on the first floor, the kitchen is cornered to a L-shape off the dining space. Double patio doors open out on to a large balcony overlooking a communal green area. There is a large guest WC on this level also. \r\n\r\nThe next level offers three bedrooms, 2 double with one en-suite and a single bedroom which could also be ideal for a home office. The main bathroom with a shower unit is also on this floor. \r\n\r\nSli Gheal is off the Ballymoneen Road which has sea views and only a walk away to Cappagh Park, Barna Woods and Silver Strand. \r\n\r\nThere are several national schools in the area including Educate Together, Col\xc3\xa9\xc3\x82\xc2\xa1iste Na Coiribe, Irish seconday school is within walking distance. No 153 Sli Gheal would make an ideal owner occupier or investment property. \r\n\r\nThe apartment is available for scheduled viewings. Please email or call for a viewing.
