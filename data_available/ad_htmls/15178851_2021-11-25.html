    Merrion Village is one of Dublin s premier residential developments enjoying an enviable location in the heart of Dublin 4. Built in c.1986 this lovely family home is the ideal choice for those seeking an extremely desirable and secure property in one of Dublin s most highly regarded and sought after locations. Number 70 is a wonderfully bright and spacious home with exceptionally well laid out accommodation. In brief, the accommodation comprises of welcoming entrance hall, beautiful sitting room with doors to the south west facing garden, dining room with shower room off, family/t.v. room, well equipped kitchen/breakfast room, a guest w.c and a cloakroom on the ground floor. Upstairs, there are four fine sized bedrooms (main ensuite) three of the bedrooms have a balcony. There is also a family bathroom. The property is further enhanced with a large attic which would be ideal for adding additional accommodation should one s needs require (subject to PP).\nThere is off street parking to the front along with a pretty garden. The well stocked back garden enjoys a south westerly aspect, and is deal for outdoor dining. \n \nLocated at the back of the development, the property enjoys a quiet and peaceful setting. A wealth of amenities is available on your doorstep including Merrion Church, St. Vincent s Hospital, Merrion Shopping Centre, Sydney Parade DART Station. The excellent amenities of Ballsbridge are within a short stroll which include many specialist coffee shops and restaurants, there are numerous recreational amenities such Herbert Park, The Aviva, The RDS showground s and Sandymount Strand for leisurely strolls.
