    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    The property comprises a first-floor contemporary purpose-built apartment, with a B3 energy rating, off Shanowen Road close to DCU and all required local amenities. Apt. 23 has the enviable position of being the only apartment accessed on this floor in this section giving a great deal of privacy. The accommodation extends to approx. 75sqm (812 sqft) an comprises a spacious entrance hall with a great storage room, a contemporary kitchen, in open plan format with a living/dining room. There is a private balcony that is not overlooked by other balconies, has an east facing aspect an looks over sports grounds ensuring that the view will not be interrupted by further development. There are two large double bedrooms and a large bathroom. There is gas fired heating and double-lazed windows. This apartment has one designed underground car space. The management fee is also reasonable by industry standards. This is a wonderful bright apartment that will appeal to both investors and home hunters alike. For investors a significant factor would be that if purchased this would be a first letting and therefore not affected by rent control rules as well as offering superb accommodation. For those looking for a principal residence the apartment offers superb accommodation, a great balcony, parking and a reasonable management fee and a communal roof terrace. Milner's Square is ideally positioned within minutes of Omni Shopping Centre and Beaumont Hospital. The property is a 20-minute drive from the City Centre and 10 minutes from Dublin Airport and Dublin City University. There are also a number of efficient bus routes on your doorstep.
