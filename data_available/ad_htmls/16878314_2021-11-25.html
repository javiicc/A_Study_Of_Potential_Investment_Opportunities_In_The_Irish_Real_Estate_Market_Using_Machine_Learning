    McGovern Estates is delighted to present 273 Charlesland Park. This 2 bedroom home benefits from a low maintenance private rear garden, and potential to convert the attic which would provide additional space for any discerning purchaser. The property is located in a tranquil cul de sac setting while also in close proximity to Charlesland shopping complex. Internal viewing of No.273 is highly recommended.\r\n\r\nThis immaculately presented two bedroom town house extends to 81m2 (893 sq.ft.) The accommodation briefly comprises a kitchen / dining area, living room, a downstairs WC, 2 bedrooms and a family bathroom & large attic space. No.273 is located in Charlesland Park, a peaceful setting with minimal traffic and opposite an amenity green. The property is located within close proximity to a wide range of amenities and the Charlesland shopping centre is just a short stroll away.\r\n\r\nGreystones Town is within easy reach and all your needs will be catered for with a wonderful selection of bistros, boutiques, restaurants and shops, the village library, churches and a large number of highly regarded schools.\r\n\r\nA host of sport and leisure clubs abound in the area including sailing, rowing, football, rugby, tennis and GAA. The Shoreline Leisure Centre and swimming pool is on the doorstep, as are a great choice of renowned golf clubs, including Greystones, Powerscourt and Druids Glen.\r\n\r\nGreystones is situated in a stunning location between the coast and the Wicklow mountains, giving easy access to the local beaches, countryside and nearby villages of Delgany and Enniskerry.\r\nThe commuter will be delighted at the easy access to local bus stops and the DART station both within walking distance. The N11/ M50 is also within easy reach making for an easy commute to the city, airport and beyond.\r\n\r\nACCOMMODATION \r\n\r\nEntrance Hallway \r\nTiled floor, cloak area.\r\n\r\nKitchen Room\r\nTiled floor, fully fitted kitchen with ample wall based & floor based storage cupboards, countertop space with integrated appliances & stainless steel sink unit, spot lighting.\r\n\r\nDining Area \r\nSolid oak wood floor. \r\n\r\nInner hallway \r\nSolid oak wood floor, smoke alarm. \r\n\r\nLiving Room \r\nSolid oak wood floor, carpeted stairs to landing, feature gas fire, double doors leading to rear garden. \r\n\r\nLanding \r\nCarpeted, hot press off, attic access via stira stairs, smoke alarm.\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 \r\nCarpeted, floor to ceiling built-in wardrobes.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 \r\nCarpeted, floor to ceiling built-in wardrobes. \r\n\r\nFamily Bathroom \r\nTiled floor, WC, pedestal wash hand basin with tiled surround, chrome heated towel rail, fully tiled bath unit with power shower. \r\n\r\nRear Garden \r\nLarge decking area, lawn area & patio to the rear, timber framed garden shed also features.\r\n\r\n\r\nSPECIAL FEATURES \r\nPrivate cul de sac location \r\nOff street parking provided \r\nLarge amenity green close by \r\nLarge attic space with conversion potential \r\nGas fired central heating & double glazed throughout \r\nImmaculately presented \r\nBroadband available \r\nClose to the Charlesland shopping complex\r\nAmenity green located close by\r\nAn excellent BER rating of C1 \r\nClose to the N11 & M50 road networks
