    DNG has the great pleasure in presenting The Steading, Drummin East to the market. The house was formerly known as Drummin Cottage, being part of the curtilage of the original Drummin House estate, serving as a coach house and subsequently staff accommodation. This quite unique property will certainly tick many a box for the discerning purchaser. It offers privacy in abundance being positioned on approx.7.65 acres of land, wonderful character in the current dwelling which dates back to the early 1800's, massive potential in terms of further development of the existing house and of course at some stage in the future it certainly has development potential within the 7.65 acres as the property abuts Zoned lands under the current Delgany Development plan.\r\n\r\nThe existing accommodation which overlooks a west facing courtyard is drenched in sunlight for most of the day and comprises living room, open plan kitchen dining area and a study, all with floor to ceiling windows that provide a huge solar gain to the property and provide lovely views of the courtyard. Off the study is a utility room with guest WC. Upstairs there is a bathroom, 4 double bedrooms and a walk-in closet and en-suite to bedroom 4.\r\n\r\nThe property is wonderfully convenient to the N11 and the thriving villages of Delgany and Greystones. Between the two villages practically every convenience a growing family could require is available. The village of Delgany is just 2km away and Greystones a further 2.5km. \r\n\r\nPersonal inspection is a must to appreciate how wonderful this property is.\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nDetached period 4-bedroom property dating to the early 1800's \r\nOriginal couch house to Dromin House\r\nAccommodation of approx. 224 Sq M\r\nSubstantially upgraded, replumbed, rewired, double glazed c.2015 \r\nPositioned on very private grounds of approx. 7.65 acres\r\nIdeal for someone looking for privacy or the potential to keep horses \r\nTwo separate entrances to the property\r\nWonderful potential to extend existing accommodation \r\nAbutting residentially zoned grounds under Delgany development plan \r\nPotential to provide development sites at some stage in the future\r\nSuperbly sunny west facing courtyard to front of the house\r\nCharm and character in abundance throughout the property\r\nMains water and septic tank waste disposal system\r\n \r\n\r\n\r\nLiving Room 5.8m x 5.3m.\r\n\r\nKitchen 4.1m x 3.1m.\r\n\r\nDining Room 5.8m x 3.6m.\r\n\r\nOffice 5.5m x 4.4m.\r\n\r\nUtility Room\r\n\r\nGuest WC\r\n\r\nLanding\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 5.4m x 3.6m.\r\n\r\nWalk in Closet\r\n\r\nEn-suite\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 4.1m x 3.2m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 3 4.1m x 3.2m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 4 5.8m x 3.7m.\r\n\r\nBathroom
