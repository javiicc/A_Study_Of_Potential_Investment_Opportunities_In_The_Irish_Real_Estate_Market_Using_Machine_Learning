This is truly a unique 5/6-bedroom semi-detached extended home comes to market in great condition. \n\nThe house itself was upgraded 5 years ago - new windows and doors, new floors/tiles in hall, kitchen, dining room and sitting room. New floor put into the spare reception room downstairs in 2019. Triton shower installed in the upstairs main bathroom in 2019 as well. Large open wardrobe/Wardrobe room installed 5 years ago, A new efficient boiler was fitted in 2016 as well as foam insulation in the attic and foam boards fitted on the interior adjoining external walls.\n\nDownstairs there is a large hallway with main living room to the right and additional bedroom or reception room to the left. The large living room has a marble fireplace with the stove insert, giving the room a warm cosy feel, walking through the double door leads you into a well-proportioned dining room with French doors leading to a southwest facing garden. To the rear of the property is a beautiful large kitchen with modern kitchen and integrated appliances. This property also boasts a utility room that is plumbed for washer and separate dryer, under the stairs there is also a W/C. The total size of the property is 141m2\n\nOn the first floor there is a large landing with 4/5 bedroom and family bathroom three double bedrooms and two single being used as a walk-in wardrobe. From the master bedroom there is a modern jack and gill ensuite that also leads to the 4th bedroom / walk in wardrobe. There is also a family bathroom on this floor. \n\n\n\n\nThe house is at the edge of the estate, and it beats a lot of traffic coming from Daletree and Hunterswood. You also have the option of going over the mountains to Dundrum. Lidl is only 5 mins in the car and it's a 3-minute walk to Spar and Woodstown facilities. It is serviced by the 15-bus route which runs regularly (every 10-15 minutes). It is also within walking distance from the 49, 65b and 175 bus stops. It's also only ten minutes from the Square, Dundrum shopping centre and Liffey Valley and only 20 minutes to Blessington. There's a view of the Hellfire Club from the front and back garden. Tibradden Woods, Cruagh Wood, Bohernabreena Reservoir, The Hellfire Club and Massey's estate are all within 5km from the house so it's a great location for walkers.
