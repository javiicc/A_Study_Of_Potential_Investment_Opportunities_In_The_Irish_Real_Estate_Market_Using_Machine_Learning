    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    This is a spacious and beautifully finished five-bedroom family home, set in a small development of just seven houses. Located a short distance from Ballybofey town centre and overlooking the River Finn, this attractive Georgian-style property extends to approximately 2500 sq.ft. Accommodation comprises of entrance hall, large living room, bedroom/study, family den, sunroom, kitchen, utility room and downstairs WC. Upstairs are a further four bedrooms (two of which are en-suite) and a family bathroom. Presented in first class condition and with the benefit of a detached garage, the property enjoys easy access to a number of local amenities including supermarkets, a good choice of primary and secondary schools, hotels/ restaurants, Drumboe Woods walking route etc. This home is complemented with a garden to the rear with a view of the River Finn.
