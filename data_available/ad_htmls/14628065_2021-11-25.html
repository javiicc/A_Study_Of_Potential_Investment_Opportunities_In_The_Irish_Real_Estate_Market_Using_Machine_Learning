    Sherry Fitzgerald Stack are delighted to bring this characterful 3 bedroom, 2 bathroom farmhouse residence to the market. \n\nSet on a half acre site, the property is situated in a peaceful countryside setting in a small cluster of dwellings at the end of a private cul de sac. \n\nThe residence is within a 5-7 minute drive to the villages of Moyvane and Athea respectively and just 10 minute drive from the heritage town of Listowel while the N69 main Limerick route lies just 12km away. \n\nGround floor accommodation comprises: Entrance hallway, kitchen/dining room, utility room, lounge/sitting room, 3 double bedrooms (one ensuite) and main bathroom with whb & w/c. On the first floor the attic has been partially converted - for storage purposes but has the potential to provide for two further bedrooms - subject to planning approval. \n\nThe residence has been fully refurbished and extended in recent years to include the installation of geothermal underfloor heating and a high level of insulation. The residence has the benefit of mains water supply, on site septic tank and double glazed uPVC windows fitted throughout. \n\nThis deceptively spacious residence would make an ideal family home, retirement home or holiday retreat. The outbuildings may be suitable for further development.\n\nViewing is recommended and is by appointment only.\n\n\nEntrance Hallway 6.20m x 1.00m. with ceramic floor tiles\n\nLounge/Sitting Room 5.02m x 3.59m. with solid fuel stove, carpet flooring, 1 window & french doors to rear\n\nKitchen/Dining Room 5.32m x 3.84m. with ivory wooden fitted units, ceramic wall & floor tiles & 3 windows\n\nUtility Room 3.28m x 2.27m. with geothermal heatpump system, washing machine, ceramic wall & floor tiles, wooden fitted units, sink & window.\n\nBedroom 1 3.48m x 3.26m. double room with carpet flooring & window\n\nEnsuite 2.15m x 1.23m. with pumped shower, ceramic wall & floor tiles,, whb, w/c & window\n\nBedroom 2 3.73m x 3.55m. double room with carpet flooring, vanity unit with whb & window\n\nBedroom 3 4.32m x 2.55m. double room with carpet flooring, vanity unit with whb & window\n\nMain Bathroom 3.28m x 2.85m. with ceramic wall & floor tiles, pumped shower, bath, whb, w/c & window\n\nAttic 6.39m x 3.42m. with wooden fitted units and vanity unit with whb, carpet flooring & 1 velux window\n\nAttic 4.29m x 3.30m. with carpet flooring & 1 velux window, plumbed for whb\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nEircode: V31 WP73 GPS No' s: 52.4743108 -9.3312605 Leaving Moyvane and heading South towards Knockanure, continue for 2.2km turn left continue for 2.7km, turn right and continue for 550m, property located on right hand side corner, look out for Sherry Fitzgerald Stack sale board.
