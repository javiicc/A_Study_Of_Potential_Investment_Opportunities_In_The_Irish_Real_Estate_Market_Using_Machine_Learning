    A most attractive 5 bedroom residence extending to c. 241 sq.m. to include a fine attached double garage sitting on c.1 acre of beautiful mature lawns located in a very sought after area Tara which offers excellent accessibility to Dublin City.\n\nMacetown Tara is a very quaint residence which has been carefully extended whilst keeping the traditional feel of this fine home. The residence is approached from the entrance with stone piers and wrought iron gates, rolling lawns to the front with many mature trees along with an extensive lawn to the far side of the site. There is beautiful cobblelock paving to the front of the property and a paved patio area to the rear an ideal space for outdoor entertaining. The gardens are mature and home to an abundance of mature trees and shrubs. The residence benefits from an attached double garage and a large detached garage.\n\nInternally the property is excellently presented and emphasises how well the extension marries with the original house. The lounge and dining rooms are traditional and the kitchen is modern with a built in Heritage kitchen. There is an additional room ideal for a home office or a downstairs bedroom if required. The stairway features a double height ceiling with arched window. Like the living accommodation there is plenty of space on the first floor where there are 5 bedrooms.\n\nAccommodation briefly comprises of Entrance Hall, Kitchen, Lounge, Dining, Utility, Guest w.c., Study / Bedroom 6, 5 Bedrooms (Main En-suite), Attached Garage. Detached Garage. \n\nExcellently located c. 1.7 km from L1005 Skryne / Rathfeigh Road and c. 8kms N2. Located in a strong community area 3.5kms from the historic Hill of Skryne and the infamous Mrs O\xe2\x82\xacTM Pub. Accessibility to the M1, N2 and N3 is excellent leaving Dublin City & Airport within a 35 minute journey.
