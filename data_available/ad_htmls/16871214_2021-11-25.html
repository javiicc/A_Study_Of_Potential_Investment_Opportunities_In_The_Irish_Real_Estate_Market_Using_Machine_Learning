During the property\xe2\x80\x99s extensive refurbishment approximately 20 years ago, no expense was spared to provide comfortable living with great attention to detail. This attractive property will no doubt suit both the growing families and downsizers alike.\n\nThe accommodation briefly comprises at ground floor level of an entrance hall, cloakroom with guest w.c., spacious utility room, an impressive living room overlooking the front of the property, a spacious kitchen/dining room, a large family room opening through to the dining room overlooking the rear garden, a delightful master suite with double doors opening to the rear garden, a second bedroom currently laid out as a study/home office and a cloak cupboard.
