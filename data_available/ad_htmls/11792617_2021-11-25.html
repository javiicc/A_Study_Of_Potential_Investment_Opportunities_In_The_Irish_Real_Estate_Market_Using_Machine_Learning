    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 279 m\xc2\xb2
    Castlecove House is a large, bright family home situated down a secluded lane and within its own mature grounds of c. 6 acres (c. 2.42 hectares) offering delightful views across Bandon River and towards Compass Hill. The house is located on a generous private waterfront site and offers some 400 m of peaceful riverside setting. The perennial gardens are mature offering a variety of hardwoods, shrubs and hedges as well as a wealth of privacy and plenty of space.\r\n\r\nThe house extends to total floor area of c. 279 sq m (c. 3,000 sq ft) and comprises a kitchen cum dining area, a sunroom and a conservatory on the ground floor. There are also four bedrooms on the ground floor. The master bedroom is ensuite and has its own dressing room. In addition there is a family bathroom and a utility room. On the first floor the house offers a sitting room with a stove and a large feature window making the most of the beautiful views looking out onto the estuary. There is also an additional bedroom, bathroom and study/playroom/bedroom. \r\n\r\nThe house has been designed to make the most of the beautiful views and mature gardens. Large windows offer stunning views from all living areas and the master bedroom. French doors in many of the downstairs rooms allow for direct access to the gardens from nearly every room. The house provides an idyllic spot to witness wonderful sunsets on the river and is well placed for glimpses of the abundant wildlife on the estuary. \r\n\r\nExternally there is a private slip way on the site to launch your own boat. There are also some outhouses and a large external workshop.
