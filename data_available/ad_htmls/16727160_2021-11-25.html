    Ray Cooke Auctioneers are delighted to present 24 Ardee Court to the residential sales market. An excellent opportunity to acquire a stunning property in a prime city centre location. 24 Ardee Court has been decoratively well maintained and viewings are highly recommended. Ardee Court is located on the west side of Cork Street as one drives towards the city. Located at the junction of Ardee St and Cork St, location in Dublin 8 is second to none. Grafton street is within a 20 minute walk, the RED LUAS line at Herberton is within a 10 minute walk and the regeneration of Cork St continues around you. \r\n\r\nOn entering the property one is immediately struck by oodles of natural light due to its southwest orientation. The apartment has natural tones and colours throughout, that blends well with the abundance of natural light on the 3rd floor. Accommodation is all laid out over a central corridor. The large double bedroom benefits from wooden floors and built in wardrobes. The apartment also benefits from an extra-large storage/utility room off the main hallway. When storage can be at a premium in apartment living, this room does not disappoint. The bathroom is fully tiled and benefits from both a bath and shower enclosure. The focal point of the apartment will be the extra-large main living area. Combining living room and kitchen, the space has direct access over the sun drenched South West facing Juliet style balcony. The balcony has stunning views over South Dublin and as far as the Dublin Mountains. \r\n\r\nFeatures:\r\nBER C2 \r\n50 sqm \r\n3rd floor apartment\r\nBuilt 2004 \r\nSouthwest facing balcony \r\nExcellent condition throughout \r\nStorage/utility room\r\nBuilt in wardrobes \r\nExtra-large living space \r\nLOCATION LOCATION LOCATION
