    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    GVM are delighted to bring no.11 An Cluain to the market which is a beautifully appointed two bedroom apartment superbly located in a well established development, overlooking a well manicured large green. This residence oozes location and is so conveniently positioned adjacent to all the fantastic amenities linked with the hugely popular Clonmacken area. The locality has numerous landmark amenities including The Limerick Institute of Technology, The Jetland Shopping Centre, The University Maternity Hospital, Medical Centres, Supermarkets and Banks. The vicinity is also very well serviced with high profile Primary and Secondary Schools. The Ennis Road has become a popular touring base and has a range of Bed and Breakfasts and Hotels with conference and leisure facilities and restaurants many of which have been renovated in recent times. Sport is synonymous with Limerick and the Ennis Road with The IT Gaelic Grounds, home of Limerick GAA in very close proximity. If you continue past the Gaelic Grounds, one comes across the famous Na Piarsaigh GAA club. Along the Ennis Road, the Limerick Lawn Tennis Club is prominently located and of course the iconic Thomond Park is just a few minutes\xc3\xa2\xe2\x82\xac\xe2\x84\xa2 walk away. There is easy access also to both Shannon Airport and The Motorway.
