    Impressive and tastefully decorated detached stone-clad residence and workshop with beautiful Ilen River estuary views, set on 1.1 acre approximately, just 4 miles from Skibbereen town centre. \n\nThe property enjoys privacy and is approached via a gravel drive intersecting beautiful mature gardens, specimen trees and shrubs.\n\nThe house extends to 1454 sq. ft approx., is well presented and designed to maximise light. The many unique features of this fine house include modern kitchen units, tiled floors, oil fired central heating, recessed feature ceilings, central vac system, solid fuel stove in the sitting area and underfloor heating throughout. \n\nThe accommodation includes a spacious kitchen / dining room, a generous sitting room, utility and wc on ground floor. The first floor, accessed via a newly installed bespoke staircase, comprises 2 bedrooms and a bathroom.\n\nThere is a detached studio/workshop, plumbed and fully wired, measuring 525 sq. ft approx. and another small garage on the grounds. The gardens consist mainly of lawns interspersed with trees and shrubs. \n\nThe property is just 4 miles from Skibbereen town and within driving distance of some of West Cork s premier coastal villages and attractions.
