    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to present an excellent opportunity to acquire this spacious two bedroom duplex where attention to detail and luxurious finish is evident throughout. Situated on the top floor this beautifully appointed, bright spacious modern duplex with excellent proportions is ideal for trading down, first time buyers, or young professionals seeking a property in turn key condition. \n \nInternally there are two double bedrooms (one ensuite) on entry level. Upstairs there is an open plan area incorporating living/ dining room and a modern kitchen. A showerroom and storage closet completes the accommodation. The property is further complemented by two balconies with panoramic views of Dublin City, the Dublin and Wicklow Mountains. The living room balcony also benefits from a southerly aspect. \n\nSituated just off the N2, it is within an easy commute of the city centre, DCU, Dublin Airport, the M50 and the villages of Drumcondra, Phibsboro and Glasnevin, ensuring all amenities and services are only a stone s throw away.
