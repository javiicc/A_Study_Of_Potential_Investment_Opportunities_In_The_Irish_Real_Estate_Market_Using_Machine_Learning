    Residential farm set in a scenic rural area of Ireland\n\nThe property offers beautiful views of the McGillycuddy Reeks and surrounding countryside.\n\nFarm house residence plus some out buildings with 124 acres (50.1935 hectares) of mixed quality land. The lands are fenced with a large pond located on the property.\n\nThe lands extend up to the summit of Knocknafreaghane Mountain (1,087 feet). From this peak you can view Kenmare Bay & Carrauntoohil the highest mountain in Ireland . \n\nThe house was originally built in the 1800 s and was renovated and extended in 2008. The house is now in need of some repairs.\n\nComprising of 121 sq.mts (1,302 sq.ft). GF: Livingroom, sitting room, Kitchen/dining area, bathroom, utility room/parlour: FF: Bedroom (en-suite). There is also a lofted space above the living room area. 33.23 sq.mts (357 sq.ft). This space offers potential for further uses. \n\nThere is a well preserved souterrain in that back yard indicating that this private place has been inhabited for over a 1,000 years \n\nWithin 5.7 km of Sneem Village. 28 km of Kenmare and 45 km to Killarney.\n\nThe access road and parts of the property are in poor condition. Caution to be exercised when viewing the property.\n\nServices: Spring well on site (100 meters south-east of house). Oil central heating. Septic tank. No landline telephone - eircom, radio, telephone service has been used at the house. Electricity currently disconnected. SCC broadband available in the area.\n\n\nGround Floor\n\nLiving Room 8.10m x 4.86m. Large light filled room, patio doors leading to west facing side patio. Timber flooring. Large open fire place. TV point.\n\nSitting Room 3.18m x 3.80m. Old style fireplace.\n\nKitchen/Dining area 4.62m x 3.85m. Rayburn stove fitted into old fire place recess. Belfast sink with storage presses.\n\nUtility Room/Parlour 3.33m x 3.68m. Fitted electric cooker with 4 rings and double oven. Hotpoint washing machine (7.5kg). Hoover dryer. Large American style fridge/freezer. Ceiling recessed spot lights; fitted press units. Stain glass window on eastern gable\n\nBathroom 2.74m X 3.93m.
