    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to offer for sale 23 Fontenoy Street.\n\nThis charming split level Victorian villa style residence has a wonderful homely feel and it has been lovingly maintained by its current owners. Light is something this house has in abundance as it boasts an enviable south facing orientation to the rear and natural light floods this home all day long. The accommodation briefly comprises a welcoming entrance hall with the living room to the left which features beautiful high ceilings, centre rose and ceiling coving. Steps from the hall lead down to the well-appointed kitchen/dining room with access to a private sun drenched south facing city garden and the bathroom is also at this level. Upstairs, there are two fine sized double bedrooms, one with built in wardrobes. The rear garden is maintenance free and is a lovely area for al fresco dining or simply relaxing!\n\nThe location is excellent, Fontenoy Street is a short stroll from Phibsborough Village, with its many shops, restaurants, cafes, and is within comfortable walking distance of the city centre. There is ample permit parking to the front of the property, and the area is well served by public transport including cross-city buses, the Luas and a future MetroLink station. Amenities in the immediate area include the sports grounds in Mt. Bernard Park, Blessington Basin and the Royal Canal Walk. The property is within minutes of the Four Courts, Law Library, King s Inns, Blackhall Place, Mater Hospital, Phoenix Park, Botanic Gardens, Smithfield Village and DIT Grangegorman.\n\nAll in all - an enviable purchase! This elegant property is the perfect choice for those seeking a quiet and mature residence in what is undoubtedly one of Dublin s most central locations\n\n\nEntrance Hall 1.63m x 2.3m.
