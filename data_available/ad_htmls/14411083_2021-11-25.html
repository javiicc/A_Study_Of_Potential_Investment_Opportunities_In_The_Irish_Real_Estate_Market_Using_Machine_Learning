    5 Bedroom Residence displaying Wonderful Characterisics\r\n\r\nThis is a fine Georgian style residence dating back to 1830, commanding a prominent position in this quaint picturesque and historic village of North Leitrim.\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nFull of character and great history attached to this property which once was such a vibrant part of the community hosting a period style residence, Bar and Guinness bottling stores, the spacious buildings are still intact and included with the property.\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nThis gives commercial potential to the property which could lend itself to several ventures or further development. Spacious accommodation includes 5 bedrooms, 2 reception rooms, kitchen, utility, family bathroom and one en-suite.\r\nGood Broadband source is available in the village.\r\n\r\nA little history.\r\n\r\nBuilt in and around 1830 by The Tottenham family, who were the local landlords and lived in the nearby Glenfarne Hall. The village was built by Charles Henry Tottenham in 1831 and named 'Sarahville' to commemorate the death of his daughter Sarah who was killed in a horse riding accident in 1828. He first built the Market House and then built corresponding corner buildings which was when the corner house was established.\r\nThe Corner house has gone through many changes . The current family have owned the property for many decades and created a vibrant business there, including a bar and Guinness bottling plant, which gave great employment to the local community and was a hive of activity down through the years. They managed to maintain it to a high standard which is evident in its presentation today.
