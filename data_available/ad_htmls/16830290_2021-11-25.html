    Colbert & Co estate agents are delighted to offer 2 Hillside to the open market. This magnificent 3 bedroom home was built in the early 1900s and hosts a huge amount of original features. From the moment you walk in through the door with its stained surround glass you get a feel of warmth and history. Whilst the property alone is a great asset, it is the view out over Cork Harbour that is the cherry on top.\r\n\r\nThe beautifully tiled hallway contrasts wonderfully with the original staircase which acts as the center piece to the home. Off the hallway are two large living areas both with fireplaces, perfect for the larger family that require space or the 2nd room could be kept in its current stature as a study.\r\n\r\nTo the back of house you have the kitchen area where the large stone feature wall grabs your attention immediately upon entering. There is a 5 ring gas cooker for chef in the family, ample worktop space and a large island with storage built in. Behind the kitchen is a neatly tucked away utility and accompanying toilet. The dining area is situated next to double French doors that open out on the side garden which is stepped and gives breathtaking views out over East Ferry, Rostellan and Cobh.\r\n\r\nUpstairs you have 3 bedrooms and 2 toilets. Both bedrooms to the front of property have calming views out over the water and large enough to cater for fitted wardrobes. Between these bedrooms is the first of 2 toilets and has a fitted shower which is ideal for guests.\r\nTo the rear of the property the 3rd bedroom has large open space which could be easily used as walk in wardrobe. The main bathroom next door has a fitted bath, basin and toilet.\r\n\r\nLower Aghada and Rostellan are some of the best places to live in East Cork due to their proximity to the water and healthy lifestyle. rarely do properties come to the market in this area, especially with views as unspoilt as this. There are a host of amenities close by including The People Walk which is on your doorstep, The Rostellan Chocolate Factory, Eurospar, Rosies resteraunt, Aghada National School, Aghada GAA, Lower Aghada Tennis Club, The Pier petrol station, Cork Beg AFC, Aghada Triathlon club and The Rostellan Woods which hosts a number of walks, bike tracks and fairy trails. All Primary and secondary schools are serviced via bus from road in front of Hillside.\r\n\r\nThis is a property that has been around since the 1900s and the new owners may decide to add a new twist as it has plenty of space to extend both up at the back and out on the eastern side. There is a full virtual tour available below and viewings can be arranged by calling either of our office in Midleton or Carrigtwohill.\r\n\r\nViewings are strictly by appointment with sole agents Colbert & Co
