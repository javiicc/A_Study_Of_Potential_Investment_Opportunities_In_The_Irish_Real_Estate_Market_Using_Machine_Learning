    6 Mandeville Terrace is a two-storey terraced cottage dating back to the late 1800s, located on Abbey Lane, a quiet street within easy walking distance of the centre of Kinsale town. The property is in need of modernisation and represents an excellent opportunity to put a modern stamp on a character-filled property.\r\n\r\nThe property has a livingroom, kitchen / diningroom and shower-room on the ground floor and two dormer bedrooms upstairs. There is a small yard to the rear which will make an attractive patio garden.\r\n\r\nThe property once renovated will make for an ideal bolt-hole or starter home or down-sizing property for someone wishing to be based within easy walking distance of all Kinsale town has to offer.
