    Gohery Properties are delighted to bring to the market this exceptional, superbly presented 5 bed detached family home which was built to exacting standards in 1995. This 5-bed house extends to c. 127. sq. mts. (1,367 sq. ft.) and exudes quality from start to finish.\r\nThis beautiful family home will be very appealing to the family that needs internal space and large gardens and the flexibility to benefit from top speed broadband allowing you to work from home easily.\r\nLocated in the very popular Ashbrook development in the heart of Oranmore, this family home offers residents unrivalled access to Galway City and all the services in the very progressive village of Oranmore. This home is located on the West side of Oranmore, allowing easy access to Galway city via dual carriageway or coast road as well as a short stroll to the train station allowing easy access to Galway and Dublin.\r\nThe home has spacious and adaptable accommodation including an entrance hall, sitting room, open plan kitchen / Dining, large utility area. The ground floor accommodation is completed by the 2nd reception. / bedroom 5.\r\nTo the first floor we have 4 bedrooms, all very tastefully appointed with the master having an en-suite bathroom and a very well finished main bathroom.\r\nThis house is being presented in show-house condition and has been internally decorated to a very high standard with excellent quality fixtures and fittings. The property is certified with a C 1 BER Certificate, heated by Oil Fired Central Heating and a solid fuel stove in the living area. Double glazed PVC windows feature throughout.\r\nOutside the grounds are mature and manicured with tarmac driveway to the elevation, large cobble lock patio to rear of the house and a large side garden which allows huge potential to extend or possibly allow for further development (perhaps of a second residence - Subject to appropriate planning permission being obtained)\r\nWe recommend early viewing of this property to appreciate the level of finish, attention to detail and the many extras this beautiful family home has to offer. This is a rare opportunity to acquire a really special home and is in fact, the first detached home to come for resale in this development since 1995!!!
