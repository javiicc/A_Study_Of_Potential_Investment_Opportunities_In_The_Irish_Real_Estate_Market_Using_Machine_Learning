    Superly located on the sunny side of this exceptionally convenient and sought-after cul de sac, this very well presented home provides 1,470 sq. ft./136 sq. m. (approx.) of wonderfully bright accommodation, with excellent off-street parking to the front and a lovely 70ft (approx) long sun-drenched back garden.\n\nIn the same ownership since it was built in c. 1957, the property, which was originally built as a 5 bedroomed family residence with garage to the side, has in more recent years been rearranged as a 4 bedroomed family residence with bathroom and shower room. The garage has been converted to a cosy TV/Family Room or Home Office.\nSituated on this lovely quiet, tree-lined cul de sac, at the Ballsbridge end of Nutley Lane, the property enjoys an exceptionally convenient location, with every possible amenity within easy reach, including Merrion Shopping Centre, St. Michael s School and St. Vincent s Hospital, all of which are located at the end of the road.\nOther amenities located in Ballsbridge and Donnybrook include Elm Park Golf Club, numerous specialty shops, cafes, restaurants, the R.D.S. and hotels. Other schools within walking distance include The Teresians, Colaiste Iosagain, Colaiste Eoin and Muckross Park. The extensive recreational and educational facilities of UCD Belfield are also within a pleasant stroll, as is Sandymount Strand.\nAll in all, this is a wonderful opportunity to acquire a delightful family home with a lovely sunny back garden, in this superbly convenient and quiet cul de sac setting.\n\n\nEntrance Hall: 7ft 2in x 18ft 4in. welcoming bright and spacious entrance hall with ceiling coving.\n\nGuest w.c.: 3ft x 5ft. with w.c. and wash hand basin, part tiled walls.\n\nLiving Room: 11ft 10in x 13ft x 4in. overlooking the front garden, this lovely reception room is flooded with natural light. Tiled fireplace with open fire, ceiling coving and sliding doors to the dining room.\n\nDining Room: 11ft 10in x 11ft 8in. overlooking the back garden, ceiling coving and patio doors to the garden.\n\n\nBedroom 4: 9ft x 9ft. superb bright single bedroom with built in drawers and study desk. Floor to ceiling built in storage presses, part wood panelled walls. Enclosed side passage with built in presses, sink unit and is plumbed for a washing machine. Access to storage shed at the rear and doors to the front and back gardens.\n\nUPSTAIRS: Spacious landing with access to the attic.\n\nMain Bedroom: 11ft x 16ft. spacious double bedroom overlooking the front garden.
