This 4-bedroom, semi-detached bungalow is located in a mature residential area of bustling Crumlin Village.\n\nWith excellent potential for modernisation and extension, and plenty of secure off-street parking, this 1930 build is just five minutes' walk from Our Lady's Children's Hospital, William Pearse Park and a host of local amenities. The ideal home for a family to put their very own stamp on; it would also suit a buy to let investor as the property's rent could be set to current market rates. \n\nBoasting a well-proportioned front garden and a generous rear garden space, no.3 is also well-served by public transport including the 18, 27 and 150 bus routes. \n\nThe property briefly comprises a porch and long entrance hall; a spacious, inviting living room; an elegant dining room (currently in use as a living space) with double doors connecting to the kitchen; a bright spacious kitchen/diner with laminate countertops, ample cabinet space and a back door leading out to the rear garden. The property features four bedrooms (2 single, 2 double) which are bright and well-proportioned - 2 of which include in-built wardrobes/storage. The master bedroom includes a fully tiled ensuite with electric shower. The main bathroom is also fully tiled and includes a shower/bath.\n\nA superb location with an excellent local community, no.3 Crumlin Village is a short distance from a host of shops, restaurants, schools, parks and sports facilities. Crumlin is also within easy reach of the M7, M50 and is just 5km from Dublin City Centre.
