    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Home & Away Real Estate are delighted to showcase this exceptional 4 bed detached property situated on a beautiful private site, nestled in the rugged surroundings of rural East Cork, with splendid coastal views. This impressive four bedroomed family home is located in Ardnahinch, Shanagarry within walking distance of the stunning beach of Ardnahinch thus offering an idyllic lifestyle for those wishing to move to the area. The popular village of Shanagarry offers a wide range of amenities including shops, restaurants and pubs. This East Cork little village is a short drive from the lovely seaside town of Ballycotton and Garryvoe, while Midleton is just a 15-minute drive away. Presented in immaculate condition, viewing to this property is highly recommended. It is evident that this home has been well loved and cared for and finished to a very high standard. The open entrance hall immediately creating an impression. This home enjoys spacious living with the living room opening directly to the kitchen/dining, both rooms flooded with natural light. The exposed beams are a real feature in the kitchen/dining with double doors leading out to garden decking for that extra space. The double height ceiling in the living room is a real luxury with a free-standing stove as the main features. Conveniently there are two bedrooms downstairs. One with an ensuite. Upstairs there are two more bedrooms, one with ensuite. Family bathroom and Office space also upstairs. Outside there is a large garden to the rear with a garden shed for extra storage. This property offers the opportunity for a beautiful family home in a vibrant coastal village with an active community. This is an ideal opportunity for those looking for a home in a peaceful setting within walking distance of the sea, yet only 15 minutes from the thriving town of Midleton. Rarely do houses of this quality come on the market.
