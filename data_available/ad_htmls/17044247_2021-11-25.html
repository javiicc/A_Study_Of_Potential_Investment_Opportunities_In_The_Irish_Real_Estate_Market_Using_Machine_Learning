    DNG is pleased to present 16 Rochfort House to the market. The apartment is presented in top class condition and is turn-key ready. It offers generous accommodation of c. 85 sqm. which includes an entrance hall, living room / dining room, kitchen, 2 double bedrooms (master ensuite) and a bathroom. A designated car space is complemented by numerous visitor spaces, again all within the communal grounds.\n\nCabinteely is one of south Dublin's more picturesque villages in the heart of this leafy suburb. In effect the village itself is part of a cul de sac which offers a quaint library, a handful of trendy eateries, the Horse & Hound public house and a Garda Station. For those familiar with Cabinteely you will know there is a strong and vibrant community here along with a very active Tidy Towns committee keeping the village and surrounds looking their best all year around.\n\nWithin the village is also the exclusive development known as Brennanstown. Built by Tudor Homes, this attractive, low density development is set around acres of landscaped grounds and provides a much sought after place to live whether you're just starting out, a family with children or looking to downsize from the locality or neighbouring suburbs such as Foxrock, Blackrock or similar, such is the flexibility of the type of homes in Brennanstown.\n\nThe location of course is the biggest draw. Served by bus and Luas, access to the city and its surrounds is easy and frequent. The N11 & M50 are also convenient. Cabinteely Park is one of South Dublins most favoured attractions with childrens play areas, acres of parklands, paved walkways and a period residence open to he public for various historical talks and its famous tea rooms. Dunnes of Cornelscourt is one of Irelands most impressive grocery stores where locals can avail of the impressive array of groceries and convenience stores, all with free parking.\n\n\nFEATURES\nStunning two bed apartment \nPresented in tun-key condition \nModern fully fitted kitchen\nApprox. 79 sq.m / 850 sq.ft. \nWell-appointed and stylish apartment.\n1 designated parking along with visitor parking \nGFCH & double glazed throughout \nWalking distance to Cabinteely Village and public transport.\nOverlooks manicured landscaped gardens
