    Whitethorn is located in a quiet and peaceful setting standing on circa 8 acres / 3.3 hectares of gardens and woodland. Built in 2004 of masonry construction and extending to 232 sqm/2497 sq ft, this property has a mature whitethorn hedgerow to the front boundary and a gravel driveway. This charming property offers a great sense of balance, a modern property with a rustic farmhouse feel. The old has been blended with the new which creates an informal cosy and cocooning effect. There are Larch handmade interior doors with old style black metal knobs, natural wood flooring, terracota floor tiles, inframed custom made kitchen which are just a few of the finishes which reflect the style of the house. The house sits comfortably with the land, which has a detached garage and a winding pathway through the woods which is approx. 1km, over a little wooden bridge. \nThe bright accommodation comprises of entrance hall, sittingroom, study, open plan kitchen/dining/living, ground floor bedroom / playroom and bathroom.
