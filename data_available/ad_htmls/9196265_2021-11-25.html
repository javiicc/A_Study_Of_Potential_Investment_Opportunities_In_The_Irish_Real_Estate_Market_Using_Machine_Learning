Whitethorn Village is positioned adjacent to the centre of Naas, a bustling town with an abundance of amenities for families of all ages. Several retail parks and a shopping centre are located on the fringes of the town, while for commuters, a new junction on the M7 at Naas is due to open in late 2020 allowing quick access to Sallins & Clane and linking up to the M4.\r\n \r\nFamilies can choose from a host of well-established primary schools and secondary schools, while several cr\xc3\xa8che and pre-school options are also available in the neighbourhood. And with Whitethorn Village being in such a central location, many schools and leisure facilities are within walking distance.\r\n \r\nThe spacious homes in Whitethorn Village are built with the rigours of modern family life in mind. A thoughtful layout with a large and well-equipped kitchen as the centre of the home gives plenty of room for family get togethers, while energy-efficient features ensure that these homes are as practical and cost-efficient as they are beautiful. Plenty of storage space throughout keeps clutter to a minimum - a must for any busy family. Much thought is also given to green space outside the home, both public and private, giving residents the opportunity to enjoy the outdoors even in such a convenient location.
