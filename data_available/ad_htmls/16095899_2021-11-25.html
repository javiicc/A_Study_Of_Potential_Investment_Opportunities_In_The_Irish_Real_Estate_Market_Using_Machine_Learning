    Peter Wyse is delighted to present this bright spacious third floor Apartment to the market. This impressive top floor two bedroom apartment is ideally situated in this highly desirable development in the heart of Georgian Dublin. Practically designed, the accommodation comprises of an entrance hall, a bright and spacious living/dining room, kitchen, two double bedrooms, en-suite and a separate family bathroom completing the accommodation. \n\nSituated in the heart of Dublin 2, the convenience of the location cannot be overstated, being within minutes' walk of St Stephens Green. A superb choice of amenities are on your doorstep including The Iveagh Gardens, Grafton Street, not to mention a superb choice of restaurants and coffee shops.
