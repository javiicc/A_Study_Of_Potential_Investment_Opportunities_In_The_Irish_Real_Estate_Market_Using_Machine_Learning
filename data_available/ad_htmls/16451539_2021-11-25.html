    Seeking offers in excess of 350,000.\nA taste of country life only 3km south of The Talbot Hotel in Wexford town!!\nSt Michaels is a 131 sqm (not including the garage) detached split-level bungalow in this great location in Wexford Town, only 3.5 km to Wexford quayside and 2.3 km to join the N25 Rosslare Road at Drinagh. The property is on a slightly elevated site, south facing to the front, standing on 1.14 acres with Wexford Golf Course to the rear and only a few minutes drive to Johnstown Castle and Gardens.\nThis is not a standard bungalow by any means. The internal layout is very unusual and while ready to move into, this is a home that could be adapted in many ways to make it fit your lifestyle. In its current layout there are 3 reception rooms, a small kitchen with utility (north facing) and in the raised part of the house three bedrooms (the south facing room has an ensuite) and family bathroom.\nThe living accommodation is south and west facing with the bedrooms on the east facing side of the house so the sun pours into the living areas during the day while you can enjoy the sun setting from the living room with curved glazed wall to the front.\nAdded to this there is a garage (accessed from the outside) with masses of storage space, a lovely south facing decked area, and patio area to the rear.\nSt Michaels is a rare find not just for what and where it is but what it could be. The house is ready to move into giving you time to consider how you would mould it to suit your lifestyle.\nSeptic tank (new soakaway installed 2018), mains water, OFCH, South-facing to the front, garage, boiler house to the rear, 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms. Walls pumped and attic insulated. Folio WX17373. Built approx. 1975\nSubject to planning, the plot could be further developed to add new accommodation on the site.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 1.70m x 2.40m. with linoleum flooring\n\nLiving Room 7.10m x 4.30m. Bright room with half-glazed curved wall facing south. Carpet flooring, feature open fireplace and west facing window\n\nSitting Room 3.50m x 3.40m. snug room facing the rear (north) of the house. With carpet flooring, feature open fireplace with brick surround and built in shelving\n\nDining Room 4.10m x 4.80m. Runs along the south facing front, with solid wood flooring and steps to bedroom accommodation\n\nKitchen 2.40m x 3.40m. with linoleum flooring, fitted kitchen units, tile backsplash, electric hob, electric oven and integrated dishwasher\n\nUtility Room 1.60m x 0.80m. with linoleum flooring and plumbed for washing machine and dryer\n\nBathroom 1.60m x 2.60m. with tile flooring and bath, bath, WC and wash hand basin\n\nBedroom 1 2.70m x 2.60m. with carpet flooring\n\nBedroom 2 3.00m x 3.50m. with carpet flooring\n\nMaster Bedroom 4.10m x 4.60m. with carpet flooring and walk-in closet\n\nEn-Suite 0.70m x 2.20m. with tile flooring and shower, shower, WC and wash hand basin\n\nGarage 9.30m x 4.70m. fantastic addition, with roll-over door and concrete flooring\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom Wexford (at the Talbot Hotel) continue south for approx 2km (towards the N25).
