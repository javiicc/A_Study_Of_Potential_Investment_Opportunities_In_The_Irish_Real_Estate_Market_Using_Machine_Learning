    Development Description

      Please note, all homes in The Heath are now sale agreed.\r\n\r\nNestled between local parks and a thriving community, The Heath offers a location that is tranquil but connected. Designed by Allies & Morrison LLP Architects, the 4- bedroom detached, semi-detached and terraced homes at The Heath feature an elegant mix of brick and render exteriors, boast thoughtfully laid out interiors and are built with modern day living in mind.\r\n\r\nWith an exceptional specification, we are confident that the homes in The Heath will exceed your expectations. Brilliantly laid out over three floors and with two well-appointed reception rooms which are ideally on separate floors, it is hard to find faults in these modern family homes. The houses are set back from the road with landscaped gardens to the front and benefit from 2 parking spaces. Each of the houses feature a fully fitted, Kube kitchen with appliances including a fridge-freezer, electric oven and dishwasher. Appliances are provided subject to signed contracts being returned within 28 days (from issue). All bathrooms and en-suite bathrooms will be fitted with high quality sanitary ware throughout, including chrome heated towel rails and feature stylish tiling fitted to the wet areas. Each bedroom comes complete with luxurious fitted wardrobes. In addition, you will enjoy high levels of comfort at a low cost, due to the energy efficient and well insulated design, and an air to water heat pump system which will directly reduce monthly energy bills.\r\n\r\nPlease register your interest on www.theheath.ie or contact Savills New Homes on 01 618 1300 for further information.

      Location

      Perfectly situated between Airlie Park and Tandy\xe2\x80\x99s Lane Park, the Heath at Adamstown boasts close proximity to several excellent educational facilities, while Adamstown train station is just minutes away, providing quick and easy access to the city centre and beyond. The area is also very well serviced by Dublin Bus and within easy access to the M50, N4 and N7. \r\n \r\nAdamstown itself, is a vibrant community that offers an array of local amenities. There are numerous sports clubs including Adamstown Cycling Club and Cricket Club. The historic village of Lucan and its surrounding environs is nearby and offers many amenities ranging from sporting pursuits such as Golf, GAA and rugby clubs to a wide range of dining experiences - there\xe2\x80\x99s something to suit everyone\xe2\x80\x99s tastes.\r\n \r\nThe Heath is a few minutes\xe2\x80\x99 drive from Lucan Shopping Centre which caters for all your family\xe2\x80\x99s needs. It includes a SuperValu, Dunnes Stores and a host of eateries from Base Pizza to Mao, a pharmacy, hairdressers and beauty salon. Also close by is one of Ireland\xe2\x80\x99s largest shopping centres, Liffey Valley, home to a vast array of high street stores, a multiplex cinema and many restaurants and cafes all within a 10-minute drive.
