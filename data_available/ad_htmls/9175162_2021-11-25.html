    Development Description

      Savills New Homes and Quintain are delighted to share that the 4 show houses in Somerton are now for sale. \r\n\r\nSet off the Newcastle Road in Lucan, the Somerton show houses offer an attractive and varied mix of terraced and detached 3 & 4 bedroom house types ranging in size from 1,130 sq ft to 1,883 sq ft. These A rated homes are beautifully proportioned and enjoy light, bright and spacious interiors. Designed by renowned architects Duignan Dooley, the show homes in Somerton are constructed to the highest standard, with high quality finishes throughout and the latest technology in heating and energy efficiency.\r\n\r\nThese BER A2 rated homes enjoy high levels of insulation and airtightness and will be heated by highly efficient air to water heat pumps to ensure ambient room temperatures year-round and reduced energy bills. The landscaped amenity spaces have been designed to maximize purchasers' enjoyment of their environment and future residents will benefit from the onsite playground and proximity to the newly opened Tandy's Lane Park. \r\n\r\nThe area is well connected with excellent transport links. Adamstown train station is less than 20 minutes' walk away, the N4 takes you in to Dublin City Centre and the M50 connects you to the rest of the city and beyond including Dublin Airport which is a mere 25 minutes' drive. \r\n\r\nThere is an abundance of shopping opportunities for Somerton residents as it is conveniently located moments from Lucan Shopping Centre and the newly built Lidl which is 5 minutes' drive away as well as one of Ireland's largest shopping centres, Liffey Valley Shopping Centre with over 80 stores and a Vue Cinema. In addition, Quintain will shortly begin work on Adamstown Station which will see the delivery of an established Urban Core for Adamstown along with over 975 residential units. This commercial hub will provide 11,000 sqm of retail accommodation comprising two large grocery stores, gym, cafes, restaurants, and neighbourhood retailers.\r\n \r\nFor further information or to arrange a viewing, please contact Savills New Homes on 01 618 1300.
