    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 209 m\xc2\xb2
    This property is situated in the prestigious Ardbrack area in Kinsale and offers vistas to Kinsale harbour. The property is within walking distance to Kinsale town and is located in a small estate of only a handful of houses. This is a stunning architecturally designed contemporary home in a wonderful location.\n\nThe house offers a wealth of space on a total floor area of c. 209 sqm. All the living areas are located on the first floor with the bedrooms on the ground floor. There is a modern open plan living area which comprises of a living, dining cum kitchen area. There are large floor to ceiling windows providing wonderful light and views and a balcony off the kitchen. The contemporary kitchen offers modern appliances and just off the kitchen is a separate utility room leading into a cloakroom with wc. Glazed sliding doors lead onto the landing and into a spacious sitting room with vaulted ceilings and large sliding windows to take advantage of the southerly aspect and to provide plenty of natural light. Just down the beautiful stairwell, on the ground floor there are three double bedrooms, two of them en-suite and with walk-in wardrobes. There is also a large family bathroom with a jacuzzi bath. \n\nAll rooms throughout the house have hardwood floors and the bathrooms are fully tiled. The house is highly energy efficient with a heat pump, underfloor heating throughout, heat recovery system and triple glazed windows.\n\nThere is a lawn area to the front of the house, patio areas off two of the bedrooms and parking for two vehicles.
