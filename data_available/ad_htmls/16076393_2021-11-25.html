    AT LAST YOUR LOCATION IS HERE. We introduce Battery View which is located on Battery Road, Longford in a highly sought after area. This deceptively spacious 4 bedroom 2 storey house of approx. 1650 sq ft has recently been renovated and re-decorated to an exceptional high standard from top to bottom and is just a short leisurely stroll into town.\r\n\r\nThe accommodation consists of a tiled entrance hall leading to the family room which has a semi solid wood floor. To the left of the hallway is a bright living room with a semi solid wood floor and to the rear of the house is a bright new modern kitchen and dining area which are tiled. From the dining area is a door leading to the utility room and a bathroom which has a low-level rain shower. Also from the utility room is a door which leads outside to a small patio area. Upstairs: There are 4 elegant double bedrooms of which 2 rooms are en-suite with showers and tiled. All the bedrooms are carpeted throughout. The main bathroom is fully tiled. Features include: OFCH, wired for electric car charger, recess lighting, double glazed windows and doors. Outside there is a large garden and patio area of approx. 430 sq ft. This property is serviced by mains water and mains sewerage.\r\n\r\nIt is indeed a very rare find to have one of these houses on the market as it is an absolute gem and the location is most enviable.
