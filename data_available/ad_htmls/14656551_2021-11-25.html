    Sheen Falls Country Club is located on the grounds of the worlds renowned Sheen Falls Estate in the picturesque village of Kenmare. This development of luxury dwellings comprises of 4 bedroom detached houses ranging in size from 165 to 230 sq meters. \r\nMountain View is sited on the elevated meadows and mountainside overlooking Sheen Falls Country Club. The houses in Mountain View are orientated to maximise the stunning views from its elevated position while creating protected south- west facing terraces.\r\nBeing an exclusive member of a stylish country club means that pursuits such as shooting, fishing, horse-riding, croquet, tennis and swimming, as well as a luxurious health club are available to you on your doorstep.\r\nMembership to Sheen Falls Country Club is exclusive to the owners of the houses in this exceptional development. This is not only about having a luxurious home, this is equally about having the pleasure of all the amenities, facilities and services already offered by Sheen Falls Lodge, located in one of the most breathtaking parts of Ireland.\r\nSOFT FURNISHING IMAGES ARE INDICATIVE, HOUSE IS CURRENTLY UNFURNISHED EXCEPT AS OUTLINED BELOW.

    Property Features

    • Large 4 bedroom, all en-suite, detached house. Set in a secure gated Country Club in a woodland setting.
    • High end finishes throughout - Marble & Limestone tiles, Solid timber stairs and Oak flooring. Bedrooms carpeted.
    • Top of the range bespoke kitchen complete with Neff appliances.
    • Large terrace which surrounds the living and dining rooms and the ground floor bedroom, with Sandstone paving.
