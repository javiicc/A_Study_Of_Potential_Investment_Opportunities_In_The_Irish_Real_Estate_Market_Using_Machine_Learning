    The landscape of south County Wexford is often referred to as a special place of low lands and high skies, and Ballyregan House has to be one of its treasured jewels.\nThis 311 sq m house is set in these fertile surrounds only 20 minutes drive from Wexford town and Rosslare Harbour, our gateway to Europe, and less than 2 hours from Dublin Airport.\nIt is a period house said to date from the early 19th Century in the local style that stands on a peaceful setting, resonant for an atmosphere of timeless quality. The property has been in one family for many generations, and has played its part in Irish history, having been a safe house in 1922 and hosting visitors such as Rory O Connor and Jack Yeats.\nOne hundred years later, Ballyregan House still charms visitors with its inherent sense of unique peace and place, and an inner calm and tranquillity. Today, it truly is a place that you will look forward to coming home to.\nThe house is set well back from the road on a 4.8 acre plot, surrounded by low maintenance gardens, handsome trees, paddocks and lawns, and is approached by a sweeping gravel front drive. In keeping with houses of the period. A rear drive serves the house, old farmyard and outbuildings.\nThe house has been well minded and is laid out over 311 sqm to provide three spacious elegant reception rooms, a modern kitchen and living room, 6 bedrooms (2 ensuite) together with the ancillary stores attic and so on. It is a wonderful family home not just of itself, but also by virtue of its setting with the gardens and atmospheric rear yard buildings.\nThe original farmyard behind the house is set around a sunny courtyard with a range of lofted stores and sheds, former stables and byres, some of which are stone walled and others cob walled. These buildings have seen many changes over the life of the house and are calling out for a new owner to make their mark. \nIf you have been dreaming about a move to a country setting, where you want to enjoy life in a property that is large but manageable, that is unique, that is private and yet not isolated, the arrival of Ballyregan House to the market is presenting you with a remarkable opportunity and answer to your search. This is an opportunity that needs to be grasped.\nViewings strictly by prior appointment. Usual Covid qualifications apply.\nBER exempt, OFCH through Stanley range. Septic tank, folio no. WX6423.
