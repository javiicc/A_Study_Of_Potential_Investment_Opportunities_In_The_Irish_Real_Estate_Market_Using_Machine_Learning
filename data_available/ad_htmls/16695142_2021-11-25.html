    DNG are delighted to presented this excellent two bedroom home in the ever popular pocket of Firhouse, No. 20 Old Court Lodge. It has been fully refurbished by its current owner to include upgraded kitchen and bathroom, solid fuel stove, internal insulation and a tasteful decor throughout. The well proportioned rooms provide ample space for day to day living. This property would be an ideal starter home or suitable those wishing to downsize from a larger style property. Accommodation briefly comprises entrance hallway, living room, kitchen / diner, 2 spacious double bedrooms and family bathroom. Outside to the rear there is a private garden which is not overlooked, stretching c.41 ft and is ideal for entertaining guests or dining alfresco in the summer months. \n\nOld Court enjoys the best of both worlds, away from the hustle and bustle while still enjoying access to every conceivable amenity. Conveniently located within close proximity of a wide range of shopping centres, local shops, primary and secondary schools and the M50 network.
