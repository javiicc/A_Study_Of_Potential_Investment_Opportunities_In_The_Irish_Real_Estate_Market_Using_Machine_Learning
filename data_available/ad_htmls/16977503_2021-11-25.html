    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Bruac Lan Cottage is the epitome of modern design and while designed as a Cottage from street level once you pass the hall door the accommodation takes full advantage of the bright elevated site on which the property sits. It is located on a country land within 500m of Moycullen town centre just off the Mountain Road with only one house located beyond Bruac Lan cottage. Located in a semi-urban setting the proeprty overlooks agricultural land and has a lovely view of Moycullen and Lough corrib beyond.\n\nThe property offers accommodation over two floors with the ground floor accommodation introduced by a welcoming entrance hall with open cloakroom, Large open plan Kitchen/Sitting/Dining Room with solid oak flooring opening to a modern Cucina fitted kitchen with corian work surface and integrated appliances. The fireplace comprises an in-built stove at an elevated level on a Liscannor Hearth, beyond this is a large utility with rear external access. Also at ground floor level is a separate Lounge/office showerroom amd bedroom along with understairs storage.\n\nAt first floor level the accommodation comprises three double bedrooms all of which are en-suite. The master bedroom has exceptional views over Moycullen village and Lough Corrib beyond. It has a double vanity unit, double shower and over sized bat. all bedrooms have porcelain tiles.\n\nThe finish in the house is to the highest standards with solid oak joinery throughout. The property was built to a modern eco aware standard and includes Geo-thermal heating, heat recovery ventilation system, 200mm Hygroscopic sheepswool insulation and an Intello air tight membrane.\n\nExternally the site is finished in a maintenance free fashion with a large deck surveying a planted garden separated by a pebbled rear garden. the garden has been professionally landscaped and has the benefit of a garden shed and seperate wild garden to the rear.\n\nGround Floor\n\nEntrance Hall - 1.03m (3'5") x 3.01m (9'11")\nBright Hallway featuring porcelain tiles, cloakroom and under stair storage.\n\nTV Room/Home Office - 3.03m (9'11") x 4.09m (13'5")\nTiled porcelain floors, double doors to rear patio and recessed spot lights.\n\n\nKitchen/Dining/Sitting Room - 11.06m (36'3") x 8.03m (26'4")\nOpen plan with solid timber floors, a solid fuel stove and feature fire place. Full length doors to rear deck.
