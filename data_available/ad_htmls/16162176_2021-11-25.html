    This 2-bedroom detached cottage has picturesque views of the Wicklow mountains and the Wicklow Harbour. It is located within walking distance of Wicklow Town centre with all its amenities and a 4 minute walk from the train station which runs daily communtes to and from Dublin. The cottage holds unique charm and opputunity as it is one of three cottages built along the Murragh.\r\nThis recently renovated property has the possibility to be a holiday home, Air & B or forever coastal side home. \r\nWicklow town has much to offer as it is surrounded by the sea and Wicklow Mountains. It boasts some of the best educational facilities in Ireland. It also has a fantastic family and community spirit, enlivened by the annual Wicklow Regatta, Taste of Wicklow event and numerous social, community and sporting groups and associations. Living on the coast you will enjoy a wide variety of activities on your doorstep including fishing, windsurfing, swimming, scuba diving, kayaking, hill walking, golf and tennis, all these activities are within walking distance of the property. Wicklow town also hosts a large range of shopping facilities from the larger supermarkets to craft butchers, banks, cafes and restaurants. \r\nWicklow also has regular commuter bus and train services to Dublin and Co.
