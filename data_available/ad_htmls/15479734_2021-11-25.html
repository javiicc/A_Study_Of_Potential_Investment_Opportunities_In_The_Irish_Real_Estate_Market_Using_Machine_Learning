    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    Located on the largest Island on the majestic River Shannon A house sitting on an acre of land on Inchmore Island on Lough Ree. \r\nThe original house is built from local stone with concrete floors downstairs & timber floors on the first floor. The roof is slate and in sound condition. The windows are timber sash windows. The house is a two up two down with a large ground floor extension on the side. There are two outhouses, one for firewood and one for the compost loo. The side extension built in the 1970s is formed of timber with an asphalt flat roof. \r\nThe house is off-grid, so there is no electrical supply or water connection. Cooking can be done using the large open fire which is how everything was done historically or there is a gas cooker for convenience. \r\nA small rainwater collection tank supply, the sink water can be collected from the lake but it is not potable. \r\nThe house sits on the rear of an acre of land which is mostly cleared and is protected by chestnut, sycamore & native sally trees. \r\n\r\nThere is a small public harbor to land your boat on, from the shore, it is a short walk to the house. \r\n\r\n For further details and videos google: www.islandhouse.site
