    James L. Murtagh Auctioneers are delighted to bring this detached property formerly known as "Little Smiles", Coole to the market. \r\n\r\nThe property comprises of a single storey, detached dwelling set on a private c.0.4 acres site. The property comes in excellent condition throughout having recently been extended. Accommodation consists of a bright and spacious open plan kitchen/living/ dining area with double doors leading to the rear garden, utility, study/office, 4 bedrooms (1 ensuite) and bathroom. There is a private canopy/patio area leading from a bedroom with access to the large rear, landscaped gardens. \r\n\r\nThe property is located on the R395 in Coole village, in the picturesque area of North County Westmeath. Located within close proximity of Castlepollard and Multyfarmham, both providing an array of essential and social facilities such as schools, medical centre/GP, pharmacies, newsagents etc. Additional local recreational amenities includeTullynally Castle, Mullaghmeen Woods, Fore Abbey and Lough Derravaragh. \r\n\r\nInspection of this property is highly recommended - by appointment only
