    Ref: P5207\r\n \r\nCorcannon, Inch, Gorey, Co Wexford \r\n \r\nWell Located c. 1.5 Acre Site (S.T.P.P.) For Sale By Private Treaty \r\n\r\n \r\nLOCATION: \r\nThis site is located along the Kilanerin road, just 1km off the R772 at Toss Byrne's pub at Inch with the popular Canavan\xe2\x82\xacTM Londis Convenient Store & filling station within walking distance of the site. It is 5km from Gorey and 4km from Coolgreany with local primary school. Gorey is one of north Wexford\xe2\x82\xacTM most noted towns and offers an excellent choice of schools in primary, secondary, post leaving and adult education, Educate Together and a Gaelscoil, along with a wealth, restaurants, shops, pubs, award winning hotels, a vast array of local leisure amenities such as endless sandy beaches, golf courses (Ballymoney and Courtown), swimming and leisure centres making it a most desirable place to live. Wexford is a 30 minute drive, Enniscorthy a 20 minute drive while Dublin is a comfortable commute of one hour.\r\n \r\nDESCRIPTION: \r\nThe site has mature natural boundaries and is bounded on one side by the Inch River, it has the benefit of good road frontage. This particular site had planning permission for a residence some time ago which has since lapsed. It is being sold subject to planning permission and a Local Needs clause will most likely apply to anyone looking for planning permission.\r\n \r\nDIRECTIONS: \r\nFrom Gorey take the Old Dublin Road (R772), continue for approximately 7km to Inch. Just before Toss Byrnes Pub, take a left turn (signposted for Kilanerin (L1001). The site is 1km up on your left hand side with a QUINN PROPERTY signboard.
