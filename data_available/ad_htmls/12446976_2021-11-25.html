This location has excellent transport links including the numerous bus routes to surrounding towns and Sutton Dart station a mere 7 minute walk away. Designed by the highly regarded Tyler Owens Architect, this spacious 400m2 home boasts the very best in contemporary design. Arranged over 3 floors with basement, the high specification finish is complimented by ample car parking and provides the owner with all the amenities of a dream home. The well-proportioned accommodation provides 5 light filled bedrooms (2 en-suite), separate sitting room and a generous open plan kitchen/ diner/living room opening to a patio area and a large rear garden. There is a beautiful south-facing terrace off the master bedroom's private terrace enjoying stunning views over Dublin bay and city.\n\n\nThe location of this development site is second to none, just a short stroll from Sutton village and a 20-minute drive to Dublin airport. The exclusive coastal village of Sutton is located 7 miles from Dublin City Centre and also just 7 miles from Dublin airport. With many famous inhabitants, Sutton is one of Dublin's most sought after locations with the residents enjoying village life whilst benefiting from the amenities of a town. The property is adjacent to one of the most prestigious tennis clubs in the country, Sutton Lawn Tennis Club, which offers state of the art facilities including both indoor and outdoor tennis courts, squash courts, snooker tables and a gym. There is an array of excellent primary and secondary schools in the area including Sutton Park School just a 3-minute drive away, widely renowned as the premier international school in Ireland.
