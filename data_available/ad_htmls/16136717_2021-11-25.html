    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    McGuirk Beggan Property is thrilled to present No.259 Templeogue Road to the market; a wonderfully spacious four-bedroom detached family home. The property also has the added benefit of a garage, offering ample potential to extend and convert. Ideally located in the heart of Templeogue Village it is a short stroll from a wealth of local amenities and well-established schools. The property extends to an impressive 139.35sq.m/1,500sq.ft. (including garage area) and although in need of modernisation it offers tremendous scope to create a fine home with everything a growing family could possibly wish for. To the front of the property there is a driveway providing off street parking for 2 cars, it is also laid in lawn with mature planting shielding the property from the main road. There is a wonderfully private rear garden which is bursting with an array of mature shrubbery & trees. It is laid mostly in lawn and there is also a paved patio area. \r\n\r\nThe location is an unrivalled one; Templeogue Village has a fantastic array of amenities to hand including Rathfarnham S.C. with Tesco's and Rathfarnham and Terenure Villages are very close by with all they have to offer. There are ample recreational facilities in the area with Templeogue Tennis Club, Bushy & Tymon Park on your doorstep and Marlay Park a short drive away. The property is also in close walking distance to a selection of well-established schools such as Our Ladys, Terenure & Templeogue College and St Pius X. The area is well serviced by frequent and dependable bus routes and the property also enjoys the added bonus of easy access to the M50 motorway network. \r\n\r\nThe accommodation briefly comprises large entrance porch, entrance hall, living room, dining room, kitchen, conservatory, 4 generous size bedrooms, bathroom, garage, downstairs W.C. and storage shed & covered over side passage. \r\n\r\nAll in all, this is a fantastic opportunity to acquire a fine property in a prime location offering boundless potential to create your dream home with all the necessary requirements needed for a growing family!\r\n\r\n\r\nNotice\r\nPlease note we have not tested any apparatus, fixtures, fittings, or services.
