    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to present 273 Navan Road to the market. This fine, double fronted, semi-detached residence with side garage has been very well maintained with large reception rooms and ample bedroom accommodation. High ceilings, well-proportioned bright rooms, original fireplaces are but a few special features to mention. The property also provides the discerning purchaser with the opportunity to further extend (subject to planning permission).\n\nThe property encompasses approx. 158 sq m of bright and spacious accommodation which includes a welcoming hall with understairs storage, living room, family room, dining room, kitchen, breakfast room and utility with garden access. There is also a downstairs WC and the internal garage could be converted into the main house if further reception rooms are needed. Upstairs there are three double bedrooms, and 2 x single bedrooms, shower room, bathroom and separate wc. The property also benefits from a large rear garden and off street parking to the front.\n\nA Navan Road address is held in high regard due to its variety of solid houses, mature location and close proximity to the Phoenix Park and the city centre. The immediate vicinity includes an array of local shops, renowned schools, and a host of sports and leisure clubs. In addition, there are regular bus routes which will provide access to and from the City Centre while the M50 is easily accessible from the Navan Road as is Blanchardstown Shopping Centre and Dublin Airport. There are also 2 x train stations within walking distance at Ashtown Station and the new Pelletstown Station .\n\n\nEntrance Porch Attractive brick arch leading to tiled porch.\n\nEntrance Hall Wide entrance hall with understairs storage and built in cloak closet.\n\nLiving Room With feature Bay window, period fireplace with tiled inset. Picture rail and coving. Double doors lead to TV room.\n\nFamily Room Feature period fireplace with tiled inset, picture rail and decorative coving, arch to dining room.\n\nDining room Overlooking the rear garden, door to kitchen, decorative coving.\n\nKitchen Fully fitted Oak kitchen with a good selection of floor and eye level presses. Integrated extractor fan, plumbed for dishwasher, arch to breakfast area.\n\nBreakfast area Ideal space for a seated dining area, feature tiled fireplace.\n\nRear lobby Access to understairs storage.\n\nWC Comprising of wc and wash hand basin, tiled floor and partly tiled walls.\n\nUtility Room With fitted work counter and presses, Plumbed for washing machine. Door to rear garden.\n\nLanding With access hatch to attic, hotpress.\n\nMain Bedroom Located to the front of the house large double bedroom with feature Bay window, period fireplace with tiled inset.\n\nBedroom 2 Located to the rear of the house double bedroom with period fireplace with tiled inset.\n\nBedroom 3 Located to the rear of the house single bedroom with period fireplace with tiled inset.\n\nBedroom 4 Single bedroom to the front of the house.\n\nBedroom 5 Double room to the front of the house.\n\nShower room Comprises of a tiled shower and wash hand basin.\n\nWC WC located off the landing, tiled walls.\n\nBathroom Comprises of a bath and wash hand basin with tiled walls.\n\nGarage With power supply, up and over security door.
