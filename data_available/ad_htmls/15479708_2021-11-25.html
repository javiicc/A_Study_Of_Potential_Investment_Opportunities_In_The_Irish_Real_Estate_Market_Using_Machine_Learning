    Description

      Part of New Development at Sli Na Coille

    • Overall Floor Area: 134 m\xc2\xb2
    We are delighted to offer to the market four new-build semi-detached bungalows in the prestigious development of Sli Na Coille, Belturbet, Co Cavan.\r\n\r\nThe units are 1440 sq. ft. approx., with a spacious hallway, lounge, kitchen/dining area, and built-in utility unit, two bedrooms serviced by a shower/wet room, airing cupboard, and storage cupboard, all on the ground floor. On the first floor, a generously sized bedroom is located which is light-filled by way of Velux windows. An ensuite completes the accommodation.\r\n\r\nThese units will be completed by end of November 2021 and can be bought off plans. They also qualify for the Help-To-Buy scheme.\r\n\r\nThe construction is of Quinnlite blocks, insulation, and plaster finish with a decorative stone finish to the frontal area and comes complete with tiling, floor coverings, and painted finish. \r\n\r\nThere is a garden to the rear which comes professionally fenced with gardens prepared for lawn sewing. All areas come finished with concrete footpaths, tarmacadam drive, kerbing, and street lighting. \r\n\r\nThere is excellent broadband on-site. The heating system is air to water with a building energy rating of A2 and an approximate annual heating cost of \xe2\x82\xac450. \r\n\r\nSli Na Coille is situated on the town edge, less than a mile from the town centre, and serviced by footpaths and public lighting. \r\n\r\nThere are bus services available from both private and public providers to Cavan town, Enniskillen, and Dublin city and airport. \r\n\r\nBelturbet is a peaceful and picturesque town through which flows the meandering and timeless River Erne, renowned for its fishing and boating. A fine 9-hole golf course is situated at the town's edge. It is well serviced by schools, shops, places of worship, creches, and playparks with a fine hotel in situ on the main street. There is an abundance of restaurants nearby, a beautiful walkway through Turbet Island in the town centre, and numerous walk areas and places of interest within a 20-mile drive including; the Marble Arch Caves, Cuilcagh Mountain trail, Florencecourt, and Castle Saunderson to name a but a few. There is also a BCU recognised canoe centre nearby and the prestigious Slieve Russell Hotel is only 9km away, boasting a 9-hole and 18-hole golf course, swimming pool and spa and extensive woodland walks. \r\n\r\nCavan town is just a 15-minute drive away, Enniskillen is 20 minutes away, and Dublin and Belfast are 90 minutes away. \r\n\r\nFor further information please contact Val McCaul on 0879717354 or our office on 0494380434.
