    Description\r\nA lovely traditional 4 bedroom family bungalow in this very popular wee cul de sac on the outskirts of Drogheda town.\r\n\r\n** Watch our walk through video tour! **\r\n\r\nEdenoir is a mature family home dating from the 1960s which although dated in dcor offers lots of options for the new owners to put their own stamp on it. It has a particularly large kitchen/dining room which has obvious potential could easily be transformed into a modern open-plan family living area. This in turn has double glass panelled doors leading into the sitting room which has a lovely south facing aspect overlooking the front garden.\r\n\r\nThere are four double bedrooms all of which have their own hand basin and the main bedroom has an en-suite shower room with electric shower. There is a large private and sheltered back garden with mature apple trees and a timber garden shed. On a practical note, the house is double glazed throughout, has oil-fired central heating and has a BER rating of D2, very good for a house of its vintage. Outside there are mature gardens to front and rear, and the additional bonus of off street parking to the front.\r\n\r\nLocation:\r\nForest Hills is a small mature and private cul de sac situated just off the Ballymakenny Road on the outskirts of Drogheda town. A very popular and convenient residential area, the neighbourhood offers easy access to the town centre, schools, church and sporting facilities.
