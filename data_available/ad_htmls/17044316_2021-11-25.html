Constructed with modern family living in mind, where space and comfort are of paramount importance, this two storey dormer style property will appeal to all discerning buyers seeking a substantial modern home. The layout comprises of a large entrance hall with high celings, an ultra-modern open plan kitchen / dining area, generous guest bathroom and a large living room which is flooded with natural light through the clever use of glazing, taking advantage of the sunny west facing orientation. The living room opens onto a sunny terrace which becomes an extension of the living space in the summer months. The bedrooms are located on the first floor with the master bedroom boasting a large ensuite bathroom. A second double room, single bedroom and main family bathroom complete the accommodation. The rear garden spans an impressive 19m which is unheard of in new developments. \n\nThis concrete home is easily heated with underfloor heating on both the ground floor and first floor, coupled with an air to water heat pump and mechanical ventilation to ensure low running costs and a comfortable temperature all year round.
