    4 Cloister Gate arrives to the market in walk-in condition throughout leaving little to no work to be done by prospective purchasers. Built in 1993, this most attractive family home has been carefully looked after by its current owners & has been upgraded in recent years. The property strikes the perfect balance with well-proportioned living accommodation coupled with a very sunny south facing rear garden.\nThe accommodation comprises of a welcoming entrance hall with guest WC. A large living room to the front with a feature multi fuel stove. To rear of the property is the modern open plan kitchen/dining room that overlooks the private rear garden. This level is completed with the fourth bedroom. Upstairs the landing leads to three bedrooms, the main to the rear with an en-suite shower room, and main shower room. There is attic access via a Stira type stairs, this space is ideal for storage or conversion (subject to P.P). The rear garden benefits from a southerly aspect attracting all day and evening sun, when you enter the garden there is a large decking area with integrated solar lights which is perfect for al fresco dining, the rest of the rear garden is laid in artificial grass with raised flower beds and there is a feature decorative structure complemented by the summer tones throughout the garden. Furthermore, there is a large storage shed, outside tap and light. A side passage leads to front driveway which is laid in cobble lock with granite curbing surround, shrubbery, raised flower beds and substantial off-street parking.\nCloister Gate is ideally located in one of Dublin s most sought-after coastal suburbs. It has the great advantage of being a short stroll to Blackrock & Stillorgan villages with its enviable host of amenities including costal walks, shopping centres, cafes, restaurants, bars, and a wide variety of shops. There is an excellent selection of highly regarded educational facilities such as Carysfort National School, Sion Hill, Newpark, Blackrock College, St Andrews College, Smurfit Business School & UCD. No. 4 also enjoys proximity to the magnificent public park immediately adjoining the development. The area also has the advantage of great links into the city centre, with the DART, multiple bus routes (with QBC s from both the Rock Road & N11. There is the added bonus of the M50 within easy reach.\nThis wonderful property is sure to appeal to a wide variety of purchasers and viewing is highly recommended to truly appreciate this lovely home.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 1.45m x 3.65m.
