      Edwardian red-brick terraced house ideally situated beside the Dundrum Luas stop.\r\n\r\nThe groundfloor is comprised of a hallway that leads into a living room with a gas fireplace. The bathroom has a shower, hand basin and toilet. \r\n\r\nThe kitchen currently has a dining bar installed, but there is space to add a dining table.
