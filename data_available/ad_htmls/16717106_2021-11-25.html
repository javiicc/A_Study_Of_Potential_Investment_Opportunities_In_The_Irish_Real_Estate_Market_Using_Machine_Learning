    A stunning Period house with an impressive B3 BER rating and an impressive layout. Located on the sunny side of this sought after street, and minutes from shopping, trains, hospitals ( Mater, Temple St & Rotunda ) and colleges, no expense has been spared in the creation of a most comfortable and stylish Period home. Refurbished in recent years, No 33 retains its elegant Period feel yet it includes all of the luxuries of modern living including a very practical and layout, a bright eat-in kitchen with a range of integrated Neff appliances, a quality insulation and heating system and luxurious travertine, walnut and Pitch Pine flooring. Great care has been taken to preserve and replicate the original features including the high coved ceilings, timber sash windows and fireplaces. Lots of storage is available to the new purchaser including a spacious attic room accessed from a fitted Stira in the spacious and light filled landing.
