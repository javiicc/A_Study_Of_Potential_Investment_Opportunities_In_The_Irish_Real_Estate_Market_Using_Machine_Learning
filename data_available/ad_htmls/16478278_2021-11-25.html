    DNG are proud to present 11 Cadogan Road, Fairview, to the market. This attractive red-brick period home was constructed around the turn of the 20th century and retains many original features. Whilst in need of refurbishment and modernisation, this superb property boasts generous ceiling heights and could make either an ideal starter home or investment opportunity for those searching for a property close to the city in a quiet, central location. \n\nThe accommodation extends to a total floor area of approx. 107 Sqm. And comprises an entrance hallway with original archway and coving, front reception room with fireplace and a rear living room, a small kitchen and bathroom to the rear completes the ground floor level. Upstairs, there are 4 bedrooms and a bathroom on the return level. To the rear there is a small yard.\n\nThe property is located in a very mature residential area opposite Fairview Park and within walking distance of Dublin City Centre. It is well served by the Clontarf Road DART station and can also be reached by way of several Dublin Bus routes from the City Centre. Cadogan Road is within easy reach of an array of recreational and sports amenities including Westwood Gym, Croke Park Stadium, and a host of local shops, restaurants and cafes and schools. It is also within easy access to the M50 and Dublin International Airport is a 15 minute drive away. \n\n\nHallway 7.6m x 1.8m (at widest).
