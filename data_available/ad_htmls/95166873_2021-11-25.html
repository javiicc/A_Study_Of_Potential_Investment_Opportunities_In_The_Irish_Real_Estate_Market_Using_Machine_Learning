    Development Description

      Ard an Oir is a high-quality development of detached, semi-detached and terraced homes located on the famous Ring of Kerry in the picturesque village of Sneem in South Kerry. \r\nheart of the village adjacent to the South Square, close to the numerous local shops, hotels, bars and restaurants that the award winning village has to offer, the homes offer stylishly designed energy efficient family/holiday homes for modern living constructed to the highest A -rated standards.\r\n\r\nThe four styles measuring a comfortable 111 -121 sq mtrs, have spacious fitted kitchen/dining, utility with wc, seperate sitting rooms with electric fires, three bedrooms with master en-suite and seperate family bathrooms.\r\nSurrounded by extensive landscaped areas, these homes are finished to a very high standard, they offer an intelligent use of space, a keen eye for style and are sure to appeal to any purchaser seeking a unique in home in a tranquil setting or for those who are looking for a base to the many attractions and beaches that South Kerry has to offer.
