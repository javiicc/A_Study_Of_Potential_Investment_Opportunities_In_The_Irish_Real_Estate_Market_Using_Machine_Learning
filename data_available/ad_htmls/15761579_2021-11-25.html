    Skellig Rest is a beautifully presented 2-bedroom cottage that is set in one of the most picturesque locations in the world. The panoramic views over the UNESCO world heritage site of the Skellig's and Ballinskellig Bay is a sight you will never tire of. At night you can enjoy the beauty of the Dark Sky reserve. The cottage is over 100 years old and has been beautifully restored throughout with a mixture of traditional and modern features. The cottage has been tastefully decorated with strong bold colours. The cottage is 968 sq. ft, the welcoming entrance is ideal as a dining area as it takes advantage of the beautiful view's, leading from this is the cosy living room with traditional wooden beams, floor and deep windows with natural stone wall. There is also a large wood burning stove. The kitchen is a modern fitted kitchen. There is also a utility room and guest toilet on this level. Located on the first floor are 2 double bedrooms both with views over the Skellig's & Ballinskellig Bay. The bathroom with its free-standing traditional bath is luxurious. The Sauna & shower room is an ideal space to relax in.
