    Description

      After a successful letting, this wonderful 4 bedroom detached home returns to the rental market.\n\nThe property is located in the beautiful estate of Allen Court situated in the quaint village of Allen, just a short drive from Naas. Allen Village is overlooked by the Hill of Allen, and is as picturesque as it is promising. Nearby, one will find the Bog of Allen Nature Centre, playschool and primary school facilities, a pub, service station and the Church of the Holy Trinity. For those who enjoy the history of old Ireland, Allen is also widely known as the seat of Fionn Mac Cumhaill. \n\nSituated behind electric gates, the residents of this lovely property will benefit from countryside living with quick and convenient access to major routes. The property is less than 20 minutes drive to the M7/N7 for access to the City Centre. The village also has a bus route with links to the nearby town of Newbridge, Milltown and Kilmeague. \n\nThe property itself briefly comprises of marble tiled hallway, two spacious ground floor bedrooms complete with shared en suite, beautifully bright living room, conservatory with views of the countryside surrounds and generous size garden, kitchen with all mod cons, separate utility room complete with washing machine and tumble dryer. \n\nThe upstairs of the property is equally luxurious, with added space on the landing which lends itself to a mezzanine, two spacious first floor bedrooms - one with a walk in wardrobe and en suite, main bathroom including electric shower. \n\nThe gardens at the property are tastefully landscaped and are complete with a shed. \n\nThe property is heated via Kerosene oil. \n\nTo register your interest in this beautiful property, please send your enquiry today!\n\n**References will be requested with at least 2 days notice to view**\n\n**Due to the high number of applicants, Stewart Property Solutions apologizes to those who do not receive a response to their enquiry**
