    Churches Estate Agents Blackrock are delighted to present this stylist one-bedroom apartment on the first floor of this private development in the heart of Blackrock. Avoca Hall is a desirably estate situated in the landscaped Avoca Park, just 10 minutes walk into Blackrock Village, with access to it's many shops, pubs and restaurants plus the DART and stunning coastline.\r\n\r\nSituated on the first floor, accessed via wide staircase or lift, No. 20 is a surprisingly generous one bed apartment extending to 49.65 sq m. Including large open living room with traditional fireplace and windows to the rear gardens; separate kitchen with plentiful storage; spacious main bedroom with built-in wardrobes; and large main bathroom with bath/shower.\r\n\r\nThroughout the apartment is floored with original wood floors, and is tastefully decorated by the current owners. All the rooms feel generous and would be a comfortable home for many an owner of all types. One designated car parking space is included, and there is ample free visitor spaces around the property.\r\n\r\nThe property benefits from an impressive location, secure in a private gated development in a beautiful landscaped estate which links directly into the wonderful Carysfort Park, which includes UCD Smurfit Business School. From here it is easy walk into Blackrock Village, for the DART into Dublin; or alternatively the N11 is also nearby.\r\n\r\nBlackrock is one of Dublin's most popular small villages, with it's own unique attractions. Multiple pubs and high quality restaurants are in the area, plus excellent shopping, from the small high street boutiques to the two giant shopping centres at Frascati and Blackrock.\r\n\r\nThis apartment is must see for anyone looking for a stylist and comfortable one-bedroom property!\r\n\r\n-ACCOMMODATION-\r\n\r\nEntrance Hall\r\nLiving Room (6.7m x 2.75m)\r\nKitchen (2.5m x 2.5m)\r\nMaster Bedroom (4.9m x 2.7m)\r\nBathroom (1.8m x 1.6m)
