    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Charming Traditional Style Residence With Nine Varied Stone Built Outbuilding On C.3 Acres.\r\n\r\nThis delightful property is located about 8 miles to the North West of Dunmanway Town occupying a private and seclude C.3 acre site with extensive unspoiled view of the surrounding rural landscape extending to the Sheehy Mountains. The residence is a traditional stone built structure with extensions to the front and rear. The residence has retained many of the traditional features is well maintained having the benefit of modern conveniences. Adjacent to the residence is a range of stone built outbuildings which are utilized as workshops, and storage areas. A timber framed and clad studio provides the ideal workplace retreat. The location of this property is ideal if you want to enjoy the peace and quiet of a wonderful rural setting. The lands have been organically farmed for the past 25 years.\r\n\r\nAccommodation: Conservatory, Kitchen/Dining Room, Sitting Room, Bathroom, 3 Bedrooms.\r\nServices: Private Water Supply, Septic Tank Drainage, Electricity, Oil Fired Central Heating.
