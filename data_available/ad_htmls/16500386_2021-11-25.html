    Corry Estates are delighted to welcome to the market Apartment 22 Red Arches Avenue, a property that represents a wonderful opportunity to acquire a bright and spacious two bedroom second floor apartment with its own front door. Built in 2017 this super efficient "A" rated apartment comprises of porch entrance complete with large storage press which leads to the main living accommodation of reception hall, open plan lounge / kitchen / dining area, two generous double bedrooms with master en-suite and main bathroom. Both bedrooms and the living area have access to balconies. Further features include PVC double glazed windows, gas fired radiator central heating and designated car parking. The property is ideally located within minutes' walk of Clongiffin Dart station, local shops, schools & commuter bus links. Viewing comes highly recommended to appreciate this fine apartment.
