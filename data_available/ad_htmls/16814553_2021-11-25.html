    Fort View, Quay Road, Duncannon is a truly charming double fronted residence located in the heart of the village and immediately opposite the famous Strand. The flexible and authentic living accommodation of c. 100sq m is currently arranged to provide a welcoming entrance hall, sitting room with open fire, dining room with solid fuel stove, kitchenette, utility and bathroom complete the ground floor. Upstairs there are 3 bedrooms. Outside there is a lovely rear garden which is ideal for Summer BBQs and entertaining. The sale of Fort View presents an outstanding opportunity for someone to acquire a wonderful coastal home in this most enviable location and setting.
