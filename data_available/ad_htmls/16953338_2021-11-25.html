    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Description\nThis is a unique opportunity to acquire two houses on the one site, within the village of Ballyhale. The houses are just minutes from the M9 (Dublin / Waterford motorway) and c. 20 minutes from Kilkenny city. The the two bungalows sit on a large site which measures over c.1 acre and has a large front garden and a field to the rear. Both houses are in good condition, however, they both require modernisation and decoration. Originally, both houses were constructed to identical plans, each with a spacious hallway, living room, bathroom, two bedrooms, dining area/living area, kitchen and garage. A slight modification was made to the house on the right when a portion of the garage was converted into a third bedroom. \nAccess is via a single driveway and there is a large lawn area at the front of the house and access to the paddock to the rear. The houses have separate oil fired central heating systems and have separate electricity meters. There is a high pitch roof and ample room to the rear if one was looking to extend the houses or create one large residential property. \nThe houses are located within the village, adjacent to the national school and close to the village amenities. The motorway is conveniently located close by, as is the Mountain view golf course and restaurant while Waterford city is just c.20 minutes away.\n\nFeatures\nTwo houses on one site.\nHouses in good condition.\nLarge site measuring c.1 acre.\nAdjacent to Ballyhale national school.\nClose to Ballyhale secondary school.\nKnocktoper is just 2 miles away.\nLots of room for extensions.\n\nBER Details\nBER: F - G\n\nDirections\nFrom Kilkenny/Knocktopher, travel through the village and the houses are located on the left hand side, just before the national school.
