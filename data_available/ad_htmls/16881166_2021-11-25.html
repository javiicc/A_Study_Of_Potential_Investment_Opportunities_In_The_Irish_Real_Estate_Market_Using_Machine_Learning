    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to bring No. 17 Hollystown Park to the market. This is a stunning 3-bed family home located in the exclusive development. The property provides off street parking for 2 cars and also features a large secure back garden with gated side access.\nNo. 17 Hollystown Park offers spacious accommodation across two floors briefly comprising of a spacious entrance hall, a large living room, a dining room, a spacious kitchen area that opens out to a bright conservatory, a utility room with additional access to the back garden of the property. The ground floor also includes an additional living room to the front of the property and a wc. The first floor features a generous main bedroom with fitted wardrobes, a walk-in wardrobe and a fully fitted ensuite. This floor also includes 2 additional double bedrooms, a spacious main bathroom and a bright landing area that provides access to the hot press and the attic. \nHollystown Park is a peaceful sought-after development with a wealth of local amenities. There is a good selection of shopping and leisure facilities both locally and within a ten-minute drive. The M50 offers seamless access to Dublin airport and the surrounding area. \n\nEntrance Hall 1.5m x 7.1m. a spacious entrance hall that features a WC and a storage cupboard. Most of the ground floor of the property is fitted with Cherrywood flooring.\n\nSitting Room 3.5m x 4.8m. a bright room with a large window that overlooks the front of the property, this room is spacious in size and is currently being used as a home office.\n\nLiving Room 3.5m x 5.89m. located at the front of the property, this is a sizeable room with 3 windows that flood the room with natural light. The room also features a gas fireplace with a feature mantlepiece.\n\nDining Room 4.5m x 3.05m. a spacious room with double doors that open to the large kitchen at the rear of the property. There is a large window in this room that provides plenty of natural light.\n\nKitchen Dining Room 7.29m x 3.6m. a large room fitted with tile flooring located at the rear of the property, the kitchen features integrated appliances (fridge / freezer and dishwasher). There is a breakfast bar style island and a utility room off the kitchen. This room is plumbed for a tumble dryer and a washing machine, has additional access to the back garden. The gas boiler is in the utility room.\n\nConservatory 3.29m x 4.02m. a bright room that opens off the kitchen through French doors, featuring tile flooring, fully fitted blinds and access to the back garden.\n\nWC 2.55m x 0.9m. this room features tiled flooring, half tiled walls and is fitted with a wc and a wash hand basin.\n\nLanding a bright landing with a large Velux window that floods the area with natural light, there is access to the hot press and the attic from the landing. The stairs are fitted with the carpet flooring and the landing is solid pine flooring.\n\nMain Bedroom 5.17m x 3.27m. a sizeable room to the front of the property fitted with solid pine flooring. This room features spacious built-in wardrobes as well as a walk- in wardrobe. There is a balcony off the main bedroom which overlooks the front of the property and a fully fitted ensuite located off the bedroom also.\n\nEnsuite 2.34m x 1.9m. fitted with tiled flooring and walls, the ensuite features a corner shower, a wc, a wash hand basin and a Velux window which provides the room with natural light.\n\nBathroom 2.36m x 2.26m. a bright room with tiled flooring and half tiled walls, the bathroom is fitted with a wc, a wash hand basin and a bath with a shower attachment.\n\nBedroom 2 2.89m x 4.02m. located to the rear of the property, this is a sizeable double bedroom with a large window that overlooks the back garden. This bedroom features built-in wardrobes and solid pine flooring.\n\nBedroom 3 2.72m x 4.27m. located to the rear of the property, this is a sizeable double bedroom with a large window that overlooks the back garden. This bedroom features built-in wardrobes and pine flooring.\n\nBack garden a spacious garden, fully fenced with side access from the front of the property. The garden features a paved patio area, a large lawned area and a wooden shed.\n\nFront entrance a paved driveway with off-street parking with room for two cars, there is a spacious front porch and a low maintenance front garden.
