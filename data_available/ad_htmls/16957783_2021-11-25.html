    The Purple Heather is a gem in the crown of Kenmare culinary premises. On the market as a going concern, this is a prime opportunity to purchase one of the most iconic properties in the heart of Kenmare town. \n\nThe Purple Heather has been open since the 1960 s and has been renowned for its exceptional food. The ground floor of about 2,086 sq. ft. has a 56-seater restaurant & bar which also has a benefit of a full bar license. Located off the restaurant area is the commercial kitchen. To the rear are the ladies and gents toilets and dry goods room. The property extends to Henry Lane at the rear where there are various store rooms, which are ideal for further development. \n\nOn the first floor is a private 2-bedroom apartment,1,083 sq. ft. (approx). This is ideal as an upstairs living quarters or a home. The large living room to the front has beautiful high ceilings with original cornicing and sash windows. There are also 2 bedrooms, a bathroom, kitchen, living and dining room. \n\nOn the third floor is an attic with a home office, 422 sq. ft.
