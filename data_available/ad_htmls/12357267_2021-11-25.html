    This magnificent 5 bed bungalow with large attic conversion, sits on a large site of approximately 1.1 acres and has spectacular views overlooking Lough Ree. It is located at end of quite lane approximately 2km from the beautiful village of Lecarrow. The property itself was built in early 2000's and extends to c. 390m2 (including attached garage) electric gates and \r\nPolytunnel 4meters by 10meters. The central heating is underfloor heating. \r\n\r\nThe accommodation on the ground floor consist of kitchen, living room, dining room, sitting room, utility, 5 bedroom (2 en-suite) . There is also a large games room to the rear of the garage. The attic conversion has a large bedroom with walk in wardrobe, en-suite and landing area walk in robe with more spectacular views over the lake.\r\n\r\nThe village of Lecarrow, located 2km away, is connected to Lough Ree via the Lecarrow Canal and has modern marina facilities, pub, restaurant, primary school montessori after school care and shop. The property is located 20 mins from the larger towns of Roscommon and Athlone which provide for all necessary services.\r\n\r\nThe property needs to be seen to be appreciated.
