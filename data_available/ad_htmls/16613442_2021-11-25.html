Arriving at this lovely home we see a bungalow that has been modernised to suit any family with new windows and doors throughout. Entering through the front door there is a bright hallway leading to the open plan kitchen-dining and TV/seating area. The kitchen is new and has a modern styling. Off the kitchen is a large utility room and back door leading to the rear of the property and the log cabin and back gardens. \r\nLeaving this area into the bedroom hallway there is an additional sitting room and two further bedrooms and a stylish modern family bathroom. \r\nUpstairs there is a carpeted landing that is bright and fresh and leads to 3 further good sized bedrooms. These rooms are ideal as bedrooms or would make the perfect home office studio or work spaces. \r\n\r\nLog Cabin: The log cabin is ideal for that extra family space required with full heating and insulation. There is an open plan kitchen/dining and TV area as well as two good sized bedrooms and a family bathroom. \r\nThe property is ideally located just 1.2km from Kilmeague village and 3km from the bustling village of Allenwood with primary schools, GAA club, pub, shops and post office all with a great local community. This location has great motorway access for a quick and easy commute to Dublin with the M7 motorway junction 10 just 10 minutes away and M7 junction 11 Newbridge is 20 minutes away. Be it for shopping or commuting this property is well serviced with train access to Dublin with Newbridge Commuter Station 15 minutes away and Sallins(Naas) only 20 minutes away. Bus Eireann also offer a daily bus service from Allenwood to Dublin city centre and services to Newbridge and Naas. \r\nFor the fishing and sporting enthusiast the Grand Canal is a 3km from this property and the Curragh Race Course, Punchestown and Naas races courses are all within driving distance. \r\nThe towns of Newbridge and Naas are both circa 15 minutes away, offering an excellent array of shopping, recreational and educational facilities. Naas offers such retailers as Tescos, Boots, Argos, Lidl, Aldi, Smyths, Woodies, Currys, PC World, Harvey Normans, B & Q, Powercity and Newbridge has the benefit of Whitewater Shopping Centre, Penneys, Dunnes, Tescos, Newbridge Silverware, T.K.
