There's a c.2\xc2\xbd acre field across the road from the gated entrance to the yard, where a stream meanders through and around the field featuring a pond in the centre; a pure haven of tranquillity. There's a polytunnel at the top part of the field with water overhead and electricity, there are also a few apple trees and a chicken coop. The well & pump are sited in this field also.
