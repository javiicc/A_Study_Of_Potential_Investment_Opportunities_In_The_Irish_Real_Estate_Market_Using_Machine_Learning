The property is part of the sought after development \xe2\x82\xacBallymore Lane\xe2\x82\xacTMand is within a 5 minute stroll to Craughwell village and all the amenities therein \xe2\x82\xac" shops, restaurants, pubs, a service station, church, GAA Club, athletics grounds, basketball court, kids playground, community centre, primary school and creche. The property benefits from PVC double glazed windows throughout and laminate floors.\r\n\r\nThe two-tone kitchen is fitted with high gloss cabinet doors \xe2\x82\xac" white for the overhead cabinets and grey on the lower and white, scratch proof quartzite countertops.
