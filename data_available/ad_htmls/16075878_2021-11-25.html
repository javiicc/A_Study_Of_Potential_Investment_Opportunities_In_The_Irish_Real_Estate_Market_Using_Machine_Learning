    Stunning three-bedroom duplex, situated in the original house within the Seven Oaks development.\r\n\r\nThe duplex takes many of original features from the house, including the dramatic hallway and spectacular window. On entering you are greeted by the dramatic double height hallway that features the original ceiling arch and cornicing. The staircase features the original balustrades and pauses at a landing that frames the large nine pane window. \r\n\r\nThis wonderful staircase leads up to the living area. The u-shaped kitchen is accessed through glass panelled doors. The kitchen is well laid out and features attractive cabinetry. The living room is a large luxurious space that is well lit by two large sash windows. This room accommodates a dining area and would be ideal for relaxing and entertaining alike. From this room there is a secondary entrance to the duplex. \r\n\r\nThe bedrooms are all downstairs, each feature sash windows and built-in wardrobes. There is a main bathroom and ensuite bathroom. Each are well finished with attractive tiling.\r\n\r\nThe development is ideally situated on one of the proposed bus corridors, with the city centre a short journey away. The Memorial Gardens is accessed by a pedestrian bridge and from here the Phoenix Park is only a short walk away. Nearby is Inchicore Village which is home to a host of independent eateries and coffee shops.
