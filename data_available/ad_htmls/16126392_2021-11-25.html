    Ardeen House is a substantial detached home standing proudly on circa 0.5 Acre of gardens with lovely countryside views. The property will appeal to buyers looking for a spacious family home convenient to all local amenities on the edge of Castlecomer. \n\nThe bright and spacious accommodation extends to 205 Sq. M. / 2,207 Sq. Ft. approx. over two levels. The accommodation at ground level briefly comprises: open porch, entrance hall, living room, family/dining room, kitchen/breakfast room, utility room, four spacious bedrooms and a family bathroom. The layout upstairs comprises: landing area and three spacious attic rooms.\n\nGARDENS AND GROUNDS:\nThe property is well set back from the main road and is fully walled to the front with a tarmacadam driveway leading down to the front and side of the property. There is secure parking for several cars. A detached garage is located to the side of the property. A block built shed and a storage container are practical for further storage needs. The rear garden has lovely views of the surrounding countryside and is an ideal secure children s play area. \n\nLOCATION:\nThe property is ideally located only minutes to all local amenities in Castlecomer such as shops, supermarket, pubs, bank, post office, credit union and Castlecomer Discovery Park and the Avalon House Hotel. There are a number of schools in close proximity including Wandesforde Mixed National School, Castlecomer Community Schools and Castlecomer Arts Academy. Castlecomer Golf Club, Erin s Own GAA Club and Castlecomer Basketball are all in close proximity. Castlecomer is a 25 minute drive to Kilkenny City offer an excellent choice of shopping, recreational and educational facilities. Buggys Coaches operates a bus from Castlecomer to Kilkenny City four times daily. \n\nViewing of this property is highly recommended. \n\n\nGROUND FLOOR\n\nEntrance Hall (incl. stairs & cloakroom 7.56m x 1.72m + 1.35m x 1.22m. Parquet tiled floor directly inside the front door and the rest of the hall is fitted with carpet. An enclosed staircase leads up to the first floor. Access to the hot press. A door give access to a cloakroom under the staircase.\n\nLiving Room 4.86m 3.65m. A spacious and light filled room with dual aspect windows overlooking the front and side gardens. A light fitted room to the rear with dual aspect windows. Wooden floor and ceiling coving.\n\nKitchen / Breakfast Room 4.31m x 3.63m. A spacious room to the rear with views of the back garden. The kitchen area is fitted with wall and floor units with a tiled backsplash. Electric hob, and extractor fan. Double drainer sink unit. The breakfast area can accommodate a table and chairs for family dining. Terracotta tiled floor. A glass panel door gives access through to the utility room.\n\nUtility Room 3.16m x 2.85m. Plumbing is in place for a washing machine and there is plenty of space for a tumble dryer and a chest freezer. A door leads out to the rear garden.
