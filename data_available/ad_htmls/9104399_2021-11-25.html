    Development Description

      Savills is delighted to present the stunning homes at Castle Heights being constructed by Hallmark Developments. Phase 6 of this popular development is now available with the first release of homes all agreed. This stunning development comprises a selection of brand new A2 rated three and four bedroom family homes. These homes are all eligible for the Help to Buy Scheme. \r\n\r\nCastle Heights offers a wide range of high quality and energy efficient homes in the vibrant town of Carrigaline. House types include three and four bedroom semi-detached and four bedroom detached homes. All houses feature Daiken air to water systems, heat recovery systems, underfloor heating and high levels of insulation resulting in a reduced energy demand and cleaner, healthier living. \r\n\r\nCarrigaline is located approx. 12kms south of Cork city, originally a commuter town, it has become a hugely popular town for families. Carrigaline has a wide array of amenities and transport facilities available and a large number of services including schools, supermarkets, restaurants, hotel, gyms and many more.\r\n\r\nRegister your interest now at castleheights@savills.ie
