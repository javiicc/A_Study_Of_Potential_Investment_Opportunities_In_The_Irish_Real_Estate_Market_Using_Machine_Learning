    This end of terrace townhouse is nicely situated in Academy Street just off the Dublin Road in Navan. The property has been extended to provide more spacious living accommodation. Number 5 will benefit from redecoration, but its location in the centre of Navan Town in a very quiet residential street means that any future investment in decoration will be reflected in the properties overall value.
