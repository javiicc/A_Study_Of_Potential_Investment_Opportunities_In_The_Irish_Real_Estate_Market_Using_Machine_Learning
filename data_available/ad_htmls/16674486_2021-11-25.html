    Excellent mid terrace property comes to the market in good condition with the potential for a purchaser to create a wonderful home\r\n \r\nThe property has an attractive fa\xc3\xa9\xc2\xa7ade, with off street parking and a pathway leading to the front door. There is a high efficiency glass door that leads into the porch. The original door opens into a welcoming hallway. To the right of the hallway is the living room. This is a bright room featuring a bay window an art deco fireplace. Behind this is what would have been the dining room, for modern living purposes this is used as a second living room. This is a comfortable space with sliding doors that lead out to the garden. At the end of the hallway is the kitchen / dining room. The kitchen forms the extension which is lit by a velux window. Behind the kitchen is a utility area and downstairs bathroom. From here a door leads out to the garden. The garden is a very private space that benefits from lane access. Upstairs there are three bedrooms and the main bathroom. The two larger rooms feature the original fireplaces. The bathroom fully tiled and fitted to a high specification.\r\n \r\nThe house is well situated between Rialto Village and the Grand Canal. Rialto Village is undergoing a renaissance and is home to a number of coffee shops and restaurants. The Grand Canal is a wonderful amenity and the LUAS red line is close by. The area is undergoing a positive transformation with the opening of the New Children\xe2\x82\xacTM Hospital.
