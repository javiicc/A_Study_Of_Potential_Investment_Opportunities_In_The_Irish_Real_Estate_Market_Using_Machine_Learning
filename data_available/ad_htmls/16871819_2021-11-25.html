    No 1 Bar na Carraige is a four bedroomed detached family home located in the popular development of Fort Lorenzo, Taylors Hill. Built in the mid 1990 s by Brennan Developers the property is in good decorative order throughout and benefits from having a private split level south facing rear garden and off street parking.\n\nThis home was constructed and fitted to a very high standard and is very well maintained both internally and externally. The ground floor comprises of a large living room to the front with double doors leading to kitchen/dining area. A utility room and guest toilet completes the ground floor accommodation. Upstairs there are four good sized bedrooms, one of which is en-suite and the main bathroom. A large hot press completes the upstairs accommodation. \n\nBar na Carraige is an established, mature and quiet residential area, close to all services including The Gateway retail park in Knocknacarra where Dunnes, B&Q & Harvey Norman are the anchor tenants. A short stroll from the property, down Threadneedle Road, will bring you to Salthill Promenade.\n\nOverall a truly impressive home in an outstanding location, must be viewed to be appreciated. \n\n\nEntrance Hall Solid timber flooring, guest toilet under the stairs.\n\nLiving Room To the front of the house with two windows overlooking the front garden, solid timber flooring, solid fuel fireplace with feature fireplace with timber surround and cast iron inset. Double doors to dining area.\n\nKitchen/Dining Room Solid pine kitchen units, plumbed for all essential appliances, tiled floor and splash back, door to hall and utility. Sliding doors to patio area.\n\nUtility Room Tiled floor, plumbed for washing machine/dryer, sink, shelved for extra storage space, window to front and door to rear.\n\nMain Bedroom Double room to the front of the house with timber floors, built in wardrobes & shelves.\n\nEn-Suite Partly tiled, electric shower, wc, wash hand basin, side window.\n\nBedroom Single room to the front of the house with timber flooring, built in wardrobe & shelves.\n\nBathroom Partly tiled with bath, wc, wash hand basin and side window.\n\nBedroom Double room to the back of the house with timber flooring.\n\nBedroom Double room to the back of the house with timber flooring, built in wardrobe and shelves & large bay window.\n\nStorage Shed Large concrete built storage shed with ample storage space for all your gardening utensils.\n\nGarden The front garden is in lawn and benefits from having mature shrubbary and tall trees to the side, which provides ample privacy. Two side entrances with security gates lead to the south facing rear garden. A patio area outside the dining area benefits from having a raised bed with a stone wall boundary. Steps from here lead to the rear lawn which is surrounded by mature trees and shrubbary and a block wall defines the boundary.
