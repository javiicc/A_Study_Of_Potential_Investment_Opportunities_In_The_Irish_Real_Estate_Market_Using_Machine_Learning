    New to the market Dog and Bone Kennels and Cattery, Clooncullen, Ballymahon, County Westmeath. A unique opportunity to relocate to a well maintained cottage/family home situated on a C.9 acre site and take over a busy family business.\r\n\r\nThe cottage is well maintained and has some unique features. The accommodation consists of sitting room (5.04m X 3.697M ); kitchen with fitted presses (4.99 X 4.03) ; Office (1.95m X 3.38m) ;Conservatory (6.97m X 3.98m) ; Bedroom 1 (3.02m X3.66m) Ensuite (3.06m X1.41m); Bedroom 2 (3.5m X 3) ; Bedroom 3 ( 3.5m X 2.3m) Main Bathroom (2.7m x 1.96) Porch (4.04m X 2.53m) ; There is a well maintained patio area ( 5m x 5m )to the rear of the cottage. Also outside the cottage is a customed built shed (3.5m X3.4m ) for a hot tub. The property is serviced by a private well and septic tank. A new Firebird Heating System was installed in the property recently. A CCTV security system is installed in both the kennels and dwellinghouse. The security system is accessible via Mobile phone.\r\n \r\nDog and Bone Kennels and Cattery is a well established family run business which is located \r\napproximately 5km from Ballymahon town. The business has expanded over the past few years and the kennels have been upgraded along with additional new play areas. The Kennels are very centrally located and come highly recommended by holiday makers in Centre Parcs Longford Forest which is located only 3km from the property. This Business has been successfully trading for over 20 years and enjoys great turnover due its fantastic reputation. There is huge potential for growth. Details of turnover on request. \r\n\r\nThe property is centrally located approximately 5km from Ballymahon; 20km from Athlone; 25km from Longford and Mullingar ; The town of Ballymahon is well serviced with a National School; two secondary schools; Bank; Post Office; shops ; Hotels and many more other businesses . Ballymahon has a wide variety of sporting activities to include Gaa, Soccer ( Astro Pitch) and Water sports. If you enjoy walking you will enjoy the walks along the River Inny and Royal Canal and also within a short distance of Ballymahon are the newly developed bog walks. The property has to be seen to be appreciated. For a viewing contact O'Meara Auctioneers on 0906438400 or 086 8575864.
