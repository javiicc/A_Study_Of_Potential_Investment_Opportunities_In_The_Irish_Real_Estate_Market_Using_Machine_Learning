    We are proud to present to the market this prestigious five/six bedroom residence on a beautifully manicured 1.1 acre site. This c. 3035 sq. ft. dwelling is situated in a tranquil and scenic location, a short drive to all amenities.\r\n\r\nGround floor accommodation briefly comprises of a vast entrance hall, a living room with stove, kitchen/dining room, a sun lounge, a sitting room with open fire, utility room, WC, and a large bedroom with ensuite. \r\n\r\nOn the first floor, there are four bedrooms, four ensuites, a bedroom/storeroom, a bathroom, and a hot press.\r\n\r\nThe property is heated by way of oil-fired central heating complemented by a stove with a back boiler in the living room and an open fire in the sitting room. Water is supplied by a group water scheme and sewage is treated by a bio-cycle system. There is a highly convenient beam vacuum system installed with sockets throughout the property. High-speed fibre broadband is available. An excellent B1 energy rating will ensure energy efficiency and will also extend the possibility of a competitive mortgage rate.\r\n \r\nThe mature gardens dotted with colourful shrubbery are a huge feature. A pathway leads to a detached studio/garage/workshop to the rear of the property which offers multiple potential uses including residential use, subject to planning. There is a car bay adjoining the rear of the property.\r\n\r\nThe local national school is within walking distance as are churches of different denominations. Located close to all amenities as Redhills village is only 1.5km away, Ballyhaise village is 5.4km away and Cavan town is 12km distant.\r\n\r\nWe highly recommend this truly magnificent property and viewing can be arranged through the sole selling agents. For all information please call our office on 0494380434.
