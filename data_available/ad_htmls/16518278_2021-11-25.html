Wonderful gardens, fine lawns, beautifully maintained and tree lined. Galway Cricket facilities are to the rear adding to the genteel ambiance this property is offering. Superb outside lighting, excellent utility area - this property has it all.\r\n\r\nThe current owners want the new owners to enjoy this quality property with all it has to offer. They have done much of the difficult work of renovating and modernising - now it's ready for your personal touch. What a great place for a family to grow - lots of local facilities, schools also close by. Oakley Lodge is like no other home - it's special and you have got to see it! \r\n\r\nACCOMMODATION: Entrance hallway with skylight, superb sitting room with open fireplace, hall closet, breakfast room, family room, kitchen, utility room; bedroom one with built-in units; bedroom two with built-in units plus interconnected doors to en-suite, superbly tiled; main bathroom, w.c. and w.h.b.; Master bedroom three with built-in units plus gorgeous bathroom, w.c. and w.h.b. plus shower en-suite, superbly tiled; bedroom four with built-in units plus shower, w.c. and w.h.b. en-suite; lower ground floor bonus room, plus shower, w.c. and w.h.b.; exceptional garage.\r\n\r\nExcellent oil fired heating system, top-notch plumbing throughout, garage also offers an abundance of storage space - to say this property is exceptional is a total understatement.\r\n\r\nDetails with John Quinn.
