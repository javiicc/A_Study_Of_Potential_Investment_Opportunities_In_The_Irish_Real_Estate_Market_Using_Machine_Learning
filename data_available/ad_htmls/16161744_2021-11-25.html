    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 8.67 ac
    Fenelon Properties are delighted to present, this wonderful scenic elevated site of 8.67 acres for sale by private treaty. The Subject site is located close to the picturesque village of Enniskerry, just off the N11 motorway. The site is set in an idyllic location and hosts breath-taking views of the Sugarloaf mountains, surrounding countryside and the sea. \n\nThe parcel is identified by Folio WW6980 and by co-ordinates N53.1964697\xc3\x82\xc2\xb0 W-6.2099361\xc3\x82\xc2\xb0. The property borders surrounding farmland and forestry. The land was previously overgrown and has been recently cleared to make way for a hardcore lane way along with an extensive drainage upgrade. There are currently a small number of derelict stables/outhouses which could be upgraded and refurbished, making it suitable for several uses. \n\nEnniskerry Village which is considered one of the prettiest Victorian villages in Ireland is on your doorstep and is home to the famous Powerscourt Estate and Golf club. A wealth of schools and social amenities are within easy reach.\n\nThis little piece of Heaven could be a great escape for those with busy work/life schedules looking for some of their own amenity space or to those of a rural background or working in the agricultural sector. Viewing is strongly recommended.\n\nThe Site is being sold as is and will NOT be sold subject to planning. \n\n\n\nDirections: \nFolio WW6980 \nCo-ordinates N53.1964697\xc3\x82\xc2\xb0 W-6.2099361\xc3\x82\xc2\xb0\nSee pin pointed map
