    Earnest are delighted to present No.228 Phibsborough Road to the market for sale. \r\n\r\nA unique four-bedroom house located in the heart of Phibsborough that is packed with character and full of potential. This is a rare opportunity to acquire such a property in this neighbourhood. The position is hard to beat with every conceivable amenity right on your doorstep. It will appeal to both homeowners and investors alike.\r\n\r\nThe accommodation comprises of an entrance hall with downstairs WC, reception, dining room and kitchen with access to the garden. Upstairs are four good sized bedrooms with large family bathroom including a bath and separate shower. The front garden is walled and gated and the house is set back a few meters from the street. There is a large, tiered back garden with mature planting and decked areas. There is a large attic space and on street parking to the front.\r\n\r\nPhibsborough has become a highly popular and trendy neighbourhood with charming streets which have become more bustling than ever. The Mater and the proximity to Dublin City Centre, makes for a perfect location. With a plethora of amenities at your doorstep and numerous parks including The Botanic Gardens, Blessington Basin, Mount Bernard Park and the banks of The Royal Canal. The area is extremely well serviced by excellent bus routes and the LUAS Cross City stop at Connaught Street is only a couple of minutes walk away. It is also located for easy access to the M50 and Dublin Airport. \r\n\r\nApprox measurements can be found in the floor plans.\r\n\r\nEarly viewing is essential to appreciate all this wonderful home has to offer.
