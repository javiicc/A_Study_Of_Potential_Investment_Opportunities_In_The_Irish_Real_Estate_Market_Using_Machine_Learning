    No. 18 Yeats Hall is a truly superb, 3 bedroom penthouse apartment located in the highly desirable development of Carrickmines Wood within the heart of leafy Dublin 18. Constructed in 2001 by renowned Park Developments, this 2nd floor, extremely spacious and well-designed property has an impressive overall floor area of (approx.) 154 sq m / 1,657 sq ft. Accessed via lift, or communal stairs which lead from ground to 1st floor, then a private staircase with key access leads to no.18 on the 2nd floor landing, which is not shared by any other apartment, giving this magnificent home a winning combination of exclusivity and privacy. The glorious light filled interior is enhanced by high ceilings throughout, and details such as decorative ceiling coving, dado rails, solid oak doors, Junkers flooring, picture windows and roof terrace access from all living and bedroom quarters, adds to the overall appeal. Of note is the expansive, triple aspect living /dining room which runs the full width of the property and offers two points of access via French doors to the delightful wrap around terrace, measuring approx. 52 sq m. From here there are wonderful panoramic views across the ever changing skyscape, overlooking the mature treetops of the landscaped gardens and beyond to the Dublin mountains in the distance. The fully fitted kitchen features integrated appliances, wooden countertops and a selection of wall and floor units. The luxurious principal bedroom suite includes a recently upgraded ensuite shower room with double shower enclosure, a walk in wardrobe and access to a separate paved roof terrace. There are a further two double bedrooms, the larger of the two with fitted wardrobes and ensuite shower room, a family bathroom with full bathroom suite completes the internal layout. \n\nUndoubtedly a favourable feature of this fine penthouse apartment is the expansive outdoor terrace space, extending to a total of 70.5 sq m / 758 sq ft. In addition the Development itself enjoys mature landscaped gardens enjoying excellent privacy to the back of Yeat s Hall. There are also wonderful open green spaces with original cut stone wall and entrance to Cabinteely Park. A further entrance provides a short-cut to the convenience stores at The Park Retail Centre to include a Tesco Express, Dry Cleaners, Chemist and Medical Centre.\n\nOutside there are two allocated parking spaces. Carrickmines Woods is a stylish and sought after location, rich in all the amenities offered by the nearby villages of Foxrock and Cabinteely, between them offering an abundance of social, leisure and essential amenities, to include local shopping, restaurants and coffee shops, with a wider variety of shopping available in Corneslcourt and Carrickmines Retail Park.
