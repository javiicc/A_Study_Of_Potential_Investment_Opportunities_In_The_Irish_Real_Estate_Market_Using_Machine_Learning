    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to present 7 The Crescent. situated in a quiet cul-de-sac off Gardiner s Hill which is one of Cork s most sought after and impressive addresses. \n\n7 The Crescent was built in c. 1900 s is a three-bedroom terraced family home which has been renovated and boasts light filled accommodation throughout. This home has some original features with high ceilings, sash windows to the front (which are draught-proofed), original internal and external doors, timber flooring, picture rails and fireplaces. There is a west facing front landscaped area to the front with gated access with small yard to the rear.\n\nThe bustling St. Luke s cross is just a stone s throw away, offering all of life s essential amenities such as shops, pubs, restaurants and bus service, and is within a fifteen-minute walk of the city centre with easy access to the northern ring road network.\n\nThis ideal family home is sure to tick many boxes for those seeking a quality home within a mature and established location. \n\nViewing is a must in order to appreciate this beautiful Cork City property!\nHallway 1.39m x 7.37m. Welcoming entrance hall with original tiling, picture rail and ornate arch at the bottom of the stairwell. There is also a storage area under the stairs.\n\nSitting Room 4.04m x 3.50m. Spacious and bright sitting room which you enter from the hall with a bay window and timber panelling overlooking the front garden. This room has an original timber floor, original shutters, picture rail and an ornate open fireplace.\n\nDining Room 3.55m x 3.60m. This room overlooks the rear yard with a picture rail, and an open fireplace.\n\nKitchen 2.31m x 3.70m. This is a galley style kitchen has fitted floor and eye level units with a breakfast bar. The kitchen has an integrated double oven and hob with tiling over the countertop and a door to the rear yard.\n\nUtility Room 1.04m x 1.74m. This area is to the back of the hall with a tiled floor and is plumbed for a washing machine.\n\nShower Room 1.74m x 1.42m. This room has a three-piece shower suite with pump shower and is fully tiled.\n\nLanding Bright landing with original timber floor, picture rail and has access to the attic.\n\nBedroom 1 3.58m x 3.57m. A bright double bedroom to the front of the home with original timber floor and picture rail.\n\nBedroom 2 3.00m x 3.56m. This double bedroom located to the rear of the property has an original timber floor, picture rail and built-in wardrobes with a sink.\n\nBedroom 3 1.86m x 3.54m. Located to the front this room also has original timber flooring and picture rail. This is currently used as a home office.\n\nBathroom 1.81m x 3.86. Three-piece bathroom suite with pump shower fitted over the bath. The floor is tiled along with the walls surrounding the bath and half way up the walls.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom Cork city centre, make your way up Summerhill North. Go straight through St. Luke' s Cross and take the next right after the traffic lights on to Gardiner' s Hill. At the bottom of the hill turn left into The Crescent and the property is located on the right-hand side. See agent sign.
