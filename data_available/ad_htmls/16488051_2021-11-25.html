    We are delighted to offer for sale this seven bedroom two-storey residence which includes a shop unit. This is a unique opportunity for any potential purchaser to develop a substantial business which may include a B&B or Airbnb, Retail shop, Filing station, Hardware store or Storage facility. The property comprises of entrance hall, sitting room, living room, kitchen/dining area, utility, ground floor wc, shop unit with street access. First floor includes seven double bedrooms, one ensuite, bathroom and first floor wc. Outside comprises of two garages, boiler house, patio area, two stables and log cabin. To the side of the dwelling there stands petrol pumps, forecourt, canopy, car wash, and two additional two-storey commercial units measuring 80 sqm. At the rear of this property there is a 340 sqm warehouse with lorry access that stands on a fully concrete hard-cored yard. Services include mains water, septic tank with the opportunity of a mains sewerage connection, oil fire central heating and a solid fuel range with back boiler.
