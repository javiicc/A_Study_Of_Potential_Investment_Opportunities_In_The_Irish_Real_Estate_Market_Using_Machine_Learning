    Sherry FitzGerald Durcan proudly present to the market this stunning executive residence which has been finished and maintained to the highest of standards. This luxury dwelling boasts a whole host of attractive features which are sure to impress even the most discerning of potential purchasers.\n\nBuilt in 2006 the residence extends to approximately 315 square metres (3,390 square feet) laid out over two floors with further development opportunity in the large attic area. An impressive stamped concrete driveway leads up to the property before enwrapping the dwelling to allow the safe and convenient circulation of traffic, a large paved area to the rear of the house provides ample parking.
