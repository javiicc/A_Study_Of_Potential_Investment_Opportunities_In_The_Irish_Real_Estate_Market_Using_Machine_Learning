    4 bedroom Bungalow c. 161 m\xc2\xb2 (c. 1,733 Sq.Ft.) plus large garage. 5 Mins drive from Shannonside village of Rooskey & convenient to Kilglass Lake.\r\n\r\nThe house features a very large front sitting room with open fireplace and three south facing windows, creating a really bright living area. The kitchen has built in units, and a solid fuel stove with back boiler, there is a separate dining room. \r\n\r\nThere are four double sized bedrooms all with built in wardrobes and two bedrooms have ensuite shower rooms. There is a separate family bathroom. \r\n\r\nOutside there is a large garage with separate sections for a pantry store and utility room with a WC. Behind this is a separate fuel shed.\r\n\r\nThe house is on a double sized plot of c. 0.93 acres, with a large lawn to the side of the house. There is a double entrance driveway to the front of the house and to the rear there is low maintenance paving and decorative stone. \r\n\r\nThe property is c. 5 km east of Rooskey Village and 7 km from Scramoge and the N5 Castlebar Road. Rooskey is situated just off the N4 Dublin - Sligo road, a riverside village with a popular local supermarket and a number of good pubs. Kilglass lake on the Shannon system is nearby and there are nice quiet roads for walks in the area. There is boat access at Kilglass marina 3 mins drive away.\r\n\r\n\r\nSERVICES :\r\n-\tMains Water & well available\r\n- Septic tank, \r\n-\tDual system central heating (oil & solid fuel)\r\n\r\n\r\nEircode: F42 F952 \r\n\r\nGPS: 53.823, -7.998
