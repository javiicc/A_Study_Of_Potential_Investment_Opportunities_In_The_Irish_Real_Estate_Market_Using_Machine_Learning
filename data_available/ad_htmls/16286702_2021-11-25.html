    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    We are delighted to offer for sale this exceptionally well-located two-bedroom apartment in Dunfril House; just a short walk from Ballybofey's Main Street and amenities including supermarkets, a good range of primary and secondary schools, hotels / restaurants, Finn Valley Leisure Centre and the very popular Drumboe Woods walking route. Located on the second floor of the building, apartment accommodation comprises of hallway, a bright and spacious open plan sitting room / kitchen, two bedrooms and a bathroom. Features include a gated parking area to the side of the building, an intercom system to allow access for visitors and gas central heating. This property would be ideal for first time buyers trying to get their feet on the property ladder or for anyone seeking an investment opportunity.
