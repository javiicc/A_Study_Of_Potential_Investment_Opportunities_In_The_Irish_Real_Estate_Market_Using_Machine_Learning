    Sherry FitzGerald McCreery are truly honoured and delighted to present to the market this very fine home of distinction in a sought-after location on the edge of Kilkenny City.\n \nGrangeford House is a stunning executive residence standing on circa 1.2 acres of magnificent and fully enclosed landscaped gardens.\n \nThe property was built in the early 1990 s by local builder Gerard O Brien, as his private residence.\n\nThe house extends to 270 Sq. M. / 2,917 Sq. Ft. approx. of luxury accommodation with extensive front and rear gardens.\n\nThe layout comprises an entrance porch with mosaic tiles, leading to a spacious square hallway. To the left of the hallway is a large living room with feature marble open fireplace. Double doors lead into a dining room with dual aspect windows overlooking the grounds at the front of the house. At the other end of the living room are French doors leading into a garden room with panoramic views of the gardens and patio area. The patio can be accessed from the garden room through French doors. There are two further doors leading from the hallway. The first door leads into the home office which has French doors onto the patio. Just off the home office is a downstairs cloakroom. The second door leads into a bright and spacious open plan kitchen, dining and a family area with ample kitchen units, double Belfast sink, electric range cooker and wired for satellite television. Leading from the kitchen is a large L shaped utility room with an American style fridge freezer, dishwasher, washing machine and a tumble dryer. The utility room has a double Belfast sink and plenty of storage units. Both the kitchen and the utility room have a generous amount of metro tiling. Leading from the utility room is a tiled floor hallway with a fully tiled storage room. Also of this hallway is a bedroom with a fully tiled en-suite bathroom complete with an electric shower. There is also a side entrance door off the hallway. Two other features of the utility room are the boiler room which houses the Vokera gas boiler and a large fully insulated and floored storage area which is accessed by way of a stira staircase. All downstairs floors areas are a mixture of quality laminates and tiles. Leading from the entrance hallway is a bespoke staircase to the first-floor galleried landing with dual aspect windows. On this floor are four spacious bedrooms, three of which have fully tiled en-suites. There is also a fully tiled family bathroom with a very large bath. A double door hot press with shelving. Three of the bedrooms have large wardrobe space. The staircase continues from the first to the second-floor landing with built-in wardrobes. Two further rooms are on this floor one of which is a home gym, the other was used as a leisure/sitting room. Just off this room is easily accessed floored storage space. \n \nGARDEN AND GROUNDS\nThe landscaped and mature grounds extend to circa 1.2 Acres / 0.49 Hectares and are a wonderful oasis of peace and tranquillity being particularly mature, sheltered and well stocked with an abundance of mature shrubs, trees and plants and providing all year-round colour and privacy. The property is well set back from the road and accessed up a long stone driveway complete with decorative street lanterns. There is plenty of parking to the front and further parking to the rear accessed through wooden farm gates. The neatly cut front and rear lawns are bordered by mature Conifer hedging. The outside patio area is surrounded by well planted shrubbery. There is plenty of space for alfresco dining and entertaining. A Steel shed sitting on a concrete base is positioned to the side of the house and is practical for all storage needs.\n\nLOCATION\nGrangeford House is located at the start of the N76 Callan Road and just 150 meters from the Kilkenny Ring Road and 1km from Kilkenny City Centre. The property is within 500m of Hotel Kilkenny and is easily accessible via the Ring Road to the M9 corridor. A fifteen-minute walk will take you into Kilkenny City Centre to enjoy many of the wonderful attractions including Kilkenny Castle, Kilkenny Design Centre, Rothe House and St Canice s Cathedral. The Medieval Mile Museum, located in the heart of Kilkenny City, houses within the finest example of a medieval church in Ireland. The Medieval Mile isn t just focused on ancient history. Kilkenny is one of Ireland s most cosmopolitan cities, with many blooming festivals, eateries and creative people resulting in an electric atmosphere. Amenities such as Loughboy Shopping Centre and Market Cross Shopping Centre are all within walking distance. The property is convenient to excellent primary and secondary schools such as St. Patricks Boys National School, St. John of Gods Girls National School, Gael Scoil Osrai, Presentation Secondary School St. Kieran s College, CBS national and secondary schools, St Canice s national school and Loreto girls secondary school.\n \nViewing is highly recommended.\n\n\nGROUND FLOOR\n\nEntrance Porch 3.15m x 1.20m.
