    Floor area c. 69 sqm / 742 sqft\nFlynn Estate Agents are delighted to welcome number 3 Maple Glen to the market. This exceptional 3 bedroom semi-detached home comes to the market in excellent condition with no expense spared by the current owner. Nicely situated in this highly sought after mature estate in the heart of Laurel Lodge. This beautifully presented home is in exceptional condition throughout and would make an ideal family home. Boasting an updated heating system and new double glazed pvc windows. The accommodation in brief comprises of bright living room with bay window and double doors leading into spacious fitted kitchen / dining room with French doors to South facing rear garden, three bedrooms and main bathroom. Also boasting a stunning South facing rear garden and quiet cul-de-sac location. Maple Glen has easy access to all amenities such as Scoil Thomais National School, Laurel Lodge Shopping Centre, bus stops and train routes to the City Centre, the Blanchardstown Shopping Centre, Castleknock Hotel, sports clubs and many more.
