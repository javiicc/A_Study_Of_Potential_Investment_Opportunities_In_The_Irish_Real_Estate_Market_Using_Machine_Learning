    Vigo Lodge is a 1930s stone built house, currently a listed structure built by Lord Clarina as a hunting lodge located at the end of the much sought after Wood Road area that overlooks Inchiquin. There are many original features in this home such as fireplaces, timber panelling and window shutters. The property has undergone major upgrading in recent years such as new roof, new heating system, newly wired and new shower room. Viewing is strictly by prior appointment with sole selling agents.
