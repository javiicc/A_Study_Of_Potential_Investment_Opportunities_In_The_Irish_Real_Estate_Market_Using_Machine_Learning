    Aherne Loughnan Auctioneers are proud to launch to the market number 13 The Moorings, Harty's Quay, Rochestown, Cork. A highly impressive one bedroom, second floor apartment with a stunning projection view of the waterfront with panoramic views. Located on the Rochestown Road in a mature and quiet well maintained gated development, it offers a bright, light filled and cleverly designed interior. The property is in show house condition and includes a fully fitted kitchen with top quality integrated kitchen appliances. There is lift and stair access to the apartment. There is also one designated car space. Harty's Quay enjoys a convenient location within easy reach of Douglas Village with all amenities close by such as schools, shops, bustling restaurants/pubs and the Rochestown to Blackrock Castle walk-way is located just behind the complex.
