    Rarely does a Gate Lodge come to the market in such immaculate condition. The Gate Lodge, 100 Mount Merrion Avenue has been thoroughly renovated and extended by the current owners, bringing the property up to modern day living standards whilst still retaining the charm of the original gate house. Dating from the late 19th century, the property originally served as a gate house, providing entrance to the Earl of Pembroke\xe2\x80\x99s estate. \n\nThis property is sure to suit a host of buyers from those looking to upsize to a spacious three bedroom home in the heart of Blackrock or alternatively those wishing to downsize to a large bungalow in pristine condition. \n\nThe accommodation briefly comprises; A welcoming entrance porch that opens to the living area which retains the period charm of the original gate house, with and sash windows. This living room is open to the large bright dining area with vaulted ceilings and double patio doors to the garden. There is a large modern kitchen open to the dining room with all the modern built-in appliances including a Rangemaster cooker. Feature double doors divide the living room from the vaulted skylit hallway, creating a bedroom wing with three large double bedrooms all with built in wardrobes and master with en-suite. Finally a modern bathroom and outdoor utility/storage room complete the accommodation. \n\nThe Gate Lodge boasts two manicured garden areas. Through newly fitted car gates is the 65ft larger garden, boasting a south-easterly aspect, granite & limestone paved driveway partially gravelled with space for at least two cars, lawned area, mature plants, shrubs and trees, all bordered by a new garden fence, large composite decking area perfect for dining \xe2\x80\x98\xe2\x80\x99Al fresco\xe2\x80\x99\xe2\x80\x99 and boasting an amazing garden room with which makes a perfect home gym or office with separate garden storage. The second garden is situated to the rear of the property and is mainly laid in lawn with decked path, granite walls and storage room/ utility. \n\nThe location is of unparalleled convenience being situated within walking distance of Blackrock Village with its wide array of restaurants, cafes, boutique style shops, two shopping centres and the vibrant amenities at the Blackrock Market. The property is also situated within a pleasant stroll of Blackrock Park, Carysfort Park and many pleasant coastal walks. Transport options are excellent being close to the Quality Bus Corridor (QBC) as well as the DART making access to the city centre very simple and the M50 is also close at hand opening up the national road networks. Further amenities are available at Stillorgan.
