The apartment is situated beside all amenities which include a wide range of schools, child-care, sports facilities and good public transport to the city centre, such as Milltown Green Line Luas, bus routes 14, 15a, 15b, 15n, 49n and 65. You enter through a beautiful Georgian door to a common hall. The apartment is accessed via a hall to a very bright and extremely spacious living room with beautiful original sash windows. The master bedroom is luxurious and has a high quality zip & link orthopaedic king size bed plus two x two door double wardrobes. The large single bedroom is located to the rear of the apartment overlooking the large private shared rear garden. The bathroom is newly renovated. The kitchen is brand new and bright.
