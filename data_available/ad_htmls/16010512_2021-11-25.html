    Smith & Butler Estates are truly delighted to present this beautiful, mid-terraced house to the market. Number 18 is a very well maintained and laid out accommodation. As soon as you enter the property you are presented with a spacious lounge/living room with a distinctive, decorated gas log fireplace, laminated wood flooring and separate kitchen with half tile walls and a small storage space. On the first floor there are two double bedrooms with built in wardrobes, a family bathroom which is tiled floor to ceiling. The property further boasts double glazed windows and gas fired central heating\r\nFinn Eber Square, Finglas is a mature, quiet and secluded street ideally located just a short distance from a host of local amenities such as schools, sports clubs, parks and Finglas village. The location also benefits from excellent public transport links by bus and access to both the city centre and M50 are within easy reach via the Finglas road. \r\n\r\n\r\nEntrance hall - 1.63 x 0.94 with laminate wood flooring and access to sitting room and dining area.\r\n\r\nKitchen - 4.31 x 2.11 Inc alcove with tiled floors and a good selection of wall and floor mounted units, tiled splash-back, integrated oven and hob and fitted blinds.\r\n\r\nLiving room - 6.25 x 4.06 Inc alcove with laminate wood flooring, storage access, feature fireplace, TV point and ceiling coving.\r\n\r\nLanding - 3.88 x 0.90 Inc stairwell is carpeted.\r\n\r\nBed 1 - 3.51 x 3.05 with laminate wood flooring, built-in-wardrobes and fitted blinds.\r\n\r\nBed 2 - 3.51 x 2.83 with laminate wood flooring, built-in-wardrobes and fitted blinds.\r\n\r\nBathroom - 2.95 x 1.75 floor to ceiling tiling, w.c., w.h.b., vanity units above sink, bath with shower above and folding glass screen.\r\n\r\n\r\nTotal 65 m sq approx
