    Situated in a choice location at the bottom of a quiet, tree lined cul de sac, 61 Flower Grove has been carefully updated over the years to provide spacious, flexible accommodation to suit modern day living. \r\nTo the front of the property there is a generous paved parking apron boarded by mature hedging with a side passage to the south east facing low maintenance rear patio garden with raised beds and a workshop / utility room.\r\nThe property comprises a reception hall with deceptively spacious shower room off, kitchen/diner, living room and sitting room on the ground floor. Upstairs there are 4 bedrooms and bathroom together with stairs leading to a stylish attic room / home office with Velux windows and views of the Obelisk.\r\nIts situation, off Avondale Road offers convenient access to many amenities in a secure, suburban location.
