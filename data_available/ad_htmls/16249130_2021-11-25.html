    REA Halnon McKenna are delighted to offer for sale this attractive 2 bedroom apartment found in the award winning development of Gort Na Coiribe located on the Headford Road, Galway. Constructed by premier builders O Malley construction in 2003, the estate itself is meticulously appointed with beautifully presented green areas, mature trees and shrubbery. The development of Gort na Coiribe comprises a mixture of majority 2 & 3 bedroom apartments. \n\nNo 148 Gort Na Coiribe is a spectacular ground floor apartment with a spacious, modern feel. The property has been maintained and presented in wonderful condition and would prove to be the ideal owner occupier residence, holiday home or gilt edged investment. \n\nThe property briefly comprises:\n\nEntrance hall with a storage closet and hot press\n2 x well sized double bedrooms with fitted wardrobes\n1 x bathroom tiled floor to ceiling with bath/electric shower, wc and sink\nA large open plan kitchen/living/dining area with feature fireplace and plenty of cupboard space\nA private terrace\n\nGort Na Coiribe is well positioned, adjacent to Terryland retail park, Galway Shopping centre and the Liosbaun industrial park. It is also is within walking distance of Galway City centre, UCHG, & the NUIG campus. \n\nViewing comes highly recommended.\n\nDisclaimer: Whilst every care has been taken in the preparation of these particulars, and they are believed to be correct, they are not warranted and intending lessees should satisfy themselves as to the correctness of information given.
