    POSITIVE PROPERTY is proud to present this magnificently spacious 6 bedroom detached home situated in a secluded Cul de Sac in the desirable low-density estate Ard na Mara. Occupying a most enviable site circa. 950sqm this home has been owned by the same family since it was built in 1965. \r\n\r\nOn entering through the front gate one immediately notices the mature gardens with many plants, trees and fauna that gives a somewhat exotic feel. The detached dormer bungalow that imposes behind this wonderful garden with its light coloured brick and dark wooden facade adds to all of it's character. \r\n\r\nThe entrance door is located along the side of the property with a storm porch and oak front door that leads into the entrance hall. \r\nDouble doors open to the spacious front living room with wooden floors and a feature fire place, the kitchen/ diner is a very generous in size with accees to the rear garden and the garage. \r\nThere are three bedrooms on the ground floor in total, two bedrooms facing to the side of the property and one bedroom facing to the rear with built in wardrobes. At the end of the hall is the family bathroom with bath, separate shower, whb & wc. The rear living room with feature fireplace has access to the conservatory that overlooks the wonderful garden. \r\n\r\nUpstairs is the landing with reading space, two large bedrooms the the front and rear, and a further bedroom to the side. There is a bathroom with wc & whb and storage room. \r\n\r\nThe property has been well maintained over the years but has great potential to be modernised and possibly extended subject to consents. \r\n\r\nTo appreciate this property in its entirety please email your details for the next viewing or contact Dan Fitzsimmons on 0862292450 to discuss. \r\n\r\nPlease note: *We are advise that planning was obtained for the attic conversion. \r\n*Information provided is to the best of our knowledge.
