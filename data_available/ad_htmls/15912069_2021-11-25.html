    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Enjoying a highly convenient location, No 2 Glencoe is a beautiful presented three bedroom townhouse located on Castle Street, Dunmore, only minutes away from all the local amenities of Dunmore, County Galway boasting a wide selection of shops, bars, wonderful park, excellent schools and local walk ways. The M17 motorway is only tens minutes drive away which is situated in Tuam. Regular daily bus services from Bus Eireann and Burkes Bus to Galway which makes commuting hassle free. We expect strong interest in this lovely walk in condition townhouse from first time buyers, those looking to downsize and investors alike. Accommodation comprises hallway, sitting room, kitchen/dining area, utility, Upstairs landing, bathroom and three bedrooms.
