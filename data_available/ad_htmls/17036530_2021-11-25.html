    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    This appealing property is situated in the beautiful countryside setting of Kilferagh on the outskirts of Kilkee town. Kilkee is a picturesque coastal town in West Clare delightfully set on the wild Atlantic Way. Kilkee offers a large variety of services and amenities that includes supermarket, pubs, cafes, restaurants, golf, beach, fishing and scenic walking routes.\r\n\r\nThe house is an old-style single storey stone cottage that has been renovated to a high standard over the years. The property comprises of a kitchen, living room, sunroom, shower room and three bedrooms. One of the bedrooms is currently used as a dressing room so the property is very flexible. \r\nThis is also illustrated with the outdoor space and the extensive selection of outbuildings. The sheds form a lovely courtyard with the back of the house and each shed has its own use that includes utility, fuel storage, pottery shed etc. The garden extends to a half-acre site on the western side of the house, with a beautiful spacious tree lined area that also includes a restored mobile home. This space has been used for a home office for two people again highlighting all the additional features of this wonderful property.\r\n\r\nACCOMODATION:\r\n\r\nKitchen: Tiled floor, wall and base units, solid fuel stove with back boiler. 4.6 x 4.0\r\n\r\nSunroom: Timber floor. 2.7 x 5.2\r\n\r\nBedroom 1: Laminate floor, curtains. 2.9 x 2.2\r\n\r\nBedroom 2: Laminate floor, curtains. 2.9 x 2.2\r\n\r\nLiving Room: Laminate floor, TV point, curtains. 3.0 x 4.5\r\n\r\nBedroom 3: Laminate floor, fully shelved-currently dressing room. 2.0 x 2.2\r\n\r\nShower Room: Fully tiled walls and floor, electric shower, wc, whb, built-in storgae/shelving. 2.2 x 2.0
