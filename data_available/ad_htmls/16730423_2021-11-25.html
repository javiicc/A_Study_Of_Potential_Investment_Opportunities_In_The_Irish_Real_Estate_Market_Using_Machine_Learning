    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to present 59 Sandymount Road to the market. This beautiful four-bedroom semidetached family home is situated amongst the leafy surrounds of this mature residential area, just a leisurely stroll from Sandymount village. This home benefits from having many of the necessary attributes a family could possibly want - off street parking to the front, a secluded front and rear garden, a garage to the side and all this within striking distance of proximity to schools, transport links and local shops. \n\nThe accommodation offers the feeling of space and brightness throughout and briefly comprises of entrance porch, entrance hallway, guest w.c. dining/reception room to the front with pocket doors leading to the living room. There is an open plan kitchen and breakfast room with double doors giving access to the rear sunny southwest garden and a block-built storage shed. On the first floor there are three double bedrooms, a single bedroom and a family shower room.\n\nOutside the property doesn t disappoint, there is off-street parking to the front as well as a landscaped front garden. Side access leads to the sunny southwest facing rear garden and patio. The garden measures approx. 22.4m x 10.7m and is mainly laid in lawn with mature hedging and shrubbery as well as a large patio area positioned perfectly to catch the sun.\n\nThe location of this home cannot be understated. Superbly located in the heart of the village. There is off street parking but given the proximity of all the amenities on its doorstep a car may not be required. Grafton Street, Merrion Square, St. Vincent s hospital, Grand Canal Dock and the IFSC are all within easy access by foot, car or public transport. Dublin airport is a mere 20-minute drive via the Airport tunnel.\n\n\nEntrance Porch With tiled flooring\n\nEntrance Hall With wooden flooring, under stairs storage, alarm panel, ceiling coving, feature stain glass window and door to garage\n\nGuest WC With tiled flooring, w.c, wash hand basin and tiled splash back\n\nDining/Reception Room With a bay window to the front with feature stain glass, ceiling coving, picture rails, feature open fire, tv & phone points and pocket doors leading to living room\n\nLiving Room With a bay window to the rear, feature gas fire, tv & phone points, ceiling coving and picture rails\n\nBreakfast Room With wooden flooring, ceiling coving, picture rails, double doors opening onto sunny rear garden and open plan to kitchen\n\nKitchen With tiled flooring and tiled splash back, recessed lighting, a range of floor and wall units, integrated fridge/freezer, integrated oven/hobs, provision for dish washer, under cabinet lighting, window overlooking rear garden and stainless-steel sink with drainer\n\nGarage With concrete flooring, vehicle door to the front, stainless steel sink with drainer, tiled splash back and plumbed for washer/dryer.\n\nLanding With access to the attic and large shelved hot-press\n\nBedroom 1 Large double room with window to the front and built-in shelving\n\nBedroom 2 Large double room with window to the rear\n\nBedroom 3 Double room with tv points and window to the rear\n\nBedroom 4 Single room with window to the front
