    2 Bed bungalow c. 81.3 m\xc2\xb2 (c. 875 Sq.Ft.) plus integrated garage, in an attractive rural setting convenient to Ardagh Village and Longford Town.\r\n\r\n\r\nThis house is an extended cottage, in the original part there is an entrance porch and a Sitting / dining room with an open fireplace. The heart of the house is the living room / parlour that has a solid fuel Stanley range that heats the central heating system. Off this room there is a front bedroom. In the extension there is another bedroom, a fully tiled shower room with electric shower and a small kitchen. The kitchen has built in units and is beside the integrated garage of c. 134 Sq.ft., which could be converted to further accommodation subject to planning permission. Outside the house is on a site of c. 0.35 acres. There are two open fronted garages to the rear of the house. \r\n\r\nThe house is situated on the outskirts Ardagh village on a quiet cul de sac road with a number of one off houses in the area surrounded by agricultural lands. Longford Town centre is c. 11 km away. Edgeworthstown & N4 Dublin Rd is 10 km. Ardagh Village is steeped in history, winning numerous awards including the national tidy towns award. There is a genuine community feel to the area the village has Lyons' Pub, Shop & Post Office along with Church, National school and GAA grounds. There is fibre to the home broadband available on this road, so could be the ideal place to remote work from, with lots of space to make an ideal home office.\r\n\r\n\r\nSERVICES: \r\n- Mains Water & Septic tank, \r\n- Fibre to the home Gigabit broadband available\r\n- Solid fuel central heating.\r\n\r\n\r\nEircode: N39 PP78 \r\n\r\nGPS: 53.657, -7.704
