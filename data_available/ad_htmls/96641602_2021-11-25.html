    Development Description

      Introducing Bolton Grove, an exclusive development of 6 brand new luxury homes smartly nestled off the Ballyboden Road next to the exclusive Bolton Hall development. Bolton Grove provides a choice of homes ranging in size from 82 sq. m. to 119 sq. m. These new A rated homes have been architecturally designed to a very high specification and come with many superb features. \r\n\r\n3 x 2-bedroom Ground floor units each with individual south facing gardens\r\n\r\n2 x 3-bedroom duplex units with private balcony\r\n\r\n1 x 2-bedroom duplex unit with private balcony\r\n\r\nQuality retail facilities nearby including Dundrum Shopping Centre, Rathfarnham Shopping\r\nCentre, RathfarnhamVillage and Nutgrove Retail Park\r\n\r\nPremier location close to St. Ends Park, Marlay Park, Bushy Park and the beautiful Dublin Mountains\r\n\r\nSituated just off Ballyboden Road in the heart of Rathfarnham, Bolton Grove is within close proximity to some wonderful local amenities including St. Endas Park, Rathfarnham Village and Rathfarnham Shopping Centre to name but a few.
