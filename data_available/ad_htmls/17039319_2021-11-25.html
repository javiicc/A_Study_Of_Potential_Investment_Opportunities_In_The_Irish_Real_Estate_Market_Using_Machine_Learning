    Located in the beautiful countryside of Curraghduff/ Ballysop boarding JFK Arboretum and Slievecoillte the sale of this fine 4 bed residence offers a rare opportunity to acquire a home in a scenic and sought after area close to New Ross and Campile. The well-presented and flexible living accommodation of 170sq m comprises of a welcoming entrance hall, living room with feature bay window, study/ office, double bedroom, spacious kitchen/ diner, sunroom, bathroom and utility all on the ground floor. On the first floor there are 3 bedrooms and bathroom. The property stands on c. 1.1 acres with expansive views across the surrounding countryside and includes a double garage, 3 loose boxes and tack room.
