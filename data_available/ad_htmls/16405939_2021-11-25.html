    93 Shandon Park is an exceptional end of terrace red brick period house ( c. 85 sq. m ) situated in this highly sought after mature Phibsboro location, just minutes from the village and within walking distance of shops, parks and public transport. The Grangegorman DIT campus and the Mater Hospital are also nearby. Although requiring some upgrading the house is reminiscent of a bygone era with all original features intact. Accommodation includes an entrance hall, sitting room, kitchen living room and guest wc at ground level and the first floor has three spacious bedrooms and a family bathroom. Located at the junction of Shandon Park and Shandon Crescent the property enjoys a generous garden offering a pleasant southerly aspect with separate garage to the rear.
