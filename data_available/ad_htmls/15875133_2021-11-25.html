    Sandy Lane is a detached one-of-a-kind residence with mature professionally landscaped gardens with many aspects set on a site of approximately 0.4 acre, within the beautiful woodland setting that is Monkstown Castle Demesne. Architecturally designed, with the sea on its doorstep, this unique residence boosts a lot of amazing features as a lot of thought and care went into the finish of the house first day- a concrete floor at first level and a natural slate roof. This lovingly maintained home, which is just 20 years old, is a spacious, light filled luxury accommodation, both inside and out and is extremely well presented, pristine and alluring. There is a strong balance of living and bedroom accommodation making this a great family home. On the first floor, there are two amazing balconies that offer spectacular coastal and woodland views. The private, fully enclosed grounds are well laid out with lawns and a choice of sunny patios for outdoor enjoyment to the south and west. There is a large area to the front of Sandy Lane which offers parking for at least 8 cars on the sweeping driveway and unique detached garage that is constructed to the same high spec as the house, which offers great storage or potential outdoor workspace. \n\nSandy Lane s situation, on the fringe of Monkstown Golf Course, one of Cork s most prestigious and historic golf clubs dating back to 1908, offers fantastic privacy while having top class facilities and amenities on its doorstep. Monkstown Marina, Monkstown Lawn Tennis & Croquet Club and Monkstown Sailing Club are just minutes away. This picturesque waterside village is home to good local bars and high-class eateries, Italian caf deli and an art gallery all withing walking distance from this home. Along with an excellent mixed primary school and Montessori both within walking distance from the property, the secondary school is within a 10-minute drive. Cork airport is a 20-minute drive away, Cork City 20-minute drive away and the nearest sandy beach is a 20-minute drive away. \n\nMonkstown Castle Demesne is a most highly sought-after location where properties rarely come to the market. Sandy Lane is a wonderful opportunity for those seeking a truly great family home which offers luxury living at it s very best, in a private setting with an activity for all the family close by. \n\nSandy Lane, luxury living on the bay in Monktown, Co.
