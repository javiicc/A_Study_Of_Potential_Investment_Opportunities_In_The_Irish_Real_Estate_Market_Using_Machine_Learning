    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    24 Crescent House is a delightful one bedroom first floor apartment which comes to the market presented in superb condition. The accommodation comprises of a welcoming entrance hall with intercom system, Dining/living room with feature fireplace, kitchen/breakfast room, bedroom with slide robe wardrobes, and the bathroom completes the picture of this charming apartment. Crescent House is a well maintained residential block of apartments, the communal gardens are beautifully landscaped and feature mature plants, shrubs and trees throughout. The building is serviced by a lift and the apartment comes with one designated car parking space. The location couldn't be more convenient, situated just off the Howth Road in Clontarf and overlooking Marino Crescent Park, Crescent House is situated within a short distance to Clontarf DART station, East Point Business Park, Fairview shops and park, Westwood Leisure Centre, Clontarf s Promenade, St Anne s Park, a very well serviced bus route and within 3kms of the city centre. Sporting enthusiasts are spoilt for choice with numerous facilities in the area having access to a generous array of clubs including tennis, rugby, cricket, Gaelic, sailing, Westwood Gym and golf at Clontarf Golf Club, Royal Dublin and St Anne s Golf Club.
