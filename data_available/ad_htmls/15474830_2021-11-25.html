    Large 4/5 bedroomed detached family home standing on almost 0.6 acres and with spectacular views over the surrounding countryside.\n\nThis 4/5 bedroomed two-storey plus attic conversion property is a fine sized fmaily home positioned in an attractive scenic setting, just outside Ballinspittle village, with woods and several beaches within minutes drive and The Old Head and Kinsale town just a little further. The location is very attractive for families as there are excellent primary schools in the immediate vicinity and secondary at Kinsale and Bandon. The local community spirit a vibrant village such as Ballinspittle offers, makes it the ideal place to consider relocating to. \n\nThe spacious floor area is laid out over three floors and extends to 243 sq m (2615 sq ft) and offers an adaptable layout which is ideal for families and those working from home. The property is presented to the market in immaculate condition having been recently redecorated and newly carpeted, offering a fresh neutral palate for a new owner to move in immediately without having to renovate or upgrade. \n\nAccommodation includes entrance hall with doors opening to the livingroom on the right and the kitchen/dining/living on the left. The property is designed to maximise natural light, with windows to the east, south and west. The large reception room runs along the western side of the house and has a step up to a higher area which could be formal dining or second seating area with doors opening to the wrap-around deck, the lower level enjoying the benefit of the stove. The kitchen/dining/living is on the eastern side of the property and is also split level, with a fine elm fitted kitchen and plenty of room for dining space and living space of the kitchen, with this area also having a solid fuel stove. The sunroom opens to the deck also. A useful utility room with guest wc and door to deck is located at the rear of the house. \n\nUpstairs on the first floor are three double bedrooms including the master suite with large bathroom en suite and walk-in wardrobe and the family bathroom is also on this floor. The attic has been converted and offers a fourth bedroom and an office/fifth bedroom and bathroom. \n\nHeating is underfloor oil fired and there are two stoves, making for very comfortable living. The drive is tarmacadam for low maintenance and there are lawns all around the property and a useful garden shed.
