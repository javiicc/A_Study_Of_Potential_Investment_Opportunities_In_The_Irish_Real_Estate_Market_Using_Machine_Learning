    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to bring to the market, Number 2 Rexdon Court, no expense has been spared on this stunning three bedroomed ground apartment with a unique and creative bespoke interior, ideally positioned in this small boutique development of only 11 apartments & 1 penthouse with a communal roof garden providing spectacular views of Dublin city and beyond. With its chic, light filled interior, excellently proportioned rooms and enviable Dublin 4 location this fine apartment will not fail to impress the most discerning of buyers. \n\nUpon entering the impressive hall, you are immediately drawn through double doors to the large living/dining room which offers great space and light and access to the large south facing terrace with a bar overlooking the manicured grounds, ideal for relaxing or an evening s entertainment. The contemporary kitchen enjoys a range of integrated appliances. There are three generously proportioned double bedrooms, all with their very own private terrace, two of which are en-suite and a family bathroom. An ever-useful utility room and spacious shelved hot press off the hallway complete the accommodation. The property is further enhanced with a designated car parking space accessed by a car lift and a lock up storage unit.\n\nLocated on one of Dublin s most popular and accessible roads, this Ballsbridge fine development is 200 meters from Sandymount DART station and within a stone s throw of Sandymount Village which provide a fine selection of restaurants, pubs and boutiques within a leisurely stroll. Invigorating walks can be enjoyed on Sandymount Strand which is within a short distance. Some of Dublin s premier Hotels such as the InterContinental and The Clayton are just across the street. The Facebook new Headquarter is just a block away. Recreational facilities are well catered for with the RDS just few minutes away and the Aviva Stadium close by, as are some of Dublin s principal places of business including AIB Bank Centre, St Stephen s Green, Merrion Square, and the IFSC.\n\nSPECIAL FEATURES\n\nGated development in boutique Dublin 4 development. \nBeautiful luxurious interior\nVery spacious living/dining room\nModern and well fitted out kitchen\nBeautiful tiled floors in the bedrooms\nExcellent storage\nSouth facing large terrace with cocktail bar overlooking the communal gardens\nEach bedroom with their own private terrace screened by beautiful trees providing excellent privacy\nHeated floors\nDesignated underground parking space\nWell maintained and manicured communal grounds and roof garden.\nLift\n\nSERVICES:G.F.C.H.\nIntercom\nAlarm\nTV & Telephone points\n\nService Charge 5,447 per annum \n\n\nEntrance Hall Spacious and welcoming entrance hallway with porcelain tiled floor, alarm, T.V intercom, recessed spot lighting and shelved hot press.\n\nLiving/Dining Room Beautiful open plan room flooded with natural light with windows and patio door to enviable south facing terrace with cocktail bar overlooking communal grounds, oak wood flooring, recessed lighting.\n\nKitchen Contemporary kitchen with a range base and eye level units with soft close function, polished white granite worktops, porcelain tiled floor and stainless-steel sink unit with Caple Spiro spray chrome mixer tap.
