    RAY COOKE AUCTIONEERS are delighted to present 81 Dunmore Park to the market. This two bedroom bungalow comes to the market in good condition throughout boasting huge potential. Kingswood is a mature and desirable pocket of Dublin 24, highly sought after by first time buyers and those looking to downsize so this property is sure to cause a stir amongst a wide range of buyers. Kingswood Drive finds itself within a short stroll of local shops, bars and sports facilities. Esteemed schools are in abundance with St. Killian\xe2\x82\xacTM Junior School always proving a firm favourite, and the newly constructed Kingswood Community College is continuing to be a major pull factor for the area. Following on from this Tallaght Hospital and The Square Shopping Centre are found minutes by car, the M50 Motorway and the Red Luas Line are both very easily accessible. Interior living accommodation of c. 780 sq. ft. comprises of entrance hallway with large open plan lounge / dining room, kitchen, two large double bedrooms and family bathroom. The front of the property offers ample parking space, to the rear is a sunny south-west facing rear garden with barna shed and large side access.
