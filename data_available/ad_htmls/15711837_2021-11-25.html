    Corliss House is a splendid three-storey residence in the heart of the historic market town of Kilrush. This striking residence is delightfully located on the doorstep of the Wild Atlantic Way and the Shannon Estuary on the west coast of County Clare. The property is situated within walking distance of all services and amenities that the local area has to offer. \r\n\r\nKilrush is a charming yet deceptively busy town offering a wide range of services and amenities to the people of west Clare. Historically renowned as a port town located on the Shannon Estuary the town has numerous attractions such as Scattery Island, the Marina, the Walled Vandeleur Gardens and Cappa Pier to name but a few. Kilrush also has a large range of supermarkets, restaurants, pubs, cafes, banks, etc. \r\n\r\n\r\nFrances Street boasts some magnificent merchant houses and Corliss House is no exception as it stands three-storeys tall and was built in the mid 1800's. The property has been renovated since its original construction with many of its original features remaining intact. The house requires some renovation work and comprises of eight bedrooms, two reception rooms, a large kitchen/dining area, four bathrooms and a two-bed apartment to the rear.\r\n\r\nThe kitchen/dining area opens up onto a sun-drenched courtyard area with a fabulous walled garden area on an upper level to the rear. The residence enjoys rear access via a laneway at the back of the garden and a bow-way from Frances Street offers an entrance to the courtyard. The apartment to the rear consists of two bedrooms, open plan kitchen/living room and a shower room.
