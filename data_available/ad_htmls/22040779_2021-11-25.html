      The Grange is in the heart of Stillorgan, only 25 minutes by Luas from St. Stephen\xe2\x80\x99s Green, giving you the natural beauty of Dublin Bay on your doorstep and easy access to the cultural attractions of the city centre. Sandyford Luas station is just a 15-minute walk from your door while the N11 is right outside, taking you north or south.
