    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to present 5 Alexandra Terrace to the market. This superbly presented and extensively refurbished Victorian residence is perfectly located in the heart of Dublin 6, within walking distance to the villages of Terenure, Rathgar and Rathmines. The property is presented in excellent condition throughout and retains many original features synonomous with the Victorian era. \r\n\r\nNo. 5 Alexandra Terrace briefly comprises a welcoming entrance hall, two interconnecting, light filled receptions rooms and a well appointed kitchen. To the rear there is an extremely private and sunny West facing rear garden that benefits from pedestrian rear access. Upstairs there are two bedrooms and a shower room. The attic has been converted and is accessed via a Stira. This verstile part of the house suits a multitude of uses, such as a home office, or can be used for extra storage space.\r\n\r\nAlexandra Terrace is a charming residential road with every conceivable amenity on its doorstep, including excellent local shopping, several fine delicatessens and coffee shops, numerous restaurants and pubs, and a range of leisure facilities and parks. There are also regular and frequent transport links providing ease of access to the city centre.\r\n\r\nEarly viewing is strongly advised.\r\n\r\n\r\nEntrance Hall\r\n\r\nReception Room 1 3.52m x 3.35m.\r\n\r\nReception Room 2 3.65m x 3.27m.\r\n\r\nKitchen 2.86m x 2.24m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 4.58m x 3.40m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 3.33m x 2.86m.\r\n\r\nShower Room 2.48m x 2.18m.
