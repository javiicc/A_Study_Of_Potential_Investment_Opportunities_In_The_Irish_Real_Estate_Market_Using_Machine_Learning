    Moovingo is delighted to present this beautiful end-of-terrace house to the market. The house is full of character with a nice rear extension and is presented well. The property also enjoys a desirable south-facing orientation which floods the house with bright natural light. Superbly located a stone's throw from Dalkey village, this property will appeal to all manner of buyers including first time buyers.\n\nThe accommodation comprises an entrance hallway, spacious living room, bright kitchen/dining area and a family bathroom. Upstairs there are two double bedrooms. The rear garden is private with large stone wall at the back. It's south-facing orientation provides sun throughout the day. There is side access to the front driveway which has off-street parking for 2 cars.\n\nArea Info\nOne of the most sought-after locations in Dublin, Dalkey Village still maintains a great community feel while also enjoying an enviable location close to the sea and within easy reach of the city centre. The village has a wealth of amenities including specialty shops, coffee shops, restaurants, pubs, wine bars and public library. There are a good selection of schools in the immediate vicinity also including Loreto Dalkey, Harold Boys NS, and Castle Park.\n\nFor outdoor enthusiasts, local swimming spots include the Forty Foot, Vico Baths, Coliemore and Bullock Harbours, while there are also scenic walks on Killiney Hill nearby.\n\nDalkey DART Station provides quick and easy access into the City Centre and is a short walk from the property.\n\nDisclaimer: Any intending purchaser(s) shall accept that no statement, description or measurement contained in advertisement or any other document or published by the vendor or by Moovingo, in respect of the premises shall constitute a representation inducing the purchaser(s) to enter into any contract for sale, or any warranty forming part of any such contract for sale.
