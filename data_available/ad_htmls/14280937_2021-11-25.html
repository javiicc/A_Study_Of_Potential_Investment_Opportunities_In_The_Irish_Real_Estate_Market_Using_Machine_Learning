    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    DNG Martin O'Connor Land Sales are delighted to offer for sale by Private Treaty this once in a lifetime opportunity to acquire a large holding of land with a cottage and various outbuilding ruins in one of Connemara's most sought after destinations on Inishnee Island in Roundstone. In three sections, together with an area of commonage land, the property is an excellent opportunity to acquire a property with so much potential. Sitting across the bay from Roundstone village the cottage could be redeveloped in to a magnificent home with splendid views of the hills to the rear and the sea inlet to the front. \r\n\r\nThe land is well fenced and offers a mix of grazing lands with a good stretch of ground by the seaside. The property is ideally suited to those looking to expand existing holdings in the area or for those looking to relocate to a perfect get away destination property if they would consider rebuilding the cottage and having a hobby farm. There is an old bog road going through the land at the rear which overlooks Cloonile Bay, the perfect place to go trekking with ponies.\r\n\r\nThe property is ideally located just 3 kms from Roundstone, within 16 kms of Clifden and approximately 75 kms from Galway City centre. \r\n\r\nRoundstone is one of the oldest and most picturesque fishing villages in the West of Ireland. It is located amidst spectacular mountain scenery and beautiful coastline of Ireland's western region known as Connemara. Roundstone Village has increasingly become a growing community for many escaping city life. The area offers a variety of activities including fishing, golf, pony trekking and multiple glorious beaches at Gurteen & Dog's Bay as well as a number of wonderful restaurants and licensed premises.\r\n\r\nFor further details and maps please contact the sole selling agents DNG Martin O'Connor Land Sales on 091866708.\r\n\r\nFeatures\r\nCottage and land\r\nc. 29.95 acres with commonage\r\nIn three large sections, one plot with sea frontage\r\nExcellent opportunity to redevelop SPP in this wonderful area of Connemara\r\n3.6 km to Roundstone Village\r\nExcellent Hill & coastal views from cottage\r\nMains water, electricity passing the property\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nBER Details\r\nBER: Exempt\r\n\r\nDirections\r\nFrom Roundstone village take the R341 for approximately 2.5 km and take the right turning for Inishnee. Drive for 1.3km and the cottage is located on the left hand side just before a house with Eircdoe H91A3VH. Please refer to maps for the other plots of land.
