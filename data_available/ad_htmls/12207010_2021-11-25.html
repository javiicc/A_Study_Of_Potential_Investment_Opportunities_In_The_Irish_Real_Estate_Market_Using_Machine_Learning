    Gort Road, Tulla, Co.Clare\r\n\r\nc. 0.6 acre site for sale by Private Treaty.\r\n\r\nNestled on edge of Tulla Village, this site commands a central location within close proximity of all that Tulla and it's surrounds have to offer. Tulla is a thriving town, which acts as the commercial centre for the Parish of Tulla and surrounding areas, with every imaginable amenity available to it's inhabitants. There is a Primary and Secondary School, Doctor's Surgery, Health Clinic, Church, Funeral Home, Supervalu Supermarket, Petrol Station, Post Office, Pharmacy, Health Food Store, Farm Supply Stores, Veterinary Clinic, Hairdressers, Beauticians, Restaurants, GAA Pitch, Montessorri Schools, Creches, Library, Art Gallery, Playground, Farmers Market, Garden Centre and Local Bus Service that's just to name but a few. So you see, everything you need is right on your doorstep!!\r\n\r\nThe site itself is located just on the edge of the village on the Gort road, identified by our sign. The site is approx 15mins drive from Ennis (M18 motorway) and Shannon, and 35 mins from Limerick. In terms of location this site offers the tranquillity of country living with the added bonus of being walking distance of the village itself. \r\n\r\nTulla is surrounded by picture postcard scenery, nature walks and lakes for the outdoor enthusiast. It is also served by a local bus route to Ennis and surrounding villages and towns.\r\n\r\nDirections: Coming from Ennis take the left turn at the 4 crossroads for Tulla. Continue passed the secondary school on the left and further the primary school on the right hand side. Following the signs for Gort the site is on the edge of the village on the right hand side, clearly identified by our sign\r\n\r\nMaps and further information are available on request.
