    A magnificent Protected Structure in the heart of Drogheda fully refurbished to the highest of standards. This fabulous 4 storey red brick iconic building comprises of a Public House to Ground Floor with Storage Basement below together with three floors over consisting of 4 no. 2 Bedroom apartments and a converted roof space over. A fabulous investment mixed use building in turn key condition and fully furnished with high end sanitary ware, kitchens and floor finishes throughout. Each Apartment unit has a state of the art air to water heat pump for low running costs and each unit has wonderful bright south and south-west facing aspects with views across Drogheda. The residential accommodation is further enhanced with its own access off Peter Street into a bright light filled hall and landings from the elaborate light-well. Opportunity like this has never been available in Drogheda in such a beautiful Protected Structure too. The property is available in two units.
