    Welcome to 125 Ardilaun a truly magnificent Sorohan built semi detached family residence situated in an envious position in this mature and sought after residential development just off Carrickhill Road within walking distance of all local amenities excellent schools (including Portmarnock Community School) and, of course, the picturesque Velvet Strand and seafront.\n\nHidden beneath its attractive and distinctive red brick facade lies gracious well proportioned accommodation and upon arrival interested parties will instantly admire the light filled space which has been tastefully decorated and lovingly maintained by its current owners. Many outstanding features include a split-level kitchen dining space with stunning fitted units and worktops, a cosy garden room extension and a superb newly fitted stylish family bathroom. The property also benefits from extensive internal insulation upgrades throughout. \n\nThe gardens are another outstanding feature of the property with mature lawn to front with extensive cobblelock driveway providing ample off street parking.
