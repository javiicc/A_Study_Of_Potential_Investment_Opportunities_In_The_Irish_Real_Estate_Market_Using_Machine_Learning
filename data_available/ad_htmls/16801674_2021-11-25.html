    I am delighted to bring to the market a delightfully impressive property in the heart of west Clare. West Clare is renowned for it's beautiful scenery and this property both takes advantage of this and adds to it. It also has a large idyllic garden with lots unique features and a large rear shed. This is a must see property so call today to view this excellent property.\r\n\r\nThe house is one of those unique properties that people are always looking for but rarely find. It is just outside the town and yet incredibly close to the town been only 1.0km from the large Tesco store in Kilrush. Kilrush itself lies on the Wild Atlantic Way and is a charming market town with a selection of traditional pubs, restaurants, modern cafe's, shops and the renowned Vandeleur Wall Gardens. There are many impressive walks and cycling opportunities in Kilrush. The continuous expansions of our Kilrush Marina which has gone onto do sailing, kayaking and all other related activities to boating means there is endless experiences for all ages.\r\n\r\nThe property itself is a two storey house that was built with a great eye for detail and loads of traditional features that hopefully will make this feel like home from the moment you walk in. The property has an impressive balcony that overlooks the garden and catches the morning light. Throughout, this property there is a melding of inspiring opportunities with a treasure trove of traditional features.\r\n\r\nWhen you enter the porch you have a number of options from your living room to the left and your sitting room to the right which also leads onto the dining room. There is a traditional bathroom, fitted kitchen, a large utility room and a large extra room that can meet your families needs and dreams. It can be a playroom, work space or an extra bedroom. The options are yours to choose. \r\n\r\nUpstairs, you are greeted by a large landing and at the end of the landing you will walk out onto your balcony to enjoy all what the great Clare countryside has to offer. There you will find four large open bedrooms and a bathroom. This is a supreme property and would be ideal for anyone who wishes for a property in the countryside and yet within walking distance to the town and close to all amenities.
