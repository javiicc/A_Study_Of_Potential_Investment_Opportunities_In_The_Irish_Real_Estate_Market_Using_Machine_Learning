    Auctioneera is delighted to present 10 Huxley Crescent. This 4-bed semi-detached property comes to the market in excellent condition, sure to appeal to investors and growing families alike.\r\n\r\nThis spacious property comprises two bedrooms, two bathrooms (one an ensuite), a kitchen and a living room on the ground floor. Finished to high specification, the kitchen boasts all modern appliances, and the bedroom and ensuite are bright and accommodating.\xc2\xa0\r\n\r\nUpstairs consists of two more bedrooms and a third bathroom. The property enjoys plenty of light throughout, with built-in storage units in the bedrooms allowing for ample storage space.\r\n\r\nOutside in the large, walled garden space is a stylish outbuilding, tastefully decorated and wired for electricity, perfect for use as a home office/gym. Additionally, there is onsite parking for up to 3 cars.\r\n\r\nThis stylish, modern property enjoys an ideal location close to the city centre. Located off Cork Street in a small cul de sac, there are a wide range of amenities within walking distance, as well as excellent public transport links. The city centre is 15 minutes away by foot, and The Coombe and St James Hospital are within easy reach. Viewing comes highly recommended!
