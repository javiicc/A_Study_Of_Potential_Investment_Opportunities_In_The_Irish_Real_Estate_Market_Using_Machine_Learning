    We are delighted to offer this beautiful 3 bedroom, semi-detached property for sale which comes to the open market in pristine condition. \r\nThe property is located in the small, unique, residential development known as 'The Paddocks' in the town of Castlepollard. \r\nBriefly it comprises entrance hall, kitchen / dining area, utility room, sitting room and large WC on the ground floor with 3 bedrooms (2 double, 1 single, master ensuite with dressing area), main bathroom and hotpress on the first floor. \r\nThere is a low maintenance, private garden to the rear which has been recently landscaped to include a patio area. The garden can be accessed from the kitchen / dining room via double doors. \r\nThe property has many added benefits which include a newly fitted front door and letterbox, solid fuel stove in the sitting room, full alarm system, large storage space underneath the stairs, beautiful, modern, interior decor throughout, designated parking and Oil Fired Central Heating. In the past 3 years, new blinds have been fitted as well new flooring throughout. \r\nThe property is well located on the Dublin road (R395) with all the amenities the town has to offer both social & essential all within walking distance. \r\nThe picturesque villages of Fore & Collinstown, Lough Lene & Mullaghmeen Forest are all within 5km while Mullingar, the N4 & N3 can all be accessed within 20km.\r\n\r\nThis property would make an ideal home for first time buyers.\r\n\r\nViewing is Highly Recommended.
