    No. 7 Millpark is a very attractive, conveniently located and well-presented dormer residence positioned on the outskirts of the popular Castlebridge village. This 3 bedroom property is ideal for anyone looking for a home, with all the facilities one could need within walking distance including shops, school, post-office, church, pubs, restaurants, pharmacy, sports clubs, to name just a few. The stunning Curracloe beach and Raven forest is minutes driving distance and Wexford town centre is less than 10mins. Access to the N25 & N/M11 are within very easy reach also. The property itself comes to the market in superb condition and boasts a bright and light filled interior, all complemented by the tasteful and neutral d\xc3\xa9cor.\n\nThe property has a very warm and welcoming atmosphere from the moment you enter the front door, with a spacious kitchen/dining room leading from the hallway opening onto the well maintained and spacious rear garden to enjoy. The front living room is very snug and a downstairs bedroom offers more versatility for the home whilst complimenting the well-proportioned bedrooms upstairs.
