    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to present to the market 44 Elm Road. \n\nNo. 44 is an extended four-bedroomed and well proportioned terraced property overlooking a small green to the front. Presented to the market in very good condition and having been lovingly cared for by its current owners this charming home extends to approximately 91sq.m (980sq.ft), the accommodation briefly comprises, welcoming entrance hallway leading to a spacious living room with access to understairs storage, a bedroom/study, a large wet room and kitchen/breakfast room on ground floor level. Upstairs there are three good sized bedrooms - a large double bedroom to the front and a double and generous single overlooking the garden to the rear. The rear garden has a westerly aspect with a shed for additional storage and the walled garden to the front is mainly laid in lawn. \n\nThe property is located just off Malahide Road close to a host of amenities including shops, primary and secondary schools, restaurants, pubs, sporting and recreational facilities, as well as St Anne s Park and Dollymount Strand. The area is well serviced by public transport with numerous bus routes available on Collins Avenue and the nearby Malahide Road, as well as Killester DART which is just a short walk away. The M1, Dublin airport and M50 are all within 15 minutes drive. \n\nThe virtual viewing of this property is available on all websites, however, if you would like a physical viewing please email us or call the office on 01 8336555 to arrange an appointment.
