      Location

      The Elysian takes pride of place in the heart of Cork with 211 apartments that afford residents new opportunities to enjoy the cosmopolitan vibe of this true city of culture. \r\n\r\nBefore you explore the city, you have the convenience of Aldi and The Bookshelf Caf\xc3\xa9 onsite, ensuring your kitchen is always well stocked. \r\n\r\nYou can stroll to the South Mall, just 600 metres away, and admire some of the original Georgian houses that line this street while you enjoy a coffee on the terrace of the Imperial Hotel. Great eateries, caf\xc3\xa9s and bars are peppered throughout the city centre, just waiting to be discovered. \r\n\r\nPatrick Street, around the corner from the South Mall, is only 700 metres from The Elysian, giving you easy access to the cream of cultural attractions such as Cork Opera House, Crawford Art Gallery and Opera Lane, the home of style enthusiasts.\r\n\r\nThe renowned English Market, one of the city\xe2\x80\x99s treasures with its entrance on Grand Parade, is sheer bliss for connoisseurs of good food, offering you the finest local produce. If you like staying near the Marina, you can enjoy food and crafts at Marina Market every weekend on Centre Park Road, or a leisurely walk along Marina Way to Blackrock Castle and village, only two kilometres away. \r\n\r\nYou can admire the greenery of nearby Kennedy Park, which is merely a 10-minute walk away on Victoria Road, giving you plenty of recreational space. \r\n\r\nTravelling beyond the city is easy with Kent railway station just one kilometre from The Elysian, and the South Link Road outside your door, connecting you with Cork International Airport and the M8 motorway.
