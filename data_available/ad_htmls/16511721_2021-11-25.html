This property will ideally suit those in search of a large family home in a great location. The accommodation comprises of hallway with guest wc, open plan living room, kitchen /dining room and utility room providing access to the garage. Outside the south facing rear garden is mainly laid lawn with a decking area, mature trees and shrubberies. The front driveway is paved and offers ample off street carparking.
