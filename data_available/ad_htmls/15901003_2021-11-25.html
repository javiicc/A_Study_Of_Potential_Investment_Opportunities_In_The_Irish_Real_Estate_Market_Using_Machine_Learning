    Savills is delighted to present No. 5 Manor Avenue, Maryborough Ridge a splendid 4 bed semi-detached home in one of Douglas most sought-after neighbourhoods.\n\nThis generously sized property is decorated to an excellent standard with painstaking attention to detail on the interior design and presentation.\n\nThe entrance hall is spacious and welcoming with a beautiful tiled floor. Beneath the stairs is the guest WC, which has been tastefully tiled and decorated. \n\nThe livingroom is well proportioned with a beautiful ornate fireplace and has double doors leading to the kitchen area giving the whole downstairs living area a spacious and airy feel. There is also a bay window allowing in more natural light and accentuating the lovely view of the green to the front of the property. \n\nThe kitchen/dining/sitting room is very spacious with two patio doors leading to the garden, perfect for relaxing.\n\nThere is a beautiful contemporarily designed fully fitted kitchen that has a great selection of appliances including a five-ring gas hob, grill/oven, extractor fan, fridge freezer and dishwasher. The floor of the kitchen is tiled and there is a selection of light fittings. In addition there is also a separate utility room completing the experience, with side door access.\n\nOn the first floor is one double and one generous single bedroom facing the rear of the property. To the front of the property is a stylish large double bedroom that has a fantastic ensuite that is tiled and a full contemporary style en-suite. There is also a separate walk-in wardrobe just off this room. Also on this floor is a fully tiled modern main bathroom with a selection of superb sanitary wares.\n\nClimbing up to the second floor, you will find the master bedroom, which is an extremely generously sized double bedroom with a balcony, dressing room and an en-suite servicing it. This room is ideal for privacy from the rest of the house and could easily have an additional suite of furniture making it more self-contained.\n\nMaryborough Ridge is less than 10 minutes by car to Cork city centre and is close to all amenities including, shops, schools, sports clubs, leisure centres, hospitals, university, bus stops, the Jack Lynch tunnel and Cork Airport, to name but a few.\n\nDirectly across the road from Maryborough Ridge is a mini-market, hairdressers, pharmacy and there is a bus stop at the entrance to the development.\n\nThe development has many beautiful green areas and plenty of mature trees giving a soothing backdrop for in which to enjoy life at Manor Avenue.\n\nBER No: B3\nBER Number: 100220987\nEnergy Performance Indicator: 142.9 kWh/m2/yr\n\nVIEWING\nBy appointment with Savills Cork\n(021) 427 1371 or email:cork@savills.ie\n\nGround Floor \n\nEntrance Hallway Tiled flooring, radiator, telephone point, recess lighting, under stairs storage and beautiful carpet on stairs to first floor.\n\nGuest WC Fully tiled, WC, WHC, extractor fan, light fitting, radiator.\n\nLivingroom Spacious, well portioned room with a bay window, a lovely carpet, ornate fireplace, blinds, curtains, light fitting, radiator, TV and telephone point, corniced ceiling.\n\nKitchen/Dining Room/Sittingroom Bright and very spacious room with a fully fitted contemporary kitchen, five ring gas hob, grill/oven, extractor, fridge/freezer, dishwasher, two patio doors to rear garden, tiled flooring and splash back, blinds, curtains, recess light fittings, radiators, TV.\n\nFirst Floor \n\nLanding Carpet, power points, radiator, light fitting, blind.\n\nBedroom 2 Facing front garden with bay window, this sunny large double room could easily be the master bedroom if desired.
