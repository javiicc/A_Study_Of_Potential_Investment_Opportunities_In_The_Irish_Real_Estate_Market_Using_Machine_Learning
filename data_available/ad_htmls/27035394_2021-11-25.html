    Description

      Marina Village Greystones is a luxurious sea side residential living with stunning views towards Bray Head and across Irish Sea. The Greystones area provides you a mix of modern and village life with the apartment being short stroll from Greystones High Street boasting a wide range of shops, boutiques and choice of top class restaurants, bars and cafes suiting modern day lifestyle. \r\n\r\nThe location is 10 mins walk to Greystones Train station which takes you to Grand Canal Station in heart of Dublin city within 45 mins by DART. The nearby M50 and recently improved N11 make travelling by car to Dublin city centre and beyond within quick reach.\r\n\r\nThe property comprises of two double bedrooms and two bathrooms, including an En-suite. The spectacular views of the Irish Sea can be enjoyed from your master bedroom through the winter garden linked to it. The winter garden can act as a separate office/study space with a picturesque natural backdrop of sea and mountains or a place to relax with a beautiful view and soothing sound of sea waves.\r\n\r\nThe living room is bright, spacious with stunning views of the Sea Coast and mountains. It has an open plan Kitchen from Nolte by McNally with high quality appliances such as Electric Hob, Dishwasher, Fridge & Freezer and Microwave.\r\n\r\nAll Bathrooms are modern and finished with stylish Villeroy & Boch sanitary ware.\r\n\r\nThe property is fitted with High Efficiency Heat Recovery Ventilation (HRV) system, to provide tempered fresh air to the apartment 24/7.\r\n\r\nThe property is pre-wired for Virgin Broadband\r\n\r\nAllocated parking in Secure Underground Car Park \r\n\r\nThe energy efficient A2 BER rated apartment is heated through condensing heat pump system which is thermostatically controlled to ensure maximum comfort.\r\n\r\nViewing highly recommended.
