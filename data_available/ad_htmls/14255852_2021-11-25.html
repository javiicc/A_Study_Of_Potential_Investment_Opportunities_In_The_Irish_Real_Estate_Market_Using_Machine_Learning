To arrange an appointment, please email rathmines@sherryfitz.ie \n\nEnjoying an idyllic location on the westerly side of this lesser travelled leafy road, in the heart of Dublin 6, No. 11 Merton Road is a very handsome 5-bedroom property residing on approximately 0.2 acres of south-west facing gardens and is one of just seven houses on the sunnier side of the road. Much loved by the same family since 1986, it has been tenderly cared for, upgraded and extended over the years and now comes to the market as a superb family home with a wonderful balance of accommodation enhanced by a wealth of its original features.\nA gravelled driveway, with ample space for a number of cars, leads up to arched, glass panelled double doors, which are softly framed by evergreen foliage. The entrance lobby, which excludes the outside elements from drifting into the house, leads through to the entrance hall, which is bright and airy - a sense that prevails throughout the house. The interconnecting reception rooms are both elegant in proportions and presentation with matching tall original wood surround fireplaces, a bay window to the front and double doors leading out to the rear garden. They are subdivided by glass panelled double doors with a fanlight overhead, ensuring the flow of natural light continues through. To the rear of the house, the kitchen, complete with Aga, was modernised into an open plan family room, in 2003 and is now the heart of this home. The extensive seating area, centred around a wood burning stove, feels as if part of the garden on account of the use of extensive glazing, marrying the two areas and enhancing the enjoyment of the garden all year round. There is a utility room off the kitchen, which connects to both the rear garden and the secure covered side passage providing great shelter and storage for bikes etc. A study/home office, previously a garage, is also positioned off the entrance hall, whilst a guest wc completes the accommodation at this level. \nAs you alight the stairwell, there is a large storage room on the return and off the first-floor landing there are four very generously proportioned bedrooms, (1 x en-suite) along with a modern family bathroom. The current owners undertook an attic conversion in 1993, providing for a fifth bedroom in use today as the principal bedroom suite, with bathroom area and excellent storage. The dormer style conversion affords the occupants a spacious and serene retreat with a view over the garden, into the distance to the spire of Rathgar Church. \nTo the rear of the house there is a fantastic 25 metre( 82ft) south-west-facing garden. Laid mainly in lawn, it is bordered by beds stocked with a colourful array of shrubs, plants and trees. Basking in the sun throughout the day, it is a real haven of privacy and seclusion, an ideal place for children to play, al fresco dining and outdoor entertaining. \nThe location and convenience of this home is incredible: a quiet road with a true neighbourhood feel and superbly positioned just a short stroll from Cowper Luas; it is located approximately 3km from both St. Stephen s Green and Dundrum Town Centre. Both Ranelagh and Rathmines are nearby with local shopping at Dunville Avenue and Upper Rathmines Road within a gentle stroll. Ranelagh and Rathmines offer superb shopping and a choice of popular restaurants and bars, boutique shopping and specialist delicatessens. Some excellent sports facilities close by include Milltown Golf Club, Brookfield Tennis Club, Fitzwilliam Lawn Tennis Club, David Lloyd Riverview and Leinster Cricket Club.\nFor families, some of Dublin s best schools are within a short walking distance including Junior schools, Scoil Bhr de, Lios na n g, Sandford National, Kildare Place and Rathgar. Senior schools also within walking distance include Alexandra College, Gonzaga College, Sandford Park, Muckross Park and St Mary s College to name a few.\n\nLocal amenities include Palmerston Park, Belgrave Square, The Dodder Walk and Windy Arbour playground. \n\n\nGround Floor\n\nEntrance Porch glass panelled arched double doors, tiled floor, door to:\n\nHallway 2.38m x 5.65m. glazed hall door flanked by glass panelling, coving, picture rail, original timber floors, alarm panel, door to:\n\nLiving Room 4.07m x 6.17m. generous reception room with handsome Edwardian open fireplace with tiled inset and mahogany surround and over mantle, coving and picture rail, French doors to west facing patio and garden, door to:\n\nDining Room 4.07m x 5.77m. elegant reception room with bay window, handsome Edwardian style open fireplace with mahogany surround and mantle and tiled inset, coving, picture rail, glazed double doors through to:\n\nKitchen / Dining / Family Room 5.54m x 11.05m.
