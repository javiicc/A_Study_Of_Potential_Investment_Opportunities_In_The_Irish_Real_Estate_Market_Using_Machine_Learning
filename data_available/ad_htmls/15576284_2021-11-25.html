    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    This is a wonderful property which has been well maintained by the present owners and has been a lovely family home for over 18 years. The house has a large extension to the side giving access to the garden and spectacular south facing views of the countryside and the sea. Property is approached from the road with lovely mature trees nearby, in a very tranquil setting.\r\n On the 2 acres also there is two large stone buildings/stables which could be suitable for many uses.\r\n\r\nProperties of this type are very rare to find and early viewing is advised. \r\n\r\nButlerstown is a lovely traditional village within walking distance to the house is steeped with historical references and interests There is a very active GAA in the village. Barryroe shop and national school is close by. The sea is close by with lovely beaches at hand also. There is lovely walks in the area also.\r\n\r\nCourtmacsherry village and Hotel are within 10 minutes drive.\r\n\r\nBandon and Clonakilty towns are within 10 miles.\r\n\r\nCork City and Airport are within 1 hours drive.
