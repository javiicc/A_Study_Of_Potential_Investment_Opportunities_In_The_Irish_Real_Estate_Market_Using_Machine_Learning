    RE/MAX are delighted to present to market a magnificent 8 bedroom detached house located in Ballinamona, Menlough, Ballinasloe, Co. Galway. Windrush House sits on an elevated 3 acres, including landscaped gardens, paddocks, large shed and equestrian facilities. This distinctive property is designed to an exquisite finish both internally and externally.\n\nThe property was constructed in 2001, extends to 3,400 sq. ft. and is flooded with natural light. Accommodation is spread over 3 floors offering an abundance of luxury living space. Ground floor accommodation is comprised of an entrance hall, living room, formal dining room, games room, kitchen/casual dining area, utility, W.C and downstairs bedroom with en-suite. Proceeding to the first floor is 5 bedrooms - master of which has an en-suite and main bathroom. Second floor accommodation has a large landing, 2 bedrooms and main bathroom. The property also features an attic.\n\nThe property is certified with an impressive B3 BER and heated by Oil Fired Central Heating. There is a solid fuel Hunter stove with red brick surround in the living room, a Stanley solid fuel range in the kitchen and solid fuel marble feature fireplace in the formal living room. Double glazed windows feature throughout and the attic is insulated. \n\nExternally there is a beautifully landscaped garden both to the front and rear. The garden features manicured lawns, shrubs, flowers and a wraparound tarmac driveway providing ample parking space. To the rear is a large 60ft x 60ft shed incorporating 3 loose boxes, 4 double kennels and ample storage space.\n\nThe property is in immaculate condition and would suit anyone looking to escape to an area of pure beauty. This bright and imposing property features unique features that only an inspection would provide the full appreciation of the warm and welcoming atmosphere created within this first class home. It could be a luxury family home or alternatively has immense potential for use as a fabulous boutique style guesthouse/B&B.\n\nWindrush House is situated just 2 minutes from Menlough Village, 25 minutes from Galway City, 20 minutes from Ballinasloe and 20 minutes from Loughrea. Amenities close to Menlough include a Credit Union, Spar, Post Office, Andy\\'s Driving School, funeral home, Garbally National School and Menlough GAA Football Club.
