    No 4 Cumberland Road is an utterly unique and transformed 18th century coach house, ideally located in this much sought-after location just off Fitzwilliam Place and only 750m from St Stephen s Green. The mews is located behind a high wall and gates which provides the ultimate in privacy and security.\nThe property was extended and beautifully refurbished in 2013 with outstanding results, now benefitting from an impressive B3 energy rating. As part of the refurbishment programme, a spacious light filled modern extension was added to create a large open plan kitchen / living / dining room with wall-to-wall glazing overlooking the landscaped garden. The bedrooms and bathrooms are located in the original coach house creating two distinct spaces for entertaining and sleeping.\nThe private garden is bordered by the original stone walls and has been beautifully landscaped with an outdoor fireplace and off-street parking for two cars behind electric gates. It benefits from attractive Ballylusk gravel, granite paving and a selection of low maintenance borders.\nThe property enjoys one of Dublin 2 s most convenient and sought-after locations, being within a pleasant stroll of an extensive range of the amenities including St. Stephens Green, Merrion Square and the great variety of shops, bars and restaurants that the city centre has to offer.
