    This beautifully architecturally designed 4-bedroom detached residence enjoys a private and peaceful setting while still being close to all major facilities such as Apple HQ, Cork City Centre, several hospitals and U.C.C. Standing on an elevated site of c.025 Acre, Dun An Oir enjoys panoramic south facing views over Cork City and the countryside. \n\nAccommodation extends to an impressive c.1,800 sq. Ft with attached garage and is presented in excellent condition throughout. In more recent times a series of upgrading works have taken place, such as installation of double glazed windows, laminated timber flooring throughout the ground floor, condensed oil boiler, extra insulation in the hallway and solid fuel stove in the living room.\nThis property is sure to appeal to those looking for the best of both worlds, a unique opportunity to live in a stunning countryside setting yet so easily accessible to modern day city living. \n\nAccommodation is spread over two floors and includes:\n\nEntrance Hallway: Laminated Walnut timber flooring, cloak room, fitted burglar alarm, smoke alarm, featured stain glass windows. \n\nLivingroom: 4.83m x 4.22m : Solid oak timber flooring, feature Portuguese limestone fireplace with arrow solid fuel stove. Sliding door to rear garden with magnificent south facing views. Recess spot lighting.\n\nKitchen/Dining Room: 6.15m x 3.14m : Laminated walnut timber flooring, Bay window to dining area with garden and south facing city views. Fitted kitchen units at worktop and eye level. Integrated hob, oven, grill and extractor fan. Fitted blind and curtains. \n\nUtility: 2.39m x 1.97m: Laminated walnut timber flooring, rear door access, fitted sink unit with storage. Heating control panels, plumbed for automatic machines. \n\nBathroom: Fully tiled 3-piece suite with modern bathroom ware. Fitted shower unit with retractable door and pump shower. Fitted extractor fan, spotlights.\n\nBedroom 1: 4.24m x 3.99m: Laminated walnut timber flooring, fitted wardrobes, beautiful south facing and countryside views. \n\nBedroom 2: 3.41m x 3.16m: Laminated walnut timber flooring, fitted wardrobe. \n\nBedroom 3: 3.31m x 2.54m: Carpet flooring, fitted wardrobes, fitted sink.\n\nOffice: 3m x 2.41m: Carpet flooring, fitted shelving.\n\nFirst Floor:\nStairs and Landing: Teak staircase, carpet flooring.\n\nLounge: 4.74m x 3.49m : Carpet flooring, open marble fireplace, spot lighting, sliding door to balcony.\n\nBedroom 4: 3.64m x 3.18m : Laminated timber flooring, spotlights,\n\nShower room incorporating laundry room: 4.24m x 4.23m: Laminated timber flooring, W.C., W.H.B, shower units with electronic Mira shower. Laundry room/hot press located here.\n\nSunroom/ Gym: 3.54m x 2.97m : Tiled flooring beautiful garden and south facing city views.\n\nGarage: 5.6m x 3.6m : Double door entrance and rear garden access.\n\nOutside: To the front, ample parking. To the rear, large garden with mature plans and shrubbery.
