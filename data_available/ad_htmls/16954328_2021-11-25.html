    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to offer No.46 Elmbrook Avenue to the market. Conveniently located opposite Willsbrook Park, walking distance to Lucan Village and within St Mary s Parish.\nThe accommodation is both large and bright while a large south facing rear garden will offer scope to extend if ever required. \n\nThe downstairs offers a large living room with double doors opening into the kitchen/dining room, a guest w.c. and storage is tucked under the stairs\nUpstairs the theme of space and light continues with 4 generous bedrooms, family bathroom and en-suite.\n\nOutside the property offers a large front garden with plenty of off-street parking for the family cars, while a side gate opens into an enclosed rear garden.\nTo the rear the beautifully maintained, south facing garden is mainly laid in lawn. While a centrally located patio offers the perfect spot for outdoor entertaining. Raised flower beds enclose the garden to the side and rear while a spacious Barna shed offers further storage. \n\nThe property takes in wonderful views of Willsbrook Park with its playground which is a great amenity for a growing family right on your doorstep.\nThis home is sure to appeal it offers easy access to an excellent road network, public transport, local shopping both large and small and excellent schools.\nIf you re looking for a home that only requires the turning of the key then look no further.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 1.64m x 5.06m. The room also offers solid timber flooring, ceiling coving, double doors leading through to kitchen/dining.\n\nKitchen/ Dining 4.63m x 5.10m. Extending across the full width of the property this bright open plan space is flooded with natural light thanks to the property s south facing aspect. \nThe kitchen area offers a wealth of Shaker style wall and floor storage units, while stylish granite countertops cover all of the work surfaces. A centrally located breakfast bar defines this room and also provides further storage. All fitted appliances will be included in the sale including, double oven, American style fridge/freezer, 5 ring gas hob + extractor, dishwasher and washing machine.\n\nThe dining area flows seamlessly from both kitchen and living room.
