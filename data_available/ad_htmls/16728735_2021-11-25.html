    Moovingo are delighted to present this superb family home to the market. Constructed in 2018, the property is in excellent turn-key condition throughout and enjoys a sizeable attic conversion also with plumbing. Ideally located close to the Luas and Citywest Shopping Centre, this property will appeal to first-time buyers and families looking to upgrade to a larger home with more space.\n\nThe accommodation comprises a bright entrance hallway, large living room, open plan kitchen/dining room, adjoining utility room and WC. On the first floor there is a spacious master suite with ensuite shower room, two additional double bedrooms and a modern family bathroom. The attic has been converted into additional space and has been plumbed for installation of a bathroom/WC if required. The rear garden is laid out in lawn and patio and has side access to the front of the house. The house comes with 2 parking spaces.\n\nAREA INFO\n\nConveniently situated 20 minutes from Dublin city centre, this property is housed within a modern development, ideal for families. Citywest Village is supported by excellent transportation links, located just off the N7 and 3 minutes from the M50.
