The end-of-terrace bungalow is distinctive in the lane, with its front porch and pebbled front garden bringing charm to the neighborhood. \n\nElegant number 18 has an extensive floorplan with the modern family in mind or perfect for those looking to downscale to a bungalow.\n\nThe accommodation comprises of a front sitting and dining room with fireplace, and large kitchen with access to the courtyard.
