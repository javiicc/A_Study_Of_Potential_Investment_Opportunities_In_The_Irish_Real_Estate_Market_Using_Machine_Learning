    Jindabyne is a spacious detached family residence nestled on approx. 1 acre of manicured gardens. Approached by a sweeping driveway bordered by manicured lawns on both sides, it is clear that Jindabyne is something special. Built in the Potton style with wooden beams and large red brick Inglenook fireplace, the accommodation has a great flow to it and boasts well proportioned reception areas that interconnect making it ideal for entertaining. Only a short distance from both Delgany and Greystones, all essential amenities are within easy reach. This home offers something a little bit different - a countryside setting with stunning gardens, having access to schools and leisure facilities just minutes away. \n\nThe accommodation comprises hallway which to the left gives access to the guest wc and a cosy TV room. To the right of the hallway is the large living room which is flooded with natural light being triple aspect over the gardens. The central point of this room is the stunning red brick Inglenook fireplace, a magnificent addition to any home. The living room sweeps around through to the dining room which boasts another fireplace, making both of these areas super cosy spots. From here there are double doors opening into a large family/sun room which is flooded with light with a high vaulted ceiling and large atrium rooflight. The kitchen is to the rear of the house and has been fitted with Newcastle design wall and floor level units and looks out onto the rear garden. From the kitchen there is access to a large utility and also to a ground floor double bedroom and en suite. A separate study/office has been fitted with Oakline custom made units. There is a stairs from this side of the house bringing you up to a studio loft area which can be used as a home/office or teenage den space. As this whole area is to the side of the main house, it could be ideally used as a self-contained unit for an older family relative or childminder if required. Upstairs in the main house there are four bedrooms, the main bedroom has a walk-in wardrobe and en suite bathroom. One of the other bedrooms has a door thru to the main bathroom or you can also access the bathroom from the landing\n\nJindabyne is in the highly sought after location of Delgany. Delgany village boasts, amongst other amenities, The Delgany which houses The Pigeon House Caf and The Firehouse Bakery, the Bear Paw deli opposite Farrelly s craft butchers providing another source of epicurean delight. Greystones town is a short drive and has a variety of bijou shops and eateries to choose from as well as beautiful coastal walks including the Greystones to Bray Cliff Walk. Sports facilities and clubs are in abundance with rugby, tennis, sailing and golf being just a small example of what is on offer. Transport links include easy access to the N11/M50, Greystones DART station, Aircoach and Dublin Bus routes. \n\n\nThe gardens of Jindabyne extend to approx. 1 acre, approached by a tarmac driveway which provides parking for numerous cars. There are large lawn areas which are interspersed with flowerbeds and shrubbery. There is a secret orchard at the back of the garden, an idyllic hideaway screened off from the rest of the gardens with apple, pear and plum trees. A large Indian sandstone circular patio is the ideal spot for outdoor dining and is surrounded by colourful plants and shrubs including Cherry Blossom, Rhododendrons, Camelia and Hydrangea Annabelle. The pergola which is encased in Wisteria is a beautiful sight to behold when in bloom. Outdoor Victorian lighting lends an authentic rustic ambience to this beautiful outdoor space. A greenhouse is the perfect spot to grow vegetables and herbs and there is also a detached garage which provides extra outside storage.\n\n\nHallway 3.49m x 4.54m. with carpet flooring, recessed lighting, understairs storage.\n\nKitchen 6.77m x 4.09m.
