    25 Leeview is a three-bedroomed semi-detached bungalow with a dormer extension nicely positioned in a quiet cul-de-sac within easy walking distance of all services and amenities on offer in Ballincollig.\n\nTo the front of the house there is a driveway offering private, off-street parking and a lawn area lined with mature shrubs and flowers. The rear garden consists of a patio, raised lawn area, planting beds and a large, detached block built shed with power offering great potential for conversion to a home office or gym. \n\nInternally, the property arrives to the market in very good condition throughout. Ground floor accommodation is comprised of a hallway, living room, open-plan kitchen / dining room, main bathroom and a multi-purpose room that would make an ideal home office / childrens playroom.\nOverhead, there are three bedrooms (one en-suite).\n\nThe location here is ideal, being only minutes walk to Ballincollig town centre but also benefitting from a quiet cul-de-sac environment. \nThis is a great opportunity to acquire a lovely home in such a sought-after location that should not be missed.\nEntrance Hall 2.46 x 1.05. The open-plan kitchen / dining room spans the full width of the house and features tiled flooring, floor and eye-level units, ample worktop space, integrated oven and gas hob, plumbing for washing machine, centre light, wall lights and access to the rear garden via sliding door.\n\nOffice / Playroom 2.49 x 2.88. Multi-purpose room to the front of the house that would make and ideal home office, childrens playroom or spare bedroom. There is Junckers wood flooring, Roman blinds and recessed lighting.\n\nBathroom 1.46 x 2.37. Three-piece bath suite with shower hose. Tiled flooring and walls.\n\nBedroom 1 2.85 x 4.54. Single bedroom to the front of the house with carpet flooring, two velux windows and fitted floor to ceiling wardrobe.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nOn entering Ballincollig from the city side, turn right at Costa coffee, continue down the hill, take 2nd left and property is located on the right hand side.
