    Ideally situated within this outstanding residential location, this three bedroom semi detached home is complemented by a large south facing rear garden which attracts the sun throughout the day. Ideal for families and keen gardeners, this is a blank canvass for a prospective purchaser to make their own mark on a home and whilst there is some updating to be done to the house, there are many original features retained in the property.\n\nThe accommodation is both spacious and well proportioned with three good size bedrooms and a large family bathroom on the first floor, a bright, welcoming entrance hall, downstairs WC and two separate receptions, kitchen and lean to/utility on the ground floor. Outside the gardens are fantastic, rarely does a property with so much external space in the heart of the city come to the market. With space to extend (subject to PP) there is enormous scope to create the dream home and still have plenty of garden left. There is a driveway for off street parking as well as a separate garage. \n\nThe location is excellent, close to the city centre within this mature residential area. Thomond Park is just a short stroll from the house, as are Limerick Gaelic Grounds and the Ennis Road, with various shops and services practically on the doorstep.\n\n\nEntrance Porch With front door.\n\nEntrance Hall With front door, staircase with guest WC with handwash basin under and tiled floor.\n\nLiving Room With picture rail and varnished timber floor.\n\nDining Room With picture rail, feature fireplace and timber floor.\n\nKitchen With range of wall and floor level kitchen units, drawers and work surfaces, sink and draining board and cooker point.\n\nUtility Room With plumbing for washing machine and door to garden\n\nLanding Access to attic.\n\nBedroom 1 Double bedroom with varnished timber floor.\n\nBedroom 2 Double bedroom with range of fitted wardrobes.\n\nBedroom 3 With varnished timber floor and storage.\n\nBathroom With tiled walls and floor, shower cubicle with Mira electric shower, wash hand basin, panelled bath and WC.\n\nGarden The front garden is enclosed by walls and hedges to the side and front, with a gravel border and gated driveway leading to a single garage with double doors. At the rear the garden is enclosed by hedges with lawn and patio, mature plants and trees.
