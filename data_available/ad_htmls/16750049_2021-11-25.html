    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 174 m\xc2\xb2
    This is a very attractive three bedroom bungalow with a granny flat as in integral part of the building but with it's own separate entrance. The property is located in the very popular Clonmoney Hurlers Cross area a five minute drive to Shannon and close to the N.18. It is approximately 15 minutes to Ennis and 15 minutes to Limerick. The house sits on a half acre of beautiful natural landscaping with a lovely mixture of trees, shrubs and flowers. It has many attractive features with two ensuite bedrooms, self contained granny flat, three livingrooms and driveway parking for at least four cars.\nIt would make an ideal family home with the added benefit of separate accommodation suitable for an older relative or the potential to rent out. Entrance lobby (2.65 x 1.78) with tiled floor. Hallway (c.4.02 x 2.16) with solid wood floor and cloak closet. Livingroom (c.4.70 x 4.19) featuring cutstone fireplace and laminate wood floor. Kitchen/dining (c.6.51 x 4.22) with solid wood floor and grey fitted kitchen units. Integrated electric oven and gas hob. Brick fireplace with solid fuel stove. Sittingroom (c.4.26 x 4.16) with solid wood floor and sliding patio door to paved patio area. Utility (c.2.46 x 2.76) with floor tiling. Guest w.c. (2.62 x 1.14) with floor tiling. Inner hall (c.7.89 x 0.89) with solid wood floor. Bedroom 1 (c.3.47 x 3.09) with laminate wood floor and sink unit, fully tiled w.c. and shower ensuite. Bedroom 2 (c.4.41 x 3.01) with laminate wood floor and sink unit, built in wardrobe. Bedroom 3 (c.4.53 x 2.88) with solid wood floor and fully tiled w.c. ensuite. Large Bathroom (c.3.08 x 3.12) fully tiled. Jacuzzi bath, separate corner shower cubicle, w.c. w.h.b. and bidet. Granny Flat - Hallway (c.2.36 x 1.18) with floor tiling. Bedroom (c.3.28 x 2.92) with laminate wood floor. Kitchen/dining/living (c.3.29 x 3.07) with fitted kitchen. Shower room fully tiled and electric shower. Electric heating in Granny Flat, Oil fired central heating in main house. Landscaped gardens. Out buildings. Half acre site.
