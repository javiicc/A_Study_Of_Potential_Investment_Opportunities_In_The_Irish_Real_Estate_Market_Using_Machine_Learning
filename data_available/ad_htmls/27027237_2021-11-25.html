    Description

      ***REPLY BY EMAIL ONLY***NO CALLS PLEASE**EXCLUSIVE PROPERTIES are delighted to bring to the market a 2 bedroom 2 bathroom apartment in Maple Court, Rathgar. Just renovated to the highest standard. This apartment is a bright well-presented ground floor apartment superbly located in this much sought after mature development off Terenure Road East, Rathgar. Internally the property extends to approximately 60 sq.m. to include an impressive living/dining room with feature fireplace. \nOff the living room is a newly fitted fully kitchen equipped with all mod cons. There are two bedrooms one en-suite. Car-parking space in the communal car-park. Terenure Village and Rathgar Village are just a short walk away where there is a host of local amenities including a variety of bijou shops, banks, churches and a choice of restaurants. Bushy Park is also within walking distance with the added benefit of playing fields, tennis courts and various walks. The area is exceptionally well serviced by public transport with the very regular service of the 15a and 17 bus routes. The M50 is also very convenient.
