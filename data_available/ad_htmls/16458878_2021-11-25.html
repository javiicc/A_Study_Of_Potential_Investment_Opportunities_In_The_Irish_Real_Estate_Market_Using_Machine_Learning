    Welcome to this No.5 Avondale road, a most convenient two bedroom mid-terrace period residence only moments from Phibsborough village. These most attractive homes are much sought after by a wide variety of buyers from First Time Buyers to investors. Internally the property has been well maintained and updated by its current owners but still allows plenty of scope for the new owners to put their own stamp on it.\n\nInternally the accommodation briefly comprises of entrance hall with opens to the living room at the front of the property. A doorway leads into the generous kitchen/breakfast room, off here can be found the guest w.c. and door to the back garden. Upstairs there are two double bedrooms and a shower room completing the picture.\n\nSituated just off North Circular Road this property enjoys a superb location just a short stroll from Phibsborough Village with its many shops, restaurants and cafes and is within comfortable walking distance of the city centre, The Phoenix Park, The Botanic Gardens, Stoneybatter & Smithfield Village s. There are numerous bus routes passing through Phibsborough as well as the nearby LUAS. The property is within minutes of The Four Courts, Law Library, King s Inns, Blackhall Place, The Mater Hospital and the new DIT Campus at Grangegorman.\n\n\nLiving Room 4.20 x 3.05.
