    Sherry FitzGerald O Reilly are pleased to introduce you to Glebe House, a superbly spacious dormer bungalow in a countryside setting, yet only four minutes from Blessington town.\nThis is a sizeable family home which has been carefully extended and upgraded by its current owners over the years and it offers much potential for a growing family.\nThe grounds of Glebe House are large with a broad yard and lawns to front, rear and side, possibly allowing for further expansion.\nIt is just over 2kms from the centre of historic Blessington with its many shops, restaurants, bars, schools and leisure amenities such as watersports, horse riding, gyms, GAA. The nearby Russborough House and the Blessington Greenway offer lots of walking and biking trails. \nFor the commuter, the N81 gives easy access to the city centre, and the Luas at Citywest is an easy 25 minutes away.\nThe accommodation in this fine property briefly comprises of: Entrance porch, hallway, sitting room, living/dining room, kitchen, Four bedrooms (two en-suite), kitchenette, two attic rooms and bathroom. Outside - double garage and two metal sheds.\n\n\nHallway The welcoming hallway has a laminate oak floor, a storage press (1.72m x 0.66m) and attic access.\n\nSitting Room 5.87m x 3.3m. The sitting room is a bright room overlooking the front garden. It has a marble fireplace with a wooden surround and a laminate oak floor.\n\nPorch 1.77m x 0.66m. With tile floor and PVC sliding door.\n\nLiving/Dining Room 5.84m x 3.62m. From the hallway, step into the living/dining room, which has a galley kitchen off. The living area features a cast iron fireplace with wooden surround. French doors from the dining area lead to the patio outside.\n\nKitchen 3.21m x 2.4m. The kitchen includes a range of cream Shaker style units with ceramic hob, oven, microwave, fridge freezer and dishwasher. The floor is tiled.\n\nHallway to bedrooms With laminate oak flooring.\n\nBedroom 1 3.66m x 3.18m. This is a generous double room to front with a wall of fitted wardrobes and a laminate oak floor.\n\nBedroom 2 4.28m x 2.82m. A spacious double bedroom to front with lots of storage in an array of fitted wardrobes. With laminate oak floor.\n\nBedroom 3 3.52m x 2.46m. This double bedroom with en-suite opens to a kitchenette with hall area and own door entrance giving the opportunity to be used as a self-contained apartment.\n\nEnsuite 2.28m x 0.87m. With wc, whb and shower unit with Triton electric shower. The floor and shower are tiled.\n\nKitchenette 4.54m x 2.21m. With kitchen presses topped with laminate worktop, tile splashback, sink and extractor fan. It has a laminate oak floor.\n\nHall 1.84m x 1.11m. With door to yard.\n\nFamily bathroom 3.6m x 1.8m. The bathroom has a tiled floor and part tiled walls, and includes shower unit with electric shower, whb and wc, and a range of storage presses.\n\nUpstairs\n\nBedroom 4 5.52m x 2.78m.
