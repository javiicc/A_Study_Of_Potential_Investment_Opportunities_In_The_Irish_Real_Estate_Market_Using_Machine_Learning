    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    ****SOLD****We have been instructed to offer for sale by Private Treaty this beautifully maintained three bedroom bungalow with first floor accommodation. The property is in turn key condition and standing on a well maintained site bounded by mature trees, hedging and shrubbery, with ample ground space to extend with the required planning permission. The property is located within easy driving distance of the Heritage Town of Listowel and villages of Lixnaw and Finuge, Killocrim National School is located a couple of minutes drive away. The ground floor accommodation comprises open plan kitchen/dining room with wall to wall fitted units, sink unit, worktop area, ceramic tiled flooring in the kitchen area, and laminate wood flooring in the dining area, solid fuel stove, French doors leading to the garden. The adjacent utility room is plumbed and wired for the usual range of appliances, sink unit and worktop area, broom press, ceramic tiled flooring. The sitting room is tastefully decorated with hardwood flooring and open fireplace. Bedroom One has the benefit of a built in wardrobe. The family bathroom has matching toilet, wash hand basin, bath and shower unit. Access to the first floor accommodation is via a hardwood stairs, the landing area is bright and cheerful owing to the window on the landing. The master bedroom is tastefully decorated with hardwood flooring, built in wardrobes, the en-suite has matching toilet, wash hand basin and shower unit, extensive use of ceramic tiling. Bedroom Three is tastefully decorated with hardwood flooring, fitted wardrobe and lit by a Velux window. \r\n\r\nThere are a number of lock up storage sheds at the rear and separate WC facilities, mains water, sewerage by septic tank, dual heating system, solid fuel and oil, and off road parking.\r\n\r\nViewing is highly recommended with the Sole Selling Agent.
