    Brophy Estates are delighted to bring to the market No. 16 Applewood Court. This is a Large one-bedroom own door apartment set in a secluded location in the ever-popular Applewood development.\r\n\r\nThe spacious accommodation comprises; entrance hallway that leads through to an open plan kitchen/living space. A large window floods the space with natural light and overlooks the private walled garden. A feature fireplace with electric insert creates a real focal point. An internal hallway leads to the bathroom and spacious double bedroom. Outside the property overlooks the communal areas and the private walled garden creates an ideal space to dine alfresco.\r\n\r\nApplewood is ideally positioned a short drive from the town centre of Swords which offers a full range of local amenities including schools, shops, restaurants, cafes and excellent public transport links including the Swords Express offering direct access to the city centre. Dublin International Airport, M50/M1 motorways are all within easy access of the property.
