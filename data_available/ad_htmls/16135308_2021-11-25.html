    This residence at Lislanahan is a five-bed detached dormer bungalow that requires modernising but has excellent potential for refurbishment work. The house comprises of an entrance hall, kitchen, living room, bedroom and bathroom on the ground floor. Upstairs consists of four bedrooms and a shower room. The house enjoys great natural light and has a solid fuel heating system in operation. There is ample car parking space to the front of this delightfully elevated site while there is a lawn area to the rear.
