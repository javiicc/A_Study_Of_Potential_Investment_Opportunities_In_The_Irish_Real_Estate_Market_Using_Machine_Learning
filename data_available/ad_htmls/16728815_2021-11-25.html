    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to bring to the market No.26 Mount Eden Rise which is a well presented, three-bedroom, end of terrace home that is located just off Blarney Street and benefits from views to the front over the city. No.26 is ready and waiting for its new owners to move in, unpack and stamp their own individual mark on it.\r\n\r\nThis end-terrace home offers a great balance between living and bedroom accommodation with a welcoming entrance hall, sitting room, a generous kitchen/dining area and a sunroom on the ground floor. On the first floor there are two double bedrooms and a single bedroom. No.26 also benefits from a private, two-tiered garden area to the rear that is ideal for relaxing in the afternoon and evening sun.\r\n\r\nNo.26 is situated in a mature residential area off Blarney Street with off street parking space. This home is within walking distance to local shops, schools, bus stops and all of life s essential amenities. \r\n\r\nThis is a great opportunity to acquire an end of terrace home in a nice mature location. \r\n\r\nDo not delay arrange a viewing today!\r\n\r\nEntrance Hall 1.80m x 3.60m. The bright and spacious entrance hall has a solid wood floor and gives access to the sitting room, kitchen/dining area and the sunroom. Also benefits from a storage room under the stairs.\r\n\r\nLiving Room 3.68m x 3.60m. This is a well-proportioned reception room to the front of the home with views over the city. The room benefits from an open fireplace with shelving units to either side.\r\n\r\nKitchen/Dining Area 5.62m x 3.16m. With fitted floor and eye level style units, this well-proportioned kitchen/dining area has a tiled floor and a tiled splashback. There is a door that enters into the sunroom and pulls all the natural light in from the rear garden through to the kitchen. The kitchen is plumbed for a washing machine and dishwasher.\r\n\r\nSun Room 4.90m x 2.27m. With a full wall of glass, this sunroom is a bright, airy and spacious with a view of the two-tiered garden.\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 3.01m x 3.43m. This is a bright and spacious bedroom with a window overlooking to the front that overlooks Cork City and benefits from a floor to ceiling wardrobe.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 3.44m x 3.39m. This is a bright and spacious bedroom with a window overlooking the rear garden and benefits a floor to ceiling wardrobe.\r\n\r\nBedroom 3 2.52m x 2.49m. Bright single bedroom to front of the home which overlooks Cork City.\r\n\r\nBathroom 2.10m x 1.66m. The family bathroom comprises of a three-piece bath suite with electric shower over the bath. The bathroom area is fully tiled.\r\n\r\n\r\nDIRECTIONS:\r\nFrom Cork City, make your way up Shandon street and take the first left onto Blarney Street. Continue straight up Blarney Street and Mount Eden Rise is located on the right-hand side. You will see a car park on the right, No.26 is located up the steps on the right-hand side behind the carpark. See Agent sign.
