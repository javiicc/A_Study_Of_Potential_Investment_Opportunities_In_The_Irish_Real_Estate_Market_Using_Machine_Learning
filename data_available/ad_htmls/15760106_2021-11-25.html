      Part of New Development at Ruxton Oaks

    Glenveagh Homes are delighted to introduce their third modern and stylish new Development in Navan Town. \r\nRuxton Oaks is located at Blackcastle, off the Slane Road a prime well regarded and mature residential setting positioned North of Navan Town Centre. \r\n\r\nThe site itself is steeped in history and was once home to an amazing estate surrounding Blackcastle House a six bay mansion which was built in 1828 for Mr Richard Ruxton.\r\n \r\nRuxton Oaks offers the opportunity to purchase a stunning home in one of Navan\xe2\x80\x99s prime residential addresses. \r\n\r\nThe development consists of 2 and 3 Bedroom family homes. \r\n\r\nLocation\r\n\r\nRuxton Oaks is ideally located off the Slane Road within walking distance of Navan Shopping Centre, and Navan Town Centre. Just 5 minutes from Junction 9 and Junction 10 of the M3 Motorway, it's a 35 minute commute to Blanchardstown and a 40 minute drive to Dublin Airport. Navan has an excellent bus service to Dublin with a bus stop walking distance from the development. For rail links, the park and ride facilities at Dunboyne Train Station are only 20 minutes' drive away. \r\n\r\n\r\nTransport\r\n\r\nTransport links to the Capital are excellent with the M3 motorway, M2, efficient 24-hour coach transport and the park and ride facility at Dunboyne. The bus service runs every 20 minutes daily. There are several private coaches with stops located minutes walk from Ruxton Oaks and the main Bus Stop on Abbey Road is close by.\r\n\r\n\r\nAmenities\r\n\r\nNavan offers a host of excellent amenities including shopping, cosy pubs, restaurants cinema and numerous hotels. It is home to a number of the big retail names, boutiques and there are several retail parks on the outskirts of the town including Navan Retail Park and the Blackwater Retail Park. \r\nRuxton Oaks is within easy walking distance to Blackcastle Shopping Centre which consists of a large Centra Store, pharmacy, post office, newsagent, the famous Zucchini Restaurant and many other facilities. \r\nFor Leisure Aura Leisure Centre and the beautiful walking track in Blackwater Park is close by. Ruxton Oaks benefits hugely being a comfortable walk to Navan Town itself and all its amenities. \r\nThere are a host of sporting and recreational facilities in the Navan. GAA is well accommodated in both Simonstown and O\xe2\x80\x99Mahonys GAA clubs, rugby in Navan Rugby Club and the golfers are spoiled for choice in Royal Tara, Headfort Golf Club, Killeen Castle and Knightsbrook Golf Course. Proximity to the River Boyne is ideal for fishing and there are a host of indoor sporting activities in the Town also. \r\n\r\nEducation\r\n\r\nNavan benefits from a wide ranging and quality school system that caters for both primary and secondary students. St Oliver\xe2\x80\x99s National school, St Paul\xe2\x80\x99s National school (new school currently under construction) and Scoil Naomh Eoin National School are all within walking distance. Scoil Eanna Gael Scoil and Navan Educate Together National School are both located in the Town. \r\nThere are a number of established and well regarded secondary schools located in the Town included St. Patrick's Classical School, St. Joseph's Mercy, St. Michael's Loreto, Colaiste Na Mi and Beaufort College. \r\nThere are a host of childcare facilities including day care and after school within close proximity to the development including Mother Hubbards Childcare, The Children\xe2\x80\x99s House Montessori, Scoil na nOg Montessori and Scallywags playgroup and after school. \r\n\r\nFor further information please visit ruxtonoaks.ie to register your details or contact one of our sales agents at Raymond Potterton & Co. 046 9027666 or email info@raymondpotterton.com.
