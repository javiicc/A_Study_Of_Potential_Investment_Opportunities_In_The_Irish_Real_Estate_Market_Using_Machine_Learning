    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    *** NEW TO THE MARKET ***\r\n\r\nWe are excited to bring this superb property to the market. Located on a beautiful well manicured site on the Cavan Leitrim border along the R205, this spacious 4 bedroom bungalow has a lot to offer, just 7.5km Ballyconnell and 11km from Ballinamore with beautiful views overlooking the scenic countryside. This home consists of a large bright solid wood kitchen with dining and living room area, a spacious sitting room with a beautifully handcrafted natural stone fireplace and solid fuel stove, a bright and airy entrance hall which leads to a large family bathroom and on to 4 spacious double bedrooms, the master being ensuite. There is also a large utility room and a separate W.C. The outside of the property is also finished to a high standard with a large lawn which sweeps around the house surrounded by beautiful mature trees. The tarmac driveway leads to a well finished concrete surface to the sides and rear of the property with purpose built gates which add extra security. This ready to walk into property would make an outstanding family home or would be an ideal country retreat for someone looking to move from the hustle and bustle of city life. It is close to all the local amenities Ballyconnell and Ballinamore have to offer and is only 35 minutes from Enniskillen and 2 hours from Dublin and Belfast. \r\nViewing Highly Recommended and Strictly By Appointment Only.
