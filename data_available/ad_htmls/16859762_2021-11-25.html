    A unique period home dating from the early 1800s, situated in this enviable position in the heart of Rathfarnham, within a stones throw of Rathfarnham Village and shopping centre. This attractive home is situated behind a pillared entrance, offers generous proportions and retains an abundance of period features of the era to include high ceilings, generous room proportions, period fireplaces, and timber sash windows. \r\n\r\nInternally the accommodation briefly comprises of a welcoming reception hall with ornate pillars and feature staircase with door opening into a study with beautiful tiled floor, and glazed panel doors opening into a porch, which in turn opens into the rear garden. A well-proportioned reception room with marble fireplace and large picture window overlooking the rear garden, retains attractive coving and intricate centre rose detail. A dining room opens into an inner hallway with a secondary staircase to first floor level, off which is the kitchen/breakfast room with a modern fitted kitchen with granite worktops, tiled floor and dual sash windows overlooking the front. A guest w.c. completes the accommodation at this level. Upstairs there are four well proportioned bedrooms, and two bathrooms. \r\n\r\nThe location of 1 Orchardstown House will be of major interest, being only a short 5 minute walk to Rathfarnham Village and shopping centre, with Terenure, Knocklyon, and Dundrum Town Centre only slightly further afield. This area is particularly sought after due to it's close proximity to an excellent choice of local schools, including Loreto High School Beaufort, Templeogue College, Terenure College, and The High School Rathgar. National schools in the area include St Mary's Boy's National School, Ballyroan Boy's School, and Rathfarnham Parish School. Local public transport routes include the 15B bus route, with the city centre located approximately 7km away.
