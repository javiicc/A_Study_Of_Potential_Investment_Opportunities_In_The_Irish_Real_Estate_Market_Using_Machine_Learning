    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    Nestled along the Wicklow-Kildare border is Ballylea, a fine residence presented to the market by Seek & Sell Auctioneers. With c.2,000sq.ft (186 sq.m) of living accommodation over two floors this property is ideal for families looking for something with good gardens, close to Dunlavin - (4km) Grangecon - (4.4km) and within easy access of the M9-(9km) making commuting north to the Capital or south towards Waterford easy . The ground floor is laid out to include a large light filled lounge overlooking the front & side gardens, a dining room-sunroom, kitchen, 4th bedroom, two shower rooms and room that is ideal as a home office or has potential for a 5th bedroom. There is a second door which provides access from the side of the house granting direct access to the office (bed 5) and a shower-room without going through the main house. There is also a second driveway making this ideal for those running a home business. On the first floor there are three bedrooms and a bathroom. The gardens are mature with fabulous trees, shrubs and a large lawned area. There is a patio area off the sunroom. West Wicklow is a beautiful area with many vibrant Villages, access to some of the best hillwalking in Ireland and stunning lakes. Viewing advised as properties in this location don't last long in this market!
