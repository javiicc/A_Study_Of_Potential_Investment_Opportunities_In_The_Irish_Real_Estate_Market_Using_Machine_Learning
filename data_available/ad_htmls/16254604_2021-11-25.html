    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Fantastic opportunity to acquire a landmark property on the original site of the signing of the Treaty of Limerick on the 3rd October 1661. Holding prime position on Limerick`s Thomond Bridge, commanding spectacular views of King John`s Castle, The River Shannon, The Treaty Stone, St. Mary`s Cathedral, St. Munchins old, and new, Churches, Curragower Falls and Clancy Strand. Thomond Bridge House is woven in the historical fabric of Limerick City due to its dominant position on Thomond Bridge and has been the location of many iconic images from the Limerick Soviet of 1919 to the barricades of the Irish War of Independence. Its vibrant location is further enhanced by its close proximity to the famous Bowles Pub, The Curragower Bar & Restaurant and its own award winning caf\xc3\xa9/restaurant Jack Monday`s. The start and finish of one of Limericks most beautiful walks and a must for river activities having its very own access to the River Shannon. A stone`s throw from Thomond Park and a thoroughfare for thousands of colourful fans on match day.\n\nThe property is spread over three floors with renowned caf\xc3\xa9/restaurant occupying the first and ground floor. Spectacular views from every angle with a large outside decking area, renowned for its morning ambiance. The first floor consists of HSE approved cold storage area, hygiene clad and tiled with sluice room. A large dry storage room. A HSE approved, fully functioning kitchen and an office area with own access to river. Opportunity, subject to planning, to create an apartment on the first floor beneath the caf\xc3\xa9 as was originally there. The core and exterior of the building were completely gutted and restored in 2011, having been completely rewired, re-plumbed, re-floored and plastered, all signed off by Limerick City Fire Chief & Limerick City Planning Department, Certificates available on request.\n\nThe third floor, with separate access, has been lovingly restored to accommodate family living. Incorporating original features like deep set windows and exposed red brick and granite, oak beams and red bricked arches showcasing the historical features of the building. All doors, frames and skirting are solid oak. Extremely large, comfortable living spaces, finished to the highest of standards and captivating some of the best views in the city.
