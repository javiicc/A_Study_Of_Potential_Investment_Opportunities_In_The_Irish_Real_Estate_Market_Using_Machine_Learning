This mid terrace artisan cottage is located in the heart of Stoneybatter with an array of independent cafes, shops, bars and restaurants all on it's doorstep. Within walking distance of Smithfield Village, the City Centre, the Phoenix Park and TUD Grangegorman. Great public transport links via Dublin Bus, Dublin Bikes and the LUAS at both Museum and Grangegorman make this a commuters dream location. Accommodation briefly consists of lounge, lobby area, kitchen with dining area, two double bedrooms and a shower room. There is a West facing patio garden to the rear. The property also benefits from GFCH and an extended kitchen. This cottage offers an ideal opportunity for those looking for an original Dublin Artisan home in a vibrant City village that is known for it's great can do community spirit and a culinary destination.
