    Sherry FitzGeralds proudly brings to the market No.98 High Street which is a delightful two-bedroom mid terrace home that is presented in walk-in condition and is located within a couple of minutes walk to the city centre.\n\nThis two-bedroom property has a great balance of living and bedroom accommodation. On the ground floor there is an entrance hall that is full of character and opens into an open plan living/dining area, a well-equipped galley style kitchen followed on by the main bathroom. Upstairs there are two good sized double bedrooms.\n High Street is prime location and a wonderful address. It has all essential amenities nearby including schools, shops, pubs etc. The city centre is a stroll away and the south ring road network is easily accessed from here.\n\nThis is an opportunity that should not be missed. \n\nArrange a viewing today!\nEntrance Hall 3.22m x 0.85m. The welcoming entrance hall enters in from the front of the house. It is nice and bright and is floored with a classic check tile.\n\nLiving Area 2.96m x 2.37m. A lovely bright space that looks out to the front of the property and benefits from being open plan with the dining area. The living area features a solid wood floor, recess lighting along with an open fire.\n\nDining Area 2.94m x 3.35m. Accessed from the entrance hall is a very nice dining area that is open plan and runs out to the living room and provides access to the kitchen area. The dining area benefits from a semi-solid wood floor, recess lighting and provides access to the back of the house.\n\nKitchen 3.93m x 2.05m. The galley style kitchen is and located to rear of home. It is fitted with ahigh quality floor and eye level style units on either side with the floor being tiled throughout. There is a window that overlooks the yard area and provides natural light.\n\nMain Bathroom 1.88m x 1.91m. Comprising of a three-piece bath suite with a shower over the bath. The floor and walls are fully tiled to a high standard. There is a window to the side of the house for natural ventilation.\n\nLanding 0.87m x 1.38m. The landing area gives access to both bedrooms.\n\nBedroom 1 2.92m x 3.55m. Bright double bedroom located to the front of the home.\n\nBedroom 2 3.35m x 2.55m. Bright double bedroom located to the rear of the home and benefits from a storage space over the stairs.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nIf walking from the City Centre, head for the Main Douglas Road passing Paddy the farmers pub on your left-hand side. Continue straight and take the first right on to High Street. Continue straight and No.98 is on the right-hand side. See agents sign.
