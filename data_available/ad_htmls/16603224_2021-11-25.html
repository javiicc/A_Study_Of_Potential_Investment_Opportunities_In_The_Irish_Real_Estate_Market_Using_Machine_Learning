    REDMOND PROPERTY GROUP are delighted to present 21 Belfry Grove to the market for sale. This bright and deceptively spacious three bedroom Duplex is presented in excellent order throughout and is ideally situated on a quiet cul-de-sac in the ever popular Belfry development close to all local amenities (shops, schools, parks, bus and road routes N7, N81, M50) and just a short walk away from the LUAS and Citywest Shopping Centre. \r\n\r\nThis lovely property has been well maintained by its owners and briefly comprises of an entrance hall, living room opening onto the west facing rear garden, \r\nfully fitted kitchen that has been extended, three great sized bedrooms all with fitted wardrobes, Family bathroom and cloakroom/W.C. The rear garden is accessed by a side entrance and at 50ft long is rare in modern built estates. The private garden has a large paved patio area and to the front is ample resident and visitor parking.
