    A high quality three bedroom end of terrace town house in this popular quiet cul de sac location that offers easy access to primary and secondary schools. This property is in turn key condition and it has many extra features including an en suite from the master bedroom, a low maintenance garden with patio, external insulation and many more. It has on site parking to the front of the house and the large garden is ideal for children and pets. The house also offers easy access to the lovely Shannon river walks and it is also convenient to the Shannon Free Zone. It would make an ideal family home or investment property.
