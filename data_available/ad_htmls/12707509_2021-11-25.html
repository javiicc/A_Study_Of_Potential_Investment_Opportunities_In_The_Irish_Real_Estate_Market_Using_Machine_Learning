High Speed Fibre broadband available in this area. \r\n\r\nThe property offers geothermal and gas under floor heating on ground floor and first floor. Double glazed upvc windows and doors. Large walk in hot-press shelved.\r\nUpstairs offers a spacious landing. Five double bedrooms with en-suite off two bedrooms. A mixture of carpet and timber flooring and walk-in or fitted wardrobes in each of the bedrooms. All rooms are tastefully decorated.\r\nA large entertainment cinema room, sound proved.
