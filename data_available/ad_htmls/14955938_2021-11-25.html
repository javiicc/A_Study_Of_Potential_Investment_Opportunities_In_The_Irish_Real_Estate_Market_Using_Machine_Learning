    Historic architecturally converted coach house hidden behind a 6ft limestone perimeter wall with double solid wooden electric gates and set amidst an abundance of mature rustling foliage in a fully private southerly setting. This is a most exciting unique detached residence designed by the prominent Limerick architect, Michael Healy. The ground floor open plan reception areas delight the senses encompassing a mixture of floor to ceiling wall to wall glass, original limestone walls, split level areas with varied double height ceilings and coloured glass block wall features, resulting in a stunning home of distinction and character. The Coach House is set back from the North Circular Road via a private shared lane with Eden Terrace and is perfectly hidden in a tranquil secluded spot. A rare opportunity to acquire a detached home of note on the sought after North Circular Road, Limerick's most desirable address which is within walking distance of Limerick City and a selection of primary and secondary schools, including JFK, Salesians, Ardscoil Ris and Villiers, Limerick Lawn Tennis Club and Thomond Park.
