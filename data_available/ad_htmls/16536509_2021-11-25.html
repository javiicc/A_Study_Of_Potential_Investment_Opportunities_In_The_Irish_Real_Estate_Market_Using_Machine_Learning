    An most impressive Double fronted 1920\xe2\x82\xacTM family home requiring modernisation and set on one of Monkstown's premier Roads. Clonlea extends to approximately 1876sq.ft/174.3 sq.m. and retains the many architectural features synonymous with its era. With a generous front and rear gardens the property offers great potential to extend and update this fabulous family home located within a short walk of Monkstown Village.\nOn entering, a bright and welcoming entrance hall greets you featuring original ceiling cornicing and Staircase and a Guest cloakroom . Off the hall, the spacious drawing room with bay window is to the front and dining room to the rear. The Kitchen leads through to the living room overlooking the garden. There is a generous utility room off the kitchen with door to rear garden. Upstairs there are four bedrooms and family bathroom. Approached through Electric gates leading to a tarmac driveway which has ample parking for a number of cars. The front garden has mature planted borders and hedging. The rear garden is easterly facing and extends to approximately 22m x 13m. The garden is mature laid out in lawn with Patio area, water feature ,mature planting and hedge borders. There is also a very useful large garden shed. The rear of the property can be accessed by a side entrance.\nKnapton Road is located within easy reach of both Dun Laoghaire and Monkstown, and an easy walk from the DART. Charming Monkstown Village offers a selection of pubs, restaurants and specialty food shops and boutiques. Some well known traders of Monkstown Village include Avoca, Cinnamon, Caf\xc3\xa9 Du Journal, That\xe2\x82\xacTM Amore Italian restaurant, Seagreen Boutique and Searsons wine merchants .\nProximity to coast is one of the many great advantages of living in Monkstown. A quick swim at Seapoint and sailing in Dun Laoghaire or just taking a stroll along the coast are all within a few minutes from Knapton Road. There is also an excellent choice of schools in the area including Castle Park, Loreto Dalkey, CBC Monkstown Park, Blackrock College, Mount Anville and St. Andrew's College\nTransport links are excellent with the DART nearby and a wide choice of bus services available.
