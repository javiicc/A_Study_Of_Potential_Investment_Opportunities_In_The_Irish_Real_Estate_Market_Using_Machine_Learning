    A winning combination, a superb two-bedroom apartment in a superb location! An own door, ground floor, apartment presented in great condition throughout boasting south facing rear patio, a C1 energy rating and quiet setting within the development. When you own an Ayrfield home you will appreciate the finer things in life with every modern convenience on your doorstep. This beautiful home holds all the ingredients of the perfect apartment home including nicely proportioned living and bedroom accommodation, neutral tones throughout, a private sunny rear patio and a great location. Ayrfield is a development of predominantly detached and semi-detached properties conveniently tucked away off the Granges Road opposite Loreto girls Secondary School, down the road from St. Canices Co-Ed Primary School and right next to Ayrfield Medical Practice. Built by McCarthy Bros. in 2007, No. 8 The Avenue is a solid built, two-bedroom, ground floor, terraced apartment. It is set in a terrace of only ten properties comprising two four-bedroom end of terrace houses, four three-bedroom duplexes and four two-bedroom apartments. Apart from its hard to beat location the property has lots to offer! It is positioned to the rear of the development with minimal passing traffic, it enjoys an open plan Kitchen / Dining / Living area, a large ensuite double bedroom & built-in wardrobes in both bedrooms. The property also benefits from the use of communal refuse and bike sheds. It has ample car parking immediately to the front of the property. A walking commute to work downtown is entirely possible - a short 1.3km stroll will take you to High Street. The River Nore Linear Park walk is also nearby via The Sycamores or Riverside Drive.
