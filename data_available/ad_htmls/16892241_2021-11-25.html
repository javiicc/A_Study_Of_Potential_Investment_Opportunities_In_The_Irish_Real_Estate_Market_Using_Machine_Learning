    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 1.5 ac
    Tucked away in a most secluded corner of the townland of Cartron and walking distance to the local village of Tulrahan lies a most enchanting plot of ground upon which to construct a wonderfully secluded family home.\r\nThis is a delightfully peaceful location which is surrounded by the wonders of nature and offers the ultimate in privacy. If you choose to put your roots down here- then yours would be the only dwelling along a picture perfect country lane cul de sac, which is an ideal location for young families and remote workers alike- especially those who yearn for their own private corner of the world.\r\nThis is a substantial parcel of ground and measures 1.5 acres in area.\r\nTo add to the intrigue there is also the remains of a 150 year old cottage on site, with the option to restore to its former glory as a second property or to raze to the ground to facilitate your new development.\r\nThe site is located just off the R327 regional road which just a 10 minute drive to Claremorris town, 5 minutes to Cloonfad, an hour to Galway City and just 25 minutes to Knock International Airport.\r\nThere is water and electricity already available on site and given the fact that there is also an existing dwelling present, it is our understanding that a planning application should be a relatively straight- forward process.\r\nThe provisional architectural concepts for the cottage renovation have already been drawn up along with a second dwelling house for the area of ground located just past the cottage.\r\nA multitude of options await the purchaser of this plot which will also make a stunning location for a one off family home.\r\nBrought to you by Fair Deal Property Ltd.\r\nThe Premium Estate Agency Without the High Fees\r\nBecause you Deserve A Fair Deal.
