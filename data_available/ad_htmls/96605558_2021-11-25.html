    Development Description

      A new level of luxury living has arrived in Dublin 4 with the opening of the first completed blocks and the opening of the Residential Amenity area in the recently completed Templeton Building in Lansdowne Place. \n\nChartered Land have opened the doors of the newly completed Templeton, Barrington and Blake Buildings, the first completed blocks in the luxurious development in the heart of Ballsbridge. The development has been designed by award-winning architects O Mahony Pike in collaboration with London-based interior architects Goddard Littlefair, to deliver the elegant interiors for the residential amenities. \n\nA selection of completed show apartments and the stunning Residents Amenities are now available to view by appointment with Sherry FitzGerald New Homes. Two of the new show apartments have also been dressed by Goddard Littlefair. The third show apartment has been dressed to provide an alternative look and feel by Irish interior architect Sara Cosgrove.\n\nResidents at Lansdowne Place and their guests will enjoy the benefit of a full 24/7 Concierge Service, The Morning Lounge, The Reading Room, The Pembroke Business Suite, The Clyde Function Room as well as a fully air-conditioned Cinema Room with an Adobe surround sound system. This lifestyle initiative, never seen before in Ireland, also houses a fully equipped gym, a sauna, steam room and multipurpose treatment rooms.\n\nEach apartment at Lansdowne Place is bright and spacious with large open plan living spaces and generous ceiling heights, up to 2.7 m (8 10 ), along with floor to ceiling windows throughout. A selection of penthouses will enjoy double height ceilings in the living space.
