    53 Marrsfield Avenue is a substantial 4-bedroom end of terrace family home extending to 138sqm/ 1,485sqf brought to the market in excellent condition throughout. The highly efficient energy rating of A3 and the south facing rear garden are just a couple of the reasons why this will make a fine family home for many years.\n\nThe living accommodation comprises of an entrance hall, guest WC, living room and an open plan kitchen/ dining room with double French doors leading to the rear garden. The first floor has two generous bedrooms, the utility room, and the family bathroom. The second floor accommodates the main bedroom with en suite and a further bedroom. The rear garden is beautifully displayed with a patio area and an in-lawn garden, this is all helped by the south facing orientation.\n\nThe location of this property is simply excellent. Situated within walking distance to several local amenities including shops, creche facilities, excellent national and secondary schools. Clarehall and Donaghmede shopping centres are also located close by and provide every retail amenity imaginable. The development has numerous children s play areas and the popular Father Collins Park is just 1 minute away from the front door. There are excellent transport links close by including the DART from Clongriffin Station, and numerous bus routes which provide easy access into the city centre and beyond. Dublin airport is only 10-15 minutes drive from Clongriffin.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 5.10m x 1.40m. Laminate timber effect floor, alarm control panel, understair storage.\n\nGuest WC 1.68m x 1.53m.
