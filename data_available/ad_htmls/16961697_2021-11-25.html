    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    CONERNEY ESTATE AGENTS are delighted to present to market this fabulous North-Westerly facing apartment, set in the extremely popular Garthy Wood in Knocklyon. \r\nThis is a modern, spacious, light filled two bedroom apartment, situated in the very desirable leafy green and tranquil suburb of Knocklyon. Nestled between The Rookery, a prestigious cul-de-sac and the Knocklyon Shopping Complex you will discover the charming Garthy Wood development. Ideally located, this turn-key property is close to the M50, bus stops, parks, in addition to an abundance of leisure and sporting facilities including, restaurants, schools, shopping centres and golf clubs.\r\nBeing a small development with a large majority of owner occupiers, this private complex is a unique investment opportunity or a lovely home. Tastefully veiled behind electric gates and mature trees, this property comes with an allocated car-space in a secured underground car-park which is accessible by a choice of elevator / stairwell. The property layout is built to a very attractive and space conducive spec, boasting large windows in all rooms, none of which are overlooked, this highly desirable structural design maximises the light throughout the day as this property has the added bonus of being positioned south-westerly facing. The living area is open plan with large double doors which lead you to the wooden veranda/ balcony. Both double bedrooms are large in size, with the master bedroom having the added luxury of a private en-suite bathroom. The main bathroom is also spacious in size, fitted with both a bath and shower. Overall, this is a unique opportunity to attain a property which has the advantage of being both a valuable real-estate investment, in addition to being a superb modern home.
