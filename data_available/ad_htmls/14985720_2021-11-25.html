    NEW TO THE MARKET MAY 2021\n\nThree bedroom property (with two en-suites) situated in an excellent location at Curlew Hill, which is just off Shore Road and is within walking distance to Belmullet town. This is a modern property and is well maintained throughout. The ground floor consists of one bedroom with fitted wardrobes, the spacious fully fitted kitchen and dining area, a utility room adjacent to the kitchen, (with access to the rear of the property), the sitting room with an open fireplace (featuring a back boiler which heats the water / radiators), the main family bathroom and a storage area underneath the stairs.\n\nThe first floor contains the two remaining bedrooms, one of which features fitted wardrobes and both bedrooms have en-suites with showers & adequate storage spaces.\n\nThis property is being sold fully furnished with all electrical appliances included in the sale. There is also a large garage to the rear, along with a secluded garden area and a small lawn to the front.\n\nThe property was constructed in 2002 and has an overall floor area of 1,200 square feet.
