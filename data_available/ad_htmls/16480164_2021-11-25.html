    Moovingo is delighted to present Elm Park Lodge, a charming detached one bed bungalow in a superb location. The current owners have maintained the property in excellent condition throughout while also periodically upgrading features over the years. Situated close to Marlay Park and within easy access of Rathfarnham, Balinteer and Dundrum Villages and just 9km south of Dublin City Centre, this property will appeal to first-time buyers, downsizers and investors.\r\n\r\nThe bright and spacious accommodation briefly comprises an entrance hall that leads through to a large open plan kitchen and living area with double doors that open up onto a crescent shaped garden. There is an ensuite bedroom and a main bathroom in the property too. There is also scope to extend the property into the attic subject to planning permission.\r\n\r\nThe private bungalow has a south westerly orientation with a fireplace creating a very cosy feel to this special home. There are ample parking spaces and the property also has access to a large communal garden at the rear of Elm Park House.\r\n\r\nArea Info\r\n\r\nElm Park House is ideally situated next to an array of excellent amenities. There is a wide choice of recreational activities, sports clubs, shops and restaurants, not to mention numerous primary and secondary schools within walking, cycling or a short drive. Dundrum Shopping Centre and the M50 road network are close by and Dublin City Centre is approx 20mins drive.\r\n\r\nScenic mountain walks are in close proximity along with public parks and playgrounds, most notable Marlay Park. The parkland comprises woodlands, ponds and walks. Recreational spaces include a nine-hole, par-three golf course, tennis courts, six soccer pitches, five GAA pitches, a cricket pitch, a dog park, two children's playgrounds and a miniature railway run by the Dublin Society of Model and Experimental Engineers. There is also a craft courtyard with home craft shops and a coffee shop.
