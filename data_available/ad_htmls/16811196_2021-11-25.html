    Sherry FitzGerald Durcan are delighted to introduce to the market this very attractive 4 bedroom detached bungalow which stand on an elevated site of 0.4 acres overlooking Richards Lough .\n\nCentrally located in a very appealing rural setting convenient to both Straide and Ballyvary Villages with Foxford, Swinford, Ballina and Castlebar all just a short drive away.\n\nWhile the property has been newly roofed in recent years it would benefit from further modernisation and renovations making it the ideal property for the DIY enthusiast.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 3.66m x 1.02m.\n\nBedroom 1 4m x 2.67m.\n\nBedroom 2 2.2m x 3.28m.\n\nBedroom 3 3.28m x 2.54m.\n\nBathroom 3.3m x 2.24m. with wc, wash hand basin and bath with shower.\n\nBedroom 4 4.24m x 12.\n\nKitchen Dining Room 4.24m x 6.68m. with fully fitted kitchen units, breakfast counter and solid fuel stove.\n\nLiving Room 3.76m x 2.62m.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nTake N5 out of Castlebar to Ballvary, turn left onto N58 to Foxford, take 2nd road on left L5796, continue on this road and cross over railway line, take next left, at the next T junction turn left, the property is the 2nd house on right.\n\nEircode: F26 WN66.\nGoogle Maps: 53.918358, -9.15113
