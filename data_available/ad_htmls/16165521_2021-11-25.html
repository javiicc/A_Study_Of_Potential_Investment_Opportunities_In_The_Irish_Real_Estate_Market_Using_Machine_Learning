    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Tender
    Private and secure, Rose Cottage, located in the tranquil and lakeside neighbourhood of Hodson Bay, is a beautiful and mature family home or lakeside retreat just 90 minutes from Dublin city and airport respectively, or 50 minutes from Galway. \r\n\r\nJust minutes walk from Lough Ree and Athlone Golf Club. Hodson Bay boasts the ideal location, with an abundance of lakeside amenities on it's doorstep, including the well known Hodson Bay Hotel and Spa (with gym, pool, restaurants and bars), Baysports water sports and water park, and the Hodson Bay pier and marina. Excellent schools are within a cycle or short drive away and the M6 motorway is a 6 minute drive from the house, allowing for easy commuting to east or west of the country. \r\n\r\nThe house itself sits on an acre of mature lawns with a tree lined boundary around the perimeter. Electric gates offer extra security and privacy to the property. \r\n\r\nIn keeping with the spirit of a country cottage, the modern kitchen opens up to the beautifully paved courtyard through french double doors. Another set of double doors opens to a breakfast / sun room, allowing for morning sunshine throughout the year. \r\nThe kitchen also has direct access to a dining / reception room with open fireplace and views of both front and back gardens. \r\nThe kitchen also has a small library area. \r\n\r\nA 2nd reception room with large windows and fireplace allows views onto the front mature garden. \r\n\r\n3 double bedrooms have views of the courtyard and back garden, while a 4th and 5th bedroom overlook the front garden. \r\n\r\nThe 5th bedroom has recently been used as a home office. This room has it's own front door, parking space outside and a separate room behind it which in turn has a patio door to the back courtyard and garden. This could easily be adapted to a granny flat / au-pair/nanny quarters, or continue as an office space with own storage room etc. \r\n\r\nMaster room has an ensuite shower & WC. \r\nBedroom 4 and 5 share a Jack and Jill style Shower & WC room. \r\n\r\nFamily Bathroom with bath. \r\n\r\nDual Central Heating; Kerosene\r\nSolid fuel glass fronted stove/room heater in one Reception Room. \r\nBoiler room. \r\n\r\n\r\nRose Cottage is offers you an excellent lakeside home/retreat with close proximity to Athlone Town and surrounding amenities.
