    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    A generously proportioned family home, situated within a large plot on this highly desirable, quiet road within walking distance of both Templeogue and Terenure villages with Bushy Park and a superb selection of schools on the doorstep. The property is extremely bright and airy with excellent scope to create a home to ones own tastes and requirements. \r\n\r\nNo 43 offers excellent potential and the wrap around footprint lends itself to an exciting final design should one wish to extend/develop further.\r\nBriefly comprising: an entrance hall and porch, living room, dining room, kitchen//breakfast room, family room. Upstairs there are four double bedrooms (one en-suite) and a family bathroom\r\n\r\nAshfield is a much sought-after and highly convenient location in one of Dublin\xe2\x82\xacTM most popular and mature residential areas within walking distance of Templeogue and Terenure villages. Every conceivable amenity is within a short stroll. It is a 5-minute walk to the local grocery store, pharmacy, medical practice, salons and other neighbourhood shops. Bushy park is also close by providing further opportunity for local walking/running routes, cycling, woodland trails and a weekly farmer\xe2\x82\xacTM market. Dublin City Centre is approx. 5km away and the area offers quick and easy access to the M50.There is an excellent range of well-established and highly regarded primary and secondary schools all close by.\r\n\r\nViewing is highly recommended to appreciate what is on offer.
