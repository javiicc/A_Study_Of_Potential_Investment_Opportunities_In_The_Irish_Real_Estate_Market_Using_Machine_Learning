    Sherry FitzGerald Lewis Hamill are delighted to offer for sale this magnificent 6 bedroom, 6 bathroom detached 2 storey family residence of distinction set in what is widely regarded as one of the most sought after and exclusive leafy enclave of 12 individual homes in Tullamore and is only 20 minutes walk to the town centre. This substantial residence, built in 2007 with the owner s attention to site orientation and detail of design/finish is evident from the kerb and throughout. The property is built to maximise the light potential throughout the day and this is immediately obvious upon entering the building. The property provides a superb blend of living and bedroom accommodation. The bright and light filled entrance hallway sets the tone with its marble floor tiling, tasteful colour scheme and open plan design of 2 stunning reception rooms (a formal dining room & a quiet sitting room) either side of the hallway, each with solid walnut flooring. The sitting room has an open fire with marble fireplace and matching mirror and a built in bookcase. The dining room has a glass door leading into a stunning kitchen which features an array of German made high gloss wall and floor units, centre island with Corian work surfaces and a rangemaster cooker with 2 electric ovens and a gas hob. A lovely feature of this room is the full length window dining area which allows light to flood through in addition to the lovely glass roof over the kitchen. The dining area also has double glass doors leading into a cosy living room, which has a solid fuel stove, again with solid walnut floors. The kitchen/dining room has double patio doors leading to a patio and a lovely back garden filled with flowers and shrubs. This open plan kitchen/dining room is perfect for family and entertaining. From the hall there is a generous, beautifully presented double bedroom on the ground floor with large luxurious 4 piece bathroom / wet room adjacent. A beautiful bespoke stairs leads to a spacious landing with windows back and front which could be used as an office/study or quiet reading area. There is also a large, shelved walkin hot press. The 5 spacious double bedrooms each have beautifully appointed En-Suite bathrooms which are fully tiled. A stairs leads to a large, floored attic with office/playroom and storage.
