    Imposing detached Victorian style country residence boasting tranquil gardens set behind high walls and electronic gates with lovely views over the countryside and mountains. \r\n"Elgan" is a contemporary family home with extensive accommodation sure to suit any family's way of life measuring c. 300sqm. Situated on c. 0.75 of an acre, this stunning home give an immediate WOW factor.\r\n The spacious entrance hall with a double staircase, the large wrought iron chandler hanging above the hall and the highly fashionable luxurious interior are picture perfect. The accommodation is well appointed with generous portions and double doors leading from room to room on the ground floor giving the house an open and spacious feeling. The interconnecting drawing rooms with dual aspect views and imposing marble fireplaces lead through to a light filled dining room and again into very large kitchen. \r\nNo expense has been spared with French oak cabinets and granite work tops, the kitchen boasts quality built in appliances with a range cooker, separate ovens and large double sized fridge freezer. Utility room, shower room and large study ( 5th bedroom) complete the ground floor of this stunning home. \r\nThe first floor boasts a large landing, 4 double bedrooms which include 2 very spacious en-suites and a family bathroom with a modern roll top bath and walk in shower. The high quality fittings and fixtures are immediately evident. The master bedroom is spacious with views over the countryside, a dressing room and feature en-suite bathroom with a large circular Jacuzzi bath and separate shower. A door leads from the hall up a stairs to a very large and spacious floored attic area. \r\nOutside the generous gardens compliment the home with the added bonus 2 beautifully appointed chalets. \r\nThis unique and stunning home will not disappoint. \r\nThe house is located in a commanding position just minutes from the towns of Arklow and Gorey with easy access to the M11 and Dublin approx 50 minutes drive. The village of Coolgreany is just under 1km from the house and provides a shop, post office, bars, hairdressers, cafe, national school and various other community amenities and activity clubs.
