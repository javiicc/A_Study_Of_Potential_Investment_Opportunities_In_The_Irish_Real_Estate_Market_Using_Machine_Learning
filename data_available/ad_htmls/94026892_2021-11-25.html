    Development Description

      **Viewings by Appointment**\r\n\r\nOak Park is an exclusive development of 55 units by Richmond Homes launching Thursday 14th October. Located to the north of Kinsealy Lane and within walking distance to Malahide Castle, Oak Park Phase 1 will offer a selection of A2 rated three and four bedroom luxury homes situated around a large communal green. \r\n \r\nLess than 5 minutes walk from the gates of Malahide Castle, a leisurely stroll through the grounds brings you to the heart of leafy Malahide Village with its abundance of amenities including restaurants, bars, boutiques, sporting facilities and the renowned Malahide Marina and coastline. Malahide Train Station with DART and commuter trains allows for quick and easy access to the City Centre in 20 minutes while Dublin Airport is a mere 7km away. The M1 and M50 road networks are also only a short drive. \r\n \r\nThese contemporary homes have been meticulously designed to ensure every aspect of luxury living has been considered with a high specification throughout. Launching later this this year, this exciting new development is sure to appeal to those looking for a new contemporary home in the most convenient of locations. \r\n\r\nRegister your interest with Savills New Homes at Oak-Park.ie to receive updates including floor plans, images and specifications once these details are available. We look forward to sharing further information with you on this stunning new development of A2 rated homes.
