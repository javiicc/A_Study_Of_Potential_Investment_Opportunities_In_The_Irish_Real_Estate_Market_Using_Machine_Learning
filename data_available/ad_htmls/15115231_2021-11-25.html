    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    Ideally set overlooking Griffith Park, no 5 Woodville Road is a beautiful end of terrace period residence which has been refurbished by its current owners and decorated with great style. Many of the original features have been retained such as cast iron fireplaces, polished wooden floors and ceiling covings which are combined with all the modern comforts. The high ceilings give a great sense of space in the house and the setting overlooking beautiful trees and green spaces gives this property a feeling of being in the country rather than the city. The property has the added bonus of a garage to the rear which is ideal for off street parking or storage.\n\nThe accommodation comprises a large hallway with tiled floor, there are two interconnecting reception rooms both with polished wooden floors and cast iron fireplaces, the living room to the front has views of Griffith Park while the dining room overlooks the rear garden. The kitchen is fitted with a range of floor and wall units. Upstairs there are two large double bedrooms, the front bedroom spans the width of the property and both have cast iron fireplaces. A very spacious bathroom on the return completes the accommodation.\n\nThe garden to the rear measures approx. 4m x 2.8m and is paved, a door leads to the garage approx. 5.7m x 5.3 and can accommodate parking or storage.\n\nThe location is very convenient being within walking distance of the city centre and convenient to the numerous shops, cafes, bars and restaurants Drumcondra Village has to offer. The Botanic Gardens and Griffith Park are closeby and provide beautiful open green areas. Dublin City University is within easy reach with a variety of good sports clubs, national and secondary schools locally. Commuting further afield is made very easy with the M50 Motorway, The M1 and Dublin Airport within a few minutes drive. The area is very well serviced by public transport, through the many bus routes which pass close by and Drumcondra Rail station nearby.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 7.7m x 1.5m. Large entrance hallway with tiled floor, ceiling covings and understairs storage.\n\nLiving Room 3.8m x 3.5m. This is a beautiful living room to the front with polished wooden floor, ceiling covings and a pretty cast iron fireplace as the main focal point. An opening leads to the dining room.\n\nDining Room 3.5m x 3.5m. Another bright reception room with matching cast iron fireplace, polished wooden floor and ceiling covings.\n\nKitchen 4m x 2m. The kitchen is fitted with a range of floor and wall units with tiled splashback and floor, there is plumbing for a dishwasher.\n\nBedroom 1 5.1m x 3.7m. Spanning the width of the property, this is a very spacious main bedroom with two windows flooding the room with light, features include a cast iron fireplace and ceiling covings.\n\nBedroom 2 3.7m x 3.6m. Generous size double room with cast iron fireplace and ceiling covings.\n\nBathroom 3.8m x 2m. Large bathroom on the return, fully tiled with suite comprising w.c, wash hand basin, bath and shower cubicle with Triton shower. There is plumbing for a washing machine and dryer.
