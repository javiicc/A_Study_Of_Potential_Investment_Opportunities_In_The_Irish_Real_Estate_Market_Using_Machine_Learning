    Smith & Butler Estates are delighted to bring to the market this beautiful end terrace, 2 bedroom bungalow with double glazed windows, high ceilings, on street parking and private courtyard. This beautiful home has been lovingly taken care of and is presented for sale in superb condition throughout. The accommodation comprises a private courtyard leading to a welcoming entrance hallway. The kitchen and bathroom are both off the hall. The reception room is located at the end of hallway, with high ceilings, spacious proportions, natural light and a feature fireplace. This property also provides huge development potential with planning permission previously granted to extend an additional upper floor over the entire building. \r\n\r\nThe location is second to none. The property is a 10 minute walk from O'Connell Street and 3 minute walk\xc2\xa0to Croke Park. The property further offers easy access to Dublin City University, St. Patrick's Training College, Dublin Institute of Technology Bolton Street, Dublin Institute of Higher Education, Mountjoy Square, the IFSC, East Point Business Park, Dublin Airport, Beaumont, Mater & Temple Street Hospitals, DCU and Trinity and the M1 & M50 motorways. Other nearby amenities include Griffith Park, the National Botanic Gardens, the Royal Canal. The area is very well serviced by a great public transport system, it's only a 5 minute walk to Drumcondra train station and same distance to the air coach for Airport. 1A Holycross combines the best elements of city and suburban living with the peaceful road's close proximity to the city making it a rare and unique location.
