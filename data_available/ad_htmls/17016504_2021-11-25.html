    7 Chestnut Lawn is a spacious 3 bedroom semi detached home extending to c. 98 sq.m. well positioned to the front of Chestnut Lawn development overlooking a large green area. This is a very impressive family home with enclosed rear garden, side access and front garden with private driveway.\n\nThis property comes to the market in good condition and with some upgrading it has potential to be an excellent family home. The property offers well proportioned living and bedroom accommodation. There is a spacious walled in front driveway and landscaped garden. \n\nChestnut Lawn is located close to all the local amenities in Johnstown Village and the Bailis Shopping Centre. St Stephen\xe2\x82\xacTM National School and Colaiste na Mhi are adjacent to the development. This property enjoys easy access to Junction 8 M3 and the N2 is also close by leaving Dublin City & Airport very accessible. \n\nAccommodation includes Entrance Hall, Lounge, Kitchen / Dining Room, Guest W.C., 3 Bedrooms (Master Bedroom Ensuite) and Family Bathroom.\n\nACCOMMODATION:\n\nEntrance Hall - 15'9" x 5'10"\nWith carpet, hardwood door with leaded glass side panels, coving and centrepiece. \n\nLounge - 15'8" x 11'4"\nWith feature wooden fireplace with open fire, wooden flooring, bay window, T.V. point, coving, centrepiece and double doors to the dining room. \n\nKitchen / Dining Room - 17'9" x 11'7"\nWith tiled flooring, built in wall and floor units, oven, hob, extractor fan, fridge freezer, dishwasher, plumbed for washing machine and sliding doors to the rear garden. \n\nGuest W.C.\nWith vinyl floor covering, w.c. and w.h.b.\n\nLanding\nWith carpet and hotpress.\n\nBedroom 1 - 12'9" x 11'4"\nWith tongue and groove flooring, built in wardrobes, telephone point and T.V. point. \n\nEnsuite - 6'1" x 5'4"\nWith vinyl floor covering, partly tiled walls, shower, w.c., and w.h.b.\n\nBedroom 2 - 11'9" x 9'6"\nWith tongue and groove flooring and built in wardrobes.\n\nBedroom 3 - 9'1" x 7'10"\nWith tongue and groove flooring and built in wardrobes.\n\nBathroom - 6'1" x 6'0"\nWith vinyl floor covering, partly tiled walls, w.c., w.h.b and bath.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\n\nFrom Dublin travel into Navan on the M3. Take junction 8 Navan South. Travel to the roundabout and take the 1st exit. At the first set of traffic turn right. Continue straight and at the roundabout take the first exit. Take the next right and right again into Chestnut Lawn. The property is located on the left hand side identified by our for sale sign.
