    DETACHED 3 BAY 2 STOREY GEORGIAN HOUSE WITH PLANNING IN PLACE FOR 2 NO. 2 BED HOLIDAY HOMES & 2 NO. 2 BED APT.\r\n\r\nFinlay House occupies a prominent and highly visible location on Dublin Street opposite the Market Square in Monasterevin Town Centre. Monasterevin is a town situated on the M7 and River Barrow in west Kildare approximately 63 kms south west of Dublin, 19 kms south west of Naas/Newbridge and 19 kms north of Athy and has a population of 4,246 people (Census 2016). There is good road and rail links to Dublin and the rest of the country. The M7 connects Monasterevin with Limerick and Naas and also the M50 into Dublin City.\r\n\r\nACCOMMODATION:\r\nThe property comprises detached two storey licensed premises with off licence on the ground floor with former restaurant on the first floor. Internally, the property provides front area bar, rear lounge and games area together with a snug. To the rear there is a toilet block, rear smoking area, cold room and various stores. There is an off-licence with own door access. Upstairs, there is a former restaurant (incl. dumb waiter) together with rear stores/office and toilets. Externally, there is a private car park.\r\n\r\n(GIA approx.)\r\n\r\nGround Floor - 182.96 sq.m. / 1,969 sq.ft\r\nFirst Floor - 156.80 sq.m / 1.688 sq.ft.\r\n\r\nTOTAL: 339.76 sq,m. / 3,657 sq.ft.\r\n\r\n\r\nHISTORY:\r\nFinlay House comprises a detached three-bay two storey Georgian House dating back to circa 1751 and is one of the earliest surviving and prominent landmark buildings in the town.\r\n\r\nPLANNING:\r\nPlanning Permission (Ref. No. 19/1192) was granted in August 2020 for demolition of storage sheds in rear yard, the construction of 2 no. two storey, two bed holiday homes in rear yard, conversion of first floor to staff living accommodation, re-location of cold room, provision of bin storage area, and all associated site works\xc3\x82\xef\xbf\xbd.\r\n\r\nThe property is a protected structure under the Monasterevin Local Area Plan 2016-2022. It is also listed in the National Inventory of Architectural Heritage (NIAH).\r\n\r\nTITLE: Freehold.\r\n\r\n\r\nSERVICES:\r\nWe understand that all main services are available to the property.
