    Brophy Estates are delighted to introduce No. 73 The Water Rill, Malahide to the market. This beautifully presented own door, two bedroom ground floor apartment is a bright spacious home which briefly comprises of an entrance hall, open plan living/dining area, separate kitchen, two bright spacious bedrooms & main bathroom complements this fine home. \r\n\r\nAdditionally there is designated parking space and ample visitor parking spaces available. This apartment is located in the highly sought after development of Waterside just off the Swords Road. Waterside is ideally located between Malahide Village and Swords, the best of both worlds with Malahide and easy-going seaside lifestyle and Swords with excellent shopping amenities including the Pavilion Shopping Centre. \r\n\r\nSuperbly located with easy access to M1, M50 and Dublin Airport with convenient transport links including Dublin Bus and Swords Express. Excellent choice of both national and secondary schools along with cafes, restaurants, sports facilities all within easy reach. \r\n\r\nThis fine property would suit first time buyers and investors. Early viewing is highly recommended
