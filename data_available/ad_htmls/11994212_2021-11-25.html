    * REDUCED TO SELL * A wonderful opportunity to acquire an extremely well positioned commercial premises located on the edge of the picturesque village of Avoca, a renowned tourist area. This two-storey property comprises a fully licensed bar & lounge on the ground floor & a substantially sized restaurant on the first floor (c.4000 sq.ft. total floor area). The property benefits from an adjoining parking area/outdoor entertainment space plus a very large separate car park opposite. There is also an option to purchase additional adjoining land (c.1.18 Hectares). Ground floor accommodation includes; a fully equipped bar & lounge area, characteristic and in excellent condition throughout - first floor; a restaurant with a commercial kitchen and wonderful views. The property is also convenient to the seaside towns of Wicklow & Arklow and an easy commute to Dublin which makes this area increasingly popular and attractive, particularly to those interested in country pursuits. If the dream is to run a successful hospitality business catering for locals and tourists alike then this property may tick all the boxes! Viewing is strictly by appointment only.
