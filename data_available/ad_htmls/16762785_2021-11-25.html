    DNG are excited to introduce to the market this stunning bungalow style residence situated 5 minutes from the popular town of Kilmallock and 15 minutes from Charleville. The residence is set on circa 1 acre, boasts beautiful mature, landscaped gardens and is surrounded by panoramic views of the Ballyhoura Mountains. Accommodation comprises of a bright spacious entrance hall with sky tube window, sitting room, kitchen, separate dining room, 3 bedrooms and main bath /shower room. The home is in fantastic condition throughout and includes a large garage and workshop which is plumbed and tarmacadam driveway with a gated entrance.
