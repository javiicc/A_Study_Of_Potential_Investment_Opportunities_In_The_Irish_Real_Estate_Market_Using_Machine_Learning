This property boasts a large 54 foot long west facing garden to the rear and offers fantastic potential to extend, subject to planning.\r\n\r\n47 Woodside would be ideal for first time buyers and benefits from a driveway to the front along with gated pedestrian side access. Further benefits include an ensuite off the master bedroom, an efficient C3 BER rating and a Barna shed in the garden.\r\n\r\nLight filled accommodation downstairs comprises of a wide entrance hallway, large living room to the front and an openplan kitchen/dining to the rear. Upstairs is a bathroom and three well proportioned bedrooms, with the master bedroom boasting an ensuite shower room.\r\n\r\nOutside the garden is a real gem, it measures 54 foot long x 23 foot wide and has an enviable west facing aspect. There is ample room to extend, a project successfully undertaken by some neighbouring homes.\r\n\r\n Located within one of Leixlip's most sought after and highly regarded locations. 47 Woodside enjoys the best of both worlds, as it is away from the hustle and bustle, while still enjoying easy access to every conceivable amenity. Woodside is readily accessible to Intel and Hewlett Packard. Confey train station and the N4/M4/M50 are also within striking distance.
