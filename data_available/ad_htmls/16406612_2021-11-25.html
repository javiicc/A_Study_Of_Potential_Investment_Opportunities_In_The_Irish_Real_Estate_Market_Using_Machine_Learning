    RE/MAX Professional Partners present to market an immaculate 4 bedroom detached house located at Roveagh, Kilcolgan, Co. Galway. \r\n\r\nSylvan House is a bright and spacious property presented in walk-in condition. Extending to c. 2,045 sq. ft., living accommodation is spread over 2 floors. Ground floor accommodation is comprised of a welcoming entrance hall, open plan contemporary style kitchen/dining room with sunken living area, utility, 2 double bedrooms and a main bathroom. Proceeding to the first floor is a landing, 2 large en-suite bedrooms and an office/study. The property also has the convenience of a separate garage/games room/gym to the rear of the house which has a sunny, south westerly aspect.\r\n\r\nBuilt in 2004, the property is certified with an impressive C1 BER Certificate and is heated by oil fired central heating and a remote-controlled gas feature fireplace in the living area. Double glazed windows feature throughout and are plentiful allowing light flow right through the property.\r\n\r\nThe open plan kitchen/dining/living area is the main hub and is perfect for entertaining or larger families. It simply must be viewed to be appreciated. From the dining room, French doors lead to a large decking area and a stunning Westerly facing landscaped garden extending to c. 0.53 acres. Surrounding the property are manicured lawns, an array of shrubs, mature trees and a decorative gravel driveway. Toward the back of the garden is a large garage/games room and a separate timber garden shed. The games room extends to c. 474 sq. ft. and is well finished internally. An extensive list of the property's features can be found at the bottom of this advert.\r\n\r\nIn terms of location, this property has all that one would require and is ideally located within easy reach of Clarinbridge, Kilcolgan, Oranmore and Galway City.
