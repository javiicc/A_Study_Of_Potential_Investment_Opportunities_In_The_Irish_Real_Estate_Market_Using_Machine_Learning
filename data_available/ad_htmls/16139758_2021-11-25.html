    Detached House - 2 Beds -1 Bath 0n 19 acres of land with extensive range of out-houses and multi-purpose sheds. A rare opportunity to acquire this unique and charming two bedroom country cottage on Ca; 19 Acres of agricultural land, this property comes with an extensive range of out-houses and multi-purpose sheds. Great for extra storage or suitable for garage, workshop, fuel shed, etc. This house is full of character, with old-fashioned wooden ceiling beams in the living room and surrounded by beautiful mature trees. Set in the heart of the countryside, with private stream at the end of the lawn, this property offers the ultimate in privacy, yet it is only a 20 minute drive to the town of Ballina, 10 mins to Crossmolina and the local village of Moygownagh is only 3 minutes drive, with supermarket, pub, school, community centre, football pitch & church. Also close to The Blanemore Forest Walk, a 5km loop walk, with Enniscrone Beach just a half an hours drive away and Knock Airport less than an hours journey this gem of a property is well worth a viewing. Located at Lessanny, Garranard, Moygownagh, Ballina, this house is South facing and is heated using a solid fuel range and oil fired central heating.
