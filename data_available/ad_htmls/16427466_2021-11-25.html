    MOVEHOME ESTATE AGENTS... are honoured to offer a very generously extended 3 bedroom property that still enjoys the benefits of both charm and character since its construction in 1933 . Behind the handsome exterior lies c. 175 m\xc2\xb2 / 1884 ft\xc2\xb2 of generous accommodation, particularly on the ground floor with the great benefit of a substantial extension to the rear, a large garage with access from the main house and ample space to entertain. Needing a little modernisation, No. 102 represents a rare opportunity to purchase a substantial family home set in a well-established Dublin location. It is hard to believe such a peaceful area is so close to the city centre and so convenient to everything the growing family might need. \r\n\r\nThe accommodation briefly comprises of a welcoming hallway that leads to the front inter connected living area with rear doors to the second living room or TV room. From there the pleasant surprise is the rear extension which hosts a very large living/dining area with access off this room to the rear kitchen and separate garage access. Double doors lead out to the low maintenance west facing garden. Upstairs consists of three bedrooms (two double and one single). The large bathroom with standalone power shower and bath completes the accommodation on this level. \r\n\r\nThis location is second to none with every possible amenity close by including an excellent choice of schools, shops, churches, pubs and restaurants. The area is well serviced by excellent bus routes, offering easy access to the City Centre which is only 3.5 miles away. Close to Dublin Airport and numerous business and industrial parks, it also offers the added attraction of being within reach of the Omni Park shopping centre and multiplex cinema, Beaumont Hospital and Dublin City University. There is a host of retail amenities nearby complementing the balanced mix of residential, leisure and commercial facilities within the area. In addition, there is an abundance of leisure amenities in the general area to enjoy. Whether rest or play, there is something for everyone. There is also a wide choice of sporting and recreational facilities nearby and with easy access to M1, M50, and Dublin Airport making this property most central to a host of local amenities.
