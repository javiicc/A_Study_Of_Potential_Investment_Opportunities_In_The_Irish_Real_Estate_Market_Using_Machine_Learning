    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 166 m\xc2\xb2
    Attractive 4 bedroom bungalow residence set in 1.25 acres approximately of exceptional quality landscaped gardens and located adjacent to Ballinlough Lake and convenient to Leap, Skibbereen and the many coastal villages in this area of West Cork.\n\nThe house, built in the 1970 s as a modern school, is carefully renovated and modified into a comfortable and spacious home. The many attractive features of the house include large picture windows, pvc double glazed windows and doors, oil fired central heating, modern multi fuel stove and fibre broadband.\n\nThe accommodation includes entrance hall, sitting room, kitchen / dining room, conservatory off, master bedroom en suite, bedroom 2, bedroom 3, bedroom 4 / study and family bathroom. There is a south facing conservatory / orangery type room, a garage, potting shed and other storage areas. \n\nThe gardens are magnificent, combining a mix of garden influences including Asian, Japanese, woodland, rockeries, orchard, vegetable growing areas, various private patio areas and lawns. The garden includes a myriad of native and other specimen trees and shrubs that are integrated to create a home that is full of charm and tranquility. \n\nThe School House offers an opportunity to purchase a quality home and garden set adjacent to Ballinlough Lake, whilst being just a short drive from Skibbereen, Leap, Glandore, Union Hall and Rosscarbery.\n\nServices: Private water supply, septic tank, fibre broadband.
