    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to bring to the market no 6 Quinns Cottages. A fantastic opportunity to acquire a character filled house in Limerick City Centre. No 6 is perfectly located within walking distance to the city centre with shops bars and restaurants all within an easily accessible range and walking distance to Mary Immaculate College.\n\nThe property contains three spacious bedrooms, two on the first floor and one on the ground floor. Both first floor bedrooms are comfortable doubles, and the ground floor bedroom is a single. The Kitchen/dining room has been extended to offer an open plan layout with velux windows bringing plenty of light into the room. The living room features a solid fuel stove at centre and laminate flooring. The bathroom comes fully tilled with bath/w overhead shower.\n\nThere is a courtyard to the rear of the property which is accessed from the kitchen.\n\nNo 9 is an ideal starter home or an investment purchase due to its close proximity to Limerick City and Mary Immaculate Collage. Viewing comes highly recommend.\n\nAccommodation: Ent Hall, Living Room, Kitchen/dining room, 1 Bathroom, 3 Bedrooms. \n\nNotes: GFCH, 77 sqm approx. Previously rented for 950 p/m.
