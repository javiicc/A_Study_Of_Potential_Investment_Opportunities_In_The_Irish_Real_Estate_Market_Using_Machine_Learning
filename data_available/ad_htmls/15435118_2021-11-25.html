    Auskurra House is a truly uniquely designed, spectacular home perched above the beautiful fishing village of Dunmore East in County Waterford.\r\n\r\nDesigned by award winning Architect, Stephen Nelson and John Duffy Design Group this contemporary and elegant home is a roomy 8000 sq feet and is set on circa. one acre. The split level design provides magnificent, unrivalled sea views over Dunmore East Village, Hook Head Peninsula and as far as the Saltee Islands.\r\n\r\nBuilt in 2005 the house design incorporates local traditional architectural features with the use of natural slate & cedar wood soffits, limestone & sandstone, together with rendered external wall finishes, but all with a tasteful contemporary twist. \r\n\r\nThe house has an energy efficient B2 rating with concrete floors on all levels and underfloor heating throughout.\r\n\r\nThe house is built over three floors with the main entrance at ground floor level. The large main hall leads to an open plan living and kitchen area with adjoining lounge room and a spacious office. The two feature staircases are constructed of glass, hardwood treads and steel. There is a passenger lift that covers the three floor levels.\r\n\r\nAll the joinery is bespoke, including grand floor to ceiling height doors on the ground floor living areas. The windows are solid oak custom made in Austria. The flooring throughout is Maribu, an African Hardwood which provides an additional departure from the norm.\r\n\r\nThe kitchen area features a Varena kitchen by Poliform with high gloss finishes and a Dupont corian worktop. All appliances are top of the range by Gaggenau.\r\n\r\nThe first floor family floor level boasts three bedrooms including the spacious master suite which incorporates a large walk in wardrobe, en suite and adjoining jacuzzi room. There are seven bathrooms throughout the house in total. All bathrooms are impressively finished with Porcelanosa tile finishes with glazed shower enclosures. All bedrooms feature Poliform Italian designed fitted wardrobes.\r\n\r\nThe lower ground floor includes two en-suite spacious guest rooms, a Cinema Room / Yoga Studio and Gym Room. Both guest bedrooms open to private patios and have own door access\r\n\r\nEach floor has staircase access that leads to the Pool House with a 25 foot heated pool which is air conditioned. The Pool House also incorporates a bathroom, sauna, integrated steam room and double shower area.\r\n\r\nNo expense has been spared in the construction and fit out of this spectacular home and this a rare opportunity to secure one of the finest homes to come to the market in Dunmore East and indeed in Waterford and the South East in recent years.
