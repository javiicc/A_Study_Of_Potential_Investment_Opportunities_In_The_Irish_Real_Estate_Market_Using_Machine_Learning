    RE/MAX Professional Partners present to market a 4 bedroom detached house with 2 bedroom apartment located at Koshy, Cloonacauneen, Tuam Road, Galway City - an enviable location between Claregalway and Galway City. \n\nThe property is presented in good condition throughout and extends to c. 186 sq. m. overall. \n\nCertified with a D1 BER Certificate, the property is heated via oil fired central heating with 2 boilers, one for the main house and the other for the apartment. There is a solid fuel fireplace in the living room and a Rangemaster in the kitchen. Double glazed windows are fitted throughout. \n\nExternally the property sits on a generous c. 0.56 acre site with spacious lawn to the rear and mature trees and shrubs. The patio area has extensive decking with many areas to sit and catch the sun. There is gated side access both sides of the property and a tarmac driveway with gated access to the front with ample private parking. \n\nCloonacauneen is a beautiful and quiet area located off the Galway-Claregalway road and found within close proximity to Galway city as well as village of Claregalway. Cloonacauneen Castle is just a short walk away and McHugh's award winning traditional pub is just at the top of the road. Also the world famous Galway Race Track is one minutes drive.\n\nThe area is conveniently located, and although surrounded by lush country side it is just 4 miles to Eyre Square and the centre of Galway city by either the N83 or N84. It is a further short distance to Salthill and the beautiful Galway Bay.
