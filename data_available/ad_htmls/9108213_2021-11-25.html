    Development Description

      * Final 2 Bedroom Apartment available to View by Private Appointment - Ideal for First Buyer, Investor or Homeowner *\r\n\r\nStunning one & two bedroom apartments with spectacular views towards Bray Head and across the Irish Sea. The apartments, which are bright and spacious, are characterised by their open plan living areas and unique features include a terrace area or winter garden (sun room), combining the beauty of outdoor living with indoor comforts . The two bed apartments range in size from approx. 81 sqm - 874 sqft to 128.8 sqm- 1,386 sqft, excluding terrace / winter garden area with completions from Autumn 2019. The apartments will appeal to a wide range of purchasers from the young to the older generation who are looking to downsize to a modern, energy efficient luxurious apartment, with marina living on their doorstop and a short stroll away from Greystones main street.\r\n \r\nTo receive further information please register your interest at www.marinavillage.ie.\r\n\r\nGreystones was originally a small fishing village which has grown significantly over the past number of years but it has not lost its friendly village atmosphere.
