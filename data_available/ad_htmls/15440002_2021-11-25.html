    In the small and private develoment of An tSean Scoil, we bring to the market a most beautiful home. One of only 7 houses, this end terrace house is in superb condition and is turn key. There is a kitchen and dining room which opens into a sitting room, a utility room, downstairs W.C. and upstairs there is a family bedroom and three bedrooms (one of which is ensuite and has a balcony). The house is warm and inviting with lots of natural light thanks to its orientation and position. There is a very generous corner garden which is beautifully landscaped and tiered over two levels which captures the sunlight throughout the day. The garden is also totally private and very easy to maintain. The house is close to all the amenities of the town and Aldi and Lidl supermarkets and is a very short stroll from the centre of Clifden town. This is a house that is sure to suit many a different house hunter; be it a family home, a holiday home or for long or short term rentals!\n\nBER: C3.
