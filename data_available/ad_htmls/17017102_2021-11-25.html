    Get Property Estate Agents are delighted to bring this excellent 2-bedroom mid-terrace house to the market. Located in the heart of Glasthule, a short walk to the wonderful bustling village and minutes from the forty foot bathing area and Killiney Hill. This property also provides ease of access to the larger town of Dun Laoghaire, and the many amenities it has to offer. \r\n\r\nThe house has recently been renovated to a very high standard with a previous rear extension. It has the bonus of approved planning permission for a two-storey extension.\r\n\r\nBuilt in 1899, this redbrick mid-terrace home has been modernised and extended to 63.2sqm. It has lots of character with very high ceilings, a bright new kitchen with all mod cons, an open plan living space with lots of storage, and a new bathroom. The property is recessed back from the bustle of Eden Road Lower and has a community park running the length of the terrace. The house has an entrance hallway that leads into the bright open plan living area. The new kitchen is a soft grey, with a dark grey counter, and white splashback tiles over the gas hob. The appliances are integrated and there is plenty of useful storage. Moving into the living room there is an open fire, that would accommodate a wood burning stove, and more useful understairs storage. At the rear, is the TV area, that is light-filled thanks to the large ceiling windows and the south-facing aspect. Adjacent is the bathroom with a toilet, whb, a bath, and a heater towel rail, with wall and under-sink storage. There are mosaic tiles on the floor and wall tiles alongside the bath, with an extendable shower door. Outside, is the south-facing patio area with extra storage.\r\n\r\nUpstairs there are two double bedrooms, both with plenty of space for storage, and the second bedroom has a Stira folding staircase up to the attic \xe2\x82\xac" this has been insulated and provides extra storage.\r\n\r\nThe house has GFCH, with a high efficiency boiler, and the windows have been upgraded to new double glazed wooden sash units in keeping with the original sash windows. There is on-street parking at the front of the house. Planning permission has been granted for a two-story extension which will extend the house from its current 63.2sqm to 72.6sqm: it offers the option of 2 larger bedrooms upstairs, with a master bathroom and an en-suite bathroom, while also extending the living space downstairs.\r\n\r\nAccommodation: Kitchen/dining/living room: 10.25m x 4.35m Wooden laminate flooring, new double-glazed sash windows, open fire, ample storage, south facing sky-light windows.\r\nBathroom: 2.75m x 1.2m. Mosaic tiled flooring bath, toilet, whb, heated towel rail, storage.\r\nBedroom 1: 3.9 x 2.7m. Wooden laminate flooring, new double-glazed sash windows \r\nBedroom 2: 3.4m x 2.9m Wooden laminate flooring, new double-glazed windows. Stira access to attic.\r\n\r\nThere are excellent public transport links with Sandycove & Glasthule Dart station a 5-minute walk away, while the commuter and Rosslare Europort train service stops at Dun Laoghaire. There are bus routes servicing the city and surrounding areas on the Glenageary Road and Glasthule Road. It is a 10-minute drive to the N11, and a 15-minute drive to the M50. It is just over 10km to St Stephen\xe2\x82\xacTM Green.\r\n\r\nPrimary schools nearby include The Harold School located across the road, Glenageary Killiney NS, Castle Park, Loreto Dalkey, Monkstown Educate Together, Dalkey School Project, Dominican Primary School and St Joseph\xe2\x82\xacTM Primary school, while secondary schools include Castle Park, CBC Monkstown, Loreto Dalkey, Loreto Foxrock, St Joseph of Cluny, and Holy Child Killiney.\r\n\r\nClose sports & social clubs include Cuala & Cabinteely GAA clubs, St Joesph\xe2\x82\xacTM, Dalkey United & Granada football clubs, Glenageary & Sandycove Tennis clubs, and Killiney golf club, as well as multiple water sport associations. \r\n\r\nThis property is in show-house condition and viewing is highly recommended.
