    *** To bid on this property please register on www.mysherryfitz.ie ***\nSherry FitzGerald Brady O Flaherty are delighted to present 34, Ryebridge Green to the market. Just over 4 years old, this spacious, well-appointed 3 bedroom semi-detached home, extends to approx. 1265 sq. Ft/ 118 sq. m, plus an additional multi-functional approx. 300 sq. ft / 28 sq. m attic room. The entire accommodation features contemporary design and a well thought-out layout ideal for modern family living. The current owners have put a lot of thought and care into the d cor of this home, and it is immaculately well presented throughout.\n\nEnjoying a lovely southerly aspect with private sun drenched rear garden. An array of features combine to ensure that this A rated house has lower energy usage and higher levels of comfort, including exceptionally high levels of insulation as well as PV Solar panels. \n\nTo rear is a fine walled in rear garden, not overlooked which is partly laid in lawn with a separate paved patio terrace which is accessed through double doors from the kitchen/dining area, ideal for Al-fresco dining. A timber shed provides convenient storage space. To front is a paved driveway to fit two cars.\n\nAt ground floor level, the accommodation briefly comprises of entrance hall with under-stair storage, guest wc, open-plan kitchen/dining room, utility room, and large beautifully appointed living room. Upstairs are three fine sized bedrooms (1 ensuite), all with fitted wardrobes, with a family bathroom also. While on the second floor you will find the multi-functional attic room ideal for a multiplicity of uses. \n\nRyebridge has become one of the most popular residential developments in Kilcock, now boasting a lovely riverside pedestrian walkaway linking the development to the town centre. Set on the western side of Kilcock, just off the Summerhill Road and easily accessible to the M4. Dublin City Centre is within comfortable commuting distance while the vibrant university town of Maynooth is less than 10 minutes drive. Kilcock Rail Station, Royal Canal Greenway, Schools, Shops etc are all within easy walking distance. This is a wonderful opportunity to acquire a fantastic turn-key, energy efficient family home, in this most sought-after location.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 6.99m x 3.10m.
