    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    We are delighted to offer for sale a superb mixed use investment opportunity located in one of Dublin s most up and coming areas. Kelly s Newsagents is a landmark building located on the approach to Chapelizod Village coming from the city centre. No.20 is a handsome red brick residential 5 x bedroom house and corner shop. There is an enclosed yard and a roof top terrace with panoramic views of Chapelizod. The corner shop (c.84 sq.mtrs.) also has access to a rear yard and separate store. \n\nThe residential house has retained some of its original period features and the current owners recently renovated and decorated the property to a very good standard and it is ready for immediate occupation. The property enjoys a prominent position along Chapelizod Main Street in this well-established location providing excellent access to the City Centre (7km from O Connell Bridge). The house also benefits from having a large roof top terrace with panoramic views across Chapelizod, St.\nLaurence s Church and the Phoenix Park. This is a wonderful outdoor space that could be utilized for a number of uses. The retail shop itself has a good floor area, store room, cloakroom with wc and whb, kitchen area and pedestrian access to the rear yard and separate store/stock room.\n\nChapelizod main street is a busy thoroughfare and the property is ideally located on the corner of the Main Street and Park Lane with direct pedestrian access to the Phoenix Park ensuring guaranteed foot fall and passing traffic coming from the Phoenix Park, Castleknock, Ballyfermot and Lucan. Chapelizod benefits from its proximity to both the City Centre and the Phoenix Park ensuring plenty of daily and weekend visitors. It has is a vibrant village setting and local neighbours include The Twirly Gate Coffee Shop, Baa Baa Caf , The Villager Public house and the Mullingar Public house as well as a local Spar and Chemist. Chapelizod is also serviced by numerous Dublin Bus Routes and has easy access back onto the M50 via the N4.\n\nSuitable for investors, owner occupier or developers, this property presents an outstanding opportunity in the heart of Chapelizod.\nInvestment Highlights include: \n- Excellent mixed-use investment / development opportunity. \n- Benefit of income from the retail unit and residential property\n\n- The property will be sold with full vacant possession\n- 5 bedroom residential accommodation allowing for additional income stream or private residence\n- Development opportunity to the rear subject to planning permission \n- Retail unit suitable for a range of potential uses\n\nFor more information or to arrange a viewing please contact Julian Cotter, Sherry FitzGerald.\n\n\nShop 12.8m x 5.87m.
