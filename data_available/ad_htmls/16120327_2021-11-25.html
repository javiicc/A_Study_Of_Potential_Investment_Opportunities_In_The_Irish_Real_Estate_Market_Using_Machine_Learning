    Handsome, well proportioned 3 storey Georgian residence circa 200 sq metres perfectly located in one of Waterford's most sought- after residential areas. Dating from c.1820, this beautiful home is situated approximately 100 metres from the city park and within a short walk from many excellent schools, both public and private, to include Newtown, Christchurch, St.Declans, St John of God, De la Salle and the Ursuline. St Annes Tennis Club is also within walking distance. Large garden to rear laid out in lawn, which benefits from a sunny south facing aspect and tremendous privacy . The property is in excellent condition throughout and boasts original features to include cut-limestone steps to front with wrought iron railings, timber panelled front door with spoked fanlight; (Internal) staircase, fireplace,Victorian 4 panel internal doors, window shutters and coving. The property enjoys superb natural light and high ceilings throughout . The heart of Waterford city with vast choice of restaurants, bars and shops is within a 5 minute walk. Additionally University Hospital Waterford and Whitfield Clinic are within a ten minute drive.\r\nWindows -PVC double glazed . Oil fired central heating. Deck to rear.
