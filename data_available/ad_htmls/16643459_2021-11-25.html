    Sherry FitzGerald proudly brings to the market No. 34 Ballincurrig Park which is a well presented, mature four-bedroom semi-detached family home located just off the Douglas Road.\n\nThis property offers a great balance of living and bedroom accommodation throughout and is an excellent opportunity to acquire a family home in a highly sought-after area. The property is presented in very good condition throughout and gives the prospective purchaser free reign to come in and put their own personal stamp on it. \n\nNo. 34 is situated on a lovely site with off-street parking and a low maintenance gravel area to the front. To the rear is a fully enclosed, private garden with a mature lawn and boundary that will be a joy to anyone with green fingers to make their own.\n\nThe location gets no better than this, enjoying close proximity to the city centre and Douglas Village, as well as local schools (both primary and secondary), many bus routes, shops, banks, etc. are all on the doorstep.\n\nThis property will appeal to many home buyers given its layout, condition and location. It is a fantastic opportunity that should not be missed and must be viewed to fully appreciate what this home has to offer.\n\nDo not delay arrange your viewing today!\nEntrance Hall 3.69m x 2.58m. The bright and welcoming entrance hall enters from a porch and gives access to the downstairs living rooms and kitchen area. It has a solid wood oak flooring.\n\nLiving Room 3.69m x 4.07m. The spacious living room overlooks the front garden and driveway and is awash with natural light. The room benefits from an open fireplace.\n\nFamily Room 3.62m x 4.07m. Located off the entrance hall is another bright spacious reception room with a window overlooking the rear garden. This room benefits from an open fire.\n\nKitchen/ Dining Area 3.68m x 3.63m. Flowing in from the entrance hall, this bright and spacious area overlooks and provides access to the rear garden. It consists of floor level kitchen units and has vinyl flooring.\n\nLanding 0.91m x 3.71m. Bright spacious landing area which gives access to all four bedrooms and the main bathroom. Also provides access to the attic.\n\nBedroom 1 3.63m x 4.04m. A large double bedroom to the front of the home which overlooks the front garden. It benefits from a wall of built-in wardrobes.\n\nBedroom 2 3.63m x 2.96m. A large double bedroom to the rear of the home with a window overlooking the rear garden. The bedroom consists of built-in wardrobes.\n\nBedroom 3 2.69m x 2.96m. Single bedroom to the rear of the home which overlooks the rear garden.\n\nBedroom 4 3.63m x 2.60m. Single bedroom/office to the front of the home which overlooks the front garden and benefits from a built-in storage area.\n\nMain Bathroom 2.66m x 1.72m. Comprising of a three-piece suite with an electric shower over the bath. The bathroom walls around the bath are fully tiled and the floor area is covered in vinyl. Benefits from a window to the back of the house for natural ventilation.\n\nGarage 4.75m x 2.94m. Access from both the front driveway and the back of house is a good sized garage.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nA 10-minute walk from Douglas Village towards the city centre. Pass the Briar Rose Restaurant on the right-hand side, continue straight and take the next turn on the left into Ballincurrig Park. Immediately turn left, No. 34 is located on the left-hand side.
