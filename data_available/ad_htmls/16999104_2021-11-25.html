    Quillsen is delighted to bring this inviting and deceptively spacious 3 bedroom period home to the market. Enviably situated adjacent to all that both Donnybrook and Ballsbridge have to offer, and within strolling distance of St Stephen's Green and the city centre, No 18 also boasts amongst its many appealing features, a quiet Cul de Sac setting, a sunny southerly orientation and vehicular laneway access to the rear.\r\n\r\nThis is a character filled home that needs updating and has some lovely original period features intact. It offers the discerning purchaser an attractive blank canvass and an excellent opportunity to blend the contemporary design qualities of today with the classic elegance of a bygone era, to create a family home of distinction. \r\n\r\nExtending to approximately 109 sqm / 1,170 sqft, the accommodation comprises, on the ground floor, an entrance hall, living room, kitchen, conservatory and utility /cloakroom. Upstairs there are 3 bedrooms and a family bathroom. \r\n\r\nSituated just off Morehampton Road in the heart of Donnybrook, the convenience of the location speaks for itself. It has quite literally everything on its doorstep including the beautiful expanses of Herbert Park and the abundance of bijoux shops, trendy bars and restaurants in Donnybrook and Ballsbridge. Baggot Street, St Stephen's Green, Grafton Street, the National Concert Hall, Trinity College and the Royal College of Surgeons are all within strolling distance. UCD Belfield is just out the road and other nearby amenities include the Fitzwilliam Lawn Tennis Club, the Aviva stadium, the RDS and Elm Park Golf Club.\r\n\r\nTo complete the picture and further underscore the convenience of the location, the Docklands together with the Bord Gais Theatre and the European headquarters of Google and Facebook is within walking distance. The hub of Dublin's financial and banking industry at the IFSC, together with the National College of Ireland, the National Convention Centre and the 3 Arena are also all within easy reach.\r\n\r\nAccommodation\r\nGround Floor\r\n\r\nEntrance Hallway - 8.32m (max) x 1.63m\r\nBeautiful black and white tiled floor.
