    "Alma House," Park, is an exceptionally exquisite Georgian home, dating back to the 19th Century, with later additions and extensions. The house represents an integral component of the mid nineteenth-century domestic built heritage on the outskirts of Wexford and retains a wealth of classic Georgian features including cornicing throughout, ceiling roses and a variation on the so-called "Wexford Window" sash-and- overlight glazing pattern. The estate sits in a stunning rural position comprising of c. 8.5 acres approx / 3km from Wexford town centre with views of the River Slaney. Purchased in 1998 by the current owners, the house has been lovingly maintained to preserve a distinguished and elegant property, whilst creating a functional space ideal for family life. The house is approached up a long tree lined drive giving glimpses of the striking southerly facade of the house, the drive sweeps round to a graveled parking area to the front of the house. Upon entering the property, an entrance hall leads into a stair hall with a staircase that climbs to the second storey of the dwelling. Not only is this a lovely welcoming space, but it highlights the Georgian elegance that is seen throughout much of the property.\r\n\r\nThe accommodation is arranged over three floors, offering well-appointed and elegant reception rooms with both the drawing room and library overlooking its' mature gardens towards the pond and beyond. The dining room, with its high ceilings, ornate fireplace and crystal chandelier makes for the perfect setting for entertaining. The kitchen/ breakfast room is to the rear of the property with back-room area comprising the utility room, boot room and cloakroom. A stunning olde worlde staircase ascends from the reception hall to the generous first floor landing leading to the principal bedrooms which benefit from adjoining bathrooms & dressing rooms. In addition to these two bedrooms there are three further bedrooms all with en suite, & a number of storerooms over the second floor. A modern extension to the dwelling provides the perfect leisure area and includes a swimming pool, Jacuzzi, sauna, gym and changing room.\r\n\r\nThe Sale of this Property is a " One in a lifetime" Opportunity to Acquire one of the most Premium and Elite Properties that could ever be offered on the open market.\r\n\r\nLocation\r\nThe property is located within walking distance to Wexford town\r\ncentre.
