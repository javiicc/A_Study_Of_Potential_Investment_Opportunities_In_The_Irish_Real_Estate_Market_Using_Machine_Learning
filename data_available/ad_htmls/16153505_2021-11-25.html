    Ardrahan is a substantial home of some 186 sqm standing on a rectangular fenced site of 0.74 acres. Built by the current owners in 1999 this property has been a very happy place for many years.\nThe house is usually accessed through the patio doors to the large kitchen/dining area which is the centre of this lovely home. This is a large bright room with countryside views to the rear. The kitchen area offers great storage and worktop areas, and if cooking is your thing a Rangemaster (with gas rings and electric plates) will suit you down to the ground. The entire room has been tiled so the kitchen and dining area flow naturally into each other with glazed double doors leading out into the hallway. The plumbed utility section off the entrance hall offers more storage and access to the rear of the property. The front-facing living room with stove is the perfect room for cosing up on a cold evening. There are two bedrooms on the ground floor with the main bedroom having an ensuite. Upstairs are two large double bedrooms one with a large room-off - perfect for storage or a home office - and large family bathroom.\nLiving here you are in close proximity to thriving villages such Rathangan, Baldwinstown, Bridgetown, Kilmore Quay and Wellingtonbridge offering amenities such as pubs, restaurants, schools (National and Secondary), shops, supermarket - everything you could need. Add to that all the beautiful beaches and scenery south County Wexford has to offer and this really is a fantastic place from which to enjoy life.\nIn short, Ardrahan is a large comfortable home with lots of natural light standing on a site that is level and fenced offering different areas that can be opened up/closed off as required.\nSW facing to the front, well water, OFCH, Bio septic tank, detached garage (also wired for generator), cavity block construction with bonded bead insulation, alarmed, broadband.
