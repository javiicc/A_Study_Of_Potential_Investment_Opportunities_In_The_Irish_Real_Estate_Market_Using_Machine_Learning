    *** 3 Bedroom Semi-Detached Home with Attic Conversion***\n\nREA Forkin are proud to present 80 Herbert Park to open market For Sale. Offering circa 82 sq. m of living space and a further 21 sq. m of converted attic space, this home is sure suit a variety of purchasers. Presented in good condition throughout but in need of some modernisation and upgrading. \n\nAccommodation briefly comprises; entrance hall, lounge with open fire, fitted kitchen with door to side passage, landing with hot-press, two double bedrooms, one single room. The attic has been converted with full stairs access.\n\nTo the front there is parking for 2 cars and a good sized side passage way. To the rear is a back patio and lawn area with ample room to extend subject to planning permission.\n\nHerbert Park is a mature residential development located on the Herbert Road in Bray. The property has a wide range of services and amenities within walking distance such as primary and secondary schools, shops, restaurants, sports and leisure clubs, Dublin Bus routes and Bray Dart Station. Access to the M11 motorway is just a few minutes drive away.\n\nViewing highly recommended!\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nTravelling south from Dublin take the exit off the M11 at Kilbride Church on the Herbert Road, just past Deignans Garage on the left. Proceed for approximately 650 metres and turn on to Kilbride Lane in the right. Take the immediate left turn in the Herbert Park and continue straight. Take the third turn on the right and then the second left. No 80 is located on the right hand side at the front of the cul de sac.
