    HWP The Property Specialists are delighted to present the most elegant detached double fronted residence situated in the prestigious Abington development, convenient to Malahide Village centre and every conceivable amenity including Malahide Castle and the train/Dart station. Dublin Airport is also within a short commute.\r\n\r\nNO.32, Oozing style and character, this fine residence enjoys gracious, well-proportioned accommodation and the entire has been tastefully decorated and meticulously maintained throughout. This stunning home needs to be seen to be appreciated.\r\n\r\nThe accommodation measures circa, 5,000 sq.ft over three levels. At ground floor level guests are greeted by an impressive reception hall with hardwood staircase and stone flooring throughout. Double doors lead to a formal drawing room with feature fireplace and there is a large conservatory to the side, suitable for multiple use. To the front there is a sun filled sitting room with feature fireplace and access to a truly magnificent family room with an abundance of natural light and three sets of French doors providing access to a superb patio and side garden. A stunning fitted kitchen stocked with high end appliances is perfectly located to cater for family needs and there is a spacious utility room off. A formal dining room completes the ground floor level. On the top floor, accessed by a spiral staircase there are four excellent bedrooms with en-suites and a study. A superb Master Bedroom suite with walk in wardrobe is sure to impress the most discerning of buyers.The garden level has an additional bedroom, guest w.c and office suite (which can be used as a sixth bedroom). Adjoining one bed apartment suitable for the au-pair, or nanny or older family member with en suite. There is Underfloor heating throughout.\r\n\r\n\r\nApproached by electronic security gates the gardens are an outstanding feature and extend to 0.6 acre (approx.). There is ample parking for multiple vehicles to the front as well as a large double garage to the side with accommodation overhead. To the rear there are extensive gardens well stocked with colorful shrubbery, here you will also find an enclosed childrens play area and on the south westerly aspect of the property there is a truly magnificent patio barbecue area which compliments the large Family Room. The professionally landscaped the gardens enjoy wonderful privacy and are bounded by mature trees and hedgerow. \r\n\r\nThis really is an outstanding opportunity to acquire a magnificent family residence in Malahides premier addresses.\r\n\r\n\r\n*** VIEWING IS STRICTLY BY APPOINTMENT ONLY***
