    Hyde Auctioneers are delighted to bring to the market this well maintained end townhouse, large duplex 3 Bedroom Apartment property located in the heart of the picturesque village of Carrigtwohill. This highly desirable address in East Cork is within walking distance to the village of Carrigtwohill and only 15/20 minute drive to Cork City Centre, with only a ten minute walk to the local Train Station and less to the local Bus Stop. \r\n\r\nOffering spacious accommodation which includes, entrance hallway, Sittingroom with ebony timber flooring, a bright Kitchen / Diningroom area, with spectacular views over the local hills and countryside, and decorated with Italian tiles on the Kitchen wall and Floor. There are three good size bedrooms upstairs with the Master Bedroom En-suite. A real feature of the property is the rear south facing balcony with timber decking The property is set in a quite cul-de- sac with own parking. \r\n\r\nViewing is highly recommended. Brendan Hyde
