    Gallagher Quigley ask you to ignite your imagination and picture yourself living within the comfortable and character-filled surroundings of this most charming edwardian era family home. No.1A Lomond Avenue, Fairview is a handsome red-brick detached residence with an attractive bay window facade and neat south-facing rear garden.\n\nA bright and inviting entrance hall welcomes you on arrival and leads to interconnecting reception rooms. Period features include original fireplaces and high corniced ceilings in both reception rooms. Bi-folding doors allow for each room to enjoy private seclusion or opened up as one single connected living space. A traditional galley style kitchen is situated to the rear and set off a bright and airy dining room where french doors open out to the garden. Upstairs, there are three generous bedrooms altogether along with a family bathroom. The front facing master bedroom is particularly generous and spans across the full width of the house. A clean and crisp white tiled family bathroom features a modern suite and is situated on the first floor return of the house. \n\nOutside, the railed front garden is attractively presented and in keeping with the charming red brick streetscape of Lomond Avenue.The neat and well-tended rear garden is a real sun-trap thanks to its south facing orientation. Side access to the garden is also available. \n\nResidents of Fairview enjoy a wealth of amenities on their doorstep. There are numerous caf\xc3\xa9s, restaurants and bars in the neighbourhood alongside a selection of supermarkets and other retail shopping. Active outdoor enthusiasts can enjoy leafy Fairview Park and Clontarf's seafront promenade within minutes. In addition, excellent transport links include numerous bus routes in and out of the city, Clontarf Road Dart Station, Connolly Station & LUAS Stop.
