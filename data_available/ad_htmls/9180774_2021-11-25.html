      Location

      Carrigtwohill is ideally located being a 14-minute drive from Cork City Centre, less than an 8 minute drive to Midleton and Cork Airport is a mere 20 minutes away. The development, Elmbury, is also located within a 10 minute walk of Carrigtwohill Railway Station which gives residents an exceedingly easy commute to Cork City, Midleton and the picturesque seaside town of Cobh. \r\n\r\nElmbury has endless amenities on its doorstep and Carrigtwohill is really the gateway to East Cork, offering an endless choice of stunning beaches, woodlands and great facilities for fishing, water sports and much, much more.
