    Property Partners Earley present to market a 3 bedroom bungalow located in Pollranny, Castleplunkett, Castlerea, Co. Roscommon. The bungalow comes to market with Approx. 3.13 acres of agricultural land.\r\n\r\nThe bungalow extends to Approx. 821 sq. ft. and is presented in good condition throughout. The living accommodation is comprised of a porch, entrance hall, living room, kitchen, dining room, 3 bedrooms and main bathroom.\r\n\r\nThe property is heated via a Stanley solid fuel range in the kitchen with back boiler and a solid fuel feature fireplace in the living room. Double glazed windows are fitted throughout.\r\n\r\nExternally the bungalow sits on a generous sized site with manicured lawns, mature trees and shrubs and a large driveway leading to the rear where there is ample parking space and storage sheds. The site offers the opportunity to extend the property if required and subject to planning permission.\t\r\n\r\nLocated 3 minutes from Castleplunkett, 8 minutes from Tulsk, 10 minutes from Castlerea and 25 minutes from Roscommon Town. The property is within easy reach of a host of amenities the areas have to offer including schools, shops, supermarkets, post office, banks, various sports facilities, pubs, restaurants and public transport links.\r\n\r\nPorch: 1.8m x 1.18m\r\nEntrance Hall: 1.13m x 4.25m\r\nInner Hall: 0.96m x 4.74m\r\nLiving Room: 3.76m x 4.14m\r\nKitchen: 1.73m x 2.62m\r\nDining Room: 3.16m x 3.21m\r\nBathroom: 1.69m x 1.92m\r\nBedroom 1: 3.16m x 4.15m\r\nBedroom 2: 3.19m x 2.69m\r\nBedroom 3: 3.21m x 3.02m\r\n\r\nTotal Area: Approx. 821 sq. ft
