    A unique traditional style cottage that oozes charm and character. A deceptively spacious and interesting property extending to approx. 1281 sq. ft., comprising living room, kitchen, bathroom and two ground floor bedrooms, while upstairs has two large bright storage rooms with velux and gable windows. This semi-detached cottage has been vacant for some time and requires renovation to reawaken its charm and create a stunning home.
