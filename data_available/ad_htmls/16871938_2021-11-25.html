    Moovingo are delighted to present this superb 1st floor apartment to the market. Measuring an impressive 82 sq.m. (excluding balcony space), this 3-bed apartment has been maintained well and is spacious throughout. The apartment enjoys a favourable southerly aspect which floods the apartment with natural light and is ideal for enjoying the sunshine on the large balcony. Excellently located close to a number of amenities, this property will appeal to first-time buyers and investors alike.\r\n\r\nThe accommodation comprises a long entrance hallway, spacious open plan kitchen/living area, three bedrooms, ensuite shower room and family bathroom. The aforementioned balcony can be accessed through both the living area and the master bedroom. There is lift access in the building and there are ample parking spaces available for residents, both underground and at street level.\r\n\r\nHunters Wood was built by Ellier Developments. Hunters Hall was completed in 2007. The development and the Ballycullen area is extremely popular amongst families and first time buyers. The grounds are well cared for and there are a host of useful facilities close by. The development is set just off the Ballycullen road and has direct access to the M50 and is well served by Dublin bus (15, 15b, 49, 65b).\r\n\r\nKey amenities close to the vicinity include, Lidl at Ballycullen, shopping at Dundrum Town Centre, Tymon Park, Marlay Park, Ballyboden GAA, multiple golf clubs and other leisure and sports facilities are available in abundance.
