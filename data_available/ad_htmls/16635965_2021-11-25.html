    The Property Shop are delighted to present 63 Kirwan Street Cottages to the Stoneybatter Market. Set in a quiet enclave of Artisan homes just of Manor Street, this charming cul-de-sac has long been a highly sough after location for first time buyers, downsizers and investors alike. Number 63 is within a stones throw of Stoneybatter village with it's array of independent shops, bars, cafes and restaurants and within walking distance of Smithfield, TUI Grangegorman, The Phoenix Park and of course the City Centre itself. Stoneybatter enjoys excellent transport links with Dublin Bus cross city service, Dublin Bikes and the LUAS at Grangegorman and Smithfield. The property itself comprises of; Living room, Kitchen, shower room and two bedrooms, one double and one single. There is on-street residential disc parking available to the front and a good sized north facing patio yard to the rear with original stone wall bordering the Grangegorman campus sports grounds. The property is fitted with double glazed windows and electric heating. Residents take great pride in this charming cul-de-sac and the feeling of community spirit is tangible.
