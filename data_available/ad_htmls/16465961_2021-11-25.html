    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to present 60 St Alban s Park to the market. This three-bedroom semidetached family home is situated amongst the leafy surrounds of this mature residential area, just off Sandymount Strand. This home benefits from having many of the necessary attributes a family could possibly want - off street parking to the front, a secluded front and rear garden, a garage to the side and all this within striking distance of proximity to schools, transport links and local shops. \n\nThe accommodation offers the feeling of space and brightness throughout and briefly comprises entrance hallway, living room to the front with pocket doors leading to the dining room and doors leading to the rear garden, kitchen and breakfast room with a door leading to the guest w.c. as well as a door giving access to the rear garden and storage sheds. On the first floor there are two double bedrooms, a single bedroom and a family bathroom.\n\nOutside the property doesn t disappoint, there is off-street parking to the front as well as a landscaped front garden. The rear garden and patio area measures (approx. 11.3m x 9.6m) which is mainly laid in lawn with mature trees and shrubbery.\n\nThe location of this home cannot be overstated. Superbly located close to Sandymount Strand and Sandymount village. There is off street parking as well as residents permit parking available but given the proximity of all the amenities on its doorstep a car may not be required. Grafton Street, Merrion Square, St. Vincent s Hospital, Merrion Centre, Grand Canal Dock, Aviva Stadium and the IFSC are all within easy access by foot, car or public transport. Dublin airport is a mere 20-minute drive via the Airport tunnel.\n\n\nEntrance Hall With wooden flooring, ceiling coving and ceiling rose, feature stain glass windows, under stairs storage, alarm panel and storage cupboard\n\nLiving Room With wooden flooring, bay window to the front with stain glass, ceiling coving and ceiling rose, picture rails, tv & phone points, feature fireplace with electric insert and pocket doors leading to dining room\n\nDining Room With wooden flooring, ceiling coving and ceiling rose, picture rails and glass doors opening onto rear garden\n\nPantry/Breakfast Room With wooden flooring, feature open fire and open plan to kitchen\n\nGuest W.C.
