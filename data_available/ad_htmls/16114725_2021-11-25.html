    A delightful, modern, spacious, three bedroom, two bath, mid terraced townhouse in the heart of Dublin 4. The property is accessed through an attractive private sunny patio/garden to the hall door. On the ground level, the property boasts a fully equipped kitchen and open plan living/dining room, bedroom and a utility room. The Living/dinning room is very bright and opens on to the south west facing patio/garden. Upstairs, there are two large double bedrooms, master ensuite and a family bathroom.
