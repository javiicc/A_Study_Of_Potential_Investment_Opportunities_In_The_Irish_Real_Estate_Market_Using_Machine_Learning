    Quillsen is delighted to present this peaceful and deceptively spacious top floor apartment to the market.\n\nSituated by the banks of the river Camac, Old Kilmainham Village is an established and highly regarded development that is situated within a gentle stroll of the city centre and adjacent to an abundance of local conveniences and amenities. In addition to its much sought-after location, No. 41 boasts amongst its many appealing features, a sunny south facing aspect and a secure designated parking space.\n\nNo 41 is warm, inviting and a place you would immediately call home. Extending to 42 sqm / 450 sqm approx., it briefly comprises an entrance hall, a very comfortable living room with a feature south facing box bay window and views down to Bow Bridge, a fully fitted kitchen area, a spacious bedroom with fitted wardrobes and a bathroom. To complete the picture, it takes full advantage of its top floor positioning by means of storage space in the attic. \n\nBeing within a stone\xe2\x82\xacTM throw of the city centre, the advantages of the location speak for themselves. This much sought-after development finds itself within easy reach of a host of facilities and every day amenities such as the Phoenix Park, Heuston Station, St. James Hospital and the Digital Hub. Other amenities close by include the Royal Hospital & Gardens Kilmainham, the Irish Modern Art Museum and the Guinness Hop Store to name but a few.
