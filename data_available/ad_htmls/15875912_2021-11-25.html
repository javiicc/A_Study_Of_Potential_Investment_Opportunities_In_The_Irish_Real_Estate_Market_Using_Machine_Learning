    Designed and built in 2001 Kiljames House is a stunning residence, on C. 10.91 Acres of post and railed lands. The house extends to C.529m or 5,612 Sq. Ft and boast the very finest of finishes throughout. The property is located 3 Km from the Market Town of Thomastown and is within 20 Km of Kilkenny City and 42 Km from Waterford. The Mount Juliet Estate with Michelin Star Lady Helen Restaurant, Championship Golf Course and Mount Juliet Hotel is just 8.6 Km away.\n\nThis is a home of distinction starting with its regal cut stone splayed entrance, wide tree lined and railed avenue and the stately facade of the main home.
