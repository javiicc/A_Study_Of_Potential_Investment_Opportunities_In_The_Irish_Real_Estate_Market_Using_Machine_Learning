    Offered to the market by James Murphy & Co, No 6 Old Mills, Kinsale is a most luxurious, 4 bedroom, 4 bathroom semi-detached house with its own garden in an exclusive, gated complex of just seven homes within the grounds of the converted 400 year old mill in Brownmills (on the Cork side of Kinsale and within 1.50 miles of Kinsale). \n\nOffered for sale for the first time, this home has been very tastefully and sympathetically restored to a luxury standard and has a generous floor area of 241.09 m2 (2,595 Sq ft). The courtyard areas are beautifully maintained and the original mill wheel illuminated to enhance the history and heritage of the Old Mill. \n\nThe accommodation consists of four double bedrooms, 3 of which are en-suite, an enormous family bathroom, large open plan, fully fitted kitchen/dining room with centre island unit leading to a large sunroom to the rear with direct access to the patio and garden with a footbridge to another garden area. \n\nThis house is beautifully presented and features include natural slate, hardwood flooring, marble tiling and worktops, wooden floors, zoned gas central heating, underfloor heating to sunroom, enormous American style fridge freezer, extensive shelving, panelling, storage and robes in all bedrooms, fireplaces, parking, garden, conservatory and so much more. \n\nA totally unique offering that must be viewed to be fully appreciated. \nStrictly by appointment with sole agent only.\n\nGround Floor:\n\nLarge Entrance Hall: 0.95m x 5.7m\n\nOffice/Bedroom (1): 4.7m x 2.7m En-suite: 1.4m x 3.4m\nReal open fireplace. Beautifully designed marble top storage units.\n\nHP: 0.8m x 2.0m\nStorage room: 1.2m x 1.2m\n\n\nLocation \nKinsale is one of Ireland's most picturesque, popular and fashionable towns which is situated only 17 miles from Cork city, making this an ideal location for those wishing to commute to the city. The pretty seaside town of Kinsale is a mere 25 minutes drive from the cosmopolitan city of Cork. This area of South West Ireland is of outstanding beauty with its dramatic coastline, unspoilt locations and safe beaches. Leisure activities are well catered for, Kinsale provides excellent sailing, yachting and fishing facilities from its two marinas. There are three world-class golf courses including the renowned Old Head of Kinsale. Other recreational pursuits include walking, bathing, surfing, and horse riding. There are numerous cultural events held throughout the year, such as the world famous Kinsale Arts Festival, the Cork Jazz Festival, Cork Film Festival, and Kinsale Gourmet Festival along with a thriving music and opera programme. Cork has several fine theatres, and Cork Opera House hosts many exceptional productions throughout the year. Kinsale is also home to some of Ireland's finest restaurants, contributing to its title as Gourmet Capital of Ireland; along with many top-class hotels and lots of welcoming pubs. Cork International Airport is conveniently located 20 minutes drive from Kinsale with direct links within Ireland and to Major European and UK cities. \n\n\nJames Murphy & Co Auctioneers & Valuers act as agents for the vendors or lessors of this property whose agents they are, give notice that: (i) the particulars are set out as a general outline for the guidance of intending purchasers or lessees, and do not institute, nor constitute part of, an offer or contract.
