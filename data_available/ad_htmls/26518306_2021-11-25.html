      Three-storey-over-garden level late Georgian restored house to let at the junction of Raglan Road and Elgin Road.\r\n\r\nThe accommodation comprises outer reception hall which leads to an inner hallway, two formal reception rooms with parquet floors, marble fireplaces and Waterford Crystal chandeliers with a conservatory winter-garden and a library/study.\r\n\r\nThere is a Bulthaup-kitchen and utility room with Gaggenau ovens which leads into a Hamptons-built conservatory.
