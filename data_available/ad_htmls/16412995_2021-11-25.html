    22 Carriglea Downs is a spacious three bedroom detached family home conveniently located in a quiet cul de sac within easy access of all local amenities.\r\n\r\nBuilt in the late 1980's this super detached property would make an ideal family home as it offers generous living accommodation which is further enhanced by a large conservatory. \r\n\r\nThis home is filled with natural light and takes full advantage of its south westerly rear aspect. The side entrance is wider than average and offers additional potential. \r\n\r\nCarriglea is an extremely popular neighbourhood and highly sought after by those looking for a family friendly environment which is within walking distance of shops, schools and many local amenities.\r\n\r\n \r\nAccommodation briefly comprises: Entrance hallway with under stair storage, a large living room to front with open brick fireplace, double doors lead to a lovely dining room with the separate fully fitted "County Style" hand painted kitchen just off. A large conservatory spans the width of the rear with French doors opening to a raised decked patio with views over the mature rear garden. \r\n\r\nUpstairs there are three bedrooms the master of which is en-suite. In addition the second bedroom on this level boasts a separate wc. The third bedroom is a large single with built in wardrobes and a full family bathroom complets the first floor layout.\r\n \r\nThe front garden is low maintenance, with decorative loose stones, framed by hedging and the driveway provides off street parking for two cars. The larger than average side entrance provides easy access to the rear garden.\r\n\r\nOutside to rear the garden is mainly in lawn with two mature Eucalyptus trees, a variety of plants, shrubs and a loose stone patio/ BBQ area.\r\n \r\n22 Carriglea Downs enjoys a very convenient position close to all local amenities. There is a superb selection of schools quite literally within minutes of the property, including Firhouse Educate Together National School, Gaelscoil na Giuise and Scoil Treasa to name just a few. There are numerous convenience stores almost on the doorstep but for a greater choice of shopping and entertainment both The Square Tallaght and Dundrum Town Centre are an easy drive. A wide selection of sporting facilities to suit all disciplines are within close proximity. Firhouse is an ideal location for those who enjoy outdoor pursuits such as hiking, walking and cycling etc as it is nestled in the foothills of the Dublin Mountains.\r\n\r\nThe area is well served by public transport with Dublin Bus routes stopping locally and the M50 motorway network just a few minutes away making for easy access to the city and beyond.\r\n\r\n \r\nDirections\r\nTraveling south on Firhouse Road turn left on to Ballycullen Avenue following the curve and passing Scoil Treasa take the second left on to Carriglea Avenue and the second right on to Carriglea Downs into the cul de sac No. 22 will be on your right side identified by our JLL For Sale board. \r\n\r\n Accommodation\r\n\r\nHallway\t 1.980m\t4.984m\r\nLounge to front\t 3.949m\t4.984m\r\nDining room\t 3.031m\t3.642m\r\nKitchen\t 2.890m\t3.497m\r\nConservatory\t 6.086m\t3.151m \r\nBedroom 1\t 3.523m\t3.809m\r\nBedroom 2 (incl wc) 2.789m\t3.805m\r\nBedroom 3\t 2.415m\t2.555m\r\nMaster ensuite\t 1.905m\t1.378m\r\nBathroom\t 2.096m\t1.796m
