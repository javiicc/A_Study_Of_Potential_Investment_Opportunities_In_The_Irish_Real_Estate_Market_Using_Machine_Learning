The sense of calm and tranquillity of the location is further enhanced by the stunning views from the living room and kitchen, which look out over the beautifully landscaped gardens towards the sea, and the patio area which is accessible from both bedrooms, enjoys a south facing aspect. Upgraded and lovingly maintained over the years by its long-term owner occupier, No. 9 boasts many notable features including a bespoke fitted workstation and storage, newly replaced doors, Epoxy stone flooring, modern fitted kitchen with island, and elegant sanitary ware in the bathroom.
