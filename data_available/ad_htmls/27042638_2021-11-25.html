    Description

      Award winning design by Houzz Interiors. This is a classic contemporary mid terraced new build home located in the heart of Ballsbridge situated right beside the RDS and Facebook. With the DART only a 5 min walk, surrounded by a wealth of amenities, restaurants, cafes, gym and Intercontinental Hotel right on your door step, this property is not to be missed. The accommodation comprises in brief: An eloquent interior fitted to the highest standard. Wide plank wooded floor throughout the hall kitchen Livingroom and utility. The high ceilings give a great air of grandeur. Double doors to a spacious living room with custom made tv cabinet and antique mirror. Under stairs storage and powder room, black taps, off entrance hall. Steps down to an exquisite top of the range kitchen with bronze handles, white marble worktop. Large fridge, cooker, hob, dishwasher. Door to utility separate washing machine and drier. Large feature doors open out onto a sun drenched patio, south facing garden laid out mostly in lawn. Garden feature wall with recessed alcoves and wall lights. Black stained fence. Totally private. Upstairs (top of the range velvet wool carpet) 3 double bedrooms (fitted wardrobes in each) Main ensuite Fully tiled with walk in shower. Dressing room with custom made wardrobes. Main bathroom at top of landing. Viewing is very high recommended.
