    Moovingo is delighted to present this freshly refurbished 3/4 bedroom mid-terraced period property to the market. Number 73 extends to c. 110 sq. m. (1,184 sq. ft.) inclusive of a c. 15 sq. m. attic room and is located just a stones-throw from the heart of Smithfield and Stoneybatter.\r\n\r\nThere is a private roof top terrace accessed from the attic room and the property further benefits from an approved planning permission for the construction of a 3rd storey (Ref: WEB1253/17).\r\n\r\nThe accommodation comprises an entrance hall leading to an open plan kitchen / living space to the front of the house - the kitchen has been recently fitted by Luke Hickey Kitchens. To the rear of the ground floor there is a large double bedroom with en-suite, a utility area and access to an external courtyard. \r\n\r\nAt first floor level there are two double bedrooms one with ensuite, a main bathroom and an extra living space / potential 4th bedroom. There is a stairs leading from the 2nd floor to the converted attic space which could be used as a study / office, this leads to the private roof top terrace.\r\n\r\nThe current owners refurbished the property to the highest of standards as can be noted from the excellent B3 BER rating. There is also the option to avail of 3 on street parking permits.\r\n\r\nAREA INFO \r\n\r\nSmithfield has become one of the most sought-after addresses in the heart of Dublin City in recent years as an atmospheric urban village with a fantastic selection of amenities from bars, restaurants and caf\xc3\xa9s to arts and culture destinations. The Jameson Distillery is a big attraction all year round and The Phoenix park just a 15 minute stroll away.\r\n\r\nTransport links are excellent with the Luas connecting Smithfield to the city centre and most major city destinations are a short walk away.
