    CHARMING DETACHED 3 BEDROOM BUNGALOW ON C. 0.47 ACRE\r\n\r\nBrooklands is a fine detached home approached by a gravel drive set amid c. 0.47 acre site mainly in lawn with flower beds, paved patio area, all enclosed by mature trees with a detached garage. The house has the benefit of oil fired central heating, PVC double glazed windows and PVC fascias/soffits. To the rear is a separate home office ideal for remote working or alternatively a home gym. Situated in a nice quiet rural setting only 1\xc2\xbdkm from Kilkea Castle Hotel, Golf Course and Leisure Centre on 180 acres of mature parkland. Nearby towns include Athy 10 km, Carlow 14 km and Kilcullen 24 km. For commuters the train service is available from Athy and the property is 66 km south west of the M50 via the M9 Motorway access at Junction 3 c. 8 km.\r\n\r\nACCOMMODATION:\r\nEntrance Hall: 3.58m x 1.34m with oak floor and coving\r\n\r\nSitting Room: 4.55m x 3.60m with oak floor, coving, pine fireplace with cast iron and tiled inset\r\n\r\nKitchen/Dining: 6.94m x 3.15m Rangemaster cooker, Rangemaster extractor, tiled floor, coving, built in ground and eye level presses, Creda electric oven, tiled surround, plumbed and s.s. sink unit.\r\n\r\nBedroom 1: 4.52m x 3.57m with oak floor and coving.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2: 3.63m x 3.00m with closet and coving.\r\n\r\nBedroom 3: 2.70m x 2.67m with coving.\r\n\r\nBathroom: Vanity w.h.b., w.c., bath with shower attachment, electric shower, recessed lights and fully tiled floor and walls.\r\n\r\nAMENITIES:\r\nGolf in Kilkea, Athy and Carlow. Train Station available in Athy or Carlow. Shopping in Athy or Carlow. Other sporting activities in the area include rugby, GAA, horseriding, swimming and canoeing.\r\n\r\nOUTSIDE: \r\nApproached by a gravel drive standing on c. 0.47 acre mainly in lawn with paved patio area, flower beds, all enclosed by mature trees;\r\n\r\n* Separate detached garage 4.4m x 3.5m\r\n* Home office/gym 5.75m x 3.63m\r\n* Boiler house 3.64m x 3.04m\r\n* Toilet\r\n* 2 stores and a lean-to storage area\r\n\r\nSERVICES:\r\nMains water, septic tank drainage, oil fired central heating and electricity.\r\n\r\nSOLICITOR:\r\nPoe Kiely Hogan Lanigan, 21 Patrick Street, Kilkenny.
