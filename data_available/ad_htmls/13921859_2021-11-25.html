    DNG Martin O'Connor Land Sales are delighted to offer for sale by Private Treaty a very unique holding of coastal land in various plots with some of the plots having obvious development potential for those with a local housing need. A lot of the plots enjoy excellent sea views and direct sea access. The lands with good road frontage are been offered for sale in the following lots. \r\n\r\nLOT 1- Extending to a total of c. 6. 43 Acres of elevated land laid out in various field sizes which are mainly enclosed by drystone walls. The land in good pasture with excellent sea frontage enjoys spectacular views of the surrounding coastline into Connemara. The land due to its unique seaside location is full of natural nutrients from the sea and is ideal ground for rearing Connemara Ponies. The property with good road frontage offers the potential for a site to those that can demonstrate a local housing need. \r\n\r\n\r\nAsking Price \xe2\x82\xac35,000\r\n\r\nLOT 2 - This is a road side plot of land with good frontage which extends to approximately 2.64 Acres. The land enclosed by dry stone walls has a wonderful stone built cottage ruin on the holding. There are excellent views from this property. With its good road frontage this plot has the potential for a site to those that can demonstrate a local housing need. \r\n\r\nAsking Price \xe2\x82\xac20,000\r\n\r\nLOT 3 - This is a fine plot of grazing land in one plot which extends to c. 5.31 acres. Fully enclosed by stone walls the land offers good grazing. The lands also enjoy good road frontage and has the potential for site development to those that can demonstrate a local housing need. \r\n\r\nAsking Price \xe2\x82\xac25,000\r\n\r\nLOT 4 - Consists of three individual parcels of land which amount to a total of c.16.12 acres of lands. The large of the plots is a roadside plot of 9.11 acres which extends to the coastline. The fine plot offers good grazing with the potential for the development of a dwelling house subject to a local housing need. \r\n\r\nAsking Price \xe2\x82\xac40,000\r\n\r\nPlots of this size rarely present themselves to the market thus providing an excellent opportunity to existing land owners to acquire additional land or they are ideally suited to those who wish to build in the area and acquire sizeable plots of land with wonderful views. \r\n\r\n\r\nFor further details and maps please contact the sole selling agents DNG Martin O'Connor \r\nTel 091866708\r\n\r\nFEATURES:\r\nSea side holding\r\nTo be sold in one or 4 lots\r\nSea frontage\r\nExcellent grazing potential \r\nUnique location\r\nPotential for sites (SPP)\r\nGood road frontage\r\n\r\nBER:\r\nBER: Exempt\r\n\r\nDIRECTIONS:\r\nThe lands are located in the townlands of Creelough and Knock, Lettermore.
