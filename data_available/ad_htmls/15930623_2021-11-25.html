    This is a fantastic luxury home set into the heart of the beautiful West Cork countryside and in short distance to Rossbrin Harbour. The house was built to very high standards both internally and externally offering an abundance of modern living space with a total floor area of c. 205 sqm. \r\n\r\nThe large open plan kitchen cum dining area extends into a lounge and offers an open fireplace with a Valentia slate feature surround and large window fronts to take advantage of the wonderful setting and natural light. The kitchen has fully fitted contemporary units and a breakfast counter. Double doors lead from the dining area into the spacious conservatory with direct access to the patio and the gardens. Also, on the ground floor there is a formal living room with an open fireplace and beautiful stone surround. There are four double bedrooms, one on the ground floor with an en-suite and three on the top floor. The master bedroom en-suite is extremely spacious and offers a separate walk-in wardrobe. A utility room and a guest wc are located just off the kitchen. The floors are tiled in the kitchen cum dining area, conservatory and hallway and hard wood floors in all other rooms. All bathrooms are fully tiled and fitted with quality sanitary ware. Heating is oil fired central heating with underfloor upstairs and downstairs. \r\n\r\nThe property sits on c. 0.6 acres of land which has been beautifully landscaped with a hard landscaped alfresco dining area with seating, built in firepit and a BBQ area. The remainder of the land is laid to lawn. The garden is completely enclosed with wooden fencing and the entrance is gated. There is plenty of parking and a garage.\r\n\r\nLocation\r\nThe property offers a stunning private location while being within a 5 minutes' drive to Ballydehob village, which has a variety of shops, pubs, restaurants and is renowned for its artisan and wholefood shops. It also hosts an annual Jazz Festival and a Traditional Music Festival. Rossbrin Harbour is only around the corner and in walking or cycling distance. \r\n\r\nThe house is also located within a short drive to Skibbereen and Bantry which can both be reached within c. 15-20 mins drive. Skibbereen and Bantry are both market towns and offer all amenities including shopping facilities, golf courses, restaurants, welcoming pubs, a cinema etc. \r\n\r\nThis area of South West Ireland is of outstanding beauty with its dramatic coastline, unspoilt locations and safe beaches. Leisure activities are well catered for with excellent sailing, yachting and fishing facilities as well as golf courses all close by. Other recreational pursuits include walking, bathing, surfing, and horse riding. \r\n\r\nCork International Airport is conveniently located about 1.5 hour\xe2\x82\xacTM drive with direct links to major European and UK cities and holiday destinations.\r\n\r\n\r\nDisclaimer/Privacy Policy\r\nFinest International Real Estate Ireland Ltd. t/a Engel & Voelkers act as agents for the vendors or lessors of this property whose agents they are, give notice that: \r\n1.
