      Owen Reilly is delighted to present this superb, top floor, penthouse in the heart of Dublin 4 with an amazing setting over the Grand Canal from a generous, private terrace. The luxurious interior is designed and styled to the highest of standards by Bushell Interiors. The kitchen is from the exclusive German brand Allmilmo with 40mm thick Calacatta marble worktops and fully-integrated LED lights. The bedrooms and the living space are fully fitted with world renowned floors Ebony & Co, using their trademark dark herringbone design. The bedroom, hallway & lounge were fitted out by Bushells using their exclusive Italian brand Presotto Italia.\n\nLocated on Percy Place, in an exclusive, niche development, this apartment is in a beautiful canal side setting and is just a short walk from Baggot Street, Ballsbridge and city centre. Offering an abundance of natural light, this apartment is presented in stylish decorative condition.\n\nAccommodation comprises entrance hallway, spacious open plan living/dining room which opens on to a large terrace overlooking the canal, luxury kitchen, two double bedrooms, master en-suite and a further bathroom all finished with modern fittings and stylish interior. An impressive terrace completes the accommodation. With many desirable features, and an unbeatable location, this apartment must be viewed to be appreciated.\n\nMore about the location...\nPercy Place is a tranquil location, on the south bank of the Grand Canal and adjacent to Baggot Street.
