    This distinctive residence is situated within a spectacular lake and mountain setting in the ever so popular area of Aghadoe. The house is situated on an elevated site overlooking the beautiful McGillycuddy Reeks and the Killarney lakes. The stunning setting offers privacy and tranquillity combined with the comfort of being in striking distance of Killarney town and all its amenities. Finished to a very high standard the property offers an abundance of space for the entire family, both inside and outside.\r\n\r\nThis bright and airy property has a total floor area of c. 270 sqm and offers a large kitchen cum dining room with fully fitted kitchen units and granite worktops. Double doors lead to a bright spacious conservatory offering a perfect lounge space to enjoy the beautiful gardens and countryside. The separate living room has an open fireplace with marble surround. All front rooms downstairs benefit from panoramic windows affording stunning views across the surrounding countryside, the lakes and the mountains. There are five double bedrooms, three of them en-suite and with walk-in wardrobes. Further accommodation includes a home office, a gym, two family bathrooms and a large utility room off the kitchen. Floors are tiled in the kitchen and conservatory and hard wood floors in the living room, all the bedrooms and hallways. The bathrooms are fully tiled and have quality sanitary ware.\r\n\r\nDouble doors lead from the conservatory to a gorgeous hard landscaped courtyard with terrace area. The property stands on a fully fenced site of c. 0.82 acres which is partly laid to lawn and landscaped with mature planting. There is also plenty of parking and a separate garage.\r\n\r\nLocation\r\nLocated in the ever so popular Aghadoe area, the property offers tranquillity and privacy and yet is only 10-15 minutes away from the bustling town centre of Killarney. Tralee town with all its commercial activity is only 20 minutes drive from the property. The new Fexco RDI centre & remote working Hub is a 10 minutes drive from the property.\r\n\r\nWith stunning views to the McGillycuddy Reeks and the Killarney Lakes and within close distance to the picturesque heritage town of Killarney, it is the perfect location from which to discover the South West of Ireland. The area is one of the most beautiful and unspoilt locations in the country and boasts all amenities of a modern infrastructure. Pubs, caf\xc3\xa9s, fabulous restaurants and shopping facilities are close by. The immediate vicinity is ideal for fishing, walking, hiking, biking, swimming or golf. The beaches in Rossbeigh & Inch are within a 20 minutes driving distance and there are three fabulous golf courses in Killarney, all of them within 5 minutes\xe2\x82\xacTMdrive, with a wealth of other courses easily accessible within an hours\xe2\x82\xacTMdrive including Dooks Golf Links, Waterville Golf Links, the Ring of Kerry Golf & Country Club Kenmare or the Kenmare Golf Club.\r\n\r\nThere are a number of fabulous national and secondary schools the vicinity and there are plenty of activities for all ages. \r\n\r\nOnly 15 minutes\xe2\x82\xacTMdrive from Kerry Airport and one and a half hour's drive from Cork International Airport transport links within Ireland and to most major European and UK cities are in easy reach.\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nDisclaimer/Privacy Policy\r\nFinest International Real Estate Ireland Ltd. t/a Engel & Voelkers act as agents for the vendors or lessors of this property whose agents they are, give notice that: \r\n1.
