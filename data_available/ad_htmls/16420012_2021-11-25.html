    Watch the landscape and the seasons change from this top floor (fourth floor) apartment which takes in panoramic views of Three Rock Mountain with floor to ceiling windows incorporating balcony access at each end of the property to take full advantage of the picturesque vistas. \n\nA fantastic sense of space and light prevails in this tastefully presented apartment. An excellent standard of finish is evident throughout and turning the key will be the sum of your efforts to move in. The entrance hall has beautiful hardwood floors, which continue through to the generously appointed open plan kitchen / living / dining, with a classic cream kitchen incorporating ample storage, integrated appliances and polished granite worktops. The tall ceilings add an extra sense of space to the living / dining which opens to the south facing balcony with a spectacular vista. There is a bathroom and the bedroom has a large wardrobe and sliding doors to a further balcony. \n\nThe development sits in well maintained, secure grounds and the apartment comes with a secure underground parking space and storage unit. \n\nThe enviable location of Belville Court ensures that residents enjoy great ease of access to a wide variety of amenities, including excellent local shopping and plenty of eateries in the villages of Cabinteely and Foxrock, Dalkey and Dun Laoghaire town. Kilbogget Park and Cabinteely Park are close by and are a fantastic asset to residents. Other recreational facilities in the area include racing at Leopardstown, golf in Foxrock, and Leopardstown and tennis clubs at Carrickmines and Leopardstown. The area is well serviced with public transport with the Luas at Carrickmines, the N11 with its numerous bus routes giving great ease of access to the city centre and the M50, providing access to all the major national route ways.\n\n\nEntrance Hall hardwood floor, storage closet, recessed lighting\n\nLiving/Dining Room 2.68m x 3.47m. hardwood floor, recessed lighting, door to balcony, radiator cover\n\nKitchen 2.68m x 3.47m. built in wall and floor units, polished granite countertops, integrated fridge / freezer, integrated four ring gas hob and extractor fan, integrated electric oven, integrated washing machine and dishwasher\n\nBathroom 1.95m x 2.36m. tiled floor and walls, heated towel rails, w.c. with recessed cistern, bath with shower\n\nBedroom 3.80m x 3.40m. double bedroom with wall mounted shelving, sliding door to balcony with boiler closet, large clothes closet
