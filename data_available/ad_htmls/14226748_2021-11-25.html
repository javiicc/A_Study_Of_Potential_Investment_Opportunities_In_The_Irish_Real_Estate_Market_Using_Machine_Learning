This is truly a home with flexible accommodation that would suit any number of occupiers. The clever and unique design ensures that all of the rooms are easily accessible and as a result of all the living and sleeping accommodation being on the ground floor this would be the ideal purchase for a retiring couple, or someone with reduced mobility. \r\n\r\nThe kitchen and large open plan living and dining area are to the rear of the property and benefit hugely from having a South facing orientation. The large sliding doors off the Living room lead straight out to a lovely patio area that is a real sun trap. \r\n\r\nThe kitchen is fitted with the necessary ground and floor presses and still has room for a breakfast table and chairs. The utility room has ample space for a washing machine, dryer and large fridge freezer. Tiled floors in the kitchen and utility room make this a clean, stylish maintenance free area. To access the bedrooms you pass through what is one of the most interesting features of the property, A glass corridor that connects the front and rear of the house. This creates an internal courtyard that is again ideal for relaxing in privacy. The two bedrooms are to the front of the house along with the bathroom. Both bedrooms are doubles and both have built in wardrobes and timber flooring. The main shower room has been partially tiled and benefits greatly from having a walk in shower. \r\nThere is a very useful storage area at the rear of the property that is ideal for golf clubs, bikes and garden equipment. \r\n\r\nIn terms of location one could not be better positioned within striking distance of all local amenities such as a local Centra shop, Collins Pub, Dooradoyle medical center, dental surgery and Rosedale pharmacy. \r\n\r\nLocal primary and secondary schools are also close by including Crescent College, St Pauls National school and St Nessans National school. The motorway network surrounding Limerick is close by and to top it all off the Crescent shopping center is practically on your doorstep! \r\n\r\nAccommodation: Large open plan Living / Dining Area, Kitchen, Utility Room, 2 Bedrooms, Bathroom, Guest wc, Outdoor storage room. Gas central heating.
