    Property Partners Earley are delighted to present this fantastic Detached home on a large mature site. This home is the definition of turnkey and ready for immediate occupancy.\r\n\r\nIt is very rare that a home of this level comes to market in this area. Measuring in at approx 1,592 sq ft this home offers a perfect mix of indoor and outdoor space with the site itself measuring in at just under approx 0.54 acre.\r\n\r\nThis beautiful home is a 4 bedroom 3 bathroom and offers spacious and well-proportioned living accommodation comprised of an entrance hall, sitting room, living room, kitchen/dining room area, utility, W.C, Master bedroom of which benefits from an en-suite and finally the main bathroom. Also benefits from an insulated attic. \r\n\r\nThis Eco friendly house is certified with an impressive C2 BER Certificate, heated via oil fired central heating, open fire place. The open plan kitchen/dining room is both modern and spacious. Bought fireplaces in each room are handmade with marble and wrought iron. There is walnut floors in both sitting rooms.\r\n\r\nSituated in an idyllic and scenic area just outside the town of Elphin. Carrick-On-Shannon & Strokestown are a mere 15 minutes away and Roscommon can be reached within 25 minutes. Enjoying easy access to ample amenities including primary and secondary schools, preschools, shops, supermarkets, pubs, restaurants, parks, playgrounds, church, community centre, day care centre, health centre, garda station, Elphin mart, post office, award winning butchers.
