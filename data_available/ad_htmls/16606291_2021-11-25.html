    1 Stonebridge Avenue, off Maudlin Road, Kells is nearing completion and will be released and available to view over the coming weeks. Register your interest and we will notify you in advance to the house being released.\r\n\r\nA new spacious four bedroom NZEB compliant detached family home located on a private site within the town centre. The property will comprise of a living room, kitchen / sitting / dining area, guest WC, store, utility room on the ground floor. The first floor includes three double bedrooms, a single bedroom, a family bathroom and a large back garden.\r\n\r\nThe house is eligible for the home to buy scheme.\r\n\r\nThis small housing development of only two houses located off Maudlin Road is within the town centre and all its amenities yet only minutes from the M3 Motorway, only 30 minutes to Blanchardstown, 15 minutes to Navan and only 40 minutes to the M50.\r\n\r\nThis house provides accessibility to good employment, a strong community culture and a better way of living.
