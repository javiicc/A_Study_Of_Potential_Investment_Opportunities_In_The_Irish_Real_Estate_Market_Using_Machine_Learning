    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 107 m\xc2\xb2
    Corry Estates are delighted to present to the market No. 25 Ridgewood Close a bright and spacious 4 bedroom end of terrace house which benefits from an exceptionally large side and rear garden. The house has just been decorated to include painting throughout and new carpets on the stairs, landing and all 4 bedrooms. Upon entering viewers are greeted by a light filled entrance hall which leads to a spacious living room that runs the length of the house. There is a generous kitchen/dining area complete with island unit and a guest w.c completes the ground floor accommodation. On first floor level there are 4 bedrooms with master bedroom en-suite and a family bathroom. Further features include, PVC double glazed windows, gas fired radiator central heating and designated car parking space. Ideally located on a quiet cul-de-sac the house is within walking distance of, local shops, schools and commuter bus links while Swords Main Street, The Pavilions shopping centre with a host of cafes, restaurants, boutiques and shops are all within close proximity. Viewing comes highly recommended to appreciate this fine family home.
