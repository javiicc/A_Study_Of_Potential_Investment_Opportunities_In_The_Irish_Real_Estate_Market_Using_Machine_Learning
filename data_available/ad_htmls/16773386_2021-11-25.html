    DNG are delighted to present this 3 bedroom semi-detached family home to the market. No. 69 Giltspur Brook is presented in excellent condition. Recent upgrades include A rated triple glazed windows and hall door, brand new dual roller blinds, RING doorbell with security camera, insulated attic, resanded and revarnished beech hardwood flooring, travertine kitchen floor tiles and burglar alarm.The property boasts a welcoming hallway, a spacious living room to the front with a multifuel burner stove, open plan kitchen/dining room to the rear and a lovely sunroom overlooking the rear garden. Upstairs there are three well-proportioned bedrooms with an en-suite off the main bedroom and a family bathroom.The front of the property provides off street parking on a tarmac driveway with an attractive brick surround. The side entrance leads to an extremely private and cleverly designed landscaped rear garden. There is a delightful sunken cobble lock patio area edged with raised beds. This is the perfect space for entertaining al fresco during the summer months. Planted with an abundance of mature plants and shrubs which include eating and cooking apple trees, plum tree, jasmine, pyracantha, virgina creeper, Japanese maple and climbing ivy. Located on a small quiet cul de sac, this home offers a safe, peaceful and quiet ambience for those looking for an attractive home with everything to offer. Shops are within walking distance as are regular bus routes. Bray town centre is a few minutes away and there is a Park and Ride facility for the DART. The property is within walking distance of 3 major supermarkets e.g. Lidl, Aldi and Supervalu. Schools are in their abundance as are all conceivable amenities. For those looking for more, Dundrum Town Centre is located about 15 minutes away as the N11 leading to the M50 is closeby.
