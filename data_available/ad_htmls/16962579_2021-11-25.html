    131 Raheny Road is a beautiful home positioned on a sought-after mature road located close to Raheny village. Number 131 is an extended three bedroom semi-detached property which will be of strong interest to buyers looking for a refurbished family home. Internally this bright, stylish home has been well designed to suit a growing family. The accommodation comprises: entrance hall, living room, dining room, open plan kitchen/breakfast room. A understairs guest wc complete the accommodation at ground level. Upstairs there is a bright landing, two good sized double bedrooms, a single bedroom and a family bathroom. To the front of the property the driveway is laid in granite paving with parking for two cars. The private west facing rear garden is laid in lawn with a decking area. There is a useful enclosed passage to the side of the property that can be used for storage or a utility space. Situated close to Raheny village with its choice of shops, supermarket, restaurants and bars. The abundance of amenities in the locality is second to none with a wide range of leisure facilities and Saint Anne s Park. There are excellent primary and secondary schools in close proximity. There are a number of public transport links with an excellent bus service and Raheny DART Station providing ease of access to the City Centre and beyond. The property is a ten-minute drive to the M50 and M1 and fifteen-minute drive to Dublin Airport.
