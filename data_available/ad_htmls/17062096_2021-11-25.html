    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Manor Properties are delighted to present this gorgeous semi detached bungalow to the Sales market.\n\nA deceptively spacious three bedroom two reception room property which internally is in excellent condition and has been well looked after.\n\nThe Bungalow comprises of the entrance hall, lounge with an open fire, newly renovated bathroom fully tiled fitted with bath and double shower, two double and one single bedrooms, kitchen/diner and a south facing conservatory.\n\nThe gardens are very well maintained with a concrete built and wooden shed and a concrete drive to the front for at least two cars. \n\nThe beautiful Copper Coast Geopark is a designated area comprising a stretch of the southern coast of Ireland in County Waterford, extending for some 25 km of coastline from Kilfarrasy in the east to Stradbally, all of this only a 5 min drive from the Village of Kill.\n\n\nKill Village is also located close to Tramore and Kilmeaden and is a 15 minute drive to access the M9 and N25 motorways. Waterford city, Waterford Institute of Techology, UPMC Whitfield, University Hospital Waterford, The Greenway are all located within a 20 minute drive. \n\nNote: These particulars are given on the understanding that they will not be construed as part of a contract, conveyance or lease, and that all negotiations take place through Manor Properties.
