    Balinascorney House on 65 acres of parkland and woodland. A tastefully refurbished and extended period house set amidst idyllic surrounds.This desirable property offers a discerning purchaser a truly premier private family home with the driveway setting an immediate peaceful tone as it extends along the well maintained lawn\r\n\r\nThe property, originally known as Dillon Lodge, was built as a shooting lodge for the Dillons of Belgard. The house is of exceptional historical importance as Robert Emmet and a band of his followers stayed here while on the run following the rebellion of 1803. The property was built around 1800 as a five-bay house, with single-bay additions made to each end of the house around 1840. \r\n\r\nThe extensive renovation and restoration works embraces the best of the original structure while enhancing its striking contemporary extension. The new and the old expertly designed to create what can only be described as a magical home suitable for modern living. The property extends to over some 700 sq.mtrs. In all, the accommodation between the original house and the new house includes, 7 bedrooms, 11 bathrooms, formal dining room, formal living room, two kitchens, family room, snooker room, art room, Indoor pool, gym / reception area, and lift. \r\n\r\nThe floor to ceiling windows used throughout the property guarantee ample natural light, and the well planned layout ensures all principal living spaces and bedrooms enjoy the panoramic woodland views. \r\n\r\nThere are large sheds adjacent to the property, which have also been refurbished, restored and insulated, thus allowing an opportunity to utilize them for a multitude of uses such as large stables or offices.\r\n\r\nThe gardens & vast estate surrounding the property are truly magnificent and include a lengthy drive way, picturesque bridge & stream. One is certain to be fortunate enough to view the abundance of wildlife and birds in their natural habitat on the picturesque drive down towards the property. This property is truly enchanting in its unique build and design. \r\n\r\nWhilst the property is located in the serenity of the Dublin Mountains, there is a host of local amenities close by, including the local villages of Terenure, Templeogue, Rathfarnham & Tallaght, Tallaght Hospital, the Institute of Technology, The Square Shopping Centre, numerous restaurants, pubs, local shops, restaurants, churches and sporting facilities.
