    ERA Downey McCarthy Auctioneers are delighted to offer to the market this bright and spacious first floor two bedroom apartment which is situated in the most popular and mature development of Tivoli Woods, Cork. This property is in great condition throughout and is close to all amenities including The Silversprings Clayton Hotel and immediately accessible to the North Ring Road and the Jack Lynch Tunnel. The apartment would be ideally suited for a first time buyer or alternatively, it would be a great opportunity for a shrewd investor looking for a secure and lucrative opportunity.\r\nAccommodation consists of a reception hallway, guest w.c., living room, kitchen, two bedrooms and two en-suite bathrooms. A cloakroom is located off this room and a solid door allows access to the guest w.c.\r\n\r\nGuest W.C - 1.52m x 1.4m\r\nThe guest w.c has a two piece suite, tiled flooring, partly tiled walls, one centre light fitting, one wall mounted light fitting and one extractor fan.\r\n\r\nLiving Room - 4.61m x 4.65m\r\nThe main living room has extensive natural light due to the dual aspect with one large window overlooking the front of the property and another window which overlooks the side. The en-suite is fully tiled from floor to ceiling, has one frosted window overlooking the side of the property, one extractor fan, one wall-mounted light fitting and one centre light fitting.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 - 3.05m x 3.55m\r\nThis bedroom has one window overlooking the front of the property, neutral d\xc3\xa9cor, one centre light fitting, carpet flooring, a built-in storage unit, ample power points throughout, one storage heater and a door which allows access to an en-suite.\r\n\r\nEn-Suite 2 - 2.32m x 0.92m\r\nThis en-suite has a two piece suite and a built-in shower cubicle with a Gainsborough 950 electric shower.
