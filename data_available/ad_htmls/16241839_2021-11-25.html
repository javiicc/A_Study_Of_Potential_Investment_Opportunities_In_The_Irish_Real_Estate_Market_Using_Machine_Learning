    REA Halnon McKenna are delighted to present this superb one bedroom, second floor apartment overlooking the River Liffey.\r\n\r\nThe accommodation briefly comprises an open plan kitchen/dining room with plenty of cupboard space, 1 x double bedroom, a family bathroom and a private balcony overlooking the Liffey. The apartment comes with one designated parking space.\r\n\r\nNeptune House is located in the desirable development of Bellevue, located alongside the River Liffey. The landscaped communal gardens offer expansive green space ideal for picnics and relaxing on a fine day. The Phoenix Park is located within a few minutes' walk from the apartment, one of the largest walled city parks in Europe offering ideal recreational space. \r\n\r\nThe city centre is easily accessed via public transport, including the Red Line Luas which is located a short stroll away.
