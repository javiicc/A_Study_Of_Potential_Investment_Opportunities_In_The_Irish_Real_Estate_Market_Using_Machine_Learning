    Mullery O'Gara are pleased to present 11 St. Thomas Road, an attractive classic pebble dash facade terraced house with front and rear gardens. Entrance hall, living room with doors to lounge/dining room, kitchen which has been upgraded in recent years has a tiled floor, built in kitchen units, sink unit, gas hob and extractor. The utility area has plumbing for washing machine/washer dryer and storage unit. Downstairs shower room has also been upgraded in recent years with electric shower, wc, whb, tiled floor and partly tiled walls. \r\n\r\nUpstairs are 2 spacious bedrooms. Bedroom 1 to the front has fitted wardrobes, Bedroom 2 is to the rear.
