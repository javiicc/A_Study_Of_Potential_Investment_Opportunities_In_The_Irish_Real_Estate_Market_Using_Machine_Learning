    Flynn & Associates are delighted to present no.63 Boroimhe Aspen to the open market, this property is a 3 bedroomed mid terrace (with attic conversion) family home presented in walk in condition. This spacious residence extends to c. 112 sq.m / 1206 sq. ft boasting many features such as a generously sized attic conversion, beautiful low maintenance landscaped rear garden, a utility room, ample living space and double glazed windows throughout. The ground floor comprises of entrance hall, a guest WC and a large living room with a feature fireplace. The kitchen is fully fitted with integrated appliances including oven, hob & extractor fan whilst the utility room is plumbed for washing machine. Upstairs off the landing is the main family bathroom with corner bath and overhead shower, three spacious bedrooms all with built in wardrobes and a master bedroom en-suite. Off the landing is access to attic conversion. \n \nBoroimhe is an exclusive and sought after residential development located just a few minutes from the historic village of Swords and approximately nine miles from the city centre. The property is superbly located just minutes` walk to Boroimhe Shopping Centre & a short drive to Swords Village with an abundance of amenities including shops, schools, parks and sporting facilities. Frequent running bus routes just a minutes stroll from your doorstep including the Swords Express Service linking Dublin City Centre in 25 minutes door to door. A mere stone`s throw from Airside Business & Retail Park, the M50 / M1 Motorways & Dublin International Airport.
