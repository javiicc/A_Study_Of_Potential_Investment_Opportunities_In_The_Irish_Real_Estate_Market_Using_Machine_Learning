    This large modern 4 bedroom detached dormer bungalow is located just 2.8 km from the busy market town of Cahersiveen, when you enter this home you are immediately struck by the spacious nature of its lay out. The entrance Hallway measures 2.40 M across and this feeling of open space & Light is continued into the Living/Dining/Kitchen area, which have large windows on three sides of this large area, providing plenty of space for a gathering of friends and family. The Kitchen area at the rear of the house also leads out to the patio area. There are two ground floor Bedrooms, with a bathroom off the Hallway on the ground floor also. On the First Floor their is a landing space and 2 Large Bedrooms each with Walk in wardrobes and ensuite.
