    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    If you are looking for views then this is the property for you! \r\n\r\nThis partially completed dwelling house sits on an elevated site extending to 0.65 acres which commands the most spectacular views over the Atlantic Ocean and the Ox Mountains as well as the iconic Benbulben mountain, Knocknarea and Queen Maeves grave and all the way to Donegal on a fine day. The house is situated in a rural, scenic and peaceful location.\r\n\r\nThe property is constructed to a shell finish and gives the eventual purchaser the option to reconfigure the layout if needed and to make decisions on finishes and heating options. All services are on site and connected. The house extends to a generous 174.26 sq.mts and the accommodation is well proportioned. There is excellent living space that has a wonderful flow from room to room and it all maximises the amazing views to the rear and side of the site. The sunroom really makes the most of the views of the surroundings and one can imagine relaxing in there taking in this amazing scenery. \r\n\r\nAccommodation comprises Kitchen/Dining room, Living room, Sunroom, Utility room, Bathroom, 2 ensuites, 3 ground floor bedrooms and 2first floor bedrooms, one of which is the master suite which from its first floor position . \r\n\r\nThe house is fully certified to its current level of finish and everything that has been done has been completed to a very high standard. \r\n\r\nViewing is highly recommended through the sole selling agent.
