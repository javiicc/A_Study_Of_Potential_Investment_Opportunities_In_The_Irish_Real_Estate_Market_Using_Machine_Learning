    51 Old City Music Hall is a well presented 3rd floor apartment ideally located in this quiet development on Cows Lane in Temple Bar, excellently positioned in the heart of the city centre. The accommodation briefly comprises an entrance hall with storage, an open plan living /dining / kitchen, 2 double bedrooms, and a main bathroom. There is also access to a communal roof garden and communal courtyard garden. \n\nNestled along the south banks of the river Liffey, Temple Bar is one of the most iconic, historical and cultural hubs of Dublin City. The convenience of this location cannot be over stated, with public transport facilities such as Tara street dart station, the Trinity Green Line Luas stop, Jervis Red Line Luas stop and multiple bus routes all a short stroll away.
