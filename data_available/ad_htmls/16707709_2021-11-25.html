    No. 4 Clos Na Manach is a beautifully presented three bed mid terrace home in walk-in condition occupying an enviable, quiet cul-de-sac location. Ideally situated in the heart of Quin village, close to all local amenities and set against a background of the historical Quin Abbey.\r\n\r\nOn entering the main entrance hallway, the quality solid oak flooring leads to an equally stunning main reception room where a feature solid fuel fireplace provides an unexcelled ambiance of comfort and homely feel. On leaving the main reception double doors lead to a bright spacious open plan kitchen/dining room with a quality fitted Cherrywood kitchen and integrated appliances. A rear patio door from the dining area merges onto an extensive self-maintained garden. Rounding off the ground floor is the guest WC. On the first floor there are two double bedrooms (one of which features an en-suite), a single bedroom and bathroom complete the accommodation.\r\n\r\nExternally to the front there is a tarmac communal parking area. The back garden features the paved patio area & a self-maintained pebbledash garden with a side access gate.\r\n\r\nViewing is highly recommended to appreciate what this home has to offer.\r\n\r\n\r\nEntrance Hall 3.51 x 1.86.
