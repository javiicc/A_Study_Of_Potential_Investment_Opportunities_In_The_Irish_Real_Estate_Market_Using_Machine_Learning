    Description

      Two bedroom second floor apartment set in this secure development off Harcourt Street. Iveagh Court is superbly located at the junction of Harcourt Street and Harcourt Road, less than one minute walk to the Harcourt LUAS Station. It is just a five minute walk to St. Stephen\\'s Green and every imaginable City Centre amenity. Accommodation has been upgraded to a very good standard and is flooded with natural light. Entrance hall leads to lounge cum dining area with access to balcony. There is a fully fitted, integrated kitchen off the lounge complete with washer/dryer, oven, hob, dishwasher and full size fridge/freezer. There are two double bedrooms off the entrance hall and small study/storage area. The master is complete with en-suite. The main bathroom is located off the entrance hall with bath and power shower. Other features include wood flooring throughout, alarm, under floor heating. Available to view immediately. Available to let as of 15th December 2021 on 12 month lease
