    Quillsen is delighted to present this 4 bedroom semi-detached home to the market.\r\n\r\nTucked away to the rear of this small development, the house looks out to a large green area at the front and it boasts that all important sunny south westerly aspect to the rear. There is a good-sized rear garden with pedestrian access and backing on to the Irish Rail Works Depot. It is not overlooked and benefits from a high degree of privacy. \r\n\r\nExtending to 85 sqm / 915 sqft approx., the discerning purchaser is presented with an attractive blank canvass from which they can create a warm and inviting home to suit their own personal taste and preferences.\r\n\r\nThe accommodation is bright, spacious and well-appointed. The ground floor comprises an entrance hallway with downstairs w.c. off, a sitting room to the front and a bright southerly facing kitchen / breakfast room to the back. Upstairs there are 4 bedrooms and a family bathroom. \r\n\r\nThe convenience of the location speaks for itself. Abercorn Square is ideally located in a quiet cul-de-sac in the sought after CIE Railway Works estate which is the oldest walled estate in Ireland and Britain. There are a number of independent shops, cafes, bars and restaurants nearby as well as a number of excellent outdoor leisure facilities including The Memorial Gardens, IMMA and The Phoenix Park. The city centre is easily accessible by Dublin Bus or the Red Line LUAS.\r\n\r\nAccommodation\r\nGround Floor\r\nEntrance Hallway - 1.70m x 4.30m \r\nGuest WC - 0.75m x 1.50m\r\nSitting Room - 2.95m x 5.00m \r\nKitchen / Breakfast Room - 4.75m x 4.00m \r\n\r\nFirst Floor\r\nBedroom 1 - 2.62m x 3.35m\r\nBedroom 2 - 2.62m x 3.65m \r\nBedroom 3 - 2.00m x 2.90m (at widest)\r\nBedroom 4 - 2.00m x 3.35m (at widest) \r\nBathroom - 1.70m x 1.95m \r\n\r\nAll information provided is to the best of our knowledge.
