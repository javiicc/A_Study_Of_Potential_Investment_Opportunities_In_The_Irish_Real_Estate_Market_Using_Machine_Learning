    Floor Area c. 86 sq.m / 926 sq.ft\nFlynn Estate Agents are delighted to welcome 58 Bantry Square to the market. This beautiful 2 bedroom property is nicely situated in the very popular Waterville development. Bantry Square has easy access to all amenities such as schools, shops and bus stops to the City Centre. Also within easy reach of the Blanchardstown Shopping Centre and village, the National Aquatic Centre, Connolly Hospital and both the N3 & M50 get you to Dublin Airport within minutes. This wonderful bright spacious home is presented in pristine condition throughout and has been well maintained by its current owners. The property consists of a very spacious living room/dining area with access to rear garden, fitted kitchen with tiled floor, two double bedrooms (master ensuite) with fitted wardrobes, family and bathroom. This property is ideal for a home owner or investor alike.
