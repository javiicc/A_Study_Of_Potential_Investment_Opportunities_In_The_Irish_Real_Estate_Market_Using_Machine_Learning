    3 Meadow View is a beautifully presented 3 bed semi detached house located in the highly sought after village of Ashford in Co Wicklow. This fine property has been upgraded and renovated to a very high standard and is in showhouse condition throughout. The property has the added benefit of an attic conversion and a home office/gym set in the beautifully landscaped low maintenance back garden.\r\nThe property extends to 80.5 Sq metres plus the attic conversion and the home office.\r\n\r\nAll the rooms throughout the house are generously proportioned with an entrance hall, living room and an open plan kitchen/dining room on the ground floor. The upstairs accommodation comprises 3 well appointed bedrooms and a family bathroom. The main bedroom has a walk in wardrobe and there is a number of clever storage options throughout the house. There is the added benefit of a converted attic room which is ideal for any number of uses. \r\n\r\nResidence has double glazed windows, oil fired central heating with a new boiler, and spacious landscaped gardens to the front and rear.\r\nExternally to the front the property enjoys an extensive parking area. To the rear the property enjoys a spacious low maintenance garden with sunny decking area and a beautiful home office/gym. The covered side access has been transformed to a useful utility area with storage and laundry facilities.\r\n\r\nThis fine family home is close to all local amenities with shops, schools, pubs and restaurants just minutes away. The property has been beautifully maintained and is wonderfully presented throughout. 3 Meadowview represents a well-priced purchase for first time buyers or those looking for a reasonably priced family home in Ashford.
