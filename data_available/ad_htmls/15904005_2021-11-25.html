    Gullane is an exceptionally situated detached residence enjoying an enviable position in close proximity to Rosscarbery town and Glandore harbour village.\n \nApproached via a private entrance off a minor public road creating a wonderful sense of seclusion and privacy. The grounds (circa 1 acre) are superbly landscaped and mature with a wide variety of specimen trees and shrubs with ample parking.\n\nOn first approach, the main residence is deceptive in size, extending to over 3,400 sq ft (315 sq m). The layout of the accommodation is well-appointed consisting of open plan kitchen/dining, sitting room, living room, conservatory, 5 bedrooms, 2 en-suite, family bathroom, study & utility/boot room. There is also a detached garage measuring approximately 340 sq ft (31 sq m) which includes a gym and home office.\n\nThis area of South West Ireland is of outstanding unspoilt natural beauty. Leisure activities are well catered for at Glandore harbour village (circa 6km) which provides a sailing/yachting club while Rosscarbery (circa 3km) is one of the most popular towns on the West Cork coastline with award winning restaurants, shops, pubs & caf\xc3\xa9s. \n\nThe well-known seaside town of Clonakilty is approx. 10 mins drive with a wide selection of public amenities. The hidden gem that is Mill Cove (circa 3km) is a beautiful tiny shallow estuary with a small stone jetty and moorings for about 20 boats.
