    INISHNEE, ROUNDSTONE, CONNEMARA, CO. GALWAY H91 HD2K\nOnce in a while, a house in superb condition in a stunning location comes to the market. In Inishnee, Roundstone, we present such a house. With spectacular views of Roundstone harbour, the village and the sea in between, this house is sure to please. Roundstone village is a well-known and popular touristic and fishing village located in a highly scenic area of the beautiful Connemara and Inishnee is the beautiful island that is located just off it. The house, which is turn key is circa 208 sqm in size and occupies a large site. It is contained within folio GY9950. The attractive house has a cottage feel true to the local vernacular. The house which is spacious and light filled is circa 16 years old has 4 bedrooms, (2 of which are ensuite), family bathroom, living room, utility room, kitchen / dining with large patio at the gable. Upstairs there is ample storage and lots of potential for further accommodation subject to the necessary planning permits. The rooms have beautiful sliderobe fitted wardrobes. The patio on the gable is a sun trap and one can enjoy the views of the sea, the harbour and Errisbeg hill in privacy. There is a large steel tech shed to the rear of the house which has been insulated and plastered and is used not only as storage but also a utility area.\nBoat lovers and water sport enthusiast\xe2\x82\xacTM will revel in the pier at Inishnee which is only a very short stroll from here and the village of Roundstone with its world-renowned restaurants is 5 minutes away by car. One cannot deny the natural beauty of the beaches in this area, with Gurteen and Dogs Bay being the most notable.\nViewing by appointment only.\n\nBER: C2 BER Number: 114313489 Energy Output Indicator: 197.1kWh/m2/yr\n\nACCOMMODATION\n(Measurements are approximations only)\nGround Floor: circa 140 sqm\nLiving room: 4.23m x 4.75m\nDining Room: 2.87m x 4.75m\nKitchen: 2.56m x 4.75m\nUtility Room: 2.44m x 1.77m\nBedroom One: 3.12m x 3.29m\nBedroom Two: 3.97m x 3.47m\nBathroom: 1.97m x 3.12m\nBedroom Three: 3.12m x 3.65m\nMaster Bedroom: 3.47m x 4.38m\nWalk in wardrobe: 3.47m x 0.86m\nEnsuite: 0.96m x 2.65m\nUpstairs:\n68 sqm of space and storage\n\nSERVICES\nWater: Mains\nWaste: Bord na Mona Waste Treatment Plant\nHeating: Oil fired central heating\nHob: Gas\nPower: Mains\n\nDIRECTIONS\nEir Code: H91 HD2K\nFrom Roundstone village travel in the direction of Cashel / bog road and take the first right hand turn, travel over the bridge onto Inishnee island and continue for approx. one mile.
