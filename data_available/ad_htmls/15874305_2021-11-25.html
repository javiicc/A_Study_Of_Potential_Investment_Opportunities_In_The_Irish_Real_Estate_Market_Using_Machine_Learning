    Welcome to 48 Rialto Cottages, a charming artisan cottage located in the heart of Rialto. Ideally located in this quiet cul-de-sac, this home offers the perfect starter package or indeed trade down option for any discerning purchaser. \n \nNo 48 has been lovingly maintained by its current owner creating a cottage filled wih charm and character with some original features on display. The roof of the extension - which incorporates the double bedroom - has been recently replaced with Trocal roofing, with a life span of between 30 and 40 years.\n\nBriefly comprising of living room , kitchen, bedroom, and a fully tiled bathroom completes the ground floor accommodation . The attic has been cleverly converted creating an abundance of additional space.\n\nThere is a small, private yard area to the rear which can be accessed from the kitchen and the rear bedroom. Overlooks a beautiful green area. \n\nThe location boasts a wealth of amenities both social and essential for everyday living. Close to the city centre, and the canal giving those who cycle a secure cycle path to the city centre, there is also ample public transport, including LUAS, and is only a few minutes drive to the M50 and a stones throw from the New National Childrens Hospital. \n\nAll in all, this property is sure to have broad appeal as a charming city centre pad or investment property.
