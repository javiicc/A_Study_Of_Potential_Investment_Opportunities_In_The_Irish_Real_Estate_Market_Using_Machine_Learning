    Richmond Park is a leafy, highly sought-after residential enclave situated close to Monkstown Village, the coast and the DART. \n\nNo.25 is an attractive two-bedroomed home presented in turnkey condition throughout. The property has been meticulously upgraded to exacting standards boasting soft neutral tones throughout. This fine home includes light filled reception rooms, a modern fitted kitchen and a new bathroom. All in all, this is a fantastic opportunity to acquire a superb property in a great location. It is sure to attract interest from first time buyers to those right sizing alike.\n\nUpon entering, one is instantly greeted by a great sense of warmth, space and light throughout. The ground floor comprises; entrance porch with stairs to first floor, leading through to the interconnecting large kitchen/dining/living room and double doors leading through to the conservatory and south facing rear garden. On the first floor there are two double bedrooms, both with fitted wardrobes. A modern Davies Bathroom completes the accommodation.\n\nResidents of Richmond Park enjoy a mature setting, ideally positioned with all the amenities Monkstown has to offer within close proximity. There is a convenient pedestrian shortcut to Monkstown Village which offers an array of boutiques, specialist shops, cafes, pubs and some of the finest restaurants in South County Dublin including FXB s, Lobstar, Bresson and Salt at Avoca. There are several highly regarded schools close by including CBC Monkstown, Blackrock College, Newpark, Scoil Lorcain and Carysfort National school to name but a few. Nearby transport links include the DART at Salthill & Monkstown as well as several Dublin bus routes, including the Aircoach serving Dublin Airport, which provide easy access to the city centre and beyond.\n\nThe enviable south facing private rear garden is a peaceful space in which to relax during the finer weather and the perfect place to enjoy al fresco dining! It is a low maintenance space, laid in patio with raised flower beds hosting an array of different species of plants to include Viburnum Tinus Lisarose, Olive tree, Houttuynia Cordata, Callistemon Linearis to name but a few. The garden was professionally landscaped three years ago and there is a large storage container & electricity point. There is ample parking to the front of the house.\n\nAll in all, No.25 Richmond Park provides an excellent opportunity to acquire a lovely home in an excellent location.
