This stunning residence is located in one of Waterford's most popular residential districts within a short walk from University Hospital Waterford, and is well served by a regular bus service to both the hospital and city centre. The development lies within the catchment area for some of Waterford's best schools and is within a short walk from the urban village of Ardkeen with coffee shops, bars and restaurants. \r\nEnjoying a perfect balance of bedroom and living space, due to the addition of a tasteful sun room to the rear, while an inviting open plan design lends itself to entertaining and is easily altered with existing double doors from living room to kitchen/dining room.
