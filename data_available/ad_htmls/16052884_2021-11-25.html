    LEONARD WILSON KEENAN ESTATE & LETTING AGENTS \r\nOur business is you .... \r\nLeonard Wilson Keenan and agent Richard Todd are delighted to introduce 2 Whitworth Avenue to the Drumcondra property market.\r\n\r\nNumber 2 is a stunning red brick, mid-terraced house that has been fully renovated throughout. It is a bright, well-maintained home with plenty of character and attractive features that make it stand out in the Drumcondra market.\r\n\r\nRecent upgrades include insulation, rewiring, a new bathroom, under-stair storage, new windows and front door.\r\n\r\nIn turnkey condition, the property comprises of a large living area with an elegant fireplace as central feature. Double doors lead to the recently-renovated kitchen, with large velux windows in the roof keeping the space bright and welcoming. Also downstairs lies the new bathroom, with its huge shower and carefully-chosen sanitary ware, decorated tastefully with subway tiles.\r\n\r\nThe upstairs landing offers high ceilings, with views over the rooftops of Drumcondra. The main bedroom spans the entire depth of the property, offering plenty of light with windows at the front and back of the room. The room is carpeted and has fitted wardrobes, allowing for plenty of storage space. The second bedroom offers views over the laneway at the front of the property.\r\n\r\nThe property is within walking distance of both Drumcondra and Phibsborough villages, and only 20 minutes walk from the city centre. This central location is close to many coffee shops, gyms, bars and restaurants. Local green areas include the National Botanic Gardens and Griffith Park. \r\n\r\nDublin Airport is 17 minutes drive away, with Drumcondra train station and many bus routes only a short distance from the property.\r\n\r\n***Viewing By Appointment ***\r\n\r\nAll information provided is to the best of our knowledge.
