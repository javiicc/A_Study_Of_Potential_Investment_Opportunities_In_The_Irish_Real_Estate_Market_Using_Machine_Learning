    Floor Area c. 94 sq.m / 1011 sq.ft\nFlynn Estate Agents are delighted to present this charming two bedroom semi-detached cottage set on the mature residential road of Blackhorse Avenue and on the doorstep of the beautiful Phoenix park and Castleknock village. Presented in excellent condition throughout, the accommodation includes a bright entrance hall, main bathroom, spacious dual aspect lounge, under floor heating throught downstairs area, open plan dining and kitchen area with access to rear courtyard. On the first floor you are met with two double bedrooms both with built-in wardrobes and (master en-suite). Benefitting from a low maintenance walled driveway to the front which sets this property off from others. Location is second to none being just off the Navan Road and within walking distance of the Phoenix Park, Ashtown train station and the new Pelletstown station, bus stops, shops, local supermarket, schools, cr\xc3\xa8ches, coffee shops and a host of local amenities. The City Centre, M50 motorway and Blanchardstown Shopping Centre are a short drive away.
