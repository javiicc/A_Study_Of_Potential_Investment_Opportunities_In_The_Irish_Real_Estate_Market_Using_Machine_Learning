    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to offer 34 Great Western Villas to the market. Tucked away on a quiet residential street located just off Great Western Square, this charming two-bedroom mid terrace period property is within comfortable walking distance of Phibsborough, Stoneybatter and the city centre. Adjacent to Phibsborough Luas stop and array of amenities, this home is presented in excellent condition throughout and is ready to walk into. \n\nThe accommodation briefly comprises entrance hall, open plan living and dining area, kitchen, two bedrooms upstairs along with the main bathroom. \n\nResidents of Great Western Villas enjoy the benefits of a sleepy neighbourhood, nestled in a prime location, within close proximity to a host of amenities which include a vibrant array of local shops, restaurants and caf s along with a community garden, utilised and cared for by residents. A popular area for young professionals and first-time buyers, it is within short distance of Phibsborough, Stoneybatter, the city centre, Phoenix Park and serviced by several Luas Stations and numerous bus routes. It is within comfortable walking distance to DIT Grangegorman. The M50, M1, and Dublin Airport are also easily accessible.\n\n\nEntrance Hall\n\nLiving/Dining Room with wooden flooring, open fire place with cast iron surround, under stairs storage and spacious dining area\n\nKitchen with ample floor and wall level high gloss kitchen units, plumbed for a dish washer and washing machine, extra under stairs storage space.\n\nBedroom 1 double room spanning the width of the house with painted wooden floor boards and recently fitted mirrored slide robes.\n\nBedroom 2 single room with painted wooden floor boards.\n\nBathroom partly tiled with bath and power shower, wc and wash hand basin.
