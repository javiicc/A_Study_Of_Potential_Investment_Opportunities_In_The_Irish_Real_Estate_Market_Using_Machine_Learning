    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    Maple Run, Kilcarn is a beautifully proportioned home on a 1 acre site situated on the Oldtown Road, just minutes from Old Johnstown Village and just a 10 minute drive (or 7.5km) to Navan town centre. The original house was extended by its current owners and they in turn have maintained the house to an exceptional standard. \r\n\r\nThe ground floor is set out with an entrance hall, a large private living room, a lounge, a guest double bedroom, a sun room and an open plan kitchen / dining space which opens onto a family living room. Upstairs there are five large bedrooms with as well as the main bathroom and a closet. The master bedroom has a walk in wardrobe. \r\n\r\nThe landscaped gardens and professionally designed outdoor area surrounding the house ensures long term privacy, the house is also well set back from the main road yet close to all the facilities of the town centre, as well as being close to the M3 motorway and within 30 minutes of Blanchardstown / Dublin.\r\n\r\nIt is the agent's view that the house represents good value when you consider the costs of acquiring such a suitable site along with the cost of construction. A potential purchaser will have the good balance of being within Navan yet will also obtain the attractive feature of being surrounded by countryside in a spacious house. The spacious and well-proportioned accommodation is spread over two floors and extends to approx. 283sqm / 2,723sq. ft.
