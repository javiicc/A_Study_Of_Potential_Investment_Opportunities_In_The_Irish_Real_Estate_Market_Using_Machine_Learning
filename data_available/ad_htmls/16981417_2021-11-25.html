    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to present to the market 20 Brookwood Grove. \n\nBuilt in the early 1960 s, No. 20 is a fine three-bedroomed semi-detached house with a garage to the side and is situated on this popular and convenient road just off Brookwood Avenue. The accommodation is both spacious and light filled throughout and briefly comprises, entrance porch and hallway with under stairs storage, a spacious front living room with tiled open fireplace and an open plan kitchen/dining room with access to the rear garden. Upstairs there are three fine sized bedrooms, two doubles and a generous single and a large family bathroom. \n\nThe walled garden to the front is mainly laid in lawn with off-street parking. A pedestrian side entrance provides access to the south-easterly facing garden to the rear which is walled and mainly laid in lawn with a small concrete shed for storage. There is excellent potential to extend both to the rear and over the garage at first floor level (subject to pp). \n\nBrookwood Grove is set in a great location close to all amenities with a selection of shops on Rosemount Avenue, along with excellent primary & secondary schools, St Anne s Park and Dollymount Strand close by. Both Killester and Clontarf Village are within walking distance and boast excellent restaurants, bars, cafes, boutiques and recreational facilities. There are numerous transport links close to the property including the DART service from Harmonstown Station and several bus routes in the immediate area. Dublin Airport, Beaumont Hospital, the M50, M1, and the city centre are also easily accessible. \n\nViewing is highly recommended to appreciate the potential this family home offers along with the mature and desirable setting.
