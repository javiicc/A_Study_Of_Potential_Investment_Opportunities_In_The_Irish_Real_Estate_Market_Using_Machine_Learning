    Sorrento Lodge is a magnificent compact refurbished detached property, with beautifully proportioned well balanced accommodation, taking in the wonderful sea & Mountain View\xe2\x82\xacTM from all aspects of this charming property.\r\n\r\nSet on approx .75 of an acre of walled gardens the property has undergone a full refurbishment programme. The results of which are obvious throughout the house with great attention paid to the original character which bringing the accommodation up to modern day standards in terms of heating, plumbing and insulation.\r\n\r\nThe property was originally built in the 1800s with all works carried out in 2015 and 2016.\r\n\r\nThe internal layout which extends to approx. 2,000 sq ft has been designed to take full advantage of its coastal setting with the main reception rooms and bedrooms enjoying panoramic sea and mountain views. Picture windows and doors in each room capture the stunning views over the lawn across the bay. The 18 foot high ceilings also add to the grandeur of the property. The master bedroom and bedroom two both have en-suites and their is a main bathroom too.\r\n\r\nThe house is filed with natural light, perfectly proportioned and beautifully designed with fine details and creative touches creating an enduring high quality interior. The main kitchen living area is open plan and comes with a bespoke kitchen and brick fireplace. There is also a separate outhouse that could be renovated into a studio or home office.\r\n\r\nEntered through wrought iron gates, a gravelled driveway provides generous vehicular access to the beautifully landscaped walled residence. There is also a picturesque pedestrian entrance to the front of the property.\r\n\r\nArea Info\r\nThis truly is a unique home set amid its own private grounds, exceptionally well located with its myriad of amenities including specialty shops, coffee shops, restaurants, pubs, golf and sports clubs. Nearby amenities include the very famous Arnold Palmer designed golf course, Tralee\xe2\x82\xacTM sailing club all within 3/4 km. away. Amazing Restaurants some within walking distance.\r\n\r\nOcean vistas change all the time with shifting weather and seas and lots to watch from fishing boats and yachts to seals, kayakers, rowers and intrepid snorkelers. With so few houses enjoying direct shoreline access viewing is highly recommended to appreciate the quality and beauty Sorrento Lodge has to offer.\r\n\r\nLocated on the Spa approximately 4km from Tralee and 20mins from Kerry Airport this property is easily accesible.
