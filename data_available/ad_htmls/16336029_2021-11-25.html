    Wonderful 3 bedroom end terrace dormer bungalow which is presented in excellent condition throughout. Situated within this ever popular, mature cul de sac development and benefiting from private front and rear gardens with the front facing just accross from a large green area. \n\nThe house is laid out over two floors and comprises a spacious entrance hall with guest wc, 3rd bedroom downstairs currently being used as a living room, kitchen/living/dining room with Amtico flooring to the rear with access to the beautifl garden area. Upstairs there are 2 bedrooms and good sized family bathroom with bath and shower. \n\nClose to Rush town centre with a wide range of amenities including shops, restaurants, with short walk to beach, Milbank Theatre and an array of sporting facilities. Rush/Lusk train station is c.5 minute s drive away; the M1 is c.15 minutes drive away and the M50 & Airport are c.20-25 minute s drive.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 2.39m x 4.92m. with tiled floor, understairs storage entrance to downstairs w.c.\n\nDownstairs W.C. 0.70m x 1.74m. with tiled floor, w.c., w.h.b., partly tiled walls.\n\nBedroom 3 / Sitting Room 3.17m x 4.41m. with timber floor, ceiling coving, feature fireplace with electric fire insert.\n\nKitchen/Living Room 5.63m x 5.81m. with beautiful Amtico floor, feature fireplace and sliding patio doors to rear. kitchen has tiled floor, built in presses, pine ceiling, cooker, plumbed for washing machine and dish washer.\n\nUpstairs\n\nLanding 2.88m x 4.49m. with carpet\n\nBathroom 2.87m x 2.40m. with linoleum floor, w.c., w.h.b., bath and shower.\n\nBedroom 1 2.76m x 6.00m. with carpet, built in sliderobes,\n\nBedroom 2 2.82m x 2.49m. with carpet flooring, velux window.\n\nOutside The front garden has concrete driveway giving ample off street parking with well maintained mature planted garden. To the rear is an extremely private not walled/fenced garden with paved areas intermingled with mature planting along with a decked seating area to the rear. Large porched shed. Garden is not overlooked to the rear.
