    Property Partners Buggy are delighted to welcome you to this stunning architectural designed family residence which is amongst the finest houses ever to come to the market in the region. Superbly located just outside Abbeyleix, this visually stunning 6-bedroom detached home contributes significantly to affirm this as an area of real distinction. A handsome and imposing detached two Storey property, Ballytarsna is extremely impressive both inside and out.\nApproached along a winding tree lined avenue the property offers immense privacy and even the most discerning are struck by the gorgeous stone fa\xc3\xa9\xc2\xa7ade limestone window sills and brick reveals, that give this property a traditional charm complimented with the highest of modern standards. Set on four acres, the residence with its stunning picture book looks is surrounded by beautifully mature and spacious landscaped gardens with a large paddock, a modern stable block, tack room, potting shed, and a fully wired and plumbed garage also.\nThe accommodation is unrivalled with an open plan, living, dining, kitchen area at the centre of the home whilst 6 huge bedrooms, two reception rooms, a large office and several bathrooms all combine to create a family home of true distinction and style. Built to exacting standards the property boasts many fine features including six-inch Breton slab insulated floors, bespoke fitted solid oak kitchen, leaded glass front doors and perhaps one of the finest features of the property is the large annex/living area built into the main residence.\nAbbeyleix is serviced by three primary schools whilst a bus service brings children safely to Heywood Community College, regarded as one of the best secondary schools in the entire region. Sports and recreation facilities including the local GAA, Soccer and Tennis clubs are all on hand. Abbeyleix is famed for its excellent food and drink outlets with Morrissey's Pub and Mueller & O'Connell bakery amongst the best known in Ireland making Abbeyleix with its exceptional community spirit a wonderful place to live.\nSituated 50 minutes from Dublin via the M7 and this four-acre private paradise is just minutes from all conveniences. Viewing of this rare gem is highly recommended and is strictly by appointment.
