To the right is a large double bedroom which backs on to the property's main bathroom, featuring a bathtub and separate shower cubicle. Beyond that is another bedroom (master) with an ensuite and walk-in wardrobe.\r\n\r\nTo the left of the entrance hall, a short hallway leads past the staircase and into a cosy living room featuring an ornate pine-effect fireplace with decorative tiling and double glass doors that open onto the property's spacious kitchen/diner. With double patio doors to the garden and contemporary kitchen units this area and adjoining sitting room are ideal for entertaining. A hallway off the kitchen leads to an adjoining utility space and connects back out into the reception hall.\r\n\r\nUpstairs there are two more roomy bedrooms - one of which has been adapted as a spacious home office by the current owner - together with another generous storage space, suitable for conversion into a shower room should wish. \r\n\r\nThe d\xc3\xa9cor throughout this Bunatubber property is bright, contemporary, neutral and clean, lending it a homely, welcoming air and rendering it ready to occupy for buyers with minimal input required from a decorative point of view.\r\n\r\nDirections\r\nSet close to the Mayo border, the townland of Bunatubber is set off the main N84 Galway road. It is just 5km from Corrandulla village and 9.7km from Headford town.\r\nLocal national schools, Kilcoona and Castlehacket are both within 3kms of this property.\r\nGalway city is just a 20-minute drive away while Ireland West Airport Knock is within an hour's commute.\r\n\r\nViewing\r\nA property of this nature is a rare find on the market and is likely to appeal to a wide variety of purchasers.\r\n\r\nViewing is strictly by appointment only.\r\n\r\nProperty Partners Emma Gill strives to be ultra-flexible around property viewings so don't delay in booking yours.
