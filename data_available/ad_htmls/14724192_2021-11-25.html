    TOM MCDONALD & ASSOCIATES - EXPERIENCED, PROFESSIONAL, TRUSTED - are delighted to offer to the market this Stunning, 3 Bed Family Home presented in Excellent Condition throughout. Situated in a Private Cul-de-Sac just a short walk from the Town Centre, Schools, Shops, Train Station & All Amenities. Decorated to a Very High Standard, this Superb Property has been well cared for and maintained boasting numerous features. Accommodation of this 'Turn-Key' Home is comprised of Living Room; Kitchen/Dining Area; 3 Bedrooms; Family Bathroom, En-Suite & W.C. In the Bright Kitchen/Dining Area, there is a Quality Fitted Kitchen with Additional Units providing Ample Storage and includes a Double Oven, Microwave, Hob, Integrated Fridge Freezer & Dishwasher. A New Energy Efficient Gas Boiler was recently installed. There is a Patio Door in the Dining Area which accesses the Secure Rear Garden with Timber Garden Shed. The Tastefully Decorated Living Room with Fitted Shelves has a Feature Solid Fuel Open Fire creating a warm atmosphere of an evening. Under the Stairs is plumbed for Washing Machine. The Main Bedroom with Bay Window has an En-Suite with Tiled Floor & Electric Shower. The Family Bathroom with Tiled Floor & Bath Surround has a Pump Shower & Fitted Storage Units. 2 Bedrooms have Fitted Wardrobes. The Partially Floored Attic with Standing Head Height is accessed by a Stira Ladder. The Property is Fully Alarmed. This Beautiful Family Home must be viewed to be appreciated. Early Viewing Advised as first to see will buy! Built c.2003. Services: Mains Water, Sewerage, Electricity, Natural Gas & Broadband.
