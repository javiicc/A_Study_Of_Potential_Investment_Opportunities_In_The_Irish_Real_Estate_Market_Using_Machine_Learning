      Email Only!! MTS Property are delighted to offer this stunning two bedroom house to the rental market. Darley Street is a wonderfully charming home, ideally positioned on the south-side of the road in this ever popular enclave of charming residences tucked away off Harold s Cross Road. The accommodation briefly comprises a bright, open-plan living room with a feature fireplace, open into the sleek modern well-appointed kitchen and breakfast room, beautifully finished shower room, and rear courtyard. Upstairs the main bedroom spans the width of the house to the front while the second bedroom is to rear. The convenience of this property is second to none, positioned in the heart of one of Harold s Cross most popular residential addresses. Darley Street is within a gentle stroll of Rathmines, Portobello & the City Centre. There is a vast array of local shops, cafes & restaurants on your doorstep, while Harold s Cross Park is just moments away.
