    24 Littlewood is a fully refurbished and generously proportioned two-bedroom south facing duplex apartment conveniently located within easy reach of Stepaside Village and the LUAS.\r\n\r\nPresented in show house condition throughout, the accommodation comprises of an entrance hall into the open plan living / dining room with access to the sun filled south facing patio, kitchen and guest wc. The first floor is presented in landing with shelved hot press, two generous and beautifully appointed double bedrooms with master featuring an ensuite and access to the private south facing balcony, and family bathroom. Outside, there are communal landscaped grounds with generous visitor parking. The apartment also benefits from generous parking.\r\n\r\nThe location is second to none with the bustling village of Stepaside being just a short walk away with its wide range of shopping and dining amenities. There are also a range of convenient stores and eateries within the Belarmine development itself. Also nearby are Dundrum Town Centre, Sandyford Business Park, Beacon South Quarter and Leopardstown Racecourse.\r\n\r\nThere are a wide range of activities nearby including Fernhill Gardens, Westwood gym, golf clubs, equestrian centres, not to mention an abundance of nearby hill walking and mountain bike routes. Public transportation is well catered for including the Luas Green Line and several Bus routes. Dublin City Centre is approximately 25 minutes by car.
