    We are delighted to present No. 47 Arnott Street to the market. This terrace of 4 homes offer contemporary, spacious accommodation split over three floors. This 2 bedroomed house is in turn key condition with excellent energy efficiency and solar water heating and would make a wonderful home for those looking to live within easy walking distance to the centre of town. \r\n\r\nEntering the hall there are white stained Oak floors which feature throughout the property. There stairs go down to a spacious bedroom with slide robes and its own small terrace. Also on this level is a wonderful bathroom and a plant room were the gas boiler and controls for the solar heating on the roof are. Going up the steps from the hall you enter the open plan living room and kitchen. White stained Oak floor are again a feature of this space with a Juliet Balcony and an enclosed gas fire giving a focal point to the living area while a Maise kitchen enhances the overall contemporary feel. The dining area is up a couple more steps with a glass balustrade overlooking the kitchen adding to the feeling of space and light. There is also a wintergarden with Teak decking and glass louvres overlooking the street on this level. Taking the final few steps to the top of the house you reach the master bedroom with ensuite and the roof terrace. The master bedroom is a bright, spacious room with a generous ensuite bathroom. The roof terrace has Teak decking and toughened glass balustrades with a Teak planters and artificial hedging. It offers panoramic views of the surrounding area. \r\n\r\nArnott Street is a pretty Victorian Street at city end of South Circular Road, adjacent to Heytesbury Street, it is within a 10-minute walk of St Stephens Green. The immediate area is home to excellent restaurants and delis while the neighbouring Camden Quarter provides for all your shopping needs including fresh fruit, veg and flowers from the local street traders. The Green LUAS stop at Harcourt Street is a short stroll while several prime bus routes pass along the main South Circular Road ensuring quick and easy access throughout Dublin and its environs.\r\n\r\nAccommodation\r\n\r\nHall Level\r\nHallway \t\t\t5.29m x 1.64m \r\nWhite stained Oak floor.
