    Clare Connolly Estate Agent is delighted to bring this stunning property to the sales market in conjunction with Lisney. Woodford House started life as a stable and carriage house, and has been transformed into a magnificent, detached period family home occupying a wonderful position in a quiet cul de sac setting off Booterstown Avenue and surrounded by 0.5 acres of lush gardens. \r\n\r\nThe property has been renovated to the highest standard while still retaining it's period charm and character. The large welcoming reception hall boasts a cloakroom and w.c. Just off the hall is a spacious ground floor bedroom with newly renovated en-suite. To the front is a circular sunroom with vaulted ceiling and panoramic windows that capture the surrounding leafy gardens. The inner hall opens onto the dining room with walnut flooring and vaulted ceilings, double doors lead into a spacious triple aspect drawing room. To the rear there is also a cosy living room with floor to ceiling windows, magnificent timber panelling and door to the rear garden. The large kitchen has great space for a growing family with a wide array of fitted units and leads to a very fine conservatory with stunning views over the picturesque rear garden. A plant room and store room complete the downstairs accommodation. \r\n\r\nThe spacious feel and charm of Woodford House is also prevalent throughout the upstairs accommodation. Accessed via a magnificent stairs in the entrance hall or an ornate cantilevered spiral staircase to the rear, the upstairs offers three large bedrooms with picture windows overlooking the gardens, two with en-suites and master with walk in wardrobe. There is also a further fourth bedroom and shower room. \r\n\r\nThe real jewel in the crown of Woodford House are the 0.5 acres of manicured lush, leafy gardens which wrap around the house creating a peaceful outlook out every window. To the front there is excellent off street parking for numerous cars while the rear is mainly laid out in lawn with a patio surrounded by mature plants and shrubs and box hedging. The rear garden also enjoys a south facing aspect and unparalleled seclusion and privacy. \r\n\r\nBooterstown Avenue is an area where there is consistently strong demand for residential property. A particular feature is the close proximity to the QBC and the DART making it an ideal meeting point between city living and all the coastal charms of South County Dublin. Also nearby are some of the country\xe2\x82\xacTM best known schools to include Blackrock College, St. Andrews, St. Michaels, Col\xc3\xa9\xc2\xa1iste Eoin and Mount Anville to name but a few.
