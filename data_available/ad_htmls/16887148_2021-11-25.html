    Alma Park is a lovely enclave of townhouses situated in the picturesque coastal town of Monkstown. Ideally located just a stones throw from the centre of the village, this attractive townhouse will be the perfect starter home or ideal for anyone downsizing in the area.\n\n7 Alma Park is a spacious three bedroomed, terraced townhouse which offers bright and airy accommodation with a south facing rear garden. This delightful home is bright and well laid out, extending to approximately 92sqm / 991sqft. The accommodation briefly comprises; an entrance hall, guest wc, kitchen / breakfast room and a spacious living room, which has an attractive timber fireplace and double doors leading to the rear garden. \n\nUpstairs there are three bedrooms, two of which are doubles and a spacious single. Plus, a family bathroom and a hot press on the landing. \n\nThe tiered garden which is predominantly paved provides a lovely outdoor dining area, perfect for having a BBQ on the long summer evenings with its enviable south facing orientation. A private garden of this size is a rare find in Monkstown. There is communal parking to the front of the property. \n\nThe location is second to none, boasting all the amenities Monkstown has to offer within proximity including one of the most popular swimming spots in Ireland at Seapoint, just an eight-minute walk away. Positioned centrally between the town centres of Blackrock and Dun Laoghaire (both only a 15-minute walk), the house has access to a vast array of shops, bars, restaurants, and leisure facilities. There are several highly regarded schools close by including CBC Monkstown, Blackrock College, Newpark, Scoil Lorc in, and Carysfort National school to name but a few. Nearby transport links include the DART at Salthill & Monkstown as well as several Dublin bus routes which provide easy access to the city centre and beyond. All in all, 7 Alma Park provides an excellent opportunity to acquire a lovely home in an excellent location. Viewing is highly recommended.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 1.61m x 5.10m.
