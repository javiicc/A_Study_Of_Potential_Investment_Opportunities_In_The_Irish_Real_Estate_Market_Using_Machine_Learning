    ***OFF STREET PARKING/ SOUTH FACING GARDEN/ GAS FIRED CENTRAL HEATING/ EXCELLENT LOCATION/ EXTENDED AND MODERNISED/ WIRED FOR ALARM***\n\nKELLY BRADSHAW DALTON are delighted to present to the market 55 Donnycarney Road. \nThis property could not be better located, positioned on a quiet side road just off the Malahide Road. It is a fantastic residential development to live in that attracts young families, first time buyers and those looking to downsize alike due to its excellent sense of community.\nAnyone that views No.55 will be impressed by the renovations carried out to a very high standard by its current owners and completed in 2009. This property definitely oozes warmth, a great sense of space, excellent proportions and light. On arrival you will see there is off street parking. \nThe entrance hallway to the right of the property has under stairs storage and the current owners have made clever use of space by adding a utility cupboard here.\nThe family living room is open plan, while still retaining a great sense of charm and character due to its cosy open fireplace, creating a main focal point in the room. It has laminate wood flooring running throughout this space and is painted in neutral tones. Dimmer spotlights add to the ambience and makes this space the perfect place to relax in the evening after a busy day. \nThe dual aspect kitchen/ dining room is again open plan and meets the needs of modern living to suit today's informal lifestyles and a natural hub for the home and greater sociability while cooking or entertaining. The kitchen and dining room also have the same flooring and are flooded in natural light from the south facing garden. The kitchen features ample storage, wall and floor kitchen units in a high gloss finish, a wood grain work space, subway backsplash and is decorated in muted tones creating a lovely ambience. \nFrom the kitchen and dining space French doors lead out to the Indian Sandstone patio of the south facing fenced and walled garden that is low maintenance and features a shared side access and shed to cater for all your garden needs. The garden is a real oasis and seems a world away from the hustle and bustle of city life. \nThe family bathroom was renovated in 2017 to a modern bathroom suite, glass bath screen, electric shower, and chrome heated towel rail is very tastefully decorated and completes the ground floor accommodation.\nUpstairs comprises of 3 bedrooms, the master bedroom features a wonderful ensuite bathroom, a great addition to the property, slide robes storage and carpet flooring. The second bedroom to the rear has built in wardrobes to cater for all your storage needs and carpet flooring. The third bedroom again is of a generous size and has fitted wardrobes and carpet flooring. A hotpress closet completes the accommodation on this floor.\nThe location itself needs no introduction and is second to none with every amenity close by. Donnycarney Road is a well-established residential area in a central location close to the Omni Park Shopping Centre, Super Valu Shopping Centre, Beaumont Hospital, DCU, Eastpoint Business Park and many major employers. Public transport, schools, churches, and many sporting and recreational facilities are within walking distance. Dublin city centre is approximately 4km away. The M1 and M50 Motorways are easily accessed from this location. Clontarf Golf club, Parnell Park, Ellenfield Park and Fairview Park are great amenities to have literally on your doorstep.\nSeldom do superb properties in this area come on the market and 55 Donnycarney Road should create great interest and get people talking.
