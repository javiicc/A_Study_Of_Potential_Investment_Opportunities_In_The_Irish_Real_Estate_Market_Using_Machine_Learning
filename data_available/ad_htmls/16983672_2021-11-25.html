    Number 31 is a beautiful three bedroom detached bungalow presented in beautiful condition.\r\n\r\nThis fine home has been renovated in recent years and is in walk in condition. This property offers a spacious living room with open fireplace, large kitchen, utility room and dining area. Off the dining room is a sunroom which leads out to the garden. Bedrooms include 3 double rooms. The master bedroom is ensuite. The main bathroom is tastefully fitted.\r\n\r\nThe south east facing garden features a lovely patio area and you can enjoy sun all day when it shines.\r\n\r\nNumber 31 is within walking distance of the village.
