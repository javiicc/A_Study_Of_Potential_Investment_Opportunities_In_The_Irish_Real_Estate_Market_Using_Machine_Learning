    DNG McKenna Healy are delighted to present Tearmann Na Mara to the market. A wonderful example of a very fine contemporary residence with breath taking sea views of Lambay Island. The views from this home are one of the most scenic, private and captivating in Dublin. This home offers a peaceful and setting with access the Beach close by for one to enjoy throughout the seasons. A beautiful stone cut home that has been finished with high quality fixtures and fitting which features include polished porcelain tiled floors, solid wood flooring, large picture windows, spacious luxury bedrooms and contemporary bathrooms.\r\nThe spacious light filled accommodation extends to approximately 230sq.m is set over two floors. The ground floor comprises bright entrance hall which leads to the vast open plan sitting room, dining room and kitchen area taking in the best of the views. Two sets of double doors lead to the a private cobblelock patio. The fully fitted kitchen comes with polished granite stone worktops. Off the kitchen is a utility room. Also located on the ground floor is the main living room, guest wc and bedroom. Upstairs there is a large master bedroom with most impressive picture windows taking the upmost advantage of the sea views. The master bedroom suite includes an en-suite bathroom. The bedroom has double doors leading to the balcony area overlooking the water. There is a further ensuite bedroom on this floor with its own balcony. Also on this floor is a further two bedrooms and family bathroom. The garden to the rear of the property is south facing and has the benefit of a large patio area to the rear and side of the house with a manicured lawn. To the front of the property is a cobble lock forecourt with parking for 3 to 4 cars.\r\n\r\nLocation\r\nSituated in Rush, a much sought after bustling seaside town in North County Dublin, Tearmann Na Mara combines the joy of a seaside location while also being within a short drive of the city centre. The town will provide for all day to day needs, with a range of restaurants, cafes pubs and shops. There are a number of primary and secondary schools within Rush. Further afield the towns of Lusk, Skerries, Swords and Howth will provide for additional amenities. Being so close to Dublin, Rush is extremely well provided by public transport and a large network of roads. The M1 Motorway connecting Rush to Dublin City is within 15 minutes' drive.
