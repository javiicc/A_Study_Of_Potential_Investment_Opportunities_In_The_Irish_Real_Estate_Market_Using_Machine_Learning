    Description

      No 159 The Waterside is an attractive, 3rd floor, newly renovated one-bedroom apartment in a prime location at Grand Canal Dock. The apartment's entrance hall has a super storage room, leads to an open plan living / dining area. Fully equipped kitchen. One spacious double bedroom, main bathroom with electric shower. Juliette balcony overlooking landscaped gardens. Parking is available on request at an nominal registration fee. This superb development is right on the doorstep to the City's key business, shopping and cultural centres such as the IFSC, the Docklands, (Google and Facebook,) Grafton St., Bord Gais Energy Theatre, The Convention Centre, The 3 Arena & Aviva Stadium and many more. The area is also well serviced by cycle lanes and public transport - Grand Canal Dock DART station is only a minute away together with a number of Dublin City buses routes serving the immediate area.
