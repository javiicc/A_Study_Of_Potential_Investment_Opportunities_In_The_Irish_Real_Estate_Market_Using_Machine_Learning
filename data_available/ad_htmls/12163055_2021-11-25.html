    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Home and Away Real Estate are pleased to present this 2 bed apartment located in the heart of Cloyne village. The location of the property is superb, with a wide range of local amenities such as Shops, Post Office, Sporting Facilities, playground, and Cloyne National School all within a short walk. This property has been very well maintained and continuously modernised throughout the owners time here and the time, effort and money spent on the apartment is immediately evident upon entering. Presented in immaculate condition, the apartment consists of a spacious open plan kitchen, dining and living room. The clever use of space in this bright and airy room provides great functionality. The apartment also consists of two large double bedrooms newly decorated with a neutral palette. Built in wardrobes in both bedrooms. This newly refurbished apartment offers a great opportunity to acquire a quality home in a great location. Cloyne is located just 5 minutes from Midleton Town and 15 minutes from The Jack Lynch Tunnel. With its ideal location it will sure appeal to the discerning buyer. An excellent opportunity for first time buyers, those trading down, or investors to acquire an excellent apartment in turn-key condition. Viewing comes highly recommended.
