    Smith & Butler Estates are delighted to present 418 Collins Avenue Extension to the market. This attractive 4 bedroom house enjoys a superb location. The property further benefits from a large back garden, which is not overlooked and can provide rear lane access. The property also boasts a large garage ideal for home office/gym conversion. Internally, the property extends to a generous 118 sq.m of well-proportioned accommodation which briefly comprises of a bright & spacious porch, welcoming entrance hall, front living room, a dining room, and kitchen to the rear. Upstairs there are four double bedrooms, one of which has built in wardrobes, and a family bathroom with w.c, w.b and bath shower combination.\r\n\r\nThe location is second to none with every local amenity at your doorstep including shops, schools, restaurants and recreational facilities with DCU and Ellenfield Park just a short stroll away. The immediate area is also well serviced by numerous bus routes and excellent transport links to Dublin City Centre and the surrounding country via the nearby M50 and M1 motorways.
