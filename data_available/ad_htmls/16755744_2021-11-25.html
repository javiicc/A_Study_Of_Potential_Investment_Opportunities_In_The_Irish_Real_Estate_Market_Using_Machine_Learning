    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    *** NEW TO THE MARKET ***\n\nSituated in a quiet country lane just off the Carrigallen to Mohill Road (R201) this property has lots of potential. The house consists of 3 bedrooms, kitchen, living room and bathroom with out-buildings. Located less than 5km from all the amenities the town of Carrigallen has to offer, many shops, public houses, Corn Mill Theatre, Carrigallen National School, Carrigallen Vocational School and Carrigallen GAA Club but to name a few. This area is well known for it's excellent fishing and with so many beautiful lakes in the vicinity it is in a prime location for any fishing enthusiast. This house is an ideal property for anyone looking for a start up home, a holiday home or for someone looking for a beautiful location to retire. Viewing is Highly Recommended and Strictly By Appointment.
