    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to present Apartment 2, 32 Cook Street to the market. This spacious first floor apartment is well appointed and boasts a central location in the heart of Dublin city centre.\n\nThe accommodation briefly comprises; entrance hall with storage off, family bathroom, ensuite, two double bedrooms and an open plan kitchen/living/dining area. The apartment further benefits from a designated parking space, ample storage and a generous living area that provides direct access to an attractive courtyard.\n\nThe location is second to none with Christchurch Cathedral, Dame Street and Temple Bar all on your doorstep. Its tranquil setting coupled with its close proximity of the City Centre will certainly appeal to those looking to get their foot on the property ladder or indeed an astute investor. The apartment also benefits from many public transport links including the Luas, an array of bus routes and a convenient Dublin Bike bay around the corner.\n\nEntrance Hall 5.48m x 1.19m. With laminate flooring, alarm and access to storage.\n\nLiving / Dining Room 5.65m x 4.91m. Direct access to courtyard, intercom and TV connection point.\n\nKitchen Tiled floors and splashback, integrated floor and wall level units, plumbed for washing machine, fridge, oven, hob and extractor fan.\n\nBathroom 2.54m x 1.95m. Fully tiled floors and walls, WC, wash hand basin and bath with recessed shower attachment.\n\nMain Bedroom 3.96m x 3.66m. Double room with built in wardrobe and ensuite.\n\nEn-Suite 2.74m x 0.87m. Fully tiled floors and walls, WC, wash hand basin, and mains shower.\n\nBedroom 2 3.53m x 2.90m. Double room with built in wardrobe.
