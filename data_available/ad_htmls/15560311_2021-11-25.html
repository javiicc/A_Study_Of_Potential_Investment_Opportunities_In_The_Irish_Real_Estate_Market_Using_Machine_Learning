    Ballynew, Cleggan, Clifden, Connemara, Co. Galway H91 F8C5\r\nWe are excited and delighted to bring to the market this beautiful home in an area of tremendous natural beauty. This 4-bedroom home which is in turn key condition enjoys spectacular views from a south facing elevation. The Ballinakill lake is framed by the majestic Twelve Bins which affords a view that needs to be seen to be believed! Further accommodation in the house includes a large living area, kitchen, lounge, two bathrooms, and utility room.\r\nThere is also further accommodation in a separate building on site which subject to the necessary planning permits would make for lovely additional guest accommodation or can be rented out for extra income.\r\n\r\nThe area of Ballynew is steeped in natural history and there is evidence of ancient settlement throughout this whole area. Indeed, there is a genuine Court Tomb in the garden of this property which is estimated to be circa 5,000 years old. This house is tastefully and lovingly decorated and we believe it would make a wonderful family or holiday home. The touristic fishing village of Cleggan is located only 5 minutes by car away and from here the ferry to Inishbofin Island departs. The larger town of Clifden is 15 minutes from here.\r\nPrice: \xe2\x82\xac595,000\r\nBER: D1.
