    Positioned just off Monkstown Avenue, Laurel Bank is a secure, gated development on a tranquil, lesser travelled road just a few moments stroll form the bustling boutique village of Monkstown with its fashionable shops, restaurants and cafe s. It is a low density, aesthetically pleasing mix of apartments and townhouses with the added bonus of designated parking and ample visitor parking. No.27 is a lovely three bedroom home laid out over three levels which is nicely appointed and smartly presented throughout and set against a low maintenance rear garden. \n\nOn the ground floor there is a well-equipped kitchen with a classic cream Shaker style kitchen. The living/dining has a feature fireplace with wood burning stove and doors lead to the garden. On the first floor there are two bedrooms, one of which spans the entire width of the house, and there is a also a family bathroom. On the top floor there is a double bedroom with en-suite. \n\nTo the rear of the house there is a low maintenance garden mainly pebbled with a deck for al fresco dining. There is also garden shed. \n\nThe property is located in this extremely popular residential area close to a wide array of amenities in Monkstown and Dun Laoghaire. The area offers a choice of shopping and recreational facilities, including gourmet restaurants, boutiques, cafes and bars, whilst the seafront offers marine leisure facilities, year-round swimming and coastal walks. It is also close to a wide selection of excellent local schools including Monkstown Educate Together, Scoil Lorc in, CBC Monkstown, Rathdown and more. Public transport links are well catered for with the 46a, 4, 75 and 63 buses all nearby and it is a short walk to Salthill & Monkstown DART / train station.\n\n\nEntrance Hall wood floor and under stair storage\n\nKitchen 2.53m x 3.30m. tiled floor and splashback, cream Shaker style wall and floor units, integrated gas oven and hob with extractor fan, integrated fridge freezer, plumbed for dishwasher and washing machine, serving hatch to living/dining.\n\nLiving/Dining Room 4.46m x 4.14m. wood floor, feature fireplace with wood burning stove, recessed lighting, double doors to garden.\n\nBedroom 2 4.40m x 3.20m. double bedroom spanning the entire width of the house with built in wardrobes.\n\nBedroom 3 2.60m x 3.20m. with under stair storage and built in wardrobes.\n\nBathroom 1.75m x 3.20m. with tiled floor and part tiled walls, bath with shower, w.c. and w.h.b.\n\nPrincipal Bedroom 2.60m x 3.88m. double bedroom with built in wardrobes\n\nEn-Suite 2.60m x 1.09m. wood floor, w.c., w.h.b., wall mounted medicine cabinet and step in shower cubicle
