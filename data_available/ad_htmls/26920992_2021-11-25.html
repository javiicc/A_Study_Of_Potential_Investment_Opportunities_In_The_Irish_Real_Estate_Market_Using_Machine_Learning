    Description

      This stunning 2 bed, 2 bathroom A rated apartment is located in Jameson Orchard in the heart of Malahide. Situated at the top of Seamount Road in a quiet development, surrounded by stunning mature trees and enjoying panoramic views out across Dublin Bay & the Wicklow Mountains, Jameson Orchard offers a unique rental opportunity for those wanting a secure home in Malahide. \n\nThe development is situated adjacent to acres of parklands & coastal walks offering the ultimate in relaxed, coastal living. This stunning apartment has been finished to the highest standard. The impressive, light -filled accommodation comprises of an open-plan living space with floor to ceiling windows. This apartment is stylishly arranged and comes fully furnished, with integrated appliances. The large bright bedrooms have ample storage, one which has an en-suite with a walk-in shower. The apartment also has the added bonus of a private furnished balcony on which to appreciate the breath-taking views! \n\nJameson Orchard is situated in an ideal location and is within walking distance to Malahide Village, where there is a host of amenities on hand including schools, restaurants, retail and transport services including the DART and Dublin Bus. Dublin Bus has a stop which is a five minute walk from your front door which runs a route directly into Dublin City Centre. Dublin airport and the M1 Motorway are just a fifteen minute drive away. \n\nTaking in the magnificent surroundings this apartment has to offer and the fabulous location for its residence with Malahide Castle a leisurely ten minute walk away.
