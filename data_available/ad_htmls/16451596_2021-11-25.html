    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 210 m\xc2\xb2
    This spacious detached family home is located just outside of Crooke on the coast road between Passage East and Woodstown. The White House stands on an elevated south east facing site c. 0.5 acre with magnificent sea views taking in Creaden Head and the Hook peninsula. uPVC double glazed windows, oil fired central heating, C2 BER. \r\n\r\nThe property comes to the market in excellent condition having been recently upgraded by the present owners. The accommodation consists of: Porch, bright and roomy entrance hall with open fireplace and marble surround. Glazed double doors to living room and glazed door to kitchen. Spacious living room with stove in marble surround, arch to dining room. Dining room with built in storage, patio doors to garden and glazed pocket doors to kitchen. Bright and stylish kitchen / dining / living area with large island / breakfast counter and three Velux roof windows. Floor to ceiling built in units with integrated appliances. Hot water tap. Tiled utility with built in storage and door to garden. Five double bedrooms, master dual aspect with built in wardrobes and tiled ensuite shower room with power shower. Tiled family bathroom with Velux roof window, large bath and separate power shower. Guest WC off hall. \r\n\r\nPrivate gardens to front and rear in lawn, west facing patio to rear. To the side is a double garage with potential for conversion to additional accommodation or home office. Drive with parking for several cars. Convenient access to local beaches and coves as well as the popular coastal village of Dunmore East, Waterford City is only 9km distant.
