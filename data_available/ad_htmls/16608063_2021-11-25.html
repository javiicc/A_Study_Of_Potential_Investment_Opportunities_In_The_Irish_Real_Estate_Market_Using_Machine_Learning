    Property Partners are delighted to present 22, Bessborough Avenue to the open market. This hidden gem is excellently located 2/3 bedroom period terraced property in a quiet cud-de-sac off the North Strand in a much sought after residential area. This double fronted terraced house is smartly presented throughout with a kitchen extension to the rear. The property offers spacious and well-proportioned accommodation with lots of character and potential to make it your own. Internally, this property enjoys generously proportioned light filled rooms and briefly comprises of: an entrance hallway, sitting room, bedroom/music room, 2 further bedrooms, kitchen cum dining room and bathroom. In the large rear garden there is a shed and W.C.\r\nOutside to the rear low maintenance garden which offers complete privacy and is ideal for al-fresco dining. There is also shed for additional storage. To the front there is ample on street parking. The property is presented in good condition throughout. The benefit of this includes gas fired central heating and double glazed windows throughout. Number 22 is an ultra-convenient location, offers any prospective owner the best of both worlds - quiet, peaceful living in a tranquil setting, yet with the hustle & bustle of the vibrant City Centre being only a matter of minutes away. The villages of Fairview and Clontarf are right on the doorstep providing an excellent range of Shops, Supermarkets, Restaurants, Caf\xc3\xa9s and Bars including award winning Da Mimmo Italian restaurant and Cloud Picker cafe. The 3 Arena, Bord G\xc3\xa9\xc3\x82\xc2\xa1is Theatre, Croke Park, Fairview Park, Clontarf Seafront & Westwood Gym are all within the vicinity, and Temple Street Hospital, The Rotunda Hospital & The Mater Hospital are all only a few minutes drive away. East Point Business Park, IFSC, City Centre & Grand Canal Dock are within a 25 minute walking distance. Transport links to & from the City Centre and beyond, are in abundance, with many bus routes literally just outside the front door, on the North Strand road. In addition, Connolly Train/Dart station, Busarus and the Luas are just a 10-minute walk away. The Samuel Beckett and William Casey bridges further enhance the area and its ease of access to the South Docklands (Facebook, Google, LinkedIn etc.) Easy access is also enjoyed to Dublin Airport, DCU, Beaumont & Mater Hospitals,M1 and M50 motorways. The area is also conveniently located close to many prestigious schools. The new North Strand Greenway is now opened between the Docklands & North Strand Road.
