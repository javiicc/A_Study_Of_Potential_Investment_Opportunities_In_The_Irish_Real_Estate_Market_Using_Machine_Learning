To arrange an appointment, please email norma.healy@sherryfitz.ie\n\nSherry FitzGerald is delighted to present for sale this unique and utterly charming two-bedroomed cottage situated in the heart of Coachford village that has been fully restored and upgraded to a wonderful standard.\n\nThe property is set back nicely from the road and boasts a large, south facing garden to the front with a tarmacadam area offering private, off-street parking for several cars. \nThe garden path leads to the front door which opens into the beautifully presented, open plan kitchen / living / dining space which is nicely broken up by a fireplace with a wood burning stove and is flooded with natural light. \nThe remainder of the accommodation is briefly comprised of two bedrooms, an office space and main bathroom. \n\nImmediately upon entering, it is evident that great time, effort and money were invested to achieve such a tasteful, welcoming and functional home and it truly is a credit to the current owners in the way that they have seamlessly combined style and practicality. \n\nThis gorgeous cottage will strongly appeal to first time buyers, those looking to trade down or simply those who simply enjoy a minimalist lifestyle. \n\nThe location is ideal being within walking distance of all services and amenities Coachford has to offer to include local shop, GAA club, soccer club, two water skiing centres, secondary and national schools, church and is only a short drive from Farran Woods and Lee Valley Golf Club.\nKitchen / Dining Room 3.96 x 4.44. Beautiful kitchen / dining area that gets excellent natural light and features laminate wood flooring, floor and eye-level kitchen units, integrated appliances, kitchen island with pendant lighting and access to the rear patio via French doors. Open plan to living area.\n\nLiving Room 3.29 x 4.44. Cosy living space which is open plan to the kitchen with laminate wood flooring and fireplace with wood burning stove.\n\nOffice 2.92 x 2.07. Incredibly practical multi-purpose room to the rear of the house, currently in use as a home office. There is tiled flooring, recessed lighting and access to the rear patio.\n\nBedroom 1 3.11 x 2.36. Positioned to the front of the house, this bedroom overlooks the garden and has beautifully incorporated the original fireplace and door.\n\nBedroom 2 3.22 x1.94. Located to the rear of the house, this bedroom features laminate wood flooring and centre light.\n\nBathroom 2.24 x 2.07. Three-piece electric shower suite with attractive tiled flooring and shower walls.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nHeading west from the city along R618, go in to Coachford Village passing the petrol station on your right.
