    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    APP Kirrane Auctioneering are honoured to be favoured with the opportunity to bring this 49 acres approx. residential farm with outbuildings to the open market. \r\n\r\nLadies and Gentlemen this is the complete package. 49 acres of good quality farmlands located in Aghamore, Co. Mayo which is known for its high quality producing dry uplands.\r\n\r\nThe three bedroom residence has been recently extensively renovated and would make a beautiful family home or holiday rental/Air B&B.\r\n\r\nThe farm buildings comprise of 2 bay double slatted shed with suspended passage plus 3 bay double lean-too and 3 calving cubicles with separate cattle crush. Silage slab with dimensions 30ft x 90ft. \r\n\r\nThe land speaks for itself. The majority is in one plot on the same side of the road as the residence and farmyard.\r\n\r\nThere is a plot of 8.57 acres (3.47 hectares) across the road with road frontage onto two roads that would make it ideal for development potential for a couple of one off houses subject to receiving planning permission. Again, this is ideal for Family members to build there own one off family homes in a beautiful countryside setting.\r\n\r\nThe land is currently laided out in a mixture of grazing, silage ground with 5 acres in bird cover until December 2021 and 1 acre planted for Christmas trees.\r\n\r\nThis is a must see property with great potential for a young farmer to start their own enterprise or extend the existing family farm. The farm will be sold with entitlements 18.91 hectares with value of 314.16 per hectare approximately. Grants received in 2021 were in the region of 12,000 euro in total.\r\n\r\n It is being offered in one lot but if the market dictates it could be sold in two or more lots.\r\n\r\nViewings strictly by appointment with the Sole Selling Agents APP Kirrane Auctioneering.\r\n\r\nThe lands for sale are located on Folios MY32679, MY32678, MY4504. Mayo. \r\n\r\nAll offers are to be placed through the Auctioneers by email and proof of funding will be required.\r\n\r\nDue to Covid 19, all persons viewing properties are required to bring and wear their own face mask throughout the viewing.
