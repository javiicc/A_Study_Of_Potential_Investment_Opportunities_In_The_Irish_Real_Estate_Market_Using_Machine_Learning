Situated in the village of Monagea approx 10 minutes drive from the town of Newcastle West. \r\n\r\nThis property is ideally located across the road from the Monagea National School and adjacent to the church and parish hall. Built in c. 1890 this stone property benefits from beautiful stone work facade. It is serviced by oil fired central heating, mains water and septic tank.
