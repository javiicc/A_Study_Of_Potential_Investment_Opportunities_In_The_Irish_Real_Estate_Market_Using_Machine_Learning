    This wonderful four bedroom home is an opportunity not to be missed. Situated in a quiet cul de sac close to the Train Station, and within a few minutes walk of open space, schools and sports amenities. The property has been updated, extended and lovingly fitted to give a now very spacious, modern, open plan family home in one of the most convenient locations in Skerries.\n\nThe well planned extension gives an extremely bright and spacious open plan living/dining /kitchen area of approx. 55m/600sq ft on the ground floor as well as a superbly large master bedroom on the first floor. The living area interacts seamlessly through solid oak bifold glass doors with the sunny, south-westerly rear garden.\n\nThe walled garden also contains a large solidly built games room with utilities to the rear of the property which could usefully be used as work from home office, hobbies room or for many other creative uses and pastimes.\n\nSt. Patricks Close is within easy walking distance of a wide range of town amenities including two beautiful beaches, town park amenity area, town centre, railway station and is close to both primary and secondary schools. Skerries is renowned for its awarding winning restaurants, numerous cafes and many attractions including Skerries Mill and Ardgillan Castle and gardens.
