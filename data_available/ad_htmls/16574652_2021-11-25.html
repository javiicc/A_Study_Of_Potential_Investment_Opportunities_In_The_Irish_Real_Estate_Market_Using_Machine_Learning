    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to introduce 4 Rathmore Villas to the market. A simply stunning and unique two bedroom cottage, nestled away just off Terenure Road North. Having been fully refurbished and interior designed by Wesley O Brien from top to toe to an exceptional standard. This sublime home is perfectly suited as a city base, a sound investment or charming home and is sure to appeal many a discerning buyer.\n\nThis cottage has been very cleverly designed to maximize the space with an excellent balance of reception and bedroom accommodation. A fantastic open plan kitchen/living/dining room which has been designed to maximise natural light throughout the space. A generous double bedroom, versatile reception room or additional bedroom and a family shower room complete the accommodation. The property is further complemented with a door leading to a private and granite paved south facing patio garden. There is also rear access to a rear pedestrian laneway. \n\nLocated just off Terenure Road North, this cottage is within walking distance of Terenure, Rathgar and Rathfarnham Villages. It has every conceivable amenity on its doorstep, some of which include excellent local shopping facilities, primary and secondary schools, several fine delicatessens, restaurants and leisure facilities at Bushy park, not to mention good transport links to the city centre and M50.\n\nEarly viewing comes recommended! \n\n\nEntrance Hall 1.25m x 1.08m.\n\nLiving Room 2.84m x 5.44m.\n\nKitchen 2.59m x 3.57m.\n\nShower Room 2.56m x 1.21m.\n\nBedroom 1 2.81m x 2.63m.\n\nBedroom 2 2.18m x 2.57m.
