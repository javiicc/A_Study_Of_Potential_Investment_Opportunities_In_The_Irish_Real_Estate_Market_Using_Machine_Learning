    We are delighted to present for sale this most attractive three bedroom end-of-terrace property within a small development, in a mature residential area of Fermoy town. This unique historical residence, extending to c. 1445 sq. ft., was once part of the military barracks which was built in c. 1860. The property boasts many beautiful features such as carved limestone exterior with decorative red brick panels, arched windows, ornate cornicing and spacious rooms with 9 ft. ceilings, filled with an abundance of light. Accommodation consists of kitchen/dining area, living-room, utility, 3 bedrooms (master en-suite) and bathroom.
