    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Maypark Lane is a leafy suburban road, the convenience of this remarkable location cannot be rivalled throughout the city. Adjacent to a wealth of amenities including University Hospital Waterford, Waterpark Rugby Club, Waterford Castle and Golf Resort and superb shopping facilitates including Ardkeen Shopping Centre, The Uluru Bar and various restaurants, all of which are within walking distance. This detached family home is quietly nestled on a mature corner site which offers complete privacy from the world and yet is situated in the heart of the most sought after address Waterford City has to offer.\r\n\r\nBanff Lodge is a spacious bungalow accessed via wrought iron gates and surrounded by a stone wall. The accommodation comprises of an impressive entrance hall with a guest WC off, a bright living room which features exposed timber beams and an impressive brick fire-place, family dining room, kitchen, utility and two sunrooms, one east facing and one west facing rear. There is also three bedrooms, the main bedroom is ensuite and family bathroom.\r\n\r\nThe large mature gardens offer an oasis of calm and tranquility and there is ample parking for several cars in the drive. There is also the possibility to develop a second property on the site, subject to securing the necessary planning permission (circa 0.5 acres).\r\n\r\nUntold possibilities await the lucky buyer.
