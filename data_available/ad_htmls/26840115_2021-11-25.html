      JB Kelly is proud to bring this modern, luxury detached property to the market which was constructed in 2020 and located in the idyllic setting of Grey's Lane, Howth. \r\n\r\nWith accommodation of over 3,000 sq ft, no expense has been spared on the exceptionally high quality finish throughout. Architecturally designed with flare and distinction, this contemporary home is sure to impress. \r\n\r\nGenerous accommodation spans over two level, the ground floor boasting a spacious high-spec kitchen/dining room with utility room, downstairs bathroom, a bright living room with an interconnecting room with pocket sliding doors, ideally placed to extend either room or to use as a play room. There is also a valuable home office on this level. Light pours through the centre of the house through the imposing entrance lobby to the second level landing area. The first floor consists of four bedrooms - the master with walk-in wardrobe and en-suite bathroom, two bedroom with en-suites, family bathroom and additional bedroom. All bathroom feature stylish contemporary fittings.\r\n\r\nThe high specification of this property includes an air-to-water heating system contributing to it's A1 Building Energy Rating, electronic blinds and a feature mood lighting system throughout. Landscaped garden with parking for several cars to the front and garden to rear. \r\n\r\nHowth has an array of amenities and facilities to offer. This property is located 5 minutes drive to several primary and secondary schools, including Sutton Park School. Howth DART Station offers commuters a 30 min journey to Dublin City Centre.
