    A great opportunity to acquire a superbly presented 4 bedroom bungalow of approx. 1400 sq ft., with a double car garage of approx. 370 sq ft. The location of this property is excellent with it being just a 4- minute drive from Newtownforbes, a 10-minute drive to Longford town, approx. 1 hr drive from Sligo and 1.5 hrs from Dublin all via the N4. The N4 is within easy access to the property making it an ideal location for the commuter.\r\n\r\nThis property comprises of a tiled entrance hall, coat closet, and hotpress. To the right of the hallway is the living room that is carpeted and has a large bright window and a coal effect gas fire. The dining room is also carpeted and has a sliding door that leads outside. Off the hallway is a tiled kitchen/dinette that has a door leading to the utility room and a W/C. There is access outside of the utility room. Bedrooms1, 2, and 3 are double rooms and bedroom 4 is a double en-suite room with an electric shower. All the bedrooms are carpeted and have built-ins. The main bathroom is fully tiled with an electric shower and a bathtub. Features include OFCH, alarm, and double-glazed windows and doors. Outside there is a wrap-around tarmac driveway, large garden, double car garage, concrete yard, and a 2-bay shed. There is also a large garden to the rear of the house which is ideal for the garden enthusiast. It is serviced by mains water and a septic tank.\r\n\r\nThis bungalow is located in a much sought-after area and viewing is highly recommended. BER: D1
