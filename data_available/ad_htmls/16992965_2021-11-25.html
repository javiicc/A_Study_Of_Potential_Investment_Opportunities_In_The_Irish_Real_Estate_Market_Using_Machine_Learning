    The property is situated in the heart of Kildare close to the villages of Nurney & Kildangan & enjoys country living yet still within close proximity to villages with schools, shops & sport clubs. There is good access to the M7 Kildare (9 minute drive) & Monasterevin (14 minute drive). The M50/ Spawell/ Redcow is (40 minute drive). The area is renouned for its equine industry with Kildangan Stud (5 minute drive), Irish National Stud (11 minute drive), Curragh Racecourse (15 minute drive) & Punchestown Racecourse/ Naas (32 minute drive). Kildare Town & Village is (10 minute drive) and has Public Transport with Bus & Train.\n\n\nThis is a superb modern two storey four bed, three bath upgraded residence with converted garage & separate block build workshop. The property has just got new flooring, kitchen & bathrooms. In total the residence extends to c. 184sq.mts / 1980sq.ft. Accommodation comprises of large entrance hall, two good sized sitting room, sun room, large kitchen & utility with W.C. Upstairs has four double bedrooms, family bathroom & master ensuite.
