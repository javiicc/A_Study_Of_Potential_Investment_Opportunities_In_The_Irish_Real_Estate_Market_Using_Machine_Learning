    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    Fantastic opportunity to acquire a two-bed bungalow in the peaceful area of Hollymount. Located on the outskirts of Carlow town the property is within an 8-minute drive to Carlow Town Centre. \r\nThis private property is nestled behind a large beech hedge to the front giving privacy to the residents with a side gate allowing access to the large garden to the rear with the whole site measuring circa 1 acre. The property has a lovely patio area to the rear of the property leading to the large lawn with scattered trees to the rear and side giving privacy. The garden would suit any buyers with a keen love of the garden. \r\n\r\nThe property was an original cottage that was extended by the current to add a tastefully designed sitting room/ sunroom and dining area. The property would benefit from a further extension to the back and side subject to the relevant planning permission. \r\n\r\nThe property comprises an entrance hallway, dining area, and adjoining sitting room, utility, family bathroom, kitchen with wood burning stove, double bedroom, and master bedroom with patio doors to garden and ensuite. \r\n\r\nViewing is highly recommended and strictly by appointment only.
