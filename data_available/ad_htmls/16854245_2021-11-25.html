    GVM present to the market a truly magnificent "Trophy" 4 bedroom executive style two storey detached residence standing on a superb circa 0.8 st acre site of lush and mature gardens and maintained in truly superb decorative condition throughout. This wonderful home must be seen to be appreciated and is accessed via a striking natural stone wall entrance, automated gates to a sweeping tarmac driveway arriving at a large set down area with generous parking. The living and bedroom accommodation extends to Circa 2,500 sq. ft of bright, practical and well proportioned rooms creating an oasis of contemporary living in a location that is very much sought after by those aspiring to acquire a quality home that really oozes location location location. The property is located just off the main road linking Limerick to Newcastle West (N21), just 1 km from Croagh and 9 km from the idyllic and renowned tourist village of Adare. There is ease of access to marvellous primary schools and top class sporting facilities including Croagh GAA Grounds. Adare Manor together with the beautiful local walkways and parks all add to the ambiance and popularity of the area.
