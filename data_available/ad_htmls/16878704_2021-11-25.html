    37 Dunbur Park is a detached, 2 storey house that simply oozes potential, in the highly sought after location of Dunbur Road in Wicklow Town. This wonderful property is superbly situated, just a couple of minutes walk from Wicklows Main Street, Wicklow Golf Club and beautiful Wicklow harbour. There are stunning sea views upstairs to the front of the property.\r\n\r\n37 Dunbur park is in good condition throughout but it does need renovation and modernisation. There is clear potential to add a substantial extension to side and to the rear (subject to the relevant planning permissions). \r\n\r\nDownstairs there is a cosy living room with open fireplace, a large open plan kitchen/dining room over looking the back garden, a sunroom. Upstairs there are three spacious bedrooms and a family bathroom.\r\n \r\nThe property is fitted with double glazed windows and uses an oil fired central heating system. \r\n\r\nExternally the property includes a front garden mainly in lawn with a tarmac drive and parking area.. The rear garden is west facing and also mainly in lawn. There is a small patio area and the entire is surrounded by mature hedgerow (which could be cut back considerably to create more space). The is also a large garage that adjoins and is accessible from the house. The garage space could easily be used for a substantial side extension.\r\n\r\nAccommodation comprises: Entrance Hall, Living Room, Kitchen/Dining Room, Sunroom, Boiler Room, Bedroom 1, Bedroom 2, Bathroom, Bedroom 3.\r\n\r\n37 Dunbur Park is an absolute gem, in a very highly sought after area of Wicklow Town. While undoubtedly the property needs renovation and modernisation, it really has endless potential to be a beautiful family home in a beautiful location. Don't delay, this one could go quickly!
