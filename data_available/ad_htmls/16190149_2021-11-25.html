    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Sherry FitzGerald Geraghty offer Cloghan, Ardcath to the market, a lovingly cared for 2 bedroom bungalow standing on approx. .29ha / .72acre with integrated garage to the side, walled front garden, ample parking, storage rooms in the attic space and a generous kitchen/breakfast room with a Stanley Oil Fired Cooker as the focal point.\n\nThe property is located in a quiet rural setting off the Garristown/Ardcath road, having easy access to the M1 and M2 Motorways and has potential to expand the living space allowing a would-be-purchaser create their dream home to suit their own requirements. \n\nThis lovely home, with lots of potential is ready for immediate occupation and viewing comes highly recommended\n\n\nEntrance Hall (stair access to storage rooms in attic)\n\nSitting Room having stove insert, storage cupboard and coving\n\nKitchen Dining Room The kitchen area is fitted with storage cupboards, has a breakfast seating area and a Stanley Oil Fired Cooker with Oil Burner for the heating system. The Airing cupboard is housed here and the tank is lagged\n\nRear Hall Access to garden from here - door double glazed\n\nBathroom Fitted as a wet room, this area is fully tiled, has a Mira Elite shower fitted and window for natural light and ventilation\n\nBedroom 1 This double bedroom has two sets of fitted wardrobes\n\nBedroom 2 This bedroom is positioned at the rear of the residence\n\nAttic Space Converted to storage rooms - an excellent addition to the house\n\nOutside Garage and Lean-to, having front access and side access to the rear - electrics laid on (lights and sockets)\nPump House for well water supply\nTarmac driveway and parking area\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom Garristown Village, with the church on your right, proceed towards Ardcath village. Turn right at Goughs (former shop/fuel pumps) and proceed for approx. 1.6km / 1 mile. The residence is located on your right hand side - see Sherry FitzGerald Geraghty for sale board.
