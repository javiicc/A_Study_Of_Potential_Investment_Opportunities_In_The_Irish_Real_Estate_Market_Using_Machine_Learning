    OCPM are delighted to bring 11 Thornchase, a spacious, well-presented 3 bedroom semi-detached family home to the market. Downstairs comprises of an entrance hall, guest WC, spacious kitchen cum dining room to the front, and a large sitting room with patio doors leading on to a large rear garden. Upstairs comprises: three generous bedrooms (master with en-suite) and a family bathroom. There is communal parking to the front.\nNo 11 is located in a quiet cul-de-sac in Thornchase, a small well-built development just outside Rush, a very popular coastal village. Within walking distance of Rush village with a wide range of amenities including sports clubs (including golf, cricket, sailing, football, GAA and tennis). On your doorstep are stunning North and South beaches, pubs, shops, restaurants, Tesco supermarket, primary and secondary schools. The area is also well serviced by Dublin Bus with the M1, M50 and Dublin airport all within 20 minutes' drive.
