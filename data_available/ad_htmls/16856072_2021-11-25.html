    Wonderful opportunity to acquire a residential site (Subject to Planning Permission) extending to approx. 0.8 acres, in a picturesque location on the outskirts of Aughrim Village. \n\nThe site enjoys road frontage on two sides and boasts beautiful views of the surrounding countryside.
