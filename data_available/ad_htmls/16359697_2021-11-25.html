    Sherry FitzGerald is proud to introduce Portland House a most attractive end of terrace 5 bedroom Victorian residence; a truly beautiful family home, or lucrative investment property, located on the highly popular tree-lined South Circular Road. Enjoying beautiful period detailing and well proportioned accommodation throughout, this is a really special property that has been carefully maintained and upgraded over the years by the current owners.\n\nExtending to 164 sq m / 1,765 sq ft (approx.) the accommodation comprises at ground floor; welcoming porch and hallway, two interconnecting reception rooms awash with period detail, dining room and kitchen. Externally there is a utility area, outside wc and an enclosed storage space.\n\nUpstairs there are three well-proportioned double bedrooms (each with tiled en-suites and showers), a single bedroom and a main bathroom.\n\nThe fifth bedroom in the attic is an original feature and benefits from a shower room. This is a versatile space, ideal as a home office or guest bedroom and is full of natural light.\n\nThe south facing rear garden has been designed with low maintenance in mind and provides the all important street rear parking, accessed via vehicular gates onto Madison Avenue. There is also pedestrian rear laneway access.\n\nThe location of this home is fantastic being within just a short stroll of a wealth of amenities both social and essential including an abundance of shops, bars and restaurants. The discerning purchaser would also be in close proximity to St James s Hospital, the new Children s Hospital site and a selection of Dublin Bus Routes, Luas and Heuston Station, and many of the city s main business headquarters around St. Stephens Green and the IFSC.\n\nSure to be of appeal to a wide variety of purchasers, No.555 has it all location, space, charm and endless appeal.
