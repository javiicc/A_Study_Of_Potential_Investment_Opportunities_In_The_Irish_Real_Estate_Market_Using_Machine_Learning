    A rare opportunity to purchase a substantial residence on land within an easy commute of Clonmel, Cashel and Fethard. Brought to the market by P.F Quirke & Co. Ltd. Donoughmore, Lisronagh is a fine five bedroomed residence being offered on 2.75 acres with an option of a further seven acres to the rear. The property is in outstanding condition throughout and accommodation includes an Entrance hall, Living room, Dining room, Sunroom, Kitchen/Diner, Utility room, Bathroom and Study-Bedroom 5 at ground floor with four Bedrooms and two Bathrooms at first floor. Outside is a large garage, lawns and paddock. The property is surrounded by well maintained beech hedging, post and rail fencing, electric gates and has fabulous views towards Slievenamon. Also available is 7 acres to the rear of the dwelling currently in agricultural use. These properties do not come to the market often.
