    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 136 m\xc2\xb2
    The Station House was once the residence of the Station Master for Manulla Train Station and has now been lovingly renovated into a spacious 4 bedroom family home. The house sits in the middle of a c.1 acre site: the front garden has views over Lakeland Lough to Croagh Patrick and the Partry Mountains while the back garden is very secluded.\r\n\r\nAfter entering the house through the front porch, the original internal walls have been removed to allow for an open plan living area comprising of an office space, living room, kitchen and dining area. The living room and office space has a raised ceiling with sky light and feature beams, and retains the charm of the original cottage, while the modern extension, containing the kitchen and dining area, opens out through French doors to the patio in the back garden. Both the porch and kitchen/dining area floors are finished with travertine tiles.\r\n\r\nDown the hallway there are 2 double bedrooms on the right hand side while at the end of the hallway is a larger double bedroom with ensuite. All of these rooms are part of the original cottage with lovely splayed windows getting the morning light.\r\n\r\nThe master suite is to the back of the property with the bedroom enjoying views over the garden. There is a large walk through wardrobe, giving access to a spacious ensuite.\r\n\r\nThe main bathroom has both a bath and separate walk in shower, with all of the rooms finished to a high standard with travertine on the floors and walls.\r\n\r\nThe back garden has been completely transformed and is where most of the outdoor living is enjoyed. There are two split level lawns divided by a winding path, bordered by a wall of railway sleepers. The path leads down to the very back of the property which has been left to nature by the current owners. The patio is bordered by railway sleepers and finished in Indian sandstone and there is also a large garden shed, with two further covered outdoor storage areas.\r\n\r\nThere is a small store room accessed at the side of the house and also two large attic spaces, one of which has been floored. \r\n\r\nThe house has oil fired central heating with the stove in the living room connected by a back boiler to the hot water tank.
