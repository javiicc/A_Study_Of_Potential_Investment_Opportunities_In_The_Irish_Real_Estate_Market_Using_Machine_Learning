    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    DNG are delighted to present a superb opportunity to acquire this charming end of terrace family home in the heart of Dalkey. \r\nThis beautiful home offers flexible and well laid out accommodation with 3 bedrooms, master ensuite and main bathroom. Sea views from the master bedroom and bathroom. A reception room and guest shower room just off entrance hall and a large light filled kitchen/dining/living space to the rear. There is also a side passage offering ample storage. The property is in superb condition throughout. Other features include attractive timber flooring, fitted kitchen and island, light filled accommodation with ample storage, guest shower room, double glazed windows throughout, lovely sunny rear garden and vaulted ceiling in the large living room to name a few. There is off-street parking to the front. \r\n\r\nThe village boasts a host of amenities to include the best of schools, shops, cafes, fine dining, famous pubs and churches of all denominations. There is also an abundance of leisure facilities to include GAA Club, tennis, sailing & rugby clubs as well as beautiful stunning coastal walks from Dun Laoghaire Pier to the Hills of Dalkey and Killiney. Sea swimming has become extremely popular in the area and you are only minutes walking distance from Hawk Cliff. Transport is well catered for with the Dart at Dalkey station and bus routes all within 5 mins walk. \r\n\r\nViewing highly recommended.
