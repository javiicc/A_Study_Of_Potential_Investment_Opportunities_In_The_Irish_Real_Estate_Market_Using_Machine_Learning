    DNG Spillane present to the market a mixed residential investment property in a popular location of Harbour Row in Cobh town. This building consists of a, commercial unit on the ground floor with separate access door to the over-head one bedroom apartments. The property is being sold with tenants unaffected and lends itself as an ideal investment property given its location and potential to increase the current rent.. The commercial unit is suitable for a wide range of usage and enjoys a high profile setting with volume of passing vehicular and footfall traffic.
