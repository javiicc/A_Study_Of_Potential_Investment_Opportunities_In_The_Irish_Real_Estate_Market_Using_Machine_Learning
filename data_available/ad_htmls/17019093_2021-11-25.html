    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to present to the market 7 Woodley Park, a delightful light filled three bedroom semi-detached home located on this popular tree lined road in the heart of Kilmacud. Boasting a private rear garden and having been lovingly maintained by the same family over many decades, this charming residence is brimming with scope for a further expansion for future generations. It is ideally situated close to a number of highly regarded primary and secondary schools, not to mention the Kilmacud Luas stop. \n\nThe accommodation is comprised of a welcoming and bright hallway leading to interconnecting reception rooms; the living room is to the front and a dining room overlooks the rear garden. There is a spacious kitchen/ breakfast room with double doors to an exceptionally large conservatory room providing access to the rear garden. From the kitchen is a second internal hallway providing access to the garage, guest w.c and utility area. On the first floor lie three bedrooms; there are two spacious double bedrooms both with built in wardrobes. The main bedroom is to the front with views of the Dublin mountains. There is also a spacious single bedroom. A separate shower and w.c complete the accommodation. There is also access to a spacious attic. There is unmistakably potential for further expansion of this home at both ground and first floor level as many near neighbours have done relevant to planning permission being obtained. To the rear there is a private garden, mainly laid in lawn with mature shrubbery. At the rear of the garden is a sun trap, a perfect space is for young families to play. \n\nKilmacud & Stillorgan is renowned for its wealth of shopping and leisure facilities. There is an abundance of amenities located within a short stroll, including shops, Airfield Trust and numerous leisure facilities. In addition, a number of the country s premier junior and senior schools are located nearby, such as Mount Anville, St. Laurence s, St. Theresa s primary school, Blackrock College, Colaiste Eoin and UCD Belfield. The area is well serviced with excellent transport links, with the M50, N11, QBC bus routes and the Luas at Kilmacud.\n\n\nEntrance Porch leading to\n\nHall Laminate wood flooring, under stairs cloak room.\n\nReception Room 3.94m x 3.80m.
