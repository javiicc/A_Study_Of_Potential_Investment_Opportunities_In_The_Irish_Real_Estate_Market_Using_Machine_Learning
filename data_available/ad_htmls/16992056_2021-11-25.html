    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Sherry FitzGerald are pleased to introduce No 54 Foxfield to the market. This immaculate mid-terrace townhouse is an ideal first time buy or downsize option, set to the rear of a beautifully maintained residential cul-de-sac at the heart of Dooradoyle. \n\nThe popular development of Foxfield is comprised of attractive red-brick family homes, set within minutes of Dooradoyle s Crescent Shopping Centre. The area benefits from a superb selection of residential amenities, including award winning childcare services, a selection of high end gym & sporting facilities, shops, restaurants, and primary & secondary schools. Both St Pauls & St Nessans National Schools are within walking distance, as is the highly regarded Crescent Comprehensive College. The area offers immediate motorway access via the Rosbrien exit, in addition to plentiful public transport links.\n\nNo 54 is comprised of a generous entrance hall on the ground floor with modern laminate wood flooring, leading through to the open-plan living/dining area to the left. Wonderfully bright, with an excellent flow of natural light throughout, the room is finished with attractive wood flooring and complete with a feature fireplace at its heart.\n\nThe solid-wood fitted kitchen to the rear of the home contains inset appliances, plentiful storage space and a fully tiled floor and splashback.\nA beautifully presented rear garden offers a low-maintenance patio, complete with attractive planting hemmed by raised flower-beds. \n\nOn the first-floor there are two large double bedrooms, each with built in-storage, in addition to a beautifully fully fitted and newly refurbished main bathroom.\nThe first floor has newly fitted neutral grey carpets and the property has been fully painted.\n\nOutside is a low maintenace pebbled front with mature shrubs and ample parking.\nThis is a most attractive towenhouse and Viewing comes highly recommended as soon as possible.\n\nACCOMMODATION:Ent Hall, Living/Dining, Kitchen, 2 Bedrooms (2 Double), Main Bath & Hotpress\n\nSERVICES:70 Sqm Approx. BER: C3, GFCH, Private Rear Garden, Off Street Parking
