    ''San Antoine'' is a well-kept 3-bedroom terraced home situated in Clontarf Estate, a mature residence just off Skehard Road. Having all major amenities nearby such as, Mahon Point Shopping Centre, Scally's SuperValu, Blackrock National Hurling Club, and with a regular bus service right on its doorstep, this location is perfect for those desiring a lifestyle of convenience. \r\n\r\nThe property is in good condition throughout but needs modernisation, an ideal opportunity for a first-time buyer to turn this into a warm family home. The property benefits from being sold with vacant possession and the discerning purchaser will know this is an opportunity not to be missed! \r\nThis property extends to 97 Sq. m of accommodation that boasts spacious rooms and a main feature would be the large rear garden which offers maximum privacy. \r\n\r\nAccommodation: \r\n\r\nHall: 4.2m x 3.6m carpet floor, 1 radiator, understairs storage, doors off to living room, sitting room and kitchen/dining. \r\nLiving room: 4.5m x 3.4m carpet floor, one large window facing to front of the property, open fireplace, one radiator, one light fixture. \r\nSitting room: 3.9m x 3.5m carpet floor, stone feature open fireplace, 1 window looking out to rear of property, one radiator, one light fixture. \r\nKitchen/Dining: 4.3m x 2.6m laminate floor, eye and floor level units, electric cooker and hob, built in oven, one window facing out to rear garden, door off to rear garden.\r\nW.C.:1.8m x 1.5m tiled wall, W.C. and W.H.B\r\n\r\nFirst floor: \r\nBathroom: 2.7m x 1.8m tiled walls and floor, 3-piece suite, ''Triton'' electric shower. \r\nBedroom 1: 3.9m x 3.6m laminate floor, built in wardrobe, one window overlooking rear garden, one radiator. \r\nBedroom 2: 3.5m x 3.4m laminate floor, built in wardrobe, one window overlooking front of property. \r\nBedroom 3: 3.1m x 2.5m carpet floor, one window, opportunity to turn this room into a study/walk-in-wardrobe if necessary. \r\n\r\nOutside: Concrete steps to front door, laid to lawn, mature shrubbery. \r\nA large private rear garden, with 2 garden sheds and mature hedging. \r\nDetached garage with roller shutter.
