This fine Victorian residence is ideally situated on one of Dublin's most popular, convenient and highly sought after squares. \r\nNo. 28 is a light-filled, elegant family home which retains many charming period features including ornate cornicing and coving, period fireplaces, original floorboards and sash windows.
