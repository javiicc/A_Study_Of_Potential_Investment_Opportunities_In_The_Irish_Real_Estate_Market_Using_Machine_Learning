    DNG are delighted to represent the sale of 22 Brookwood Park, Artane, a substantial 3-bedroom semi-detached family home. This stunning home offers the potential purchaser a great opportunity to put their own stamp on it. This magnificent home will be of special interest to young families looking for a quality home in a highly sought-after location. To the rear is a secluded west facing garden with side access and benefits from a huge garage. \n\nThe accommodation extends to a total floor area of c. 1098 sq. ft. and comprises entrance hallway, living room, kitchen/dining, reception room and garage. Upstairs, first floor accommodates 3 spacious bedrooms and a large family bathroom. This stunning property benefits from a large rear garden with second garage at the back. \n\n22 Brookwood Park is next to none when it comes to location. There is an excellent bus and rail service within a short stroll giving direct access to the city centre and a host of local amenities closeby. St. Marys Holy Faith Secondary School is a 2 minutes walk away. Property in this condition and location are in high demand and interested parties are advised to act quickly to avoid disappointment.
