    McGregor Properties are delighted to offer for sale this three bedroom (1 en-suite) detached bungalow known as "Forest Fields " situated at Inch East, Lisselton, Listowel, Co. Kerry. The property is located on the main Listowel/Ballybunion road and approximately 4km from the Heritage Town of Listowel and 4km from the seaside town of Ballybunion and in turn key condition. The accommodation comprises the entrance hallway, sitting room/dining room, kitchen, utility room, WC, sun room, living room, three double bedrooms including the master bedroom en-suite and a fully fitted family bathroom. Features include PVC double glazed windows & doors, oil fired central heating system, mains water, sewerage by septic tank, large 1 acre (approx.) site with well maintained front and rear lawns with mature trees and hedging, block built garage with up and over door and a gravelled driveway. Fixtures and fittings only passing with the sale. The wooden storage shed at the rear of the property will not be passing with the sale.
