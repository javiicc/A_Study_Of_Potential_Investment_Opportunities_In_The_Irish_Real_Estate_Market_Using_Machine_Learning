    WELCOME TO SHELBOURNE HOUSE DISTINCTIVE APARTMENTS, \r\nLOCATED IN THE MUCH SOUGHT AFTER AREA OF\r\nTHE NORTH CIRCULAR ROAD IN LIMERICK, \r\nOFFERING ELEGANT, CONTEMPORARY, LIVING IN UNIQUE & HISTORIC GEORGIAN SURROUNDINGS. \r\n\r\nOriginally named Shelbourne, this house was the home of Sir William Petty-FitzMaurice, Second Earl of Shelbourne, and Marquis of Lansdowne. It was inhabited by the Russell family in the 1880s, who built the Flax Mill on North Circular Road, later taken over by the Cleeve family who extended the mill into what became the Golden Vale complex. The brick-making McNamara family inhabited the house at the turn of the twentieth century. The school dating to 1955 was designed by Robinson, Keefe and Devane.\r\n\r\n\r\nShelbourne House Distinctive Apartments have been carefully & beautifully restored with large Georgian floor to ceiling windows flooding each area with natural light & with bespoke interiors creating incredible, elegant living fused with all the modern conveniences whilst retaining its Georgian period character. Upon opening the grand Georgian doorway, an inviting, splendid entrance hall with a magnificent fireplace welcomes you and gives stair and lift access to all floors and apartments. \r\n\r\nShelbourne house offers four luxurious apartments all of which have large, bright, open, living spaces and spacious double bedrooms and all are finished to the highest of standards, luxurious d\xc3\xa9cor and specifications. These beautiful unique apartments offer discernible, spacious, bright, modern, living set in historic splendour. The apartments come with secure private car parking and communal landscaped garden/patio area with seating.
