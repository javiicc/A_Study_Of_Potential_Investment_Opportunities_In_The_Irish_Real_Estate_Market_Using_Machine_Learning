It is clear that great time, effort and money have been invested here over the years with the current owners maintaining and upgrading the home to a fantastic standard. \n\nTo either side of the entrance hall is a reception room with the remainder of the ground floor accommodation extending to a large kitchen, dining room, sunroom, utility room and wc. \n\nOn the first floor, you will find four very generous double bedrooms (main bedroom en suite) main bathroom and a Jack and Jill bathroom between bedroom 2 and 3. \n\nThe rear of the property enjoys a lovely south westerly aspect and features a large patio and decking area to make the most of the sunshine. There is also a low maintenance gravel area with a fish pond, as well as a lawn area lined with mature trees and a large detached garage. \n\nThis wonderful family home is the perfect example of how to seamlessly combine style, practicality and functionality and is an opportunity that should not be missed.\n\nEntrance Hall 4.51 x 5.03. This room features carpet flooring, centre light x2 with ceiling roses, open fireplace and a bay window.\n\nKitchen 6.31 x 3.63. Large kitchen with granite worktop and splashback, tiled flooring.
