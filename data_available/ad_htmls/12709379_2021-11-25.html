    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Cois Cuain a unique 5 bedroomed residence in excellent condition, new to the market. Cois Cuain is a newly renovated, stone faced bungalow with tiled roof located on a panoramic site overlooking Portnahally Bay at Ashleam, on the south west side of Achill Island.\r\n\r\nThis property offers an idyllic residence for family or potential tourist accommodation or a retirement haven.\r\n\r\nCois Cuain, is a well appointed property situated south of the Ashleam road on approximately 0.22 hectares or 0.54 acres in area. The property is completed with boundaries walls, tarmacadam driveway, lawn garden front & rear and seating area on the south side of the residence overlooking Pornahally Bay and the Atlantic drive.\r\n\r\nCois Cuain comprises of approximately 143 square meters in area and consists of:- Glass porch 2.15m X 1.25m, Kitchen fully fitted 3.2m X 5.3m with interconnecting sitting room and open fire 4.2m X 3.5m, entrance hall 1.83m X 4.05m, Bedroom one with fitted wardrobes 2.4m X 3.27m, Bedroom two with fitted wardrobes 2.7m X 3.27m, Bedroom three with fitted wardrobes 3.4m X 3.37m, Bedroom four 3.3m X 2.99, Bedroom five with ensuite 4.2m X 4.9m, Bathroom 1.5m X 3.27m.\r\n\r\nGarage: The garage comprises of 15.28 square meters in area\r\n\r\nServices: The property is serviced with water, electricity, septic tank and oil fired central heating.\r\n\r\nCois Cuain has magnificent sea views from the sitting room and the three bedrooms on the south west side. This is a turn-key property and viewing is highly recommended.\r\n\r\nRef No: A1041 Ber Rating: E2
