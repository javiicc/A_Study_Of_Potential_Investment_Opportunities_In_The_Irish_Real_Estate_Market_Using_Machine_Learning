    Prime Prominently Situated Spacious Residential/Commercial Property.\nFormerly a popular restaurant and bar this centrally situated property offers an excellent investment opportunity with a wide variety of development opportunities. The premises offers spacious living accommodation to its first and second floors with the commercial area being contained on the ground floor. Located within the popular harbour village of Castletownshend this premises affords an enthusiastic investor the opportunity to create and develop their own business within one of West Corks most picturesque coastal villages.\nGround Floor: Entrance Lobby, Bar/Restaurant Area, Kitchen, Prep Rooms, Store Rooms, Cold Room, Ladies & Gents Toilets, Entrance Hall.\nFirst Floor: Kitchen, Living/Dining Room, Bedroom, Bathroom.\nSecond Floor: 2 Bedrooms (1 En-Suite).\n\nServices: Mains Water & Drainage, Electricity, Oil Fired Central Heating.
