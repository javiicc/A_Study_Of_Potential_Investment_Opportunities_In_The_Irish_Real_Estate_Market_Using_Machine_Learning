    Seamount Rise offers modern, stylish living in a breath-taking coastal setting. All homes at Seamount Rise combine modern design and high-quality finishes. Residents will benefit from a new 13-acre park with playground to be developed by Fingal County Council, adding to the abundance of scenic open spaces and parklands that Malahide already offers. Seamount Rise boasts great transport links with bus services to the airport and city centre and the added benefit of Malahide Train Station, just a 15-min walk away offering both DART and suburban commuter links.
