    The property consists of two development sites with full planning ((Ref P10/1013) which is now out of date) for 2 detached residential units with garages on large sites of 0.24 Ha.each. Ideally located in a highly sought after area on the outskirts of Ennis Town in a popular residential area. This is indeed a rare opportunity to acquire 2 sites, situated in a highly sought after area on the outskirts of Ennis Town.
