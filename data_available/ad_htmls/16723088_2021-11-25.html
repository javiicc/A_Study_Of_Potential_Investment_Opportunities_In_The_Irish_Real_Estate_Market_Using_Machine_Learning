    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to present to the market 123c Malahide Road. \n\nBuilt in 2018 and located in a small enclave of houses, No. 123c is superb property in turn-key condition and boasts an A3 energy rating. Set over three floors, this impressive three-bedroomed family home is both light filled and spacious with excellent quality fixture and fittings throughout. The accommodation briefly comprises; welcoming entrance hallway leading to a bright front reception room with large picture window. Off the hallway there is also a guest wc and utility and to the rear is a generous extended open plan kitchen/dining/family room with a glass wall and glass sliding doors out to the private south-west facing rear garden. The stylish kitchen is fitted with wall and floor level units with ample worktop space and tiled splash back. On the first floor there is a bright landing with skylight and two spacious double bedrooms both with built in wardrobes and en-suites and a large contemporary family bathroom. There is another double bedroom on the second floor with built in wardrobes. The south-west facing garden is very private as it s not overlooked, it s mainly laid in lawn and is bordered by a selection of plants with a patio space ideal for entertaining during the summer months. The low maintenance garden to the front provides off street parking.\n\nThe location of this fine property is very convenient and the area is well serviced by regular bus routes with both Killester & Clontarf Dart Stations also within a short 1.5kms. Set in a quiet and highly sought-aftersecond of the road with a host of amenities and facilities within a short distance including local shops, primary and secondary schools, IFSC, Beaumont Hospital and within easy access of the city centre, DCU, Dublin Airport and main artery routes M50 and M1. Sporting enthusiasts are spoilt for choice with numerous facilities close by with a selection of clubs including tennis, rugby, cricket, Gaelic, sailing, Westwood Gym and golf at Clontarf Golf Club, Royal Dublin and St Anne s Golf Club. The ever-popular St Anne s Park, Fairview Park and Dollymount Strand are also easily accessible. Locations don t come more central or family friendly!\n\nViewing is highly recommended to appreciate this wonderful family home along with the prime and desirable setting.
