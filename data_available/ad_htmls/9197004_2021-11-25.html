    Development Description

      * * * New release of 3 & 4 bedroom Semi-Detached + Final 4 Bed Detached just released overlooking the canal and Connaught Street * * *\n\nLedwill Park first opened its doors in the summer of 2019 and to date this beautiful development has secured in excess of 100 sales with many purchasers already moved in.\nComprising large 2, 3, & 4-bedroom terrace, semi-detached and detached homes, located within the townland of Branganstown, a short stroll from the town of Kilcock, County Kildare, Ledwill Park offers modern homes to those starting out or seeking a larger home within a thriving community approximately 35km west of Dublin.\n\nKilcock train station is less than a 10-minute walk from the development which services Kilcock to Dublin with the Arrow train, taking approximately 25 mins to Connolly Station.\nThe M4 interchange is less than 5-minute drive from the development and the travel time to Dublin city centre off peak is 40mins (35 km) via the M4.\nBus ireann has regular services via routes 115 and 115 A running from Dublin to Mullingar via Kilcock.\nKilcock has three primary schools, Scoil Choca Naofa, St. Joseph s BNS and Gaelscoil U Riada and is home to secondary school Scoil Dara, located on Church Street.
