    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    Bealkelly, Ogonnelloe, Co. Clare \nHOLDING OF LAND\n \n42 Acres approx. of agricultural farmland in a slightly elevated rural setting, situated 2.5km from Tuamgraney and 3kms. from Ogonnelloe village. (Scariff 5kms. and Killaloe 15kms.)\nFor Sale by Private Treaty\nThis is a north-facing farm of land on the western side of the Bealkelly road and it has road frontage of 130 metres approx. on to this road.\nThe farm contains c.42 acres and has a cattle-crush close to the road. It is all in grazing land. It is of mixed quality, ranging from very good to fair, it is all in one large block, laid out in a number of individual fields suitable for grazing. While much of the land is clear there are areas with undergrowth but with reclamation this could be made into a very productive farm.\nThe lands stretch back from the road, along by a stream that makes its northern boundary. \nThis is a good-sized division of land which may be of interest to a neighbouring landowner or anyone requiring a substantial division of land as an outside farm. \nThe land is currently rented on a lease expiring on 31st December 2019. \nNon-residential farms of this size and quality, in such a convenient roadside location, are a rarity on the market in this locality.\nInspection is recommended by appointment.
