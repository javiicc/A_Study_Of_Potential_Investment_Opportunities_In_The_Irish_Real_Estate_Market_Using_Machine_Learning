    Charming 3 bedroom cottage on a large mature site with approximately 4 acres of land. The house was renovated in recent years and would make a great family home or holiday home. The house is set back from the road and maintains an elevated position on the site. There are a selection of stone and block built outbuildings to the rear of the house which could be used for storage but also could be renovated to provide additional accommodation.\nThere is a small orchard and an area for growing vegetables to the side of the house. There is also a frame of a greenhouse on the site which would just need to be recovered. The additional land would easily accommodate a number of horses or other livestock. \nThe property is just over a mile to the N17 Galway to Sligo road and Kilkelly village.. Ireland West Airport Knock is only 6 miles away\n\n\nSun Room 5.8m x 3.3m. With lino flooring, exposed wooden beams as a featured. Painted tongue and groove celing. Nice views of the surrounding countryside.\n\nSitting Room 4.4m x 4m. With lino floor, in-set stove with a marble surround and coving to the ceiling.\n\nBedroom 1 3.3m x 3.5m. Double room to the front of the house with a solid timber floor and coving to the ceiling.\n\nWalk-in Wardrobe 2.3m x 1.2m. With a carpet floor and shelving.\n\nEnsuite 2.3m x 2m. With a lino floor, a large tiled corner shower unit, wash hand basin, vanity unit, WC and a heated towel rail.\n\nUtility Room 3m x 1.5m. With storage for the washig machine, oil boiler and the hot water cylinder.\n\nKitchen 2.2m x 3.8m. With a slate effect tiled floor, solid timber fitted units, a wood burning stove with a back boiler, electric hob and belfast sink.\n\nBathroom 1.8m x 2.2m.
