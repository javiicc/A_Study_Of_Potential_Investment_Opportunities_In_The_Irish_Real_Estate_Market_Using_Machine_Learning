    Sherry FitzGerald is pleased to present 36 Sandyford Downs to the market - an attractive Sorohan built four bedroom semi-detached family home that has been designed to maximize natural sunlight throughout the living accommodation. Situated in a quiet cul de sac location within this highly popular and much sought after development close to a range of exceptional amenities and just a short stroll to Sandyford village. \n \nExtending to 110sq m (1,185sq.ft) the accommodation comprises an inviting entrance hall which leads to a lovely bright reception room. Double doors lead to the light filled dining room which in turn leads to the kitchen. Upstairs, there are four bedrooms (principal bedroom en-suite) with a family bathroom completing the accommodation. The front of the property boasts off street parking with gated side access leading to a private low maintenance garden, perfect for al fresco dining. There is a large block built shed. All in all, this home certainly caters for any growing family, combining well-appointed accommodation and a wonderful location, to create the perfect mix for modern living. \n \nA description of this property would not be complete without mentioning its convenient location. Tucked away in this quiet residential enclave just off the Dundrum side of Sandyford Village, the property is within easy reach of all the amenities that Dundrum and Stillorgan have to offer. Within minutes of the Sandyford / Stillorgan Business Park and the LUAS providing speedy access to St Stephens Green. The M50 and Luas is only a short distance away and there are a host of leisure facilities nearby to include Leopardstown Race and Golf Course and not forgetting lovely walks nearby in the Dublin Hills. \n\nSure to appeal to many, viewing of this property comes highly recommended!\nEntrance Hall with timber flooring and storage closet\n\nLiving Room 4.03m x 5.29m. Timber flooring and sliding door to rear garden.\n\nKitchen 2.90m x 3.42m. Fully fitted with wall and floor units, sink, oven, hob, fridge/ freezer. Double bedroom with timber flooring, fitted wardrobes.\n\nBedroom 3 2.73m x 2.63m. Double bedroom with fitted wardrobes.\n\nBedroom 4 2.71m x 2.73m. Single bedroom with laminate flooring and fitted wardrobes.\n\nFamily bathroom Full bath, w.c and w.h.b. Skylight.\n\nFront Exterior Cobble locked driveway provides parking for 2 cars.
