    For Sale by Private Treaty. Beautiful family home situated in quiet mature surrounds on the outskirts of Tuam town. No 36 Millstream Park is a distinctive four bed semi-detached property that will appeal to purchasers seeking a property in turn-key condition at particularly good value. \r\nExternally the setting is private, towards the rear of the development on a cul de sac. \r\nUpon arrival to this home you are greeted by a tree lined driveway that has the benefit of good off street parking.\r\nThe ground floor is arranged between a large bright sitting room with double doors leading to the Living room. An open-plan Kitchen & Dining area complete the downstairs layout. Upstairs offers four bedrooms with fitted wardrobe space and a family Bathroom. There is oil fired central heating with open fireplace in the Sitting Room.\r\nExternally there is a garage attached 5.32m x 2.32m. There is a private garden to the rear with patio area. All services mains supplied & excellent broadband signal available.\r\nViewing highly recommended, strictly by appointment through Property Partners Long, High Street, Tuam, Co Galway 093 28218.
