    MOVEHOME ESTATE AGENTS ... are delighted to bring Number 7 Clonturk Avenue, a two bed terraced property spanning approx. 50 sq.mtrs / 538 sq ft. to the open market. This little hidden treasure is discreetly located off the main Drumcondra Road and is a wonderful blank canvas that requires modernisation throughout. No. 7 hosts an entrance hall, living room, kitchen area and access to the downstairs bathroom. Upstairs originally hosted two bedrooms, a double and single room yet the layout was changed slighly with access to the second bedroom is currently from the main double room. The rear garden is low maintenance, private and north facing and gives the new owners an opportunity to extend further subject to P.P. \n\nThe location of Clonturk Avenue is brilliant within minutes of all amenities, The Botanic Gardens, for tranquil walks and the more urban amenities of Drumcondra and Fairview close by for socialising. Third level colleges, Dublin City University and All Hallowes are within easy walking distance. Trinity College Dublin and University College Dublin are both on direct bus routes, along with a variety of local sports clubs including Na F\xc3\xa9\xc2\xadanna GAA Club, Clontarf Golf Club and Shelbourne FC. Located within walking distance of the City Centre and convenient to the numerous shops, cafes, bars and restaurants Drumcondra Village have to offer. Easy access to Dublin Airport, Beaumont, Mater & Temple Street Hospitals, DCU and Trinity and the M1 & M50 motorways. The area is very well serviced by a great public transport system. The green open spaces nearby and its close proximity to the city make it an unique location.
