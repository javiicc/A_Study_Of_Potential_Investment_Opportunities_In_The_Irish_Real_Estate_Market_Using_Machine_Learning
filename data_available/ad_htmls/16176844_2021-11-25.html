No. 20 is one of the original houses that lined either side of this wonderful city centre side street. \r\nWilliam Street is located in the centre of Kilkenny City and directly opposite the Town Hall. \r\nNo. 20 William Street is literally a stone throw off High Street. The property is ideally located for use as a family home or a commercial business.\r\nThe layout to the house is set over 3 floors with the ground floor having a rear extension added. \r\nNo. 20 is all about location and potential and using the huge amount of space that the house holds. There is work to be completed on the property but the site and location of it is truly amazing.\r\nRenovation projects and upgrades are always about end results, based upon the basic criteria of property type and location. Number 20 William Street has both. Stepping into No. 20 you are very much aware of the space to the ground floor. Ok it does need work but the potential to develop this into a family area, ground floor apartment or commercial space, is enormous. There is ground floor rear access off of Guard Lane which runs from High Street at the back of William Street.\r\nThe first floor of No. 20 offers similar potential, massive space with oodles of character and light. Access is easy from the ground floor, with a wide slow stairs making it easy to get there. Again, the top floor has the same potential, plenty of character and space.\r\nThe layout of two double rooms on each of the upstairs floors give great options. The main bathroom is located on the first floor. You really have to look at the overall potential of this property.
