    Sherry FitzGerald is proud to present to the market number 18 Clonfadda Wood, built in 1996, this is an impressive and cleverly extended four bedroom detached family residence providing 207.2sq.m/ 2.230sq.ft of accommodation. This house has been lovingly maintained and well-presented by its current owners. This exclusive gated development is ideally positioned just off Mount Merrion Avenue, ideal for those looking for a first-class family home in a private and secure setting for young children to play in a safe haven. Located close to a wide choice of leading schools such as Willow Park, Blackrock College, Mount Anville, Loreto Foxrock and St Andrews and close to UCD and the bustling Village of Blackrock & Stillorgan, the DART and sea front.\nInternally the well-appointed property is bright and spacious. As you enter, you are greeted by the spacious entrance hall. The hall also leads to the lounge spanning the full length of the house and has access to the rear garden. The light filled kitchen/dining room is well equipped and also opens onto the garden. The family room is another lovely bright room with two sets of double doors leading to the rear garden and paved patio area and there is a lovely, pitched roof light which floods the whole area with natural light. The ground floor is finished with a study and a guest w.c. Rising upstairs there are four double bedrooms, one with en-suite, and all with built in wardrobes. There is a family bathroom which completes the accommodation on the first floor. \nThe property is located on a large corner site and benefits from side access from both sides, leading to the spacious c. 80 ft. wide (max) rear garden which is mainly laid in lawn a perfect space for children to play and for entertaining and relaxing!\nThis is a wonderful opportunity for a wide range of discerning purchasers- Early viewing is advised!\n\n\nEntrance Hall 4.22m x 4.37m.
