    DESCRIPTION\r\nMichael Pigott Auctioneer & Valuer is delighted to offer for sale this wonderful 0.35 acre site situated in the hugely popular & exclusive coastal location in Myrtleville.\r\nThe site comes to the market with ready to go full planning permission to build a modern contemporary detached property consisting of 2,517 Sq ft, enjoying beautiful views over the surrounding harbour.\r\nIt has the luxury and convenience of being within walking distance to the well-established and highly popular Lodge Bar & Restaurant, Bunny Connellans Bar & Restaurant and the local shop (O' Connells). It is just minutes drive to Crosshaven village and Carrigaline town where a host of amenities both social and essential are available and it is a short commute to Cork City Centre, Cork Airport and Ringaskiddy\r\nFEATURES\r\nF.P.P for a detached dwelling (234 sq m/ 2,517 sq ft)\r\n0.35 Fully serviced site\r\nDelightful views over harbour\r\nMinutes walk to Myrtleville Beach\r\nMinutes walk to Bunny Connellans & The Lodge Bar & Restaurants\r\nShort drive to Crosshaven\r\nShort commute to Cork City, Cork Airport & Ringaskiddy\r\n\r\nDIRECTIONS\r\nFrom Carrigaline, drive down the Crosshaven Road passing Carrigaline GAA Grounds. Pass Carrigaline Business Park and take the next right (signposted for Fountainstown/Myrtleville). Continue on this road and keep left at McWilliam Sailmakers for Crosshaven. Continue up the hill for Crosshaven and just before Crosshaven rugby club, turn right signposted or Myrtleville. Continue on this road towards Myrtleville, driving down to the beach and follow the road around to the left. Drive up the hill a few hundred metres and turn left into Atlantic View. Upon entering the development, follow the road around to the left and continue down to the hill, with the site situated at end, indicated by our For Sale sign.
