    **VIEWING BY PRIVATE APPOINTMENT - TO BOOK YOUR TIME SLOT PHONE KATIE DIRECTLY ON 0872416913**\r\n\r\nKATIE CHADWICK PROPERTY SERVICES are delighted to present The Coach House to the sales market.\r\n\r\nFormerly the stables of Kilgarron House, The Coach House has been lovingly converted, upgraded and cared for by its current owners for the past 30 years.\r\n \r\nLord Powerscourt originally built Kilgarron House for his daughter, Lady Wingfield, on land that then formed part of the vast Powerscourt Estate. Kilgarron was subsequently home to a Mrs Dobbs, who sold it to the famous author Frederick Forsyth. Forsyth lived in Kilgarron House during the 1970s before returning to the UK in 1980 when he sold to the Plunkett family. The Plunkett Family subsequently divided Kilgarron into several separate properties and, in 1985 sold what had been the stables of the main house to the current owners; creating The Coach House. \r\n\r\nThe Coach House offers generously proportioned living accommodation to the discerning purchaser. \r\n \r\nThe accommodation briefly comprises; a double ceiling height, inviting entrance hall with cloakroom and downstairs wc, a spacious kitchen/ dining / living room with the added bonus of a wood burning stove and door to rear patio area, as well as a shower room & w.c. \r\n\r\nDouble doors from the living area lead to a separate sun room, again offering doors leading out to the sunny rear patio area. Further downstairs accommodation comprises a home office, utility room, drying room and a snooker room with further garden access via a conservatory ideal for entertaining.\r\n\r\nUpstairs boasts a sunny landing area with a balcony overlooking the entrance courtyard, leading to the master bedroom with wall to wall built in wardrobes, walk in wardrobe area, a spacious master ensuite with shower, bath and steam room as well as double doors to a decked balcony area. A shower & wc, a mezzanine area overlooking the kitchen and three further bedrooms (one ensuite) complete the upstairs accommodation.\r\n \r\nSeparate guest accommodation with own entrance, easily incorporated back into the main house - comprises a modern fully fitted kitchen, wc, spacious living area with wood burning stove and a double bedroom with ensuite and patio area. \r\n \r\nOutside offers the most unique, quirky setting with numerous different garden areas and nooks and crannies including decked balconies, unique patio areas, a stone round tower as well as an entrance courtyard and rear patio garden with sheltered dining/entertaining area ideal for a cooler summers evening barbeque. A separate car port also offers car parking for two cars with the added bonus of a large overhead secure storage area. \r\n \r\nThe Coach House is located in this much sought after area, the charming village of Enniskerry, only a short stroll away down Kilgarron Hill, which offers a variety of shops, cafes, restaurants and boutiques.\r\n \r\nEnniskerry is renowned for its scenic beauty and the location is nothing short of idyllic yet just a short drive from the N11/M50 which connects to Dublin city centre and airport.
