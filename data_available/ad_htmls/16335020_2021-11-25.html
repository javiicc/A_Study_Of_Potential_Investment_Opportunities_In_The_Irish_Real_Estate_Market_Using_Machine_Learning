    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to bring Mandeville to the market. this is a detached property situated on a quiet residential road within easy distance of Limerick city, excellent primary and secondary schools, Limerick lawn tennis club and the M7 road network. This makes Mandeville the ideal choice for any family looking for convenient city living with the benefit of a private mature site and off street parking.\n\nMandeville is a detached property with garage to one side and it offers very obvious potential to the new owner to extend and improve the property to suit ones own needs. the garage is suitable for conversion to additional living space or home office and there may also be potential to extend the first floor accommodation subject to planning permission being granted.\n\nAt ground floor level there is an entrance hallway which opens into two very spacious and bright reception rooms which open out to a sun room to the rear. There is an open fireplace and a bay window to the front of the living room.\nThere is a breakfast room and a kitchen also off the hallway and a guest w.c.\nat first floor level there are three bedrooms. 2 doubles and a single, there is also a large family bathroom and a hot press.\n\nTo the front of the property there is a lawned garden with off street parking and access to garage. There is a side entrance to the rear garden which is in lawn with shrubs, hedge and paved patio area.\n\nAccommodation: Hall, living, dining, breakfast room, kitchen, sun room, guest w.c. 3 bedrooms, bathroom, Garage,\nOFCH\nBER E2
