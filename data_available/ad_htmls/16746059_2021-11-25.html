    This stunning 3 bed family house come to the market in show house condition with little or no work required. Located in a modern, popular development close to the town centre, the property is nicely situated within a small cul de sac close to a large green area at the front of the development. Accommodation briefly consists of Livingroom with double doors to kitchen cum dining room, upstairs there are three bedrooms with one master en-suite, main bathroom. \n\nHampton Gardens is situated along the Naul Road, almost adjacent Millfield Shopping Centre and west of Balbriggan Town. This is a very convenient location within a very short walk of the beach, many sports clubs and Balbriggan town centre with an array of restaurants, bars, cafes, cinema etc. Shopping is available close by and schools are immediately at hand St George s national school adjoins the site. The development can access the M1 motorway and Balbriggan Town in only a few minutes drive.
