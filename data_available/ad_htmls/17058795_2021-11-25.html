    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to bring to the market No.5 Tonyville Mews which is a well presented, three-bedroom, mid terrace home that is located just off High Street. No.5 benefits from the addition of an attic conversion as well as having parking to the rear of the property. No.5 is ready and waiting for its new owners to move in, unpack and stamp their own individual mark on it.\n\nThis mid-terrace home offers a great balance between living and bedroom accommodation with a welcoming entrance hall, a kitchen/dining area, living room and a conservatory on the ground floor. On the first floor there are two double bedrooms and a home office/bedroom. Access is also provided from the first floor to the attic space. No.5 also benefits from a private, gated yard to the rear as well as a lawn area to the front.\n\nNo.5 is situated in a mature residential area off High Street with an off-street parking space. This home is within walking distance to local shops, schools, bus stops and all of life s essential amenities. \n\nThis is a great opportunity to acquire an end of terrace home in a nice mature location. \n\nDo not delay arrange a viewing today!\nEntrance Hall 2.97m x 1.87m. Bright and spacious entrance hall that consists of a laminate wood floor and gives access to the kitchen/dining area and the sitting room.\n\nKitchen/Dining Area 3.82m x 2.87m. Located to the front of the property and overlooks the front garden. With fitted with floor and eye level style units, this well-proportioned kitchen/dining area has a laminate wood floor and a tiled splashback. The kitchen is plumbed for a washing machine and dishwasher.\n\nLiving Room 3.77m x 4.85m. This well-proportioned reception room is located to the rear of the home and overlooks the yard area. The living room benefits from a solid fuel burning stove, laminate wood floor and provides access to both the conservatory and a storage closet.\n\nConservatory 1.74m x 2.50m. The conservatory is a bright, airy and spacious. It is accessed from the living room and provides access to the yard at the back of the house.\n\nLanding 3.89m x 1.82m. This area gives access to all bedrooms, hot press and the main bathroom. Access is also provided to the attic space from here.\n\nBedroom 1 3.30m x 2.94m. This is a bright and spacious bedroom with a window overlooking the yard area at the back of the house. It benefits from the addition of a storage space.\n\nBedroom 2 2.94m x 2.93m. This is a bright and spacious bedroom with a window overlooking the front garden. It benefits from the addition of a storage space.\n\nHome Office/Bedroom/Storage 1.28m x 1.82m. Bright space that can be used as a home office, single bedroom or an additional storage space. Located to the front of the home and overlooks the front garden.\n\nMain Bathroom 2.18m x 1.83m. The family bathroom comprises of a three-piece bath suite with an electric shower over the bath. There is a window to the rear which provides the area with natural ventilation.\n\nAttic Space 3.40m x 3.11m. This is a bright and spacious area which benefits from a large Velux window that fills area with natural light. This space is currently being used as an additional bedroom.\n\nAttic Ensuite 2.34m x 0.79m.
