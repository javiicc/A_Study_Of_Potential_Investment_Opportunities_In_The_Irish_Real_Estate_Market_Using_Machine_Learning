    Located in the centre of Goleen village, these two prominent properties enjoy a prime location with street frontage and substantial grounds to the rear. \n\n\xe2\x82\xacHartes\xe2\x82\xacTM measuring c. 4,000 sq ft, is laid out over three floors. In an imposing corner location, it has frontage to the Main Street and the road to the eastern side, which leads to the adjacent harbour and pier.\n\nThe second property, formerly \xe2\x82\xacHarringtons\xe2\x82\xacTM also has street frontage and is in need of extensive restoration & renovation. \n\nOn c. 0.75 acres, the properties benefit from substantial gardens and an outbuilding to the rear. \n\nThis rare offering to the market has excellent potential for a variety of uses in the commercial and/or residential sector.\n\nLocated on the Mizen Peninsula, Goleen is a popular tourist destination on the Wild Atlantic Way.
