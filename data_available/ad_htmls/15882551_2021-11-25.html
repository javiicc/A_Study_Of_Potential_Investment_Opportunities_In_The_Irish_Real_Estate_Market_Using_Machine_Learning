    Perched on an amazing cliff top site of c. 0.6 acre overlooking the Atlantic Ocean Leacaban House is located next to the Old Head Golf Links and only a short distance to the blue flag beaches in Garrettstown, and Kinsale town. The site offers utmost privacy and uninterrupted spectacular views that can only be described as spectacular with sea and countryside views as far as one can see from the Old Head Peninsula, all the way towards Summercove and Hangman\xe2\x82\xacTM Point. \r\n\r\nThe single storey house is in reasonable condition and on a total living area of c. 130 sqm offers plenty of space. The spacious living room has large panoramic windows affording stunning views in every direction. The vaulted ceiling provides a fantastic sense of space. The kitchen with breakfast counter also affords fine views to the sea and the surrounding countryside. There is access to the dining area from the kitchen and then on into the living room. There are three double bedrooms and a Jack-and-Jill family bathroom. The master bedroom is en-suite. All bedrooms have built-in wardrobes.\r\n\r\nWhile the current house is in reasonable condition the stunning site offers tremendous potential for further development. \r\n\r\nThere is a large enclosed garden which is mostly laid to lawn. The large property offers plenty of off-street parking and an internal single garage.\r\n\r\n\r\nLocation\r\nLeacaban House is set into a spectacular elevated position overlooking the Atlantic Ocean and views as far as Kinsale harbour. Located on the Old Head Peninsula the property is beside the Old Head Golf Links and c. 15 minutes away from the pretty seaside town of Kinsale and 40 minutes drive from the cosmopolitan city of Cork. \r\n\r\nThis area of South West Ireland is of outstanding beauty with its dramatic coastline, unspoilt locations and safe beaches. Leisure activities are well catered for, Kinsale provides excellent sailing, yachting and fishing facilities from its two marinas. \r\n\r\nThere are two golf courses including the renowned Old Head of Kinsale. Other recreational pursuits include walking, bathing, surfing, and horse riding. \r\n\r\nThere are numerous cultural events held throughout the year, such as the world famous Kinsale Arts Festival, the Cork Jazz Festival, Cork Film Festival, Kinsale Gourmet Festival along with a thriving music and opera programme. Cork has several fine theatres, and Cork Opera House hosts many exceptional productions throughout the year. Kinsale is also home to some of Ireland\xe2\x82\xacTM finest restaurants, contributing to its title as Gourmet Capital of Ireland; along with many top class hotels and lots of welcoming pubs. \r\n\r\nCork International Airport is conveniently located 20 minutes drive from Kinsale with direct links within Ireland and to major European and UK cities.\r\n\r\n\r\nDisclaimer/Privacy Policy\r\nFinest International Real Estate Ireland Ltd. t/a Engel & Voelkers act as agents for the vendors or lessors of this property whose agents they are, give notice that: \r\n1.
