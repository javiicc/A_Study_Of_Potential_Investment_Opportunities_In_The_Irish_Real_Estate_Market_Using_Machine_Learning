    No 16 Silverpines is a well presented three bedroom semi-detached family home over looking a communal green area situated in an extremely popular residential enclave of Bray.\n\nOriginally a 4 bedroomed semi-detached house, two of the bedrooms have been amalgamated into one larger principle bedroom. The property is presented in a good condition throughout and has spacious light filled accommodation with a large separate living room to the front and a large kitchen/diner, which extends the width of the house at the rear leading to the sunny predominately south facing garden. Upstairs there are three bedrooms (two doubles and a large single) and a family bathroom.\n\nOutside to the front of the property there is off street parking for a couple of vehicles. There is also a communal green at the front of the property which is used for the residents enjoyment and children to play on. A side passageway leads to the rear garden which comes complete with a solid block built shed (ideal for storage or conversion into a small home office).\n \nThe property is centrally located within close proximity of both Bray main street and the seafront, which offers a variety of local businesses, shops, cafes, eateries etc. Bray is a popular coastal town with an excellent choice of both primary and secondary schools, supermarkets, transport links including the 145 Dublin Bus (which stops outside the development) and DART station and the N11 all providing easy access to Dublin and beyond. Other local attractions include Bray Head and the cliff walk, Kilruddery House & Gardens, the Mermaid Centre and Bray s famous mile long victorian promenade.\n\nViewing is strongly advised and and is strictly by appointment only\n\n\nEntrance Hall 1.72m x 4.83m.\n\nLiving Room 5.42m x 3.29m.\n\nKitchen Dining Room 5.28 x 3.58.\n\nBedroom 1 5.23m x 3.67m.\n\nBedroom 2 2.76m x 3.65m.\n\nBedroom 3 2.38m x 2.64m.\n\nBathroom 3.17m x 1.64m.
