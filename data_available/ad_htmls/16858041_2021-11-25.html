    Outstanding one bedroom south facing apartment overlooking the River Liffey.\r\n\r\nThis superb property features an entrance hall with wooden floors and a hotpress. To the left of the hall is a generous sized family bathroom with a tiled floor and partially tiled walls. The kitchen is situated to the right of the hall and also has a tiled floor, splashback tiling and plenty of counter & cupboard space. The kitchen is separate to the open plan living room & dining area. This bright & modern room is south facing with floor to ceiling windows and fantastic views of the River Liffey. The spacious double bedroom can be accessed through double doors from the living room and can also be accessed from the hall. The bedroom enjoys sophisticated fitted wardrobes and presses, wooden floors and views of the River Liffey. This beautiful apartment also benefits from a secure designated underground car space which is serviced by a lift.\r\n\r\nParkgate Place is ideally located on Parkgate Street which has plenty of shops, bars & cafes. The Phoenix Park is also on your doorstep as is Heuston Station & The Red Line LUAS. The City centre is easily accessible by foot, bus, bike or LUAS.
