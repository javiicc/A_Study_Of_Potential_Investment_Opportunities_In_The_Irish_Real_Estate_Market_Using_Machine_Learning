    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Jerry Flynn & Co are delighted to bring to the market this substantial detatched residence. This is a rare opportunity to purchase an exceptionally located residence with stunning views of the surrounding countryside. This 6 bedroomed large spacious home is for sale on the West Limerick Drive. The house comprises a very large Deck, 6 Bedrooms, 5 Bathrooms, Kitchen/Livingroom, Utility room, Diningroom, Sunroom, Livingroom, large entrance hall, Basement, Car port and large garage with potential to convert to a granny flat. The house is set amongst mature gardens with lawns, shrubs, orchard and fruit trees on a C. 0.74 acre site. This house is located C.1.7km from Abbeyfeale town, C. 32km from Tralee, C. 40km from Killarney, C.15km from Listowel, C. 30km from Ballybunion(nearest beach), C.54km from Limerick City, C.30km from Kerry Airport and C.41km from Shannon Airport. This is an ideal family home with many commuting options available.
