    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    DNG Brady are delighted to bring to the market this beautiful architect designed dormer bungalow on a C.1 acre site. On entering the property you are meet by a large spacious foyer, spacious kitchen & family room with adjoining conservatory, there is a second sitting room, bedroom and family bathroom with large jacuzzi on the ground floor. Upstairs you have 2 double bedrooms (master ensuite with built in storage) 2nd bedroom has walk in wardrobe and a single bedroom. Outside of the property has a beautiful tree lined gravel driveway with gardens to the front and a large decking area and private garden to the rear. The property is located in the beautiful countryside of Monasterboice just 10 minutes from Drogheda town centre and within easy access to M1 motorway just 2 minutes away. Close to all local amenities such as primary school, Donegans Bar/restaurant, GAA club and the town of Dunleer. Viewing of this property is highly recommended.
