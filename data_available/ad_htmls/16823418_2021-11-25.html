    This large south-facing family home on c. 2 acres is located in a beautiful countryside setting. The house has been finished to an exceptional standard by its present owners.\r\n\r\nThe spacious hall leads you to a large, open plan kitchen-cum-dining room which extends into the sunroom with garden views and access to the limestone paving patio. There is also a utility with built-in storage, a WC, and a further room which could be used as a playroom, study or accommodation. On the first floor is a master bedroom en-suite with jacuzzi bath and separate shower, and built-in wardrobe. There\xe2\x82\xacTM a further four bedrooms; one of which is en-suite, a family bathroom, and spacious landing with linen cupboards.\r\n\r\nFlagstone floor runs throughout the kitchen, sunroom, utility & hallway and wool carpets throughout the living room, bedrooms and landing.
