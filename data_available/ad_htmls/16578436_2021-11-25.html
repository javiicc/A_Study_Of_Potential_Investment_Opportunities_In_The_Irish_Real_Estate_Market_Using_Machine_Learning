    McGovern Estates is proud to present No.1 Blacklion Springs. This is a magnificent & spacious architect designed detached family home set on a private elevated site with walled mature south & west facing gardens. Situated in a private gated enclave at the end of a tranquil cul de sac of just 4 residences, No.1 incorporates commanding sea & mountain views. Constructed under conventional building methods using solid concrete block construction in 2003 by the current owners, the accommodation includes 5 bedrooms & 3 reception rooms all with double glazed Aluclad sash windows throughout. Internal viewing of this fine property is highly recommended.\r\n\r\nThe heart of this exceptional family home is in the open plan kitchen / dining area which incorporates an AGA and easy access through a stable door to the delightful south & west facing walled gardens. This room has high ceilings and also includes a living area and a utility room off. A large living room with a feature inglenook fireplace with wood burning stove is adjacent. A separate office / tv room also features.\r\n \r\nThe master bedroom boasts not only an en-suite & walk-in wardrobe but also a newly upgraded balcony with stunning sea views & overlooks the walled garden & neighbouring St. Killian's Church (Listed building). A magnificent games room (c.32m2) which boasts a pool table is tucked away from the main house. A large c.42m2 double garage / workshop also features. \r\n\r\nThe location is second to none with Greystones town & harbour & marina only a short walk away giving quick access to the Dart & Dublin city centre.\r\n\r\nGreystones Town is within easy reach and all your needs will be catered for with a wonderful selection of bistros, boutiques, restaurants and shops, the village library, churches and a large number of highly regarded schools.\r\n\r\nA host of sport and leisure clubs abound in the area including sailing, rowing, football, rugby, tennis and GAA.
