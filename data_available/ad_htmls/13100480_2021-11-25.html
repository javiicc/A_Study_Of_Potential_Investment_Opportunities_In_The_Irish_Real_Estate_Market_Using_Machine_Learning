    5 Bedroom Bungalow c. 213.5 m\xc2\xb2 (c. 2,298 Sq.Ft.) plus attached shed on the outskirts of Longford Town. \r\n\r\nSituated on an elevated site, upper Ferefad is a popular residential location with a number of one off houses. \r\nThe area is a five minute drive from the town centre and also convenient to the N4 Dublin Sligo road yet is surrounded by agricultural lands.\r\n \r\nThere is a large front sitting room with solid fuel stove that opens into the kitchen / dining areas and has double glass doors through to a living room with a high timber panelled ceiling and a gas stove. The kitchen has built in units and is complimented by a separate plumbed utility room and rear utility storage. There is also a west facing conservatory with high wood panelled ceiling. There are five bedrooms the master bedroom has a walk in wardrobe area and an ensuite shower room. There is a separate fully tiled shower room serving the other bedrooms. Attached to the house is a large bright workshop with substantial glazing. \r\n\r\nOutside there are lawns to the font of the house with mature shrubs and trees. To the rear there is a stone patio area that is south facing and a real sun trap.\r\n\r\n\r\nSERVICES: Mains Water & Septic tank, Oil fired central heating. \r\n\r\nEircode: N39 D4C8 \r\n\r\nGPS: 53.711, -7.767
