    Fabulous country living and space comes together to deliver this very impressive 4 Bedroom dormer style property with on a c0.6acre site. Beyond its attractive automated gated and grandeur entrance, the opulent property extending to Approx. 294m /3,150ft is positioned on beautiful mature and elevated site all overlooking the surrounding beautiful countryside landscape and fabulous sea views. This fine property is situated on its own idyllic site but potential purchasers also have the opportunity to acquire an additional c0.3 acre site with its own site entrance and outbuildings which may be suitable for refurbishment or renovation to that additional recreational space/home office/gym or even stables for the equine enthusiast.\r\nThe generous accommodation comprises of a Kitchen/Dining room, two very large reception rooms (Sitting Room & Dining Room) and another Family room, Office/Bedroom 4, Utility, Store and family Bathroom with Jacuzzi style bath all grouped off the striking reception Hall with its feature two-way staircase all to the Ground Floor. The First Floor is equally as impressive as the Ground Floor with its large 3 double Bedrooms that includes a suite style Master Bedroom with panoramic views and a recently refurbished Ensuite.\r\nThe property enjoys many desirable features such as a hand painted Kitchen, granite worktops with a free standing Italian made cooker/hob (gas & electric), elaborate marble fireplaces to both reception rooms together with decorative moulded centre pieces and covings to all ground floor accommodation.\r\nOutside there is an elevated direct south facing suntrap decking area overlooking the beautiful countryside with sea views and the mature landscaped gardens along with an extension and desirable tarmacadam surface surrounding the property.\r\nThis ideal home for the growing family looking to escape the hustle and bustle to countryside living is only 2.5km from the recently built Walshestown National School and 5.2km from Dunleer s Secondary School. The property is also conveniently located only 6.5km from Junction 12 on the M1 motorway, 11.2km form Drogheda and only a 40 minutes drive from Dublin Airport and the M50 orbital motorway. House and location must be seen and experienced to appreciate the full value of this home.
