    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    NEW TO THE MARKET - MARCH 2021\r\n\r\nFour bedroom dwelling house known locally as Doolough Lodge, which is placed on approximately 58 acres of excellent quality agricultural land, together with applicable basic farm entitlements. There is approximately 40 acres of land around the house and the remaining 18 acres is located across the Doolough road. There is also an additional 12.15 Acres of bog land included in the sale which is located on the main Belmullet to Geesala road (approx. 2 km from Doolough lodge).\r\n\r\nDoolough Lodge is accessed by a private 250 metre long road which leads into the property and outhouses in a secluded setting. The lodge which has an extensive floor area of 2,600 square feet enjoys attractive sea views from the front and is within walking distance to the famous Doolough Beach. \r\n\r\nThree of the bedrooms, the living area, the kitchen, a large storage area and the family bathroom are located to the rear of the property. \r\n\r\nThe living room which features a large back boiler stove (heats the water and radiators) is placed near the entrance to the property. The newly refurbished fully fitted kitchen & dining area are located off the living room. The main family bathroom was also refurbished in recent years and contains a bathtub and electric shower. \r\n\r\nAll of the four bedrooms feature high ceilings and fireplaces, three of which are situated to the rear of the house, the master bedroom is spacious and is placed to the front of the property with sea views. There are also two large reception areas to the front of the property, both of which feature high ceilings and have sea views. \r\n\r\nThe heating is mainly provided by O.F.C.H. and is assisted by solar panels which were installed in recent years and by the back boiler stove in the living room with double glazing throughout. \r\n\r\nThe outhouses contain eight individual rooms placed over two floors. There are also sheds and a courtyard area to the side of the property. \r\n\r\nThere are spacious gardens to the front and side of Doolough Lodge which is situated 4 km from the village of Geesala, 15km from Bangor Erris and 14km from the town of Belmullet. \r\n\r\n\r\nPlease see below a breakdown of the guide price,\r\n\r\n\r\nFour bedroom house - \xe2\x82\xac150,000\r\n\r\n58 acres of land - \xe2\x82\xac310,000\r\n\r\n12.15 Acres of bog land - \xe2\x82\xac15,000\r\n\r\n\r\nPlease contact us to receive a copy of the land maps, or for further information and pictures.
