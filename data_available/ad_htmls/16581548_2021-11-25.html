    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to present to the market No. 1 St. John s Drive, Clondalkin.\n\nThis beautiful two bedroom home comes to the market in very good condition, having been recently carpeted and painted throughout, with off street parking and newly installed Smart energy meter to the front.\n\nFrom the entrance hallway, the sitting room is located to the right and front of the property. \n\nTo the rear, the kitchen/dining area complete with fitted kitchen opens out on to a most beautiful and private garden, with plum trees, apple trees and raised flower beds, while a block-built garage, with electricity and plumbed for washing machine, is located at the end of the garden. This can be easily converted to a home office, studio or indeed a child s playroom.\n\nUpstairs, two double bedrooms and a family bathroom complete the living accommodation. \n\nSt. John s Drive is ideally located right beside the beautiful Corkagh Park, with its scenic walks, playground and wide open spaces, while the local shops are less than a five minute walk.\n\nThe development itself lies just off Naas Road, with the M50 only a five minute drive away, giving easy access to both Dublin City centre and Dublin Airport respectively.\n\nCall or email for viewing details.\nPrimary bedroom 3.3m x 3m.
