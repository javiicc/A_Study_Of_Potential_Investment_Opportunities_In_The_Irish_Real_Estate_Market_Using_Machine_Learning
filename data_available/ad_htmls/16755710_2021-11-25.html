    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    RE/MAX team fogarty are delighted to present No 8 Newtown Woods to the market. This stunning home is situated in a prime location in Newtown one of Waterfords most sought after areas. \r\nNo 8 is a spacious bright light filled home and enjoys a south westerly aspect to the rear and river views from the first floor.\r\nPrivate parking for two cars is available to the front of the house and there is side access to a lawned garden. \r\nAccommodation on the ground floor includes a spacious kitchen dining room, sitting room with open fireplace and access to the rear garden.\r\nThe first floor holds three bedrooms and family bathroom. The master bedroom is en-suite and opens to a sunny balcony overlooking the garden.\r\nNewtown is a much sought after neighbourhood and needs little introduction. Within walking distance are Waterford's premier schools including Newtown junior and secondary schools, De La Salle College, Waterpark, St Declan's, John of God's, Yeats college, also within walking distance is University Hospital Waterford, the Ardkeen shopping centre, Waterford city centre and the people's park. There are excellent sporting and leisure facilities close by including St.Anne's Tennis club, golf and rugby clubs. \r\nNewtown Woods is an exclusive development of 6 Apartments and 4 semidetached homes. Properties rarely come to the market in this prime location. This is a unique opportunity to acquire a wonderful home in a superb location.
