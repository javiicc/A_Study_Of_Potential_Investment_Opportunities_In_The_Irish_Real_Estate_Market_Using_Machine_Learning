    The possibility of acquiring a large period property on an acre of mature gardens in a secluded setting yet within 1km of Cork City seldom presents itself. This unique opportunity has arisen with the launch of Knockrea House on the Douglas Road to the market. The property offers an unbeatable location, impressive site size and substantial period house.\n\nKnockrea House is a spectacular property extending to approx. 348 Sqm (3,750 Sq. Ft) of accomodation spread over two floors and boasts many of its original features, offering spacious living and bedroom accomodation throughout. The property dating back to the early c.1820\xe2\x80\x99s is listed on the National Inventory of Architectural Heritage and is appraised as \xe2\x80\x9cKnockrea, previously known as Summerville, represents a modest early nineteenth-century house, which was modified later in the Victoria period with the addition of architecrural embellishments favoured at the time, including the pediments and surrounds to the windows and front door, and the canted bay windows\xe2\x80\x9d. \n\nThis remarkable home offers all the original features and characteristics that you would expect from a building of this age and architectural structure to include high ceilings, sash windows with shutters, stained glass in pannelled doors, original cornicing, centre roses and picture rails, plus gas fired central heating. \n\nThe accomodation extends to entrance hall, reception hall, drawing room, lounge, study, family room, kitchen, back kitchen, pantry, toilets and cloakroom all on ground floor level. The first floor is home to five double bedrooms and two guest toilets which completes this level. Access to the property is through large recessed double entrance gates with piers and a curved driveway lined with mature laurels leading to a gravel surfaced area with ample parking space. \n\nThis superb property sits on a large site of manicured lawned gardens extending to approximately 0.4 hectares (1 acre) with extensive planting of mature shurbs and trees benefiting from a southerly facing aspect. The property is situated in a higly sought after residental locaton just before the entrance to Knockrea Park and just 1.4km from Douglas Village which has a range of services and amenities including two shopping centres, shops, restauraunts, and pubs. There is convenient access to the city centre and the South Ring Road network.\n \nGROUND FLOOR \nEntrance Hall (2.23m x 2.11m )Feature front door leading through to reception hall.\nReception Hall (5.36m x 4.82m )Double doors with stained glass and fan light to include stained glass leads from the entrance hall through to this spacious and bright reception hall with winding staircase leading to first floor. Access to drawing room, lounge and family room finished with carpet flooring.\nDrawing Room (7.28m x 5.19m )Located to the front of the property with feature bay window and two large picture windows this room enjoys feature fireplace (although currently blocked off) and is ideal for entertaining . Original features to include cornicing, high skirtings and brass door furniture\nLounge (7.27m x 5.17m )A sister image of the drawing room this room looks in the opposite direction and captures the evening westerly sunshine. Fireplace fitted although currently blocked off.\nStudy (3.87m x 2.96m )Ideally located just off the reception hall, this room is a perfect study area with carpet flooring. Combined with the current kitchen this area could become the heart of the house once more. Side door to gardens.\nPantry (2.01m x 2.00m )Located to the back of the kitchen.\nLanding Sweeping staircase with painted newels and varnished handrail. High stained-glass window at half landing return with access through to annex.
