    Moovingo is delighted to present 41 Willows Drive to the market. This 3 bedroom semi-detached family home has been extended to the rear providing a total area of 100 sq. m. (1,076 sq. ft.).\r\n\r\nAt ground floor level the accommodation comprises a living room with laminate flooring and open fireplace. To the the rear of the property there is a kitchen/dining room which has been extended to allow for a large family area. Sliding doors provide access to the rear garden which has a large garden shed. A guest w.c underneath the stairs completes the ground floor. At first-floor level there are three bedrooms (two double and one single) all fitted with built in wardrobes and a family bathroom. Many of the homes in the development have converted the attic space.\r\n\r\nThe property has the benefit of a side access and gated off street parking to the front as well as being located at the end of a quiet cul de sac with ample on street parking if required. It overlooks a large green area to the front.\r\n\r\nArea Info\r\nThe property is located in a sought after mature estate in Clonsilla village, just a 15 minute walk to Clonsilla station with frequent trains to Dublin Connolly (20mins) and Pearse, as well as Maynooth and Dunboyne. There are also buses to Dublin and Blanchardstown Centre. It is a low rise area with plenty of green space, next to the canal and a short walk to Beech Park, Clonsilla allotments, soon to open Shackleton Gardens and shops in Clonsilla.
