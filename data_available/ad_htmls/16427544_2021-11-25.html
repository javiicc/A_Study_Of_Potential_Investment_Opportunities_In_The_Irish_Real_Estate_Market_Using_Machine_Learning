    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    A spacious house in excellent condition. The property overlooks Castlepoint Pier with a view of Leamcon Castle. The property has magnificent views of Toormore Bay & Goleen Harbour. 5 miles east to the picturesque Schull village with its artisan shops, busy restaurants and pubs. Numerous marine activities - sailing and boat trips to nearby Long Island, Cape Clear Island & Fastnet Rock Lighthouse.
