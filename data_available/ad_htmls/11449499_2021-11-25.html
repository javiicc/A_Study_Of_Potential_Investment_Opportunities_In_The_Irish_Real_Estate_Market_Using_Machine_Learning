    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Superior 6000 sq.ft 4-bedroom property situated on an elevated one acre site, taking in scenic views in the heart of Ballyconnell close to the famous Slieve Russell Hotel. Superb property with the potential of another site taking in many views from the elevated site, bay windows and conservatory this property is a must see!! It is located within walking distance from Ballyconnell town, 2 mile from the Slieve Russell Hotel & 18 hole Championship Golf Course & Country Club and the Woodford River & Canal runs close by. This property is ideal for a large Family Home or if you would like to start the Bed and Breakfast Business. Two spacious sitting rooms lead off from a large tiled entrance hall. The first sitting room contains a cast iron fireplace and french doors leading to the outside space. The second sitting room leads on to a very large kitchen fitted with bespoke kitchen units and a large island in the centre of the kitchen. Off the kitchen is a spacious utility room. A very large conservatory, complete with fireplace, also has double french doors leading to the back of the house. On the first floor are four very large double bedrooms, three of which have large ensuite bathrooms and walk-in wardrobes. There is also a very large family bathroom with freestanding bath. The second floor has two rooms which could be used for games room/office/playroom with en-suite bathrooms.
