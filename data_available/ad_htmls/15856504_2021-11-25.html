    ***Amazing Value*** \r\nNo 5 Kinsale Holiday Village, is part of a 16 house holiday home complex, that was built in 1992. The complex is managed by a registered management company that takes care of the insurance (external), gardening, external maintenance (painting & lighting) and refuse collection. The annual maintenance fee is approximately \xe2\x82\xac1,500. The property is just a short walk into the centre of Kinsale which means that a car is not required for those wishing to use this property as a holiday home. \r\n\r\nThis is very attractive for holidaymakers for rental purposes. Kinsale is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Ireland and is just a short drive from Cork International airport. It is an ideal location for a holiday as well as being a great base from which to tour the south of Ireland. Because of this, and the fact that it is just a short commute to Cork city, Kinsale is popular with both owner-occupiers as well as the investor market. \r\n\r\nNo 5 Kinsale Holiday Village, has been updated substantially by its owners who use it as their personal holiday home during the low season and rent it to holidaymakers during the high season. The gross annual rental achieved averages approximately \xe2\x82\xac12k. Some of the upgrades undertaken by the owners include installation of electric storage heating which is economical and efficient, two electric showers (bathroom and ensuite), kitchen upgrade and leather suite and flat-screen TV in the lounge area. \r\n\r\nThe current owners do not live in the area but have been able to run it successfully themselves for holiday home rentals by having: A reliable, reasonably priced, locally-based maintenance man. A good cleaner and A professional laundry service. These service providers are willing to transfer to the new owner if required. The house is a three bedroomed mid terrace property. First floor: one double bedroom with ensuite (electric shower), twin bedroom, spacious single bedroom and family bathroom (with electric shower over bath). Ground floor: spacious lounge (working open fire), kitchen /dining room. Outside, small patio area. UPVC double glazing throughout. \r\n\r\nThis property should appeal to Irish people living abroad who wish to make a good investment and have a holiday base to return to while they visit family, overseas buyers that wish to have a holiday home in Kinsale as well as general investors and local first-time buyers. The house is being sold with contents so it is ready to move into or to rent out to holidaymakers.\r\n\r\nAccomaditaion:\r\nA floor plan with all of the room measurements and layout is contained with the broucher.\r\n\r\nViewings:\r\nStrictly by appointment with sole agent only\r\n\r\nLocation: \r\nKinsale is one of Ireland's most picturesque, popular and fashionable towns which is situated only 17 miles from Cork city, making this an ideal location for those wishing to commute to the city. The pretty seaside town of Kinsale is a mere 25 minutes drive from the cosmopolitan city of Cork. This area of South West Ireland is of outstanding beauty with its dramatic coastline, unspoilt locations and safe beaches. Leisure activities are well catered for, Kinsale provides excellent sailing, yachting and fishing facilities from its two marinas. There are three world-class golf courses including the renowned Old Head of Kinsale. Other recreational pursuits include walking, bathing, surfing, and horse riding. \r\n\r\nThere are numerous cultural events held throughout the year, such as the world-famous Kinsale Arts Festival, the Cork Jazz Festival, Cork Film Festival, and Kinsale Gourmet Festival along with a thriving music and opera programme. Cork has several fine theatres, and Cork Opera House hosts many exceptional productions throughout the year. Kinsale is also home to some of Ireland's finest restaurants, contributing to its title as Gourmet Capital of Ireland; along with many top-class hotels and lots of welcoming pubs.
