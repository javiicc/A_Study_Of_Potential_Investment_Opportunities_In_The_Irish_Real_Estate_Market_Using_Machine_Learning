    In the heart of old Ballinteer ! A charming three bedroom residence built in 1932 in this private cul-de-sac setting close to both Ballinteer and Dundrum villages and a host of local amnesties. The properties bright and well-presented accommodation is complimented by a wonderful private rear garden which extends to 19m / 60 ft in length and offers obvious potential to substantially extend if further accommodation was required.\r\n\r\nThe accommodation briefly comprises entrance hall leading to a living room area which directly leads to the kitchen to the rear with lobby off which leads to shower room. Upstairs there are three well-proportioned bedrooms. The property also has freestanding ancillary accommodation to the rear situated in a timber framed structure which would suit a variety of uses. \r\n\r\nAs mentioned Ballinteer Gardens is a delightfully private enclave located within a short distance of Ballinteer and Dundrum Villages and Dundrum shopping centre. The easy access to the M50 means excellent access to the entire national road network and Dublin Airport. The property is within a twenty minute drive of Dublin city centre. It is convenient to Marlay Park, SuperValu Centre at Ballinteer, several schools (Scoil Naithi; St Attracta's; St Columbus and Divine Word to name but a few), leisure facilities such as Meadowbrook Swimming Pool; St Enda's Park; The Grange Golf Club and Ballinteer St John's GAA Club and public transport (Dublin Bus Route14) & Luas Green Line at Balally being the closest stop.\r\n \r\nEntrance Porch (1.97m x 0.90m )with porcelain tiled floor leading to\r\nEntrance Hall (1.40m x 1.00m )with staircase to upper floor and door to\r\nSitting Room (4.00m x 3.80m )with attractive tiled fireplace with tiled hearth, large picture window overlooking garden to front and door to\r\nKitchen (2.50m x 2.50m )with modern filtted kitchen with range of cupboards, worktops, electic hob and oven, sink unit, tiled floor, part-tiled wall and door to\r\nRear Lobby (2.60m x 1.00m )with storage area, door to rear garden and door to\r\nShower Room (2.45m x 1.60m )with Trition T80SI Electric shower w.c., w.h.b., tiled floor and walls.\r\nUpstairs \r\nBedroom 1 (5.10m x 3.05m )with built-in wadrobe and shelving area, window overlooking garden to front.\r\nBedroom 2 (3.55m x 2.70m )To rear of house\r\nBedroom 3 (2.60m x 2.55m )To rear of house\r\nOutside Ancillary accomodation. There is also a large self-contained timber framed structure with double-glazed windows providing ancillary accommodation which measures 5.9m x 2.9m with plumbing for washing machine and dryer. The garden to the front is set out predominantly in level lawn with bordering shrubs and plants. The rear garden extends to approximately 19m (60ft) in length. There is a large decked patio area to the rear of the property and a paved pathway leading to a storage shed to the rear.
