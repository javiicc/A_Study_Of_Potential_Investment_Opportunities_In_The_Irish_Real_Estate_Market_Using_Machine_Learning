    McGuirk Beggan Property is delighted to present this superb three bed family home to the market. The location is one of exceptional convenience, being situated within walking distance of countless local amenities including excellent primary and secondary schools. The M50 road network is also close by. \n\nThe front garden is fully walled and offers off-street parking.
