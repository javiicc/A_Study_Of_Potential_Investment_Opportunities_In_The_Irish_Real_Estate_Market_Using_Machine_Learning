    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    This is a unique opportunity to acquire a beautiful house in the heart of the popular tourist destination of Schull in west Cork. Located opposite the Catholic Church and enjoying beautiful south facing views overlooking Schull Harbour, Cape Clear Island, The Calves Islands and the beacon at the end of Long Island. The house itself is oozing with character with a huge effort by the present owners to retain as much of the old features as possible. The property has private parking at the rear. \nThe house consists of entrance hallway, sitting room / bedroom with a beautiful open fireplace with a black cast iron tiled surround, ground floor shower room and toilet, a lovely bright south facing kitchen / dining area and an open area presently used as a pool room. The 1st floor has a beautiful living room with two windows and French doors leading out to a south facing balcony overlooking Schull Harbour. There are two double bedrooms, both with en-suites and one with a walk in wardrobe. Overall a fantastic property, within walking distance to all the activities and attractions that Schull has to offer and enjoying exceptional sea views.\nAccommodation.\nEntrance Hallway:\nFloor tiled, radiator.\n\nGround Floor Toilet & Shower Room:8'x 3'6".\nWalls & floor tiled.
