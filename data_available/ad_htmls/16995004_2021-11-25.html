    28 Sweetman house is a beautifully presented 3 bedroom duplex property within easy access of all local amenities. This family home is in walk in condition and offers great living space with views over the communal grounds and Howth hill.\n \nThe living accommodation comprises of a hallway with access to the hotpress, 2 good sized bedrooms, the open plan kitchen/ living/ dining room and the main family bathroom on the ground floor. Upstairs there is a large main bedroom with double height ceilings with en suite. There are 2 substantial balconies off the main bedroom with beautiful views. \nThere are 2 designated underground parking spaces and outside you have access to a secure, communal , landscaped courtyard for just the residents of this block.\n\nLocation is superb being set within a 5 minute walk of the DART station with train commute time to Dublin City Centre at only 20 minutes. There is also a host of local amenities on your doorstep including schools, shops, church, parks, coastal walk & cycle ways, bus stops as well as sports & leisure facilities.\n\n\nHallway Porcelain tiling, alarm system, Hotpress with storage\n\nLiving Room Timber tongue and groove flooring, window to the side, TV point, intercom system\n\nKitchen/ Dining Room Timber tongue and groove flooring, window to the side, fitted kitchen with built in fridge/ freezer, plumbed for washing machine\n\nBathroom Tiled flooring, WC, WHB, bath tub with shower fixture\n\nBedroom 2 Timber tongue and groove flooring, built in wardrobes, Juliette balcony\n\nBedroom 3 Timber tongue and groove flooring, built in wardrobes, window to the side\n\nBedroom 1 Laminate flooring, double height ceiling, built in wardrobes, two large balconies\n\nEn Suite Tiled flooring, WC, WHB, enclosed shower unit, window to the side\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nHeading east on the Grange Road through Baldoyle village, continue on until the traffic light junction just passed the Applegreen petrol station, turn right on to Longfield Road and take the right turn at Hot Sandwich Co and number 28 will be the last door on the left overlooking the communal green.
