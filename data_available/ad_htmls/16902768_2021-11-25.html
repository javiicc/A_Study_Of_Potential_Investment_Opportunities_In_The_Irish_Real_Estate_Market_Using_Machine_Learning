    A PRIME DEVELOPMENT OPPORTUNITY IN A SUPERB LOCATION. THIS SUBSTANTIAL BUILDING WITH LARGE ZONED SITE TO THE REAR AND VIEWS ALONG ENNISCRONES 5KM SANDY BEACH HAS ENDLESS POTENTIAL \n\nSuperb development opportunity in an enviable position. This mid terrace, two storey property is situated on the main thoroughfare in to Enniscrone and is adjacent to the access road for Enniscrone Beach and is suitable for refurbishment or indeed redevelopment. The property has exceptional development potential to the rear given the large c. 0.5 acre site extending back to meet Enniscrone Pitch and Putt Course and with views of the sand dunes and golden sandy beach. The property is zoned 'Residential' in the current Local Area Plan. The building is currently laid out with a small commercial unit to the front of the ground floor and living acccommodation to the rear and bedrooms on the first floor. \n\nAccommodation comprises: Kitchen/Dining Room: 6.66m x 2.99m tiled floor, fitted kitchen and solid fuel stove Utility: 1.76m x 1.64m tiled floor, plumbed for washing machine Hall: 6.46m x 0.81m tiled floor Bedroom 1: 3.02m x 2.61m laminate wood floor, built in wardrobes Bedroom 2: 4.85m x 3.50m Laminate wood floor, large windows affording views over the town and beach Bedroom 3: 2.39m X 3.38m Bedroom 4: 3.03m x 2.43m Laminate floor and built in wardrobe Bed 5/ Office: 2.24m x 1.62m laminate floor and built in wardrobe Bathroom: 1.83m x 1.99m tiled with bath, W.C., W.H.B. Commercial unit: 6.00m x 4.5m \n\nSITE AREA C. 0.5 ACRES \n\nPrice on Application
