    Very rare opportunity to create an attractive property is a stunning position on St. John's Point Peninsula. \r\n\r\nThe subjects comprise a small dilapidated stone built cottage with (2) x detached outbuildings set within a substantial plot, extending to approximately 3.76 ha / 9.2 acres, and benefitting from outstanding coastal views. The property offers an excellent opportunity for a purchaser to create a small estate or substantial luxury residence, with a good proportion of the land in one of Ireland's most scenic settings. \r\n\r\nThe property fronts onto the main access road leading to St. John\\'s Point Beach (700m) and Lighthouse (2.8 kms). Commanding just under 150 meters of frontage the subject property has a number of potential scenic building sites located thereon - subject to necessary planning approval being obtained. The existing cottage accommodation comprises a living room, kitchen together with a bedroom and bathroom and is suitable for redevelopment - SPP. Enquiries about the prospects of development should be made to the Donegal County Council. \r\n\r\nServices\r\nMains electricity and water is located nearby.\r\n\r\nViewings\r\nBy appointment with the Selling Agents only.
