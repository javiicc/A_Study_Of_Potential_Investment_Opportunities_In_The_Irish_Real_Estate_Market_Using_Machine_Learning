    ***STUNNING LARGE 3 BED BUNGALOW WITH MAGNIFICENT LARGE CORNER GARDEN***\r\n\r\nMurphy Mullan Rathgar are delighted to bring to the market this superb 3 bedroom detached home with a stunning private rear garden with garage, situated in a highly convenient location within walking distance of the KCR and Terenure Village with access to a range of local shops, restaurants and public transport routes. This property is suited for a growing family or first-time purchasers/trade up. Features such as the landscaped gardens to front and rear, ample room to extend to side and rear and second to none location all combine to make this property a dream come through.\r\n\r\nThe accommodation comprises downstairs of an entrance porch, large entrance hallway, large open plan living room which leads to a sun-drenched conservatory, a kitchen/dining room, 3 large double bedrooms and a new family bathroom. There is a garage to the side with an extended utility to the rear and a solar panel for water heating.\r\n\r\nOutside, to the front, there is off-street parking on a cobble-lock driveway and a magnificently manicured garden. The large and private rear garden is landscaped to perfection with cypress trees and ample room to extend subject to planning permission.\r\n\r\nSituated off Kimmage Road West, the convenience of 6 Dangan Park cannot be overstated located within close proximity of Terenure, Rathgar, Templeogue Village, Rathmines and the City Centre. No.6 is close to a wide array of local and recreational amenities such as the Ashleaf Shopping Centre, Terenure Village, The Hungry Duck Cafe, St Mary\xe2\x82\xacTM and Terenure Rugby Clubs, Bushy Park, and the Carlisle Gym. The best of schools both primary and secondary are on its doorstep including St Pius X, Our Lady\xe2\x82\xacTM School, St Josephs NS, Terenure College. The property is well-serviced by bus routes such as the 9, 15a, 18, 83, 150 towards the City Centre and 17 towards Dundrum. The M-50 motorway is within a few minutes drive and provides easy access to all major route-ways and Dublin airport.\r\n\r\nThis property is a real gem in one of Dublin 12s finest locations.
