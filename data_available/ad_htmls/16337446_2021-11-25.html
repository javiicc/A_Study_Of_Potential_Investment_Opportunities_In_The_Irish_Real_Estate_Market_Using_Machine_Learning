    This 2 bedroom cottage at Derrycarhoon is presented to market in superb condition. Set on an elevated c. 2 acre plot this property enjoys spectacular Sea, Island & Countryside views. The property is approached via a private drive with car parking to the rear in order to allow the elevated gardens & grounds to enjoy uninterrupted views.
