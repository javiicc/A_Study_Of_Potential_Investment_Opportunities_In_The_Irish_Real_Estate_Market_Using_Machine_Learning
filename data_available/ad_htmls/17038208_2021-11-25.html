    Spacious superbly positioned three bedroomed mews with parking and garden close to the heart of Glasthule village. \r\n\r\nNumber 8 Eden Park Mews is a spacious three-bedroom mews hidden away on this quiet mews lane close to the heart of Glasthule village. The attractive cut stone exterior leads to a contemporary interior with all the living accommodation on the first floor. \r\n\r\nHardwood timber floors, an excellent modern fully fitted kitchen, spacious open plan reception at first floor level and a private terrace off the principal bedroom and main reception at first floor level are just some of the features that make this Mews something rather special and appealing. The accommodation comprises entrance hall with excellent understairs storage and hot press, Two large ground floor bedrooms and a bathroom. The integral garage which offers further potential to extend the living space if required completes the ground floor. At first floor level we have a large bedroom with ensuite and a bright and voluminous open plan Living, Kitchen / dining room with private terrace off. \r\n\r\nThe location is second to none. Positioned within a two-minute walk of the bustling town of Glasthule with its colourful array of shops, restaurants, and hostelries. Glasthule Dart station is also within a two-minute walk. The sea and Scotsman s Bay are also on your doorstep for a daily stroll or a brisk Dip for the more adventurous. \r\n\r\nViewing is recommended to appreciate all that this property has to offer.\r\n\r\nEntrance Hall Solid oak floors and stairs to first floor reception accommodation. Inset lights and double doors to large under stairs store room and Hotpress. Separate built in understairs storage also. Door to integral garage.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 (Rear) 4.12m x 3.09m. Large double bedroom with attractive angular window. Plenty of space for built in wardrobes. Door to rear patio and garden.\r\n\r\nBedroom 3 (Front) 2.21m x 3.80m. Another good sized double bedroom positioned overlooking the lane to the front.\r\n\r\nBathroom 2.21m x 1.69m. Attractive modern main bathroom with fully tiled walls and floor. WC. Modern circular wash hand basin set in vanity unit with bathroom cabinet and light over. Bath with shower attachment and solid glass screen.\r\n\r\nIntegral Garage 2.36m x 5.48m. Large garage with pedestrian access to the rear patio and garden. Vehicular access to the front from the lane. This garage has been converted to an additional bedroom at this level in other homes on the lane so could offer potential to do similar if required (sub to pp)\r\n\r\nFirst Floor\r\n\r\nMain Reception Room 3.60m x 8.82m. Large bright open plan Living /Dining room with modern kitchen off. The main reception features a partly vaulted ceiling, solid Oak floors, a modern wall mounted gas fire, angular window and double doors to the raised private sun terrace at the rear. It is a n excellent space for entertaining and is open plan to the kitchen.\r\n\r\nKitchen 3.60m x 8.82m. The kitchen again is a good size and features a modern fully fitted kitchen with a variety of larder units and magic corners to maximise storage. There is a fully tiled floor and Corian worktops and splashbacks. The kitchen is bright with a Velux window over and offers built-in appliances including a Neff gas hob, stainless steel oven and extractor, an American style fridge freezer and built in dishwasher.\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 2.58m x 5.69m. Off the main reception we have the master bedroom. The same size as the garage below this is a fine big room which could provide for a flexible layout depending on a buyer s needs. Double doors lead to the raised private terrace to the rear. Door to ensuite.\r\n\r\nEnsuite Shower room 1.66m x 1.47m. Modern shower room with fully tiled walls and floor. Wc and modern rectangular wash hand basin. fully tiled shower.\r\n\r\nOutside To the front on the mews lane there is designated parking for one car.\r\n\r\nTo the rear we have an L shaped garden with attractive cut stone walls. The garden Measures 6.9m x 4.8m plus 2.8m x 3.1 approx. The entire is low maintenance with false grass and a patio. There is a spiral staircase from the garden to the private terrace above.\r\n\r\nThe private terrace is accessed via bedroom 1 and the main reception. It measures Approx 3.1m x 2.3m and has a spiral staircase to the garden. it is decked and features frosted glass panels making it very private.
