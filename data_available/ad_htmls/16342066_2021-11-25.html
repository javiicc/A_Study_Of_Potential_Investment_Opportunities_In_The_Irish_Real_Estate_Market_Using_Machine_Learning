    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    A hillside farm with an old traditional style residence and farm payments of about \xe2\x82\xac11,500 per annum. ( amount received in 2020). It comprises of c.18.24 acres of private lowland (grasslands) and c. 50 acres of mountain grazing right with an old single storey traditional style dwelling.\r\n\r\nThis is a rare opportunity to purchase a farm in view of the McGillycuddy reeks and only about 10 miles from Killarney and Killorglin. The very attractive village of Beaufort is only a few miles away. It is bounded on one side by a beautiful little river ( the Owenacullen). The location is extremely scenic. set in the embrace of the McGillycuddy reeks,there are beautiful mountain and countryside views all around. \r\n\r\nIt would be a heavenly retreat for someone wishing to enjoy the beautiful nature here, with such natural fresh air and a wide variety of wildlife and flora and fauna plus a nice income as a bonus. It is close to many famous attractions ( hill walking, fishing, Killarney national park, beaches, etc. etc. It is the ideal property for a hobby farmer, someone wanting to extend or to start their own farming career). It is seldom a property like this comes on the market and much interest is expected. Water and electricity are available to the property ( the dwelling house had electricity connected previously).
