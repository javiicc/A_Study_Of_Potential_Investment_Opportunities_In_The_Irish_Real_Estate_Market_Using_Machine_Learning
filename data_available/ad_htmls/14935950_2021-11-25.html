    Smith & Butler Estates are delighted to present 687 Collins Avenue Extension to the market. This attractive 3 bedroom house with attic conversion enjoys a superb location, set back from the three lined Collins Avenue with ample off-street parking. The property further benefits from a large (aprox 35m long) south facing back garden, which is not overlooked to the rear and has mature hedging on both sides as well as to the front. The property also boasts generous side access and a garage which has been partially converted to a laundry room. \r\nInternally the property extends to a generous 101 sqm of well-proportioned accommodation which briefly comprises a bright & spacious porch, welcoming entrance hall, front living room with marble feature fireplace and double door access to a dining room, a kitchen extension to the rear and conveniently a downstairs bathroom with shower. Upstairs there are two double bedrooms both of which have built in wardrobes, a good sized single bedroom and a family bathroom with w.c, w.b and bath shower combination. The property further benefits from an attic conversion with a large format velux window, enjoying southerly views over mature trees. This space is ideally suited to a home office. \r\nThe location is second to none with every local amenity at your doorstep including shops, schools, restaurants and recreational facilities with DCU and Albert College Park just a short stroll away. The immediate area is also well serviced by numerous bus routes and excellent transport links to Dublin City Centre and the surrounding country via the nearby M50 and M1 motorways.
