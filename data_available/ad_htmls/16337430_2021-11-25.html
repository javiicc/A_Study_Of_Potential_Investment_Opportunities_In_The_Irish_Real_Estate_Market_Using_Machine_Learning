The local Secondary Schools (Garbally College and Ard Scoil Mhuire) and Portiuncula University Hospital (an acute general and maternity hospital) are all a short walk away. The location is ideal in a mature and safe residential neighbourhood while being a short drive to the town centre.\r\n\r\nThe house is a large 205 Sq. Meter/2200 Sq. The remainder of the property is an open yard with ample parking for several vehicles. It has huge potential for further possibilities as the site is zoned for residential and commercial development.
