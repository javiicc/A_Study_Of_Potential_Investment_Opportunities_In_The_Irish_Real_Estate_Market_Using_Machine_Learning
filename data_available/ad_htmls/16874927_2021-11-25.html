    Moovingo are delighted to present this superb semi-detached house to the market. Refurbished in 2019 to a high standard, the house has had new kitchen, bathroom, flooring, radiators, and wardrobes installed and now presents in excellent turn-key condition. The property also enjoys a spacious private rear garden with side access to the front also. Nestled at the end of a quiet cul-de-sac between the beautiful Havelock Square and the vibrant Bath Avenue, the property will appeal to first-time buyers and investors alike.\r\n\r\nThe ground floor is laid out in a long living/dining room with adjoining modern kitchen. Upstairs there are two double bedrooms with fitted wardrobes and a modern family bathroom. Viewing is highly recommended.\r\n\r\nArea Info\r\nThe location of this property needs little introduction. There are a wealth of amenities in the immediate vicinity with some of Dublin's finest restaurants and gastropubs located minutes away on Bath Avenue. Local shops include Lotts & Co and Avoca Handweavers.\r\n\r\nSporting amenities are also abundant with the Aviva Stadium, Shelbourne Racecourse, Landsdowne Tennis Club, and ESB SportsCo all within short walking distance. For outdoor enthusiasts, Sandymount Strand, Herbert Park and the Grand Canal are all close by.\r\n\r\nThere are an excellent selection of schools in the area, both primary and secondary, and Trinity College is also within walking distance.\r\n\r\nWhile the city centre is within easy walking distance, Grand Canal Dock and Lansdowne DART stations are both a short walk away and there are a number of bus routes servicing the nearby Shelbourne & Merrion Road.\r\n\r\nDisclaimer: Any intending purchaser(s) shall accept that no statement, description or measurement contained in advertisement or any other document or published by the vendor or by Moovingo, in respect of the premises shall constitute a representation inducing the purchaser(s) to enter into any contract for sale, or any warranty forming part of any such contract for sale.
