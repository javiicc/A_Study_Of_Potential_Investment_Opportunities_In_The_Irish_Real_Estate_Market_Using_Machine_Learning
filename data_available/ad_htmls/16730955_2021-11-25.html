    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Flynn and Associates are delighted to bring number 12 Gleann Ribh in Lusk to the market for sale. This is a well presented, larger than average, 2 bedroomed, end of terrace home that stands out from the market due to its sought after location at the end of a quiet cul de sac in the centre of the village, a large balcony off the master bedroom, bright and well presented internal space and a large wooden chalet to the rear that has power and light providing even further space.\n\nThe upgraded front door leads into a light and bright hallway with access to the lounge, kitchen/diner and stairs to the first floor. The lounge is flooded with light due to its south west facing aspect and offers a cosy living space. To the rear is the open plan kitchen diner with tiled floors, lots of storage and prep space, a utility/WC off to the side and sliding doors giving access to the private low maintenance garden. Up stairs there are 2 large double bedrooms, the master bedroom has bespoke built wardrobes and storage as well as sliding doors onto a large balcony. The family bathroom is clean, modern and tiled. The 1st floor is finished off a hot press and hatched access to the attic. \n\nTo the outside of the property the front has a lawned garden and mature flower beds and there is side access leading to the rear garden. This a private and low maintenance space that is majority paved and has a detached wooden chalet style building to the rear with power and light. Previously used as a gym, it makes an extra flexible space that could be a home office, further storage or living space. \n\nThe Gleann Ribh development is a small collection of properties in the heart of Lusk Village. Situated at the end of a quiet cul de sac this offers a unique balance between a peaceful location, but within easy walking distance of the heart of Lusk with the shops, cafes and transport links it offers. Lusk also boasts great schools, sports clubs and facilities and the Rush/Lusk train station giving easy access to Dublin City. Further out you are surrounded by the rolling countryside of North County Dublin, with the coastal towns of Rush and Skerries to the East and North and Dublin city, the M1/M50 network and Dublin international airport to the South.\n\nThe property is available immediately, so call or email now to find out more.\n \nAccomodation:Hallway - 3.38m (11'1") x 2.05m (6'9")\nWith solid wood floors, doors to the lounge and kitchen/diner and stairs to the first floor landing.\n\nLounge - 3.38m (11'1") x 4.51m (14'10")\nWith solid wood.\n\nKitchen/Diner - 4.79m (15'9") x 3.81m (12'6")\nWith tiled floors. The kitchen has a range of matching base and eye level units including space and plumbing for washing machine, space for fridge/freezer, integrated electric oven and 4 ring electric hob with overhead extractor hood, space and plumbing for dishwasher, tiled splash backs, sliding door to rear garden, door to under stairs storage and door to utility room/WC.\n\nUtility / WC - 1.59m (5'3") x 1.67m (5'6")\nWith tiled floors, WC and pedestal wash hand basin.\n\nFirst floor landing - 5.13m (16'10") x 2m (6'7")\nWith carpets, doors to bedrooms 1 and 2, bathroom, hot press and hatched access to attic.\n\nBedroom 1 - 3.67m (12'0") x 4.04m (13'3")\nWith carpets, integrated wardrobes and storage and sliding doors to the balcony.\n\nBedroom 2 - 3.16m (10'4") x 3.7m (12'2")\nWith carpets and integrated wardrobes.\n\nBathroom - 2.55m (8'4") x 2.06m (6'9")\nWith tiled floors, a panelled bath with overhead mixer shower, WC and wash hand basin.
