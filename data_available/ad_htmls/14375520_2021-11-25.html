    Most attractive large three bedroom two story residence of character in a very good position overlooking the 15th fairway of this world renowned championship Golf Course.\nA major refurbishment costing in the region of e70,000 was carried out to the property in recent years to bring it up to its present condition.\n\nThe North Paddocks is an exclusive modern courtyard of just 10 houses, with an imposing entrance, nestled in the heart of this iconic estate, offering peace and tranquillity in a place of unparalled beauty. It is within a short walk of the clubhouse, the leisure centre, the Manor House hotel and restaurants.\n\nTHE MOUNT JULIET ESTATE\nThis property is situated on the Mount Juliet Estate, which is one of the country' s finest golf resorts, incorporating a 5 star hotel with a Michelin Star Restaurant, an 18-hole Jack Nicklaus designed Championship Golf Course and Clubhouse, Leisure Centre, spa, bars, restaurants. The Mount Juliet Estate is located approximately 1.5 km. northwest of the N9 Dublin-Waterford Road and convenient to Thomastown mainline train station.\n\nThe property is located approximately 3 km. west of Thomastown, 16 km. south east of Kilkenny City, 48 km. north of Waterford City and 120 km. south west of Dublin.\n\nMount Juliet - Renowned Estate. When you pass through the gates of Mount Juliet you are in a different world. The Manor House is a magnificent Georgian mansion, named by the Earl of Carrick after his wife Juliana, always known as Juliet. Their home set on a hill overlooking the River Nore evokes a feeling of old fashioned graciousness.\n\nSteeped in heritage, Mount Juliet Estate is Ireland' s leading country estate and enjoys a strong family culture, whilst maintaining the highest level of service and hospitality for which Mount Juliet is internationally renowned.\n\nThis property is ideal for owner occupation or as an investment or holiday home. There is strong demand for rental accommodation on the estate.\n\nNo. 6 North Paddocks extends to 155 sq.m (1,668 sq.ft) with the following accommodation;\n\n\nEntrance Hall: Cloakroom with w.c. and w.h.b. off it \nLiving & Dining Area: (25' x 19' ) with French door to barbecue and patio area\nKitchen & Breakfast Area: (15' x 10' 6" ) well fitted with an array of presses and shelving, polished marble work-tops, store off it, also hot press and press for washing machine & dryer\n\nUpstairs:\n\nBedroom 1 (17' x 13' 6" ) very large, timber floor with bathroom off it with bath, w.c., and bidet; also room of it with window (7' 6" x 6' ), formerly a dressing room but currently set up with a single bedroom with a window\n\nBedroom 2 (15' x 12' 6" ) double bedroom with timber flooring, built-in wardrobes, ensuite\n\nBedroom 3 (7' 9" x 7' 6" ) single bedroom but fits two beds\n\nSunny BBQ area at the back.\n\nGuide Price: 650,000 euro
