    Substantial, beautifully presented four bedroom end of terrace family home for sale, situated in the popular and very well maintained development Leargan just off the Western Distributor Road in Knocknacarra. The development was constructed by OMalley Construction and has both a playground and Montessori on-site. Number 108 was one of the last units to be constructed and was completed in 2012.\n\nThis home extends to 149 sq.m of living space laid out over three levels comprising entrance hallway, living room with open fire, guest WC, kitchen and dining area and utility at ground floor level. There are three bedrooms (one en-suite) on the first floor, along with the main bathroom. On the upper level there is large, light-filled, master bedroom with en-suite and feature balcony. This home has a host of features to add to its appeal; piped natural gas heating, solid wood flooring in the living room and master bedroom, fitted wardrobes to all bedrooms and a sunny rear garden which is not overlooked. \n\nThe property ideally overlooks a large green amenity area and there is designated parking to the front of the home. The home boasts a B2 BER rating. Ideally located close to all amenities including both National and Secondary schools, selection of shops and restaurants along with Salthill promenade only a short drive. There are also many sports and recreational facilities close by. There is a regular bus service on the Western Distributor Road to bring you into the city centre.
