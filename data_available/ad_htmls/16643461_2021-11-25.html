    Sherry FitzGerald proudly bring to the market No.21 Curragh Woods which is a spacious four-bedroom detached home that is extremely well situated at the front of the development with a green area to the front. It presents itself in good decorative condition throughout and is ready for its new owners to move in and put their own stamp on it. \r\n\r\nNo.21 benefits from private parking to front along with a mature lawn area that is fully enclosed. The rear of the property is accessed by two side entrances where there is a private patio area along with a lawn area that is surrounded by a mature boundary consisting of shrubs and trees.\r\n\r\nThis is a great family home with a strong balance of living and bedroom accommodation throughout. Downstairs, there are two good sized reception rooms, a large kitchen/dining area, a guest WC and a utility room that provides access to the rear garden. On the first floor there are four good size bedrooms with one being ensuite and a main bathroom.\r\n\r\nCurragh Woods has always been a desired location within Frankfield. It is just a short distance from Douglas village, which itself is a hub of society, with all of life s amenities both essential and social on the doorstep. This area also offers some very good schools, both primary and secondary, a regular bus service and easy access to the south link road network.\r\n\r\nDo not miss out, arrange your viewing today!\r\nEntrance Hall 4.83m x 2.35m. This is a spacious and welcoming entrance hall that gives you a glimpse of what is to come. The hall features decorative ceiling coving and provides access to all rooms on the ground floor.\r\n\r\nLiving Room 5.06m x 3.70m. Bright and spacious reception room that is located to the front of the home and overlooks the front garden through a bay window. Access is provided through a set of double doors from the entrance hall. It features decorative ceiling coving as well as a fireplace with a gas insert and marble surround as a centre point to the room.\r\n\r\nSitting Room 3.86m x 3.76m. The sitting room is bright and spacious and benefits from a west facing aspect which overlooks the private back garden. The patio area can be accessed from here through a sliding window. Benefits from a fireplace with a gas insert.\r\n\r\nKitchen/Dining Area 5.74m x 3.07m. The kitchen/dining area is open plan and overlooks the private west facing rear garden through a large window. The kitchen is well fitted with a good range of floor and eye level style units along with ample countertop space. The tiled floor runs throughout the kitchen/dining area and into the utility room.\r\n\r\nUtility Room 1.76m x 1.32m. The utility room is located directly off the kitchen with a side door to the garden. It is plumbed for a washing machine and tumble dryer.\r\n\r\nGuest WC 2.12m x 0.74m. Two-piece suite located just off the entrance hall. The floor area is fully tiled and there is a window to the side of the house for natural ventilation.\r\n\r\nLanding 3.96m x 3.09m. The bright and spacious landing gives access to all bedrooms and the main bathroom. The attic is also accessed from here.\r\n\r\nMain Bedroom 3.83m x 3.86m. This is a bright and spacious double bedroom to the front of the home which overlooks the front garden and green area. The bedroom benefits from an ensuite and features a built-in floor to ceiling wardrobe.\r\n\r\nEnsuite 0.80m x 2.96m. Three-piece shower suite with an electric shower. The shower area and floor are fully tiled.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 3.76m x 3.06m. Located to the rear of the home and overlooking the west facing garden is a bright and spacious double bedroom. It features a built-in floor to ceiling wardrobe.\r\n\r\nBedroom 3 3.68m x 3.09m. This is another bright and spacious bedroom situated to the rear of the home and overlooks the west facing back garden. It features a built-in floor to ceiling wardrobe.\r\n\r\nBedroom 4 2.61m x 2.23m. This bright and spacious bedroom is situated to the front of the home and overlooks the front driveway and green area.\r\n\r\nMain Bathroom 1.82m x 2.00m. The main bathroom comprises of a three-piece bath suite with a shower over the bath. The floor and bath area are fully tiled. There is also a window to the side of the house which provides natural ventilation.\r\n\r\nGarden To the front of the home there is off-street parking for a number of cars on a private driveway. There is also a lawn area that is surrounded by a mature boundary. On either side of the house there is a gated side entrance which provide access to the back garden. \r\n\r\nThe fully enclosed, private, west facing rear garden has something for everyone. There is a large lawn area along with a mature boundary of shrubs and trees. There is a well-proportioned patio area that is ideal for entertaining especially as it benefits from the west facing aspect.
