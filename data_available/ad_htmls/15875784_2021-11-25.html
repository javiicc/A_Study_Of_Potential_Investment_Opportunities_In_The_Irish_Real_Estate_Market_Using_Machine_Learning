    Stunning and incredibly spacious two bedroomed apartment (118 sq.m) tucked away and set behind electronic gates in the ever popular development of Cairnbrook. Presented with beautiful style and attention to detail, combined with its exceptional private terrace and access to the communal gardens to the rear this home is ideal for any discerning purchaser looking for the convenience of apartment living with all the scope and outdoor space one could ask for.\n\nThe interior takes full advantage of the secluded setting to create a warm and inviting living environment. The wide entrance hall and high ceilings create the perfect entrance into this exceptionally spacious apartment. The enviable living/dining area boasts stylish dark hardwood floors, gas fireplace with French doors leading to the utterly privately and large covered terrace providing a perfect place for al fresco dining as well as an additional entrance to the apartment. The contemporary kitchen has an abundance of storage and integrated appliances. There are two exceptionally spacious double bedrooms both with built in wardrobes and access to the terrace. The main bedroom boasts a walk in dressing area and contemporary ensuite with the modern shower room completes the accommodation. \n\nBuilt by Newlyn Homes Cairnbrook is tucked away behind electric gates and is ideally located just off the Glenamuck Road with both Stepaside and Foxrock Villages nearby with their bijous eateries, gourmet shops and boutiques. Carrickmines Retail Park is across the road and Leopardstown Shopping Centre is less than a 5 minute drive. There is easy access to the M50 making Sandyford and Stillorgan Business Park easily accessible along with The Beacon Hospital, Hotel and The Dundrum Shopping Centre with its wide array of restaurants, shops, cafes, cinema, theatre, and bars. Outdoor enthusiasts will be spoilt for choice with world-class golf courses and yacht clubs. Fitness fans can select from the Equestrian Centre or the Croquet and Lawn Tennis Club in Carrickmines, while the nearby Westwood Club is also an energetic option. The commute to the city centre is only 30 minutes approx., there are excellent public transport facilities available nearby including the 63, and 46 D which serve the LUAS, N11, University College Dublin and the City Centre and also the 44 N Nitelink Service from the City Centre.\n\nThis is a great trade down or first home for those seeking a property in turn key condition.\n\n\nEntrance Hall Large hallway with high ceilings, spot lighting, ceiling coving and access to the hot press and storage cupboard.\n\nLiving/Dining Room 5.17m x 9.80m.
