      Location

      Rolestown is a well-regarded, quiet and safe rural village in North County Dublin based around a picturesque river and is considered a sought after place to live. Rolestown village itself has a highly regarded primary school, service station with large retail Centra store and is within close access to several local pubs and hotels. Being just 7km from the Main Street of Swords, within easy access of Dublin Airport (9km) and 15km from the M50 giving easy access to the wider Dublin area.
