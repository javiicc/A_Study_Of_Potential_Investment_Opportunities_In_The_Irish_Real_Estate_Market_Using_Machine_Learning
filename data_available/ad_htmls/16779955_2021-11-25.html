    We are delighted to bring to the market this excellent 5 bedroom residence including 1 bedroom apartment, situated in the heart of the Co. Laois countryside with the convenience of being located within walking distance of Ballybrophy train station, which serves the Dublin/Cork commuter trains and the branch line for Dublin/Limerick. The property is c. 4km from the M7 motorway access. The local schools and Townlink bus stop is adjacent to the property. This spacious property is perfect for those looking for a home situated in a peaceful, rural setting with the easiest of access to excellent road and rail links. The property boasts a well-maintained, attractive garden to the side and rear which is partly laid in lawn, with mature trees and shrubs, charming cut stone flower beds, a herb garden, a vegetable garden, green house, fruit garden, peaceful seating area, a fish pond and water feature and ample car parking and courtyard.
