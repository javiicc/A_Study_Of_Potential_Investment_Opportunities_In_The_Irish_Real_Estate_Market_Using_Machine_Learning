The current owners have maintained the property in excellent condition throughout while also upgrading features over the years, including building a 15 sq. m. extension at the rear of the house. Conveniently located next to a number of amenities, this property will appeal to first-time buyers and families looking to upgrade to a larger home with more space.\n\nThe accommodation comprises an entrance porch, long hallway, bright living room, open plan kitchen/dining room, spacious family/sun room, and WC. Upstairs there are three bedrooms with fitted wardrobes and a family bathroom. The rear garden is a good size and has side access also. At the front of the property there is a driveway and lawn.\n\nAREA INFO\n\nThe property enjoys an enviable location with a host of amenities right on its doorstep including shops, restaurants cafes and childcare facilities. For outdoor enthusiasts, Cabinteely Park is a stone's throw from the property and both the Dublin and Wicklow mountains are just a short drive away. There are a number of excellent schools in the area also including St Brigid's National School.\n\nThe property is just minutes from the N11, which offers numerous Dublin bus routes, and the M50 can also be accessed in just 4 minutes.
