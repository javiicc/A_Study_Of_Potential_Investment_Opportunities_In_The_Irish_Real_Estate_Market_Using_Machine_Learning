    13 Wayside Cottages, Kilternan, Dublin 18, D18 Y773\nThis semi-detached cottage on approx. 0.36acre offers a range of possibilities to prospective purchasers.\n\nThe property consists of an entrance hallway, living room, kitchen, three bedrooms and a bathroom.\nPVC double glazed windows and oil fired central heating throughout.\nOff-street carparking is provided to the front and side of the property, with hedges lining the boundaries. \nThe kitchen looks out on the substantial rear garden, which has a great mix of mature trees and shrubs.\n\n\nWhile in need of refurbishment, the potential to extend and reconfigure the property into a substancial family home is fantastic.\n(Subject to PP)\n\n\nFLOOR AREAS\nLIVING ROOM\t\t4.51m x 2.95m\nKITCHEN\t\t2.78m x 2.69m\nBEDROOM 1\t\t2.01m x 2.71m\nBEDROOM 2\t\t2.36m x 3.60m\nBEDROOM 3\t\t4.52m x 3.12m\nBATHROOM\t\t1.52m x 2.52m\nENTRANCE HALL\t2.03m x 0.95m\nBACK HALL\t\t0.91m x 1.43m\nPORCH\t\t\t1.00m x 0.96m\n\nLOCATION\n13 Wayside Cottages is ideally located in a quiet cul-de-sac just off the Enniskerry Road (R117) and is only a couple minutes for the wide range of amenities that Stepaside, Foxrock and Kilternan village has to offer.
