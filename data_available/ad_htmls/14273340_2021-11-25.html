This has resulted in one of the most stylish and contemporary dwellings to come to the market in Dublin 8 in recent times, that hosts two separate self contained units, with Apartment 1 on the ground and lower ground floors. \n\nThe ground floor Apartment is accessed from the main hall door to a communal hallway where there is access to both units. The accommodation on the ground floor apartment is directly into the large front living room with feature fireplace, high ceilings and overlooks to the front. Steps in the corridor lead on down to the lower ground floor where there is a large kitchen cum breakfast room, laundry or storage area and access to the main bathroom. A door leads on out to the private rear courtyard style garden, that is only available to the occupants of the ground floor. \n\nHamilton Street is a quiet red brick residential street situated off the South Circular Road. The location of this property on Hamilton Street is second to none. It is within short walking distance of the city centre and yet has quiet and peaceful surroundings. Weekend and evening living is ideal with the likes of The Headline Bar, Bastible, Noshington, MVP, Little Bird Cafe, BiBi's and Clanbrassil House all within a short stroll. Oscar Square is around the corner which is very popular with dog owners who will also appreciate walks along the canal and the likes of St. Patrick's Park and Iveagh Gardens which are nearby. The Green and Red LUAS lines are also within walking distance and a number of regular bus routes travel to the city along the South Circular Road. Other notable amenities convenient to this property include Camden St, Griffith College Dublin, Trinity College Dublin, Griffith Barracks Multi-denominational School, St Catherines, Portobello Educate Together, St. James' Hospital, Heuston Station and The Phoenix Park.
