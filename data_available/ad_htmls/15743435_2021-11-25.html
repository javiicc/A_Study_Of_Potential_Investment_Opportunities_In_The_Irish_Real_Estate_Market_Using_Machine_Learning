    Opportunity to purchase a fabulous property adjacent to the busy Killimer Tarbert car ferry with stunning views across the Shannon Estuary to Dingle and beyond. With a spacious four bed house and enclosed garden attached to the commercial premises this property offers a multitude of opportunities to an interested buyer. On the Wild Atlantic Way this section of the N69 links Killarney and Dingle with The Burren, The Cliffs of Moher and Galway, which are all easily accessible. Local Link Transport Route 337 service 3 times daily between Ennis and Kilrush on the estuary route. A unique opportunity in West Clare to acquire a sizeable residential and commercial premises on a beautifully elevated site. Viewing is strictly by prior appointment with Sole Selling Agents.
