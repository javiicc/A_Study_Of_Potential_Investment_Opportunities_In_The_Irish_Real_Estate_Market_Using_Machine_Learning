    Coldwell Banker Carlton Estates are delighted to present 27 Clonlara, Kerry Pike to the sales market. This four bedroom semi detached property comes to the market in turnkey condition, built in 2019 and extended in 2020 this really is an ideal family home. There is a large paved driveway to the front which can easily fit three cars. Clonlara was built by the well established builders O' Flynn construction, the finish and attention to detail in this property can be seen once entering the home. The hallway has wood flooring and the Wc has a tiled floor with wheel chair accessibility.\r\nThe lounge boasts a multi fuel stove and wood flooring, this is a large spacious room ideal for family living. To the rear is a large open space area which was extended in 2020 and has bi fold doors which lead to a south facing rear garden. The kitchen is tiled and boasts floor and eye level units, there is a utility also off the kitchen. The kitchen is a lovely soft grey colour which looks fantastic in this modern home.\r\nThere are four bedrooms on the first floor with the master ensuite. There is a large built in wardrobe in the master with wood flooring. There are two other double bedrooms and one generous single bedroom. The main bathroom is fully tiled with a large bath and modern features throughout. \r\nKerry Pike is within close proximity to Blarney and is a mere 5 minute drive from Apple' s European HQ and a 10 minute drive from Cork City.\r\nViewing by appointment only!!!
