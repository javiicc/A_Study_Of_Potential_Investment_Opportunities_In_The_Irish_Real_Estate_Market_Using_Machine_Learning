The property begins with a hallway providing full length access to the house. The rear of the property is filled with the open plan kitchen/diner with bi-fold doors out to the large conservatory. The kitchen features sleek modern d\xc3\xa9cor with a kitchen island, integrated appliances and a range style cooker. This then flows into the private rear garden which has a combination of paved patio and lawned areas, ideal for entertaining or for families. Upstairs is a master bedroom with en suite shower room and a balcony opening up to calming, picturesque sea views. Two further bedrooms and a family bathroom complete this floor.\n\nTayleurs Point is a family friendly neighbourhood positioned at the Harbour end of Rush Village and the entrance boasts spectacular panoramic views of Lambay Island. The green space in front of the estate is a dedicated area for Tayleurs Point residents to enjoy. Rush is a quaint seaside town with a fantastic community and a fine selection amenities including shops and restaurants, schools, sports clubs and facilties and much more. Rush & LuskTrain Station is within a 12-minute drive, with regular services to Dublin city centre, while access to the M1 motor way is 15 minutes away. \n\nThis flexible property could suit a wide variety of needs from a lovely family home to a wise investment.
