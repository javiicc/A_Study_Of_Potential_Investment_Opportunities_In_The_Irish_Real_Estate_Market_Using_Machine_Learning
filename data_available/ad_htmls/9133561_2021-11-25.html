    Development Description

      Current Phase Sold Out - Register your Interest for Next Phase at www.berford.ie\r\n\r\n\r\nBerford is an outstanding new development of 3 & 4 bedroom A rated homes, in the village setting of Duleek, Co Meath. This unique development offers homeowners the benefits of a relaxed village pace of life, with the thriving large town of Drogheda only 8km away and Dublin city centre a 40km commute.\r\n\r\nBerford offers A rated contemporary 3 & 4 bedroom homes, ranging from 111 to 131 square meters ( c1,194 to 1,410 sq.ft). Built to exemplary standards by Ashcroft Property Development Ltd., with an abundance of natural light throughout, good use of space for storage and energy efficiency, these homes come to the market ready for the practicalities of everyday living.\r\n\r\nThe well designed floor plans create large living and sleeping spaces, with high end kitchen and bathroom fittings adding to the sense of luxury throughout. The smart layout of each home comprises of a formal living room located to the front of each property with a kitchen/dining to the rear. There are large double doors opening out to the generous gardens at the rear of the property. There is also a utility room off the kitchen with dual appliance and storage spaces.\r\n\r\nEach home has a bright hallway with under stair storage and a guest WC. Upstairs, each home has a large master bedroom with en suite, a Large bathroom, with additional bedrooms , all with fitted wardrobes as standard throughout.\r\n\r\nAshcroft Property Development Ltd. is an Irish owned residential development company managed by staff with over 25 years' experience in the delivery of quality housing developments. Working with top professionals in the Irish residential sector and drawing from a pool of high quality crafts people and technicians, Ashcroft brings this well designed and carefully built development to the market with pride and confidence.\r\n\r\nLocal amenities in the town include pharmacies, playgrounds, creche facilities, local shops, health centre, pub, GAA club, pitch and putt and parish church.\r\n\r\nRegistered for the Help To Buy Scheme\r\n\r\nPlease register your interest at berford.ie

      Location

      Duleek takes its name from the Irish word diamh liag, meaning house of stones and referring to an early stone built church, St. Cianan\xe2\x80\x99s Church, the ruins of which are still visible in Duleek today. The Development name of Berford takes its name from the Berford Cross, originally a wayside monument at Downstown, less than 2km from Duleek Village. \r\n\r\nThis historical town is well placed within the Dublin commuter belt and is strategically located between the N2 and M1 motorways and less than 10km from Drogheda Train Station. The M50 Finglas junction of the M50, including Charlestown Shopping Centre and Cinemas are less than 25 minutes\xe2\x80\x99 drive. Berford also boosts convenient access to the range of coastal amenities of Laytown and Bettystown.
