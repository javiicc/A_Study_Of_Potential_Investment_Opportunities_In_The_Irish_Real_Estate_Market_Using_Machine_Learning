    No. 47 Charnwood a three/four bed semi-detached family home positioned along a quiet road in this popular residential development a short distance from Bray Town Centre and the seafront. Developed in the 1970 s, properties in Charnwood are a rarity to the market and given the nature and location of the estate, it is sure to be a popular choice for buyers. \nThe accommodation is set over two floors and at ground floor level a garage conversion ensures the floor area has been fully utilised and now provides for a home office or fourth bedroom with wet room ensuite. The wide entrance hall has room for storage under the stairs. To the rear there is a modern high gloss kitchen with breakfast bar and access to the rear garden. The main dining/living space has a great dual aspect stretching from the front to the rear of the property and features a coal effect gas fire. Upstairs there is a contemporary family bathroom and three good size bedrooms all with fitted wardrobes. \nExternally, on approach, a driveway with off street parking for two cars, there is a lawn area bordered by mature hedging and rose bushes. The impressive rear garden is accessed from the utility room. This private space benefits from a sunny easterly orientation, a maintained lawn, patio area and mature hedging either side of the lawn. At the end of the garden a Barna shed caters for additional storage.\nThis desirable location enjoys a superb choice of amenities within a pleasant stroll including Tesco Supermarket which is adjacent to the entrance of the estate, schools, recreational facilities, and Bray Main Street with its array of shops and eateries is 1km from the property. Leisurely coastal walks along the promenade, the Bray to Greystones cliff walk, and in the grounds of nearby Kilruddery House, can also be enjoyed. Transport is also well catered for, with the DART and a number of Dublin Bus routes passing through the town, as well as public transport connections to the Luas. By private transport, the N11 which acts as a gateway to the M50 or southeast, is easily accessible.\n\nSERVICES\n GFCH \n Double glazed \n Broadband available \n Telephone and TV points \nFeatures \n Mature residential development \n Close to local amenities \n Extended floorplan \n Hard wood flooring throughout \n Private mature garden\n Flexible living space
