    McGuirk Beggan Property are delighted to present number 69 St Mary's Park, Walkinstown to the market. The current owners have spared no expense transforming this 1940's house into a stunning family home with a B2 energy rating to meet the needs of today's modern family. Every inch of this property has been meticulously designed & planned, leaving nothing for the new purchasers to do but put their own stamp on it. The garage to the side has been converted into a working office that could also serve well as a double bedroom. Off this room you will find a new extension with a fully tiled shower room and utility room that is an invaluable addition to the house. The owners have opted for an open plan extended living/kitchen/dining space incorporating bold colour with natural floors and a mix of modern and old in keeping with the traditional style of the property. This area provides the family with a multi-use space for all the family to enjoy.\r\n\r\nUpstairs you will find three good size bedrooms all featuring a neutral colour scheme, polished tongue & groove flooring and plenty of built in storage. The family bathroom has been decorated with equal flair featuring beautiful tiling, heated towel rail, modern vanity unit, bath, wc & whb.\r\n\r\nThe rear garden has had an equal amount of care lavished upon it. Professionally landscaped it sports Kilsaran paving, raised flower beds, mature shrubbery, tongue and groove timber feature wall and good size garage with vehicular access. The property is not overlooked and benefits from a south facing aspect making it the perfect place to spend a lazy sunny day. The front garden is fully walled with original refurbished steel gates and has off-street parking on the cobble-lock driveway. It features a small well-manicured lawn with electric up lighting as well as a young maple and magnolia tree. \r\n\r\nThis property will no doubt cause a stir on the market.
