    Michael Ryder Auctioneers are delighted to bring this four bedroomed bungalow house to the market it is not very often a detached bungalow of this calibre comes on the market in Tuam Town. Church View is a short walk to the centre of Tuam town and directly across the road you have a service station with convenience shop. \r\nTuam town is a premium residential location. It is situated adjacent to the N17, just one minute drive to the motorway network. Walking distance to the town centre with an excellent variety of Shops, Restaurant's, Leisure centre located in the Palace Grounds Park that has a walkway, Children's playground. Tuam town has National Schools and Secondary Schools all close to the town centre.\r\nTuam is a designated Hub town and has seen great rejuvenation in recent years with the rollout of high-speed broadband and the opening of the M17/M18 Motorway. Tuam is the ideal commuting town to Galway City and offers tremendous value for money in comparison to Galway city properties. \r\nAccommodation consists of:\r\nEntrance Hall 5' 0'' x 14' 0''\r\nLaminated flooring, \r\nSitting room 15' 0'' x 11' 11''\r\nOpen fireplace, Laminated floor, \r\nKitchen/Dining/Living area 15' 3'' x 23' 3'\r\nsolid fuel stove that heats the water for the house.\r\nLaminated floor in the dining area, Lino floor covering in the Kitchen \r\nUtility 4' 0'' x 9' 10''\r\nThe washing machine & dryer are in the Utility room, there is a PVC door leading to the Garden \r\nBathroom 9' 0'' x 8' 10''\r\nElectric Shower fitted over the bath \r\nBedroom (1) 9' 1'' x 9' 4''\r\nBedroom (2) 11' 10'' x 13' 6''\r\nBedroom (3) 11' x 10'' x 13' 6''\r\nBedroom (4) 12' 6'' x 11' 2''\r\nThere is a hot-press off the Sitting room, three of the bedrooms has laminated flooring and one bedroom has carpet.
