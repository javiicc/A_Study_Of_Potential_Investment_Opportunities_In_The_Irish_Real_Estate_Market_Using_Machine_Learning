    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Sherry Fitzgerald Stack are delighted to bring this spacious and inviting 3 bed, 3 bath detached residence to the market.\nIdeally located, the residence is set in a peaceful village setting on a private development just a short walk to all local amenities inc. school, creche, shop, pubs, church etc. The market town of Abbeyfeale and the heritage town of Listowel are both located circa within 8km to the East & West. \nAccommodation comprises: Entrance hallway, lounge/sitting room, kitchen/dining room, utility room & W/C. 1st floor accommodation comprises: 3 bedrooms (2 double with one ensuite), single bedroom and main bathroom with whb & w/c. \nThe residence has both oil fired central heating and open fireplace, double glazed windows fitted throughout and is connected to all essential public services. \nThere is pedestrian access to the rear of the property via side gates. Externally the property has a tarmacadam driveway with a lawn area to the front and rear. \nThis property would be ideal for 1st time buyers or those seeking a base in the South West as it is close to beaches and convenient to the established tourist centres of Killarney, Dingle, Ballybunion etc. The newly-renovated 40km Limerick Greenway (off road walking and cycling route) is within a 5 minute drive from the property.\nViewing is recommended and is strictly by appointment only.\n\n\nEntrance Hallway 4.42m x 2.08m. with ceramic floor tiles & 2 windows\n\nLounge/Sitting Room 4.76m x 3.34m. open fireplace with cast iron & painted wooden surround, wooden flooring & bay window\n\nKitchen/Utility Room 5.22m x 3.95m. with wooden fitted units, ceramic wall & floor tiles, window and french doors to rear\n\nUtility Room 2.35m x 1.59m. with washing machine, ceramic floor tiles, wooden fitted units, half glazed door to rear\n\nW/C 1.89m x 1.05m. with ceramic floor tiles, whb, w/c & window\n\nLanding 3.53m x 3.32m. spacious landing area, opening to attic, wooden laminate flooring & window\n\nBedroom 1 3.53m x 3.32m. double room with wooden laminate flooring & window\n\nBedroom 2 3.95m x 3.52m. double room with wooden laminate flooring & window\n\nEnsuite 2.24m x 1.08m. with electric Triton T90 SR, ceramic wall & floor tiles, whb, w/c & window\n\nWardrobe 1.58m x 0.80m. shelved with wooden laminate flooring\n\nBedroom 3 3.30m x 2.22m. single room with wooden laminate flooring & window\n\nMain Bathroom 2.07m x 2.23m. with ceramic wall & floor tiles, bath, whb, w/c & window\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nEircode: V31 X223 GPS No' s: 52.411494 -9.384513\nOn entering Duagh Village via Abbeyfeale, look out for Sherry Fitzgerald Stack directional sign, turn left, look out for Sherry Fitzgerald Stack saleboard.
