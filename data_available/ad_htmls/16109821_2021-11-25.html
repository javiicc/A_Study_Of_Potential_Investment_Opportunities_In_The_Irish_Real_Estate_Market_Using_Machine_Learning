    TOM MCDONALD & ASSOCIATES - EXPERIENCED, PROFESSIONAL, TRUSTED - are delighted to offer to the market this Luxurious 4/5 Bed Detached Family Home on a Beautifully Landscaped Private Site (approx. 0.59 acres) with Large Detached Garage (approx. 70sq.m.) & Pergola. Accessed via an Electric Gated Entrance & Stone Driveway, this Impeccable Property boasts too many Features to list. Built & Maintained to the Highest Standard, this Stunning Property with Tipperary Sandstone Cills is presented in Excellent Condition throughout. Extending to approximately 300sq.m., it is in 'Walk-In-Condition'. The Exquisite Entrance Hall with Double Height Ceiling & Panelled Walls showcases the Bespoke Oak Staircase leading to the First Floor. The Expansive Kitchen has a Quality Fitted Kitchen with Italian Marble Work Surface, Range Master & Feature Island. There is a Separate Utility Room off the Kitchen with Independent Electronic Access to the Manicured Rear Garden & Pergola with lights and Electricity. There are 2 Magnificent Reception Rooms off either side of the Kitchen with Feature Gas Fireplaces making this an Ideal Home for those Family Gatherings. The Property which is compatible for an Air to Water Heating System has a Heat Recovery System with Air Filtration. The Ground Floor has Underfloor Heating throughout with Radiators on the First Floor. There are Solar Panels for Hot Water which are regularly serviced. Fully Alarmed, this Wonderful Property also has Multiple CAT5 points and High-Speed Broadband, Perfect for Working from Home. The Detached Garage with Dual Access has Electrics on Ground & First Floor and is suitable for Conversion subject to PP. Accommodation of this Superb Family Home is comprised of Entrance Hall, Kitchen, 2 Reception Rooms, Utility Room, 2 Bedrooms, Bathroom, En-Suite, W.C. & Plant Room on the Ground Floor with 2 Bedrooms, Office/5th Bedroom, Bathroom, Walk-in-Wardrobe & Storage Cupboard on the First Floor. There are Concrete Floors throughout and both Bathrooms which are Fully Tiled have Jacuzzi Baths and Separate Walk-in-Showers. To Truly Appreciate this Unique Property and All it has to Offer, it is a Must View. The First to see will Buy!! Country Living, yet just a Short Drive to Portarlington, Tullamore, Edenderry & M7 Motorway. Services: Mains Water, Electricity, Broadband & Septic Tank. Oil Fired Central Heating. Built c. 2008.
