    Ardmillan is a premium and small development of just seven large detached family homes built by KTL Construction who are renowned builders from Wexford.\n \nNumber 2 Ardmillan was purchased by the owners from new in late 2015. Working with the builder and their own architect, the owners upgraded and re-designed the interior layout of the house to create large and light filled rooms suitable for a growing family. \n \nThe spacious and light filled accommodation extends to 266 Sq. M. / 2,863 Sq. Ft. approx. over two levels. The layout at ground level comprises: large entrance hall, living room, sunroom, exceptionally large open plan kitchen/dining/family room, large utility room, shower room/guest WC, walk-in hotpress and a spacious understairs storage cupboard. A large cloakroom located off the entrance hall and completes the accommodation at ground level. The layout at first floor level is as equally impressive and comprises: landing area and five generous sized double bedrooms (two with en-suites and the master bedroom has a walk-in wardrobe). A large family bathroom completes the accommodation at this level.\n\n The owners also added a number of additional features at the building stage including triple-glazed PVC windows throughout, underfloor heating downstairs, low-temperature energy efficient Aluminium radiators upstairs. The owners also upgraded the plumbing system and insulation from the original specification. The house includes wired computer network cabling and sockets downstairs.\n\nGARDEN AND GROUNDS:\nThe large and private grounds extend to circa 0.59 Acre. The property is well set back from the road behind a cut stone wall with a sweeping tarmacadam driveway leads up to the front, side and rear of the house. There is secure off-street parking for several cars. The front, side and rear gardens are laid in lawn bordered by mature hedging and trees. A detached garage is located to rear on the left hand side.\n\nLOCATION:\nArdmillan is located in the townland of Jerpoint West close to the market town of Thomastown. A short walk will take you into the centre of Thomastown and to Thomastown Train Station. There are excellent amenities in Thomastown including good primary schools and a secondary school, as well as tertiary schools such as the Pottery Skills School, Grennan Craft Mill, the Grennan Equestrian School and the School of Food. There are a good selection of coffee houses, restaurants, public houses, shops and supermarkets including SuperValu and Lidl. The area is well serviced for the outdoor enthusiast located close to the South Leinster Way with many forest walks including the Thomastown Loop Walk, Woodstock Gardens and Arboretum in Inistioge which date back to the 1740 s and is a hidden gem for locals and tourists alike. Mount Juliet Estate Hotel and Golf Club which remains one of Ireland s finest country houses is only a five minute drive from the property. A twenty minute drive will take you into Kilkenny city and 30 minute drive to Waterford city. The property is located close to Thomastown railway station and is on the Dublin to Waterford Intercity route. The charming villages of Bennettsbridge and Inistioge are both also in close proximity. \n \nViewing of this executive property is highly recommended\n\n\nFIRST FLOOR\n\nEntrance Hall 4.57m x 3.19m + 3.47m x 1.41m.
