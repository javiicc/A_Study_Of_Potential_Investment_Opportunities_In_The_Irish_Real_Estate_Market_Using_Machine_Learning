    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    For Sale, this four-bedroom cottage/farm-house with out-houses and sheds on Ca; 24.72 Hectares (61.08 Acres) of average quality agricultural land. Plus 1/14th share of Ca; 184.73 Hectares of commonage (32.60 Acres) With entitlements. The sheds consist of; A three bay slatted shed, a two-bay hayshed, a one-bay dry shed, a two-bay open workshop, two old cattle sheds & a cattle yard & cattle crush. To be sold in one lot. Located at Derryhillagh, Castlehill, Ballina, Co Mayo. The house is in good condition throughout and has recently been re-decorated. Accommodation includes; Entrance hall, (side entrance) sitting/living-room, kitchen, four bedrooms (one en-suite) shower & toilet (newly tiled and all new sanitary ware installed) This is a fantastic opportunity to acquire this country residence with farmland, commonage and a fine range of out-houses and sheds. There is a possibility of a proposed windfarm on the commonage. Situated under the shadow of Mount Nephin, this is a rare opportunity to acquire such a property as this! Just a short drive to the town of Crossmolina. Call Gerry on 0868459414.
