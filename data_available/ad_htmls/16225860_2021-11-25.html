    103 Highfield Road is an attractive three bedroom semi-detached house which extends to c.135sqm including garage in this leafy and mature private cul de sac. The property is perfect for those looking for a home in a close-knit community with added benefits of some of the best local senior and junior school at its doorstep. This property also benefits from side access and a side garage with great potential to convert or extend (sub to p.p). There is a large paved driveway to the front and the beautiful sunny rear garden is a real gem laid in lawn with hedging and colourful shrubs throughout gathering sunlight and not over-looked. The front of the property provides off-street parking for a number of vehicles.The spacious accommodation comprises a welcoming entrace hallway with a living room and sitting room either side, dining room, kitchen, sun room to the rear and garage with W.C. Upstairs there are three bedrooms and the main bathroom completes the accommodation.Located in a much sought-after mature neighbourhood, a host of local amenities are to be found just minutes away in Dundrum Village, Churchtown and Milltown. On the doorstep is Dundrum Town Centre and the property is within minutes' walk of Dundrum LUAS offering ease of access to the City Centre. There are also numerous bus routes in the vicinity and the M50 is only minutes' drive away.
