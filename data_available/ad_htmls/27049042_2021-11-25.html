      2 Bed Apartment to rent in Woodlands Court. A leafy sylvan setting within walking distance of Greystones south beach and town centre. This well established development is extremely desirable due to its convenient location. \r\n\r\nThe state of the art Marina is host to many activities one can enjoy such as sailing, rowing, and the infamous cliff walk can be accessed from here.\r\n\r\nOUTSIDE The front communal space features clipped topiary hedging, attractive seating areas and pergolas. Mature rear gardens are westerly, sunny and private with mature specimen trees and shrubs. There is also provisions for bins and bikes. \r\n\r\nDIRECTIONS Heading south proceed through Greystones town passing Greystones DART Station on your left hand side.
