    Development Description

      Skylark has been designed with the purchaser in mind; from the high end specification to the energy efficient air to water heat source pumps and generous gardens. These well - appointed homes are situated adjacent to Skylark Park, a wonderful new planned green space. Skylark Park, which, upon completion, will extend to three acres of beautiful parkland for families to enjoy. \r\n\r\nSkylark is in close proximity to the gorgeous coast of Portmarnock Velvet Strand. Living at Skylark you will enjoy the new coastal cycle track between Portmarnock/Baldoyle, local shops including Dunnes Stores, Lidl, boutique shops, florists and more. Portmarnock golf club, one of the most respected venues in the world is on your door step while Malahide Golf Club, Portmarnock Golf Hotel & Links and Sutton Golf Club are all within easy access. Portmarnock promotes a healthy lifestyle with stunning coastal walks and a number of sports and leisure clubs, GAA clubs, tennis, squash, swimming and many more. Malahide Village is a hop on the Dart or a quick drive from Skylark and boasts a lively nightlife and a ton of eateries to choose from; Avoca, Donnybrook Fair, Kajal, Siam Thai and not to forget Gibney's Pub, whilst Howth harbour is just a 10 minute drive away where you can walk the Howth cliff path loop and take in stunning views of the coast followed by a seafood feast in West Pier. \r\n\r\nSkylark is well serviced by transport including: Portmarnock DART (500m), Dublin bus (located Station Road providing easy access into City Centre/Swords), M50 / M1 and Dublin Airport (approx. 8km away). Local schools include St Marnock's National School, Portmarnock Community School, an abundance of creches and Montessori's, Malahide community school and many more.\r\n\r\n\r\n*Please note, images are for indicative purposes only*
