    Fintan Cahill Auctioneers are delighted to bring this 4-bedroom detached property to the market. \r\nThe house sits high on just shy of an acre site with a fabulous view of Lough Ramor.\r\nThe spacious accommodation comprises of the original entrance hall complete with fireplace, a large kitchen and dining area, and a well-appointed living space with magnificent views of the countryside and Lough Ramor.
