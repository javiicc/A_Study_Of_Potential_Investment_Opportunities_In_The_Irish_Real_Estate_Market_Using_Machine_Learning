    GVM are delighted to bring to the market this truly magnificent "Trophy" residence constructed and completed to a wonderful standard enjoying many high end bespoke fixtures, fittings and finishes creating an oasis of luxurious and contemporary living and standing on a truly outstanding and mature site extending to Circa 3.2 st acres. When arriving at Ballyvollane one is pleasantly greeted by a cut stone entrance, automated wrought iron gates and a sweeping tarmac driveway to a generous set down and circulation area. The living and bedroom accommodation are both very generous in size and layout providing a beautiful flow through the house creating a relaxed ambiance that really must be experienced and seen to be appreciated. There is extensive site area incorporating an array of shrubbery, manicured lawns and stud railed paddocks. Substantial garage area lofted. This fantastic home enjoys all the benefits of peaceful and tranquil country living yet is just a few minutes drive from The University of Limerick, Plassey Technological Park, Annacotty Village, Castletroy College, Primary Schools, Monaleen Recreation Park, quality local restaurants and gastro pubs, hotels, gyms and fantastic local sports clubs such as UL Bohs RFC, Monaleen and Ahane GAA and Aisling Annacotty Soccer Club. The area also has excellent local walkways and cycle lanes together with a very regular local bus service. The Motorway is also very accessible providing great connectivity to out national road network. Overall this home oozes sheer class and ticks all the boxes.
