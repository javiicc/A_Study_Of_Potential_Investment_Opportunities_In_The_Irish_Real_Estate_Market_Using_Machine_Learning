    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Sherry FitzGerald Sherry are excited to present 33 Cnoc Tiarnach to the market.\nThis dual aspect, four-bedroom, semi-detached home stands on the largest site in the highly south-after development in Cnoc Tiarnach, Dunshaughlin.\nThis attractive two-year-old property was built and finished with the finest of fixtures and fittings and features an impressive energy efficient A rating, includes underfloor heating, powered by air/ water, a cobble locked driveway to accommodate two vehicles and a wide, gated access to the generous sized garden, which extends from the rear of the property to the side of the property and accommodates two large sheds. \nBeautifully finished accommodation comprises entrance hallway, living room adjoining a large, open-plan kitchen/ dining room and guest w.c.. Upstairs hosts four bedrooms (en-suite in master bedroom) and a family bathroom completes the picture for this exceptional home.\nThe rear garden is accessed from the front gate or from the sliding door in the kitchen/ dining room which finds a wonderful outdoor space with a large patio area finished with outdoor lighting and two large sheds, one for storage, one for workshop or can be converted to a gym or office space. \nIdeally situated in Dunshaughlin Village with excellent junior and secondary schools, supermarkets, restaurants, boutiques etc within walking distance. Commuting is very comfortable from Dunshaughlin as the M3 Parkway rail station at Pace is a 7 minute drive, as is access to the M3 which in turn, gives access to M50, Dublin City and Airport. Also a bus service may be availed of within just minutes walk. \nViewing comes highly recommended!\n\n\nEntrance Hall Beautiful introduction with tiled flooring extending to kitchen/ dining room, living room and guest w.c., ceiling coving and understairs storage drawers.\n\nLiving Room Large living room with extra window to side of property, ceiling coving and glass double doors leading to kitchen/ dining room.\n\nKitchen Dining Room Generous sized kitchen/ dining room with extra window to side of property, island and kitchen units finished with granite countertops and sliding door to patio area and garden for an extended entertaining area.\n\nGuest w.c.. Off hallway with w.h.b. and w.c..\n\nBedroom 1 Double bedroom to rear of property.\n\nBedroom 2 Larger double bedroom to rear of property.\n\nBathroom Spacious family bathroom with w.h.b., w.c., bath tub and heated towel rail.\n\nBedroom 3 Master bedroom with bay window and en-suite, adjoining to walk-in wardrobe area.\n\nBedroom 4 Single bedroom, currently used as walk-in wardrobe.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom Dublin, on approaching Dunshaughlin, continue through two sets of pedestrian lights at Bank of Ireland, turn right, continue for about 100 meters and keep left for Ratoath road. Find #33 Cnoc Tiarnach on the right-hand-side.
