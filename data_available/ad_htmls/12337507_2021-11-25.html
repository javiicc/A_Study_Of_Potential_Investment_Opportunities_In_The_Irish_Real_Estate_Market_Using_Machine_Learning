    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    LOCATION / DESCRIPTION:\nThis is a beautiful level site of 0.75 acres being sold subject to planning for 1 dwelling. Services such as water and ESB are at hand. The site has a lovely setting and a great location in one of Wexfords most sought after areas. The site is just 1km off the N11 Wexford to Enniscorthy Road and close to Crossabeg village with school, church and pub. Oilgate is 2 km away and the new M11 Motorway is within minutes. The towns of Wexford and Enniscorthy are within 10 minutes drive.\n\nNOTE: COUNTY COUNCIL LOCAL NEED RULES APPLY.\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom Wexford take the N11 northbound towards Enniscorthy, and from Ferrycarrig Bridge continue for 6km and turn right signposted 'Castlesow'. The site is 1km along this road on the left-hand side.\nFrom Enniscorthy take the N11 southbound towards Wexford and when you pass through Oilgate Village continue for 2.3km and turn left signposted 'Castlesow'. The site is 1 km along this road on the left-hand side.\n\nVIEWING: \nStrictly by appointment with the sole selling agents, Benny Sullivan Estate Agents.
