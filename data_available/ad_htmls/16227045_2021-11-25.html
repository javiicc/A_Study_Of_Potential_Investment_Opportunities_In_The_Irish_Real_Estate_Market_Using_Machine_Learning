    Northumberland Avenue is quickly becoming one of the most desirable roads in the area. Lined with elegant two storey over garden level properties on one side and picturesque villa style homes on the other, the streetscape is incredibly attractive. A one way road running from Georges Sreet Upper, the residents here are just a few short steps from the endless amenities Dun Laoghaire town offers and the abundance of leisure facilities along the seafront. \n\nNicely set back from the road with ample off-street parking, this is a handsome and imposing, double fronted property offering an abundance of flexible accommodation. Previously in use as a bed and breakfast, its former glory is there to be grasped with many of its original features remaining including tall ceilings with ceiling coving and centre roses and beautiful feature fireplaces. On the hall floor level there is a gracious drawing room stretching front to back and a mirroring room on the other side of the hall has been subdivided into two bedrooms (1x en-suite) and could easily be opened up again. On the return there is a further double bedroom with en-suite and a bathroom. On the ground floor there is a separate entrance lobby and along one side of this level there is an open plan, living / dining / kitchen which runs from the front to the rear of the property. On the other side of this level there is a morning room, linen room, further bedroom, study and a bathroom. At the top of the property there is a guest w.c. on the return and four double bedrooms (3 x en-suite). \n\nTo the rear the house there is a low maintenance garden with an attractive granite wall. It is partly paved for al fresco dining and there is also a lawn area bordered by raised beds. To the front of the house there is part cobble-locked and part pebbled off street parking area for a number of cars.\n\nThe location is excellent, situated within a short stroll of Dun Laoghaire town centre, with superb shopping facilities on the doorstep, along with a variety of cafes, bars, restaurants, breathtaking sea front walks, The Lexicon, National Maritime Museum and The People s Park with its popular farmers market are also nearby. The house is conveniently located on a one-way street of similar properties, which reduces passing traffic. There are several highly-regarded schools within close proximity, including Rathdown, CBC Monkstown, Blackrock College, St. Joseph of Cluny and Loreto Dalkey. Nearby transport links include the DART, while several Dublin Bus routes including the 7, 7 A, 46 A, 45 A and 111 are within striking distance.\n\n\nGarden Level\n\nEntrance Lobby tiled floor\n\nHall
