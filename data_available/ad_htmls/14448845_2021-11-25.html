    A most substantial & impressive detached family home occupying a prominent and commanding position within its own secluded landscaped grounds of approx 1/2 an acre. This is undoubtedly one of the finest properties ever to come to the market in Tralee enjoying a unique and convenient setting on the edge of Tralee so close to the many amenities & 10km from Kerry Airport. The modern structure provides a very comfortable residence which has been constructed with great taste & flair around today's modern living standards.\r\n\r\nHome owners will appreciate the practical layout of the property boasting 4 formal reception rooms, 6 bedrooms 2 ensuites, one which is adjoining the master bedroom upstairs that includes a dressing room and a loft style office. The kitchen/breakfast room features a crescent style kitchen area which is fitted with an extensive range of shaker style beech and aider wood units with granite & junckers work tops. The breakfast area again features French doors that open onto the timber decking that form part of the landscaped gravel garden.
