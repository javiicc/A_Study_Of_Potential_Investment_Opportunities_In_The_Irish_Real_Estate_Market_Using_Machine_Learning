    APP Kirrane Auctioneering are delighted to bring to the market this fantastic stand alone dwelling located within walking distance of Oughterard town. Oughterard is a small town on the banks of the Owenriff River close to the western shore of Lough Corrib in County Galway. Nestled between Lough Corrib and the start of the Connemara Mountain range. A prime fishing destination on the shore of Lough Corrib known for it's trout and salmon fishing. It is located 26km northwest of Galway City, the City of the Tribes, on the N59.\r\n Oughterard offers an array of sporting, leisure facilities and service providers including; national and secondary schools, an Irish speaking mixed school and creche facilities, post office, a variety of shops and regular bus service. The village also offers a GAA club, Rugby club, children's playground and 18 hole championship course with a club house, pro-shop and restaurant. Oughterard is both a picturesque and historical town with the ruins of the Aughnanure Castle, a well preserved medieval tower house located just 3km outside the town and the Quite Man Bridge located down the Leam road just 8km from the town which was the setting for the 1950's film The Quite Man starring John Wayne and Maureen O'Hara. Also close by are the Glengowla Mines.\r\n\r\nThe property was built in 2002 and is sitting on a large site of 0.39 Hectares (1 acre) approximately with ample opportunity for further development. Natural stone walls accommodating the property name place 'Waterside Lodge, lantern lights and electronic roller gate to the front of the property offering privacy to the dwelling. Mature gardens surround this magnificent home. Surrounded by similar bespoke detached dwellings. The site also accommodates outbuildings suitable for keeping horses or ponies. A shared parcel of commonage adjoining the property with mature trees creates privacy, shelter and the assurance that no further dwellings will be constructed there on.\r\n\r\nDetached garage currently used as temporary accommodation that is suitable for a teenagers private retreat, games room or guest room. Bay window with decorative glass, curtain pole, ceiling cornice and centrepiece bespoke chandler.
