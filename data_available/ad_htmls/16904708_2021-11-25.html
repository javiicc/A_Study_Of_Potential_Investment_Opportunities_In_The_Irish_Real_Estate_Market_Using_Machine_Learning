This attractive 3- bedroomed first floor, own door duplex apartment with large, sunny south facing balcony is a great choice for any buy-to-let investors or owner-occupier.\n\nThis bright and spacious family home is presented in good condition and has been well maintained and upgraded throughout the years by the current owners. On entrance to the property, potential purchasers are greeted by a spacious front hallway which leads to a well laid out kitchen/breakfast area, downstairs bathroom and bright and spacious living/dining room. The entire was upgraded to include new carpets, flooring, painting and decorating as well as LED spotlights and boiler and pump in 2020. Upstairs, the property consists of 3 spacious bedrooms currently laid out as master with vanity unit and ensuite with shower, double guest room with ample built-in wardrobes, and a large box room suitable for use as a single room or home office, with views out to the green. A family bathroom and hot-press complete the rooms on this floor. \n\nA key selling feature of the property is its large south facing rear terrace providing ample space for entertaining during the warm summer months and a private retreat for afternoon BBQs and evening dinners as it is not overlooked. A real sun trap from dawn till dusk. \nThere is ample storage in the property with space under the stairs as well as a large, floored attic. Designated space to the front of the property is allocated for bins and built-in wardrobes provide space for day-to-day wares. Parking is also available.\n\nNumber 248 Charlesland Park, is located in the peaceful, sough-after Charlesland development within close proximity to a wide range of amenities and the Charlesland shopping centre.\nGreystones Town is located within easy reach of the property on foot, by car or local bus service, connecting the development to the DART and Commuter trains (Park & Ride service) as well as the surrounding hinterland (M11/M50). Dublin City and airport as well as Wexford and Rosslare Port can all be reached within an hour. This sought-after location boasts a wonderful selection of bistros, bars, boutiques, restaurants and shops, the village library, churches and a large number of highly regarded schools. \n\nThere are also plenty of local activities including GAA, football, Rugby, Sailing, Rowing and Sea Swimming. The area is home to a number of top Golf Clubs and a short drive to the world famous Powerscourt and Gardens as well as Druids Glen. The Shoreline Leisure Centre and Swimming pool is located a 5 minute drive from the property and provides sporting activities and events all year round.\nSituated in this stunning location Greystones gives residence access to local beaches, countryside and nearby villages of Delgany and Enniskerry.\n\nViewing is Highly recommended.
