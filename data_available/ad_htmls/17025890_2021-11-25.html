    St James's Wood is a private development tucked away, off the South Circular Road close to St James's Hospital. Built in 2001 by the Cosgrave Brothers, this popular gated residential complex with private parking and communal garden space, offers excellent value for money, in a location becoming more and more desirable by the day.\r\n\r\n\r\nNo. 34 is a spacious, first floor two bedroom apartment. With dual aspect, the accommodation extends to approx. 64 sqm and briefly comprises of a welcoming entrance hall with a spacious storage press. The open plan living/dining room features an electric fireplace with a marble surround and timber mantel, timber floors and a sliding door onto a sunny west facing decked balcony. The modern kitchen is fully equipped with slate floor tiles. There are two double bedrooms with built in wardrobes and a bathroom. \r\n\r\n\r\nThe property is only a 15 minute cycle to St. Stephen's Green and 500m from the entrance to St James's/New Children's Hospital. The Suir Road LUAS station is a short walk away with the 68, 68A and 123 Dublin Bus routes also providing excellent transport links. The Kilmainham area has been rejuvenated over recent years and a number of local shops and eateries have opened. The area is also serviced by schools, The Phoenix Park, The National War Memorial Park, The Irish Museum of Modern Art, The Hilton Hotel and Kilmainham Gaol Museum.\r\n\r\n\r\nAccommodation\r\n\r\nEntrance Hallway: \r\nwith alarm panel, spacious storage press and timber floor\r\n\r\nLiving/Dining Room: 5.6m X 4.6m\r\nSpacious, west facing aspect with living and dining space, feature electric fireplace, Venetian blinds, timber flooring, sliding patio door to private decked balcony\r\n\r\nKitchen: 2.8 x 1.8m\r\nModern shaker style kitchen with tiled splashback, stainless steel sink, fridge/freezer, oven, hob and washer/dryer\r\n\r\nBedroom 1: 4.2 x 2.5m\r\nDouble bedroom with fitted wardrobes, Venetian blinds and timber flooring\r\n\r\nBedroom 2: 3.4 x 2.4m\r\nDouble bedroom with fitted wardrobes, Venetian blinds and timber flooring\r\n\r\nBathroom: 2.16 x 1.67m\r\nBath with shower attachment, WC and WHB.
