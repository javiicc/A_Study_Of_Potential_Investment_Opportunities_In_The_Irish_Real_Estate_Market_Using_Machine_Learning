    Hogan Durkan are pleased to present No 1 Monks Walk, Chapel Lane, Blackabbey Gate, a most impressive and meticulously presented three-bedroom, two story residence, ideally positioned in this highly desirable residential setting off the Blackabbey Road, in the village of Adare, Co Limerick. \r\nNo 1 is ideally located within this attractive estate, just a short stroll from the centre of Adare Village. It comes in turnkey condition, has been maintained to the highest standards by its current owners and will attract attention from a host of potential buyer types. \r\n\r\nBlackabbey Gate, located next to the site of the Old Creamery in Adare, is a four-minute walk to the centre of Adare Village and the immediate area is surrounded largely by similar type residential properties. The estate benefits from being in close proximity to the highly successful Adare GAA Club (10-minute walk). The world-famous Adare Village itself boasts a range of impressive amenities and services such as the world-renowned Adare Manor Hotel & Golf Course (future home of the Ryder Cup), Dunraven Arms Hotel, Fitzgeralds Woodlands Hotel, Adare Heritage Centre, Adare manor Golf Club, various fine dining and chic boutiques, popular bars and supermarkets, various local sports clubs and Pitch & Putt course as well as various national schools and the impressive Manor Fields to name but a few. \r\n\r\nAdare is one of both Limerick and Irelands most popular villages and appeals to those looking for the countryside living with the benefit of being just 15 minutes from Limerick City and all its associated amenities. The village of Adare is serviced by Bus Eireann Expressway Routes 13 & 14 and Bus Eireann Route 321 and is located a short distance from M20 with links to the National Road Network. The property itself is located approximately 18.9km from Limerick City via M20 & N21 with all its associated services and amenities and approximately 43.5km from Shannon International Airport via N18. \r\n\r\nAccommodation\r\n\r\nUpon entry, one is immediately struck by the bright spacious feeling of the property, with the impressive light filled living room located to ones right and w/c located immediately to ones left in the entrance hallway. The kitchen/dining area is located to the rear of the property overlooking to the superbly designed and maintained rear garden with side access to the property.\r\nThe first floor boasts three well-appointed bedrooms, main bathroom and storage closet. The dual aspect master bedroom is accompanied by a suitably stylish en-suite and fitted built in wardrobes, while the second (dual aspect) and third bedrooms also benefit from built in wardrobes. \r\n\r\nNote: Please note that no apparatus, fittings, fixtures, or services have been tested. Measurements and photographs provided for guidance purposes only. \r\n\r\nDirections: From Pat Collins Bar, head southwest on Main St/N21 toward Blackabbey Rd/L1422.Turn right onto Blackabbey Rd/L1422. Turn right onto Abbots Way and left onto Chapel Lane and the property is at the end of the road. \r\n\r\nViewing Details: Please note that all viewings will be held in accordance with HSE Guidelines and must be adhered to at all times. Strictly by appointment only. Virtual Viewing Available.
