    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    This beautifully presented home is situated on an elevated site affording stunning unobstructed panoramic views to Kenmare Bay and and the Macgillycuddy Reeks mountain range. The property is only a short drive to Kenmare town with all its amenities whilst offering the beauty of countryside living in a stunning natural setting and beautifully landscaped gardens. This single storey property offers a wealth of space for the entire family, both inside and outside and has been handsomely maintained by its current owners.\r\n\r\nThe bright and airy property has a total floor area of c. 269 sqm. The living room offers a fabulous space with an inset gas stove and views of Kenmare Bay and the Kerry coastline through double height panoramic windows. Double glass doors lead into the kitchen cum dining room. There is a large modern fully fitted hardwood kitchen with island unit and a wood burning stove. Just off the dining area sliding doors lead to the conservatory. All four double bedrooms are very generous in size and two of them are en-suite. The master suite is situated overlooking the bay and has a walk-in wardrobe and spacious en-suite. All bathrooms are fitted with contemporary sanitary ware. The kitchen, conservatory and hallway are tiled and the living room, lounge and all bedrooms have carpets. One of the bedrooms is presently used as a lounge/library. The high building energy rating of B3 is a testament to the quality of the build which includes triple glazed windows.\r\n\r\nThe property stands on a site of c. 2.5 acres of landscaped gardens with mature planting, a pond and various patio areas. There is plenty of off-street parking and a double garage.
