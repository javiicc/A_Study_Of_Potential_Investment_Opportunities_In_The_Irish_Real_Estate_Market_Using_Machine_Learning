This 3 bedroom end-terraced property comes to the market in gorgeous condition, perfect as a new home for a growing family, or indeed as an exciting investment opportunity. Built in the 1820s, the property was fully renovated in 2007, boasting stunning original oak floorboards, original coving on downstairs ceilings, bespoke tilt and turn windows, a working Victorian-era fireplace, as well as beautiful views of Cork city. \n \nThe ground floor of the property is comprised of living room, dining room, kitchen and guest bathroom, with a gorgeous patio area to the rear of the home. The living room features oak flooring while the dining room features beach flooring. Both the kitchen and guest toilet boast gorgeous, modern tiling, and the kitchen is equipped with all the latest appliances, with fridge-freezer, microwave, diswasher, washing machine and tumble dryer all included in the sale price.\n\nUpstairs is comprised of 3 bedrooms, one with ensuite, and a master bathroom. There is brand new carpeting on the stairs and landing area, and the bedrooms boast stunning oak flooring. With ample storage space throughout, all rooms also enjoy plenty of sunlight during the day, as well as gorgeous views of Cork city. Brand new carpets, as well as a new central heating boiler have also been installed.\n\nTo the rear is a large balcony area which leads out to the spacious and well-maintained gardens. There are 3 garden areas in total, all boasting mature greenery, including fig, apple, pear and eucalyptus trees. Additionally, there is a large attic space at the top of the house, perfect for use as a home office, workspace, or teenager's den.\n\nLocated only a stone's throw from the various amenities of the city centre, this property is in a highly sought-after area, with a great community spirit. There are many excellent primary/secondary schools in the area, as well as many cafes, restaurants and boutiques to visit. Viewing is highly recommended!
