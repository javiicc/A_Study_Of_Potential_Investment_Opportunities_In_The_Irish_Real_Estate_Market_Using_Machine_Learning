    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to present to the market this four bedroom, west facing property in the ever popular Hybreasal Development in Kilmainham. \n\nThe property comprises of welcoming entrance hall, living room, open place kitchen / dining room and utility room at ground level. Upstairs there are four bedrooms, shower room and wc. The property comes with great storage, with a utility room, under stairs storage and lots of attic room.\n\nHybreasal is a hidden gem in Dublin 8, located in the immaculately manicured grounds of the beautiful former convent. Set in a very exclusive and sought-after development with a majority of owner occupiers, the grounds of this development are immaculately kept, set behind secure electric gates and with scenic park benches throughout. Ideal for a with a young family who want to live close to the city and have the conveniences of community living. \n\nSet in a quiet and very popular location, close to shops, bus routes, the Luas close by, the N11, M50 & Dublin city just a stone s throw away. With the fantastic location, all your needs will be catered for with a wonderful selection of bistros, boutiques, restaurants and shops, churches and a large number of highly regarded schools. The N11/M50 is also within easy reach making for an easy commute to the city, airport and beyond. Also just on your doorstep is the Dublin bike-sharing station at the gate of the development.\n\nAmenities abound in the area including the Phoenix Park and beautiful walks along the River Liffey from Island Bridge. Kilmainham is full of culture and history with nearby sites such as the Irish Museum of Modern Art, Kilmainham Gaol and The Royal Hospital Kilmainham.
