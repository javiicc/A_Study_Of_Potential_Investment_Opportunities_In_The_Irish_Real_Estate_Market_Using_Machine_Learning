    Luxury Residence with Creche\r\nDon't miss this incredible opportunity. This property is beautifully positioned overlooking the River Barrow in a sought after location. Grangemellon, Athy is situated on the main Carlow road just 10 minutes from Kilkee Golf Club and has easy access to main road networks and Athy railway station. The property is a superb bright spacious home of c. 2,200 sq ft. built and finished to a high quality. The purpose built creche measuring approx 1,800 sq ft is a very unique feature of this property. It is fully in compliance with planning regulations and all regulations regarding child care and creche facilities. This unit could alternatively be used for many professional services such as an accountants office or doctors surgery (spp). It could easily be converted to Bed and Breakfast, Independent living, Offices, Retirement home, Gym, Treatment rooms, Studio (Dance, Arts, Music, Martial Arts), Multi-generational living.
