    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to bring 32 Ashcroft to the market. This wonderful three-bedroom terraced property has the benefit of a west facing rear garden and is within a short 5 minute walk to Raheny Village and DART station.\nThe living accommodation comprises of an entrance porch, hallway, open plan living/ dining room and kitchen with access to the rear garden. Upstairs there are two generous double bedrooms, a single bedroom and the main family bathroom. There is off street parking to the front while the rear garden provides for low maintenance and privacy. The west facing garden is laid out with in lawn and is finished with stone chippings and a barna shed. The location is second to none, Ashcroft is in the heart of Raheny with a wealth of amenities right on your doorstep including Raheny DART station, shops, excellent schools, pubs, library and St Anne Park.
