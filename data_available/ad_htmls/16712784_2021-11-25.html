    Number 17 is a truly wonderful semi-detached family home boasting a quiet cul-de-sac setting. Recently renovated, the property has a beautiful bright and airy feel. This fine home offers a stylish, modern interior which is in immaculate condition. It is evident that no cost was spared by its current owners when undergoing the renovations and the high quality finishes are there to be admired as soon as you set foot through the door. The current owners' attention to detail ensures that this really is a home of true distinction located in a most sought after South Dublin area.\nBrookfield Green is a small, mature and utterly quaint development just off the KCR within easy reach of Terenure, Harold's Cross and the city centre. Built in the early 90's, Brookfield Green enjoys a wide variety of amenities on its doorstep to include top primary and secondary schools, shopping facilities, cafes, restaurants, sports clubs and parks to include Bushy Park. There are a number of transport options nearby and the property is situated just minutes from the M50 road network which connects to all major national routes. \nNumber 17 is wonderfully situated at the end of the terrace which is overlooking a nice green space and also boasts a beautiful and enviable South facing rear garden. The accommodation downstairs comprises a porch, an entrance hall, beautiful kitchen/diner, living room and downstairs WC. Originally a three bed, the upstairs has been re-configured and now comprises two very generous bedrooms (both with ensuite). There is also a large multi-purpose block-built shed which could be useful as a home office, workshop, gym, games room or whatever one wishes. This property is not just aesthetically pleasing, it also possesses an impressive C1 BER rating.
