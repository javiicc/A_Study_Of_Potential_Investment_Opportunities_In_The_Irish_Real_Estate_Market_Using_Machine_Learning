Within easy reach of St. James Hospital, City Centre and M50, is situated next door to the new Hilton Hotel which has a gym + swimming pool. All mod cons.\r\n\r\nThe apartment is modern, clean and fully equipped with a dishwasher/washer-dryer/microwave, wi-fi etc. Safe storage for bicycle also available. The complex is very secure with CCTV in common areas, secure access codes for entry/exit and intercoms. The grounds are landscaped.\r\n\r\nThe kitchen has a contemporary design with granite worktops. The apartment has high ceilings and hardwood floors throughout. The bedrooms have full-height fitted wardrobes with integrated shelves and hanging rails. Natural Gas central heating and pressurised hot water system.\r\n\r\nFurnishings and appliances, all kitchen utensils, maintenance of common areas, refuse and recycling costs are all included in the rent.\r\n\r\nThe Chocolate Factory is located beside the Kilmainham Hilton hotel featuring the Living Well fitness Centre comprising of a fully equipped gym; hydrotherapy pool, sauna rooms, Jacuzzi and treatment rooms.
