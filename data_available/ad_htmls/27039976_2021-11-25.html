No.7 will let furnished as seen. \n\n*** Please register you interest by email only - pls include your mobile number *** \n\nBrighton Lodge is situated in the heart of Foxrock Village (beside the post office), a small scheme of only 21 red brick houses within a gated development. No. 7 built c. 1993 was the original showhouse, it is an attractive, two bedroom bungalow with off street parking, rear garden and communal grounds. The accommodation extends to 48sq.m/517sq.ft. approximately and comprises entrance hall, living room/dining room, kitchen, two bedrooms and a bathroom. \n\n*No pets allowed *No smoking allowed \n*One designated car space only \n\nThis development is located off prestigious brighton Road with Foxrock Village literally on your doorstep. It is within walking distance of a host of amenities; Foxrock Golf Club, Carrickmines Tennis Club, local shops, restaurants, boutiques and excellent transport links, including the LUAS, M50 and N11 with QBC. \n\nACCOMMODATION \n\nENTRANCE PORCH HALL 1.0m x 5.23m With laminate wooden floor, ceiling coving and dado rail \n\nLIVING/DINING ROOM 4.18m x 3.69m (maximum measurement) This room is to the front aspect with large bay window, ceiling coving, laminate wooden floor and a timber fireplace with wrought iron inset, ornate tiles and granite hearth. \n\nKITCHEN 2.15m x 2.05m The kitchen with a velux window and tiled floor is fitted with a range of wooden wall and floor units with tiled splashback. There is an integrated oven, hob and extractor fan, washing machine.\n\nBATHROOM 2.02m x 1.97m The bathroom has a tiled floor and partially tiled walls, the suite comprises a corner bath with shower attachment, w.c. , pedestal w.h.b., mirror and velux window. \n\nBEDROOM 1 3.55m x 2.92m A spacious double bedroom with a range of fitted wardrobes and storage units; it overlooks the rear garden. \n\nBEDROOM 2 2.66m x 2.18m A single bedroom with a window/door to rear garden and access to the attic \n\nOUTSIDE There is paved parking area to the front and the rear garden is laid out in lawn with mature foliage and it enjoys a south facing orientation. There are mature communal grounds. \n\nPARKING One designated car parking space and visitor parking spaces. \n\nSERVICES GFCH and Alarm
