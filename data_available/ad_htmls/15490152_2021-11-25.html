The property enjoys the benefits of tranquil country living and situated only 5 minutes from Ballinasloe Town.\n\nBuilt c. 2002 the property is built as a Georgian house replicate and extends to c. 2,500 sq. ft., presented in excellent condition with luxurious finishes throughout. Living accommodation is comprised of an entrance hall with a beautiful sweeping staircase, living room, kitchen/dining room, utility, main bathroom and bedroom 5/office. The third floor has been converted for use as a cinema room and storage space.\n\nCertified with a C1 BER Certificate, the property is heated via natural gas fired central heating, a gas fired feature fireplace and a solid fuel stove. The cavity walls have been pumped, there is concrete hollow core flooring and double glazed windows feature throughout.\n\nExternally the property sits on a c. 0.75 acre site which has been extensively landscaped and is wildlife friendly. The garden features mature trees and shrubs with mature hedging sealing the perimeter of the site. To the rear, with access via double doors in the dining room, is a lovely paved and wall patio area which leads around to the front. There is an extra-large shed to the rear, which could be converted for use as a workshop/apartment/gym or for storage space. Electrical gated access leads onto the large tarmac driveway which wraps around to the back of the property and provides ample private parking space.\n\nPerrsepark is ideally located just 5 minutes from Ballinasloe Town with amenities including supermarkets Aldi, Lidl & Tesco, an array of shops, pubs, restaurants, hotels, cafes, schools, GAA pitch, public transport, Ballinasloe Golf Academy and Driving Range, Dubarry Factory Outlet Store, Coral Leisure Club and the M6 Business Park.\n\nSituated just 5 minutes from Aughrim village, 15 minutes from Athlone, 30 minutes from Galway City and 6 minutes from access to the M6 Motorway, the property is ideally located within easy reach of surrounding towns, villages and commuting routes.
