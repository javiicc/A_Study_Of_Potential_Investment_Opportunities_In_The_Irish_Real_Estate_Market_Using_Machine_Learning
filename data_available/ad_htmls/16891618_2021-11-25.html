    Clareville, Lansdowne Villas, Ennis Road, Limerick\r\nSherry FitzGerald are pleased to bring this very fine turn of the century property to the market. Clareville is a semi-detached two-bay, three-storey red brick family home, built c. 1900, with timber-framed entrance porch. \r\n\r\nLike many late Victorian and Edwardian houses the design incorporates a multitude of architectural detailing. The property retains original sash windows, door leaf, sidelights and overlight. \r\n\r\nInternally architectural features including high ceilings with cornicing, marble fireplaces and original floor tiles. This house forms part of the turn of the twentieth-century development of Landsdowne Villas designed by Clifford Smith, who was also responsible for the design of Shannon Rowing Club.\r\n\r\nClareville offers three floors of perfectly appointed living space in one of Limerick s most exclusive residential addresses. It combines comfortable family living with the convenience of an address close to some of the finest residential amenities Limerick has to offer. \r\n\r\nThe property is within walking distance of some of the city s most renowned primary and secondary schools, including JFK, Ardscoil R s and Villiers. Nationally regarded sporting facilities including Limerick Lawn Tennis Club and Thomond Park are just a stone s throw away, as are an unrivalled selection of award winning hotels, restaurants and superb riverside walks.\r\n\r\nThe front door is set well back from the Ennis Road affording the residents of Clareville a high degree of privacy. Upon entering the property one is greeted by a large welcoming hallway with 2 gracious inter-connecting reception rooms to the left. Both have feature period fireplaces, there is a large bay widow overlooking the front garden and there are original polished timber floorboards. To the rear of the ground floor there is an extended kitchen/breakfast room with AGA cooker and sky lights allowing natural daylight to flood in to the dining area. A utility room and guest w.c are positioned to the rear of the kitchen and there are double doors to the back garden.\r\n\r\nThe first floor return of the property houses a large family bathroom and a double bedroom. On the first floor there is a large master bedroom with stunning ensuite bathroom adjoining it. There is also a generous dressing room which was originally in use as a bedroom.\r\n\r\nThe top floor of the property provides two further large double bedrooms and a single room to complete the very generous family accommodation which is more than ample for family living.\r\n\r\nThe gardens to the rear of Clareville are very generous and feature rear vehicular access with electric gates and parking for a number of cars. There is also a detached garage. The rear access via the Farranshone Road offer potential to the new buyer to develop the rear of the property (subject to relevant planning permission)\r\n\r\nAccommodation: Hall, living, dining, kitchen/breakfast, utility, guest w.c., 5/6 bedrooms, ensuite, bathroom.\r\nGFCH.
