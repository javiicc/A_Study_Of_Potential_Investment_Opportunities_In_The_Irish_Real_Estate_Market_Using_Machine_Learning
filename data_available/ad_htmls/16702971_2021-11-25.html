      Part of New Development at Monterey

    • Overall Floor Area: 213 m\xc2\xb2
    No 4 is a semi-detached west facing home which offers 213 sq.m./2,293 sq.ft. of spacious accommodation providing a large kitchen/dining area with an additional generous living room at ground floor level along with a wash room. On the upper floors it provides 4 bedrooms, 2 en-suite, with a generous family bathroom along with an additional large living room ideal for larger families requiring an additional space. Bedroom no. 2 offers access to a private terrace which benefits for the morning sun.\r\n\r\nKitchens and utility room are fitted with beautiful Leicht kitchens by McNally Living with Miele appliances throughout, a top of the range Bora induction hob is also included. Bathrooms are by Waterloo Bathrooms which feature large format porcelain tiles to select walls and floors.\r\nA stunning light well floods the entrance hall and landings with light through the house making it a bright open and welcoming addition to the house.\r\n\r\nUnderfloor heating is provided throughout the house with an Air Source Heat Pump proving energy efficient heat and hot water with the additional benefit of a Mechanical Ventilation unit which provides a healthier more energy efficient home.
