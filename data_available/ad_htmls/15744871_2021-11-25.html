    This unfinished dwellinghouse sits wonderfully in the heart of the countryside with beautiful views of the Ox Mountain range from the front and the river Moy to the rear. Only a short distance from Cloonacool village and local amenities including shop and national school this property has huge potential. The site extends to circa .74 of an acre. The property is well proportioned and the ground floor has ample family living space with a kitchen and dining area leading to a bright living room, that has French doors, together with separate sitting room with fireplace and French doors, utility, bathroom and bedroom.
