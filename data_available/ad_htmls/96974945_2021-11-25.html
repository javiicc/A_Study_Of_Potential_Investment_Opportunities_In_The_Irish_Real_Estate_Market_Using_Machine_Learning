    Development Description

      Hallwell is a development of very spacious three and four bedroom homes in Adamstown, Lucan. The project is being developed by Hugh Mc Greevy & Sons who have been building quality homes in Lucan and across Dublin for over 40 years.\n\nPhase 2 will have 2 & 3 bedroom homes for sale. \n\nThe large two bedroom houses are approx.90 sq.m / (965 sq.ft.) and both bedrooms are en-suite. \n\nThree bedroom homes are particularly spacious and will vary from approx. 114 sqm / (1,226 sqft) to approx. 121sqm / (1,302sqft) \n\nHallwell is situated in Adamstown off the Newcastle Road, Lucan The area offers a wealth of amenities a brand new Lidl store is now open beside the development and a large new park is being created adjoining Hallwell along with two other large parks within Adamstown. Lucan Golf Club is very close to the development along with an excellent range of other sporting amenities.\n\nHallwell offers exceptional value, extremely spacious and well-built homes and a superb location with great connectivity to Dublin by train, bus or car.
