    Property Partners O'Brien Swaine are delighted to present Red Row House to the market. This beautiful, circa 250sq m, 4 double bedroom, 6 bathroom, detached family home is located on a 0.5 acre private site, set back from, and providing access to, the Red Row Road. \r\nThis exceptional property boasts a large modern kitchen / dining room with top of the range appliances - including a Stoves range cooker (imported from the UK), a Miele designer extractor fan, separate built in Neff oven (with steam function), Neff warming drawer and Neff microwave - and island unit with granite work surfaces. A utility room, with stackable washing and tumble dryer (not included), shower and toilet are all situated off of the kitchen, as is a large sunroom, currently used as a full-time office, ideal for those needing space and privacy to work remotely. To the left of the welcoming entrance hall is the large sitting room, with feature fireplace, whilst a second reception room is located to the right of the hall. This reception room is currently set-up as a full gym, with the highest standard commercial grade equipment (the equipment can be purchased separately - see full listing for details). Ideal for the fitness enthusiast or GAA family. Both reception rooms have large bay windows, allowing natural light to enter the property. Upstairs boasts 4 large double bedrooms, all en-suite, along with a main bathroom with jacuzzi bath.
