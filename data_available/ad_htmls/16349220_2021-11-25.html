    TOM MCDONALD & ASSOCIATES EXPERIENCED, PROFESSIONAL, TRUSTED - are delighted to offer to the market this Wonderful 3 Bed End of Terrace in Portarlington extending to approx. 1,250sq.ft. This Concrete Built Property with a Concrete First Floor is located within walking distance of Portarlington Town Centre & All Amenities. Accommodation of this Spacious property, which is Tastefully Decorated throughout, is comprised of Living Room, Kitchen/Dining Area, Study/4th Bedroom, 3 Bedrooms, En-Suite, Bathroom & W.C. Newly Painted & Decorated throughout, this Property has a Quality Fitted Kitchen with Laminate Timber Floor with Oven, Hob, Integrated Dishwasher & Fridge/Freezer. French Doors lead you from the Dining Area to the Sunny South Facing Rear Garden. In the Living Room there is a Feature Solid Fuel Open Fireplace creating a Cosy atmosphere on a Winter evening. The Study on the Ground Floor could be used as a Home Office with Excellent Broadband in the Area or a 4th Bedroom. The Main Bedroom has a Large En-Suite with Electric Shower. The Fully Tiled Family Bathroom has a Separate Bath & Pump Shower Unit. The Property is heated by a Natural Gas Fired Central Heating System & has a Recently Installed Gas Boiler. There are Fitted Blinds on all Windows & French Doors. To the Front of the Property is a Green Area. This Property which is Fully Alarmed & has a Cobble-lock Driveway and Pedestrian Access to the Rear Garden. This Superb Property would make an Ideal Family Home. A Must View. Built c. 2006. Services: Mains Water, Sewerage, Electricity, Natural Gas & Broadband.
