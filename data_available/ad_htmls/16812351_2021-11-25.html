    Cherry Creek Lodge is a beautifully positioned and an immaculately presented 3 bed detached residence located in a sylvan setting off Grange Square, only a short walk to the beach and charming village of Fethard-On-Sea. Set in mature landscaped grounds offering great privacy the property which comprises of c. 145sq m, includes a welcoming entrance hall, sitting room, spacious kitchen/ diner with range of handcrafted units, large utility, 3 bedrooms (master with en-suite shower room) and shower room. Outside there are well planted gardens, detached garage suitable for a workshop or additional storage. The property is ideally located for anyone seeking a substantial residence in this sought after and scenic part of the sunny South East.
