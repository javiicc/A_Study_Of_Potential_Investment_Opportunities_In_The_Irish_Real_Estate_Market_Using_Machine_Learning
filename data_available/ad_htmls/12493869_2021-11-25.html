    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    A modern bungalow, south facing, set in the picturesque glen of Glenties. Occupying a spacious site which measures one acre, this home was constructed in 2000/2001. Glenties town is approximately 4km distance with the Glen Tavern, a noted Irish music centre just 1km distance from the property. Internal accommodation comprises of sitting room, kitchen, three bedrooms (one of which is en-suite) and family bathroom. Some of the features include flagstone floors, solid wooden kitchen units, large attic which is partly floored and detached garage.
