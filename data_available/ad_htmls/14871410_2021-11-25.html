    Keogh Auctioneers are delighted to bring Apartment 17 Old Monastery (Marina Terrace) to market. \r\n\r\nLocated in the prestigious Old Monastery complex, which is arguably the finest luxury apartment development in the East Cork West Waterford area. \r\n\r\nThe private complex is gated with car parking on a cobble locked courtyard and waterfront gardens bounding the seawall and comprises of just 17 apartments in total, which have varying designs and all with individual interiors.\r\n\r\nThe location is super convenient being within an easy stroll of both the historic town centre with all its amenities and the beautiful beaches and promenade.\r\n\r\nApartment 17 epitomises elegant luxury living but it is the magnificent panoramic water views from its open concept living area which are truly breath taking. The motion and sounds of the tides, the passing boats, and the ever-changing sky and sea offer a mesmerising vista and a level of tranquillity, which is extremely hard to find. \r\n\r\nConstructed in the mid-1990s, Apartment 17 is a 2-bedroom penthouse and internal accommodation includes an L shaped Reception Hall, Guest Bedroom, Master Suite with Ensuite Shower Room, Bathroom, Hot Press, and Open Concept Split Level Living Area comprising of a fully fitted Kitchen, Dining area, Lounge and Balcony. The property has solid timber Honduras pine floors throughout except for the wet areas and the Lounge and Balcony which are tiled, and all the walls are painted white. The colour palette complements the external sea and skyscapes and gives the interior a light, calm and comfortable atmosphere. \r\n\r\nApartment 17 is in turnkey condition and we very much look forward to showing this incredibly special property which will not fail to delight the discerning buyer looking for an exceptional upmarket coastal home.\r\n\r\nAccommodation comprises:\r\n\r\nReception Hall with Storage Press\r\n\r\nOpen Concept Split Level Kitchen Dining Living area with patio doors to Balcony circa 7.5m x 6.2m\r\n\r\nMaster Bedroom circa 5m x 4m\r\n\r\nEnsuite Shower Room circa 2.5m x 1.2m\r\n\r\nGuest Bedroom circa 3.4m x 3.1m\r\n\r\nBathroom circa 2.5m x 1.6m\r\n\r\n\r\nSpace Heating: Electric \r\n\r\nElevator from ground floor to Apartment front door \r\n\r\nGated Complex\r\n\r\nSecure Parking\r\n\r\nSeaside Gardens\r\n\r\nAnnual Service Charge Applies\r\n\r\nTitle: Leasehold
