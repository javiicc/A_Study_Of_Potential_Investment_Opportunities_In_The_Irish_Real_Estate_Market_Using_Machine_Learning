    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Madden Property Consultants are delighted to present this stunning duplex penthouse apartment in the heart of Dublin City. The apartment is located on the crossroad where Dublin 2 and Dublin 8 meet. The location is famous for where Handels Messiah was first performed back in 1711 on the west end of Temple Bar. The area is full of some of the citys best bars, restaurants, and tourist attractions. \r\nThe apartment complex is well maintained to high standard, with secure access on Essex Street West. The apartment itself is located on the 5th floor with a south facing aspect.\r\nThe ground floor consists of entrance hall, storage room and bathroom. The two double bedrooms are bright and spacious. The master bedroom comes with its own balcony.\r\nThe 1st floor comes with separate dining area and open plan kitchen/living room. The high ceilings and south facing aspect light the room up from any natural light. The open plan living space gives a feeling of tranquillity in this city centre dwelling.\r\n\r\nLocation:\r\nThe apartment is just off Fishamble St next to Christ Church. Excellent link roads to North and South of the City.\r\n\r\nTransport: \r\nDublin Bus 1 min walk. Dart 8 min walk. Luas 4 min walk. Dublin Bike 2 min walk.\r\n\r\nAmenities:\r\nTrinity College, The IFSC, Guinness Store House and Dublin Castle are within walking distance.
