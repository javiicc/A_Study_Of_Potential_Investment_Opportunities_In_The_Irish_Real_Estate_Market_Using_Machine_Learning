    A once in a lifetime opportunity to acquire a Lakeside Holiday Village with excellent further potential\r\n\r\nGulladoo Holiday Village is on 2.48 Acres with a shoreline of approximately 550 metres in perimeter with a 6 boat floating berth and 3 raised decking areas overlooking Gulladoo Lough. There is ample room for boats and parking in front of the holiday homes.\r\n\r\nThe adjoining Boat House could be refurbished to accommodate a caf\xc3\xa9/bar/restaurant building with a large decking area overlooking the lake. There is also potential to use the old retreat centre building for an alternative use, i.e. facilities building, water sport centre, arts & crafts, health spa, holistic \r\nhealing etc. This building was used as a retreat and rehab centre in the past to include painting/arts and crafts.\r\n\r\nThere is a tranquil woodland setting along the east and south of Gulladoo Lough and the easy access for boats would be very attractive for fishing and water sports.\r\n\r\nBungalow (1) = 175.6 sq.mtrs/1890 sq.ft\r\n\r\nHouses 2,3 & 4 (Dormer Bungalows) = 119 sq.mtrs/1280 sq.ft each\r\n\r\nBungalow (2) = 116 sq.mtrs/1254 sq. ft\r\n\r\nFacilities building = 79 sq.mtrs./850 sq.ft\r\n\r\nBoathouse = 98 sq.mtrs/1053 sq.ft\r\n\r\nDecking area = 196 sq.mtrs/2111 sq.ft.\r\n\r\nGulladoo Village is located in Cornafest along the western shoreline of Gulladoo Lough to the south east of County Leitrim. Gulladoo Lough is nearly 3 km in length and 0.75 km in width to the south end of the lough which extends in to County Longford.\r\nGulladoo Village is located 4 km from Carrigallen Village and the waters around the area form the upper reaches of the great River Erne System and the lakes to the south and west empty in to the River Shannon.\r\nThis is a very popular fishing area and given its central location it attracts strong course fishing interest from Ireland and Northern Ireland.\r\nGulladoo Village is located within 20 km from the Northern Ireland border and 2 hours drive from Dublin. C.40 km from Carrick on Shannon Town.\r\n\r\nWe understand the property is held under freehold title.\r\n\r\nWe understand that the properties benefit from the usual amenities with electricity and water supplied. However interested parties may want to upgrade the services and are required to satisfy themselves on the adequacy and availability of all services to the property.\r\n\r\nThis is a unique opportunity to acquire a lakeside Holiday Home Village overlooking Gulladoo Lough.\r\n\r\nAs the Vendor owns more land adjoining we are in a position to increase the total site area should clients require.
