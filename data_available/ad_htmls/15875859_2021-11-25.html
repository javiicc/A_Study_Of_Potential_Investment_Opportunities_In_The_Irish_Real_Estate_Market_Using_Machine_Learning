    Excellent three bedroom semi-detached home superbly located in the centre of Ballycanew Village. Within walking distance of village amenities such as a school, shops and a pub and also less than 10 minutes from the M11 and Gorey town centre with its shops, restaurants and chic boutiques. \nThis property enjoys an abundance of natural light and comprises briefly; bright entrance hall, spacious sitting room, kitchen / dining with a fitted kitchen and guest WC completing the ground floor. Upstairs there are three generous bedrooms, one with ensuite and a family bathroom completing the well-appointed accommodation. Number 5 has been totally refurbished by its current owner to a very high standard and presents a fabulous opportunity for those seeking a home that is literally in walk in condition for its lucky new owners.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 5.02m x 1.88m at widest point. Linoleum flooring.\n\nSitting Room 4.50m x 4.40m at widest point. Laminate wood flooring, feature bay window, feature open fireplace with black granite surround.\n\nKitchen / Dining Room 3.60m x 6.38m at widest point. Linoleum flooring, built in kitchen units, sliding patio doors to rear garden.\n\nGuest WC 1.50m x 1.50m at widest point. Tiled floor and walls, WC, wash hand basin.\n\nLanding 3.11m x 3.10m at widest point. Solid wood flooring.\n\nBathroom 1.80m x 2.70m at widest point. Solid wood flooring.\n\nMaster Bedroom 3.50m x 4.40m at widest point. Solid wood flooring, feature bay window, TV point.\n\nEn-Suite 0.90m x 3.18m. Tiled floor and walls, Triton T90si, WC, wash hand basin.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom Gorey drive up the main street and turn left at the main traffic lights.
