    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    A unique opportunity to acquire a fine residential property in a most central and convenient location. Ulster Lodge was built as an officer\xe2\x82\xacTM house c.1830 and retains a few of its original features. Set on a mature private site with ample privacy, it offers an abundance of space both internally and externally. Opportunity to further enhance and upgrade.\r\nSpacious living accommodation with a large sitting room, a dining room with patio door access to the side garden, family-room and a generous kitchen. First floor has four double bedrooms and a bathroom. All rooms are light filled and well proportioned.\r\nEnclosed yard to the front with ample car parking and a double garage. Set on c.0.3 acres. Additional storage sheds to the side of the residence, in an enclosed yard. Well maintained mature walled garden to side rear which offers tremendous privacy. Rarely does such an opportunity arise.\r\nViewing of this residence comes highly recommended.
