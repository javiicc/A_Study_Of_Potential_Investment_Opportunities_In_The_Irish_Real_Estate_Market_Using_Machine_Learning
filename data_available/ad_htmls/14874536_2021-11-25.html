    Smith & Butler Estates are delighted to present to the market this fine 2 bed, 2 bath property ideally situated on a quiet cul de sac in this mature suburb. The property comprises of a hallway with access to a generously sized front sitting room. There is a kitchen to the rear with access to w.c, shower/utility room and a generously sized back garden which benefits from block built shed & raised sunny terrace to the rear. Upstairs there is a bathroom and 2 well proportioned bedrooms both with built in wardrobes.\r\n\r\nOriginally built in the 1940's this well designed and finished home is ideally situated between the Howth & Malahide roads and is only a short 10 minute walk to Killester DART station. The surrounding area has numerous schools, sports clubs, shops & Killester village is less then a 700m walk away.
