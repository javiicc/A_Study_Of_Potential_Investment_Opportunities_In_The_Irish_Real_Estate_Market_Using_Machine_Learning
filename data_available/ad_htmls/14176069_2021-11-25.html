    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    We are delighted to offer for sale this comfortable two bedroom property, situated in the heart of Edgeworthstown Town Centre. This home comes on the market in pristine condition and has recently been refurbished with no expense spared. It also has the benefit of a platinum stairs lift. Edgeworthstown has a lot to offer which includes a train station, schools, churches, doctors surgery, pharmacy, restaurants, public houses, super markets, shops and a beautiful recreational/walking park. All of these facilities are within walking distances to this property. Accommodation includes entrance hall, sitting room, kitchen/dining area, utility, wc. First floor has two double bedrooms and a shower room. Services include oil fire central heating, town sewerage, mains water, sky and broadband availability. Outside has a large back garden and a newly built garage.
