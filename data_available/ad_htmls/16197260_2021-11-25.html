    Colliers are delighted to present this superior two-bedroom apartment with car parking to the market, situated in this highly desirable modern development built by renowned developers Dorville Homes located just moments from heart of Dundrum Town Centre.\r\n\r\n\r\nNo. 82 Southmede offers discerning purchasers an opportunity to acquire a superbly well balanced, light filled apartment with generous proportions and high specification finishes throughout enjoying immaculately kept landscaped grounds that wrap around this exclusive low density development.\r\n\r\n \r\nThe accommodation which stands a comfortable 67 sq. m / 721 sq. ft. is laid with quality hardwood flooring, tastefully decorated throughout and briefly comprises a large welcoming entrance hall with store room off, a bright and spacious contemporary open plan living/dining room with floor to ceiling glazing, a modern fully equipped Wenge kitchen with all high end appliances and finished with fawn polished granite work surfaces, an extra large double bedroom, with generous fitted wardrobes and Italian pod ensuite shower room, a generous second double bedroom with large fitted wardrobes and a tastefully decorated and tiled bathroom with underfloor heating completes the accommodation.\r\n\r\n\r\nNo. 82 Southmede is neatly positioned just off Ballinteer Road and accessed remotely through automated entry system gates. The development is located to the south of Dundrum Village and Luas stop and north of the M50 enjoying an unrivalled location close to a wealth of social and essential amenities that are just moments away as well as being within walking distance to Marlay Park and the Wicklow Way making this a modern living location that is truly second to none. The property comes to the market in excellent condition and will appeal to both first time buyers and investors alike.\r\n\r\n\r\nViewing is by private appointment.
