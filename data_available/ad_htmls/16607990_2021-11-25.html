    Campbells are delighted to have been instructed to bring this traditional stone built cottage to the open market which is sure to appeal to many as an ideal family, rental or holiday home.\n\nLocated on the island of Arranmore, the property occupies an elevated site which boasts spectacular panoramic views of the Wild Atlantic Ocean and the main land Donegal coastline whilst it is situated just a few minutes walk from the Arranmore Harbour and main thoroughfare of the Island. \n\nInternally the property has bundles of character comprising of a spacious living room with feature stone fireplace, dining area, fully fitted kitchen and a large bedroom with traditional style cobblestone flooring. \n\nIn addition to the dwelling there are two stone ruins located on the site which present an interesting redevelopment opportunity. \n\nViewing is highly recommended and strictly by appointment only.\n\nOur Re: K096\n\nOpen Plan Living / Dining Area - 7.05m (23'2") x 4m (13'1")\nCobblestone Floor, Half Door, Double Doors Opening up to Patio Area at Southern End Enjoying Views Over the Atlantic Ocean and Mainland Donegal, T & G Vaulted Ceiling, Recess Lighting, Chandelier & Wall Lights, Elevated Feature Stone Fireplace with Sleeper Style Mantle\n\nKitchen\nFully Fitted, Cobblestone Floor, Oil Fire Stove, Tiled Splash Back Area, Granite Worktop, Belfast Sink, Recess Lights\n\nBathroom\nFully Tilled, Bath, WC, WHB, T & G Ceiling, Extractor Fan\n\nBedroom - 3m (9'10") x 3.8m (12'6")\nCobblestone Floor, Raised T & G Vaulted Ceiling\n\nGeneral\nWooden Sash Windows Throughout\n\n\n\nNotice\nPlease note we have not tested any apparatus, fixtures, fittings, or services.
