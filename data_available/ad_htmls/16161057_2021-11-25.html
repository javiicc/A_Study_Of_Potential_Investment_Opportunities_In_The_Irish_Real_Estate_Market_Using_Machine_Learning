    A superb opportunity arises to acquire this charming period family residence tucked away on this quiet traffic free cul-de-sac just off the Sydney Parade end of Ailesbury Road. Behind the most attractive fa ade of this superbly convenient home you will find a wonderfully spacious interior with elegantly proportioned rooms and many period features synonymous with properties of this era which include high ceilings with elaborate cornicing and centre roses, stained glass front door and beautiful fireplaces. This fine family home is further enhanced with a pretty railed front garden and its lovely private south-east facing back garden. \n\nIn brief, the accommodation comprises gracious entrance hall leading to the very fine interconnecting reception rooms, there is access from the dining room to the back garden. The kitchen/breakfast room on the hall floor return is a fine sized room and is flooded with natural light. Upstairs, there are 3/4 bedrooms, the original 4th bedroom has been for many years used as a sitting room. There are stairs leading from here to the spacious converted attic, which is ideal for a variety of uses. A large bathroom and a separate w.c complete the accommodation.\n\n The convenience of the location cannot be overstated, being within a stone s throw of Sydney Parade DART station, the Merrion Shopping Centre and St Vincent s Hospital. Sandymount village with its selection of boutiques, restaurants and coffee shops is within a short stroll not to mention Sandymount Strand and Promenade for leisurely strolls. Many of the cities principal schools are within close proximity including St. Michaels College, St. Andrews College, Muckross Park and The Teresian school. The highly regarded Star of the Sea and Lakelands primary schools are also within walking distance. Recreational amenities are well provided for with Herbert Park, Elm Park Golf club the RDS showgrounds and Aviva Stadium all within easy reach.\n\n\nEntrance Hall: with stained glass hall door, elaborate ceiling coving, centre rose and generous under stairs storage.\n\nDrawing Room: beautifully proportioned room with bay window, elaborate ceiling coving and centre rose, mahagony fireplace with tiled inset and open fire.
