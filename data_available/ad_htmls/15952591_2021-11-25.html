The site offers private gated access from Clon Road Roundabout with substantial tarmacadam drive which extends to the front and is bordered by sleepers and mature shrubbery giving fantastic views of the River Fergus. This town centre dwelling would suit a number of possible uses, be it for retirement, first time buyer, family home or offices.
