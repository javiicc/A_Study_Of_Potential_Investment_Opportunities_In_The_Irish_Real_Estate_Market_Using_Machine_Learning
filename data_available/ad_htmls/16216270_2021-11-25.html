    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to present for sale 60a Glasnevin Downs, an exceptional property combining space and light with a fashionable address. \n\nThis three bedroom detached family home is discreetly nestled away within a cul de sac position and is presented in immaculate condition throughout. Internally, the property is thoughtfully laid out with an easy flow and well-proportioned rooms. The accommodation comprises of a welcoming entrance hallway with living room to the front with a box bay window that allows in plenty of light. To the rear of the property, there is an large open plan kitchen/dining area with separate utility room and guest wc. Upstairs, there are three large bedrooms (one with ensuite) and family bathroom. The property further benefits from a converted attic with ample eave storage and ample off street parking to the front. The rear garden is very private with a large patio area and side green space and is a lovely suntrap for relaxing.\n\nGlasnevin Downs has always been a popular location for families and they are rare to come to the market for sale. Located within close proximity of the city centre and close to numerous local amenities, a great selection of national and secondary schools and DCU are all within a few minutes walk. Drumcondra Village & Phibsborough Village is also closeby with its many shops, cafes and restaurants and a well serviced public transport system. This spacious property ticks all the boxes for convenient family living and is just a short stroll from the renowned Botanic Gardens.\n\n\nEntrance Hall Welcoming entrance hall with wood flooring and glass stain feature window.\n\nLiving Room 3.88m x 3.66m.
