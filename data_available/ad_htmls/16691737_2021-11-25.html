    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to present No. 5 Waterville Terrace to the market for sale. This 2 bedroomed, own door, 1st floor apartment comes to the market in good condition and is ready for immediate occupation. No. 5 is excellently located, with a south/east facing balcony to front which overlooks Waterville Park and is not overlooked to front or rear. There is one designated parking space for the apartment and ample guest parking available.. \n\nThe property briefly comprises of an open-plan living/dining room with access to the balcony, adjoining kitchen, two double bedrooms with fitted wardrobes and a family bathroom completes. \n\nThe convenience of the location cannot be over-emphasised having every conceivable amenity just a short walk away at the Blanchardstown Shopping Centre. There is a local Spar shop, as well as a creche, just a 5 minute walk and Waterville Park provides a beautiful spot for walking, with a children s playground. Blanchardstown Village, TU Dublin Blanchardstown Campus, Connolly Hospital and the National Aquatic Centre are also within easy reach, as well as having excellent road networks including the M3 and the M50 only minutes away, as well as Dublin Airport being just a 20 minute drive approx. There is an excellent bus service to Dublin city centre (No. 38) just outside the entrance to Waterville Terrace also.\n\n\nHallway Carpet flooring, with good-sized hotpress.\n\nLiving/Dining Room Spacious, open-plan living/dining room with laminate, timber flooring. An attractive, gas fireplace provides a lovely focal point to the room. The room also enjoys access to the private, south-east facing balcony which is not overlooked.\n\nKitchen Located just off the living/dining room, with tiled flooring. Plumbed for washing machine, with integrated oven/hob.\n\nBedroom 1 Double bedroom with carpet flooring. A double fitted wardrobe provides ample storage space.\n\nBedroom 2 A second double bedroom with carpet flooring and fitted wardrobe.\n\nBathroom Family bathroom with linoleum flooring. Comprises shower over bath, wc and wash hand basin.
