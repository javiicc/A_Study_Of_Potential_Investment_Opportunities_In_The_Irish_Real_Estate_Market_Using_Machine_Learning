    No. 2 Windsor Terrace is an outstanding example of how brilliant design, flair and imagination, along with a high level of attention to detail and quality materials can create a home of true distinction. The bespoke interiors and spacious accommodation are complimented by the superb setting overlooking the leafy banks of the Grand Canal. All in all, creating a residence of exceptional calibre.\n \nSuperbly appointed throughout, this stunning Victorian property provides the discerning purchaser with light filled, elegant and well-proportioned accommodation throughout, making it the perfect choice for those seeking a sophisticated pad in the heart of Portobello. \n \nSet behind electric vehicular and pedestrian gates, the driveway is partially paved with attractive granite and enhanced by outdoor marine lighting. Moving inside, the hugely impressive living room is cleverly divided from the kitchen/dining room by large tri-folding glazed doors which allow natural light to flow and can slide open to provide a spacious open plan ground floor ideal for entertaining. Off the light filled kitchen/dining area are another set of large tri-folding glazed doors opening directly to the rear garden ensuring regular al fresco dining during the summer. Cleverly tucked away off the kitchen there is a utility storeroom.\n \nRising up the custom-made walnut staircase, the first floor is flooded with natural light by sagaciously positioned skylights. Off the spacious landing with raised ceilings is the hot press with built in shelving. The principal bedroom complete with ensuite bathroom is positioned to the front and boasts a generous walk in wardrobe which was previously a fourth bedroom. The second bedroom also enjoys an ensuite shower room and the third single bedroom completes the first-floor accommodation.\n \nThe location is second to none, ideally positioned in the heart of fashionable Portobello with an enviable outlook over the banks of the Grand Canal. Home to a veritable mix of mainly Victorian residences - big and small Portobello is one of the city s most sought-after districts, popular with young and old, professionals and families, residential and commercial tenants, artists and accountants! Local eateries (to include the neighbouring Lock s restaurant), bakeries, coffee houses, places of worship, schools, gyms not to mention the picturesque walkways of the Grand Canal and the hustle and bustle of the Camden Quarter are all on your doorstep. The Luas green line at Charlemont and Harcourt is also close by as is the Dublin bike stations at Grantham Street and Portobello, making the car a luxury as opposed to a necessity. \n \nViewing of this beautiful city residence, that is sure to impress and captivate, is highly recommended.\n\n\nFront Garden Electric gated driveway with vehicle and pedestrian access, partially graveled with granite paving stones, outdoor marine lighting and an outdoor tap.\n\nLiving Room Open plan living space featuring a wood burning stove, polished concrete floors, a tv port, open understairs space, a feature stone effect wall, a log storage alcove, CCTV, and recessed lighting.
