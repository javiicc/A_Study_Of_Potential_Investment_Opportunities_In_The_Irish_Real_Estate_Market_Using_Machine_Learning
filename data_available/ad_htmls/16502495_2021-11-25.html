    O'Connell Auctioneers present this imposing three bed detached house to the market. The property is located in the quiet village of Burnfort on a site of c. 1.2 Acres on a very prominent corner. Set back in off the road and with the potential of a number of sites subject to planning permission, this is a great opportunity to acquire a property which you can call your forever home. The property requires some renovation and upgrading however with its very well-proportioned rooms, the opportunities are endless. The accommodation extends to approximately 1,650 sq. ft. (153 sqm) and comprises bright spacious rooms. The ground floor rooms are all nine foot ceilings while the first floor have eleven foot ceilings. There is a sweeping tarmacadam driveway in from the road and private enclosed rear courtyard along with garage and outbuildings.\n\nAccommodation: \n\n- Entrance Hall 6.7m X 2.1m A small porch leads in to a large Entrance Hall with carpet flooring leading to all rooms downstairs\n\n- Sitting Room 4.2m X 4.1m. Bright spacious room with carpet flooring and large window flooding light in to the room. There is also a large stove \n\n- Kitchen 4.2m X 3.4m Fully fitted with eye level units and linoleum flooring \n\n- Living Room 4.2m X 3.9m Bright spacious room with carpet flooring and large window flooding light in to the room. There is also a stove \n\n- Utility Room 3.6m X 3.5m Shelved out and with linoleum flooring \n\n- WC 1.5m X 1.5m WC & WHB\n\n- Bedroom 1 4.2m X 4.1m.
