    Beautiful 1.3 acre site located approx. 4 km from Ballymurphy and 8km from Borris. Sold subject to acquiring full planning permission. \n\nLocated in rural area with breathtaking views of the surrounding country side. Folio no : CW25825 F \n\nFurther details supplied upon request by contacting Sherry FitzGerald Mc Dermott.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom Ballymurphy turn right. Continue on this road passing Rocksavage. At the Y in the road keep right and stay on the road taking the 3rd turn on the right. Follow the road around a very sharp left and the site is located on your left hand side.\n\nFrom Borris head towards Clashganny. Drive 3.2 km past Mount Leinster Rangers GAA pitch. Take the L70603 and continue up hill to the T junction and take the left signposted Ballymurphy / Enniscorthy, take the next right and drive for 1.5 km the site is on your right. It has a grey gate. \nSign outside.
