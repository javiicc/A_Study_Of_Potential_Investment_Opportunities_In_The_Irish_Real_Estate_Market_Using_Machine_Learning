    Sherry Fitzgerald proudly bring to the market No.11 Grange Terrace, which is a well presented, mature, three-bedroom end of terrace home that is located on Grange Road.\n\nThis property offers a great balance of living and bedroom accommodation throughout and is an excellent opportunity to acquire a home in a highly sought-after area. While in need of modernisation, the property overall is presented good condition throughout and gives the prospective purchaser free reign to come in and put their own personal stamp on it. \n\nNo.11 benefits from off-street parking to the front along with a fully enclosed, south facing private garden to the rear \n\nThe location gets no better than this enjoying close proximity to the city centre and Douglas, as well as local schools (both primary and secondary), many bus routes, shops, banks, etc are all on the doorstep. \n\nThis property will appeal to many home buyers and is a fantastic opportunity that should not be missed and must be viewed to fully appreciate what this home has to offer.\n\nDo not delay arrange your virtual viewing today!\nEntrance Hall 6.35m x 1.05m. This is a bright and welcoming entrance hall that enters in from the front of the house. It provides access to both the downstairs living rooms and kitchen/dining. It is floored with a laminate flooring.\n\nSitting Room 3.23m x 3.41m. The bright sitting room is located to the front of the house. It benefits from retaining its original picture rails as well as an open fireplace that is now covered up but could be opened again if required.\n\nLiving Room 6.62m x 2.84m. Located just off the entrance hall is another bright and spacious reception room with a window overlooking the back garden. This living room benefits from a fireplace.\n\nKitchen/Dining Area 3.64m x 3.03m. The kitchen/dining area is fitted with a good range of floor and eye level style units and looks out to the side of the house. It benefits from a tiled floor and gives access to the back of the house and main bathroom.\n\nMain Bathroom 1.99m x 1.63m. Located just off the kitchen/dining area is a walk-in shower suite. Benefits from the walls being fully tiled and there is a window to the side of the house for natural ventilation.\n\nLanding 4.62m x 1.63m. The landing area provides access to all three bedrooms on the first floor along with the attic space.\n\nBedroom 1 3.00m x 4.56m. A large and bright double bedroom that runs the width of the home and looks out to the front of the house through two windows. Benefits from a wall of built-in wardrobes.\n\nBedroom 2 3.25m x 2.82m. A large double bedroom to the rear of the home with a window overlooking the south facing rear garden.\n\nBedroom 3 4.36m x 2.97m. A large double bedroom to the rear of the home with a window overlooking the south facing rear garden.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom South Douglas Road travel in the direction of Donnybrook and continue up Donnybrook Hill. At the first set of traffic lights take a right-hand turn onto Grange Road. No.11 Grange Terrace is located on the left-hand side. See agents sign on left-hand side.
