    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Superb Residential land holding, located at Cloonohill, Drumlish, Co. Leitrim. This property comprises of a bungalow type residence, which is partially restored on approximately 28.55 acres of agricultural land. The property incorporates outhouses, a hayshed, and a three-bay single slated shed in excellent condition. The original three bedroom single storey residence, is partially restored, with provisions to develop a first-floor level. The lands are all under grass, well fenced throughout, with adequate water supply. There is a small portion of bog also. This attractive property is well located and convenient to Drumlish, Mohill and the N4 national route. Situated just a few minutes drive from the magnificent Lough Rynn Castle Hotel, this residential land holding has huge potential, with substantial road frontage.
