    Number 10 Barnhill Grove is a semi detached two-story family home situated within this private cul de sac off Barnhill Road. Measuring an impressive overall 195sq.m/2,098sq.ft approx the accommodation offers excellent living accommodation in a well sought-after location in Dalkey Town. Having been extended and upgraded over the years the property is ready for occupation. \n\nInternally the rooms are well proportioned creating excellent open space to entertain. Upon entering there is a spacious hallway with a study/ playroom located to the right, however this could easily be used as a fourth bedroom at ground level ideal for an au-pair and guests. To the left of the hallway there is an open plan living/dining room flooded with natural light, sliding doors open into the sunroom making this a wonderful entertaining space which leads out to the garden. A door enters through to a spacious kitchen/dining room with sliding doors opening out to the garden. Off the kitchen there is a large utility room and a shower room. On the second floor there are 3 bedrooms, the main bedroom originally two bedrooms is an exceptionally generous double with impressive wardrobe space and an en-suite. There are two further rooms, a double room with access to a balcony and a single bedroom to the front. A family bathroom room completes the accommodation at this level. The attic has been converted to provide extra living space. There is further scope to extend to the side of the property and above the balcony subject to planning. \n\nThe location is superb, being a stone s throw from the ever-popular town of Dalkey. It offers a host of amenities including schools, renowned restaurants, shops and the DART and beautiful sea and hill walks nearby. The M50 and N11 are easily accessible close by and minutes by car.
