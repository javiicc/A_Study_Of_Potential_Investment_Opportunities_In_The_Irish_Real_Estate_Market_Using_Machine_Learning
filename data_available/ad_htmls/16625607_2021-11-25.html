    Gordon Hughes Estate Agents are delighted to present this 5 bedroom bungalow which has been newly renovated & also includes an additional 1 bedroom self contained annex which would suit or holiday accommodation or letting as an apartment. Ideally suited for family home or B&B opportunity the property is located opposite Lock 9 on the Shannon Erne Waterway system. The property is equal distance from the picturesque tourist town of Carrick on Shannon and the busy cultural centre of Ballinamore & comprises of the following accommodation; Hallway, living room (open fireplace), Kitchen (solid fuel back boiler), No.5 double bedrooms (2 en-suite), bathroom with separate shower & separate 1 bedroom annex. Features include; Only 10 min drive from Carrick-on-Shannon, excellent potential with attic rooms suitable for conversion to living accommodation & beside Shannon-Erne waterway. Viewing of this property is Strictly by Appointment Only with Sole Selling Agent.
