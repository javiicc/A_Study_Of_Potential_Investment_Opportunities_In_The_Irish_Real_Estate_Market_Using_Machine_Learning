    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    51 Inis Clair is a popular housing development located off the Ennis/Clarecastle road in the Inis Clair housing development and is positioned within a half moon shaped courtyard cul de sac within this popular estate. The interior accommodation is set out over 3 floors with the ground floor entrance hallway leading to the main reception and open plan kitchen/dining, separate utility and downstairs WC. The main reception is finished with walnut style timber flooring, wood surround feature fireplace with a modern fitted kitchen and extensive tiling and quality fixtures to the remainder of the ground floor. From the first floor landing are three double bedrooms, two of which have built in wardrobes and all boasting T & G polished wood flooring with extensive tiling and quality sanitary ware to the main bathroom. Rounding off the interior accommodation is the second floor master bedroom which is en-suite and boasting built in wardrobes with integrated shelving and glazed doors and all bedrooms in the house have the benefit of TV and telephone points. Outside, ample carparking to the front leads to gated side access to the nicely manicured rear gardens, very nicely presented with mature shrubbery and lawned area and has the added benefit of not being overlooked. Viewing of this bright and spacious four bedroom semi detached family home is highly recommended and strictly by prior appointment with sole selling agents.
