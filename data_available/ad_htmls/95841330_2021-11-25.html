    Development Description

      Gort na Fuinse is a new high quality residential development of 3 and 4 bedroom houses situated only 20 minutes (23km) from Galway city in the beautiful and welcoming town of Headford. Gort na Fuinse offers a better way of living whilst providing potential buyers with a good opportunity to acquire a new home at an affordable price close to Galway City. Register your interest for pre-launch information on these spacious 3 and 4 bedroom houses which have commenced construction and are due for release soon. \r\n\r\nSee our 3D video of the external and internals of the exceptional standards with the sales team at Gort na Fuinse. The agents have additional floor plans, specification and 3d walk throughs for interested parties to review. \r\n\r\nLocated within the bustling town centre of Headford residents at Gort na Fuinse can avail of the broad range of local amenities within a strong community setting. Residents will be surrounded by 3 schools, Headford GAA club, Corrib rugby club, numerous retail options and coffee shops. Angling and boating enthusiasts are close to the beautiful Lough Corrib and locals enjoy scenic walks and childrens play areas. \r\n\r\nHeadford is well connected to the N84 and together with good bus services running to Galway City, Gort na Fuinse provides accessibility to good employment, a strong community culture and a better way of living. The hospitality and culture of city living is only a short distance yet you can still enjoy living within the peaceful and scenic countryside surroundings. The M16 motorway close by links the town with various commuter belts as it links with the M6 as it travels across the midlands to Dublin city centre. \r\n\r\nHome owners will enjoy a short commute to some significant employers such as Boston Scientific, Merit Medical, Metronic, Oracle, Cisco, Baxter, SAP, HP and EA Electronics. Galway train station, the Galway races, golf clubs, Galway General Hospital and the National University of Galway are also within easy access. \r\n\r\nOCC Construction and Sathel Property Development will feature a variety of attractive house types which will be set amongst generous communal lawns and extensive landscaping. All homes feature an excellent specification and level of finish and will be built to a high quality standard. Each house benefits from A2 energy rated efficiency. The contemporary interior layouts are designed to suit modern family living.\r\n\r\nWe look forward to welcoming you to Gort na Fuinse in the near future. Register your interest at https://gortnafuinse.ie/ for more information.

      • Gort na Fuinse offers potential buyers a better standard of living within the picturesque town of Headford.

      • Affordable houses with the extra incentive of the Help to Buy Scheme.
      • Exclusive development of large NZEB compliant A2 ratd 3 and 4 bedroom A rated homes.
