    Superb one bedroom ground floor apartment comes to the market in turn key condition.\r\n\r\nThis stylish property is ideally located in an attractive brick facade complex in a quiet cul-de-sac. The apartment consists of an entrance hall with wooden floors which leads to a spacious living room with ornate coving and a feature fireplace. The living room opens to a modern open plan kitchen & dining area with a tiled floor, splashback tiling as well as plenty of counter & cupboard space. Sliding doors lead to a paved sunny courtyard which is the ideal place to relax & entertain. There is a spacious double bedroom with wooden floors and the accommodation is completed by a generous sized fully tiled shower room. There is also free parking on street.\r\n\r\nRainsford Avenue is ideally located with a range of amenities on Thomas Street including shops, bars, cafes & restaurants. Stephens Green. Grafton Street, Trinity College and the city centre are all within walking distance.
