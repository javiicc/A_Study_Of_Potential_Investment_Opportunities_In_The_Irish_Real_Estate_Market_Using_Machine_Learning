    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 0.6 ac
    A very attractive 0.6 acre site to be sold subject to planning permission. This site would be ideal for the self-builder looking to live in this popular area. The site is just a few minutes from Clonmoney National School. It offers easy access to Bunratty village and to Shannon Airport and it also offers easy access to the M/N18 to Limerick, Ennis and Galway. The site is ideal for construction and will be sold subject to a successful grant of planning permission.
