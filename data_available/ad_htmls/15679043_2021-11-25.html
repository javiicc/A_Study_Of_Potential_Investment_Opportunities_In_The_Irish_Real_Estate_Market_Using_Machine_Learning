    The property is set just over 2 miles from Moycullen village and 7 miles from the regional capital of the West, Galway City, with its mainline road, rail and extensive and substantial cultural, commercial and retail facilities. Shannon Airport is some 1.5 hours from the property. Moycullen is a welcoming, progressive community with the village offering a comprehensive range of facilities, with schooling, sports facilities, restaurants, supermarkets, cr\xc3\xa8ches and a range of boutique and local shops.\n\nConstructed circa 2006 this detached family home provides 2,300 sq. feet of accommodation which is well presented throughout and provides a light and airy feel to the property. The property is of block construction with a rendered exterior under a slated roof and set within well maintained gardens. The accommodation comprises on inner hall, sitting room, large open plan kitchen dining room, utility room, ground floor bedroom with walk in wardrobe and en suite. To the first floor there is a lovely light large landing with access to 3 further double bedrooms (one with en suite) and family bathroom. The property is extremely well presented throughout and due to its location totally private. Outside to the rear there is a large detached garage providing circa 1,000 square feet of space and could be converted subject to necessary planning constraints. \n\nA summary of the accommodation, with room aspects and approximate measurements is as follows:\n\nFront door leading to entrance hall.\n\nAccommodation\nINNER HALLWAY: - 15'6" (4.72m) x 11'6" (3.51m)\n(NE) with terracotta tiled flooring throughout, doors leading to sitting room, kitchen/dining room, ground floor bedroom and hollow core stairs rising to first floor.
