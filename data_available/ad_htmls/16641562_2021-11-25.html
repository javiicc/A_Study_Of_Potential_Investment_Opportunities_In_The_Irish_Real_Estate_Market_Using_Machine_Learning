    The Property Shop are delighted to present 8 Blackhall Place to the Dublin 7 market. Beyond the handsome brick front, number 8 offers bright and spacious living accommodation comprising of, entrance hallway, living room, dining room, kitchen and shower room all on the ground floor. Upstairs there are two spacious double bedrooms, The property is further enhanced by a good sized east facing patio yard to the rear. There is also residential disc parking available to the front of the house. Sitting just a stones throw from the centre of Stoneybatter village, number 8 enjoys easy access to the all of the wonderful local amenities, such as the many independent shops, cafes, bars and restaurants. Smithfiled village, the TUI Grangegorman campus, The Phoenix Park and of course the City centre itself are all within easy walking distance of the property. The area is well serviced by Dublin Bus, the Luas and also Dublin Bikes. The property boasts gas fired central heating and many original features throughout.
