    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    The Old Barracks is a period property of considerable character. The property is exceptionally bright and well balanced, extending to c. 94.4sq.m of extremely well-proportioned accommodation throughout comprising an entrance hallway, a relaxing sitting room, kitchen/dining and family bathroom on the ground floor with two spacious double bedrooms on the first floor.\nA former RIC and Garda barracks, the property still maintains some of the features from it s original use such as a holding cell which now incorporates the family bathroom. This beautiful 2 bedroom period home is located in a very much sought after location in north county Wexford, a leisurely stroll to Cahore South beach.\nOutside the grounds are a testament to the current owner with the garden really coming into its own during the summer.\nA wide range of activities and amenities are on your doorstep, with beautiful beaches, forest walks, top golf courses and internationally acclaimed spa s catering to your every whim. This friendly coastal location provides numerous eateries and cafes and with amusement parks, crazy golf, ten pin bowling easily keeping the kids entertained are all located within close proximity.\nIn an excellent location within approx. fifteen minutes drive of Gorey town and Junction 23 on the M11 as well as a short distance to the coast.\n\n\nGROUND FLOOR\n\nEntrance Hallway 3.00m x 1.60m. at widest point, solid maple wood flooring.\n\nSitting room 4.74m x 4.13m. solid maple wood flooring, feature brick fireplace with solid fuel stove.\n\nBack Porch 0.99m x 1.38m. solid maple wood flooring.\n\nKitchen/Dining 4.66m x 3.65m. laminate wood flooring, fitted kitchen units, electric oven, electric hob, dishwasher, under counter fridge, feature fireplace with solid fuel stove.\n\nBathroom 2.46m x 2.38m. linoleum flooring, tile shower, freestanding bath, shower, WC, wash hand basin, heated towel rail.\n\nFIRST FLOOR\n\nLanding 1.24m x 0.95m. at widest point, carpet flooring.\n\nBedroom 1 4.70m x 3.80m. carpet flooring, feature fireplace.\n\nMaster bedroom 2 4.60m x 3.92m. carpet flooring, feature fireplace, walk-in wardrobe.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom Gorey, proceed out the Courtown road and at the roundabout follow direction for Courtown. Just before Courtown, take the right turn for Riverchapel. Proceed through the village and on as far as Ballygarrett village. Proceed straight through Ballygarrett village and the property is on the right hand side of the road after a short distance. It is just before Clonevan Church. Eircode: Y25 DN27
