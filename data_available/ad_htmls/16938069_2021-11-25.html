    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Excellent 3 bed semi detached residence located in a much sought after and convenient location in Rosslare Harbour enjoying a superb corner position with generous driveway, side access and beautifully landscaped private garden to rear with sunny aspect. The property itself is presented to the market in excellent condition featuring well laid out bright & spacious accommodation. All facilities shops, school, church, supermarket, bank, pharmacy etc. are within walking distance as is Rosslare Europort with ferries to the UK & Europe.
