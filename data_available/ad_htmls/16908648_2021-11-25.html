    REA Grimes take great pleasure in presenting 4 Poynton Place to the market. A generously proportioned 4 bedroom semi-detached home extending to c. 130 sqm (c. 1,399 sq ft) with flexible living accommodation. Located off the R156, this family home has the added benefit of overlooking a green area to the front, within walking distance of Ballivor village and all local amenities. \n\nA versatile family home, the accommodation briefly comprises entrance hallway, living room, study/playroom, dining room, kitchen, utility and guest wc. The first floor offers three double bedrooms (master ensuite) and one single bedroom and comes complete with a family bathroom. Externally, the large rear garden contains a patio area and wood shed with one side entrance. The front driveway has off street parking for two cars. \n\nThe location is second to none, with the best of rural living, yet within close proximity to Trim (c.16.2 km) and Mullingar (c.27.3 km).
