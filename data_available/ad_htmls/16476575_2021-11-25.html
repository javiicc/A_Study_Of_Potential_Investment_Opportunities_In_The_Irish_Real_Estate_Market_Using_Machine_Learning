    Moovingo is delighted to present this newly refurbished house to the market. Fully refurbished over the past 12 months, the property has been fully rewired, new windows installed, solar panels, new bathroom, new wood-burning stove, and new security system with alarm and cameras. The property also boasts an extensive rear garden with beautiful views of the Dublin & Wicklow mountains to the south. Superbly located in the heart of Stepaside Village, this property will appeal to first-time buyers and families looking to upgrade to a larger home with more space.\r\n\r\nThe accommodation comprises a long entrance hallway, spacious living room, modern kitchen, and two additional rooms which could be used as a home office or playroom. Upstairs there are three generous bedrooms and a family bathroom. The aforementioned rear garden is long and laid out in patio and lawn while there is a lawned garden at the front of the house also. The property is ideally situated on a cul-de-sac in this mature residential estate.\r\n\r\n\r\nArea Info\r\n\r\nSituated in the picturesque village of Stepaside, St Patrick\\'s Park is close to a wealth of amenities yet it maintains a countryside charm. The house is within a stone\\'s throw of the village with its various shops and eateries and close to good transport links - both bus and Luas. Only a short drive away are the villages of Enniskerry, Dundrum, Foxrock and Stillorgan which all offer a variety of excellent amenities. Sandyford Business District is also within easy reach by car, Luas or bike. There are an excellent selection of schools in the area in addition to multiple childcare facilities.\r\n\r\nThere are also a great selection of sporting clubs in the area including numerous golf clubs. Just a short walk away, Fernhill Park and Gardens has recently been opened to the public providing great outdoor recreational space with further improvements planned by the council. The 47 bus route towards the city centre goes through the village while the Luas Green Line is a 20 minute walk away. The M50 can be accessed minutes away and Dundrum Town Centre is less than 10 minutes by car.
