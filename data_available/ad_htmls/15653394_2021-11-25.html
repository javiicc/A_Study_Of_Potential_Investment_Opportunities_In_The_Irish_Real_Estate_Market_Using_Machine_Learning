    Sherry FitzGerald Durcan are delighted to introduce to the market this stunning four bedroom detached family home. The first floor comprises three double bedrooms (the master bedroom is en-suite), one single bedroom, a hot press and family bathroom. The attic is accessible via a folding attic stairs.\n\nThe entire residence is very tastefully decorated throughout with a contemporary soft palette of neutral colours which provides extremely relaxing living spaces and the kitchen / dining has a number of French doors which lead to the living room, conservatory and side patio all of which create a beautiful flow which continues throughout the property.\n\nFAQ s:\n4 beds / 3 baths.\n140 sq. metres / 1,506 sq. feet\nAll mains connections to utilities.\nOil fired central heating plus solid fuel stove in living room.\nBER C1\nBuilt 2006.\n\n\nGround Floor\n\nEntrance Hall 5.4m x 2.7m. with ceramic floor tiles and recessed downlights.\n\nGuest WC 1.45m x 1.45m. with contemporary tiling and sanitary ware including wc and wash hand basin.\n\nDining area 4.75m x 4.14m. with ceramic floor tiling, french doors to the living room, conservatory and side patio area.\n\nKitchen 2.57m x 2.92m. with fully fitted kitchen units and ceramic floor tiles.\n\nUtility Room 2.67m x 1.88m. with fitted units, ceramic floor tiles and side access door.
