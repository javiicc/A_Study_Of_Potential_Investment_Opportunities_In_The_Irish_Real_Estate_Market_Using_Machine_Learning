The kitchen area has ample, solid oak wall and floor units, a tiled floor and a tiled splashback. The dining area features semi-solid wood flooring and sliding patio doors which lead to the sunny, south-east facing rear garden, complete with concrete shed and side access. At first floor level, there are three bedrooms, all of which have laminate flooring. The main bedroom also benefits from having large built-in wardrobes.
