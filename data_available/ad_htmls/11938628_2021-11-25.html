    Substantial four bedroom five bathroom two storey family home located at Cordiver, Ballyshannon P.O., Co. Leitrim. Nestled in a clearing close to a wooded area, the property offers privacy and tranquility. The grand feature entrance hall with centre staircase draws you in to the top quality finishes throughout the house. Accommodation extends to circa 260 Sq Metres (circa 2800 Sq ft), is laid out over two floors to provide entrance hall, dining room, kitchen, utility room, living room, playroom, bathroom and four bedrooms (all ensuite). The large living room boasts an open fire with back boiler while the fitted kitchen is finished with granite worktops, feature centre island unit and coloured glass splashback feature. The separate dining room is complimented with an open fire. The standard of finish is to the highest quality.\r\nThe master ensuite features both a roll top bath and separate shower unit. There is a detached garage on site along with additional storage outhouses.\r\nThe residence is approached via hardcore lane off the county road. Contained in Folio LM17227F, title to the property is Freehold.\r\nFor further details and enquiries please contact Sole Agents DKP Auctioneers.
