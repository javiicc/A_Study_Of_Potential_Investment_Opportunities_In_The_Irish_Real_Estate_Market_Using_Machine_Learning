    Approached by a beautiful entrance and hidden away by a tree lined entrance, "Quito House" enjoys a magnificent situation in the heart of Ravensdale Village, Quito House has been in the same family ownership since construction in the 1950's. The house sits on a large and very mature sylvan site with a tributary of the Flurry river bordering its eastern side.\r\n\r\nA solidly built 2 storey residence the property is now ready for refurbishment to the latest standards. Offering enormous potential and character, the rooms are very well laid out and of excellent proportion. The 3 reception rooms on the ground floor are complemented by 5 bedrooms and a study on the first. The ground floor also has a utility section that features a coal house, boiler room, wash room and shower and WC
