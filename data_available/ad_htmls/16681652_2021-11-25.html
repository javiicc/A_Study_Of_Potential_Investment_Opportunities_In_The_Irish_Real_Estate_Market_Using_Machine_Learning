    Mason Estates are delighted to present to the market this 4/5 bedroom detached home in La Touche Close which is just off the centre of Greystones in the heart of the coastal town. La Touche Close is a small cul de sac of approximately 15 houses with every local amenity on your doorstep, just minutes off the Main Street. \r\n\r\nWith the beach and the marina an easy stroll from La Touche Close the property is located a short distance from Greystones Dart station along with Dublin Bus and Aircoach services to Dublin City. There is a choice of several primary/secondary schools literally on its doorstep. The property is very convenient for shops, schools, restaurants, recreation and transportation. An ideal location for working and stay at home families alike. \r\n\r\nBuilt in the 1960's, No 15 is well laid out with flexible accommodation. Upon entering the property through a sun-drenched double-glazed porch the ground floor comprises of a spacious hallway, 2 connecting open plan reception rooms, which comprises of sitting room with gas fireplace and dining room with gas fireplace. There is a kitchen with dining area and a further room to the front of the house with shower/w.c. en-suite which offers the unique benefit of being a home office/ playroom or a 5th bedroom with a guest suite. \r\n\r\nThe first-floor landing leads to the master bedroom with a separate shower room /wc. There are a further 3 bedrooms (2 large doubles and a small double), family bathroom and hot press. The attic is partially floored and recently insulated to a high standard which is currently accessed through a Stira folding stairs. The upstairs offers a remarkable view of the sea, Bray Head and Sugarloaf.\r\n\r\nThere is a side entrance to the rear garden which features two outbuildings, one presently used as a workshop and the other as a garden tool shed.
