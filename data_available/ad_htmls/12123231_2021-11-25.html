    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Olivia Needham is delighted to bring to the market this exceptional four bedroom detached bungalow set on appealing gardens. The property is well presented and maintained to the highest standard.\r\n\r\nThe property enjoys delightful gardens, extending to c.0.41 acres, laid out with generous patio areas and private, sunny, extensive rolling lawns. The gardens are a truly outstanding feature of this property and are bordered by stone walls, mature high hedging, specimen trees, shrubs, a driveway to the front providing ample parking. There are outstanding views directly to Croagh Patrick and beautiful views to Clewbay, Murrisk Abbey and the surrounding countryside. There is also a second seperate entrance to the rear suitable for boat storage.\r\n\r\nMurrisk Village is well known for its excellent community spirit. The village is picture perfect and features well in the tidy village category of the tidy town competition. The village is at the base of Croagh Patrick and is well suited to people interested in outdoor activities on the door step with Croagh Patrick, the new greenway walk and cycle track to Betra Beach is well advanced, as well as the cycle track to Westport. There are also several scenic local walks both coastal & landways. There is also horse riding available in the Village. The village also has two bars and the award winning Tavern restaurant.\r\n\r\nMurrisk is well serviced by local schools with a local national pre-school & national school within walking distance of the property. There is bus access nearby to secondary schools in both Louisburgh and Westport. \r\nAccommodation comprises:\r\nEntrance Hallway \r\nTiled floor, feature doors\r\n\r\nReception Room \r\nFeature fire place, picture window, polished porcelain tiles, double doors to kitchen/diner\r\n\r\n\r\nKitchen/diningroom \r\nRange of wall and floor units, built in oven, integrated fridge, gas hob insert, extractor fan, stainless steel sink, door to utility room, polished porcelain tiles, dishwasher, French doors to beautiful patio area\r\n\r\nUtility Room \r\ntiled floor, door to rear\r\n\r\nMaster Bedroom \r\nBuilt in wardrobes, wooden floors.\r\n\r\nEnsuite \r\nFully tiled, WC, wash hand basin, shower, velux window, recessed lighting.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 \r\nLarge storage cupboards, Wooden floor\r\n\r\nBedroom 3\r\n Wooden floor\r\n\r\nBedroom 4 \r\nWooden floor \r\nBathroom \r\nFully tiled bathroom with bath tub
