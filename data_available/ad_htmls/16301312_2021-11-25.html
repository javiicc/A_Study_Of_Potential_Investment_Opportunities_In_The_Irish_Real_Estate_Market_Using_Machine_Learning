    Extensively refurbished in 2014 this fine property comes to the market in very good condition throughout offering bright spacious accommodation and a generous and private rear garden. Mourne Lodge offers the best of both worlds offering all the comforts of modern day living yet situated in a mature well established neighbourhood with all local amenities on the doorstep.\n\n \n\nAccommodation briefly comprises: Entrance hallway with cloakroom/ guest wc, a spacious fully fitted cream high gloss kitichen comes complete with integrated appliances leading to a large bright living room with French doors opening to the mature and private rear garden. A double bedroom completes the layout on ground floor level.\n\nUpstairs there are two double bedrooms, one of which is en-suite and a full family bathroom. \nThe front garden is low maintenance, with decorative loose stones and an attractive mature tree. The driveway provides off street parking for several cars.\n\nOutside the walled rear garden is mainly in lawn with a paved patio and is very private. \n\n1 Mourne Lodge enjoys a very convenient position close to all local amenities. There is a superb selection of schools quite literally within minutes of the property. There are numerous convenience stores locally along with cafes and parks. A wide selection of sporting facilities to suit all disciplines are within close proximity. \n\nThe area is well served by public transport including the Red Line LUAS just minutes from the property and Dublin Bus routes stopping locally. The N7 /M50 motorway network just a few minutes away making for easy access to the city and beyond.\n\n\nDirections\nComing from the city travel west along the Grand Canal continuing to Dolphin Road and taking the second left on to Mourne Road. Travel along through the third roundabout and Mourne Lodge will be on your left side just past the Church of Our Lady of Good Counsel.
