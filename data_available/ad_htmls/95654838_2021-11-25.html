    Development Description

      Mullen Park is a development of well designed high quality three and four bedroom energy efficient A rated houses being built on a very attractive site at the thriving town of Maynooth.\nThere will be over 100 houses to be sold which are due for completion in three tranches, the first batch end of 2021, the second spring 2022 and final ones in Summer 2022. \n\nHooke & MacDonald are planning to release the second phase early 2022. Viewing will be by appointment only under Government safety guidelines.\n\nThe living accommodation is extensive and well laid out, the bedrooms are spacious, bright and airy with fitted wardrobes; the Bespace kitchens are well fitted. The bathrooms and ensuites are stylish. There is good landscaping in the gardens and in the open spaces and play areas.\n\nThere are numerous advantages of being so close to the array of amenities and facilities at Maynooth. There are excellent transport links, primary and secondary schools, Maynooth University, shops, hotels, restaurants, cafes, sports clubs, leisure facilities, parks and employment centres.\n\nMullen Park is located within walking distance of the bustling Maynooth town centre, which offers the ideal blend of urban and rural living. It also has a fine selection of schools for all ages. The fast and regular train service from Maynooth Station takes commuters to Connolly Station in just 45 minutes, while the N4 allows drivers access the M50 and Dublin City Centre quickly and conveniently. There is also a good bus service linking Maynooth with all parts of Kildare and Dublin.\n\nMaynooth is a vibrant town just outside Dublin. It benefits from a multitude of healthy leisure pursuits, canalside walks, and a main street that has retained its individuality and character. It has a thriving retail hub. The town has a fine selection of supermarkets, boutiques, fashion shops, restaurants, pubs and bars. \n\nCarton House Hotel is located close to Mullen Park, home to two championship golf courses and the Golfing Union of Ireland' s National Academy. Castletown House and the Royal Canal Greenway are other local attractions with beautiful walkways and 9km of cycle trails. Rugby, soccer, GAA, athletics and horse riding are also well established in the area. \n\nPlease register your interest at sales@hmd.ie
