    DNG Martin O'Connor Land Sales are offering for sale a fine plot of land in the townland of Gortbeg by Private Treaty. The plot extending to c. 2.24 Ha (5.54 Acres) is made up of three fields offering excellent road frontage. The land in meadow presents itself as an excellent opportunity to acquire good quality farmland in the area. Furthermore it is ideal for those seeking to acquire a small holding for the keeping of ponies or to have a hobby farm. Perfectly located within close proximity to Corofin, Ballyglunnin, Abbey & Turloughmore while Tuam and Galway City are within an easy commute. The M17 motorway is located 5 km to the west of the property. The property has obvious future site potential for those with a local housing need.
