    No. 25 is a newly refurbished three bedroom duplex apartment within a well managed student village on the west side of Galway city. The apartment accommodation is made up of 3 bedrooms. One Double Room, One Single Room and a Twin Room all an ensuite There is a bright open plan living area with a living/dining area and adjoining kitchen. The student village benefits from an on-site launderette and a shuttle bus service for the short trip to NUIG. The apartment is a short walk to Westside Shopping Centre and all the amenities it provides.
