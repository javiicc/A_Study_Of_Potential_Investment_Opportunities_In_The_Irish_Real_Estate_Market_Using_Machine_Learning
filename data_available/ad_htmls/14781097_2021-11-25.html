    A lovely detached red-brick family home located on leafy Vernon Avenue within minutes of local shopping and the wonderful c.240 acre St Anne's Park . \r\n \r\nThe impressive accommodation extends to almost c.2400 Sq.Ft inside. Outside, the long front garden has an extensive lawned area and parking for several cars. The well-proportioned rear garden is completely private with a lawned area and a good sized storage shed. The large paved patio enjoys a perfect sunny southerly aspect which is ideal for barbeques and relaxing outdoors. \r\n\r\nThis spectacular home is set well back from the avenue and is presented in excellent order throughout.\r\n\r\nDownstairs off the entrance hall is a home office, a formal dining room, and a main living room. The main living room has an attractive open fireplace and a square bay window overlooking the long front garden. Double doors lead from the main living space into a comfortable sitting room which in turn opens into the feature extended Kitchen cum dining room. This is an over-sized space tastefully finished in natural wood and featuring a high vaulted ceiling with exposed timber beams and a large island unit in the centre of this space. South facing patio doors fill the interior with natural light and slide open to the rear garden while a large utility space is extended beyond with access to the rear garden also. \r\n\r\nUpstairs, there are 4 double bedrooms, with a main bedroom ensuite, and a family bathroom. The main bedroom has extensive wall to wall built in wardrobes and the shower room ensuite is as large a bedroom. The property enjoys views out over the Vernon avenue streetscape and with almost 2400 sq ft of floor area there is certainly ample space to cater for every family members need inside and out. \r\n\r\nThe convenience of living in this remarkable location cannot be rivalled as the village and the wonderful c.240 acre St Anne's Park are within a short walk of home.
