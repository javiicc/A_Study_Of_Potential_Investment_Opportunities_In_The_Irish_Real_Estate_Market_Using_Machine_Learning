    Development Description

      **Current Phase Sold Out**\r\n\r\n**Please register your details in the event of a cancellation and for future releases**\r\n\r\nThe Willows comprises a fine mix of three and four bedroom semi-detached houses with very good layouts and specification.\r\n\r\nThese are well designed energy efficient homes in an attractive village setting, with good transport facilities, the railway station nearby as well as the new M3 motorway and bus routes.\r\n\r\nThe fact that the Willows enjoys easy access to Dublin city centre is a considerable advantage. It also enjoys convenience to numerous amenities and facilities including schools, shops, restaurants, sports and leisure facilities, Fairyhouse Racecourse. There is a large employer base close by including Intel, Dublin Airport, Connolly Hospital, Blanchardstown Town Centre and many more. The cross Luas links connect up with Broombridge bringing Dublin city centre even closer.\r\nDunshaughlin has a rich history and is famous for an ancient Crann\xc3\x83\xc6\x92\xc3\x82\xc2\xb3g or settlement from the 7th century where a number of Irish antiquities were discovered. It is close to the Boyne Valley, an area of outstanding beauty and with many other important historical features such as the Hill of Tara, Newgrange, Oldbridge House, Trim Castle and Kells. \r\n\r\nThe Willows is an ideal location for first time buyers and young families trading up who are wishing to stay in or move to such an attractive vibrant location. Dunshaughlin has two primary schools and a secondary school, Dunshaughlin Community College, which was selected as one of 12 schools worldwide to take part in Microsoft' s Innovative Schools Programme. In 2006 a town park was opened by President Mary Robinson. These attributes add to the attractiveness of the location and quality of life. \r\n\r\nSporting enthusiasts can savour the multitude of activities and clubs in Dunshaughlin and surrounding locations including gaelic football and hurling, rugby, soccer, golf, athletics, horse racing, Rathbeggan Lakes activity centre; the magnificent Killeen Castle Golf Course, designed by Jack Nicklaus, the venue for the Solheim Cup 2011.\r\n\r\nDunshaughlin is well serviced by both rail and road. The M3 motorway significantly reduces travel times into Dublin City Centre. Alternatively, commuters can avail of the rail line which runs from Dunboyne to the Dublin Docklands. It is the largest Park & Ride facility in the country, located at Pace, only 9 kilometres from Dunshaughlin. Trains run every half hour with a peak journey time of only 27 minutes. Dunshaughlin is considered a peaceful village setting within close proximity to Dublin city and an ideal option for a young family.\r\n\r\nThe GEM Group\r\n\r\nGEM Group has been leading from experience since 1978 by building trust, certainty and value for their clients. They have completed many notable residential projects and have established a brand of quality and service which is reflected in customers and clients long term satisfaction. They are a CIRI registered builder and have successfully completed a number of housing developments as Assigned Builder under the new building control regulations.\r\nThe development is funded by Activate Capital and it qualifies for the Help to Buy scheme which is proving very popular.\r\n\r\n** Photos: Please note the exterior photos are not of the actual house as this purchase is off plans but are representative of the standard of finish. **\r\n\r\nPlease contact Hooke & MacDonald on 01 6318402 or Joint agents Dillon Property Service Providers, Dunshaughlin on (01) 825 9154\r\n\r\nVisit: www.thewillows.ie for more information
