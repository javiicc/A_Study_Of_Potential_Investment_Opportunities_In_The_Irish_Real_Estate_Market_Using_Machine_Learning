    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 12 ac
    Offered for sale is this compact block of established forestry, measuring 12 acres in total. Planted in 2000, this plantation consists of approximately eight acres of good quality Sitka Spruce trees, one acre of oak trees and one acre of ash trees. There are two acres unplanted which have high voltage power lines passing over same. Access to this plot is via a right-of-way. Located in a very scenic area, midway between Stranorlar and Convoy. Further details available on enquiry.
