    The property comprises a detached cottage residence built circa 1952 but has benefitted from recent refurbishment, circa 6 years ago, to include new roof, wiring and plumbing. The cottage is accessed over a private gated gravel driveway from the local road and sits on an elevated site overlooking the surrounding countryside. The property has excellent potential and can be extended up to 40 sq.m to the rear without planning permission, however this can be increased given the extent of land available to the front and rear of the dwelling house (subject to planning permission). \n\nTo the front of the property there is a small garden and beyond same is an old dwelling house, which has a lean-to built at both side, this is currently used for storage, but again would lend itself to further development (subject to planning permission). There is also an open fronted hay barn located in close proximity to the cottage. \n\nThe lands included in the sale comprise c.4.90 hectares (c.12.11 acres) of land divided by the local road to the north and listed under Folio MY13143. Overall the land would be considered reasonable quality grazing land.\nThis property offers the best of both worlds, country living with the convenience off all amenities close by.\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nLocation:\nThe Holding is located a short distance from the tourist village of Newport, c.3km distant. Newport, is a picturesque town nestled on the shores of Clew Bay with the Black Oak River flowing through its centre. \nThe Greenway is a 42km walking and cycling trail which passes through the picturesque town of Newport. Renowned for its excellent fishing, Newport offers something for everyone - whatever your level of expertise. Sea angling is available in the bountiful waters of Clew Bay, while Newport River and Beltra Lake are regarded as some of the best freshwater fishing grounds in Europe.\n\n\nAccommodation Comprises:\nPorch:\t\t\t1.95m x 2.84m\nHallway:\t\t5.02m x 1.04m\nKitchen/Dining:\t\t4.92m x 3.72m\nLiving/Bed 1:\t\t3.39m x 5.26m\nBedroom 2:\t\t4.15m x 2.66m\nBedroom 3:\t\t4.14m x 2.05m\nShower Room:\t\t1.05m x 3.28m\n\nApproximate Internal Floor Area:\tc.70 sq.m (c.753 sq.ft)\n\n\nDirections:\nFrom Newport, proceed out the Glenhest road (R317) for c.1.7km, take left turn (See FM Auctioneers directional sign), proceed to next T junction and take a right.
