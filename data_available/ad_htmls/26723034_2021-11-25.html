      Smart Property is delighted to bring to the rental market this stunning and elegant Georgian style four bedroom terrace property in the ever popular coastal development of Robswall, Malahide.\n\nLiving within Robswall you can enjoy the beautiful coastal walk into Malahide and Portmarnock. Both areas are full of the best restaurants, bars and boutiques to be found. There is a large selection of schools, sports clubs and amenities nearby.\n\nThis property is spread over three floors has stunning sea views and presented in turnkey condition. \n \nUpon entering the tastefully chosen tiled hallway of No. 13 The Crescent on the ground level is a large office and downstairs WC. First floor is the bright and spacious kitchen/dining area which leads out to very low maintenance west facing garden, there is also a utility room off the kitchen area. Impressive living area with high ceilings and large bay windows offering stunning sea views. Second floor is the modern bathroom and spacious double bedroom with built in wardrobes. Third floor along with good sized single bedroom there are two large double bedrooms, both with built in wardrobes and master being ensuite. This property is gas central heating throughout.
