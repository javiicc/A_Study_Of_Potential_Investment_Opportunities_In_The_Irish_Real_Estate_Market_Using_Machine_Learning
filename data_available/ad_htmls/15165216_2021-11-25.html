    Hunters Estate Agent are truly delighted and honored to introduce this very fine detached period residence to the market.\r\nSt. Peters, 91 Ailesbury Road, is a home of distinction commanding a prime position on this tree lined road in the heart of Ballsbridge. The property presents rooms of gracious proportions and light filled interiors extending to 530 sq.m./5,702 sq.ft., and boasts a wealth of original features, majestic fireplaces, decorative coving, feature high ceilings and an elegant staircase. One of just a handful of detached residence on the road, the property boasts a number of very fine reception rooms and seven double bedrooms.\r\nThe exactness of the internal proportions is further complimented by the vast road frontage and gardens, richly stocked and planted with specimen trees and shrubs, and providing ample off-street parking for a number of cars. The dual side passage leads to the south facing rear garden.\r\n\r\nSt Peters has the benefit of planning permission granted in 2015 to demolish the rear extensions and to remodel this very fine home adding a two-storey addition over new basement in harmony with style of existing structure, designed by Sheenhan & Barry Architects, resulting in an overall footprint of 607 sq.m./7,500 sq.ft. The basement will comprise of two ensuite bedrooms, a laundry and plant room, playroom/study and w.c. There will be access to a rear sunken courtyard, and floor to ceiling windows to rear. The ground floor will retain the gracious reception rooms whilst adding a kitchen/breakfast room that will delight in terms of light and space with floor to ceiling windows and access to myriad of terraces. The first floor will present three further bedrooms and a linen room. All bedrooms will benefit from dressing rooms and shower/bathrooms. An internal lift shall service all floors.
