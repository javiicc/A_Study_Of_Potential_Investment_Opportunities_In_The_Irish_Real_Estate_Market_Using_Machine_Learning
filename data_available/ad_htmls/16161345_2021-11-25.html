    Sherry FitzGerald Catherine O Reilly are delighted to present to the market Tinakilly Upper, a lovely light-filled 4-bedroom detached bungalow right in the heart of renowned Aughrim Village sitting on ca. 1.36 acres of landscaped gardens with front and rear vehicular access and a spacious integrated garage. \n\nAccommodation, comprising of a lounge, sitting room, kitchen/dining, hall, bathroom and 4 bedrooms, must be viewed to fully appreciate this home s true potential. \n\nThe property is just a few minutes walk to the village s main street providing an abundance of amenities including shops, school, church, restaurants and pubs with some great views of the Wicklow Valley surrounding this picturesque village. Aughrim Village is a multi-award-winning village in Ireland s Tidy Towns competition and a strong sense of community spirit. \n\nThe village also boasts an angling lake, bowling green with pavilion (where a farmers market is held every Saturday). There is also plenty to do for the sports enthusiasts with soccer, GAA, horse riding, tennis, some lovely walks in the beautiful surrounding woods and a short drive to nearby golf courses at Brook Lodge in Macreddin village, Woodenbridge & Coollatin. \n\nAughrim is within commuting distance of South Dublin via the N11/M11/M50 link or by commuter train services calling at Arklow, Rathdrum located only 10 kilometers away.\n\nViewings are by appointment only and in strict adherence to current health-related protocols. \n\n--- View & Make offers directly on MySherryFitz.ie ---\n\n\nEntrance Hall 7.63m x 3.68m.
