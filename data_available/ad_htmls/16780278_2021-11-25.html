    Earnest are delighted to present no 85 Castle Hall to the sales market. No 85 is the largest of the two bedroom apartments within Swords central and it offers prospective purchasers a fantastic opportunity to acquire a property within such close proximity of a major shopping center while still maintaining a quiet and ambient feel to it. Situated on the 3rd floor ( Lift ) from internal courtyard level , the accommodation briefly comprises of a large welcoming hallway with semi solid flooring leading into a large open plan kitchen , living and dining area. The L shaped kitchen is a modern with floor and wall units and tiled back splash. The living and dining area offers an abundance of space measuring c42Sqm with access to a spacious south east facing balcony. The master bedroom is well proportioned and includes an en suite and the second double also is good size. Both bedrooms are carpeted. The main bathroom is fully tiled and includes a bath, and shower facility. There is storage as part of a large hot press in the hallway.
