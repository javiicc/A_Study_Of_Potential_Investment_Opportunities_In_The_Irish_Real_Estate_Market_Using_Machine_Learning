    Welcome to 2 Greenlands, Sunbury Court in Dartry, Dublin 6. This own-door two bedroomed ground floor apartment is ideally situated on the sunny side of the development with a generous lawn leading to the private entrance door. Presented in excellent condition throughout having been meticulously cared for and tastefully renovated by the current owners. Conveniently adjacent to the wonderful parks at Palmerston and Dartry, with the scenic Dodder walk only moments away. This apartment is incredibly well-catered for by public transport with the LUAS at both Cowper and Milltown nearby combined with the vast array of Dublin Bus stops in the locality. The trendy villages of Rathgar, Rathmines and Ranelagh are within walking distance along with the boutique amenities offered by the gentrified suburb of Portobello. Enjoying an enviable dual aspect orientation with incredible privacy and natural light in a small block of 6 apartments, this is a great opportunity for a discerning purchaser to acquire a turn-key apartment in an ever-sought after location that is sure to appeal to first-time buyers, down-sizers, and investors alike. Early viewing is highly advised.\r\n\r\nPeacefully nestled back on Dartry Road with a dual aspect providing natural light and views of the delightful communal grounds, the accommodation comprises; an entrance porch which leads through to the living/dining room. This room spans the full property width, and a glorious picture window frames the room with a private view of the greenery outside. A hallway with ample storage space leads to the fully equipped kitchen. The two double bedrooms are both situated to the rear with a westerly aspect and fitted wardrobes. The recently refurbished shower room completes the accommodation inside. This apartment also benefits from one designated car parking space. The communal grounds provide private outdoor space for residents to enjoy an evening of dining al fresco on those long summer evenings. The combination of the convenient location, exceptionally maintained communal grounds and the fine condition of the residence itself provide a wonderful opportunity for those looking to acquire a property in the area.\r\n\r\n2 Greenlands is a 10 minute walk to the Luas at Milltown and a 15 minute walk to the Cowper Luas stop. There is an extensive choice of amenities including excellent local shopping, educational and recreational facilities at Rathgar & Rathmines which are both a 10 minute walk away respectively, Ranelagh which is a 20 minute walk away, and Portobello which is a 25 minute walk away. Sport facilities are well catered for and include LA Fitness, Brookfield Tennis Club, Riverview Racquet & Fitness Club and Leinster Cricket Club, Milltown Golf Club to name a few. Some of Dublin s best schools are within easy reach including Gonzaga, St. Mary s, Sandford Park, Muckross and Alexandra, Kildare Place, Sandford National, Scoil Bride and Ranelagh Multidenominational. The convenient location of this apartment means that every amenity that Dublin City has to offer, both social and essential, are within easy reach.\r\n\r\n\r\nEntrance Porch Stained glass door and laminate flooring with entrance to:\r\n\r\nLiving/Dining Room 4.58m x 3.58m.
