    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    22 Main Street, Kilcoole, is a 2 bedroom end of terrace property ideally located in the heart of Kilcoole within a stones throw from an array of amenities the village has to offer. The property located on a corner site, measures approximately 69 sq. m (742 sq. ft) and is presented in good decorative order throughout. \r\n\r\nUnlike the neighbouring properties no. 22 is accessed through the rear of the property via a pedestrian gate, with entry into the kitchen/dining area. The accommodation comprises of kitchen/dining room, downstairs bathroom, living room and an additional room to the front of the property which can be utilised as a study/playroom or another bedroom if required. \r\n\r\nOn the first floor there are two bedrooms, both of which are fitted with built in wardrobes. The principle bedroom is located to the front of the property and the second bedroom provides commanding views of the Irish Sea and over looks the garden to the rear. \r\n\r\nOutside to the rear is an exceptionally large garden almost 26 meters in length. There is also the added benefit of gates to the rear which provides off street parking for up to one vehicle. There is also a storage shed situated in the garden area. \r\n\r\nKilcoole village boasts, amongst other amenities, many shops, bars, restaurants and cafes, with the renowned Druids Glen Hotel only minutes away by car. Greystones town is a short drive and has a variety of shops and eateries to choose from as well as beautiful coastal walks including the Greystones to Bray Cliff Walk. Sports facilities and clubs are in abundance with rugby, tennis, sailing and golf being just a small example of what is on offer. Transport links include easy access to the N11/M50, Kilcoole Train station, and Dublin Bus routes.
