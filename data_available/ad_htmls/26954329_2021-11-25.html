      The Beach House , Blackrock, offers a unique opportunity to rent a home with one of the best and most breathtaking views along the coastline of Co Louth. \n\nThe house itself is a modern architect designed property with a real edge. It is seldom a property comes on the market that offers a rare combination of easy access to a wide range of amenities and services while at the same time being neatly and securely tucked away at the south end of Blackrock s sandy beach.\n\nLocation\nBlackrock is a much sought after destination. A calm and atmospheric seaside village with its own beach and promenade, Blackrock also offers local shopping, a choice of restaurants, coffee shops, schools and sporting facilities. The area is home to the popular 18-hole Dundalk Golf Course while the beach has become popular as a safe area for sailboarding and kitesurfing enthusiasts.\nThe village is only minutes from Dundalk and the vibrant energy of the town centre, with its fabulous selection of modern and old world amenities; all overlooked by the picturesque Cooley Mountains and dramatic and exciting coastline of Dundalk Bay. \nDundalk boasts an exceptionally high standard of amenities and facilities, such as retail parks, shopping centres, traditional and high street shopping, restaurants, bars, cafe culture, recreation, business, and sport. There is also an outstanding educational infrastructure with plenty of excellent schools and the ever expanding Dundalk Institute of Technology\nFrom racing nights at Dundalk Stadium to family activities in the town s public parks or leisurely walks in the nearby Cooley Peninsula, there is no shortage of things to do in the area and all of this in the revitalising setting and the natural beauty provided by both sea and mountains. \nJust off the M1, the nearby motorway provides easy access to the two capital cities of Dublin and Belfast and their associated airports.\nWith its abundance of natural assets, its seaside location, its thriving pub and restaurant scene, Blackrock is an ideal place to live.\n\n\nThe House\nThere is a great sense of space and modernist luxury afforded by the layout of this fine 192m2 (2066sq ft) home. Accommodation includes: Entrance Hall, 4 Bedrooms (3 en-suite), sitting room, living dining room, large kitchen, bathroom and utility room. \nWhilst, the property is a large residence it has a warm and friendly feel. The principle reception rooms, kitchen and master bedroom which are bright and airy, have spectacular views over Dundalk Bay, stretching from Blackrock s Main Street to Gyles Quay, the Cooley Mountains and along the horizon between Ballagan Point and Dunany Point.\nThe current owners have given great care and attention to detail in fitting out this stunning home, adding features that include: solid oak kitchen with granite worktops, oak joinery and solid oak flooring throughout, solid fuel Stanley stove, Smarthomes central music and sound system, natural gas under floor heating system throughout, which can also be controlled remotely via internet or text message, feature coving, built in wardrobes and central vacuum system.\nThe wonderful property is entered through two sets of electric gates with a paved driveway leading to the forecourt of the house which has far reaching and breath taking views of the extremely impressive coastline. \nThe front of the property has low maintenance flowers beds bounded by railway sleepers and stone wall. There is also secure and private access down to the rock pool and magnificent shoreline.
