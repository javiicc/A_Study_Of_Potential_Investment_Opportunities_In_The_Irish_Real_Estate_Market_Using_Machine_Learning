    Prime location -This 4 bedroomed detached home is situated a stone s throw from Lismore town centre with schools, shops, playground and churches all a short distance away. Conveniently located, Lismore is a mere 45 minute drive from Cork, 1 hour from Waterford and 20 minutes from Dungarvan.\n\nThe living room with its large front window is located to the front of the property and has an open fireplace with a door that leads to the adjoining dining room/ The kitchen/dining area is very spacious and has a door from the kitchen directly to the rear garden. The rear lounge is a fine room with large window and sliding glass door to the garden. This room also has an open fireplace. \n\nAll of the bedrooms are off the main hallway and are of ample size with the master facing to the front and having extensive fitted wardrobes. There is a bathroom also off the hallway which services the bedrooms.\n\nExternally, the property has a walled front garden with a driveway and a decent size garden the rear. With a bit of moderisation this property has fantastic potential.\n\nThis is a unique opportunity to acquire a well located bungalow in the heart of Lismore. \n\n OFCH.
