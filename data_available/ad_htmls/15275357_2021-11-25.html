    Sherry FitzGerald Reilly is delighted to bring to market this stunning five bedroom, four bathroom detached residence extending to approx 335 sq m / 3,600 sq ft in a delightful setting adjacent to Coill Dubh village. (Naas 16.5 km). This wonderful home has been finished to a high standard with a host of eye catching features. This is a most impressive property arranged over two floors, every square foot of the home has been carefully considered to ensure it is a special family home. It has five double bedrooms ( 2 ensuite ), a spacious inviting entrance hall, a generous lounge, a large open plan kitchen / living / dining area, dining / tv room, bathroom, large utility / bootroom, guest wc and walk in hotpress . The finish throughout is impressive with many special features, the property feels spacious, bright and airy throughout, with wide hallways, high ceilings and mainly sash windows adding to its undoubted appeal. As you enter the property through electronic entrance gates along a tarmac driveway, this imposing residence instantly impresses. On entering the home you are greeted with a spacious bright hallway, off to the right is a ground floor bedroom with ensuite, to the left is the lounge, next on your left is the large kitchen/living/dining area with a magnificent bespoke fitted kitchen that has plenty of storage, it is fitted with high spec appliances and finished with granite countertops and an island breakfast bar, just off this area is the spacious utility room again with plenty of storage units and a guest wc and walk in hotpress off. just off to the right of the hallway is the dining/family room. \nThe first floor has four bedrooms, one of which is the super master suite with dressing room and ensuite, the master / family bathroom boasts a large bath and a separate shower. This is a truly special and unique home that has been finished to a high standard. As well as looking stunning throughout, the property is a practical family home with well - sized bedrooms, numerous bathrooms, plenty of living space and good storage.
