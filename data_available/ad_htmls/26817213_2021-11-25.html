      Sherry Fitzgerald Lettings are delighted to present this stunning 2 bedroom apartment to lettings market. Recently refurbished and painted throughout, this property briefly comprises of an entrance hall, a large light filled living room which gives way to a large balcony with breath taking views of Burrow beach and beyond, a recently renovated fully fitted kitchen, a main bathroom, two double bedrooms with the master containing a second balcony overlooking burrow road. This exclusive seaside beachfront development is much sought after and has its own access directly onto Borrow beach.\r\n\r\nThis small, private and gated development offers secure parking.
