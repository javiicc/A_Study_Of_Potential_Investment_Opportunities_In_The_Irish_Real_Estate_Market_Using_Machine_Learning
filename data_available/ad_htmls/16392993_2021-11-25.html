    O'Connor Murphy are delighted to bring to the market this five bedroom detached family home. Rarely does an opportunity present itself to acquire a property such as this, offering the perfect balance of location and rural living with Limerick City just a short drive away! \n\nApproached by a magnificent tree lined long driveway, this detached five bedroom detached bungalow sits well within this established area of Meelick, enjoying bright rooms throughout. Set on a superb private circa six acre site of mature grounds, this house is sure to please any purchaser looking to take on a project for extra space or wishing to up size. There is plenty of off street car parking with 4 stables & 2 column barns & an abundance of mature trees & hedging with stunning views. Whilst in need of complete refurbishment & modernising, there is potential here to create a wonderful home!\n\nAccommodation briefly consists of: Entrance Hallway, Living Room, Kitchen/Dining Room, 5 Bedrooms & a Family Bathroom. \n\nUpon entering the house, a large porch area offers shelter from those rainy days. A large living room with an floor to ceiling red brick fireplace with an open fire along with dual aspect windows giving an abundance of light whilst offering wonderful views of the garden. A Patio door give rear access to the patio area. The next room is the kitchen/dining area; A good size space with fully fitted units, feature fireplace, tiled flooring & country views instils a sense of peace & calm. There are five good sized bedrooms all with built in wardrobes & wash hand basins each enjoying excellent views of the surrounding gardens. A family bathroom completes the accommodation.\n\nOutside, the land is fenced off & to the rear there are four stables along with two column barns. With plenty of space to renovate & extend if you so desired (subject to planning). Mature trees & hedging make for a private, secure & secluded site.\n\nCappantymore is located circa 8km north of Limerick City within the Clare hills. The general area is of farmland and once off housing however it is located within a short drive of the village of Meelick, Co/ Clare. Meelick located on the north suburbs of Limerick City, would supply essential amenities such as Shops, Church, Sport Activities etc. for the area. \n\nSurrounded by beautiful countryside this home has such great potential with the right vision a superb home could be created!
