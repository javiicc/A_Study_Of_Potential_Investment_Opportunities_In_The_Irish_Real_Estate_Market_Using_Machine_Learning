    A truly remarkable and most impressive cottage residence presented to the market in lovely condition throughout. With vast amounts of natural light, an excellent layout well laid out accommodation ideal for family life, this super home will impress from the moment you enter the front door! Briefly comprising kitchen / diner / sunroom, lounge room, 3 bedrooms, all lending themselves perfectly for everyday living. Ideally located, adjacent to Killinick village, and minutes drive to numerous beautiful beaches at Rosslare Strand, St Helens, Kilrane etc. Rosslare Harbour is approx. 5 mins drive, Wexford town centre is approx.15 minutes driving distance.
