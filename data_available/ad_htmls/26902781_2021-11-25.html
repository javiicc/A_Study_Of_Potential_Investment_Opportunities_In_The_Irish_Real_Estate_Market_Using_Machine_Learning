The city centre is only moments away either by car or by bus. There is a bus terminus just outside the complex and a number of others bus routes from the KCR and Kimmage Road West. There is a park located right behind the complex which offers great recreational use. The whole of the apartment has just been totally refurbished throughout. There are large windows which overlook the large green area to the front while also offering exceptional light as it has a south facing aspect. The colour scheme is off white with a grey feature wall, the furnishings include a large grey 3 seater couch, black glass/steel nest of tables along with a glass dining table and upholstered grey high back chairs.
