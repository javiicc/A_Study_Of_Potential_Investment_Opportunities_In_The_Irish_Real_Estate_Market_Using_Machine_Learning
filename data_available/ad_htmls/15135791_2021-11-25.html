    Sherry FitzGerald Carroll are absolutely delighted to present Dromena Hill, Castlebellingham to the market, a highly important sale of a truly magnificently presented two storey family residence.\n \nThis superior elegant home has a charm and generosity of scale that is instantly impressive to the senses, with encapsulating sea views effortlessly framed against the backdrop of the Cooley Mountains and bolstered with an infectious warmth of character that few such impressive properties can offer.\n \nApproached from a winding driveway, flanked by mature trees lies this very impressive, elevated one of a kind residence. A home with a difference might be an apt description for this unique and deceptively spacious family residence with spectacular views. Discretely tucked away in a mature secluded and highly desirable location just outside the historic village of Castlebellingham offering ease of access to the M1 Dublin / Belfast Motorway and Dundalk town centre. \n \nStanding amidst extensive landscaped and tenderly manicured gardens of just under an acre, the residence was built in c. 1970 and extends to approx. 3,044 sq. ft. offering versatile internal accommodation presented in superb condition with rooms of generous proportions incorporating impressive entertaining spaces eloquently balanced with elegance and charm to the fore. \n \nA spacious entrance hall introduces the home and leads to a sizable reception room with an inset solid fuel stove and an abundance of light from dual aspect generous glazing and patio doors that open out onto the beautiful stone wrap around patio that envelopes the rear of the property and offers rolling views over the lush gardens and surrounding fields. A formal dining room carefully leads through to the stylish kitchen area with its own informal dining space and sliding door to the magnificent sun drenched patio, the hub of this residence is further enhanced by a cosy family living area with additional feature stove. An adjoining beautifully fitted out utility / boot room and further games room or downstairs bedroom offering a host of storage and shower room facilities together with a well-proportioned study adds to the versatility of the ground floor of the property. At first floor there are four bedrooms including a generous sized master suite with walk-in wardrobe and bathroom and additional family bathroom completes the picture. \n \nThe exterior benefits from an elevated tennis court, numerous discrete sheds and areas for storage that have been cleverly created to house life s day to day running of such an impressive property yet not to interfere with the rolling vista. \n \nThe present owners have expertly managed to combine taste, flair and style to create a home generating a sense of timeless repose that stands out as one of North Louth s finest residences that must be viewed.\n\n\nGROUND FLOOR\n\nEntrance Hall 8.30m x 4.36m.\n\nDrawing Room 4.76m x 8.48m.\n\nDining Room 4.42m x 3.64m.\n\nKitchen 3.88m x 3.64m.\n\nStudy/Office 3.46m x 3.21m.\n\nUtility Room 4.06m x 3.08m.\n\nLaundry 4.90m x 1.28m.\n\nBathroom 1.33m x 3.08m.\n\nGames Room/ Bed 5 5.65m x 6.00m.\n\nShed 2.34m x 3.08m.\n\nFIRST FLOOR\n\nMaster Bedroom 5.95m x 5.52m.\n\nEnsuite 1.73m x 2.49m.\n\nDressing Room 3.33m x 2.49m.\n\nBathroom 2.41m x 3.21m.\n\nBedroom 2 4.44m x 3.64m.\n\nBedroom 3 4.42m x 3.64m.\n\nBedroom 4 2.78m x 4.36m.\n\nStorage 1 1.05m x 0.72m.\n\nStorage 2 0.65m x 0.72m.
