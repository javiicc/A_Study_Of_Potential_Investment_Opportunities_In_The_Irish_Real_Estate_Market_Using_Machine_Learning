    Stunning 3 bedroom (originally a 4 bed) detached family home with full P.P. to extend over playroom to add on an extra 2 bedrooms and full P.P. on the attached 33ft site to the side for a 3-bedroom detached property, No.2 also benefits from a sunny westerly aspect.\n\nMcGuirk Beggan are delighted to bring to the market this stunning & beautifully upgraded 3 bedroom detached family (originally a 4 bedroom) complete with full planning permission to add on an extra two bedrooms & bathroom. On the 33ft side garden (there is full planning permission for a 3-bedroom detached property). The property also benefits from a sunny westerly aspect, so is drenched in natural light to the rear from early afternoon right until the sun sets in the evening, the garden is also not directly overlooked so offers immense privacy. There is also a large detached Shomera Room complete with central heating and plumbing, currently in use as a home bar/gym. \n\nThe current planning permission allows for an extension over the playroom on the existing house which will add on an additional two bedrooms and bathroom. On the 33ft site attached to the side of the property there is full planning permission in place for a 3-bedroom detached dwelling offering huge potential for the successful buyer to extend the original home and develop the site or possibly go for a change in planning and add on a double storey extension on the existing property to double the size of the original home. The potential to utilise the additional space for each individual buyer is boundless. \n\nOriginally a four bed, the original owners incorporated two of the bedrooms to create an exceptionally spacious master bedroom. The property has been updated in recent years, this stunning home has been newly painted, carpeted, has had fitted solid oak internal doors throughout and also features Travertine marble flooring in the hall, kitchen and dining area. Hardwood double French doors overlook the sunny west facing rear garden.\n\nThe accommodation briefly comprises entrance hall, living room, open plan kitchen/dining, playroom/home office, guest WC, 3 bedrooms (main bedroom was originally 2 separate rooms), bathroom, en suite, block built shed fully plumbed & electricity, large detached Shomera with central heating and plumbing. \n\nCypress Downs has always been a popular choice amongst families. It is within walking distance of a wealth of excellent schools including Bishop Shanahan/Galvin, Templeogue & Terenure colleges, Our Lady`s and St Pius national. Ample recreational and shopping facilities are in the area with St. Jude`s GAA Club and St. Marys Rugby Club close by, Bushy & Tymon Park on your doorstep. Rathfarnham Shopping Centre and all the amenities of Templeogue & Terenure villages are a short stroll away. The area is well serviced by frequent and dependable bus routes and the property also enjoys the added bonus of easy access to the M50 motorway network.\n\nThe front garden is in lawn with a driveway for off street parking for 2 cars. There is a 33ft side garden with full P.P. to build a 3-bedroom detached dwelling. The front and rear gardens are well shielded with mature shrubbery. A side entrance allows access to the exceptional rear garden, benefiting from a sunny westerly aspect, it features a large-decked area, large Shomera which is currently in use as a home bar/gym, block built shed, lawn and a well-stocked garden which is awash with colour throughout the growing season. \n\nThere are untold possibilities and great versatility which await the lucky buyer, thus making this an ideal home for a growing family. To fully understand the boundless opportunities this fine property has to offer, viewing is highly recommended.\n\n\n\n\nDirections\nComing from Templeogue Village take a right hand turn at the traffic lights onto Cypress Grove Road. Take the third left hand turn onto Cypress Drive, Cypress Downs and the second turn left onto The Avenue, Cypress Downs.\n\nNotice\nPlease note we have not tested any apparatus, fixtures, fittings, or services.
