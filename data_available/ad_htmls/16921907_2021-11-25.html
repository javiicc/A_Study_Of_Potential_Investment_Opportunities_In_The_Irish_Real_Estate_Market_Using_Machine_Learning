    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    DNG are delighted to launch 9 Monkstown Road, a stunning double fronted villa style Victorian residence built c1852 to the open market. This charming property boasts bright and spacious accommodation and a glorious south facing rear patio garden. This truly unique family home has been upgraded and modernised over the years and offers the discerning purchaser a terrific home with an array of special feature including, sash windows throughout, ceiling coving, original fireplace, designer kitchen and master bedroom ensuite. The property offers ample parking in a large gated, gravelled, private driveway to the front.\n\nSuperbly located on the prestigious Monkstown Road and within striking distance of the cosmopolitan villages of Monkstown and Blackrock with a wide range of amenities including shops, cafes, pubs, fine dining, boutiques and churches of all denominations to name but a few. There is also a selection of leisure facilities including stunning coastal walks from Seapoint to Dun Laoghaire harbour and beyond and many sporting clubs which will suit every interest. Transport is also well catered for with the Dart at Seapoint station, QBC, Rock Road, N11 and M50 all close by.\n\nThe spacious and extremely bright accommodation extends to c.1,450 sq ft and briefly comprises of entrance hallway, living/diningroom, kitchen, conservatory, 4 bedrooms (2 ensuites) and family bathroom. \nViewing comes highly recommended to appreciate all that this fine property has to offer.\n\nFeatures:\nAttractive, double fronted Victorian home\nSet back behind large wooden gates\nLarge, private, gravelled driveway\nExtends to c.1,450 sq ft\n4 bedrooms\nGFCH\nSouth facing rear garden\nClose to excellent schools, shops, transport links\nConservatory\nAmple off street parking\nClose to Monkstown & Blackrock villages\n\n\n\n\nEntrance Hall 3.71m x 2.62m.\n\nLiving Room/Dining Room 6.12m x 4.65m.\n\nKitchen/Dining Room 3.99m x 3.63m.\n\nConservatory 3.66m x 2.82m.\n\nMaster Bedroom 5.54m x 2.97m.\n\nEnsuite 1.83m x 1.83m.\n\nBedroom 2 3.10m x 2.90m.\n\nEnsuite 2.62m x 1.78m.\n\nBedroom 3 3.89m x 2.97m.\n\nBedroom 4 3.05m x 1.98m.\n\nBathroom 2.72m x 2.06m.
