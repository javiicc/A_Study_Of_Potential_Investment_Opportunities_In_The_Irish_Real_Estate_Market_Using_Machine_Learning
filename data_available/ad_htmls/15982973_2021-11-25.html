    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Sitting on an elevated site and enjoying beautiful views over the surrounding countryside, this superb and well presented 4 bedroom detached residence with spacious accommodation is a short drive from Cootehill town and is located in an exclusive development of only 14 detached residences. The property has a tarmac driveway, landscaped garden to the front and rear with a garden shed.\nCootehill is centrally located between Cavan and Monaghan and is convenient for commuters to travel to the larger towns and cities. Cootehill was formally established in 1725 and and has several fine architectural buildings and the surrounding lakes, rivers and forest provides a beautiful scenic backdrop. There are two national schools: The Darley and St. Michael's, the local secondary school St. Aidans Comprehensive School serves the area with a good practical and academic education and The Holy Family School caters to the students with special needs. There is a medical centre, churches and a good selection of shops, restaurants, pubs and hotels. There are a variety of sporting activities in the town and surrounding areas such as football, soccer, fishing, water sports, education adventure centre, cycling and horse riding.
