    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    DNG are proud to bring to the market Number 17a McAuley Drive. Meticulously designed by the current owner, this beautiful two-bedroom terraced family home is ideally positioned upon a quiet row of homes in a mature and quite residential area. Behind the red brick and fully insulated fa\xc3\xa7ade lies an exceptionally well lit home. It is evident throughout that the current owner has take great care in the design of this home with the accommodation briefly comprising of spacious living area with feature fireplace at the heart of the space, modern fully fitted kitchen with an abundance of natural light from the dual over head velux windows, under stairs bathroom, whilst upstairs is the bright and airy fully tiled family bathroom and the first of two good sized bedrooms with quality built in wardrobes. There is gas fired radiator central heating and the windows are upvc double glazed throughout. The front of the property is both paved with special allowance to provide for off street parking for one car. The property is ideally nestled adjacent to a green area whilst the rear garden is secure and private in nature. The location of this property is second to none, with Artane Castle Shopping Centre within a short stroll and local schools such as St John of Gods and St David's in close proximity. For those who enjoy the outdoors, St Anne's Park is within walking distance as well as McAuley Park, while Clontarf Seafront is a short drive away. Harmonstown DART station is also within walking distance and there are several bus routes serving the area.
