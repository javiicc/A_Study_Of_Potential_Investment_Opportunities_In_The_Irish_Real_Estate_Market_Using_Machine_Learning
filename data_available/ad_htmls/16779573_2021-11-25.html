    Ackareen House is an attractive country house situated on a 0.88 acre site with Stunning Views of Tralee Bay, Barrow Harbour and surrounding countryside.\n\nThe house has been well maintained but is in need of refurbishment and is located close to the world famous Tralee Golf Club, Barrow Beach, and is within a short drive of both Tralee Town and Ardfert Village. \n\n\nGround Floor\n\nEntrance Hallway 5.78m x 3.02m.\n\nLiving room 1 6.94m x 4.11m.\n\nLiving room 2 5.79m x 4.10m.\n\nKitchen / Dining 11.5m x 4.26m.\n\nUtility 2.04m x 3.36m.\n\nWC 0.99m x 1.71m.\n\nFirst Floor\n\nBedroom 1 4.13m x 4.37m.\n\nBedroom 2 3.04m x 3.5m.\n\nMaster Bedroom 5.76m x 4.12m.\n\nMaster Bathroom 3.2m x 3.05m.\n\nBedroom 4 4.6m x 3.07m.\n\nEnsuite 2.12m x 1.1m.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nLeaving Tralee take the Fenit Rd,\ncontinue for approx 2 miles at the Junction take the Churchill Rd, and continue towards Barrow Golf club, pass over the hump back bridge and take the next RHT, the property is on the right hand side with a directional sign on it.
