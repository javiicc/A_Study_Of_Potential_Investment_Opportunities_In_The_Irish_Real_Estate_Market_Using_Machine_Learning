    CONERNEY ESTATE AGENTS are delighted to present to market this fabulous residence in the heart of the beautiful village of Roundwood. This beautiful property boasts the peaceful and tranquil living conditions associated with some of the most picturesque countryside in Ireland, while also offering every amenity on your doorstep.\r\nThis property is oozing charm and character with one off unique features throughout. Inside the property is simply fabulous, with a number of upgrades and renovations. Lovely features such as double glazed windows throughout, feature stove in the sitting room with solid hardwood floors, integrated country style kitchen with Belfast sink and dual double doors in the kitchen leading to newly decked wooden patio and then onto a long south-east facing garden to the rear that catches the sun throughout the day. There is also a large utility conveniently just off the kitchen. The sitting room is large yet homely with quaint dual facing windows and solid fuel feature stove. There is a guest toilet just inside the front door and the main bathroom upstairs is fully fitted with bath and shower. \r\nUpstairs there are three large double bedrooms, all fitted with built in wardrobes and the master is en-suite and stretches the full width of the property. The master also looks over the rear garden via double doors leading onto a small balcony. \r\nThere is also off-street parking to the rear for two cars assigned with the property.\r\nAll in all, this is a fabulous property. Extremely well fitted with a wonderful country feel. Set in a beautiful and tranquil part of Wicklow with private garden and lots of natural light, this property really needs to be viewed to be fully appreciated. \r\nFor an immediate viewing, please apply online, leave your name and number and we will get right back to you. \r\nVIEWING HIGHLY RECOMMENDED!!
