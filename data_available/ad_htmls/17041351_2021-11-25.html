    DNG Martin O'Connor Estate Agents are delighted present this excellent opportunity to acquire a beautiful 4 bedroom family home, presented in walk-in condition in the popular area of Ballynahallia, Moycullen. \r\nThe beautiful home sits on a perfect south facing site among mature trees and gardens which creates an atmosphere of peace and calm as a back drop to the family residence. The house itself was renovated from a cottage in the early 2000's to include upgrades to insulation & heating system and now enjoys spacious modern living accommodations throughout. \r\nAn entrance porch leads into a very warm & welcoming living room area with a feature stove fireplace, this room is the heart of the home with access to all areas from here. To the left is the kitchen/dining area, which has been finished to a high standard with ample fitted units and centre island unit with breakfast bar. The dining area, has a south facing aspect where patio doors allow the natural light to flood through this space. Off the kitchen is a good size utility room with access to the rear garden, as well as a downstairs shower room. A corridor also leads from here to a home office where a corner window illuminates the room to make the perfect work or study space. This if required could easily be converted into a fifth bedroom or playroom.\r\nThe first floor accommodation is accessible from the living room also and comprises of 4 good sized bedrooms, all of which have solid wooden floors. One bedroom is located at the gable end of the property, enjoying a south facing aspect with the remaining bedrooms including the master bedroom located to the rear of the property. The spacious family bathroom and hot press are also located on this floor to the front of the property. The exterior of the property is a real joy and adds so much to this family house, a lovely stone barn sits at the rear with its old world character having excellent potential for renovation. The garden extends to natural boundaries of mature trees and shrubs and gives full privacy to this magnificent space. A tarmacadam driveway leads to the property, where another smaller garden area sits to the east of the property. The property is conveniently located only 3.3 km from Moycullen village and short commute to the city and to all local amenities including shops, supermarkets, cafes & restaurants, national school and children's playground. The area of Ballynahallia has always been considered to be among one of the more prestigious addresses in Moycullen and benefits from being within a short walking distance to the local GAA pitches and state of the art handball alley. There are also beautiful scenic walking routes nearby with Lough Corrib just a short distance away. To arrange a viewing of this beautiful property, contact DNG Martin O'Connor on 091 866708.
