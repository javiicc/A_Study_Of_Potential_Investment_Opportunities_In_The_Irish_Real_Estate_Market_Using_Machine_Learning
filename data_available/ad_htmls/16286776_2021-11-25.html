    Tucked away behind electric gates with secure off street car parking, Padua is an exceptional detached double fronted residence, standing on 0.2 acre which has undergone a thorough refurbishment programme under the design and supervision of Cantrell and Crowley Architects in c. 2006. The property has been tastefully extended to provide a wonderful balance of impressive living and bedroom accommodation extending to approximately 282sqm/3,041sqft. During the refurbishment, the ground floor was dropped resulting in higher ceilings and the doors are taller.\n\nThe spacious and bright ground floor accommodation features attractive floor tiling with underfloor heating and comprises of entrance hallway, living room, dining room, family room, sunroom, and a large family friendly kitchen / breakfast room with utility room off. A guest wc completes the accommodation at this level. On the first floor, there are four bedrooms off the spacious landing, all with ensuites.
