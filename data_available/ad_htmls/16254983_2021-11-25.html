    O'Connell Properties are delighted to bring "Radcliffe Cottage", Millview Road, Malahide to the market. A well-presented, 3 bedroom, double fronted semi-detached cottage with front garden and very large back garden. The property is over 100 years old, oozes character and charm and was originally built as a property for workers in the nearby Malahide Castle. There is considerable potential to extend or redevelop subject to planning permission. The entire site is c.1/5 of an acre.\r\n\r\nAccommodation is all on one level and comprises an entrance hall, living room, large and extended open plan kitchen/dining room, 3 double bedrooms and bathroom. The property is presented in good condition throughout but would benefit from redecoration and modernisation. There is considerable space at the back of the house to extend STPP.\r\n\r\nLocated in one of the most desirable and sought-after Villages in Dublin and walking distance to Malahide Village, Beaches and Malahide Castle. There are several excellent local golf courses, beautiful beaches and numerous local shops, cafes and restaurants. An excellent choice of local national and secondary schools and walking distance to both train and bus services.\r\n\r\nNeedless to say that we advise early viewing of this "rare gem" of a property.
