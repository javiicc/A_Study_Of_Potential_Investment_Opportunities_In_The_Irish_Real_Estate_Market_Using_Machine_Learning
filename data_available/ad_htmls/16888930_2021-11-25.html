    DNG Martin OConnor are delighted to present to market this traditional two storey farm house in an enviable seaside location at Callowfinish, Carna, Co. Galway on the Wild Atlantic Way. The property with horse stables, on a large site of c. 2.35 acres (0.95 Ha) is conveniently located a few minutes walk from two of Ireland's best undiscovered sandy beaches 'An Tra Mor', known locally as Callowfinish beach and a coral beach beach known as 'An Tra Bui'. From Callowfinish beach, Finish Island is also accessible by foot with tidal times.\n\nThe property, extending to c.1,486 square foot, in need of renovation, offers excellent potential and provides the prospective purchaser with the opportunity to create their dream home in the heart of Connemara, or would also make a delightful holiday home in the picturesque area where all the family needs can be catered for, with its holding of land and stables one can even bring the pony on holidays.\n\nThe property's accommodations consist of an entrance hallway, living room, dining/living area, kitchen to the rear, bathroom / utility, ground floor bedroom with W.C., 4 bedrooms upstairs enjoying beautiful hill and distant seaside views. The property enjoys views of Inis Mor (Aran Island) in the distance from the upstairs accommodation. To the side of the house there is a small storage shed.\n\nOn a large plot of land with good road frontage the property has a separate entrance which leads to modern top quality double horse barns which were constructed in recent times. They come complete with sliding doors, water drinker & grain holder. Next to the barn the foundations, for a larger sized shed have been completed.\n\nConveniently located between the villages of Carna & Kilkieran with excellent amenities including schools (national & second level) shops, pubs, restaurants, & pharmacy with the bus to Galway City passing right outside the door of the property.\n\nRecreational and sports facilities in the region include angling, pony trekking, walk trails, traditional music sessions. Golf is available at the many Golf Courses throughout the region.\n\nCarna a traditional Gaelic speaking area is a picturesque region with breath taking scenery, wonderful coastline and its many beaches which makes it a terrific holiday destination in which to unwind and explore Connemara.\n\nTo arrange an inspection of this very special property please contact DNG Martin OConnor Estate Agents on (091) 866708.\n\nRooms\nHallway -\n\n\nLiving Room -\nWith open fireplace.\n\nDining/Living -\nWith Range.\n\nKitchen -\nPositioned to the rear of the property.\n\nBathroom/ Utility -\n\n\nBedroom 1 -\nPositioned to the rear of the property.\n\nW.C. -\n\n\nBedroom 2 -\nWith hill views.\n\nBedroom 3 -\nWith sea views in the distance.\n\nBedroom 4 -\nWith hill views.
