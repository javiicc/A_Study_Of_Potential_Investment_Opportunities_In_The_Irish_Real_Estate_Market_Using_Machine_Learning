    Positioned on this popular tree lined road No.128 Lower Drumcondra Road is a beautiful period property set on this elegant terrace of large red brick houses. This property has been extensively refurbished, extended and decorated by the current owners with considerable style. Great care has been taken to retain original features which are complemented by the modern extension to the rear. This fine home extends to 174 Sq M and benefits from landscaped gardens to the front and rear. The side garden provides off street parking. \r\n\r\nThis fine home is entered via an electric pedestrian gate from Drumcondra Road. The front garden sets the tone for what lies behind the front door, an exquisite period home. The internal accommodation comprises a very large hallway with original detailed coving, varnished wooden floor boards, under stairs storage, storage press and guest WC. The dining room lies to the front of the property featuring an original marble fireplace, coving and centre rose. The large bay windows provides stunning views of the front garden. Double doors lead to the well serviced kitchen. To the rear of the property is a spacious, well lit, modern living room and an office or play room. On the first return lies bedroom 2 with en suite, on the first floor lies the elegant main bedroom and bedroom 3. On the second return lies the 4th double bedroom along with shower room. \r\n\r\nLower Drumcondra Road has long been regarded as one of the most prestigious locations in Drumcondra, convenient to all the amenities of the village and within a gentle stroll of Drumcondra Train Station and the city centre. There are many amenities such as Griffith Park, the Botanic Gardens and Na Fianna GAA club close by.
