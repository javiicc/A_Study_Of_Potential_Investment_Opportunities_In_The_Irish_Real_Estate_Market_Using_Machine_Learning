    • Sale Type: For Sale by Public Auction
    REA Dooley Group bring to market this impressive two storey residence built in the late 18th Century. Property is approached by a long sweeping tree lined avenue and is set on approx 14 acres of a mix of good grazing land and mature woodland. The river Douglasha runs though the woodland area, there is also a water well that can be connected on site if desired, mains connection was fitted last year and there are several outbuildings. Accommodation comprises of: entrance hall, sitting room, living room, kitchen/dining area, 4 bedrooms, reception, ladies & gents wc.\n\nLocated within 2.5km from Glenstal Abbey, 3km from Murroe Village, 3.5km from Cappamore and 15km from Limerick City\n\nThe Villages of Murroe and Cappamore have several pubs, grocery shops, a hardware store, GP surgery, pharmacy and veterinary clinic. Other services include a library and arts studios complex, a church, community centre and day care centre. The village also has a fire brigade serving the surrounding area. Cappamore hosts an annual Agricultural Show in August each year.
