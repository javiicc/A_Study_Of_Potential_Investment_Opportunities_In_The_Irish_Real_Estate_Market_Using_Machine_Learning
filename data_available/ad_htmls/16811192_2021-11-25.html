    Located in this very popular and much sought-after quiet development just off Monkstown Road, stands this spacious two-bedroomed, end-of-terrace townhouse which offers bright and airy accommodation just a short stroll from the heart of Monkstown Village.\nThis delightful home is bright and well laid out, extending to approximately 62sq.m. (677.83sq.ft.) The accommodation briefly comprises of an entrance hall which leads into the kitchen and a spacious living room, which hosts a large open fireplace. The living room opens out to the west facing rear garden. Upstairs there are two double bedrooms and a large bathroom. There is access to an attic, perfect for storage.\nThe private garden with a west orientation is a real oasis of calm from the hustle and bustle of everyday life, and it is the perfect space for entertaining friends, family and dining al fresco. The garden, which is predominantly grass and gravel, is perfect for having a barbecue and dining in the long summer evenings. A side passage leads to the front which has one designated car space.\nThe location is second to none, boasting all the amenities Monkstown has to offer within close proximity including one of the most popular swimming spots in Ireland at Seapoint, just an eight-minute walk away. Positioned centrally between the town centres of Blackrock and Dun Laoghaire (both only a 15-minute walk), the house has access to a vast array of shops, bars, restaurants and leisure facilities. There are several highly regarded schools close by including CBC Monkstown, Blackrock College, Newpark, Scoil Lorc in and Carysfort National school to name but a few. Nearby transport links include the DART at Salthill and Monkstown as well as several Dublin bus routes which provide easy access to the city centre and beyond. All in all, 1 Southdene provides an excellent opportunity to acquire a lovely home in an excellent location. Viewing is highly recommended.\n\n\nKitchen 3.67m x 2.58m.
