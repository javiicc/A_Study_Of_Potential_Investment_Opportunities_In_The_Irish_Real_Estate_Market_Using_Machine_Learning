    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    Welcome to 'The Cottage Greenwood' a truly impressive detached house set on a private site just off Baskin Lane. The house which has been upgraded and extended over the years is ideally suited for modern family living with large open plan living areas. Upon arrival viewers will be instantly impressed by the large gravel driveway which is set behind private electric gates. The house is presented in excellent condition throughout and comprises of porch entrance, reception hallway complete with built in bookshelves and semi-solid oak flooring. There is a large open plan kitchen / living / dining area, spacious lounge with wood burning stove, 3 bedrooms all with en-suite and a guest w.c and utility room completes the ground floor accommodation. The attic was converted at first floor level and now comprises of two further rooms both with ensuite. Outside there are mature landscaped gardens with lawn and patio area along with a sun room which benefits from sliding double glazed doors. Further features include a B1 energy rating, solar panels for hot water, double glazed windows and mains sewage connection. Perfectly located just minutes from Dublin international airport, Malahide village with its abundance of boutiques, cafes and restaurants, Swords Pavilions shopping centre and a host of bus links from the Malahide road. Viewing is a must to appreciate this fine family home.
