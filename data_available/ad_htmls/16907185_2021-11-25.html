It is just a stones throw to the heart of the bustling village of Claregalway along with all of the connectivity which that provides.\r\nNo 27 is a stunning contemporary home. Everything about this house has been thoughtfully considered and brilliantly executed.\r\nThe property has been lovingly maintained by its current owner and no expense has been spared on finishes and d\xc3\xa9cor. There is premium standard flooring throughout- high end wooden floors and tiling downstairs along with brand new neutral toned carpet on the stairs, landing and bedrooms.\r\nThe kitchen is modern and contemporary and features on trend Brazilian Birch finish. The living area is beautifully appointed with designer marble fire place and is flooded with natural light. The master bedroom is large and stylish and overlooks acres of open countryside which affords uninterrupted views of nature. Both the accompanying ensuite and the family bathroom are much larger than might be expected and are finished to match the overall theme of modernity.\r\nThere is another double bedroom and a single bedroom which comes complete with a brilliantly designed built in storage area. There is also a large attic space which is partially floored and has a fitted attic stairs and Velux windows. This is the ideal addition for all those who can work from home.
