    Portarra Lodge, Moycullen, Co. Galway

    Detached

    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    The Bed & Breakfast Lodge at Moycullen in Galway is situated on the shore of the Lough Corrib and was specially constructed with superb en-suite accommodation and suitably adapted as an Angling Centre. The property has been very well maintained and benefits from excellent Accommodation with timber flooring throughout and open log/turf fires in the Lounge & Reception areas.\n\nThis superb property benefits from 12-bedrooms, large commercial kitchen, lounge area and open reception. The property itself sits on 15 acres with direct access to the well known Lough Corrib. The property also benefits from a boating dock and an additional 2 potential sites that are subject to planning permission. \n\nThis property is perfect for any outdoor enthusiast as there are 10 walking routes throughout the Moycullen area with many of them being close to the lodge. There is also a host of other outdoor activities nearby such as cycling tracks, golf courses, horse riding, boating and many other outdoor recreational activity spots. This property benefits from it all. \n\nWe expect good level's of interest in this property and viewing is by appointment only...\n\n\n\n\nPlease find below links to a video walk-through of the property and a 3D Virtual tour. \n\n3D Tour: - https://view.ricohtours.com/c6aa4c14-c928-464c-931c-461aaa1bb6c1\n\nVideo Walk-through: - https://youtu.be/vxswuVxdRdw\n\n\n Property Reference :1181-1841
