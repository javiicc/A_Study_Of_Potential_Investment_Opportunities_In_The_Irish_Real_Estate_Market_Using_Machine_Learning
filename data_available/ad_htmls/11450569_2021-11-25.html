At large 2 storey house built close to road turn left and continue for 2.6km. Site on left.\r\n\r\nFrom the church in Doonbeg proceed in direction of Kilkee for 3.5km's. You will see a recently built 3 storey house on your right at a crossroads. At this cross roads turn right and continue on this road for just over a kilometer to Baltard National school. At the school keep straight for 1.5km's. You will a tiny little cottage on your left and the field is next to this. The Eircode V15 K280 will take you the cottage beside the field.
