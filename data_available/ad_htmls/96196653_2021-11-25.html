      CARRIGALINES NEWEST AND EXCLUSIVE QUALITY HIGH SPEC A RATED 3 & 4 BEDROOM HOMES.\r\nEXCELLENT VARIATION OF SEVEN HOUSE TYPES ON OFFER RANGING FROM 108 SQ. M.- 144 SQ.M WITH THE FIRST PHASE OF HOUSES TO BE RELEASED TO THE MARKET IN SEPTEMBER.\r\n\r\nDennehy Auctioneers in conjunction with Dwellings are delighted to offer opportunity to purchase a 3 or 4 bedroom home of quality construction made for modern living in exclusive development of only 50 houses in the convenient and picturesque town of Carrigaline. \r\n\r\nCarrigaline is ideally located just 15 minutes from both Cork city centre and Cork Airport. Crosshaven with all its beaches and marine leisure activities is only 10 minutes away by car or can be accessed by a stunning walk/jog/cycle along Owenabue River Walk if you so choose.\r\n\r\nAmenities:\r\nCarrigaline has an abundance of amenities. There are numerous preschools, creches, primary and secondary schools in the vicinity. There is also an excellent range of sporting clubs and activities available locally.\r\n\r\nFEATURES:\r\n\r\nBER RATING\r\nA2 N.Z.E.B. BER Rated.\r\nAir to water heat pump system with underfloor heating to ground floor and radiators to the upper floors.\r\n \r\nEXTERNAL FINISHES\r\nFinish to the front elevation is brick and Napp plaster. \r\nReconstituted granite sills to front elevations. \r\nA black flat tile finish to the roof.\r\nuPVC fascia, soffit and guttering.\r\n\r\nDOORS & WINDOWS\r\nuPVC double glazed windows and doors with cream exterior and white interior. \r\n\r\nINTERNAL FINISHES\r\nHigh quality insulated timber frame.\r\nSpacious bright living areas.\r\nHigh level ceilings to ground and first floor. \r\nSolid timber staircase with hardwood handrail.\r\nQuality interior joinery to include oak internal doors and contemporary skirting and architraves.\r\n\r\nKITCHEN/UTILITY\r\nTwo tone fitted kitchens. \r\nSeparate utility area. \r\n\r\nBATHROOM/ENSUITE\r\nAll bathrooms and ensuite rooms fitted with high-quality sanitaryware.\r\nChrome towel rails in main bathroom and ensuite.\r\nMixer handset fitted in main bathroom.\r\nShower doors fitted in ensuite.\r\nPump showers in Master ensuite.\r\nElectric Shower in Bedroom 1 ensuite.\r\nAttractively chosen tiles on all bathroom floors and wet areas.\r\n\r\nELECTRICAL\r\nElectrical specification as per standard unit. \r\nHouse wired for intruder alarm system. \r\n\r\nGARDENS\r\nHigh quality paving to the front driveways. \r\nGardens landscape levelled and seeded. \r\nOutdoor tap fitted to rear gardens. \r\n\r\nGUARANTEES\r\n10-year Global Homes Warranty Structural Guarantee. \r\nLatent Defects Insurance and Mechanical & Electrical Inherent Defects Insurance.\r\nApproved for the Help to Buy Scheme.
