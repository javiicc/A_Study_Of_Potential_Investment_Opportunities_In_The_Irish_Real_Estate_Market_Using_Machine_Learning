    Quillsen are delighted to present this very stylish and modern 2 bed apartment to the market. Built in 2006, Beechwood court is a highly exclusive and meticulously maintained development just off the Stillorgan road. Boasting amongst its many appealing features a master ensuite and west facing balcony providing an abundance of natural light, this property really is modern living at its best.\n\nMeasuring a spacious 65 sqm / 700 sqft , the accommodation is well laid out and comprises an entrance hall, open plan kitchen/dining area with a large sunny west facing balcony off, two bedrooms (master ensuite) with fitted wardrobes and a family bathroom. It has a car parking space and ample visitor parking within the development. \n\nSituated just off the Stillorgan road the location could not be more convenient, with Blackrock and Stillorgan villages and their abundance of boutiques, cafes, bars and restaurants on your doorstep. The Sandyford Luas stop is a mere 15 minutes walk providing direct access to the city centre.
