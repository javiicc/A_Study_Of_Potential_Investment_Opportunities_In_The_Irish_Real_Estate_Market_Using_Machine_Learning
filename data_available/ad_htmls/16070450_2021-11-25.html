    Dalton Auctioneers are presenting this well maintained and newly decorated 4 bedroom detached bungalow of approx. 1250 sq ft located at Moneylagan, Co Longford which is just off N4 Dublin/Sligo route. This property boasts space and comfort and offers all the ingredients for a family living. This property is in a beautiful country setting approx 1km from Longford town and is finished to an excellent standard.\r\n \r\nAccommodation comprises of a bright entrance hall which is tiled and has a coat closet. Off the hallway is a large light-filled living room with a solid fuel fireplace, semi-solid wood floor, and two large windows. There is a modern kitchen/dining room which has a semi-solid wood floor and door leading to a private back garden. The dining area has a solid fuel fireplace. There are 4 double bedrooms with built-ins. Two bedrooms are carpeted and two bedrooms have semi-solid wood floors. The main bathroom is fully tiled with an electric shower.\r\n\r\nOutside there is a large detached garage and a circular tarmac driveway. The house is serviced by mains water & septic tank.\r\n\r\nThis property is within walking distance of Longford Leisure Center, Soccer, Rugby, and GAA sports facilities, filling stations, and the N4 Axis Centre which has many outlets ie. Argos, Homestore & More, Esquires & Mc Donalds to name a few. This house is in mint condition and ideally situated within a leisurely stroll of the town and would make an ideal family home BER: D1
