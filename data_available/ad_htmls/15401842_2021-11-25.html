    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Superb four bedroom detached bungalow c. 160 sqm standing on c. 1.16 acres in a lovely location adjacent to the Copper Coast. \nBuilt approx. 20 years ago to the highest standard of design and finish, Caltra House is an impressive family home with generous light filled and tastefully decorated accommodation appropriate to the needs of modern day living.\nStanding well in from the road, the property enjoys picturesque views of the Comeragh Mountains, the sea and surrounding landscape.\nCaltra House is approached by a tarmacadam driveway which is bounded by shrub and flower beds, rockeries, trees with well attended lawns. There is a detached garage/workshop which would be suitable for conversion to residential accommodation or a home office. \nTo the rear of the property is a raised patio area and an enclosed paddock which is ideally suited for horticultural use.\n\nThe property is located 1.5km from Bunmahon Village and c. 23km from Waterford City. \nLocal amenities include Bunmahon Beach, The Engine House Restaurant, The Copper Coast Geopark and Bunmahon GAA\nThe property is within a short drive of Dungarvan, Kilmacthomas, The Greenway and the National Road Network.\n \n\n\n\nAccommodation\nEntrance Porch\n2.20m x 1.80m Tiled door. uPVC front door.\nEntrance Hall\n4.30m x 2.40m\nSitting Room\n4.80m x 4.60m Fireplace with antique reproduction overmantle and inset tiled hearth.
