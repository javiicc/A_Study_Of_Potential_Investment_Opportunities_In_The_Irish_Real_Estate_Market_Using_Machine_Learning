    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    REA Forkin are delighted to offer for sale this spacious and nicely laid out 2 bedroom apartment (c.70sqm) . The Mill dates back to the 1930s however in the 1990s the building was extensively extended and modernised with offices on the ground floor and residential above. This area is one of the most convenient residential locations to Bray Town Centre. merely 2 minutes walk away. The Maltings development is superbly laid out with green areas, mature trees and a series of short cul de sacs comprising mainly two and three bedroom homes. The standard of renovation carried out at The Mill was extremely high and the standard of finish and design of the apartments was excellent. The beamed ceiling in the main area is a really attractive feature and highlights the high ceiling level.\n\nThe light and space that No.8 offers is amazing and will offer a great home for a wide range of purchasers. The living area/dining area and kitchen is a superb room overlooking the communal gardens and the balcony opens off the reception area. The two bedrooms are nicely proportioned as is the bathroom. A mezzanine (c20sqm) accessed via solid stairs from the reception room offers further potential.\n\nThe vast amenities of Bray Town are within easy reach with a variety of shops, retail units, supermarkets , library, the seafront for long leisurely strolls, a myriad of walks to suit all \n\n\n\nAccommodation\nEntrance Hall\n5.26m x 1.27m\nKitchen/Dining/Living\n6.78m x 4.48m\nBedroom 1\n3.32m x 2.40m\nBedroom 2\n3.13m x 3.33m\nBathroom\n2.07m x 1.79m\nMezzanine\n6.34m x 3.48m\nDIRECTIONS:\nTravelling south into Bray Town the entrance to The Malting is on the right after the bridge .\nContinue towards the Maltings and The Mill is on the left
