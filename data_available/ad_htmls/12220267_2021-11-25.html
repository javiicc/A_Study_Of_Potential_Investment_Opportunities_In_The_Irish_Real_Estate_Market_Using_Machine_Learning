    A very impressive six bedroom detached home in this very popular residential location in Bunratty that offers easy access to the M18 to Shannon Airport, Limerick, Ennis and Galway. This unique house has a two bedroom self contained apartment/granny flat which can also easily be used as part of the family accommodation. The well laid out accommodation offers plenty room for a growing family and the local National School is only a few minutes drive. The house sits on a lovely site with a sunny south west facing garden offering generous space for children and/or pets. The house has many attractive features including the very attractive spiral stair case. It is very convenient for all of the amenities on offer in Bunratty and it would make an ideal family home.\r\n\r\n6 Bedrooms, 4 Reception Rooms, 7 Bathrooms, House, Detached, Garden, Private Parking, Rural, Village, Central Heating, S or W facing Garden, Good decoration, 286.0 Approx Sq Metres
