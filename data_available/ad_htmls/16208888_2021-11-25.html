    We are delighted to bring to market this fabulous property, that has been totally refurbished and extended by the current owner, transforming it into a spacious, bright, large family home.\r\n\r\nThe property is located just 20 minutes from Kilkenny City Centre and Carlow Town Centre, 15 minutes from the M9 and just 5 minutes to Bagenalstown and same to Borris. Ballinkillen Primary School is less than 2km's from the property.\r\n\r\nThe property sits on c.1 acre, just off the Bagenalstown / Borris Road, on entering through the electric gates, there is a double garage to the side of the house, that can also be accessed from within the house. On entering, there is an open plan living room with double sided wood burning stove and 2 large windows with bi-folding doors that look out onto a meadow garden, decking area and a natural pond. The kitchen is large, with fitted units and an island with built-in oven and hob, again with bi-fold doors out to the patio. The utility room is just off the kitchen and it is there that you can access the garage.\r\nThere is a second large sitting room with picture window and a wood burning room heater stove.\r\n\r\nJust off the entrance hall there is a guest W.C. and coat room. Also to the front of the house there is a large room, with it's own entrance from the front of the house, at the end of the room is a full bathroom. Currently this room is used as a formal dining room, but could also lend itself to other uses, such as accommodation for an elderly person, a work from home office etc. \r\n\r\nUpstairs there are 4 double bedrooms, 2 of which are ensuite and have walk-in closets, the other 2 double bedrooms have a "Jack and Jill" bathroom between them.\r\n\r\nThis property has been lovingly restored and extended and has been fitted out with high end fixtures and fittings.
