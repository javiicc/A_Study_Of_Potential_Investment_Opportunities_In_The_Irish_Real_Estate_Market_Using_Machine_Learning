    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Sherry FitzGerald Reilly are pleased to bring to the market a charming 2 bedroom end terrace cottage located in the heart of Robertstown village. This attractive home consists of living room, kitchen, 2 bedrooms and a bathroom with features including oil fired central heating, pvc double glazed windows and a tarmac yard with off street parking.\nRobertstown is an idyllic, canal side village where the country atmosphere is virtually unspoilt, yet it still offers an array of excellent local services and is convenient to towns such as Naas, Kildare and Newbridge where all amenities including large retail outlets are available. Dublin city is easily accessible via the M4 and M7 motorways. \nViewing is strongly recommended.\n\n\nLounge 3.5 x 3.4. With feature fireplace, vinyl flooring, coved ceiling, telephone point, tv point.\n\nKitchen/Dining Room 4.3 x 3.7. With fitted kitchen, electric oven, hob, extractor, fridge/freezer, washing machiene, tiled floor, tiled splashback, coved ceiling, airing cupboards.\n\nBedroom One 3.4 x 2.9. To front with fitted wardrobes, laminate flooring.\n\nBedroom Two 3.8 x 3.8. To side with fitted wardrobes, laminate flooring.\n\nBathroom 3.9 x 1.3. With bath, wc, whb, tiled floor, tiled splashback, shaving light, mirror.\n\nCorridor 1.5 x 1.1. With tiled floor and front door access.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom Clane proceed to Prosperous village, turn left just before Naas Oil petrol station, continue to Downings cross, proceed straight on, cross over a canal bridge, continue to Mylerstown cross, turn right, continue into Robertstown, turn left after the post office and Mullaneys pub, continue for approximately 80 metres, Grove Lane will be on your right.\n\nGPS: 53.269296, -6.816923
