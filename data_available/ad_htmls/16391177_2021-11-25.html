    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to present this fine four-bedroomed semi-detached family home located on this premier road in the heart of Mount Merrion. This lovely red-brick home will benefit from a full refurbishment but has the potential to be a superb forever family home. The property boasts some original features from the era which add to the character and charm of this lovely home. It has a beautiful 140-foot long south-east facing private rear garden which all the family can enjoy. \n \nAs one enters, the feeling of light prevails with a large welcoming hallway giving access to the main living areas to include two reception rooms, both with original tiled fireplaces, the living room has a lovely box window overlooking the front garden. There is a large family room extension to the rear from which you can access the kitchen and utility room, there are two sets of double doors giving access to the rear garden Three large roof lights flood this room with natural light, making it a lovely room to enjoy the garden or just relax. There is a large cloakroom which is accessed from the hallway. Upstairs there are four bedrooms, three good double bedrooms and a fourth single bedroom. The main double bedroom has a good range of built-in wardrobes. There is a family bathroom which completes the accommodation. \n\nThe large south-east facing private rear garden is a real asset. Completely surrounded by mature trees, this garden offers total seclusion and privacy and with a bit of TLC this garden could be a haven for children to play, outside dining, gardening and simply relaxing and enjoying the sunny aspect. \n\nLocal shops on The Rise, which are a focal point for Mount Merrion are within easy walking distance, as is the vibrant community centre, churches, a wealth of sporting facilities, UCD University Campus at Belfield is literally on the doorstep and an excellent selection of some of the country s best known schools in south Dublin are close by. Situated beside the Stillorgan Quality Bus Corridor and Aircoach, direct service to the airport and the N11, the M50 is easily accessible, providing ease of access to all points north, south, east and west.
