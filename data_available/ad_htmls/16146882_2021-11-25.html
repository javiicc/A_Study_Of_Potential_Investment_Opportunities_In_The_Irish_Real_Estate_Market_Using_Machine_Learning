    BARRY AUCTIONEERS & VALUERS are delighted to bring to market this large unusual charming 3 bedroom semi-detached home. The house extends to c.126sq m and comprises of a lounge, family room, kitchen/dining room, two double bedrooms, a single bedroom and a main bathroom. The house overlooks a large green area with uninterrupted views of the River Lee and Lough Mahon. The Mahon Point shopping Centre is only a short walk away where all the necessary amenities are available. ACCOMMODATION Kitchen / Dining Room - 5.5m x 2.6m Velux windows, sink unit, build in kitchen , tiled floor. Family Room / Conservatory area - 3.3m x 1.8m Open plan living room off kitchen. Sitting Room - 5.6m x 3.9m Bright living room with open fireplace with fitted solid fuel stove and timber flooring. Downstairs W/C - 2.0m x 1.0m Fully tiled 2 piece suite Bathroom - 2.8m x 2.2m Elegant 3 piece bathroom suite with tiled floor and walls. Power shower. Bedroom 1 - 6.8m x 3.9m Large Double bedroom with double doors to balcony. Carpeted. Bedroom 2 - 4.0m x 3.4m Double bedroom with en-suite toilet , electric shower , tiled walls and floor. Bedroom 3 - 3.9m x 3.1m Double bedroom with velux windows. Carpeted.
