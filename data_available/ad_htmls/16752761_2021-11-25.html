    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to present to the market Apt.111 Griffith Hall. This light filled and spacious dual-aspect, one bedroom ground floor apartment is ideally situated off Griffith Avenue within this popular gated Griffith Hall Development. The apartment has the added bonus of a large South-west facing rear balcony and a designated underground car parking space and ample visitor parking. \nThis tranquil development is surrounded by mature landscaped grounds and is the perfect opportunity for any discerning purchaser looking for a central, peaceful and secure apartment that benefits from a wide variety of amenities on its doorstep.\nInternally the accommodation is well presented, and the spacious feeling is enhanced by floor to ceiling windows which is a feature in the main living accommodation. Extending to 55sq m/ 596 sq ft the accommodation comprises of an entrance hall, bright and spacious living / dining room with sliding patio door opening onto the balcony, kitchen, double bedroom and bathroom. \nThe location is simply superb, Drumcondra village is within walking distance and this gives resident s ease of access to the City Centre with an efficient public transport system available. Reputable schools, DCU and St Patricks College provide good education opportunities and there are a number of sports and leisure facilities within close proximity. The M1, M50, Port Tunnel and Dublin Airport are only a short commute.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 2.85m x 2.66m. Wooden floors and intercom\n\nKitchen Range of floor and wall units, integrated oven, hob, stainless steel extractor fan, integrated fridge/freezer, stainless-steel sink, tiled floor and partly tiled walls\n\nLiving Room / Dining Area 6.23m x 4.32m. Bright and spacious room with floor to ceiling windows with views over the landscaped grounds, wooden floors and access to large south-west facing balcony\n\nBedroom 3.55m x 3.47m. Spacious double bedroom with built-in wardrobes and wooden floors\n\nBathroom 2.00m x 1.95m. Tiled floors, partly tiled walls, bath with shower attachment, W.C. and wash hand basin
