    SUMMARY\n\n17 The Walk in Woodpark, Ballinteer is a great opportunity to live in one of South Dublin\xe2\x82\xacTM established, family friendly areas with the added benefit of being in close proximity to a myriad of leisure, transport and educational options.\n\nACCOMODATION\n\nNo.17 extends to approx. 100 sq.m.(1,076 sq.ft.) and comes to the market with the added benefit of being recently repainted throughout. \n\nThe ground floor accommodation consists of a large light filled living room with an open fire, a connecting dining room and kitchen.\n\nDouble doors in the kitchen lead out to a generous back garden with a utility shed that is plumbed for a washing machine. Modern wooden fencing to the right and back with a block wall to the left provide privacy and security.\n\nUpstairs there are 3 generous bedrooms, a hot press and family bathroom. Both the master bedroom and second bedroom benefit from built in wardrobes with lots of storage. \n\nOutside, the property is well located in a quiet cul de sac with ample parking (on street and off street). The aspect is good in that it is not overlooked to the right of the street giving the property a real sense of space. The end of the cul de sac leads to a large green area, perfect for walking dogs and children's play. \n\nLOCATION\n\nWoodpark is located close to a wide range of schools and colleges including , Wesley College, St. Benildus, Alexandra College, Mount Anville and UCD to name a few. \n\nIn addition, the property is 17 minutes walk from the Dundrum shopping Centre and Balally Luas. Marley Park with all its amenities and the Dublin Mountains in Ticknock are also walking distance. There are multiple public transport options with Aircoach and a wide selection of buses including the 16, 19, 75. \n\nThe M50 Junction 13 is a 2 minute drive while the property is a short commute to the Sandyford business district. \n\n\nWoodpark is an established neighborhood with ample green space. This light filled house is waiting to become a fantastic family home. \n\nBER D1\n\nViewing is by private appointment
