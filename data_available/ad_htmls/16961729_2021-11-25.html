    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Remax Property Specialists are delighted to present No 65 Bishopsfield to the market. Set in the much sought after Williamstown area of Waterford City, Bishopsfield is a family-oriented development with great access to all amenities. \r\n\r\nUniversity Hospital Waterford and Tesco Ardkeen are within a short distance of the development, as well as having immediate access to the outer ring road. Ballygunner Primary School and Gael Scoil Port Lairge are a short distance from the property and Waterford City Centre is a 10 minute drive. \r\n\r\nThis bright spacious and well proportioned three bedroom home was constructed to a very high standard. It is presented in excellent condition.\r\n\r\nAccommodation on the ground floor, comprises Entrance Hall, Sitting Room with fitted solid fuel stove, Kitchen/ Diner with French doors to the rear garden. A Utility and Guest WC complete the ground floor.\r\n\r\nUpstairs there are three double Bedrooms with the master ensuite and a Family Bathroom. The property features a laundry chute from the landing to the utility.\r\n\r\nParking for two cars is available to the front with gated access at the side to the enclosed lawned rear garden. \r\n\r\n\r\nHeating is gas fired and the Building energy rating is C1\r\n\r\nViewing is highly recommended.
