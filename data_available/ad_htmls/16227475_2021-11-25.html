    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Mystical Rose is an "A" fronted dormer bungalow set on a mature 0.27 acre site on the edge of the Marion Village of Knock, Co. Mayo. Ease of access to the motorway linking Galway City and Sligo. Only 10 minute drive to Claremorris, Ballyhaunis or Ireland West Airport Knock.\r\n\r\nGround Floor Accommodation:\r\nEntrance Hallway staircase.\r\nLiving room 4.67m x 4.40m fireplace, carpet flooring, cornice, painted, curtains.\r\nDining room 6.18m x 3.27m fireplace, carpet flooring and access to patio area.\r\nBedroom/Office 3.3m x 1.8m.\r\nBedroom two double bedroom 3.95m x 3.11m, carpet, curtains, free standing wardrobe.\r\nMain Bathroom 3.27m x 1.78m fully tiled and fitted with separate shower, bath, toilet and wash-handbasin. \r\nKitchen/living room 4.3m x 3.29m integrated appliances, fitted kitchen, feature fireplace.\r\nUtility room off kitchen offering additional storage space and access to the rear garden and patio area.\r\nSunroom 5.67m x 3.81m feature fireplace and access to the patio area and rear garden.\r\n\r\nFirst Floor:\r\nMaster bedroom 5.25m x 3.68m with en-suite with access from two bedrooms.\r\nEn-suite is 2.05m x 3.35m large tiled and fitted with bathroom ware.\r\nBedroom four double bedroom 3.6m x 3.8m built in wardrobes, carpet flooring, curtains.\r\nBedroom five 3.53m x 4.65 oversized bedroom. Dormer window, carpet flooring, hand basin and fitted wardrobes and toilet in the cupboard.\r\n\r\n\r\nEnter through the side entrance into the L shaped hallway with solid wood staircase to the first floor. To your left is a very large open plan living room through to dining room. The living room is 4.67m X 4.40m in size with a feature fireplace, carpet flooring and cornice patterned ceiling. The living room is open on either side of the chimney breast and flows through to the dining area which has double stain glass doors to the outside paved patio area. The dining room 6.18m X 3.27m in size with a feature fireplace and carpet flooring. There is a small bedroom/office off the dining room 3.3m x 1.8m in size. Two original feature fireplaces sitting back to back in the living come dining area. Beautiful coving and cornice and pelmet curtains. There will be an option to purchase some of the furnishings there really do complete the look and era of this property.\r\n\r\nDown the hall is a large double bedroom with carpet, curtains and free standing wardrobe. This room is 3.95m X 3.11m. Across the hall is the main bathroom with separate bath and shower fully tiled. The bathroom is 3.27m x 1.78m. Located at the end of the hallway is a kitchen come living area separated by a large open arch, again feature fireplace, coving and cornace, solid dark wood kitchen with integrated appliances. Step down from the kitchen into a bright utility area with sliding doors to the back garden area and access to the side patio located off the living area hosts a huge sun catching sun room with timber vaulted ceiling and velux skylights. The kitchen is 4.3m x 3.29m and the sunroom is 5.67m x 3.81m. This room also has a feature fireplace making it a useable space all year round. Access from the patio to the rear garden and large timber patio area.\r\n\r\nLocated on the first floor you have the master bedroom 5.25m x 3.68m with en-suite that doubles up as a jack and jill for the rest of the upstairs bedrooms with access into the en-suite also from the landing. The Jack & Jill bathroom is 2.05m x 3.35m in size. Velux windows add light into the spacious landing. Second bedroom measuring 3.6m x 3.8m offers build in wardrobes, carpet floor, stained glass window and curtains. Bedroom three 3.53m x 4.65 is oversized and has dormer window as well as the windows in the gable end. There is a handbasin built into the wardrobe units and a surprise in the cupboard.... yes, a W.C. (see photographs). Due to the size of this property and the property location it would make an excellent B&B or Airbnb property or could be a beautiful family home. It is located across the road from the Basicila ground and rear entrance it is within walking distance of all the village of Knock has to offer. \r\n\r\nPlease take a look at our walk through video.
