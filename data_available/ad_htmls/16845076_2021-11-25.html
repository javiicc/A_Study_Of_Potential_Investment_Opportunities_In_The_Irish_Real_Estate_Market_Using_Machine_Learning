    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Positioned in a leafy cul de sac in the hugely sought-after Ballinakill Downs development, 31 The Pines is a 4/5 bed semi detached property in lovely condition and overlooks a green area to the front.\r\n \r\nBallinakill Downs is located on the Dunmore Road, in close proximity to a range of amenities such as Tesco Ardkeen, Ardkeen Stores, University Hospital Waterford and a number of Primary and Secondary Schools. \r\n\r\nWaterford City is a ten-minute drive from the property and the outer ring road is a five-minute drive, giving easy access to all major routes. \r\n\r\nThe property offers bright and spacious accommodation, comprising entrance hall, sitting room, dining room, Kitchen/Diner, Utility Room, Living Room/playroom/ bedroom 5 and wc on the ground floor. \r\n\r\nThe first floor holds four bedrooms, master with ensuite and family bathroom. There are lawned gardens to the front and rear with gated side access. The rear garden offers excellent privacy and is not overlooked. Spacious paved parking is available to the front, heating is gas fired and the energy rating is C1. \r\n\r\nThis is a simply superb location and viewing is highly recommended.
