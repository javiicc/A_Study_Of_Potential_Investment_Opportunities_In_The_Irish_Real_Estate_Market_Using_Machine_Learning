    Charming one bedroom double fronted cottage located in a quiet Cul De Sac close to the Canal in a sought after neighbourhood.\r\n \r\nThe house is nicely located in a quiet cul de sac. On entering there is a comfortable living space, featuring high spec laminate flooring a Velux window and ornate fireplace. The bedroom is a comfortable space that accommodates wardrobe space and is finished with laminate flooring. The bathroom is well finished with large format tiling and an electric shower.\r\n \r\nThe property benefits from an ideal location close to Harold's Cross Bridge. The canal provides a wonderful amenity and the city centre is within easy walking distance. In the immediate vicinity Harold's Cross is home to a wide variety of independent retailers, coffee shops and restaurants.
