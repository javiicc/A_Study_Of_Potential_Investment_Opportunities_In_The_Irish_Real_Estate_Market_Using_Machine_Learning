    Development Description

      Mariners Way, an attractive new development by Richmond Homes, is superbly positioned in the heart of Rush Village and only a 250 metre walk from the beautiful sandy beach at Rush.\r\n\r\nThe development offers a fine selection of 2, 3 and 4 bedroom semi-detached and terraced family homes set along tree lined avenues with landscaped greens and a children's play area. This high-quality development features a wide range of different house designs, providing layout choices to suit individual lifestyles. Each home comes with two car parking spaces. The homes are designed and constructed for contemporary living and they are ideal for first time buyers, downsizers and growing families seeking a central village location close to all local amenities. These A2-rated homes are designed to a very high specification to include stylish kitchens, contemporary bathrooms and en-suites, and elegant fitted wardrobes. The high build quality and generous specification reflect the traditional values of craftsmanship and are design worthy of this vibrant and highly sought after location. Richmond Homes are a registered developer under the Help to Buy scheme for first time buyers.\r\n\r\nThe seaside village of Rush, along with the nearby towns of Skerries, Swords and Malahide offer a wide variety of amenities, such as cafes, shops, restaurants, playgrounds, parks and beaches. The locality also offers a superb choice of sporting facilities from GAA, cricket, golf and horse-riding to windsurfing, kite-surfing and sailing. There are three primary schools and an excellent secondary school in the locality, and many secondary schools accessible by train. As well as its close proximity to Dublin City centre, Mariners Way also boasts easy access to the M1, M50, Port Tunnel and Dublin Airport with regular bus and rail services to the city. Rush is an ideal commuter town and very attractive to those seeking to enjoy all the benefits of coastal living.\r\n\r\nThis location makes for perfect family living.\r\n\r\nFor further details please contact Dermot Grimes or Louise Shannon at REA Grimes Skerries \r\non 01-8490129 or E-mail marinersway@reagrimes.ie \r\n\r\nPlease register your interest visit www.marinersway.ie\r\n\r\nNote:\r\nThis information, including any plans, images, descriptions, specifications or dimensions, or provided or communicated separately is indicative and intended to act as a guide only.
