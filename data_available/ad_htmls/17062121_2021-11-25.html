    This three bedroom semi-detached property, built in c. 1992 comes to the market on Stonebridge Road; a quiet and mature residential area boasting a highly convenient location adjoining the Ongar Distributor Road. One of the most appealing features of this house, other than its fantastic location is that the house is not overlooked at the front nor the rear and has a private gated cobble-lock driveway with parking for two cars. The property also offers enormous potential for a purchaser to put their own stamp on it and some refurbishment works (see features) have been carried of recent to include new flooring throughout. The heating system is gas fired central heating and the windows are double glazed throughout. \r\n\r\nDownstairs, the accommodation comprises of the living room off the entrance hallway with a fireplace and a gas fire, a bay window overlooking the cobble lock driveway to the front and double doors leading to the dining area off the kitchen. The open plan dining area and kitchen are positioned at the rear of the house with Patio doors access to the rear garden. Upstairs, there are three bedrooms with the front smaller bedroom being spacious enough for a 4ft bed. The hot press is positioned on the landing as well as the fully tiled Bathroom which has a bath and electric shower.
