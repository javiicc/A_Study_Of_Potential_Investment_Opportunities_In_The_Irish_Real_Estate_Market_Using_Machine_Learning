    Glenheather comprises a substantial (407 Sq.m or 4,380 sq ft Inc the attic rooms). detached Orpen style home standing on mature well stocked south west facing gardens totalling C.0.66 of an acre.\n\nGlenheather whilst not protected, is a superb example of a handsome wisteria clad Orpen style residence. Lovingly cared for by its present owners and retaining a wealth of period features to include fine fireplaces, exposed beams, pine timber doors, floors, bay windows with seating areas capturing commanding views of the grounds. Elegantly proportioned light filled and adaptable accommodation throughout and all positioned within walking distance of all local amenities. \n\nInternally the property is warm and inviting with a wonderful balance of living and sleeping accommodation. The ground floor comprises; porch with stained glass detail, inner hall with a stunning period fireplace, off this lies a dual aspect drawing room and a cozy family room with views over the gardens and the hills beyond. There is a large kitchen/breakfast room with a separate utility room and a large dual aspect dining room. A shower room completes the ground floor accommodation. On the first floor the main bedroom features a sun drenched south facing balcony / verandah. There are 4 additional bedrooms, one ensuite, a bathroom and guest wc There is in addition a wonderful self-contained guest quarters with an open plan living / dining / kitchen, a double bedroom and a shower room which also has access to the main house to add extra space to this already expansive home. A secondary staircase accesses the floored attic comprising 3 attic rooms.\n\nIdeally located on leafy Brennanstown Road within a short stroll of Foxrock and Cabinteely villages and their trendy eateries, chic boutiques and popular coffee shops. The Park shopping plaza is nearby with a wide selection of shops and restaurants. Sporting and leisure facilities include, Carrickmines Golf Club, Carrickmines Croquet and Lawn Tennis Club, Leopardstown and Foxrock Golf clubs, Westwood and Leopardstown Racecourse, Cabinteely Park with its acres of parkland offer many recreational facilities including a large children s playground. There is a wide choice of Primary and Secondary schools nearby including the well reputed St Brigid s boys and St Brigid s girls primary schools with a wealth of choice for Secondary schools close by including Loreto Foxrock, Lycee Francais, Wesley College, Blackrock College, CBC Monkstown, Mount Anville and the new highly regarded International school Nord Anglia opening Autumn 2018.
