    Blue property Sales l Rentals and delighted to bring to the market spacious 6 bed two story over basement detached property. This property is located on the Breaffy-Balla road only a 5-minute drive to both Castlebar and Balla. \r\n\r\nThis property is on 0.12 hectares (1/3 acre) and has ample parking to the front and side driveway to access and park to the rear of the property also. \r\n\r\nThe property itself is laid out over 3 floors with the entrance level being the middle floor. Living accommodation on this level comprises a spacious entrance hall, a bright living room with solid fuel stove, large open plan kitchen diner with back door and utility, 2 double bedrooms and shower room.\r\nUpstairs there is a spacious landing area and 2 spacious bedrooms both with build-in wardrobes and a family bathroom. \r\n\r\nDownstairs in the basement level, there are a further 2 spacious rooms both of which could be used for a variety of uses such as a playroom or home office, with fitted wardrobes and full windows. At the end of the hall, there is a shower room. There is also a large unfinished room with a patio door to the garden. This room could be used for a number of different uses such as a further living room/cinema room/bedroom suite/playroom/office \r\n\r\nOr with the planning permission, the whole basement level could be converted into a self-contained apartment with its own rear access, suitable for a granny flat.\r\n\r\nThis property is also fitted with a central vac system. \r\n\r\nGround Floor\r\n\r\nEntrance hall with carpet 5.30 x 2.52 mtrs \r\nLiving room with carpet 4.51 x 4.02 mtrs \r\nKitchen Diner with tiled floor 6.85 x 4.25 mtr \r\nUtility 1.56 x 1.79 mtr\r\nBedroom 1 with carpet floor and 2 x built in wardrobe 3.34 x 4.24 mtr\r\nBedroom 2 with carpet floor and built in wardrobe 3.63 x 4.24 mtr \r\nShower Room fully tiled 2.10 x 2.02 mtr\r\n\r\nFirst Floor\r\n\r\nBedroom 3 with solid wood floor and built in wardrobe 3.76 x 3.06 mtr \r\nBedroom 4 with solid wood floor and built in wardrobe 4.60 x 3.24 mtr \r\nLanding with solid wood floor 3.04 x 2.04 mtr\r\nBathroom with skylight window 2.10 x 2.02 mtr\r\n\r\nBasement\r\n\r\nRoom 1 with carpet and built in wardrobe 4.33 x 4.76 mtr \r\nRoom 2 with carpet and built in wardrobe 3.15 x 4.32 mtr \r\nShower Room fully tiled.61 x 1.73 mtr\r\nHallway with carpet 9.65 x 1.09 mtr\r\nLarge unfinished room 6.43 x 4.42 mtr and 4.23 x 3.04 \r\n\r\n\r\nThese particulars are not to be considered a formal offer.
