    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to offer for sale this wonderful detached family home with a fantastic view to the Dublin Mountains. \n \nExtending to approx. 127.5 sqm this property boasts many features such as its bright, spacious and well-proportioned accommodation.\n\nIn brief this wonderful family home comprises a porch which opens into the large entrance hall with a cloakroom and guest w.c., The spacious living room overlooks the front garden and there is an additional reception room to the rear. The large kitchen / breakfast room accesses the rear garden. Upstairs there are 3 generous bedrooms (originally a 4 bed) an extra large ensuite and a family bathroom completes the upstairs accommodation. \n \nSet overlooking the green the location is ideal for families looking for a safe haven for children to play. A wealth of essential amenities are within whistling distance to include the best of primary and secondary schools, SuperValu S.C. Knocklyon, Marlay Park, excellent bus routes with several local sports clubs and the M50 motorway just a short commute. Warm and welcoming, this fine family home offers comfort and relaxation at its best with endless scope to enhance at will.\n\nGARDEN\nThe front garden is walled and has a low maintenance cobble lock driveway which provides ample off street parking. A gated side entrance leads to the rear garden which captures the evening sun. It has been paved for ease of maintenance and it is bordered by mature plants and shrubs. The rear garden also contains a block built shed.\n\n\nEntrance Hall Wooden flooring, alarm panel, ceiling coving and cloakroom.\n\nLiving Room 4.04m x 6.31m. Feature bay window, wooden flooring, ceiling coving and rose, open fire with marble hearth, surround and wooden mantle.\n\nGuest w.c. Wash hand basin, w.c and window to side.\n\nKitchen Breakfast Room 2.72m x 5.70m. Wooden flooring, floor and eye level units with tiled splash-back, integrated fridge freezer, dishwasher and washing machine, stainless steel sink and drainer, window to side and window over-looking rear garden.\n\nFamily Room 3.33m x 4.44m. Double doors accessing reception room with wooden flooring, ceiling coving and rose, sliding doors accessing rear garden, open fire with marble hearth, surround and wooden mantle.\n\nBedroom 1 3.96m x 4.15m. Origianlly two bedrooms, this extra large double bedroom comes complete with fitted wardrobes, wooden flooring, recessed lighting and balcony over-looking south facing green.\n\nEn-Suite 2.10m x 2.70m. Large en-suite bathroom with double shower, glass shower surround , mirrored vanity unit, wash hand basin and pedestal, w.c., and window to front.\n\nBedroom 2 3.34m x 3.22m. Double bedroom with carpet flooring, fitted wardrobes and window over-looking rear garden.\n\nBedroom 3 2.77m x 3.58m. Double bedroom with carpet flooring, fitted wardrobes and window to rear.\n\nBathroom 2.28m x 1.95m. Fully tiled bathroom with corner bath, shower attachment and glass shower surround, heated towel rail, w.c., wash hand basin and window to side.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nComing from Templeogue village take a left over Templeogue bridge and then a right turn onto the Firhouse Road. Take the next left hand turn onto Knocklyon Road and pass the petrol station. Take the second left into Templeroan estate.
