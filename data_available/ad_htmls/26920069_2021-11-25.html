    Description

      Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to bring to the market this two bed penthouse apartment located just meters from the famous Promenade in Salthill. Threadneedle Court is an exclusive high quality gated development of only 8 units. No.8 is one of two penthouses situated on the second/upper floor. This apartment is immaculately presented throughout with high quality fittings internally, with the added benefit of a private secure garage to the rear and lift accessibility right to your entrance door.\n\nThe development is well maintained offering secure living with electric gated entrance, cobble lock driveways, flower beds and landscaped garden amenity area to the rear. Internally the rooms are bright and spacious with lots of natural light throughout. The large open plan living/dining area with kitchen is impressive and is perfect for entertaining, complete with a vaulted ceiling and picture window. Both bedrooms are good sized double rooms with built in wardrobes and one en-suite. \n\nPrivacy and luxury are two of the many thoughts that spring to mind with this development with the excellent location and quality of design sure to impress. All amenities such as the Salthill Promenade, local shops, restaurants, bars and much more are close to hand with Galway City Centre, UCHG and NUIG easily accessible.\n\nViewing is strongly recommended to totally appreciate the property and can be arranged on request
