    Sherry FitzGerald O Reilly welcome you to 2 the View, Johnstown Manor, a most impressive 4 bedroomed detached home set in a family friendly estate in Johnstown. This house has been lovingly maintained by its current owners who have created a warm and appealing family home. This is a bright, spacious and well-proportioned property, with large windows maximising the light into each room. \nLocated in the pretty village of Johnstown, with restaurants, shops and creche within walking distance, it is also close to Naas town with its many boutiques, restaurants, bars, Theatre, cinema, hospital and schools. Local sporting facilities include the Palmerstown House Golf course, the K club and Naas Golf club. For the racing enthusiast, Punchestown, Naas and the Curragh racecourse are nearby.\nFor commuters, access to the M7 is nearby at Junction 9, the Arrow train service is 10 minutes away in Sallins and the Citywest Luas stop just 15 minutes drive. The 126 bus to Dublin and Kildare also stops in the village.\nThe accommodation in this fine property comprises downstairs entrance hall, sting room, family room, kitchen/dining room, utility and guest wc. First floor 4 bedrooms (one en-suite) and bathroom.\n\n\nEntrance Hallway 6.2m x 1.7m. The welcoming hallway has an oak wood floor.\n\nSitting Room 5.78m x 3.95m. This is an impressive room, tastefully decorated, with a large window to front, solid oak floor and stone fireplace with a gas fire inset. With glazed double doors to the dining area.\n\nFamily Room 3.55m x 2.37m. This is a versatile room to front which could also be used as a fifth bedroom or home office. With oak floor and tv points.\n\nKtichen/Dining Room 8.44m x 4.4m. The substantial kitchen/dining room spans the rear of the home. It is a bright and sunny room with two windows and double doors to the patio outside. It features a superb range of walnut shaker style kitchen units, with larder press, wine rack, soft close drawers and display units. To centre is a sizeable island with storage and seating. All are topped with a granite worktop. \nThe kitchen incorporates a Bosch ceramic hob, Neff double oven and microwave, integrated dishwasher and Neff American style fridge freezer. The kitchen floor is attractively tiled in porcelain tiles.\n\nUtility Room 2.51m x 1.63m. The utility room offers lots of storage presses and sink, washing machine and dryer. It contains the gas boiler and from here the back door leads to the side passage. With tiled floor and splashback.\n\nGuest WC 1.86m x 0.75m. Recently updated, the guest wc has painted wall panelling and feature wallpaper. With wc and vanity unit.\n\nUpstairs\n\nLandin 3.1m x 3.06m. The stairs and landing have a wooden floor. With hotpress off and Stira stairs to the part floored attic which is suitable for conversion.\n\nBedroom 1 4.87m x 3.53m. This generous bedroom to front boasts a large number of high quality fitted wardrobes.\n\nEn-Suite 1.8m x 1.3m. The en-suite is part tiled and comprises a suite of wc, whb and shower unit.\n\nBedroom 2 3m x 2.81m. A spacious double room with rear view, it has a wooden floor and fitted wardrobes.\n\nBedroom 3 2.85m x 2.85m. This is a double room with view of the rear garden. It features built- in wardrobes and a wooden floor.\n\nBedroom 4 3m x 2.2m. Currently used as an office, this is a single bedroom with fitted wardrobe and shelving.\n\nFamily Bathroom 2.56m x 1.46m. The family bathroom is part tiled and includes wc, whb and bath.\n\nOutside To front, the garden is in lawn with laurel hedging and trees such as Bay, birch and juniper. The cobblelock drive can accommodate three cars off street.\nTo rear the garden is not overlooked. It offers two patios, lawn and a range of planting, with hedging, climbers, birch and cypress trees. Metal shed (4m x 3m) included.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom Naas, take the Dublin Road. At the roundabout take the exit to Johnstown. Follow the road into the village. Take the turn right just before Centra, turning onto Johnstown Manor. Take the next right onto The View and number two is on your left.
