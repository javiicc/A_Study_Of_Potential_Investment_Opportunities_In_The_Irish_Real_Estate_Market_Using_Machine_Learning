    Earnest are delighted to present no 64 Manor street to the sales market. No 64 is a 3 storey period house comprising of two residential units which are both vacant. The lower ground floor unit is a two bedroom with its own entrance while on the upper ground floor and 1st floor there is a three bedroom unit accessible from the original granite steps at the front. The house has many period features to include arched front door, coving and cornice center pieces and also shutters at the front window. The house is in moderate condition and will appeal to both home owners looking for a large house in a sought after location with also the possibility for homeowners to rent ground floor unit or an investor. The property benefits from having a large westerly facing c150ft back garden which offers up many opportunities for potential purchasers.\n\nLocation :\n\nManor street is simply one of Dublin's most popular and vibrant locations. \nThe area is known for its variety of cafes , pubs , restaurants such as Mulligans grocers, slice and Lilliput store to name a few. Other nearby areas of note within a short walk are the phoenix park, Smithfield Square, Kings Inn and the new TUD in Grange Gorman. \nExcellent transport links with busy bus corridor on Manor and LUAS stops nearby in Smithfield and Museum Luas Stop. \n\nAccommodation :\n\nUnit 1 : The lower ground unit comprises of own door entrance leading into the kitchen and living area.\nThe hallway is off the living area and provides access to the 1st bedroom. Continuing along the hallway the ground floor unit extends at the rear of the house over two storeys comprising of main bathroom , entrance to patio area and garden on the lower ground floor with the second storey comprising of the second bedroom. \n\nUnit 2: This is a three bedroom unit situated over the ground floor and first floor. The property has its own down entrance accessible by granite steps at the front. The accommodation comprises of welcoming hallway, bright and spacious living room at the front , separate kitchen overlooking the garden. The first floor comprises of three bedrooms and the bathroom. \n\nExternally the property has a classic period building fa\xc3\xa9\xc2\xa7ade to include arch brickwork design over the door and windows , front patio area with granite steps and wrought iron rails and fencing. At the rear there is a large garden c 150ft with westerly aspect with patio area and storage shed. *Please note the garden goes further beyond the fence at the rear in the pictures.
