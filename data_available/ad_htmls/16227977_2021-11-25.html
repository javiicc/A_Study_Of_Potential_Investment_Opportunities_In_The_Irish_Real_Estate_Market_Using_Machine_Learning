    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 6.3 ac
    Once in a generation does a property of this calibre come to the market for sale in Milverton, a truly wonderful part of Skerries, short distance to the village yet magically tucked away in quiet countryside. The Grange is a two storey period residence of enormous character and set on a very private enclosed site. Milverton is a sought after location which is only minutes to the town of Skerries, M1, Dublin Airport and the M50. This fine period farm house which is located in an idyllic situation surrounded by wonderful mature grounds. The house itself has the potential to be a superb family home, incorporating all the attributes one seeks when acquiring a country residence, from the undeniably charming fa ade with mature gardens - to the spacious and well laid out accommodation. \n\nNicely set behind large iron gates, surrounded by painted dashed pillars leading to a gravel driveway, carries you to a mature front garden with wonderful side garden with mature fruit trees and detached garage with double doors. The living accommodation is well arranged and begins with the entrance hallway that guides you to the dual aspect living room, then the dining / kitchen to the rear of the property, also on the ground floor is a 4th bedroom or 2nd reception room. On the 1st floor there are 3 good size bedrooms with a large main bathroom and separate shower room with hot press. To the rear there are an abundance of out buildings and sheds with two sheds been attached to the main house and large stone house to the rear which has seen some renovation in recent times. Also there is an open farm barn and large single storey shed which could be easily converted to give many uses including stables. The land at the rear has an entrance from the rear of the property and an opening onto the main road. The rear garden also has the benefit of an excellent raised patio accessed from the kitchen and give you a panoramic view of the coastline of Skerries.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 1.9m x 2m. Entrance Porch with tiled flooring\n\nLiving Room 4.7m x 4.65m. with wood floor, feature fireplace with wood burning stove sitting on solid hearth, dual aspect, t.v point\n\nDining room 3.5m x 2.85m. with wood floor, storage cupboard, door to kitchen\n\nKitchen 4m x 3.2m. with excellent range of fitted wooden kitchen units, presses & drawers, doors with raised patio area overlooking the rear and side gardens.\n\nBedroom 4 2.45m x 4.65m. dual aspect room to the right of the house\n\nLanding Upstairs landing with garden views\n\nBedroom 1 4.7m x 4.5m. with dual aspect, feature marble fire place and open fire.\n\nBedroom 2 3.65m x 3.15m. large double room with built in press\n\nBedrom 3 4.8m x 2.65m. large single bedroom\n\nShower room with w.c. w.h.b, marble tiled floor and walls, shower tray with waterfall shower head.\n\nMain bathroom with wc, whb, tiled walls and floor, large bathroom with shower head connection, feature stainless windows.\n\nOutside Large gardens extending over c. 6.3 acres which extends to the side of the property enjoying sunny north west east facing rear garden with raised patio area. Attached to the property are two large outhouses, large detached stone house to the left and 3 other out building which could be easily convert to suit many uses.
