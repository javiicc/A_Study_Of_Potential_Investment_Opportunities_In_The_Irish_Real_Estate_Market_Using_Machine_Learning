This spacious home is in need of some modernisation and boasts an outstanding South West facing rear garden that demands inspection to assess it's true quality ! This is an ideal property for young active families looking for a generous outside space. In addition to the house, there is also a side garage and significant storage shed capacity. Belgrove primary school and senior schools are within a short walking distance.
