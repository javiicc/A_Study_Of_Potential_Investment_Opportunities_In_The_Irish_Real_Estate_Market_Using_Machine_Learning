    This stunning architect designed contemporary property was constructed in 2009, just two miles from Clonmacnoise Heritage site. Clonmacnoise is a monastic settlement with a round tower, monastery and Celtic crosses on the banks of the Shannon dating back to the 6th century . It is a stunningly beautiful site, and a well established tourist destination. It is an extremely peaceful tranquil location with serene walks. The property is just off Pilgrims Road which leads to Clonmacnoise. Set on 1.161 acres with views over the surrounding countryside. This magnificent family home is B2 rated with solar panels and a rainwater harvesting system. The accommodation is extremely well laid out, bright and spacious with double height ceilings in the hallway and the main reception room. The kitchen is stunning with an island, a breakfast bar and a stove with a back boiler. There is also oil fired central heating. The main reception room is particularly impressive with a double height ceiling, large gothic style windows and a wood pellet stove. There is an en suite shower room to the master bedroom, en suite shower room to the bedroom on the ground floor, a family bathroom with a shower and guest WC. The master bedroom also has a walk in wardrobe. Throughout the house there are walnut shaker doors and architraves and oak staircase. There are panoramic windows with distant views of the river Shannon from the master and second bedroom. The loft was designed to have additional bedrooms and bathrooms but is not currently converted. There is a large double garage, with a storage shed and large loft and also a separate turf shed. The property is extremely private and well located, within 15 mins of Athlone and 15 mins from Ferbane. Early viewing is advised
