    Sherry FitzGerald are proud to introduce No 3 Cairnsfort to the market. This beautiful three bedroomed semi-detached property is presented in truly immaculate condition, meticulously maintained and tastefully decorated by its current owners. No 3 benefits from a beautifully landscaped and private southerly rear garden with extensive decking area. \n\nThe current owners have also extended to the rear with the addition of a large sunroom, making a wonderful open plan space off the kitchen.\n\nA home of this calibre in such a prized location is sure to generate the very highest levels of interest, we recommend viewing without delay!!\n\nCairnsfort is a small and tranquil residential development of just 53 houses positioned off the highly desirable Golf Links Road in Castletroy. There is a host of superb amenities on your door step including the Golf Club, Castletroy College, and a wide range of award winning restaurants and speciality shops. The University of Limerick and its beautiful riverside walks are just a stone s throw away, as is Plassey Technological Park and immediate access to all major road networks. As a setting to establish your family home, Cairnsfort leaves little to be desired. This is the perfect home for any 1st time buyers & young professionals looking to step onto the property ladder or could also be a wonderful downsize option. \n\nNo 3 is presented in show house condition and ready for immediate occupation. A most attractive property with excellent features such as high ceilings, modern tiles and flooring, an attractive fitted kitchen with integrated appliances, impressive skirting & doors to mention but a few! All of this is presented in tasteful neutral tones.\n\nThe principle reception room to the front of the property has a feature fireplace, wood flooring and beautiful built in storage. There is a fabulous sunroom off the kitchen dining room with sliding glass doors that open to the meticulously maintained garden. Upstairs there are three bedrooms (2 doubles and a single. The master boasts an ensuite bathroom and excellent wardrobe space.\n\nThe attic has also been converted and is an ideal office space or extra bedroom. Perfect for storage also.\n\nThe south facing rear garden is really special, offering a beautiful and tranquil area for the purchaser to relax or young children to play! The front of the property offers parking for two cars in the driveway and there is extra parking space across from the drive. Situated at the end of a quiet residential cul de sac. A perfect family home!! Viewing is recommended as soon as possible and by appointment only.\n\nServices: GFCH, Side Entrance, Alarm, Astro turf lawn at rear, Shed.\n\nAccommodation: Living, Kitchen/Dining, Sunroom, guest wc, 3 Bedrooms, Ensuite and Main Bathroom.\n\nFloor Area: 144 Sqm Approximately.
