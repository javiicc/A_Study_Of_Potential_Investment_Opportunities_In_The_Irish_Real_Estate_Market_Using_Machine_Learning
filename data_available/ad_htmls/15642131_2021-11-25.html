    Sale Agreed subject to contract.\nWattle Grove is an elegant detached property of some 220 sqm standing on a private site within 2 km of Rosslare Strand.\nThe quality of this home is immediately apparent with a paved circular driveway, electric gates, detached garage and mature planting and lawns.\nOn entering the bright tiled hallway you have two living rooms from which to choose - a south east-facing room with double aspect windows and open fireplace, or the south west-facing sitting room with gas fire in marble fire surround, double aspect windows and an entrance directly into the dining room/kitchen. The kitchen/dining area runs almost the entire of the back of the property with double doors leading through to the sunroom on one side. This is a generous space that will accommodate small intimate groups or larger gatherings alike and with French doors from the sun room to the garden it makes the space even more sociable. The utility provides extra cupboard/storage space and is fully plumbed room and a guest WC is always a handy addition.\nUpstairs the landing is a large light filled space with four double bedrooms and generous family bathroom (two of the bedrooms have the benefit of being en-suite rooms).Wattle Grove is a beautifully appointed detached home with generous room sizes throughout. Its location allows for easy access to the N25, Rosslare Strand, Wexford town and beyond.\nIf you are looking for an impressive detached home from which to enjoy family life, a viewing of Wattle Grove is a must.\nElectric gates, alarm, detached garage, mains water and septic tank, built 2002, South facing to the front.\nWe are currently working on the boundary map and the site will be at least 0.6 acre (we will confirm when maps are finalised).Please note the name of the property is on the pillars at the entrance.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 5.68m x 3.05m.
