    Sherry FitzGerald takes great pleasure presenting 48 Castlepark. This spacious four bedroom family home is ideally located on a corner site, enjoying a generous rear garden, with the added benefit of a fantastic southerly rear aspect, for day long sunshine throughout the year. \n\nWith bright, well proportioned and extremely spacious accommodation, the house measures an impressive 145sqm. (approx.) with cleverly laid out accommodation which is ideal for family life. There is a bright, wide entrance hall with living room to the left, from which there are double doors to the large, open plan kitchen/dining room. There is a spacious utility to the rear of the house with guest WC located just off here. At the front is a further reception room, an ideal family room/study, or even a fifth bedroom on the ground floor. Upstairs are four bedrooms with the main bedroom ensuite and a large family bathroom. Outside there is ample off street parking at the front, whilst to the rear is a lovely, sizable rear garden measuring 55 x 43 (approx.), which is enclosed and very private.\n\nPositioned towards the far end of the cul-de-sac within this well established tree lined development, the location could not be better. Caherconlish is a well sought after village within a short commute of Limerick City, offering a semi rural position, but still close enough to all amenities. With shops, eateries and pubs all close by, the National School is at the end of the cul-de-sac, ideal for young families. Only a short drive to the N24, providing a good access point to the M7 and M20 road networks, Shannon Airport is also easily accessible.\n\n\nEntrance Hall With front door, coved ceiling, staircase with storage underneath, tiled floor\n\nLiving Room With coved ceiling, wood flooring, feature fireplace with open fire, double doors to dining area\n\nFamily Room With coved ceiling\n\nKitchen Dining Room With coved ceiling, range of floor and wall level kitchen units, drawers, work surfaces, sink and drainer, cooker point, plumbed for dishwasher, cooker hood/extractor, tiled floor, sliding doors to garden\n\nUtility Room With tiled floor, kitchen units, plumbed for washing machine, door to garden\n\nGuest WC With tiled floor, WC and pedestal wash hand basin\n\nLanding With shelved hotpress and access to attic\n\nBedroom 1 Double bedroom with fitted wardrobes\n\nEnsuite With tiled shower cubicle and electric shower, extractor, WC, pedestal wash hand basin and tiled floor\n\nBedroom 2 Double bedroom with fitted wardrobes\n\nBedroom 3 Double bedroom with velux window\n\nBedroom 4 With fitted wardrobe\n\nBathroom With part tiled walls and tiled floor, bath, WC, pedestal wash hand basin with splashback and mirror over\n\nGarden To the front is a lawned garden with walls to side and off street parking.
