    We are delighted to bring this luxurious 9 bedroom house to the market. \r\nSituated at Drum, Knock 5 minutes walk to the Marian Shrine. The house is designed and finished to the highest of standards. It offers beautiful designed reception area which includes large sitting rooms, large dining room and kitchen area. It has 9 luxurious bedroom all En-suite with each room boasting a high standard finish including top range bedroom furniture and fittings. The property has a large elegant hallway and beautifully designed open areas. The property also includes its own laundry and ironing area. The splendid lighting effect and decor throughout the house is exceptional with total attention to detail. Beautiful floral displays, chandlers, mirrors, flooring, curtains, mats and carpets adds up to making this house perfection. Outside tarmacadam driveway, paving and colorful displays of seasonal flowers to complement the whole area. To the rear of the property is a luxury 2 bedroom self catering apartment with all its own facilities self contained. The location is ideal 10 mins drive to Ireland West Airport and good links to Claremorris, Galway and Westport. The property is currently operating as a guesthouse and has huge income potential.
