    MOVEHOME ESTATE AGENTS ... are delighted to present a fantastic refurbishment opportunity with this period blank canvas spanning 55 m\xc2\xb2 / 590 ft\xc2\xb2 on the secluded and central Avondale Avenue right in the very heart of Phibsboro Village. This original two bedroom family home was extended slightly on the ground floor and now hosts a bathroom to the rear. Requiring modernisation throughout, Number 8 is an ideal opportunity to transform and modernise a mid terrace quaint period house, as the property exudes a welcoming atmosphere throughout. The accommodation briefly comprises of entrance hall, front living room, rear kitchen area with access to the rear ground floor extension which is hosting a bathroom and also side access to the garden. Upstairs on the first floor there are two bedrooms and attic access. The private rear garden overlooks a private recreational area and the garden is north facing and low maintenance, with additional outside storage. \r\n\r\nThe house is located just a two minute walk to the Phibsboro LUAS station, less than ten minutes to the Mater Hospital and fifteen minutes walk to the City Centre. Avondale Avenue is surrounded by a host of green spaces, Great Western Square and Blessington Street Basin and Grangegorman Campus and a fifteen-minute walk to The Phoenix Park. There are many excellent schools and education facilities in the area. Amenities in the immediate area include the sports grounds in Mt. Bernard Park, Blessington Basin and the Royal Canal Walk. Both the Botanic Gardens and the Phoenix Park are within walking distance. There are plenty of shops, restaurants and cafes in the bustling Phibsborough Village. The City Centre is within walking distance and served by a great public transport system including numerous bus routes and the future Broadstone-DIT stop of the Luas Cross City line, whilst Dublin Airport is only a short drive away.
