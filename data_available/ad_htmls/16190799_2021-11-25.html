    DNG have the pleasure to present "Hillview" 3A Monastery Cottages to the market. This 5 bedroom detached dormer bungalow was built c.2002 with flexible accommodation depending on family requirements and well maintained gardens of approx. one third of an acre.\n\nThe very private setting coupled with the beautiful grounds are sure to impress.\n\nThe well laid out accommodation comprises porch with glass panelled door, an almost 2m wide hall with cloak room and guest WC off leads to the open plan kitchen dining room and living room, the living room features a multi fuel stove and glass panelled double doors to the lounge, the lounge features a vaulted ceiling and French patio doors to the sunny garden, the open plan kitchen dining room with tiled flooring has an array of kitchen cabinets and granite counter tops, there is ample space for a dining table and 6 chairs, a utility room has a Belfast sink and further cabinets.\n\nThe bedroom accommodation is split over the two floors with 2 double bedrooms on the ground floor, bedroom 1 is currently in use as a home office and boasts a bay window. Bedroom 2 features an en-suite shower room. Upstairs the remaining 3 bedrooms are all double rooms, the master bedroom overlooks the rear garden and benefits from an en-suite shower room and walk in wardrobe, bedroom 5 is currently in use as a TV room and also enjoys views of the lovely rear garden.\n\nNestled behind the original Monastery cottages, "Hillview" stands on approx. one third of an acre of very well maintained grounds. A private 50m gravelled drive leads you through electric gates to a spacious parking area and onward to the very private and sunny garden. A large lawn bordered by mature planting makes this the ideal family friendly garden for all ages. A flag stone patio area off the lounge is ideal for outdoor dining and entertaining. Should the existing accommodation need extending the garden offers ample opportunity to do so. There is also a 3.6m x 3.6m garden shed with loft space which could be used just for storage or maybe a home office if required.\n\nMonastery Cottages are situated on the border of the picturesque village of Enniskerry with a footpath to and from the village. Nearby is a service station with convenience store and the No.44 Dublin Bus service to Dublin City and DCU. Carrickmines Luas station and shopping centre is only 8 minutes away. Within the village is a lovely selection of cafes and eateries along with a pharmacy and hotel. \n\nViewing comes highly recommended to appreciate the impressive gardens, flexible accommodation and very convenient location.\n\n5 Bed detached dormer bungalow \nManicured gardens of approx. 1/3 of an acre\nBuilt c.2002, double glazed windows and oil fired central heating\nFlexible accommodation of approx. 196 Sq M\n5 Double bedrooms with 2 en-suite shower rooms and family bathroom\nPrivate 50m drive with electric gates and ample parking\nRear garden of approx. 33m in length\n8 minute drive to Carrickmines Luas station\nSituated only 1.6km walk to the picturesque village of Enniskerry\nDublin Bus route No.44 only minutes away to Dundrum, Dublin City and DCU\nNearby are the highly regarded Powerscourt and Dun Laoghaire Golf Clubs\nScandinavian pine panelled doors throughout the property\n\n\n\nPorch 1.83m x 1,8m.\n\nHall 5.31m x 1.8m.\n\nLiving Room 4.66m x 3.68m.\n\nLounge 4.44m x 2.68m.\n\nKitchen Dining Room 5.32m x 4.21m.\n\nUtility Room 3.43m x 1.97m.\n\nGuest WC 2.27m x 1.12m.\n\nBedroom 1 3.48m x 3.45m.\n\nBedroom 2 4.62m x 3.57m.\n\nEn-suite 2m x 1.6m.\n\nLanding\n\nBedroom 3 3.5m x 3.7m.\n\nBedroom 4 5.53m x 3.12m.\n\nEn-suite 2.11m x 1.89m.\n\nwalk in wardrobe 2.4m x 1.8m.\n\nBedroom 5 4.96m x 3.49m.
