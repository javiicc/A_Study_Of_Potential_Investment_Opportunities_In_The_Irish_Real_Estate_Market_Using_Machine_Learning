    A wonderful own door one bedroom apartment with the benefit of a south west facing patio and located in the exclusive Morehampton Square development in Donnybrook.\r\nBuilt in 1984 by Cosgrave Brothers the development comprises of a mix of townhouses and duplex apartments set in mature and beautifully landscaped grounds.\r\nNumber 7 is located close to the entrance and the accommodation extends to 41.5 sq.m which includes an entrance porch with storage, living/dining room with feature fireplace, and patio door to a sunny patio area. The kitchen is enclosed with floor and wall cabinets, a double bedroom with built-in wardrobes, and a tiled bathroom.\r\nThis gated development is located just off Morehampton Road on Bloomfield Avenue, within a short walk of shops, restaurants, and cafes in Donnybrook Village as well as neighbouring villages of Ranelagh and Ballsbridge. The magnificent 32 acre Herbert Park is nearby and St Stephens Green and all the city centre has to offer is within approximately 15 minutes walk. Transport links are excellent with numerous bus services running along Morehampton Road including the Aircoach and LUAS Green Line 15 minutes walk away.
