    This fine 1300 sq. ft home, which is currently occupied by the original owner has been extensively modernised and refurbished in recent years and comes to the market in turn-key condition.\r\nThe property is flooded with natural light throughout and is both spacious and airy. The ground floor comprises of an entrance hall with understairs storage, large sitting room with open fire, fully fitted kitchen - dining area with patio doors leading out into the superb walled rear garden. There is also a back door with access to the side of the house from the kitchen. Going back out the hallway to the right is a large WC. \r\nUpstairs there are 4 generous sized bedrooms (3 double & 1 single and the master having an en-suite) and recently refurbished main bathroom with shower. There is also a hotpress and generous attic space on this level.\r\nThis property has cobble lock paved off street parking, side access and has the additional benefit of a beautifully well maintained walled rear garden with shed. Oil Fired central heating throughout with solid fuel fireplace in the sitting room. Double glazed windows and solar panels ensure optimum energy efficiency. \r\nThe Greens is a very attractive residential development and within walking distance to both primary & secondary schools, public transport, shops, restaurants, and all other amenities.
