    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Tommy Carmody's Property House are delighted to bring this Rural Getaway to the market. The site has the benefit of being regular in shape, is approx 1 acre, has 2 x cottages and ancillary buildings, a septic tank and water supply. \r\n\r\nImagine being able to wake up to views of The Devils Bit, imagine living in the most scenic rural location in the mid-lands, imagine being able to go off grid, imagine the sounds of nature in your peaceful country side garden...It will take some work to bring the two cottages and accompanying buildings up to standard but it will be worth it.\r\n\r\nThe cottages have some great features, great block work, high ceilings and part of the renovation has taken place.\r\n\r\nThe property is close to villages and larger towns such as Templemore. If you were considering this property as an eventual investment property it is likely there would be demand from the Gardai in Templemore to rent the home. If the property would be for yourself and a family - there is a national school a few minutes away. There are Studs close by also.\r\n\r\nThe current owner has supplied a floor plan which outlines what her vision was for the cottages and includes 3 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms.\r\n\r\nWhilst every care has been taken in the preparation of these particulars, and they are believed to be correct, they are not warranted and intending purchasers / lessees should satisfy themselves as to the correctness of information given.
