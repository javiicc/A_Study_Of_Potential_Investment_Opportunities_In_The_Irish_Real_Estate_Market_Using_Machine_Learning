    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Home and Away Real Estate are delighted to present this magnificent 4 bedroom, 4 bathroom detached property for sale. Situated on an elevated site, overlooking the countryside, this home is finished to a very high standard and in turnkey condition throughout. Ideally located outside Dungourney, only 8 miles from the bustling town of Midleton. Built in 2009 this property is immediately impressive upon arrival and has a huge amount to offer. Inside the home boasts beautifully decorated rooms with a bright and spacious feel. With double height ceiling in the entrance hall, this provides a bright and elegant welcome. Finished in a neutral palette throughout, this home is expertly presented with great attention to detail and finish while maximising all the space and light available and would truly make the perfect family home. Offering well-proportioned accommodation with a very practical floor plan. Accommodation includes a very impressive stylish open plan kitchen/Dining/conservatory. Ideal for family living. This space is flooded with natural light. There is also a second large living room with a built-in stove with a stone surround as the main feature of the room. Conveniently there is also a utility, WC and office/games room downstairs. Upstairs there are 4 large bedrooms with 2 ensuite and a family bathroom. The house is finished to a very high standard and would make for very comfortable family living. There is also a large garage on site and a well-maintained garden to the rear of the house with a large driveway for ample parking. This one is a great opportunity not to be missed. Viewing of this prestigious property comes highly recommended.
