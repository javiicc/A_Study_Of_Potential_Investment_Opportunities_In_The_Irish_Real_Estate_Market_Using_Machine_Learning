    DNG are delighted to introduce 9 Alma Court, a terrific 1st floor, 2 bedroom apartment to the open market. No. 9 has recently undergone a superb renovation and is presented in turn key condition, oozing style and flair. The spacious and bright accommodation extends to c. 104 sq mt/ 1,119 sq ft and comprises: entrance hall, large open plan kitchen/dining/living room, home office/study, 2 large double bedrooms (master ensuite) and main bathroom. The property has been finished to a very high standard and will be sure to appeal to a host of potential purchasers including first time buyers, those trading down or investors. This property further benefits from a lock- up storage unit in the basement.\nAlma Court is ideally positioned midway on Alma Road and sits equidistant to Monkstown & Blackrock villages which offer every possible amenity. The DART at Seapoint is minutes away and the M50 & N11 are also closeby as are a choice of excellent bus routes serving the city centre. Alma Court is a beautiful, mature residential development and comes highly sought after. There is ample residents disc parking available.
