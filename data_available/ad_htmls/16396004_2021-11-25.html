    A handsome, refurbished bay windowed two-storey over garden level period residence with a superb wrap around garden and double garage, enviably positioned and well set back off this highly sought-after road, only 5 minutes walk from the Cowper Luas stop and local shopping on Upper Rathmines Road. Wonderfully transformed both inside and out by its current owners, this attractive home has been skillfully extended and refurbished (measuring approx.: 2519sq.ft / 234sq.m. including attic room). \n\nThis gracious period home combines the best of contemporary design for practical modern day living whilst retaining original period detail with high ceilings, fine period fireplaces, attractive coving and centre roses, and large sash windows to name a few. This beautiful home is further enhanced by a large double garage to the rear (46sq.m. / 495 sq.ft. approx.), complete with shower and guest w.c., offering a multitude of uses. It also comes with the benefit of planning permission (planning references: 2318/19 and 2094/20) to provide for a first and second floor extension.\n\nThe property boasts a versatile, flexible, and spacious interior, offering a perfect balance of reception rooms and generous bedroom accommodation. At garden level the layout comprises a contemporary open plan living / kitchen / dining room, with particularly impressive floor to ceiling glazed bi-fold doors opening out to the private rear garden, along with guest w.c.. This floor enjoys all day sunshine capturing the easterly, southerly and westerly light at different times of day. At hall level there is a very elegant drawing room with original period features intact. The main bedroom suite is also at this level with shower room / guest w.c., however this could easily be relocated, and the interconnecting doors reopened to provide a second reception room. Upstairs, there are 3 bedrooms; two double and a single and a well-appointed family bathroom. This fine home is further enhanced by an attic conversion with shower room including w.c. and w.h.b., along with great storage. \n\nTo the front of the property, there is a railed south-facing garden laid out in lawn and bordered with mature shrubs and trees providing great privacy and seclusion. This garden is home to a great purpose-built tree-house. The garden wraps around to the side and back where it is bathed in westerly sunshine. There is a sizeable patio area, which captures all-day sunlight, with a split-level limestone patio area, perimeter planting and herbaceous borders providing excellent screening and privacy. A large double garage (46sq.m. / 495sq.ft. approx.) with both vehicular (remote electric) and pedestrian access to Cowper Mews laneway offers a superb footprint and will serve a variety of uses from secure parking for 2 vehicles; to excellent storage to a home office/gym/games room/studio as it comes complete with a shower and guest w.c.. \n\nEnjoying an almost unparalleled sense of convenience, the property is just 2 miles from St. Stephen s Green, in a location second to none with Rathmines, Rathgar, Ranelagh and Dartry on its doorstep and within a stroll of excellent local shopping at Upper Rathmines Road and Dunville Avenue. Some of Dublin s best schools are nearby including St Mary s, Sandford National, Sandford Park, Alexandra College, Gonzaga College, Kildare Place, Zion National and Stratford College, to name a few. The renaissance of nearby Ranelagh and Rathmines gives a choice of trendy restaurants and the Luas stop at Cowper Road. Sports facilities nearby include Milltown Golf Club and David Lloyd Riverview, with Brookfield Tennis Club and Dartry Health Club literally on the doorstep. Verdant, lush Palmerston Park is a short stroll away. \n\n\nHall Level\n\nHall A generously proportioned entrance hall featuring panelling-effect walls and ceramic tiled flooring.\n\nDrawing Room 3.90m x 4.50m.
