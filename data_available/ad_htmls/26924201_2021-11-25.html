    Description

      Stunning four bedroom mid terrace house available to rent in one of the most sought after areas of Dublin 8, The South Circular Road. This house is furnished to an extremely high standard. Accommodation comprises of a welcoming entrance hallway leading to a bright spacious open plan living /dining area with solid wooden flooring & stylish d\xc3\xa9cor throughout. We have an under-stairs toilet & elegant purposely built shower room also available at ground level. The kitchen is large, luxurious & modern in design. Upstairs we have two bright large double bedrooms with solid wooden flooring, fitted wardrobes & ample storage space provided in each room. Upon the soft grey deluxe carpet on the landing we have another two rooms that can be used either as single bedrooms or study / living quarters. The family bathroom is a decent size and is fitted with all mods cons & features. The front garden is south facing & the magnificent rear garden is north east facing this completes the finish of this must see property! The location of this home is second to none being within just a short stroll of a wealth of amenities both social and essential including an abundance of shops, bars and restaurants. The discerning purchaser would also be in close proximity to St James s Hospital, the new Children s Hospital site and a selection of Dublin Bus Routes, Luas and Heuston Station, and many of the city s main business headquarters around St. Stephens Green and the IFSC.
