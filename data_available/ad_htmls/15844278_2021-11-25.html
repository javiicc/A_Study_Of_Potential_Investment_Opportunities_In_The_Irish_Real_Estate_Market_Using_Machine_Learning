    Eldron property consultants are honoured to bring this stunning family home to the market. \r\n\r\nBallinapark House was originally built in 1890 and was lovingly restored over the last 10 years by its current owner. This home oozes charm and atmosphere and finds a real balance between old and new with several upgrades that bring this home right up to the 21st century. This charming and much loved detached family home offers a unique and welcomed addition to the local property market. \r\n\r\nSet on c. 1.5 acres there certainly is room to live the good life, with ample space for pets of all sizes. The setting of this property is truly outstanding with traditional outbuildings positioned to rear and side, which could be suitable for a number of uses. Possibilities are endless for the right buyer. \r\n\r\nIn the Kitchen, you will find a large open plan space that is flooded with natural light. The bespoke Old mill kitchen, fitted with pull-out units, a Liebherr Fridge and Freezer, and a Rangemaster are perfect for helping you entertain guests on summers evening. Double french doors provide easy access out to the garden for alfresco dining, while a corner snug to the rear creates the perfect place for a cosy evening in front of the Inglenook style fireplace. \r\n\r\nOn the ground floor you will also find a utility room with ample storage and more, as well as a further storage room, plumbed for w.c and shower if required. \r\n\r\nThis Victorian residence is fitted with bespoke cabinetry, and nowhere is that more evident than the original living room. With a custom-made bookshelf, a second inviting wood-burning Inglenook style fireplace, and charming panelling on the wall, you will instantly feel comfortable and right at home. Across the hall you will find a second reception room, currently being used as a dining room but with so much potential for a quiet and charming study, hidden away from the hustle and bustle of the kitchen. \r\n\r\n\r\nThere are four bedrooms spaciously laid out in the second and third levels of this home, as well as a large family bathroom with a bespoke sink unit, pumped DBI shower, and spacious bath. Throughout all four bedrooms upstairs you will find charming whitewashed original flooring. An Exposed feature wall shows off the original stone in one of the bedrooms, which adds so much character and charm. In the two larger bedrooms you will find independently, a walk-in wardrobe and an ensuite. All four bedrooms are spacious, light, and airy but still full of charm with sash and bay windows. \r\n\r\nTucked away with a feeling of calm and seclusion, you would be forgiven for thinking you were in a rural remote paradise. However, this attractive property in Co. Wicklow has quick and easy access to Dublin via the M11. \r\n\r\nThe scenic villages of Ashford and Wicklow Town, with two excellent golf courses, are minutes away by car, as well as Mount Asher grounds and Elephant castle are all on your doorstep. And last but not least, the beautiful blue flag sandy beach of Brittas Bay is only a ten-minute walk away.
