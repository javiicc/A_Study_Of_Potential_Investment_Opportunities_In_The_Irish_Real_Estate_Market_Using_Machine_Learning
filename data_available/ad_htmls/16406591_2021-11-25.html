    Guiry Real Estate are delighted to bring to the market this impressive, 4 bedroomed executive residence situated on 2.5 acres of mature, manicured gardens and grounds. Situated only 5 minutes from the heart of Waterford City, close to many picturesque walks to include The Greenway, Nature Park and an easy drive to the seaside towns of Tramore and Dunmore East. The property is The property is in turnkey condition and viewing is a must in order to appreciate the elegance of this stunning residence. Built in 2000 the property is accessed via electric gates and up a tree lined, lamp post lit sweeping driveway. There are elegant flag stone steps to the grand front door. The large entrance hallway is bright and airy with cream porcelain tiles. To the left of the hallway way is a large sitting room with a stove and double aspect windows and high ceilings. Double doors lead to a large formal dining room with a large window and French doors leading to the patio area. The kitchen is large and inviting with an Aga stove, solid oak kitchen and large dining area. French doors lead to the patio with views out over the gardens. The second Sitting room is bright and inviting with double aspect widows and a stove. A large utility room with a sink and a guest WC complete the ground floor of this home. \r\nUp the sweeping staircase with a double height window brings you to the landing and 4 spacious double bedroom, the master of which is ensuite with a bath and a separate shower, his and her sinks and a bidet. A family bathroom and a large walk in airing cupboard off the landing complete the first floor. All the bedrooms have double aspect windows, large Fitted wardrobes and high ceilings.\r\nThere is a large triple detached garage to the side of the property and there is also ample parking to the front of the house.\r\nTo the right of the house is gated enclosure which houses a large heated outdoor American barn which would suit many purposes. There are also a number of well fenced paddocks from the barn, a separate cabin which could be used as an office space and a storage building/shed which could be used to store garden equipment and animals feeds. \r\nThe grounds to this property, truly have to be seen to be fully appreciated. The gardens are mature and landscaped. Walking around the property gives a sense of tranquility. There is a long pergola over a gravelled pathway alongside the garden and paddocks which leads to a manicured lawn. Up ornate steps to the patio which has a garden pond with lots of fish and an ornamental fountain.\r\n\r\nViewing of the spectacular home is highly recommended and strictly by appointment through Guiry Real Estate.
