    Development Description

      Dillon Marshall New Homes are proud to present Thornbrook, a luxury development of three and four bedroom homes situated in this lush woodland setting on the outskirts of Bray, County Wicklow, Thornbrook makes the most of the dramatic views of the Sugarloaf and Wicklow mountains, and includes retention of the existing gate lodge and former Danish embassy, Kilbride Hill house.\r\n\r\nThese stunning two story houses are carefully arranged to provide appealing streetscapes looking onto the shared landscape areas.The orientation maximise the sunlight and ensures the large back garden is benefit from the sloping nature of the site and views of the surrounding countryside.\r\n\r\nEach house type are arranged to provide balanced streetscapes, addressing a series of shared landscaped areas. The house types are orientated to maximise sunlight and amenity. All houses have large back gardens which are entitled to benefit from the sloping nature of the site.\r\n\r\nThornbrook comprises of 14 two-storey 4-bed detached homes, 1 two - storey 3-bed detached home, 16 two-storey 3-bed semi-detached homes, with a total of 86 on-street car parking spaces.\r\nThe charming existing entrance to Kilbride will be retained, and the beautiful landscape will benefit from additional planting, ensuring that the woodland feel of the site remains for generations to come.\r\n\r\nThere will be extensive public open space totalling 5,000 Sqm (plus incidental open spaces totalling 9,500sqm) including generous landscaping, a children's play area and pedestrian links to the wood to the north west of the site.\r\n\r\n**** VISIT OUR WEBSITE AT www.thornbrook.ie ****\r\n\r\nNote These particulars are detailed for the purposes of representing the development only.       Location

      Thornbrook is located on Herbert Road in Bray, approximately 2km from Bray Town Centre.\r\nBray Is a very appealing seaside town offering a complete array of amenities and attractions, including Bray Head walk, the shore, Kilruddery House and Gardens, numerous boutiques, salons, restaurants, caf\xc3\xa9s, as well as sporting facilities including Bray Sailing Club, Bray Golf Club, Brennanstown Horse Riding School and County Wicklow Lawn Tennis Club.\r\n\r\nThornbrook benefits from excellent transport links. The N11/M11 motorway is a short distance away, with access to the M50 and Dublin. The area is served by Dublin Bus routes 145, 84X and 185, and in turn links to Dun Laoghaire, Blackrock and Dublin City Centre. \r\nBray DART/train station is 3.5 kms from the site, providing frequent services to Dublin City Centre and Rosslare Europort.
