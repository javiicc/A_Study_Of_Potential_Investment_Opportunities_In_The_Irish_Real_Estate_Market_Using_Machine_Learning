This 5 bedroom detached property comes to the market in excellent condition, perfect for a large family seeking a spacious new home.\n\nThe ground floor of the property is comprised of a large front living room, kitchen, laundry room with guest WC, dining room, master bathroom and 3 bedrooms. Upstairs is comprised of 2 bedrooms and a third bathroom. All rooms are bright and spacious, enjoying plenty of sunlight throughout the day. The kitchen is equipped with all modern appliances, with dishwasher, fridge, oven/hob and microwave included in sale price. \n\nThe property boasts a large and well-maintained garden surrounded by mature trees, including apple trees. There is a large block-built garage on the property which is wired for electricity. The ride-on lawnmower in the garage is also included in the sale. The entire property is also wired for CCTV, with the ability to view cameras via a phone app. \n\nThe wooden annex, previously used as a granny flat, is in need of complete refurbishment, which includes a rebuilt concrete base, as well as phone lines, water and sewage connections - this represents an excellent opportunity for buyers willing to invest some extra time into putting their own stamp on the design and decor of their home.\n\nLocated in a quiet and peaceful area away from the hustle and bustle of city life, the property is also not too far from local amenities, with the nearby shops, pubs and restaurants of Glencullen village within walking distance - which includes the popular Johnnie Foxes pub. Shopping at Dundrum, Carrickmines and Stepaside is also easily accessible via public transport links in the area, with Luas lines also accessible. There are many beautiful nature walks in the area, as well as tranquil surroundings. For those commuting, the M50 is only a short drive away. Viewing is highly recommended for this stunning home!
