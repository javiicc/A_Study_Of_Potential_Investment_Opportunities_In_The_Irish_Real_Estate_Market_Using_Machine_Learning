    A truly exceptional brick finished residence set in a secluded countryside setting just outside the village of Tinure. The 5 Bedroom property with purpose built 5 stables and tack room is situated on a mature elevated site/landholding of approximately 2.5 Acres. This impressive dwelling commands beautiful panoramic views over the surrounding countryside extending to distant coastal and mountain views. A winding driveway through a wooded area leads up to this substantial family home extending to Approx. 356m /3875ft including an attached garage. The property further complimented by the stables and paddocks with separate access is sure to impress and attract any equine enthusiast for personal or commercial use. Nivard House is conveniently located only 1.7km from essential local amenities such as a convenience store, cr che and National Primary School in Tinure village. Drogheda town centre only 7.5km away is within easy access and has a wide variety of facilities including schools, shopping centres, retail parks, cinema and a comprehensive public transport network. Dublin International Airport and the M50 orbital motorway is only a 30 minute drive away accessed via the M1 motorway, which is less than five minutes from the property by car. Properties of such size and opportunity seldom come to the market.
