    Number 16 in Harbour Cottages is a really impressive property - for size as much as its condition and added extras. \r\nThis is an unusual property - it is a spacious duplex home that extends to 170 sq m and provides very spacious accommodation across 2 levels plus vestibule entrance. \r\n\r\nIn the lovely Harbour Cottages character, this property is finished with solid wood floors throughout, as well as that charming pared back natural aesthetic. Designed to maximise natural light, this is a bright property to which the current owner has applied several beneficial features. \r\n\r\nAccessed via its own door at ground floor level, you enter a lovely vestibule which enjoys double height and staircase. At first floor the landing is quite bright and doors with glazed panels lead into a very large dual aspect open plan living dining with adjacent kitchen. This room has lovely features like balconette and window seat, as well as very smart cabinetry. The kitchen is well appointed with a simple but smart finish. \r\n\r\nThere are two double bedrooms and main bathroom at this level, as well as a clever utility / closet. The rooms are both double and the bathroom has a bath with a separate shower. \r\n\r\nAt second level are two additional bedrooms - the third is a double with lovely views and a spacious ensuite, and the fourth is even more than a triple in size! Again. space and light performing well here.\r\n\r\nAll in all, 16 Harbour Cottages is a very well designed and well presented home. It has a designated parking space and is well located, being very convenient to the village and the sailing club, so if you're seeking a low maintenance property that will deliver on comfort and space, then we strongly urge you to take a closer look at this property.
