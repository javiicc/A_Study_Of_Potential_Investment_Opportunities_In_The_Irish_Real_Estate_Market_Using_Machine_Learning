    A PRIME RESIDENTIAL HOLDING MEASURING 16.53 ACRES OF TOP QUALITY LANDS TO INCLUDE COTTAGE AND OUT-OFFICES. \r\nINCLUDED IN THE SALE IN IS A PLOT OF BOG (C. 1.927 ACRES) LOCATED AT MEELICK. \r\nFOR SALE IN ONE LOT.\r\n\r\nLocated on the outskirts of Williamstown, is this quality residential holding. Extending to circa 16.53 Acres of top quality agricultural lands with extensive road frontage, this attractive traditional-style farm is sure to appeal to a range of potential purchasers. \r\n\r\nBoth the cottage and farmyard have their own separate gated entrances.\r\n\r\nThe comfortable cottage-type residence (whilst it may benefit from some modernisation works) is suitable for immediate occupancy and stands on a superb landscaped site. The accommodation consists of Entrance Porch, Open Plan Living Room, Kitchen, Shower-Room and Three Bedrooms. \r\n\r\nThere are a range of traditional style farm outbuildings to include:- a three-bay hayshed, storage shed, turf shed and a stone out-office with slated roof.\r\n\r\nThere is also a plot of bog located at Meelick which extends to Circa 1.927 Acres included in this sale.
