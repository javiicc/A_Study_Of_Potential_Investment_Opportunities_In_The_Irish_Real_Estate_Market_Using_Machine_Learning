      Turley Property Advisors are very proud to bring 70 The Hibernian to the market to let. This property which stands at an impressive 70sqm briefly comprises a large entrance hall with an excellent utility/storage room off, large shelved hot press, a bright and spacious living room with floor to ceiling glazing and accesses to the outdoor balcony area, a modern fully equipped kitchen with all appliances comes finished with high quality polished granite work surfaces, two large double bedrooms, one of which is ensuite, both complete with fitted wardrobes. A tastefully decorated and tiled bathroom completes the accommodation.
