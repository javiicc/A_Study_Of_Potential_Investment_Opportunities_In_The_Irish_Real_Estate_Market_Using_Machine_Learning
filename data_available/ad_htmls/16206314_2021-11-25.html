    Oakley Road Gem! Young's Estate Agents are delighted to bring No. 52 Oakley Road to the market. Oakley Road is a highly regarded road in Ranelagh, one of Dublin's most loved suburbs. No 52 has been a happy family home to its current owners for over 50 years. This is a lovely semi-detached home spanning approx. 107.7 m2 / 1159 ft2 (not including garage) of bright, spacious accommodation, off street car parking and south facing back garden. The house is in need of modernisation but this is a plus for those looking for a Ranelagh property that they can make their own. The property consists of entrance hall, two reception rooms, kitchen come dining with access to the garden. Upstairs there are three bedrooms and a bathroom. To the front of the house is a garden and off street car parking for two cars, a garage that currently provides storage but could easily be transformed into living accommodation on the ground and upper level and a south facing back garden with side entrance access from the front, which means it ticks all the boxes for those looking for a Ranelagh home The location of this property is second to none. Situated in the affluent village suburb of Ranelagh the property is a gentle stroll to the village which is awash with trendy cafes, global restaurants, gastro pubs, stylish boutiques, shops and a host of other local services and amenities which make every day living a pleasure in a true village community. Some of Dublin's best Junior and Senior Schools are within easy walking distance. These include Scoil Bhride, Ranelagh Multi-Denominational, Gonzaga College, Sandford Park College, Sandford National School and Muckross Park, Alexandra College and St. Mary's College are within easy walking distance. It is also within walking distance of Rathmines and Donnybrook villages. Also the LUAS at either Ranelagh Village or Beechwood are a stone's throw away meaning access to the city centre, Dundrum Shopping Centre and Sandyford is made stress free. We look forward to showing you around this wonderful property.
