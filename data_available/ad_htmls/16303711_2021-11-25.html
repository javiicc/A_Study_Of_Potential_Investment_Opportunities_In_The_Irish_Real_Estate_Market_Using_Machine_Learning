    David Scally of David Ross Estate Agents your local property experts is delighted to present new to the market 202 Mourne Road for sale. This fine 3-bedroom, mid terrace home comes to the market in excellent condition throughout and offers all potential buyers the opportunity to buy a family home in this quiet and well matured location of Dublin 12\n\nBoasting well proportioned accommodation and light filled living spaces, No. 202 is sure to grab your attention for all the right reasons.\n\nThe accommodation briefly comprises of entrance hall. A large open plan light filled reception and dining room with feature solid fuel burning stove and solid wood flooring is to the front aspect. Continuing through the property you are led to the extension where you will find a good size fully fitted kitchen and tucked off this area is a utility area and storage. Also contained within the extension is a large fully tiled family bathroom.\n\nUpstairs you will find 2 double bedrooms both with fitted wardrobes and laminate wood flooring and a good size single bedroom.\n\nTo the rear is large sunny, private and well matured garden providing an ideal setting for outdoor dining and relaxing with their peaceful nature of this mature enclave could really be appreciated, in addition to offering scope to extend further subject to p.p.\n\nThere are several well established junior & senior level schools and an array of supermarkets in the vicinity. The city centre is just minutes away and easily accessible by car, bus or the RED Luas which is a mere 5 minutes walk. Locations further afield can be reached via the M50 motorway which is just 8 minutes drive away.\n\n\nFeatures\n\nEvery possible amenity on your doorstep\nDouble Glazed throughout\nG.F.C.H..\nPrivate rear garden\nOff street parking\nRear extension\nMature residential location
