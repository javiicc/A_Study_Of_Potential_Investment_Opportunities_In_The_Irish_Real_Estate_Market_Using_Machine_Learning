    17A Mill Road is fine 4 / 5 bedroom detached family home presented in excellent condition throughout. It has been recently upgraded to a very high standard and comes to the market with the valuable benefit of a coveted 'B1' BER rating. It also boasts amongst its many appealing features a 110 ft garden to the rear that backs on to the Citywest Hotel Golf course and off-street parking for several cars to the front. To complete the picture, it is located adjacent to all the amenities that the picturesque villages of both Saggart, Rathcoole and their environs have to offer. \r\n\r\nComprehensively re- insulated together with the installation of 'A' rated double glazing and a new energy efficient combi gas boiler within the last 5 years, the accommodation is spacious, well balanced and has a lovely flow of natural light throughout. Extending to 122 sqm / 1,313 sqft approx., it comprises an entrance porch, entrance hall, living room to the front that opens through to a dining room that has a sliding patio door to a very private patio area at the rear. The downstairs accommodation is completed by a kitchen, a family room / study that can also be utilised as a 5th bedroom, a shower room and a boiler room / utility area. Upstairs there 4 good sized bedrooms and a spacious bathroom together with a spacious floored attic and Stira stairs.\r\n\r\nThe convenience of the location speaks for itself. Mill Road is situated just off the main street in the heart of picturesque Saggart village. It is within walking distance of a host of local amenities including the Citywest Hotel & Golf Course and all that the villages of both Saggart and Rathcoole have to offer. The Luas is invaluable and a mere 10 minute walk from the front door.
