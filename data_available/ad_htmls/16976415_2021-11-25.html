    36 Home Farm Road is a recently renovated four bed end of terrace period house with a magnificent full brick finish. The property offers a myriad of features that come together to make this a very exciting prospect for house hunters focusing on Drumcondra and Glasnevin. The beautiful fa\xc3\x83\xc2\xa7ade, that is further enhanced by the impressive granite sills and lintels, is indicative of the many wonderful period features that remain intact within including Stain glass detail to front door, decorative coving, centre roses, ornate plaster work. The property has a railed front garden and a south facing garden that offers both off street parking and pedestrian access. The accommodation extends to approximately 145 sqm (1,561 sqft) excluding the attic room. On entering the property, you come into a wonderful reception hallway with high ceilings, decorative coving, centre rose and ornate plasterwork indicative of the era which this property was built. The main reception room has a wonderful bay window overlooking Home Farm Road, a beautiful marble feature fireplace with a wood burning stove. Double doors lead to a beautiful kitchen/breakfast room with a feature fireplace and pedestrian access to a decking area and in turn to the south facing rear garden. There is a high gloss contemporary fitted kitchen with integrated appliances. The property\xe2\x80\x99s accommodation offers great versatility throughout and at the ground floor return level there is studio accommodation with a galley kitchen, bathroom and access to the rear garden that may serve as ancillary accommodation for a family member or indeed provide an income. There is a pedestrian side access so this portion of the property can be accessed other than through the front door and main hallway. At first floor return level there is a double bedroom that currently serves also as a home office and the bathroom. On the first floor there are a further three bedrooms, two doubles and a single. The master bedroom has a wonderful bay window overlooking Home Farm Road. The sense of spaciousness in the well-proportioned accommodation is accentuated by the high ceilings throughout the property. A stair leads to a wonderful open plan attic room which has Velux windows with south facing views over the city and a feature exposed brick gable end wall. With every room as you move throughout this property the accommodation flows very well and the beauty of the property is further enhanced by the abundance of original features that have remained intact while the property\xe2\x80\x99s recent upgrade by the current owners also provides a comfortable and stylish modern home. Home Farm Road is a most well regarded residential road in the heart of Drumcondra village. No. 36 is situated within walking distance of a host of local amenities including shops, eateries, The National Botanic Gardens and excellent schools and universities. It is also situated within walking distance of Dublin city centre and the surrounding area is well serviced by numerous bus routes. The M1 and M50 motorways and Dublin international airport are within a fifteen-minute drive of the property. Drumcondra train station and the proposed Metro station at Griffith Park are both within a ten-minute walk.
