The free flowing accommodation laid out over two levels extends to approximately 147sq.m (1,582sq.ft). At the hall level the living space incorporates an entrance hall which opens through to the extensive open plan living/dining room, kitchen/breakfastroom, sunroom, open home office area, two bedrooms, bathroom and utility area.
