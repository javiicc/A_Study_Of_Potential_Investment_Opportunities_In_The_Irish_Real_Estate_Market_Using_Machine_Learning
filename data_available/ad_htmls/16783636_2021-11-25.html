    Sherry FitzGerald are proud to present for sale No.45 Belmont Park which is a three-bedroomed, semi-detached house with an attic conversion that is located in the hearth of Ballinlough.\n\nThe location here is paramount, being positioned in one of Corks most mature, well established and desirable locations. The property enjoys close proximity to Cork City, Mahon Point, Blackrock village and a host of services and amenities both social and essential.\n\nThe property itself arrives to the market in need of complete renovation throughout and as a result, this gives the prospective purchaser the opportunity to acquire a home in such a sought-after location and make it completely their own. \n\nAccommodation consists of an entrance hall, living room, family room and kitchen/dining area on the ground floor. The first floor provides access to three bedrooms, a family bathroom and the attic space. \n\nExternally, there is a driveway offering private, off-street parking and a lawn area. To the rear of the property there is a fully enclosed private garden that is laid to lawn.\n\nThis is a fantastic opportunity that should not be missed and should be viewed to be fully appreciated. \n\nDo not delay arrange a viewing today!\nEntrance Hall 4.52m x 1.86m.
