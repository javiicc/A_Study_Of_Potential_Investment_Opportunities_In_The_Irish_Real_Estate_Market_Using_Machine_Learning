    Heathfield Castle is a very imposing and exceptionally attractive detached residence measuring circa 3500 sq ft with beautifully appointed accommodation in addition to wonderfully mature, secluded gardens which extend to 1 acres (approx). This sublime residence is a chance in a life time opportunity to be the owner of a dream home which has been lovingly created by working together with various interior and landscape designer. The property incorporates a limekiln, which originates from the beginning of the 18th century, and is situated in the heart of Ballinruane Wood approached by a tree lined avenue off the West Limerick scenic drive, a short distance from Adare and Limerick City. The perfect hideaway of luxurious, comfortable living and cutting edge building design. Internally the property is presented with great flair and impeccable taste creating a luxurious yet remarkably comfortable and manageable home providing wonderfully elegant light filled accommodation. Undoubtedly one of the winning features of this fine home is the gardens which are beautifully landscaped and enjoy remarkable seclusion and privacy..
