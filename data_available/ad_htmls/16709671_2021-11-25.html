    We are delighted to offer this spacious and well maintained owner occupied 2 bedroom ground floor apartment for sale. A great starter home, down size or investment buy with so much to offer its new owners, beautifully presented throughout, this home is sure to impress.\nThere is communal parking to the front. The block itself has 2 x floors and this corner ground floor apartment is well laid out and has direct access to the large communal green area to the rear of the block. Accommodation has been arranged in order to maximise living space throughout the property. There is a small entrance porch to the side which leads to a large living area with a modern contemporary feel, giving access to the kitchen/dining area with built in units and tiled flooring. Both bedrooms are large double rooms offering built in wardrobes and large windows giving great natural light. A family size bathroom completes the accommodation.\nThe convenience of the location cannot be emphasised enough having every conceivable amenity within reach. Ongar and Clonee Villages are close by and offer a wealth of services. Both the N3 and M50 Motorways are within a short distance. Public transport is within walking distance as are the nearby shops at Littlepace and Ongar Villages. There is also a LIDL and ALDI on Clonee main street which is less than a 5min drive away. \nView now to fully appreciate this property!\n\n\nEntrance Hall 1.95m x 1.7m.
