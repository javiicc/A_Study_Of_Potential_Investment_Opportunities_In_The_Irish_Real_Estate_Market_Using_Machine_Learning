    DNG Spillane is delighted to offer to the market this beautiful, 3008sqft detached property located just a few minutes' drive from Tallow village. Within reach of four local primary schools (Knockanore, Inch, Tallow, Curragh glass) and only 15 minutes from Lismore and Youghal, this property is sure to cater to all needs. \nThe positioning of the house on site is perfect, with a full sun aspect to the south-facing front along with panoramic vista views of the Knockmealdown mountains to one side, and the Blackwater to the other. Upon entering the site, you are greeted by a heritage dry stone wall entrance with a deciduous tree-lined drive and small wood. Another major benefit is a spacious large, detached workshop with mains power that sits to one side of the house, perfect for a small business owner or hobby enthusiast.\nExtra width to the hallways and double entrance to the kitchen/dining room creates a feeling of space filled with natural light. Peaceful and scenic rural area within easy reach local amenities. Over 7kms of forestry walks on your doorstep; mountains and beaches within a short drive.\n\n\nThe property itself consists of: -\nGround Floor- Beautiful spacious open plan kitchen/dining area, living room, two double bedrooms, generously sized office, and luxurious sized bathroom. \nFirst floor - Main bedroom with ensuite. Walk-in hot press/airing cupboard. Two large bedrooms and an additional toilet. Additional bedroom or playroom.\n\nOutside\nProperty situated on c. \xc2\xbe acre site. Large workshop and storage area.\nFour shed structure suitable for bicycles/pet owners.\nSubstantial outside area front and back, secured with wooden gates for additional privacy.\nConcrete pathways around the house and driveway suitable for multiple cars.\n\nDon't delay, contact sole agents DNG Spillane on 021-4812397 to arrange a private viewing.
