    Development Description

      The Terrace, Foxrock is a luxurious new development of 8 homes located in the heart of Foxrock village, a most prestigious address in this iconic location. The elegant collection of private homes will form part of an exciting new look to the village considering their central location whereby every design feature has been so carefully considered by both developer and architect alike allowing the design and creation of one of South Dublins most elegant and sophisticated developments.\n\nFrom the moment you step through your front door you can expect high levels of luxury, set off by sumptuous materials and exquisitely crafted finishes. Enjoy a life of supreme comfort and ease in luxurious, light-filled spaces that have been meticulously designed to meet your every need. No detail has been overlooked, from the quality of the fixtures and fittings to the beautifully crafted finishes to the high speed lift bringing one from ground floor to third floor in a mere 11 seconds.\n\nThe Terrace residences are set amidst a delightful landscaped front area where uniformity will always ensure year-round sophisticated finish. The tranquil outdoor space to the rear is laid over two patios where you can relax and escape from the hustle and bustle of city life in the privacy of your outdoor terrace and garden, both finished to exceptionally high standard. Located in this prime residential area of South Dublin, the development places you right at the centre of the best that this village has to offer. \n\nOne of Foxrock s most talked about new residential developments, The Terrace reaches four stories offering unparalleled views of Foxrock s environs. The Terrace is the result of an in-depth design process and a detailed exploration of every aspect of building design, construction and performance. Designed by architectural practice Karol O Mahony Architects, the result is a striking building that is finished to the most exacting specifications, set within a superb living environment, delivering the highest living standards.\n\nWith generous internal living space extending to approx. 2,868 sq.ft (266.44 sq.m), the design and layout of these superb homes has been carefully planned to ensure maximum daylight while taking advantage of the panoramic views on the top floor all with balconies. The epitome of sophistication, these magnificent homes will boast a second reception area on the first floor with four bedrooms spread out over the remaining floors with a dedicated floor for the principal suite. \n\nFoxrock is a leafy and highly sought-after residential location in South County Dublin positioned within 12 Km of the St. Stephens Green and within 37 Km to Dublin Airport. The location boasts many amenities from the bijous and speciality shops locally to the choice of many eateries and restaurants on your doorstep and indeed close by. The location is moments away from some of South Dublin s reputable schools both primary and Secondary with the International school Nord Anglia within five-minute drive away in Leopardstown. Many options are available for shopping with Dundrum shopping centre and Carrickmines Retail Centre only a few minutes drive away. There is a wealth of sporting and leisure amenities on your doorstep with Leopardstown Golf Course and Racecourse adjacent to the development and Foxrock Golf Club within walking distance. Cabinteely Park with its acres of parkland and many sporting and leisure amenities. Carrickmines Croquet and Lawn Tennis Club is a popular club for both sport and social events. A fantastic location with every conceivable amenity and transport option at hand.\n\nContact Sherry FitzGerald Foxrock for further information 01 2894386 or view the floor plans at www.theterracefoxrock.ie
