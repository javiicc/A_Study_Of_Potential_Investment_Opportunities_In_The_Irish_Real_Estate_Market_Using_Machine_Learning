    Gilford Terrace is an ideally located quiet residential cul de sac, just off Gilford Road and close to the Strand, DART and Sandymount village. No.16 is a charming period two bedroomed cottage, with naturally bright accommodation. Built c.1896 it has been completely refurbished in recent years, so allowing for all the comforts of modern living but retaining all the charm of a bygone era.\n\nThe accommodation briefly consists of an entrance hall with tiled floor which leads to a bedroom on the left-hand side, there is a living room with a feature fireplace on the right-hand side, this leads to the second bedroom with a door to the private rear yard. There are also double doors leading from the living room to a fully equipped kitchen and a lobby and finally a fully tiled shower room completes the accommodation.\n\nCity centre, Fitzwilliam and Merrion Square, South Docks, Grand Canal Dock and IFSC are all within easy reach by foot, bike, car or public transport. The airport is a mere 20 minute drive away via the Port Tunnel. This property has many sporting and recreational facilities on its doorstep as well as all the amenities of Sandymount village.\n\nEntrance Hall tiled floor.\n\nBedroom 2 window overlooking front.\n\nLiving Room feature fireplace, window overlooking front and double doors to kitchen.\n\nBedroom 1 door and window to rear yard.\n\nKitchen range of fitted units, electric oven and hob, tiled floor, stainless steel sink, sky light and door to rear yard.\n\nLobby hot press and plumbed for washing machine.\n\nShower Room fully tiled, shower, w.c., wash hand basin and sky light.
