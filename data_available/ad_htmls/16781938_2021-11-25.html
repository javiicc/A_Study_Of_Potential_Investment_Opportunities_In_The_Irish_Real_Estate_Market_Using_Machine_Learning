    Delightful extended dormer bungalow (approx 185 sqm/1,991 sq ft) situated at the end of a private cul de sac in a sought-after location.\r\n\r\n* Watch our walk-through video *\r\n\r\nSituated at the end of a quiet cul de sac, No 117 Betaghstown Wood enjoys a private setting and is not overlooked to the front or the rear. A front porch leads through to a generous inner hall and off to the left is the kitchen / dining area offering a good range of storage units together with a central island which also acts as a breakfast bar and has useful built-in storage underneath. Included in the sale: electric oven, top grill, electric hob & overhead extractor and a free-standing fridge. An external door opens into an outside room, floored and roofed, which provides additional storage and there is an outside tap and water softener. Doors lead to the front and back of the house.\r\n\r\nDouble doors lead from the kitchen / dining through to a generously proportioned sitting room with a marble effect fireplace (gas fire inset) which serves as a homely focal point. Sliding patio doors open on to a deck and garden area.\xc2\xa0 Across the hall, there is a utility room with plenty of storage space, a sink unit and an external door. It is plumbed for a washing machine and dryer. The first of the four double bedrooms is to the front of the house whilst the second bedroom is to the rear with a fully tiled shower room directly opposite. Off the hall corridor, there is a cosy TV room / den which, in turn, connects into a really lovely sun / garden room complete with wraparound windows and a glass door to the deck and garden area. \xc2\xa0\r\n\r\nStairs lead from the hall up to a bright landing area with under eaves storage. There are two large double bedrooms at this level, each having its own fully tiled en suite shower room and built-in wardrobes. Both bedrooms are bright and spacious with dual aspect windows. The stair, landing and bedrooms are fitted with high quality carpet throughout.\r\nOutside, a cobblelock driveway provides parking for two cars and there is a planted shrubbery bed to one side. The rear garden is a joy to behold! There is a deck, flag stone walkways, pergola, raised beds, ornamental pond, evening sun trap and seasonal flowers and shrubs to enjoy throughout the entire year. All in all, a treat is in store for prospective buyers!\r\n\r\nLOCATION\r\nSet in an established residential area just off the Eastham Road, 117 Betaghstown Wood is within easy reach of Bettystown town centre and its many amenities (Tesco, variety of shops, caf\xc3\xa9s & restaurants). The Village Hotel is 5 minutes' walk away for more local neighbourhood food & drinks. Excellent local amenities include a wide choice of both primary and secondary schools and top-class sporting facilities. The main-line rail station at Laytown and the proximity of Dublin Airport via the M1 in less than 30 minutes' drive, brings the area into very reasonable commuting distance.\xc2\xa0 Drogheda town is 10 minutes away by car and there are regular local and city (Dublin) private / public bus services.\r\n\r\nFEATURES\r\nQuiet private residential setting.\r\n4 double bedrooms, 2 en-suite.\r\nGorgeous maintenance-free gardens with deck. pergola & seasonal flowers & shrubs.\r\n3 reception rooms.\r\nTop quality fixtures and fittings throughout.\r\nPresented in walk-in condition.\r\n\r\nACCOMMODATION\r\nEntrance porch; tiled floor\r\nInner hall: 3.3m x 4.0m; solid wooden floor; radiator cabinet; cloakroom\r\nKitchen/dining room: 4.0m x 5.2m; feature island / breakfast bar with built-in storage; generous wall and floor kitchen units; electric oven, grill oven, electric hob & overhead extractor; fridge; tiled floors.
