    Vanke is delighted to present 53 Trinity Plaza for sale, a superb top floor one bedroom apartment which also has the added benefit of a designated parking space. Trinity plaza is a mature and modern development ideally located on the corner of Tara Street and Townsend Street opposite the Irish Times building. \n\nNo. 53 is presented in excellent condition and makes for the ideal city centre base or rental property. Measuring 43 sq.m / 463 sq. ft in size this apartment is more than generous compared to the average one bed around the city. The accommodation briefly comprises; an open welcoming entrance hall, a tastefully finished living/dining room and kitchen with an attractive bay window, one large double bedroom, and a beautifully finished bathroom. The apartment benefits further from a secure underground parking space, a communal garden at the first floor, and a lovely roof top garden. \n\nThe location of this apartment is simply unbeatable. It is just a moments walk from Trinity College, Grafton Street, and O'Connell Street, pithing walking distance fo both the Green and Red Luas lines. Every conceivable amenity that the city has to offer is right on your doorstep. \n\nEntrance Hall: Welcoming entrance space with intercom and hard wood flooring. \nKitchen: With multiple floor and eye level units, hard wood flooring and a tiled splash back. Plumbed for washing machine. \nLiving/Dining Room: A bright and airy living space with hard wood flooring and a large bay window allowing natural light to the apartment. \nBedroom: A fine large double bedroom with built in wardrobes and hard wood flooring. \nBathroom: Partially tiled bathroom with w.c, w.h.b and bath with shower attachment. \n\nPlease email us to arrange viewing.
