    Quaint and Cosy\r\nWelcome to Robin s Cottage, a charming artisan cottage located in a tranquil setting with the benefit of an artist s studio to the rear of the garden. Located in between the towns of Cahir and Clogheen, this home offers the perfect starter package or indeed trade down option for any discerning purchaser.\r\n\r\nAn attractive and unique style dwelling in a most pleasant setting. Standing on a fine spacious, private, mature and colourful site with many special features both inside and out. \r\nThis cottage rests on c. 0.39 of an acre approx.\r\nHaving undergone substantial renovations over the years (original cottage renovated in 2016/2017 Extension 2019) the accommodation is now cleverly thought out. Lovingly maintained by its current owner creating a cottage filled with charm and character. \r\nThis charming 2 Bedroom property offers c. 80 sq mts of accommodation including: Kitchen, utility room (1st fix complete), main bathroom, bedroom one, Living area hosting the stairs to a cosy mezzanine floor hosting a writing area and Bedroom 2.\r\nThe interior of the property is finished to an olde world charm style home in keeping with a traditional rural cottage style. Externally, the property has an artist studio/workshop to the rear (50sq mts over 2 floors) with a kitchenette, shower room, w.c., open plan artistic space and storage overhead. This studio overlooks the mature apple and pear trees. To the front of the property is a small enclosed garden which would make an ideal kitchen/cut flower garden. The kitchen window hosts wonderful views of the purpose built pond attracting an array of birds.\r\nThis property is in an idyllic tranquil setting whilst close to all the surrounding main towns.\r\n\r\nViewing Strictly by appointment.\r\n\r\nFEATURES:. Extensively renovated - windows, doors, wiring, insulation, flooring, radiators and multi fuel stove\r\n. South west and south east facing which gives natural light and warmth\r\n. Vaulted ceiling in living room\r\n. Oil fired central heating\r\n. Views of the surrounding countryside and mountains\r\n. Off street parking\r\n. Approx 8km from Cahir Town, 16km from the Vee, 25km from Clonmel Town.\r\n. Organic Plot enclosed with hedging and lamb fencing secure for pets.\r\n. Two storey timber chalet (artist studio) with multiple uses as office/playroom, workshop or home gym. \r\n\r\n.\r\nLiving Room 4.57m x 3.58m. Laminated wood flooring. Multi fuel stove with feature beam. Vaulted ceiling. Stairs to 1st floor.\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 2.85m x 4.25m. Laminated wood flooring. Built in wardrobe.\r\n\r\nKitchen/Dining Room 4.42m x 3.76m. Fitted kitchen units with integrated hob and oven. Tiled floor. Door to utility room.\r\n\r\nBathroom 2.44m 1.93m. Tiled floor and wet area. Heated towel radiator. Bath with mixer taps, w.c., w.h.b.\r\n\r\nHallway 1.98m x 1.22m. Tiled floor. Hotpress, coat rack. Fitted storage units.\r\n\r\nUtility Room 4.0m x 2.1m. Plumbed for utilities, insulated and plastered. (To complete - 2nd fix electrial, tiling and fitting of units.) Door to rear garden.\r\n\r\nMezzanine 4.3m x 3.3m. L Shaped. Laminated wood flooring. Overlooking living area.\r\n\r\nArtist Studio/Workshop 50 sq mt over 2 floors. W.c., kitchenette, artist area, overhead storage.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 2.9m x 4.3m. Laminated wood flooring. Views of the surrounding mountains and countryside from the window.\r\n\r\n\r\nDIRECTIONS:\r\nFrom Cahir take the Clogheen Road R668.
