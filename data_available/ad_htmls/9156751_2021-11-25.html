Lansdowne Place comprises a selection of luxury apartments located on Lansdowne Road in the heart of Ballsbridge. Each property offers residents the opportunity to enjoy a new level of luxury as well as a sense of space and comfort. Apartment types consist of one, two and three bedroom homes as well as penthouses. \r\n\r\nArchitects O'Mahony Pike and interior architects Goddard Litttlefair have brought together the highest quality timbers, natural stone and select metals in Lansdowne Place, all hand selected and made to measure by master craftsmen. \r\n\r\nResidents Club \r\n\r\nAll Lansdowne Place residents become members of The Lansdowne Residents Club, a purpose-built leisure facility and 24-hour concierge service.
