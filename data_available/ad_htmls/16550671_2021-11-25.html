    Moe, Kilclaran, Feakle, Co. Clare V94WR98\r\n4 Bedroomed Bungalow \r\non an elevated site with views over local countryside. (on 1.2 acres approx.)\r\n \r\nSituated on the eastern side of the Feakle to Gort road just 150 metres from Kilclaran RC church, this well-built spacious bungalow has been remodelled and extended to be a spacious modern residence. With a BER rating of D2 the house has been insulated to a good standard. The property contains some interesting features including a nice modern house, an amazing view, a spacious site and a range of storage sheds. \r\nThe house generally sits on the north-western side of its elevated corner site, set back from one road and closer to the other road. The house is serviced with a deep-bored well and on-site septic tank. \r\nThe house contains: entrance porch leading to hallway, sitting room with open fireplace, kitchen/dining room, utility room, bathroom, hot press, walk-in shower room, 4 bedrooms one of which could be a work-room or an office. \r\nThe kitchen/dining area is on the northern part of the house. The kitchen area is fitted with ample units and includes a gas hob, electric oven and dishwasher. \r\nThe windows are in double glazed uPVC frames. The house is heated with an energy saving condensing oil-fired boiler. \r\nThe utility area leads to the back door and to the large yard that could park a number of cars.\r\n\r\nThere are a range of outbuildings, including a storage shed on a lower level. There is also some grassed area at the back of the house, to the north and to the south of the house. \r\nThis most desirable property is 6km. from Feakle, 15km. from Scariff and 24km. from Gort. The property is 10km. from the East Clare Golf Club, 32km. from Limerick, 200km. from Dublin and 40km. from Shannon Airport. Feakle has its own National School while Raheen Wood Steiner School is 18km. away. Scariff and Tulla each have good Secondary Schools within easy reach. \r\n\r\nThis is an ideal opportunity for anyone seeking a nice, very well positioned residence, in a beautiful, rural and tranquil setting in this lovely area close to Lough Graney. \r\nViewing is strongly recommended.
