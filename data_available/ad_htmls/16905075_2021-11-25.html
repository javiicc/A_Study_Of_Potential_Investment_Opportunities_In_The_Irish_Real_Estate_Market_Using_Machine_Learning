    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    14 Sean Lee is a three-bedroom townhouse situated in a quiet and peaceful estate. From the entrance hallway, there is a sitting room and kitchen with oil fired range. Upstairs there are 2 double bedrooms, 1 single bedroom and a main bathroom. The property has a front garden with off street parking and a long garden with shed to the rear of the property. It is a short walk to River Erne through the very scenic River Island walk. There are many local facilities on your doorstep which include shops, restaurants, pubs, excellent schools (both Primary & Secondary) and leisure amenities. Situated in Belturbet just 15 minutes outside Cavan and 10 minutes from Ballyconnell. The M3 means Dublin is only a 90 minute drive away. Castle Saunderson and the Slieve Russel have lovely walks and an 18 hole Golf Course which is very close by. Viewing is highly recommended.
