No.8 Saint Peter's Road is Flooded with natural light, the living spaces are bright and airy with a warm feeling throughout. This property will be sure to attract all keen first time buyers and second time buyers alike given the convenient location and the array of nearby amenities and services, while investors will note the huge potential for a dual income with some slight development to the unit to the rear.\n\nThe accommodation briefly comprises entrance hallway, living room with feature fireplace , a stunning open plan kitchen / dining area with built in appliances and island unit. Upstairs there are 3 spacious modern bedrooms, the master offering built in sliding wardrobes , and family bathroom tiled throughout with heated towel rails. The attic has been converted with en-suite. An spacious rear garden accessed via the side passage with south west orientation, provides an ideal setting for entertaining guests or enjoying a family BBQ. Out front the property has security gates and a maintenance free garden with parking for multiple vehicle's. \n\nIn summary NO.8 offers a wonderful spacious design, contemporary colours and high end fittings throughout, with a south west facing rear garden flooding the property with natural light, all of which create an elegant, bright family home. This property is sure to impress the most discerning of buyers\n\nNo.8 enjoys an ideal position in this area of Walkinstown enjoying a quiet position in this extremely popular development. Conveniently located to all necessary amenities this property is within walking distance of all schools (including the new Riverview Educate Together), shops, churches, sporting and recreational facilities. Public transport is catered for with bus routes to include the No.no.9, 15A, 77A, 27,56A & 150.
