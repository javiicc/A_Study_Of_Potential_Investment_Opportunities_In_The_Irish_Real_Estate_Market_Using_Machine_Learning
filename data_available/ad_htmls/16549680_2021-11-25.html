    This bright and spacious, private ground floor apartment is in lovely condition throughout. Boasting ease of access on the ground floor the main door to this apartment is shared with just 2 other apartments and with parking just outside the door this property very is private and convenient. The gardens are well maintained and attractive and are only for private use of the apartment residents of this historic building. Refuse collection is included in the management fee as is block building insurance. Sienna convent is a landmark building located on its own grounds within the heart of Drogheda Town centre which is walking distance to every amenity. The accommodation comprises: Large entrance hall, sitting room, fully fitted light filled kitchen/dining room, 2 spacious bedrooms (main en-suite), bathroom, storage cupboard. Feature high ceilings, timber floors and natural gas central heating with a recently installed new gas boiler. Viewing is by appointment only. Please email agent through this advert.
