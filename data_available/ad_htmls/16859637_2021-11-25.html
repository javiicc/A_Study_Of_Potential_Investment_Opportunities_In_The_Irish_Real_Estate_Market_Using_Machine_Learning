    323 Howth Road is a fine five-bedroom family home that has been well maintained over the years but also provides the prospective purchaser to place their own stamp on the property. The well-proportioned accommodation encompasses approximately 136 sqm of bright and airy living space which briefly comprises entrance porch, hallway, living room, dining room, extended kitchen with access to the rear garden and guest wc . Upstairs there are five bedrooms a shower room and a family bathroom. The integrated garage provides the excellent opportunity for conversion (subject to planning permission).\n\nThe rear garden enjoys a highly coveted southerly orientation and is laid in easy to maintain lawn with a selection of attractive plants and shrubbery. The front garden is laid in cobble lock and provides for off street parking for two to three vehicles.\n\nSet on a highly sought after and mature tree lined street, the location of 323 Howth Road cannot be overstated. The property is located just a short walk to Raheny Village with its abundance of shops, excellent national and secondary schools, supermarkets, and a host of sporting and recreational clubs. The pedestrian entrance to the ever popular St Annes Park is just a 200 yard walk away. There are excellent transport links right on your doorstep including the QBC and the DART which is a mere three minute walk away. \n\n\nEntrance Hall 2.42m x 4.50m.
