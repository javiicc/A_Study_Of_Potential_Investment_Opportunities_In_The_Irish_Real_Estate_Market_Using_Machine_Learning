    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    9 Saint Patrick`s Terrace is a three bedroom mid-terrace house extending to approx. 78 sq. m. The accommodation comprises of entrance hallway, living room, open plan kitchen/dining area, three bedrooms and bathroom. There are patio doors off the dining area leading to the rear of the house which comprises of a patio area, shed and parking. \n\nPlease note, the property requires upgrades and modernisation throughout internally, however it is a great opportunity for those looking to put their own stamp on it. \n\n9 Saint Patrick`s Terrace is located in the Slane Village, off the N51 Road with access to the Slane Castle across the road. All of Slane`s amenities are within walking distance off the property. Navan town is approx. 12km away and Drogheda town centre is approx. 15km away from the property.\n\nEntrance Hallway: 3.85m x 1.85m\n\nLiving Room: 3.86m x 3.79m\n\nKitchen/Dining Area: 5.93m x 2.65m\n\nMaster Bedroom: 3.80m x 2.76m\n\nBedroom 2: 2.95m x 2.71m\n\nBedroom 3: 2.64m x 3.96m\n\nBathroom: 1.65m x 1.81m\n\n\n\nDirections\nFrom Navan, travel the N51 road towards Slane.
