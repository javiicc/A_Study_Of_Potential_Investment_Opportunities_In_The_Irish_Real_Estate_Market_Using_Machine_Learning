    Churches Estate Agents are delighted to present to the market No. 30 Belville Court, a truly unique opportunity to purchase a stunning penthouse apartment finished to an extremely high specification complemented by its fantastic south westerly private terrace with uninhibited views over south county Dublin.\r\n\r\nLocated on the top floor there is an immediate sense of space when you enter the apartment. You are greeted with a wide bright entrance hallway leading around the corner to its modern open plan layout surrounded by its expansive terrace. Large feature floor to ceiling windows allow an abundance of natural south facing light flood through the spacious living room with feature fireplace. The modern fitted kitchen dining area combined creates a comfortable living area and entertaining space.\r\n\r\nBoasting two generously sized double bedrooms and a third single bedroom with fitted wardrobes. The master ensuite includes floor to ceiling corner windows and built in wardrobes. The second double bedroom, with floor to ceiling windows and built in wardrobe also uniquely includes its own private covered balcony to the rear. Off the central hallway is its main bathroom suite.\r\n\r\nBelville Court is a private and highly sought after development located in one of South County Dublin\xe2\x82\xacTM most popular suburbs. It is situated conveniently off Johnstown Road, only short stroll to Killiney shopping centre, Cabinteely Village and Dun Laoghaire with their wide array of amenities and facilities such as shops, cafes and restaurants. Several well-regarded primary and secondary schools are also just a short distance away.\r\nTransport links include the N11 dual carriageway offering ease of access to Dublin city and beyond along with the M50. The Aircoach gives swift access to Dublin Airport as well as multiple Dublin Bus routes also close at hand.
