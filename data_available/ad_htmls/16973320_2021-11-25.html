    A traditional farmhouse residence occupying a private idyllic location nestled into the rolling landscape. This delightful property presents as a superb family home incorporating all the attributes of a country residence paired with a modern interior space. \n\nThe accommodation has been remodelled and arranged to incorporate 168 sq. m approx. of comfortable accommodation. To the front of the property the main reception room, an inviting space with a feature red brick fireplace and solid fuel stove, to the rear of the property a superb modern kitchen with access to the dining room, study /bedroom and wet room. On the first floor there are 4 good size bedrooms and the main bathroom. \n\nTo the side of the property a detached building 80 sq. m approx. currently utilised as a home gym and could easily be customised to a workshop or a multiplicity of other uses.
