    EXPANSIVE 7-BED DETACHED DORMER BUNGALOW WITH DELIGHTFUL SEA VIEWS\r\n\r\nWelcome to 59 Granville Road, an impressive 2,800 sq ft dormer bungalow, featuring up to 7 beds on a large plot of nearly 0.2 acres. Situated in an elevated spot, with unimpeded views of a quiet park immediately in front and Dublin Bay beyond. The property has massive potential to modernise and extend the existing space into something truly incredible! (Subject to Planning Permission)\r\n\r\nGranville Road is an exclusive road in one of Dublin's most popular suburbs. The area is predominately populated with large detached family homes and spacious dormer bungalows. Situated close to Blackrock, with its excellent amenities and renowned schools, but also with quick access to the N11 and to the rest of Dublin.\r\n\r\nNo. 59 is one of just two houses on this quiet part of the road, with a unique position looking out across the park area opposite and onwards across Blackrock and Dublin Bay beyond. Even from ground floor level, the front living room has stunning views and they only improve from first floor level. The upstairs bedroom / study has amazing views and is a highlight of the property.\r\n\r\nThe house is situated in the middle of the plot, with plentiful front garden with U-shaped turning circle, off-street parking and part-covered carport. To the rear, a perfect 60' by 50' South by South-West facing garden with patio, which is not overlooked or overshadowed, an include two sheds/outbuildings.\r\n\r\nThe property presents an impressive frontage of granite stone and white render, with giant front facing windows. To the rear there has been previous extensions to make the house a considerable size. In addition to a large living room, adjoining dining room and separate kitchen, the ground floor also includes five bedrooms, two bathrooms and an additional family room.\r\n\r\nUpstairs currently provides two large bedrooms, the front having breathtaking sea views, a shared bathroom, plus extensive storage rooms and eaves attic spaces. There would be tremendous opportunity to extend or rearrange this space (STPP).\r\n\r\n59 Granville Road is presented in excellent move-in condition but would benefit from modernisation. This is great opportunity to reimagine the property and use the existing space inside and out for a modern layout. This house could become an incredible modern family home with endless possibilities.\r\n\r\n- ACCOMMODATION -\r\nGROUND FLOOR\r\nEntrance Hall - (1.9m x 3.4m) Carpet, leading to:\r\nGuest WC - (2.4m x 0.9m) WC, hand basin;\r\nCloakroom - (3.0m x 3.0m) Storage with window;\r\nLiving Room - (4.0m x 7.3m) Large living room, carpeted, windows to front, marble fireplace, gas fire, ceiling & wall lights;\r\nDining Room - (4.9m x 3.7m) Double doors to adjoining living room, carpeted, window to side, leading to kitchen;\r\nKitchen - (4.3m x 2.7m) Parquet wood effect vinyl floors, white kitchen cabinets & doors, electric appliances, window & door to side carport;\r\nRear Family Room - (6.4m x 3.0m) Hallway from dining room to staircase & rear family room, carpeted, french doors to garden;\r\nBathroom 1 - (3.7m x 1.8m) WC, basin, bath with stand-in shower, glass folding screen;\r\nBedroom 1 - (3.9m x 3.7m) Large double bedroom, windows to rear garden;\r\nBedroom 2 - (2.9m x 2.4m) Single bedroom, windows to rear garden;\r\nBedroom 3 - (2.8m x 3.1m) Small double, windows to side car port.\r\nBedroom 4 - (3.8m x 3.9m) Large double, windows to side car port.\r\nBedroom 5 - (3.6m x 4.9m) Double bedroom, built-in wardrobes, windows to rear garden;\r\nBathroom 2 - (1.8m x 3.6m) WC, basin, walk-in shower;\r\n\r\nCarport - (5.4m x 3.9m) part-covered carport, side entrance to kitchen, concrete driveway;\r\nRear Garden - (60' x 40') Sunny South-South-West facing garden.
