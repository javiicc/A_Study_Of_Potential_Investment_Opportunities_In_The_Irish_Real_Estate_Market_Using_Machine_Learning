    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Dillon Marshall Property Consultants are delighted to present No 5 Millrace Court to the market - a very spacious three bedroom home located in the heart of Saggart. This property will be sure to attract an array of buyers given the convenient location and the array of nearby amenities and services.\r\n\r\nThe property is bright and airy with a very welcoming feeling throughout. The accommodation briefly comprises a porch, entrance hallway, off this you will find the modern kitchen/ dining area and a reception room that can be used as an office/ further bedroom. The guest wc completes the description at ground floor level. Upstairs, there is a living room, main bathroom and a double bedroom with ensuite. On the top floor there are two large and spacious double bedrooms with built in wardrobes one of which has an ensuite. There is access to balconies on all levels.\r\n\r\nThe location is superb - just a short stroll from Saggart & Rathcoole villages which offers a host of local schools, shops, cafes, bars and restaurants. There is ease of access to all main roadways the N7, N81 and M50 within close proximity. Public transport is also well catered for with the LUAS stop nearby in Saggart and bus stop 2 mins walk making Dublin city centre easily accessible. The Citywest Hotel, The Square Shopping Centre, Tallaght Hospital and Tallaght IT are all within a short distance away.
