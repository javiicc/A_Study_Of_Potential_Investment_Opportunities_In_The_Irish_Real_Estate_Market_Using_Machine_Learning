    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    DNG are delighted to welcome to the market No. 10 Hyacinth Street North Strand, a highly deceptive and deeply captivating terraced residence within walking distance of the City Centre yet quietly secluded in a small cul de sac of just 16 cottages. \r\n\r\nThe property has been designed to allow for a completely open plan layout with a stunning mezzanine level overhead, allowing the property to avail from an abundance of light and space throughout. All opening up works are fully planning compliant and create a fantastic modern feel and new age theme to these turn of the century period cottages. The property is laid with quality laminate wood flooring, is double glazed throughout, offers gas fired central heating and comes with a charming wood burning stove in the main living area. \r\n\r\nFrom the entrance hallway, you are immediately greeted with a stunning open plan and double height space, with the owner having utilised every inch of space from the ground floor to the roof. The living room leads to a kitchen / dining area with a wrap around breakfast counter which was created using reclaimed brick from the walls of the original property. The bedroom overlooks the ground floor from the mezzanine level which benefits from a modern and enchanting roof garden and balcony area which is not overlooked. There are also a number of storage areas and cupboards neatly concealed throughout the property and there is ample on street parking to the front. \r\n\r\nNearby amenities include a large selection of shops, cafes, restaurants and recreational facilities. Eastpoint Business Park, IFSC and Dublin City Centre are all within easy walking distance. The area is well serviced by an extensive public transport network, with Connolly Dart Station, Red Luas Line, QBC and Eastlink all a short distance away. The opening of the Royal Canal Greenway is also a welcome addition to the area making surrounding areas quickly accessible on foot or by bicycle.
