    Savills are delighted to present to the open market Rose Hill House, Lee Road, a deceptively spacious period home in excellent decorative order situated within a quiet cul de sac setting.\n\nThe property was re-wired, replumbed and refurbished in recent years whilst keeping its period property charm and character. \n\nOriginally built c. 1895, the property has grown in size along the way and today is set out over three levels with each level maximising space and natural light thanks to its southerly orientation. Rose Hill House is a six bedroom plus study, family home situated in a little known cul de sac just off the start of the Lee Road, it is within minutes of UCC, CUH and the Bons Secours hospitals. \n\nUpon arrival you are greeted by a coded pedestrian entrance and a large double garage with electric roller shutter doors providing off street parking for two cars. Making your way into the front garden brings you to a real city oasis where you can hear the sound of the Lee fields river cascading down the channel. This is an inspiring and private space made up of a level and manicured lawn space, limestone patio, raised flower beds and a repointed stone wall with recently fitted cast iron railings. Here you will also find an array of planting to include Wisteria plant making its way around the arched doorway and a beautiful Olive tree.\n\nStepping over the threshold of Rose Hill House brings you to an inviting hallway with high ceilings and original plasterwork. On the ground floor you will find a relaxing drawing room, library, a calming dining room, bright and private kitchen/breakfast room, a large utility room, guest lavatory and a perfectly placed wine cellar.\n\nMaking your way up the gradually ascending staircase, you will find a laundry room and services room on the return of the stairs followed by three bedrooms on the first floor including the maser bedroom which is home to a large dressing room and en-suite. The top of the house which is an addition to the original building provides three more bedrooms, a large office space and shower room. \n\nThe rear garden space can be accessed from both the ground floor and first floor. Here you will find two tiers of garden space bounded by an original stone wall. This space is almost completely private, the upper tier would make an ideal herb and vegetable garden.\n\nRose Hill House is ideally situated for anybody looking to be close to the convenience of the city whilst living in a beautiful period home which has been renovated and refurbished.\n\nContact us today about this fantastic home.\n\nVIEWING\nBy appointment with Savills Cork\n(021) 427 1371 or email:cork@savills.ie\n\nWithin 10 minutes of Fitzgerald park, The Mardyke Arena, The Lee Fields and Sunday' s Well tennis club.\n\nGround Floor \n\nHallway Inviting and bright space accessed via solid wood door with overhead fanlight window, solid oak wood floor, original stairs, dado rail, original plasterwork and open plan under stairs storage area.\n\nDrawing Room Well-proportioned room with sanded and varnished wood floor, cornicing, archway, double height skirting board, feature marble fireplace and large south facing sash window.\n\nDining Room Beautiful room with sanded and varnished wood floor, stunning marble fireplace with brass hood, tiled insert and base, picture rail, cornicing and wall lights.
