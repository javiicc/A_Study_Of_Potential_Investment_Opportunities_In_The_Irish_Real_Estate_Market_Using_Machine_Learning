      Available unfurnished or furnished. Get Let is delighted to bring this bright spacious 3-bedroom cottage to the market. Currently being renovated to a very high standard, this modern property is coming to the market in excellent condition. \r\n\r\nThe house briefly consists of a large bright open-plan living space with Velux windows that create a light-filled living space complete with a gas fire. There is a new modern kitchen complete with all mod-cons adjacent to this room. At the rear of the house is the main bathroom, and there are 2 upstairs bedrooms, and a garden level master bedroom that is en-suite, with a toilet WHB and large shower. There is wooden laminate flooring throughout the house with tiles in the bathrooms and kitchen. There is a rear yard, good attic storage and on-street parking. The property has a decent D1 BER with a new pump so excellent water pressure, and is wired for high speed VIRGIN media. \r\n\r\nThe location is excellent with Donnybrook and Ballsbridge villages both within short walking distance. It is a 2 minute walk to the N11 and the many bus routes that run along the QBC, while there are numerous parks and amenities all within walking distance. Viewing comes highly recommended.
