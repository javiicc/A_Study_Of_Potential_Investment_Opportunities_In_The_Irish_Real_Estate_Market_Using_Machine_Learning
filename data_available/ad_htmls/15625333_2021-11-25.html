    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Moran Auctioneers are excited to bring to the market an excellent opportunity to purchase an architecturally designed residence in need of refurbishment which occupies a magnificent site with secluded grounds over looking Lough Conn situated in the idyllic lake district of Northeast Mayo between Lough Cullen and Lough Conn. Lough Cullen has a number of sheltered beaches with the most popular one at Drummin Wood which has a number of forest and lakeshore walks. \r\n\r\nThe property is only 400m from Lough Conn and Pontoon Lakes is known for a number of activities including fishing, kayaking, cycling and hiking.\r\n\r\nWith stunning views of Lough Conn and within close distance to the picturesque heritage town of Foxford, it is the perfect location from which to discover the West of Ireland. The area is one of the most beautiful and unspoilt locations in the country and is close to a number of towns including Ballina (13km) and Castlebar (22km) with all amenities of a modern infrastructure. The property is 30km from Ireland West Airport Knock.\r\n\r\n\r\nThe accommodation comprises:\r\nEntrance Hall; Living room/ dining room( 32'7"X 21'2")\r\nKitchen ( 10'3"x 12'); Utility( 5'1"x 7'2")\r\nDoor to garage( 13'5"x 18'3")\r\nBedroom 1( 18'x 14'7"); Bathroom\r\nSpacious landing\r\nBathroom( 8'x 8'5")\r\nBedroom 2( 12'9"x 16'5")\r\nHot press with immersion\r\nDetached garage.\r\n\r\n \r\n\r\nNOTE: Any information, description, measurement or statement so given or contained in any such sales particulars, webpage, brochure, email, letter, report issued by or on behalf of Moran Auctioneers or the vendor are for illustration purposes only and are not to be taken as matters of fact or to be taken as forming any part of a resulting contract.
