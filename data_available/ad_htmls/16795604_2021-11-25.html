    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    This beautiful bugalow residence is presented to the market in what can only be described as top class condition. Accommodation is spacious, bright, and very well laid-out. It enjoys a pleasant, peaceful countryside location, just off the R733 (i.e. Duncannon Line). Local shops, schools and churches are within a short driving distance at Wellingtonbridge, Clongeen, Foulksmills, etc. There are a host of beautiful beaches nearby at Bannow Bay, Cullenstown, Kilmore Quay and Fethard on Sea to name a few.\n\nIt is very accessible to the N25 and N / M11, Rosslare Europort is within easy driving distance and Dublin City and Airport are around a 2 hour car journey. This is a very cosy, charming property with a gated entrance, short driveway, parking and gardens. It is full of mature shrubbery and trees giving plenty of privacy.\n\nVIEWING IS HIGHLY RECOMMENDED.\n\nAccommodation Comprises:\nEntrance hallway - (4.3m x 1.8m), Spacious with storage, laminate flooring.\nSitting Room - (4.8m x 3.9m), Feature fireplace, laminate flooring. feature fireplace.\nLiving Room - (4.1m x 3.9m), Feature fireplace, laminate flooring.\nKitchen/Dining - (3.7m x 2.8m), Fully fitted kitchen units at eye and waist level, breakfast counter, laminate flooring, plumbed for dishwasher.\nUtility Room - (1.6m x 1.0m), Plumbed for washing machine & tumble dryer, built in storage.\nBack Hall - (1.8m x 1.5m), Laminate flooring, garden access.\nGuest WC - (1.7m x 1.2m), WC, WHB, laminate flooring.\nBedroom No.1 - (2.8m x 2.8m), Built in wardrobe, laminate flooring.\nBedroom No.2 - (2.8m x 2.6m), Built in wardrobe, laminate flooring.\nBedroom No.3 - (3.8m x 2.7m), Built in wardrobe, laminate flooring.\nBedroom No.4 - (3.8m x 2.8m), Built in wardrobe, laminate flooring.\nBathroom - (2.8m x 1.8m), WC, WHB, Bath, part tiled walls, laminate flooring.\nHot Press - (1.2m x 1.2m), With shelving.\n\nOutside: Detached garage 5.5m x 4m, boiler house, mature gardens to front and rear, spacious parking.\nServices: Mains water, private sewerage, oil fired central heating.\nBER: E1 Ber No: 113263669 Performance indicator: 322.3 kWh/m2/yr\nApply: Keane Auctioneers (053) 9123072\nViewing: Strictly by appointment with the sole selling agent.\nEircode: Y35Y2X9
