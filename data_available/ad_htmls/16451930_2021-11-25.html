    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to bring 33 College Gate to the market. This semi detached property is located in a quiet cul de sac within this popular development and is situated within walking distance of Mary Immaculate college and is also located close to a selection of primary and secondary schools and Limerick City Centre. The M7 road network with links to Shannon airport, Dublin and Cork is also a very short drive from College Gate.\n\nThe front of the property is paved with off street parking for one car. The entrance hallway has a tiled floor and opens into a large reception room with timber flooring, bay window and open fireplace. There are double doors through to the kitchen breakfast room which has tiled floor and a fully fitted range of floor and wall units and a tiled splashback. The kitchen is plumbed for washing machine and dishwasher. There are double doors opening out to the back garden. Guest w.c. is located under the stairs.\nAt first floor level there are three bedrooms. These are spacious rooms, 2 doubles and a single. The main bathroom is fitted with bath, wc, and whb with fully tiled walls. The main bedroom to the front has a fitted ensuite.\n\nThere is a side entrance to the rear garden which is mostly paved and enjoys a good degree of privacy.\nThe property is a very well located property with great potential to become a convenient and stylish first home for any first time buyer or it could also be purchased as a lucrative investment or possible downsize due to the convenient location close to Mary Immaculate College and the city centre.\n\nVieiwng is recommended without delay and by appointment only.\n\nAccommodation: Entrance Hall, living, kitchen/breakfast, w.c.
