    This detached three-storey georgian townhouse with walled garden has a superb location on the Main Street of Castletownshend village, very adjacent to the pier area on Castlehaven harbour.\r\n\r\nCastletownshend is a most unique period village on the West Cork coastline. It boasts many fine period houses, a shop, pubs & restaurant and a wonderful harbour, suitable for all sailing and boating activities.\r\n\r\nThe White House, which was once owned by a well-known Victorian author, Edith Somerville of Somerville & Ross fame, has been restored and renovated to a high standard. During the course of this, great care was taken to preserve and enhance its many period features. \r\n\r\nTo the east of the house, there is a well-landscaped walled garden with separate access from the street. \r\n\r\nCastletownshend is one of the most sought-after residential locations on this coastline.
