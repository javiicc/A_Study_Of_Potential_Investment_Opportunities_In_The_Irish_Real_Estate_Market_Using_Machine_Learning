On the first floor level there are 2 double bedrooms and main bathroom On the second floor level there is a large master bedroom with feature window and Juliet balcony leading to and en suite bathroom . Outside there are parking bays to front with ample visitor parking, gardens front and rear., the rear garden is not overlooked . \r\nSituated within short walking distance to Balbriggan town centre with wide range of amenities and excellent transport links to and from Dublin city centre within (45min.)
