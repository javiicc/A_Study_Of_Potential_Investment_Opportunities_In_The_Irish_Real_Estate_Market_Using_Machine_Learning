    Blue Sky Property are Delighted to bring to the market one of the finest examples of Victorian Splendour No. 4 Seatown Gardens, Dundalk. Built circa. 1900 this large 6 bedroom townhouse is set out over 3 floors and has its own separate private arched entrance for vehicle access, a large back garden with workshop to the rear., which is accessed by a private lane via Mill Street. \n\nWhen approaching No. 4 you will be immediately taken by its stunning red bricked facade, its original terracotta and black tiled approach with ornate railings on a granite plinth, the original front door welcomes you into the impressive entrance porch with the wonderful original victorian tiled floor still intact. As you move into the hallway you are instantly taken with the high ceilings, impressive staircase, ornate coving, panelled doors and mahogany staircase all reflecting the skilled craftsmanship of an age gone by. To the front you have the generous size living-room with wonderful bay window, original marble fire surround and double doors leading into the drawing-room with a further feature fireplace and french doors to the private terrace and garden beyond. From the drawing-room you move into the dining-room which is conveniently located just off the kitchen and guest wc. \n\nOnto the first floor, the gorgeous staircase takes you to the 5 Bedrooms, Bathroom and WC. Finally you reach the second floor with a further bedroom, office and store room. Outside you have off street parking and a nice mature secluded back garden. No 4 Seatown Gardens is a must see for those who are looking for a large period townhouse in one of Dundalk's premier locations. \n\nBlue Sky Property are guiding \xe2\x82\xac495,000.
