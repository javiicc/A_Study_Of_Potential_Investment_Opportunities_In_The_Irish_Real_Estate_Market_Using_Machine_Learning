    Substantial three storey period house located in the heart of Shinrone Village. This fine premises was at one time home to the local village shop, which for many years traded as Ryan s,, catering for all needs. The old shop front is still intact and there is a separate entrance to the adjoining house. \nAccommodation is laid out over three floors, each floor measuring approx. on average 90 sq. mts, laid out in five rooms on each level. Beautiful spacious rooms with high ceilings, some of which are restored and some of which are in need of complete restoration. \nThe house was re roofed to include replacement of all timberwork approx. five years ago. Period features include a wide staircase, original internal doors, some window shuttering & casement, and high ceilings.\nA side access gate leads you into an enclosed rear courtyard with original stone coach house and cobbled yards. Behind the coach house there is a paddock/garden which is included in the sale.. The total site area that the house stands on is .75 of one acre. A stream runs along the garden boundary. \nThe property is a protected structure (ref no14822009) and exempt from a BER Cert. If you are seeking a restoration opportunity this may be the one for you.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 3.04m x 2.85.\n\nBedroom 1 3.4m x 3.2m.
