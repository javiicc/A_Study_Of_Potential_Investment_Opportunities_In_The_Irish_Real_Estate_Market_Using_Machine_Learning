    Savills is delighted to present 24 Merrion Court an amazing 4 bed detached home decorated to the highest standards\n\n\nThis home has a wonderfully sunny rear patio and private garden space. The lounge is a spacious room with an ornate mahogany and marble fireplace, beautiful cornicing to the ceiling and a large bay window allowing natural light in.\n\nTo the rear of the property there is a spacious kitchen/dining room with a tiled floor and fantastic fully fitted country style kitchen with a great selection of appliances. This already bright room has the addition of a velux in the ceiling allowing in more sunshine to stream all day and into the evening.\n\nAlso, to the rear of the house is the sittingroom with patio door leading the garden/patio area, this is the perfect extra room for the kids or to relax on a Sunday to read the newspapers.\n\nOn the first floor is the master bedroom, a large double room with built-in wardrobes overlooking the front of house. In addition, there is two other double bedrooms overlooking the rear garden and one single room facing the front. The master bathroom is fully tiled and spacious with beautifully selected bathroom ware and lighting.\n\nThe back garden is extremely private and has a neat lawn and patio. This area gets the best of the sun from early afternoon into the late evening, ideal for those who lovely to relax and have a barbeque.\n\nThere is also a good quality solid build shed to the rear with power and lighting. The front garden is laid out to lawn and there is a selection of plants and shrubs. The drive has adequate private parking for two cars.\n\nIdeally located, Merrion Court offers a wide range of amenities close by including schools (primary, secondary, third level), shops, sports clubs a variety of businesses all within easy walking distance. Cork city centre (1.7km) is only a short walk away or five minutes by car.\n\nThis home represents a really superb opportunity for those wishing to trade up or looking for a beautiful property in a most desirable residential neighbourhood only a short walk from the city centre. This room has a tiled floor and a fully fitted country style kitchen with units at both floor level and eye level and plenty of storage. Also included is a blind, ceramic electric hob, grill, oven, dishwasher, washing machine, fridge/freezer, extractor fan and under counter illumination. This space also benefits with beautiful views of the private rear garden and a velux window in the ceiling allowing great additional light in the evenings.\n\nLounge Bright room with direct access through patio doors to the garden/patio to the rear.
