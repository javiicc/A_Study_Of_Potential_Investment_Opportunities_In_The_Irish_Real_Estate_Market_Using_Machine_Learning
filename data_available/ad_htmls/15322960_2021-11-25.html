    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Stan Bradbury is delighted to bring number 36 The Parade to the market, formally the show apartment for the Bastion Court development, this apartment boast space and contemporary design, while surrounded by leafy and mature Connaught gardens . \nBastion Court is a centrally located development on the Left Bank of town, just a two minute stroll from Athlone Castle, Luain Gallery, St Peters, the riverbank and a host of amenities and award winning restaurants, bars. With such an abundance of facilities and amenities Bastion court is an ideal place to downsize to for a retiring couple or a perfect base for a young professional.\nThe apartment is located on the top floor and has a wrap around balcony with south / west and east aspects, a mix of timber decking and paving make this a great space for relaxing in the summer evenings.\nThe apartment comprises of an entrance hall, store, 3 double bedrooms west facing, ensuite, main bathroom, large open plan kitchen living room and separate utility room/ larder. Access to the private balcony is from the living room. Fully tiled bathrooms, timber floors to the hall, bedrooms and living rooms.\nThe kitchen is a stylish mix of high gloss cabinets and walnut presses with a insert stainless steel sink with draining board. Fully tiled splashback, extractor fan over the hob.\nThe apartment comes with 3 car spaces in the common car park. \nLift access to the apartment from the lobby.
