    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 3.29 ac
    Opportunity to acquire land with development potential (subject to Planning Permission) located on the northern edge of Tralee town centre fronting onto the Tralee / Abbeydorney road (R556) opposite Lios Ard residential estate. Extending to approximately 2.5 acres/ 1.01 hectares, this land currently operates as Tralee Caravan and Mobile Home Park and boasts over 60 meters of road frontage onto the R556. Several amenities nearby include primary and secondary schools, convenience stores and GAA grounds. Mains services (including sewer) are in place to serve existing Caravan Park. Folio KY23305.
