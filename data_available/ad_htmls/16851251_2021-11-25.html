    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to present 8 Ennafort Drive to the market. This fine four bedroom home is set in the much sought after location, which has long been a popular choice for growing families. Boasting well-appointed and light filled accommodation throughout, this superb home is sure to appeal to those looking for a wonderful home.\n\nThis home has been well cared for over the years and extends to approx. 122 Sq M/ 1,313 sq ft comprising of a most welcoming entrance hall, living room, dining room, kitchen and sun room which provides access to the garden. A fourth bedroom with ensuite completes the living accommodation on the ground floor. Upstairs, there are three spacious bedrooms and a large family bathroom. The rear garden of 8 Ennafort Drive is laid in lawn and bordered by an attractive selection of plants and hedging. The front garden is paved and provides off street parking.\n\nEnnafort enjoys a most convenient location just off the Howth Road. The location is second to none with Raheny, Killester and Clontarf villages all within easy access, and as a result there is an abundance of amenities to choose from. There is an excellent choice of national and secondary schools close by, as well as a plethora of sporting and recreational facilities. St Anne s Park and stunning coastal walks are right on your doorstep.
