    DNG are delighted to present to the market an excellent property in an exclusive development of only 39 houses, No. 10 Temple Woods. This 3 bedroom family home been constructed to the highest building and environmental standards, resulting in premier A2 rated energy efficient home. \r\n\r\nThis property will immediately impress with high quality design for family living, with well-proportioned rooms and excellent finishes throughout. Notable features include higher than average ceiling heights, custom-designed fitted kitchen with quartz worktops and generously tiled bathrooms. The bright and spacious accommodation briefly comprises of an entrance hallway, living room, guest bathroom, open plan kitchen / dining room and utility. Upstairs there are 3 generous sized bedrooms, en-suite bathroom and a main family bathroom. The attic space is currently floored and future proofed with trusses to facilitate conversion to habitable use, (subject to future planning permission) giving you potential to create an additional forth bedroom. Outside to the rear there is spacious garden laid in a maintenance free synthetic lawn and a paved area at the end of the garden. To the front there is off street parking for 2 cars.\r\n\r\nTemple Woods is a superb development ideally located on the Greenhills Road and moments away from Tymon Park. This convenient location has an excellent range of local amenities which include schools, shops, churches, bars and a pharmacy close to the development. It is minutes from the M50 and only 10 kilometres from the city centre. \r\n\r\nEarly viewing highly recommended!
