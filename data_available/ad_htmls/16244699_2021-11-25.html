    89 Homefarm Road occupies a prominent corner position on this popular tree lined residential road which links Upper Drumcondra Road and St Mobhi Road. The property has been very well maintained over the years, extended and decorated with great style and flair. Many of the features from the 1930 s have been retained which add greatly to the charm of the house. The accommodation is bright and spacious and will appeal to a growing family seeking a home in a fantastic location.\n\nThe accommodation comprises porch leading to the hallway with under stairs storage and shower room. There are two interconnecting reception rooms, the living room to the front has a fireplace with electric fire, while the dining room to the rear also has an electric fire (both original fireplaces still remain in situ). The kitchen has been extended and newly fitted with a great range of modern floor and wall units. There is plenty of space for a dining table. A door leads to a sun room which in turn has a door to the rear garden.\nUpstairs there are four bedrooms, all a generous size. A bathroom completes the accommodation. \n\nThe front garden provides plenty of off street parking, a sweeping lawn and mature hedges offer great privacy. To the rear the low maintenance garden is laid in paving.\n\nThe location is very convenient being within walking distance of the city centre and convenient to the numerous shops, cafes, bars and restaurants Drumcondra Village has to offer. The Botanic Gardens and Griffith Park are closeby and provide beautiful open green areas. Dublin City University is within easy reach with a variety of good sports clubs, national and secondary schools locally. The Four Courts, King s Inns, Beaumont Hospital and the Mater Hospital are all easily accessable. Commuting further afield is made very easy with the M50 Motorway, The M1 and Dublin Airport within a few minutes drive. The area is very well serviced by public transport, through the many bus routes which pass close by and Drumcondra Rail station nearby.\n\nPorch With tiled floor.\n\nEntrance Hallway The porch opens into a bright hallway with under stairs storage and a useful downstairs shower room. The floor is laid in laminate.\n\nShower room 2.1m x 1.6m. Fully tiled with suite comprising w.c, wash hand basin with storage beneath and shower cubicle with electric shower.\n\nLiving Room 4.4m x 3.1m. Beautiful reception room to the front with bay window flooding the room with light. A fireplace with electric fire creates a lovely focal point. Double doors lead to the dining room.\n\nDining Room 4.4m x 3.2m. Another large reception room with feature electric fire and picture rails.\n\nKitchen/ Dining Room 4.8m x 3.3m. The kitchen has been extended and is fitted with a range of floor and wall units with a large window overlooking the rear garden. There is plenty of room for a dining table.\n\nSun Room 4.7m x 1.5m. Covered area located off the kitchen with door to the rear garden.\n\nBedroom 1 4.4m x 3.2m. Large double room to the front with original fireplace, picture rails and bay window.\n\nBedroom 2 3.3m x 3.1m. Also a double room with picture rails and dual aspect windows.\n\nBedroom 3 3.2m x 3.2m. Double room to the rear with fireplace.\n\nBedroom 4 3.2m x 2.2m. With space for a double bed, this room has dual aspect windows.\n\nBathroom 2.2m x 1.5m. With suite comprising w.c, wash hand basin and bath.
