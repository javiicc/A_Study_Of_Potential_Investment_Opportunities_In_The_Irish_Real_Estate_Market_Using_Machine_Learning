    DNG are delighted to present 23 Oaklands Crescent to the open market. Set in this quiet enclave off one of Dublin's most sought after roads lies this two bedroom, ground floor own door apartment. Deceptively spacious this charming property is flooded with natural light.\n\nNo. 23 has been carefully maintained over the years and offers extremely well proportioned accommodation for a property of this type although benifit from some modernisation. A unique feature of these wonderful apartments is the fact they boast their own private gardens and separate private garages. This is a small development set around a picturesque green, properties rarely change hands in this development and with so much on offer it is easy to understand why. \n\nThe garden sits to the rear of the property and is beautifully landscaped, it is low maintenance with a garden shed tucked away in the corner, \n\nThe convenience of this location speaks for itself as it is only a short walk from the city centre. Rathgar maintains a nice village feel as it has a bakery, a delicatessen, a butcher, a bike shop and a wine shop all within 20 yards of the main cross. There is a wide range of award winning restaurants in the area catering for every taste. Dodder Park consisting of over 100 hectares of parkland and remnant countryside is also within a short walk. Cowper Luas stop is approx 1km away, and there are numerous and frequent buses linking to the city centre. St Stephens Green is about a 20/30 minute walk away. The property is close to a number of well regarded schools and St Lukes hospital. Surrounded mainly by properties from the Georgian and Victorian era, this property offers a fantastic opportunity to acquire a spacious property in the area. \n\nThis fine property ticks the boxes in terms of value per sq.ft, space and location, viewing comes highly recommended.\n\nAccommodation comprises entrance hall, hallway, living room, kitchen/breakfast room, 2 double bedrooms and the main bathroom.\n\nEntrance Hall\xc2\xa0 \xc2\xa0 Laminate wood flooring\n\nLiving Room\xc2\xa0 \xc2\xa0 Front facing, laminate wood flooring\n\nMaster Bedroom\xc2\xa0 \xc2\xa0 Rear facing large bedroom, laminate flooring, built in wardbrobes\n\nBedroom Two\xc2\xa0 \xc2\xa0 Laminate wood flooring\n\nBathroom\xc2\xa0 \xc2\xa0 Partly tiled, WHB, WC, electric shower\n\nKitchen\xc2\xa0 \xc2\xa0 Laminate wood flooring, whb, eye and base level storage\n\nDining Room\xc2\xa0 \xc2\xa0 Rear facing bright & spacious dining area leading to conservatory\n\n\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nSee online map for accurate location or contact DNG on 01-4909000 and we would be happy to assist.
