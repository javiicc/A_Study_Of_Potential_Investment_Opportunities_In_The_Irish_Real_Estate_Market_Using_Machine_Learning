    This is truly an exceptional property which boasts a combination of a fabulous modern two storey detached home with a large adjoining commercial unit all standing on a c. 2 acre site. Located approx. 1.3km from Camolin village and within 1km of R772 making this an excellent locality for both residential and business usage. \nThis stunning family home boasts fabulous and impeccable styling throughout and extends 217sq. m (2,346 sq. ft.) approx. The outside of the property is as stunning as the interior with stone wall entrance, landscaped gardens and a small paddock to the rear. In addition to the residence is a fabulous self-contained studio which is currently used as an office and gym but would be ideal for use as an apartment/granny flat. Just behind this is an excellent sand arena for horses together with a useful shed containing two stables and storage shed. This building extends to approximately 144sq.m. \nThe large separate commercial property and grounds are secured with steel perimeter fencing and large electric sliding gate opening to a sizeable forecourt with the commercial building featuring a large office, reception office and large floor area workshop with two double height rolling shutter doors. This space was formerly a two bay commercial truck garage with recessed pit and storage loft. Although the property has planning permission for use as a haulage business premises, this space could host a myriad of different business needs for either your own business needs or as a lease option to produce some welcome regular income. This commercial building is comprised of almost 4,000sq. ft. of ground floor workshop area together with modern office accommodation of c.1000sq. ft. with a mezzanine of c. 1000sq.ft., over the offices opening to the workshop area.
