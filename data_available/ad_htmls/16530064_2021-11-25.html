    Property Partners OBrien Swaine are proud to present this truly outstanding red brick period home which was designed by renowned architect Robin Mandal. This award winning house is laid out over three levels and 1600 sq. ft. with 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms. This home was redesigned and renovated to include a stunning new glass extension to the rear and when complete it won the Opus award and was selected for an exhibition in the RIAI regional awards. \r\n\r\nNo. 38 boasts a double height sunroom, living space on the first floor to maximise the wonderful views across the canal, a double height open plan livingroom/kitchen/ diningroom, a home office space, an ensuite bathroom and a walk in wardrobe in the master bedroom, a utility room, a feature staircase with a brushed steel handrail and a landscaped back garden designed by DFLA (Dermot Foley Landscape Architects) which is South facing with 2 patios accessed by the conservatory.\r\n\r\nIdeally located on the Leeson Street end of Adelaide Road with a fantastic array of boutiques, cafes, restaurants, delicatessens, shops and bars on your doorstep. St. Stephens Green is a 5 minute walk away and the villages of Ranelagh, Donnybrook and Ballsbridge are also just a short stroll away. The property is also close to some of the best junior and secondary schools that Dublin has to offer such as CUS, Sandford Park, Loretto on the Green and The Institute of Education. For green areas, the property is situated close to 3 of Dublin's Georgian Square Gardens. There is an abundance of public transport close by with the Luas at Charlemont, multiple bus routes and the Aircoach within a couple of hundred metres.
