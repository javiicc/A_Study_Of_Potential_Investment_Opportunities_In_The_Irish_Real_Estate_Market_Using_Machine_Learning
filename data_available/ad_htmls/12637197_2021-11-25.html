    This is an ideal opportunity to acquire a ready to go development site in this excellent location. The site is an area of. c. 0.86 acres and has access to the Scholarstown Road via The Rookery. \r\nPlanning permission for five large detached houses was granted by An Bord Pleanala (Ref: 304162-19). There is the possibility of a sixth house. The site surrounds the existing house which in turn is surrounded by gardens. \r\n\r\nThe location is one of the most sought after in the area with all main amenities within walking distance, Knocklyon Shopping Centre is literally minutes walk away as are numerous primary and secondary schools. Transport links are easily accessible, the M50 also within easy reach. \r\n\r\nThe planning permission is granted for five detached houses of c. 168 sqm each, comprising four bedrooms, two bathrooms, three reception / study rooms with parking to the front and gardens to the rear. \r\n\r\nViewing by appointment and in line with current COVID-19 protocols. \r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nFor more information see: http://www.sdublincoco.ie/Planning/Details?regref=SD18A%2F0227
