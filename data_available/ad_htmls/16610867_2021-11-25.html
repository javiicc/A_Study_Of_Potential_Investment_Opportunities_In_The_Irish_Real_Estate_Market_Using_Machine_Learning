    Located in one of Salthill s and Galway s most sought-after developments. The location in Salthill been within a 2-minute walk of Blackrock diving tower and The Salthill Promenade makes it a highly sought-after location. \n\nA pleasant surprise awaits you upon entering this deceptively spacious three bedroomed townhouse, which was recently modernised and now offers a contemporary designed residence. No. 73 is exceptionally well finished and cared for by its current owners and enjoys stunning views towards Galway Bay from the west facing balcony. \n\nSpanning over two floors, the first floor features a kitchen/dining area to the front with large living room to the rear, while upstairs there are three bedrooms, one en suite and a main bathroom. \n\nOverall, a stunning property, in an exclusive convenient location, close to all amenities Salthill has to offer.
