    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to bring this charming 3 bedroom semi detached family home to the market, situated on the ever popular Balkill Road. Number 38 benefits from a large side access which could be extended (subject to the relevant PP) and there is stunning views over Lambay Island and Irelands eye from the rear bedrooms on the first floor.\n\nThe living accommodation comprises of an entrance hall, living room and kitchen/ dining room on the ground floor. Upstairs there are 3 good sized bedrooms and the main family bathroom. There is access to the basement level from the kitchen which could provide space for many useful activities. There is off street parking to the front of the property. The rear garden is large and benefits from mature shrubberies and trees which are planted around the boundary.\n\nLocation is superb being within walking distance of the picturesque seaside village of Howth with all amenities on your doorstep including excellent schools, boutique shops, cosy bars and popular restaurants, an array of sporting facilities including golf, tennis, football and GAA clubs. There are great walking and hiking options around the peninsula including harbour walks, cliff walks and hill climbing, all with breath taking views. Howth is conveniently located in North County Dublin with good access to Dublin city centre via bus & DART as well as being close to Dublin airport.\n\n\nHallway 4.21m x 1.79m.
