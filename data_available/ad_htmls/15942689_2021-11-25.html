    Occupying a prime position in this prestigious development, there are only two delightful three-bedroom own door duplex homes in Stone Park. With wood floors in the principal reception areas and a quality grey carpet in the bedrooms, internally the accommodation is luxuriously appointed. The stunning kitchen/dining room features a superb Karl Cullen hardwood and in frame kitchen with integrated appliances and a large slider to the open balcony. Extending to 129 sq m/1,388 sq ft there are three bedrooms and beautifully appointed bathrooms with the bedrooms on the second floor enjoying glorious views towards the mountains. \n\nSituated in the heart of Mount Merrion the development is within close proximity of many Neighbourhood shops including Supervalu, Ryan' s Pharmacy, The Food Shop and other local retail outlets are just a gentle stroll away, while UCD Belfield, churches and a host of recreational and leisure amenities including the beautiful Deer Park are within easy walking distance. The M50 and city centre are easily accessible while the nearby Sandyford Business Park with more than 1,000 companies and 25,000 people working in the park offers a vibrant atmosphere and is home to major global corporations.
