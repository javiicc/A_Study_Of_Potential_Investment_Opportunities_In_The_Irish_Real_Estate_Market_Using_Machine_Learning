    1 Churchview Park is a superb four bedroom detached family home in walk in condition: the perfect property for a growing family with its generous accommodation measuring approx.187.66m2 (2,020ft2). It comes to the market with a host of benefits including off street parking for 2/3 cars, private south facing rear garden, rewired and replumbed, upgraded insulation, gas fired central heating, double glazed windows, overlooks a large recreational green and is within minutes walk of Kilbogget Park.\n\nThe bright and spacious interior is well laid out with a wonderful balance of living and sleeping accommodation. The ground floor comprises of a large light filled porch, hallway, guest wc, living room, office/study room, kitchen/breakfast/dining room and sun room. On the first floor there are four double size bedrooms, en-suite, and main family bathroom.\n\nThe location is second to none; situated off the highly convenient Churchview Road, with local shopping facilities at Killiney Shopping Centre, schools and recreational activities all close at hand. Kilbogget Park is around the corner providing a kids play park, duck ponds, a running track and park walks. There are highly regarded schools nearby including Johnstown Boys and Girls National School, Clonkeen Boys Secondary School, and St. Joseph of Cluny Secondary School. There is a range of public transport links including the DART, N11, M50 and the Cherrywood Luas stop close by.
