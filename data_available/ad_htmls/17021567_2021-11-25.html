    The Corner House is such a warm and comfortable home, located in the heart of Shinrone in an idyllic village setting. In recent years it was transformed into a very comfortable family home with a complete renovation. \nA fine corner building occupying a perfect location in the village, it offers Village life with all services and amenities within walking distance, modern and spacious accommodation all in very good order, and a small split level rear garden which has been decked and gritted. A south facing, private and secure space catching all the sun rays. \nAccommodation comprises an entrance hallway, a sitting room with an open fireplace, a large kitchen with plenty of storage space which opens onto a dining area with sliding doors to rear garden/deck, and a very useful ground floor shower room. At first floor level there are four bright bedrooms, and a family bathroom. \nThe village of Shinrone is convenient to both Roscrea and Birr. It has a great local Primary School, a Centra Store, a community centre and sports available locally. Lough Derg, the Slieve Bloom mountain range with great biking trails, the wonderful grounds at Birr Castle are all to hand.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 5.8m x 2.3m.
