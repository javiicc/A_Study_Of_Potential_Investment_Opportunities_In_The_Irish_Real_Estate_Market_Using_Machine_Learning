    This attractive property has a most appealing and private setting on the edge of the village of Manor Kilbride, located on a short cul-de-sac with only 9 other residences. Nicely tucked away from the main road the property enjoys a beautiful rural aspect while being a short walk from the village shop and local primary school. \r\nThe Residence stands on a 1-acre plot with generous front gardens and parking area. The rear gardens look onto established grazing ground without any fear of being overlooked in the future, and the spacious front lawn enjoys uninterrupted views towards the Wicklow Hills.\r\nBuilt within the past 20 years the Residence is modern throughout and very well presented, with large window sizes to capture the appealing views while allowing in a wash of natural light. The original 4-Bed residence has the benefit of a converted attic used for storage space which is accessed with a feature spiral stair.\r\nRoom sizes are well proportioned, with a particularly attractive Lounge and interconnecting Kitchen and Dining spaces.\r\nManor Kilbride is an established commuter location with easy access to South Dublin, within 12Km of City West Business Campus and Leixlip, Celbridge, Sandyford and Nass all within 20Km. The village is serviced with a local Primary School, Village Shop with petrol pumps, Community Hall and RC Church with COI Church nearby but the strongest feature must be the proximity to the Blessington Lakes with its associated sporting options including the excellent Blessington greenway development, to the Wicklow Hills on your door step for recreational walks, to a choice of sporting clubs including local golf clubs, and only 6Km to Blessington Town for local shopping and community services. Truly an excellent location for family life.\r\n\r\nAccommodation\r\nEntrance Hall:\r\nLarge entrance hall with spiral staircase to dormer level and double doors to Lounge.\r\nLarge walk in Hotpress with range of shelving in the inner hall.\r\n\r\nLiving Room:\r\nFeature French doors to front gardens. Uninterrupted views towards the Wicklow hills. Feature stone fireplace with stove inset.\r\n\r\nKitchen:\r\nFull range of eye and floor level units. Ample worktop areas and an attractive island unit. Tiled floor.\r\n\r\nUtility room:\r\nPlumbed for washing machine. Tiled floor.
