    Oates Breheny Group are very proud to present to the market 'St Teresa's'. To the rear of the property lies a spacious fully fitted kitchen which extends over to an elevated dining area along with a newly fitted utility room. There are three generous bedrooms along with a newly refurbished main bathroom completing the accommodation. Mature gardens to front and rear with an added bonus of excellent off street parking.
