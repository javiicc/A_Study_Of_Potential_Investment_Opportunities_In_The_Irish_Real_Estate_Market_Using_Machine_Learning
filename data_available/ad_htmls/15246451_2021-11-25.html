    Idyllic bungalow residence within walking distance of the beautiful sandy Dunworley beach.\r\nSituated in a peaceful wooded setting on a gorgeous plot of land, this property is 1 mile from Barryroe Co-op, Barryroe National School and within ten minutes drive of Clonakilty, Timoleague and Courtmacsherry. \r\n\r\nWith so much to offer here this elevated c.0.85 acre plot has plenty of nooks and crannies for the children to explore and a wonderful private deck to the rear, ideal for sitting out in the evenings. \r\nInside the property has an easy, manageable layout, extending to c. 1,030 SqFt and includes 3 double bedrooms (1 ensuite) and good sized living accommodation including a kitchen, bathroom and spacious sitting room. \r\nThis bungalow dwelling would suit first time buyers or someone wishing to trade down to an easy to manage house in a convenient location. \r\n\r\nArea Information\r\n\r\nLocated less than 1 hour drive from Cork City & International airport. Nearby Courtmacsherry is a well-known as an international sea angling centre, the harbour boasts an ever-increasing traffic of all type of boats from pleasure to yachts to fishing boats & has become increasingly popular over the last few decades. \r\nBusy in summer yet quiet in winter & the offseason, the area harbours a very distinct atmosphere which is welcoming to all. There are some fine amenity beaches within walking distance & further afield are the much renowned beaches of Dunworley, Broad Strand & Blind Strand. \r\nLocal amenities include the now famous Seven Heads Walks, various wildlife activities in the estuary & beyond & shore fishing in a variety of areas. \r\nRiver fishing for salmon, sea trout & brown trout can be availed of in the nearby Argideen River. \r\nNearby activities include tennis courts, horse riding stables, sailing & dinghy school including wind surfing & power boating. \r\n\r\n\r\nThe parish of Barryroe hosts a variety of different activities & organisations which are very active throughout the year. The local primary school is well regarded & there is a regular bus service to the secondary schools in either Clonakilty or Bandon. One of the best supermarkets in West Cork is located nearby at Barryroe Co-op.\r\n\r\n\r\nAccommodation c. 96 m\xc2\xb2/ 1030 ft\xc2\xb2\r\nPorch and Entrance Hall 1.7 m x 3 m & 5 m x 0.95 m\r\nBright spacious entrance with sliding doors from front porch to hallway. Tiled throughout with recessed lighting. There is also access to a storage closet in the hallway.\r\n \r\nSitting Room 4.2 m x 3.7 m\r\nLight and bright sitting room with large west facing window. Feature fireplace and polished timber floor. Stylish fitted shelving and recessed lighting.\r\n\r\nKitchen / Dining room 5.4 m x 3.4 m \r\nBright kitchen/dining room with dual aspect and large windows. Fitted kitchen with integrated oven and dishwasher. Tiled floor and recessed lighting.\r\n\r\nBathroom 2.3 m x 2.1 m & 1 m x 1.2 m\r\nSpacious bathroom with WC, wash hand basin and access to hot press.\r\n\r\nBedroom One 4 m x 3.44 m\r\nBright double bedroom with wooden floor and recessed lighting.\r\n\r\nBedroom One - Dressing Room 1.55 m x 3.9 m\r\nSpacious dressing room.\r\n\r\nBedroom One - Shower Room 1.55 m x 3.9 m\r\nShower room tiled throughout with walk in shower and recessed lighting.\r\n\r\nBedroom Two 2.7 m x 3.55 m\r\nBright west facing bedroom with large window, timber floors and recessed lighting.\r\n\r\nBedroom Three 3.1 m x 2.5 m\r\nBright west facing bedroom with large window, timber floors and recessed lighting. \r\n \r\nServices\r\nAll main services are connected including water, electricity and telephone services. Heating is via oil fired central heating and a multi fuel open fireplace. Drainage via septic tank.\r\n\r\nOutside\r\nThe property has entrance gates leading up to a gravel surfaced car parking area. There is a large tree lined lawned area to the front and side. To the rear is a a fantastic wooded section with plenty of mature shrubs, trees and vegetable garden area. Also to the rear of the house is a lovely elevated timber deck. \r\n\r\nDirections\r\nThe property is situated on the southern side of School cross, which is 1 mile west of Barryroe Co op on the road to Clonakilty.
