    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    This property comprises an attractive, single storey residence, which stands on it's own grounds of circa 0.5 of an acre located in a quiet rural setting. The residence has recently been refurbished and is in very good structural and decorative repair throughout. The property has dual back boiler and oil fired central heating and double glazing. \nAccommodation includes, entrance hall, sitting room, kitchen, living room, conservatory, 2 ground floor bedrooms and an attic bedroom, utility room and a wet room.\nThere is a range of stores and sheds to the rear of the residence and an attractive yard. The property is located at the end of a quiet laneway and is 5 Kms from Mullinahone and 25 Kms from Kilkenny City.
