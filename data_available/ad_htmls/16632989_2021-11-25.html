    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    This tastefully renovated traditional thatched roof cottage with slated hip roof extensions to the rear is located on a mature ample site of 0.25 hectares or 0.62 acres at Slinaunroe, Clogher, Westport, F28 TW97.\n\nThis attractive property is centrally located approximately 8.3km from Westport, 10.1 km from Newport and 15.3km from Castlebar and is easily accessible off the R311 County Road that interconnects Castlebar and Newport.\n\nThe property comprises of:\nGround floor: entrance porch, kitchen cum living room with solid fuel stove and stone fireplace, sun lounge with access to garden, bedroom with patio door to garden, toilet/shower room, office/storage room and hotpress.\nFirst floor: stairwell from kitchen to loft room and storage area. Stairwell from office to loft bedroom with toilet and WHB and storage area.\n\nThis property is completed with tiled and timber flooring throughout.\n\nThe property is serviced with water, electricity, septic tank and solid fuel heating system. (with provision for connection to oil fired heating system)\n\nThe large stone built shed with galvanized roof is located adjacent to the main residence and is completed with electricity and electrical sockets. The polytunnel is located within the site area and is suitable for the growing of fruit, vegetables and plants. \n\nThis property is located in an idyllic rural setting with the convenience of Castlebar, Westport and Newport town within a brief distance.\n\nViewing Strictly by Appointment only.\n\nRef No: A1034 BER: F
