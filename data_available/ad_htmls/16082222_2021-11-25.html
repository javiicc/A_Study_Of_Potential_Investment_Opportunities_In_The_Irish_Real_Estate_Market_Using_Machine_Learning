Located off the Main Dingle road on a quiet country lane. Ideally suited for First time buyer/family/holiday home. \r\n\r\nClose to Inch Beach c. 12 km, \r\n\r\nWalking distance of Fybagh National School c. 500 Meters\r\n\r\nConvenient to Tralee c. 25 km, Dingle c. 34 km, Killarney c. 31 km, Kerry Airport c. 23 km, Killorglin, c. 19 km\r\n\r\nThe property has the benefit of a number of outbuildings and attached shed, landscaped gardens to front. \r\n\r\nAcc: Entrance Hall, Sitting Room, Kitchen/Dining Room, Living Room, Toilet/Shower, 5 Bedrooms Bathroom\r\nServices: Mains Water, Septic Tank, Oil Heating\r\n\r\nAbout Castlemaine:\r\n\r\nCastlemaine (Caisle\xc3\xa9\xc3\x82\xc2\xa0n na Maigne - Castle on the River Maine) KEEL and BOOLTEENS (Na Buailt\xc3\xa9\xc3\x82\xc2\xacn\xc3\xa9\xc3\x82\xc2\xac - Forge).\r\n\r\nExperience the rural idyll that is the Keel, Boolteens and Castlemaine area with its backdrop of the Slieve Mish Mountain Range, a vista of the imposing McGillycuddy Reeks, location of Ireland's highest peak Carrantuohill, and bordered on its southern edge by the River Maine and Castlemaine Harbour. Stunning scenery along the Short Mountain Road and B\xc3\xa9\xc3\x82\xc2\xb2thar-Na-gCloch give views over the entire area. Here lies a treasure trove of walking routes from mountain tracks and leafy country lanes to marked forest loops. For the more energetic, take a hike up Caherconree Mountain, on top of which lies one of the rare examples of an inland promontory fort. Enjoy salmon and trout fishing on the River Maine, observe the mussel farming in Castlemaine Harbour and discover the enchantment of The Quay at Lachtacalla with its view of mountains, sea and farmlands. Here the changing hues on the heather and fern clad mountains, the plaintive call of seabirds and the bleating of sheep serve to envelop you with its irresistible charm. Castlemaine is famed in song as the birth place of the 'Wild Colonial Boy' - Jack Duggan, the infamous Australian outlaw. The village itself developed at a bridging point where a Castle traversed the River Maine (the only such example in the country). Visit the historic Mass Rock at Killoclohane Wood, scene of the martyrdom of Fr. Thaddeus Moriarty or enjoy a looped forest walk. View the Ogham Stones at Ardcanaught or simply relax and enjoy a chat with the friendly locals. Dine with a difference at the bohemian style Phoenix Vegetarian Restaurant in Keel, where you can learn belly dancing and salsa dancing. Try your hand at quilting or felting workshops at the Sewing Shed in Keel, a craft enthusiasts dream! The area has many warm pubs where locals will entertain you. Friendly bed and breakfasts and farmhouses are here for you to stay among the natives and experience rural Irish life. This is the gateway to the Dingle Peninsula and within easy reach of the Ring of Kerry.\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nRoom Details: \r\nEntrance Hall: 1.514 x 1.261\r\nWith tiled flooring\r\n\r\nSitting Room: 4.464 x 4.131 (water views and McGillycuddy reeks)\r\nWith tiled flooring, inset stove, feature stone fireplace\r\n\r\nKitchen/Dining Room: 2.974 x 6.374 (Views of Inch Beach)\r\nWith tiled flooring, Built in eye and ground level presses, stainless steel sink unit, tiled kitchen surround, mulberry solid fuel stove. \r\n\r\nHot Press with immersion \r\n\r\nToilet/Shower: 1.80 x 1.860\r\nWith WC, WHB, Shower, Fully tiled floor and walls\r\n\r\nLiving Room: 2.944 x 4.397 (water views and McGillycuddy reeks)\r\nWith laminate flooring, open fireplace with stone surround\r\n\r\nFirst Floor: \r\nLanding: 2.58 x 2.42\r\nWith carpet flooring\r\n\r\nBedroom 1: 2.990 x 4.421 (water views and McGillycuddy reeks)\r\nWith carpet flooring, double bedroom, high ceiling\r\n\r\nBedroom 2: 3.51 x1.655 (water views and McGillycuddy reeks)\r\nWith carpet flooring, middle single bedroom\r\n\r\nBedroom 3: 2.405 x 4.460 (water views and McGillycuddy reeks)\r\nWith carpet flooring, double bedroom, high ceiling\r\n\r\nBedroom 4: 3.113 x 3.319 (Views of Inch Beach)\r\nWith carpet flooring, double bedroom\r\n\r\nBedroom 5: 2.923 x 3.309\r\nWith carpet flooring, double bedroom\r\n\r\nBathroom: 2.219 x 2.571\r\nWith WC, WHB, Bath, Tiled flooring and walls\r\n\r\nOutside: \r\nAttached Shed/Utility: 5.744 x 3.633\r\nWith concrete floor, plumbed washing machine, electricity. \r\n\r\nAccess from yard and house door into garage\r\n\r\nWorkshop/Garage: 5.232 x 6.396\r\nWith concrete flooring, pit, corrugated roof, block built, electricity. \r\n\r\n\r\nDISCLAIMER:\r\n\r\nJim Burns & Co.
