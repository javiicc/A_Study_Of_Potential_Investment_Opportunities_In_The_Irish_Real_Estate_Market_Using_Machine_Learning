    Saint Mary's Place is very conveniently located just off Irishtown in Clonmel within a short walking distance of the town centre and all amenities therein. This charming mid-terrace single storey residence has the benefit of a large west-facing paved garden to the rear. The accommodation comprises a hallway, sitting room, kitchen/dining, 2 bedrooms and bathroom. This would be an ideal property for a first time buyer, a downsizer or an investor given its' excellent location. The Londis Shop is on the corner and St. Mary's Church is easily accessible. There is on-street residents car parking on Saint Mary's Place.
