      RELOCATING TO DUBLIN? Borgman Earls are delighted to offer this 3 Bedroom Duplex Penthouse to the Dublin Rental Market. No thought or expense has been spared in its completion, fit out and stylish interior which runs throughout. \r\n\r\nThe property is AVAILABLE IMMEDIATELY and includes two secure underground car spaces. This Impressive Penthouse, extending to approx. 202 square metres, captures stunning views across the City, including the Aviva Stadium, Grand Canal Dock & the Dublin Mountains. It is located within Shelboure Plaza Development which is ideally positioned in the heart of Dublin's South Docks. The location is now one of Dublin's most distinctive urban quarters, within walking distance to Grand Canal Dock, The Aviva Stadium, The Grand Canal Theatre, Ballsbridge Village & the IFSC. Dublin Airport is easily accessed as the Port Tunnel is less than a 10 minute drive away. There are a host of renowned & high-end restaurants, cafes, and local amenities close by including: Lotts & Co; Juniors Restaurant, Paul's Pizza & Charlotte Quay to name but a few. Sportsco is a fantastic sports facility which is a 5 minute walk away and which offers a state of the art gym, pool, fitness classes & racquet sports. \r\n\r\nAccommodation in brief comprises: impressive hallway with solid wood flooring throughout. There are two luxurious double bedrooms leading off, one of which is ensuite. An additional bathroom is also available. The owners have created a home office with built in storage and desk units. A Cloak Room / Storage Area is also located at entrance level. A solid wood staircase leads to the extensive living area on the upper floor. The lounge / dining room is beautifully furnished, & and includes feature gas fire, access to the balcony which boasts panoramic & tireless views of Dublin's City scape. The high gloss contemporary kitchen includes a full range of modern appliances. The master bedroom can be found on the upper level and includes a full ensuite with bath & shower. In addition there is a walk in wardrobe providing excellent storage space. A large pantry also leads off the upper landing. The development offers a state-of-the-art energy saving technology for heating and is fully alarmed. \r\n\r\nThis property must be viewed to be fully appreciated.
