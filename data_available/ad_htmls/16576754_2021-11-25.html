    No. 185 Beechwood Court enjoys a wonderful position on the top floor with stunning views of the Sugar Loaf Mountain and coastal views from Howth to Dun Laoghaire. Beechwood Court is a well-established development built by Jackson Homes in 2006. No. 185 features bright and spacious accommodation extending to 72sq.m/775sq.ft. approximately, and it is presented in excellent decorative condition throughout with stylish interiors. The accommodation with large picture windows comprises an entrance hall with hot press, kitchen, large bright living/dining room, two double bedrooms (1 ensuite) and a family bathroom. The balcony (12sq.m/129sq.ft) is accessed via the living/dining room and one of the bedrooms. There is a roof garden, one underground parking space, visitor parking, landscaped communal gardens and an onsite cr\xc3\xa9\xc3\x82\xc2\xa8che. \n \nBeechwood Court is conveniently located off the Stillorgan Road; with the N11 and QBC within immediate access. Stillorgan Village is nearby with a range of amenities including the shopping centre, restaurants, shops, library and local schools. There are several transport links, including the LUAS at Sandyford, and the No. 46a bus route on the QBC.
