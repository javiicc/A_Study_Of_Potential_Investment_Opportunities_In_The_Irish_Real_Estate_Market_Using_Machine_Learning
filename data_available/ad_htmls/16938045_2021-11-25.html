    Mason Estates are delighted to present 74 Cherrywood to the market. A lovely bright detached two-bedroom bungalow with the perfect south-westerly facing aspect to the rear capturing the afternoon and late evening sunshine. There is a lawned front garden with ample off-street parking and wide side access from either side of property to a low-maintenance rear garden. Cherrywood is a lovely established neighbourhood with No. 74 located in a quiet cul-de-sac within the development and is an ideal purchase for those wishing to downsize or indeed for those looking to buy their first home.\r\n\r\nIn brief, the accommodation comprises an l-shaped entrance hall leading to a lovely bright spacious sitting room to the front with 3 windows overlooking the front garden, fitted kitchen, two double bedrooms and bathroom/w.c., The heating is gas fired and all windows are uPVC double glazed. The location is one of great convenience with a choice of shopping amenities easily accessible and well as schools and public transport.\r\n\r\nThe LUAS at Cherrywood is approx. a 15-minute walk as well as the New Cherrywood Town Centre which will bring a huge number of facilities to the location. Shopping amenities include Cornelscourt Shopping Centre, Tesco in Ballybrack, Killiney Shopping Centre and Dun Laoghaire. There is a good choice of buses servicing the area and the M.50 is just a short distance away.\r\n\r\nACCOMMODATION:\r\n\r\nL-SHAPED ENTRANCE HALL: 2.98m x 1.14m & 3.00m x 0.82m\r\nWith cloakroom and access to attic, wood floor.\r\n\r\nSITTING ROOM: 3.56m x 6.64m\r\nBright spacious sitting room with 3 windows overlooking the front garden, central open fireplace/marble surround. \r\n\r\nKITCHEN: 3.76m x 2.90m\r\nWith range of fitted eye-level and floor cupboards, breakfast bar, electric cooker and overhead extractor fan, plumbed for washing machine and dish washer. Tiled floor and splash-back. \r\n\r\nBEDROOM 1: 2.67m x 3.85m\r\nDouble bedroom with wood floor. \r\n\r\nBEDROOM 2: 3.30m x 2.64m\r\nDouble bedroom with wood floor.\r\n\r\nBATHROOM: 1.90m x 2.68m\r\nFully tiled bathroom incorporating bath/shower attachment/shower screen, w.c., wash-hand-basin, wall mounted mirror and stainless-steel towel rail. Hot press.\r\n\r\nGARDENS:\r\n\r\nGarden to the front laid out mostly in lawn. Gated side access on either side of house.\r\nAmple off-street parking.
