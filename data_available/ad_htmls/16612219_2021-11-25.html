    Hunters Estate Agent is truly delighted to present to the market this very elegant residence superbly located close to Sandymount village and Sandymount Strand.\r\n\r\n12 Londonbridge Road is a very fine three/four-bedroom mid terrace family home that provides balanced light filled interiors extending to c. 166sq.m/1,786sq.ft further complemented by an external home office of c. 21sq.m/226sq.ft. The residence has been tastefully upgraded, remodelled and extended to provide for all that a discerning purchaser may require for modern day living in a home of distinction. \r\n\r\nThe accommodation comprises of a large entrance hall with drawing room and dining room off, an inner hall leads to the guest cloakroom, laundry room, and a large kitchen with breakfast and family area in the rear return. Upstairs there are 3 double bedrooms, and a generous family bathroom and rising to the attic level there is a generous space, in use as a bedroom with an ensuite. To the rear there is a low maintenance garden with a westerly aspect. The garden also features an external office space with a kitchen area and a shower room. There is pedestrian access to the rear of the property from here.\r\n\r\nThe property enjoys one of Dublin's most convenient and popular locations, having all of Sandymount's many amenities within walking distance. Sandymount Village and Ballsbridge are within close proximity and offer a vast array of coffee shops, restaurants, bars and boutiques. Some of Dublin\xe2\x82\xacTM premier schools including St. Michael's, Muckross Park, Teresians and Blackrock College, to name but a few, are all easily accessible. Public transport is well catered for with Lansdowne Road DART station only a short stroll away as well as multiple bus routes.\r\n\r\nLondonbridge Road is also just a short distance from some of Dublin's principal places of business such as Fitzwilliam Square, Merrion Square, Baggot Street, Leeson Street and the IT Hub at Grand Canal Dock. Numerous sports grounds are also close by including the RDS and Lansdowne Lawn Tennis Club, while The Aviva Stadium and Lansdowne Football Club are both within striking distance.\r\n\r\nViewing is essential and highly recommended.
