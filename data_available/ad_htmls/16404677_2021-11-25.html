    Set in a wonderful sylvan setting and approached via a private avenue, 1 Mount Pleasant Villas enjoys a tranquil rural style environs yet only located within a leisurely stroll of Bray Town centre. This elegant double fronted Victorian 4 bedroom residence together with a 1 bedroom self-contained Studio Apartment has been carefully restored and renovated to combine the olde world charm with the benefits of modern living. Tastefully appointed many of the period features are intact. Presented in excellent condition, the exceedingly bright interiors extend to c. 208 sq m (2,238 sq ft) and comprise well balanced accommodation with rooms of fine dimensions and feature high ceilings. Entrance Hall, Guest Cloakroom, Drawing Room, Dining Room, Kitchen, Utility, 4 Bedrooms (2 En Suites) and the family Bathroom. The Studio Apartment offers an open plan Lounge/Kitchen, 1 Bedroom and Shower Room. The cultivated gardens surround the house and enjoy a totally private aspect and an all day sunny orientation. Ideally situated close to all the amenities offered by Bray Town Centre, easy access is afforded to the DART, N11 and M50.
