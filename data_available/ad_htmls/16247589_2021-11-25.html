    Perched on a stunning elevated landscaped and tree bound mature 1 acre site, this fantastic 5 bedroom family home is a must view to truly appreciate the abundance of top quality features experienced throughout the bright and spacious living accommodation. As you enter the stone walled entrance, the sweeping tarmacadam drive leads to the round stone faced facade, to the side double garage and to the rear. On entry the vaulted ceiling porch lead glass doors lead to the main entrance hallway with the L-shaped centre carpeted stairs to the first floor. At ground floor level is the main reception with open hearth fireplace and views onto the manicured gardens and decking, the split level open plan kitchen/ living/ dining with traditional kitchen units and stone surround fireplace connect to the outside decking and gardens via sliding doors. Off the kitchen is the spacious utility and completing the ground floor accommodation are three bedrooms, 1 en-suite, and main bathroom.\r\nTo the first floor are a further two bedrooms, both double, with the master boasting a walk in wardrobe and fully fitted en-suite. It's the incredible family room to the first floor that is simply outstanding. This vast open space, with it's solid fuel stove, raised ceiling, kitchen/ prep area and views onto the garden rounds off a well thought out family home design.\r\nOutside, immaculate lawns, mature trees, raised shrubbery beds, dry stone wall with brick borders, a double garage extending to over 1,400 sq ft are just some of the many features worth mentioning.\r\nWith Knockanean School just 150m, Roslevan and Ennis Town just a 5 minute drive, the M18 Motorway equally just 5 minutes along with amazing countryside setting this house is the ideal family home purchase. Viewings are strictly by prior appointment with sole selling agents.
