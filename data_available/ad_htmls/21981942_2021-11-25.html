      Location

      Conveniently located only 5 minutes away from Docklands rapid rail station (DART) and being right next to spencer dock light rail station (LUAS). By foot you can reach Point Square in a minute, get to your office at the IFSC in 4 minutes or be in the town centre in less than 30 minutes. Dublin airport is just an easy 20-minute taxi away. Not to mention, Quayside Quarter is walking distance of the leading Dublin City landmarks including the Conference Centre Dublin, St. Stephen\xe2\x80\x99s Green, the Aviva Stadium at Lansdowne Road, the 3 Arena and Trinity College. Pubs, restaurants and state-of-the-art venues like 3Arena and BGE Theatre have also ensured the Docklands is more than just a commercial hub, but a place to socialise and enjoy world class events. Quayside Quarter sits in the heart of these Docklands, a thriving centre for commerce, a hub for innovation, world leading technology and financial institutions. Part of Dublin Landings, an ambitious 5.8-acre waterfront regeneration project already home to icons such as WeWork, The Central Bank of Ireland and the National Treasury Management Agency (NTMA), this vibrant community is bringing a new energising spirit to the area. The neighbourhood draws its inspiration from the fabric of Dublin and the rich industrial heritage of the docklands - infused with contemporary Scandinavian architectural accents and Brooklyn loft-style design. Providing squares of curated wild landscapes nestle next to bustling streets of restaurants, caf\xc3\xa9s and stores, anchored by a riverfront boutique hotel creating a vibrant new neighbourhood.
