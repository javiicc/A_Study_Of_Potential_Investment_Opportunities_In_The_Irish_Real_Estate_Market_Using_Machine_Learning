    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Nestled under a mature canopy and located within this peaceful scenic location, Campbells are delighted to have been favoured with instructions to bring this beautiful building to market. The property oozes character and charm and was previously a shop and pub in the past and comes boasting over 2.4 acres of mature gardens and trees. This property also enjoys a share in commonage to the northern end of the property which equates to a share of approximately 12 acres.\n\nThe old Burtonport to Derry railway line is immediately to the rear of the property and has been incorporated into the gardens of same. Entrance into the gardens are via the old railway gate with the old round pillars still evident today.\n\nRarely does such a charming property come on to the open market and you could not ask for a more tranquil setting, with over 2 Acres of mature gardens and trees.\n\nGround floor accommodation consists of two living rooms, dining room, kitchen utility, bathroom and porch while first floor accommodation contains four bedrooms (one en-suite) and bathroom.\n\nViewing comes highly recommended and by strict appointment with Campbells only.\n\nOur Ref: K347\n\n\n\nKitchen - 9.3m (30'6") x 2.4m (7'10")\nTiled Floor, Fully Fitted\n\nDining Room - 3.9m (12'10") x 4.6m (15'1")\nCarpet Floor, Open Fireplace\n\nLiving Room - 3.6m (11'10") x 3.9m (12'10")\nCarpet Floor, Open Fireplace\n\nSitting Room - 4.5m (14'9") x 5.2m (17'1")\nCarpet Floor, Open Fireplace\n\nConservatory - 8.1m (26'7") x 1.5m (4'11")\nTiled Floor\n\nUtility Room - 4.5m (14'9") x 2.9m (9'6")\nTiled Floor, Shower\n\nBathroom - 2.3m (7'7") x 1.9m (6'3")\nFully Tilled, WC, WHB\n\nBedroom 1 - 2.7m (8'10") x 3.8m (12'6")\nCarpet Floor\n\nBedroom 2 - 3m (9'10") x 2.5m (8'2")\nCarpet Floor\n\nBedroom 3 - 2.5m (8'2") x 3m (9'10")\nCarpet Floor\n\nBedroom 3 En-Suite - 2m (6'7") x 1.6m (5'3")\nFully Tilled, WC, WHB\n\nBedroom 4 - 2.5m (8'2") x 3m (9'10")\nCarpet Floor\n\nUpstairs Bathroom - 3m (9'10") x 2.4m (7'10")\nFully Tilled, WC, WHB, Shower\n\nUpstairs Hallway - 9.1m (29'10") x 0.6m (2'0")\nCarpet Floor\n\n\n\nNotice\nPlease note we have not tested any apparatus, fixtures, fittings, or services.
