    Circa 1.4 acre of beautifully tended gardens in an unrivalled setting overlooking Countryside. With over 230sq m of generous 2 storey house, this is a large family home. This well-presented property is in a private and tranquil setting. This is a wonderful location for those who enjoy outdoor pursuits for fishing, golfing, hill walking, and mountain biking. The property is situated in a rural but central location with expansive views of the country side close to Ballaghaderreen (6 miles), just ten minutes from Knock Airport, a thirty five minute drive to Sligo and Castlebar, Ninety minutes to Galway and just a two hour drive to Dublin. \n\nThe accommodation on the ground floor consists of a bright entrance hallway which leads to an open kitchen/living area and a sitting room. Running off the kitchen there is a utility and a conservatory which is being used as a dining room. There is also a bedroom with an en suite on the ground floor. Upstairs, there are four bedrooms, two of which have en suites.\n\nThe gardens surrounding the property have been maintained very well.
