    RAY COOKE AUCTIONEERS take great pleasure in introducing this magnificent, extended three bedroom semi detached property to the market ideally positioned within the quaint cul de sac of Beverly Lawns in the heart of Knocklyon, Dublin 16. On a transport note there is a choice of bus routes directly to the front of the development and the M50 Motorway is only a stones throw away. \r\n\r\nInternal living accommodation spans to c. 115 sq.m and downstairs comprises of entrance porch, lounge, living room/home office(garage conversion), kitchen with separate utility space, shower room and rear sun room. Upstairs you will find three bedrooms, the family bathroom and master bedroom ensuite.\r\n\r\nThe peaceful cul de sac suits growing families down to the ground. The paved front offers off street parking and the recently upgraded rear garden boasts an idyllic, sunny westerly orientation.\r\n\r\nRecently sales in Beverly have been snapped up and there is likely to be no change here.
