    19 Sapphire is a large (c. 110sqm) and beautifully appointed three-bedroom south-westerly apartment located in the highly sought after development of The Grange. Known as one of the landmark developments in South Dublin, this third floor apartment is situated with every conceivable amenity on the doorstep. This property is in absolute turn-key condition and will appeal to many looking for a substantial apartment with a large balcony facing the sun. \n\nThe superb accommodation comprises a welcoming entrance hallway with the main bathroom just off. Three double bedrooms, all have access onto the large balcony. The master bedroom also enjoys a high quality ensuite. The large open plan living / dining room is flooded with natural light and is complimented with the wrap-around balcony outside together with a super south-west facing orientation. The kitchen is modern and offers an abundance of fitted units and appliances coupled with excellent counter top space. There is secure underground parking in this development where there is a designated car space for this apartment along with visitor spaces too.\n\nSet in a strategic high profile location off the N11, The Grange is on a quality bus corridor with excellent bus services to the city centre; convenient to the Luas line at Sandyford; within walking distance of Stillorgan village and within easy reach of Dundrum, Blackrock, Foxrock and Donnybrook villages. It is located on one of the main arterial routes into Dublin City Centre with easy access to the M50, Dublin Airport and surrounding counties. The area is host to a wealth of amenities including local shops, Dundrum Town Centre, Airfield Trust, Leopardstown Racecourse and UCD Belfield to name but a few. The management company who oversee The Grange are also located on site, a huge benefit for anyone who lives here.\n\nEntrance Hall 3.63m x 3.93m.
