    No. 33 The Printworks is a fantastic two-bedroom apartment conveniently positioned on the ground floor within this secure gated development. Centrally located in Bray, residents here enjoy superb access to all local amenities, attractions, and transport connections. This modern apartment is immaculately presented and ready for immediate occupation. In addition to the good decoration, the property benefits from an allocated parking space and its own private south/westerly garden, which is unique for apartment living.\n\nPassing through the communal hallway, the front door is to the right-hand side at the end of the corridor. Internally the layout comprises of an entrance hall with a spacious storage press / hot-press and large bathroom. To the right of the hall are two bedrooms, one double and one single. Both bedrooms overlook the communal garden while the principal bedroom enjoys the convenience of an en-suite shower room. To the rear of the apartment there is a naturally bright open plan living/kitchen/diner with direct access to the garden. \n\nThe Printworks is a gated community with both pedestrian and vehicular access points. The development is within striking distance of Castle Street Shopping Centre and Bray s main street which offers an additional variety of shops and eateries. There are a host of well-regarded junior and secondary schools in the vicinity and there are several sport and leisure facilities nearby. For walking and adventure enthusiasts the People s Park, charming promenade and Bray Head are all easily accessible. The location also prides itself on an efficient and excellent transport service, including the N11/M50 routeway, multiple Dublin Bus routes, Aircoach and Bray DART station.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 1.22m x 3.37m.\n\nKitchen Living Dining Room 6.18m x 3.66m.\n\nBed 1 3.58m x 4.01m.\n\nEnsuite 2.25m x 1.22m.\n\nBed 2 2.32m x 2.90m.\n\nBathroom 2.25m x 2.27m.\n\nStorage Press 1.26m x 1.87m.
