    We are proud to bring to the market this beautiful family home in Ballyferriter West, Dingle with spectacular Sea Views and in immaculate condition. The Property consists of Entrance Hall way, Sitting Room with feature Fireplace and Stove, Conservatory to the front, Kitchen/Dining room and 3 bedrooms with 2 ensuite and a Main Bathroom. There is a concrete shed to the rear. This would make an ideal family home or holiday home. The property is located on the Wild Atlantic Way and only a 1/2 mile to Ballyferriter Village, 2 miles to Beaches and 2 miles to Ceann Sibeal Golf Club. Dingle Town is only 8 miles away with all its bars, restaurants and Tourist attractions.
