    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Superb 3 bedroomed detached bungalow style residence which was newly constructed in 2019 and extending to circa 2000 sq. ft. It offers spacious, well apportioned accommodation and is offered complete with quality fixture, fittings etc. It occupies a prominent corner plot with mature gardens and generous off street parking.
