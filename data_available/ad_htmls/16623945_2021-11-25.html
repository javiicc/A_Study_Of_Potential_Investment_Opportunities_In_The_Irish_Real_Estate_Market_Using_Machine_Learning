    Hume Auctioneers - We Get The Pick Of The Crop Deceptively spacious this beautiful three bedroom bungalow sits on a magnificent site of circa 1.1 acres. The property has been recently fully refurbished and now boasts a host of modern features. Situated on the outskirts of Rathdowney town and just a few minutes from the services at Junction 3 on the M8, the property offers immense convenience. Rathdowney offers excellent facilities with good shops and some of the highest regarded schools in all of County Laois. Drive through the private gate and the sheer size of the site is immediately impressive. Fully fenced in, the grounds stretch to over 1.1 acres with lawns to front and rear and a huge paddock surrounding. The property also enjoys a concrete built garage. Step inside and the beautiful fusion of old and new is truly stunning. The sitting room enjoys n open fireplace and dual aspect windows flooding this cosiest of room with natural light. The three bedrooms are exceptionally spacious and are freshly finished with high quality flooring. The heart of this home is the beautiful and modern kitchen. With a beautiful picture window overlooking the surrounding countryside the layout maximises the use of light and a large array of modern cabinets compliments this beautiful room The family bathroom is massive and fully tiled and boasts a large bath and electric shower The inner hall offers more circulation space.
