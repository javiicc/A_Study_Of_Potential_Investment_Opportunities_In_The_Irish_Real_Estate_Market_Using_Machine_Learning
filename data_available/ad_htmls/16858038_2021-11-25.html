    Joe Organ Auctioneers are proud to introduce 222 Fernwood, Glyntown, Glanmire to the property market. Immaculately decorated with solid wood flooring, wall to wall slide robes with two mirror doors in the centre, an attractive colour palette on walls and matching curtains on the windows and door and finished with pleated blinds giving.
