    A true gem in the heart of Moycullen village, this charming 3 bedroom semi-detached property is neatly tucked away in the lovely mature and popular development of Pairc na gCaor.\r\n\r\nFrom first sight the house is well presented where a sheltered entrance leads to a bright and airy hallway lit up by a stunning arched window to the front. As you move through the newly carpeted hallway the convenient under stairs cloakroom guides you towards the living area to the back of the property. On the right is the living room, with feature open fireplace, the perfect place to relax in the evenings to the backdrop of the mature garden. The kitchen/dining area is to the left with fully tiled flooring, this area spans the full width of the house, with the ample solid pine fitted units to the front and open fire place to the centre of in the dining area. The rear garden access is from here through a sun porch to the side.\r\n\r\nThe ground floor accommodation is completed by a large study or home office, a bright and spacious room which overlooks the world going by to the front of the house. This fine room is ideally suited as a fourth bedroom. \r\n\r\nThe properties three bedrooms are all located on the first floor, one with built in storage and all with laminate flooring and brightly painted, to contrast well with the white doors and architrave. The bathroom, with separate shower area, and hot press is also accessible from the landing. \r\n\r\nExternally, mature hedging and trees in the rear garden provides excellent privacy which is another attraction of this home. From the paved patio area, a path leads to the timber shed at the bottom of the garden. To the front a green lawn runs along the driveway to the front paving where a low double stone wall bounds the property. \r\n\r\nPairc Na gCaor is a mature residential development of various house types located in the heart of Moycullen Village. This tree lined estate is very well maintained and is very popular both with those looking to down size and with young families starting out in life. This location will not disappoint with such convenience to all of Moycullen villages amenities such as shops, restaurants, bars, school and a just 10 minutes drive to the city.\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nHallway\r\nNewly carpeted, feature arch window, under stairs cloakroom.\r\n\r\nKitchen/ Dining Room\r\nKitchen area with solid pine fitted units, integrated hob and oven, tiled backsplash, feature open fireplace with back boiler in the dining area. \r\n\r\nSun Porch\r\nPositioned off the kitchen/dining area.
