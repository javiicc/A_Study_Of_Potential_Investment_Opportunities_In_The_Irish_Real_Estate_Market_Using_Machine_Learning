    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to offer Ardbeg, a beautiful five-bedroom detached family home on a very private south facing site of approximately 0.37 acre.\n\nThis is a modern construct built 20 years ago to replace an existing house. Ardbeg offers the charms and comfort of a period style home with warmth and character throughout. \n\nAccommodation is light filled with most of the rooms being south facing which maximizes the sun and overlooks the private gardens. All reception rooms flow seamlessly offering an open plan arrangement with the option of separation via bi fold / double doors should one wish to meet different family needs. All reception spaces enjoy double patio doors leading directly onto the garden. Upstairs are five double bedrooms and two bathrooms which are led to via an attractive gallery landing. \n\nOutside the gardens are accessed through electronic gates to a graveled drive with garden shed and open shed area to one side. The main garden is south facing with well-maintained lawns, mature shrubs & trees and recalled sandstone slab patio area. Tucked away to one side is a separate vegetable patch with an adjacent brick detached potting shed. Ardbeg also has its own private direct pedestrian access onto the Model Farm Road. \n\nLocated on the city end of Model Farm Road, one of Corks most prestigious and sought-after address within close proximity to and easy walking distance of CUH, Wilton Shopping Centre, Bon Secours Hospital, St Catherine s School, Mount Mercy, Spioraid Naoimh secondary School, Lee fields, UCC, CIT and Cork city Centre. \n\nSuch a superbly convenient location makes for an attractive move for buyers wanting to live in this beautiful mature location.\n\nVIEWING COMES HIGHLY RECOMMENDED\n\nPlease call Sheila O Flynn on 086 2574948 or Johnny O Flynn on 086 6015560 \n\nFront Hall 7.6m x 3.7m. This dual aspect room has plaster coving and access to under stairs storage / wine cellar.\n\nDrawing Room 7.4m x 4.5m. Comfortable day to day living space with double doors leading to the south facing garden and bi-folding doors to the kitchen/dining room. Open fireplace with reclaimed wooden fire surround, plaster coving, wall shelving and wired for surround sound.\n\nDining Room 7.4m x 4.5m. Large open plan dining area accessed from the front hall through feature floor to ceiling height glass paneled original doors. Double patio doors leading to the south facing rear patio and garden. This room has plaster coving and recess lighting.\n\nKitchen 3.8m x 3.0m. Opening directly into the dining room the fitted kitchen by Lenihan s Design is painted with a free-standing solid butcher block island unit.
