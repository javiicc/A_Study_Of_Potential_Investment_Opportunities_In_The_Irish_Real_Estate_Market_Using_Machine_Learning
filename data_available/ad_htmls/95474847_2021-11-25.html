    Development Description

      Welcome to Pearsons Brook, an outstanding new development of exquisite homes, primed to set the standard for luxury living in Gorey town. Designed and built by James Millar Developments Limited, a name distinguished with a reputation for excellence in building new homes. Over decades they have poured their expertise into delivering inspiring and sustainable homes, dedicated to going that extra mile with every detail. \r\n\r\nPearsons Brook is a remarkable collection of 3 & 4 bedroom contemporary new homes with a classical combination of brick and render exterior, cleverly designed accommodation, enhanced with an abundance of green space. Modern and innovative building methods coupled with high quality materials combine to create a home that looks elegant and feels comfortable for generations to come. These timeless masonry built A Rated homes feature significant levels of energy efficiency that are far superior to the average home to ensure low energy usage and higher levels of comfort for the homeowner.\r\n\r\nEach home has been attentively planned, both inside and out, with the emphasis on providing you with the space you need. The houses offer practical storage, versatile kitchen and living space, complemented by generous driveways and gardens. Every element of these family homes has been thought through to ensure they meet the needs and requirements of modern family life.\r\n\r\nThe location is of unparalleled convenience that has everything at hand for all ages, only a short stroll from town centre, schools, transport links to Dublin and Wexford. From pre-schools to primary and secondary schools, every child can enjoy an excellent education, all within walking distance from your home. The development has many recreational facilities nearby as well as all the amenities of Gorey town, host to an array of fashionable shops, boutiques and restaurants.\r\n\r\nThe Gorey of today is a dynamic and distinctive, well-established town that offers a relaxed pace of life, enhanced by surrounding countryside and coastline. The town has much to offer by way of lifestyle options, enriched with a vibrant town centre, recognised as one of the most desirable locations in the region. Recreational amenities are well catered for including golf, water sports, cycling, team sports with a host of clubs, all within the town and environs.\r\n\r\nPearsons Brook is truly the perfect place to call home in a most enviable setting within a town of distinctive charm. Stunning new homes, designed for living, built for a lifetime !\r\n\r\n\r\nNOTE\r\nThese particulars are detailed for the purposes of representing the development only.
