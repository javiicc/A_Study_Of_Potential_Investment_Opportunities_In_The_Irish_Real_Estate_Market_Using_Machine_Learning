    An exciting opportunity to acquire a particularly elegant two storey over garden level Victorian townhouse of exceptional character, well set back from the road with generous off street car parking for 4 / 5 cars, close to the junction with Burlington Road and a short walk from St Stephen s Green. This is without question one of the finest properties to come on the market this season, and one of Dublin 4 s most sought after addresses being so close to the city centre and a range of local amenities. Leeson Street Upper is within walking distance of some of Dublin s finest junior and secondary schools, while UCD Belfield is a short bus ride away. The LUAS is also close by in Ranelagh Village.\n\nRefurbished and extended with great flair and impeccable taste in 2008 under the supervision of deBlacam and Meagher Architects; new period-style wooden sash windows were installed throughout, front exterior was expertly repointed; elegant period features remain. This period residence provides 254sqm / 2,735sqft (approx.) of stunning, light filled accommodation. From the moment you enter the imposing hallway, you know you are entering into a property that is special, where the old and the new combine seamlessly to create a home of great style, charm and character. Off the hall, there are two well-appointed interconnecting reception rooms featuring high ceilings with original cornice, centre roses and period mantelpieces. Located to the rear of the hall, there is a spacious double bedroom (ensuite), overlooking the garden.\n \nUpstairs there are three further bedrooms; original fanlight shines light into a luxurious family bathroom featuring deBlacam & Meagher s signature vaulted ceiling; floor-to ceiling glazing overlooks the private rear garden.\n\nThe garden level offers an exceptionally spacious open plan family area. With a separate entrance to the front, it opens into a large space with travertine floor and under floor heating comprising of a living area, kitchen / breakfast room and family / playroom which opens out to the exceptionally peaceful garden surrounded by original stone walls. A separate utility room, pantry and guest wc complete the accommodation at this level.\n\nLeeson Street Upper is undoubtedly one of Dublin s most convenient residential roads within walking distance of St. Stephen s Green and many of Dublin s principal places of business including Silicon Dock at Grand Canal Dock. Herbert Park is within a short walk and provides superb recreational amenities including tennis, bowling and walks around its 34 acres. Excellent local shopping and restaurants in Ballsbridge, Donnybrook, Baggot Street and Ranelagh are all within a short stroll as are some of Dublin s finest schools such as Gonzaga College, St. Conleths, St Michael s College, Sandford Park School and Muckross Park.
