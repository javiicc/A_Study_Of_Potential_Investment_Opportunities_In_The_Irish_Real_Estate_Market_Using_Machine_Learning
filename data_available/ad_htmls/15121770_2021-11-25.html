    O Connor Murphy are delighted to introduce to the market this superb five bedroom home located on the North Circular Road. This impressive family residence exudes charm and character throughout. It's bright, light infused accommodation that extends to a very comfortable c. 1760 square feet over two levels. It occupies a wonderful position on this part of the prestigious North Circular Road, located close to shops, churches and a wide variety of schools. Palm Court offers a unique opportunity for the discerning purchaser to create a very exceptional home. This charming, red bricked facade home is sure to please anyone looking to up size whilst looking for this North side address.\n\nUpon entering the house, you are greeted by a sun porch; a wonderful space for you to enjoy peaceful garden views. Internally, a bright entrance hallway with under stairs storage, To the right there is a spacious living room with feature bay window & wooden flooring. Off this room is a generous sized living/dining area with patio doors leading to the enclosed rear garden. Through the hallway, there is a substantial utility area along with a fully tiled wet room and a convenient downstairs bedroom. To the rear of the property is a large kitchen/dining area with fully fitted units, this area is the heart of this home flooded with natural light & benefiting from timber framed glazing which capture all day sunshine and provides a view to the rear garden beyond. A rear door allows access through to the garden. A small storage provides extra space for any of your extra storage needs.\n\nUpstairs there are four ample sized bedrooms two with built in wardrobes. A shower room completes the accommodation at this level. An additional detached storage room provides ample space for your extra storage needs.\n\nApproached to the front through cast iron gates this low maintenance tarmac driveway offers secure and off-street parking for several cars, bordered by mature hedging. Gated side access can be obtained to the rear garden. The rear garden is secure & mature that enjoys great privacy. A combination & abundance of mature plants, hedging & shrubbery along with a small patio area provides a peaceful setting. Both front & rear gardens ensure complete privacy and seclusion and provide an oasis of calm and tranquillity.\n\nConveniently located on this mature and highly sought-after road. The location alone speaks volumes about this property situated in a quiet residential area of the North Circular Road offering the occupier a level of privacy and quietness that is difficult to replicate in any other part of Limerick. Within walking distance of the famous Riverbank walk, Limerick Lawn Tennis Club, many schools and excellent local shopping facilities and the city centre just a short stroll away.\n\nPut simply, this property has the potential to become a superb home in a much sought-after mature area, superbly positioned, enjoying a location that is located within an unrivalled area of Limerick city!\n\nViewing comes highly recommended.\n\n\nServices:\n\nOil Fired Heating\nOutside Tap\nGarden Shed\nLarge Garage\n\nAccommodation\nGround Floor\n\nSun Porch\n\nEntrance Hallway - 4.05m (13'3") x 2.02m (6'8") : 8.18 sqm (88 sqft)\nStorage\n\nLiving Room - 4.05m (13'3") x 4.05m (13'3") : 16.40 sqm (177 sqft)\nWooden Floor \nBay Window\n\nKitchen/Dining Room - 6.05m (19'10") x 3.04m (10'0") : 18.39 sqm (198 sqft)\nFitted Kitchen\nHot Press\nUtility Room\n\nLiving Room/Dining Room - 7.06m (23'2") x 3.03m (9'11") : 21.39 sqm (230 sqft)\nFireplace\nPatio door to rear\n\nDownstairs Bedroom - 3m (9'10") x 2.09m (6'10") : 6.27 sqm (67 sqft)\n\nInner Hall - 3.05m (10'0") x 1.04m (3'5") : 3.17 sqm (34 sqft)\n\nDownstairs Wet Room - 3m (9'10") x 2m (6'7") : 6 sqm (65 sqft)\nFully Tiled \nElectric Shower\n\nBedroom - 3m (9'10") x 2.08m (6'10") : 6.24 sqm (67 sqft)\n\nBedroom - 4.05m (13'3") x 4.04m (13'3") : 16.36 sqm (176 sqft)\nBuilt In Wardrobe\nWHB\n\nShower Room - 3.02m (9'11") x 1.08m (3'7") : 3.26 sqm (35 sqft)\n\nBedroom - 3.07m (10'1") x 3.07m (10'1") : 9.42 sqm (101 sqft)\n\nBedroom - 3.07m (10'1") x 2.08m (6'10") : 6.39 sqm (69 sqft)\nBuilt in Wardobe\n\nStore Room\n\n\n Note: \nPlease note we have not tested any apparatus, fixtures, fittings, or services.
