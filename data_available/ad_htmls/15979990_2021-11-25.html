    Quality detached dormer style five bed home set on 0.5 2 acre site which has been lovingly maintained by its current owners since built in 2004. This home offers a tranquil setting surrounded by views of Moycullen Bogs and Galway Bay and is approx. 3 km from Barna Village. \n\nThis home is a very well designed family home. The ground floor consists of a large bright living room with feature gas fire, open plan kitchen/diner with separate sunroom with multi aspect windows, two double ground floor bedrooms one of which is en-suite and is also accessible from the utility room. The upper floor has three good sized bedrooms, one en-suite and walk in wardrobe, the main bathroom and office workspace on the landing. \n\nBarna is a popular seaside village and sought after residential location. It is well serviced with award winning restaurants, shops, hotel, pier, sandy beach and offers easy access to Galway City which is only approx. 12 km away. This home offers a great blend of country living set in tranquil surroundings with easy access to both the Village and the bog where one can enjoy walking and experiencing nature at it s best! \n\nViewing is highly recommended. \n\nGarden\nA gravel driveway to the front of the property with stone wall boundaries leads to large concrete rear garden with ample parking for a number of vehicles. The rear boundary is lined with mature plants and shrubs and has views over Moycullen bogs. There is a Steeltech shed.\n\n\nEntrance Hall Open plan porch area with side windows leading to entrance hall with tiled floor, carpet on stairs, coving and light centrepiece.\n\nLiving Room Laminate timber flooring, bi-aspect windows, gas fire with marble surround, coving and light centrepiece.\n\nKitchen / Dining Room Open plan kitchen/ diner with tiled floor and splashback, floor and wall kitchen units and island feature, integrated gas cooker and double oven, separate Pantry room, double French doors to rear garden.\n\nSun Room Tiled floor, timber clad ceiling, double doors to kitchen/diner, multi aspect windows.\n\nUtility Room Tiled floor, extensive built in storage units, plumbed for washing machine and dryer, indoor oil boiler, access to hot press, rear aspect window and rear access door.\n\nBedroom 1 Laminate timber flooring, front aspect windows, currently used as office.\n\nBedroom 2 Laminate timber flooring, side aspect window.\n\nEn-Suite Tiled floor and part tiled walls, wc, wash hand basin, electric shower, Jack and Jill doors to bedroom and utility, rear aspect window.\n\nLanding Carpet flooring, attic access, office workspace, Velux window.\n\nBedroom 3 Main bedroom, laminate timber flooring, side aspect window with views to Galway Bay, Velux window to the front, large walk in wardrobe - with storage shelving and Velux window.\n\nEn-Suite Fully tiled, wc, wash hand basin, power shower, Velux window.\n\nBedroom 4 Laminate timber flooring, Velux front window and side aspect window.\n\nBedroom 5 Laminate timber flooring, Velux rear window and side aspect window.\n\nBathroom Fully tiled, wc, wash hand basin, bath, separate shower unit with power shower, Velux window.
