    For those of you looking for a home in one of South Dublin's most respected residential locations then look no further than this well presented apartment at 16 Stanford in Ardilea. Located near Harlech Grove and only moments from the Clonskeagh Road leading to Ranelagh, Milltown, Mount Merrion and numerous other landmarks popular in this suburb on the edge of Dublin City Centre.\n\nLocated on the first floor with just one other apartment on this corridor, it is really nice and peaceful. The entrance hall is L-shaped with the bedrooms to the right and the living room and kitchen at the end. The livingroom and kitchen are open-plan and generous in size. A balcony area is located off the living room also. Both bedrooms are double with the main one also benefitting from a dressing area and ensuite bathroom off. There is a further bathroom off the hall. Access to this level from the entrance foyer or underground designated and visitor parking spaces is via a lift or stairs.\n\nClonskeagh is one of Dublin's most sought after residential addresses. There is a wealth of educational, recreational and shopping amenities nearby. Some of Dublin's best schools are within easy reach including Gonzaga, Sandford Park, St. Killians, Mount Anville, Blackrock, Alexandra College, Muckross Park, The Teresian School, and UCD where there are also acres of scenic walks and forest trails. The city centre is very convenient from the property. The villages of Ranelagh, Milltown, Dundrum and Donnybrook are all within easy access with a host of stylish shops and eateries. The icing on the cake however is this gorgeous apartment is only a 3 minute walk to UCD Belfield where the delights of the campus await including many acres of beautiful walks, sports fields and lots more.\n\nHall\xc2\xa0 \xc2\xa0 L-shaped entrance hall with accommodation and bathroom off. Also with Airing cupboard.\n\nLiving Room 4.2m x 3.4m. Generous reception room with timber floors, coving to ceiling, TV point and door leading to the balcony area. Balcony c. 2.5m X 1.15m overlooking the landscaped grounds.\n\nKitchen / Breakfastroom 3.75m x 3.4m. Floor and eye level fitted units. Appliances including oven, hob, dishwasher and washing machine. Tiled floor and splashback.\n\nBedroom 1 3.85m x 3.2m. Large double bedroom with an additional dressing area comprising fitted wardrobes c. 2.2m X 1.55m.\n\nEnsuite Bathroom 2.2m x 1.65m. Bath with shower connection, toilet and wash-hand-basin. Tiled.\n\nBedroom 2 3.7m x 2.73m. Double bedroom with built in wardrobes. Ceiling coving.\n\nBathroom 2m x 1.65m. Bath with power shower over, wc & whb. Tiled.
