    DESCRIPTION \r\n\r\nThe holding extends to approximately 1.05 acres with frontage to Lower Lucan Road and bordered by Rugged Lane to the rear. There were originally two semi-detached cottages on the land which were at one point occupied by ferrymen who provided a ferry service across the River Liffey. Early Ordnance Survey Ireland maps show a ferry crossing the River Liffey marked at this location. The black and white photograph in this advertisement may well be the original cottages here. \r\n\r\nThe lands are now totally overgrown with vegetation and only the ruins now remain of the original two cottages. The colour photographs in this advertisement show the both the current structure and the state of the buildings in the recent past. \r\n\r\n\r\nZONING \r\n\r\nThe holding is zoned to protect and enhance high amenity areas on the Fingal Development Plan 2017 - 2023.\r\n\r\n\r\nPLANNING PERMISSION \r\n\r\n\r\nThe most recent planning decision here is planning refusal from An Bord Plean\xc3\xa1la in 1997 ( Ref PL 06F.102554) where planning permission was refused for the demolition of two semi-detached derelict cottages and reinstatement by one single storey cottage. A copy of same is available from our office.\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nVIEWING ARRANGEMENTS\r\n\r\n53.364515, -6.408882
