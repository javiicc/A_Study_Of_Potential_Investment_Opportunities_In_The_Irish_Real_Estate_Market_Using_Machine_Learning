    No 52 Bertram Court is a superb, light filled two bedroom apartment with parking ideally positioned on the 1st floor of this well located development on Lamb Alleey, Cornmarket in the heart of the city centre. The location of this well-appointed apartment makes it the perfect choice for those seeking a home, or base in the city centre, as well as investors looking for a steady income. \nThis apartment has a lovely balance of living and bedroom accommodation. The accommodation comprises a spacious open plan living/dining room which offers access to the west facing Juliet balcony overlooking communal areas with a fully integrated kitchen lying adjacent to here. There are two generously proportioned double bedrooms with built in wardrobes. A spacious entrance hallway, large hot press/ storage room and main bathroom complete the accommodation. \nBertram Court is a superbly located development surrounded by an abundance of restaurants, cafes, bars, art galleries and shops as Temple Bar, Dublin Castle and the Grafton Street area as well as some of Dublin s principle places of work and education are right on your doorstep.
