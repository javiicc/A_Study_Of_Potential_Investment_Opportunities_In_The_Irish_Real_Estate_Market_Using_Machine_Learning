    Stunning two bedroom south west facing apartment, featuring large balcony, comes to the market in turn key condition. \r\n\r\nThe apartment is well situated in the Phoenix Park racecourse development, enjoying own door access on the top floor. The complex is well maintained and beautifully landscaped, ideally located close to the city and beside the Phoenix Park. On entering there is a wide hallway that leads into a light filled living space which benefits from high ceilings and solid wooden floors which run throughout. This area incorporates a sitting room and dining area with the kitchen ancillary to this. The room boasts floor to ceiling windows with access to a south west facing balcony. The kitchen features stylish oak units and incorporates a large granite work top. The unit accommodates two spacious double bedrooms, a main bathroom and an ensuite. Both bedrooms feature built in wardrobes. The bathrooms are well finished with high spec sanitary ware and stylish subway tiles. \r\n\r\nThe Phoenix Park Racecourse development is adjacent to Europe s largest enclosed Urban Park. Spanning 1760 Acres of meticulously maintained parkland. Castleknock Village is very close by with a choice of restaurants, shops and bars. There are two train stations situated with a 5/6 minute walk providing an excellent service to the City Centre and IFSC. Millrace road is situated within two miles of Dublin s City Centre. There is unrivalled access to the M3 and M50.
