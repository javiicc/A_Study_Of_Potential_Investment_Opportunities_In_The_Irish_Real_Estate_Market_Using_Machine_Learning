    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to present No.43 Rosehaven to the market for sale. This superb, 3 bedroomed, first floor apartment presents in very good condition, having been newly painted throughout as well as having new laminate timber flooring in the living room, hallway and dining room. Ideally located off the Carpenterstown Road, just moments from Coolmine Train Station, the development is accessed via an attractive tree lined avenue leading to an open courtyard, with a small surface car park as well as an underground car park, which each owner has access to. \n\nThe accommodation briefly comprises of an entrance hall with hotpress and separate storage press. This gives way to the fantastic living room with a striking bay window and a large south-facing balcony, which is not overlooked. There is a separate kitchen, with adjoining dining/breakfast room. No. 43 has 3 generous double bedrooms (main ensuite), all with built in wardrobes. A family bathroom completes. \n\nThis property is excellently positioned just moments from Coolmine Train Station, while also being just a 7 minute walk to a Spar shop, The Carpenter bar, Insomina caf , a takeaway and pharmacy, as well as having an option of schools nearby. There is also easy access to both Rosehaven Shopping Centre and Blanchardstown Shopping Centre. As well as the highly convenient Train Station, there is an excellent bus service just around the corner from the development, allowing easy access to the city centre. \n\nViewing is highly recommended, to appreciate the size and privacy of this property, which is sure to impress. \n\n\nEntrance Hall With newly laid laminate timber flooring.
