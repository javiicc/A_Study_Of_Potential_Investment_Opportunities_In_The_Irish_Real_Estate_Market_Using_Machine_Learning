    AMOVE is delighted to bring The Cottage to the market, situated at locally known Cahills Cross and this home couldn't command a better location in Monasterevin. This property extends to 0.19 hectares (0.46 acres) and is fully enclosed by a road front 8ft wall with 2 vehicular entrances onto the main Nurney Road. A home that sits in a private garden oasis with mature pink and white cherry, cypress, silver birch, apple, beech and copper beech trees. The Cottage as it stands now was built in 1993 and is attached to a derelict thatch. The house sits comfortably on this generous site and offers potential buyers the chance to buy a wonderful home on a stunning site within walking distance of this thriving town. Alternatively, the site is zoned existing residential in the Local Area Plan 2016-2022 so could be developed subject to the necessary PP (architecture feasibility attached). \r\n\r\nACCOMMODATION: Entrance Hall, Lounge/ Dining room / Kitchen, Utility, 2 double bedrooms, master En-suite, house bathroom & walk in hotpress. Self-contained detached office / Art Studio and garage. To the rear of the property there is a large steel Shanette Shed. \r\n\r\nThe town is on hand as is the new secondary complex (less than a minutes walk from this properties door) and the parkland that is Moore Abbey Woods. Monasterevin is known as the Venice of the Midlands with excellent fishing to be had on the River Barrow and the Grand Canal. Ballykelly Mills Distillery and The Barrow Blueway is currently under construction and is due for completion in June 2023. Within easy reach is Emo Court & Gardens, The Irish National Stud, Kildare Village shopping, the Monasterevin M7 motorway at Junction 14.
