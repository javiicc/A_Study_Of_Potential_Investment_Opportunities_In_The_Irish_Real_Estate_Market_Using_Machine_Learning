Freshly Painted throughout, this 'Turn-Key' Property is presented in Excellent Condition. Accommodation of this Superb Property consists of Living Room, Kitchen/Dining Area, 3 Bedrooms, Bathroom, En-Suite & W.C. The Quality Fitted Kitchen with Tiled Splashback was Recently Painted and comes with Electrical Appliances. The Spacious Living Room has a Feature Gas Fireplace as the Focal Point, with access to the Dining Area through Double Doors. Patio Doors lead from the Dining Area to the Secure, Partially Walled, Rear Garden. The Family Bathroom has a Tiled Splashback Area around Bath & Sink. The En-Suite off the Main Bedroom has Partially Tiled Walls. All Bedrooms have Fitted Wardrobes. There is Newly Laid Carpet on the Stairs, Landing and Bedrooms. Heated by a Natural Gas Fired Central Heating System and Double-Glazed Windows throughout, this property would make an Ideal Family Home. Main Services: Water, Sewerage, Electricity, Natural Gas & Broadband. Built c.2001.
