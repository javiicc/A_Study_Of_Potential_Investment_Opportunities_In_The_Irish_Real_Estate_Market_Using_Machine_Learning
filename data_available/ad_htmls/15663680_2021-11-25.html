    A magnificent end of terrace three storey over basement Georgian residence enjoying a highly convenient location within a short stroll of Monkstown Village with its superb amenities and transport including numerous bus routes which run along Monkstown Road together with the DART station at Seapoint. The property currently enjoys a self contained two bedroom basement apartment providing an ideal home income or granny flat while upstairs this beautiful family home has been exceptionally well maintained and upgraded over the years. At hall level there are very fine interconnecting reception rooms with period detail together with large kitchen/breakfast room and utility. An attractive staircase with feature landing window overlooking the rear leads to the first floor where there is a truly impressive drawing room spanning the front of the property while to the rear there is the main bedroom and bathroom. On the third floor there are three further bedrooms (one en suite) and family bathroom. \n\nThe location of Montpelier Parade needs little introduction being situated between Monkstown and Blackrock villages and within a short stroll of the seafront. This property is in a truly exceptional location being only a pleasant stroll from Monkstown village with its range of specialist shops, restaurants, pubs and churches. There are also a wide variety of amenities available in both Dun Laoghaire and Blackrock including major shopping centres, schools, churches and many sporting venues within the immediate area to include several tennis clubs, the yacht clubs at Dun Laoghaire as well as the marina and many interesting walks along the seafront and several golf clubs all within easy reach.
