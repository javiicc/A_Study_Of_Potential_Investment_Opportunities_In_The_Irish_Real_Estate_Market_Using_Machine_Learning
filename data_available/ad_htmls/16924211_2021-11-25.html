    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 117 m\xc2\xb2
    Corry Estates are delighted to present to the market No. 54 Castlemoyne, a beautifully maintained three bedroom property which comes to the market with the added benefit of a sun room extension and south facing rear garden. The house which is in simply walk in condition and over looks a green area to the front enjoys bright and spacious living accommodation throughout. Comprising of entrance Hall, lounge complete with marble fireplace and bay window, open plan kitchen / dining area which leads to the sun room extension and access to the rear garden. The guest w.c. and understairs utility room completes the ground floor accommodation. There are three bedrooms with master bedroom en-suite and a family bathroom on first floor level. Further features include a mix of PVC double and triple glazed windows, two designated car parking spaces, gas fired radiator central heating and a burglar alarm system. Ideally located within this much sought after development within close proximity of local shops, schools and restaurants while transportation links to Dublin City Centre are provided by a host of Bus routes and the dart stations at Clongriffin & Portmarnock. Viewing comes highly recommended to appreciate all this home has to offer.
