    Luxury 4 bed detached house c. 192.7 m\xc2\xb2 / c. 2,074 Sq.Ft. on mature gardens, in the Village of Newtownforbes.\r\n\r\nOriginally built in 1993 the house was extended and refurbished in 2012 to create a more open plan modern feel to the house.\r\n\r\nThe heart of the house is the kitchen, dining and living area. The bespoke kitchen has walnut wood doors and a cream quartz stone countertops. It features an island / breakfast bar with integrated appliances including electric cooker, Microwave, hob, extractor fan, dishwasher, wine fridge warming drawer and coffee machine (a must for anyone remote working !). \r\nThe kitchen is complimented by a large utility with built in units plumbed for washing machine & Dryer, off this is a shower room. The kitchen opens onto the dining living area via pocket sliding glass doors, which can be closed over to give a more separate feel. \r\nThe dining and living area has engineered oak wood floors and glazing to the south and west of the house ensuring a bright space throughout the afternoon. A glass door gives access to the patio seating area at the side of the house. The living room has high ceilings and a gas insert fire with wiring to mount a flat screen TV. There is a separate large sitting room that has a dual aspect with sliding glass doors to the rear garden and an open fireplace. \r\n\r\nUpstairs the master bedroom has an ensuite shower room and built in sliding wardrobes. A fully tiled family bathroom serves three other double sized bedrooms.\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nLOCATION : \r\nMeadow court is a small cul-de-sac of large detached houses, just off the Main street of Newtownforbes village and the N4 Dublin - Sligo Road. \r\n\r\nNewtownforbes is a popular residential location with an excellent Primary school, and modern GAA complex. The village has local shops, post office, restaurants and churches. Being on the N4 Dublin - Sligo Road there are bus stops along the main street and Longford town centre is just a 5 minute drive.\r\n\r\n\r\nOUTSIDE :\r\nThe house is set amidst beautiful gardens, laid in lawns with mature trees & shrubs affording good privacy. There is a paved patio area on the south west corner of the house sheltered by a block built garage / shed and the house making it an ideal BBQ area. The house backs onto agricultural lands at the rear.\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nSERVICES & FEATURES : \r\n\r\nMains Water and Sewerage, \r\nOil fired central heating (Condensing boiler)\r\nSolar Panels providing hot water throughout the year.\r\nBroadband (up 90 Mbs download) \r\nOak wooden doors and joinery throughout. \r\n\r\n\r\nEircode: N39 E096 \r\nGPS: 53.763, -7.837
