    DNG take great pleasure in presenting to market No. 50 Strandville Avenue, a most attractive and perfectly proportioned red brick Villa style period property set back in a prime and central location. The property has been sympathetically improved by its current owners whilst still preserving a host of wonderful period features to include 12 foot high ceilings, ornate coving, restored architraves, cast iron feature fireplace in the bedroom and the original staircase and floorboards. The property also boasts a number of attractive contemporary features to include double glazed windows, gas fired central heating, an excellent C3 BER rating and replacement wiring, plumbing and heating system as part of renovation works c. 2009. The clever blend of new and old is exceptionally well married and only adds to the appeal of this fantastic family home and makes for a truly fantastic example of these early 1900's period properties.\r\n\r\nEntrance level accommodation comprises a double height hallway with impressive 12 foot ceilings and original staircase and an elegant front sitting room with ceiling coving and bespoke custom built alcove storage units. Lower ground level is connected through a charming outdoor courtyard and porcelain tiled hallway with a large double bedroom off, family bathroom with separate bath and shower cubicle and a contemporary extended kitchen which floods with natural sunlight from the South / Westerly aspect. Completing the accommodation on first floor level are two further double bedrooms. The rear garden is exceptionally well done with a comfortable decked area, low maintenance Astro turfing and mature shrubbery border. \r\n\r\nIdeally set back on a quiet neighbourly street just off the North Strand Road and just a short stroll to Dublin City Centre, Strandville Avenue is well serviced by an excellent range of local amenities including shops, cafes, pubs, schools, sporting facilities, Westwood Gym, Clontarf promenade and Fairview Park, along with being within walking distance of Croke Park, 3 Arena, and the Aviva Stadium. It provides excellent transport links with nearby Bus and Dart/train services and Fairview and Connolly Stations. This location lends itself to both vibrant city living and idyllic parks and beaches with St Annes Park and Dollymount Strand only a 5 minute drive.\r\n\r\nViewing in an absolute must to truly appreciate all this stunning property has to offer.
