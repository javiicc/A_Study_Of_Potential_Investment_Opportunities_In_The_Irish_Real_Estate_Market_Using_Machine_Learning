      Location

      Property location:\r\n\r\nClog Na Leinn is located in the village of Collinstown, County Westmeath in the Hidden Heartlands of Ireland. This area is known for natural beauty and outdoor activities, centred around the village community. Enjoy the rural quality of life with many amenities and services, and excellent schools within a stone throw. The area is highly accessible by excellent motorways and road systems, and centred between Mullingar, Castlepollard, Delvin and Kells which are all an easy drive away. \r\n\r\nPublic Transport: On Bus Eireann route 111 Dublin via Athboy. 20 minute drive to Mullingar rail & bus station with daily bus and train commuter services available on the Dublin & Sligo routes.\r\n\r\nSchools Facilities: \r\nPre-school Kidz Aloud Nursery and Montessori School within walking distance \r\nPrimary school St Marys Co-Educational National School within walking distance \r\nSecondary school Castlepollard Community College 10 minute drive \r\n\r\nCommunity and recreational Amenities: \r\nCollinstown has a RC Church, Community Hall, GAA Club, Playground, Swimming Area, Midland regional fishing association and a Pitch and Putt course. Mullingar has a Golf Club, Rugby Club and Sailing Club with a choice of Leisure and Fitness Centres only 15 minutes drive. \r\n\r\nLocal attractions within easy reach: \r\nFore Abbey, Tullanally Castle, Lough Crew House and Gardens, Lake amenities, Belvedere House and Gardens. Mullingar Town. \r\n\r\nShopping: The larger centres of Mullingar and Navan offer a choice of top brand shopping. Liffey Valley and Blanchardstown Retail Parks are less than an hour drive via the M3 and M4 motorway routes. Dublin City Centre is easily accessible by train from Mullingar. \r\n\r\nTravel Distances: \r\nMullingar By-Pass 15 minutes \r\nM4 20 minutes \r\nKells M3 30 minutes \r\nNavan 30 minutes M50 via M4 55 minutes
