    Gerry Conway of APP Kirrane Auctioneering, Bohermore is pleased to bring this magnificent site at Lissarulla, Claregalway one mile from the National School, Secondary School and Educate Together School to the market. The site is in a lovely rural setting yet is close to all schools and amenities and adjacent to Claregalway village. It is only a fifteen minute drive to the medical device companies and other businesses in Briarhill Business Park .Located beside H91 W5Y0.\r\n\r\nPlanning Permission was previously granted on this site (08/3647 and 11/113)\r\nfor an architecturally designed house of 258.9 square metres and a large domestic garage which can be made available to a purchaser if required. The roadway to the site and the general location for the house has been filled and compacted with hardcore which will facilitate the laying of the foundation for any type of dwelling house.\r\nThe area is well served with the Church, two medical practices, dentist, pharmacy, two hotels, two supermarkets and a number of restaurants.\r\nThere are extensive amenities in the immediate area with Claregalway Mens and Ladies Football Club, Turloughmore Hurling and Camogie Club, Fishing and Kayaking on the River Clare and Cregmore Golf Club provides excellent facilities for the golf enthusiasts.\r\nThis is an excellent opportunity to live in a thriving area with direct access to a host of facilities and amenities and easy access to the City and yhe motorway.\r\nThe site can be sold subject to planning approval.\r\nSite Area : 0.5 of an Acre.\r\nGuide Price : 80,000\r\n\r\nFor further information and an appointment to view please call Gerry Conway Auctioneer on\r\n 089 2548533.
