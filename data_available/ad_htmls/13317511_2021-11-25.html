    Sherry Fitzgerald Stack are delighted to bring this 3 bed, 1 bathroom semi detached residence to the market. The residence is ideally located in the village of Moyvane and all amenities inc. school, shops, church, pubs etc. The neighbouring village of Knockanure is within 3.6km south of the property while the heritage town of Listowel and all amenities is just a ten minute drive away.\nGround floor accommodation comprises: entrance hallway, lounge/sitting room and kitchen/dining room. 1st floor accommodation comprises: 3 bedrooms - 2 double, 1 single and main bathroom with whb & w/c. \nThe property has the benefit of oil-fired central heating (with solid fuel insert stove) and double glazed windows fitted throughout. The property has connections to all mains essential services. \nThere is a lawn area to the front of the property and a yard to the rear with a 30 sqm approx. detached garage (ideal for storage/DIY).
