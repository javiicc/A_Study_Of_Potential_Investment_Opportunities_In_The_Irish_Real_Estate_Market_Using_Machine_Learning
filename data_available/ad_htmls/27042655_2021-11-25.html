      JB Kelly is proud to present this modern detached home located in Casana View, a quiet leafy prime residential estate situated off Thormanby Rd. in Howth. \r\n\r\nThis property is in superb condition throughout having been completely renovated in recent years and features a contemporary style large extended open plan kitchen/dining/living area to the rear. \r\n\r\nThis beautiful property is located in a tranquil location, enjoying privacy tucked away in a quiet cul-de-sac.
