    Sherry FitzGerald O Reilly welcome you to number 204 The Oaks, a superb 5 bedroomed detached home, located in a mature development in Newbridge town. This is a bright and spacious family home, with its stone front making it unique in this development.\r\nThe Oaks is conveniently situated within a short walk of Newbridge town centre, shops, restaurants, schools, cinema and leisure facilities. It offers easy access to the M7/N7 motorway and the Arrow train station. \r\nThe well-proportioned accommodation in this fine property briefly comprises of: entrance hall, sitting room, kitchen/living room, dining room, guest wc. Upstairs - family bathroom, 5 bedrooms (1 en-suite).\r\n\r\n\r\nEntrance Hall The entrance hall is the central hub of the home, with access to all downstairs rooms. It is a bright space with a Canadian maple floor. With heated storage closet (1.37m x 0.7m).\r\n\r\nSitting Room 8.2m x 3.4m. Double doors lead from the hall to the spacious sitting room. This room is full of light, being of dual aspect to front and rear. The room features an impressive marble fireplace with granite hearth. It has a carpet floor, wall and central lighting, and double doors leading to the dining room.\r\n\r\nDining Room 4.55m x 3.54m. The dining room has a solid maple floor, wall and centre lighting and doors to both hall and kitchen.\r\n\r\nKitchen/Living Area 10.22m x 3.03m. The kitchen/living room is of triple aspect with sliding doors to the garden. It is fitted with an ample range of oak units and at its heart is the Belling range with 8 gas rings. The kitchen ceiling is beautifully clad in wood panelling, while the floor and splashback are tiled. A solid fuel stove with back boiler in the living are makes the room very cosy indeed.\r\n\r\nGuest WC 2.33m x 1.73m. With wc and vanity unit, the room is fully tiled.\r\n\r\nLanding 6.36m x 4.63m. The T-shaped landing has a wonderful decorative ceiling, with fitted Sliderobe storage and attic access.\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 5.88m x 4m. This is a very generous double room to the front of the house. It has a carpet floor and an array of built-in wardrobes.\r\n\r\nEn-Suite 2.18m x 1.16m. With wc, whb and shower unit with electric shower. It is fully tiled.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 3.21m x 3.05m. A double room with front view, it has a carpet floor.\r\n\r\nBedroom 3 3.35m x 3.08m. This is a double room to front with carpet floor and fitted wardrobe.\r\n\r\nBedroom 4 3.5m x 3m. Another double bedroom with rear views, it has a carpet floor.\r\n\r\nBedroom 5 2.84m x 2.6m. This double room to rear has a fitted wardrobe and carpet floor.\r\n\r\nBathroom 4.21m x 3m. The very spacious bathroom features a sizeable jacuzzi bath for two. It has a quadrant shower unit with electric shower, wc, bidet, whb and two ladder towel rails. The room is fully tiled in stylish porcelain tile.\r\n\r\nOUTSIDE\r\n\r\nStudio 7.19m x 3.35m. Preciously a dance studio, it has a sprung wooden floor, heating and toilet.\r\n\r\nShed 1 3.73m x 2.64m. The block built shed unit is divided into three sheds, each with own metal door and shelving\r\n\r\nShed 2 3.29m x 2.64.\r\n\r\nShed 3 2.64m x 1.25m. With insulated walls.\r\n\r\n\r\nDIRECTIONS:\r\nFrom the M7 - Take the exit at Junction 12 towards Newbridge. At the roundabout, take the 4th exit onto Curragh chase. At the next roundabout, take the exit to Newbridge. Follow this road for 1.4km, and take the left turn after the petrol station. Take the next left and the house is the first on the left.
