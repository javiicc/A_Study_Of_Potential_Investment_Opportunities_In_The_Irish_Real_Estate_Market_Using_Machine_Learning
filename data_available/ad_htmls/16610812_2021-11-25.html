    No. 27 Avondale Lawn is a very fine three bedroomed, semi-detached family home located just off Carysfort Avenue, within easy reach of a wide variety of amenities in Blackrock. \nThis lovely red bricked property while having been well maintained, is a blank canvas for someone to transform it to a superb family home.\nOn entering the property, there is a light filled hallway, leading to interconnecting reception rooms which both have tiled fireplaces and are separated by sliding doors, both with large picture windows overlooking both the front and rear gardens. There is a large kitchen to the rear with picture window and door to rear garden. Also on this level is a guest wc and the garage, which could be converted subject to fpp. Going upstairs there are three bedrooms, two large doubles with built-in wardrobes, one front and one rear, a large single bedroom to the front, a bathroom and a large storage room.\nOutside the property benefits from a large private rear garden with an enviable southerly aspect, which is not overlooked and could become a nice peaceful haven with a bit of TLC. There is a large garden shed for storage. The property also overlooks a lovely green area. \nFrom the front door you are within short distance of Blackrock Village and the myriad of amenities that it has to offer including a wide range of specialist shops, restaurants, two shopping centers and fantastic transport links including the DART and QBC. Enjoy pleasant walks along the seafront in Blackrock or closer to home in Carysfort Park. This property is within the catchment area of a number of highly regarded primary and secondary schools, such as Carysfort National School, Sion Hill, St Andrews College, Willow Park, CBC Monkstown and Blackrock College, making this home perfectly suited to a growing family.\n\n\nPorch 2.26m x 0.82m.
