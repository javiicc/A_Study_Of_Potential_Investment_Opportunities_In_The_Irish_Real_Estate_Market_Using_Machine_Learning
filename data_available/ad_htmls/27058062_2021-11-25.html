    Description

      This is a very well-presented 2 bedroom apartment which is one of Dalkey's most prestigious residential developments is situated in this very private complex which has panoramic views across Dublin Bay to Dalkey Island and direct access to the water's edge. Bartra is just a 10-minute stroll from Dalkey village which hosts an endless list of amenities from Shops, Restaurants, Schools, and The DART Station which is a 25 journey from Dublin City Centre. \nThe accommodation comprise of own hall door entrance with hall running past the and leading to a large open plan dining / seating area with incredible sea views. \nThere are two double bedrooms with the master en suite and both have fitted wardrobes. The resident has their own ground floor patio which leads to a communal garden area which is right on the sea. There is also a fitted kitchen with all appliances and guest WC. This property has 1 car space and is unfurnished.
