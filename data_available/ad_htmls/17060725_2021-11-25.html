    Corry Estates are delighted to welcome to the market No. 19 Waterside Grove an exceptional 1 bedroom ground floor apartment with the added benefit of own door access, side entrance and a magnificent private rear garden complete with large decking area and barna shed. The apartment has been maintained and upgrade to the highest of standards by its current owner and is presented to the market in walk in condition. Upon entering viewers will be instantly impressed by the bright and airy feel to the living accommodation which comprises of entrance hall, open plan living & dining area, kitchen, modern bathroom suite and a generous double bedroom complete with built in wardrobes. Further features include PVC double glazed windows, gas fired radiator central heating and a burglar alarm system. Ideally located within this much sought after residential development the property is within walking distance of local shops and schools while Malahide Village with all it has to offer including boutique shops, cafes, restaurants, commuter dart station and much more is also just minutes away. The property is also within easy access of Swords Pavilions shopping centre. Viewing comes highly recommended to appreciate this fine apartment.
