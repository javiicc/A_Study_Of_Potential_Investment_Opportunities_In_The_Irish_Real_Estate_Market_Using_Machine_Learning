    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    REA Halnon McKenna are delighted to offer for sale 231 Neptune House, Bellevue, Islandbridge, Dublin 8, a very stylish 2 bedroom 2 bathroom apartment with beautifully appointed accommodation. Bellevue is an exclusive development off the South Circular Road, nestled between the south bank of The River Liffey and the Mill Race - a quiet haven in the middle of the city. \r\n\r\nThe tranquil grounds of Bellevue are extensive, well maintained and uniquely placed on an island surrounded by water (where Islandbridge gets its name). \r\n\r\nSituated on the 3rd floor, this stunning two bedroom south facing apartment, has ceiling to floor windows in the living room that open onto a balcony. The balcony has ample room for a table and chairs and also features an outside wall light. \r\nThe upgraded apartment features laminate timber floors throughout the hall, living room, kitchen and carpeted bedrooms. The entrance hall has a hot press, a large storage room to the left and to the right a separate laundry room. A spacious and modern main bathroom with both a generous bath and power shower as well as a tiled floor and partially tiled walls. \r\nThe high spec kitchen comes with modern wall tiling as well as plenty of counter & well thought out cupboard space. There are a full array of quality appliances - and the inclusion of these is open to negotiation. The spacious, light filled, south facing living room unit and dining area features a full length glass door onto the balcony as well as large windows. \r\nThe master bedroom ensuite is south facing and flooded with light through the large windows complete with wooden blinds. Each bedroom also comes complete with feature fitted wardrobes. The apartment also benefits from a designated car space and access to one of two large, locked bike storage units located off the secure underground residents car park. \r\n\r\n231 Neptune House Bellevue is truly a wonderful riverside location, located along the banks of The River Liffey and at the gates to The Memorial Gardens. It is just a 3 minute walk to the Phoenix Park and the area is well serviced by several bus routes to Dublin city centre (OConnell Bridge about 8 minutes - Merrion Square about 20 mins). Both the Red Luas Line and Heuston Station are only a 15 minute walk away while the national road network is very accessible (N4 is a 3 minute drive by car while the M50 is about 8 minutes away).
