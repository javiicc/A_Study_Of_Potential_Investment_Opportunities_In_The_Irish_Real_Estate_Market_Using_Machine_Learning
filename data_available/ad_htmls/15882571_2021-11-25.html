    Newly developed 5 bed semi detached residence extending to approximately 1,800 square feet and situated at the centre of Kanturk town. The property is accessed by private roadway from Percival Street and immediately adjoins Kanturk Primary School to the rear. \r\n\r\nThe residence is situated in close proximity to shops, cafes, bars, restaurants and Kanturk town Park. It is also in close proximity to multi million euro investments in the development of a new primary school complex, Fire station and Aldi retail store. \r\n\r\nKanturk town is 55 kilometres north east of Cork city, 22 kilometres west of Mallow and a 35-minute drive from both Kerry Airport and the incredibly beautiful town of Killarney.
