    Finisk Lodge - a simply stunning residence set on 1 acre approx. in a most scenic and peaceful countryside location. \n\nThe property has a myriad of features inside and out and is impeccably finished. \n\nTotally secluded from the road by large entrance gates and meandering driveway, Finisk Lodge is set in the surroundings of the Finisk Valley on the outskirts of Cappoquin and a mere 10 minute drive to the famous Lismore Heritage town and 15 minutes from Dungarvan. \n\nInside, the property has a spacious interior with 3 reception rooms on the ground floor. The lounge is a cosy room with a fitted stove and windows to the front and rear garden. The study is off the entrance hall and is a fine room that also has a window to the rear garden. The living room is on the approach to the kitchen which has an eye-catching fitted units and an impressive feature AGA.\n\nUpstairs, there are 4 ample size bedrooms on the first floor with the master bedroom spanning the full width of the house and having its own ensuite. \n\nExternally, the grounds are immaculate with mature trees and a quaint rear courtyard that has outbuildings and access gates from the road.\n\nThis is a unique opportunity to acquire one of the finest residence s in the West Waterford area.
