    Mullery O Gara are delighted to present this beautifully presented and deceptively spacious townhouse to the market. With a unbeatable array of amenities all within walking distance this exclusive development offers the best in terms of location, setting and standard of finish.\r\n\r\nNo. 4 is presented in excellent order throughout and offers spacious, luxurious and bright living spaces. Accommodation comprises entrance hall, kitchen/dining room, spacious living room with double doors to suntrap west facing garden, reading area/snug, upstairs there are 3 spacious bedrooms, master en-suite and the main bathroom as well as a spacious attic space. \r\n\r\nPretty as a picture sums up the fa\xc3\xa9\xc2\xa7ade and surrounding grounds whilst the interior is stylish, practical and in absolute turn key condition throughout having been tastefully and cleverly upgraded by its current owner.\r\n\r\nSuperbly located a short distance from both Milltown and Ranelagh villages, offering a variety of restaurants, cafes, shops and a host of other local services and amenities that make everyday living a pleasure. The property is also well serviced by public transport links providing easy access to the city centre. This delightful home will appeal to a wide range of purchasers including those seeking a home close to the city centre, yet enjoying the a genuine village community that Milltown provides.\r\n\r\nThe area offers an excellent choice of independent and artisan shopping as well as an abundance of local parks including the stunning Dartry parking leading to Dodder park which forms part of the new proposed Dodder Greenway.
