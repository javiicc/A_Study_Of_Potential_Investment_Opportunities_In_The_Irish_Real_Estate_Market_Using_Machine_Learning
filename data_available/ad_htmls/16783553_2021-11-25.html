    **EXCEPTIONAL TWO-BEDROOM AND TWO-BATHROOM END OF TERRACE RED BRICK COTTAGE IN TURN KEY CONDITION*** \r\n\r\nMcIntyre Property Consultants are delighted to present to market this stunning 2 bedroom 2 bathroom end of terraced cottage. The accommodation is well proportioned throughout and comprises an inviting entrance hallway that leads to a cosy living room with an original fireplace. The spacious and bright light-filled kitchen, which can be accessed through double glazed doors from the living room, comes with a tiled floor and splashback. It also provides ample counter and cupboard space as well as comprising a bright dining area. There are two large and bright double bedrooms, with the master en-suite bedroom overlooking the side yard area. The Yard is also accessible through a side gate. The spacious attic can be accessed on a stairway ladder, while there is also scope to potentially convert the space (subject to planning permission). The property also benefits from free on street parking. All in all this is a stunning property which is in walk in condition throughout and will appeal to both owner occupiers and investors.\r\n\r\nLOCATION: Ideally located just off St. James Walk right beside the Red Luas Line and within a host of local shops, caf\xc3\xa9s and amenities. The campus of St. James Hospital including the new Children's Hospital, the Digital Hub and the Guinness Enterprise Centre are in the near vicinity. There are also sports and leisure centres nearby. Dublin City Centre and St Stephen's Green is a mere 20 minute walk.
