    Burnaby Mews is one of Greystones hidden gems, tucked away behind secure electronic gates and a short stroll down to both the village and Dart station this well-maintained development is arguably one of Greystones best locations. No 19 Burnaby Mews is an exceptionally well maintained and spacious three bedroom top floor corner apartment with panoramic sea views. \n\nThe accommodation briefly comprises of a welcoming entrance hall, open plan living/dining room which benefits from floor to ceiling windows to ensure the view is captured at its best, kitchen, three double bedrooms (main with en-suite) and a main bathroom completes the internal accommodation. The balcony which can comfortably fit a good sized table and chairs is perfect for dining al fresco to enjoy the serene vista and watch the ships go by.\n\nThe development is gated affording great privacy and security. A pedestrian gate also leads directly to The Meridian Centre for ease of access to the village. There is also a two designated car spaces.\n\nThe location is second to none and Greystones needs little introduction. It is a beautiful seaside town with some fantastic restaurants, specialist and boutique shops, cafes and delicatessens. The family town has a host of leisure pursuits and excellent clubs. These include the local Rugby, GAA, Soccer, Tennis and Sailing clubs, Greystones & Delgany Golf Clubs, Shoreline indoor swimming pool and fitness centre, two beaches, harbour, marina and much more.\n\nThe N11 leading to the M50 is just minutes away as is the DART just across the road together with the 84, 84 X and 184 bus routes and the Aircoach to Dublin Airport.\n\n\nEntrance Hall Wooden flooring, spot lighting, access to Hot press, attic access.\n\nLiving/Dining Room 4.20m x 7.88m.
