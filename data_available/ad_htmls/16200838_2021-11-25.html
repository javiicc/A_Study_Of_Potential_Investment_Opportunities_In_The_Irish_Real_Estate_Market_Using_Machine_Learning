    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    3 Bed detached house set on .56 of an acre in the townsland of Bishopstown, Rosemount, Co. Westmeath. Very clean and tidy home presented to the market in "move in " condition. Surrounded on 3 sides by evergreen laurel hedging, lawn area with mature trees and shrubs. Accommodation comprises of 3 double bedrooms( 1 with shower), family bathroom, livingroom with wooden floor and open fire place, kitchen and dining area with patio doors opening to garden. Services include well water, septic tank, electric and OFCH. Situated in a quite country setting. 10 minutes drive to Bohar & Rosemount National Schools and directly on the Secondary School bus route. Easy commuting distance to Mullingar, Athlone, Tullamore and M6 junctions at Moate.
