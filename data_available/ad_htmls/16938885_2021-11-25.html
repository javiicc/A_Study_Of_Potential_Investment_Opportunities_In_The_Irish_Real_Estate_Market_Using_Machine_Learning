    DNG have the pleasure to present No.60 Charlesland Park to the market. This well presented 2-bedroom apartment boasts a sunny southerly aspect and comes with a designated car parking space. The bright and well laid out interior is sure to appeal.\n\nThe accommodation comprises entrance porch with tiled floor and door to living room. The open plan living dining room has a gas fire and an attractive mantlepiece. Glass panelled French doors lead to a southerly aspect patio area. Off the living room is the kitchen with a selection of cabinets, generous countertop and a breakfast bar for informal dining. A small hall leads you to the bathroom and two bedrooms both with built in wardrobes. Throughout the apartment there is a solid timber flooring.\n\nThe apartment is positioned just 500m from the Supervalu shopping centre and overlooks a large green area. Charlesland is just 2km to Greystones village and there are cycle paths to and from the village. Dublin Bus and Aircoach bus routes also operate close to Charlesland. Nearby you have a new primary school and in the village there are several more primary and secondary schools to choose from.\n\nThe property is currently vacant and there is no onward chain, a personal inspection is recommended.\n\n\n\n2 Bed own door apartment \nAccommodation of approx. 70 Sq M\nGas fired central heating\nDouble glazed windows\nSoutherly aspect patio area\nDesignated parking space\n500m to Supervalu shopping centre\n2.6km to Greystones Dart station and beach\n600m to Aircoach and Dublin Bus stops\nCycle lane to and from Greystones village\nManagement company Petra property management\nAnnual service charge of approx. \xe2\x82\xac500\n\n\n\nHall 1.83m x 1.32m.\n\nLiving Dining Room 5.06m x 4.47m.\n\nKitchen 3.66m x 2.4m.\n\nBathroom 2.31m x 1.88.\n\nBedroom 1 4.2m x 2.64m.\n\nBedroom 2 3.2m x 2.3m.
