    DNG Duffy are delighted to bring to market No 24 Cois Farraige. This impressive 4 bedroom semi detached residence is set in a small development and just a short stroll to the seafront in Blackrock Village. Patio doors lead to an enclosed garden offering a perfect safe space for children and entertaining. Accommodation: Entrance hallway, sitting room, kitchen, laundry room/storage, WC.First Floor: Master bedroom, ensuite, bedrooms 2 and 3, family bathroom. Second Floor: Bedroom 4 with ensuite and storage room.Nestled in the seaside village of Blackrock, No 24 Cois Farraige is convenient to all local amenities including Dundalk golf club, newly renovated Fairways hotel and leisure Complex. It also offers easy access to Dundalk town and the M1 motorway.
