      ***************A LOVELY THREE BEDROOMED REFURBISHED HOUSE***RIGHT ACROSS FROM SUPERVALU IN RANELAGH. *************** \n\n\n\nTemple Place is a lovely terraced property located in the heart of fashionable Ranelagh and literally seconds from the LUAS station. The property has been refurbished, including painting and cleaning and some comfortable seating. It is exceptionally bright and is a haven of tranquility, especially the lovely private garden to the rear. The accommodation comprises a large living room with a feature fireplace and very attractive wooden floors. This room is furnished nicely with new couches, lamps, pictures and occasional pieces. The kichen is of a very generous size and comes with all possible modern appliances. There is a large dining table in the kitchen, overlooking the private garden through double glazed doors. \n\nThere are three bedrooms and a bathroom at first floor level, comprising one double room and two single rooms, all of which have plenty of storage. The bathroom is positively gleaming with lovely white sanitary ware, feature mosaic tiling around the bath and mirrors and lots of natural light, by means of a Velux window. In terms of location, this place is unrivalled, being located right in the heart of Ranelagh and across the road from SuperValu and LUAS. What more could one ask for, truly? One is spoilt for choice in terms of shops, restaurants, boutiques, video stores, organic restaurants to name but a few. Email Geraldine Keary at Keary Estates to arrange a viewing. Viewing times are very flexible to suit today's fast pace of life!
