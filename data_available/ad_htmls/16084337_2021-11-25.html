    O'Connor Murphy are delighted to present a unique opportunity to acquire a truly special home, 19 The Glen is a truly stunning detached family residence situated in a most attractive and well-kept development on the grounds of Ballyneety Golf Club boasting an 18 Hole championship golf course.\n\nA spectacular property has been cleverly designed to ensure all living accommodation is orientated to benefit from a south facing aspect ensuring maximum sunlight throughout the day. This deceptively spacious four-bedroom property extends to an impressive 234.68 sq m. Boasting all modern comforts any discerning purchaser would require, whilst clever in design, with an emphasis placed on light, style and everyday family living which enhances the property's already wide appeal. \n\nPresented in turnkey condition throughout, this home has been lovingly cared for by its current owners. This well-designed accommodation spread out over two floors; Internally the generously proportioned accommodation at hall level flows wonderfully from room to room overlooking the manicured gardens that surround the property perfectly. Accommodation briefly comprises; Entrance hallway, Kitchen/Dining room, 3 reception rooms, Sunroom, upstairs 4 substantial bedrooms, two of which are ensuite & a family bathroom.\n\nNo 19 is a superb family home with excellent living accommodation, Bright & well-proportioned reception rooms, and wonderful space throughout, on arrival the entrance hallway is warm, welcoming & spacious, with hardwood flooring & feature coving. Boasting a convenient WC as well as under-stairs storage, this room provides an elegant arrival to your home. The spacious living room to the left of the hallway overlooks the front garden again has feature coving, recessed lighting & a marble feature fitted fireplace. Leading into the dining room to the rear is a great space for entertaining with double doors leading to a light filled sunroom that has been cleverly designed allowing sun all day long and is the perfect spot for outdoor dining on the patio whilst taking in the picturesque setting of the mature all important south facing rear gardens. To the right of the hallway is an ample family room with wooden flooring, coving & feature fireplace overlooking the front garden, a bright room with a cosy feel.\n\nThe kitchen is undoubtedly the centre piece of this superb home. It is a bright and carefully designed room with double french doors leading out to the garden and a stylish fit-out with integrated appliances, porcelain tiling, granite worktops & recessed lighting . The thoughtfully designed kitchen island offers a dining table and island hybrid. Off the kitchen is a utility with fitted units & is fully plumbed with access to the rear garden from here. To the right of the kitchen is a very spacious TV room/games room with wooden flooring.\n\nAn impressive staircase leads up to the exceptionally generous landing drenched in natural light . At this level there are four generous bedrooms, two of which have en suites. Each bedroom has been tastefully decorated & the main bedroom is decorated in neutral tones and offers a generous en suite and walk in wardrobe. The family bathroom tiled floor to ceiling & completes the accommodation at this level.\n\nOutside, 19 The Glen rests on a unique setting with an enclosed south facing rear garden with a natural stone paved patio area - perfect for BBQ's, whilst also enjoying a large lawn area with an abundance of mature planting, shrubs & hedging. Side access from the front & rear of the property. A small shed is located at the side of the property. Similarly, the front is well laid out with tarmac giving ample off-street parking & a large lawn with mature hedging.\n\nIt is rare that such a large, well-designed home with such large gardens is available & therefore the quality of this home needs to be viewed to be fully appreciated & will undoubtedly appeal to those of you considering up sizing or simply looking for something a little bit special. A truly perfect home for any young families looking for space & convenience in what only can be described as a superb residential location.
