    DNG are delighted to represent the sale of 48 Edenmore Grove, Raheny, a well maintained two-bedroom terraced home (Originally a three bedroom ) . This property is sure to appeal to all those looking for that something very special. This home has everything to offer including space, design innovation, and safety. Whilst in need of some modernisation this property offers an excellent opportunity to any potential buyer to put their own personal stamp on it.\n\nThe accommodation extends to a total floor area of c. 764 Sq ft. and comprises entrance porch, entrance hallway, living room, kitchen/dining room, downstairs bathroom, two bedrooms and family bathroom complete the upstairs accommodation .\n\nEdenmore Grove is located just off the Springdale and Tonlegee Roads in Raheny. Situated within close proximity of a wealth of amenities including the Donaghmede S.C., Raheny village, a selection of schools and recreational facilities and many transport services run directly by including the Dart at both Raheny and Harmonstown.
