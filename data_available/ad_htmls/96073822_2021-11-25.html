    Development Description

      Tandy's Lane is the next exciting development by Quintain in Lucan. \r\n \r\nBuilt to the highest standard, these tastefully designed, 3 & 4 Bedroom Houses offer an attractive and varied mix of house types perfect for every life stage. \r\n \r\nDesigned by Conroy Crowe Kelly Architects, the homes at Tandy's Lane will be thoughtfully designed for easy modern living and will include attractive exterior facades, light filled spacious interiors and high specification kitchens and bathrooms. \r\n \r\nLess than half an hour's drive from Dublin City Centre and nestled amidst green tranquil surroundings, Lucan is the best of both worlds. Adamstown train station is only 15 minutes' walk from Tandy's Lane and provides an excellent service to Park West, Heuston Station, Connolly Station, Pearse Street Station and Grand Canal Dock. The N4 takes you in to Dublin City Centre and the M50 connects you to the rest of the city and beyond including Dublin Airport which is a mere 25 minutes' drive.\r\n\r\nTandy's Lane is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts. Moments from the development is the newly opened Tandy's Lane Park with its large playground and a second brand new park, Airlie Park is due to open in 2022. Sports enthusiasts are well catered for too with Sarfields GAA club, Lucan United Soccer Club and Weston Hockey club all close by. The Hermitage and Lucan golf clubs are also a short distance away.\r\n\r\nAll the everyday essentials are taken care of as there is an abundance of retailers nearby. Lucan Shopping Centre and the newly built Lidl are within 5 minutes' drive away as well as one of Ireland's largest shopping centres, Liffey Valley Shopping Centre with over 80 stores, a Vue Cinema and a wide array of restaurants and bars. \r\n \r\nEducation is at the heart of this family friendly community and there is a wide variety of both primary and secondary schools in the area surrounding Tandy's Lane. \r\n \r\nTo register your interest, please visit www.tandyslane.ie
