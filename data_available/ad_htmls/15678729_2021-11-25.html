    Achill Island Property is delighted to bring to market this large dwelling on a substantial plot of 0.74 hectares, in the village of Dooniver on Achill Island. Located only 550m from the golden sands of Dooniver Beach, one of Achills hidden jewels, you can enjoy the best of what Achill has to offer virtually on your doorstep.\r\n\r\nWhile in need of some works, this home boasts ample accommodation and outdoor space to allow you to enjoy simply living. If you are thinking of a family home, holiday home or a project to put your mark on over time, this could be for you!\r\n\r\nResidential accommodation comprises:\r\n\r\nGround Floor\r\nReception Hall: 6.21m x 1.25m\r\nLiving Room 1: 3.27m x 4.57m\r\nKitchen: 4.43m x 3.49m\r\nBedroom 1: 4.56m x 3.28m \r\nBedroom 2: 4.59m x 3.30m\r\nBedroom 3: 2.16m x 2.82m\r\nDining Room: 3.45m x 3.54m\r\nBathroom: 2.17m x 1.78m\r\n\r\nFirst Floor\r\nLanding: 3.36m x 1.40m\r\nBedroom 4: 3.68m x 6.22m\r\nLiving Room 2/Bedroom 5: 6.54m x 3.68\r\n\r\nFeatures:\r\n\r\n- Traditional Outbuildings\r\n- Large Site\r\n- Ample Accommodation\r\n\r\nServices: Mains electricity and water. Septic tank system\r\nHeating: Oil fired central heating\r\n\r\nAchill Island Property is your property partner in Achill and available at all stages of the process to help and advise you!\r\n\r\nAchill Island is host to a range of activities to cater for all tastes. Water is at the centre of the leisure industry on the island, having many providers ready to spoil you, and the rolling, rugged landscape offers a multitude of choice for those with a preference for terra firma. Whether surfing, sailing or snorkelling, cycling, hiking or golfing, Achill Island has something for everyone.\r\n\r\nAs a muse to many over the centuries, Achill has a strong cultural and artistic community that is active all year round, for the enjoyment of locals and visitors alike. If you are thinking of making Achill Island your home, there is much for you to discover!\r\n\r\nStrategically located at the heart of The Wild Atlantic Way, Achill Island is less than an hours drive from larger centres such as Westport and Castlebar, with Ireland West Airport Knock only a little further out the road for those from further afield!\r\n\r\nImportant: \r\nAchill Island Property gives notice that these particulars/dimensions, whilst believed to be accurate are set out as a general outline for guidance and do not constitute any part of an offer or contract.
