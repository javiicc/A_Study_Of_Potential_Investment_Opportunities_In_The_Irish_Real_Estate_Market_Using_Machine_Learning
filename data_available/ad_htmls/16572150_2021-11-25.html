    Brambleton is a beautifully presented detached family home set on 1 acre approx. of mature gardens. Set amidst a tranquil countryside setting this sunny home is filled with natural light and captures vistas of the Sugar Loaf to the front and Djouce mountain to the rear. The perfect location for those looking for a peaceful and laid-back country lifestyle with a large, detached garage and easy access to the Wicklow Way walking and cycle routes while still have a close proximity to Dublin and the Southeast from the N11/M50. \n\nHaving undergone a complete renovation in 2014/15 this spacious home has been transformed into a contemporary retreat. Stepping through the open porch into a wide and welcoming entrance hall that leads to a cosy living room to the front of the property with a feature solid fuel stove and high-quality oak flooring that runs throughout the house. The heart of this home is the large split-level kitchen/dining/family room. The bespoke hand painted kitchen with large island topped with white Quartz flows seamlessly into the great dining area that looks over a glass balustrade into the sunken family room. This area has floor to ceiling windows and a large sliding glass door that connects the space to the rolling lawns of the rear garden, The sleek modern stove with feature wooden mantle makes a lovely focal point. This home is further enhanced by a great balance of living and bedroom accommodation with 4 bedrooms 2 of which are en suite, a generous family bathroom and a laundry room. Upstairs there is an attic conversion that is split into two rooms, the smaller room is currently used as a home office and the second is used as a playroom with a convenient guest w.c. Both still allow for generous eves storage. \n\nThe property is within 8km of Bray and Greystones and within easy access of Enniskerry, Roundwood and Kilmacanogue Villages which are all packed with scenic treasures such as Powerscourt Waterfall & Gardens, and Avoca Handweavers. Enniskerry Village offers a selection of shops and eateries along with reputable primary schools and a choice of secondary schools nearby. For the golf enthusiasts, Powerscourt, Glen of the Downs, and Dun Laoghaire Golf Clubs are all within close proximity. The N11 is easily accessible which acts as a gateway to the M50 or sunny southeast. Bray DART station can be reached by private transport under 15 minutes and the number 44 Dublin Bus terminal is in the Centre of Enniskerry Village. The St Kevin s bus service serves the area twice daily which commutes to Glendalough and Dublin City Centre.\n\n\nGARDEN \nWith stone pillars one enters these lovely gardens through wrought iron electric gates and sweep down the gravel drive to the house. The front garden is bordered by mature trees and is mainly laid with lawn, there are wonderful vistas over the top of the Sugar Loaf mountains and to the surrounding countryside. There is a large, detached garage to the side of the property. Coming around to the rear garden one is surrounded by the views of the countryside towards Djouce and Roundwood. Neighbouring farmland allows for views of livestock and changing colours during each season. The rear garden has two large patio areas one coming from the kitchen/family room at the rear of the house and the second sunken slightly into the surrounds both capture the afternoon and evening sun. The large lawn stretches to the neighbouring properties and is perfect for those budding footballers.\n\n\nEntrance Hall An open covered porch leads through to a wide entrance hall with engineered oak flooring; enclosed under stairs storage and decorative radiator covers.\n\nLiving Room A lovely dual aspect reception space to the front of the house. The bespoke hand pained Beech kitchen was designed by Fitzgerald s is accented by the white Quartz countertop on the large island. There is an intergrated fridge; dishwasher and stainless-steel Rangemaster Professional range with gas burners and double oven. There is a separate fully shelved pantry for all the essentials. The custom cabinetry continues into the dining area with room for a very large table that looks over the family area through the glass balustrade.\n\nBedroom 4 Double bedroom with oak flooring to the front of the property.\n\nBedroom 3 A second double room to the front of the property with oak flooring.\n\nBathroom This stylish family bathroom incorporates a w.c; w.h.b and bath with electric shower and glass patrician.
