    Wonderful 3 Bedroom detached bungalow on a prominent corner of St. John's Road in the leafy heart of Wexford Town. Originally built in 1929, this has been a one family home since the fifties. Along the way it was a Bord Failte B&B then in Alastair Sawday's Special Places to stay and is still up on Airbnb. All because of its great location close to all Wexford Town has to offer. \r\n\r\nThe detached house with garden on three sides means that you have the best of both worlds.\r\n\r\nIt even comes with a postern gate giving separate access to the back patio, poly tunnel and Kitchen door. There is also a heated modern garage which has been subdivided for laundry, storage and propagation. There is access to this garage by right of way over the adjacent lane. \r\nThe lane is not part of the property.\r\n\r\nThe energy rating is deceptive and merely reflects the age of the property as the current owners have done much to enhance it.\r\nThere are two Stanley stoves in the house plus the amazing Kachelofen in the sitting room which some might recognise from Austrian Ski Resorts! \r\nIt is a clay oven which radiates heat and is so efficient it only needs cleaning out every 6 months or so.\r\n\r\nAll the windows are double-glazed (either in 2000 or 2009) and the attic is very well stacked with insulation.\r\nThere are also Solar panels on the roof for hot water and a 500 litre tank to go with them. \r\nThe boiler has a new burner and pump and the heating is zoned within the house, including the OFCH underfloor heating in the main kitchen and electric UFH in the largest bathroom.\r\n\r\nThe house itself is very much in the style of John's Road with the half pebble dash, half render look and the fine detailing of the fascias.\r\nThe pretty front garden has a great history of organic cultivation.\r\n\r\nOriginally a three bedroom bungalow, this was extended to the rear in 2000 adding a kitchen and another ensuite bedroom.\r\nThe original bedrooms were reconfigured with ensuites as befits its B&B status.\r\n\r\nThe Sitting room has the Kachelofen and the door to the two storey extension, a lovely west facing bay window to the front and a side window looking townward.\r\nUpstairs in the lovely modern extension is the ensuite bedroom and downstairs is the kitchen/dining room.\r\nThe bedroom has a large window with wonderful views over the town and even as far as the harbour and the Raven Forest, I kid you not!\r\n\r\nThis side of the house has its own modern double glazed door and its own gate to the street.\r\nLocal residents parking is only \xe2\x82\xac10 p.a.\r\n\r\nAll in all, this is a fabulous opportunity to obtain a wonderful house on the most sought after road in Wexford Town.\r\n\r\nServices: ESB, Oil-fired central heating, solar panels, three stoves. Fibre Broadband.\r\n\r\n\r\nAccommodation\r\n\r\nEntrance via Hall Door with rain porch and solid wooden door with stained glass panels and sidelights.\r\nHallway (4.5m x 1m) with carpeted concrete floor.\r\nSitting Room (3.59m x 3.5m) with timber floor and bay window. Kachelofen.\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 (3.45m x 3.3m) original wooden floor, enjoys the radiant Kachelofen, window to the south facing rear.\r\nEnsuite (1.8m x 1.35m ) Wooden floor, shower cabinet, wc & whb. Heated towel rail.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 (Now the Airbnb Kitchen) (4m x 3.7m) original wooden floor. Fully fitted kitchen and dining area. Stanley Oisin Stove.\r\nUtility area with washing machine and dryer. Modern PvC half door leading to south facing yard and garage.\r\n\r\nBedroom 3 (3.86m x 2.38m) carpeted over original wooden floor with window to front, fireplace.\r\nEnsuite (2.7m x 2.4m) with window to front, tiled with curved bath with shower, wc & whb. \r\nHeated towel rail and heated floor.\r\n\r\nGarage (5.8m x 4.8m) converted and heated workshop ideal for potting, storage and laundry with wc & whb.\r\n\r\nThe garden has been an urban small-holding with polytunnel, a 2 bay brick composter and rainwater harvesting all available to be utilised by the new owners.\r\n\r\n\r\nDirections\r\n\r\n5 minutes walk from Whites Hotel, head up Georges St. and through the crossroads with John St. \r\nCarry on up and Cuasn\xc3\xb3g is on the second left turn at the junction with O'Connell Ave.\r\nThe Eircode is Y35 EH21.\r\n\r\n\r\nViewing Directions\r\n\r\nViewing by appointment with the sole agents Kearney & Co.\r\nStrict Covid protocols will be observed.
