    Lisieux , Greenville Close, is an elegant, stylish & modern 5/6 bedroomed (approx. 207 sq mts) property, superbly located in a small exclusive cul-de-sac within walking distance of Enniscorthy town with an array of shops, restaurants, cafes, excellent amenities, schools and public transport to hand. This cleverly designed home is in excellent condition and the new owners will be delighted with the light & space this home offers. It comprises an entrance hallway, kitchen/dining room with a solid-wood, shaker style kitchen, sitting room with light-filled window & solid-fuel stove, home office or bedroom 6 with accessible en-suite on the ground floor. The first floor consists of five bedrooms, one of which has an en-suite and a family bathroom. The property captures a wonderful sense of space and light both inside and out, the gardens are landscaped and very private and also has a large detached garage. There is an excellent balance of well-proportioned and tastefully detailed rooms, in which quality features throughout. Lisieux is guaranteed to appeal particularly to families seeking a home of quality in a quiet, mature setting in a nice neighbourhood.
