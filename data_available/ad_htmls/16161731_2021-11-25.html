    O'Connor Estate Agents are delighted to be bringing to the market 6 three bedroom apartments in Gateway Student Village and offering a great opportunity to acquire an Investment in an established and well managed Student Accommodation complex. \r\n\r\nThe apartments represent a fantastic Investment Opportunity with a Leaseback Agreement in place the apartments are fully managed and Let with each providing a Net Annual Income of approx. \xe2\x82\xac15,000 (approx. \xe2\x82\xac90,000 in total). \r\n\r\nEach apartment is fully furnished and comprises a Living Room and Kitchen with built in units, fridge freezer and an oven/hob.
