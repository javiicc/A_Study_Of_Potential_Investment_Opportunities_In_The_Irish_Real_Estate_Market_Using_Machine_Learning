    ** SOUTH FACING REAR GARDEN, 4 BED PLUS CONVERTED ATTIC **\r\n\r\nFinnegan Menton are delighted to present No. 28 Glencairn Park to the market - a beautifully presented, semi-detached home boasting many notable features.\r\n\r\nNestled in a quiet cul de sac overlooking a green space, in the hugely popular Gallops development, this wonderful family home has been lovingly and impeccably maintained by it's long time owner occupier, and as such it comes to the market in excellent decorative order throughout, leaving very little for the new owners to do except unpack. Behind the facade of this deceptively spacious family home, generously proportioned accommodation and light filled living spaces are on offer, with the sunny rear aspect flooding the living spaces with natural light, ensuring a bright and airy feel throughout the house.\r\n\r\nAmong the many notable features are a large converted attic with fantastic views of the Dublin mountains, modern fitted kitchen and bathroom suites, guest w.c., and master bedroom ensuite. The features continue outside with a wide side entrance, OSP to the front and a fantastic south facing rear garden which is not overlooked, ideal for al fresco dining and soaking up the tranquil setting of this quiet mature neighbourhood.\r\n\r\nNo. 28 enjoys an enviable location, tucked away from the hustle and bustle while still enjoying every conceivable amenity on its doorstep, with the villages of Sandyford, Stepaside, Foxrock and Cabinteely all close by. Dundrum Town Centre, Carrickmines Retail Park, and Sandyford Business District are only minutes drive away and there is also easy access to the M50 motorway and major road networks. Several creches, and many primary and secondary schools including Holy Trinity National School, Gaelscoil Shliabh Rua, Stepaside Educate Together and Our Lady's National School are close by, as are a wealth of local amenities including numerous golf clubs and Leopardstown racecourse. Practically equidistant from both Glencairn and The Gallops Luas stops, it is well serviced by public transport with a host of bus routes on its doorstep and the Gallops Luas stop just 300m away, the journey into the city centre and Cherrywood could not be easier.\r\n\r\n\r\nFEATURES: \r\n\r\nBeautifully presented semi detached home in a quiet cul de sac\r\n4 bed plus large converted attic with views of Dublin mountains\r\nSouth facing rear garden\r\nBright spacious accommodation\r\nScope to extend (s.p.p.)\r\nModern fitted bathrooms and kitchen\r\nOSP to front\r\nWide side entrance\r\nWell serviced by Dublin Bus and Luas\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nACCOMMODATION: \r\n\r\nGROUND FLOOR: \r\n\t\r\nEntrance Hall: c.6.22m x c.2.54m (*AWP)\r\n\t\r\nLiving Room: \tc.5.42m x c.4.15m (*AWP)\r\n\t\r\nDining Area: \tc.3.84m x c.2.90m (*AWP) \r\n\t\r\nKitchen: \tc.2.34m x c.2.81m (*AWP)\r\n\t\t\t\r\nBedroom 1: \tc.3.56m x c.3.11m (*AWP) \r\n\t\r\nEnsuite: \t c.1.78m x c.1.66m (*AWP) \r\n\t\r\nBedroom 2: \tc.2.63m x c.2.60m (*AWP)\r\n\t\r\nBedroom 3: \tc.2.61m x c.2.67m (*AWP) \r\n\t\r\nBedroom 4: \tc.3.81m x c.3.22m (*AWP) \r\n\t\r\nBathroom: \tc.1.80m x c.2.03m (*AWP) \r\n\t\r\nAttic Space: \tc.4.42m x c.6.03m (*AWP)\r\n\t\r\n*AWP (At Widest Point)\r\n\r\nOutside: South facing rear garden not overlooked.
