    RAY COOKE AUCTIONEERS take great pleasure in presenting this three bedroom semi detached property to the market with one of Dublin 22's most prestige of addresses. Also within a stone's throw you will find a number of bus routes, the N7, the M50 Motorway and The Red Cow Luas Stop. \n\nBright & spacious internal living accommodation is c. 95 sq m (c. 1023 sq ft) of comprises of entrance hallway, lounge/dining room, kitchen, rear extension, three bedrooms and main family bathroom. \n\nNo. 53 is in need of modernisation but boasts magnificent potential to be transformed into the perfect forever home.
