The property sits on just over half an acre of ground from which you can enjoy peace and tranquility and beautiful riverside walks are just a few minutes away \r\n\r\nThere is fantastic space both within and outside the property for families to enjoy. Ground floor accommodation consists of a large living room, TV Room, kitchen / breakfast area. A sun room has been added to the property off the TV room and this provides a pleasant space in which to relax and take in the surrounding countryside. To the front of the property is a bedroom that benefits from having its own fully tiled en suite shower room thus adding to the flexible nature of the accommodation on offer. \r\n\r\nOn the first floor are a further 3 Bedrooms and a main bathroom that is again fully tiled. The master bedroom on this floor also has its own en suite shower room. \r\nBedroom 2 on the first floor deserves special mention as it is an exceptionally large room and spans the depth of the property. There is a good standard of finish throughout the property and the floor finishes in most of the ground floor rooms are either tiles or wooden floors giving a stylish but low maintenance finish. \r\nThe property is serviced by an oil central heating system, mains sewerage supply and mains water supply. \r\n\r\nA detached garage measuring 30 sq mtrs has been positioned to the side of the property and would be ideal as a home office or workshop. The position and location of the property affords the occupier an opportunity to appreciate the peaceful countryside surroundings without having to compromise on the convenience of being close to the suburbs of Limerick. Schools in the vicinity include St Munchins College, St Marys girls school, Parteen National School and Scoil Ide national school. Tesco express and Lidl in Corbally provide a close and extensive shopping amenity while Barrys Shop and McMahons Public house offer more immediate amenities. An early viewing is recommended to appreciate the quality of the property and site that are being offered for sale just 6kms from the City Centre.\r\n\r\nApprox Area - 180 Sq Mtrs ( Sq Ft)
