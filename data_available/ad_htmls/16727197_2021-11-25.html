    Spacious five bedroom detached bungalow, circa 225sq m, situated on a spacious site with beautiful overland views. Located in Carrickphilip, which is a beautiful tranquil setting, circa 5 minute drive from Kilmeaden Village and circa 20 minutes from Waterford city. Spacious accommodation comprises of entrance hallway, sitting room, kitchen, dining room, utility room, four bedrooms, en-suite and family bathroom on the ground floor. The attic space has been converted to include a fifth bedroom with en-suite and a play room. The property also has the benefit of a spacious detached garage to the rear with electric roller shutter door, which currently hosts an entertainment room/bar. The gardens are set in lawn with a beautifully maintained patio area. Oil fired central heating, PVC double glazed windows, private well and septic tank. Viewings are highly recommended through sole agents Barry Murphy Auctioneers. Ground Floor Entrance Hallway: Tiled floor. Kitchen: 6m x 4.8m.
