    Seek & Sell Auctioneers are delighted to present No.84 Church Hill, Dunlavin. This three-bedroom mid terrace property benefits from a neat ground floor kitchen extension. An ideal first home for buyers looking to get on the property ladder, also an ideal investment opportunity for those looking to take advantage of a strong rental market. The front garden has attractive paving and well-maintained flower beds, the rear garden is maintenance free and has a gate to laneway, there is solid fuel central heating and no.84 is within one-minute walk of all amenities in the Village. Dunlavin caters for all day to day living including shopping, schools (both primary & secondary), coffee shop, take away, pharmacy, and several local independent businesses. Dunlavin also has an award-winning playground and a great yearly Arts Festival, and is only c.18km from Blessington, c.30km from Citywest and c.19km away from Naas making it extremely attractive for commuters to the Capital.
