    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    O'Connor Estate Agents are delighted to be bring to the market a superb Investment Opportunity within an excellent location and one of the more sought after developments in area. Positioned across 2 separate blocks within the development the 10 apartments are broken down as follows:\r\n\r\n7 x One Bedroom Apartments ranging from 46sqm to 77.5sqm (500sqft to 835sqft)\r\n\r\n3 x 2 Bedroom Apartments ranging from 65sqm to 76sqm (700sqft to 825sqft) \r\n\r\nThe apartments are all large, finish to a high standard and located across the Ground, First and Second Floors. \r\n\r\nBuilt in 2007 Corn Mill is a modern popular development and each apartment is finished to a high specification to include built in modern kitchen units with appliances, built in wardrobes and all 2 beds have an ensuite off the Master Bedroom. Each apartment also comes with a designated underground car parking space. \r\n\r\nThe Corn Mill development is ideally situated within walking distance from the City Centre and Drumcondra village is a short stroll away offering an abundance of shops, bars, cafes and restaurants. The development is located just minutes from Croke Park.\r\nFurther information on Rents / Pricing available upon request. \r\n\r\n**TRANSPORT** - Drumcondra Train Station is only a few minutes walk away, there are numerous bus routes in the immediate locality, Dublin City Centre is within walking distance and the M50 and M1 are easily accessible offering excellent transport links to the rest of the county and country. Dublin Airport is also within close proximity.\r\n\r\n**AMENITIES** - Conveniently located off Clonliffe Road there are a number of shops, bars and restaurants in the busy thoroughfare of Drumcondra and Fairview. For the sports enthusiast, Croke Park is your neighbour and there are numerous gyms, football clubs and sports grounds nearby.
