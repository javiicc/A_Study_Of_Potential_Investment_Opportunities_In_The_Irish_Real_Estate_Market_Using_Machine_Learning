    ***SOLD***McGregor Properties are delighted to offer for sale by Private Treaty this four bedroom detached bungalow with first floor accommodation at Clounmacon, Listowel, Co. Kerry. The property is finished to a very high standard with extensive use of stone, hardwood and ceramic tile throughout and located 3km (approx) from the Heritage town of Listowel and standing on a spacious site with the benefit of a block built garage/storage shed and mature gardens front and rear. The access to the first floor accommodation is via a hardwood stairs and the accommodation comprises three double bedrooms and a fully fitted family bathroom. Features include PVC double glazed windows and doors, extensive use of stone, hardwoods and ceramic tile throughout, mains water and sewerage by septic tank, spacious well maintained front and rear gardens, gravelled driveway, detached lock up garage/storage shed.
