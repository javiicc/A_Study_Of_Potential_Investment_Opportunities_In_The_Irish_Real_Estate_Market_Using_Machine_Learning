    'A Gem' is the easiest way to describe this upgraded 3 bedroom detached bungalow, that also has a detached garage adjacent to it. Located on the outskirts of Johnstown and a short drive from the motorway access and Kilkenny City, this lovely family home offers a splendid opportunity for someone local or someone relocating, to purchase a really private home in turn key condition.\nThe property is close to schools and so central to other locations such as Laois and Tipperary, it leaves the location of it ideal. The outside space to this property with its sweeping drive and mature trees, create an idyllic setting. The wrap around insulator which has been fitted to the exterior of the house, guarantees a massive snugness and lower heating costs as well as a maintenance free exterior. The garage sits beside the house and could easily be added to or attached to the house, either way there's oceans of space around the house.\nStepping inside you can feel the contentment of the house and it has a happy and useable feel to it. The livingroom is located to the left as you enter and is in turn key condition, with timber floors, ceiling cornice and a solid fuel wood burning stove. There are fitted units to either side of the chimney breast and an oversized window filling the room with light.\nThe central hall gives easy access around the house. The kitchen/dining area is positioned to the rear and is gifted with daylight flooding through dual aspect windows. It's a lovely bright space with a contented feel and oodles of potential. The layout of the kitchen units, give long worktop space and breakfast counter, its ideally laid out.\nThe dining space in the kitchen is to one end and is a comfortable space to dine. The utility room is located off the kitchen with rear access to the outside. The utility is cleverly laid out and with a maintenance free wall finish. A composite half door is an unusual addition and lovely feature.\nThe family bathroom has been upgraded and a splashback installed with a floor to ceiling tiled finish. The finish is immaculate and no further spend is required. The three bedrooms all have bespoke storage space and are all in excellent condition with timber floors leaving them almost maintenance free. The interior doors throughout the house were recently changed and all the windows throughout are PVC. There is attic space with further potential walking through the house its obvious as to the care given to it and the maintenance of it. There's no further spend required with this home.\nThe exterior garden space is fantastic and leaves endless potential. The views alone are worth buying!!
