    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    Cedar Hall is a mature well located development just off the Milltown Road, just a 10 minute walk from Ranelagh Village and Donnybrook Village. No 26 is a beautifully presented 4th floor apartment offering bright and exceptionally spacious accommodation. The accommodation comprises of an entrance hall, living room, dining room/ study, main bedroom with an en-suite shower room, large double bedroom and a bathroom. The apartment has been refurbished within recent years to include replacement double glazed windows, a newly fitted kitchen, bathroom, en-suite shower room and a new gas fired heating system. The view from the balcony is of a fabulous tree lined canopy. The communal grounds are superbly maintained and offer residents and visitor parking. There is also a tennis court and a gym(local charge) for the exclusive use of the residents. Close to the LUAS. The complex also has a locked storage room for bicycles.\n\nViewing of this apartment is highly recommended.
