    Sherry Fitzgerald are delighted to bring to the market, Kiltubrid, Emyvale, Co. Monaghan.\n\nA residential farm of lands located less than 0.5 km from the village of Emyvale, Co. Monaghan.\n\nThe property incorporates a detached cottage situated on 2.98 HA (7.36 Acres) of land.\n\nAccommodation comprises and entrance hall, living room, kitchen, one bedroom and bathroom.\n\nThe lands are held in one parcel to the rear of the dwelling and are currently laid in pasture.\n\nThe property can be sold in one or two seperate lots.\n\nMaps and further details available upon request. \n\n\nEntrance Hall:\n\n\nKitchen: 3.1m x 2.3m.\n\nLiving Room: 3.7m x 3.7m.\n\n\nBedroom 1: 3.6m x 3.7m.\n\nBathroom: 2.9m x 2.6m.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nIn Emyvale village turn off the main street at The Post Office and on to Market House Lane. Continue on this road for 0.3 km approx. and the access to the property is on the right hand side, see directional pointer, turn right onto the lane and the dwelling and lands are located on the right hand side.
