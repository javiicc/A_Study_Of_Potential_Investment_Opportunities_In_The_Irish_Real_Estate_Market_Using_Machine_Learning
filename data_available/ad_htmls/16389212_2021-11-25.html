    Hunters Estate Agent is delighted to present to the market this very fine restored 4/5 bedroom period home. Set on sought after Eaton Place just off Belgrave Square this Victorian property extends to a generous 229sq.m/2,465sq.ft. From the grand first floor salon there are stunning views across gardens to the sea and Howth beyond. The house was built in circa 1850 and retains much of the character redolent of the era and throughout excellent proportions and a wonderful quality of light abound making this a stunning home. The house is set over three floors providing flexible accommodation for purchasers and could provide for between 3-5 bedrooms depending on the preferred configuration. To the rear there is a town garden laid out in patios and mature planting with the advantage of a detached garage with vehicular access.\r\n\r\nEntering through the old wrought iron gate one's eye is drawn to the wonderful Victorian tiles in the porch and the original front door. Once inside the stunning proportions of the house are evident with the grand staircase rising to the upper floors. To the right the light filled drawing room with its open fire, built-in bookshelves and bay window opens through to the dining room with its solid fuel stove and French doors to the garden. Off the dining room there is a well-equipped galley kitchen and a shower room completes the accommodation at this level. Rising to the first floor return there is a spacious family bathroom with travertine tiling and Villeroy & Boch bath. A further flight of stairs brings one to the first floor which holds the jewel of this house the amazing grand salon, currently in use as a bedroom this fine room boasts an extravagant marble chimneypiece, plaster cornice and large bay window with stunning views to the sea and Howth beyond. To the rear a fine double bedroom has the benefit of a stylish ensuite. Rising to the top floor one finds double bedroom with further stunning views out over Dublin bay, off this bedroom is an Jack & Jill ensuite which also serves the fourth bedroom. A further room at this level is currently laid out as a kitchenette/utility but would easily convert to a fifth bedroom should it be required.\r\n\r\nOutside to the rear the south-facing garden is set out in terraced patios with mature planted beds. Two outhouses serve as a utility room and boiler house respectively. Too the rear there is a large detached lean to garage with sliding doors opening to a laneway with rear vehicular access. To the front the original wrought iron railings and gate border a landscaped garden with granite steps, gravel path and mature planting. \r\n\r\nEaton place is situated in an enviable location at the border between Monkstown and Blackrock. With only a couple minutes walk to Monkstown Village there is an enviable selection of boutiques, restaurants and gourmet shops including a branch of Avoca at your fingertips. It is an easy walk to both Blackrock and Dun Laoghaire allowing one to enjoy both the pleasure of fine restaurants and enjoyable coastal walks. There is superb public transport with the Dart Station at Salthill. Nearby schools include CBC Monkstown, Scoil Lorcain, Blackrock College, St. Andrew's, Castlepark, Loreto Dalkey, Sion Hill Blackrock, the Harold and Monkstown Educate Together to name but a few. The location offers a myriad of parks and the popular People's Park with its weekly market is within walking distance. For those water enthusiasts there is local swimming at Seapoint, and Dun Laoghaire offers four yacht clubs.\r\n\r\nViewing is highly recommended.\r\n\r\nSPECIAL FEATURES\r\nStunning 4/5 bedroom period property\r\nExtending to circa 229sq.m/2,465sq.ft\r\nWealth of period features retained\r\nParquet flooring\r\nBreath-taking sea views\r\nRear vehicular access\r\nDetached garage\r\nSouth-facing rear garden\r\nLight filled interiors\r\n11 foot ceiling height\r\nGas-fired central heating\r\nSuperb restored condition throughout\r\nExcellent public transport links including Dart at Seapoint\r\nClose to Monkstown Crescent\r\nExcellent schools nearby including CBC Monkstown and Blackrock College\r\n\r\nACCOMMODATION:\r\n\r\nENTRANCE HALL\r\n1.86m (6.10ft) x 6.63m (21.75ft)\r\nCarpet, staircase to first floor with mahogany handrail.
