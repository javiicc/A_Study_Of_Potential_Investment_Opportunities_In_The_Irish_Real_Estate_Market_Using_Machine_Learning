    DNG Martin O'Connor Estate agents are delighted to present to market for sale this wonderful elevated 0.65 acre site at Trabane, Lettermore over looking the sea and only a short walk from the wonderful white sandy beach 'An Tra Bhain'.\n\nThe site is conveniently located only a 10 minute drive to Lettermore village and all local amenities including shops, National School, Post Office, & Church. Nearby there are also many excellent public Quays for gaining access to the sea. The site is for sale subject to planning to those that have an essential housing need as outlined by Galway County Council.\n\nThis is a terrific site enjoying a splendid setting and for those looking to build in the vicinity it is certainly worth looking at.\n\nFor further details and to arrange a site inspection please contact DNG Martin O'Connor on 091 866708.\n\nFeatures\nElevated site of c. 0.65 Acres (0.263 Ha)\nFor Sale SPP to those with a local housing need\nExcellent views of surrounding countryside\nOverlooking the sea\nWalking distance to 'An Tra Bhain' beach & Trabane Harbour\n10 minute drive to Lettermore village & local amenities including shops, National School, Post Office, & Church\nPublic Quays nearby\nModern national school in the area\n20 minute drive to Carraroe village\nIn close proximity to Luibin Garumna coastal trail\n\nBER Details\nBER: Exempt\n\nDirections\nFrom Galway, take the R336 coast road to Barna, Furbo, Spiddal, Inverin, continuing onto the R374 to Costello, Bealadangan, and into Lettermore village. In Lettermore village, continue past the church (Saint Cholmchille's Church), remaining on the R374 for a further 4km, until you reach Tiernee Church on your right. After Tiernee Church, take the second left, and continue straight along this road for a further 1.4 km, then taking a right and the 1st left, and continue along the road for a further 1.9 km. The property is located on the left hand side, just after the property with the eircode H91 TFR6 and by the DNG Martin O'Connor For Sale sign.
