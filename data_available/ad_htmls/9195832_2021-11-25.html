    Development Description

      Linenfield is the most exceptional new home development in the North East, offering 3 & 4 bedroom homes which surpass all standards of build, finish and efficiency. \r\n\r\nNestled in idyllic countryside, on the Ballymakenny Road, within one of Drogheda's most exclusive and established communities , Linenfield is only minutes to Drogheda town centre and a half hour from Dublin. Linenfield is located 100 metres from St. Oliver's National School, Ballymakenny College and Presentation Primary School. The development will provide a footpath and cycle lane directly along the Ballymakenny Road to the schools that will be completed by January 2021.\r\n\r\nLinenfield is a uniquely distinct superbly designed configuration of homes, streets and open spaces. The development presents a circular arrival plaza and community park, where pedestrians take precedence over vehicles, thereby enhancing neighbourhood safety and community interaction.\r\n\r\nNZEB stands for 'Nearly Zero Energy Building' meaning a building that has a very high energy performance. NZEB is equivalent to a 25% improvement in energy performance on the 2011 Building Regulations. At Linenfield, we are proud that all our homes are NZEB compliant.\r\n\r\nDevelopment Features\r\n\r\nLinenfield offers what no other new builds have, including: Near passive energy efficiency, underfloor heating, floor to wall tiling and fitted wardrobes.\r\n\r\nKey features are:\r\n\r\n-\tNZEB rating\r\n-\tDemand Control Ventilation (DCV) system for automatic fresh air control\r\n-\tA-Rated air to water heat pumps\r\n-\tAir to water heating/cooling system- the house can be cooled during the summer\r\n-\tUnder floor heating on ground floor\r\n-\tEnergy saving LED light fittings throughout\r\n-\tRecessed lighting and pendant lighting in the hallway\r\n-\tShaker style kitchens with a choice of cabinet and worktop colours\r\n-\tSplashback tiling in kitchens\r\n-\tSonas sanitaryware throughout\r\n-\tFloor to ceiling tiling in bathrooms\r\n-\tVanity units and heated towel racks in upstairs bathrooms\r\n-\tQuality fitted wardrobes in all bedrooms\r\n-\tFolding attic stairs\r\n-\tUSB ports in kitchen, living room and main bedroom sockets\r\n-\tOutdoor electric socket in rear garden\r\n-\tCobble lock front drive and paved rear patio\r\n-\tHard wired for electric car charging point\r\n-\tHard wired for solar photovoltaic\r\n-\tHard wired for house alarm\r\n-\tBroadband connectivity\r\n-\tHelp to buy approved
