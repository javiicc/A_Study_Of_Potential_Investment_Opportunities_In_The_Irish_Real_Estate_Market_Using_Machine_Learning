    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Sherry Fitzgerald are delighted to present number 31 Curragh Birin, Castletroy to the market. This excellent 3 bedroom mid-terraced property is the ideal starter home for buyers looking to take their first step onto the property ladder and/or investment option for investors/parents seeking accommodation for students attending university.\n\nFamilies will be drawn to the unrivalled residential amenities within walking distance, while investors may be drawn to lucrative rental market in such a thriving location. While, as always, the University of Limerick continues to be a busy hub in the Castletroy region, the large-scale development of the Northern Trust facility and Plassey Technological Park is sure to increase demand in this location. \n\nCurragh Birin offers a host of superb amenities on your door step, including Castletroy Golf Club, Castletroy College, and a wide range of award winning restaurants and speciality shops. The University of Limerick and its beautiful riverside walks are just a stone s throw away, as is immediate access to all major road networks. \n\nAccommodation internally comprises an entrance hall with under-stairs WC, living room, open-plan kitchen/dining area and a utility room to the rear of the house. Upstairs there are three bedrooms (2 Double, 1 single) in addition to a main bathroom. The bathroom is tiled and complete with WC, wash hand basin and a shower.\n\nServices: GFCH, Off street Parking, 77 sq.m approx\n\nEntrance Hall With front door and staricase.\n\nGuest WC With WC and wash hand basin.\n\nLiving Room With feature fireplace.\n\nKitchen / Dining With part tiled walls, wall and base level kitchen units and drawers, work surfaces, sink and drainer, integrated electric oven and hob with extractor/cooker hood over, tiled floor.\n\nRear Lobby With door to garden and plumbed for washing machine.\n\nLanding With hotpress and access to attic.\n\nBedroom 1 Double bedroom with fitted wardrobes.\n\nBedroom 2 Double bedroom with fitted wardrobes.\n\nBedroom 3 With fitted wardrobes.\n\nBathroom With part tiled walls, panelled bath, WC, pedestal wash hand basin and tiled floor.
