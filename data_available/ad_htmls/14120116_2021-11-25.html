    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    DNG are delighted to present to the market 65a and 65b Leinster Avenue, a very special and unique maisonette style property just a short 1KM distance from Dublin City Centre. Once belonging to Stoney Road Press, these stylish City Centre pads offer a lucrative investment opportunity for the acute investor. \n\nOnce a bustling ground floor corner shop, 65a has been made over to comprise a one bed room open plan space to include kitchen, living room, bathroom and double bedroom with access to the rear courtyard area. \n\nDesigned by Tom de Paor, the unit above benefits from impressive ceiling height, a spacious balcony area, open plan layout and is complete with a separate storage lock up below on Stoney Road. \n\nIdeally set back off the North Strand Road and just a short stroll to Dublin City Centre, Host House is well serviced by an excellent range of local amenities including shops, cafes, schools, sporting facilities, Westwood Gym, Clontarf and Fairview Park. Lidl and Aldi supermarkets are only a 5 minute walk and Fairview village is a short 10 minute walk. East Point Business Park, Dublin Docklands, the Port Tunnel and Dublin Airport are all also easily accessed by car.
