    This very spacious , well maintained & highly desirable 4 bed home is new to the market in a very sought after area of County Donegal . This property comes set on very spacious gardens with stone walls on the side and rear boundary, also a timber shed for storage sits to the rear of the property .\n\nThe very spacious (150sq m ) accommodation comprises a large open plan kitchen and living room combined. This comes with an open fireplace which has a back boiler fitted, timber / tile flooring throughout , double patio doors which open to the side of the property. The utility room is off the kitchen with a back door to the rear of the property. Entry through the main front door opens to a wide spacious hallway which provides the gateway to the 2 down stairs bedrooms and the fully tiled bathroom with built in bath and separate shower. Off this the natural wood staircase leads to the landing where entry to the very spacious walk in hotpress can be found. From the landing is the very generous sized main bedroom with its own fully tiled en-suite and a separate and large walk in wardrobe. The other very large bedroom also boasts its own fully tiled en-suite and comes with a walk-in wardrobe for storage. The house is also heated with oil fired central heating. \n\nThe property which was constructed in 2008 also features unobstructed views of mount Errigal and is close to the Airport, local schools and all amenities. It's moments off the N56 on the R257. Some of the finest beaches in the country are only a short distance away. This would be an ideal family home or would serve as an ideal holiday home due to its perfect location\n\n\nFeatures:\n\n- Situated on R257 off N56 within easy reach of beaches, golf, hiking, airport, spa, leisure and beauty centres, swimming pool, gym, restaurants.\n- Open-plan sitting room, dining and kitchen \n- Modern interior throughout \n- Both first floor bedrooms are en-suite\n- Large lawn garden to front and side\n- Off-street parking\n- Constructed in 2006\n- Oil central heating and double glazing\n- Can be sold furnished if required
