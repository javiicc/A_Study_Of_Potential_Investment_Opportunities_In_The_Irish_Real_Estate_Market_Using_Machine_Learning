    A large 5 bedroom detached dormer style residence on a c. 2.1 Acre site. This spacious family home with a B3 Building Energy Rating extending to Approx. 320m / 3450ft enjoys basement accommodation with door access from the back of the sloping site. This entire floor would be suitable for granny flat accommodation or possible B&B accommodation subject to Planning Permission. Furthermore there is a detached garage to the rear with roller shutter door and suitable for stable accommodation that leads out to c1.5 acre paddock for the equine enthusiast. Location of the property is only 10km from Drogheda in a lovely countryside setting yet only 2km from Tinure village with its own National School and local shop. The Valley Inn Bar & Restaurant is conveniently only a few seconds walk with the added advantage of being only a short drive from Junction 11 & 12 of the M1 motorway, Dunleer, Drogheda and 40 minutes from Dublin airport and the M50 orbital motorway giving easy access to the national motorway network.
