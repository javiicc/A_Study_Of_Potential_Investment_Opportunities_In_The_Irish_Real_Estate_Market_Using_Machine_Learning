    Welcome to No. 7 The Mews, Robswall a beautifully presented two bedroom house that presents a simply magnificent opportunity to acquire a family home in this much sought after residential development. Comprising of entrance hall, spacious lounge complete with double door access to rear garden, kitchen / breakfast area and guest w.c on ground floor level. First floor comprises of two generous double bedrooms with master en-suite and a family bathroom. Further features include PVC double glazed windows, Gas fired radiator central heating, a large attic space with access from a pull down ladder, a well maintained private rear garden complete with barna shed & patio area and designated car parking space. Perfectly located within close proximity of the Coast Road, Malahide village with its host of boutiques, restaurants, shops, cafes, schools and commuter transportation links including Malahide Dart Station. Viewing is a must to appreciate this fine home.
