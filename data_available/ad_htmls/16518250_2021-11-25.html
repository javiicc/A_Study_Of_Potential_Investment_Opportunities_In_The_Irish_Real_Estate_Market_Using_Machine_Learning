    PROPERTY PARTNERS O'BRIEN SWAINE are proud to present one of the finest homes to come to the market in some time. Mount Nebo is a stunning 4 bedroomed, 4 bathroom detached family home situated on superb grounds with exceptional outdoor space to the front and rear complimented by a lovely westerly rear orientation. This superior property was built in 2007 and boasts wooden flooring, a top of the range kitchen and bathrooms, 4 double bedrooms, master en-suite plus walk in wardrobe, a utility room, a home office and a playroom. There is a veranda to the rear of the house which becomes a sun trap in the evening time. Outside the property is approached by a sweeping driveway which leads to the front of the house with a detached garage completing this beautiful home. The upstairs level in the garage has been converted to a hi spec studio which is currently used as a hobbies room. This space is fully insulated and could be used for a variety of purposes. The ground floor of the garage consists of a separate storage room and the main garage.\r\n\r\nMeticulously maintained to an extremely high standard, Mount Nebo is a beautiful, luxurious home in an enviable location approximately 5km from Gorey Town. On entry you are greeted by a large reception hall with an impressive vaulted ceiling which runs through to a bright and spacious livingroom with high ceilings and double doors that lead out to the patio / veranda area. Doors also open through to the spacious kitchen / diningroom. The ground floor also benefits from a home office, utility room and a childs playroom. A feature staircase leads from the reception hall to the upper floor which hosts four large double bedrooms, master ensuite plus a walk in wardrobe and a family bathroom. \r\n\r\nIdeally located in this hugely popular area which is approximately 5km from Gorey Town which boasts a vast array of quality shops, boutiques, restaurants and bars. Gorey is one of north Wexfords finest towns and offers an excellent choice of schools from primary, secondary, post leaving and adult education, Educate Together and a Gaelscoil, along with award winning Hotels and a vast array of local leisure amenities such as endless sandy beaches, golf courses, rugby, gaelic and football clubs, swimming and leisure centres. There is a great choice of sandy beaches close by, while Ballymoney and Courtown with a vast range of social activities, including Ballymoney and Courtown Golf Clubs, are a short drive away. The M50 and South Dublin are little over an hours drive away for those looking to commute.\r\n\r\nThis spacious well laid out accommodation leads in from an impressive reception hallway and comprises of a living room, kitchen/dining room, utility room, an office, playroom, guest wc, 4 bedrooms, master has an en-suite and a walk in wardrobe, an en-suite and a family bathroom.\r\n\r\n\r\nEntrance hallway : Tiled floor.\r\n\r\nLivingroom: 7.17m x 5.48 m, Wooden flooring, high ceilings, stove, tv point.\r\n\r\nKitchen / Dining room: 9.77m x 5.61m, Modern fully fitted kitchen with a good range of wall and floor units, bay window.\r\n\r\nPlayroom: 5.38 x 2.41m, Wooden flooring.\r\n\r\nGuest w.c: 3.24 x 2.3m, W.C, wash hand basin, step in electric shower, fully tiled walls, wooden flooring.\r\n\r\nHome Office: 4.38 x 3.55m, Wooden flooring.\r\n\r\nUtility room: 3.79 x 2.10m, Shelving and storage, plumber for washing machine\r\n\r\nBedroom 1: 5.15 x 3.59m, Walk in wardrobe, bay window.\r\n\r\nEn-suite: 2.69 x 1.46m, W.C wash hand basin, step in shower.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2: 4.84 x 3.90m, Built in wardrobe.\r\n\r\nBedroom 3: 4.84 x 3.77m, Built in wardrobe, bay window\r\n\r\nBedroom 4: 4.84 x 4.08m, Built in wardrobe.\r\n\r\nBathroom: 2.88 x 2.77m, W.C, wash hand basin, bath, step in shower, fully tiled walls and floor.
