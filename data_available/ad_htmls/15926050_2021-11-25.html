    Gallagher Auctioneers are delighted to bring to the market this charming stone farmhouse with detached workshop. Set back from the road this property is located in the picturesque area of Arigna in Co. Roscommon. The property comprises of entrance hall, living room, dining room, galley kitchen and bathroom, stairs to first floor with four bedrooms with a selection of built in wardrobes. The property has ample parking and also benefits from circa 5 acres of land. The workshop benefits from a garage pit and an old style forge which is steeped in history. The larger towns of Drumshanbo and Carrick on Shannon are only a short drive away. Close to the Arigna Mining Experience & the Shannon Blueway.
