This impressive 5 bedroom detached home is sure to impress. The generous front garden, very sunny south westerly aspect rear garden, the contemporary and very bright interior along with the superb location are sure to tick many boxes for the discerning purchaser.\r\n\r\nThe superbly appointed and wonderfully bright accommodation of approx. 222 Sq M comprises an entrance hall with stairs to the first floor and a guest WC off. The living room overlooks the front garden and is almost 25 Sq M in size. It boasts timber flooring and a gas coal effect fire with ornate surround. The real hub of the house is the open plan kitchen dining lounge area that runs almost the full the width of the rear of the house. This wonderful family space will no doubt make a great entertainment space. Large windows flood the room with natural light and provide lovely views of the garden. A sliding patio door leads you to the sunny garden and the patio area which in the summer will be an extension of the dining area. The modern kitchen boasts a large island with sink and waste disposal unit, granite counter tops to the cabinets and island plus a selection of appliances to include the 7 ring Belling Kitchen Range and double oven, American style fridge freezer, microwave and dishwasher. Off the kitchen is a generous utility / laundry room with washing machine and tumble dryer. The ground floor accommodation is completed with a ground floor bedroom and en-suite shower room. The current owners use this room as a home office.\r\n\r\nUpstairs there is a spacious landing with eaves access, 4 nicely proportioned bedrooms and a recently remodelled bathroom with freestanding bath and separate shower. Bedrooms 1 and 2 have built in wardrobes while bedroom 4 has a walk in closet and remodelled en-suite shower room.\r\n\r\nTo the front is a gated and walled garden. Gravel and cobble locking combine to make it very low maintenance. There is ample space for numerous cars. To the rear is an equally large garden that boasts the ideal orientation of south west, allowing the gardens to bask in sunshine from early morning to late evening. The large patio area off the kitchen will enjoy late evening sunshine and is a superb outdoor dining space. The rear garden is very private and not overlooked. There is a wide side entrance and two storage sheds for that essential garden storage.\r\n\r\nManderlay is positioned on Novara Avenue in the very centre of Bray Town. The Dart station and sea front are just 550m from the house and there is a wonderful selection of creches, schools both primary and secondary within a 1 km radius. Bray Main street is just a short stroll away as is the lovely Sidmonton Park and playground. The new town centre with cinema will be about a 5-minute walk from the house.\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nFeatures of note!\r\nDetached 5-bedroom home\r\nAccommodation of approx. 222 Sq M\r\nLarge open plan kitchen dining room opening onto the sunny garden\r\nSuperbly sunny south westerly aspect rear garden\r\nRemodelled kitchen and bathrooms in last 5 years\r\nOff street gated parking for numerous cars\r\nNatural Gas Central Heating System with remote control.\r\nTown centre location\r\nJust 550m or a 5-minute walk to Bray Dart station and seafront\r\nOnly 250m to the lovely Sidmonton Park and Playground\r\nSelection of creches and schools within a 1km radius of the house\r\nSatellite TV.\r\nFibre broadband available to house\r\n \r\n\r\n\r\nEntrance Hall 4.74m x 2.73m.\r\n\r\nLiving Room 5m x 4.89m.\r\n\r\nGuest WC 2.13m x 0.84m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 5 / Study 5.32m x 3.07m.\r\n\r\nEn-suite to Bed 5 1.78m x 1.44m.\r\n\r\nKitchen 5m x 3.91m.\r\n\r\nLounge Dining Room 6.92m x 3.91m.\r\n\r\nUtility Room / Laundry 2.99m x 2.72m.\r\n\r\nLanding 5.73m x 2.31m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 4.45m x 2.67m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 5.44m x 3.5m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 3 3.52m x 3.16m.\r\n\r\nBathroom 4.15m x 2.75.\r\n\r\nBedroom 4 4.99m x 4.11m.\r\n\r\nWalk in Closet to Bed 4 2.19m x 2.05m.\r\n\r\nEn-suite to Bed 4 3.43m x 1.64m.
