    Situated in the scenic countryside of Ballinamult, this 2 bedroom cottage with with outbuildings is standing on 0.5 acres approx. The property has a double entrance driveway driveway with an adjoining paddock to the rear ideal for keeping animals or for conversion to a vegetable patch. \n\nInside, the property is well proportioned with two fine size reception rooms within. The living room is the full width of the cottage and has an open fireplace and a window to the front and rear. The lounge/dining is a cozy room and has a Rayburn stove. The kitchenette is just off this room as are the two bedrooms. \n\nThere are fine size outbuildings to the rear that would be suitable for several uses. \n\nThis property would make the perfect starter, holiday or retirement home and is just minutes from Touraneena GAA field and village.\n\nOFCH.
