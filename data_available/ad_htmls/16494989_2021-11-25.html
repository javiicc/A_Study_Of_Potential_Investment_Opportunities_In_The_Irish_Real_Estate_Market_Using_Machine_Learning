    Cairnsfort is an attractive architecturally designed development located on the ever-popular Golf Links Road which is in close proximity to a host of amenities such as City East Retail Park, Castletroy College, Plassey Technological Park, The University of Limerick, Castletroy Golf Club and a wide range of award-winning restaurants and specialty shops. Cairnsfort is also very accessible with immediate access to all major road networks. \r\nNo. 48 is arranged over three floors and boasts four large double bedrooms and three bathrooms. The property is finished to a very high standard throughout where an emphasis was placed on creating a bright, airy and energy efficient (B3 BER) space with an abundance of generous natural light filled rooms throughout the home. Some superb features include 9ft ceilings, granite fireplace with wood burning stove, wood panelling, fully integrated kitchen, extended sunroom offering extra living space and private landscaped rear garden. \r\n\r\nThis is the perfect home for first time buyers & young professionals looking to step onto the property ladder or buy to let investors alike. It could also be a wonderful downsize option.
