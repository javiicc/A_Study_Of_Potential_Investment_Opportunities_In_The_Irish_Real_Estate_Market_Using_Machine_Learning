    Radharc Na Lougha - View of the Lakes... this magnificent home certainly lives up to its name! \n\nLocated just a 9 minute drive from Killarney town centre with close links to the main Cork and Mallow roads, this dormer bungalow boasts beautiful views of the mountains and Lakes of Killarney. Even on an overcast November morning, its clear to see the views are pretty special. With a floor area of 2,000ft\xc2\xb2, the house sits on a site of approx. 0.84 acre, with lawned gardens to the front and side with mature shrubbery and trees.\n\nThis home has heaps of potential and offers the prospective buyer the opportunity to really put their own stamp on it. \nThe entrance hall is bright and spacious with oak flooring. \nThe lounge is a large bright room with a fabulous feature bay window to enjoy the magnificent views.
