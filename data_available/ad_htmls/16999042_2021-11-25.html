    Beautifully restored farmhouse with an array of outbuildings and spacious studio.\r\nLocated at the head of Waterville Lake with stunning mountain and lake views. On an elevated yet level site extending to approximately 0.75 acres.\r\nThis property has been restored to a high standard with the aim of comfortable full time living and enduring quality finishes have been applied throughout.\r\nOnly recently completed featuring a new kitchen, two bathrooms, separate utility room, master bedroom suite to first floor, guest bedroom to ground floor with open plan kitchen/dining/living and 2nd\r\nreception/sunroom.\r\nOutside the grounds are fully landscaped and laid mainly to lawn with a stone driveway and ample parking and turning. There is a large stone built store and newly developed studio workshop.\r\nFabulous views just a short drive along the lake shore road, approximately 4.5km from Waterville Village with its beaches, promenade, restaurants and pubs.\r\nAwaiting BER. \r\n\r\nDimensions:\r\nEntrance Hall: 5.65m x 3.01m - Glazed sliding entrance door to main entrance hall and conservatory,\r\ntimber floor, glazed on three sides\r\nKitchen/Dining: 4m x 4m - Open plan, timber floor, fully fitted kitchen, timber worktops, range cooker\r\nLiving Room: 3.84m x 4m - Open plan, timber floor, solid fuel stove\r\nSunroom: 5.75m x 3m - Magnificent lake and mountain views\r\nUtility Room: 3.45m x 2.51m - Solid wood door leading to rear garden, walk in airing cupboard/hot-press, plumbed for washing machine/dryer, housing boiler\r\nBedroom 1: 2.8m x 2.55m\r\nBathroom: 2.27m x 1.5m - Bath with thermostatic shower over, w.c, wash hand basin, heated towel rail, fully tiled\r\n\r\nFirst Floor\r\nLanding: 2.75m x 1.4m - Wardrobe Area\r\nBedroom Suite: 5m x 4m\r\nBathroom: 2.8m x 2m - Fully tiled walls and floor, oversized shower with rain head, thermostatic pressure shower, heated towel rail\r\n\r\nOutside\r\nStone Barn: 12.9m x 5.5m\r\nStudio (first portion to left): 6.7 x 5.75m\r\nStudio (second portion): 5.75m x 3.1m - Feature solid fuel stove, well lit with windows\r\nPotting Shed/Store: 2.5m x 4.5m - To side of studio
