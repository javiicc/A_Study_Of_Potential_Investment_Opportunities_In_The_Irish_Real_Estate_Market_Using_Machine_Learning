    DNG are delighted to represent the sale of "Glenwood", 52 Howth Road, Clontarf, a very attractive 5 bedroom red-brick semi-detached home built in 1898. This spacious property provides accommodation over 3 levels and has an amazing 170 ft. long rear garden with detached garage and vehicular access. Internally, the property retains many original fireplaces, coving and ceiling roses and boasts generous floor to ceiling heights. Set back off the Howth Road, there is generous parking for several cars to the front.\n\nThe accommodation extends to a total floor area of approx. 1,970 sq. ft. and comprises: porch, entrance hallway, interconnecting living and dining rooms with matching marble fireplaces and a spacious bay window, kitchen/living room and a downstairs wc. Upstairs, there is a bedroom and bathroom at return level with 3 further bedrooms on first floor level. The 5th bedroom and kitchennette are located at second floor level and complete the accommodation.\n\nGlenwood is located along the Howth Road, Clontarf between the Charlemont Road and Copeland Avenue junctions. There are a host of local amenities and services on the doorstep including an excellent selection of schools, a variety of shops, churches, restaurants, pubs, cafes and sports and recreational facilities including Westwood Gym. The Seafront Promenade and Dart Station at Clontarf are just around the corner and there are a number of transport services which run very close by providing easy access to Dublin city centre, the IFSC and East Point Business Park. Dublin Airport is also within 15 minutes drive away.\n\n\nPorch\n\nEntrance Hall 1.65m x 8.26m.
