    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Horan Estate & Letting Agents are delighted to introduce apartment in The Eden to the Dublin property market. The Eden is in an ideal location with many local amenities including shops, schools, restaurants, bus stops, etc in close proximity.This beautifully decorated, two bedroom first floor apartment is presented to the market in turn key condition throughout and would instantly impress first time buyers and investors alike! \nSome of the features include: \n- Entrance hallway \n- Fully fitted kitchen \n- Bright and spacious living room with floor-to-ceiling windows and balcony attached overlooking mature communal gardens.\n- Gas fired central heating and the windows are double glazed.\n- Long hallway on the upper floor with built-in shelving units for maximizing storage as well as a separate storage cupboard. \n- Two double bedrooms\n- Tiled en-suite to main bedroom with bath and shower and separate fully tiled communal toilet and shower. \n- Main living space is south facing and gets light and sun all day, balcony attached is a suntrap in the summer time. \n- Secure underground car parking with parking space included. Building has lifts servicing all floors. \n\nQuiet location close to all amenities including:\n- 4 mins drive to Clearwater shopping centre \n- 15 mins walk to Finglas village which includes bus routes 40 and 140 into Dublin city centre \n- 15 mins walk to St Canices Boys National School \n- 15 mins walk to St. Mary's Secondary School\n- Within walking distance to the National Botanic Gardens, the Met Office, Glasnevin Cemetery and Bon Secour Hospital. \n- Located on a direct bus corridor to city centre and walking distance to Luas and trains at Broombridge. \n\n\nThis and much more! Viewing is a must!
