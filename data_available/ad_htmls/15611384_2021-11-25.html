    Brophy Cusack Real Estate Team are delighted to bring number 15 Seacliff to the market. Number 15 is a a semi detached property located in the ever popular Seacliff Development. The property also benefits from electric heating, double glazed windows & a rear patio.\r\n\r\nThe property is located in The Seacliff development adjacent to The Dunmore East Golf Club which incorporates The Lighthouse Bar & Black Friars Coffee Shop. \r\n\r\n\r\nSevice Charge : \xe2\x82\xac1200 per annum\r\nSize: Circa 100 Sq.
