    REA Grimes are delighted to present the exceptional 32 Maple Drive to the market. Extending to c.208 sqm (c. 2,236 sq ft) of generously proportioned living accommodation, this substantial family home is in walk-in condition. Ideally situated in the ever popular residential development of Archerstown Wood, within walking distance of all local amenities. This beautiful family home will impress even the most discerning of buyers. The internal layout is practical and family friendly, making the most of all available space with great flow throughout. The imposing red brick front facade is complimented by a cobble lock driveway with off street parking. Space and privacy are an added benefit with the property overlooking a large recreational green area to the front and enjoy warm sunny evenings in your large private rear garden with a south west facing orientation. Upstairs, the first floor, as you would expect from a home of this quality, doesn`t disappoint. There are five bedrooms (two en-suite) and a family bathroom.
