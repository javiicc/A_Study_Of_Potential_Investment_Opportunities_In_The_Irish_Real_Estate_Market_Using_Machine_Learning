    Idyllic 3 bedroom detached house with triple aspect coastal views extending to approximately 144.55 sq, m (1,556 sq. ft). Set on approximately one acre which includes a mature garden and a paddock.\n\nThe property must be seen to truly appreciate the West Cork coastline views. It is situated on an elevated position in the popular area of Ardfield. The property itself is only 5 minutes drive from the local beaches including Inchydoney, Red Strand, Long Strand and Duneen. It is 10 minutes from Clonakilty Town centre and 5 minutes from Ardfield Village and the amenities that it offers including Scoil an Ard Primary School, St. James Catholic Church, St. James\xe2\x82\xacTMGAA club and the renowned Mountain House pub & restaurant.\n\nThe house comes to the market in a well presented condition. The ground floor consists of front porch, kitchen/dining room, living room, three double bedrooms, family bathroom and a rear porch. On the first floor there are two large store rooms currently in use as additional bedrooms and a shower room.\n\nThe house is approached by a tree lined lane. There is a pebble driveway to the front which provides plenty of parking, a raised lawn area and a garage which provides extra storage. The front garden benefits from a southerly aspect. It has a patio, lawn, fruit trees and mature hedging. The property also benefits from a gently sloping half acre paddock.\n\nServices: Septic Tank, Mains Water, Oil Fired Central Heating, Double glazed windows
