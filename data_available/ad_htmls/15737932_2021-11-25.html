    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Pat Considine Auctioneers are delighted to introduce this four-bed bungalow with stunning sea views and all within 500 metres of the White Strand beach in Doonbeg on the west coast of County Clare and on the much acclaimed Wild Atlantic Way.\r\n\r\nThis property is idyllically located in the countryside setting of Killard approximately 500 metres from the White Strand beach. Doonbeg village is 3.5km away while Trump International Hotel and Golf Links Resort is 9km away. Kilrush which is the largest trading town in west Clare is a 15 minute drive from the property.\r\n\r\nDoonbeg village is a quaint coastal village referred to as the long village that offers a range of pubs and restaurants, shops, church, school, astro pitch and Trump International Hotel and Golf Links Resort. There is also a selection of beautiful sandy beaches such as Doughmore and aforementioned White Strand.\r\n\r\nThis well-appointed four-bed bungalow is beautifully set on a spacious 1.1 acre site complete with tennis court and garage. The property comprises of an entrance porch, kitchenette, living room/dining room, shower room, toilet, four bedrooms and a laundry room. The dwelling has oil fired central heating with double glazed windows with a septic tank sewage system. \r\n\r\nExternally there is a large garden to the front and eastern side of the house with a small archway linking the dwelling and the garage. The majority of the stunning views remain to the rear of the house so any possible extension work to that area will take further advantage of the beautiful sea views.
