    Rare opportunity to acquire this unique, superior cottage, newly refurbished to the highest of standards. Idyllically set in one of Dublin's most exclusive residential enclaves and affording easy access to Dublin city via DART. In addition to a prime location by the sea, the property has many outstanding features including walking distance to the famous Dalkey town with its host of amenities. Accommodation comprises; Entrance hallway with tiled floor.. Both double bedroom to the front of the property boasts built in wardrobes and wooden floors. Open plan living/dining/kitchen room steeped in natural light from apex roof windows, tiled floor throughout and wood burning fire. Fully fitted kitchen with granite stone worktops and integrated appliances, with large transformer door to sunny and wind protected outdoor rear patio area. Large bathroom with corner bath, roof light. built in cabinets and heated towel rail.
