    DNG McKenna Healy are delighted to bring to the market No.1 Cabra Hill, a wonderful three bedroom cottage, built c.1910, which retains the warmth and charm of the original cottage while offering plenty of potential to extend further. Set on a large West facing site of approx. 0.5 acre the house and rear garden are divided by a right of way lane way. Cabra Hill is conveniently located just a short stroll to Skerries town centre with its array of shops, restaurants and pubs and scenic coastal walks right on your door step. \nAccommodation comprises of a spacious living room with the original high ceiling and fireplace, a well laid out kitchen to the rear, three bedrooms and family bathroom. \nTo the front is a private garden with pedestrian access, while to the rear is a spacious garden offering vehicular access and a large lawn area which host beautiful views over Skerries town and islands.\n\nSkerries is a thriving seaside village famous for its sandy beaches and bustling town. Sports facilities abound in the immediate area with Golf, Sailing, Rugby, GAA and Tennis clubs all close to hand.\nThe area is well serviced by Dublin rail and bus with the train station located within a short walk of Cabra Hill. Located approx. 12 miles from Dublin airport and M1 motorway providing an easy commute to the City Centre.\n\nAccomodation:\nEntrance Porch 3.06m x 2.2m\nTiled floor, beautiful sea and Island views, patio door to front garden\n\nHallway \nWood floor\n\nLiving Room 3.88m x 3.66m\nWood floor, open fireplace, TV point\n\nBedroom 1 3.65m x 2.65m\nCarpet to floor, double room, over looking front garden'\n\nBedroom 2 3.69m x 2.82m\nCarpet to floor, built in wardrobes, spacious double room\n\nBedroom 3 3.23m x 1.81m\nCarpet to floor, single room, over looking rear courtyard\n\nKitchen 3.19m x 3.03m\nWall and floor mounted units with tiled splash back, plumbed for washing machine, integrated oven and hob, door to rear garden\n\nHall \nTiled floor\n\nBathroom 2.12m x 1.99m\nFully tiled walls and floor, WC, WHB, shower unit with Triton electric shower, window\n\nOutside\n\nFront\nLawn area, pedestrian access, mature shrubs\n\nRear\nWest facing, courtyard style garden with outhouse stone sheds, pedestrian access to rear lane, remainder of garden is vast in size and mainly in lawn with some off street parking\n\nFeatures:\n0.5 Acre Site\nWest Facing Rear Garden\nDouble Glazed Windows Throughout\nOff Street Parking\nO.F.C.H.\nScenic Windmill and Sea Views from the Garden\nSpacious Bedrooms\nPotential to Extend S.T.N.P.P
