    Turley Property Advisors are delighted to bring to the market, 2 Monaloe Crescent, Blackrock, Co Dublin. This Property has been recently very tastefully renovated with emphasis toward the already abundant amount of natural light to the rear of the house. This Idyllic 3 Bed semi-detached bungalow sits on (120 sq. m.) and has been brought up to the most recent of standards whilst still maintaining its original integrity, all of which are encompassed in one of Dublin's most sought after areas. There is a large garden to the rear and side and the owners have received full planning permission for a detached 284sqm 3057sqft bungalow. \r\n\r\nThe open plan nature of the interior living and kitchen areas of the property allows for great manoeuvrability and ease of living and accompanied by the tiled finish on both kitchen floors and stone worktops areas will naturally help against the everyday wear and tear of family life. The master bedroom comes fitted with both an en-suite bathroom and plenty of wardrobe storage, with the other two bedrooms perfectly fitted for children but very easily refitted to a more mature liking either to keep as a bedroom or change for more professional needs. \r\n\r\nThe house and garden are being brought to the market in three lots: \r\nLot 1: The existing home with large south facing garden to the rear with side pedestrian access with two car spaces (\xe2\x82\xac795,000) \r\nLot 2: The site with FPP for a large, detached bungalow (\xe2\x82\xac395,000) \r\nLot 3: The Entire (\xe2\x82\xac1,190,000) Even with all the obvious advantages \r\n\r\n\r\nNo. 2 Monaloe Crescent offers, there is the added bonus of having Cabinteely Park just 7 minutes' walk and Dunnes Stores Cornelscourt Shopping Centre and Cornelscourt village featuring an ample choice of eateries, a good selection of highly regarded primary schools in the area. There is also an excellent transport infrastructure with great public transport options and easy access to the M50 all within a short drive.
