    3 Ard na Tana is the sort of house we can confidently describe as 'having it all'! \r\n\r\nA superb detached 5 bed property occupying a large 0.6 acre elevated site in Riverstown, and part of a small, exclusive neighbourhood of 6 impressive homes. \r\n\r\nArd na Tana is a modern home that's filled with style, comfort and a very smart finish. Presented in turn key condition, this house is going to give buyers a lot to think about, offering over 230 sq m in space, with 5 double bedrooms, 3 reception rooms and a large 0.6 acre site. \r\n\r\nThe property is situated on an elevated site, enjoying breathtaking views over Dundalk Bay at the front, and a beautiful backdrop of Maeve's Gap and Tain Way to the back. The large corner site is enviably set out to be able to enjoy the setting from dawn to dusk. Ard na Tana is a cluster of 6 detached dwellings that each enjoy individual space and privacy. The area is excellent - within under 10 minutes you will reach fantastic amenities such as Carlingford village, a fantastic selection of beaches for walking and swimming, several fantastic walks such as Tain way and Maeve's Gap, and, for leisure venues, Strandfield Cafe & Market, Carlingford Brewery, O'Neill's nursery Fitzpatrick's Bar & Restaurant and Ballymascanlon Hotel are very convenient. For golfers, Ballymascanlon and Greenore 18 hole courses are very popular. The house is c 15 minutes drive from the M1 motorway (J18) and c 20 minutes from Dundalk. \r\n\r\nThe front door and entrance hall immediately set the scene for this dwelling. A Georgian style front door leads into the marble tiled entrance hallway which is flanked at both sides with the well proportioned reception rooms: to left, a dining room with dual aspect and cast iron solid fuel fireplace, to the right a living room with beautiful marble fireplace. These rooms are finished with solid distressed oak flooring, whilst the marble tiling in the hall runs through the remainder of the ground floor. \r\n\r\nIn the kitchen - this is a very large space that offers a lovely sense of open plan living - it leads to a triple aspect sunroom to the left - perfect spot for enjoying the garden and also the light as it has a southerly aspect. At the opposite end of the kitchen is the first bedroom - very large dual aspect room with waking views of the Cooley Mountains outside. An adjacent shower room - very smartly finished with extensive tiling and great accessories - makes the ground floor bedroom a great future proofing / accessible feature. \r\n\r\nUpstairs, the landing - finished in the same distressed oak flooring - is filled with light thanks to the feature landing window. The bedrooms feel like 5 star hotel rooms, given their luxurious and yet pristine finish! All four are doubles, all are finished with the same oak flooring (the continuity of flooring adding to the great sense of space). The main bedroom has a walk in wardrobe and ensuite, and enjoys dual aspect. Another bedroom is interconnected to the main bathroom - which is very finely finished and has corner bath as well as separate shower. \r\n\r\nJoanne commented: The overall sense of space and excellent finish make this an enviable and very attractive turn key home. The house has v fast broadband speeds of 142 mbps & 30 mbps download and upload respectively, so working from home, with beautiful countryside and mountains on your doorstep, will be very easy from this stunning property."
