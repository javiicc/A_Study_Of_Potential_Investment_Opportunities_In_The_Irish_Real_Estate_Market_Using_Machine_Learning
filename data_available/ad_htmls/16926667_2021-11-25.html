    Following the sale of all new 3 and 4 bedroom houses at Delvin Mews, Dwellings are now delighted to introduce the show house for sale. This four bedroom A-rated home is not overlooked to the rear, has a west facing back garden and overlooks a large green space. the show house was designed and furnished by an interior designer and all furniture, fixtures and fittings will be sold with the house at a reduced cost separate to the guide price. Delvin Mews is nearing completion with all residents due to move in shortly.\r\n\r\nThis housing development is located within the town centre yet only minutes from Junction 9 of the M1 Motorway, it is only 25 minutes to Dublin Airport, 15 minutes to Skerries and only 25 minutes to Malahide.\r\n\r\nAs a well established commuter location Stamullen enjoys excellent transport links to Dublin via good bus services every few minutes during commuting times via Bus Eireann or Matthews Coaches. There are also good rail links with Gormanstown Train Station only a 5 minute drive and Balbriggan Train Station is an 11 minute drive from Delvin Mews. The city centre, the M50, Dublin Airport and the Port Tunnel is easily accessible.\r\n\r\nAmenities\r\nStamullen offers a wide range of recreational facilities including a good array of retail, restaurants, community groups and educational facilities. The local community centre offers state of the art facilities with St Patricks GAA football and hurling, badminton and bowls on offer. Stamullen is only a short drive to Gormaston Beach and other beaches nearby. Stamullen also provides a good range of shops and services locally with larger retailers available nearby in Drogheda, Balbriggan and Swords.\r\n\r\nThere are excellent educational and childcare options for home owners at Stamullen, some of which include St. Patrick\\'s National School, Gormanstown College, Bremore Educate Together, Loreto in Balbriggan and residents can also avail of other schools within Drogheda.\r\n\r\nViewings by appointment - contact Lydon Farrell Property on 046 9010126.
