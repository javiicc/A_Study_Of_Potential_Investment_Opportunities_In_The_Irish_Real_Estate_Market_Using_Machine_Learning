    DNG Martin O'Connor present to the market this superbly appointed 4 bedroom detached home set on a mature site of circa 1.35 acres on the banks of the Drimneen River. This property enjoys a prime location with long road frontage onto the N59 Galway-Clifden road.\n\nThe house is well set back and sheltered from the road by mature trees and shrubs - in a stunning position within the site, close to the river banks with a series of footpaths leading from the residence to various areas within the gardens and to the river. The gardens are a mix of lawned gardens and natural woodlands with herbaceous borders containing Rhododendron and Azalais. To the west of the home you will find a series of out-buildings to include a garage, workshop and fuel store.\n\nThe property extends to circa 1,504sq.ft. is bright and spacious with accommodation to include an entrance hallway, east facing living room, dining room, kitchen, sunny west facing conservatory, 4 bedrooms (master-suite), bathroom and utility room.
