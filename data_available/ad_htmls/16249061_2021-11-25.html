    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Howley Souhan Estate Agents are delighted to present to the market this stunning newly constructed detached three/four bedroom property, built and finished to exacting standards, combining a contemporary, modern style with both space and light. This detached family home with converted attic space to provide an additional bedroom is architecturally designed and extends to an impressive approx. 112.37sq.m. / 1209.54sq.ft. \r\n\r\nThe property features the latest in sustainable technology and utilising high-quality materials to provide an exceptional finish throughout.\r\n\r\nTo the front the garden is fully walled, it features a paved driveway providing off-street parking for 2 cars. A side entrance gives access through to the rear garden which features a stunning granite tiled patio, and lawn. \r\nThis well-proportioned home comprises an entrance hallway, front living room/bedroom, downstairs WC, open plan kitchen/dining/family room & utility room. Upstairs offers 3 spacious bedrooms and a shower room. In addition to this, the attic has already been fully converted and offers immense space for an additional bedroom. \r\n\r\nThe prospective purchaser has the choice of selecting their kitchen, flooring, tiles and paint scheme for a truly complete turn-key dream home. \r\n\r\nThe location is an unrivalled one, Rockfield Avenue is a lovely tree-lined road just off Whitehall Road West. There is a fantastic array of amenities to hand including the local Spar, a chemist and The Laurels and The Pines. There is also The Ashleaf, SuperValu and Rathfarnham Shopping Centres with all they have to offer. There are ample recreational facilities in the area with Bushy Park & Tymon on your doorstep and Marlay Park a short drive away. The property is also in very close proximity to a selection of well-established schools such as Our Lady's, Terenure & Templeogue College and St Pius X. The area is well serviced by frequent and dependable bus routes and the property also enjoys the added bonus of easy access to the M50 motorway network.
