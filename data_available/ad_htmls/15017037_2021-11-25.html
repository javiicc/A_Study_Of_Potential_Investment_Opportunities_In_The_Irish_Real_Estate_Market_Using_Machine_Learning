    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Excellent opportunity to purchase an operating business cafe with overhead living accommodation which would make an ideal investment with excellent rental potential on both. To the ground floor is a fully operational cafe with seating area to the front and fully operational kitchen area to the rear. To the first and second floor is a 3 bedroom duplex apartment unit. This property is ideally situated in a prime location on a busy street in the heart of Kilrush town with communal parking to the front. Viewing is strictly by prior appointment with sole selling agents. PSL 002295.\n\n\nAccommodation\nCommercial Unit \nCafe 9.75m (32'0") x 5.5m (18'1")\nLarge window frontage and open plan seating area to the front. Well fitted out with tile flooring, recessed lighting, air conditioning unit and decorative wall panelling.\nTo the rear is a serving station, large amount of storage, coffee machine, prep areas, built in shelving and serving counter. To the back there is a storage area with a walk in wheelchair accessible toilet, fully tiled ceiling to floor, low level WC, and quality wash hand basin.\n\nRear Area 5.6m (18'4") x 4m (13'1")\nIncorporates staff toilet which fully tiled ceiling to floor, low level WC. Small storage, preparation area incorporating kitchen to the rear, tiled floor and fully HASAP approved. Prep areas with equipment including upright stainless steel fridge freezer, electric griddle, fat fryers, six ring commercial gas cooker, Salamander combi oven and a holding oven, salad preparation fridge with overhead heated pantry, dishwasher, second upright stainless steel freezer. Rear access to the property from this area with a gas storage facility and a facility for storing bins.\n\nResidential \nDuplex Overhead \nStairway access leads up to the front door.\n\nEntrance Hallway 6.3m (20'8") x 2m (6'7")\nHallway with carpeted staircase leading to first floor, recessed lighting. Under stairs storage space.\n\nReception 4.7m (15'5") x 3.3m (10'10")\nCarpeted flooring, built in light fitting and large window overlooking the car park.\n\nKitchen/Dining Area 4.55m (14'11") x 3.5m (11'6")\nTimber style wall and base kitchen storage units with tiled splashback. Integrated sink unit, integrated ceramic hob, integrated extractor fan, space for washing machine and dryer, Space for dishwasher and fridge freezer and large window with pull down blind.\n\nShower Room 3.05m (10'0") x 2m (6'7")\nTiled floor, corner electric shower unit with quality shower door, low level WC, wash hand basin and tiled splashback.\n\nFirst Floor Landing Area 2.5m (8'2") x 2.12m (6'11")\nTimber flooring and built in hot press.\n\nBathroom 2.5m (8'2") x 2.1m (6'11")\nCorner bath unit, low level WC and wash hand basin.\n\nBedroom 1 4.1m (13'5") x 3.25m (10'8")\nTimber style flooring.\n\nBedroom 2 4.45m (14'7") x 3.2m (10'6")\nDouble room with timber flooring.\n\nBedroom 3 3.15m (10'4") x 2.5m (8'2")\nTimber flooring and stand alone wardrobe.\n\nPlease note we have not tested any apparatus, fixtures, fittings, or services.
