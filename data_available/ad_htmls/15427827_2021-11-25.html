    DNG WH Giles are delighted to bring to the market this superb family home full of character and charm with a very large wonderful garden right in the heart of the Town. Located walking distance to University Hospital Kerry, Manor Retail park and approx. 15 minutes walk to Tralee Town centre this property is without doubt a hidden gem! \r\n\r\nThe property at present is presented as a three bedroomed home with living room, kitchen, utility and bathroom to the front section of the property and to the rear a further two bedrooms with an additional lounge, kitchen and bathroom available which can be utilised as separate granny flat, apartment or studio. Both sections of this property are accessible from the ground floor currently and both have their own front entrance. \r\n\r\nThroughout this home it has been extremely tastefully decorated with many of its original features brought back to its former glory with beautiful timber floors, original doors, stairs and fireplaces. The kitchens are perfectly designed in line with the character of the home and the living area in both portions of the house are wonderful one very cosy and one an excellent open plan room with double doors that spill out to what can only be described as an extraordinary garden. \r\n\r\nThe garden has everything you would want on your wish list a patio area, decking area, vegetable patch, fruit tree section and many many areas to just sit and enjoy your magnificent setting. In front there is an enclosed garden with front patio and driveway. This home would be suitable for many property house hunters, families, two families that wish to buy together, a family with a elderly relative or someone looking for an investment and family home in one. \r\n\r\nViewing is highly advised.
