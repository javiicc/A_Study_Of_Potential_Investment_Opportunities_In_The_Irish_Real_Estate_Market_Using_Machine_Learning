    A one-off opportunity arises to acquire a ready to move-in property in the sought after development of Whitefield Manor finished to an extremely high standard throughout. This fine 4/5 Bedroom detached three storey family home (Approx. 2,050 Sq Ft) coupled with its enviable location less than 2km from the seaside village of Bettystown and spacious accommodation will impress all who view. Features of this perfect energy efficient home include a desirable large open plan kitchen/living/dining room perfect for any family, conservatory, fully tiled bathrooms, a B1 BER Rating with solar panels, all new floor finishes throughout, 4/5 Bedrooms, Master Ensuite with Dresser room, a converted roof space for that luxury extra bedroom, secure west facing hard landscaped rear garden and a cobble block driveway. Ideally located along a well serviced public transport route, close to all amenities of Laytown/ Bettystown/ Mornington, less than 1km to Donacarney National School, nearby to a host of beaches, 2.7km to Laytown/Bettystown Golf & Tennis Club and 10 minutes drive from both the M1 motorway and Drogheda town centre all makes this a very desirable property.
