    This beautifully appointed, two storey, four bedroom, detached family home is set on one acre of mature landscaped grounds and enjoys an enviable location, being within a five minute drive to the villages of Oranmore, Maree and Clarinbridge. Built in the 1970s, the accommodation extends to 2,583 sq. ft and comprises of entrance hall, off which there are two reception rooms to include a formal dining area, kitchen and separate breakfast room, utility and guest WC. First floor accommodation comprises of a family bathroom, four bedrooms, three of which are en-suite and the master bedroom has a large, walk in, dressing room. The property includes two block constructed stables and a galvanised and steeltech shed.\r\nThe property oozes old world charm featuring gothic style, double glazed windows and solid wood, double entrance doors, with decorative stained-glass frame; hardwood floors, stairs and internal doors, skirting and architrave; classic, white marble, sitting room fire surround; traditional sanitary ware and vintage tiles.\r\nThe original layout had five bedrooms, the fifth bedroom was reconfigured to form a walk-in dressing room off the master bedroom, featuring wall to wall, full height wardrobes and a substantial bathroom suite with bath and separate shower. \r\nA very functional, compact kitchen features solid Cherry wood kitchen cabinetry with an oil-fired range cooker with boiler which has the facility to control the central heating and cook at the same time. Other appliances include a separate fitted electric hob, cooker and dishwasher. There is direct access from the kitchen to the formal dining area of the family room. \r\nDouble sliding doors lead from the breakfast room to rear of the house and there is also a side door off the utility leading to the garden and stables. \r\nThe heating is oil fired central heating with an open fire in the sitting room and a gas fire in the family room. \r\nThis property offers spacious accommodation in a much sought after location and viewing is very highly recommended. \r\n\r\nRoom description and measurements in meters;\r\n\r\nEntrance hall: 1.84 x 7.12.
