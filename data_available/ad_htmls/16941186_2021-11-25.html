    11 Burnell Court is a well presented fourth floor, two bedroom apartment situated in this highly sought after complex. The spacious accommodation comprises entrance hallway, 2 double bedrooms, master en suite, a fully tiled bathroom and a large bright living area with a South facing balcony and modern kitchen. The apartment is fitted with double glazed windows and a security intercom system. Burnell Court is a popular development and boasts ample secure parking, communal green areas and a rooftop terrace for residents. 11 Burnell Court is presented in good condition and will appeal to first time buyers, investors or someone looking to downsize. There is a designated parking space in the underground car park which facilitates safe and easy access to the apartment. The location couldn't be more convenient with all local amenities on your doorstep. Burnell Square is accessed through the entrance across from Meadows and Byrne at Northern Cross, host to a large variety of local shops and boutique restaurants.
