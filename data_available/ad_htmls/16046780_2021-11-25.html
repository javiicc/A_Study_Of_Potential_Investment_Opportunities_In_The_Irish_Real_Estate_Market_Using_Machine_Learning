    Ocean Property Management are delighted to offer for sale this stunning 3 bedroom, 3 bathroom detached house located in an estate in the popular Doughiska area. This pristine property has been well maintained over the years by its current owners and is in immaculate condition both internally and externally. The owners have largely redecorated the property in recent years with new furnishings, most of which are included in the sale price. The property was originally a 4 bedroom house but the current owners have since converted the smallest room into a spacious walk-in wardrobe accessed through the master en-suite bedroom. There is a floored attic space which can be accessed by way of a stira stairs providing additional storage space. The well maintained back garden can be accessed by a patio door or by the gated side entrance of the property. There is a large shed located in the garden along with a beautiful veranda which was installed by the current owners. The house is only a short walk to Briarhill Shopping Centre, Briarhill Business Park, Parkmore Industrial Estate, The Galway Clinic, Merlin Park University Hospital and more. The area boasts many other local amenities such as a creche, schools, shops, playgrounds, restaurants and the beautiful Merlin Woods. There is a regular bus route to and from Galway City at the entrance to the estate. Accommodation: Front Hall - 6m x 2.1m (laminate flooring) Kitchen / Dining - 5.9m x 3.9m (tiled flooring, fully fitted, patio entrance to back garden, double door entrance to living room) Living Room - 4.1m x 5.4m (laminate flooring, gas fire, TV point) Downstairs WC - 1.6m x 1.4m (tiled flooring) Upstairs Landing - 2.9m x 2.5m (laminate flooring, access to floored attic space via stira stairs) Master Bedroom - 3.6m x 3.6m (laminate flooring, walk-in wardrobe, en-suite) Converted Walk-in Wardrobe - 2.8m x 2.5m (laminate flooring, large amount of storage space) En-suite - 2.6m x 0.8m (tiled flooring, shower) Bedroom 2 - 3m x 2.8m (laminate flooring) Bedroom 3 - 3.7m x 3m (laminate flooring, freestanding wardrobe units) Upstairs Main Bathroom - 2.6m x 1.6m (tiled flooring, bath / shower) Private back garden with side entrance, shed and veranda.
