    Campbells are thrilled to have been favoured with instructions to place this distinctive property onto the open market.\nFull of character and history, this property boasts many many features, however the location of this property must be top of the list. Sitting on the Harbour, the property enjoys the slow pace of life and tranquillity that being so close to the water brings.\nBuilt in 1820, this property was used for building boats and has the last ever boat built there resting in the rafters of the living room. The walled and mature gardens to the rear are unique to this area and offer multiple variety of plants, shrubs and trees including Maple, Oaks, Holly, Bamboo, Roses and a delightful Bluebell Wood to the rear.\nThere is potential for Airbnb with the included self contained accommodation on the ground floor with its own entrance, kitchen, living, bedroom and bathroom, ideal for Guest Accommodation.\n\nViewing is by Strict Appointment with Campbells.\n\nOur Ref: K382\n\nEntrance Hall -
