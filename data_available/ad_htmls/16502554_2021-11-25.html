    Located in the popular Woodhaven housing development, fronting onto the vast and mature open green, and within a short stroll to all of Ennis town amenities, No. 25 is a bright and spacious 5 bedroom detached family home boasting ample off street parking and private rear gardens (not overlooked). To the ground floor, from the entrance hallway leads you to the spacious main reception with double connecting doors into the open plan kitchen/dining featuring shaker solid oak built in units with granite work surfaces and from the kitchen is a door leading to the utility. Also at ground floor level is an En-Suite bedroom. To the first floor are 4 further bedrooms all of which have built in wardrobes and the master bedroom being En-Suite and main bathroom. Viewing is highly recommended and strictly by prior appointment with sole selling agents.
