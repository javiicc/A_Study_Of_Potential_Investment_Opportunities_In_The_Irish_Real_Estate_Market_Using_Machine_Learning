    8 Fortfield Gardens is a handsome period property located in the heart of Rathmines. This charming three-bed plus attic residence enjoys a westerly orientation to the rear, east to the front which floods the property with natural light throughout, a wonderful home in the heart of Dublin 6. An added bonus is the rear garden which is a real suntrap, perfect for outdoor entertainment in the summer evenings and a large shed for storage. The property also benefits from an additional separate garage located in a laneway across the road. The location is second to none, being within a gentle stroll of all of the amenities of Rathgar Village and Rathmines Town Centre.\nBehind this simple, classical exterior lies a warm and modern family home. Maintaining many of its original features including decorative arches and coving, the property comprises a welcoming entrance hall, which provides a wonderful flow from front to back. Two well-appointed reception rooms and a guest wc are located in the original house lead to a contemporary extension with kitchen/breakfast room. Double doors in the kitchen lead to an impressive rear garden enjoying a westerly orientation. Upstairs there are three (2 dbls) bedrooms and a large family bathroom room. Finally there is a large attic room, which is currently laid out as a study/bedroom.\nLocated on this quiet residential road, between Cowper Road and Fortfield Terrace the property is positioned approximately 2 miles from St Stephens Green. Easy access is afforded to all of the amenities of Rathgar, Ranelagh Village and Rathmines Town Centre has to offer to include trendy eateries, bars, food stores and boutiques.
