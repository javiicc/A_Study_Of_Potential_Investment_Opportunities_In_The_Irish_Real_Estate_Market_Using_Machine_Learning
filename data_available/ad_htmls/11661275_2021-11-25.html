    This magnificient coastal property is situated on the northern shores of Clew Bay located approximately 4 miles from Westport in West County Mayo.This impressive residence would make an excellent Family home or alternatively it is well suited to receiving guests.\r\n\r\nThe residence has been finished to an exceptional quality finish and is presented in pristine condition with exceptional fittings throughout.\r\n\r\nRosmoney is a spectacular elevated headland featuring a sheltered ocean inlet affording wonderful views westwards over Mayo Sailing Club, The Clew Bay islands, Croagh Patrick, Clare island, Old head, The Achill mountains.\r\n\r\nThis impressive property was architecturally designed by an accomplished Irish designer.The bespoke design allows for interior creativity without the cost of expensive structural change.\r\n\r\nThe property extends to almost 450 sq m/4,800 sq ft. Ground floor consists of main reception featuring open plan design with lots of natural light, to include the living/dining/kitchen area.The utility room exits onto the rear of the house and has a bathroom to its side.Two Jack & Jill bedrooms share a fully tiled ensuite. Upstairs the hall reception area can accomodate a games room, with two master bedrooms and walk in dressing rooms and third bedroom with its own ensuite. The main bathroom is again finished from floor to ceiling in bespoke tiling. \r\n\r\nExternally the house has been clad in local stone, featuring a long tarmacadam drive, supporting a raised planted area adding to the privacy of the grounds which are specifically designed to allow for swift lawn management which brings out the very best in large lawn maintenance.\r\n\r\nVoted Best place to Live in Ireland by The Irish Times newspaper.
