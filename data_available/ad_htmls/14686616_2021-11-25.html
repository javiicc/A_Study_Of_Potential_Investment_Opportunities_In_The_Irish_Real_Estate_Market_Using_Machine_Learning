    HWP The Property Specialists are delighted to bring this stunning apt on Mountjoy Square South to the market. This two-bedroom third floor apartment overlooks Mountjoy Square Park and comes to the market in turnkey condition. The building's exterior showcases numerous Georgian-era features, including a hipped roof, consistency of style with neighbouring buildings, steps from front door to footpath, fanlight above the front door, symmetrical design and sash windows. \r\n\r\nThe building's atrium is very bright, with natural light entering through the fanlight and side windows, and the stylish chandeliers create an instant impression. The apt itself has been refurbished throughout , the entrance hallway of the apartment is bright and spacious and has solid wood flooring throughout . The cosy kitchen features a range of high spec appliances, including an integrated hob, and all points of the work triangle are within easy reach of one another. There are two double bedrooms in the apartment, both with wooden flooring and both benefitting from plenty of natural light. The bathroom has a classy, modern appearance with high-quality sanitary ware, vanity units and a rainfall shower head among its features. . While there is no assigned parking spaces; there is a secure underground parking facility, with frequent opportunities to rent car parking space and there is a communal courtyard at the back of the building where bikes can be stored.\r\n\r\nThe management company for the building is well established, having managed the property for the past 18 years, and a healthy sinking fund is in place.\r\nThe windows present a fine view overlooking the park in Mountjoy Square, a much sought-after central location in Dublin city. It is an ideal location for commuters, with a 3-minute walk to the nearest bus stop, while Parnell LUAS stop (red line) and Connolly Station (LUAS, DART and rail services) are within a short walk. Croke Park Stadium is within a 10-minute walk, with the Mater, Rotunda and Temple Street Hospitals all nearby. Other local amenities include the Dublin Writers Museum, GAA Museum, bars, shops and schools.
