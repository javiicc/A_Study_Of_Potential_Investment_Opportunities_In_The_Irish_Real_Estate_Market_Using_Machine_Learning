    Rooney's are delighted to present to the market this spacious property ideally located near to all amenities. No 25 is a spacious, bright, modern, turn key 3 bedroom semi-detached home of approx. 94.44sq.m. (1016sq.ft.) which is conveniently located in the Dromroe Estate on a cul-de-sac residential avenue. Dromroe is an established residential estate within easy distance to Limerick City Centre and also to the University of Limerick. There are numerous amenities within walking distance including primary and secondary schools, the Parkway Shopping Centre, Childers Road Retail Park, St John's Hospital, Grove Island Leisure Centre and local sports clubs along with beautiful canal and river walks. \r\n\r\nThe accommodation comprises of an entrance hallway with storage, spacious reception room, open plan kitchen-dining room, main bathroom and 3 bedrooms with the main bedroom having an ensuite bathroom. There is a private, enclosed rear garden and patio area , storage shed and gated side entrance. To the front is a lawn and driveway.
