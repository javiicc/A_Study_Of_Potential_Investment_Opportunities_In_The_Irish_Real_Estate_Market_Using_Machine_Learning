It is home to Maynooth University (part of the National University of Ireland and also known as the National University of Ireland, Maynooth) and St Patrick's College, a Pontifical University and Ireland's main Roman Catholic seminary. Maynooth is also the seat of the Irish Catholic Bishops' Conference and holds the headquarters of Ireland's largest development charity, Tr\xc3\xb3caire. Maynooth is located 24 kilometres (15 miles) west of central Dublin.\r\n\r\nThe town contains a fire station, in addition to the area's part-time Garda station, a health centre, a public library, and a credit union as well as various restaurants. Maynooth Community Library hosts regular public events, including book clubs, a film club, children's story time, and author visits. It also offers services and events relating to National Healthy Ireland and Work Matters initiatives.\r\n\r\nMaynooth railway station is one of the busiest in the Dublin / Kildare region, as it serves two major educational institutions and is a major commuter town for people working in Dublin. Bus \xc3\x89ireann services 20 (Galway to Dublin Airport), 22 (Ballina to Dublin Airport), 23 (Sligo to Dublin Airport) and 115 (Mullingar/Kilcock to Dublin) serve the town. Passengers may travel from Maynooth westbound or to Dublin Airport. Airport Hopper route 767 provides an additional higher frequency link to Dublin Airport.\r\n\r\nCarton House Golf Club is in Maynooth.
