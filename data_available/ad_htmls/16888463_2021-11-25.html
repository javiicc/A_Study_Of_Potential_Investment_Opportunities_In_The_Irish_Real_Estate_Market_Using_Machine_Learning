    DNG are delighted to present this beautiful Victorian red brick home on Wolseley Street to the market. Superbly located within walking distance of Dublin city centre, No.10 enjoys c.120 sq.m. of well proportioned accommodation and is ideal for anyone seeking a family home or investment property.\n\nThe accommodation briefly comprises; entrance hallway, front living room, dining room, large kitchen and a downstairs w.c. Upstairs there are 3 double bedrooms, a family bathroom and an attic room. Outside is a rear garden. There is on street permit parking to the front of the property.\n\nThe location of this property is second to none. It is within short walking distance of the city centre and yet has quiet and peaceful surroundings. Weekend and evening living is ideal with the likes of The Headline Bar, Bastible, Noshington, MVP, Little Bird Cafe, BiBi's and Clanbrassil House all within a short stroll. Oscar Square is around the corner which is very popular with dog owners who will also appreciate walks along the canal and the likes of St. Patrick's Park and Iveagh Gardens which are nearby. The Green and Red LUAS lines are also within walking distance and a number of regular bus routes travel to the city along the South Circular Road. Other notable amenities convenient to this property include Camden St, Griffith College Dublin, Trinity College Dublin, Griffith Barracks Multi-denominational School, St Catherines, Portobello Educate Together, St. James' Hospital, Heuston Station and The Phoenix Park.\n\nEntrance Hall\n\nLiving Room 3.81m x 3.61m.\n\nDining Room 3.81m x 3.44m.\n\nKitchen 5.83m x 3.72m.\n\nW.C\n\nBedroom 1 3.81m x 3.44m.\n\nBedroom 2 3.81m x 2.67m.\n\nBathroom 3.30m x 2.00m.\n\nBedroom 3 2.94m x 2.32m.\n\nAttic Space
