    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    O Connor Murphy are pleased to bring to the market a charming two bed detached cottage located on the coastal town of Doonbeg, Co .Clare. This superb well-presented cottage is an ideal escape with endless activities to choose from! Recently refurbished, Creegh Cottage is a little piece of heaven with stunning coastal views. Set on 11.78 acres, (Folio CE20465) close to beach & adjacent to the award-winning Trump International Golf Links Spa & Restaurant. The cottage is ideally situated to enjoy facilities in the immediate area & is extremely accessible to all surrounding areas.\n\nAccommodation briefly comprises: Entrance Hallway, Kitchen/Dining Room, Living room, Two Bedrooms & 1 Bathroom. On arrival you are immediately struck by the sense of warmth & calmness this wonderful, detached cottage has to offer having undergone a complete refurbishment making it a spacious, bright & inviting home. The current owners have finished to the highest of standards making it a 'move in' home. \n\nOn arrival a small hallway leads through to a separate spacious, bright living room with a convenient electric stove. A relaxing space to enjoy a movie or read a book whilst listening to the waves. To the left, the kitchen/dining area comprises of fully fitted kitchen with a large island unit fitted with all modern conveniences. Off the rear hallway is a family shower room fully tiled & contains an electric power shower. To the rear of the living room are two bedrooms; one large double bedroom & one single bedroom that completes the accommodation at this level.\n\nOutside, to the front & rear of the property, there is a gravelled area allowing ample room for parking. To the rear of the property are striking views of the blissful Doughmore Dunes, together with the surrounding countryside. There is also a secure shed for storage of sports equipment etc. \n\nCreegh Cottage has a lot to offer walkers, golfers as well as water sports enthusiasts with plenty to do within the stunning surrounding countryside, cliffs, and coastline. There are also unrivalled opportunities to watch sunsets from Doughmore beach, just a five-minute walk from the cottage. Numerous Golf Clubs located close by including Trump International Golf Club, Lahinch Golf Club & Kilrush Golf Club are all within a 30-minute drive. Whilst lots of Watersports available including surfing, paddle boarding & other water activities at Doughmore Beach, White Strand Beach & of course Doonbeg.\n\nDoonbeg is just a one-hour drive from Shannon Airport & is located on the 'Wild Atlantic Way', a stunning coastal route along the West Coast of Ireland. Trump International Golf Links Spa & Restaurant is a 5-minute walk from the cottage & can be seen from the rear. Surrounding towns include the coastal towns of Kilkee Lahinch, Doolin are a short drive away. \n\nWhether it's to relax & enjoy the beautiful scenery with walks along the coastline, hit the Atlantic with your kayak or surfboard or enjoy a round of golf, Creegh Cottage is the perfect base from which to explore what this amazing place has to offer!\n\nPut Simply "Creegh Cottage" has so much to offer a broad spectrum of purchasers & we highly recommend viewing!!
