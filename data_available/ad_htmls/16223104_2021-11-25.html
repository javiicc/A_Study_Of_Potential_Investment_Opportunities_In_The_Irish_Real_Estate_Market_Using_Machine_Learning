    69 Siul na hAbhann represents a superb opportunity to acquire a 5 bedroom family home in one of the most popular, mainly owner occupier developments in the Corbally area. \r\n\r\nSiul na hAbhann is beautifully set amidst a backdrop of mature trees and shrubbery and really generates a feeling of being nestled in the countryside while still only being a few minutes drive from Limerick City Centre which is undergoing a revolutionary change over the last number of years. Practically on your doorstep you will find pre-schools and primary and secondary schools, fantastic river walks and a top quality coffee shop. Nearby is Grove Island Centre which includes a large Supervalu supermarket, caf\xc3\xa9, pharmacy and a Leisure Centre including an indoor pool, gym and sports complex! To really spoil you for choice there is also a recently opened Lidl store and Tesco express. \r\n\r\nThe property itself has been laid out to maximise all of the available space for a growing family. The large main reception room to the front of the property enjoys the benefit of a bay window, wood flooring and feature fireplace gas fire. \r\nThe kitchen / dining area is fitted with an ample amount of solid, wall and floor storage presses and there is a sliding door that allows a huge amount of natural light into the property. There is a tiled floor and a tiled splash back in the kitchen area. Just off the kitchen is a very useful utility room that again has plenty of fitted storage units and a sink. The extra reception room at the front of the property is really where interested parties will sit up and take notice as this gives you the option to separate children and adults when the need arises! \r\nA WC in the hallway completes the ground floor accommodation. The master bedroom is to the rear of the property and has the benefit of extensive built in wardrobes and also an en suite shower room. The double bedroom to the front of the house is a superb size and has a bay window which affords a uniquely attractive view from a bedroom area. Bedroom 3 is larger than your average single bedroom and has built in wardrobes. A further 2 bedrooms occupy the second floor of the property and as you would expect they do not disappoint either.
