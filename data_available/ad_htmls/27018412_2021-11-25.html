    Description

      Owen Reilly is delighted to present this beautiful, double fronted, detached house in a quiet mature, cul de sac setting within walking distance of all the amenities of Milltown and Ranelagh Villages. The accommodation comprises of a welcoming entrance hall, living room, office/playroom to the front, guest WC, sitting room, kitchen, conservatory with access to a sunny rear garden with a separate utility room and garden shed. There is side access to the garden on both sides of the house. There is a double length garage to the side of the house. Upstairs there are three double bedrooms, master en-suite, two single bedrooms and main bathroom. The attic space makes for great storage via a electric Stira stairs.\r\n\r\nMore about the location....\r\nIdeally located in the heart of Milltown Village with its range of amenities including the Dodder river walk. The area is serviced by many transport links including the LUAS green line, which is a short stroll away, 44 & 61 Dublin bus route and the N11 & M50 are within easy reach. The area is also served by several schools to Alexandra college, Gonzaga and St Mary's College in Rathmines. UCD & Trinity are a short commute away.
