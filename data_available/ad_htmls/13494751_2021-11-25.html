    Ballyvannan, Tuamgraney, Co. Clare\n \n0.6 Acre Site with great views of the surrounding area\nElevated roadside site in a nice rural area, within easy reach of Tuamgraney village. The property can be sold subject to the purchaser securing planning permission. This site in Ballyvannon is located within walking distance of the centre of the village of Tuamgraney and is just over 1\xc3\x82\xc2\xbd miles from Scariff town centre. It is elevated with nice views over the countryside to the north and the east. It is an ideal residential location, with its proximity to the local primary school, Raheen Wood Steiner school, second-level school, churches, shops, Tuamgraney handball alley and Bodyke G.A.A. field. \nThe site is rectangular and it fronts on to the southern side of the road that goes from Tuamgraney village to Caherhurley. It is a very pleasant rural location that has other one-off houses nearby. \nTuamgraney is within commuting distance of Limerick, Ennis, Shannon, J27 on the M7 and Nenagh.\nSubject to planning this is a great site in a very beautiful part of east Care.\nInstructions: Take the R463 from Tuamgraney and just outside the village take the first turn to the right. Follow this road for 400 mtrs and the site is on your left.
