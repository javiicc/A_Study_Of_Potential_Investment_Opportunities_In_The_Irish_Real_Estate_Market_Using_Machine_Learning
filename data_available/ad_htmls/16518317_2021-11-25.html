    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to bring to the market this exceptionally spacious and bright top floor apartment located in this extremely popular development of only 8 apartments on the corner of Merrion Road and Sandymount Avenue. \n\n7 Lynton Court is a wonderfully spacious apartment and is flooded with natural light throughout. The tastefully appointed accommodation is well laid out and extends to 144.9 sq.m (1560 sq.ft approx.) briefly comprising a welcoming entrance hall with large cloaks press. The L-shaped drawing/dining room is very nicely presented, just off the dining area is a bright and airy kitchen with a very good range of built-in wall and floor presses and integrated appliances. An inner hallway leads to the three bedrooms, the main bedroom benefits from having a large ensuite bathroom with a walk in dressing room and wardrobe. There is also a family bathroom. The property is further enhanced with an enclosed west facing balcony, private parking, ample visitors car parking and an external storage room.\n\nLocated on one of Dublin s most popular and accessible roads, this development is within a stone s throw of Sandymount DART station, Ballsbridge and Sandymount Villages which provide a fine selection of restaurants, coffee shops and boutiques within a leisurely stroll. Invigorating walks can be enjoyed on Sandymount Strand which is within a short distance. Some of Dublin s premier Hotels such as the InterContinental and The Clayton are within a few minutes walk. Recreational facilities are well catered for with the RDS , the Aviva Stadium and Herbert Park close by, as are some of Dublin s principal places of business including , The new Facebook Headquarters , St Stephen s Green, Merrion Square and the IFSC.\n\n\nEntrance Hall welcoming and spacious entrance hallway with built in cloaks press\n\nDrawing/Dining Room very spacious L-shaped drawing/dining room which is flooded with natural light. Built in display cabinet. Door to enclosed balcony.\n\nKitchen Siemetic kitchen with an excellent range of built in wall and floor presses. Integrated NEFF oven, hob and extractor fan. Integrated NEFF oven and microwave. Plumbed for washing machine and dryer. Breakfast counter, granite worktops, wooden flooring and recessed lighting.\n\nInner Hallway to the bedrooms with storage press and large hotpress.\n\nMain Bedroom superb double bedroom with walk in dressing room and wardrobe.\n\nEnsuite Shower Room: with shower cubicle, w.c. and wash hand basin with storage press, towel rail. Fully tiled walls and floor.\n\nBedroom 2 superb double bedroom with built in wardrobes and overhead presses.\n\nBedroom 3 with built in wardrobes and overhead presses, built in display cabinet and shelving.\n\nBathroom bath with shower attachment, w.c , wash hand basin with storage press, built in cabinet, towel rail, fully tiled walls and floor.
