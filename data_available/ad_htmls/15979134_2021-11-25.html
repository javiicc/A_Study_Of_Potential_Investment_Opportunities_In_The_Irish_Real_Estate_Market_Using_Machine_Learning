    DNG Martin O'Connor Land Sales are delighted to present to market this large holding of land of c. 62.30 acres, in addition to a 1/6th share to 318 acres of commonage which equates to c. 53 acres, together with entitlements at Atticlough / Shanakeever, Clifden, Co. Galway. This is a very unique opportunity to purchase such a large holding in the Connemara Region. The property runs adjacent to Connemara national park, a section of a lake. The land would be ideal for farming, forestry, ponies or there is also potential for a profitable business venture. The land is currently used for cattle and sheep grazing.\n\nThe land itself is inscribed with history with a medieval stone pathway and a Neolithic field system up to 12,000 years old running along the shore of the lake that lies to the front of the house as well as a Megalithic tomb just past Lough Auna.\n\nFor further details and maps please contact the sole selling agents DNG Martin O'Connor Land Sales on 091866708.\n\nFeatures\nHill Farm\nc. 62.30 acres of land with a 1/6th undivided share in c. 318 of commonage (equates to c. 53 acres)In the Atticlough / Shanakeever valley\nBordered by the Letternoosh River\n3 km from Clifden town centre\nExcellent facilities available in the area\nDirect access on to Glenbrickeen Lough & Shankeever Lough\nEntitlements included\nDwelling House on 1 acre also available to purchase - https://bit.ly/3jIkDzM\n\nBER Details\nBER: Exempt\n\nDirections\nFrom Clifden take the Westport Road for 1.7 km and take the right turning for the camping site, drive for 0.8 km on this road and take the left turning. Drive for a further 0.4 km and the property is the first house across the river on the right hand side. The land is located beside the property with the Eircode H71W285.\n\nViewing Details\nViewings are by prior appointment only through the sole selling agents DNG Martin O'Connor Land Sale Tel 091866708
