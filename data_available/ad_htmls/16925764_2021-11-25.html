    No. 52 Foxrock Manor is a most impressive and substantial four bedroom detached property situated in this prime, much sought after residential development off the Leopardstown Road. Beautifully proportioned rooms offer a wonderful balance of living and bedroom accommodation, which is immaculately presented throughout, and has an overall floor area of 1,670 sq ft / 155sq m (approx.) Having been in the same family ownership since first built and meticulously maintained and upgraded over the years, to include double glazing, a new composite hall door, new gas boiler and updated bathrooms and family kitchen, this superb home now awaits a new owner to create their forever home. On entering there is an immediate sense of spaciousness and light, complemented by the neutral d cor and pure wool carpet across both floors. To the right of the entrance hallway is the graciously proportioned living room with feature fireplace and large square bay window overlooking the front garden. Interconnecting doors from here lead through to the dining room where French doors open out to the rear patio and garden. The well fitted kitchen can be accessed either directly from the dining room or from the entrance hallway, enhancing the seamless flow of accommodation. The entrance hall also boasts a modern guest wc and understairs storage. Turning left along the hallway, you will find a highly prized third reception room, which could be utilized as either a den, playroom or indeed a home office or second cosy sitting room. Upstairs are four bedrooms, three double bedrooms and a single, all with fitted wardrobes. The fine sized principal double bedroom with ensuite shower room is positioned to the front alongside the single bedroom. A further two doubles are to the rear with views over the rear garden. A family bathroom with corner whirpool bath and electric shower completes the layout.\n\nOUTSIDE:The gated front garden is surrounded by an attractive low redbrick wall, and mature planting. A cobble lock driveway provides off street parking for two cars. Double gates open to the wide side passageway which leads to the south westerly orientated rear garden, laid out mainly in lawn and bordered by an array of mature shrubs, including jasmine, hebe, roses and bamboo to name but a few. A patio area provides a great space for barbeques and al fresco dining in the summer months. There are two garden sheds, one block built and one wooden. \n\nLocation simply couldn t be better situated off the Leopardstown Road at the N11 end with Blackrock, Foxrock and Stillorgan villages all within close proximity offering a myriad of amenities including a wide range of specialty shops, restaurants, cafes along with three shopping centres. There are excellent transport links including the Luas, N11 with QBC and access to the M50 close by. There is an also a fine choice of highly regarded primary and secondary schools within the immediate area. All in all, this is a splendid family home and one that is sure to appeal.\n\n\nEntrance Hall Composite hall door with glass side panel opens to carpeted hallway with understairs storage.\n\nGuest wc With tiled floor, part tiled walls, wc and wash hand basin, wall mounted mirror, frosted window to side passageway.\n\nLiving Room Well proportioned living room with carpeted flooring, ceiling coving, open fireplace, wooden mantel with decorative tiled inset, window to front garden.
