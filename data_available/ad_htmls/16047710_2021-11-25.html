      Part of New Development at Thornhill Oaks

    • Overall Floor Area: 90 m\xc2\xb2
    Lisney and Oakview Property Developments are delighted to bring Thornhill Oaks to the market. \r\nThornhill Oaks is a prestigious new development of luxury 1, 2 and 3 bedroom apartments, in addition to a limited number of 3 and 4 bedroom family houses. This stunning scheme offers a unique opportunity to become a resident in one of Dublin\xe2\x80\x99s most exclusive new addresses.\r\n\r\nThe houses and apartments at Thornhill Oaks are being built and finished to the highest of standards, and benefit from the unique characteristics of this beautiful location. The development is enclosed in a walled sylvian setting, with an abundance of mature trees visible from every house and apartment, the most striking being the protected 250-year-old evergreen oak that stands at the very centre of the site. \r\n\r\nMount Merrion is one of Dublin\xe2\x80\x99s most exclusive residential areas, characterised by low density suburban dwellings with a very settled community feel. Key amenities on the doorstep include Deer Park, a 32-acre public parkland owned. Its attractions include walking paths, woodlands, sports fields with GAA and soccer pitches, and Deerpark Tennis Club. The wider area includes UCD and some of the country\xe2\x80\x99s best schools. There is great shopping and a wide selection of dining options near by, in addition to a superb network of transport options. \r\n\r\nThe luxury apartments at Thornhill Oaks will come to the market in Quarter 4 of this year. These apartments will feature exceptionally spacious interiors, with 2 bedroom apartments averaging close to 1,000 Sq.Ft, while 3 bedrooms are in excess of 1,400 Sq.Ft. Each apartment will feature top of the range fixtures and fittings in addition to generous private balconies and terraces that will offer premium views of Dublin Bay, the Dublin Mountains, and the stunning landscaped grounds within the Thornhill Oaks development. The 3 and 4 bedroom family houses will be launched in Spring-Summer 2022. \r\n\r\nPlease register your interest in this stunning new development at thornhilloaks@lisney.com or on www.thornhilloaks.com . We look forward to welcoming you to this beautiful new Mount Merrion address.
