    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to present 69 A Glasnevin Park to the market. This is a superb opportunity to acquire a spacious and attractive, three-bedroom, detached residence which has been designed in a Contemporary style and finished to a high specification with a stylish neutral colour scheme and is perfectly suited to the demands of modern day living. The property further benefits from a south-west facing rear garden. \nThis deceptively large property would make a perfect family home and would be ideal for those looking to move into a modern spacious property. An attractive front exterior open through to a home which is abundant in space and has a wonderfully warm atmosphere. The accommodation comprises of a porch, entrance hall, living room, open plan sitting room, kitchen, dining area, utility room and shower room to complete the downstairs accommodation. On the upper level there is two double bedrooms (master ensuite) a single bedroom and family bathroom. \nThe front of the property is presented with a pedestrian gated entrance with sleeper planters and mature shrubs. The south west facing garden is accessed via electric gate providing off-street parking. The low maintenance rear garden is surrounded by sleeper planters with mature shrubs and sandstone paved area ideal for al fresco dining.\n\nThe location is close to many local shops, cafes and amenities including Hampstead Park and the Botanic Gardens, frequent bus services, excellent primary and secondary schools, Dublin City University and Beaumont Hospital.
