    Development Description

      Annesley Gardens by Seabren Developments, represents a rare opportunity to acquire a new, well designed and expertly constructed home in Ranelagh one of Dublin s oldest and most sought after suburbs. \n\nLocated off Annesley Park, Annesley Gardens has been sensitively designed by the award-winning architects Metropolitan Workshop to sit alongside their nineteenth century red-brick neighbours within this historic and leafy residential area. \n\nThe development will comprise twenty large 3 and 4 bedroom homes finished in red brick and a lighter buff brick to reflect the front and rear elevations of the surrounding period houses. The 4-bed homes will range in size from approximately 1,835 sq. ft. (166.8 m2) to 2,050 sq. ft. (186.7 m2) over 3 levels with some featuring roof terraces at 1st floor level. The 3-bedroom homes, also arranged over 3 levels feature approximately 1,568 sq. ft. (145.6 m2) to 1,628 sq.ft (151.2m2) of living space with homes featuring a double height dining area, a curved staircase rising through the centre of the house and a private West facing roof garden. \n\nThe location of Annesley Gardens epitomises the convenience and character of Dublin s oldest residential neighbourhoods. This much sought-after area is well served with local cafes, shops and Morton s family supermarket. Access to public transportation and the Luas Green Line is a short walk to the Beechwood station on Dunville Avenue. Also within walking distance from their front door is Ranelagh village where you will find a host of restaurants, delis, bars, cafes, boutiques and gourmet food shops.\n\nGrafton Street is 12 minutes by bicycle or 15 minutes by car and Dublin Airport is 35 minutes by car. \n\nExcellent local schools are also in plentiful supply including Ranelagh Multi Denominational School, Sandford Park, Gonzaga, Scoil Bhr de, Muckross Park, Alexandra College St Mary s College & St Louis. \n\nParks, sporting and leisure facilities are in abundance around this area. These include Mount Pleasant Tennis Club, David Lloyd Riverview, Milltown Golf Club, Belgrave Square Park and Ranelagh Gardens Park.\n\nPromising a New Level of Living Seabren Developments is continuing this tradition of excellence at Annesley Gardens.\n\nFor further information please register your interest at www.annesley-gardens.ie or contact us at 01-6671888 to arrange a private viewing.
