Accommodation extends to approx 162 sq.m / 1744 sq.ft and comprises of hall, sitting room, study, open plan kitchen/ living/dining, guest wc, and utility.\nOn the first floor there are 5 bedrooms, 2 ensuites and shower room.\n\nAn ideal location for commuters and walkers, on the Dublin side of Trim and the Sl na Slainte (healthy walk) route. Just minutes walk from a regular bus service, making commuting to and from the city centre and the surrounding areas very easy.
