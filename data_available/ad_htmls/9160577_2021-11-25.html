    Development Description

      * New Phase Launching Soon * \r\n\r\nGlenveagh Homes is delighted to introduce Silver Banks, a modern and stylish new development in the heart of Stamullen Village in Co. Meath. The development consists of 3 and 4 Bedroom Family Homes.\r\n \r\nThe Poplar, The Oak and The Ash are available to view virtually in our Show Village.\r\n\r\nLocation\r\n\r\nLocated in the hearth of Stamullen Village, minutes from Junction 9 of the M1 Motorway, it's a 45 minute commute to Dublin City and a 25 minute drive to Dublin Airport. \r\n\r\nAs an established commuter location, Stamullen enjoys excellent transport links to Dublin via the M1 with its accessibility to the Port Tunnel. \r\n\r\nThere is an excellent bus service to Dublin via the highly regarded Matthews Bus private service. For rail links, Gormanston Train Station (4.5 kms from Silver Banks) and Balbriggan Train Station (11 minutes drive from Silver Banks) offer regular daily services to the Capital. \r\n\r\nAmenities\r\n\r\nStamullen offers a wide array of recreation facilities. The Community Centre adjacent to Silver Banks offers state of the art facilities with St Patricks GAA football and hurling, badminton and bowls on offer. For walkers Silver Banks is only a few minutes' drive to Gormaston Beach. \r\n\r\nStamullen offers an array of shops and services locally with larger retailers available in the nearby Ballbriggan, Drogheda and Swords. \r\n\r\n\r\nEducation\r\n\r\nStamullen is well catered for in terms of quality education and childcare. St. Patrick's National School is adjacent to Silver Banks. Secondary schools options are available in Gormanston College and Bremore Educate Together and Loreto are in Balbriggan. Schools in Ashbourne and Drogheda are also on offer. \r\n\r\nFor further information please visit silverbanks.ie to register your details or contact one of our sales agents at Raymond Potterton & Co. 046 9027666 or email info@raymondpotterton.com.

      Location

      Silver Banks is located in the heart of this beautiful village, and is surrounded by mature housing and the state-of-the-art St Patricks GAA grounds. The attractive and well-laid-out main street offers an array of shops and services, including a large supermarket, pharmacy, butchers, hairdressers, restaurants and caf\xc3\xa9s. City North Hotel is also just a short drive away.
