    Those in search of fantastic family home in a highly convenient location need look no further. 37 Blackthorn Green is well presented three bedroom terraced property. Accommodation comprises entrance porch, entrance hallway, under stairs storage, open plan kitchen/ dining/ living room, two spacious double bedrooms, one single and family bathroom. It has the benefit of not being directly overlooked from either the front or rear and large double glazed windows ensure plenty of natural light. \n\nOutside to the rear the garden is SOUTH facing benefiting from all afternoon and evening sun. Those without green fingers needn't worry as it is attractively paved and therefore minimal maintenance is required. A block built shed is to the rear which is wired for electricity and great for all your storage needs. In addition there is rear access via a laneway at the back of the house removing the need to carry bicycles and suchlike through the house. To the front there is a driveway with off street parking surrounded by mature hedging. \n\nEvery conceivable amenity is within easy reach including Dundrum, Stillorgan and Sandyford villages with their extensive range of shopping facilities, restaurants and bars. Many of Dublin's finest schools and colleges are close by including Alexandra College, Wesley College, Rosemont School, St. Rapheala's, Oatlands, St. Benildus, St Olaf's and UCD to name but a few. Public transport is extremely well catered for with the Luas only minutes away and a regular bus service also only within a short stroll. The M50 is also close giving easy access to the city centre and surrounding area.\n\nFEATURES\nApprox. 88 sqm/ 947 sq.ft.\nGas heating with smart home heating system\nDouble glazed throughout\nSunny south facing garden\nNot directly overlooked front or rear\nPresented in excellent order\nConvertable attic (subj. to p.p.)\nLUAS less than 10 mins walk\nM50 less than 5 mins drive\nDundrum Town Centre 1.5km\nTop schools in close proximity\n\nACCOMMODATION\nEntrance porch 1.88 x 0.82\nHallway 3.62 c 1.97\nKitchen 5.55 x 3.45\nLiving room 4.35 x 3.43 \nBathroom 2.02 x 1.95\nBedroom 1 3.42 x 3.69\nBedroom 2 3.42 x 3.34\nBedroom 3 2.53 x 2.42
