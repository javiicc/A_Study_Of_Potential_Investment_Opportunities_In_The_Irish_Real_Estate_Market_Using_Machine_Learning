    Built in the 19th Century, The Coach House is situated on the edge of Galway Bay in an idyllic setting sheltered by the woodlands of Barna nestled between Rusheen Bay and Silver Strand. A more pleasant location is hard to imagine. \nBarna Demense is an exclusive development hidden away behind large security gates. Situated on 6 acres of well-maintained grounds incorporating Barna House which dates back to the 1700 s and was built by The Lynch family one of the historic tribes of Galway. The Coach House was originally the Stables for Barna House and was converted into three townhouses in c.2005. Care was taken to restore the building to preserve the original stonewalls and to sensitively reconfigure the window openings in order to maximise the stunning views, making sure that all windows face south to maximise the light and the views from each unit. \n\nUpon entering this two bedroomed townhouse, you will undoubtedly be impressed by the stunning views but you will equally be surprised by the space, light and high quality of finish throughout. A large open plan kitchen/living/dining room benefits from having high ceilings, modern kitchen units and a gas fire. Stairs lead down to two double bedrooms, one of which is en suite and a main bathroom. Doors from both bedrooms lead to the rear garden, which runs down to Rusheen Bay. \nA public park with Victorian style gardens, bird observation area, lake and wetland area, tree nursery and a swan sanctuary has been development adjacent to Barna Demesne. \nNo 3 The Coach House, is a truly exceptional property. It offers potential buyers the opportunity to acquire a quality well fitted two bedroomed townhouse, in a prime exclusive development, in a very scenic setting, yet close to all amenities. Must be viewed to be truly appreciated. \n\n\nKitchen/Living/Dining Room Feature timber floor, fully fitted kitchen, breakfast counter separates the kitchen from the dining area. The living area features a modern wall mounted gas fire, high ceilings with feature wall paper on the gable wall, double doors to south facing balcony. Shutters on all doors & windows.\n\nStairs Modern stairs with glass panelling and carpet floors.\n\nBedroom 1 Double bedroom with carpet flooring, extensive built in wardrobes, double doors to garden, feature window. Shutters on doors & window.\n\nBedroom 2 Large double room with carpet flooring, extensive built in sliderobes, large arch window with double doors to garden, shutters windows & doors.\n\nEn-Suite Fully tiled with built in cistern for wc, wash hand basin, large mirror, granite counter top, large shower tray with power shower.\n\nUtility Press Two presses for extra storage.
