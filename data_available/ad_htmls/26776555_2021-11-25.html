Nearby amenities include Dartry Health Club, Brookfield Tennis Club and Milltown Golf Club. There is a wide variety of excellent schools in the area including Alexandra College, Gonzaga College, Sanford Park, Rathgar Junior, St Mary's College, Gaelscoil na Fuinseoige, St Killians French and German Schools. Palmerston Park with a popular children's playground is within a few minutes walk of St. Kevins Park. \r\n\r\nThe property has been recently redecorated and fitted with new carpets on the stairs and bedrooms, elsewhere there are wooden/polished floors and ceramic tiles. The accommodation comprises of spacious entrance hall, 2 stunning interconnected reception rooms with folding doors, original decorative moulded plaster ceilings, polished floors, large bay window to the front of the house and a second bay window with French doors leading to the side of the house, 2 mahogany/marble fireplaces with brass fittings.\r\n\r\nA modern extension to the rear of the house with floor to ceiling glazing seamlessly marries the old with the new. The fully fitted kitchen has extensive cupboard space, marble work tops and a large island with sliding doors opening onto the beautifully landscaped south facing garden and patio. A two sided wood burning stove connects the kitchen to the sun filled family room which also overlooks the garden. The utility room gives access to the side of the house. There is also a guest wc on the ground floor.\r\n\r\nUpstairs there are five bedrooms, on the first floor there are three double bedrooms (1 with wooden floors) with fitted wardrobes and one single bedroom/study, main family bathroom and a separate shower and wc. The top floor has a further double bedroom with wall to wall fitted wardrobes and a spacious shower room and wc.\r\n\r\nOutside there are front and rear beautifully landscaped gardens, spacious garage/games room and a pedestrian side entrance.\r\n\r\nRENT: 6200 per month including garden maintenance.\r\n\r\nBER: D1\r\n\r\nViewing highly recommended.
