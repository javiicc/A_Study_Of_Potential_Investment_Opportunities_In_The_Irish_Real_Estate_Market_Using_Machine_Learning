    1 Castlepark Residence is a modern and stylish one-bedroom apartment situated on the ground floor of this gated development off Castlepark Road. Located a short distance from Dalkey Town, Sandycove, Glasthule, Bullock Harbour and a host of excellent amenities.\n\nThe accommodation briefly comprises of; entrance hall with a separate utility room plumbed for a washing machine and dryer, open plan kitchen/living/dining room opening onto the outdoor terrace. A double bedroom with wall-to-wall fitted sliderobes, and a modern bathroom with a walk in power shower completes the accommodation. The property comes with one secure underground car parking space. The apartment also comes with one storage locker and the development boasts ample visitor parking.\n\nDalkey is widely regarded as one of Dublin s most desirable suburbs. Situated within a short stroll of the town centre, Castlepark Residence is convenient to a myriad of specialty shops, restaurants, bars, and gastro pubs. There is a host of scenic walks on both Coliemore Road, Harbour Road and Vico Road not to mention the wonderful amenity of Killiney Hill. The Dart is nearby at Dalkey or Glenageary, while the N11 and M50 are both within easy reach along with bus services on Breffni Road. There is also excellent shopping at nearby Sandycove, Glasthule and Dun Laoghaire.\n\n\nEntrance Hall: With hardwood flooring, skirting board and intercom system.\n\nUtility Room: With tiled flooring, plumed for washing machine & spectate dryer.\n\nLiving / kitchen /\n\n Dining Room: With hardwood flooring, recessed lighting, floor to ceiling glass window with fitted plantation shutters, sliding door through to the outdoor terrace, tv point. \nKitchen with a range of floor and eye level fitted cabinets, 4-ring Mile induction hob, Miele, extractor fan, integrated Miele dishwasher; Miele oven, integrated fridge / freezer.\n\nBedroom: A good sized double room with hardwood flooring, floor to ceiling window and wall to wall fitted sliderobes\n\nBathroom: With tiled wall & floors, heated towel rail, recessed lighting, large walk-in power shower, w.c, wash hand basin and large vanity mirror.
