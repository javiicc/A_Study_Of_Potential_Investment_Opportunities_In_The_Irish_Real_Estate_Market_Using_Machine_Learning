    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to present to the market this three-bedroom end of terrace family home, situated amongst the leafy surrounds of this mature residential area, in the heart of Sandymount village. Although in need of refurbishment, this property has been a much-loved home for three generations offering high ceilings and stunning original features. It also benefits from having many of the necessary attributes a buyer could possibly want - a sunny south west front garden, side pedestrian access and all this within striking distance of proximity to schools, transport links and local shops. \n\nThe accommodation offers the feeling of space and brightness throughout and briefly comprises entrance porch and hallway, living room to the front with double doors leading to the dining room, kitchen/breakfast room with a door leading to a storeroom/utility as well as a door giving access to the rear lane and storage sheds. On the first floor there are two double bedrooms, a single bedroom and a family shower room.\n\nOutside the property, there is on street permit parking to the front as well as a sunny front garden (approx. 10.6m x 9.0m). Pedestrian access leads to a rear laneway with storage sheds and communal garden which is mainly laid in lawn.\n\nThe location of this beautiful home cannot be understated. Superbly located in the heart of the village yet on a quiet residential terrace. There is residents permit parking available but given the proximity of all the amenities on its doorstep a car may not be required. Grafton Street, Merrion Square, St. Vincent s hospital , Grand Canal Dock and the IFSC are all within easy access by foot, car or public transport. Dublin airport is a mere 20-minute drive via the Airport tunnel.\n\n\nEntrance Porch With granite steps to front door, feature stain glass and ceiling coving\n\nEntrance Hall With ceiling coving, alarm panel and under stairs storage\n\nLiving Room Dual aspect room with large sash windows, ceiling coving, original fireplace with electric insert and double door leading to the dining room\n\nDining Room With large sash windows, tv points, feature open fire and door leading to entrance hall.\n\nKitchen/Breakfast Room With tiled flooring and partly tiled walls, a range of floor and wall units, feature gas fire, door to store/utility\n\nStore/Utility With door leading to rear laneway/storage rooms and plumbed for washer/dryer.\n\nLanding With skylight and ceiling coving\n\nBedroom 1 Large double room with ceiling coving and dual aspect\n\nBedroom 2 Double room with original open fire and ceiling coving\n\nBedroom 3 Single room with fitted wardrobes\n\nShower Room With tiled flooring and walls, recessed lighting, walk in electric shower, wash hand basin with storage, w.c. and shelved hot press.
