    Development Description

      There are many different house styles in Redwood, comprising three and four bed semi-detached, bungalows, two and three bed townhouses and large four bed detached homes. These nZEB A2 rated, concrete built Homes wrapped in insulation will result in optimum comfort with low cost energy overheads.

      These particulars are detailed for the purposes of representing the development only. Visual representations, finishes, layouts and/or scales may be approximate or representative of the development rather than exact specifications of the actual unit.       Development Features

      • Elegant high quality fitted kitchens with soft close doors and drawers
      • Air to water heat pump system provides domestic hot water and efficient heating
      • Passive ventilation assisted by localised humidity controlled extract fans in wet areas
      • nZEB Eco Friendly concrete built homes
      • Town centre site location
      • Timeless attractive ranges of brick & rendered finish
      • Reconstituted stone surround to feature window
      • A2 Building Energy Rating (BER)
      • Higher than standard ceilings
      • Composite front doors with side & top glass paneling
