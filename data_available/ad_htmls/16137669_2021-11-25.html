    Sherry FitzGerald are proud to welcome you to The Spa House, an extremely handsome Georgian residence (build circa 1770) located on a picturesque square in the village of Johnstown. \n \nWhilst in need of modernisation, all the elements of a supremely elegant and comfortable home await.\n\nThe accommodation is exceptionally well proportioned throughout and extends to 260 Sq. M. / 2,799 Sq. Ft. approx. over two levels. The layout at ground floor level comprises: entrance hall, drawing room, dining room, sitting room rear hall and the kitchen/breakfast room. Bedroom eight (with an en-suite) and a dressing room completes the accommodation at this level. The layout at first floor level comprises: landing area, seven bedrooms, main bathroom, a guest WC, rear landing with a back staircase and a walk-in hot press. \n\nOUTSIDE:\nThe property is superbly positioned overlooking a pretty square with manicured lawns, flowering plants and mature trees. There is parking to the front of the property. A vehicular entrance accessed at the side of the property gives access to a large concrete yard. There is secure parking for several cars. The yard is fully enclosed with a stone wall with a number of outbuildings including a cut stone coach house (suitable for conversion subject to planning permission). A pretty garden and orchard area at the end of the yard is accessed through a stone archway. \n\nLOCATION:\nJohnstown, historically known as Coorthafooka, is a small town in County Kilkenny. Bypassed in December 2008 by the M8, the town lies at the junction of the R639, the R502 and the R435 regional roads. St Kieran s Primary School and Colaiste Mhuire Secondary School are all in close proximity. Johnstown is a 27 minute drive (28km) via R693 to Kilkenny City Centre. A 7 minute drive will connect you to the M7 Motorway and the distance to Dublin is 132km and travelling time by car is 1 hour and 35 minutes. \n\nViewing of this wonderful property is highly recommended. \n\nGROUND FLOOR\n\nEntrance Hall (including stairs) 1.46m x 5.42m.
