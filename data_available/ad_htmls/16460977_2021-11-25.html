    ***Property sold with tenant in situ***6.5% rental yield***\n\nMadden Property Consultants are delighted to present this stunning two bed apartment in one of The Docklands most sought after areas. Spencer Dock apartment complex was built in 2007 and is located next to the River Liffey and IFSC business district. The apartment complex comes with underground secure parking, energy efficient centralised heating system and concierge service. The grounds and gardens of the complex are maintained to a high standard to ensure all tenants enjoy a clean and tranquil living experience. This dual aspect apartment comes with east and west views to ensure a bright environment throughout the day.\n\nAccommodation:\n\nHallway\n2.00 x 8.18m\nWell-presented entrance hallway with solid wooden floors, utility room and hot-press\n\nLiving/dining room \n4.47 x 5.71m \nEast facing living/dining room with floor to ceiling windows and solid wooden floors\n\nKitchen \n2.88 x 2.42m\nFull fitted kitchen with all mod-cons \n\nMain bedroom\n2.88 x 4.58m\nComes with wooden floors and access to west facing balcony\n\nEnsuite\n2.88 x 3.18m \nTiled floor to ceiling ensuite\n\nBedroom 2\n2.72 x 4.14m \nDouble bedroom with wooden floors and access to balcony\n\nBathroom\n2.52 x 1.97m\nFully equipped bathroom\n\nLocation: \n\nSpencer Dock is ideally located beside the IFSC and Convention Centre in the Dublin Dockland area. The apartment is surrounded by an array of cafes, restaurants, The Odeon Cinema, and local shops, along with many of the major firms in the finance and tech industry such as Citibank, Morgan Stanley, KPMG, PWC and over the coming year, Salesforce. East Point Business Park is a short walk away and across the River Liffey is the ever popular Grand Canal Dock where many international companies like Facebook, Accenture and Google have set up their European Headquarters. This is a very exciting time for the area with many new additional hotels and restaurants in development. The property is extremely accessible: the LUAS red line (Point Village) is right on your doorstep; the area is also serviced by several Dublin Bus routes in short walking distance (less than 5 minutes); and is easy walking distance from Connolly Station and Busaras. The airport journey time is approx. 15 minutes and both the Aircoach and the 747 buses stop less than a 4 minute walk from the apartment. The Port tunnel is a 3 minute drive away with access to both the M50 and M1.
