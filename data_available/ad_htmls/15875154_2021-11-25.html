    Site

    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Site in Tivoli Estate is a fully serviced site set in a quiet cul de sac. This elevated site is 1 acre approx. with stunning views and comes for sale subject to planning.\n \nThe location is hard to beat with the city centre only minutes drive away and easy access to the North ring road, Jack Lynch tunnel and all modern conveniences. \n \nFor those looking for a rare ready to go chance to construct your own property on a prime serviced site, look no further.\n\nBER: Exempt\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom Cork City Centre, drive past Kent Station and continue out the Lower Glanmire Road. Go over skew bridge and take the next left on to Trafalgar Hill. Continue straight on to Lovers Walk and take the next right into Tivoli Estate. Follow the road. The site is located at the top of the hill on the right. See Agent Sign.
