    Brophy Cusack Real Estate Team are delighted to bring this dormer style property to the market. The property sits on circa 1.5 acre site surrounded by mature trees. The ground floor consists of a spacious entrance hall, Kitchen/living room, sunroom, utility, bathroom & two spacious bedrooms. Rising to the first floor we have the master bedroom which is ensuite & a sitting room. The property also benefits from oil fired central heating, double glazed windows, a balcony, sweeping pebble driveway, timber floors throughout, fitted kitchen, tiled bathrooms, solid timber staircase & a solid fuel burning stove.\r\n\r\nGround Floor Dimensions:\r\n\r\nEntrance Hall - 2.86 x 6.25.\r\nSunroom - 2.71 x 3.56.\r\nKitchen - 7.8 x 4.7.\r\nBathroom - 2.90 x 1.98.\r\nUtility: 2.92 x 2.14.\r\nBedroom 1: 3.40 x 4.91.\r\nBedroom 2: 4.12 x 3.02.\r\n\r\nFirst Floor Dimensions:\r\n\r\nMaster Bedroom - 5.05 x 3.05.\r\nWalk in Wardrobe - 2.26 x 2.15.\r\nEnsuite- 2.63 x 3.78.\r\nSitting Room - 6.84 x 6.25.\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nFolio: KK27426F\r\nAcre: C. 1.5 Acres\r\nSize: 160 Sq M \r\n\r\nLocation\r\nThe Rower is a small village in County Kilkenny, Ireland. The Rower is on the R705 regional road, roughly 30 km from both Waterford and Kilkenny.
