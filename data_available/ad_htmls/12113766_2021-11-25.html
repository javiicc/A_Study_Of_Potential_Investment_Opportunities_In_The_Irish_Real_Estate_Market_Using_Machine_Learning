    No.6 Homeleigh is an exceptional family home of impressive proportions ideally position at the end of a quiet and private cul-de-sac just moments from Castleknock Village. The house is well set back from the road standing in the center of the 1 acre (approx.) site, with mature gardens all around affording it unrivalled privacy. \n\nThe quality of this home (built c.2008) has been painstakingly overseen by its owners who worked closely with their architects, A.J. Whittaker & Associates, to achieve a family home of true distinction and character yet maximising functionality. It is evident throughout that only the most skilled craftsmen have worked on the construction of the house. The fine craftsmanship and bespoke interior of this home has been finished to the highest standard and extends to approx.12,270 sq. ft./1,140 sq.m (excluding extra converted attic). \n\nUpon entering the foyer you are immediately drawn to the impressive atrium and floating staircase to each level. The foyer features a dramatic 8.2m (27 ft) approx. ceiling and is filled with an abundance of natural light from the rear atrium style windows, and is also serviced by a lift which runs from the basement level to the first floor. Off either side of the foyer is the generous living accommodation. \n\nTo the left there is a cozy music room with sliding wooden doors to the formal dining room and drawing room. The magnificent double height drawing room features a 6.3m (20 ft) approx. ceiling, a feature marble clad wall, a double-sided fireplace, is open to the dining room and mezzanine level above, impressive sliding glass doors that open onto the exterior patio. The beautifully appointed country style kitchen has a breakfast table that seats 8, with an imposing kitchen island with a marble countertop, also with sliding glass doors to the patio. There is a generous pantry/utility room off the kitchen with a separate wc. \n\nTo the right of the foyer lies an impressive entertainment/games room and bar. The entertainment room spans the full right wing of the ground floor with an impressive 120 sq.m (1,290 sq.ft) approx. of floor space with full height windows and sliding doors to the rear garden. To the front of this room there is a partially separate family sitting room. There is also a home office, comms room, and a second wc. \n\nThe wonderful accommodation continues upstairs with 5 double bedrooms, all ensuite, a home gym, and laundry room. The magnificent main bedroom suite features a walk-in dressing room with a balcony, and an ensuite with a wet room style shower, his and hers sinks, and a large jacuzzi bath. The remaining 4 double bedrooms all have stylish tiled ensuites and fitted wardrobes. The staircase continues up to the attic level which provides an additional two rooms for storage/studio, and a shower room. \n\nThe basement, which can be accessed from main foyer or the sweeping driveway outside which leads to the electric roller shutter security door, has ample secure parking for a number of cars. The basement also contains a swimming pool and the services are in place for the new owner to complete and put their own stamp on the property should they wish.\n\nBeautifully mature and private gardens set on approx. 1 acre, surrounded by large conifers and hedgerow offering unrivalled privacy. To the front and side there are gates leading to the gravel driveway and to the ramp that leads to the underground garage/basement. There is a children s play area to the side, and a private pedestrian gate leading to Porterstown Park. \n\nHomeleigh is an exclusive, small enclave located just off the Porterstown Road, close to Castleknock Village and is enviably positioned between two excellent golf courses, Castleknock Golf Club and Luttrellstown Golf Club. A private gate opens to Porterstown Park should you wish to walk or jog the 4km path which further enhances the backdrop of this spectacular home. The extensive gardens and grounds that envelope this home have matured beautifully. Expertly maintained and manicured, a combination of lush green lawns bordered with specimen conifers. There is also easy access to the M50, the City Centre, and Dublin Airport. The merits of this superb home can only be fully appreciated upon viewing. \n\n\nEntrance Foyer 4.19m x 12.77m.
