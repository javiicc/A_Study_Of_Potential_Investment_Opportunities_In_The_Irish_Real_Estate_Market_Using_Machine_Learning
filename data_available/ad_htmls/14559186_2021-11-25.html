    NEW TO MARKET\r\n\r\nThis unique, thatched Irish Cottage is located off the world famous Ring of Kerry/Wild Atlantic Way and in the heart of the MacGillycuddy's Reeks District. The traditional three-bedroom house boasts magnificent views of Dooks Bay, Caragh River, and the Seefin Mountains. Additionally, a mere ten-minute stroll leads directly to a secluded beach at the bottom of a quiet country road. The area is renowned for its spectacular walking and hiking trails, providing a stark departure from the trappings of urban living.\r\n\r\nThe recently renovated property features modern conveniences (including strong Wi-Fi), ample attic storage space, and a separate utility shed. The thatch roof provides additional external insulation, and the wooden patio deck is ideal for entertaining or simply birdwatching while enjoying views of Dooks Bay. \r\n\r\nThe charming village of Glenbeigh sits 1.5km away and offers a smattering of pubs, restaurants, a gourmet delicatessen, bakery, hair salon, and supermarket. The surrounding region includes easy access to Kerry Airport, Tralee, Killorglin, Killarney, and several schools. \r\n\r\nThe new owner " whether active retirees, first-time buyers or a family " will find a variety of outdoor activities to choose from, such as world-class fishing, golfing, kayaking, and horse-riding. Nearby attractions include Rossbeigh Blue Flag Beach, Dooks Golf Course, Dingle, and Caragh Lake. For stargazers, an International Dark Sky Reserve overlooks neighboring Kells Bay. \r\n\r\nAccommodations: Foyer, Large Sitting Room, 3 Bedrooms (1 Ensuite and Walk in Wardrobe), Bathroom, Kitchen/Dining Room, Sunroom.\r\n\r\nServices: Mains Water, Septic Tank, Oil Fired Central Heating\r\n\r\nExterior: spacious car parking, gravel driveway, landscaped lawn/gardens, and a fully wired metal shed (4.1m x 3.1m) suitable for washing machine and dryer.
