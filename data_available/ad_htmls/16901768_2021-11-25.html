    Behan Irwin & Gosling are delighted to offer for sale this impressive 4 bed detached luxury house in superb condition throughout. The property was completely remodeled & architecturally designed incorporating an open plan living space and extended on both levels in 2018 by Sigma Homes Cork. There is a generous open plan kitchen/ dining/ living room with feature high ceiling over living extension. There is also a 4.2m wide 20/20 Glazing sliding patio door which leads to a bespoke composite decking & patio area with a canopy ideal for evening entertaining/ barbequing. The landscaped gardens beyond are laid to lawn with mature shrubbery & trees. The property is situated in a small, exclusive development overlooking a large two acre maturely planted green area. The property is within 500m of Crossbarry village with a supermarket, garage, pub, beautician & takeaway. The property is also conveniently situated only a 5 minute drive from Innishannon, 15 minutes from Kinsale & 20 minutes to Cork City Centre. This property offers a turnkey opportunity for the discerning purchaser who wants a unique architecturally designed house within striking distance of the City whilst also enjoying the benefits of country living.
