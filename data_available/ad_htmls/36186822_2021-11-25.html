    Description

      We are looking for a friendly mature housemate to move into the master bedroom and take on the position of the lead tenant. This is a unique opportunity to share with two working professionals in their mid-twenties occupying a single and a double room with their own shared bathroom. We are only in the house 2-3 weeknights per week and less than 1 weekend per month, rarely using the living spaces. \r\n\r\nThe property is bright and spacious with a large sitting room and balcony. The light catches the kitchen in the morning and the balcony in the evening. There is a fully equipped kitchen; washing machine and tumble dryer. Space to work from home if necessary as we have a strong Wifi connection. Bins are included. Gas central heating; WiFi and Electricity separate. TV in the sitting room has Eir TV / Netflix / Disney / Prime.\r\n\r\nIn addition to the principal use of the living spaces, this offers a master king room with an en-suite. There is room for more storage in the bedroom or a work from home space, along with a hot press for additional storage. Bath in the shared bathroom is also available for a bath.\r\n\r\nThis is not an owner-occupied house share. The property is occupied by two ladies in their mid-late twenties. One works long days in the Galway Clinic and normally stays Mon-Weds night. This is subject to change as they have been offered a new position and may need to move out shortly.\r\nThe other works from home in Limerick and from the office in Galway 2-3 days per week. They may work from here from time to time if necessary and have a setup in their room to do so. One tenant smokes outdoors.\r\n\r\nThe apartment is located 3min walk from Merlin Woods and a perfect commute to Parkmore. Close to the sea near Roscam for gorgeous coastal walks. Many amenities: shop; creche; chemist; GAA club etc.\r\nBus (409) - 5 min walk from the house and runs every 20min.\r\nLidl 10 min walk from the house. \r\n\r\nWe are looking for an honest and trustworthy individual who is easy-going, clean and respectful of each other's space and belongings. \r\n\r\nPlease include a short personal description of yourself so that we can reply to suitable applicants.\r\n\r\nOne month's rent in advance as a security deposit.
