    Excellent detached four bedroom family home comes to the market in show house condition.\r\n\r\nThis duplex apartment is accessed by steps and enjoys an elevated position within the Kilmainham Orchard Complex. The exterior has a cedar wood cladding on a cream plaster finish giving a modern urban feel. This is complemented by high quality Rationel windows and an inviting entrance suitable for potted plants. The interior accommodates a large reception room, a kitchen cum dining room, four bedrooms and three bathrooms. On entering the house the hallway and living room are floored with a solid pitch pine floor providing a warm and practical finish. The hallway accommodates generous under stair storage and a guest wc. To the right of the hallway there is a generous light filled fully integrated kitchen. The kitchen is shaker style and features tiled splash backs, terracotta floor tiles and a stainless steel extractor hood. The appliances include; a dishwasher, washer dryer, large separate fridge-freezer and double oven. The dining space is large enough for eight diners and is ideal for family meals and dinner parties. The hallway leads down to a large light filled reception room. This room is ideal for a wide range of activities from relaxing, family activities and entertaining. Double doors lead out on to a large private decked area, providing an ideal setting for summer barbeques and socialising. Upstairs there and three large double bedrooms and a single bedroom which could be used as a study. Each of the rooms has built in wardrobes providing ample storage. Solid pine floors are common throughout, this combined with wooden blind, cream curtains and large windows provide for cosy light filled accommodation. Each of the bathrooms are tiled, with power showers in the en-suite and main bathroom.\r\n\r\nThe house is ideally located in Kilmainham, only 8 minutes from the nearest LUAS stop and 20 minutes from Heuston Station. Inchicore Village is a short stroll with a wide variety of shops and services. Local amenities include the River Camac, Memorial Gardens, Royal Hospital Kilmainham and the Phoenix Park.
