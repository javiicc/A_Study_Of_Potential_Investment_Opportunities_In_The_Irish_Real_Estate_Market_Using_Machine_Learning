    DNG are delighted to present this superbly bright and spacious large three bed apartment to the market. No 20 boasts light filled and modern accommodation throughout. The property is one of the largest and best within the development. The property also benefits from a south west facing balcony to the rear, an additional bedroom and off street parking.\n\nGleann na Ri is a highly sought after development situated just off the N11. This apartment is within easy reach of a host of local amenities including Cabinteely Village, Dunnes at Cornelscourt and Killiney Shopping Centre, both of which anchor an array of shops, bars, restaurants and more. Excellent leisure facilities can be found close by including Killiney Hill & Beach, Cabinteely Park and Shankill Tennis Club. Transport is excellent in the area with The LUAS at your doorstep and a variety of Dublin Bus Routes within walking distance. The N11 and M50 motorway are also situated outside the development.
