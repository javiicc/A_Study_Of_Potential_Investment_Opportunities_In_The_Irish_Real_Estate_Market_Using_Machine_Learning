    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    A most delightful and charming property, this cottage in Derryinver is a most welcome addition to the market. The views over Derryinver Bay and the mountains beyond are simply mesmerising. Everything about this property is warm, inviting and magical. The house enjoys an elevated setting well in off the road and the mature and stepped gardens can be enjoyed from the well-placed patios and paths that surround it. The house is presented in excellent condition and is tastefully decorated.\nThe accommodation consists of an entrance porch, kitchen/ dining room, separate dining room, utility room, family bathroom and two bedrooms (one of which is en-suite) downstairs and upstairs, there is a sitting room (with outstanding views), office, bathroom and lounge. If one required, the lounge upstairs could be used as an additional bedroom. There are beautiful patios and gardens located off the kitchen/ dining room, master bedroom where one can really enjoy the sea views and abundance that nature has to offer all around.\nFrom the lounge upstairs, which is located to the rear of the house, there is door where one can access the garden from at the higher level. In this higher garden, there is some raised beds for vegetables and a small shed. Site size is approximately 0.346 acres.\nThis is one property which needs to be seen to be believed!
