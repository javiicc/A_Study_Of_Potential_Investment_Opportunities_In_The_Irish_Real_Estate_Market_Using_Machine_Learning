    Allen & Jacobs takes 21 Vavasour Square to market. This is a very attractive, double fronted, early Victorian residence measuring approximately 121 sq/m. Beautifully presented and deceptively spacious, no. 21, which has been upgraded, extended and renovated very recently, oozes character and charm. Special attention was given to carefully preserve and tastefully incorporate and accentuate the elegant period features including double glazed sash windows, period fireplaces, and high ceilings to name but a few. An exceptional, contemporary extension to the rear offers further living accommodation while a west facing rear garden allows for plenty of natural light. The accommodation briefly comprises entrance hall, livingroom, diningroom, open plan kitchen/breakfast room/lounge, two large bedrooms, walk-through wardrobe, ensuite bathroom and main bathroom. \r\n\r\nVavasour Square, a long rectangle green lined by trees, is a highly sought after residential address in this hugely convenient location. With an abundance of amenities within walking distance including Grand Canal Square and Sandymount Village with its selection of bars, restaurants and boutique shops. The Aviva Stadium adds to the vibrancy of the location.
