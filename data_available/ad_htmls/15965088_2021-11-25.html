    Farrell Auctioneers, Valuers & Estate Agents Ltd are delighted to bring to the market this fantastic site extending to 0.697 Ha with full planning permission with sea views in the picturesque Geehy North, Kinvara, Co. Galway. The site has full planning permission to reconstruct and extend existing derelict dwelling and to construct waste water treatment system. The proposed dwelling extends 169.21 sq. m.
