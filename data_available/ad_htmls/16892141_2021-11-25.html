    RAY COOKE AUCTIONEERS are delighted to introduce this bright and spacious ground floor one-bedroom apartment to the market ideally positioned within the highly sought-after Discovery Building, Ashtown. \nThis property comes to the market in need of a minor cosmetic upgrade but has the benefit of double glazed windows, spacious living/kitchen and a large south facing balcony. \nInternal living accommodation of c. 446sq.ft comprises of entrance hallway with storage room, living/dining, fully fitted kitchen, one double bedroom and fully tiled main bathroom. \nAshtown offers a wealth of amenities within the immediate area such as supermarkets (Supervalu and Aldi), local pharmacy, local GP available to take new patients, restaurants, caf\xc3\xa9s and leisure clubs. Pelletstown Educate Together School in addition to numerous childcare facilities (Giraffe and Queen B, all providing montessori and afterschool service) are within walking distance.
