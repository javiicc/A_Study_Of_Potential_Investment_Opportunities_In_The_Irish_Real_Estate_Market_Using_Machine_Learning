    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to bring to the market number 33 Ardkeale, a three-bedroomed, semi-detached home overlooking an open green area which boasts a private back garden and is located in a quiet cul-de-sac in the popular Mount Oval development. \n\nThere is a nice balance of living and bedroom accommodation with an entrance hall, guest WC, living room, kitchen, and family room on the ground floor with three nicely sized bedrooms (one en-suite) and a main bathroom overhead. Internally, the property is presented in good condition throughout having been well maintained by its current owners. \n\nThe front of the house offers private, off-street parking for two cars and the rear enjoys an attractive, low maintenance lawn area that is private and not overlooked.\n\nMount Oval is a very well-located popular development in Rochestown. It is extremely convenient to all the local amenities, Douglas Village is close by and there is easy access to the south ring road network.\n\nViewing comes highly recommended.\n\nEntrance Hall 4.98m x 1.98m. Bright and welcoming area with a tiled floor that is accessed from the driveway. The hall gives access to all rooms on ground floor and WC under stairs.\n\nLiving Room 4.62m x 3.68m. Nicely sized living room with a semi-solid wood flooring, bay window overlooking the driveway along with a fireplace and decorative ceiling coving.\n\nFamily Room 3.59m x 2.97m. A well-proportioned reception room with a sliding door onto the patio area and garden. The family room is entered through either double doors from the living room or from the entrance hall.\n\nKitchen 4.35m x 2.81m. With fitted floor and eye level style units, this well-proportioned kitchen area has a tiled floor and overlooks the back garden.\n\nGuest WC 2.01m x 0.82m. Located just under the stairs and comprises of a two-piece suite with a tiled floor.\n\nLanding 3.29m x 2.08m. Bright spacious area giving access to all bedrooms and the hot press.\n\nFamily Bathroom 2.10m x 2.08m. The family bathroom comprises of a three-piece bath suite with a shower over the bath and a window to front. The walls around the bath are fully tiled.\n\nMain Bedroom 4.11m x 3.59m. The spacious main bedroom overlooks the front of house and is floored with the original wood flooring. It has built in wardrobes and an en-suite.\n\nEnsuite 0.99m x 2.89m. Consists of a three-piece shower suite with fully shower area and floor.\n\nBedroom 2 3.14m x 3.58m. Double bedroom overlooking the back patio and lawn area. It is floored with the original wood flooring and has built in wardrobes.\n\nBedroom 3 3.79m x 2.65m. Good sized bedroom overlooking the back garden through a Velux window. It is floored with the original wood flooring.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom Douglas, head out the Rochestown Road passing the Rochestown Park Hotel on the left. Continue to the roundabout and go straight through. Take the second right on to Clarke' s Hill. Continue straight up hill and at the first set of traffic lights turn right into Mount Oval Village. Continue passed the shops on the right-hand side and take the third right into Ardkeale. No.33 is along on the right-hand side. See agent' s sign.
