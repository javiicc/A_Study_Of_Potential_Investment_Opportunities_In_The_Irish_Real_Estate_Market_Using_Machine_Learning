    Attractive West Cork, 3 bedroom renovated and extended farm type house, set on over 1 acre approx., having a sheltered sunny aspect with magnificent views over the surrounding countryside and located in a rural setting between Castletownbere and Allihies Village.\n\nThe main house containing over 1,156 square feet of living space is renovated and extended whilst maintaining the original style. The features of the house include original walls, beam ceilings, bright rooms and oil-fire central heating.\n\nThe accommodation includes entrance lobby, sitting room, kitchen/dining room, back porch and guest wc. The First Floor has 3 spacious bedrooms, one being en-suite and a family bathroom. \n\nThe property is set in a rural setting and the house is approached via a gated gravel driveway. The site contains mature gardens interspersed with native trees creating a unique property that is full of character.\n\nThe village of Allihies and the town of Castletownbere are just a 10 minute drive away. The world renowned Dzogchen Beara Retreat Centre is just a short drive also.\n\nThe Beara Peninsula is renowned for its walking trails along the Wild Athlantic Way that take in many historical and archaeological sites. The Beara Peninsula s principal town, Castletownbere is just a 10 minute drive and is one of Europe s premier hubs for fishing fleets. The waters around the harbour are ideal for sailing and fishing enthusiasts.
