    Cluain Mara is a development of just 27 A-rated detached, semi-detached and terraced homes on the outskirts of Kinsale town built by Centurion Homes in 2017. The development has been finished to a high standard with well planted common areas adding to the overall appeal of the properties. \r\n\r\n16 Cluain Mara is a mid-terrace three bedroomed townhouse located in a quiet cul de sac at the end of the development, with the green across the street and a very pleasant outlook from the house to the surrounding countryside. Parking is off-street to the front and finished in cobble lock. The quality of the finish of the property is apparent from the moment you enter, with triple glazed alu-clad windows, solid front and rear doors, modern heat pump system with pressurised hot water, wood-burning stove in the livingroom, Kube fitted kitchen and Kube fitted wardrobes in all three bedrooms, Geberit bathroom ware including wall-hung wc's and monitored security alarm.\r\n\r\nThe property is presented to the market in pristine condition, with solid oak floors throughout and a neutral decor which will work well with a new owners furniture. Accommodation briefly comprises entrance hall with understairs storage, guest wc, livingroom with large window to the front and kitchen/diner with corner window overlooking the attractive rear garden; upstairs are three bedrooms, two doubles and a single all with excellent fitted wardrobes with hanging space and drawers, the family bathroom is a good size, has a bath and separate shower and is beautifully tiled. A Stira leads to the attic storage space which has been floored and plastered and has a Velux window and could be converted easily to habitable space subject to PP. \r\n\r\nThe south facing back garden has sunshine throughout the day and has been attractively finished with a pergola seating area, gravelled and paved patio, lovely planting and a large shed with electricity. There is a lean-to bike store at the side which could also serve as a wood store.
