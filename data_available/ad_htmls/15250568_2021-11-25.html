    It is with great pleasure that DNG TormeyLee have been presented with the opportunity to bring 2 An Pairc Wotton to the summer market. This luxurious four bedroom detached property is one of ten properties in a small exclusive countryside estate. Just a short drive will bring you from the hustle and bustle of city life, home, to peaceful and tranquil surroundings. It is not often a property with so many WOW factors comes to the market, early viewing is advised! \r\n\r\nThis home is presented in showhouse condition with luxurious d\xc3\xa9cor creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere throughout the house.\r\n\r\nThe property is laid out over two floors. The ground floor consists of a bright light filled entrance hall creating warmth and style, a guest wc, open plan kitchen come dining area. The formal dining room can be accessed from the kitchen area through double doors, a utility room, a large living room providing lots of space for entertaining. A TV/Study finishes off the ground floor area. The rear patio & garden can be accessed through panoramic sliding doors from the kitchen/dining area, the formal dining room and the living room have also have access to the rear garden. \r\n\r\nThe first floor comprises a master bedroom with an ensuite and a sauna room, three further double bedrooms (one of which is now currently in use as a walk in wardrobe). A second ensuite bathroom and a family bathrooms completes the accommodation of this superbly designed property. \r\n\r\nThis house boasts an array of high quality extras including stylish, modern d\xc3\xa9cor throughout, sauna in master bedroom, Italian solid wood flooring, Italian solid wood feature wall in living room, under floor heating on the ground floor level, air to water pump (A rated property), integrated hob that has an integrated extractor fan led surround lighting in living room & dining room on ceiling, solid wood flooring and surround in living room, CCTV system. There is full planning permission for a granny flat to be built to the side of the property and for a detached garage to the rear. \r\n\r\nSuch a property will not stay on the market for long, to avoid disappointment please call our office on 01-835-7089 to arrange your viewing of this dream home!
