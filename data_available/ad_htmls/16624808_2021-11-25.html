    DNG Brady are delighted to bring to the market this stunning 4/6 bedroom modern detached spacious bungalow. The property is finished to a very high standard along with bright spacious accommodation and extends to 204 sq. m. ie. 2200 sq. ft. The Property has the added bonus of beautiful mature landscaped gardens and patio areas.\r\nLocated outside the Village of Castlebellingham and close to towns of Ardee and Dundalk just off the M! at J15, within minutes of all the local amenities that the village has to offer such as shops, bars, schools and of course the famous Bellingham Castle and Darver Castle Hotel. The property is within 40 minute drive to Dublin & Belfast Airport, 30 minutes from Newry and 5 minutes from Dundalk. Accommodation consists of Large Living-room, Large sitting room/Family room, Kitchen/Dining-room, 4/6 bedrooms (main ensuite) one used as storgae and one used as utility room and two further bathrooms and new detached garage. Viewing of this property is highly advised.
