    DNG Lyons & Bracken are delighted to bring to the market this superb property at Cahernaglass close to the villages of Monivea and Abbeyknockmoy and approx ten minutes from the M17/M18 motorway. Finished to a very high standard this spacious home provides a generous living space of c. 261.56 Sqm (2815.43 Sq Ft.) and is sited on circa 2.50 acres ( 0.75 of Landscaped Gardens & 1.75 of land with site potential) \r\n\r\nSporting lovely views of the surrounding countryside Cahernaglass is five minutes from the popular village of Abbeyknockmoy with its host of modern amenities and less than ten minutes from the M17/M18 motorway making it an easy commute to the towns of Athenry, Tuam and Galway City.\r\n\r\n\r\nA secure front door brings you to a bright and spacious entrance hall with wall lighting, coving, wainscoting panels in the hall and stairway which takes you to the first floor accommodation.\r\nMoving from the entrance hall, to the right, is the spacious sitting room, complete with a feature fireplace which is fitted with a solid fuel insert stove, quality flooring and French doors leading to the garden area. To the left of the entrance hall is the kitchen/ dining room which has wainscoting, coving with hidden lighting, quality flooring complete with a pellet stove. The Kitchen, is furnished with an island unit, quality fitted cream units, brick splash back area top of the range appliances including de dietrich induction hob, de dietrich fan oven, a second de dietrich fan oven with microwave function and Samsung American fridge freezer complete with French double doors leading to the garden area. Just off the kitchen is the utility and the large guest wc completes the ground floor accommodation with touch mirror light, wet area power shower and back door leading to the garden area. Upstairs are four good size bedrooms the master has fitted wardrobes, the en-suite has a double shower with seating facility. The second bedroom is also en-suite with corner shower. The beautiful family bathroom has a fitted sink unit with polished stone counter top, Jacuzzi for two people finished with exceptional quality bathroom ware. The walk-in clothes press completes the accommodation at this level. The attic which is accessed by a permanent stairs is a lovely carpeted seated area which has a bar, tennis table and pool table a nice area to relax after a busy day\r\nWith a B2 energy rating the property is heated by oil fired central heating with a thermo-dynamic water heating system as backup in the event of sub-zero conditions. The pressurised water system has uv and carbon filtration softner. The house has a night guard monitored alarm system with camera which can be controlled by app on the phone or keypad. \r\n\r\n There is a large garage/ shed built with industrial grade steel beams, non-drip roof, Led lights and remote control electric roller doors. The large shed which can have many uses is presently used for horse stables and machinery storage. Due to the size of the site (c. 2.50 acres) there is ample room for a pony or two if required. The stone driveway has continuous kerbing \r\n\r\nThis spacious modern home with its many perfect features is a must view for any person or family who requires a top end home in the country.
