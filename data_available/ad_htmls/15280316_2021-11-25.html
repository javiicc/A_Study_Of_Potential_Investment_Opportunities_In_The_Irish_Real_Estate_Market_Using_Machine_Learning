    DNG McCormack are pleased to present this beautiful, detached dormer bungalow situated in the Castle Oaks development on the Dublin Road. These high specification properties are built to the highest standard. Number 1A The Crescent extends to a spacious 179m\xc2\xb2 and was being used as an office space. This is a unique opportunity for a home owner with an eye for potential to acquire a property in one of the most highly sought after developments in Carlow town. The property could very easily be adapted to a beautiful residence subject to the relevant planning permission of change of use being acquired.\r\n\r\nAccommodation comprises of an entrance hallway, back hallway and kitchen with large room off, suitable as a living/dining area. There are 2 rooms suitable to use as bedrooms with toilet and wash hand basin off, currently the ladies and gents toilets. These could easily be made into en suites for each of the bedrooms. Also, on the ground floor the smallest room could be used as a utility/laundry room and is currently plumbed with a wash hand basin. The largest room on the ground floor, with a wet room off, lends itself to many uses eg. home gym, games room or second sitting room. The L shaped staircase off the back hallway leads to a further 2 large rooms on the first floor, bedroom 3 and home office / guest bedroom 4.\r\n\r\nExternally the gardens are exceptionally well maintained and comprises of cobble lock driveway, patio area and 360\xc2\xb0 pathway around the building. Manicured lawns with beautifully maturing shrubbery and trees are the cherry on top of the rear garden which is accessed through an iron gateway at the side of the property and would be a perfect area to entertain guests. The low maintenance front garden boasts cobble-lock paving and provides ample parking space. \r\n\r\nCastle Oaks is situated within walking distance to several primary and secondary schools, also to Carlow Town Centre.
