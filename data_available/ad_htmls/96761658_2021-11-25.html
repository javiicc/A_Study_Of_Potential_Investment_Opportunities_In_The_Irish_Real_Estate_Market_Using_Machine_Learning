      *** LAUNCHING SOON register your interest on WWW.OAKCHURCHKE.IE ***\n\nOak Church, is an exciting new beautifully designed and attractively located development of three and four bedroom homes on the fringes of Kildare town. The development offers beautiful, spacious properties to suit all lifestyles. These family homes are constructed to the highest standards using the latest building technologies. All the properties benefit from, landscaped open green spaces that maximise the natural beauty of this delightful location.\n\nSTANDARD FEATURES\nInterior \nEnergy Efficiency\n All homes are A-rated and incorporate sustainable and renewable technology resulting in lower energy costs\n High level of insulation incorporated in floors, walls and roofs\n All houses are constructed to provide a high level of airtightness in order to retain heat\n\nElectrical/Heating\n Generous lighting and power points throughout\n Smoke detectors\n TV connection in living and master bedroom\n Energy efficient gas condensing boiler\n Photo voltaic panels\n\nKitchens & wardrobes\n Contemporary fitted kitchens & wardrobes as per show houses\n\nBathroom & En-suite\n Fitted as per Show House\n\nExterior Features\nWindows and Doors\n uPVC double glazed windows\n\nGardens\n Generous sized back gardens\n Driveway to front with parking spaces for two cars\n Seeded gardens to rear\n\nGuarantee\n Each Oak Church home is covered by a 10-year Homebond Structural Guarantee\n\n\nKildare Town\nKildare Town is the epitome of the quintessential Irish town historical yet modern and imbued with a welcoming sense of community.\nJust minutes from Oak Church are the Curragh plains with 5,000 acres of gentle hills, woodland and open parkland. A haven for outdoor pursuits and area of truly spectacular scenery. Offering peaceful walks through a timeless landscape of unspoilt natural beauty.\nKildare town is well served by national supermarket chains and host of independent shops, cafes and restaurants. The Kildare Retail Outlet Village offers a tremendous shopping experience of designer shopping with remarkable discounts. Then there s the Japanese Gardens and National Stud for garden and horse enthusiasts. \n\nConnectivity\nSituated adjacent to the M7, providing rapid access to the M50 in 30 minutes. There are also excellent road and rail links to the capital, Oak Church is an ideal location to call home.\n\nSport & Recreation\nFor horse racing enthusiasts, the Curragh, Naas and Punchestown racecourses are all nearby. There are a myriad of recreational and leisure facilities close by with, GAA, rugby, soccer, tennis, swimming, canoeing, horse riding, leisure centres and even hockey all within a short drive.\nThere are also some superb golf courses including The Curragh, Palmerstown House, The K Club, Castlewarden, Naas Golf Course, Cill Dara and Dunmurray Springs.\n\nEducation\nSchools include; Kildare Educate Together, Kildare Town Community School, St. Brigid s Primary School and Gaelscoil Mhic Aodha.\nYou will also find a great range of amenities in Kildare, including health and leisure facilities.\n\nNOTE\nThese particulars are detailed for the purposes of representing the development only.
