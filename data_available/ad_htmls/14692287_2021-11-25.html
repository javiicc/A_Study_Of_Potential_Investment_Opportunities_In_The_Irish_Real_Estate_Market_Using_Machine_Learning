    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    A spacious and well laid out two bedroom top floor apartment located in this popular development just off the village end of Church Road. This property is located within 1 minute's walk of Malahide Village and all the amenities that provides, excellent transport links and DART. Accommodation comprises entrance hall, large living room with feature fire place and balcony overlooking the rooftops and Malahide Estuary and Marina, dining area, separate fully fitted kitchen overlooking Malahide Estuary. Large master bedroom with ensuite, second double bedroom, main bathroom with full bathroom suite, storage room/ Laundry. Hotpress. Underground private parking. Use of manicured rear communal garden. Lift to all floors. Small development so quiet and well maintained.
