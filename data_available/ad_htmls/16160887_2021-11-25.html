    John Higgins Auctioneer is delighted to present to the market this excellent semi-detached residence at No 123 The Maples, Hazelhill, Ballyhaunis a maturing estate situated just off the main Ballyhaunis to Knock Road. This is a newly finished 3 Bedroom semi-detached in excellent condition having been decorated to the highest standards. As a result this property qualifies for the help to Buy Scheme for first time buyers. This scheme gives a refund of income tax and D.I.R.T tax paid in Ireland over the previous 4 years. First time buyers can claim up to 10% of the purchase price of the property which offers exceptional value. \n\nThe property comprises 1,076 sq. ft with accommodation including: Entrance Hallway with a tiled floor and featured staircase , Sitting Room with a bay window and open fireplace with a 5kw solid fuel stove insert, Complete fitted Shaker style Kitchen with open plan Dining Area, Utility with fitted wall and sink unit, Guest W.C downstairs with a tiled floor. Upstairs contains 3 Bedrooms (one En-suite) Hot-press and family Bathroom. This property is in mint condition throughout complete with fitted Kitchen and tiled floor, open fireplace, double doors between Sitting Room and Dining area, Back door leading out to rear garden and quality tiling throughout. All Bedrooms are of a generous size. \n\nOther features include wood grain PVC double glazed Windows; Oil fired central heating to include 3 zones and connection to all mains services. Externally the residence is complete with Tarmac driveway for private parking, front Lawn area and large enclosed rear Garden for extra privacy. The property has been finished to a high standard and is a wonderful opportunity for first time buyers, investors or those seeking to relocate to this Ballyhaunis town centre location. The property is conveniently situated within walking distance of all town amenities to include a 5 minute walk to Ballyhaunis Community School and all retail outlets. Rail commuters can avail of a regular daily service to Dublin and Westport. \n\nViewings can be arranged by appointment only with the sole Agent John Higgins Auctioneer 094 9631372
