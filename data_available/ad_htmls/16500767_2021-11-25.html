    Goldfield house is a beautifully presented 4-bedroom family residence in a tranquil countryside setting with panoramic views over the Roughty valley and mountains beyond. As you enter Goldfield house through the electronic gates and high stone walls you are welcomed by 1 acre of beautifully manicured gardens. \n\nThe residence extends over 3,300 sq. ft has been thoughtfully designed with family living in mind. Located on the ground floor is a spacious entrance hall which opens into an entertainment/family room. A large living room, Kitchen and lounge area which is filled with light, the dining room suitable for 6/8 people and a utility room. Also located on this floor is the master bedroom with a large walk-in wardrobe and master bathroom. On the first floor there are 3 generous double bedrooms, one of which is ensuite. A large family bathroom and an office. \n\nGoldfield house was built in 2005 with a high standard of construction and standard of finish. The house has a high BER rating of B2. The internal walls have been drylined. Extra insulation was added underneath all the wooden floors & tiles in the kitchen. Some of its features are a solid handmade oak kitchen, stairs, solid oak & maple floors throughout the living areas. \n\nLocated to the rear of the property is a detached garage and work shop. There is also a storage unit for wood. The large decking area off the kitchen/lounge is an ideal spot to relax in as it captures the sun throughout the day. For the hobby farmers there is even a hen run for chickens.\n\nGoldfield house is located on a quite country road just 3.7km from the heritage town of Kenmare.\n\n\nEntrance Hall Welcoming entrance hall with solid oak stairs and floor.\n\nFamily/ entertainment room Set off the entrance hall this is an ideal entertainment/family room, it could also convert to a home office.\n\nLiving Room Spacious living room with countryside views. A new Henley solid fuel stove is the centre piece of the room. The stove also heats the rads and water for the rest of the house. Solid wooden flooring.\n\nKitchen/lounge Open plan kitchen & lounge. Fitted handmade oak kitchen with large island and breakfast counter. whirlpool oven gas hob. Nordmende dishwasher. Water tap with filter system for drinking water. Samsung American double fridge freezer. Vacuum system fitted to kitchen unit. From the lounge double doors lead to the raised decking area.\n\nDining room Dining room for 6/8 people, views over the garden.
