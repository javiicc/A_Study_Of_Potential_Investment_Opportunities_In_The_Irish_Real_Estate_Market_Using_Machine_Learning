    Charming two bedroom red brick mid terrace homes comes to the market brimming with renovation potential.\r\n\r\nThis superb property features a red brick wall leading to a front patio. The attractive red brick facade of the home opens to an entrance hall with exposed brickwork. A sliding door from the hallway leads to a spacious living room with a gas fireplace, built in shelving and understairs storage. The kitchen & dining area has been extended and includes a skylight, ornate coving, splashback tiling. A shower room completes the downstairs accommodation. The sunny garden is accessed from the kitchen and comes with a block shed. Both bedrooms are upstairs, one to the front and one to the rear of the home and come with ornate fireplaces. The property benefits from free on street parking.\r\n\r\nRailway Avenue is ideally located in Inchicore, one of Dublin 8's most popular areas. There are numerous amenities on your doorstep including parks, playgrounds, shops, cafes, restaurants and a range of sports clubs. The Red Line Luas is a short stroll away making the city centre easily accessible.
