    Sherry FitzGerald is proud to present to the market No.5 Simmonscourt Terrace, a charming two bedroom cottage. Located off Beaver Row in Donnybrook, No.5 is double fronted, maintaining some of its original features including large sash windows and a south facing courtyard.\n\nAccommodation comprises an entrance porch with a bedroom to its right and a bright open living room to the left. The second bedroom is accessed through the living room while the kitchen and bathroom are located to the rear of the property, in line with the courtyard. With an open fireplace and sash windows, the cottage contains a fantastic living space. \n\nWith Herbert Park just a short walk away, Simmonscourt Terrace is a perfectly located cul-de-sac with these attractive brick facade cottages having ample permit parking to the front and various public transport routes nearby as well.
