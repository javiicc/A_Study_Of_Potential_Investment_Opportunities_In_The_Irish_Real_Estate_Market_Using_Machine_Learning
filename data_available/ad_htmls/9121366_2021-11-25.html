    Development Description

      Sherry FitzGerald Carroll are delighted to present Marlmount is located in a privileged setting to the south of Dundalk on the Old Dublin Road, a short stroll to the the seaside village of Blackrock with its dramatic coastline capturing Dundalk Bay and Cooley Mountains. To the west is the M1 motorway connecting Dundalk with Dublin to Belfast.
