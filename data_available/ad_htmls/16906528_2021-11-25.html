    RAY COOKE AUCTIONEERS excitedly present this excellent three bedroom terraced family home to the market located on Deerpark Way, Kiltipper, Dublin 24. The location is next to none as you are within minutes of The Square Shopping Centre, Tallaght Hospital, The LUAS, local shops, schools and leisure facilities. \n\nBright and spacious living accommodation of c. 950 sq ft (88 sq m) comprises of entrance hall with guest wc, lounge, open plan kitchen/dining room, three large bedrooms, main family bathroom and master bedroom ensuite. To the rear is a sunny garden with patio & storage shed. \n\nNo. 7 is presented in very good condition throughout and boasts recently upgraded gas fired central heating, hardwood flooring and three extra spacious bedrooms. This one is ideal for first time buyers or investors - Call Ray Cooke Auctioneers today for further information or to arrange viewing! \n\nAdditional Features; \n- c. 950 sq ft \n- c. 88 sq m \n- 3bed/3bath \n- BER C2\n- Gas fired central heating \n- Sunny rear garden \n- Double glazed windows throughout \n- Open plan kitchen/dining room \n- Three spacious bedrooms \n- Large green area near by \n- Highly sought after development \n- Within easy reach of The Square Shopping Centre \n- The Luas & M50 Motorway with a couple of minutes drive \n- Local shops within walking distance \n- Viewing highly advised \n\nDirections \nWhen coming off the M50 at the Tallaght exit head straight up the N81. When you see the Tallaght Stadium and the Maldron Hotel on your left hand side, turn left. Follow the road straight ahead, straight through the next two roundabouts and take a right at the 3rd roundabout. Take a right into Deerpark and then a right at the T junction.
