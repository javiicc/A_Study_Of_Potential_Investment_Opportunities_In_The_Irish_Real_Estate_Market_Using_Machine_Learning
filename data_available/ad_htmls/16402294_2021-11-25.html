    A truly superb three-bedroom cottage style residence tastefully decorated & well-presented throughout situated on a spacious corner site located only a stone s throw from Easkey village and the west Sligo rugged coastline. Rarely does such an impressive residence come to the market. \nUpon entering the site through a natural stone wall & gated entrance the sweeping pea gravel driveway leads you to the impressive front door. \nAs you enter the cottage itself you are catapulted back in time with charm, character & tasteful features throughout. \nThe deceptively spacious accommodation comprises of a welcoming entrance porch, spacious living room with vaulted ceiling with beam feature & open fireplace, cosy sitting room with vaulted ceiling with beam feature & open fireplace, well equipped kitchen / dining room with extensive range of fitted units & solid fuel stove with natural stone surround, light filled hallway with French doors to the patio, three sizeable double bedroom (one with ensuite) well equipped house bathroom, handy utility room & separate wc. \nUpstairs there is a large open plan floored attic space suitable for many different uses including a home office / workspace. \nOff the hallway French doors open onto a sheltered private sunny patio area that links well with the kitchen / dining room ideal for alfresco dining on long summer evenings. \nThe garden is mainly laid out in manicured lawns that surrounds the property and is bordered by natural mature trees & hedge rows. The site also has a second back entrance off the side road at the rear of the property. Within the building itself, it has some lovely features including brick chimneys with great detail, hardwood eve boards with detail, facia & soffit details & brick detail around the windows all giving a touch of class to the wonderful property. \nLocated in the heart of an area of outstanding natural beauty only a stone s throw from Easkey village and great amenities including the rugged coastline and beaches of Easkey, Aughris, Dunmoran, Tra Bhui & the long rock - all renowned for their great surfing waves. The ever-popular Pudding Row restaurant in Easkey and the authentic old world Beach Bar and restaurant at Aughris are also only a short drive away.
