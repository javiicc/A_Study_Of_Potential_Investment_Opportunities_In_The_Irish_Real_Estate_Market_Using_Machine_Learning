    56 West Road is a charming and stylish two storey end of terrace property presented in immaculate condition throughout and benefitting from the addition of a two-storey rear extension with pitched roof. The property has a handsome red brick fa\xc3\x83\xc2\xa7ade with granite lintels and sills and evokes great curb appeal. In the front there is a railed garden that leads to the front door. There is a fully paved rear garden with a pedestrian side access and a fully serviced domestic utility unit plumbed for the washing machine and also houses the gas boiler and tumble dryer. The unit also provides great storage space. The accommodation extends to 75sqm (807sqft) over two levels and has gas fired central heating, double glazed windows. There is ample on-street parking easily available at all times.\n\nOn entering the property, one comes into a hallway with high ceiling and continue into an open plan room with living room to front dining room and kitchen to rear. There is an abundance of natural light and this is enhanced by recessed lighting throughout the ground floor accommodation. The sense of space is further enhanced by the continuation of the timber floor from hallway to living room and dining room. The living room to the front is a comfortable area with an appealing open fireplace and library bookshelves to either side in the recess. The dining area is open plan and comfortably accommodates a large dining table. This area has a stairwell to first floor level and additional under stair storage. To the rear, in the two-storey rear extension, a contemporary kitchen has been added in high gloss finish with integrated oven, hob, fridge freezer and dishwasher. Woodgrain PVC door and window to the rear bring in a lot of additional light and the French doors open out into a beautiful private courtyard area with a mature tree and attractive paving.\n\nAt first floor level there are two double bedrooms. The master bedroom to the front it has extensive built-in wardrobes and a feature fireplace. The second bedroom is also a double bedroom with built in wardrobes, feature fireplace and a window to the side. The contemporary bathroom is fully tiled with WC, WHB, bath with electric shower over-bath and two windows overlooking the rear courtyard. A mirrored sliderobe provides extensive storage in the bathroom.\n\nWest Road is an ideal location on the doorstep of Dublin City Centre and in easy walking distance of Connolly Station for Dart & Luas with the Cross-City Luas stop at Marlborough Street, East Point Business Park, and the IFSC/Dublin Dockland\xe2\x80\x99s is also accessible on foot. There are numerous shops, restaurants, coffee houses and schools in the immediate locality.
