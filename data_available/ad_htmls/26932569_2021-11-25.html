    Description

      House to Rent - 5 Beds - 5 Baths\nBespoke detached five bedroom five bathroom contemporary house to let situated on a private corner site. Druantia was built in 2010 and was designed by OMahony Pike Architects. This three storey property features high-quality finishes including underfloor heating, double glazed windows and polished marble flooring. The property is accessed through a bright spacious entrance hall. Off the entrance Hall there is an open plan kitchen and dining area, excellent for entertaining. The Kitchen features high quality appliances and ample storage. The kitchen/dining area opens up to a spacious granite patio area perfect for outdoor dining and includes an outdoor shed for additional storage. Off the Kitchen/Dining area there is a separate utility area. On the same level, off the entrance hall, there is a guest bathroom and a large living room with a gas fireplace. On the first floor, there are two double bedrooms, one with an en-suite. There is also a shower room and a sizeable study on this level. On the second floor, there are a further two double bedrooms and a master bedroom with a spacious en-suite and dressing area. There is also another bathroom on this level. Beyond the electric gates there is a parking area suitable for three cars. The outdoor space is private and the landscaped garden is well maintained. Woodlands Park is a friendly residential area and is a highly sought-after area is South Dublin. The area provides an abundance of amenities. Nearby there are schools such as Mount Anville, Sion Hill, Blackrock College, and Oatlands to name a few. UCD university is also a short distance from this property. There are excellent transport facilities including Dublin Bus services (e.g 46a route to the city centre and various schools), AirCoach services (transport to and from Dublin Airport), the Blackrock Dart station and motor routes (M50 and N11). Dundrum shopping centre, Stillorgan and Blackrock village are also a short distance from this property. Here you will find plenty of shops, bars, restaurants and cafes.
