    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    I am delighted to offer to the market this stunning traditional two storey residence situated in Churchfield in Lispole Village. The property is in wonderful condition inside and out and has been renovated in recent years to a very good standard. The house is situated within walking distance of all local amenities of the village of lispole. This property is ideal for a buy to let holiday home investment or as a full time residence due to its central location. \r\nThe property offers a lovely mix of tradition and modern living with on the ground floor the following a beautiful large Kitchen/Dining area, Utility room with ample storage, It has a lovely cosy Livingroom space, 2 Large Bedrooms with Built in wardrobes and a lovely large patio door leading onto the rear garden off 1 of the Bedrooms. The family Bathroom is a lovely spacious room with a walk in Shower and lovely Bath tub with a beautiful feature stain glass window. On the first floor there are 2 more large bedrooms 1 of which is Ensuite and there is also a nice size Toilet & Shower room. The property has the benefit of good Internet so is ideal for anyone who is looking to work from home.\r\nIf you are looking to purchase on the Dingle Peninsula then look no further then Churchfield Lispole
