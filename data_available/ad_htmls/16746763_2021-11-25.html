    Apt. 1 comprises a bright and spacious apartment on the ground floor of this restored historical Georgian building. Built in the 18th century, the accommodation boasts many unique period features including exceptionally high ceilings and large reception rooms, as well as a modernised kitchen and three generous bedrooms. Laid out over two floors, the apartment extends to approximately 123 sqm (1,325 sqft).\n\nThe refurbishment of 4 Ardee Street was sympathetically carried out to preserve the original character of the building with feature high ceilings and beautiful timber sliding sash windows. Situated in the heart of Dublin City Centre, Ardee Street is a location of unparalleled convenience as it lies between Kilmainham and St Stephen\xe2\x80\x99s Green. The location will no doubt appeal to first time buyers, investors or those seeking a city base. The property is only a short walk to Grafton Street, St. Patrick\xe2\x80\x99s Cathedral, Portobello, Smithfield and The Four Courts. Close by attractions include The Guinness Storehouse, Christ Church Cathedral, Dublin Castle and Vicar Street. The property also benefits from the close proximity to St. James\xe2\x80\x99s Hospital, Griffith College and the Royal College of Surgeons. The area is serviced by a number of bus routes connecting to the city centre and the surrounding suburban areas.\n \nHallway (3.80m x 1.80m )with window allowing natural light to come into the hall and wired for a burglar alarm.\nDining Room (4.84m x 3.25m )with alcoves either side of the chimney breast and two sash windows overlooking the rear.
