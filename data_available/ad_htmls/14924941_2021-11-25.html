    Corry Estates are delighted to welcome to the market Apt 88 Drynam House, a two bedroom top floor apartment that offers a magnificent opportunity for first time buyers, investors or those looking to down size. The apartment benefits from bright and spacious living accommodation and comprises of entrance hall complete with storage area, an open plan kitchen / living and dining area with access to a balcony, two generous double bedrooms both fitted with built in wardrobes and laminate flooring. The bathroom completes the living accommodation. The apartment has been well maintained over the years and features include PVC double glazed windows, plenty of communal parking and electric storage heating. Ideally located within a small block of apartments which is located within minutes walk of local shops, schools and local transport links while Dublin Airport, The M1 & M50 motorways and Swords village & Pavilion shopping centre are all within easy access. Viewing comes highly recommended to appreciate this fine apartment.
