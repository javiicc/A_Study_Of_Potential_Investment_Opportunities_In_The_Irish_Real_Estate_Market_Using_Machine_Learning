    Sherry FitzGerald Geraghty offer to the market this beautifully maintained, bright and airy 4 bedroom bungalow with detached garage, standing on approx. 0.33ha / 0.82acre, located a short distance from Kilbride Village in a quiet rural setting, having easy access to the N2 (Dublin/Ashbourne Road), M2, all major road networks and Dublin International Airport.\n\nThis superbly located residence has been lovingly cared for and maintained over the years; walls have been part insulated internally (not bedroom section), uPVC facia & soffit replaced 6 years ago and the oil fired boiler was replaced 2019.\n\nTucked behind a gated entrance, the beautifully maintained gardens will catch your attention as you step out on to the driveway. Constructed in 1983 the accommodation extends to approx. 168sqm / 1808sqft, offers spacious living rooms, an open plan kitchen area, natural stone flooring, shower fitted over the double sided slipper bath and sun drenched patio and deck areas.\n\nExcellently located for local schools, the property is within easy striking distance of Ashbourne town, Kilbride village, Ratoath village, Blanchardstown Retail Parks, Hollystown Golf course and the Park & Ride station at Dunboyne/Clonee.\n\nEarly viewing is recommended...\n\n\nEntrance Hall Natural stone flooring underfoot, cloakroom with double door access, coving, downlighters.\nAiring cupboard with double door access\nHallway to bedrooms with access to attic space via fold away ladder, insulated, part floored and light installed\n\nSitting Room Fireplace with double sided cast iron stove\n\nKitchen / Dining Room / Family Room The kitchen cupboards at base and eye level provide excellent storage, doors are painted, flooring is natural stone, the dining/living space has the double sided cast iron stove and an access point to the sitting room. Sliding patio doors open to a sun drenched deck area and there is a rear access point from the kitchen. Note the coving, recessed lighting and colour glass panels in the door to the hallway\n\nBathroom Double slipper bath with shower fitted overhead, towel radiator, window for natural\n\nMaster Bedroom Fitted wardrobes\n\nEn-Suite Shower Room Fully tiled, large shower base, towel radiator, window for natural light and ventilation\n\nBedroom 2 Having built in wardrobes and laminate flooring\n\nBedroom 3 This double bedroom is positioned at the front of the residence\n\nBedroom 4 This spacious bedroom has timber flooring underfoot\n\nOutside Double garage, block built - detached from the house with metal side entrance door, up/over access door, plumbed for water supply\nOutdoor tap\nPrivate mature well-tended gardens and grounds\nPaved patios, large deck area to rear, pond area\nOutdoor Boiler and Oil tank - boiler replaced 2019\nDouble metal entrance gates and gravel driveway\nuPVC facia and soffit (replaced 6 years ago)\n\nIncluded in Sale Carpets, curtains, blinds, light fixtures & fittings, cooker and extractor hood\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom Hollystown, proceed towards Kilbride Village and turn right after Sweeney' s Public House.
