    BV Residential are pleased to bring to market this well maintained three bed duplex for sale. \r\n\r\nNo.36 is a three bedroom second floor duplex apartment within a well managed student village on the west side of Galway city. The accommodation is made up of three bedrooms. One double room, one twin and one single bedroom all with en-suites. There is a bright open plan living area with a living/dining area and adjoining kitchen. There is also access to a balcony area from the living room. This development is used for student accommodation during the academic year and is part of a pooled system with payments discharged in March & November. The management company also coordinates lettings throughout the summer months.\r\n\r\nThe student village benefits from an on-site laundrette and a shuttle bus service for the short trip to NUIG. The apartment is a short walk to Westside Shopping Centre and all the amenities it provides.\r\n\r\nDirections: From NUIG continue onto Newcastle Road R864 and take a left turn for Seamus Quirke Road. At the Browne Roundabout take the 2nd exit onto Seamus Quirke Road R338, continue past the Westside S.C. and take the first exit left off the next roundabout 'Deane Roundabout'. Follow the road around to the left to enter 'Dunaras'.\r\n\r\nCall me today to register your interest and discuss further.
