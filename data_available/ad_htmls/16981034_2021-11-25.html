    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Maher Auctioneers are delighted to bring this three bed bungalow to market. Ballagh is a picturesque rural village in Co. Tipperary. The property is approached through a driveway from the road. First impressions show attractive louvre shutters on the windows which is an indication of the attention to detail which is very evident throughout. The house is low maintenance inside and out. The bathroom is tiled floor to ceiling in a neutral palette. The open plan kitchen/living is bright, spacious and functional. This is achieved with the skylight and double doors and wooden floors. The double doors open out to the decking area which is fenced and affords great privacy and low maintenance. There are three carpeted bedrooms.
