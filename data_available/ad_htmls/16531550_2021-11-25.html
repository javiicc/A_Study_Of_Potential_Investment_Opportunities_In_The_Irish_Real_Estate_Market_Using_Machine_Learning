    No. 33 Tritonville Road is a most impressive three-bedroom Victorian family home presented in wonderful condition with attention to detail throughout. \r\n\r\nThis stunning property encompasses all the character of a period property with the conveniences of a modern home. The bright and spacious (c. 161 sq.m.) accommodation comprises an inviting entrance hall with ornate cornicing, centre rose, elegant dining room to the front and reception room to the rear, all with original hardwood flooring. \r\n\r\nOn the hall level return there is a spacious bespoke Woodale Design handmade kitchen with light filled breakfast area. The kitchen flows into to a sunny south-west facing terrace and enjoys direct garden access via an external staircase. \r\n\r\nOn the garden level there is a master bedroom with an impressive en-suite shower room and two further well-proportioned bedrooms, family bathroom, utility room and hot press. \r\n\r\nFeatures of the property include high ceilings, original ornate cornicing, centre rose, original wood flooring, double glazed windows, gas fired central heating and bright and well-proportioned rooms. Externally there is the most pleasant, secluded south-west facing rear garden and off street parking to the front.\r\n\t\r\n\r\nSituated on Tritonville Road, within a short stroll of all the amenities of Sandymount and Ballsbridge village including shops, restaurants, Dart , Aviva Stadium and a choice of excellent schools. IFSC, East Link Bridge and City Centre are within a short commute. Viewing strongly recommended.\r\n\r\n\r\nACCOMMODATION\r\n\r\nHall Level\r\nEntrance Hall: with original wood flooring, decorative arch window, ceiling coving and centre rose\r\n\r\nDining Room: overlooking the front with views of the iconic Poolbeg Towers, with ceiling coving and centre rose, custom built shelving and marble fireplace with a Chesney\xe2\x82\xacTM wood burning stove\r\n\r\nSitting Room: beautiful and elegant room with original wood flooring, marble fireplace with Chesney\xe2\x82\xacTM wood burning stove, ceiling coving, centre rose and large window to the rear with views of the Aviva Stadium \r\n\r\nKitchen/Breakfast Room: with bespoke Woodale Design handmade hardwood kitchen, integrated double oven, gas hob, integrated microwave, integrated dishwasher, undermount sink; sliding doors opening onto a sunny south-west facing terrace, ideal for enjoying the evening sun. The kitchen also benefits from direct access via steps to the rear garden. \r\n\r\nGarden Level\r\nMaster bedroom: bright & spacious room with carpet, fitted Sliderobes wardrobes, window overlooking rear patio courtyard\r\nEn-suite: extra-large marble-tiled shower enclosure featuring a rainfall shower head, double custom built vanity units, w.c., marble tiled floor, heated towel rail, window to front\r\n\r\nBedroom 1: double room with carpet, window to front\r\n\r\nBedroom 2: spacious room with plenty of natural light and extra space for home office/ play area, sliding door to rear garden \r\n\r\nFamily Bathroom: with tiled flooring and partly tiled walls, bath, w.c, wash hand basin, heated towel rail, frosted window \r\n\r\nUtility Room: tiled floor, partly tiled walls, sink, plumbed for washing machine & dryer, door to rear garden \r\n\r\nTotal Floor Area: c. 161 sq.m. \r\n\r\nExterior\r\nAttractive and ornate cast iron railed town garden to the front with off street parking and understairs storage room, ideal for bikes. To the rear the mature garden has an attractive stone built garden shed (wired for electricity) and separate understairs storage area, ideal for logs, BBQ and garden equipment etc. There is a large paved patio area and the garden benefits from an ideal south westerly orientation, perfect for entertaining during the long summer evenings.
