    Windmill House, Garristown, Co. Dublin

    5 Bed

    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Sherry FitzGerald Geraghty are proud to offer Windmill House to the market, a most impressive architecturally designed, 2 story executive style residence, with an emphasis on style and character. Set behind large wrought iron electric gates you enter this home along a tree lined avenue. \n\nWindmill House has an absolute lottery of views to the rear, from your kitchen alone you can draw your eye to the horizon where views of Ireland s Eye, Great Sugar Loaf, Dublin Mountains, Wicklow mountains and the sea are to be had. We are told the new owners will be able to sit back and enjoy, from the comfort of their new home, the firework displays from the city on New Years Eve and such events! The residence was constructed in approx. 1971 and much thought and planning went into the layout of the rooms with features that make the difference. Large windows particularly in rooms to the rear are designed to take full advantage of the stunning views.\n\nThe residence and paddock is set amidst 1.8 acres / 0.72 ha of beautiful lawns and mature grounds that have been meticulously maintained over the years. The paddock envelopes the property and is afforded excellent road frontage. South facing to the rear there are many spots to enjoy summer entertaining.\n\nA wide entrance hall showcases the space in design within which is mirrored throughout the house, all rooms are of a large scale and at its heart the open plan kitchen boasts an AGA Oil cooker. Ancillary accommodation adjoins the property, with an entrance that is separate to the main residence. This self-contained accommodation could suit an au pair, those saving for their own home, parents in need of a granny flat or a home office suite for the professional working from home.\n\nThis quite exceptional residence combines space both inside and out and at a fashionable address that is rural yet not isolated, that s just the right distance from the hassles of City life. \n\nThis hidden gem is a must see...\n\n\nEntrance Hall Bright and spacious with double glass panelled door, split level, parquet herringbone flooring, tilt and turn solid mahogany stairs to gallery landing.\n\nGuest wc With wc, whb, heated towel rail, fully tiled walls and tiled floor.\n\nDrawing Room Located to the rear of the property, feature open fireplace with gas fire insert, three large windows giving great views of the gardens and surrounding countryside.\n\nKitchen Dining Room A dual aspect room with views of both front and rear gardens. The standout feature to the room is the powder blue oil fired AGA range, island with storage and sink unit, fitted wall and floor units, gas hob and extractor fan, partly tiled walls and laminate flooring. Access to Dining room from kitchen area.\n\nDiningroom A large room for entertaining with open fireplace, coving and centre rose.\n\nLounge Snug and perfect for winter evenings, feature fireplace, solid timber flooring.\n\nStudy Located to the front of the property and the perfect spot for the home office or student, semi-solid timber flooring.\n\nOff the kitchen area\n\nPantry With built in oven and microwave, additional dry goods storage off main kitchen, tiled flooring.\n\nUtility Room Plumbed for appliances, stainless steel sink unit, tiled flooring.\n\nAnnex Separate self-contained annex with own door with accommodation comprising of a living space (plumbed for kitchen), W.C with shower and bedroom with built in wardrobes. Back hallway providing access to the rear of the property.\n\nUpstairs\n\nLanding A spacious gallery style landing with feature full length window taking in the views to front garden.\n\nBedroom 1 A large double room which is afforded the stunning views, built in glass panel sliding wardrobes, recessed lighting, carpet underfoot.\n\nEn-Suite A double basin vanity, Triton electric shower, fully tiled walls and tiled flooring with underfloor heating.\n\nBedroom 2 A large double room with built in wardrobes and carpet underfoot.\n\nBedroom 3 A large double room with built in wardrobes and carpet underfoot.\n\nBedroom 4 Also a double room with built in wardrobes, eaves storage, carpet.\n\nBathroom A large suite, standalone bath with separate shower unit, recessed lighting, partly tiled walls and tiled flooring with underfloor heating.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nTravelling from Dublin proceed to the end of the M2 motorway at the Pillo Hotel taking the third exit entering Ashbourne town. Take the first left onto the Ballymadun Road. Continue on the Ballymadun Road and proceed to Garristown Village, continue straight at crossroads (see National School on your left hand side) and Windmill House is located approx. 0.6km on your right hand side. Eircode for Google Maps: A42 KD58
