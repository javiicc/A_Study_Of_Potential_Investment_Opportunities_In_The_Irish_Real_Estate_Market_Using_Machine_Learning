    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Family home in the heart of the village of Rosegreen. This propery is a well maintained house on circa .5 acre site. \nOriginally the village postoffice and home of the postmaster the location is strategic within the village and with its large site offers great potential to a purchaser. \nThe property has the advantage of being within walking distance of the primary school, community centre with its sports fields and GAA club, local shop and garage.\nThe village itself is home to famous Ballydoyle Racing part of the Coolmore Group.\nLocated on the Cashel to Clomel Road the village has ready access to the M8 Dublin/Cork motorway and the towns of Cashel and Clonmel.\nThis property is ideal for those seeking a country lifestyle while residing in a small village with easy links to the larger urban areas.\nEntrance Hall\n\nLiving Room 6.2m x 4.75m.
