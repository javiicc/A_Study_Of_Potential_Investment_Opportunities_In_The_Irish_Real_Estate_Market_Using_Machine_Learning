    Number 29 Alma Road is a most impressive, fully refurbished, and tastefully upgraded Victorian family residence with accommodation spread over 4 floors and measures 433sq.m. / 4660sq.ft approx. It is ideally located on this sought-after quiet residential road only 2 minutes walk from Seapoint DART station and a short stroll from the famous bathing area nearby at the Martello Tower, and in close proximity to the villages of Blackrock and Monkstown.\n\nThe property is beautifully appointed and comes to the market in pristine condition throughout with all of the period details of the Victorian era, perfectly retained and intact which are a testament to an age when attention to detail was paramount. \n\nWide granite steps lead up to the gracious hall door which has a magnificent fanlight over. There is a lovely bright entrance hallway with black and white tiled marble floor, 15ft high ceilings, highly ornate cornicing and decorative archway and display alcoves lead to the inner hallway, and it gives just a hint of what is to come. Most of the rooms retain original period features including high ceilings, ornate ceiling cornicing and centre roses, marble fireplaces, window shutters and sash windows, all of which have been restored. These features marry perfectly with the outstanding craftmanship throughout from the bespoke Andrew Ryan designed kitchen to the magnificent single block of an Italian hand carved oval sandstone bath in the family bathroom and every modern convenience that is required to run a home of this magnitude. \n\nMoving through the house the formal interconnecting drawing and dining rooms are among the finest you will encounter with highly ornate Italian marble fireplaces and large sash windows overlooking the front garden and rear garden, its dual aspect allowing natural light to flood the rooms. This house is ideal for those looking for a spacious family home or who wish to entertain on a grand scale. The large kitchen/breakfast room with family room leading to the rear garden is the real heart of this home. With its Andrew Ryan designed kitchen in the traditional style, it has a huge range of built-in wall and floor cabinets with a large island, with black granite worktops, large 6 plate range, and integrated appliances, this kitchen/breakfast room also has a lovely space for informal dining. A large archway leads to a cosy family area where you can relax in front of the fire, which again is another magnificent Italian marble fireplace, or you can enjoy the garden and can access this through glazed double doors. \n\nOver the other floors there are five large double Palys some with sea views. The main bedroom has a feature marble fireplace, a walk-in wardrobe and shower room ensuite and has a large window overlooking the rear garden. All bedrooms have built-in wardrobes and there are two further shower rooms and a magnificent luxurious family bathroom, which is located on the first floor. This stunning room is fully tiled with magnificent marble tiles, double wash hand basins which are carved from sandstone with the feature oval Italian carved sandstone bath, there is also highly ornate ceiling cornicing and a centre rose.\n\nResidents of Monkstown are spoilt for choice being within walking distance of a wealth of amenities in Blackrock, Monkstown and Dun Laoghaire. Seaside walks can be enjoyed together with a range of water activities nearby and the local rugby, tennis and yacht clubs. The DART at Seapoint and a number of bus routes including the Air Coach provide excellent transport to the city centre and Airport. The new cycleway along Seapoint Avenue has added to the charm of the location. The area is also well served by an excellent choice of schools, sailing, tennis and golf clubs.\n\nThis is truly an exceptional home which offers flexible accommodation throughout, and all positioned in this highly sought-after location.
