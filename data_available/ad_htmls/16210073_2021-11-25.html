    ******Currently Under Offer******\r\n14 Carrigmore Gardens is a ground floor 2-bedroom apartment. This property is an ideal buy for a first-time buyer as it will make an ideal starter home. This property also offers excellent return for investment. \r\n\r\nThe layout consists of an entrance hallway/ porch leading into a spacious and bright living/dining area. The living area also has access to a small garden terrace which is accessed by double sliding doors which offers an element of privacy. \r\n\r\nThe kitchen is arranged neatly with a small breakfast bar. The bathroom is partly tiled with WC, WHB, bath and shaver light. The master double is spacious and includes fitted wardrobes, while the smaller double bedroom also includes fitted wardrobes and a vanity unit. \r\n\r\n14 Carrigmore Gardens is located in Citywest and is surrounded by an abundance of amenities and leisure facilities such as City West Shopping Centre, located Circa 500m from the property. \r\n\r\nCitywest Shopping Centre is home to a host of top stores including Dunnes Stores, McDonalds, Dominos, Chopped, Costa Coffee, Eddie Rockets and many more. Local schools nearby are St Marys National school and City West Educate together.\r\nSports clubs and gyms are City West Hotel and Leisure centre, St Marys GAA club and City West Golf Club are within walking distance. Lugg Woods Enchanted Forest is also within a 5 min drive.\r\n\r\nThe local public park is Rathcoole Park, incorporating playing fields plus children's play area. Rathcoole Park is located just 1 Km from the property. \r\n\r\nThe Carrigmore estate is on the door step of the City West Business Campus, the chosen location for some of the top global companies in Pharmaceuticals, IT/Software, Telecoms, Electronics, Media and Food, including Independent News & Media, Xilinx European Headquarters, Eircom, Unilever and United Drug. Citywest continues to evolve, year on year, keeping this commercial hub at the cutting edge, which offers excellent local employment opportunities.\r\n\r\nAccess to the N7 and N81 road networks are just a few minute' s drive. The property sits in the middle of Saggart and Fortunestown Luas stops on the Luas Redline. Less than 10 minute walk to both stations. Luas Red Line provides public transport from Saggart to Connolly Station / The point Village as well as 65b and 69 Dublin Bus routes. \r\n\r\nEmail Mums for further details on viewings.
