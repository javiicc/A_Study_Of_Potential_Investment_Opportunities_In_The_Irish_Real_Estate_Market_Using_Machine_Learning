    Martin Property is thrilled to present 21 Brabazon Square to the market. This one-bedroom home is in an amazing location in Dublin 8, Between Meath Street and Pimlico, there are four small residential squares, accessed from Gray Street. Each one has a terrace of houses wrapping the perimeter and, in the middle, car parking. The house consists of an open plan kitchen/living area, separate bedroom, bathroom, and garden terrace. The high ceilings in this cottage really compliment the space, giving it a bright and airy feel. The main attraction to this cottage is the red brick open fireplace, providing a wonderfully warm and cozy room.
