    Owen Reilly presents this superb two bedroom apartment with views towards the River Liffey and a westerly aspect. Situated on the 4th floor of this well regarded, upmarket development in the heart of Grand Canal Dock the apartment underwent extensive renovation in 2018 including a new high efficiency gas boiler, new Bosch kitchen appliances, new timber flooring and new lighting. Furthermore, this home benefits from a large west facing terrace, utility room, under-floor gas heating, two stylish bathrooms including en-suite with upgraded shower, a designated parking space and an internal garden area designed by Diarmuid Gavin.\r\n\r\nPresented in excellent decorative condition, the accommodation briefly comprises an entrance hall with storage closet and utility, open plan living/dining room with westerly aspect, kitchen, two double bedrooms with master en-suite and a bathroom. Viewing highly recommended.\r\n\r\nMore about the location\r\nGrand Canal Dock is a vibrant city quarter which has a selection of unrivalled amenities yet is only a fifteen minute walk from Grafton Street and the city centre. Hanover Riverside is located just off Sir John Rogerson's Quay adjacent to the Grand Canal Theatre and the Marker Hotel. Ballsbridge, the city centre, IFSC and Sandymount are all within walking distance. Large employers in the area include Facebook, Google and HSBC which ensures a strong rental market. Transport links are numerous with the Dart and Luas only five minutes away.\r\n\r\nAccommodation:\r\nEntrance hallway 3.19m x 1.69m & 2.21m x 1.16m (max)\r\nInviting L-shaped entrance hallway with a solid timber floor and a storage closet to include washer-dryer\r\n\r\nLiving/dining room 5.59m x 3.48m \r\nBright room featuring floor to ceiling glazing, access to a generous west facing terrace (7.52m 1.87m) and a solid timber floor equipped with under-floor heating.\r\n\r\nKitchen 3.20m x 1.82m \r\nContemporary kitchen featuring a range of fitted units with integrated Bosch appliances to include fridge freezer, dishwasher, oven, hob, extractor, solid granite worktops and a tiled floor.\r\n\r\nMaster bedroom 3.48m x 4.44m (max)\r\nBright double bedroom featuring built-in wardrobes, solid timber floor, access to the west terrace.\r\n\r\nEn-suite 2.19m x 1.46m\r\nStylish en-suite featuring a WC, WHB, shower cubicle and a fully tiled wall and floor.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 3.63m x 3.28m\r\nDouble bedroom featuring built in wardrobes, solid timber floor and underfloor heating.\r\n\r\nBathroom 1.71m x 2.29m\r\nBathroom featuring a white three piece suite with shower over bath and a fully tiled wall and floor.\r\n\r\nUtility room 1.87m x 1.16m\r\nProvides storage space, washing machine and dryer.\r\n\r\nStorage 2.23m x 0.84m\r\nInclude shelving for storage and boiler.
