    MOVEHOME ESTATE AGENTS ... are delighted to offer No. 10 Ferndale Road, which is a three-bedroom, semi-detached house with large side and rear garden that offers the potential purchaser excellent opportunity to development subject to FPP. Located in this peaceful and quiet cul de sac, this would be an ideal purchase to any growing family or shrewd developer.\r\n\r\nThis blank canvas has the right layout, space and light to allow a discerning purchaser to creatively remodel the interiors of this house. Extending to 102sq.m / 1,097sq.ft the property comprises of an entrance hall and front living room, rear sitting room that now benefits from an ensuite bathroom. The kitchen to the rear has access to the outside garage, storage room and outside WC. On the upper level there is two double bedroom, one single room, bathroom and separate W.C. \r\n\r\nThe front of the property is approached by a driveway which provides off-street parking. To the rear of the property is a spacious and secluded garden spanning 23m wide and approx 15mtrs deep. The grounds also offer a wonderful opportunity for further development subject to FPP and the neighbouring property adjacent now hosts a large detached home of distinction in the original garden simular to Number 10.\r\n\r\nThe accessibility of this location cannot be overemphasized benefitting from being within close proximity to many amenities some of which include well renowned national and secondary schools, local shops, parks and DCU. The R108 (Ballymun Road) is easily accessible and this gives residents ease of access to the City Centre, M1, M50 and is within 20 minutes of the Dublin Airport. There is an excellent public transport network of Dublin Bus routes moments from the front door.\r\n\r\nJohnstown Park a renowned 12.75 hectare park is literally on the doorstep of this property which boasts mature planting and tree lined walks to include tennis courts, playing pitches for soccer & GAA and a playground to mention a few. There is a wide array of shops, bars & restaurants which are all within striking distance located in Glasnevin, & Finglas. The Mater, Rotunda, Temple Street and The Bons Secours Hospital are located within close proximity and there are educational facilities in abundance such as St. Kevins not to mention DCU which is located nearby. Dublin City Centre is also conveniently located within only a few kilometres.
