    Desmond Wynne Auctioneers are delighted to bring to the market this unique property. Built and roofed house ready for the D.I.Y enthusiast or the next Dermot Bannon . A large dormer style bungalow on large site, close to the village of Drumkeerin. A nice quiet location within a five minute walk to the village. Amenities in the village include pubs, shops, butchers, schools (primary and secondary) and a park to name just a few. Other amenities such as Spencer Harbour Jetty and Corry Strand are within a couple minutes drive from this property.
