    DNG Martin O'Connor Estate Agents are delighted to present to the market this delightful 4 bedroom detached bungalow with large garage situated in the countryside just 1.5 km from Rosscahill. \n\nLocated just off the N59 between the villages of Moycullen and Oughterard this most attractive bungalow is deceptively spacious and is the perfect family home. In excellent decorative order throughout and boasting significant upgrades carried out by its current owners in recent years. These improvements include a new, kitchen, heating system, front and rear doors, double glazed windows and the installation of a new stove in the living room. \n\nAccommodation consists of an entrance hallway, sitting room with open fireplace, living/dining room with south west facing aspect, kitchen with cream painted units and breakfast bar, master bedroom with en-suite, 3 double bedrooms and a family bathroom.\n\nThe return hallway with wooden flooring provides access to the 4 bedrooms, all of which are carpeted and complete with coving.\n\nThe property is conveniently located within walking distance to St. Annin's National School, with a floodlit footpath at the end of the road to safely take you to the school entrance. Rosscahill Woods with its miles of lovely walking routes & wonderful scenery is only a short drive from the property. \nExternally, the grounds are well maintained and a tarmacadam driveway provides ample parking space to the front and side of the property. Mature hedging surrounds the perimeter of the site. The property, on an elevated site, enjoys scenic views of the surrounding countryside. \n\nAll necessary services & amenities are available nearby in the villages of Oughterard & Moycullen, both with a host of amenities including supermarkets, shops, restaurants, cafes, pharmacies, churches, GAA pitches. Galway City Centre is also only a 30 minute drive. \n\nViewing of this home is highly recommended for anyone seeking a family home close to the city yet enjoying the benefits of country living.\n\nFor further details & to arrange a viewing of this quality home please contact DNG Martin O'Connor on 091 866708.
