    HJ Byrne Estate Agents are delighted to bring this ready to go site with full planning permission to the market. Seldom does an opportunity arise to acquire a site ready to start work on immediately. The planning permission allows for the construction of a two storey three bed ultra modern residence. With these stunning plans one could easily create a \xe2\x82\xacoeorever home\xe2\x82\xac\xef\xbf\xbd in a superb location. The site extends to 320 square metres with plenty of space for the construction of the 150 square metres home standing on good gardens. This A rated contemporary home will contain a wonderful open plan living space comprising kitchen/dining and living areas, guest wc, 3 bedrooms with the master ensuite plus the family bathroom. The unique design is to create an upside down home with the living areas at first floor level making the most of the views in the distance. \r\n\r\nThis is a sought after quiet mature residential neighbourhood features delightful layout featuring different styles of properties with a good measure of green areas. The location could hardly be more convenient with the bustling village of Shankill just a short stroll away providing a good range of shopping facilities, churches and schools. Sporting and recreation opportunities abound with Shanganagh Park just minutes away with soccer and GAA pitches plus a super playground. The sea is just a mile away providing opportunities for water sports. Excellent public transport links include Dublin Bus services, DART and rail service, the Luas at Brides Glen and the N11 and M50 just minutes away. Full Plans and Drawings are available on request. \r\nPlanning Ref: ABP 300874/18 Granted June 2018.
