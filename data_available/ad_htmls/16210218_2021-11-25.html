    Mark Charles Properties are delighted to bring number 7 Norbury woods Avenue to the open market. This outstanding four/five bedroom family home is in show house condition and is nestled neatly in this very sought after mature estate. Internally, this superb home has been tastefully decorated with neutral colour schemes in mind. The bright light filled interior is designed to facilitate comfortable living and has generous room sizes. Due to the great orientation of the house, the kitchen and and living room is always bright and warm throughout the year. A particular features of this home is the conversion of two separate office spaces which could be used as they are or for a play room and or a fifth bedroom. The property also features new painting and carpets, a utility room and separate dining/living room. Externally, the sunlight hits a lovely decking area in the mornings and afternoons allowing you to enjoy your family orientated garden space even further. This back garden also includes a lovely timber shed. Norbury Woods is an ideal location in the bustling town of Tullamore. With a wealth of local amenities nearby, including both national and secondary schools, the hospital, The Court and Bridge House hotels, GAA, Tennis, Rugby, soccer and aqua facilities you are very well catered for.
