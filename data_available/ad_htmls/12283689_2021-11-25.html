    INVESTMENT OPPORTUNITY - HIGH PROFILE SITE c.6.22 ACRES. ZONED RESIDENTIAL (Low Density)\r\n\r\nUnique opportunity to acquire a working Equestrian Centre/Training Stables - with the potential for residential development. It currently comprises of: American Barn with 22 stables (which can be sectioned off as there is a dividing door) tackroom, 5 bay haybarn, 8 horse walker and fenced paddocks under grass. This establishment overlooks the very scenic Village of Borris with its famous 9 hole golf course, Viaduct, Borris House and Step House Hotel in the background. The Durin or Black River runs along its western boundary Goresbridge Horse Sales, Gowran Racecourse, Bagnalstown (train station) are all only 15 minuts drive, with Kilkenny, Carlow and Waterford all within a 30 minute drive. Excellent amenities are on its doorstep i.e. Schools, Restaurants, Hotel etc. Viewing strictly by appointment!\r\nE&OE
