    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    This detached bungalow is located at the Western entrance of the busy market town of Cahersiveen, the property has been well kept, and the mature compact garden requires very little maintenance (The garden around the house contains C. .17ha.C. .42 acres). The property is well laid out with two large reception/living rooms which provide ample space for separation of use.
