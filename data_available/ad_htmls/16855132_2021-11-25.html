    No. 12 Maryland is a sought after two-bedroom bungalow located in this wonderful retirement development in the heart of Shankill. Designed exclusively for retirees and over 55 s, there is an excellent mix of apartments and bungalows set on beautifully maintained grounds offering peace and quiet, seclusion and security. The accommodation is bright and airy and comprises of an entrance hallway, spacious living/diner, and a fully equipped kitchen. To the rear there is a contemporary wet room, large storage press, and two double bedrooms. Both bedrooms are carpeted and contain built in wardrobes. \n\nThe location is ideal, with the new Lidl supermarket adjacent to the development and just a short stroll from St. Anne s Church and Shankill Village with its selection of shops and eateries. There are excellent transport links with easy access to the N11/M11/M50 and the Dart station is within a 500m walk. Bus routes 45 A, 84, 145 and 155 pass through the village along with the Aircoach to Dublin Airport. In addition, the Luas at Cherrywood is a short distance away by private transport. The superb amenity of Shankill beach is minutes away along with Shanganagh Park.\n\n\nLiving Room 3.88m x 4.16m.\n\nDining Room 2.57m x 2.60m.\n\nKitchen 1.75m x 3.56m.\n\nWet Room 1.75m x 2.81m.\n\nBedroom 1 2.91m x 3.36m.\n\nBedroom 2 2.71m x 3.36m.
