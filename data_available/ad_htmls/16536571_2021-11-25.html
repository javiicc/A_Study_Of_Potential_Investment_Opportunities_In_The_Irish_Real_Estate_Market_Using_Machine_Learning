    Lynam Auctioneers are delighted to present Apartment 6 Hibernia, De Vesci Court, Monkstown. This mature apartment block was built in the late 1960s and enjoys a fantastic location in popular village of Monkstown on the leafy Sloperton Road. No 6 is a spacious 2 bedroom apartment in a location that boasts an array of local amenities. The apartment is within walking distance of Monkstown village which hosts a great variety of cafes, restaurants, pubs and boutiques. \r\n\r\nTransport links including the DART, QBC and Aircoach are minutes away, with Aircoach and bus routes passing nearby, allowing for ease of access to the city centre and airport. Other attractions include Dun Laoghaire harbour with its yacht clubs, marina, piers and coastal walks. The apartment is convenient to a range of South County Dublin's most prestigious schools including Blackrock College, St. Andrew's College and Sion Hill
