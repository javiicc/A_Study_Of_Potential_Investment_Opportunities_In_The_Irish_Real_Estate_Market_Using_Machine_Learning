    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    An exceptional 4 bedroom semi detached family home that stands on mature gardens, located in a quiet cul de sac and a 10 minute walk from Donabate village and train station. This bright, modern and well appointed accommodation comprises of , entrance hall, Down stairs toilet, living room ,extended Kitchen,/ Dining open plan room Studio/Utility room ,4 bedrooms, bathroom .. Priory Wood is located off the Portrane road, with schools, shops and sporting facilities all on your doorstep..A lovely location which benefits of both country and costal settings., situated close to the train station which offers easy access to the city , M1 Motorway and providing direct access to Swords, Dublin Airport & the M50.
