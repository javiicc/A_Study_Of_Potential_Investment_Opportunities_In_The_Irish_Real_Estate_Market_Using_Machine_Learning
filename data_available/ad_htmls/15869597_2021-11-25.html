    A unique opportunity to acquire a ready to go site with the benefit of Full Planning Permission (Ref: 3917/20) to demolish the existing derelict dwelling and to construct 5 apartments comprising of; 2 x two bedroom apartments and 3 x one bedroom apartments. All social and essential amenities are on your doorstep including many trendy eateries and coffee houses, while Smithfield is also just a short walk away.
