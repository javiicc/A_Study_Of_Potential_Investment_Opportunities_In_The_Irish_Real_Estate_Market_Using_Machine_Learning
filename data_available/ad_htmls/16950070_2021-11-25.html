    We are delighted to offer to the market this four bedroom detached property in a quiet location, a short drive to all amenities. The property, 2003 sq. ft. in size and of block construction, was built in 2006 and sits on a 0.64 acre elevated site.\r\n \r\nGround floor accommodation briefly comprises of an entrance hall, living room, kitchen/dining area, utility room, WC, two bedrooms; one with walk-in wardrobe, and a bathroom which doubles up as an ensuite. On the first floor, the landing leads to two bedrooms; one with an ensuite and one with a walk-in wardrobe. A first-floor bathroom completes the package.\r\n \r\nThe property is heated by way of oil-fired central heating complemented by an open fire in the living room and a stove in the dining area. Water is supplied by a local group water scheme and sewerage is treated by a bio-cycle unit. The BER rating is B3 which indicates energy efficiency but also provides an opportunity for a lower mortgage rate. The property is in need of some modernisation and minor refurbishment. There is a garage, shed, and patio area to the rear of the property.\r\n\r\nThis is an ideal home for someone looking to retreat to a peaceful setting and enjoy the space and autonomy of a dwelling on its own site.
