    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    RE/MAX Team Fogarty are delighted to present No 8 Island Drive, Riverview to the market. This beautiful four bed two bath detached bungalow with garage is one of only 12 homes located in a unique tree lined cul de sac with immense privacy to the front and rear. \r\n\r\nAccommodation includes an inviting entrance hall with double doors that lead to the Kitchen and dining room. Sliding doors off the dining room allow access to the south facing patio and rear garden. \r\n\r\nThe sitting room has an open fireplace and a floor to ceiling picture window overlooking the garden. There is access to the garage from the entrance hall which offers the possibility for conversion subject to relevant planning permission.\r\n\r\nThere are four bedrooms two doubles and two single rooms, family bathroom and wc.\r\n\r\nOutside there is off street parking to the front with gated side access at both sides to an enclosed south facing rear garden. \r\n\r\nHeating is oil fired and the energy rating is D1\r\n\r\nRiverview is a mature residential development on the Dunmore Road and is situated minutes to University Hospital Waterford, Ardkeen, Tesco and the Brasscock shopping centres. The property is immediate to the Woodlands Hotel and leisure centre and is served by a regular bus service to the city.\r\n\r\nThis is an ideal opportunity to acquire a wonderful home in a truly unique idyllic setting.
