    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    GVM present to the market a truly stunning and extended 4 bedroom semi detached residence enjoying bright, spacious and well proportioned living and bedroom accommodation and situated in this much sought after and established residential area. No 15 has been tastefully extended and refurbished in recent years providing contemporary and luxurious living in a home that really oozes sheer class. There are 3 reception rooms on the ground floor, a modern Shaker style Cream fitted kitchen, a functional utility together with 3 double bedrooms (master en suite), 1 single room and main bathroom. There is a cobbled driveway to the front providing good off street parking for 2 cars, a well designed cobbled rear patio and a detached garage. The general vicinity ticks all the boxes when it comes to location just a short walk to the University Hospital. the Crescent Shopping Centre, excellent Primary and Secondary Schools, Sporting facilities, Mungret Recreation Park and array of great sporting facilities and amenities. There is excellent public transport links together with easy access to the nearby motorway. This home is a must for those in search of a quality home in a prime location.
