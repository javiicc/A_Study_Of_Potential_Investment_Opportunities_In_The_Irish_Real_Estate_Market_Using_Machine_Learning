    GVM are privileged to bring to the market this magnificent trophy residence situated in one of Dublins most sought after, leafy and established locations. No. 66 Haddington road offers buyers the opportunity to acquire a magnificent and idyllic period property positioned in the heart of this wonderful and historical area of the city. This "Trophy" home is within walking distance of the Ballsbridge, Donnybrook and St Stephens Green. There are an abundance of amenities on the doorstep including a range of specialist shops, restaurants and recreational facilities. Dublins Silicon Docks, Aviva stadium and Trinity college are located within 5 minutes of the property. This magnificent property offers well proportioned, bright and spacious living/bedroom accommodation. The property comprises of a self contained, ground floor one bedroom apartment with a larger independent residence on the first and second floors.
