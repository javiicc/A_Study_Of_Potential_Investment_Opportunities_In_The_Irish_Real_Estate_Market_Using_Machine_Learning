    Tone House, named after Irish Patriot, Theobald Wolfe Tone who is buried in the adjacent commemorative cemetery Bodenstown, is a Victorian style detached home on approximately 2.4Ha (6 acres) set on a serene private setting of mature gardens, 3 stud rail paddocks together with a stable block consisting of 6 stables, tack room and dungsted. \r\n\r\nTone House is a wonderful easily managed family home of great warmth and elegance in a prime location, only minutes of the new junction 9A offering quick access onto the M7 for commuters to Dublin.\r\n\r\nBuilt in 2005 under the watchful eye of renowned architect Patricia Ennis, who creatively designed the house to offer the ultimate in modern family living accommodation of approx 289sq.m (3,110sq.ft) to provide a perfect balanced home for family living, entertaining and country living.\r\n\r\nThe layout of the ground floor is free-flowing - perfect for entertaining and family gatherings. From the beautiful, impressive wide entrance hallway with feature fireplace and sweeping staircase, to the elegant drawing room, with generous room proportions and high ceilings. Added to the cosy living room, fully fitted modern kitchen/dining room and large sunroom - with all rooms opening out to the large south facing cobble lock patio area, perfect for alfresco dining given its wonderful orientation. \r\n\r\nAt the first floor level are four large double bedrooms, three of which are en suite and the fourth bedroom being adjacent to the jack and jill family bathroom. \r\n\r\nThis instantly appealing family home is in exceptional decorative order with the interior finished to a very high standard throughout; inviting the purchaser to literally walk in and hang their coats. \r\n\r\nHighly desirable for those who have an interest in equine or are just simply searching for a luxurious family home on a substantial holding in an immensely private setting. \r\n\r\nWithout question the location is prime being within only 1.5 km to Sallins Train Station and 3km to Naas town centre with Millenium Business Park being adjacent at junction 9A. The K Club is just 4km and 30km drive to Dublin city centre.
