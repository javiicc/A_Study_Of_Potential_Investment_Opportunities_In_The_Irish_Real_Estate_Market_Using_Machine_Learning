    Site

    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 0.23 ac
    Excellent opportunity to acquire this superb 0.23 acres / 0.093 hectares site with full planning permission to construct a new 4 bedroom two storey detached residence c. 250sqm / 2,691sq ft with separate single storey detached garage. Granted by Wicklow County Council on the 10/05/2017, subject to 9 standard conditions. For additional information, a copy of this Grant of Permission is available from our office or may be downloaded from Wicklow County Councils website, reference number 17/89. It is worth noting that local restrictions (section 47) do not apply and planning expires on the 6/11/2022. \nThe site is located to the western end of the Ballyman Road and approx. 1.7km (1 mile) from the attractive village of Enniskerry, five minutes south of the Dublin border and 23km / 14 miles south of Dublin City Centre, with easy access being afforded to Dublin Airport from the M11/M50. Served by regular bus services to Bray Dart Station, the nearby Luas network & Dublin and its suburbs.\nThe village is a jewel of Wicklow, enjoying peaceful seclusion right at the gateway to the Garden of Ireland. It is a wonderful location in which to live and rear a family. It has a vibrant community and lovely amenities including friendly inns and coffee houses, local neighbourhood shops and restaurants, and sporting clubs such as the renowned Powerscourt & Dun Laoghaire Golf Clubs. The beautiful setting is perfect for nature lovers with its mountain walks, country lanes and proximity to Carrickgollogan and the scenic Sugarloaf Mountain. Nearby is the magnificent Powerscourt Estate, with its beautiful gardens and the famous waterfall. Enniskerry is the ideal location for those yearning country lifestyles yet within easy access to Dublin suburbia and the city centre.\n\nMains water, electricity, and foul sewer connections available. Air to water is the proposed central heating system.
