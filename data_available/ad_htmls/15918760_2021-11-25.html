This spacious 5 bedroom semi-detached property comes to the market in great condition, occupying a unique corner side with a large back garden. Perfect as a forever home for a growing family, and ideally located on one of Castleknock's premier residential roads, this property is sure to appeal to a wide range of buyers. The property's ground floor comprises a large front hallway, kitchen, living room, dining room, and a bright conservatory at the rear. The generously-sized garden gets plenty of sunlight all through the day, and boasts a mixture of mature trees and bushes. Upstairs comprises 5 bedrooms with in-built storage units, and 2 bathrooms. There is a converted attic room at the top of the house, perfect for use as a home office. Ideally located only a stone's throw from the many shops, boutiques, restaurants and pubs in Castleknock Village, Beechpark Avenue is also a short 10-15 minute walk away from the spectacular Phoenix park. The beautiful Royal Canal walking/cycling trail is also right around the corner. This property enjoys close proximity to a range of amenities including several excellent primary and secondary schools as well as Castleknock Tennis club. With exceptional public transport links in the area including bus and train, the M3, N3 and M50 roads are also easily accessible, with Dublin Airport also only a 20 minute drive away. Viewing is highly recommended for this exciting opportunity to acquire a new forever home!
