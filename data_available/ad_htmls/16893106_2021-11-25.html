    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Brown's Estate Agents are happy to present this spacious 3 bed Duplex to the market in the heart of Ongar Village, Dublin 15. This property is in a well-presented manner and has just recently been repainted. A fantastic property for first-time buyers or a potential investor looking to buy within the area. Ongar Village has a host of amenities such as shops, restaurant, bakery, Dunnes Stores, schools to mention a few on your doorstep. This duplex comprises of an entrance hall, kitchen, large living/dining area and guest w.c., on the ground floor. On the first floor is the landing, master bedroom with en suite and two additional bedrooms and a family bathroom. The property also benefits from the large outdoor terrace.\r\n\r\nLocation\r\n\r\nThe property is located close to bus transport links and also Hansfield railway station. The property is also within a short drive of Blanchardstown shopping centre, The Phoenix Park, National aquatic centre, Dublin international airport and the N3 - M50 motorways.
