    24 Granitefield Mews is a bright first floor apartment ideally located on the Killiney end of Rochestown Avenue. This spacious apartment comes to the market in excellent order throughout boasting generous accommodation, a designated underground car parking space and a west facing living area. This property is sure to appeal to those looking for a home / investment in a quiet, mature setting close to excellent amenities.\r\n\r\nThe internal layout is well thought out, stylish and ready to walk into. The accommodation briefly comprises of an entrance hall with storage cupboard and hot press, an open plan living/dining room, a fully equipped kitchen, large bedroom and a fully tiled bathroom.\r\n\r\nThe property enjoys an enviable location close to a number of amenities including shops, restaurants, bars and caf\xc3\xa9s. Most notably, Killiney Shopping Centre is just a 3 minute walk away. There is a great selection of schools in the immediate vicinity including Rathdown, Cluny, Dalkey School Project and Our Lady's Killiney.\r\n\r\nSporting and leisure clubs are equally abundant with numerous clubs located in the area including Killiney Golf Club, just a stone's throw away. The seafront at both Sandycove and Killiney are a short drive away and Killiney Hill Park with it's stunning views over Dublin Bay is a similar distance.\r\n\r\nThe property has excellent transport links with the Luas stop at Cherrywood, QBC, N11, M50 and Dart stations all within a short drive.
