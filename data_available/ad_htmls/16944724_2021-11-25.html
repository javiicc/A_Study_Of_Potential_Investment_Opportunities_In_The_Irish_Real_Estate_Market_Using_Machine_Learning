    Located just 2km above the village of Paulstown, this very nicely appointed property enjoys elevated and breath-taking views of Mount Leinster and the surrounding area.\r\n\r\nBuilt in the year 2001, this fine family home has a great latent potential for a new owner to add their own stamp. \r\n\r\nAn attractive stone wall boundary with pillared entrance sets the tone. The main house is finished with an attractive low maintenance red brick with limestone coins exterior to the front elevation, while the side and rear elevations are finished with a stone dash render requiring no painting.\r\n\r\nSituated on ample grounds of circa. 0.5 of an acre, the site has wonderful potential for improvement further to some careful landscaping. The elevated nature and orientation of the site also ensures that the site benefits from good sunlight throughout the day.\r\n\r\nEntering the front door, a generous size hallway is bright with attractive ceiling moulding and light fixtures. In to the left, a half glass door with bevelled glass leads to the main living room. The living room has a feature brick fireplace with aspect to the front and an open plan arrangement with the kitchen /dining area.\r\n\r\nThe kitchen /dining area is bright and spacious with ample fitted wall and floor presses, breakfast bar to include integrated appliances. Double glass doors off the dining area open to a wonderful south facing vista. Off the kitchen, a utility room is plumbed for washer / dryer with pedestrian door to the rear.\r\n\r\nThe main hallway has an ample size hotpress with various doors to the following accommodation; main bathroom with separate shower cubicle, bath and toilet. Adjacent, a double bedroom faces the rear of the property. Directly across the hall, a second double bedroom faces the front of the property while the third bedroom is a single room also facing the front of the property.\r\n\r\nAll said, this home has tremendous potential to be further improved and is a blank canvas for a new owner.\r\n\r\nViewing is highly recommended.
