    UNIQUE DEVELOPMENT OPPORTUNITY IN THE CENTRE OF KILCULLEN TOWN ON THE BANKS OF THE RIVER LIFFEY. LIFFEY VIEW HOUSE & GROUNDS comprising early Georgian House, c. 266 sq m with Gardens, Stables, and Paddock on approx. 3 acres along with derelict house. \r\n\r\nLiffey View House: The property comprises a substantial two storey period residence located on the banks of the River Liffey, in the centre of Kilcullen. The residence, which has a gross external floor area of approximately 266 sq. m (2,863 sq.ft.), provides entrance hall, 3 reception rooms, kitchen ( Aga excluded ), bathroom, and storage room on the ground floor with drawing room, 3 bedrooms and a bathroom on first floor level. The building has been substantially renovated and is suitable for division STPP. Access is provided to the property off the Main Street and there are a number of stables and a coach house to the rear also suitable for conversion STPP. This property currently serves as a residence with substantial private garden overlooking the River Liffey.\r\n\r\nLiffey Bank House: (Derelict Structure) This property comprises a substantial two storey over garden level period residence located on the banks of the River Liffey, in the centre of Kilcullen. The residence, which has a gross external floor area of approximately 388 sq.m. (4,176 sq.ft.), provides 10 rooms on the ground floor, 6 on the first with 8 rooms in the basement. The property is derelict and not in a habitable condition (internal inspection not permitted). Outside, there are extensive grounds which lead down to the River Liffey below with frontage to same. Access is provided to the property off the Main Street \r\n\r\nZONING: The property is zoned a combination of \\'Town Centre\\' and \\'Open Space & Amenity\\' under the Kilcullen Local Area Plan 2014 - 2020. Liffey Bank House, (derelict structure ) is classified as a 'Protected Structure' under the Local Area Plan. \r\n\r\nFurther details on enquiry: Viewing is strictly by appointment with sole selling agents, Austin Egan MIPAV, of Appleton Property, Kilcullen
