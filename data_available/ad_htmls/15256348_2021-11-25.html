    O'Connor Murphy are delighted to introduce to the market a superbly located four bed semi detached family home measuring an impressive 123.94 sqm, set in one of Limerick's most sought after locations, just off the Ennis Road. No 56 Highfield is ideally suited to a growing family; the accommodation throughout provides the perfect space at both ground floor and upper floor levels,\n\nThis property enjoys a wonderfully well proportioned home, complemented by an abundance of natural light. Upon entering the house, you are immediately struck by the bright & spacious hallway. On the right, tucked away is a convenient accessible downstairs wet room; fully fitted with tiled flooring. Across the hallway, the family room on your left & facing the front garden is a cosy, bright room with an open fire and exquisite parquet wooden flooring. To the rear of the property is a spacious living room looking out onto the rear garden, access is gained here via large patio doors Again, with an open fire & wooden flooring. Following through to the light filled kitchen/dining area; an array of fitted wall & floors units for all your kitchen needs, tiled flooring with access here to the garden.\n\nProceeding to the next level, upstairs has four good size bedrooms, One with built in wardrobes. All easily maintained with wooden flooring throughout. A family bathroom fully fitted with tiled flooring & electric shower completes the accommodation at this level.\n\nSecure off street tarmac parking for two cars behind cast iron gates with a generous lawn featuring an array of mature plants & shrubs adding to fact a convenient gated side entrance. The rear garden has an elevated patio and walled therefore, enjoys immense privacy with an all important south facing aspect. An abundance of flower beds planted with many evergreen flowers and shrubs; a generous lawn & mature trees give a great sense of peace & calm.\n\nA largely residential area that has proven very popular with a wide selection of buyers, Highfield is approximately 2.4 km from Limerick City Centre. The location offers an unparalleled sense of convenience, within a gentle stroll to many shops, schools restaurants. With proximity to a host of sporting & recreational amenities including Na Piarsaigh GAA Club , Gaelic Grounds, Thomond Park and Limerick Lawn Tennis Club. Limerick City is just a short walk away along with easy access to the Main Road Network System. This prime location is within easy reach of a number of schools including Ard Scoil Ris, JFK & Villiers school.\n\nAll in all, this family home combines all things necessary for easy living.
