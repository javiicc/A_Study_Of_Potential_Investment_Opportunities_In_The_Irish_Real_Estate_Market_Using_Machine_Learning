    Tom McDonald & Associates - EXPERIENCED, PROFESSIONAL, TRUSTED - are pleased to offer to the market this Stunning & Deceptively Spacious 2 Bed Top Floor Turn-Key Apartment. This Fully Furnished Property extends to approximately 860sq.ft. Located in a Purpose-Built, Concrete Constructed Apartment with 9 Units in the Block & within Walking Distance of Portarlington Town Centre, Train Station & All Amenities. Presented in Excellent Condition & Tastefully Decorated throughout, this Property would make an Ideal Home or Investment Opportunity. Accommodation is comprised of Entrance Hall, Open Plan Living/Dining Area, Kitchen, 2 Bedrooms, En-Suite, Bathroom & Storage Room. There is a Quality Fitted Kitchen just off the Bright Dining Area with Oven, Hob, Fridge Freezer, Washing Machine & Dishwasher. The Expansive Living/Dining Area with Intercom System & Wiring for Cable Television has Laminate Flooring and comes as seen with all Furnishings. There are Fitted Wardrobes in Both Bedrooms & the Main Bedroom has a Large Tastefully Decorated En-Suite with Shower Unit and Space for the addition of a Bath. Additional Storage is provided for with the overhead Attic Space which has a Stira Ladder installed for Easy Access. The Property is Fully Alarmed. For Value and Quality, this Property is a Must View. Services: Mains Water, Sewerage, Electricity & Broadband. Electric Storage Heating.
