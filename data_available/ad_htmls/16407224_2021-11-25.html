    Three bedroom detached cottage\nThis charming three bedroom cottage retains much of its original features and the traditional hand-crafted thatch roof, however the property comes to the market after being extended with modern amenities and is finished to a lovely standard throughout. Phoenix Estates are delighted to bring to the market this unique opportunity when it has been in the current owner's family for many years. The cottage sits on approx. a C. 0.45 of an acre site and is based in an excellent location within easy reach of both Wexford and Gorey towns & the Wexford coastline. \n(BER Exempt) - Viewing by appointment is highly recommended\n\n\nACCOMODATION:\n\nEntrance Hall: 1.8m x 1.56m wooden floor, wooden ceiling.\n\nInner Hall: 3.3m x 1.77m tiled floor, staircase to 1st floor, under stair storage\n\nKitchen: 3.66m x 4.14m slate effect tiled floor, fitted kitchen with units at eye & waist level, tiled splashback, cooker, fridge, back door.\n\nLiving Room: 3.55m x 4.68m wooden floor, solid fuel stove with natural stone surround, wooden ceiling.\n\nShower Room: 2.01m x 1.70m tiled floor, fully tiled walls, wc, whb, free standing shower Triton T90xr.\n\nBedroom 1: 3.54m x 3.37m carpeted, wooden ceiling, ornamental fireplace with cast iron fireplace.\n\nBedroom 2: 3.8m x 3.35m carpeted, vaulted timber ceiling.\n\nBedroom 3: 3.31m x 3.35m carpeted, vaulted ceilings.\n\nServices: Power supply, private well, private sewerage system, water heating, electric heating.\n\nBER: Exempt\n\nDirections: On entering Ballyedmond from the Gorey side, just as you enter the village there is a small road running parallel to the main road.
