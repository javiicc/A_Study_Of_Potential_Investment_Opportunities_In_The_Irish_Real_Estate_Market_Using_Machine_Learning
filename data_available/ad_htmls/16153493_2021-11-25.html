    Holly Lodge is an attractive single storey over garden level double fronted Regency villa enjoying an enviable location in the heart of Blackrock Village on the lower end of Carysfort Avenue. Built in the early 1800's, the property is full of character with many original features including ceiling cornicing, ceiling roses, signature fireplaces, sash windows and window shutters. The spacious accommodation has been very well maintained by four generations of the same family since 1928.\r\nThe rear garden which is mainly laid to lawn benefits from bright easterly orientation, ample scope to extend (subject to planning permission) and a gated pedestrian entrance leading to the Blackrock Business Park where there is a designated off-street parking space. \r\n\r\nEleven granite steps lead to the entrance hall door with an attractive fanlight. The bright and well-proportioned accommodation extending to approximately \r\n153 sq. m / 1,646 sq. ft comprises a welcoming entrance hall, to the left of which is a bright drawing room with an original marble feature fireplace and an ornate centre rose. There are double doors from the drawing room which open into the rear facing room which is currently being used as a bedroom but could easily be returned to another reception room. To the right of the entrance hall is a front facing room currently in use as a bedroom. Further down the hall there is one more bedroom as well as a wc and a separate bathroom.\r\nOn the ground floor level there is a large kitchen/breakfast room with a useful pantry for storage as well as a living room and a fourth bedroom. There is a shower room with wc and access to the rear garden. There is storage space under the front steps as well as access to the front of the house through a second front door. \r\n\r\nThere is a fantastic selection of shops in the recently upgraded Blackrock Village Centre and the Frascati Shopping Centre. The village also includes some of southside Dublin's finest array of restaurants and cafes. \r\nThe sea is only a five-minute walk from the house and coastal walks along Blackrock Park and Seapoint Avenue are an amble away. For those who enjoy the outdoors, the impressive Carysfort Park is located within a minute's walk with a large lake, playground, mature woodland and nature areas. \r\nAlso nearby are Blackrock Bowling and Tennis Club and Rockfield Park. \r\nThere is an excellent selection of both primary and secondary schools in the area including Carysfort National School, All Saint's National School, Willow Park, Blackrock College, St Andrew's College, Newpark Comprehensive and Sion Hill. The Smurfit Business School is located within 500 metres up Carysfort Avenue.\r\nPublic transport options are very hard to beat with the DART in Blackrock village, multiple bus routes and the Aircoach all within a short walk from the front door making access to the city centre and the airport very easy.\r\n\r\nEntrance Hall 10.12m x 1.31m.
