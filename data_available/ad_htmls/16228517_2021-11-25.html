    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to bring to the market St Margarets, a most attractive detached residence boasting a wealth of generously appointed accommodation throughout, complimented by an abundance of natural light and nicely set back from the road. This property offers potential purchasers a great opportunity to acquire a superior residence with a superb balance of accommodation set against an enviable rear garden of 0.27acre (approx). It s superb site allows great scope for extending, while still retaining a very large rear garden.\n\nThe property comprises of large welcoming entrance hallway, bright drawing room to the front, living room with beautiful views of the rear garden, and a sunroom with family room off with dual aspect. A dining room which can be used as a bedroom with wetroom, and kitchen to front with access into the garage/utility. A separate bathroom and WC with storage completes the downstairs accommodation.\nUpstairs, a bright spacious landing leads to three double bedrooms the main bedroom benefitting from en-suite facilities, and walk-in-wardrobe and two single bedrooms. A separate bath suite and WC completes the accommodation at this level. The attic which has full surface area of the upstairs complete with window has potential to be converted.\n\nThe property is conveniently located close to all conceivable amenities both social and essential. The property is superbly located on the N11 with a number of bus routes including the 145 and 46a providing frequent access to the city centre and QBC.
