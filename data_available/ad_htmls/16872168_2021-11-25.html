    Aptly named 'Orchard View', DNG Murphy Condon proudly presents this beautiful home for sale by private treaty. The house itself is situated on a hugely impressive and pristinely landscaped, c. 1.15 Acre site, in the highly sought after Sweeneys Hill location in Rathpeacon, and the property boasts 205 square metres / 2,205 square feet of living space.\n\nThis property will be sure to attract a flurry of viewing interest and offers from the discerning buyer.\n\nThe property itself was built in 1995 and extended in 1999 and built to the highest building standards. \n\nThis spectacular & beautiful 4/5 bedroom detached property comes to the market in excellent condition and the gardens are manicured and landscaped professionally to an extremely high standard. \n\nThe greater Blarney/Rathpeacon area has a shortage of high end quality housing supply at present, and this property is sure to be top of the astute buyer's list and is an ideal opportunity to acquire a fantastic family home in a highly sought after residential area, with an excellent location close to Cork City and all major road networks. This house is deceptively spacious, comprising circa 2,205 square feet / 205 sq.m in the actual house and also there is potential to convert the attached garage also if required. \n\nThe site that the house is located on extends to approx. 1.15 Acres and has beautiful landscaped gardens to the front, rear and sides and a driveway that sweeps around the house suitable for parking up to 10 cars comfortably. \n\n\nLocation; \nLocated just 3 miles from Cork City, this house truly is in the middle of nature, but just 10 minutes from town. This property has been designed to perfectly fit with contemporary family life, and you will appreciate the luxurious features and attention to detail upon viewing. The unique sense of light and space throughout the interior is enhanced through large windows and double doors. Imagine waking up every day surrounded by nature, fresh air and the beautiful Irish countryside, while the convenience of a modern city with all its services and amenities is literally just 5 minutes away.\n\nOutside; \nThe front of the property has a decorative pebble dash render finish. The front of the property faces South and gets beautiful sunshine all day. There is landscaped gardens, mature hedging, dry stone walls and mature trees for privacy on the boundary walls, and a large garage attached also to the property(possibly suitable for conversion to a studio/apartment/workshop). The rear of the property has a beautiful patio/BBQ area. The gardens benefit from the fact they are extremely private and also safe for children with gates and walls enclosing garden spaces. Nestled nicely on its own imressive 1.15 acre site and surrounded by sun trapped patios and bar-b-que areas this home has something to offer purchasers from all walks of life. With lawns spreading to all corners of the site, it is a carefully designed layout to ensure harmony and tranquility are enjoyed in the gardens. The outside of this home gives a flavour of what can be expected inside which exceeds expectation. The flow of space at upper and lower levels is well thought out and rooms can act as double functions. The use of wooden flooring, tasteful tiling and neutral colour schemes blend together to give a pleasing warm & welcoming property.\n\nEntrance Hallway 4.7m x 3.4m.\n\nLiving Room 4.9m x 4.3m.\n\nLounge 4.9m x 3.6m.\n\nBedroom One 3.0m x 2.2m.\n\nBathroom One 1.8m x 1.8m.\n\nDining Room 3.6m x 3.2m.\n\nKitchen 5.0m x 3.6m.\n\nUtility 3.6m x 1.8m.\n\nLanding 3.3m x 2.0m.\n\nBathroom Two 3.1m x 2.2m.\n\nMaster Bedroom / Bedroom Two 4.8m x 5.4m.\n\nEn Suite 2.2m x 0.9m.\n\nBedroom Three 3.3m x 3.0m.\n\nBedroom Four 5.4m x 3.7m.\n\nStudy/Home Office/Bedroom Five 4.0m x 2.0m.\n\nBathroom Four 3.0m x 2.0m.\n\nWalk In Wardrobe 2.2m x 1.2m.\n\n\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nThis property is located directly across the road from Rathpeacon National School & Rathpeacon Community Centre.
