    Sherry FitzGerald Gleeson are delighted to present Donore House to the market, an exceptional two-storey detached residence of great charm and character, with separate Garage, a Large Store / Workshop, and Tennis Court on a superb 2.32 Acre Site, on the outskirts of Thurles, County Tipperary.\n\nThe residence is situated just 3km from the centre of Thurles town, in a pleasant location just off the Horse & Jockey Road and adjacent to Thurles Golf Club.
