    Apt.1 is a spacious two bedroom ground floor apartment which comes to the market having recently been extensively refurbished and modernised. Measuring 85m2 (915ft2) this period property comes with a host of benefits including excellent location in the heart of the village and minutes walk from the seafront, off street parking with electronic gates, private patio area with full length awning and gas fired central heating. \r\n\r\nUpon entering No.53 you are immediately impressed by the grand hall with its high ceilings which continue as you enter Apt.1. The entrance hallway leads to the open plan living area connecting to kitchen/dining area which has floor to ceiling patio door and windows allowing in an abundance of natural light. The two double bedrooms are both en suite while the guest wc and utility finish the accommodation. To the front of the property is a gravelled driveway for off street parking and is serviced by electronic gates. The rear garden is finished with clear granite slabs with full length awning and heaters creating a perfect space for entertaining.\r\n\r\nSuperbly located in the heart of Glasthule village this property has every amenity on its doorstep including the ever-popular Cavistons food emporium & sea food restaurant. The Punnett and the wonderful 64 wine shop naming just a few of the top-class venues this bustling village has to offer. Leisure facilities are also very well catered for with sea swimming becoming extremely popular you are only minutes walking distance from the 40ft. Sailing, golf, rugby & tennis clubs are also located close by and stunning scenic walks of the coastline are also on offer. Excellent transport facilities with the DART and (QBC) Bus all within 2 min walking distance providing easy access to the city centre and beyond. \r\n\r\nACCOMMODATION\r\n\r\nHallway: Wooden flooring, alarm panel, leading to;\r\n\r\nLiving Room: (4.23m x 4.06m) Wooden flooring with gas fire stove, radiator and electrical points. \r\n\r\nKitchen/Dining Room: (4.75m x 4.61m) Tiled flooring with an array of wall and floor units, integrated dishwasher, oven, hob and microwave.
