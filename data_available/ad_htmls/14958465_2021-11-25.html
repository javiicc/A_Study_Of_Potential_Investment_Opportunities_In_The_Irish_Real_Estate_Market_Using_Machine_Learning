    Coolmoreen, Innishannon, Co. Cork

    1.2 ac

    Site

    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 1.2 ac
    This site lies within 2 kms of Innishannon Village and is situated South East of the village. It occupies a secluded setting in the Bandon River Valley and is facing directly South. The plot measures approximately 1.2 acres and lies 400 metres off the main Innishannon to Kinsale road in an elevated position. There is a timber structure on the property, but it is an unauthorised dwelling. The property is being offered to the market subject to planning for one dwelling on the plot. The plot has the benefit of mature trees and of hedging along some of the boundaries and lends itself to being a beautiful setting for a family home. (Please note that there are buyer restrictions on this site and that applicants will have to qualify under Policy Objective RCI-4-2 of the Cork County Development Plan).
