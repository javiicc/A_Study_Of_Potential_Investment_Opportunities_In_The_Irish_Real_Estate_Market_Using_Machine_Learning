    Penthouse living at its best, the top floor of The Oaks is dominated by this excellent two bedroom apartment. It is presented in pristine condition throughout and is turn-key ready. It boasts spectacular views over the Dublin Mountains, Airfield Farm and further afield. For such an urban area the agricultural outlook is rare to say the least and certainly provides a tranquil break from the hustle and bustle below. The apartment is very well appointed with a recent new kitchen and refurbished ensuite shower room. Quality wooden flooring is also in situe. \n\n\nThe accommodation briefly comprises hallway with storage area and hot press, luxury bathroom, bright living/dining area with access to corner balcony commanding panoramic views of Airfield Estate and the Dublin Mountains, modern fitted kitchen, two generous double bedrooms (master en suite) both with balcony access and views of Dublin Mountains. The property enjoys the use of two car spaces and well maintained communal grounds.\n\n\nRockfield is quite literally within a stone's throw from every conceivable amenity. This includes transport links such as the Luas stop at Balally and Dundrum town centre with a wide variety of shops, restaurants, cinema to name a few. Sporting facilities, gyms and of course Airfield farm are all a short walk. \n\n\nDue to its highly desirable location, this property is sure to appeal to buyers from all walks including first time buyers, downsizers and investors alike.
