      Phase 3 Sold Out - Register Your Interest For The Next Phase \r\n\r\nBay View is a stunning new homes development located just off The Coast Road, Baldoyle. This brand new development is set in a wonderfully unique coastal location between Sutton and Portmarnock. It is bounded by an 80 hectare planned park giving a perfect blend of seaside scenery and parkland, all on your doorstep. Clongriffin Train station is located only minutes' walk from your new front door providing rapid transit to the city centre and beyond, with Dublin Airport (11 km) easily accessible by both public transport, M1 and the M50. There will be a wide range of property types available catering to all buyers including traditional 3 & 4 bedroom family homes. Designed by Henry J Lyons Architects, these A-Rated homes are being built to incorporate modern living and spacious interiors with smart internal layouts. Bay View will be Help to Buy Approved for first time buyers. \r\n\r\nPlease register your interest or contact the Savills New Homes Team on 01 618 1300
