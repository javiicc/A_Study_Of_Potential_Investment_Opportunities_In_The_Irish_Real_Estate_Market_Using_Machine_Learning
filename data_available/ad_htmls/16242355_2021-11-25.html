    Located off Glasnevin Avenue, 1 A Cedarwood Green is an attractive, three bedroom detached residence with adjoining garage situated in a much sought-after, mature location. \n\nThis property would make a perfect family home and would be ideal for those looking to move into a modern spacious house. Bright and well laid out, this home benefits from a south-facing rear garden.\nAccommodation comprises an entrance hall, living room, guest W.C and utility room, kitchen and dining area with double doors opening onto a large deck area and private, fully walled rear garden. \n\nUpstairs, there are three bedrooms: two double bedrooms (master en suite), a single bedroom and family bathroom with feature free standing bath. The attic, which has wonderful views of Dublin City and mountains, is currently used as a lounge (approx. 21 sq.m).\n\nThe front of the property is paved with ample off-street parking for up to three cars. A side gate leads to the south facing rear garden which is laid out with deck and lawn areas as well as mature shrubs and trees. There is also access to the garage from the rear garden. \n\nCedarwood Green has always been a popular location for families with acres of mature parkland on its doorstep. As well as being within easy access of the M1/M50, Dublin Airport and the City Centre, Cedarwood Green is located near Dublin City University and Beaumont Hospital and is well serviced by public transport with an abundance of schools, shopping centres (Clearwater, Charlestown and Omni), sports and leisure facilities all nearby. \n\n\nEntrance Hall 4.7m x 1.74.
