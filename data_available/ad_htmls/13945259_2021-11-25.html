    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    3 Roomed Country Cottage, Outbuildings & C12 acres of lands situated at Drumalasson, Ballaghaderreen which is a much sought after, tranquil residential area on the outskirts of the town. Cottage, which is in need of renovation, offers wonderful potential and is serviced by mains water and sewage. Outbuildings comprise: 3 Bay Dry Shed together with 2 Bay Single Slatted Shed adjoining. Lands, which are in one lot, except for road dividing, are described as being of mixed quality. Option to purchase an additional C6.42 of lands to include C2 acres of turbury bog.
