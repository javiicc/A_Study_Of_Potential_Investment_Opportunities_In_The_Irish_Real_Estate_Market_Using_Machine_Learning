    Moress Farm, Moress, Inch Island, Co. Donegal F93D3C8\r\n\r\nCrana Financial Services Ltd. are delighted to be offering for sale by private treaty this wonderful 6/7 bedroom Farmhouse, set in approx. 3 acres of lovely peaceful countryside with splendid views towards Lough Swilly and as far as the eyes can see \r\n\r\nStanding proud on this magnificent elevated site, Moress Farm has stood for over 100 years, enjoying the panoramic views this lovely position provides. Approached via a winding private driveway, the beauty of the countryside is evident at every turn and this gorgeous country property is nestled within the landscape. \r\n\r\nIn immaculate condition throughout, Moress Farm has been restored and renovated to provide an exceptionally comfortable family home, with ample space within. \r\n\r\nWith five double bedrooms, two single room, three reception rooms and cosy large family kitchen.\r\nWith the added benefits of a self contained basement library area/study room \r\n\r\nThis bright and pretty family home is ideal for those searching for a country idyll, while maintaining easy links for commuting to both Derry city and Buncrana. \r\n\r\nOutside there is approx. 3 acres of land, laid out in glorious gardens and ample parking. \r\nMoress Farm is just 10 mins from Derry City and twenty minutes from Derry city airport.\r\n\r\nInch island is around 5 square miles (13 square kilometres) in area. It lies in Lough Swilly, just off the south-western coast of Inishowen near the village of\xc2\xa0Burnfoot. Inch Island is connected to the mainland by a causeway road, known as the Inch Banks. \r\n\r\nInch Wildfowl Reserve is within walking distance from Moress farm and features an 8km looped walk, catering for outdoor recreational activity including walking, running and cycling. This scenic pathway follows around the lake edge and offers panoramic views of the surrounding agricultural landscape and wetlands.\r\n\r\n Further details and appointment to view from sole selling agent
