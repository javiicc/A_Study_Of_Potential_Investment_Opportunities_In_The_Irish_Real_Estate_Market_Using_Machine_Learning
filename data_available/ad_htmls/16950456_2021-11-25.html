    McGuirk Beggan is thrilled to present this superb apartment to the market. No.36 The Courtyard is a spacious two-bedroom, 2-bathroom second floor apartment which boasts expansive light filled accommodation and is in excellent condition throughout with a fantastic sunny balcony that catches the evening sunshine and overlooks the beautifully manicured grounds. Fortfield Square is an exceptionally private, highly renowned & exclusive development tucked away off College Drive in the heart of Terenure. Accessed through an immaculate secure entrance foyer which features both lifts and stairs to all floors as well as access to the underground car park & a private well-stocked communal garden, this property offers high quality accommodation within this quiet and mature setting which was built in the early 2000's. This apartment offers the perfect opportunity for someone looking to step onto the property ladder, someone trading down looking for a more manageable home or fantastic investment opportunity. \r\n\r\nFortfield Square is a highly regarded development surrounded by beautifully landscaped grounds and within close proximity of a host of excellent amenities. The property is ideally located beside both Terenure and Templeogue villages offering a host of shops, restaurants and cafes. The recreational facilities are well catered for, Bushy Park and all it has to offer is a short 5-minute walk. Terenure College, swimming pool and Templeogue Tennis Club are also close by. The area is also close to a selection of highly renowned schools including Terenure and Templeogue colleges, St. Pius X and Our Lady's School to name a few. Fortfield Square is well serviced by bus routes to include the 15, 49, 65, 6B and 54A. The development is also within close proximity of the M50 road network which connects to all major national routes.\r\n\r\nThe accommodation briefly comprises entrance hall, living/dining, kitchen, two double bedrooms, bathroom and en suite. \r\n\r\nThis fantastic apartment offers the new purchaser exceptional living space and copious amounts of natural light throughout - viewing is highly recommended.\r\n\r\n\r\nwhat3words /// caves.dome.table\r\n\r\nNotice\r\nPlease note we have not tested any apparatus, fixtures, fittings, or services.
