    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to bring to the market no.39 South Dock Street a wonderfully upgraded mid terrace home which is sure to impress. This two-bedroom home is presented with flair and attention to detail and provides a stylish property in this mature residential location, offering with its beautiful south facing courtyard, this fine property is sure to appeal to a wide audience. \r\n\r\nThe accommodation comprises; cozy large living room to the front with stairs to upper level, open plan to the dining area, modern kitchen with door leading to the utility with access out onto the sunny courtyard and the family bathroom. Upstairs there are two double bedrooms with built in wardrobes. Outside doesn t disappoint, with a sunny south facing rear courtyard making it a perfect place to sit and enjoy the sun as well as a communal park with playground and playing pitches across the road.\r\n\r\nThe property is ideally located within walking distance of the City Centre and local villages such as Ballsbridge, Sandymount and Ringsend. Excellent amenities in the area include the Aviva Stadium, SportsCo Leisure Centre and the superb 3 Arena, not to mention Bath Avenue, host to the popular Bath Pub, Lotts & Co Grocers and The Chop House restaurant to name but a few. The property also benefits from close proximity to Grand Canal Dart Station with local employers including Google, Facebook, and some of the city s leading law firms.\r\n\r\n\r\nLiving/ Dining Room with wood floors, sash window to the front, ceiling coving, feature fire, tv & phone points, under stairs storage, radiator covers, window overlooking rear courtyard, stairs to upper level and door to kitchen\r\n\r\nKitchen with tiled flooring and splash back, range of floor and wall units, integrated oven & hobs, integrated microwave, integrated dish washer, double stainless-steel sink with drainer, window overlooking the rear courtyard, provision for fridge/freezer and door to utility\r\n\r\nUtility Room with tiled floor, storage cupboard, plumbed for washer/dryer, door to south facing courtyard and door to bathroom\r\n\r\nBathroom with tiled flooring and tiled walls, sink with storage, w.c, bath with overhead shower attachment and wall mounted towel rail.\r\n\r\nLanding with access to the attic\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 double room with laminate wood flooring, two sash windows to the front, built in wardrobes, tv points and shelved hot-press\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 double room with laminate wood flooring, window to the rear and built-in wardrobes
