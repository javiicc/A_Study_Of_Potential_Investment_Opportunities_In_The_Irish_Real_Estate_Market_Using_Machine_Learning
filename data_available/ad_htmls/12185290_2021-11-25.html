    Elegant 5 Bedroomed Roadside Bungalow \n(on c 0.74 Acres approx.)\nVery attractive and desirable detached family home within 1 km of Scariff town. This property is located on the southern side of the main Scariff to Mountshannon road (R352) and it is the second-last of a row of broadly similar one-off properties. It contains the bungalow, a large garage at the back, front and back gardens and a hard standing area at the back. \nThe house sits slightly back from the road on its site of 0.74 acres approx., which is very well laid out and maintained.\nIt is a well presented 2,165 sq. ft. bungalow that was built in 1973 and has been extended and refurbished in the intervening years. It has a BER rating of D2. It is finished to a high standard of workmanship. Rooms are well proportioned and there is a good feeling of space about the house. \nThere are many fine features in the house, immediately evident from entering the hallway, leading to a spacious sitting room with fireplace and solid fuel stove. The open plan kitchen/dining/family room is spacious and also has a solid fuel stove. \nThe same high quality finish continues throughout the bedrooms and bathrooms. The master bedroom has a large built-in wardrobe/dressing table and a luxurious ensuite with shower. \nThe other 4 bedrooms are also in excellent decorative order.\nAt the back is a sun room leading to a paved patio area. \nOn the outside is a large garage with high double doors and landscaped gardens with grass and shrubs. \n\nThe house contains: \n* Front door leading to a large main hallway \n* Large sitting room with a brick fireplace and solid-fuel stove. \n* Spacious family room with a kitchen area, dining area, and sitting room.\n * Sun-room at the rear connecting the kitchen area to a work-room area containing an upstairs office/study, a gaming room, a work area and a small utility area. \n* Bedroom 1 is a large en-suite at the back of the house. \n* Bedroom 2 is to the front of the house and has a built-in wardrobe. \n* Bedroom 3 is also at the front and has a built-in wardrobe.\n* Bedroom 4 is to the front and has a built-in wardrobe. \n* Bedroom 5 is at the back and also has a mirrored built-in wardrobe.\n\nThe house is heated with an oil-fired central heating boiler and with solid-fuel stoves in the living rooms. To improve heat retention, the walls have been pumped with insulation beads and extra insulation was placed in the attic areas.\n\nAccess to the house is directly from the Mountshannon road, on to its tarmac drive, with parking areas at the front, at the side and at the back. There are nice rural views to the rear of the property. \nTo the back of the house is a large garage with high double doors.\n\nThis property is in excellent condition throughout and would make an ideal family home. It is an ideal opportunity for purchasers seeking a well-appointed bungalow in a semi-rural setting, less than a km. from Scariff town, with all its amenities.
