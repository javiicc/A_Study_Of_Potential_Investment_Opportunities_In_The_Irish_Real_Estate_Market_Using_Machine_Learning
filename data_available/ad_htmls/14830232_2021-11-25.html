    Donal Boyd Auctioneers are excited to present Lisbryan to the market. Built in 2000 and sitting on c.0.28hectares/0.69acre, this superbly appointed 5 double bedroom property offers the discerning purchaser the opportunity to acquire a substantial family home in a quiet countryside location. The main residence extends to c.2,500 sq.ft and also benefits from a separate single storey cottage which is attached.
