    New Quay Real Estate is delighted to bring to the market this stunning residence set on an elevated position on 0.52 acres with breath-taking sea views in one of the most sought-after locations on the Wild Atlantic Way. \r\n\r\nThe residence comprises a three-bedroom two-bathroom family home in turn-key condition. On entering the house one is immediately struck with the abundance of natural light that floods the living space with all rooms enjoying fabulous ocean views. The porch entrance leads into a double height space with mezzanine landing which in turn leads directly into to a beautiful bright sitting room the focal point of which is a magnificent bespoke open fireplace with white marble surround, ornate cast-iron insert and black marble hearth. French doors adjacent connect to the rear of the house and further add to the feeling of space and light. The room has multiple power points and is finished in neutral colours with recessed spot lighting and solid wood floors. An archway leads to the bright and spacious open plan kitchen with a substantial breakfast bar and seating for eight at a large dining table, ample countertop surfaces and a multitude of storage options in solid oak cabinets. The space is finished in neutral colours and along with recessed lighting is ceramic tiled throughout. Two sets of French doors, one to the rear of the house and one to the conservatory along with three additional windows afford natural light in all directions and wonderful ocean vistas for all scenarios whether dining, preparing food or simply washing up. The conservatory has a three-metre ceiling height finished in tongue and groove wood panelling with recessed spot lighting along with solid wood floor and an oil-fired radiator. A glazed door with exterior access and double-glazed windows that sweep the entire perimeter of the space create a wonderful area to relax, soak up the rays of the sun and enjoy the beautiful views. There is also a utility directly off the kitchen with water services, more storage options and direct access to the rear of the house. \r\n\r\nThe first of the three large bedrooms is located on the ground floor and has its own fully tiled en-suite with bath and electric shower, wash hand basin and toilet along with ancillary library/lounge area. The room is very bright and spacious with a 2.77m ceiling height, two front and one side window along with fully glazed French doors to the rear. This separate access opens the room to a multitude of uses including but not limited to suitability for elderly family members, wheelchair users or seasonal rental guests. In fact, given the prime oceanside location and just 500 metres from Linnanes world famous Lobster Bar, any such rental would be likely to attract premium rates and maximum occupancies. The room is finished in neutral colours with pendant lighting and solid wood floors. On the first-floor bedroom two is large, bright and spacious currently configured as a triple along with a childrens cot yet plenty of additional space for circulation and storage. D\xc3\xa9cor is finished in tasteful neutral colours, a feature wall-papered wall and carpeted flooring. It also has the benefit of a walk-in wardrobe/storage room and a fully glazed door leading to a dedicated balcony. Bedroom three in effect mirrors the previous room in size and layout again incorporating its own dedicated balcony. This room features a double bed and full-sized pool table along with a home office section. The d\xc3\xa9cor includes a neutral finish, pendant lighting and solid wood floors. The fully tiled family bathroom positioned between these two bedrooms incorporates an electric shower, wash hand basin and toilet. Adjacent is the hot press with plenty of additional storage. \r\n\r\nOn stepping out of the residence one is immediately greeted with views of New Quay harbour, the Aughinish peninsula, Galway Bay, Connemara and beyond to the Atlantic on the horizon. Pictures and descriptions simply do not do this gem of a location justice. You have to see it for yourself.\r\n\r\nThis presents a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to acquire a beautiful home in a coveted location where opportunities very seldom come to the market.
