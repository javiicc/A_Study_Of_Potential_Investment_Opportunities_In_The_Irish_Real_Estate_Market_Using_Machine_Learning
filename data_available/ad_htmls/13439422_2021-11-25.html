    An exceptionally attractive detached two storey over basement early Victorian period residence set amidst stunning mature well stocked gardens that extend to approximately 1.46HA (3.65 acres) that provide complete seclusion and privacy securely positioned behind electric gates.\n\nMontebello House is an instantly appealing aesthetically pleasing Italianate style Victorian period residence that has been wonderfully maintained throughout. The bright and airy light infused accommodation oozes charm and character throughout its generously proportioned accommodation. The property has retained all the period embellishments of the era including ornate ceiling coving and decorative roses, period fireplaces, original floorboards, 4m (13ft) high ceilings, large sliding sash windows with shutters intact and a distinctive bow fronted porch entrance above the welcoming granite steps.\n\nInternally the generously proportioned accommodation at hall level flows wonderfully from room to room overlooking the manicured gardens that surround the property perfectly.\n\nAn impressive staircase leads up to the exceptionally generous landing drenched in natural light from the three feature windows and skylight. At this level there are seven bedrooms. The master suite consisting of a large bedroom, a generous en suite bathroom and a fine dressing room. A further bedroom, bathroom and dressing room are on the next level and a stairwell then accesses the roof garden of approximately 15m x 15m boasting stunning views out to sea as well as the Dublin Mountains.\n\nAt basement level there is a further 2/3 bedrooms, one with en suite as well as a shower room, further bathroom, central dining area, kitchen/breakfast room, two reception rooms, wine cellar and a secondary kitchen/utility. As there is separate access at this level it lands itself extremely well as a self-contained unit if so desired.\n\nKilliney is a highly regarded address and along with its own amenities, is near to Dalkey Town Centre and its surrounding restaurants, bars, boutiques and eateries, churches, schools, not to mention the beautiful strand and coastal walkways. Nearby transport facilities include the DART, bus routes, LUAS at Cherrywood and the M50 and N11 are readily accessible. There are a variety of leisure facilities in the area, including golf, swimming, sailing and stunning coastal walks around the hills of Killiney and Dalkey.\n \nBow Fronted Porched Entrance (3.50m x The long sweeping driveway leads to the front of the main house with parking space for numerous vehicles. There is a detached gate lodge to the front of the plot, currently occupied by grounds staff. There is an active vegetable garden to the rear with a large glass house and separate compost area. There is a swimming pool to the rear with a detached pool house to include changing space, shower and a sauna.
