    Situated just off the highly sought after, tree lined Mount Merrion Avenue, No.81 The Elms is a bright and well-appointed three-bedroom first floor apartment. Tucked away to the back of this privately mature, gated development, this fine apartment enjoys a peaceful and quiet setting. Ideally located, the property is situated close to a host of amenities in Blackrock, Booterstown and Stillorgan villages including excellent shopping facilities and enjoyable coastal walks. There is seamless access to the city centre with the DART at Blackrock station and numerous Dublin bus routes which run locally.\r\n\r\nUpon entering this apartment you are instantly greeted with a sense of space and light. The accommodation is approx 110sqm /1,184sqft and briefly comprises of a welcoming entrance hall with storage facilities. Double doors lead to an exceptionally spacious living room and dining room with hard wood flooring, feature gas fire place and sliding doors lead to a south facing balcony. Off this space lies a well equipped kitchen with all integrated appliances and space for enjoying your morning coffee. There are three double bedrooms, the main with a newly fitted ensuite shower room. A newly fitted family bathroom completes the accommodation of this fine apartment. \r\n\r\nThe apartment benefits from a large south facing balcony which overlooks the communal grounds within this development, offering the utmost in privacy and seclusion which presents the perfect place to dine al fresco . There is a boiler house which also can accommodate a dryer. There is also communal car parking for both residents and guests.\r\n\r\nThe convenience of this apartment cannot be overstated, Blackrock Village is within minutes walk providing a wide variety of shops, restaurants as well as two shopping Centres. Local transport links include Blackrock DART station, the Quality bus corridor along the Stillorgan Road and Rock Road. The Aircoach service also runs along Mount Merrion Avenue providing easy access to Dublin Airport. No. 81 is also convenient to several highly regarded schools.\r\n\r\nViewing is highly advised in order to appreciate all this wonderful property has to offer.\r\n\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 5.35m x 3.45m.
