    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to offer 16 A Castleknock Dale to the market for sale. This is a unique opportunity to own a detached 3 bedroomed + study new build home in a well-established location while enjoying the benefits that come with this seldom seen combination. \n \nAccommodation briefly comprises of an entrance hall with substantial understairs storage, a spacious guest wc, living room with bay window and a large open plan kitchen/dining room off which lies a shower room that offers the potential to be converted to a utility room or knocked through to offer a substantial guest bathroom. Upstairs there is a generous landing area, 3 large double bedrooms with built in wardrobes, a large study with built in wardrobes and the family bathroom completes the accommodation. \n\nTo the front of the property is a paved driveway with space for 2 family cars. The rear garden is south-west facing, maintenance free with stylish patio area, artificial grass, and neat planting. \n\nNo. 16 A is particularly well situated at the front of Laurel Lodge close to Castleknock Road. It is enviably and conveniently positioned within a 5 minute walk of Castleknock Train Station which will definitely appeal to commuters. Scoil Thomas National School and the local shops at Laurel Lodge are just five mins walk also.\n\nCastleknock Village is located just a little further on with a good choice of shops, cafes and restaurants. Blanchardstown Shopping Centre is easily reached as is the M50, M3 and N3. The Phoenix Park and Farmleigh Estate are wonderful local amenities.\n\n\nEntrance Hall Entered via composite front door with dual side lights flooding the space with light. Offering an abundance of understairs storage and access to the guest wc. Complete with wood effect tiled flooring that runs through to the rear of the property.\n\nLiving Room 5.3m x 3.6m. Complete with laminate flooring and bay window.\n\nGuest WC Mosaic tiled floor, wc, and wash hand basin.\n\nOpen Plan Kitchen/Dining Room 5.8m x 5.1m. A terrific space with single wall kitchen offering ample storage space and breakfast bar separating the kitchen from dining space. Shower room accessed from this room that can be repurposed as a utility or knocked through to the guest wc. There is access to the rear garden through sliding patio doors.\n\nShower Room 1.9m x 1.4m. Mosaic tiled floor, corner shower unit and wash hand basin.\n\nBedroom 1 3.9m x 3.2m. A large double bedroom with fitted wardrobes.\n\nBedroom 2 3.7m x 2.9m. Large study with fitted wardrobes.\n\nBathroom Part tiled with wc, wash hand basin, bath/shower unit.
