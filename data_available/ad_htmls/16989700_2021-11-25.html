    McNally Handy is delighted to present Apartment 186, Skellig Michael to the market. This excellently proportioned apartment is a cosy 1 bed located on the fourth floor of Skellig Michael block, within the development of Custom House Harbour. The apartment, which gives a new owner the opportunity to really make it their own, comprises of entrance hallway with storage, this leads to an open plan sitting/dining room with a kitchen just off. This living room leads out onto a balcony where one can take in stunning water views. A double bedroom and a bathroom complete the accommodation. This truly is an excellent opportunity for first time buyers and investors.\r\n\r\nThe property's location is second to none located in the heart of Dublin City centre and the area is home to a number of commercial and recreational amenities with Henry Street located nearby providing several high street shops, several bars and restaurants, as well as numerous residential developments. There are also a number of hotels located nearby such as The Spencer, The Mayson and The Hilton Garden Inn. The location could not be more ideal and is highly accessible by public transport links including several Dublin Bus routes servicing the area, Connolly Station which provides both DART and commuter services and the LUAS Red Line.
