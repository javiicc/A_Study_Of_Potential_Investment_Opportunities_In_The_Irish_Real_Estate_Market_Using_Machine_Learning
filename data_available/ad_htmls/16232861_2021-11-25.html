    Period waterfront residential and commercial development property with an exceptional business location within the popular fishing village of Union Hall and adjacent to the opportunities that Glandore Harbour offers.\n\nThe premises is a traditional former grocery and hardware shop with residential accommodation, spacious outbuildings and a large garden. The potential for a tourist related business to include Cafe / Restaurant and holiday lets or other uses combined with a home of character in one of West Cork s most scenic and popular holiday destinations is an opportunity not to be missed.\n\nThe accommodation in this 3,000 sq ft / 275 sq mtrs (approx.) property comprises of a spacious former retail shop to the front, sitting room, kitchen / dining room and 2 large attached stores on ground floor. The upstairs includes 5 bedrooms, study and family bathroom. There is a detached garage / workshop with access via double gates from the street.\n\nThere are landscaped gardens to the rear and these offer further potential in this unique property offering.\n\nUnion Hall is one of West Cork s premier Fishing and Tourist villages. The location on the Wild Atlantic Way with the sea being just across the street offers a unique opportunity to adapt to a different lifestyle.
