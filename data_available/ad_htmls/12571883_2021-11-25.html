    This is a fabulous equestrian holding with a large detached main residence, guest cottage, stables & barn and external and internal sand arena all set into a beautiful countryside setting. The property comprises a site of c. 38 acres of mixed land offering meadows, shrub land and landscaped gardens around the houses. It offers the discerning buyer a private setting together with the convenience of being close to Bandon, Cork City and the coastal areas of west Cork.\r\n\r\nOn a total floor area of c. 240 sqm the main house consists of a bright hallway which leads to a spacious living room with an open fireplace and built-in book shelves. Double doors lead to a bright home office. The kitchen with fully fitted hardwood units extends into an open plan dining area. Off the hallway French doors lead to a beautiful covered veranda and a paved terrace area with stunning countryside views. There is a also large utility room and a wc. Upstairs, there is a large double bedroom en-suite with dressing room and a converted attic used by the present owners as a bedroom en-suite. \r\n\r\nThe cottage has a cosy living room with an open fire and a hardwood country style kitchen. On the first floor are three bedrooms, two double, one single and a family bathroom. There is also a store room/office with a separate entrance.\r\n\r\nOn the exterior, there is a private garden which has been landscaped and has mature plants established. Furthermore, there is a barn with a block of stables with four loose boxes and a foal and mare box, tack/feed room, a storage room, a wash and wc room and hay loft. There is another small stable beside the cottage. The dressage size indoor arena is custom built and there is also a separate dressage size outdoor sand arena.\r\n\r\nGo for a virtual walk of the guest house here (copy and paste into new tab): https://my.matterport.com/show/?m=z5Ard13nkSc\r\n\r\n\r\nLocation\r\nThe property is located just outside Enniskeane near Bandon which is a c. 25 minutes' drive, and Dunmanway (c. 20 minutes' drive). Cork Airport and the city can easily be reached in c. 40-45 minutes making this property an ideal option for anyone seeking the freedom and beauty of a countryside estate and yet be in easy commuting distance to Cork city.\r\n\r\nThere are plenty of tracks to be explored on horseback in the immediate surrounding and the property is close to Bandon convenient to the meets of various hunts.\r\n\r\nAll amenities are located within driving distance including schools, restaurants, and sporting facilities. This area of South West Ireland is of outstanding beauty and the dramatic coastline and safe beaches of west Cork are also close by.\r\n\r\nCork International Airport is conveniently located approx. 45 minutes drive from the property with direct links within Ireland and to major European and UK cities.\r\n\r\n\r\nDisclaimer/Privacy Policy\r\nFinest International Real Estate Ireland Ltd. t/a Engel & Voelkers act as agents for the vendors or lessors of this property whose agents they are, give notice that: \r\n1.
