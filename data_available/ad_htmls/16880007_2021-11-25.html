Located in a thriving hub of activity, this property is sure to appeal to a host of potential buyers.\n\nLocated on the fifth floor, bright and airy are two words that immediately come to mind upon entering this spacious apartment. The well-appointed accommodation briefly comprises of an entrance hall with two storage closets, fully fitted kitchen, open plan living/dining room, two large double bedrooms (one en-suite) and bathroom. The property also benefits from a balcony off the living room. A secure designated car parking space is included in the sale.\n\nThe exceptionally convenient location is within walking distance of the new DIT Grangegorman campus, NCAD, Blackhall Place and the Four Courts, while a multitude of shopping facilities are available at Capel Street, Jervis Street and Henry Street.
