    Pink Gate Cottage is a beautiful traditional cottage set on the Kerry coastline on Valentia Island. It has wonderful views out to Dingle Bay and Peninsula and the Blasket Islands. The cottage is just a short drive to Portmagee and Knightstown, the main town on the island with all amenities right on your doorstep. Valentia Island is connected to the main land by bridge and a ferry service.\r\n\r\nThe cottage has been restored and maintained to high standards and comprises of a cosy sitting room with an electric stove. Just off the sitting room is a sunroom with large windows to enjoy the views from. There is charming kitchen cum dining room with built in units and a feature raised stove. Just off the kitchen is the master bedroom en-suite. On the first floor there are two further double bedrooms, a large family bathroom with a roll top bath and a separate WC. All bathrooms have been fitted with quality sanitary ware. \r\n\r\nThe cottage sits on approx. 0.5 acres of land which is mainly laid to lawn. There are out buildings that could be potentially be restored and plenty of off street parking.\r\n\r\nLocation\r\nPink Gate Cottage located near the village of Knightstown on beautiful Valentia Island. Valentia is located off the Ring of Kerry with the village Portmagee and the towns of Caherciveen and Waterville close by. The island is connected to the main land by a bridge and a ferry service from Knighstown to Caherciveen.\r\n\r\nThe property has access to all amenities in Knightstown, Portmagee and Caherciveen town with shopping facilities, restaurants, pubs and many other facilities. \r\n\r\nValentia has an exceptional natural beauty and boasts stunning views to the Dingle Peninsula, the Blasket Islands and the Atlantic Ocean making it the ideal place for those who love to walk, hike, swim and enjoy the nature. \r\n\r\nThe immediate vicinity is also ideal for sailing, fishing or playing golf. Various championship golf courses such as Dooks and Waterville Golf Course, some of the best courses in Ireland, are close by. \r\n\r\nKillarney town is c. 1.5 hours away by car and the property is within easy reach to the airports in Cork, Shannon and Kerry which offer direct transport links within Ireland as well as to Europe and the UK.\r\n\r\n\r\nDisclaimer/Privacy Policy\r\nFinest International Real Estate Ireland Ltd. t/a Engel & Voelkers act as agents for the vendors or lessors of this property whose agents they are, give notice that: \r\n1.
