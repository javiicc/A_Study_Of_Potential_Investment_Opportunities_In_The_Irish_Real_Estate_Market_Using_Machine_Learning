    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    DF Estate Agents are pleased to offer for sale no. 7 Shanrath, Old Cratloe Road, Limerick which is an impressive large detached four bedroom property. It's location is second to none, with Thomond Park and LIT within walking distance. There are also a host of local shops, caf\xc3\xa9s and schools close by. THis property is located in a quiet well established cul de sac . The rear garden enjoys a bright and sunny south west aspect Accommodation includes, entrance hall, living room, kitchen/dining area, there is a separate utility complete with white goods. The second living room has been converted to a bedroom with its own ensuite bathroom. Upstairs are four double bedrooms, with 2 en suites and the main family bathroom. This is an excellent opportunity to acquire a beautiful family home in a much sought-after area and viewing is strongly advised and by appointment only.
