    Sitting on an elevated 0.55 Acre site with beautiful views above Clonakilty Town, this 1990's Bungalow offers an abundance of space with c.1800sqft and benefits from modern improvements to its decor, finishes and insulation. The layout accommodates 2 reception rooms, each with open fires, a west facing kitchen which opens to the large sun-room, 3 Double Bedrooms, a main bathroom, guest wc and utility room.
