    Sherry Fitzgerald Stack bring to market this picturesque and characterful 2 bed, 1 bath cottage set on 1.11 acres approx complete with outbuildings. (Option to purchase additional lands up to 16 acres available). \nConveniently located, the residence is 1km from the main N69 route, 3.5km to Moyvane village and 8.5km to the heritage town of Listowel and all amenities inc. schools, shops, pubs, restaurants etc., \nAccommodation comprises: Lounge/sitting room, kitchen/dining room, rear porch/utility, 2 double bedrooms and bathroom with w/c and whb. \nExternally there is plenty of room to refurbish and extend on the large site. Outbuildings comprise of 21sqm store/fuel shed, 30sqm outhouses and 6sqm store (attached to residence).\nThe property has mains water supply, onsite septic tank, solid fuel central heating, teak and pvc windows fitted throughout. \nWhile in need of some modernisation and refurbishment, the property provides a lot of potential for any intending purchaser. \nImage outlined is for identification purposes only. \nViewing is recommended and is by appointment only.\n\n\nEntrance Hallway 2.97m x 1.24m. with concrete floor\n\nLounge/Sitting Room 4.54m x 3.12m. with solid fuel stove, linoleum flooring, fitted units either side of fireplace & window\n\nKitchen/Dining Room 3.45m x 2.90m. fitted kitchen units, carpet flooring & window\n\nRear Porch 1.92m x 1.87m. concrete floor, half glazed door to rear & window\n\nBedroom 1 3.16m x 2.94m. double room with linoleum flooring & window\n\nBedroom 2 4.47m x 3.00m. double room with carpet flooring & 2 windows\n\nMain Bathroom 3.27m x 1.72m. with Triton T80z electric shower, ceramic tiles around shower, ceramic floor tiles, whb, w/c & window\n\nOutbuildings\n\nStore/Fuel shed 7m x 3m.\n\nOuthouses 6m x 5m.\n\nStore (attached to residence) 3m x 2m.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nEircode: V31 AV90 GPS No' s: 52.486682 -9.417882 Leaving Listowel and heading east on the N69 route, continue for 7.8km, turn right continue for 700m, property located on the right.
