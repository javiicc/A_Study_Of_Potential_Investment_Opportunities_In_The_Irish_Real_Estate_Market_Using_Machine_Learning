    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 7 ac
    The subject lands are located in the Village of Stoneyford, just off the R713 and are accessed off the L402 Norelands Road. The lands enjoys C. 70m frontage to the L4202 and are located to the rear of Main Street. This is a prime residential location in an attractive Village just 2.5 Km off the M9 and jut 14 Km from Kilkenny City. A pedestrian right of way leads out onto Main Street. Vehicular access would be off Norelands Road.\n\nZoning:\nThe subject lands comprise C. 7 Acres and a large proportion of the lands are within the Stoneyford red line settlement development boundary as set out in the 2014-2020 Development Plan for Stoneyford. This plan sets out the development strategy for the Village subject to availability and capacity of services. All purchasers should satisfy themselves of the current status of services in the area.\n\nFor further details and viewing arrangements contact Sherry FitzGerald McCreery on 05677 21904
