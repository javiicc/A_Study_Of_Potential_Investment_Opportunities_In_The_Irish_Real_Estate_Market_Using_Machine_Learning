    Site, Clonaspoe, Knockavilla, Dundrum, Co. Tipperary

    1 ac

    Site

    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 1 ac
    Circa 1 acre site (Additional Acres Available subject to price negotiation) suitable for private residence subject to planning permission. This site does have the benefit on its maps of a structure that was only demolished and removed in recent years which should help in the planning process. The site is fronted by a hedgerow with some very mature hardwood trees which would help to create an attractive entrance. The rural location offers both scenery and quiet country roads suitable for walking. The bonus of additional land offers scope to keep animals etc.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom Cashel travel to Knockvilla on the R505. When in the village take the right turn beside the Church. Trave down this road approx 1km and the site is on the left hand side of the road.
