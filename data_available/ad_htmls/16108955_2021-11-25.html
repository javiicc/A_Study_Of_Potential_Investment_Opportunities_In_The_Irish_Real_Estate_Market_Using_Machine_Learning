    We are delighted to bring this stunning detached house to the market. The property comprises a detached four bedroom house of c.2,000 sq.ft, with a double garage and a single garage on a site of 1.65 acres. \r\n\r\nThe ground floor comprise a large bright entrance hall with double doors opening on to a large living room with a oil fuel stove. There is a large kitchen which opens on to a south-west facing patio. There is a "back door" with a utility room and downstairs toilet off the kitchen. Off the hall there is a further downstairs bedroom and bathroom. A large double garage with an electric roller shutter door can be accessed off the entrance hall.\r\n\r\nThe first floor comprises three double bedrooms including one ensuite and a main bathroom. There is scope to add a further ensuite to bedroom 3.\r\n\r\nOutside there is a further single garage, a patio and approximately 1.5 acres of well maintained ground. \r\n\r\nThe property and grounds are in excellent condition offering an incoming buyer a turnkey home. \r\n\r\nThe site offers very obvious potential for the development of a second house in the grounds subject to planning permission.
