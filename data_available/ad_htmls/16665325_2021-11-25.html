    Baxter Real Estate are delighted to present to the market, this wonderful opportunity to acquire this 3-bedroom/3-bathroom (originally a 4-bed), semi-detached family home. It occupies an area of approximately 108 sq.m./1,162 sq.ft., and is ideally located just off Auburn Avenue, in a mature, much sought-after residential area. On entering this home, you are greeted with a living room, which has a feature white marble open fireplace, laminate flooring and French doors which open to the open-plan kitchen/diner/sunroom. The dining area and sunroom both have laminate flooring, whilst the kitchen has a tiled floor and a tiled splashback. The kitchen also features more than ample wall and floor storage units. The sunroom opens to a sunny, south-facing rear garden. This garden, which is not overlooked, is fully paved and features a raised flower bed, garden shed, and side access. A fully-tiled guest w.c. completes this level. At first floor level, there are 3 bedrooms. Two of the bedrooms have built-in storage and wooden floors. The master bedroom has been newly carpeted and has extra-large built-in wardrobes. The master bedroom is further enhanced by having an en-suite bathroom, with tiled floor and partially-tiled walls. A fully-tiled family bathroom, with electric sower, completes the accommodation. This home has gas-fired central heating, double-glazed windows and off-street parking to the front.
