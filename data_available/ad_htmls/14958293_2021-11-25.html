    1 Church View is a very well presented three bedroom semi detached house with adjoining one bedroom granny flat / house located in the south east of Meath in Garristown. \r\n\r\nThis subject house is part of a small residential development and occupies a prominent position at the front of the estate, within walking distance of St Vincent's GAA club and St Patricks National School. The house is well positioned and has been extended to the rear to add additional accommodation in the form of a one bedroom granny flat / apartment. There is currently a portable home to the side of the property which will be moving with the current owners.\r\n\r\nThe house is in walk in condition for a new owner with the ground floor comprising of a large sitting room, laundry room and kitchen. The first floor includes two double bedrooms, a main bathroom and one further single bedroom. The granny flat / apartment provides a kitchen, bathroom, second living room and a bedroom all on the ground floor The driveway has a tarmacadam finish to the front of the house which provides ample parking.\r\n\r\n1 Church View is located just 12km from Ashbourne, 24km from Navan, 17km from Balbriggan and 16km from the town of Drogheda . \r\n\r\nViewings will be held by appointment at the appropriate time.
