    Exceptional well maintained beautiful Traditional Irish Thatched Cottage. \r\nThe House is on a 1 acre plot with an additional 2 acre adjacent field with stone wall boundary.\r\n(acreage is approximate)\r\n\r\nThe Cottage downstairs comprises of an entrance hall, large sitting room featuring an original stone arch fireplace with oil back boiler stove, kitchen fitted with solid Oak units, a large family bathroom with electric shower and bath, utility room/office (this could be converted to a single bedroom), a double size bedroom with featured limestone wall and solid fuel stove. \r\nUpstairs there are 2 double size bedrooms and a single bedroom, there is also ample storage space in the walk in attic. \r\nThere are 2 large sheds to the rear of the house with a smaller shed for solid fuel etc... \r\nThe cottage garden has a covered patio area and the garden is well maintained.\r\n\r\nThis property is ideally located and although surrounded by country side it is just 4 miles to Eyre Square the centre of Galway city by either the N83 or N84, it is a further 2 miles to Salthill and the beautiful Galway Bay. \r\n10 minute drive to Lough Corrib, famous for it's Salmon and Trout fishing, and just 1 mile to the world famous Galway race Course. \r\nBriarhill Shopping Centre is 5 minutes by car and the M6 motorway is 6 minute drive. \r\nBoth Shannon and Knock airports are just over an hour away and it is a 2 hour drive to Dublin via motorway. \r\nThe village Of Claregalway is 3 miles north with Shops, Restaurants, Hotels, both National and Secondary Schools, Church, Post Office and more.\r\nThe property is situated just off the N83 and within 5 minutes walking distance to Mc Hughe's award winning Traditional pub and restaurant and a 10 minute walk to Cloonacauneen Castle, pub, restaurant and events venue.. \r\nThe cottage was last thatched in August 2018. The windows and doors are all double glazed PVC. There has been many additional upgrades over the years of oil fired central heating, fitted kitchen, new windows and doors, floors etc.... \r\nThis is one of the few Original Thatched Cottages left in the vicinity of Galway city. \r\nThe property would make an ideal Family home (with room for a pony), Holiday home or Holiday let. \r\nIt has been owner occupied since 1994. \r\nThis really is a must see property.
