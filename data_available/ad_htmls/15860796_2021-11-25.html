    Frank Sweeney & Co. are offering for sale this impressive luxurious detached property at Derrylissane, Menlough, Ballinasloe, Co. Galway. The property is located minutes from the village of Menlough and 5 minutes from Monivea village.\r\n\r\nThe property is fronted by an impressive stone wall gated entrance with high pillars and metal gates. The setback area has a tarmacadam finish and there is a pedestrian side gate. A long tarmacadam driveway leads up to the house and surrounds the property. Along the driveway there are natural stone bounded borders with flowers. A 3 bay garage allows for significant storage. Lawns to the front and rear, mature laurel hedging and a natural stone pond and fountain make an impressive site in excess of 1 acre.\r\n\r\nThe house is entered via a large welcoming hallway with coving, solid oak stairs and recessed lights. Off the hallway is the sitting room with its insert solid fuel stove and dual aspect windows. There is an excellent open plan living space incorporating the kitchen/dining, living and sun room. These rooms flow naturally in to each other and provide an excellent place for family and friends to gather. The kitchen has roof light over the dining area and the living room has a second solid fuel stove. The sun room has french doors leading to the rear garden. From the kitchen it leads to a large utility with a fully tiled w.c and then a back hallway leads to a fully tiled downstairs bathroom/wetroom next to a downstairs bedroom. This would make an excellent guest bedroom away from the hustle and bustle of the house or for an elderly relative. There is one further large bedroom downstairs just off the main hallway.\r\n\r\nUpstairs there is an extensive landing area that can serve as den/playroom/office/study. All 3 of the bedrooms here are en-suite and carpeted. The master bedroom is like a hotel suite with a large bedroom area with fitted wardrobes, a separate dressing area leading to a balcony via French doors, a walk in wardrobe and and en-suite.
