    McNally Handy are delighted to present 1 Brookvale, an exceptional 2-bed end of terrace house 70.1sqm with an additional 13.83sqm of converted attic space. Its location is extremely desirable location just off Eglinton Road, within easy access to Donnybrook, Dublin City Centre and the QBC at the end of the road which gives direct access to Dublin City Centre. There are views over the River Dodder to the front of the property and access pedestrian walkway giving easy access to Clonskeagh Road.\r\n\r\nOn entering the property, the welcoming hallway leads into the fully fitted kitchen with dining area and views over the front garden further along the hallway is the living room which had an abundance of natural light along with access to the sunny rear garden. To complete the ground floor accommodation there is a guest wc. \r\n\r\nOn the first-floor level there are 2 bedrooms with the master ensuite and a family bathroom. \r\n \r\nThe largely north west facing rear garden is laid out in lawn with mature shrubbery in the perimeter bedding along with a patio area which is ideal for relaxing summer evenings. To the front of the property is off street parking via a gravel driveway. The house is heated via gas fired central heating. \r\n\r\nThe property could be extended to the rear SPP to incorporate extra living accommodation.\r\n\r\nLocation \r\n1 Brookvale is convenient to many South Dublin locations, such as the villages of Donnybrook, Ballsbridge, Ranelagh, where there are a host of coffee shops, bars, restaurants and shops to be found. The Dublin City Centre is easily accessible from the property along with access to the N11. Local recreational amenities include close by include Donnybrook Lawn Tennis Club and Energia Park, Herbert Park and Elm Park Golf Club.\r\n\r\nSome of the finest primary and secondary schools can be found in the area to include Muckross Park College, St Conleth\\'s College, The Teresian School and St Michaels, along with third level options such as UCD. The public transport options are plentiful offering easy access to the city centre and surrounding environs.
