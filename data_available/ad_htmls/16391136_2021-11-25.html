    Located on Old Golf Links Road, Tralee, this property is in a prime location, just off the Oakpark Road and c.10 minutes walk to the town centre. The property is located close to all public amenities, with Tralee Sports Complex, Tralee rail and bus station, Kingdom Greyhound Stadium all within walking distance and a new medical centre being developed by Leahy's Pharmacy only 100mtrs from the property. There are numerous local schools, churches, restaurants/cafes/delis, convenience stores and supermarkets all in close proximity.\n\nThe property has the benefit of electric gates and off-street parking to the front and a large south-facing rear garden. The garden is well stocked and mature, the vendor has a private dog run and kennel built in the garden, a covered clothes drying area and a garden shed. The oil tank is enclosed in a separate secure shed. There are two side gates to the property with access to the garden. \nFibre optic broadband is available to the property.\n\nEntrance Hall: 5'3" (1.6m) x 11'10" (3.61m) uPVC front door, tiled floor, double radiator, storage cupboards\n\nSitting room: 11'2" (3.4m) x 10'6" (3.2m) Semi-solid wood floor, wood-burning stove in fireplace with tiled mantelpiece and surround, wired for TV, radiator\n\nDining/sitting room: 15'1" (4.6m) x 14'2" (4.32m) Solid wood floor, oil-burning stove in fireplace with marble surround, hot press, wired for TV, patio doors to garden\n\nKitchen: 7'11" (2.41m) x 11'6" (3.51m) Tiled floor, fitted timber eye and floor level units, electric oven/hob with extractor hood, double-height ceiling with skylight and mezzanine, free-standing fridge-freezer\n\nUtility room: 8'3" (2.51m) x 7'3" (2.21m) Tiled floor, oil-fired boiler, eye-level units, storage cupboard, door to outside, shower room\n\nShower room: 4'7" (1.4m) x 4'7" (1.4m) Tiled floor, electric shower, WC/WHB\n\nMaster bedroom: 14'2" (4.32m) x 10'10" (3.3m) Wood-effect linoleum, fitted wardrobes, double radiator, wired for TV\n\nBedroom 2: 9'2" (2.79m) x 8'2" (2.49m) Carpet, radiator\n\nBedroom 3: 11'10" (3.61m) x 9'10" (3m) Carpet, radiator\n\nBathroom: 11'2" (3.4m) x 6'7" (2.01m) Tiled floor, fully tiled walls, corner bath, electric shower in cubicle, WC/WHB, access to attic\n\nDirections:\nFrom the Oakpark road, take the exit for the Sports Complex, take the first left and then the first right and the property is the second last on the right.
