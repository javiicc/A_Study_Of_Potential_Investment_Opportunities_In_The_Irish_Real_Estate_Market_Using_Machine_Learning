    Harkin & Associates are delighted to bring new to the Autumn market this most unique and interesting family home. This home is possibly one of the most individualistic and traditional properties to come to the market in recent years. It has been meticulously restored, extended and maintained by its current owner occupiers. Attention to detail and thought given to the materials used are testament to their superb understanding of the property. \r\n\r\nLocated on the periphery of Blarney Castle Estate and surrounded by mature native woodlands, which offers incredible privacy, yet the convenience of living on the 215 Bus Route, directly outside your door.\r\n\r\nConsisting of two stunning living rooms with solid fuel stove and wooden beams to the ceilings, a spacious kitchen/dining room with a large island with lots of seating and utility room off. \r\n\r\nUpstairs the master suite enjoys full views of the surrounding woodlands and has a walk-in wardrobe and full jacuzzi bathroom ensuite. The two other double bedrooms have their own ensuites, ideal for teenage children. \r\n\r\nOutside the bright South Westerly patio area is a stunning oasis of light and nature. There is a traditional gravel driveway with an abundance of parking and turning space.\r\n\r\nPlease see floor plans for room dimensions and layout.\r\n\r\nViewing of this home comes highly recommended to the discerning buyer looking for something special. Please reply via Daft.ie to arrange your own private viewing.
