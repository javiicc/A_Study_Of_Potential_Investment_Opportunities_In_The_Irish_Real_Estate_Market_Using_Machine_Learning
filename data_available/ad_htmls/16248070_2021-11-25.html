    No.104 Lower Drumcondra Road is a very fine period residence that extends to a substantial c.285sqm/ 3068 sq ft of gracious and character-filled proportions. This handsome red-brick residence with double-height bay window dates from 1872 and provides elegant living accommodation with classically generous rooms and exceptional floor to ceiling height. Currently in use as B&B, the property has been very well-maintained over the years and has retained many of the original period features. \r\n\r\nOn entering the main residence you are greeted by a most impressive entrance hall which exudes character and ambiance. It retains the original tiled floor, decorative plasterwork and staircase. In to the right are two very elegant interconnecting reception rooms with original marble fireplaces and ornate ceiling cornicing. The front facing reception room features a wide bay while the diningroom has a set of French doors leading out to the rear garden. The ground floor return has been extended and now incorporates a large open plan kitchen cum family room along with a utility room and quest toilet. Upstairs, comprises of eight bedrooms (six ensuite) arranged over the upper floors. The ensuites are all well-appointed with modern sanitary ware and tiling. The front facing master bedroom which spans the full width of the house is particularly impressive and features a tall wide bay window, ornate plaster ceiling cornice and marble fireplace. \r\n\r\nOutside, the rear garden benefits from a west facing orienation and incorporates a sizable garage that extends to c.50m2/538 sqft. The garage provides secure and convenient off-street parking in addition to a mezzanine floor and storage. The front garden has alot of kerbside appeal with granite capped red brick pillars, cast- iron railings and chequered tiled pedestian path. \r\n\r\nLower Drumcondra Road itself is a mature and highly regarded address within comfortable walking distance of O\\\\\\'Connell Street. The area is host to an abundance of shops, restaurants and delis. A selection of primary and secondary schools are all within easy reach as are St. Patricks College, DIT Bolton Street and Trinity College. There are public parks and numerous sporting and recreational amenities in the immediate locality. The area is well served by public transport with numerous bus routes passing by and a bus stop located immediately outside.
