    Welcome to 21 Poynton Place, a well-appointed 3 bedroom semi-detached home situated in a small development close to Ballivor village centre. \n\nOn entering the home there is an airy hallway with tiled flooring, carpeted stairs and a generously sized guest wc. The sitting room is the first room off the hallway complete with laminate flooring, fireplace with insert solid fuel stove and bay window. To the rear of the house is a large kitchen/dining room with tiled flooring and splash back, fitted wall and base units and French doors to the back garden.\n\nUpstairs 3 good sized bedrooms are off the carpeted landing. All bedrooms have laminate flooring. The master bedroom has fitted wardrobes and a fully tiled en-suite with electric shower. The family bathroom is also fully tiled.\n\nThe south facing back garden is well maintained and mostly in lawn with a gravel garden path leading to a steel shed. The front of home has a modern, red brick finish and there is lots of space for private off street parking.\n\nBallivor is a quaint village with lots of local amenities and services, all within walking distance of the property. The larger towns of Trim and Kinnegad are approx. 15 minutes drive. Dublin city centre is approx. 40 minutes. \n\nThis spacious home will be an ideal starter home or perfect for someone seeking to downsize and we highly recommend viewing.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 5.838m x 1.829m.
