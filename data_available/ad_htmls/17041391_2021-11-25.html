    No. 9 Glasnevin Oaks is an attractive and spacious two bedroom, mid terrace house located in a small enclave of only 10 houses. \nExtending to 64 Sq M the property is thoughtfully laid out with an easy flow and well-proportioned rooms. The accommodation comprises of an entrance hall, large living room, kitchen and dining room with access to large understairs storage. Upstairs are 2 double bedrooms one with a shower room and main bathroom. The south facing rear garden is enclosed and is partly laid in cobble lock. A pedestrian gate leads to the private parking which is also accessed via an electric gate. \nGlasnevin Oaks is a popular development for first time buyers, investors or someone looking to trade down. The property is set close to Drumcondra and Phibsboro close to a host of local amenities, including schools, The Botanic Gardens, DCU, The Bon Secures Hospital and is within easy reach of the City Centre, the airport and Dublin s main artery routes- the M1 and the M50. \n\n\nEntrance Hall 1.65m x 1.56m. with wooden flooring and telephone point.\n\nLiving Room 4.55m x 3.60m. large room with wooden flooring, electric fire with granite and wooden surround.\n\nDining / Kitchen 3.50m x 2.90m. with wooden flooring, plumbed for a washing machine and dishwasher. Large walk in under stairs storage.\n\nBedroom 1 3.80m x 2.88m. double bedroom, located to the rear of the property with mirrored built in slide robes and shower room.\n\nEnsuite 1.78m x 0.88m. fully tiled with whb and shower\n\nBedroom 2 3.60m x 2.97m. double bedroom located to the front of the property with wooden flooring and built in wardrobes.\n\nBathroom 1.44m x 2.60m. recently fitted bathroom that is fully tiled, wc, whb and bath with shower.\n\nHot Press
