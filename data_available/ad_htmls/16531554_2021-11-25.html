    An absolutely charming & beautiful cottage residence which has been refurbished & renovated to a very high standard and is presented to the market in top class condition offering a captivating atmosphere throughout. The property features a very nice layout comprising 3 bedrooms, bright accommodation very tastefully decorated with a neutral colour scheme throughout. It sits on a c.0.42 acre site with private mature gardens offering a peaceful tranquil setting, south facing aspect, driveway and enjoys scenic countryside views. It is located in one of the most sought after residential areas of Wexford and is close to local shops, schools, churches etc. at Barntown Village. Wexford town centre is a short driving distance and the N/M11 is within easy access making Dublin City and airport commutable within 2 hours. The N25 is also very easily connected and the property is close to gorgeous walks at Fort mountain and Carrigfoyle, also numerous beaches are within easy driving distance. The sale of this property offers a fantastic opportunity to acquire a fabulous country cottage in a beautiful setting enjoying all of the advantages of country life yet minutes from all amenities.
