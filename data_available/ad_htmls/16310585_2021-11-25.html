    Excellent location 1 mile from the scenic village of Inistioge with stunning views across the Nore Valley to Woodstock, this property sits on a 1.6 acre sloping site. Situated in a quite rural setting, just above the Inistioge / New Ross Road, the property is accessed from a minor roadway. The village of Inistioge caters for general day to day needs, with Kilkenny and Waterford within easy striking distance. The area caters well for the outdoor enthusiast with Woodstock Gardens close by with its forest walks and the River Nore which is renowned for its fishing and boating. There are also a number of Golf coursed close at hand, the most notable being Mount Juliet Country House Hotel and riding center.\r\n\r\nAccommodation comprises: Kitchen, Sitting Room, Dining Room, Family Bathroom\r\nLarge Main bedroom with en suite (spa bath, large shower cubicle), 2 double bedrooms one with en suite shower room and a further\r\nsmaller double bedroom.\r\nGym / Office and a Garden / utility room\r\nCirca 900 sqft attic area suitable for conversion to bedrooms, office or studio use.\r\nOil fired central heating, Septic Tank, Mains Water and Eir Fibre Broadband\r\nGarage, large wooden shed and brick built fuel shed\r\nSite area 1.6 acres which includes fruit trees, glass house and large timber constructed and covered BBQ area
