    Knockmount is a superior 6 Bedroom all brick finish period style two storey detached family home built in 1906 extending to Approx. 390m /4,200ft and set on spectacular mature grounds located along the exclusive Dublin Road on the south side of Drogheda. The former Church of Ireland rectory which has been extended over the years is full of character and charm with its protruding bay windows, high ceilings, decorative cornicing and ornate fireplaces. The accommodation is cleverly positioned to ensure all rooms benefit from a great sun aspect to enjoy bright and pleasant living throughout the year. All formal reception rooms and bedrooms overlook the carefully created gardens that display beautiful all year round colours that also includes a tranquil south-west facing enclosed patio area to be enjoyed in all seasons. Set on an expansive site of 1.8 acres with trees over a hundred years old also has possible future development potential to sub-divide into multiple plots subject to planning permission or sit back and enjoy your own private haven in a town centre location. Location of this superb property is perfect for any family with its close proximity to good local schools and all necessary local amenities with convenience store, coffee shop, bar and restaurants all on your doorstep. Drogheda train station is a stone s throw away for the Dublin commuter while the golf enthusiast is also catered for with a selection of well renowned links courses all within a 6km radius. Drogheda town centre is only 1km away and access to the M1 motorway via Junction 8 is a mere 3.8km making Dublin Airport and the M50 orbital motorway both accessible within 25 minutes. This convenient property with endless potential is undoubtedly one of the best opportunities to come to the North East market in recent times and surely not one to be missed.
