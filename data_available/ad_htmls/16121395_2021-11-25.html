    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 3 ac
    CBPM Real Estate introduces to the market this once off site of 3 Acres situated in Ballinakill, Co. Laois. The land has the extra benefit of road frontage. The site is surrounded with tranquil views of The Masslough, and Gills Pond. Its Location is only seconds from the open swimming pool with a new playground in progress and the famous Heywood Gardens with its breath-taking views and heritage woodland walks. The land is of prime central location to all amenities with a primary school and secondary school only minutes away. Ballinakill (Irish: Baile na Coille, meaning 'the town of the woods') is a small village in County Laois, Ireland on the R432 regional road between Abbeyleix, Ballyragget and Castlecomer, County Kilkenny. As of the 2016 census, there were 445 people living in Ballinakill. Centrally located to all major towns as in Portlaoise, Kilkenny and Carlow. Excellent accessibility adjacent to the M7 Motorway. The sale of this property offers the discerning purchaser a once in a lifetime opportunity to acquire a substantial parcel of land to design your dream home with extensive grounds in Ballinakill, or for the Investor, the potential for a substantial residential development, subject to planning. (Co. Laois). Mains Sewage and Water.
