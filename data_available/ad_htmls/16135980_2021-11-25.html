    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 0.5 ac
    DNG Martin O'Connor are delighted to present to market this fine site of c. half an acre located along the Wild Atlantic Way in the picturesque coastal village of Kilkieran (Cill Chiaran). The elevated site enjoys fantastic panoramic views over Kilkieran bay as well as uninterrupted views of the hills & surrounding countryside.\r\n\r\nPlanning Permission was previously granted on this site (Planning Ref: 96/258) for the development of a dwelling house and associated works (Planning has since lapsed). This site offers excellent potential to develop a lovely property along one of the most unspoiled coastlines in the West of Ireland.\r\n\r\nThe site is conveniently located in the heart of Kilkieran village and within walking distance to all local amenities including a shop, post office, National school, playground, AstroTurf pitch, gastropub/restaurant, health centre, among other facilities.\r\n\r\nKilkieran pier is also located a short stroll from the site- a place to moor or launch a boat. Further along this road there is a choice of wonderful beaches and safe swimming areas, including one of the West of Ireland's sandy beaches 'An Tra Mor', also known locally as Callowfinish beach.\r\n\r\nRugged coastlines surround the village of Kilkieran which offers wonderful coastal views and sandy shores. The quaint village of Carna is just 6 miles away in the centre of the Irish speaking Gaeltacht where there are many more stunning seascapes and secluded beaches to explore. Clifden is located 27 miles and Galway City 40 miles from the property.\r\n\r\nFor further information/maps or to arrange an inspection, contact the sole selling agent DNG Martin O'Connor on 091 866708.\r\n\r\nFeatures\r\nFine site of c. Half an Acre\r\nPlanning Permission Previously granted (Planning Ref 96/258 - Since Lapsed)\r\nUninterrupted sea and hill views\r\nLocated along the Wild Atlantic Way\r\nWalking distance to village and quay\r\nOutstanding beaches nearby including that of 'An Tra Mor', known locally as Finish beach\r\nLocal amenities within walking distance including Shops, Post Office, Health Centre & Pubs, & AstroTurf pitch\r\n1 minute drive to Kilkieran National School & local playground\r\n\r\nBER Details\r\nBER: Exempt\r\n\r\nDirections\r\nTaking the N59 Galway to Clifden road, continue through Moycullen, Oughterard and on to Maam Cross. Taking a left at Peacockes Hotel, continue on the R336, through Screebe, and onto the R340 road for 25 minutes (26km) to Kilkieran village. At the Junction in Kilkieran village, take a left, staying on the R340. Continue on this road for 50 metres, the site is located on the right hand side, just after Kilkieran Health Centre, identified by the DNG Martin O'Connor For Sale sign.\r\n\r\nLocation\r\nThe area of Kilkieran is host to a number of amenities including a health centre, a pub and bistro, the Arramara Teo seafood factory, a pier, a supermarket, a community hall and fish factory. There is also a playground and astroturf pitch in the village.\r\n\r\nThe quaint village of Carna is just 9 miles away in the centre of the Irish speaking Gaeltacht where there are many more stunning seascapes and secluded beaches to explore. Clifden is located 27 miles and Galway City 40 miles.
