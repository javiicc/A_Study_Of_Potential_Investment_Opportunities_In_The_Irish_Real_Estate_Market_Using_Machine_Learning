    Number 67 Garville Avenue is a most attractive double-fronted redbrick family home of substantial proportions. The property benefits from a stunning and private south facing rear garden and is ideally positioned on this quiet and mature residential road in the heart of Dublin 6. The location of this impressive property is second to none in terms of convenience with the villages of Rathgar, Terenure and Rathmines only a gentle stroll away. It is no surprise that this charming Victorian property has remained in the same family for almost 70 years.\r\n\r\nPerfectly positioned on Garville Avenue, No. 67 is laid out over two storeys, offering well-appointed and versatile accommodation which extends to 205 sq.m. / 2,200 sq.ft. approx., retaining many period features synonymous with the Victorian era. The elegant and inviting entrance hall sets the tone with its original fan light, ornate cornicing, picture rail and solid wood flooring. The impressive and light filled double reception room is awash with period details to include; original marble fireplace, solid wood flooring, two sash windows with shutters, ornate cornicing and a picture rail. The office / study and the dining room are both located off the entrance hall. Features of these rooms include; two original fireplaces, solid wood flooring, sash window with shutters, coving and a picture rail. The functional kitchen / breakfast room is situated to the rear of the property and overlooks the garden. A shower room completes the ground floor accommodation. Upstairs does not disappoint, steeped in period features and benefitting from a double bedroom, family bathroom and seperate w/c on the return level. The first floor has three double bedrooms, a large and bright dressing room / study (which was previously a fifth bedroom but has been changed inline with the PP for next door) and a shower room.\r\n\r\nA description of this property would not be complete without mentioning the stunning gardens. To the front a pathway bordered by mature trees and shrubbery leads to the hall door. To the rear you will find a superbly private and sunny south facing garden, laid in lawn, with a patio area, mature shrubbery, flower beds and trees. There is also the provision of ample outside storage areas.\r\n\r\nThe convenience of this location cannot be overstated. There is an excellent range of well-established primary and secondary schools all within easy access. The renaissance of Rathgar, Terenure, Rathmines and Dartry provides a choice of trendy restaurants and local shops. Whilst Sport clubs nearby include Brookfield & Rathgar Tennis Clubs, LA Fitness, Terenure Sports Club, Leinster Cricket Club and Rathmines swimming pool. The area is well served by excellent bus routes to and from the city centre and is approximately 3 miles to St Stephens Green. All in all, this presents a wonderful opportunity to acquire a unique home of style and distinction, on one of Dublin 6 s most sought after roads.\r\n\r\n\r\nEntrance Hall 1.45m x 6.35m.
