    BARRY AUCTIONEERS & VALUERS are delighted to offer for sale this fantastic 2 bedroom apartment with amazing water views located in Mariners Quay, Passage West. Offering an abundance of space and well laid out accommodation, this modern apartment offers the discerning purchaser comfortable open plan living accommodation, spacious rooms and parking space within the confines of this private gated development overlooking the water. The apartment is maintained to a high standard and benefits from this most convenient location. Passage West, Glenbrook and Monkstown are popular residential areas not just because of their unique setting. The cross-river ferry from Glenbrook to Carrigaloe provides easy access to Great Island and East Cork. It is only a 10 minute drive to the ferry terminal at Ringaskiddy, from where boats depart regularly for France. A regular bus service links Passage West/Monkstown to Cork City, whilst the Cobh train to Cork City stops at Carrigaloe, on the other side of the West Channel. So living in Passage West, offers small town quality of life with big town conveniences. The location of Passage West and neighbouring towns Glenbrook and Monkstown have ancient maritime links. All three have historically been seafaring towns and the relics of the past remain to be enjoyed. Passage West, Glenbrook and Monkstown are three sister towns on the western shores of Cork Harbour.
