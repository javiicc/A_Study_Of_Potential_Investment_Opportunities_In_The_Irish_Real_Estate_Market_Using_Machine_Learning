    The property is accessed through an imposing recessed entrance onto a mature tree lined avenue and gardens leading to and surrounding an elegant Edwardian red brick house dating to 1910, or thereabouts. In 2 traditional blocks, it provides 10 loose boxes to the front of a road fronted railed paddock.
