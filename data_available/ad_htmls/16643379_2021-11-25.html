    Baxter Real Estate are delighted to present to the market, this own-door, ground-floor apartment, which occupies an area of approximately 44 sq.m./476 sq.ft.. The accommodation is briefly comprised of an entrance hall, an open-plan living/dining room and kitchen, a bedroom and a bathroom. The entrance hall, living/dining room and bedroom all feature laminate flooring. The kitchen area has wall and floor storage units, a tiled floor and tiled splashback. The bedroom features built-in wardrobes. A fully-tiled bathroom, and a small store room, complete the accommodation. This home has double-glazed windows and energy-efficient electric heating.
