    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 80 m\xc2\xb2
    Brophy Cusack Real Estate Team are delighted to bring this three bedroom detached property to the market. The ground floor consists of an kitchen, garage, sitting room, bedroom & a bathroom. Rising to the first floor we have two well proportioned bedrooms & the main bathroom. The property also benefits from oil fired central heating, septic tank & mains water. The house itself sits on a circa 0.3 acre site which offers huge potential to be redeveloped subject to the relevant planning permission being obtained. The property is located just two minutes drive from from the South Kilkenny village of Mooncoin & ten minutes drive to Waterford City. The village of Mooncoin offers a large variety of amenities including both primary & secondary schools, GAA club, hairdressers, convenience stores, public houses & a fast food outlet. For further information or to arrange a viewing please contact sole selling agents Brophy Cusack on 051 511333.
