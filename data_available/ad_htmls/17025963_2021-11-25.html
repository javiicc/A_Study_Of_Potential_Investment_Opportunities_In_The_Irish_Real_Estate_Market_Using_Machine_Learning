    No.2 Station Road is a superb light filled spacious 4 bedroom plus study, three-story semi-detached home, located in the sought after area of Hansfield, spread over 153 sq m (1,647).\r\n\r\nThose seeking a modern energy efficient home need look no further, with a BER rating of A3 the property was constructed in 2016 by the well renowned developer McGarrell Reilly. The property is generously proportioned with generous living space and offers excellent off-street parking to the rear and very good sized sunny West facing rear garden with patio area. Other features include high-performance uPvc double glazed windows, zoned gas central heating with modern A-rated boiler and PV solar panels to reduce heating costs. \r\n\r\nInternally, the property comprises of entrance hallway with wooden flooring, guest W/C and utility room, large living room with double doors leading to the rear garden, large kitchen/dining room with storeroom, modern wall and countertop units and access to the rear garden. The first floor comprises of master bedroom with en-suite, a second double bedroom with fitted wardrobes and a separate study room. On the second floor there are two double bedrooms, family bathroom and storeroom.\r\n \r\nLocated next to Hansfield train station and with excellent road and bus connections, No.2 Station Road is perfect for an easy commute into Dublin City Centre. Surrounded by a superb choice of schools, parks, shopping, sporting and recreation facilities, these homes offer an ideal location designed around family life.\r\n\r\nHansfield's position on the Dunboyne commuter train line sees it served by trains to both Docklands and Connolly Stations daily. The LUAS station at Broombridge is only a short journey from Hansfield by train and the connecting LUAS Green Line is an excellent transport link for commuters travelling into the heart of Dublin City Centre and beyond. \r\n\r\nHansfield is also served by the Quality Bus Corridor that facilitates the 39 and 39a Dublin Bus routes through Dublin City Centre direct to UCD. Hansfield is only a short distance south of the M3 Motorway and access to the M50 Motorway is via the Blanchardstown interchange, which is only minutes drive away. Ongar Village is only a short stroll away and offers a variety of local shopping facilities, caf\xc3\xa9s and restaurants. The development also benefits from the nearby Blanchardstown Centre which offers a unique shopping and leisure experience.\r\n\r\nSome of the many fine attractions close to Hansfield include Phoenix Park, the National Aquatic Centre and St Catherine Park offering acres of woodland, nature walks, playgrounds and sporting facilities. The recently opened Hansfield Educate Together National and Secondary Schools are both located on Barnwell Road, adjacent to Barnwell Park, offering state of the art facilities for students. Castaheany Educate Together National School and St. Benedict Primary School are also right beside Barnwell Park, providing an excellent choice of schools within walking distance from your new home. Castleknock College, Mount Sackville Secondary School and the Institute of Technology Blanchardstown are also close by.\r\n\r\n\r\nViewing Details\r\nStrictly by appointment only with sole selling agent Kelly Walsh. Please contact Robert Wall in the Kelly Walsh office (01) 664 5500.
