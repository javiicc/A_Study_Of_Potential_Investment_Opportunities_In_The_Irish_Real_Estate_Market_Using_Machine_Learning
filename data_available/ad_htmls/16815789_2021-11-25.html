    Baxter Real Estate are delighted to present to the market, this wonderful opportunity to acquire a 3-bedroom/3-bathroom, semi-detached family home. It occupies an area of approximately 102 sq.m/1,098 sq.ft., and is located in a much sought-after residential area. On entering this home, you are greeted with the entrance hall, which has laminate flooring and built-in understair storage. To the right is the living room, which has a bay window, a feature electric fire, and laminate flooring. French doors lead from the living room to the open-plan kitchen and dining room, with laminate flooring. The kitchen area has more than ample, upgraded, floor and wall units and a tiled splashback. There is also a water filtration system in place. From the dining area, sliding patio doors provide access to the rear garden. This well-maintained garden features a large patio area, side access, and is not overlooked. A utility room and a guest w.c. complete the ground-floor. At first-floor level, there are three bedrooms, all of which have carpeted floors. Whilst two of the bedrooms have built-in wardrobes, the master bedroom benefits from having a walk-in wardrobe with carpeted floor. The master bedroom is further enhanced by having an en-suite bathroom, with tiled floor and a tiled, corner waterfall shower. A family bathroom, with tiled floor and tiled bath surround, services the other two bedrooms. This home has gas-fired central heating and double-glazed PVC windows.
