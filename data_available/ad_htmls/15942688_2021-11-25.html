    Where attention to detail and quality of finish is exceptional...\n\nNever before has such a premium opportunity to purchase a newly built three-bedroom own door ground floor apartment in the heart of Mount Merrion with a private south facing garden arisen. Representing the highest standard in luxury living and extending to a generous 155 sq m/1,668 sq ft this unique apartment is sure to appeal to even the most discerning of purchasers. \n\nLocated in an exclusive enclave with contemporary finishes both inside and out together with bright airy accommodation the property is sure to appeal to a wide spectrum of buyers. With wood floors throughout, internally the impressive, stylish accommodation boasts three generous bedrooms. The master bedroom have a walk in ward robe which leads to a exceptionally large en-suite. The stunning open plan kitchen/dining room to the rear spanning the width of the property features a quartz island, a superb Karl Cullen hardwood and in frame kitchen with integrated appliances and a useful utility room and additional separate pantry off. A large panoramic slider offers access to the winter garden - perfectly positioned to maximise the sunny orientation and long lazy summer evening sunshine, while offering access to the manicured and landscaped south facing rear garden. There are two parking spaces to the front. \n\nSituated in the heart of Mount Merrion the development is within close proximity of many neighbourhood shops including Supervalu, Ryan' s Pharmacy, The Food Shop and other local retail outlets all just a gentle stroll away. There are a host of other recreational and leisure amenities including the beautiful Deer Park within easy walking distance. Notably Dundrum Town Centre, Dublin' s premier shopping venue and home to leading and designer brands and stylish stores, is a short distance away.\n\nAbove all this home offers a unique opportunity to become part of a closely knit community that defines Mount Merrion as a special place to live and love...\nACCOMMODATION\n3 Bedroom Ground Floor Apartment - 154 sq m / 1,665 sq ft
