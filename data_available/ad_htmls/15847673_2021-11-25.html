    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    Stunning coastal, 5 bedroom residence on 0.6 acre approximately, enjoying an exceptional setting with superb coastal views of the entrance to Glandore Harbour.\n\nThe house is presented to an exceptional standard and is designed to maximise light and the superb views. The property comprises spacious accommodation of approximately 157 sq. m. / 1,685 sq. ft. laid out in well-designed rooms. The house has the benefit of oil fire central heating, open fireplace, pvc double glazed windows and tarmacadam driveway.\n\nThe accommodation includes entrance hallway, sitting room, kitchen/ dining room, utility, wc, 2 bedrooms and a family bathroom on ground floor. Upstairs there are 3 further bedrooms one with a balcony just-off and bathroom. \n\nThe grounds comprising 0.6 acre approx. are landscaped with tarmac entrance drive and parking area, patio and lawns interspersed with some shrubs.\n\nThe location is excellent and the property enjoys magnificent views to the mouth of the famous Glandore Harbour that incorporates the popular sailing village of Glandore and the fishing village of Union Hall. \n\nThe market towns of Clonakilty & Skibbereen are within a twenty minute drive whilst Cork City is just 40 miles distant.
