    We have pleasure to present this 5 bedroom detached family home to the market. Located at Parkhill, on the outskirts of Ballyshannon, this five bedroom family home ticks all the boxes. Perched on an elevated setting, there are terrific countryside views from this light filled property which is presented in excellent decorative order. The location of this property provides convenient access to all local amenities whilst still providing a rural setting.\r\n\r\nAccommodation is laid out over three floors to provide entrance hall, living room, snug, open plan kitchen/dining/living room, family bathroom and five badrooms (two ensuite). The lower ground floor provides a generous utility space which is complimented with an additional w.c.\r\n\r\nThis is undoubtedly a comfortable family home that provides adequate space to cater for the increased working from home dynamic.
