    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Introducing Sancta Maria , this deceptively large (1,680 sq ft) residence is set on an elevated and landscaped site in the historic town of Askeaton. This lightfilled and well proportioned 3 bed, 2 bath detached residence is within walking distance to all local amenities. Ideally situated, the residence is just a 15 minute drive to heritage town of Adare, 30 minutes to Limerick City and local amenities such as Askeaton Leisure Centre, Foynes Port and Sailing Club, Curragh Chase Park and Tarbert Ferry are all only a short drive away. \n\nAccommodation comprises entrance hallway, spacious lounge/sitting room, open plan kitchen/dining room/living room, utility, 3 double bedrooms (one ensuite) and main bathroom with whb & w/c. \n\nThe residence has the benefit of OFCH, oil stove and solid fuel open fireplace. It has mains water and sewerage mains connections and double glazed uPVC windows fitted throughout. \n\nThere are mature hedges to the front along with a tarmacdam driveway and rockery. There is a concreted area to the rear bounded by rockery. \n\nViewing of this fine property is highly recommended and is strictly by appointment only. Full details available from sole selling agents.\n\n\nEntrance Hallway 5.51m x 1.21m. with solid cherrywood flooring, coving & window\n\nLounge/Sitting Room 5.54m x 5.34m. marble fireplace with electric fireplace / option of open fireplace, solid cherrywood flooring, coving & 2 windows\n\nKitchen/Dining Room/Living Room 7.72m x 5.33m. oil fired stove with red brick surround, ivory fitted units with integrated dishwasher, american style fridge freezer, electric hob & cooker, sink, coving, porcelain floor tiles, window along with 2 velux windows, french doors to rear\n\nUtility Room 3.43m x 2.32m. with ivory fitted units, porcelain floor tiles, ceramic wall tiles, sink with insinkerator fitted, window and half glazed door to rear\n\nBedroom 1 4.46m x 3.58m. double room with wooden laminate flooring & window\n\nBedroom 2 4.22m x 3.54m. double room with wooden laminate flooring & window\n\nBedroom 3 4.46m x 3.73m. double room with wooden laminate flooring & window\n\nEnsuite 3.42m x 0.95m. with ceramic wall & floor tiles, Triton T90 XR electric shower, whb, w/c & window\n\nMain Bathroom 3.13m x 2.07m. with ceramic wall & floor tiles, Triton T90 XR shower, bath, whb, w/c & window\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nEircode: V94 E4 WP GPS No' s: 52.600659 -8.971751\nHeading NorthEast on the N69 route towards Askeaton, turn right onto R518, continue for 300m, property located on the right. \nLook out for Sherry Fitzgerald Stack saleboard.
