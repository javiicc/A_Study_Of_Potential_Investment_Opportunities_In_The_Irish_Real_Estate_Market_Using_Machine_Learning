    Mark Charles Properties is delighted to present 37 Priory Lawns to the open market. This lovely four bedroom family home sits on a wonderful elevated site and enjoys great views over the surrounding countryside. Priory Lawns enjoys an excellent location in the village of Rhode, with all amenities just a mere stroll away. Tullamore, Mullingar and Edenderry are a short drive from the village and the M6 motorway leaves Dublin within easy access. The current owners have done a splendid job on No.37, it is tastefully decorated and the addition of a detached block garage is a real bonus. Accommodation briefly comprises of entrance hallway, sitting room, kitchen/dining room, utility room, family bathroom, bedroom one, master bedroom with en suite, bedroom three, bedroom four, storage room and shower room.
