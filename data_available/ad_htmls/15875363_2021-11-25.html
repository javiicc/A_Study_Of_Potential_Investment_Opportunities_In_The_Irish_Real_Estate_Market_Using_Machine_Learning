    Lilly Bank is a three-bedroomed detached cottage positioned on a superb south facing site of approx. 0.27 acres on the Model Farm Road, in one of Cork s most highly sought after residential locations. \n\nThe house itself would require considerable renovation, however the site is ideal for a new build (subject to planning permission). It is rare that properties on such generous sites come to the market in this area. The property also boasts fantastic privacy and is not overlooked. \n\nThe location is paramount here, being positioned on the Model Farm Road which boasts a host of amenities close by. Cork City, Bishopstown, Wilton, CUH, CIT, Ballincollig Town Centre, and the South Ring Road network are all within easy reach. It is also within easy walking distance of the Lee Fields and the lovely country walks around the beautiful Lee Valley.\n\nThis truly is a fantastic opportunity that should not be missed. Viewing of the garden/potential site comes highly recommended.\nHall 3.57 x 0.98. The hallway has carpet flooring and centre light.\n\nKitchen 2.48 x 4.02. The kitchen overlooks the rear garden and has tiled flooring, floor and eye-level units and centre light.\n\nLiving Room 3.39 x 4.54. Positioned to the front of the house, the living room can be accessed via the porch or the hallway. There is laminate wood flooring and centre light.\n\nDining Room 3.60 x 5.01. Located just off the kitchen, the dining room has linoleum flooring, open fireplace with fitted storage units to either side and centre light.\n\nBedroom 1 4.34 x 3.49. Double bedroom to the front of the house with carpet flooring, coving, floor to ceiling fitted wardrobe and centre light.\n\nBedroom 2 2.34 x 4.70. Double bedroom with carpet flooring and centre light.\n\nBedroom 3 2.29 x 3.54. Nicely sized bedroom with laminate wood flooring and centre light.\n\nBathroom 2.94 x 1.35. Three-piece electric shower suite with laminate wood flooring and tiled walls.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom the County Hall, travel out the Carrigrohane Straight Road to the Poulavone Roundabout. Take 1st left onto the Model Farm Road. Continue past the guide dogs centre on the left-hand side and continue straight on the model farm road and house is on the right hand side. See Agents Sign.
