    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to bring to the market this exceptional 3 bedroomed family home, which presents in pristine and turnkey condition. It is further enhanced by its super sunny west facing rear garden. The lucky new owners will have nothing to do, but simply move straight in. Spacious and bright with very well-proportioned accommodation comprising 1215 sq. ft. (112.9 sq. m. approx.), this home will attract strong interest. \n\nGarden: To the front there is off-street parking for two cars and heathers and hebes are planted in a side border. A raised bed underneath the living room window has scented bay, rosemary lavender and chives. A gated side entrance leads to the much south after orientation of a sunny south west facing rear garden. It is laid in lawn with paving stones outside the back door. There is a small storage shed and a tap. \n\n13 Diswellstown Heath is located just off the Diswellstown Road and next door to St. Patrick s National School in Castleknock, right beside Castleknock Community College. (School admission policies are subject to change and should be verified). Nearby shops at Carpenterstown and Laurel Lodge ensure all conveniences are on hand. This A rated home is located just 25-30 minutes from Dublin City Centre and 5 minutes drive from Castleknock Village. Coolmine Train Station is a mere13 minute walk which will please commuters and there is an excellent bus service to City Centre.\n\nWe highly recommend viewing.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 5.93m x 2m. A black composite multi lock hall door opens to the sizeable hallway which is warm and inviting. The flooring through much of the downstairs is a quality laminate in a greige colour, neutral and modern. There is excellent understairs storage situated here.\n\nGuest wc 1.54m x 1.44. Comprising of a wash hand basin, wc and attractive natural stone flooring\n\nLiving Room 4.24m x 3.64m. A lovely reception room with a deep bay window overlooking the front garden. It has the same flooring as the hallway and roller blinds and black and gold striped curtains adorn the window.\n\nDining Room 5.54m x 2.99. The impressively sized dining room is open plan with the beautiful kitchen. A great space to dine and relax, The same flooring continues here and tall French doors open to the rear garden.\n\nKitchen 3.14m x 2.85m. This beautifully made kitchen is in a lovely Oyster colour with silver coloured hardware and grey veined white marble worktops. The flooring is tiled in natural stone. There is a one and a half bowl sink positioned at the large window and a pull-out larder unit maximises storage. The appliances include an electric oven, gas hob, extractor and an integrated fridge freezer. There is access to the Utility Room.\n\nUtility Room 1.54m x 1.07. With plenty of space for additional storage and plumbed for a washing machine, with matching natural stone flooring.\n\nBedroom 1 4.3m x 3.05m. Is a great sized double room overlooking the rear garden. Laminate flooring features along with excellent built-in wardrobes with hanging and shelving space. There is access to the ensuite.\n\nEnsuite 3.09m x 1.74m. This fine sized ensuite comprises of a good sized shower enclosure with tiled walls, a wash hand basin and wc. A chrome towel rail/radiator completes the descriptives.\n\nBedroom 2 3.84m x 2.76m. Is another great double room, this time to the front with the same high quality built-in wardrobes, black out roller blinds and cream and taupe coloured curtains.\n\nBedroom 3 2.87m x 2.68m. Is a lovely single room with a built in wardrobe, blackout roller blinds and dusky pink curtains.\n\nBathroom 2.65m x 2.02. Beautifully appointed comprising of a bath and separate shower enclosure, the wash hand basin and wc are positioned under marble counter tops. Taupe coloured tiles are neutral and a chrome radiator/towel rail features.
