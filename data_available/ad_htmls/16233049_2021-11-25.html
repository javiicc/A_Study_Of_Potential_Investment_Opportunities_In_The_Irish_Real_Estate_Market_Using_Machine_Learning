    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Wallace Estates is proud to present to the market 4 Cois Na hAbhainn, a sublime terraced house located in the vibrant cosmopolitan town of Clonakilty. Cois Na hAbhainn consists of a row of terraced houses built in the 1990's located on Western Rd, only a five minute walk from Clonakilty town centre. This property is relatively modern and visually in pristine condition.\r\n\r\nThis 3 bed, 3 bath house is ideal for a variety of homeowners. Furthermore, its splendid location in Clonakilty makes it a commercial haven for investment and a potentially high resale price. The front garden is spacious and landscaped giving an inviting initial impression to the property.\r\n\r\nThis house is a stone's throw from Clonakilty town centre, allowing the property to be located in a serene and quiet yet central area. Nearby is a commercial area where all major supermarkets such as Dunnes Stores, Supervalu and Lidl can be found. Situated 45 minutes west of Cork City and Cork International Airport. \r\n\r\nClonakilty is a vibrant, colourful and welcoming town with beautiful local beaches, a host of rich food and music culture. Strong community interaction is at the heart of Clonakilty's ethos, with it being the country's first fair-trade town and first autism-friendly town.\r\n\r\nRecently listed in 2021 Irish Times 20 Best Places to Live in Ireland, this accolade showcases Clonakilty's popularity. There is a plethora of schooling and sporting facilities nearby. The area is also extremely popular for its tourism which may attract holiday makers to this property.\r\n\r\nAccommodation\r\nGround Floor\r\nHallway (Landing 2.60m x 2.16m, Main Hallway 1.29m x 1.09m)\r\nUpon opening the elegant front door with frosted glass windows, the splendid wooden aesthetic of the property is instantly apparent. The tiling is stylish but sturdy and continues into the kitchen. This house is kept warm and comfortable with oil heating.\r\n\r\nLiving Room (4.43m x 3.83m)\r\nThe wooden flooring in the spacious living room complements the stylish timber framed mantelpiece. The room is exquisitely painted in a subtle yellow which helps alleviate the beautiful natural lighting in this room courtesy of the large window.\r\n\r\nKitchen / Dining Area (6.10m x 3.32m)\r\nThe strikingly beautiful chequered wall tiles come into view upon entering the kitchen area. The wooden cupboards and black countertops give the area a beautiful dynamic. A spacious dining area adjoins the kitchen, making this a perfect family area. A large oven, fridge freezer, washing machine and dishwasher combine to make this a fully functional kitchen area. A beautiful set of double glass doors that lead into the garden allow for the kitchen to be extravagantly well lit.\r\n\r\n\r\nGuest WC (1.73m x 1.59m)\r\nComfortably spaced and with dark tiling, this bathroom is both highly functional and accessible on the ground floor. Equipped with all basic toilet necessities.\r\n\r\nSecond Floor\r\nMaster Bedroom (3.47m x 3.37m)\r\nThe fully wooded staircase and upstairs landing (3.19m x 0.91m) leads to the beautiful master bedroom. The double glass doors leading into the balcony allow for particularly impressive lighting in this spacious bedroom. The balcony itself is quite intimate and oversees the beautiful greenery to the rear of the house. The double bed and bathroom ensuite make the master bedroom a luxurious space.\r\nEnsuite (3.35m x 2.23m)\r\nThis ensuite consists of elegant detailed wall tiling which complements the floor. The toilet facilities are fully functional, complete with a walk-in electric shower.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 (2.53m x 2.21m)\r\nAnother well maintained spacious bedroom, there are 2 single beds in this room for personal convenience. The yellow wall paint is subtle and the large window helps to make this room well lit. There is also a large closet here to add functionality.\r\n\r\nBedroom 3 (4.37m x 2.64m)\r\nThis bedroom contains a double bed and is similar in size to bedroom 2. The venetian blind allows light to flow through the room, and the wooden flooring provides a luxurious feel to the room.\r\n\r\nBathroom (2.52m x 2.08m)\r\nAn elegant bathroom with some eye-catching white tiling that is contrasted beautifully by the wooden detailing. Fully functional with all toilet necessities and includes a large bathtub.\r\n\r\nHot Press (0.95m x 0.86m) and Storage (2.2m x 0.95m)\r\nFurther elements exemplifying the excellent functionality of this house, the hot press and storage area upstairs are in good condition and perfect for everyday use.\r\nThis entirely finished home with all flooring, fixtures, and finishes applied to the highest of standards will be a dream buy for the new owner and viewings are highly recommended.\r\nServices\r\nThe property is connected to all main services including water, electricity and main sewerage. Heating is by means of oil-fired central heating and Living room contains an open fireplace.\r\n\r\nOutside\r\nThe front of the property is complimented by an attractive low stone wall and pillared entrance and small front lawn. Ample parking to the rear of property with access through gated south facing enclosed private patio area. \r\n\r\nRear Garden\r\nThe gated garden itself is quite intimate and subtle. There is plenty of space for garden furniture and a shed. The high fencing allows for extra privacy while adding an element of colour to the concrete area.\r\n\r\nVIEWING BY APPOINTMENT: \r\nViewing comes highly recommended.
