    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    This beautifully proportioned and charming stone farmhouse, sitting on circa 3 acres of meadow, comes to the market in great condition. The property is in a fantastic location, approximately 3 miles from Dromahair and 5 miles from Manorhamilton, with easy access to Sligo. \r\n\r\nThe house itself has been sympathetically modernised with a sunroom extension, oil fired central heating, solar panels, new windows and doors. The grounds also contain stone outbuildings in need of restoration, and a 3 bay hayshed that is a thing of rare beauty. \r\n\r\nViewing is an absolute must, with sole selling agent.
