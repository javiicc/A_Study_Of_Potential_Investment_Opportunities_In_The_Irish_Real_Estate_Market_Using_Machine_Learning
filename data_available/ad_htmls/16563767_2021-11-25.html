    7 Belarmine Close is a most beautifully presented three-bedroom family home with a spacious new attic conversion and a sun filled rear garden located within this family friendly cul de sac setting in Belarmine, Stepaside.\r\n\r\nLight filled and beautifully presented throughout, the accommodation comprises an entrance hall, guest wc with additional understairs storage, living room, utility, open plan kitchen & dining room with access to the rear garden. The first floor is presented with three bedrooms (with master en suite), hot press and family bathroom. The attic was converted recently providing terrific additional living accommodation and is currently in use as Bedroom 4 with generous eaves storage, and space for an office desk on the landing.\r\n\r\nOutside, the home benefits from generous resident and visitor parking to the front. A gated side passage leads one to the private and sun filled south facing rear garden which is presented in patio and lawn with colourful mature planting surrounding providing an ideal outdoor space for entertaining.\r\n\r\nNumber 7 is just a gentle walk from the charming village of Stepaside with its superb range of boutique shops and eateries. The Belarmine development itself also offers facilities such as a supermarket, restaurant, hair salon, pharmacy, creche and community centre. Other nearby amenities include Dundrum Town Centre, Sandyford Business Park, Beacon South Quarter and Leopardstown Racecourse.\r\n\r\nThere is an excellent selection of primary and secondary schools nearby including Our Lady of The Wayside, Gaelscoil Thaobh na Coille, Kilternan Church of Ireland NS, Stepaside Educate Together and Rosemount School. For the outdoor enthusiasts there are a wide range of activities including Hill walking, mountain bike trails, Kilternan Tennis Club, De La Salle Rugby Club, Wayside Celtic Football Club and approx. 80 acres of parkland at Fernhill Gardens.\r\n\r\nThe area is well serviced by public transportation such as the Luas Green Line (Glencairn) which is just a short walk away. Dublin Bus No.44, 47 and 118.
