    Delaney Estates are delighted to bring No. 147 to the market. This superbly finished two-bedroom first floor apartment with a sunny south-west facing balcony is conveniently located just off the Malahide Road in this much sought after, mature & peaceful development of Timber Mills.\r\n\r\nIn turn- key condition with impressive modern d\xc3\xa9cor of the highest standard, this light-filled and spacious apartment is sure to appeal to a wide spectrum of buyers including owner occupiers, investors, and first-time buyers alike and is the obvious choice for the discerning purchaser.\r\n\r\nThe accommodation comprises of a welcoming entrance hall with excellent storage space with two separate storage areas, two bright and spacious double bedrooms both with built in wardrobes and a modern bathroom with bath and pump shower, wash hand basin and w.c. A wonderful open plan kitchen/dining/living room completes the living accommodation. The kitchen has recently been fitted with modern solid wood floor to eye level units, integrated electric oven and hob with fan overhead and is plumbed for a combined washing machine/dryer. French doors from the living area lead to a sunny balcony where one can enjoy al fresco dining in the afternoon sun. \r\n\r\nThe property has double glazed UPVC windows throughout and has two designated underground car parking spaces. The property is fitted with 'Lucht' energy efficient and cost saving electric radiators which are controlled by a mobile phone app allowing full control of your heating. \r\n\r\nTimber Mill is set in a popular and ultra-convenient location just of the Malahide Road and is close to an array of amenities including excellent primary and secondary schools, Artane and Northside shopping centres, sports and recreational facilities. There are numerous transport links within walking distance including well serviced bus routes on the Malahide Road and Killester DART station.
