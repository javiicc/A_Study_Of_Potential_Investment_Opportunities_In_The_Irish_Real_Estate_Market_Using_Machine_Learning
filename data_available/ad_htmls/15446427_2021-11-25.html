    Site with the benefit of full planning permission for an architect designed house. Rare opportunity to enjoy panoramic countryside beauty with all the convenience of city living. Cohalan Downing \xc2\xa0is delighted to bring to the Market a fully serviced site in Moneygurney, Douglas with full planning permission for a detached home of 258 Sqm (2800Sqft). This former stables site at Leafy Lane runs to 0.168 acres, enjoys wonderful views of Garryduff woods and you might even catch a glimpse of Cork estuary on a sunny day. Situated in a most enviable location, in one of Corks most sought after areas, at the edge of a green belt area but with a regular bus service to the city centre, UCC, MTU and CUH, whoever settles here will truly enjoy the best of both worlds.\r\n\r\n\r\nAmenities in the immediate area are abundant. Within an easy stroll you can arrive at Douglas Golf course, play tennis or hockey at Garryduff sports centre or hike in Garryduff woods. If football or rugby is your preference then Douglas Hall football club is right across the road and Douglas rugby club is close by.\xc2\xa0 Award winning Hitchmoughs riding school (childhood home and training ground of Irish international showjumper Billy Twomey) is approximately 2 miles away.\r\n\r\nChildren can walk to school quite safely from Leafy Lane (there are two schools in the neighbourhood). \xc2\xa0Mount Oval village and Broadale fulfil the requirements for supermarkets, hair dressers or pharmacies and Douglas Village is a short drive to the bottom of Maryborough hill. The south ring road can also be conveniently accessed. For those looking for a rare ready to go chance to construct your own property on a stunning\xc2\xa0 serviced site offering convenience and a wealth of lifestyle opportunities, look no further!
