The opposite end of the property boasts an ensuite ground floor bedroom and reception room with separate own door access and stairs to the first floor. \nOn the first floor there are four splendid ensuite bedrooms all with walk-in wardrobes and 2 additional spacious double bedrooms and a large family bathroom. \nThe mature gardens are beautifully landscaped offering extensive manicured lawns, trees, shrubs and mature hedgerows. To the rear of the house is a detached garage and ample parking to the front. \nOn a quiet lane on the easterly edge of Gorey town the location of Ballscartin is second to none, set at the foothill of Tara Hill with scenic views . It is within easy commuting distance of the capital via the M11 motorway ( 50 mins). Close by Ballyscartin are both Tara Hill Primary School and Gorey Primary and Secondary Schools. The vibrant Gorey town itself (2 kms) , has experienced tremendous growth in recent years both in terms of commercial and residential development. It boasts a wonderful array of amenities both social and essential, including excellent schools, rail and bus links to Dublin, trendy boutique shopping, numerous high class eateries, busy bars, cinema, library and many leading retailers.
