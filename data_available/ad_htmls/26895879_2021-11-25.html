    Description

      ***ALL BILLS INCLUDED AND AVAILABLE IMMEDIATELY***\n\nSherry FitzGerald Lettings is proud to present Ballymagillen to the lettings market. They say what's rare is wonderful and this magnificent country house is certainly affirmation of the old saying! Set behind private gates the house sits on an acre of gardens and benefits from ample off-street parking. Aside from the pretty gardens to look out on, the house also has a stunning patio area where the new residents can wile away the summer evenings surrounded by beautiful plants and trees with a distinct scent of lavender emanating from the flower beds. The patio has all day sunshine and one can enjoy beautiful sunsets over the planes of County Meath. The house itself is a superb country house with a large open plan kitchen cum breakfast room acting as the heart of the home with the benefit of a working Aga keeping the house warm during winter. Off the kitchen you'll find a cosy den with a wood burning stove and plenty of room to relax. There is a generous reception room complete with an open fire, pool table and piano at the opposite end of the house and a formal entrance hall in the centre of the home which completes the ground floor accommodation. Upstairs, the theme of space continues with Three generous double bedrooms, all with built in storage, Two en-suite bathrooms as well as a Fourth single room and a family bathroom. Ballymagillen is positioned just off the M3 and is only a short stroll to the fabulous Avoca store in Dunboyne.The M3 Parkway Train Station is only a few minutes down the road while a further 5 minutes will have you in The Blanchardstown Town Centre where a fantastic array of shops, restaurants and amenities are waiting. Available immediately the house is ready to view virtually or in person through Robbie Dillon at Sherry FitzGerald Lettings. The price includes all utility bills, garden maintenance and refuse collection however TV/Internet is the responsibility of the new tenants.
