    2 Bedroom stone cottage and large one bedroom apartment \r\nThis unique property consists of an original stone cottage fully modernized and a two story, one bedroom apartment at the rear. The living room has an open fire stove set in black tiled surround and Cathedral ceiling with embedded lights. Radiators with a choice of oil heating or heating from fire both systems combined for convenience. \r\nBathroom fully tiled, tiles chosen to compliment both old and modern look. Shower.\r\nNew roof fully insulated, new plumbing and electric wiring.\r\nATTIC: The main bedroom has an attic with pull down stairs. BER- D2\r\n\r\nAPARTMENT: Large lounge full length of back, open fire stove set on a black granite hearth that heats all the radiators with choice of oil heating both systems combined.\r\nLarge fully fitted kitchen/dining.\r\nNew roof fully insulated, new plumbing and electric wiring\r\nSHED: This large shed could easily be converted to an office as it has ample electric plugs and water supply. BER.- D1\r\n\r\n GARDEN AND PATIO: Small front garden and large rear garden with mature trees and two ornamental gates. Paved patio at rear and one side of the house.
