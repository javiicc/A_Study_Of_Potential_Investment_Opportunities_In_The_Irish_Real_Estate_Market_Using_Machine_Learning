Spacious 42 sm (450 sq. ft.) attached garage. Ample on site parking.\r\n\r\nLOCATION:\r\n\r\nThe property is located off a quiet country lane and is hidden from view by mature trees ensuring total privacy, yet Durrus village, the gateway to both the Mizen and Sheep's Head Peninsulas, is only a 5 minute drive away and the renowned Sheep's Head walking and hiking trails are close by.
