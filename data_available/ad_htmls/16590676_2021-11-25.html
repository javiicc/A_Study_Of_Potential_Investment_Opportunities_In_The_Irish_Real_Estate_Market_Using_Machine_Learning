    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to present No. 23 Windmill Court to the market for sale. This 2 bedroom ground floor apartment is in good condition, measuring 62 sq.m/667.36 sq.ft. approx. and is incredibly private, as is not overlooked to front or rear. There is also the added benefit of private parking to front. \n\nThe property briefly comprises of entrance hallway which opens up to the open-plan living/dining room, with feature, electric fireplace and the adjoining kitchen. There is 1 double bedroom and 1 good-sized single bedroom, both with fitted wardrobes, and main ensuite. Both bedrooms overlook the well-maintained, communal gardens to rear. A family bathroom completes the accommodation. \n\nSituated just off the Clonsilla Road, this property is just a short stroll from Clonsilla Village and just a 5 minute drive to Blanchardstown Shopping Centre, with every conceivable amenity available. Coolmine Train Station is just a 5 minute walk, as well as the no. 37 & 39 bus stop, providing easy access to Dublin City Centre. The M50 and M3 are also a short drive away, making commuting to and from the city, as well as further afield, an ease.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 1.51 x 1.69. Tiled flooring. Alarmed.\n\nLiving/Dining Room 5.90 x 4.72. Spacious, open-plan living room with laminate timber flooring. A large window allows a wealth of natural light to enter this room. An electric fire provides a lovely focal point to the room, as well as a homely feel.\n\nKitchen 2.53 x 1.70. Located just off the living room, with laminate timber flooring and tiled splashback. There is an integrated oven and hob, with free-standing fridge/freezer, slimline dishwasher and washer/dryer, which are included in the sale.\n\nHallway 2.77 x 2.43. Laminate timber flooring, containing hotpress.\n\nBedroom 1 3.70 x 2.82. Double bedroom with carpet flooring and double fitted wardrobe. Large, single bedroom with carpet flooring and fitted wardrobe. Also benefits from views over the communal garden to rear.\n\nBathroom 1.98 x 1.80. Tiled flooring with partly tiled walls. Comprises shower over bath, wc and wash hand basin.
