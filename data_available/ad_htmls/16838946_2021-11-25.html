    Beautiful contemporary home or excellent development opportunity Full planning permission to convert to eleven apartments!\n Gleann an Dair is a slick and elegant home of stature in a nice convenient residential area on the northern side of Gorey town centre. The home itself is a large home extending to 517m.sq., and offers versatile accommodation over four levels. The clever and modern design has been brought to life in this truly amazing light filled home with open plan living accommodation and comfortable bedrooms with the top two floors as yet undecorated the options for home workspaces/gyms/games rooms etc., are endless. The house stands centrally in a nice site and is approached via a short avenue off of both the Creagh and Hollyfort roads. In addition to all of this Gleann an Dair comes with the added bonus of full planning permission for conversion of the existing structure and a large extension to the site for conversion of the entire to eleven individual apartments of five #1 bedroom apartments and six #2 bedroom apartments. The existing house was designed and built with this conversion and extension in mind which paves the way for easy conversion either now or at some point in the future. The layout and the construction of the building has incorporated items such as a lift shaft, ducting passageways and ceiling voids to allow for services etc. to be easily added and installed.
