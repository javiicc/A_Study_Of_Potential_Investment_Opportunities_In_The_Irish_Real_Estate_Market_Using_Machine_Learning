    Moovingo are delighted to present this superb first floor apartment to the market. Recently refurbished in 2018, including new kitchen and bathroom, the apartment is presented in excellent condition throughout. The apartment enjoys a desirable south-west facing orientation and beautiful uninterrupted views over Herbert Park.\n\nThe Oaks is located in the Embassy belt of Ballsbridge, one of Dublin's most sought-after locations. Set on the banks of the River Dodder, there is a wealth of amenities in the immediate vicinity, including Herbert Park which spans 32 acres, the RDS, and some of the capital's best bars, cafes, restaurants and shops.\n\nThe accommodation comprises of an entrance hallway, modern kitchen, bright living/dining room, spacious double bedroom with ample storage space, and a modern bathroom. There is lift access to the ground floor and underground car park. While the property does not come with a parking space, spaces are often available to rent in the development.\n\nArea Info\n\nHerbert Park Lane is ideally located in the heart of Ballsbridge off Anglesea Road, one of Dublin's most desirable residential areas. A great selection of amenities are just a short stroll away including the RDS, Herbert Park, the Aviva Stadium and the Bord Gais Energy Theatre. Some of the city's finest restaurants and bars are located within a minutes' walk, including Roly's Bistro, Kites, Mae, The Bridge, Mary Macs, as well as the exclusive Intercontinental and Herbert Park hotels. Grafton Street, St Stephen's Green and Trinity College Dublin are all within walking distance.
