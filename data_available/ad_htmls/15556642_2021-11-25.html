    Beautifully presented 3-bedroomed semi-detached townhouse with stunning south-facing garden, in town centre\r\n\r\n3 Cork Street is presented to the market in immaculate condition and is ready to be occupied by a new owner having been upgraded and well maintained by it's current owners over the years. The house is just a minutes stroll from the centre of Kinsale town, yet is removed from the hustle and bustle of the town. The front of the property has a very attractive rose garden with steps leading to the front door and a path to the rear garden which is secure with a gate. The very large south-facing garden to the rear is elevated and enjoys a birds-eye view of the entire town. This garden has a paved terrace at the end, which has sunshine from early morning to sunset - an ideal spot to enjoy sundowners overlooking the town! The garden is beautifully landscaped and maintained. \r\n\r\nAccommodation is well appointed and the south-facing rear ensures lots of natural light throughout the day. The livingroom at the rear of the house has French doors opening to the garden and has an open fireplace and airing cupboard storage. The kitchen is to the front and is well fitted and enjoys the evening sunshine. The shower-room is also on the ground floor. Upstairs are three bedrooms, the master with fitted sliderobes and window to the front, and bedrooms two and three have stunning views over town. A Stira leads to the floored and plastered attic storage with Velux overlooking town, which is an excellent storage area or could be converted to habitable (space subject to PP). \r\n\r\nHeating is oil fired and there is a useful boiler house which also has storage for garden tools etc. Overall, this property offering makes for a very comfortable home for those looking for their first time home or wishing to down-size or looking for a holiday property.
