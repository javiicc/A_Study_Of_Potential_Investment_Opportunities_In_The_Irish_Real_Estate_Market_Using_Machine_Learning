    MOVEHOME ESTATE AGENTS... are delighted to present a fantastic five bedroomed terraced modern home that is an expansive light filled dream spanning over three levels and in truly walk in condition throughout. \r\n \r\nBehind the exterior lies approximately 147 sq. mtrs of luxury accommodation. This home has undergone a recent renovation, resulting in the creation of a captivating and stylishly presented interior that one would see in an interior magazine. The immensely stylish accommodation will not fail to impress. Built c.2004 this really is a home of distinction which combines enormous character with a classic contemporary style and all the comforts and conveniences one could wish for. Sheer good taste and stylish elegance prevails throughout, from the entrance hallway through the entire house and no expense has been spared on the extensive upgrading they have completed. \r\n \r\nOnce over the threshold you are greeted by a beautiful entrance hall with double height panelling (that continues throughout the hallways) elegant coving and a tiled porcelain flooring that flows from the hall right through to the kitchen/dining area giving a feeling of space and continuity. The kitchen is a spacious cooking and dining space with a peninsula that seats 3/4 barstools. Painted in a soft blue, Farrow & Ball - De Nimes with an contrasting feature cooker hood, and beautiful Calacatta gold quartzite worktop this kitchen is any home owners dream. A clever use of space, this kitchen also houses a small pantry discreetly located with recessed lighting and a stylish mosaic floor. \r\n \r\nTo the front of this fine property lies a beautifully proportioned reception room with a complimentary original style and solid wood flooring. There is a guest WC located off the hallway that is beautifully finished with half height panelling and Burlington & Heritage sanitary wear.\r\n \r\nThe first floor hosts two very spacious bedrooms. To the rear of the property one of the bedrooms has a spacious balcony and the master bedroom to the front hosts a contemporary en-suite. The ensuite equipped with stylish matt black shower door and tap and playful patterned floor tile adds a sense of character to the master suite.\r\n \r\nEach bedroom is spacious, solid flooring and fully fitted wardrobes throughout. Up on top, the second floor hosts three bedrooms (Two Double; One Single) that is flooded with natural light and ample storage space. A large bathroom completes the accommodation on this level. With its effortless style, featuring a bespoke vanity unit with a natural stone top, designer wall lights and a large arched mirror that give this home the wow factor.\r\n \r\nThe convenience of this location cannot be over-emphasised, with a host of amenities just a short stroll away at Rathborne Village, including shops, restaurants, cafes and more. There is also a good selection of schools and creches nearby. Access to the city centre is easily accessible, with Ashtown train station just an 8 minute walk, as well as having an excellent bus service right on your doorstep (route 120). The magnificent Phoenix Park is within close proximity, where a wealth of recreational pursuits can be enjoyed.
