    221 Beechdale is an extended 4 bedroom semi-detached house ideally located close to Dunboyne Village and all its amenities including schools, shops, restaurants, pubs, sports clubs, Dunboyne railway station and city centre bus routes. The ground floor accommodation is spacious with a porch and entrance hallway, a large open-plan living/dining room, a kitchen/breakfast room and conservatory, while upstairs accommodation consists of 4 bedrooms and a family bathroom. It has a front garden with off street parking and a walled rear garden in lawn with a block built shed with a pitched roof. This is a very desirable location adjacent to Dunboyne Village, while the Blanchardstown Shopping Centre, Dublin Airport and all major road networks are easily accessible via the nearby M3.
