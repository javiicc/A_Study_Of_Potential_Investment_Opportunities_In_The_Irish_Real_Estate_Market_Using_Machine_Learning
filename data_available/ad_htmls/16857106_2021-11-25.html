    Sherry Property Consultants are delighted to welcome this absolutely stunning 2 bedroom luxurious apartment, situated on the first floor, in this small, exclusive and distinguished development. In pristine condition throughout, this property is a true gem, boasting bright and spacious living accommodation and magnificent views of the surrounding countryside, the beautiful River Boyne and Drogheda's very impressive railway viaduct. Concealed behind electric gates, this wonderful apartment is fully secure and private yet superbly located within walking distance of all conceivable amenities such as shops, schools, both primary and secondary, restaurants, doctors surgery and pharmacy's and only minutes from Drogheda town centre. This wonderful apartment is decorated to a very high standard with amazing attention to detail and is an absolute credit to its current owners. Accommodation consists of entrance hallway, dining/sitting room, kitchen, cloakroom, wc/shower room, 2 bedrooms, 1 ensuite and private balcony.
