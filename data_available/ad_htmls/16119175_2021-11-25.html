    Rea Fitzgerald Chambers are delighted to present this period property to the market. This spacious four-bedroom mid terraced house is excellently situated just minutes from Stoneybatter, Smithfield and Phibsborough villages, putting every imaginable amenity within reach. There is a plethora of independent shops, cafes, bars and restaurants to choose from and the TU Grangegorman campus sits directly opposite the house. The Phoenix Park and the City Centre itself are also within easy walking distance and the area is well serviced by Dublin Bus and the Luas. \r\n\r\nThe bright and spacious accommodation on the ground floor comprises two large reception rooms, kitchen with ample dining space, a utility room and a WC. Upstairs there are four bedrooms, (ensuite shower room), and a shower room. \r\n\r\nThere is a 18m2 rear with vehicular access via laneway which may have extension/development potential, subject to planning.
