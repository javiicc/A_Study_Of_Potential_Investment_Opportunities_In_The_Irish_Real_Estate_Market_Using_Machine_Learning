      ** August launch of 3 Bedroom Semi-detached houses** Priced from \xe2\x82\xac295,000\r\n\r\n\r\nGALLOWHILL is a small exclusive residential development conveniently located in the heritage town of Athy, just one hour from Dublin & Waterford and 10km from the M9, Exit 3. Commuters will not be disappointed to find these new traditional type homes on offer, with spacious modern interiors, high ceilings, excellent storage and all within easy access to the M9 and M7. \r\n\r\nGallowhill is located in a sought-after area, within walking distance of Athy Train station, Athy Golf Club and the four star, Clanard Court Hotel. These new A Rated three and four bedroom, semi-detached homes, offer generous living space with excellent standard features. There are 8 No. 4 Bedroom homes extending to 1344 sq ft (Sold Out) and 16 No 3 Bedroom semi-detached homes, extending from 1090sq ft-1186 sq ft. \r\n\r\nAthy, is an ancient heritage market town which is located on the River Barrow and Grand Canal. It is rich in architectural and natural heritage. Athy Blue Way, Athy Town by pass, the re-development of Emily Square and Heritage Centre will all contribute to the further development of the town.\r\n The town offers an amazing choice of well-established modern schools, excellent clubs and sports facilities and numerous festivals throughout the yearly calendar, - Tri-Athy, Shackleton Autumn School, Trad Athy, Bluegrass Festival, Dragon Boat Festival and many more. \r\n \r\nAs new homes are in short supply, Gallowhill offers a unique opportunity to purchase a quality family home, in an established community, just minutes from the train station and M9 motorway. \r\n\r\nA selection of attached photos of Athy will present you with a flavour of the area. \r\n\r\nBuilding has commenced with limited availability.
