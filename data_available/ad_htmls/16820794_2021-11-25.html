    O'Connor Estate Agents welcome 160 Killala Road to the market, a charming 2 bedroom extended mid terrace residence conveniently located less than 300metres from the Broombridge LUAS Station.\r\n\r\nThe current owners acquired this home in 2018 and set about the refurbishment with luxurious living and creative open plan living accommodation in mind. Created with flair and taste, this home presents many attractive features including a fixed window in the kitchen offering a stunning picturesque view of the stunning low maintenance rear garden and some original varnished wooden floors upstairs to only mention a few. This is a superb opportunity to acquire a home in a central location in a quite residential location of Cabra.\r\n\r\nOff street parking is provided to the front with concealed bin storage and a secluded rear garden is complimented by the paving & stone finish and planting & sleepers throughout offering discerning purchasers an opportunity for relaxing al fresco dining.\r\n\r\nAccommodation:\r\n\r\nLiving/Dining Area: c.6.23m x 4.86m with solid oak wooden floors, drop down lighting and under stairs storage with an additional storage area.\r\n\r\nKitchen: c.4.99m x 3.54m with built in kitchen units, HDF flooring, recessed lighting, fixed picturesque window and access is provided to the rear garden\r\n\r\nBedroom 1: c.3.50m x 3.31m with varnished original floors, wardrobe, storage and tastefully finished timber ceiling effect.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2: c.3.27m x 2.81m with varnished wooden floors and tastefully finished timber wall effect.\r\n\r\nBathroom: with HDF floors, Triton Electric shower with bath, WC and WHB.\r\n\r\nLanding: with access to attic which is almost fully floored with pull down stairs for access.\r\n\r\n**TRANSPORT** This property has the convenience of the M50 within close proximity which opens up the location to the entire county & country. There are numerous Dublin Bus routes available however most notably the LUAS line is within close proximity.\r\n\r\n**AMENITIES** There are an abundance of local services and amenities literally on the doorstep of this property. There are neighbourhood shops and it is with close proximity to Grangegorman DIT. Nearby villages include Cabra, Phibsborough, Stoneybatter, Glasnevin and Drumcondra. Cabra Community College and St.Declans College are educational facilities within the local area. The Phoenix Park is also popular among local residents.
