    * Please note pictures are CGI s for illustration purposes only *\n\nA superb opportunity to build your own state-of-the-art, contemporary home on an elevated site of approx. 0.23ha/0.47 acres with Planning Permission to build a five bedroomed home extending to approximately 743.2 sqm /8,000 sq ft. The very stylish and considered plans, by Douglas Wallace Architects, are designed to take full advantage of its elevated position affording far off sea views and incorporate a terraced garden as well as a sheltered west courtyard garden design maximising the natural topography of the site. The accommodation enjoys an excellent flow and opens up, at every opportunity, with doors leading to the various outdoor spaces creating a seamless connection which blurs the boundaries with the inside and the outside. The design is for a luxurious yet comfortable family home which provides for a relaxed family lifestyle within a spacious open, natural layout enjoying an excellent flow of accommodation that is both comfortable and sophisticated at the same time. \n\nThe accommodation over three levels comprises of a lower ground floor level, ground and first floor.\n\nArriving at ground level - beyond the hall door there is a stairs, lift, cloaks cupboard and wc. The entrance leads to formal living room which is backed by entertainment/bar/cinema room and an exceptionally spacious (approx. 2,157 sqft) open plan kitchen/dining/family room with a walk-in butlers pantry. The living accommodation at this level all opens out to the west courtyard garden. \n\nThe lower ground floor level houses the sleeping accommodation with no less than five luxuriously appointed bedrooms all with dressing rooms and bathrooms/shower rooms ensuite that all lead to secluded outdoor terraced area. There is also an integral garden store/workshop at this level\n\nThe first floor, enjoying superb views, is a large open plan space ideal as a home office or simply suitable for contemplative relaxation. There is in addition an office/library room with dual aspect to take in sea, mountain and city views with a separate gym with shower.\n\nThis exciting design has been well thought out to maximise its unique setting to create this stylish home which will appeal to today s busy family lives and the growing trend of working from home.\n\nEnjoying a delightful and convenient setting off Ferndale Road in this exclusive cul-de-sac the property is located just a short distance from Foxrock and Shankill Villages. It enjoys easy access to the N11 and M50 providing easy access to the Airport. The city centre is within a 25 minute drive. There are excellent primary and secondary schools within easy reach to include St Gerard s junior and senior schools and the newly relocated John Scottus junior and senior schools just a five minute walk. Shankill has a host of amenities to include the Dart and a variety of convenience stores and the LUAS stop at Cherrywood is less than 10 minutes by car.
