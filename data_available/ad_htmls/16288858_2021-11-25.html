    Carcur Cottage offers you the best of town living with the sense of elegance and style you would associate with a property of this era. Built in the mid - 1800 s the accommodation is far from that of a cottage with the accommodation being 238 sqm over three floors. As you would expect, this lovely home offers, among many other features, great sized rooms with high ceilings and decorative coving, sash windows and elegant fireplaces.\nThe property is located on Spawell Road approximately 1 km to Wexford s Main Street and if access to major routes is important to you are approximately a 4km drive to join the N11 and N25.\nInternally, the accommodation has been slightly extended to the rear and now offers you 2 large reception rooms at ground floor level, 2 bathrooms and sun room to the rear (over 3 floors), 3 bedrooms on the first floor, and kitchen/dining room and 2 bedrooms on the lower ground floor. Outside there is access to a coal cellar and to the rear three sheds at basement level.\nIf a period home offering large accommodation and in a great town location is what you have been searching for, Carcur Cottage is a must view.\nSouth West facing rear garden, 238 sqm detached home, off-street parking, The Cottage Ref No. 15607047 with NIAH (protected structure), OFCH.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 4.60m x 2.70m.
