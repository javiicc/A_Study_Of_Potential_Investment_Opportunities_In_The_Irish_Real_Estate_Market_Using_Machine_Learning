    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Located in the heart of the increasingly popular Crusheen village, within walking distance of all of the village amenities, 200 meters to the M18 motorway and 100 meters to public transport links, No. 51 is a bright and spacious 2 bedroom detached bungalow, completely refurbished in 2018. On entering the main entrance doorway, the solid oak timber flooring with decorative ceiling coving leads to the two double bedrooms, main bathroom and main reception with the oak flooring continuing through to the main reception and into kitchen/dining. The main reception has the wood surround feature fireplace with the solid fuel Stanley stove with bay window feature looking onto the vast green area of the Moyglass housing development. The modern kitchen/dining features under counter lighting, integrated appliances, ample work surfaces with tile splashback surround and recess ceiling lighting with access to the brick paved patio. Both double bedrooms have quality carpeted flooring with built in wardrobes and the main bathroom being fully tiled ceiling to floor with all modern fixtures and fittings. To the rear the private lawns connect to the brick paved patio and lead to the detached garden room which is finished to a similar level to the main house. Ample car parking is provided to the front with manicured lawns and double gated access to both sides of the property. This would make a fantastic first time buyer or retirement couple home with viewing highly recommended and strictly by prior appointment with sole selling agents.
