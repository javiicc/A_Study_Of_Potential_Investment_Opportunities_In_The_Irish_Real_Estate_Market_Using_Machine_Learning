    McGovern Estates is delighted to present No.3 Glenair Manor to the market - This magnificent family residence extends to 355m2 ( 3,800 sq.ft.) and is the former showhouse and comes to the market with a superb standard of finish and bright, contemporary interiors throughout. No.3 is perfectly laid out for modern family living and represents a wonderful opportunity for any discerning purchaser to acquire a spacious well-proportioned 5 bedroom family home in an exclusive development in Delgany. The charming village of Delgany less than 1km away where one can avail of all the amenities it has to offer. Internal viewing of No.3 Glenair Manor is highly recommended. \r\n\r\nThe accommodation extends to 355m2 and comprises of a spacious entrance hallway with double height ceiling from which double doors lead to the lounge with French doors out to the rear patio. Further double doors also lead to the dining room which in turn leads to the Kitchen. The Newcastle designed kitchen is oak fitted and incorporates integrated appliances and ample countertop space. Off the kitchen is a fully fitted utility room, Tv room & conservatory. A playroom and WC are also feature. Upstairs there are 5 spacious double bedrooms, 2 are en-suite with walk-in wardrobes, and a family bathroom also features. \r\n\r\nThe picturesque and historical village of Delgany hosts a wide range of services, amenities and sporting facilities along with varied woodland and mountain walks.\r\n\r\nThe Village has seen many new additions in recent years with the renowned Pigeon House Caf\xc3\xa9, artisan bakery, grocery store, delicatessen and organic butcher in the heart of the village. All this and the charming Horse and Hound pub give Delgany Village a special atmosphere.\r\n\r\nGreystones town is within easy reach and all your needs will be catered for with a wonderful selection of bistros, boutiques, restaurants and shops, the village library, churches and a large number of highly regarded schools.\r\n\r\nA host of sport and leisure clubs abound in the area. The Shoreline Leisure Centre and swimming pool is nearby, as are a great choice of renowned golf clubs, including Delgany, Greystones and Druids Glen. There is easy access to the beach, countryside and the wonderful Cliff Walk to Bray.\r\nSituated close to the N11/M50, the commuter will also be delighted with the easy access to local bus stops, the DART/Mainline rail station and the Aircoach to Dublin Airport.\r\n\r\nACCOMMODATION \r\nEntrance Porch: \r\nTiled floor, Velux windows, spotlights, alarm panel.\r\n\r\nHallway: \r\nDouble height ceiling, solid wood floor with decorative tile inset, spotlights, coving, double doors to Lounge.\r\n\r\nGuest WC: \r\nTiled floor, WC, WHB, splash back, heated towel rail, coving.\r\n\r\nLounge: \r\nSolid wood floor, feature bay window, coving, dimmer spotlights.
