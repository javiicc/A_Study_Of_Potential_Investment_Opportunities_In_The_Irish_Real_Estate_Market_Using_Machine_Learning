    Two-bedroomed apartment with stunning views over entire panorama of Kinsale harbour and steps down to private garden\n\n22 Ardbrack Heights is one of the only units within the Ardbrack Heights development which owns the garden to the front and at the side of the property. Steps lead from the balcony which spans the width of the apartment down to this garden area which is lawned and has a patio area and has storage under the balcony. \n\nArdbrack Heights is the ideal property for those looking to downsize and have a garden or wishing to have a bolthole which can be used at weekends and during the summer. \n\nThe property has breathtaking views from the open plan livingroom, the main bedroom, the large balcony and garden and has a southerly aspect thus enjoys sunshine all day long from sunrise to sunset. Accommodation extends to open plan kitchen cum livingroom, with sliding doors opening to balcony, bedroom with sliding doors to balcony, second bedroom and bathroom. Heating is electric.\n\nThe development is maintained to a very high standard by the management company, with landscaping and commons areas in top shape. The development is accessed via security gates opening to tarmac drive with cobble-block pavements leading to each apartment.
