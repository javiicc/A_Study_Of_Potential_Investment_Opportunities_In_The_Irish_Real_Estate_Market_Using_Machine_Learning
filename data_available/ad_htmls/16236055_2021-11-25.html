    Site

    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 0.41 ac
    We are delighted to bring to the open market, this well-proportioned building site measuring circa 0.196 ha (0.407 Acres) offered for sale subject to the purchaser obtaining planning permission. The subject site (Site C) is positioned in a highly sought-after scenic setting within the Fintra Bay area and within a stone's throw of Fintra Beach (approx. 950 meters). This site occupies a peaceful semi-rural setting and commands a slightly elevated position with open aspect views over the surrounding countryside and Conrad Mountain range. In addition, the site benefits from direct frontage onto a quiet country road containing a low volume of passing traffic. \n\nOffered for sale, subject to planning permission to a suitable candidate that can satisfy the Rural Housing Policy and in particular, can demonstrate a need for housing in the locality under the section 47 requirements as set out in the current Donegal County Council Development. \n\n\nThis is an ideal opportunity for a local person to design and build a house to their own specification and have hands-on involvement in the building process from start to finish in a highly desirable area.
