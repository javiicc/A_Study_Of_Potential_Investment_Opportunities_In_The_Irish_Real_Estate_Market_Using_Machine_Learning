    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    RE/MAX team fogarty are delighted to present this stunning home to the market. Rocksprings, Knockhouse is ideally situated just minutes drive to Waterford City centre and is immediate to Whitfield private hospital, WIT arena, WIT main campus and the Carriganore Greenway entrance. \r\n\r\nWestside and Cleaboy Industrial Parks are located close by along with access to the M9 and N25 motorways.\r\n\r\nThis superb detached home is approached by electric gates with video intercom offering excellent privacy and seclusion. \r\n\r\nAccommodation on the ground floor includes a double height entrance hall, a sitting room with fitted inset stove, this links to both the sunroom and living room off the kitchen diner. \r\n\r\nThe living room has bifold doors that open to a south westerly facing patio. A separate dining room, utility, back kitchen and bathroom complete the ground floor. \r\n\r\nThe first floor accommodates 4 double bedrooms one with en-suite and family bathroom.\r\n\r\nOutside the mature gardens are beautifully landscaped and extend to c. half an acre, a limestone patio opens from the living space and there is parking for several cars.\r\n\r\nThe multi purpose garage and utility area provides excellent storage and can be accessed by an enclosed link from the main residence.\r\n\r\nViewing of this substantial home is highly recommended.
