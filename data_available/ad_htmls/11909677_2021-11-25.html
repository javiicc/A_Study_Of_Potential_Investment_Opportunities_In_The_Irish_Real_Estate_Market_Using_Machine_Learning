    UNDER OFFER\r\n\r\nThis Detached Residence is located on the outskirts of Kinsale Town on an elevated site overlooking the Bandon river and estuary. The property offers a wealth of space for a family due to the split-level design of the house and is close to schools, creches, shopping facilities etc. Standing on c. 0.66 acre of tiered levels of landscaped gardens with mature planting and a beautiful sandstone patio area to the front to soaking up the stunning water views of this fabulous location.\r\n \r\nThe house extends to total floor area of approx 267 sq meters ( 2875 sq ft) and comprises of a bright living room cum dining area with an open fireplace and double glass doors leading to a balcony which overlooks the river. There is a separate lounge area with double doors leading to the country style kitchen. There are also four double bedrooms on this floor. Three of the bedrooms are en-suite and in addition, there is a family bathroom and a utility room. On the ground floor there is an additional bedroom/store room and a study. The attic is fully accessible for easy storage options.\r\n \r\nThe house has been designed to make the most of the beautiful views and mature gardens. It has been very well maintained by the current owners and is presented in very good condition. Large windows offer stunning views from all living areas and the master bedroom. The house provides an idyllic spot to enjoy the wonderful views on the river. \r\n \r\nExternally there is a separate garage and a large patio area. Additionally the property comes with a mooring.
