    Bowe Property present The Boathouse, Knocknacbinny, Kinsale. A stunning lifestyle property on a wonderful elevated site overlooking the Bandon river estuary and the beautiful surrounding rolling countryside. Enjoying a private yet convenient location a mere 6km from Kinsale town centre, the Boathouse, its grounds & out buildings offer itself as a most interesting and charming lifestyle countryside coastal property. Being approximately 12 years of age this two storey home has been designed to encapsulate and take benefit of its stunning setting offering those seeking the perfect countryside property & all the benefits of convenient countryside living at its best. Accommodation consists of a wonderfully hypnotic open plan kitchen/dining/living area with near floor to ceiling windows capturing the essence of its location & wonderful water views. A down stairs wet room & cloak/plant room compliments four bedrooms & family bathroom located on the first floor, with master bedroom encompassing a semi-open plan master suite with bespoke bathroom with oversized power shower & featuring free standing bath tub with double doors & feature glazed window out onto a glass & high tension suspended steel balcony with what can only be described as breath taking fish eye countryside & river views. The property boasts a host of features too numerous to mention but the manner in which this property has been appointed, designed & set within its site is quite simply stunningly beautiful & utterly charming. The property is approached via a private drive with car parking on a sunken level in order to allow the elevated gardens & grounds to enjoy uninterrupted views. A very large garage store with a raised internal deck is in itself a wonderful space with huge versatility & the landscaping of the grounds with manicured lawns, bespoke hard landscaping and raised decks off an L-Shaped wooden clad storage container outbuilding with bi-fold doors offered as fantastic outdoor storage.
