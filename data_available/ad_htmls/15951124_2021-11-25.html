This delightful double fronted bay window residence offers the ultimate in luxurious modern living in this premier setting within walking distance of a host of local amenities while also benefiting from a delightful and private south facing rear garden. \r\n\r\nThis outstanding home boasts a large bright welcoming entrance hall from which the living room and family room are directly accessed.
