    Savills proudly presents Bramble House, a stunning five bedroom detached home moments from Dooks beech and golf links course.\n\nThis exceptional property is set on a picturesque site with direct water frontage onto Lough Yganavan and provides uninterrupted views of the majestic McGillicuddy Reeks. \n\nUpon arrival, you are greeted by wrought iron electric gates which swing gently open and guide you down a gravel driveway that sweeps around the property 360 degrees for easy entry and exit.\n\nStepping over the threshold, brings you to a welcoming entrance hallway which grants access to a large south facing lounge, a spacious and bright kitchen/dining room with large utility room complete with fully shelved pantry. On the ground floor, you will also find two en-suite double bedrooms. Making your way up a gradually ascending staircase reveals three more bedrooms, two of which are en-suite and the master bathroom.\n\nThe property is well designed to take full advantage of the southerly orientation and breathtaking water and mountain views.\n\nMaking your way into the rear garden brings you to an oasis of calm and privacy. Here, you can begin to appreciate the beauty of this stunning site. The gardens which are level and easily accessed, are laid out in perfect balance and provide three individual walkways, two of which are treelined and arched in parts to provide shelter. These beautiful walkways guide you through the grounds to arrive at the waters edge where you will find access to a rowing boat, ideal for those who like to go fishing. Within the gardens you will find a choice of seating areas, three walk bridges, a variety of landscaped gardens to include an attractive putting green and large wooden garden shed. The gardens truly are remarkable and must be viewed to be fully appreciated. They are home to an array of tree life such as Alder, Birch, Apple and Plum trees and plant life such as Hydrangeas, Rhododendrons, Heather beds, Escallonia and Montbretia.\n\nBramble House is within a close proximity of Dooks Beach, which is a lovely sandy beach located just off the Ring of Kerry Road. The highly regarded Dooks Links Golf course is just opposite Dooks beach whilst the popular Jack' s Coastguard fish restaurant is also within easy reach. The bustling town of Killorglin is within an approx.10-minute drive, whilst Killarney is within approx. a 30 mins drive. \n\nFor those looking for an ideal family home or indeed holiday home, in the Kingdom of Kerry, Bramble House offers something very special.\n\nBER DETAILS\nBER Rating: C2\nBER Number: 112194832\nEnergy Performance Indicator: 179.59 kWh/m2/yr\n\nVIEWING\nBy appointment with Savills Cork\n(021) 427 1371 or email:cork@savills.ie\n\nDistance to Airports:\nFarranfore Airport, Co Kerry - Approx. 30 mins drive\nCork Airport, Cork - Approx 1 hour 50 mins\nShannon Airport, Co Clare - Approx. 2 hours\n\nGround floor \n\nHall Inviting space with a tiled floor.\n\nLounge Accessed via glass panelled door, this room benefits from a marble fireplace with gas fire insert, cornicing and recessed lighting, a dual aspect and breathtaking water and mountain views.\n\nKitchen/Dining Room Spacious and bright room with an abundance of eye and floor level units, granite worktops, island unit, large solid fuel stove, and stunning lake and mountain views.\n\nUtility Room Tiled floor.
