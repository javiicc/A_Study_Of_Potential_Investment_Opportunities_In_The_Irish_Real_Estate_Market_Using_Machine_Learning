    Bohan Hyland & Associates are delighted to present to the market this magnificent double-fronted detached five bedroom family home with large attic conversion. It is rare that a house of such superb quality and finish comes to market in the area. The current owners completed a comprehensive refurbishment to create a stunning ground floor layout.\r\n\r\nThroughout, there are generous proportions, and a wonderful quality of light abounds. The property is in turn-key condition awaiting its lucky new owner. A particularly attractive feature of the property is the large sunroom extension which has folding glass doors leading to a maintenance free rear garden for indoor/outdoor living. \r\n\r\nThe property has been thoughtfully modernised and improved by the current owners and includes a large attic conversion which includes an ensuite and walk-in closet and is ideal for use as a home office or family use. The windows have recently been upgraded to PVC Double glazed units with Vista Therm Glass.\r\n\r\nThe property overlooks a large open green to the front and is ideal for a young family to enjoy. The property is a 4 min walk to The Gallops Luas stop.\r\n\r\nThe property briefly comprises of: \r\n\r\nEntrance hallway with Porcelain Tiles,\r\nSitting room with Feature Fireplace and Walnut Solid Flooring.\r\nGames Room with Laminated Flooring\r\nDownstairs WC with Porcelain Tiles, WC, WHB and Heated Towel Rail.\r\nUtility Room with Washer/Dryer, Sink and Porcelain Tiles\r\nKitchen/Sunroom with Anthracite Fitted Units with Quartz Counter Tops. Feature Island Unit. Fischer & Paykell 6 Ring Cooker. Built in Microwave. Wine Cooler. Samsung American Style Fridge/Freezer. Bosch Integrated Silent Dishwasher. 20k watt Stove. Roof light, Porcelain Tiles, Underfloor Heating and Folding Glass Doors which lead to a Maintenance Free Garden.\r\n\r\nUpstairs\r\n\r\n2 large double bedrooms 1 with ensuite 3 smaller Double bedrooms, 2 with built in wardrobes. 1 bedroom is currently being used as a walk-in wardrobe.\r\nFamily Bathroom with W.C and W.H.B. Bath with Shower overhead\r\nAttic Conversion with ensuite and walk-in closet\r\n\r\nOutside the driveway is paved in granite cobblestones with off-street parking for 3 cars. Prepped for electric gate.\r\nThe Maintenance free rear garden is part decking/part artificial lawn and includes outdoor sockets and tap.\r\n\r\nThe Gallops is a very popular and much sought after mature development. With everything on your doorstep. The Gallops is extremely well serviced in terms of transport links, with the LUAS stopping at Glencairn, The Gallops and Leopardstown Valley, the M50 (exit 15) and Dublin Bus (routes 63 and 47) all offering access to Dublin City Centre and beyond. There are plenty of options for schools both junior and secondary, all within close proximity.\r\n\r\nAll your shopping and recreational needs are facilitated nearby with Dundrum shopping centre, Carrickmines retail park, Stepaside Village, Sandyford Village and Leopardstown shopping centre all within easy reach. Sandyford Business District, Leopardstown Business Park and Cherrywood Business Park are all easily accessible.\r\n\r\nThe local childrens playground, tennis court, basketball court are all a few minutes' walk from the property making this a wonderful location in which to live and rear a family. There are numerous sports and recreational facilities in the vicinity, including Westwood Gym, local rugby, football, golf and tennis clubs. The nearby Dublin Mountains offer a wide range of pursuits including, hill walking, horse riding and mountain biking. Nearby, the newly built Samuel Beckett Civic Amenity offers a gym and all-weather pitches. \r\n\r\nViewing is highly recommended.\r\n\r\nDisclaimer Bohan Hyland endeavour to maintain accurate depictions of properties in all advertising including measurements and descriptions, however, these are intended only as a guide and purchasers must satisfy themselves by personal inspection.
