    Ideally located to a very a quiet part of this mature and well maintained sought after development of Castle Manor you will find No 69, a 3 bedroom semi-detached property situated on an end site not overlooked to the front or back. Accommodation to the Ground Floor is briefly comprised of a large kitchen/dining room, sitting room, downstairs WC and a sun/family room extension to the rear of the property together with 3 bedrooms, one En-suite and a family Bathroom to First Floor. This ideal location less than 500m from both a National & Secondary school makes this a perfect location for the growing family. The location within a short walk of Drogheda town centre, Our Lady of Lourdes Hospital and many local amenities is sure to appeal to a wide variety of discerning buyers.
