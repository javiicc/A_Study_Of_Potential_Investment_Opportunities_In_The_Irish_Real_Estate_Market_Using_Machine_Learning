    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to present No 4 Adrian Avenue to the market, a stunning 1930 s built 3 bedroom semi-detached home. Presented in excellent decorative order, this home has a cosy yet deceptively spacious interior, having been extended in recent years with many tastefully restored original features remaining. A great sense of space and light permeates throughout this property, where every piece of space has been utilised to the maximum. A welcoming entrance hall with family bathroom leads to an open plan kitchen/dining room with utility room and separate living room. Upstairs there are three bedrooms with the large master ensuite. On the top floor the attic space has been converted and is currently in use as a home office with additional eaves storage.\r\n \r\nThe private rear garden is laid out in lawn and paved in part offering an ideal sun trap while the front offers secure off-street car parking for three cars. Convenience goes hand in hand with this property. \r\n \r\nAdrian Avenue is within walking distance of the villages of Rathgar, Harold s Cross and Terenure with an excellent choice of schools, parks such as Harold s Cross and Bushy Park and many shops and restaurants on its doorstep. It is less than two miles from St. Stephen s Green and a short drive from Dundrum Town Centre and the M50. In summary, this is a wonderful family home oozing warmth, space and style.\r\n\r\nEntrance Hall\r\n\r\nLiving Room 3.44m x 3.49m.\r\n\r\nDining Room 3.18m x 3.30m.\r\n\r\nLiving/Family Room 2.26m x 4.48m.\r\n\r\nKitchen 2.48m x 4.58m.\r\n\r\nUtility Room 2.12m x 2.02m.\r\n\r\nBathroom 1.66m x 2.04m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 3.24m x 3.71m.\r\n\r\nEnsuite 1.89m x 2.18m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 3.93m x 2.64m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 3 2.39m x 2.72m.
