    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Flynn & Associates welcome this unique opportunity to acquire this striking 4 bedroom detached home extending to c.90 sq m in size for sale on the open market. Positioned on a generous c.1 acre site on the outskirts of Skerries, this home is inclusive of two stone cut sheds- one being an open barn and another shed and a large garden plus multiple parking spaces. The striking fa\xc3\xa7ade befits the history of this family home, with a number of generous bedrooms, reception and bathrooms the home offers huge potential. In need of modernisation and refurbishment to create a contemporary living space and home. The property requires upgrade. However, recent works have created a comfortable home which was occupied up until recent. \n\nAccommodation comprises of entrance hallway, living room, kitchen / dining room with back entrance, downstairs bedroom with upgraded en-suite. Upstairs possesses three large bedrooms and bathroom. \n\nSuperbly located on the outskirts of the picturesque & historical seaside town of Skerries which has a thriving & friendly community. The property is a short drive from Milverton school, Skerries Schools, shops, boutiques, pubs & restaurants and many other local amenities including Sailing, Rugby, GAA & Golf clubs, all on it`s doorstep. The coast is lined with long sandy beaches and the surrounding countryside is mature with lovely tree lined roads. Convenient to Rush and Lusk and within easy commuting distance of Dublin Airport and the City Centre. The area is well serviced by bus and rail and is a short distance to the M1/M50 motorways.\n\nThese particulars and details are for identification purposes only and any interested parties should satisfy themselves as to the accuracy of same. \n\n\n\n \nAccomodation:
