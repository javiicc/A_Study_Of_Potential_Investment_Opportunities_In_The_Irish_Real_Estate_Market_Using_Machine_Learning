    **Viewing Saturday 6th November**\r\n\r\nMullery O Gara are delighted to present this comfortable 2 bedroom apartment in one of Rathfarnham's most exclusive and sought after developments tucked away behind electric gates, surrounded by beautifully landscaped grounds on the cusp of the village itself.\r\n\r\nThis turn key apartment has been well cared for by its current owners and boasts a bright airy interior throughout which is complimented by its SE facing balcony flooding the space with natural light. Built in 2001 by Cosgrave developments, this superb development offers the very best in terms of quality, privacy and location. The development has beautifully landscaped grounds including central courtyards with water fountains. \r\n\r\nAccommodation comprises entrance hallway, open plan living/dining room, kitchen, 2 bedrooms (master ensuite) bathroom.\r\n\r\nThere are a number of amenities on the doorstep including Rathfarnham Shopping Centre and Rathfarnham Village. The area is very well serviced by public transport with a number of bus routes including the 15B, 15D, 75, 61 & 17 stops near the entrance to the development. Charleville Square is c.6km from the city centre and is easily accessible by foot, bicycle, car and public transport. There is also easy access to the M50.
