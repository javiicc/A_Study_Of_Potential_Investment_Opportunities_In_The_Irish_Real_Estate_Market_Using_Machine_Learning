    DNG Martin OConnor Land Sales are delighted to offer for sale by Private Treaty this fine plot of land on the N59 the Galway to Clifden Road which also includes a large section of Ross Lake. The property extends to c. 19 acres in total which includes the lake area and a section of the Old Galway to Clifden Railway Line. The digitised map area of of the land extends to c. 4.77 Ha (11.78 Acres).\r\n\r\nThe land in one block is a well kept holding with a large stone wall along the roadside together with a cattle crush and a separate loading pen at the entrance. The land all in pasture is laid out in several fields which are all stock proof with some suited to meadow.\r\n\r\nThe property is located 4.8 km from Moycullen village centre and 3.7 km from the access point of the proposed Moycullen Village By Pass which is due to being construction this year.\r\n\r\nThe land has the unique appeal of having the Old Galway to Clifden Railway line (the proposed Greenway) running through the property which coupled with the direct lake access lends itself to so many opportunities such as an Agri Tourism enterprise, water sports venture and much more.\r\n\r\nFurthermore the property presents itself as an excellent opportunity for those looking to have a sizeable area of land to work with and for those looking to expand an existing holding in the area.\r\n\r\nFor further details and maps please contact DNG Martin OConnor Land Sales on 091866708.\r\n\r\nFeatures\r\nRoadside Plot of c.19 acres which includes a section of Ross Lake\r\nDigitised map land area of c.4.77 Ha (11.78 Acres)\r\nIn one block\r\nGood road frontage\r\nConveniently located 4.8 km outside Moycullen Village\r\nPerfect for hobby farm or those looking to expand existing holding\r\nHuge potential to be developed in to an Agri Tourism enterprise, water sports venture and much more\r\nProposed Greenway to go through the property\r\nOn Ross Lake\r\n\r\nBER Details\r\nBER: Exempt\r\n\r\nDirections\r\nTaking the N59 Galway - Clifden road, continue through Moycullen village for 4.8 km.
