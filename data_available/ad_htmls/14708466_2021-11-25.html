    A dream project for anyone seeking a traditional cottage in a scenic south-west Donegal to renovate. Originally built as a National School, and later extended and converted into a family home. Vacant this past few years, the present owners have partly cleared out the house internally, leaving it ready for refurbishment. Internal accommodation comprises sitting room, dining room, kitchen, three bedrooms plus family bathroom. There is a detached apartment / office plus a storage shed at the rear. Set on an elevated site with an expansive rural view. Located midway between Glenties town and Ardara town. Early viewing of this property is highly recommended.
