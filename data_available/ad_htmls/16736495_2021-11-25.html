    Riverfield Lodge is a spacious detached family residence situated in the heart of Delgany. Built in 1997 and finished to a high standard throughout, the accommodation has a great flow and boasts well-proportioned reception areas that link all living areas making it ideal for entertaining. Only a short walk to Delgany Village, and Greystones only minutes by car, all essential amenities are within easy reach. This home offers something a little bit different from the norm with a countryside setting, having access to schools and leisure facilities just minutes away.\r\n\r\nThe spacious accommodation comprises of an entrance porch leading into to a large hallway, where there are two reception rooms: a snug family room to the rear with a southern aspect, and a large triple aspect living room. Both enjoying direct access to the southerly rear garden and patio area. There is also an additional small tv room which connects to the spacious fully integrated kitchen/dining room to the rear. The ground floor is completed by a wc and a utility/boot room which provides access to the side exterior of the property. \r\n\r\nThe four generous double bedrooms are located on the first floor crowned by the principal suite with its large en suite, and fully fitted walk in dressing room. The remainder of the bedrooms are well proportioned and are carpeted throughout. The first floor is completed by a family bathroom equipped with a whb and bathtub with a shower. \r\n\r\nRiverfield Lodge is in the highly sought after location of Delgany. Delgany Village boasts, amongst other amenities, The Delgany which houses The Pigeon House Cafe and The Firehouse Bakery, the Bear Paw deli, Farrelly\xe2\x82\xacTM craft butchers, The Horse and Hound Pub and Restaurant, and the renowned Wicklow Arms which is due to reopen in the coming weeks along with a brand new Centra convenience store. Greystones town is a short drive and has a variety of shops and eateries to choose from as well as beautiful coastal walks including the Greystones to Bray Cliff Walk. Sports facilities and clubs are in abundance with rugby, soccer, GAA, tennis, sailing and golf being just a small example of what is on offer. Transport links include easy access to the N11/M50, Greystones DART station, Aircoach and Dublin Bus routes.\r\n\r\nOutside, to the front the property is approached by a gravel stone driveway which provides parking for numerous cars. Two side passageways lead to the private south facing rear garden featuring a patio terrace the whole width of the house (onto which the principal ground floor rooms all open) ideal for al fresco dining and entertaining.
