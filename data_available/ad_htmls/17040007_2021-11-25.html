    Welcome to Seamount Rise, a stunning new development by Ballymore. Seamount Rise offers modern, stylish living in a breath-taking coastal setting. All homes at Seamount Rise combine modern design and high-quality finishes. Residents will benefit from a new 13-acre park with playground to be developed by Fingal County Council, adding to the abundance of scenic open spaces and parklands that Malahide already offers. Seamount Rise boasts great transport links with bus services to the airport and city centre and the added benefit of Malahide Train Station, just a 15-min walk away offering both DART and suburban commuter links. Seamount Rise offers homes for living, now and for decades to come. For more information and to register your interest, visit www.seamountrise.com.\r\nSpecification:\r\nEnergy Efficient Homes:\r\nHomes at Seamount Rise feature levels of energy efficiency that far exceed the average home. An array of features combine to ensure lower energy usage. High levels of insulation are incorporated in the walls, floors and roofs to provide a greener home and a more sustainable way of living. All houses will achieve an A-rating.\r\nExternal Finishes:\r\nAttractive brick facades to each house.\r\nDriveways:\r\nThere are private driveways for two car parking spaces for each house.\r\nGardens:\r\nThe enclosed gardens offer a wonderful extension of the living space at the property. Rear gardens are seeded. Brass garden tap and wall mounted outdoor light are provided as standard.\r\nOpen Spaces:\r\nAll open space within the scheme will be landscaped to a superior standard. Homeowners will also benefit from a new adjoining 13-acre park by Fingal County Council with stunning views across the coast\r\nCeiling Heights:\r\nEach home benefits from an elevated ceiling height of 2.7 metres on both ground and first floor.\r\nTiling:\r\nHigh-quality floor and wall tiling in shower and bath enclosures is standard in bathrooms and en suites.\r\nWardrobes:\r\nWardrobes have a contemporary design with assorted storage and hanging options.\r\n\r\nInternal Finishes:\r\nAll walls and ceilings are painted throughout.\r\nSecurity:\r\nHomes are pre-wired to facilitate an alarm system.\r\nElectrical: \r\nGenerous light and power points. Contemporary switches and sockets throughout with 5-amp sockets located in the living room, master bedroom and kitchen. Recessed spot lighting in bathrooms, hallways and kitchens with pendant light fittings elsewhere. \r\nHeating Systems:\r\nAn air to water heat pump system provides domestic hot water and efficient heating. \r\nUtility Rooms:\r\nEach home has a separate utility room. \r\nKitchens: \r\nStylish and modern kitchens with full height wall units, quartz worktops and splashbacks. Other features include undercounter and kickboard LED strip lighting. Integrated appliances are provided (assuming contract is signed within 21 days) \r\nBathrooms and en suites: \r\nContemporary designed bathrooms and en suites featuring superior quality floor and wall tiling with a stylish vanity unit, modern fixtures and heated towel rail. \r\nWindows & Doors: \r\nAll windows and patio doors are energy saving aluminium. \r\nFront doors are high performance composite. Multi-point locking system to doors and windows. \r\nSide Gates: \r\nAll houses have hardwood side gates. \r\nStaircases: \r\nEach house features staircases with modern design features and oak handrails. \r\nCharge Point: \r\nAll homes are pre-wired to facilitate an electrical vehicle charge point. \r\nGuarantee:\r\nEach home at Seamount Rise is covered by a 10-year structural guarantee scheme.
