    David Ross Estate Agents your local property experts are delighted to present new to the market 8 Reillys Avenue \r\n\r\n\r\n8 reillys avenue is a little gem that was fully refurbished and extended in 2012 . Rewired, re plumbed the house has a combi boiler and a water tank, a new ceiling for the conversion of the attic and new purloins to strengthen the roof. It has surface of 70 sqm, without including the attic that could easily be used as a studio with a huge storage capability. \r\nTo deal with the problem of dampness so common in old houses the whole floor was raised and insulated with radon barrier and thermal panels, (this is the reason why this property differently from the others in the street has a step at the front door) while the exposure of the bricks as an aesthetic feature allow at the same time the walls to breathe. Roofs, floors, internal and external walls were all insulated with 80 and 100 mm panels. \r\n \r\nNo 8 is deceptively spacious, and is awaiting a new owner to add their own touch to the property, located in an excellent location a stone's throw away from the city centre. \r\n \r\nViewing is a must
