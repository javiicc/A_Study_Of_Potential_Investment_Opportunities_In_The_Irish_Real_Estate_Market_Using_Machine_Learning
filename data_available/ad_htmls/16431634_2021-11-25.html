    Contemporary, spacious 1st floor apartment with stunning West facing balcony set in this highly sought-after development within the heart of Dublin 4. The Gasworks offers secure, chic living in a convenient location suitable for those who wish to downsize, invest or a first-time buyer.\n\nNo. 22 The William Bligh is a bright spacious apartment ideal for modern day living and offers exceptionally well-proportioned and stylish accommodation throughout. In brief the accommodation comprises entrance hall with utility room and large storeroom, open plan living room / dining room, a modern stylish kitchen, bedroom and bathroom. There is a sunny balcony off the bright and spacious living area and bedroom.\n\nThe property is ideally located within walking distance of the City Centre and local villages such as Ballsbridge, Sandymount and Ringsend. Excellent amenities in the area include the Aviva Stadium, SportsCo Leisure Centre and the superb 3 Arena, not to mention Bath Avenue, host to the popular Bath Pub, Lotts & Co Grocers and The Chop House restaurant to name but a few. The property also benefits from close proximity to Grand Canal Dart Station with local employers including Google, Facebook, and some of the city s leading law firms.\n\n\nEntrance Hall With recessed lighting, utility room, large storeroom, radiator covers and intercom system.\n\nUtility/Store Room Large storage area plumbed for washing machine\n\nBathroom Suite comprising bath with overhead shower, wc, wash hand basin, wall hung mirror, electric shaver points, fully tiled walls and floor\n\nBedroom Large double room with floor to ceiling windows and extensive built-in wardrobes\n\nLiving/Dining Room Large open plan room with floor to ceiling windows, tv points and door to west facing balcony\n\nKitchen Fitted wall and floor units, stainless steel sink unit, built in dishwasher, oven with four ring gas hob, splash-back, extractor fan, built in fridge/freezer and recessed lighting\n\nBalcony South Westerly facing, decked, glass railing and overlooking the communal gardens
