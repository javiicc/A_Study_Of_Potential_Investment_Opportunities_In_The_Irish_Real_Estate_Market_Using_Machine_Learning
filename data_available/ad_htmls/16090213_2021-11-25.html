    OMD Estate Agents are delighted to present this 3 bed terraced Guinness home with an impressive south facing rear garden. The property is not overlooked to the front or rear and is in need of modernization. \r\n\r\nSpanning approx. 70 SqM, this family home consists of hall, w.c, shower room, living room and kitchen. Upstairs comprises of 3 well proportioned rooms (master en suite). \r\nThe windows are PVC double glazed, fitted in 2010 and the gas boiler was fitted in 7 years ago.\r\nIt has a shared side entrance leading to an impressive 122 foot sunny rear garden. The south facing garden has 3 sheds/ outhouses too.\r\n \r\nSuperbly situated with close proximity to a host of local amenities and within walking distance of Terenure, Kimmage, HaroldsCross and Rathmines Villages, excellent local schools, Gael Scolilenna and Terenure College, Templeogue College and St Mary's College. With many local churches of varied faiths, parks, eateries and pubs all on its doorstep. There are excellent public transport links nearby providing easy access to the City Centre and beyond.
