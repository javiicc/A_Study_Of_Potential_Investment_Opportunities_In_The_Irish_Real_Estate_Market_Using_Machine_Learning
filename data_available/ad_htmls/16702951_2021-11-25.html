    No.19 Tobar Ban is a four bedroom semi-detached house presented to the market in good condition and suitable for immediate occupancy. The house is on an elevated site with good views across Kells and onto green fields to the front and it has a large private back garden. This bright house is well laid out with an open plan kitchen dining area which is facing onto the back garden. The property commands a private position on a cul de sac within the mature estate. The current owners have consistently improved the condition and presentation of the house and as a result each room is in good condition with a good level of specification throughout.\r\n\r\nTobar Ban is conveniently located in the centre of Kells on the Oldcastle Road, residents can walk to the local schools, public transport, restaurants and to the broad range of sporting facilities within the town. Tobar Ban also benefits from convenient access Dublin with the M3 located just 2.5km away.\r\n\r\nThe ground floor accommodation includes an entrance hallway, living room, open plan kitchen / dining area and guest W.C. On the first floor the there are three double bedrooms with a master ensuite, a single bedroom and a main bathroom. Viewings by appointment.
