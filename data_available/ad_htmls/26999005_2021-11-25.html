      ***** AVAILABLE FURNISHED OR UNFURNISHED ***** \n\n\nFirst Letting of No. 2 Lansdowne Lane! No. 2 is a very attractive two storey mid terrace mews presented in excellent condition. The accommodation is bright, spacious and tastefully presented. The new open plan kitchen has a range of eye and floor level units, work top counter and dining area. The living room has central gas fire effect fireplace and wood flooring throughout. On the first floor two large double bedrooms have fitted wardrobes whilst the master bedroom also has a shower ensuite. The family bathroom completes the first floor. Features of the property include gas fired central heating, double glazing throughout and designated off street parking. There is also external storage unit. \n\nIdeal located adjacent to Ballsbridge Village and Sandymount Village within minutes walk of shops, restaurant and Dart Station. City Centre, Grand Canal Dock, Grafton Street and East link bridge are all within easy access.
