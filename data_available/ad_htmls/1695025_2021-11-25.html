    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Situated on a large site of 0.75 acres approx. is a modern three bedroom bungalow finished both internally and externally to the highest standard. Located on the edge of Killygordon the property is south facing with commanding views over a large part of the Finn Valley. The bungalow is centrally located on the site and there is potential to develop additional properties on the site subject to planning. Internal accommodation comprises Living Room, Kitchen, Dining Room, 3 Bedrooms, Bathroom and an office at the rear which could easily be converted to a fourth bedroom.
