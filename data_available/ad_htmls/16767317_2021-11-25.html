    McGovern Estates is delighted to showcase Donarea Lodge. A wonderful opportunity arises to acquire this spacious detached period family residence. Although in need of refurbishment this property offers endless potential to create a modern & contemporary property in this sought-after location while preserving & enhancing the period charm. In addition, there is plenty of off-street parking to the front and a private rear garden. Internal viewing is highly recommended.\r\n\r\nThis detached period four-bedroom family home extends to 185m2 (1,991 sq.ft.) and briefly comprises of a large living room, kitchen, utility room, family room, WC, 4 double bedrooms and a large family bathroom. \r\n\r\nKilcoole, once a sleepy coastal village, is now a thriving up and coming community with many facilities including a secondary school, two primary schools, local shopping centre, pubs, church and the world-class Druid's Glen Hotel & golf club. \r\n\r\nKilcoole is situated in a stunning location between the coast and the Wicklow mountains, giving easy access to the local beaches, countryside and nearby villages of Greystones, Delgany and Enniskerry.\r\nGreystones is located just 2 miles away and a host of sport and leisure clubs abound in the area including sailing, rowing, football, rugby, tennis and GAA. \r\n\r\nThe Shoreline Leisure Centre and swimming pool is close by, as are a great choice of renowned golf clubs, including Delgany, Greystones, Powerscourt and Druids Glen.\r\n\r\nJust 2 miles from the N11 with its new link to the M50 and 18 miles from Dublin City, Kilcoole is a perfect location for the commuter. Kilcoole is served by bus and mainline train routes to the City Centre and to Greystones for the DART.\r\n\r\nACCOMMODATION \r\n\r\nEntrance Hallway \r\nCarpeted, decorative coving, smoke alarm. \r\n\r\nLiving Room \r\nCarpeted, feature open fire with tile hearth & surround, period bay window with original shutters. \r\n\r\nInner Hallway \r\nCarpeted.\r\n\r\nKitchen\r\nTiled flooring, fully fitted kitchen with wall based & floor based storage cupboards, countertop space with stainless steel sink unit.\r\n\r\nFamily Room \r\nCarpeted, feature fire place, feature bay window with original shutters.\r\n\r\nWC\r\nLaminate wood floor, WC, pedestal wash hand basin. \r\n\r\nBedroom 1 \r\nCarpeted original fire place, decorative coving, picture bay window.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 \r\nCarpeted, feature fire place with cast iron surround & mantle. \r\n\r\nBedroom 3\r\nCarpeted, ornate cast iron fire place, West facing window with original shutters. \r\n\r\nBedroom 4 \r\nCarpeted, double doors leading to rear garden. \r\n\r\nFamily Bathroom \r\nLaminate wood floor wash hand basin with storage under, fully tiled bath unit with electric Triton T90 shower, ornate cast iron fire place. \r\n\r\nGarden \r\nDonarea is approached via a winding gravelled driveway with a lawned area with a feature granite stone wall, flower beds either side of the front door host shrubbery, flowers and have an alternative granite stone border.
