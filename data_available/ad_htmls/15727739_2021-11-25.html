    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    For Sale by Private Treaty \r\n\r\nSmall Equestrian holding on c 13 acres known as COPPERFIELD STUD, Knocklong Co. Limerick.. \r\nThis small Equestrian holding on c 13 acres is situated about 3 miles from Hospital Village on the main Hospital/Knocklong/Mitcheltown road (R513) in the hearth of prime Scarteen and Tipperary Foxhounds Hunting & Fishing countryside and in a much sought after rural location directly opposite Kilfrush Stud. This property has been the subject of considerable financial expenditure on new Equestrian facilities & these include as follows: \r\n3 Span Barn with 4 Loose Boxes. The Main stable yard newly built comprises of 11 Boxes, being one block of five & one block of six to include Tack room. Each stable approx.144 Sq feet is fitted with non-drip sheeted roof & have built in feed pots. ESB supply outside each Box & Water supply there on. Dungstead with slatted tank. There are 5 turn-out paddocks equipped with Equi-safe fencing & Paddock gates. Two furlong circular Sand Gallop. Open Barn\r\nSand arena with outdoor winter shelter. Useful range of out offices. The lands are treated with SOBAC a natural organic fertiliser and enhances mineral dense grass growth. 6 acre field all in permanent pasture with the River Cumouge renowned for its trout fishing forming the boundary at one end. \r\nThe Main residence which has recently been completely refurbished comprises of spacious Bespoke fitted Ivory kitchen with Oil-fired Aga Sorn cooker. Tiled floor. Sliding door to patio area . Office area with internet access point.Pine floor Sitting Room with open hearth Marble & Granite fireplace. Wood burning stove installed. Sliding patio door to well maintained garden area.\r\nMain En-suite bedroom with built in wardrobe. En-suite with wc,whb & shower.\r\nBedroom 2. En-Suite Double Bedroom. Tile floor Utility area with separate toilet & shower. \r\nThe property is fitted with double glaze uPVC windows and doors throughout. Oil fired central heating. \r\nOutside: Herb garden. Sizeable mature garden with Beech trees & numerous varieties of young trees & shrubs. Electric double entrance gates. Chipped driveway & forecourt. The property is ideally suited as a Breeze up yard, Race Horse Training facility, for the speculating Pinhooker, or for anyone interested in acquiring a property suited for Hunters being located in the hearth of Scarteen and Tipperary Foxhound countryside.\r\nVendor: Ms. Catherine Cooper \r\nSols; Ml P Donnell & Co Tralee Co. Kerry.\r\nPrice on Application.\r\nViewing Arrangements Strictly By Appointment Through Sole Selling Agents.
