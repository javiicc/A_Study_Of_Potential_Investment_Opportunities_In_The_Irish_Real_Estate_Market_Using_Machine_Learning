    Superb three Bedroom semi-detached property available for sale in Dun Na Ri, Ennis, Co Clare. Built in 2000 the property superbly located within minutes of St Flannans College and within walking distance of Ennis Town Centre.\r\n\r\nOn entering the main entrance hallway, the quality tile flooring leads to an equally stunning main reception room where a feature Nestor Martin oil stove, solid oak timber flooring and a large front aspect bay window provide an unexcelled ambiance of comfort and homely feel. A rear patio door from the dining area merges onto an extensive decking area and garden. It is worth noting that the decking, and garden are south facing. Rounding off the ground floor is the guest WC neatly tucked under the stairs. On the first floor there are two double bedrooms (one which features a bespoke walk-in wardrobe), a single bedroom and a recently renovated bathroom.\r\n\r\nExternally to the front there is a cobble lock driveway, and the garden is in lawn.
