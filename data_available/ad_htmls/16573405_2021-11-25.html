    Sherry FitzGerald O Reilly are pleased to present to market number 62 Millbank, a three-bedroom terraced home in Sallins Village, with spacious front and rear gardens.\nMillbank is beside the Grand Canal and perfectly situated for waterside strolls. It s just a short walk from Sallins many shops, bars and restaurants, and Naas is a few minutes away by car with further amenities and leisure facilities.\nThis property is ideally located for the commuter, as it is close to the new Sallins bypass leading to a 4 minute drive to the N7/M7 Junction 9a. It is a ten minute walk to the Arrow Rail Station with commuter trains to Heuston Station and the Docklands.\nThe house offers spacious family friendly accommodation entrance hall, living room and kitchen /dining room, utility room and guest wc. Upstairs there are three double bedrooms (one en-suite) and a family bathroom.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 5.22m x 1.96m. The bright entrance hall has a beechwood floor and carpet to stairs.\n\nLiving Room 5.4m x 3.67m. The spacious living room with large bay window has a beech wood floor and a slate fireplace with wooden surround and inset gas fire. With ceiling and wall lighting, and double doors to Dining area.\n\nKitchen / Dining Room 5.63m x 3.1m. The spacious kitchen/dining room with view of the garden has many shaker style units and a tiled splashback. It incorporates a gas hob and oven, freestanding fridge freezer and dishwasher. With a tiled floor and sliding doors to garden.\n\nUtility room 1.49m x 1.03m. The Utility includes storage presses, worktop and washing machine and tumble dryer. It has a tiled floor.\n\nGuest WC 1.45m x 0.766m. With wc and whb, it is attractively tiled.\n\nUpstairs\n\nLanding 3.3m x 1.8m. With carpet floor and hotpress off. Attic access\n\nBedroom 1 3.6m x 3.21m. The generous master bedroom to front, has a range of fitted wardrobes and carpet floor.\n\nEnsuite 1.94m x 1.84m. With wc, whb and corner shower unit, it is fully tiled.\n\nBedroom 2 23.1m x 2.4m. With rear view, this double room has a carpet floor.\n\nBedroom 3 3.46m x 3.3m. This is a double room to rear with carpet floor.\n\nFamily Bathroom 2.38m x 1.75m. The main bathroom is fully tiled, and includes wc, whb and bath. With large Velux window overhead.\n\nOUTSIDE\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nOn entering Sallins village from the Naas, go over the railway bridge, then the canal bridge and at the Lidl sign, take the left turn. Take the third left into Millbank and the house is the second on the left hand side.
