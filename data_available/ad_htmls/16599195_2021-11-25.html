    A particularly fine, exceptionally attractive detached family residence which extends to approximately 367 sq.m (3,950 sq.ft) of bright and airy, well laid out generously proportioned accommodation that has been newly refurbished and renovated throughout. The property stands on wonderfully landscaped, well stocked sunny gardens that surround the property perfectly with a large tarmacadam parking area to the front, a large double garage/guest accommodation and a further garage housing a diesel generator and the oil-fired central heating boilers. The stunning gardens and grounds extend to approximately 0.75 of an acre and are securely positioned behind wrought iron electric gates. \n\nOn entering the property, it is obvious to see how this unique family home has been transformed into something very special. The particularly bright generously proportioned accommodation has a lovely feel to it as the rooms flow wonderfully into one another. It is ideal for family living and entertaining alike. Briefly the accommodation comprises a very spacious reception hall with guest wc, that has double doors opening into the living room and the stunning sunroom. Next is the kitchen, dining room and the extremely generous games room with a full-size snooker table concludes the accommodation at this level. Upstairs a split-level landing opens into four double bedrooms, two with ensuites, whilst the master suite is a particularly fine room with two dressing areas, a generous ensuite and has a feature octagon ceiling and doors to a large balcony. A converted attic concludes the accommodation of the main house. \n\nOutside there are two garages and the guest accommodation consists of a large living room with wide plank solid timber floor, a dining room and a well fitted kitchen. Upstairs there are two-bedroom areas and a bathroom.\n\nA particular feature of this excellent family home are the stunning gardens that are spread out in different sections surrounding the house. The front garden is lawned and has a granite path leading to a large patio area with a large pergola. To the rear of the property a large granite patio is accessed via the kitchen making it ideal for al fresco dining as it faces south and is drenched in sun all day long. Behind is a Japanese Zen Garden which is extremely attractive, and a manicured lawn sits above this and has mature shrubbery and planting in the surrounding beds. There is parking for many cars to the front and side of the property and electric wrought iron gates secure the property superbly well. The property has extensive external lighting illuminating the property and gardens beautifully.\n\nThe gardens and setting of this spacious beautifully proportioned family home will be of major interest to those trying to get a foothold in the Rathmichael area. This is an ideal location in a rural style setting surrounded by mature trees and hedging and within easy reach of local shopping facilities in Shankill Village, the DART station and library as well as major shopping centres in Bray and Cornelscourt, The Park, Carrickmines and Dundrum Shopping Centre all easily accessible off the M50. The LUAS at Cherrywood allows frequent access to and from the city centre. The M50 makes commuting to the city centre, the airport and nationwide very simple.\n\nLocal schools include Rathmichael National School, St. Anne\xe2\x80\x99s and St. Gerard\xe2\x80\x99s. Churches of both dominations are within easy reach as are walks on the eastern foothills of the Dublin Mountains. There is a selection of golf, rugby and football clubs close by, as well as horse riding.
