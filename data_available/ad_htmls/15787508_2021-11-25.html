    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Sherry FitzGerald Lannon are delighted to present Crossfield to the market, a detached 4 Bedroom bungalow with detached garage, occupying a beautiful C. 0.6 acre private and tree lined site also with an extensive patio area. The property extending to Approx. 132m /1420ft with well laid out accommodation throughout enjoys a south-west facing conservatory leading out to fabulous landscaped rear garden with many beautiful mature trees. Crossfield not overlooked to front or back is situated at the end of a short lane in an extremely convenient and desirable location with a Julianstown address. This ideal family home is seconds away from great amenities such as the newly renovated and extended Whitecross National School, north and south bound Bus Eireann bus stops, Julianstown Golf and Pitch & Putt Club and the very popular Lime Kiln Gastropub all within a very short and safe walking distance. The perfect location also means a host of beaches are within 2 or 3 minute drive, Drogheda is 6.7km away and access to the M1 motorway via Junction 7 is a mere 3.9km making Dublin Airport and the M50 orbital motorway both accessible in less than 25 minutes. This convenient property with even further endless potential is undoubtedly one of the best opportunities to come to the Julianstown market in recent times and surely not one to be missed.
