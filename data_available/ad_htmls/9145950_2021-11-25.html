      Location

      Situated off the Goatstown Road in Dublin 14 and one of South Dublin s most sought after addresses, Trimbleston is nestled between the vibrant villages of Dundrum, Ranelagh and Mount Merrion and offers a wide range of cosmopolitan restaurants, pubs, cafes, boutiques and schools as well as the popular Dundrum Town Centre. \r\n\r\n\r\nOnly 5.5 miles from the city centre, Goatstown is well connected by road, rail and bus to the city centre and beyond. The Luas green line runs through the southwest corner of Goatstown and the Dundrum Luas and Windy Arbour stops are nearby making ease of access to Dublin s city centre and the surrounding areas of Sandyford and Stillorgan easily accessible. There are also a number of bus routes, the 11 and 75 that pass through Goatstown linking it with D n Laoghaire and Tallaght. Mount Merrion and Clonskeagh are also close by as is access to both the N11 and M50. The long established Goat Bar and Restaurant is situated only a short walk from the development and Lynams convenience store is just around the corner. For those who enjoy the outdoors, the large UCD campus or the River Dodder walk are only a stroll away. Many of Dublin s top schools are nearby for families and students moving into the area, Mount Anville, Taney National School, Wesley College, Our Lady s Grove, Blackrock College and University College Dublin are all within a short distance. Deerpark, a 32-acre public park in Mount Merrion caters for nature lovers, children and local sports clubs. A visit to Airfield in Dundrum is another popular option, complete with gardens and a working farm.
