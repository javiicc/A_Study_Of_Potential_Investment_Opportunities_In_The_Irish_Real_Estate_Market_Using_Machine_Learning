    Frank Sweeney & Co. are offering for sale this detached house at Cloran, Athenry, Co. Galway. Externally there is a large tarmac rear yard and garden with mature trees and shrubbery. There is a large detached two storey garage with lean to. This is an excellent location so close to Athenry yet with countryside views. Excellently located for both the Galway to Dublin and Limerick to Tuam motorways. Athenry town has rail links to Galway, Dublin and Limerick as well as the bus link to Galway city.
