    Picturesque settings don't come better than this\r\n Nestled quietly away in countryside, this superb detached bungalow is set on a delightful site with panoramic views across rolling countryside and Lough Ree. A four bedroom residence in pristine condition, with the added benefit of a Bar, games room, toys room, gym, and apartment offering spacious living accommodation in a setting that will leave a lasting impression.\r\n Accommodation briefly comprises of entrance hall, large kitchen/ dining room, sitting room, family bathroom, four bedrooms, master with ensuite & Walk in dressing room boasting commanding views of the lakes. \r\n The property is ideally positioned being located approximately 4.6 km from the village of the roses, Glasson, host to award winning restaurants, twenty minutes' drive from the bustling town of Athlone with it myriad of amenities. Ballymahon the home of Centre Parcs, one of the biggest tourist developments in the history of the state is also a short distance away c. 15 km. Closer to home, this location is perfect for walks and relaxation being surrounded in one of the most beautiful locations in Ireland.
