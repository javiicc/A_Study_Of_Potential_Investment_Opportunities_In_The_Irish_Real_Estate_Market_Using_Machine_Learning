    Development Description

      Vartry Wood is a beautiful new scheme of traditional 2, 3 & 4 bedroom homes by Durkan. Located in the picturesque village of Ashford, Vartry Wood is at once minutes from all the amenities of the town and the great outdoors.\r\nThe homes will be finished to a very high specification and offer plenty of choice for growing families, young families wishing to put down roots and those looking to downsize.\r\n\r\nAshford is located just off the N11, a 20-minute drive south of the M50, less than a 12-minute drive north of the seaside town of Wicklow. It is also in close proximity to Greystones, Enniskerry, Kilcoole & Newtownmountkennedy. This idyllic village has a wealth of amenities to offer ranging from Mount Usher Gardens and Bel Air Equestrian Centre to the local GAA club. Local primary schools include Scoil Na Coroine Mhuire Primary School and Nun's Cross National School along with a choice of secondary schools in nearby Wicklow Town.\r\n\r\nVisit www.vartrywood.ie

      Location

      Life in Ashford ensures a great blend of easy access to Dublin city and charming country living. Located in the "Garden of Ireland", Vartry Wood is surrounded by beautiful scenery with stunning landscapes and is a stroll away from Ashford village which is home to a host of superb restaurants and cafes.\r\nNumerous transport links nearby include the Bus Eireann 133 and the N11. Having the Wicklow train station just 7km away means that access to all parts of Wicklow and Dublin are within easy reach. Those who enjoy the great outdoors will not be disappointed with the choice of exceptional viewpoints in the Wicklow Mountains such as Sally Gap and Lough Dan within arm's reach, along with the picturesque mountainous walks at Glendalough and Devil's Glen Waterfall.
