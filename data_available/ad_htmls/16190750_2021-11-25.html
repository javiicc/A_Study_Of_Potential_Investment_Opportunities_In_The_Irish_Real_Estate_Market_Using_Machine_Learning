    A unique opportunity to purchase this superbly presented detached family home in this much sought after coastal location on approx. 4.5 acres of south facing lands offering far reaching sea views. In addition, there is shared ownership of the beautifully maintained communal gardens and woodlands of the Woodside Demesne extending to approx. 3.75 acres. This wonderful property needs to be seen to be appreciated and offers significant potential for an incoming purchaser.\r\n\r\nGreenogue is a well-proportioned family home extending to approximately 195 sq. m / 2,098 sq. ft. over two floors. Discreetly positioned off Windgate Road the modest entrance of Greenogue conceals a deeply impressive home behind it. Accommodation briefly comprises an entrance hall with guest WC, a dual aspect living room to the right with oak timber flooring and a feature fireplace with gas insert. Adjacent is a modern fully fitted kitchen with integrated appliances leading into a large conservatory opening onto the garden. There are two large double bedrooms on the ground floor, one of which benefits from an en-suite shower room, while a fully fitted utility room completes the accommodation on this floor. On the first floor there are two very spacious double bedrooms, both with wonderful views of both the gardens and out to sea with views towards Ireland's Eye, Lambay Island and the Antrim coast. Finally there is a well-appointed family bathroom. The house is presented in excellent decorative order throughout and features an abundance of storage, gas fired central heating and an alarm. \r\n\r\nThe real WOW factor are the south facing lands to the rear of the house which are not overlooked, have sea views and provide privacy and seclusion while enjoying all the sunlight the day has to offer. The property is bounded by mature woodland and affords the opportunity of keeping horses or developing horticultural interests - truly a children's paradise. There is separate vehicular access independent of Woodside. Greenogue also benefits from shared ownership and use of the shared private Victorian gardens and arboretum of the Woodside Demesne. Created over 100 years ago, they are of considerable botanical, historical and aesthetic interest with residents growing their own fruit and vegetables and keeping Bee's.\r\n\r\n Greenogue is superbly situated forming part Howth's special amenity area yet is only 1km from the picturesque seaside village. It boasts a variety of shops, boutiques, specialist seafood outlets and restaurants, gastro pubs and entertainment. Residents of Howth are fortunate enough to benefit from a myriad of attractions, including the breath-taking Bog of Frogs coastal walk, Balscadden beach, Howth Castle and the Martello Tower, to name a few. There are also a number of golf courses within close proximity and Howth Yacht Club for sailing enthusiasts.\r\n\r\nThe area includes numerous transport links to all parts of the capital, with a frequent bus service and a DART station at Howth harbour. The village is located just 15km from Dublin City Centre. Meanwhile Dublin Airport can be reached within a 25 minute drive. \r\n\r\nManagement Company:\r\nHartmill Management Company\r\n\r\nManagement Fee:\r\nApprox. \xe2\x82\xac1,550 per annum \r\n\r\nAccommodation\r\n\r\n Note: \r\nPlease note we have not tested any apparatus, fixtures, fittings, or services.
