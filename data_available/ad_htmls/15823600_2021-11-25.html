    Bawnlahan is a detached coastal residence enjoying an enviable position overlooking Tragumna bay. \r\n\r\nTragumna, meaning 'strand of the oak trunk', is a small hamlet and beach, with the tiny \xe2\x82\xacDrishane Island' which is very adjacent to the shore. Tragumna beach has a blue flag status. This status relates to maintaining bathing water quality, environmental education, safety and beach management.\r\n\r\nThis property is in an elevated position and approached via a private drive which creates a wonderful sense of seclusion and privacy. The grounds (circa 1 acre) are superbly landscaped and mature with a wide variety of specimen trees and shrubs. There is a most wonderful sun terrace to the front of the property where there is a large outdoor jacuzzi and another seating area to the rear.\r\n\r\nOn first approach, the main residence is deceptive in size, extending to approx. 238 sq m (2,500 sq ft). The well-appointed accommodation consists of open plan kitchen/family room, living & dining room, conservatory, 3 en-suite bedrooms, study, utility & guest WC. The flow of the accommodation makes it very suitable to modern family living.
