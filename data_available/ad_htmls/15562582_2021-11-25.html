    Thomas Byrne Auctioneers are delighted to present to the market, the absolutely stunning "Nivard House" nestled on 2.3 acres of mature grounds. Offering both countryside living (opposite the historic and storied Monasterboice High Cross) yet only minutes from the town of Drogheda and adjacent to M1 motorway. Commanding beautiful views over the village of Tinure and the Mourne Mountains.\r\n\r\nThe accommodation, with rooms of grand propertions comprises: Reception Hall, WC, Sittingroom, Sun Room, Kitchen, Living Room, Dining Room, Utility Room, Shower Room, 6 Bedrooms (Main En- Suite) and Bathroom.
