    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    Sherry FitzGerald takes great pleasure welcoming you to Silver Firs, a superb, elevated single storey home ideally positioned in this tranquil, semi rural locality within easy commuting of Limerick City. Offering stunning views over the countryside and set on approx. 0.5 acre site with a mature rear garden, which is exceptionally private and boasts a wonderful westerly orientation, attracting the afternoon and evening sun.\n\nInternally, the property is well presented throughout and will make an ideal home for both families and downsizers alike. With bright, well proportioned accommodation all on one level, on entering the property there is a bright and welcoming entrance hall, with double doors to the left leading to a large living room. Beyond here is a fourth bedroom, which could also be used as a home office, with ensuite. There is a formal dining room with sliding doors to the conservatory, leading to a modern kitchen breakfast room, which opens into a living space complete with solid fuel stove for cosy nights in. There are three further double bedrooms, with the main bed ensuite and a family bathroom.\n\nOutside the gardens are wonderfully maintained and offer plenty of space for a growing family, or a prospective owner who enjoys outdoor living. Stretching to approx. 167ft at the rear with a large lawn and patio seating area which is not overlooked and benefits from a south westerly orientation for sun throughout the afternoon and the evenings. There is a workshop/garage at the far side of the property with light and power, with access from the front to rear.\n\nThe location is superb, Roo East is just north of Ardnacrusha and is located just c.8km from Limerick City Centre, offering easy access to the wider Clare and Tipperary regions. The area is serviced by a number of renowned primary and secondary schools, namely Scoil Ide, Parteen National School & St Munchins College.\n\n\nEntrance Hall With front door, coved ceiling, storage cupboard, wood flooring, double doors to living room and access to attic.\n\nLiving Room With coved ceiling, dado rail and feature fireplace with tiled surround.\n\nDining Room With coved ceiling and centre rose, wood flooring and sliding doors to conservatory.\n\nKitchen Breakfast Room With range of wall and base level kitchen units, drawers and work surfaces, one and a half bowl sink unit and draining board, electric oven with induction hob, cooker hood, plumbing for washing machine and dishwasher, triple glazed skylight windows, tiled floor and wood burning stove.\n\nConservatory With tiled floor, double doors, light and ceiling fan.\n\nBedroom 1 Double bedroom with coved ceiling and centre rose, wood flooring.\n\nEn-Suite With tiled floor and walls, wash hand basin with storage under, WC.\n\nBedroom 2 With coved ceiling and wood flooring.\n\nBedroom 3 With coved ceiling and wood flooring.\n\nBedroom 4 With coved ceiling and wood flooring.\n\nEn-Suite With tiled floor and walls, tiled shower cubicle, wash hand basin with storage under, WC.\n\nBathroom With tiled walls and floor, tiled shower cubicle, wash hand basin with storage under, vanity mirror with light and storage, WC, extractor unit.\n\nWorkshop/Garage With light and power and access to front and rear.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom Limerick city head north on Athlunkard Street, joining the R463 at Corbally roundabout. Continue on the road until it becomes the R465 passing McMahon' s bar on the left. Continue for another approx. 2km there is a turning on to a small road on the left and the property is on the left hand side just after this.
