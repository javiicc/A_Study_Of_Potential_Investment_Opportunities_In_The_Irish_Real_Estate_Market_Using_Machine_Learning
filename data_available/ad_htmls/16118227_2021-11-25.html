    Ocean Property Management are excited to offer for sale this superb two bedroom, first floor apartment located in the well known apartment complex of Gleann Na Ri in Renmore. The complex itself consists of 14 blocks including 275 apartments, a creche, a local retail store and a popular pizza/pasta restaurant. Gleann Na Ri is only a 10 minute drive from the city centre. The estate is gated and well maintained with beautifully landscaped gardens throughout. This first floor apartment is contained within a small apartment complex and located at the front of the estate. The living area benefits from a south facing balcony offering the living space ample sunlight throughout the day. The bedrooms are bright and spacious with plenty of storage units. This apartment is a very attractive prospect for any investor due to its proximity to the city and the nearby industrial estates. Alternatively, the apartment can be owner occupied according to the owner\\'s requirements. Close proximity to GMIT, Merlin Park Hospital, Ballyloughane Beach, and Renmore Playing Fields highlights the flexible nature of this property and how it is attractive both as an investment or as a place of residence.
