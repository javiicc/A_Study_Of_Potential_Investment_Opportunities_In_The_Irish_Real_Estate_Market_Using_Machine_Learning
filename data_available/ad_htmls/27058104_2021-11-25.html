    Description

      Lovely and spacious, two bedroom ground floor apartment available to rent from mid December. \n\nAccommodation of this great apartment comprises of large open plan living room with kitchen with ample space for dining and relaxing, 2 bedrooms (one double & 1 single), and spacious bathroom. The apartment is on the ground floor so benefits from own door access and there is a small private courtyard also to the front. \n\nThis apartment is tucked away at the back of the White Oaks development and surrounded by well-maintained mature communal gardens so it has a lovely quiet and private setting. \n\nWhite Oaks is located off Roebuck Road, in Clonskeagh so it's an ideal location, close to UCD, the Luas and within walking distance of the villages of Ranelagh, Rathmines and Donnybrook. The development is well serviced by a number of buses and the LUAS is a short walk away should you wish to travel further afield. \n\nThere is parking for one car.\n\nAvailable to view by appointment.
