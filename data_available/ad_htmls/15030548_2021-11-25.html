    ***Now Selling Off Plans*** \n\n4 Bedroom Detached Dormer Bungalows \n\nOne Remaining 4 Bedroom Property at e950,000. \n\n278sqm \nThree stunning 4 bedroom detached dormer bungalows planned in a spectacular setting. These A-rated well designed homes, bespoke to your own desired finishings, are a rare gem in the property market. Hazelwood comprises of a small secluded development with just 10 houses facing a central grassy oval play area, in a cul-de-sac off Johnstown Avenue in Kilpedder. \n\nArchitect designed and constructed by a multi award winning builder, these three detached dormer bungalows are being built to a very high standard with easy access to the N11 at junction 11 Greystones South. These large open-plan homes will offer extensive living and bedroom accommodation, including an extra family room and study downstairs. \n Notable design features will include a lovely breakfast area with double height peaked roof, triple glazed windows, underfloor heating, air to water heatpump and a double garage with loft. Purchasers will have the opportunity to pick their own bespoke finishings for the kitchen, utility, flooring, sanitary ware and wall tiles, wardrobes with a range of allowances included. \n\nHazelwood is ideally situated close to the thriving coastal village of Greystones, which boasts excellent retail and leisure facilities, as well as plenty of well-regarded established schools. The 133 bus stop is outside the estate and transport links in Greystones are excellent, with regular buses, the DART service and the main M11/M50 roads only minutes away. The nearby Wicklow mountains are popular for hill-walking, cycling, horse-riding and other recreational activities. Local attractions include the Druid' s Glen Golf Club, Glenroe Farm, The National Arboretum in Kilquade, Powerscourt Demesne. Mount Usher Gardens and Glendalough.\n\nDirections: \n\nFrom the N11 exit at junction 11 Greystones South/Kilpedder. If coming from the north (Dublin) go around the first roundabout and back under the N11 underpass to the roundabout on the west side of the N11. Head west up the hill. At the roundabout at the top of the hill take the first exit signposted Kilpedder L5046. Take the third turn right, immediately after the Kilpedder Filling Station, going around the side of the Filling Station onto Johnstown Avenue. Take the first left into Hazelwood. \n\nThe photographs of the house have been taken of a 4 bedroom detached dormer bungalow in the same estate and are for illustrative purposes only. \nFor full range of floorplans and further information please contact our sales office.
