    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    ***Photos and viewings to follow***\r\nBuckley Real Estate are delighted to present No 102 Temple Gardens to the market, a first floor three bedroom apartment with balcony. The apartment and balcony benefit from an east/west and south orientation and enjoy spectacular views across Northwood.\r\n\r\nSet among 100 acres of beautiful open parkland Northwood is truly an unrivalled location providing modern living in a tranquil setting. The location also provides a range of amenities and services been located beside Gullivers Retail Park and the Crowne Plaza while also within minutes of IKEA, Omni Shopping Centre and Santry Village. The location also provides great transport links to and from the City Centre and is only minutes from the M1, M50 and Dublin Airport.\r\n\r\nThe apartment in brief comprises warm welcoming entrance hallway , a bright spacious living/dining room with a separate kitchen, two substantial double bedrooms the master with en-suite bathroom, a single bedroom and a Family bathroom and ample storage throughout. A corner balcony off the living room enjoys views of the mature green parkland. \r\n\r\nViewing is highly recommended.\r\n\r\n**AMENITIES** Northwood has an abundance of amenities and service on its doorstep to include shops, cafes, restaurants, leisure facilities, hotel to name a few. Also within easy reach are the amenities of Santry, Omni Shopping Centre, IKEA and the Swords Pavillions.\r\n\r\n**TRANSPORT** There are a range of Bus services available nearby including No's 16, 16C, 27b, 33, 41, 41a, 41b & 41c. The M1 and M5o are also only minutes away.
