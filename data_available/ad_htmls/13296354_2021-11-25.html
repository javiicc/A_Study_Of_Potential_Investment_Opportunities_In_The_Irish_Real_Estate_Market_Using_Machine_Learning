    Tastefully restored stone faced former RIC Barracks and a modern dormer bungalow constructed in 2002 on c. 1 acre. Situated at an elevated junction of three roads, the property commands an uninterrupted view of the surrounding countryside as far as the River Suir and Rock of Cashel. The walls of Castle House are three feet thick and were all restored and pointed in the 1990' s during the restoration. It comprises 6 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms (2 ensuite) and boasts many period features throughout the house. Shamrock Lodge is a 3-bedroom (2 ensuite) modern dormer bungalow built in 2002 and finished in stone to complement the main house. In excellent decorative order, the central heating is oil fired and the BER is D2. It enjoys its own adjoining private garden. The Lodge is an ideal family unit for older parents, a young couple or could be eminently suitable for Air BnB being just 7km from Cashel and the M8 and a 20 minute drive to both Thurles and Tipperary.
