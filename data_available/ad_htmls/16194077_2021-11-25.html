    Paddy Murray Auctioneers are pleased to bring to the market this well presented three bedroom detached bungalow located within a small private estate of just 7 houses. The location alone speaks volumes about this property situated in a quiet residential area of Bandon offering the occupier a level of privacy and quietness that is difficult to replicate in other parts of the town, while being just 700 meters from the town centre. Outside, to the front, there is plenty of parking with a well manicured lawn but its to the rear where the real gem is located. The rear garden is south and west facing and is wonderfully private as it plays host to the detached home office / studio. It is clear that upon entering this charming home that the owners have cherished and maintained it over the years while being almost maintenance free both inside and out. This is a wonderful property well presented throughout and guaranteed to appeal to all looking for a home in Bandon town. Viewing by appointment only.
