    **UNDER OFFER. IF YOU HAVE A SIMILAR PROPERTY TO SELL, PLEASE CONTACT US**\r\nM&C Property are excited to bring to the market, 78 Ballyglass. This superb five-bedroom home is located in one of the most popular residential developments just 7km from Limerick City Centre. The excellent design and layout of the house is absolutely perfect for modern family living and ensures that both adults and children have adequate space to enjoy the house together. The main living room in the property is a fantastic size and has a bay window which affords the property a huge amount of natural light. The finishes to the room are tasteful in nature and include very stylish wood flooring and a solid fuel stove. Double doors give access to the kitchen / dining area. The size and scale of the kitchen dining area is one of the outstanding features of the house and the excellent standard of finish from the living room is carried through to this room also. As you would expect in a room of this size there is a plentiful supply of floor and wall solid wood kitchen units. An island in the middle of the room and a tiled floor finish off the room. The utility room off the kitchen is a vital component in any family home and has ample space for a washing machine, dryer and storage presses also. A guest WC is ideally positioned just off the utility room. Moving back into the entrance hallway you will find two further rooms to the front of the property, the first room is ideally suited as a home office or study while the second room which is currently in use as a tv room could double as a play room. A useful addition in any family home with small children! \r\n\r\nOn the first floor the standard of finish and size are continued throughout. The master bedroom is a wonderful bedroom to the front of the house with a fully tiled ensuite shower room and walk-in wardrobe area. Bedroom 2 again is an excellent size room that runs almost the entire depth of the house. Bedrooms 3 is also a fantastic sized bedroom and has the benefit of a walk-in wardrobe area. Bedrooms 4 and 5 are also very good-sized bedrooms and give great scope for children to have their own space. The main bathroom which is fully tiled, features a toilet, sink, bath and corner shower, completing the first-floor accommodation. \r\n\r\nThe rear garden which is larger than average has been very well maintained over the years. Here you will find a patio area and a large decking to enjoy time with family and friends on those long summer evening. There is a side entrance to the left of the property and the large shed that sits in the back corner of the garden is a great storage facility to have at your disposal. \r\n\r\nThe location of the property within the development is ideal as you have a large green area to the front and you are positioned towards the end of a cul de sac which means safe play from traffic for children. Ballyglass has matured into one of the most popular areas in which to escape the hustle and bustle of Limerick and enjoy the peaceful surroundings in this low-density development. \r\n\r\nEarly viewing of this excellent family home is most definitely advised as this property will not disappoint! \r\n\r\nApprox.
