    Space, Location and a large rear garden are just three of the factors that make 20 Rosnaree the ideal spot in which to raise a young family. The property is tucked away in a quiet, residential cul de sac in Church Hill Meadows which has always been a popular area since its construction. You have almost everything you need just a short walk away including local convenience stores, pubs, Doctors surgery, Pharmacy, Raheen industrial estate and not to mention a number of well-regarded schools. All of this is just a short commute from Limerick City and just 3 minutes from all of the major road connections to the rest of the country. \r\n\r\nOn the ground floor of the property there is a main living room with bay window which connects to the dining room at the rear of the home. This layout ensures a bright feeling regardless of where you may choose to relax. From the dining room there are sliding patio doors giving direct access to the rear garden. The kitchen is fitted with a generous supply of floor and eye level storage presses. \r\nA very useful utility room just off the kitchen is again an essential element to a family home like this. The extra reception room just off the utility room is really where interested parties will sit up and take notice. This is a large room and provides you with any number of uses including a gym / home office / play room or indeed a 4th bedroom. A wc in the hallway completes the ground floor accommodation. \r\n\r\nOn the first floor there is also plenty of room for all the family. All three bedrooms are well proportioned and all have wood flooring. The main bedroom has the benefit of an en suite shower room which has partially tiled walls and wood flooring for an easily maintained finish. The main bathroom of the property has also been partially tiled and has wooden flooring. \r\nThe rear garden of the property affords fantastic space to the occupants and could accommodate a house extension without being compromised. Early viewing is strongly advised for those seeking a comfortable family home in a quiet and mainly owner-occupied development. \r\n\r\nApprox.
