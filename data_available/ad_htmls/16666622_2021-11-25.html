    Woodmancote is undoubtedly one of the nicest family homes to come to the market in Killiney this year. Situated less than 1km from Killiney Beach & DART station and nicely positioned off Killiney Hill Road the property occupies a wonderfully private and secure south facing site. \n\nThe property was completely rebuilt by the present owners in 2006, who have been meticulous in upgrading and maintaining the house since. The interior of the house is beautifully presented to make the most of its setting. The colour palette used throughout the house is one of neutral tones.\n\nThe accommodation extends to 251 sq. m. over two levels. The ground floor, with its dramatic double height entrance hall and floor to ceiling glass window, set the tone for the rest of the house. The use of wide plank walnut timber floors throughout the ground floor adds to its appeal. Downstairs rooms ceiling heights at 2.7 m are higher than normal which gives an airy feel. \n\nThere is a tastefully appointed formal living room with a small study room off, a family room and superb kitchen/dining area with a fitted with a high gloss McNally kitchen and Island unit. A utility room and guest w.c. complete the accommodation at this level. Overhead there are four double bedrooms, two of which are ensuite and a family bathroom. \n\nWoodmancote is extremely energy efficient and enjoys a BER 3 energy rating. \n\nExternally the grounds are as impressive as the house itself with car parking for 4 cars. The garden is split level in design with the upper garden tiered with railway sleepers, a more private and intimate walled garden wraps around the southerly side of the house with patio area with attractive illuminated borders planted Buxus plants, bamboos and beautifully scented lavender. There is a small timber storage shed. \n\nThe location needs little introduction, situated off Killiney Hill Road it is less than 5 minutes drive to the heart of Dalkey Heritage Town and just a healthy walk to Killiney Village, Killiney Beach & DART. Dalkey offers a host of excellent amenities with Dublin Bay on its doorstep as well as speciality shops, a host of restaurants, public transport links, churches and enjoyable scenic walks. There is also a selection of excellent secondary schools including Holy Child Killiney, St Gerard s, Loreto Abbey, Rathdown and St. Joseph of Cluny and at junior level, Wyvern, St. Patrick s National School and Castlepark. \n\n\nGround Floor\n\n\nEntrance Hall: welcoming double height entrance with walnut floors and cast-iron radiators\n\nGuest w.c.: with wash hand basin & w.c.\n\nUtility room: with floor level storage units, single drainer sink unit, plumbed for washing machine.
