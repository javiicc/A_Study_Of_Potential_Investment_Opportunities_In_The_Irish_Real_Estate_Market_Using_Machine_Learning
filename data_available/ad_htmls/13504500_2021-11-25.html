    ** One bedroom apartments, Two bedroom apartments and 3 bedroom duplexes **\n\nOn view by appointment.\nSlade Castle comprises a mix of quality houses and apartments set around private landscaped gardens & open spaces. Slade Castle benefits from its independent retail development anchored by Dunnes Stores. This retail scheme also contains an array of other facilities including boutiques, sports shop, hairdressers, a medical centre & physiotherapist resulting in Slade Castle being a genuine self contained address. \n\nThe village of Saggart offers excellent access to Dublin city centre, the M50 motorway and the Citywest Business Campus. The village of Saggart is located in a scenic part of South County Dublin, tucked away just behind Citywest, at the foot of the Dublin Mountains. Saggart has a history and charm refined over hundreds of years. With the backdrop of the Dublin Mountains, the open spaces of Slade Castle are impressively landscaped to a high and maintainable standard. Slade Castle is a unique approach to combined urban living in a village environment. With its extensive residential, commercial and social features it fits very well into the existing charm that is Saggart Village.\n\nThe village of Saggart offers excellent access to Dublin city centre, the M50 motorway and the Citywest Business Campus.
