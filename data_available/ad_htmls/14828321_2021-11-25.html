    Perched on what can only be described as a stunning site, elevated to enjoy spectacular views of the Burren and surrounding woodlands and countryside. This new build, energy efficient New Build Home, offers the prospective buyer the perfect opportunity to engage with the builder in the design and layout of the interior of their new home. Designed be Grealish Glynn Architects, this home is designed to take full advantage of its aspect, fantastic open plan living space and extensive glazing, taking full advantage of those incredible views. Extending to just over 2,000 sq ft to the ground floor is the open plan kitchen/ living/ dining, utility, downstairs WC and the first of four double bedrooms. To the first floor are a further 3 bedrooms, one of which is en-suite and two with walk in wardrobes. Rounding off the accommodation is the main bathroom.\nOutside the site extends to c 0.5 acres with dry stone walls, mature trees and hedgerows. This home offers the ultimate in countryside living with wonderful countryside walks and woodlands to be enjoyed. Crusheen Village is just 7 minutes drive as is the M18 Motorway. The house is a SIP Energy Build. The structural insulated panel (SIP) system is manufactured from high quality materials, giving it a high thermal and airtightness performance resulting in a very impressive A3 energy rating. The house will have the added benefit of tripled glazed windows and doors, thermostatically controlled under floor heating to ground floor.\n\nAsking Price - \xe2\x82\xac350,000 with Builders Finish; \xe2\x82\xac370,000 Turn Key Condition\n\nViewing is highly recommended and strictly by prior appointment with sole selling agents.
