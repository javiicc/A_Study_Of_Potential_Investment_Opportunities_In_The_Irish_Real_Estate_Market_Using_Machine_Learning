    Positioned just off Griffith Avenue on this lovely tree lined stretch of Clare Road, no.29 is a very well maintained semi detached period home which has been in the same ownership for many years. The property has been well cared for and now offers the new owners the opportunity to create a beautiful family home. Many of the original features remain including the original fireplaces, ceiling covings and picture rails. The rooms are bright and spacious with many having great views of the garden with it s westerly aspect and terrific views of Corpus Christi Church.\n\nThe accommodation comprises a large hallway with stain glass hall door inset. The dining room is to the front with original fireplace and bay window. Double doors lead to the living room with original fireplace and a door to a sun room which in turn leads to the garden. The kitchen is fitted with floor and wall units and a door leads to the garage. Upstairs there are three double bedrooms and a single room. A bathroom completes the accommodation.\n\nThe front garden offers off street parking and access to the garage while the rear garden extends to approx. 160ft with mature trees and shrubs and great privacy.\n\nThe location is very convenient being within walking distance of the city centre and convenient to the numerous shops, cafes, bars and restaurants Drumcondra Village has to offer. The Botanic Gardens and Griffith Park are closeby and provide beautiful open green areas. Dublin City University is within easy reach with a variety of good sports clubs, national and secondary schools locally. The Four Courts, King s Inns, Beaumont Hospital and the Mater Hospital are all easily accessable. Commuting further afield is made very easy with the M50 Motorway, The M1 and Dublin Airport within a few minutes drive. The area is very well serviced by public transport, through the many bus routes which pass close by and Drumcondra Rail station nearby.\n\n\nHallway 3.8m x 3.1m. A porch leads to a surprisingly large hallway with elegant staircase to the first floor. Original features include ceiling covings and the original hall door with stain glass inset.\n\nDining Room 3.6m x 3.5m. To the front of the house, the dining room has many features including the original fireplace with tiled inset, ceiling covings and picture rails. A bay window floods the room with light and double doors lead to the living room.\n\nLiving Room 4.8m x 3.6m. The very spacious living room is to the rear and has views of the rear garden. Features include the original fireplace, ceiling covings and picture rails. A door leads to a glass house.\n\nKitchen 4m x 2.6m. With fitted floor and wall units, quarry tiled floor and understairs storage cupboard.\n\nGarage 6m x 2.3m. Useful for parking or storage.\n\nBedroom 1 3.7m x 3.6m. Large double room with original fireplace, picture rails and bay window.\n\nBedroom 2 3.2m x 3.2m. Spacious double room with storage cupboard, picture rails and two sash windows.\n\nBedroom 3 3.6m x 2.8m. Double room with picture rails.\n\nBedroom 4 2.6m x 2.6m. Single room to the rear.\n\nBathroom 2.4m x 1.9m. With suite comprising w.c, wash hand basin and bath with Triton shower attachment.
