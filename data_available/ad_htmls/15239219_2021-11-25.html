    This lovely warm one-bedroom unit is situated in the quiet commuter town of Sallins, County Kildare. This property offers a great opportunity for the successful purchaser to make this space their own or alternatively, a savvy investment property.\r\n\r\nThe apartment is located in the quiet and well-maintained complex of Willow Grove. There is parking at the complex and a communal bin store is on the grounds for refuse (costs for this are included in the annual service charges). \r\n\r\nConveniently located, 33 Willow Grove is just 5 minutes' drive from Naas town and major routes such as the M7 & N7 for access to Dublin. Commuters who wish to travel by train have the added benefit of Sallins Train Station which is also just 5 minutes' walk away. \r\n\r\nLocal amenities such as schools, parks, shops and boutiques are within walking distance of the property. For those who enjoy nature, the canal greenway walk is also a short stroll from the property which can be followed into Naas.\r\n\r\nThe property itself briefly comprises of a well-lit double bedroom with built in storage, spacious bathroom with bathing and shower facilities, bright and airy living room wired for internet and television, comes with electric fireplace and galley style kitchen with undercounter utilities. \r\nThe apartment also benefits from a very large attic space.\r\n\r\nApartment 33 Willow Grove is ideal for those with vision and scope who wish to purchase this hidden gem and make it their own. It also makes a great opportunity for investors seeking a rental property, given the location of the property in a growing commuter village. \r\n\r\nViewing is highly recommended with one days' notice in advance - email or call Stewart Property Solutions Ltd today to arrange a viewing. \r\n\r\nApprox.
