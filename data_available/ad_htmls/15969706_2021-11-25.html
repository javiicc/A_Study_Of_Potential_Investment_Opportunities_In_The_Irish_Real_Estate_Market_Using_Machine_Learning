    DNG is delighted to present Elton, Sea Road to the market. This most elegant of period homes is sure to catch the eye and its excellent position within the village of Kilcoole is sure to impress. \r\n\r\nDating back to 1901 this charming home is certainly going to appeal to a variety of homebuyers. The flexible accommodation of approx. 177 Sq M is split into a 3-bed home of approx. 134 Sq M and a 1 bed granny flat of approx. 43 Sq M. Depending on family requirements it could be remodelled into the one property or easily kept as is.\r\n\r\nThe accommodation of the main house comprises a sunroom / porch with views of the front garden and enough space for a two-seater sofa. The hall leads to a guest WC and the living room to the left and the dining room to the right. The living room boasts a solid fuel stove and views of the front garden. The bay windowed dining room is open plan to the kitchen and both reception rooms boast 3m high ceilings. Off the dining room is the kitchen which has a large selection of cabinets and lots of counter space. Off the kitchen is a large utility room that provides access to the rear garden. Upstairs there is a family shower room and 3 double bedrooms. Off bedroom 1 is an en-suite shower room.\r\n\r\nThe accommodation of the granny flat comprises a lounge with views of the front garden. A lobby area with plumbing for a washing machine. The kitchenette gives access to the rear garden and has a good selection of cabinets. Off the kitchenette is a shower room. Upstairs there is a large landing area and a bedroom.\r\n\r\nThe large front garden of Elton enjoys a very sunny southerly aspect and is approx. 27m in length. The house is well set back from the road and the garden could easily be used as an entertainment space to take advantage of the sunny aspect. A gravel driveway provides ample off-street parking. A gated side entrance brings you to the very private rear garden which is very low maintenance in design. The rear garden also enjoys a sunny aspect and gets late evening sunshine. The garden boasts a large lean to covered area which could be used for a variety of purposes. Off the lean to and not included in the overall square meterage is a WC and a boiler room.\r\n\r\nElton is nicely positioned on the highly regarded Sea Road in the village of Kilcoole. Within minutes' walk you have the village centre with its selection of shops, primary and secondary school in one direction. In the other you have the beach and the train station with the very regular intercity train to Dublin stopping there. \r\n\r\nPersonal inspection is highly recommended to appreciate the lovely charm and generous accommodation that this property enjoys. \r\n\r\nFeatures of note\r\nSemi-detached period home dating to c.1901\r\nOverall accommodation of approx. 177 Sq M\r\nMain house approx. 134 Sq M \r\nGranny flat 43 Sq M\r\n3m high ceilings to main reception rooms\r\nApprox. 27m front garden and 8m back garden\r\nOil fired central heating\r\nDouble glazed windows\r\nLow maintenance rear garden with sunny aspect \r\nLarge lean to covered area in rear garden\r\nLarge front garden with plenty of off-street parking\r\nJust 1.1km to Kilcoole beach and train station and St. Anthony's primary school\r\nOnly 1.3km to Colaiste Chraobh Abhann secondary school\r\n\r\n\r\nSun Room / Porch 2.67m x 1.59m.\r\n\r\nHall 3.42m x 2.14m.\r\n\r\nGuest WC 2.22m x 2.15m.\r\n\r\nLiving Room 5.21m x 4.41m.\r\n\r\nDining Room 4.42m x 2.19m.\r\n\r\nKitchen 4.42m x 2.87m.\r\n\r\nUtility Room 5.02m x 2.31m.\r\n\r\nLanding\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 5.14m 4.47m.\r\n\r\nEn-suite 2.3m x 1.26m.\r\n\r\nShower Room 2.12m x 1.81m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 4.42m x 2.37m.\r\n\r\nBedroom 3 4.45m x 2.68m.\r\n\r\nGranny Flat Accommodation\r\n\r\nLounge 4m x 2.97m.\r\n\r\nLobby 3.04m x 1.65m.\r\n\r\nKitchenette 2.28m x 2.18m.\r\n\r\nShower Room 2.18m x 1.18m.\r\n\r\nLanding\r\n\r\nBedroom 4 3.02m x 2.88m.\r\n\r\nOutbuildings\r\n\r\nLean To 9.34m x 4.53m.\r\n\r\nBoiler Room 4.5m x 2.22m.\r\n\r\nWC 2.22m x 0.87.
