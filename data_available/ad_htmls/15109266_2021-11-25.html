It has been the family home of the present owners since 1850.\n\nCurrently a bed & breakfast, the main house comprises of seven bedrooms , including 2 en-suite, with two full bathrooms. Large dining room has multi-fuel stove with fine marble surround. There is a front hall and a side hall, both having fine Georgian fanlight doorways. The ground floor also has a large kitchen-diner with AGA cooker, connecting into a utility room that leads off towards the workshop & office/store room.\n\nA three bedroom property with all double rooms also interconnects the main house. The annexe property is two storey & includes its own living room with multi-fuel stove & fully fitted kitchen-dining room.\n\nAs part of the sale, included is 2x1 bedroom flats, currently both occupied with long term tenants.\nStables and an array of outbuildings make up the back section of this impressive site. Mature & landscaped gardens surround the building. Ample parking options to the side & in front of the stables.\n\nThe property is Grade 2 listed & in all could be described as 10 bedrooms with 5 reception rooms.
