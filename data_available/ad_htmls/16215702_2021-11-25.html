    Coastal residence on c. 2 acres enjoying unimpeded wide ocean views. \r\n\r\nNestled into the hill in a wonderfully sheltered location, the house is approached over a winding driveway which helps create a magnificent unique and private setting. \r\n\r\nCompleted in 2017 to a high standard, the residence (c. 1,900 sq ft) has a contemporary layout, offering bright and spacious accommodation. \r\n\r\nUpon first entering, you are greeted by a large entrance hall, located at the centre of the building. To the front, you will find the kitchen/dining and living room, both taking full advantage of the coastal aspect with large picture windows and sliding doors accessing the gravelled terrace area. Off the kitchen, to the rear, is the utility room which can also be accessed from outside. The four spacious bedrooms (two en-suite) and family bathroom are also located to the rear. \r\n\r\nOutside, there is a large detached garage with separate gated entrance which would be suitable for a number of uses. \r\n\r\nThe grounds are wonderfully landscaped, laid out mainly in lawn with terrace area to the front and gravelled pathway around the perimeter, taking full advantage of the spectacular aspect over the coastline and countryside. The garden has a wide variety of flower beds, shrubs and hedging. \r\n\r\nThe property is within easy reach of the coastline and a number of beautiful small beaches and coves nearby. Castletownshend village is c. 6km, and Skibbereen (market town), where you can find a host of public amenities is c. 7km. Cork city & Airport is approx. 90 mins' drive. \r\n\r\nCastletownshend is one of the most popular villages on the West Cork coastline, not only renowned for its safe sailing waters but also its immense beauty, situated on the inner reaches of Castlehaven harbour.\r\n\r\nWest Cork offers a variety of leisure opportunities and sporting facilities together with a peaceful pace of life. This area provides some of the finest sailing along the entire south coast, whilst golf is catered for at Skibbereen, Clonakilty and Bantry. Sandy Blue Flag beaches are to be found at Inchydoney, Rosscarbery and nearby Tragumna. There are also many fine eating establishments dotted along the coastline, two with Michelin star status. \r\n\r\nACCOMMODATION \r\n\r\nEntrance Hall: Tiled floor, doors to front and rear. \r\n\r\nOpen Plan Kitchen/Dining: 6.4m x 6.3m.
