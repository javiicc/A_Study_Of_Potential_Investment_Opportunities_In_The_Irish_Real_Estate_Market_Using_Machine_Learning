    80 The Green, Clonard Village comes to the market in immaculate condition, superbly located in one of Wexford's most sought-after locations within walking distance of all the fantastic amenities & facilities Wexford has to offer including Clonard retail park, shops, schools, churches, supermarkets, gyms etc. The ever-improving N/M11 is very easily accessed via the N25 which is minutes drive making journeys further afield & commuting convenient & hassle free. The local bus stop is located just outside the development. Wexford town is in close proximity to many idyllic beaches including Curracloe, Ballinesker, Morriscastle, Kilmore, Duncannon to name a few. The property itself is in fantastic condition throughout beautifully decorated with many added extras briefly comprising of a generous bright entrance hall, open plan kitchen/living/dining with a patio door to the enclosed spacious rear garden with south facing aspect - ideal for entertaining & family life. The 1st floor has a large sitting room - perfect to unwind in, a bedroom with fitted wardrobes & solid timber floors. The 2nd floor comprises of a bathroom & 2 bedrooms, one of which has an en-suite & balcony to enjoy.
