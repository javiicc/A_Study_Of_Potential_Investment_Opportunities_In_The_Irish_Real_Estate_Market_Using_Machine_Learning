    Development Description

      * Fnal 3 and 4 Bedroom Family Homes *\r\n\r\nBarnhall Meadows situated at the iconic Wonderful Barn site in Leixlip is the newest venture by the reputable Glenveagh Homes.\r\n\r\nThe development will consist of a mixture of large architecturally designed luxury 2, 3 & 4 bedroom homes built to the highest modern standards. These homes are designed with the buyer in mind and cutting edge features are apparent throughout, including the state of the art air to water heat source pump reducing owners running costs.\r\n\r\nGlenveagh Homes is offering a full flooring and kitchen appliance package to all Barnhall Meadows buyers in this phase with a choice of timber floors on the ground floor and carpets on the first floor included in the price. Buyers to sign contracts within 21 days to avail of this special incentive.
