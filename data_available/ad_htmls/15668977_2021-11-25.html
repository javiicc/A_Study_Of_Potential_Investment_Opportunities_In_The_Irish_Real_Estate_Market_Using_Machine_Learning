    Coolroe arrives to the market for sale offering a spacious four bedroomed detached family bungalow set on the northern side of the R608 road in Ballincollig. The accommodation is arranged all on one level and has kept its original features and character throughout. This property was built in 1963 and extends to approximately 134 sqm (1,442 Sq Ft) located on the western side of Ballincollig town centre and offers great potential for a family home or equally as a development opportunity.\r\n\r\nThis property is conveniently and centrally located to a wealth of amenities and facilities at its doorsteps and is situated within a short drive from Ballincollig town centre. Ballincollig has become a popular residential location and offers a wide range of both primary and secondary schools. It is easily accessible to a variety of local amenities such as Quish's SuperValu, The White Horse Restaurant, Oriel House Hotel, Castle West Shopping Centre, Dunnes Store, Lidl and plenty more. Ballincollig Regional Park is withing walking distance from the property and offers a natural park with wood trails, paved walkways and picnic areas throughout. There is a regular bus route 220 which is a short distance from the property and goes to and from the city centre at a frequent timetable on a daily basis.\r\n\r\nThe accommodation extends to an entrance porch, reception hall, sitting room, four bedrooms, living room, spacious open plan kitchen/dining area, utility room, family bathroom, and a guest toilet/shower all on ground floor level. The property is in need of modernisation and upgrading throughout.\r\n\r\nThe property is set on a well-positioned generous rectangular site of approx. 0.3 acres with landscaped gardens to front and rear offering mature planting and shrubs. There is a gated driveway to front which allows privacy and plenty of ample parking. The front garden benefits from a south facing aspect which remains bright and sunny throughout the day. To the side and rear of the property there are two large, detached garages and a portable office/studio offering plenty of storage and parking space.\r\n\r\nViewing is highly recommended to fully appreciate all that is on offer.\r\n \r\nEntrance Porch (1.28m x 0.23m )Enclosed entrance porch with sliding doors and access to the property.\r\nReception Hall (17.91m x 4.53m )A large and welcoming entrance hall which is fully carpeted and gives access to all the rooms throughout the property.\r\nLiving Room (3.99m x 3.69m )Located just off the reception hall, this front facing room is both bright and spacious. Finished with light fitting and is fully carpeted.\r\nOpen Plan Kitchen /Dining (4.52m x 3.94m )Accessed off the reception hall, this area offers two spaces and boasts plenty of natural light throughout. The kitchen is fitted with floor and eye level units with appliances to include a cooker, extractor van, oven, fridge, dishwasher and single drainer sink unit. There is a large window overlooking the rear garden and is finished in lino flooring with built in cupboards and presses. Access to the utility room is through this area.\r\nUtility Room (3.28m x 1.58m )Located off the kitchen/dining, this utility room offers a freezer, washing machine, dryer, built in cupboards and finished in lino flooring.
