    No.1 Sandymount Village Centre is a stunning turnkey apartment with breathtaking views across Dublin city, a beautifully appointed apartment that has been architecturally upgraded over the years to provide a home of distinction in the heart of Sandymount village. This property has undergone extensive refurbishment and comes to the market with a crisp contemporary interior.\n\nUpon entering, one is instantly struck by the natural light that fills the interior where style and flair are evident throughout. The accommodation has been cleverly designed giving a bright, airy and contemporary feel and briefly comprises of a large entrance hall area (with space for a home office setup) with double doors leading to an open plan living accommodation which is divided into a dining and living area with large glass sliding doors leading to a sunny southwest facing balcony. Off this area lies a stylish kitchen with solid granite worktops and breakfast bar. On the first floor there is a double bedroom with walk in wardrobe and a family bathroom both of which have access out onto a second southwest facing balcony with panoramic views across Dublin city and Dublin mountains.\n\nThe location of this beautiful home cannot be understated. Superbly located in the heart of the village yet in a quiet block of apartments. There is residents communal parking available but given the proximity of all the amenities on its doorstep a car may not be required. Grafton Street, Merrion Square, St. Vincent s hospital, Grand Canal Dock and the IFSC are all within easy access by foot, car or public transport. Dublin airport is a mere 20-minute drive via the Airport tunnel.\n\n\nEntrance Hall with wooden flooring, recessed lighting, phone intercom, skylight, home office area, utilities storage unit and glass double doors leading to the living/dining\n\nLiving/Dining Room a large bright and airy room with wooden flooring, glass atrium, recessed lighting, feature gas fire, spot lighting, tv & phone points, built in storage/shelving and two large glass sliding doors leading to a sunny southwest facing balcony\n\nKitchen open plan to the living/dining with wooden flooring, recessed lighting, a range of floor and wall units, tiled splash back, breakfast bar, granite worktops, double sink with drainer, integrated dish washer, integrated fridge, and provision for gas or electric oven/hobs\n\nBathroom with tiled flooring and tiled walls, Jacuzzi bath with overhead shower and jets, w.c, wash hand basin with storage, wall mounted mirror, wall mounter chrome heated towel rail, recessed lighting and a door leading another sunny southwest facing balcony\n\nBedroom large double room with Velux window, recessed lighting, tv & phone points, walk in wardrobe and glass sliding door to a sunny southwest balcony with panoramic views over Dublin
