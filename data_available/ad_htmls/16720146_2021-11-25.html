    Team Nicola Wynne -Jankola is delighted to introduce to the market number 4 Kenmare Parade, Phibsboro located just off the North Circular Road. This charming red brick mid terrace house is located within walking distance of The Mater Hospital. This lovely mid terrace offers a wonderful opportunity for first time buyers to set up home in a most convenient location providing easy access to Dublin city centre.\n\nThe accommodation briefly comprises a spacious living room , fitted kitchen and a family bathroom on the ground floor. Upstairs there are two good size bedrooms. Number 4 is enhanced by a sunny courtyard to the rear and ample street disc parking to the front.\n\nAn excellent location, a short stroll to the Blessington basin, close to Phibsboro village providing every conceivable amenity and a location with good schools/colleges and excellent public transport links.\nViewing is strongly recommended to fully appreciate this wonderful opportunity to acquire a delightful home in a much sought after residential location.
