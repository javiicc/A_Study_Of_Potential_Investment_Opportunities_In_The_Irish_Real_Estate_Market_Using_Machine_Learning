    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    This spacious dormer bungalow is situated 5klms from Wexford Town centre on the Curracloe/Blackwater Road and close to Castlebridge. The property is bright and spacious and laid out to provide flexible family living. Accommodation includes a living room, kitchen, utility, dining room, two bedrooms and a cloakroom downstairs and two bedrooms and a recently refurbished shower room upstairs. There is also a good size detached garage to the rear. The site is mature, well planted and extremely private to the rear. There is scope to add additional space upstairs by breaking into the eves. There is also storage space between the two upstairs bedrooms which would make an ideal ensuite. All in all, a great family home with flexible living accommodation on the Dublin side of Wexford town close to beaches....\nEntrance Hall 5.36m x 1.5m.
