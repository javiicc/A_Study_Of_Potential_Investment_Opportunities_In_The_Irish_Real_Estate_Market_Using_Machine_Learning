    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Situated in Ferrybank this three bedroom semi-detached property offers spacious accommodation and has been well maintained by its current owners. The property is within walking distance to all local amenities, good bus links, supermarkets, and primary & secondary schools.\r\n\r\nThis extended semi detached family home is tucked away in a quiet cul-de-sac in Rockenham, the property benefits from the addition of a garage and sun room conversion to its already spacious living accommodation. Well presented, as you walk towards the front door you enter into a large bright porch, the entrance hallway which benefits from under stairs storage space and adjoining doors opening up into two lounge areas, an open plan dining/kitchen room and bathroom/utility with shower. On the first floor there are three bedrooms all furnished with built in wardrobes and a family bathroom fitted with bath and shower.\r\n\r\nOutside to the front there is a gated entrance to a paved driveway surrounded by gorgeous shrubbery on both sides, a patio area and the side garden wraps around to the rear and is private and secluded laid predominantly to lawn with mature shrub borders, garden storage shed and entertaining patio area. \r\n\r\nNote: These particulars are given on the understanding that they will not be construed as part of a contract, conveyance or lease, and that all negotiations take place through Manor Properties.
