    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Mark Nestor Property Services present to you this truly unique country side family home. In addition to this property is external "state of the art" office units, an equestrian area and plot of land all of which is attached to the boundary of this property. \n\nThis exceptional modern detached cottage is set in the idyllic mature area of Ballymore Co. Westmeath. Located off a very quiet road, it is set into a c.5 acre site with external office units and an equestrian space including 3 stables,a tack room, office and sand arena. \n\nThe property itself includes a Bespoke kitchen with a marble top island and range master, two lounges both defining a quirky and classical vibe, downstairs bathroom, two bedrooms and a walk-in wardrobe. To the first floor there is a spacious master bedroom leading into a walk-in wardrobe and en-suite with a further spacious bedroom located to the side extension on the upper floor. \n\nThe outside benefits from a very spacious outdoor entertaining terrace including a man-made BBQ, pizza stove and outdoor bar, perfectly suited for entertaining. A play area and log cabin complete the back garden.\n\nTwo further features to this fabulous property is \n\n1) the granted planning permission for yet another side extension to the existing property\n2) the large detached office block on site that contains toilets, kitchen, large meeting areas, 3 large seperate offices and abundance of storage space. \n\nAthlone town is approx 15 minutes away and Ballymore village is 5 minutes away, with local amenities including schools and shops. \n\nViewings are highly recommended to appreciate this fantastic property and space.
