    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    1 Boyne View is a well presented 5 bedroom detached family home located in one of the most popular estates in Johnstown with the house positioned on a prestigious site at the front of the estate. The site is located at the end of a row offering potential buyers with an extra large site which in turn provides a large rear garden and a side access which is suitable for a car which can be accessed to a purpose built detached shed to the rear. The front drive way is cobble locked with the house well positioned opposite Johnstowns Peoples' Park therefore providing homes owners with 8 acres of open green space.\r\n\r\nSome of the significant features include an open plan sitting / dining room, a separate family room and large kitchen on the ground floor and there are four double bedrooms with an ensuite and two walk in wardobes. Overtime the owners redesigned the second floor which now provides convenient options for a large family or for a home office which supports parents working from home. 1 Boyne View is not overlooked to the front, it has a bright back garden and is surrounded by a small number of neighbouring houses.\r\n\r\nOn the ground floor the house has a separate family room, a sitting room with double doors opening onto the dining room. The kitchen overlooks the rear garden and has space for a comfortable dining area, there is also a utility room and guest WC also on the ground floor. The first floor includes three double bedrooms with a master ensuite and a walk in wardrobe, a single bedroom, a closet and a main bathroom. The second floor provides for a large double bedroom, a bathroom and a closet. \r\n\r\nResidents at Boyne View benefit from the large green space at Johnstowns Peoples' Park close by and the estate is within walking distance to schools, Johnstown Shopping Centre and sporting amenities. 1 Boyne View is located just 4.5km from Junction 8 of the M3. \r\n\r\nViewings by appointment.
