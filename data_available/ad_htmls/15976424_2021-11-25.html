    Lorraine Spillane auctioneers are delighted to present Derryvillane Glanworth Co. Cork to the market. Derryvillane is sitated near the Funcheon river between Glanworth and Kildorrery with close proximity to the town of Mitchelstown. Originally an old MIll house that had a Forge under neath was extended to the proud farmhouse that stands today. Many traditional features remain within this much loved family home and it is both inviting and warm upon arrival. The grounds offer extensive space for the would be buyer with unrivaled countryside views with a boundary of old stone wall and very mature trees. The grounds also offer two stables and garage outhouses while also leaving ample space for parking and enjoying the outdoors. A beautiful stone gated entrance greets you upon arrival to Derryvillane.
