    O'Donovan and Associates are pleased to offer for sale this charming, quaint cottage located in the much sought after town-land of Caherconnell, Abbeyfeale. \n\nThe residence is set on a substantial 0.5 acre plot offering privacy and scenic views of the surrounding countryside. The detached residence is only a 5 minute drive from the beautiful town of Abbeyfeale and all local amenities. Killarney is only 45 minutes while Limerick City is 50 minutes away. \n\nThe property offers great potential and could easily be extended. Accommodation includes, a large double bedroom, open plan living area, a galley kitchen and a sower room with an electric shower. The property is currently heated through a stove with a back boiler.
