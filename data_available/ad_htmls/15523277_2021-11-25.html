    A great opportunity to acquire a superb property on c. 10 acres of land including stables, an outdoor arena & outbuildings located just over a mile from Roaring Water Bay. \n\nThe house, which was built in 2007, measures over 2,000 sq ft. It is in wonderful condition and was finished to a very high standard. Features include a bespoke kitchen, solid hardwood floors, quarry slates, underfloor heating and solar panels. The property boasts an impressive B3 BER rating. \n\nThe land consists of approximately 8 acres of arable land laid out in paddocks and approx. 2 acres of unspoiled land which would be ideal as a wildlife habitat or for bee-keeping. There is a purpose built shed with 3 stables, a turn-out sand arena, a hay shed and a tack/feed room. \n\nThe owner of the property has been breeding horses for a number of years; indeed, a number of high-profile horses have been bred here including Croco Bay who won the 2019 Grand Annual Chase in Cheltenham and Thistle Do Nicely who has won 3 races and is currently in training with Jamie Snowden in the UK, their dam is still happily grazing here.\n\nWhile it has a great sense of seclusion in being well off a quiet road in the heart of the countryside, the property is situated only 6 miles from the busy market town of Skibbereen, 4 miles from the village of Ballydehob and is just over an hours' drive to Cork airport.\n\nThe grounds around the house are well landscaped with a mixture of mature trees, lawn and flowering plants. There is also a most wonderful sun terrace to sit and enjoy the evening sun. \n\nThis is a most attractive property in a very peaceful location and would be suitable as a small stud farm or as a private residence.\n\n\nAccommodation\n\nGround Floor\n\nKitchen/Dining Area: 6.8m x 5.2m. \nTiled floor, fitted kitchen with integrated appliances. \n\nLiving Room: 7m x 5.8m. \nTimber floor, stove. \n\nSun Room: 4m x 3.2m.
