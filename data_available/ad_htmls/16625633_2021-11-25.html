    Located adjacent to the Grand Canal and Leeson Street, No 3 Elm House, Mespil Estate offers a superb opportunity to purchase a property in Dublin 4. This ground floor, one-bed apartment would be ideally suited for an investor, owner occupier or someone looking for a City Centre base.\n\nThe Apartment offers c.49 sq.m. accommodation comprising: Entrance hallway, spacious double bedroom with large storage press, generous living/dining room overlooking the canal, separate kitchen and a modern bathroom with WC, WHB and shower completes the accommodation.The property comes with the benefit of Gas Fired Central Heating and Residents Parking.\n\n\nThe mature Mespil Estate offers an unrivalled setting with many excellent amenities and services within immediate reach.The Grand Canal and nearby streets have cycle lanes and bike stations while Dublin Bus and the Luas are right on the doorstep.The apartment is conveniently located next door to the Clayton Burlington Hotel. There is a great selection of restaurants, cafes and bars close by including The Canal Bank Cafe, 3fe Cafe and O'Brien's Bar on Sussex Terrace. It is just 10 minutes walk to the city centre's shops, restaurants and bars, the National Concert Hall, museums, galleries, theatres and cinema. The Air Coach on Leeson Street takes 40 minutes to Dublin Airport.\n\nAccommodation:\n\nEntrance Hall:\nAudio Intercom, wooden floor\n\nLiving/Dining room:\nLarge picture window, wooden floor.\n\nKitchen:\nFitted floor and wall units.\n\nBedroom : \nwooden floor and large storage press. \n\nBathroom:\nshower, w.c., w.h.b., tiled floor and walls.
