    135 Lansdowne Park is modern detached home in an unsurpassed quiet location in the heart of Ballsbridge. Built in hand-cut granite to a unique design by Cantrell Crowley Architects creating a bright contemporary open plan living space. Located a short walk from the Aviva Stadium, many schools and Ballsbridge village and close to the city centre this property would suit the successful professional looking to avail of all Dublin 4 has to offer.\n\nAccommodation extends to approximately 97sqm / 1,039sqft. \n\nThe entrance hallway opens up to the living room. A cleverly appointed oak sliding wall provides versatility in the configuration of the accommodation which allows the kitchen to be secreted away or opened up as required. The kitchen opens up to a glazed, circular dining area enjoying a sunny west facing orientation. \n\nDouble doors open from the living room and kitchen onto an enclosed walled sunny courtyard perfect for a barbeque with a separate rear entrance. Utilities are housed in a separate purpose built unit.\n\nUpstairs there are three bedrooms. The main bedroom to the front has a balcony for morning coffee and an ensuite bathroom. There are two further good sized bedrooms and a spacious bathroom.\n\nThe finish is to a very high standard with hardwood floors and beautifully tiled bathrooms.\n\nThe front is paved in Liscannor stone with off street parking for one car.\n\n\nGROUND FLOOR\n\nEntrance Hall Door to:\n\nLiving Room Feature marble fireplace with coal effect gas fire.
