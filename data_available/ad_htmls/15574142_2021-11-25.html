    Property Partners Little are offering for sale by private treaty this restoration project located in wonderful quiet rural area approx. 4km west of Slane village.\r\nA traditional roadside stone workers cottage with great potential to renovate and extend into a modern family home. The property has recently had some works completed including new windows and front door, roof carpentry and felt along with part of first fix wiring. Located just a short walk to Tankardstown House, there is huge demand and potential for self catering accommodation in area. The property is being offered on approx. 1 acre however owners will consider offering additional ground (up to 3 acres in total) subject to negotiation.\r\nAt present, there are no services on site and any purchaser will have to satisfy themselves as to planning requirements. There may also be potential to seek planing to demolish existing house and construct new property within boundary of site (subject to planning). If you are seeking an idyllic rural location in heart of historic Boyne Valley and with easy access to Dublin, this just might be the property for you.
