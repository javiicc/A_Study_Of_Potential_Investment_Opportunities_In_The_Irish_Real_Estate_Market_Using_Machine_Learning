    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Brophy Cusack Real Estate Team are delighted to bring this two bed terraced house to the market. The ground floor consists of an entrance hall, sitting room, kitchen & bathroom. Rising to the first floor we have two well proportioned bedrooms & a bathroom. The attic has been floored & is an ideal space for storage. The property also benefits from oil fired central heating, double glazed windows & a spacious rear garden. \r\n\r\nThe property measures Circa: 111 M Sq and benefits from Mains Sewerage and Mains Water.\r\n\r\nLocation:\r\nThe Main Street houses a bakery, butcher, grocery shop, bars, a Heritage Centre and a church. The main attraction in the village is Curraghmore Estate, a manor sitting on the outskirts which has around 40 hectares of land, is open for visits between March and October.\r\n\r\nFor further information or to arrange a viewing please contact Sole Selling Agents Brophy Cusack on 051 511333.
