    DNG Martin O'Connor Auctioneers have been instructed to offer for sale this plot of land that extends to c.3.98 Ha (9.834 Acres) acres on the outskirts of Oughterard village. \r\n\r\nLocated on The Rusheeny Road past the Knockillaree riding centre the land is fenced and extends towards Lough Mall at the rear. The land is ideal grazing land, it's relatively level with good potential for betterment through future draining. As can be seen the surrounding lands are showing sand a short distance from the surface. \r\n\r\nThis would be an ideal holding for those looking to acquire a plot of land for those looking for a hobby farm or to carry out some tree planting only a short distance from Oughterard village. For further details please contact the sole selling agents DNG Martin O'Connor Tel 091866708.\r\n\r\nFeatures\r\nLarge plot of land\r\nFenced\r\nClose to Oughterard\r\nIdeal starter holding - for keeping sheep, horses or some afforestation \r\nPossible potential for a site SPP\r\n\r\n\r\nDirections\r\nFrom Oughterard village proceed along Main Street towards Clifden passing the Garda Station on your left. Just before the Bridge turn left at Conneelys Cafe it will be signposted Costelloe and Shannapheasteen. Drive out this road past the Carpet Factory and take the second right turn on the Rusheeney just past Oughterard Motors. The property is located on the Rusheeney Road approximately 200m past the Knockillaree riding centre on the right hand side identified by a DNG Martin O'Connor For Sale sign.
