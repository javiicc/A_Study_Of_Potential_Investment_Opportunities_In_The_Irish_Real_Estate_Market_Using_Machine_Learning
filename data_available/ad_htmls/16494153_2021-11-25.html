    Welcome to No.12 Philips Vale, an lovely, spacious three bedroom home. Mark Charles Properties is delighted to have been instructed to bring to the market a home of such quality. Philips Vale is a small quiet estate neatly positioned just inside the quaint town of Daingean in Co. Offaly. The property itself is in excellent condition and will appeal to many different buyers. A particular plus for the property is the large rear extension that was added. This giving you a wet room shower room, a utility and a very spacious, open plan kitchen/dining/living room area. Its location is second to none with it being a short walk into town and local shops. Tullamore town is also only a short drive away with all its wonderful amenities. This home is a credit to its current owners, it boasts oak doors, bay windows and wonderful decor. Accommodation briefly comprises of entrance hallway, sitting room, kitchen/living/dining room, utility, wet room, three bedrooms and bathroom.
