    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to bring to the market Beal Inse . An attractive detached family home located in a sought after and mature development in the picturesque village of Parteen. There are excellent amenities close at hand including a primary school, shops, sports facilities and is also within easy commuting distance of Limerick City. The property enjoys a beautiful west facing garden that has been extremally well maintained by the current owners. \n\nBeal Inse, is a very well maintained family home with excellent accommodation which is presented in good order and offers both spacious and well-appointed reception rooms and generous bedroom accommodation. Undoubtedly the main feature of this wonderful home is the private west facing back garden which is mature and stocked with a delightful array of shrubs, plants and trees. \nViewing is highly recommended to appreciate all this special and unique home has to offer.\n\nACCOMMODATION:Entrance hallway, guest WC\nLiving/dining room: Open plan room with open fireplace and picture window overlooking front and sliding patio doors to the rear.\nKitchen/Breakfast room: Well fitted with range of wall and floor units and has very large pantry.\nUtility Room: Off the kitchen with door to back garden, fully plumbed for washing machine and dryer.\nFamily Room: Bright airy room with Velux roof windows, timber flooring, open fireplace at centre.\nUpstairs there are four bedrooms.\nMain Bedroom: Bright and spacious room with excellent built-in storage. Large modern ensuite shower room that is beautifully tiled.\nSecond Double: Comfortable double bedroom with built in wardrobes and laminate flooring.\nBedroom 3 & 4: Generous single bedrooms overlooking rear garden. The third bedroom benefits from built in wardrobes.\nMain\n\n Bathroom: Excellent size, fully tilled fitted with WHB, WC & bath/shower.\n\nGarden:\nEnclosed front garden with off street parking. Side entrance to glorious west facing rear garden, well stocked with mature flowering shrubs, trees, plants. \n\nServices & Notes:\nAlarm, OFCH New Burner, Cavity Walls Pumped, Double Glazed windows, Attic insulated, Mains Water & Waste.\n\nDIRECTIONS:Driving from Limerick via the Ennis Road, drive out the long pavement and Fairyfield is before Parteen Village on the right-hand side.
