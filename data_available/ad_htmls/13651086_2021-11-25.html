    Galvin Property & Finance are delighted to present to the market a unique opportunity to purchase two adjoining apartments, a one bed and a two bed, on the top floor of the 5 star InterContinental Hotel in Ballsbridge. This is a fantastic opportunity to convert both apartments into a very large three bed penthouse of 3,073 sq ft. This is Dublin's only true 5-Star luxurious Urban Resort with a touch of old-school glamour at its finest. Irish-owned InterContinental Dublin (formerly Four Seasons Hotel) make a virtue of their tranquil location in Dublin's most affluent neighbourhood Ballsbridge, only a short stroll from Dublin city centre.\r\n\r\n\r\nAn enviable Dublin 4 location and wonderful setting, complimented by the world class InterContinental service at your door, around the clock with whatever you need. Order dinner from the restaurant, take a swim or workout in the gym, have the concierge arrange theatre tickets or organise the hotel car to collect you from the airport.\r\n\r\n\r\nThese residences are situated on a private residents only floor overlooking the hotels gardens accessed only by residents with security cards. When converted, this very large, bright & luxurious residence has the potential to be the finest apartment Dublin has to offer. As a resident, you will have access to all of the amenities that one would expect at this 5 star hotel to include onsite gym & spa, private secure underground parking, concierge service, room service and much more.\r\n\r\n\r\nThe current layout is of two separate adjoining West facing apartments both generously proportioned and facing the landscaped gardens in the centre of the resort which enhances the exclusivity of the property. The two bed residence of 1,633 sq ft comes with both bedrooms ensuite. The living / dining room overlooks the hotel gardens with a separate kitchen / breakfast room. There is plenty of auxiliary storage space and a guest WC off the hall. The one bed residence, also ensuite, of 1,440 sq ft is similarly bright and spacious with two store rooms and an additional utility off the kitchen. Both residences are finished to the highest standard as one might imagine in such a prestigious building.\r\n\r\n\r\nLocated at the corner of Simmonscourt Road and Merrion Road, The Intercontinental occupies a pivotal corner site in the heart of Ballsbridge and as a result is within walking distance of an extensive choice of amenities such as; The RDS, Leinster Rugby's headquarters, a plethora of excellent local shops and restaurants in the nearby villages of Ballsbridge, Donnybrook and Sandymount along with the 34 acres of leisure grounds in Herbert Park. Dublin Airport is within 20 minutes' drive via the Port Tunnel and St Stephens Green is a leisurely stroll of 20 minutes.
