    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 1.07 ac
    Dowling Property is delighted to present to the market, this fabulous, c.1 acre site for sale. Located in the heart land of Kildare on the doorstep to the Curragh and enjoys wonderful rolling countryside views. The site benefits from having a previous grant of planning permission, (which has since lapsed), for a wonderful designed detached house.\r\n\r\nThe location is highly sought after and is surrounded by some of the countrys finest countryside. Kilcullen is just c.5 miles where youll find direct access to M7/M9 along with Kildare Town c.5 miles and Newbridge c.8 miles. The site enjoys excellent road frontage and an abundance of mature trees providing shelter and the opportunity to have an attractive tree lined driveway and ready made landscaping.\r\n\r\nNB * The successful buyer must be able to satisfy Kildare County Council local needs criteria. Log onto www.kcc.ie.\r\n\r\nDirections: From Kilcullen proceed out the Athy road and take right hand turn at Ballyshannon Cross (Dowlings pub on left). Continue for approx. 2.5km.
