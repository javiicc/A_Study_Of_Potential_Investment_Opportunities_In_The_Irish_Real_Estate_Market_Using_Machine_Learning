    A wonderful ground-floor one-bedroomed apartment directly overlooking the River Liffey. A perfect \xe2\x82\xacleft bank\xe2\x82\xacTMlocation that combines easy access to the city with a peaceful rural vibe.\r\n\r\nEXPLORE INSIDE:\r\n\r\nThe entrance hallway is spacious, with doors to kitchen and reception room as well as to a double bedroom and a bathroom. From the reception room you access a south facing patio which gives on to a garden with direct access to the Liffey. There is underground car parking for one car.\r\n\r\nEXPLORE OUTSIDE:\r\n\r\nDublin is not noted for its outdoor amenities, but the Phoenix Park is actually the largest enclosed park in a European Capital city. With the Park on one side & the river at its glorious best just before it enters the bustle of the city centre on the other, you can exercise, picnic, cycle, visit the zoo, or just sit and enjoy the view. \r\n\r\nPublic transport is great, with Heuston station a ten minute stroll away, including access to the Luas. A little further along the riverbank you can access the recently trendy Stoneybatter area with restaurants and bars (for a post-virus world obviously).\r\n\r\nTHE BOTTOM LINE:\r\n\r\nSize, convenience and a tranquil setting make this a no-brainer for owner occupier or a canny investor.
