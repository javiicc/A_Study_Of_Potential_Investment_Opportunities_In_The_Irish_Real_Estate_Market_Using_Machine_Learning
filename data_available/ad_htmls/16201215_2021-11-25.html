    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to present Willow House, Barnakyle, Patrickswell to the market. This extended 4 bedroom bungalow comes to the market in excellent condition and ready for immediate occupation. Built in the early 1990 s and extended in 2012, Willow House offers spacious living accommodation with 4 excellent bedrooms. Positioned towards the end of a quiet residential cul de sac, this property offers all the peace and quiet of a rural location yet just a stones throw away from all major amenities. \n\nThe property is located in one of Limerick s most sought after addresses and offers a peaceful rural setting yet is only a short drive to Raheen & Dooradoyle. The Raheen Business Park, The University of Limerick Regional Hospital, Crescent Shopping Centre, and highly regarded primary & secondary schools are just a short drive. Patrickswell village is within walking distance offering a host of local amenities such as shops, pubs, restaurants and schools at your doorstep. Access to the motorway is just a stone s throw away making this location easily accessible from all routes.\n\nACCOMMODATION:\n\nEntrance Hallway: Well-appointed spacious hallway with solid wood flooring.\n\nKitchen/Diner: Set at the rear of the property with west facing aspect. Bright and spacious room fitted with contemporary Zanwood kitchen units and granite worktop. \n\n\nUtility: Positioned just off the kitchen coming fully plumbed for washing machine and dryer.\n\nGuest W.C: Located off the utility retiled and fitted with contemporary sanitary ware.\n\nFamily Room: Extended off the kitchen in 2012. Spacious and bright room with French doors to the rear decking. The room features solid wood flooring and large windows allowing plenty of natural light into the room.\n\nOffice/\n\nBedroom 5: Positioned to the front of the house with external access to the front driveway. This room is currently in use as an office and would be a great working space for anybody working from home that might be welcoming clients. The office could serve to be a great 5th bedroom containing a full ensuite bathroom. There is endless possibilities with this space for those with young kids or family members who might be staying.\n\nBedrooms: 4 Double bedrooms all finished with carpet flooring and a neutral colour pallet on the walls. 3 of the bedrooms contain built in wardrobes. \n\nMain\n\n Bathroom: Fully tilled bathroom with separate bath and walk in shower. Fitted with contemporary sanitary ware. \n\nServices: \nOFCH, Mains water & Mains Sewage\n\nNotes: Gas Rangemaster, Solar panels for hot water, zoned heating system, attic re-insulated, alarm, approx. 0.25 acres site.
