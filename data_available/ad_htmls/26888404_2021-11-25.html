    Description

      Doran Estates are delighted to welcome this fantastic home to the rental market. Situated in the highly sought after estate of Stonebridge, the property is surrounded by a warm and welcoming neighbourhood. Property consists of an entrance hallway, a spacious and bright sitting room, a modern fully fitted kitchen, and a convenient downstairs WC. Upstairs there are three generously sized bedrooms, as well as a family bathroom, the master bedroom features a modern ensuite. There is a spacious rear garden which can also be conveniently accessed by the side entrance. Property is situated within a short walk away from Ratoath Village which offers a wide variety of recreational facilities including popular restaurants, bars, shops, etc. There are also sports clubs, community centres, and active schools nearby, creating a fantastic community atmosphere throughout Ratoath. Located a short drive away is the M50, N3, and M2 making this property very accessible to the City Centre, Dublin Airport, and surrounding areas. Please note photos have expired.
