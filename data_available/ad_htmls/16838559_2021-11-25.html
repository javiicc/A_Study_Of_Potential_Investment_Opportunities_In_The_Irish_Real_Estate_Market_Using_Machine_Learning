    Castlemoyne is a Shannon Homes development built in 2005, a development synonymous with luxury living and this property reflects just that. No. 8 is located just off the Malahide Road overlooking a natural green area. \r\n\r\nFather Collins Park is a 10 minute stroll with easy access to the city centre with numerous buses and Clongriffin DART station in the immediate vicinity. Easy access to the M50, M1 and Dublin Airport are all within a 15 minute drive.\r\n\r\nThe five-bedroom property has both space and privacy, creating an ideal home only 30 minutes from the city centre. \r\n\r\nThe property boasts a cobble lock driveway with parking for 2 cars and gated side entrance to the rear garden. \r\n\r\nOnce inside you are greeted by a bright and spacious ground floor area comprising of entrance hallway, living room with feature fireplace, kitchen/dining area, reception room, utility room and a guest WC. \r\n\r\nThe first floor comprises of 4 bedrooms, guest bedroom with en-suite, 3 spacious bedrooms, 2 with fitted wardrobes and a main family bathroom. \r\n\r\nThe eye-catching master bedroom is located on the second floor and has the benefit of a walk-in wardrobe, built in wardrobes and an en-suite, with excellent lighting throughout the year as there are windows on three of the walls.\r\n\r\nNo. 8 comes to the market available for immediate occupancy, ideally suited for those who wish to bring their unique style to this property.
