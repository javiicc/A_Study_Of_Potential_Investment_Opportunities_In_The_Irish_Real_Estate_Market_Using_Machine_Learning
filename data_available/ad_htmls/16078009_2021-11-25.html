    Dillon Marshall are delighted to present this spacious 3 bedroom Terrace home in this stunning and ever popular location. St Lukes Crescent enjoys an enviable location just a short walk from LUAS, Dodder Park, Milltown and UCD. With all amenities on your door step, including Dundrum shopping Center and Village, Milltown Village and all other local schools, bars, shops and restaurants. This property is a must see. \r\n\r\nThe accommodation has a large South Facing rear garden and boasts a fitted kitchen with, separate dinning and living rooms, two large double bedrooms and one single bedroom. There is generous kitchen and separate living area. To the front is a large garden and driveway with parking for 2 cars. The property is double glazed throughout and has oil fired central heating. A key feature is the convenient WC under the stairs.\r\n\r\nSt. Lukes Crescent is located off the Dundrum road in Milltown and benefits from a host of amenities to include an exceptional range of both primary and secondary schools and the University College of Dublin at Belfield. Nearby Donnybrook, Milltown and Ranelagh villages offer a host of stylish restaurants, bars, shops and amenities. Public transport facilities are well catered for with regular bus services and the local Milltown LUAS station. Dundrum Town centre is a 5 minute drive away while the N11 and M50 are also easily accessible.\r\n\r\nDirections\r\nComing from Ranelagh/Milltown head south towards Dundrum over Milltown Bridge and it is second right.\r\nComing from Dundrum head north towards city past The Central Mental Hospital, past traffic lights at Bird Avenue and its is the first left.
