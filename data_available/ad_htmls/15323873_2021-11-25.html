    It gives GILLESPIE LOWE great pleasure to introduce 24 Sprangers Yard, Temple Bar to the open market. This superb two bedroom 2nd floor apartment offers bright and spacious accommodation throughout.\r\nThe location is superb in the heart of temple bar surrounded by shops, restaurants, cafes and bars and only a stone's throw from Trinity College, Ha'Penny Bridge, Grafton Street and St. Stephen's Green.\r\n\r\nTemple bar is in the centre of Dublin (D2) on the south side of the river Liffey. Famed for its cobbled streets, cafes, pubs, nightlife and restaurants, it is a popular tourist destination. There is an abundance of shops, restaurants, bars and supermarkets all within walking distance. It is the heart of Dublin's cultural centre and home to Irish Film Centre. Minutes for the two main shopping areas of Henry Street and O'Connell Street it is a popular residential are for students and young . Local transport links include the Dart, Luas, Dublin Bus & mainline rail. ...\r\nAccommodation\r\nThe accommodation comprises of entrance hall with storage cupboard, open plan living/dining room, a bright kitchen with fitted units and tiled floor, two bedrooms & main bathroom.
