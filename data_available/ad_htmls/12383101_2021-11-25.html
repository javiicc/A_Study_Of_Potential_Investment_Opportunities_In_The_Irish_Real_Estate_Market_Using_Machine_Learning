    Ardea, Ballinacarriga, Dunmanway, Co. Cork

    Site

    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Exceptional sunny east facing 1 acre approx. building site located adjacent to Ballinacarriga village. Ballinacarriga is convenient to Dunmanway, Clonakilty and Bandon towns. The land is level and is an ideal site for development of a dwelling house and ancillary facilities and is for sale subject to a grant of planning permission. \n\nThe area is synonymous with the 16th century Ballinacarriga Castle and lake. The local National School is located 1.8km approx. from the property while Randal g GAA playing pitches and the Manch Branch of Lisavaird Co-Op Grocery shop are also close by.\n\nThe land does not have the benefit of planning permission and is ideally suited to a qualifying buyer as defined - Cork County Plan Development objective RCI 4-3: Tourism and Rural Diversification area. The objective sets out to ensure that local buyers will receive planning priority.
