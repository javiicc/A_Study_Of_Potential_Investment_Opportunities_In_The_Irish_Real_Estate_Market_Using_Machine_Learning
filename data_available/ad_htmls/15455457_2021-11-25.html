    Home and Away Real Estate are delighted to present this superb 3 bed mid-terraced property located in the popular residential area of Ballyphehane. This property comes to the market in good condition and is excellently located close to all amenities, schools, shops etc and also within easy access of Cork City centre. Modernisation required but has huge potential for a beautiful home. This is an ideal house for a growing family looking to locate to a popular Cork location. It would also suit investors seeking a strong yield as the property's rent could be set to current market rates. Accommodation includes large/ dining room to the rear of the property with adjoining living room. There is a second living to the front. Conveniently there is a WC downstairs. Upstairs there are 3 generous bedrooms with a family bathroom. Outside there is a large rear garden with an array of outbuilding which has access to the rear. This property enjoys maximum privacy with all the benefits of Estate Living and would make a perfect family home and is conveniently located near the Jack Lynch tunnel, Cork city centre and all local amenities. This property holds huge potential and offers a great opportunity to acquire a quality home in a great location.
