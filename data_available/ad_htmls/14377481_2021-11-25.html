    We are delighted to offer to the market this truly unique four bedroom property on "The Square"\xef\xbf\xbd in the picturesque village of Ballyhaise. With all amenities within a short walking distance, this low-maintenance property combines convenience with beauty, which is obvious once you cross the threshold of this historical property. Originally built pre-1900s, this home has been completely renovated very recently and is ready for immediate occupation. \r\n\r\nAttention was given to every detail when the renovation was undertaken, and the result is the beautifully unique design with modern features. On entering you are transported to a foreign land thanks to the continental villa feel.\r\n\r\nAccommodation is laid out as follows:\r\n.Entrance hall - 2.45m x 3.37m\r\n.Hallway - 3.20m x 0.94m\r\n.Dining/living room - 4.27m x 4.85m - Stove with back boiler heating radiators / Timber floor\r\n.Sitting room - 4.70m x 4.01m - Timber floor\r\n.Kitchen - 3.23m x 3.26m\r\n.Double bedroom / reception room / office - 4.73m x 4.00m - Solid fuel stove / Timber floor\r\n.Back hall - 2.78m x 1.17m\r\n.Accessible wetroom - 2.75m x 1.98m\r\n.Stairwell - 2.54m x 2.38m - Carpeted\r\n.Landing - 14.5 sq. m. - Carpeted\r\n.Double bedroom - 4.08m x 4.86m - New solid fuel stove / Excellent views from fire escape tilt & turn windows / Timber floor / Fits super-king bed\r\n.Double bedroom - 3.26m x 3.87m - Timber floor / Excellent views from fire escape tilt & turn windows / Fits king bed\r\n.Double bedroom - 4.39m x 2.29m - Timber floor\r\n.Bathroom - 2.72m x 2.22m - Jacuzzi bath & shower\r\n\r\nThe property is heated by way of oil-fired central heating complemented by a stove with a back boiler which heats all radiators, and solid fuel stoves in two bedrooms. New front and back doors have been installed as well as various new windows, and glass replaced with double-glazed in all windows. A power pump was installed ensuring sufficient water supply and pressure, and fans added to ceilings. Emergency lights installed to activate in the case of a power outage. The spacious yard and shed adjacent are secured by way of an electronic gate. Intruder alarm and monitoring system installed. \r\n\r\nLocated centrally in Ballyhaise, a tranquil village through which the Annalee river flows, this property is within short walking distance of shops and butchers, churches, a national school, hair and beauty parlours, a hardware store, a pharmacy, a Chinese takeaway, GAA grounds, bars, and a riverside recreational area. The historical Ballyhaise Agricultural college is nearby and there are numerous fishing locations to be found\r\n\r\nFor all enquiries please contact our office on 0494380434 or by email.
