    Green Door Properties are pleased to introduce to the market number 36 Pococke Upper. This three bedroom semi-detached house would make an ideal starter home or an investment opportunity. The interior accommodation is bright, stylish & well-proportioned throughout. Upon entering the property there is a lovely bright tiled entrance hallway which to the right of leads you into the cosy sitting room with wood burning stove. To the rear of the property is the kitchen-dining room which has tiled flooring Upstairs there are three bedrooms just off the landing area. 2 doubles and 1 single, both double bedrooms have fitted wardrobes. Access to the attic and the hotpress are also located on this level also. \r\nThe location of this property is second to none being situated in a popular estate enjoying an enviable location, with a host of local shops, schools, creches, churches and recreational amenities. Within easy walking distance to the shopping centres, Newpark & MacDonagh Junction, it further benefits from its close proximity to Kilkenny's ring road and the M9 Motorway linking Kilkenny/Dublin/Waterford and just a 15 minute walk from Kilkenny City.
