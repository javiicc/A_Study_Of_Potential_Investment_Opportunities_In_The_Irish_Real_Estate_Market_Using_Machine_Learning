Clare V94 T8X2\r\nImpressive lakeside bungalow on a a spacious 1 acre site \r\nA beautiful, one of a kind, Dormer Bungalow on the lakeshore of Lough Derg, this prime property is set in an unrivalled setting on one of the most beautiful locations in the renowned Mountshannon area. \r\nIt enjoys stunning views over the lake and surrounding areas.\r\nIt is a unique residential property with a well-designed dormer bungalow on a spacious site, in a private cul-de-sac location, close to the lake-shore. \r\nIt is 4km. from Mountshannon, 12km. from Scariff, 40km from Limerick, 30km. from Junction 27 on the M7 and 24km from Portumna. \r\n\r\nThe house was built 20 years ago and has been very well maintained.\r\nIn total the house spans 250 sq. metres, it is finished to an extremely high standard and stands on 0.4 ha. site.\r\nIt is unique in that the bedrooms are downstairs and the living area is upstairs - to maximise the view over the lake. \r\nIt is situated on the south of its elevated 1 acre, west-facing site that has an uninterrupted view over Lough Derg. \r\n\r\nIn detail the house contains: \r\nGround floor; entrance porch leading into the downstairs hallway. \r\nFrom the hallway there are doors leading to 3 good-sized bedrooms, a bathroom and a utility room. \t \r\nTwo of the bedrooms have direct access to the bathroom in a so-called Jack & Jill arrangement where occupants from two separate bedrooms can access the same bathroom.\r\nThe third bedroom has a bathroom attached, and that bathroom can also be accessed from the hallway through the utility room. The shower area has a Fench drain facility for drying water sports clothing. \r\nAt the back of the hallway is the staircase to the first floor.\r\n \r\nUpstairs; \r\nis a large open-plan living area and a galley kitchen area at the back. \r\nThe living area is a beautiful large bright room with dual aspect and a large open fire. It is a spacious sitting room, a dining area and other space suitable for a study area or for storage.\r\nThe galley kitchen area is compact and has generous storage units.\r\n \r\nThe house is heated with oil-fired central heating and an open fire-place in the sitting room. It is an energy efficient house with a C3 rating.\r\nThe house is serviced with an on-site bored well and it is also connected to the local mains water supply. \r\nThe on-site septic tank is to the north of the house.\r\nAt the back of the house, and part of it, are 2 storage sheds, one containing the central heating boiler. \r\n\r\nThe house is accessed from the road along a short driveway and there is \r\na lawn area at the front of the house, and parking area at the side and at the back of the house. \r\nThere is a raised area at the back and a field area to the northern side of the house. There are also shrubbed areas in front of the house and on the southern side of the site. \r\n\r\nThe property benefits from an admirable setting that is within easy reach of many facilities e.g churches, pubs, schools, shopping, water sports and golfing in East Clare or Portumna golf clubs\r\n\r\nThis property must be viewed to be fully appreciated.\r\n\r\nViewing very strictly by appointment with ourselves.
