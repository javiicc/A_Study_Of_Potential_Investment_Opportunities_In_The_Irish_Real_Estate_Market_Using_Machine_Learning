    Magnificent, Impressive, Striking, take your pick but words cannot describe the feeling of peace, privacy and tranquility that is evident as you turn off the public road and onto a winding avenue which is adorned by a mixture of centuries old trees leading to this splendid country residence.\r\n\r\nYes this is 'Carrigeen', Clonoulty in the heart of Co. Tipperary. \r\n\r\nThis is a real gem, standing proud and strong on almost 2.5 acres of lawns and paddock. This is a beautiful location in a scenic and open countryside in the heart of the famed Golden Vale, with the majestic Galtee Mountains to the foreground. Many stud farms are established in the area, the most famous being Coolmore Stud of world renown. \r\n \r\nClonoulty is close to the towns of Cashel, Thurles and Tipperary with easy access to the M8 Dublin/Cork motorway. Thurles train station is only a 15 min drive from the property.\r\n\r\nThe house itself dates back to the early 1800's and is associated with one Thomas Francis Meagher (Irish Patriot and leader of the Young Irelanders) who spent his last two nights of freedom in this small townland before his capture in 1848.\r\n \r\nThe house is in excellent condition and is testament to the loving care and attention afforded down through the years by its owners who appreciated the craft of a bygone era. \r\n\r\nAccommodation details:\r\n\r\n-\tPorch: 7' x 5' (2.38m x 1.6m).
