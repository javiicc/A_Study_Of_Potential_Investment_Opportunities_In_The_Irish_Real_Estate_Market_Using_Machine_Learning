    This three bedroom period house offers a chance to buy an impressive turnkey condition property located right in the heart of New Ross. With its well - designed layout it is fit for very comfortable family living. Accommodation comprises of a bright and spacious entrance hall leading into an open plan living space / sitting room and into kitchen. Double doors off the kitchen leading out to the back patio area offers plenty of natural light making this space homely. A utility room and toilet concludes downstairs accommodation. On the first floor there is a large double sized bedroom & main family bathroom. Second floor includes two very spacious bedrooms. This property has been renovated to a modern standard, including highly efficient electric radiators throughout the house. Priory Street is located next to Pearse Park, offering a running track, playground and lovely walks on your door step. Minutes from the beautiful Boardwalk which passes The Marina and continues onto many more main attractions in New Ross, such as the Dunbrody famine ship, JFK statue etc. The other direction gives you easy access to the N25 Bypass, leading to the newly opened Rose Kennedy Fitzgerald Bridge, making not only Wexford but Waterford and Kilkenny very accessible and an easy commute.
