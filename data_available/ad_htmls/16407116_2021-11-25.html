    Award winning team Nicola Wynne-Jankola is delighted to present this charming 2 bedroom 1 bath apartment in this popular & extremely sought after location in the heart of Smithfield.\n\nThis unique, 2 bedroom / 1 bath apartment is located on the first floor floor of this historical apartment block. Number 37 is presented in excellent condition throughout having been recently refurbished. Conveniently located on Bow Street the apartment benefits from both red and green Luas lines, proximity to the Four Courts, the new DIT Grangegorman Campus and Stoneybatter further enhance the appeal of the property. There are a host of local amenities available on your doorstep, including shops, bars, restaurants, cinema, as well as everything Dublin City Centre has to offer. If it is a city centre base you are looking for, this is an ideal apartment for you.
