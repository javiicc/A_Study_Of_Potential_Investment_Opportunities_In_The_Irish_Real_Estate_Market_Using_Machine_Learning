    A spacious five bedroom bungalow with attic accommodation on approximately 0.988 of an acre. Set back off the Main Road with large lawns to the front and rear and just a short walk from Glenties Town. This well proportioned home features PVC-framed double glazed windows, oil fired central heating, with all services connected and ample parking space. Ideally located as a holiday or residential home, just a short drive to Narin Beach and the large towns of Letterkenny, Donegal Town and Dungloe. Internal accommodation on the ground floor entrance hall, sitting room, kitchen/dining area, utility, two bedrooms and bathroom with two bedrooms on the ground floor with a further three bedrooms and family bathroom on the first floor. Complemented with a spacious detached garage at the rear.
