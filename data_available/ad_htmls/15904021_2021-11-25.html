    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    There is something magical about Crough Creek. When the tide is full there are fish beneath and fowl above the waters; when the tide low the place is alive with waders....this little cottage is of no interest to any of them but for us nature lovers this is a lovers nest, quite unique, secluded, private.. a paradise: the stuff that dreams are made of.....\r\n\r\nThe property consists of:\r\n\r\nOpen plan kitchen, sitting, dining room with a balcony bedroom overhead. To the rear is the bathroom and a double bedroom\r\nOutside there is a stone faced storage shed recently renovated along with a separate building which houses the sauna and the heating both oil fired and heat pump systems.
