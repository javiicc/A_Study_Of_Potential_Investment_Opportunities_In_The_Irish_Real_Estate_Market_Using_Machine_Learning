    I am delighted to bring to the market this stunning property located in the most westerly of locations on the Dingle Peninsula. The property is a large spacious dwelling with stunning views located in a beautiful rural setting approx half a mile from Dunquin village. The property is situated on a large site of circa 0.42 hectares (1 Acre) which a lovely mountain stream runs along the boundary of the Garden. The property consists of a Entrance Porch, Large spacious Hallway, Kitchen/Dining area, Conservatory, Toilet,Utility space & home office, Also on the ground floor there is a spacious livingroom with a built in kitchen and seperate access onto a lovely decking area which commands spectacular views and off the livingroom you have a large Double Bedroom & Bathroom this area as it has its own access has lended itself to be used as a self contained space for air b&b. On the first floor there are 3 further bedrooms 2 of which are Ensuite.\nIf its a property with a view and plenty of space and tranquility you are after then Gleannmor is a must view for you.
