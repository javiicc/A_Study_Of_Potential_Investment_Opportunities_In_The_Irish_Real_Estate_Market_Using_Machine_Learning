It really must be seen to be appreciated.\r\n\r\nThe location itself needs no introduction and is second to none with every amenity close by. Public transport, schools, churches, and many sporting and recreational facilities are within walking distance. Dublin city centre and Dublin International Airport are approximately 3 miles away.
