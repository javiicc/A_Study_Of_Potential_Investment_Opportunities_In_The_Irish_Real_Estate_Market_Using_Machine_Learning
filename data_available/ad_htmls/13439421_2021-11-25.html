Once inside the sylvan setting lends itself more to a house in the countryside rather than in the suburbs of south county Dublin, yet its rural coastal setting is only a half an hour commute from the City Centre. It\xe2\x80\x99s really the best of both worlds.\n\nThe property boasts road frontage onto Seafield Road of approaching 61m (200ft).
