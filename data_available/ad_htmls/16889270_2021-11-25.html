    Churches are delighted to present to market No. 6 Ridge Hall, a spacious second floor apartment finished to a very high specification throughout complemented by its south facing aspect all within a highly sought after development. \n\nRidge Hall is a meticulously maintained private development located in Ballybrack. Built in 2004, admired for its well landscaped gardens, manicured tree lined entrance and peaceful atmosphere. \n\nLocated on the second floor, upon entry you are instantly greeted by a tremendous sense of space. From the bright entrance hallway leading to its modern open plan layout making it the perfect for comfortable living accommodation. Large floor to ceiling windows allow an abundance of natural light flood through the rear balcony into the spacious living area with a neatly designed fitted kitchen to front.\n\nThis apartment boasts two large double bedrooms both with fitted wardrobes. A generously proportioned master bedroom with full ensuite and a second large double bedroom with glass door access to the rear balcony. Also located off the central hall is the main bathroom and storage closet. With excellent proportions and smart use of light this apartment is sure to pique your interest whether you are a first time buyer / downsizing or looking for a good investment.\n\nLocation is superb with Ballybrack Village on your door step offering a selection of local convenient stores, cafes, pubs and all other amenities needed. A short 10minute walk takes you directly to Killiney DART Station. The beautiful coastal suburbs of Dalkey, Shankill & Killiney Beach are all well within reach, also road access to M50 & N11road networks with the ease of two minutes driving. Numerous local Dublin Bus Routes running throughout the village.
