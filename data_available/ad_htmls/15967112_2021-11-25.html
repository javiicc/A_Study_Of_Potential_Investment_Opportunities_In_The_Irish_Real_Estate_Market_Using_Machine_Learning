    Laraheen 3.6 Acres with Stables, Gorey, Co. Wexford

    3.6 ac

    Site

    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 3.6 ac
    3.6 acres parcel of land with stable yard and four paddocks. \r\n\r\nSet in beautiful country side with panoramic views the land is entered through a large stone entrance and gates with a long drive leading down to the stable yard. The yard is centered around a sand arena with five well appointed stables incorporating a tack room and toilet. A newly constructed hay shed and small feeding yard. \r\nA private well and electricity is already installed on the site. \r\n\r\nOne of the paddocks has a partly constructed house which no longer has valid planning. The owner has obtained planning for a new house design but planning would need to be reapplied for under local planning guidelines. The current planning is only granted to the owner and not transferable. \r\n\r\n***The site is for sale as is********\r\n\r\nLocation : The site is situated between Kilinerian and Holyfort and just under 6km from Gorey. Holyfort village is just over 1km with Kilinerian just 3km away. Its also close to the m11 exit and just 50 mins from Dublin. \r\n\r\nDirections: \r\nFrom Gorey: Turn at Bobs Bar and drive out of the town past Gorey show grounds and up Creagh hill. Keep on the road for approx 2.5km until you come to a Y junction with a cottage in the middle. Keep left and drive another 1.5km to the end of the road. At the T junction turn right and proceed for 1km. You will pass a row of houses as you progress up the second hill and The property is just after these on the left hand side with a large stone entrance and silver entrance gates. \r\n\r\nFrom Arklow / Dublin \r\nProceed along the m11 until you reach the north exit for Gorey at green berry farm. \r\nTurn right for Kilinerian village at green berry farm. \r\nProceed straight through the village passing the church on your right until your reach a cross roads about 500m away. Turn left sign posted holyfort Proceed along this road for approx 2.5km passing straight through another cross roads and the property is located just on the right hand side as you proceed down the hill. A large stone entrance and silver gates.
