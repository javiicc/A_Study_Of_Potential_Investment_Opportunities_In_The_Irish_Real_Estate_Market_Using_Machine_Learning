    Sherry FitzGerald McCreery are pleased to present Bethany, Burnchurch, Cuffesgrange, Co Kilkenny to the market for sale. Ideal for the equestrian enthusiast, this impressive Architect designed family home on circa 2.97 Acres / 1.2 Hectares will attract local, national and international interest. This wonderful country retreat is superbly located in the sought-after townland of Burnchurch, a short drive from Kilkenny City Centre. \n\nA sweeping gravel driveway from the main access laneway leads to a very imposing two storey residence with extensive and light filled accommodation. Bethany was constructed in 2005 and designed by Gittens Murray Architect who are based in Kilkenny City.\n \nThe property extends to 279 Sq. M. / 3,003 Sq. Ft spanning over two levels. An attractive entrance foyer leads to the main hall with mosaic floor tiling, timber panelled walls and a striking staircase to the first floor. Double doors to the left of the main entrance hall lead to the drawing room. This spacious and bright dual aspect room features a handsome marble open fireplace, solid timber floor, ceiling rose and French doors which open out to the veranda. Directly across the hall, double doors lead to a very spacious and open-plan kitchen/dining room with tiled floors. The well equipped kitchen is very practical for a cooking enthusiast, and is fitted with high quality bespoke wall and floor units finished with solid granite countertops and incorporates an island with an extensive array of clever storage facilities. A smart black coloured oil fired Aga Range Cooker is the heart of the kitchen along with a Waterford Stanley stove and high quality appliances. Adjacent to the kitchen, a glass door leads to a cosy family room finished to a very high standard with timber panelled walls, solid timber floor and fitted with a multi fuel cast-iron stove. French doors open out to the veranda. A hallway off the kitchen leads to a large utility room with extensive storage presses, office/bedroom five and a shower room/guest WC. This completes the accommodation at ground level. The first floor accommodation is as equally impressive and comprises: four generous double bedrooms facing the front and rear of the property. The master bedroom enjoys an en-suite bathroom complete with a walk-in wardrobe. Two adjoining bedrooms have access to a Jack & Jill en-suite. A family bathroom completes the accommodation at first floor level.\n \nGARDEN AND PADDOCKS:\nThe flair and finish also extends to the exterior with a magnificent wrap around veranda and patio area which is perfect for alfresco dining and entertaining on a grand scale. The extensive grounds provide peace and tranquillity and are a haven for children to play in privacy and safety. A tastefully detached garage and carport compliment the main house. Equestrian facilities are situated adjacent to the main residence with a separate access from the main access laneway. There is a large stand alone hay shed (measuring 30ft x 45ft approx.). To the rear of the shed is a Lean-to stable fitted with 3 loose boxes (measuring 24ft x 17ft approx.) and a tack room. A stud railed sand arena is adjacent to the outbuildings and measures 50m x 28m approx.\n\nLOCATION:\nBethany is superbly located in the sought-after townland of Burnchurch which is in the parish of Cuffesgrange. A two minute drive will take you to the N76 Callan/Clonmel Road with a short commuting time of less than 10 minutes into Kilkenny City centre. A journey of 8 minutes will take you to the M9 Motorway (Junction 9 Danesfort) allowing easy access to Waterford, Kildare and Dublin. Burnchurch National School and Church Hill National School are both in very close proximity. The historic Burnchurch Castle along with Ballybur Castle are both just minutes away from the property. The town of Callan is less than a 10 minute drive away. The property is also close to a number of picturesque villages including Danesfort, Kells, Stoneyford, Bennettsbridge, Thomastown and Inistioge. A 15 minute drive will take you to the magnificent Mount Juliet Estate and Golf Club. There are a number of other golf courses in easy access including Callan Golf Club, Kilkenny Golf Club and Gowran Golf Club. \n\nViewing of this wonderful property is highly recommended.\nGROUND FLOOR\n\nPorch 2.30m x 1.53m.
