    Extended three bedroomed home with home office\nAppleton Property have great pleasure is bringing this immaculate, turn key, modern three bedroomed home to the market for sale. This bright spacious property is located at The Woods, off the Newbridge Road in Kilcullen within easy walking distance to both primary and secondary schools and all the amenities of Kilcullen Town. Comprising c. 1400 sq ft, the internal space is divided into bright entrance hallway with bespoke recently laid parquet flooring, composite front door, with guest wc and utility room adjacent, spacious bright livingroom also with parquet flooring and built in TV unit/display cabinet, open plan extended kitchen/living/dining room with modern kitchen with granite counter top, 5 ring hob, double oven, integrated dishwasher and space for American fridge freezer. With vaulted ceiling with two velux windows and recessed lighting this extension spans the width of the house to the rear and adds greatly to the living space in the property. Off the kitchen to the rear, there is an adjoining home office with separate entrance. At first floor level there are three well-proportioned bedrooms with tiled ensuite and main bathroom. This property presents as a modern turn key home with many fine fixtures, features and fittings to include thermostatically controlled zoned heating system on gas mains, upgraded bathrooms, extended kitchen with granite counter top and vaulted ceiling, parquet flooring to hall and livingroom, double glazed PVC windows, C1 BER Rating, low maintenance exterior, black PVC facia and soffit, gated side entrance with built in storage area and cobble locked low maintenance rear. Number 23 presents as a walk-in property which has been meticulously maintained and continuously improved by its house proud owners. Kilcullen is a bustling town of c. 4000 inhabitants located 26 miles south of Dublin with M9 motorway intersection. The Woods, Cnoc na Greine, is a very popular development given its proximity to the local primary and secondary schools, sporting amenities, new playground and the town itself. Kilcullen is an immensely popular location given its close proximity to Dublin, M9 Interchange, regular bus route to Dublin and town link bus to the larger town of Newbridge. Kilcullen has everything required for day to day living with larger shopping needs catered for in the nearby towns of Newbridge with the renowned Whitewater Shopping Centre and the Kildare Village Outlet also within a short drive. Properties in Kilcullen move quickly and we advise any intending purchaser to view early to avoid disappointment.
