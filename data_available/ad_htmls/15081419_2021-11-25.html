    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to present 85 Clonliffe Road to the market. \n\nBehind the pretty red brick exterior lies an extremely spacious villa style family residence which has been renovated and decorated with great style and flair. Many of the original features have been retained and complement the high ceilings, ornate ceiling covings and elegant proportions of the house which combine beautifully with all the modern touches.\n\nUpon entering this fine home is an impressive entrance hallway with high ceilings, original timber floors and ceiling coving. From here, the living room is well-appointed with original timber floors, window shutters and a cast iron fireplace providing the main focal point. From the hallway steps lead to an inner hallway with understairs storage and a double bedroom which overlooks the rear garden. To the rear of the property, the dining room is filled with natural light with ceiling coving and central rose and opens into the kitchen which has an extensive array of kitchen units and tiled splashback. The utility area is plumbed, with built in units and shelving. The modern shower room is fully tiled with walk in rainfall shower and completes the ground floor accommodation. Upstairs, there are two double bedrooms, both with modern ensuite overlooking the rear garden. The larger bedroom has a walk-in dressing room with built in wardrobes. The rear garden is south facing and maintenance free. It gets sunshine all day long and is a wonderful area for al fresco dining or simply relaxing. To the front, there is a railed garden bordered by mature hedging.\n\nClonliffe Road has long been regarded as one of the most popular locations in Drumcondra, convenient to all the amenities of village and within a gentle stroll of Drumcondra Train Station and the city centre. There are many amenities such as Griffith Park, the Botanic Gardens and Na Fianna GAA club close by. The area has quite a number of popular national & secondary schools and is convenient to Dublin City University, the Four Courts, Law Library, Mater Hospital and the IFSC, this fantastic property will appeal to those seeking a family home in a superb location.\n\n\nGround Floor\n\nEntrance Impressive welcoming hallway with high ceilings, ceiling coving, original solid timber floor and understairs storage\n\nLiving Room 4.33m x 4.20m.
