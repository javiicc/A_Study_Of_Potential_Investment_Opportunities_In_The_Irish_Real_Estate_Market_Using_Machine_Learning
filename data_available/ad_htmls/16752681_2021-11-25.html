    No. 8 Seabury Meadows is a wonderful four-bedroom, semi-detached home located in this family friendly estate. With an inviting and homely feel throughout, this property has been extended and upgraded to a high standard. The well-proportioned accommodation includes entrance porch/ boot room, hallway with guest w.c., lounge and fabulous extended kitchen / dining / family room. Upstairs there is a spectacular master bedroom with walk in wardrobe area and ensuite wet room, three further bedrooms and a large family bathroom. The rear garden a patio area and a block built shed which is plumbed and has power, currently used as a utility room/ storage.\n\nSeabury is in a mature residential location close to the scenic Broadmeadow Estuary and within walking distance of local shopping facilities.\nIt is also within easy reach of every amenity Malahide Village has to offer, including high quality restaurants, excellent boutiques and access to both DART and Dublin bus.
