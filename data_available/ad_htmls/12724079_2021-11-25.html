    Number 21 occupies a truly enviable location in this elegant terrace of handsome 2 and 3 storey over garden level period homes \nenjoying breath taking views and a spectacular vista across Dublin Bay to Howth which are equally dramatic by both day and night.\n\nTake an early morning swim at Seapoint or cycle along the wonderful dedicated cycle path followed by a coffee in one of the many coffee shops in the picturesque Monkstown village which is bustling with cafes, bistros and boutiques and has a distinctly Continental atmosphere. Enjoy the relaxed lifestyle and amenities that this very special area has to offer including , sea swimming, coastal walks, cycling, yachting and fishing to name but a few.\n\nThis splendid family home offers very generous and flexible accommodation and plenty of space to work from home together with all the original period features every modern comfort and a glorious totally private south facing garden, a beautiful oasis of peace and quiet by the sea.\n\nNumber 21 is a stunning period residence of immense character and charm situated along this popular terrace off Seapoint Avenue. Rarely do houses of this age, 1840, come to the market displaying so many original architectural features which have been sensitively refurbished. Dramatic in appearance number 21 occupies a prominent position on this popular terrace and benefits from wonderful sea views over Dublin bay towards Howth.\n\nThe extent of the substantial and superbly proportioned accommodation only becomes evident as you walk through the house. What is particularly unusual is the 4 storey return, which helps define an astonishing 5,500sq.ft. of accommodation.\n\nThe dramatic entrance hall sets the tone for the accommodation to follow. Not only are there inter communicating reception on the hall floor but the entire first floor provides for two exceptional connecting reception rooms which, like all the rooms to the front of the house, take full advantage of the wonderful views.\n\nThe extensive accommodation provides for a versatile layout of choice. Decide yourself on what the balance of accommodation should be. The current layout has a greater emphasis on reception rather than bedroom accommodation, however the property could easily provide for 5 or 6 bedrooms.\n\nFinally, it is easy to understand why the location is so popular.
