    RAY COOKE AUCTIONEERS take great pleasure in introducing this excellent two bedroom property to the market complete with a large south facing garden. Redwood Lawn is enviably located within a short stroll of The Kingswood Luas Stop and Dunnes Stores. A host of other essential amenities are all very easily accessible including primary & secondary schools, The Square Shopping Centre, Tallaght Hospital, a variety of bus routes and the M50 Motorway. \n\nBright and spacious internal living accommodation totals c. 1,020 sq ft (95 sq m) and comprises of entrance hallway, storage room, living room, kitchen/dining room, two spacious bedrooms and main family bathroom. The rear is extra large and boasts a super lawn area and a block built shed. \n\nNo. 14A is a perfect property for any young family looking to lay down roots in such a settled location and boasting an enviable position at the end of a cul de sac with a green space on your doorstep.
