    Final phase of 27 apartments in block C comprising large high quality 2 and 3 bedroom apartments and penthouses on a superb landscaped 5 acre site.\n\nThis is a unique parkland development by O' Malley Construction Company of luxurious apartments set on professionally landscaped grounds featuring mature planting which further add to the appeal of this very distinctive development.\n\nO' Malley Construction Company was formed in 1971 and for 50 years the core business of building residential communities has remained its focus. A Galway based company, they have completed a number of very successful developments of houses and apartments in Dublin since the 1990' s.\n\nThese developments have included " Churchfields" in Milltown, " Ballsbridge Wood," "Sunbury gardens" in Dartry, and " Symphony House" on Adelaide Road. The company was also involved in the delivery of Apartments at " St. Helens" in Stillorgan and most recently, Blocks 1 and 2 at " Wyckham Point" in Dundrum. Along with its core business, O' Malley Construction has completed a number of landmark buildings around Galway including Jury' s Inn hotel, Galway Bay Hotel, The Eyre Square Shopping Centre and The House Hotel. \n\nThe provision of amenities on site and very high levels of landscaping enhancing the neighbourhood and communities that they establish. The use of quality materials, Architectural Design and Management of Common areas ensures a quality lifestyle for residents. A rare opportunity to acquire newly built homes with the benefit of modern building techniques, energy savings and a highly sought after location is now available to the market from this O' Malley Built scheme. \n\n\nArdilea, Clonskeagh is widely regarded as one of the capital' s finest residential addresses. Ardilea Crescent, tucked away off Heidelberg, in a peaceful mature leafy cul-de-sac setting just minutes from the city centre, is convenient to all the amenities of Blackrock, Dun Laoghaire, Dundrum, Donnybrook, Ballsbridge, Ranelagh and Stillorgan. When complete Ardilea Crescent will have 27 houses and 75 apartments built in three blocks.\n\nThere are numerous educational, recreational and leisure facilities within a short stroll including the UCD Campus at Belfield just next door, Deer Park tennis & sports grounds, Elm Park and Milltown golf courses and Mount Merrion Football Club. Ardilea Crescent is convenient to many popular amenities including shops, excellent schools, restaurants, a host of leisure and sporting facilities, frequent bus routes, all the major business and office hubs of south Dublin. \n\nThe Luas stops at Dundrum and Kilmacud are just a short drive away provides swift and easy access to St. Stephen' s Green and to Sandyford Business Park. There are good bus services to the city.
