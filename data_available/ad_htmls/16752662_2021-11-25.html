    Beautiful, traditional stone cottage on 1.2 acres of organically certified land with Polytunnels and grazing rights to an additional one acre (can be purchased separately). Escape to the country & become self sufficient. \r\n\r\nThis is a unique opportunity to buy a little piece of heaven situated just off the Wicklow Way within commuting distance of Dublin, Carlow, Kildare & Wexford. Primary & secondary schools within a 5-15 minute drive. \r\n\r\nThe current owners have been running their own organic fruit & vegetable business from here for several years, the property includes 3 polytunnels, plenty of outdoor growing space, a large galvanised metal shed with electric connected & a small wooden tool shed. \r\n\r\nThe cottage consists of two bedrooms , with room to extend to accomodate further bedrooms (planning permission required), 1 bathroom, 1 sitting room, 1 Pool room (stone extension which could be converted to Kitchen or third bedroom) kitchen, landscaped gardens, orchard, electric entrance gate, stonewall boundry with a patio bbq area, separate patio pizza oven for external dining & a fenced dog run/meditation garden with flowering cherry trees, crab apple & oak trees. \r\n\r\nGood investment potential with proposed ban on one off dwellings.\r\n\r\nExcellent broadband with speeds up to 150 mbs & an office which is ideal for working from home.
