    Baxter Real Estate are delighted to present to the market, this spacious, 4-bedroom/1-bathroom, semi-detached family home, which occupies an area of approximately 107 sq.m./1,150 sq.ft.. On entering, you are greeted by a spacious hallway, which leads to a living room with a large picture window, a carpeted floor, and French doors opening to the dining room. The dining room also has a picture window and a carpeted floor. To the front, there is a family room, again with a large picture window and a carpeted floor. The kitchen, with tiled floor and tiled splashback, provides access to the south-east facing rear garden. At first-floor level there are four bedrooms, all of which have carpeted floors. Three of of the bedrooms have built-in wardrobes. A family bathroom, with partially-tiled walls, completes the accommodation. \r\n\r\nAccommodation:\r\nLiving Room - 14' 7'' x 11' 11'' (4.45m x 3.62m) - Carpeted floor, a large picture window, and French doors opening to the dining room.\r\nDining Room - 14' 11'' x 9' 10'' (4.54m x 2.99m) - Carpeted floor, a large picture window, understair storage, and French doors opening to the living room.\r\nFamily Room - 10' 9'' x 6' 10'' (3.28m x 2.08m) - Carpeted floor and a large picture window.\r\nKitchen - 11' 7'' x 8' 11'' (3.53m x 2.71m) - Tiled floor and tiled splashback.\r\nBedroom 1 - 11' 9'' x 10' 11'' (3.57m x 3.32m) - Built-in wardrobes and a carpeted floor.\r\nBedroom 2 - 11' 9'' x 8' 1'' (3.58m x 2.47m) - Built-in wardrobes and a carpeted floor.\r\nBedroom 3 - 10' 8'' x 10' 7'' (3.26m x 3.23m) - Built-in wardrobes and a carpeted floor.\r\nBedroom 4 - 11' 9'' x 8' 6'' (3.59m x 2.58m) - Carpeted floor.\r\nFamily Bathroom - 7' 1'' x 5' 7'' (2.17m x 1.70m) - Partially-tiled walls.\r\n\r\nThis family home is ideally located close to all amenities including schools and shops, such as the Blanchardstown Shopping Centre.
