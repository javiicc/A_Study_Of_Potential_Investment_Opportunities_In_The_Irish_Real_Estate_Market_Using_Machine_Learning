    Description\r\nBergins are delighted to present for sale this spacious and very well presented penthouse apartment, in the highly regarded Woodview development on Mount Merrion Avenue.\r\n\r\nNumber 72 comes to the market with over 275 sq meters of living accommodation with an additional and very substantial storage unit of 51 sq meters. This secured unit in the underground car park would be sufficient space to store an additional two-three cars, over and above the two additional parking spaces available on the main floor of the carpark itself.\r\n\r\nExtending to over 3000 square feet, this very elegant property provides very generous open plan living accommodation, with four well appointed and spacious double bedrooms as well as two private balconies.\r\n\r\nWoodview is perfectly positioned on Mount Merrion Avenue, a beautifully landscaped development with carefully and well tended mature gardens meandering through the site. At the junction of Mount Merrion Avenue and Cross Avenue, it is a short walk to local conveniences and just further down the road to Blackrock Village and to Frascati and Blackrock Shopping Centres. Local amenities and recreational facilities are excellent along with superb transport links, including Dart, N11 QBC, Aircoach to Dublin Airport. Schools within walking distance of Woodview are among the most prestigious in South Dublin, to include St Andrews, Blackrock College, Mount Anville, Sion Hill, Colaiste Eoin and Iosagain, Booterstown National, and also UCD.\r\n\r\nThe apartment itself with its spacious and elegant floor plan is beautifully presented and light filled. Access to the penthouse floor is via secure lift, with additional staircase. The storage unit is in the basement carpark with roller shutter and the main carpark itself is accessed with a secure fob.\r\nRooms\r\nHall -\r\nOpening from the landing and designated elevator access to Penthouse 72, this spans the width of the property connecting the four bedrooms to the right with the spacious living areas to the left.\r\n\r\nLiving Dining Kitchen - 10.4m x 9.0m\r\nA significant open plan reception room with built in custom kitchen, this room is accessed from hall at two points - by double doors directly in to the living area with its sandstone fire place and french doors to the balcony beyond.\r\nMoving through the living area the generous dining area behind this is designed for entertaining and easily accommodates a very large dining table.\r\nCompleting this space and to the right is the very well appointed kitchen with its dramatic kitchen island. Units are cherry and include a wide array of fitted low level units, with integrated appliances and a polished granite work top.\r\nThe kitchen extends around and behind the hall way and opens in to a utility room with washer, dryer, storage and the Gas Boiler.\r\n\r\n\r\nBedroom 4 - 5.3m x 2.3m\r\nMoving from the living room back in to the hall, this is the first room to the left. Currently used as a study, this could also be used as a double bedroom.\r\n\r\nMain Bathroom - 4.2m x 2.5m\r\nAdjacent to Bedroom 4 and on the same side of the corridor this is a very large bathroom with corner bath, walk in shower, WC and WHB. Part tiled and in good decorative order.\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 - 6.1m x 5.0m\r\nOpposite Bedroom 4 and main bathroom, to the right of the main corridor, this is a significantly large double bedroom, with a spacious ensuite bathroom and ample fitted wardrobes. This bedroom also has access to an additional balcony, overlooking the common areas of Woodview.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 - 6.1m x 4.0m\r\nBeyond Bedroom 1 and on the same side of the corridor, another very spacious double bedroom, with further wardrobes.\r\n\r\nBedroom 3 - 7.2m x 4.2m\r\nAt the end of the corridor, this final large double bedroom spans the width of the apartment and includes another very spacious ensuite bathroom. With fitted wardrobes there is also ample space for seating or home office/desk.
