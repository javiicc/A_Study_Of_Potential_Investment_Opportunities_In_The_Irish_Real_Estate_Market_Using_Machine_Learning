"Clonford" boasts c. 3,000 sq ft of luxurious and light filled living accommodation with stunning picturesque countryside views.\r\n\r\nClonford has been completely renovated and finished to the highest standard over the past two years. This truly is a gem of a family home..!!\r\n\r\nThe location is next to none as you are mere 5 minutes from Rathcoole Village and all it has to offer with a wide array of local amenities including schools, shops, Avoca and Rathcoole Park along with a host of bus routes, the N7 & M50 road networks and some of Ireland best golf courses.\r\n\r\nUpon entering this truly spectacular family home from the entrance hallway to the right gives access to a large double bedroom, NEWLY FITTED bathroom, NEW open plan kitchen/dining room with top of the range smart appliances and fantastic views to the hills of Rathcoole and Saggart. It is followed by a utility room and guest wc, office (which can be used as a fifth double bedroom), second living room / dining room and a large family lounge. When entering the property to the left you have three large double bedrooms including a divine master bedroom with an ensuite and access to the garden. The property also boasts an array of smart home technologies and features such as voice/app control for lights, heating, camera/gate system and blind/shutter management which can be fully integrated with Google home/Alexa. To the front, sides and rear the property is bounded by landscaped green areas, mature trees, raised decking and picturesque countryside views. Due to the sheer size of the rear garden there is massive potential to create the setting of your dreams.\r\n\r\nThe south facing aspect to the rear offers an abundance of sunlight throughout the day and to the front is an electronic gated entrance with a large tarmac driveway and ample space to park a number of cars.\r\n\r\nViewing is highly advised to appreciate the sheer quality of this magnificent family home, call Ray Cooke Auctioneers today for further information or to arrange viewing!\r\n\r\nTo list just a few of the new features in this stunning home;\r\n\r\n- Wall insulation (pumped and drylining)\r\n- Attic insulation\r\n- Completely new roof\r\n- Heat recovery ventilation\r\n- Airtight attic hatch\r\n- Bespoke kitchen with smart appliances\r\n- New conservatory roof highly insulated\r\n- Conservatory feature wall\r\n- New boiler\r\n- New well system\r\n- New gate system/entry point\r\n- New drainage/gutters\r\n- c. 3,000 sq. ft.\r\n- BER B3\r\n- Situated on c. .075 acre\r\n- Quaint, peaceful setting\r\n- STUNNING VIEWS\r\n- Viewing highly advised\r\n- M50 & N7 within five minutes' drive\r\n\r\nDirections \r\nIf travelling through Rathcoole Village turn left just before The Poitin Still and proceed ahead after passing Broadfield Manor on your right turn onto Windmill Hill.
