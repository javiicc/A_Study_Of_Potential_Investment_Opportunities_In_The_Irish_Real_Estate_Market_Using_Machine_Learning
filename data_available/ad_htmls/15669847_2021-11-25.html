    A stunning 5th floor two bedroom apartment, benefiting from a south facing dual aspect with attractive views of the River Liffey and Dublin Mountains. \r\n\r\nLocated in the luxurious and well-maintained Clarion Quay development, the property benefits from a superb central location in the heart of the IFSC. This contemporary apartment boasts a bright and very spacious interior, gas fired underfloor heating throughout, high spec fittings and a designated private parking space. The accommodation comprises an entrance hall with generous customised storage space, hot press and solid Oak flooring which runs throughout. The open plan living/dining room with floor to ceiling windows and double doors to a large balcony taking in lovely views of the River Liffey. A fully fitted kitchen with wall & floor units, center island, built in oven and hob, chimney extractor fan and recessed lighting. There are two good sized double bedrooms both with built in wardrobes and master en-suite. A fully tilled bathroom with both bath and shower completes the accommodation. \r\n\r\nClarion Quay is well served by an extensive public transport network which includes the Docklands Station for DART and inter-city trains, Dublin Bus services and Mayor Square LUAS. The area is well served by all social and essential amenities including many delightful establishments such as Marks & Spencer, Milano, Borlottie, CHQ Building, Sprout & Co, The Harbormaster & The Spencer Hotel.
