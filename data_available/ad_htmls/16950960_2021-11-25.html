    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Hickey O'Donoghue welcome to the market this 3 bedroom, two bathroom apartment. Parkville Court is a purpose built student village ideally positioned between the University of Limerick and Limerick School of Art & Design. Located c. 1.5 km from the city centre, with regular bus routes and within walking distance of the Parkway Shopping, Parkville Court could not be better situated. No. 35 is a fully furnished three bedroom, first floor apartment. The accommodation comprises an entrance hallway, an open plan kitchen/dining/living area, three bedrooms all with a study area, one ensuite and a main bathroom.This secure gated complex is centrally managed and has the benefit of ample free parking.
