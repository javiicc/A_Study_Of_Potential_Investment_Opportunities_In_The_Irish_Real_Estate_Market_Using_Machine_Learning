    Immaculately presented dormer bungalow just 2 km from the busy market town of Cahersiveen,\r\n\r\nThe house faces west looking towards Hillgrove.\r\n\r\nThe property is set in a well maintained garden space with many fixed stone features, reflective of the rugged and natural beauty of the area.
