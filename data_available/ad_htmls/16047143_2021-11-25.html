    RE/MAX Professional Partners present to market a 4 bedroom detached dormer located at Church Hill Cottage, Cleggan, Co. Galway.\r\n\r\nExtending to c. 1,873.11 sq. ft., the property offers well proportioned and bright accommodation and needs some internal and external work. Living accommodation of the ground floor is comprised of a porch, living room, kitchen/living/dining room, utility, main bathroom and 2 bedrooms one with en-suite. Proceeding to the first floor is a landing and 2 bedrooms both with en-suites. The master bedroom upstairs features a small balcony which enjoys sea views. \r\n\r\nCertified with an F BER Certificate, the property is heated via electric storage heaters, a solid fuel stove in the kitchen/living area and a solid fuel fireplace in the living room. Double glazed timber framed windows are fitted throughout. \r\n\r\nFeature of the property include a high spec vaulted ceiling in the kitchen, a balcony in the master bedroom upstairs, recessed lighting, ceiling beams, detached garage with electricity and water connections and sea views. \r\n\r\nExternally the property sits on a generous sized site extending to c. 2.94 acres, which is in need of some work but offers huge potential and boasts sea views. The site features a patio with built in BBQ area, spacious lawns, shrubs, mature trees and a gravel driveway. The site is in need of some work but offers huge potential. The property features a detached garage with stone exterior and is connected to an electricity and water supply with a sink inside and heated via electric heaters. Could be used for an array of uses including storage space, games room, office space or converted to living space if required.\r\n\r\nSituated in a scenic, tranquil area and enjoying sea views and Cleggan Bay, the property is within reach of surrounding towns and villages - Cleggan 8 minutes away, Moyard 9 minutes and Clifden 15 minutes from the property. \r\n\r\nContact Brian Deely of RE/MAX on 091 330 300\r\n\r\n \r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nNOTE: These particulars are not to be considered a formal offer.
