    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Centrally located on Irelands west coast, this coastal cottage is located on Moveen Hill in the ocean resort town of Kilkee. The property offers panoramic views of the Atlantic ocean, the town of Kilkee, surrounding countryside and part of the Shannon River. The cottage has easy access to the town of Kilkee and surrounding cliffs with walking trails overlooking the ocean.\r\n\r\nThis property is ideally located to explore everything that the Kilkee area has to offer with easy access to other areas in western Ireland.
