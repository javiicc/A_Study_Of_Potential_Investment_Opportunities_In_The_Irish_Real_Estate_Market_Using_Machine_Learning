    Scanlon Auctioneers are pleased to bring this beautiful property full of character and old world charm to the market. Situated in the village of Shanagolden and only 35mins from Limerick city and 12mins from Newcastle West. This large stone building has retained its original high ceilings and some stone walls are exposed offering a perfect blend of old and new. Excellent accommodation with four bedrooms and two living rooms. Solid fuel stove with back boiler also plumbed for oil. Outside there is a private court yard with ample vehicle parking, a stone Garage and workshop with electricity and toilet, (could be used as home office or annex).
