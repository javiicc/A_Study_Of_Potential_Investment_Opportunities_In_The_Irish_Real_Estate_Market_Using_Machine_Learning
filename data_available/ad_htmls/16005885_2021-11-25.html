    M&C Property are delighted to bring to the market, 42 Ard Thom\xc3\xa1in, Sl\xc3\xad Na Manach, a stunning, contemporary 3 bed semi-detached home. Perfectly positioned between Raheen and Mungret Village, this property offers a turn-key option for first time buyers taking their first steps onto the property market and is also well suited for a growing family wishing to upgrade. Sl\xc3\xad Na Manach is a modern development of detached and semi-detached family homes, set just minutes from the suburbs of Raheen, Dooradoyle, and Limerick City Centre. \r\n\r\nMungret Village is undergoing a rapid program of expansion, including a recently completed overhaul of its road network, investment in sport and playground facilities, and the opening of three new schools. The area also benefits from ease of access to the Crescent Shopping centre, Limericks Dock Rd (a direct route to the city centre) and in addition, the N69 and N18 / M20 road networks for those commuting to Shannon, Dublin and the wider-midwest / southwest regions. \r\n\r\nThe property itself has been laid out to maximise all of the available space for a growing family. The main living room is positioned to the front of the property featuring, wood flooring, feature fireplace, glass fronted high efficiency gas fire insert, coving to the ceiling and is wired for satellite TV. Double doors lead from the living room to the kitchen/dining area allowing light flow through the property. The kitchen / dining area is to the rear of the property and is fitted with ample wall and floor storage presses. Patio doors lead you from the kitchen / dining area to the rear garden which is fully enclosed with concrete block boundary and dividing walls, garden gate for ease of access and is not overlooked offering the new owner the perfect space to enjoy time with family and friends on those long summer evenings. There is guest W/C ideally positioned in the hallway, completing the downstairs accommodation. \r\n\r\nMoving upstairs, the stairs is fully carpeted, the master bedroom which is to the front of the property is wood floored and has the benefit of extensive built-in wardrobes and also a fully tiled en suite shower room. Bedrooms 2 and 3 do not disappoint and are excellent sized rooms, also wood floored. The main bathroom is fully tiled and has a toilet, sink, overhead wall mirror, bath and shower overhead for your convenience. \r\n\r\nThe property enjoys gas fired central heating which is fully zoned allowing 3 separate timed heating zones, downstairs, upstairs and hot water and the house is also fitted with Solar PV electric panels, generating clean green energy from daylight. \r\n\r\nEarly viewing of this excellent low energy family home is most definitely advised as this property will not disappoint! \r\n\r\nRoom measurements (in mtrs): \r\nLiving room: 4.35 X 3.88\r\nKitchen/Dining Area: 4.4 X 6.06\r\nW/C: 1.67 X 1.28\r\nMaster Bedroom: 3.58 X 4.27 \r\nEnsuite: 1.16 X 2.49\r\nBedroom 2: 3.65 X 3.44\r\nBedroom: 3: 3.76 X 2.29\r\nMain Bathroom: 2.31 X 2.28\r\nOverall: 108 Sq Mtrs \r\n\r\n\r\nDistances to some key facilities in the area: \r\nCrescent Shopping Centre: 2.7KM\r\nRaheen Business Park: 3.7KM\r\nUniversity Hospital Limerick: 2.2KM\r\nMungret Neighbourhood Park: 900M\r\nLimerick City Centre: 5.3KM \r\nLimerick Council offices: 2.6KM
