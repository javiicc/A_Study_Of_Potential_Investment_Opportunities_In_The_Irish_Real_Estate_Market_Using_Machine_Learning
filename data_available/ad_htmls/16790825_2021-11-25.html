    Welcome to this beautifully presented detached bungalow conveniently located right in the heart of Ahascragh village right opposite the local church. The house has maintained and cared for over the last several years offering a bright modern home with spacious accommodation. All this in the center of Ahascragh village where every amenity including the local primary school, shops, pubs and the newly development of the old Mill where a whiskey distilling enterprise is about to take off.\r\n\r\nAhascragh village itself is famous for its celebrity milliner and a very strong GAA stronghold over the years. The village is located 57 kms from Galway city, 33 kms to Athlone, 11kms form Ballinasloe and 15kms from Mountbellew. The house is nicely elevated onto a large L shaped site with a tarmac driveway leading to a large double shed and store. To the side of the house is a large garden which offers further development potential or ideal for a green fingered enthusiast to develop their gardening hobby even more.\r\nThe kitchen features bright modern wall and floor units which has been recently installed in the last few years finished with wonderful floor tiling. There are beautiful wood flooring throughout with excellent tiling in the ensuite and bathroom. Indeed the both the bathroom and ensuite have been totally refurbished with a modern style and flair presented with high quality sanitary ware and bathroom fittings. There are three double bedrooms and the front room could be a fourth bedroom or sometimes it can be used as a study if desired or perhaps used as an office to work from home .\r\n\r\nOverall this is a delightful property and will make a super home for those seeking a village location on a large site with great potential.
