    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to present 31 St. Martin s Park to the market. A fantastic opportunity awaits the discerning purchaser to acquire this superb 3 bed semi-detached family home located along this quiet cul de sac in the heart of Dublin 6 W. Beautifully positioned overlooking a green area, Number 31 is the perfect setting for any growing family to call home.\n \nThe generous reception and bedroom accommodation, offering well proportioned, versatile rooms will meet the needs of any growing family. Accommodation comprises of bright entrance hall, living room, large family room, kitchen, utility room, wc and garage completed by three generous bedrooms and family bathroom upstairs.\n \nLocated in an area of unparalleled convenience with every amenity on your doorstep. The property is within close proximity of some of South Dublin s best junior and secondary schools in Terenure, Harolds Cross and Kimmage. Also on the doorstep are an excellent array of local shops, churches and restaurants. Transport facilities are well catered for, with easy access to the M50, whilst regular buses will take you into the City Centre and beyond.\n \nViewing is highly recommended.
