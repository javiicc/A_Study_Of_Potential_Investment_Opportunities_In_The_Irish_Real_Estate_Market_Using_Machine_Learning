    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    4 Ossory Court is presented to the market in excellent condition throughout. The property has been recently refurbished and is newly painted and all rooms fitted with new carpets.\n\nThe property comprises of three floors, the ground floor consists of an entrance hall, guest toilet, large kitchen fully fitted with appliances and a small living room with fireplace, ideal for a home office or study.\n\nThe first floor comprises of a large living room and also a spacious double bedroom with built in wardrobes and an en-suite.\n\nOn the second floor there are three bedrooms and main bathroom. \n\nThe property has a large garden with an entrance to the side with the potential to extend subject to planning. The property has oil fired central heating and a solid fuel fire.\n\nBER to follow.\n\nBorris in Ossory is located on the R445 road with access to the M7 Limerick to Dublin motorway provided South of the village via junction 21. It is served by inter urban bus links to Dublin, Limerick, Galway, Cork and Carlow and a townlink service connects nearby towns and villages. \nBallybrophy railway station is located approximately 5km South of the village. The station is on the main Dublin to Cork railway line.\nThe village has facilities such as a school churches,community hall, convenience retail outlets and a number of service businesses. \n\nContact anytime to arrange for a viewing.
