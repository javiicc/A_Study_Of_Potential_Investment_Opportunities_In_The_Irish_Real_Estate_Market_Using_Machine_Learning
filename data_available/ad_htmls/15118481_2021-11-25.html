    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to present to the market 45 Brian Road.\n\nBuilt in the late 1930 s, No. 45 is an extended three-bedroom end of terrace property situated in the heart of Marino. This much loved family home is very well presented throughout, the light filled accommodation comprises entrance hall with understairs storage, a spacious living room, sitting room, kitchen/dining room and shower room on the ground floor. Upstairs there are three bedrooms and a bathroom. There are mature, well maintained gardens to the front and rear. There is off-street parking to the front and a long and private west facing garden to the rear.\n\nThe location couldn t be more convenient, Brian Road is set in the heart of Marino within a short distance of an abundance of local shops, cafes, restaurants and excellent primary and secondary schools. Clontarf s promenade and DART station are both within 10 a minute walk. There are a number of frequent bus routes that service the area and the city centre is within 3.5 kms. You are also within close proximity to East Point Business Park, the M50, the Airport, DCU and Beaumont Hospital.\n\n\nHall 3.11m x 2.10m.
