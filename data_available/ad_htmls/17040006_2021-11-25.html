    Beautifully presented detached bungalow residence located just outside Dunmore East. Standing on an elevated, private, south facing site c. .75 acre this property is just 3 km from Woodstown Strand, 10 km from Tramore and 15 km from Waterford City. Dual oil and solid fuel central heating, uPVC double glazed windows, C3 BER, alarm. This property was comprehensively upgraded in 2019 and is in superb condition throughout. Tiled porch, tiled entrance hall, large dual aspect living room with oak flooring, built in storage and stove. Tiled kitchen / diner with taupe solid timber units, integrated Bosch appliances and French doors to patio. Four good size bedrooms. Shower room, ensuite shower room & ensuite bathroom. Integral garage suitable for conversion. Granite patio to side with southwesterly aspect. Private surrounding gardens in lawn with apple trees and ample room to extend. Gravel driveway with curbing. Block built shed & boiler house to rear with enclosed dog run.
