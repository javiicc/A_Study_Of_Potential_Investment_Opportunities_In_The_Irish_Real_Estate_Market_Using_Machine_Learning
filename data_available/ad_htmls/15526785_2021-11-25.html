    Estate Denis Collins deceased.\n\nLocation Location Location \n\nApprox 90 acres land\n\n\nTogether with Victorian farm house, (in need of modernisation). This traditional and spacious house surrounded by mature trees with large gardens and Orchards is accessed by a tree lined avenue from roadway. \n\nThe farmyard includes stone cow sheds, stables, lofted grain loft, work shed and storage facilities to the rear. It has a second haggard containing haybarns with separate entrance to roadway. Passage way at rear of the yard leading to milking parlour. This property operated as a Dairy Farm, Farm Guesthouse, Riding School and latterly as a Farm Museum for many years. \n\n\n There are two self catering chalets also on the grounds.\n\n Because of the range of facilities and the fact there is so much outdoor space, property may be suitable for commercial use in the future. Particularly if more residential accommodation were needed. Property which is a Roadside holding on main Newmarket/Kanturk Road is within walking distance, approx 2 kilo. to Kanturk town which has many Schools including Colaiste Treasa, Convent of Mercy National and Secondary Schools. Boherbue/ Millstreet Comprehensive Schools easily accessible. Rugby, GAA, and soccer pitches. Popular golf club ,Pitch and Putt, Tennis Courts, Creches and pre school facilities close to town. \n\n Contact Mary Collins 085 706 4732.
