Kerry V93 WP52\r\n\r\nExcellent opportunity to acquire a lovely 4 Bedroomed bungalow site on c. 0.52 acre in the heart of the Reeks District. Situated off the main Ring of Kerry road on a quiet cul de sac road in a most sought after area c. 4km from Killorglin Town. The house has been finished to a good standard. Ideally suited for first time buyer/family home/holiday home. Close to all local amenities, schools National and Secondary etc. Convenient to Killarney, Tralee and Kerry Airport. Easily accessible to Caragh Lake, Rossbeigh Blue flag beach, Cromane beach, Dooks Golf club, Maine Valley Golf club, the Kerry way. \r\n\r\nAcc: Entrance Hall, Sitting Room, Kitchen/Dining Room, Utility, 4 Bedroom (1 Ensuite), Bathroom. Services: Mains Water, Septic Tank, Oil Fired Central Heating\r\n\r\nAbout Killorglin\r\n\r\nKillorglin occupies a scenic position overlooking the River Laune and backed by the dramatic slope of McGillycuddy Reeks. It is situated at the beginning of the World famous Ring of Kerry, part of the Wild Atlantic Way. Killorglin is the gateway to the Reeks District. \r\n\r\nKillorglin is an attractive place to live, work, and do business. It has good transport links, a vibrant community, social life and great cultural heritage. \r\n\r\nKillorglin is home to a variety of business across a range of sectors including Aqua Design, Astellas, Fexco, RDI Hub, Taxamo, Temler and Tweak.\r\n\r\n\r\nFeatures: \r\n4 Bed Bungalow \r\nSite area c. 0.5 acres\r\nSituated on quiet country road\r\nLarge garden to rear \r\nSuitable for First time buyer, holiday home\r\nSituated in most sought after area\r\nClose to all local amenities\r\nEasily accessible to Killarney, Tralee\r\n\r\n\r\nDISCLAIMER:\r\n\r\nJim Burns & Co.
