    1 Castle Meadow is an impressive 4 bedroom family home less than 10 minutes from Gorey town, this elegantly finished property has been meticulously maintained both inside and out. A bright open entrance hall welcomes you with an abundance of living accommodation which includes a generously proportioned lounge room with an open fireplace and double doors to the rear garden which has breath-taking views. The kitchen/dining is finished to the highest standard with granite countertops, an archway opens to a sunroom with French doors opening on to the rear garden and a door to an elegant sitting room. A well fitted out utility and a guest WC are also on the ground floor. \nThe first floor has 4 double bedrooms, 2 with ensuites including the master suite which also has a walk-in-wardrobe.
