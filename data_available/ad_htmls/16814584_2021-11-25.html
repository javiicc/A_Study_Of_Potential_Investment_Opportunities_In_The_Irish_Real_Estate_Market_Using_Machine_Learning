Kerry V93 W6K6\n\nExcellent opportunity to purchase a 3 bedroom bungalow situated on a c. 0.79 acre elevated site. \n\nThe property is located c.6km from Glenbeigh village off the Cahersiveen/Wild Atlantic Way road. \n\nThe property commands excellent views of the Dingle Peninsula and surrounding countryside. \n\nIdeally suited as a family home/holiday home. \n\nEasily accessible to Rossbeigh Blue Flag Beach 4.7km, Glenbeigh Village 6km, Killarney 41km, Killorglin 19km and Kerry Airport 41.6km. \n\nAccommodation: Entrance Hall, Sitting Room, Kitchen/Dining Room, 3 Bedrooms (1 Ensuite), Bathroom, Utility, Oil Fired Central Heating. \n\nAbout Glenbeigh: \n\nGlenbeigh is a truly magical place often referred to as "The Jewel in the Ring of Kerry". It is the ideal holiday location. Whether you are looking for a fun-filled family holiday or a romantic break away, there really is something here for everyone. Enjoy the six miles of golden, sandy, blue flag Rossbeigh Beach or the peace and beauty of the rivers and lakes. Glenbeigh is cozily tucked in to the surrounding horseshoe of hills and Seefin Mountains. The Caragh River and the Behy River flow at either side of the village into Castlemaine Harbour.\n\nThe mountain scenery here is magnificent, and the circuit of the hills from Seefin to Drung Hill is called the "Glenbeigh Horseshoe" and is part of the greater "Kerry Way", one of Kerry's finest mountain walks. However you prefer to spend your holidays, whether it's bird watching, walking, playing golf (on the famous Dooks Golf Links), fishing, hang gliding, surfing or kayaking, horse trekking on Rossbeigh Beach or just relaxing, Glenbeigh really does have it all. Immerse yourself in the friendliness and hospitality of the local people and the beauty of our wonderful destination, come and see for yourself just how much we have to offer.\n\n\nRoom Details: \n\nEntrance Hall: 1.38 x 3.055\nWith timber flooring\n\nSitting Room: 5.3 x 3.87 (with Bay Window: 2.17 x 0.477)\nWith timber flooring, feature fireplace with timber surround \n\nKitchen/Dining Room: 5.37 x 2.96\nWith Built in eye and ground level presses, stainless steel sink unit, tiled surround, plumbed dishwasher, timber flooring\n\nUtility Room: 1.883 x 2.955\nWith tiled flooring, plumbed, oil boiler, shelving\n\nCorridor: 4.819 x 0.973\nWith timber flooring, Telephone point, Hot-Press: Shelved with immersion\n\n\nBedroom 1: 2.776 x 2.9 (Front)\nWith timber flooring, front bedroom\n\nBedroom 2: 3.476 x 3.33 (Front) (Bay Window: 1.8 x 0.77)\nWith timber flooring, front double bedroom\n\nBathroom: with WC, WHB and Bath\n\nBedroom 3: 2.965 x 3.518\nWith timber flooring, back double bedroom\nEnsuite: 0.951 x 2.955\nWith WC, WHB, shower, fully tiled floor and walls, electric shower\n\nServices: Mains Water, Electricity, Septic Tank and Oil Heating, \n\n\nFeatures: \nSituated off the Wild Atlantic Way/Ring of Kerry Road\nElevated c. 0.79 acre site\n3 Bedrooms (1 Ensuite)\nOil fired central heating\nConvenient to Glenbeigh, Cahersiveen and Killorglin\nExcellent views of Dingle Peninsula and surrounding countryside \nEasily accessible to Rossbeigh Blue Flag Beach and Kells Blue flag beach\nClose to Proposed Greenway\nIdeally suited to First time buyer/family home/holiday home\nBuilt c. 2001\n\n\nDISCLAIMER: \n\n Jim Burns & Co.
