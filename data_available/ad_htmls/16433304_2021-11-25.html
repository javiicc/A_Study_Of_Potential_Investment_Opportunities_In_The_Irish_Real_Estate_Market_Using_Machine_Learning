    O'Connor are delighted to offer a wonderful opportunity to acquire a jewel in the crown of one of Limerick's finest properties. Built in 1825 an outstanding 5 bed Georgian terrace home, three storeys over basement at one of Limerick's most sought-after address. "Myrtleville" is a superb family home has been lovingly restored and cared for by the current owners. The property blends the wonderfully proportioned accommodation of the Georgian era with modern comforts with numerous notable period features, including magnificent ornate coving & cornice work with centre roses. A tastefully presented residence, this large family home offers large beautifully proportioned accommodation and is meticulously maintained throughout.\n\nAccommodation extends to sizeable 240.1m2 (2,584 sq ft) & briefly comprises: Entrance Hallway, an impressive large reception/living room opening onto an upper patio area overlooking the private southwest facing rear patio garden, kitchen / dining / living area, further patio area, utility/boot room, guest w.c/cloakroom, five bedrooms (two ensuite) and a family bathroom.\n\nInternally the generously proportioned accommodation at hall level flows wonderfully with a superb reception/living room overlooking the private matured garden that surround the property perfectly. Two ornate fireplaces, wooden flooring & Double French doors lead to the upper patio; this room truly gives you a sense of grandeur with many of the original features beautifully & carefully restored within this room. A home movie screen securely hidden in the ceiling bringing a mix of the old with the new. Moving down a level to the cloak room/wc. with feature panelling & tiled flooring, a further staircase to garden level home to a large utility room/boot room that giving separate access to the front of the property. The new and old come together as you walk away from the period features in the hall, into the contemporary open-plan kitchen, dining, and family space. A carefully designed kitchen fully fitted with tiled flooring enjoys an extensive range of high gloss wall & floor units along with large island which is a fantastic space for entertaining. The kitchen has been cleverly designed incorporating all modern appliances, stainless steel splashback & cupboard keeping the quartz work top free of any clutter, whilst an Aga range creates a homely feel! A bonus feature, tucked away behind double doors, a small office workstation, ideal for homework. The light filled living space leads to superb large patio, ideal for alfresco entertaining and dining with plenty of seating areas along with both indoor and outdoor speakers fully wired for sound!\n\nAn impressive staircase leads up to the office/study a quiet & completely separate workstation space for the world we live in today. Moving to first floor level there are two bedrooms, both ensuite. The main ensuite consisting of a large bedroom, a generous en suite bathroom with walk-in shower, and a fine dressing room. With front to back windows this bedroom boasts stunning views of the rear & front gardens. At second floor level a further 3 bedrooms, walk in hot press & a family bathroom. Each double bedroom is flooded with light whilst the large family bathroom is fully tiled with a shower & bath. A walk-in hot press/linen cupboard completes the accommodation. Finally, a stira stairs leads to a large, floored attic space above.\n\nOutside the property is surrounded by a truly amazing private garden with plenty of flowers, plants & mature trees. The property is accessed via electric gates with wrought iron railings to the front. The driveway, which is bordered by mature box hedging, plants & trees along with a high feature stone wall has ample parking for several cars. The rear garden is a spacious area with astro lawn area with box hedging and mature plants and trees offering great privacy. Beyond the astro grass lawn is a private patio area & a secluded shaded area so you can experience an oasis of peace & calm of outdoor space in your new city abode. At one side a gravel pathway leads to a gated entrance to the front driveway.\n\nLimericks South Circular Road is within walking distance of a wealth of residential amenities afforded by the City Centre, the area offers immediate transport links to the wider Limerick region, Crescent Shopping Centre and University Hospital Limerick. Situated close to numerous coffee shops, pubs & restaurants as well as some of Limericks most renowned schools & colleges by including Mary Immaculate College, The Model School, St Clement's College and Laurel Hill Secondary School.\n\n"Myrtleville" rests on truly a unique setting with an emphasis on light and space whilst retaining original features, this property is an ideal purchase for a growing family. Located in one of Limerick's most desirable areas, this home is set to draw huge interest among purchasers seeking to up size or re locate.
