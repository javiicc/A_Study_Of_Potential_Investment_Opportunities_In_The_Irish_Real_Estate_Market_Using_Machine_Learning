    Brophy Cusack are pleased to present this three bedroom bungalow to the market. The property sits on a large 0.9 acre site with amazing views of the Commeragh Mountains. Munsburrow, Rathgormack is located just 15mins drive from Dungarvan town, 10km from Waterford City, 4km from Coumshingaun Lake,10mins from Carrick on Suir & Kilmacthomas.
