    In the unspoilt and arrestingly beautiful Mason Island off the coast of Carna in Connemara, we are delighted to bring this very interesting property to the market. Contained within folio GY92250F the property consists of two ruins and the pier on circa 2 acres of ground (0.8 hectares). The ruins are both within a stone\xe2\x82\xacTM throw of the pier and this makes access to the property that much easier. As the ruins were at one time houses, we believe that planning permission may be granted here to reinstate or rebuild the ruins. Far way from the hustle and bustle of mainland life, this acquisition would offer a real get away from it all; an \xe2\x82\xacoeff the grid experience\xe2\x82\xac\xef\xbf\xbd. Natural beauty in an unspoilt location!\nPlease contact us for further details.\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nA boat journey from Mace pier is required to access this island. Please contact us on 095 21066 for further details.\n\nTo get to Mace Pier: On the main Galway to Clifden route, take the turn signposted Carna (R340), travel on this road for 18km and continue straight into and past Carna village. Approx 3 km from Carrna village, take the left hand turn and continue for 1km. At the Y bend, take the leaning right and after 1.2km turn right again. The pier will will be located soon after on your left-hand side after the boat house.\nPSRA:001605\nThese particulars are intended to give a fair description of the property only and are in no way guaranteed, nor do they form part of any contract.
