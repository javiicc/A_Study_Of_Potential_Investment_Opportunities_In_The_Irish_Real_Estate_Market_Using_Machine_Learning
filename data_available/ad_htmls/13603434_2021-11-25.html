    This beautiful unique property is located only 200m off the N21 in the much sought after area of Castlematrix. This large mature well maintained 4 Bedroom House measuring over 2,700 sq ft is situated on a 0.5ac site. It has a small lawn to the front with a more significant one to the back. It is surrounded on three sides by a well manicured secure hedge and to the road by a traditional limestone wall with two "grided" gated entrances to the front. The house is surrounded by tarmacadam. The roof of the house is completed with red Spanish Tiles. The ground floor of the house consists of an Entrance Hall, a large Sitting Room to the left of the Hall, Kitchen come Living Room, Utility Room, downstairs WC as well as a large Conservatory and Garage.
