    Lucrative investment opportunity to purchase this spacious 2-bedroom apartment superbly located overlooking the Ridgepool on the River Moy. Located in the heart of Ballina, just a short ramble to the main thoroughfare of shops, eateries, pubs and all local amenities.\n\nAccommodation extends to 79.6 sq.m. / 857 sq. ft. approx and comprises of a relaxing living area with superb views of the River Moy, kitchen/dining area, 2 spacious bedrooms, bright entrance hall and bathroom.\n\nThe property comes with a car parking space and management fees are approx. 1,400 per annum. There is a lift at basement level to all floors and car park.\n\nThe property could not be better positioned for the fishing enthusiast and would suit both investors and owner occupiers alike.\n\nViewing is highly recommended.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 9.02m x 1.05m. Carpeted flooring.\n\nBathroom 2.08m x 1.92m. Fully tiled. Bath, w.h.b., w.c.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nLocated above The Ballina Manor Hotel on Barrett Street, Ballina, Co. Mayo.
