    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Dingle Harbour View is a commanding 7 Bedroom Detached property located at Cloosmore along the Slea Head Drive a short distance outside of Dingle town. The property commands spectacular views of Dingle Harbour ideal to witness the many stunning Sunrises and Sunsets that this beautiful part of the world get. The property is an ideal family home or can lend itself to be used for a B&B due to its scenic location so close to Dingle town and the fact it is situated along the world famous Slea Head Drive route all an advantage to the breathtaking location of this property.\nThe house consists of a large Patio area ideal space to sit and relax in and read the paper or a good book. On entering the main Hall way of the property you have a large Livingroom off to the left with a stunning feature stone fire place with a Solid fuel stove & a corner Bar area, across the hallway you have a spacious Kitchen and a lovely conservatory Diningroom area with views of the Harbour. The rear of the Ground floor you have a family Bathroom, Large Guest Diningroom, 2 Double Bedrooms, Toilet/Shower, Utility room, Large Store room.\nOn the first floor you have a lovely large landing area with access onto a large balcony area ideal area for sitting out and watching the activity in the harbour or to just enjoy the beautiful scenery. On this floor you also have 5 Further good size Bedrooms one of which is currently used as a home office space. There are also two modern Toilet/shower rooms on this floor to service the bedrooms. \nThis property is truly a unique opportunity to purchase a property within touching distance of Dingle town and all its amenities and to acquire a property with breathtaking views of the harbour. If you are looking to purchase a property with a view this could truly be the property for you
