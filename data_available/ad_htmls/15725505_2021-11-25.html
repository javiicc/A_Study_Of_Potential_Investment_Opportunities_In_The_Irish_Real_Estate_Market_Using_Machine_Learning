    Farrell Auctioneers are delighted to bring to the market Ferndale, which is located in the heart of Ardrahan village. The property which was built in approx. 1860 and used as a school house and then renovated in the later years and converted in to a residence. Accommodation includes entrance hallway, sitting room, utility, w.c, kitchen, dining room, 4 bedroom and a bathroom. Ardrahan Village is just minutes from junction 17 on the M18 motorway at Kiltiernan this property is superbly located, providing easy access to Galway, Shannon and Limerick via the M18 corridor. Ardrahan train station is also within walking distance. All amenities are close at hand with a choice of primary schools in nearby Kiltiernan and Labane and Creche facilities in Ardrahan and Labane within minutes' drive. Secondary Schools are also close by in Gort, Kinvara and Oranmore. Supermarket, Restaurant and Sporting facilities are all nearby. The busy market town of Gort is just 7 miles away, Clarinbridge and Oranmore villages are both within minutes' drive. Properties of this nature in such a fantastic location rarely come available. Viewing is advisable.
