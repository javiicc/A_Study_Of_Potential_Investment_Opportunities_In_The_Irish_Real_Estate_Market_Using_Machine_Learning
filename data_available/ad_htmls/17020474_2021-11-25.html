    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to bring to the market No. 145 Whitehall Road, a spacious and bright semi-detached bungalow, ideally located on this highly regarded and much sought-after residential road in Terenure, Dublin 12. This home which has huge scope to extend (SPP) offers versatile living and bedroom accommodation and is further complimented by an exceptionally large westerly facing rear garden.\n\nInternally, the accommodation briefly comprises; entrance hall, living room, open plan kitchen /dining room, two bedrooms, and a family bathroom. The attic, although not converted offers extensive space as it spans the length of the property. The property also benefits from side access to a garage and an exceptionally large westerly facing rear garden which is laid out in lawn with mature trees and hedging. The front garden is mature with ample off street parking.\n\nThe location alone speaks volumes about this superb family residence, ideally located a short walk to local shops, close to Terenure and Templeogue Villages, Ashleaf and Rathfarnham Shopping Centres and within easy commuting distance of Dublin City Centre and M50. A great selection of schools are close at hand including Terenure College, Our Lady s, Templeogue College and St. Pius X. A number of sporting clubs, golf courses and recreational amenities are nearby. \n\nAll in all viewing this home is highly recommended! \n\n\nPorch 1.19m x 0.74m.
