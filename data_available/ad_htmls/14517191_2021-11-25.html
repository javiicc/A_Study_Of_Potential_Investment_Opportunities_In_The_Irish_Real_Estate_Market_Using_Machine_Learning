    Number 9 Effra road has everything you want, certainly I think so.\r\nThis Georgian home feels spacious and cozy at the same time. \r\nThe ground floor is an open plan kitchen family room with a bathroom and utility. There is underfloor heating throughout. Doors that lead to the front and back gardens are on this floor. \r\nThe first floor is made up of two large sitting rooms and an office. This floor is where the house shows its true character, the 11ft ceilings have had their rosses and cornicing restored, along with fire places, windows and floorboards, originals used wherever possible. The cast iron radiators are reflective of the houses period but are modern as is the heating system and the wiring.\r\nThe second floor has the main bathroom and three bedrooms, two large doubles, the master with wet room en-suit, and one spacious single room, currently laid out as a dressing room.\r\nThe Back garden has a large 'fairway' artificial grass which was added in April 2021 alone with several seating areas, flower bed and stone areas for a low maintenance garden. There is private parking to the left of the house that leads to the Garden. also added this year is a granite sun terrace to the front of the house, taking advantage of the afternoon sun.\r\n2021 upgrades also include: \r\nMiele oven \r\nUnderfloor heating throughout the ground floor serviced\r\nBoiler serviced\r\nInsinkerator serviced\r\nAll exterior doors reconditioned\r\nTouch up painting where required\r\n\r\nThe feel in this house reflects the love, care and enjoyment we have had living here, the space has lent itself to a varied range of activities. Its location is second to none. \r\nFor more information please e-mail your interest.
