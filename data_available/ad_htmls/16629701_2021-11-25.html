    DNG are delighted to present No. 27 Shrewsbury Road to the market. This spacious three bedroom home has been well maintained over the years and has modern double glazed windows and gas fired central heating. This is a wonderful opportunity for the discerning purchaser to create their forever family home in this very desirable location. \r\n\r\nThe accommodation comprises of a bright and spacious entrance hallway with understairs storage, which some neighbours have plumbed for guest WC. Off the entrance hall you have the main living room which is dual aspect to the front and rear of the property and has an open fireplace. Between the living room and and the kitchen is a dining room overlooking the rear garden. The kitchen also overlooks the rear garden and has a selection of cabinets. Upstairs there are three bedrooms (two double and one single) and a family bathroom.\r\n\r\nTo the front of the property there is ample off street parking for two to three cars as well as a garage. There is side access to the mature and very private rear garden.The gardens are large enough to offer potential to extend the existing accommodation (subject to PP) if required. \r\n\r\nShrewsbury Road is a mature and highly regarded residential road that is positioned just minutes' walk to Shankill Village, the Dart station and beach. The bustling little village of Shankill has a selection of shops including post office, pharmacy, cafe and the Lidl shopping centre to name a few. Easy access is provided for the N11 and M50 along with very regular bus services to Dublin City and Airport. The Luas is also quite convenient at the nearby Bridesglen and Cherrywood stations. \r\n\r\nViewing of No. 27 is a must to appreciate the highly convenient location, super gardens and obvious extension potential in line with some extraordinary neighbouring properties.\r\n\r\nFeatures:\r\n* 3 bed semi-det home\r\n* Accommodation of approximately 105 Sq M\r\n* Large living room\r\n* Dining/Breakfast room\r\n* Double glazed windows\r\n* GFCH (new A rated condensing Gas Boiler)\r\n* Attic insulated\r\n* Garage\r\n* Mature private rear gardens\r\n* Ample off street parking
