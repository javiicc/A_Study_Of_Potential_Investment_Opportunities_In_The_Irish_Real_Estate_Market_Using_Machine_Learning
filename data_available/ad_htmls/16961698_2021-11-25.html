    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Kings Court, Kings Channel is an outstanding development of tastefully designed homes in a truly beautiful setting. Situated off the Dunmore Road and adjacent to Waterford University Hospital, Ardkeen and Tesco shopping centres and within 5 minutes drive of Waterford city.\r\n\r\nNo 47 Kings Court is a stunning 4 bedroom 3 bathroom detached home and is presented to the market in pristine walk in condition. This home enjoys a wonderful position within the development and overlooks a large green area to the front.\r\n\r\nSpacious bright light filled accommodation includes double height entrance hall, sitting room with gas fire and double doors that lead to the dining room. The dining room in turn opens to the kitchen diner. A separate living room also features a gas fire. The utility and wc complete the ground floor. The kitchen, dining room and utility open to the sandstone patio at the rear taking full advantage of the westerly aspect and offers excellent access for alfresco dining. \r\n\r\nThe first floor has four double bedrooms a generous master bedroom with en-suite, all bedrooms have fitted wardrobes and the family bathroom is fully tiled with bath and overhead electric shower. \r\n\r\nHeating is thermostatically controlled with three separate zones and can be remotely activated on an app. The building energy rating is C1 and the property is fully alarmed.\r\n\r\nGardens to the front and rear are beautifully landscaped, the cobble lock driveway to the front provides off street parking for two cars. Gated side access.\r\n\r\nThis wonderful location allows easy access to the countryside, beaches, golf clubs, leisure centres, an abundance of services and is immediate to the scenic Kings Channel river walk and Waterford Castle.
