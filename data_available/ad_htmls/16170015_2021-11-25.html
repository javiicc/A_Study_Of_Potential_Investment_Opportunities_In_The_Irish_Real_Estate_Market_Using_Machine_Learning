    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    7 Oxford Terrace is a split level Victorian house dating back to c.1879. The property was extensively renovated by its current owners in 2017 and is immaculately presented throughout. The accommodation extends to approximately 100sq.m (1,076sq.ft) and comprises of an entrance hall, two reception rooms, three double bedrooms, two bathrooms, a utility and internal courtyard. To the front a cobble lock drive provides off street parking for two cars and granite steps lead to the entrance hall. The entrance hall and main reception room have a magnificent 3.5m high ceiling, original decorative coving, centre rose and archways. The first reception room is a bright spacious room with high ceilings, decorative coving and a feature fireplace that has a Heritage multi-fuel stove. At first floor level there are two generous double bedrooms with both windows and Velux windows enjoying a south facing aspect making these lovely bright rooms. At ground floor return level there is a hallway with understairs storage, a double bedroom overlooking the internal courtyard and a kitchen that leads in turn to the second reception room. A door from the kitchen brings you to the internal courtyard. To the rear there is a utility room with a contemporary family bathroom. Munster Joinery tri-folding doors bring oodles of natural light and warmth to the accommodation within and lead to the recently landscaped and tranquil rear garden which has a south-west facing aspect and is a veritable suntrap. There is an extensive natural Indian sandstone patio which measures approximately 4.1m x 5.5m with steps leading to an elevated grass lawn with raised flower beds on three sides. There is further potential to extend upstairs to the rear of the house.\r\n\r\n7 Oxford Terrace is a charming and stylish family home located on a peaceful and highly sought-after residential road in East Wall. The property is within walking distance of Spencer Dock, IFSC, Grand Canal Dock, Eastpoint Business Park and Dublin's City Centre. The property is also within short walking distance to the seafront in Clontarf, Fairview Park, Spencer Dock Luas and Clontarf Road and Connolly DART stations.
