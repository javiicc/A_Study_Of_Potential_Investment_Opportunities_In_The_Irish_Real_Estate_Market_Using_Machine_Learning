    Quillsen are delighted to present No. 28 Windsor Terrace to the market. Designed by the award-winning architectural firm De Blacam and Meagher, this unique property is one of a terrace of 6 architecturally designed houses which were built in 2004. These gems of modern Irish design are in stark contrast to anything else in the area. \n\nNo 28 has approximately 120 sqm of well proportioned and light filled accommodation with a reverse living design - having its living accommodation on the first floor. Given its southerly aspect to the front, the upper floors are flooded with light making a superb living space. The home boasts an enviable garden terrace overlooking the canal providing a magnificent outdoor entertaining space. In addition there is a small terrace to the rear.\n\nWhilst in need of a little decorative updating, the accommodation consists an entrance hallway with a bedroom and large bathroom to front and a second bedroom to the rear. The first floor consists of an open plan kitchen, living and dining space with large windows to the front and rear. The second floor has an office/study boasting a large garden terrace to the front with the Master bedroom to the rear with an en-suite and walk in wardrobe. \n\nSituated along the leafy banks of the Grand canal in the heart of Portobello which is one of Dublin\xe2\x82\xacTM most sought after and fashionable locations, No.28 is ideal for those seeking a modern home in this popular location, within a short stroll of Dublin city centre.\n\nAccommodation\nGround Floor\nEntrance Hallway 12.0m (at longest) x 3.4m (at widest)\nBedroom 1 3.7m x 2.3m\nBedroom 2 3.6m x 2.9m\nShower Room 3.1m x 1.5m\nStorage Closet 1.4m x 0.87m\n \nFirst Floor\nLanding 3.7m x 2.7m\nLiving Room 4.5m x 3.6m\nKitchen 3.7m x 1.5m\nDining Room 3.6m x 3.0m \n\nSecond Floor\nLanding 4.2m x 2.7m\nBedroom 3 3.6m x 2.8m\nWardrobe Area 1.6m x 1.4m\nEnsuite 2.6m x 1.6m\nStudy 2.7m x 2.5m \n\nAll information provided is to the best of our knowledge.
