    2 Louvain Glade is a superb, detached home in one of Dublin s most sought after residential enclaves. Louvain Glade is a short cul de sac of detached homes, within the popular Ardilea development off Roebuck Road. This distinctive residence measures approx. 170sq.m/ 1830sq.ft and the spacious accommodation comprises entrance porch opening to large inner reception room used as a dining room. Off the hall there is spacious light filled living room connecting to the kitchen and breakfast room. Flooded with natural light, the breakfast dining area leads out to the private rear garden. Off the hall there is a utility room, shower room and the third double bedroom completing the accommodation downstairs. Upstairs, there is a large landing mezzanine area currently used as a reading study area. There are two large double bedrooms on either side of the landing, with the principal bedroom further enhanced by a bathroom ensuite. A family bathroom completes the accommodation at this level. \n\nLouvain Glade offers the perfect balance of living and bedroom accommodation and although in need of some decorative modernization would make the perfect family home set within well stocked and landscaped gardens. To the front of this fine home, there is a driveway providing off street parking and a pretty lawned garden stocked with flowering plants and shrubs. A private, walled garden to the rear is laid in lawn with perimeter planting and herbaceous borders. A patio area off the kitchen and breakfast dining area provides the ideal spot for outdoor entertainment and al fresco dining. \n\nArdilea is well positioned in this convenient location, just a short stroll from the local shops on Roebuck Road. Within a stone s throw of UCD Belfield University Campus, Mount Anville, Blackrock, St. Andrews, St Kilian s, Gonzaga and Muckross Park College. Excellent sporting facilities nearby include Deerpark Tennis Club, Riverview, Elm Park golf and tennis clubs and Merrion Cricket Club, The QBC and Air Coach s direct service to the Airport, both on the nearby Stillorgan N11, provide excellent transport facilities.
