    We are delighted to welcome this superb detached country cottage to the open market. Formerly a railway cottage, the property offers spacious accommodation and wonderful mature gardens ideal for a small family or retired couple.\r\n\r\nThis is a wonderful property located between Skibbereen and Ballydehob and is situated on the Wild Atlantic Way. There are a large number of schools, shops and other facilities in the area and the coastline is easily accessible. Skibbereen market town is within c. 6 miles and Cork Airport is c. 80 minutes drive. The property is on a bus route directly to Cork city.\r\n\r\nInternally the property comprises of kitchen, lounge, living and dining room, bathroom and 1 bedroom on the ground floor. Upstairs there are two double bedrooms.\r\n\r\n\r\nFeatures:\r\n\r\n- Oil central heating\r\n- Multi-fuel stove\r\n- Spacious internal accommodation \r\n- Large mature gardens on 0.75 acre site\r\n- Only 6 miles to Skibbereen
