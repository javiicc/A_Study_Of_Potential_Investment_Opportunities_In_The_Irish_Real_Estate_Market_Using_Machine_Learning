    A spacious 3 Bedroom mid-terrace property in a secluded woodland setting on the edge of Bettystown village and has planning permission only as a holiday home. Open plan living sets the tone for this ready to move-in property extending to Approx. 107m /1150ft . The Courtyard development is gated and has its own beautiful landscaped and walled in communal garden for the private enjoyment of all the residents. No 8 is seconds drive away from Bettystown village, Laytown & Bettystown Links Golf & Tennis Club and conveniently located only a 10 minute drive to both Drogheda town centre and Junction 7 on the M1 motorway giving easy access to Dublin International Airport and the M50 orbital motorway within a 30 minute drive.
