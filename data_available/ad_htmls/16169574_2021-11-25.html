    Chadsley House is a substantial family home designed by renowned Irish Architect Richard Orpen. Detail throughout the house to include its gable front, box bay windows, ornate red brick chimney stacks are fine examples of the Orpen style. Internally this style is replicated with soft flattened arches which are echoed throughout the house and a staircase with turned balusters a charming feature of the entrance hallway. \r\n\r\nThe accommodation has been modernised over the years to include the addition of an octagonal conservatory to the front of the house. The hall with galleried staircase and timber panelled walls gives access to the living accommodation. This comprises of a dual aspect drawing room with box bay window and stand alone dog grate. A study located just behind the drawing room could also suit as a fifth bedroom off which is a wc. To the other side of the hall there is the family room which connects to the country style kitchen with pitched pine ceiling. From the kitchen there is a door which leads to a wc and access to the carport to the side of the house. In addition, off the hall is positioned the dining room. \r\n\r\nUpstairs there are four generous sized bedrooms. The master suite to the front of the property is particularly bright as a result of a dual aspect. The en-suite bathroom has a shower cubicle, wc, bidet, whb inset in vanity unit and sunken bath with tiled and mirrored surround. In addition there is a dressing room fitted with shelf and hanging space. There are three further bedrooms, two doubles and one single. To the rear of the property is a studio with ensuite shower room, fitted kitchen and a door to a roof terrace with spiral staircase descending to ground level. A family room completes the first floor accommodation. \r\nThis is a fine family home combining generous accommodation on a generous site creating the perfect refurbishment opportunity. \r\n\r\nJoint Agents: Sherry FitzGerald / Cushman & Wakefield \r\n\r\n\r\nSpecial Features\r\nSubstantial family home extending to 389 sqm/ 4,380 sqft (approx.)\r\nDesigned by renowned Irish Architect Richard Orpen\r\nMaximum privacy set back from road behind imposing granite wall\r\nGenerous site of 0.33ha/0.82acres\r\nElegant and well-proportioned accommodation\r\nGas Fired Central Heating\r\nCar Port\r\nTarmacadamed driveway provides parking for several cars\r\nSwimming pool pavilion in need of complete refurbishment
