    Ideally situated amidst a picture-perfect location with outstanding views of the Irish sea and neatly positioned on an elevated site resting against the cliff top, 3 Cliff Manor is an exceptional property that is sure to attract wide interest with its unique and dramatic setting and its contemporary show home interiors offering an award winning combination for any discerning purchaser looking for something special. \n \nThe warm, generous and inviting accommodation comprises: a porch, open plan dining/family room off which lies a patio area with incredible views of the Irish Sea. There is a separate fully equipped contemporary kitchen which accesses the low maintenance westerly facing rear garden, a guest wc, utility room and an additional living room complete with wood burning stove conclude the accommodation on the ground floor. Upstairs, there are three double bedrooms, main bedroom with en-suite and access to balcony to enjoy the breathtaking vista and a main bathroom completes the internal accommodation. The garden to the rear is westerly facing enjoying the evening sunshine, laid partly in marble and is a beautiful low maintenance private space with striking rock face backdrop. To the front this home boasts a patio area off the family room as well as a balcony off the main bedroom both wonderful choices to sit and enjoy the panoramic view that s on offer. \n \nThe layout of number three offers open plan living, all the internal accommodation is well proportioned, spacious and natural throughout. It is clear this home was created with the view in mind with floor to ceiling windows to capture all that is on offer. This amazing setting offers peace and tranquility yet is only minutes away from Greystones and Bray town, pictures don t justify all that this wonderful home and setting has to offer, early viewing is advised to appreciate all that is on offer. Between the mountains and the sea, the location on the southern slopes of Bray Head is both majestic, in terms of scenic amenity and convenience being as it is, midway between the two townships.\n \nCliff Manor is an exclusive community of just 7 homes built on the site of the former Cliff Manor Hotel. Commanding fine uninterrupted maritime views over the Irish Sea and southwards towards Greystones and beyond. The residents of Cliff Manor have exclusive access to approximately seven acres of private landscaped gardens to include a woodland area, a meticulous and superbly manicured lawn with an array of shrubs and plants. A pathway leading to a gazebo which is an extremely private and secluded area which offers a little bit of heaven and a place to get away from it all. \n \nSituated in this small well maintained residential development this superb location is bound to appeal to many, being in close proximity to Greystones, Bray and the Dart. The town offers a host of essential amenities to include renowned restaurants, cafes, cosy pubs, supermarkets, food stores and boutiques. There are also an array of excellent primary and secondary schools in close proximity. Those include, Greystones Educate together, St Patricks National School, St Laurences, Delgany National, St Davids, Temple Carrig, An gaelscoil, St Kevins, St Brigid s and Greystones Community National School. In addition to the DART, there are the bus routes 84, 184, 84 X and the Aircoach which brings you to Dublin Airport. No. 3 is situated just a short walk away from the bus stop at the top of the road.\nThe enviable range of local sporting amenities in the area include rugby, GAA, soccer, tennis, plenty of top-quality golf and sailing clubs plus two beaches to enjoy, not to forget the harbour and Shoreline swimming pool/fitness centre as well as the driving range. Greystones really has everything you could want in the town or a short train ride away. \n \nA fantastic place to raise a family, being in close proximity to all the amenities Greystones, Bray & Delgany have to offer. A fabulous opportunity awaits any discerning purchaser looking for a fantastic home in a one of a kind location, which offers generous proportions, convenience, breathtaking views and a highly desirable location close to a host of amenities. \n \n\n\nPorch Alarm panel, ceiling coving, tiled floor.\n\nLiving Room 4.16m x 4.96m.
