    Mace, Carna, Co. Galway H91 TPW3\r\nIn the highly scenic area of Mace, Carna, we are proud to offer for sale a bungalow with land right down to the shoreline. The cottage which was built in the early 70's is presented in very good condition despite its age and it enjoys wonderful sea views, especially when the tide is in. Across the road and within a very short stroll from this cottage, there is the local boat club and indeed there is access to the shore across the road with this very property! There are four bedrooms, a sitting room, a dining room, kitchen and one bathroom. The windows are double glazed and were replaced around 20 years ago. There is also an attached garage at the gable.\r\nThere is oil fired central heating and indeed the boiler was only replaced a year ago and the water is of the mains system. This would surely make a wonderful family or holiday home in what is a really beautiful location.\r\n\r\nBER: C3 BER Number:103301289 Energy Output Indicator: 204.21 kWh/m2/yr\r\n\r\nAlso coming to the market to the rear of this property is an old derelict farmhouse on circa 17.3 acres of ground and also an additional a one undivided 11th share of circa 59.7 acres (5.43-acre share) of commonage located on MacDaras Island of the coast of Carna.
