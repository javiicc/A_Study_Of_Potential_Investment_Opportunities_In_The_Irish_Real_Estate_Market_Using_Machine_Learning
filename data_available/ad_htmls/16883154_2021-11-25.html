    A truly outstanding modern detached house set on half an acre of landscaped gardens located on a premier road which backs onto the Castle Golf Club. This home which was essentially rebuilt in 2003 is one of the finest houses to come for sale this year. It encapsulates state of the art modern technology with spacious well-designed family friendly accommodation. The attention to detail which prevails throughout this magnificent home is truly exceptional.\n\nBright, spacious and versatile accommodation extending to 740 sq.m (7,956 sq. ft.) includes a magnificent reception hall with vaulted ceiling, three reception rooms, open plan kitchen, breakfast room, play room, utility room and two guest cloakrooms with toilets. Upstairs there are 5 bedrooms all with bathroom or shower room en-suites. On the top floor there is a self-contained 2 bed apartment with feature balcony boasting stunning panoramic views over the Castle Golf Club to the Dublin Mountains. At basement level there is a leisure suite including indoor heated swimming pool, changing room, steam room, snooker room, coms room and pump room. There is also an integrated double garage at hall floor level. \n\nOutside are professionally landscaped gardens extending to half an acre with dual side entrance and separate pedestrian access to Castle Golf Club to the rear. To the front is a large garden mainly finished in cobblelock offering generous off-street parking accessed by two sets of electronically operated gates. \n\nHillside Drive is one of the capital's finest residential addresses and is within easy reach of some of Dublin's most sought-after schools including High School, Alexandra College, Gonzaga and Sandford Park. Further amenities include shopping and leisure facilities with Dundrum Town Centre located within 5 minutes' drive. More local shopping facilities are located a short stroll away at Churchtown Village. The Dodder Linear Park and Bushy Park are also close by. Public transport is available including bus services and the LUAS station at Windy Arbour, which are within walking distance. The M50 is also located within easy reach.\n\n\n.\xc2\xa0 \xc2\xa0 Upon entering, it is quite apparent that the standard of finish and the quality of this family home with the grand main door opening onto the double height reception hall with vaulted ceiling, three reception rooms, open plan kitchen, breakfast room, play room, utility room and two guest cloakrooms with toilets. Upstairs there are 5 bedrooms all with bathroom or shower room en-suites. On the top floor there is a self-contained 2 bed apartment with feature balcony boasting stunning panoramic views over the Castle Golf Club to the Dublin Mountains. At basement level there is a leisure suite including indoor heated swimming pool, changing room, steam room, snooker room, coms room and pump room. There is also an integrated double garage at hall floor level.\n\n\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nTravelling from Dodder Pank Road, take the right hand turn onto Woodside Drive.
