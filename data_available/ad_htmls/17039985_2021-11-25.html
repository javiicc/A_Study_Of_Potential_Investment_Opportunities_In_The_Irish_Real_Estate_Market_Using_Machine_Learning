    We bring to the market this 150 yr old cottage on Dingle Peninsula. A perfect spot to explore south west coast or unwind on the famous sandy beaches. The 'Maharees' has breath-taking views over both Brandon & Tralee Bays. Within walking distance there are 2 good pubs/restaurants nearby. Good for walking trails, water sports, golf, fishing, restaurants, local food markets. The location of the cottage is at the top of the peninsula. You will be surrounded by water with wonderful views of the mountains towards Dingle or over to Tralee.\r\nThe property has traditional thick stone walls that you would expect of a property of its age. Accommodation consists of Entrance Hall; Kitchen/Dining Room, Sitting Room with traditional open fireplace. There are 4 bedrooms all upstairs and 2 bathrooms and the property is tastefully decorated throughout. Viewing is recommended. \r\nPSRA No. 002404
