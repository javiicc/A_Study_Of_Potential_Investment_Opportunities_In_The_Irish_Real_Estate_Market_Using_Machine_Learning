    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to present 53 Cedarmount Road to the market. A fine three bedroomed semi-detached home Approx 116.3 Sq.m or 1252 sq.ft which was extended in the 1960 s and features bright and spacious receptions, an exceptional mature rear garden, off street parking and an ideal position in the heart of Mount Merrion.\n\nNumber 53 is a superb original 1940 s property offering all the charm and character of its period. High ceilings and large windows make this home a bright and voluminous space. The kitchen and living room have been extended and both have picture windows overlooking the rear garden. There is a garage and utility area to the side of the property and the entire property offers incredible potential for a discerning purchaser to create their forever home.\n\nCedarmount Road is a well renowned road the heart of Mount Merrion featuring a unique variety of homes. One of South Dublin s most desirable locations, Mount Merrion is ideal for any family with its close proximity to a selection of well-established schools for example Scoil San Treasa primary school and St Andrews, Blacrock and Mount Anville schools. It is minutes away from U.C.D Belfield and all the leisure facilities of Deer Park are on your doorstep with a choice of friendly local specialist shops within an easy stroll. Major shopping centres such as Stillorgan and Dundrum are close at hand. There are excellent transport links with the N11 (QBC), DART and the LUAS providing speedy access to the city centre.\n\nThe well laid out accommodation briefly comprises entrance porch, entrance hall, dining room, extended living room, extended kitchen breakfast room, garage and utility area.
