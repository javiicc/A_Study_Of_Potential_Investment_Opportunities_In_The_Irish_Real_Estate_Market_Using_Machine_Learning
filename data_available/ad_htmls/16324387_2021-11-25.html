    Colliers are delighted to present this wonderful period architecturally revamped cottage, situated on this highly desirable street in the heart of Dublin's silicon docks.\r\n\r\nNo 4 Gerald Street has been extended, remodelled and skilfully upgraded to an exceptionally high standard in recent years and now offers discerning purchasers a superbly balanced light filled accommodation of generous proportions and benefits from a completely private L shaped city garden with a south westerly orientation capturing the afternoon and evening sun.\r\n\r\nPerfectly presented, the property which stands approximately 48 sq. m / 516 sq. ft briefly comprises a large, bright and spacious living/dining room with high vaulted ceilings and features a large Velux roof light, a crisp white high shine kitchen comes fully equipped with all appliances and finished with white subway tiles, an extra large double bedroom, with a wall of bespoke wardrobes and a tastefully decorated and tiled bathroom completes the accommodation.\r\n\r\nThe property comes to the market in turnkey condition with high quality finishes throughout presenting an excellent opportunity for both first time buyers and investors alike.\r\n\r\nNo. 4 Gerald Street is positioned just off Barrow Street, enjoying an unrivalled location close to Grand Canal Dock, St Stephens Green, the City Centre and is situated within seconds of Google HQ Dublin.\r\nA choice selection of amenities in the area includes excellent restaurants, bars and shops, all within easy reach and with excellent public transport links including the DART, Dublin Bus services and city bikes make this a city living location second to none.\r\n\r\nViewing is by private appointment with John Coleman
