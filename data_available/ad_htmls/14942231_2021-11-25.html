    Welcome to Number 4 St Catherine's Lane, Dublin 7. This quaint one bedroom cottage is situated in a quiet cul de sac off Church Street in Smithfield. Having been well maintained by it's current owner the cottage has great curb appeal with a pebble dash front, composite door and double glazed shuttered windows. The interior will not disappoint with a spacious living separate kitchen with dining area, utility room, full bathroom and one bright double bedroom, a patio garden to the rear completes the accommodation. Located minutes from the City Centre and Smithfield, Stoneybatter Village and Phibsborough Village are within a short distance all offering a unique array of independent shops, cafes, restaurants and bars. This is what Urban living is all about. Also on it's door step are the Four Courts, The Law Library, Grangegorman Campus and The Mater Hospital. Great public transport via Dublin Bus, LUAS at Smithfield and Broadstone and Dublin Bikes complete the appeal of this City property.
