    This 1615sqft modern three bed bungalow is a real gem of a property. It is situated on circa 0.44 acre site which is located in a very scenic area and is 2 minutes' walk to the entrance of Carne Golf Course which was voted 7th in the top 100th golf courses in the UK and Ireland and lies in magnificent unspoiled sand dunes overlooking Blacksod Bay and the wild Atlantic Ocean. The property enjoys panoramic views and is a mere 10-minute walk from a magnificent beach.\r\n\r\nIt is within close proximity to Belmullet town and all its amenities. Property of this quality rarely comes to the market in such an attractive location. It is in turnkey condition and this house would provide perfect family accommodation as a permanent residence or holiday home.\r\n\r\nAccommodation includes entrance hallway, sitting room, kitchen/dining room, three double bedrooms, two ensuites, family bathroom and a hot press. The property has triple glazed windows throughout. The attic has a heavy duty Stira staircase, two Velux windows and is floored. There is a remote control heating system and CCTV.\r\n\r\nThe property has a detached garage to the side. The sunny rear garden is south facing with a large granite patio area providing a wonderful space to relax. \r\n\r\nThe property comes with full planning permission to construct an extension to the northwest side of the house, linking the house to the garage (P18/455)\r\n\r\nClose to National and Secondary schools and commuting distance to Bangor, Crossmolina and Ballina.\r\n\r\nThe property is in close proximity to a number of other idyllic white sandy beaches, windswept coasts and stunning cliffs. Belmullet is a barony of Erris and is a real haven for surfers, walkers and enthusiasts of water sports. \r\n\r\nThis is a truly exceptional property and viewing is a must.\r\n\r\n\r\nAccommodation\r\nEntrance Hallway - 2.75m (9'0") x 2.34m (7'8")\r\n\r\nHallway - 3.88m (12'9") x 3.61m (11'10")\r\n\r\nSitting room - 4.95m (16'3") x 3.93m (12'11")\r\n\r\nKitchen/Dining Room - 7.09m (23'3") x 4.92m (16'2")\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 - 7.09m (23'3") x 4.94m (16'2")\r\n\r\nEn suite - 1.52m (5'0") x 2.94m (9'8")\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 - 3.63m (11'11") x 3.25m (10'8")\r\n\r\nEn Suite - 1.37m (4'6") x 2.28m (7'6")\r\n\r\nBedroom 3 - 3.01m (9'11") x 4.02m (13'2")\r\n\r\nHot press - 1.13m (3'8") x 1.78m (5'10")\r\n\r\nBathroom - 3.01m (9'11") x 2.09m (6'10")\r\n\r\nNotice\r\nPlease note we have not tested any apparatus, fixtures, fittings, or services.
