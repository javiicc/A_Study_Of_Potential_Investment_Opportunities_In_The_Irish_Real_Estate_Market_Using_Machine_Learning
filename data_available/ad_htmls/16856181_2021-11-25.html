    An impressive detached country home extending to 1733 sq ft / 161.29 sq m with working farm on approximately 21.6 acres of quality lands within a stone s throw of the River Liffey in a delightful rustic setting with convenience to Clane and Sallins villages and all services. \n\nThis country residence is a wonderful, unique, property with a spacious layout.\nBlackhall is a delightful, peaceful rustic setting where the country atmosphere is virtually unspoilt, close to the banks of the River Liffey and with convenience to Clane, Sallins and Naas where one can find all the amenities required for modern living. \nClane village has a choice of primary schools, secondary schools including Clongowes Wood College just three miles distant, a variety of shops, health club, restaurants and pubs. \nThere is access to commuter train service (Arrow) at Sallins some 3.8 miles away.
