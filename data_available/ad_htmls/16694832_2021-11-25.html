    Dunroamin is a substantial detached family home positioned on a most beautiful elevated site with breathtaking views of the countryside and Comeragh mountains beyond. The home comes with a large area of mature landscaped gardens to the front and rear and detached garage with roller door and slated pitch roof. The property has it's own private well and pump house.\r\n\r\n\r\nThe spacious entrance hallway gives access to the main rooms and sunroom. The lounge and three of the bedroom's are to the front of the property and master bedroom to the rear with ensuite powered T90 shower and tiled floor to ceiling, the centerpiece of the house is the extended kitchen/dining/family area, fully fitted utility area with its range of custom wall and base units, island unit and some integrated appliances. The main bathroom is fitted with corner bath unit and T90 powered shower and the sunroom is fitted with electronically opened Velux roof lights and stove. Gorgeous wrought iron stairs gives access to the 1st floor area, this space is currently utilised as an office/study and recreational area.\r\n\r\n\r\nThis detached home is located just 4klms outside Portlaw, well known for its amenities and only a short distance drive from the ever popular Greenway and Curraghmore Estate. The area allows easy access to Clonmel, Carrick on Suir, Lemybrien and the Waterford City bypass.\r\n\r\n Note: These particulars are given on the understanding that they will not be construed as part of a contract, conveyance or lease, and that all negotiations take place through Manor Properties.
