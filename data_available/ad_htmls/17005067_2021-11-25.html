    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    BRACKEN ESTATES are delighted to bring to the market this modern and spacious 3-bedroom Duplex. Situated in the burgeoning locality of Citywest, this property is ideal for everyone from first time buyers to a family looking to increase their living space to a potential investor seeking a strong return.\n\nThis duplex property comes to the market in walk in condition with a very warm, homely vibe from the minute you walk through the door. Space is never an issue as throughout the property, each room is generously proportioned.\n\nAccommodation comprises entrance hallway leading to separate kitchen with dining space, all mod cons included. Off the hallway is the guest W.C. with superb under stair storage opposite. The extremely large living room allows for plenty of scope when it comes to configuring furniture and fittings. Situated off the main living room is a light filled sunroom which is ideal as a playroom, home office, or simply an added leisure space. Floor to ceiling doors lead out to the dual aspect balcony. The balcony provides for private outdoor space overlooking a large greenspace.\n\nUpstairs you will find 3 very large bedrooms, all comfortably double sized. Master bedroom with En-suite bathroom. All bedrooms have built-in wardrobes. Finishing off the accommodation is the main bathroom with both bath and shower and also containing Hotpress with shelving. There is added storage space with the attic space floored. \n\nThis superb development which is maintained to very high standard is located within arm\xe2\x82\xacTM reach of the newly built Citywest Shopping Centre, Citywest Business Park, The Luas, the N7, N81 and the M50 motorway.
