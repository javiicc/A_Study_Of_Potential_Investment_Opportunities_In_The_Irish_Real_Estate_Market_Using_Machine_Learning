    Situated within easy reach of the Village, the Sea and the Dart, 51 Oakton Park is a delightful semi-detached home overlooking a green to the front and with the benefit of a south facing patio garden to the rear.\nThe accommodation extends to 97sqm and comprises reception hall, living room, kitchen / diner on the ground floor. Upstairs there are 3 bedrooms and bathroom. Additionally, there is a separate entrance to the side to the converted garage with living area, kitchenette and shower room which could suit as a 4th bedroom or indeed as a home office.\nPresented in good order throughout, this property is sure to appeal to those seeking flexible accommodation in a location close to so many worthwhile amenities including Ballybrack Village, Killiney Beach & Dart, Parklands at Kilbogget and multiple leisure and sporting clubs. The area has excellent transport links and is serviced by multiple bus routes, Dart, Luas and is just minutes from the M50 and N11.\n\nAccommodation\nReception Hall\nWith storage off.\n\nLiving Room\nTo the front with bay window, feature fireplace with stove.\n\nKitchen / Diner\nWith range of fitted units and integrated appliances.
