    Welcome to 2 Kilteragh Pines, a quite exceptional private residence superbly located in the leafy suburbs of Foxrock in a highly desirable setting and location with potential to offer a unique and extensive family home in Dublin 18, that is nestled in the picturesque surrounds of Kilteragh Pines off Westminster Road. The attractive grounds are simply magical and provide a relaxing and private position for a house that is steeped in history. \n\nThis home provides excellent accommodation on one level and although in need of some modernisation has clearly been well maintained throughout the years whilst retaining many of it s special original features. All told, the property provides an excellent opportunity for a private home, yet with all the ingredients for excellent entertainment. This property is positioned in a prominent position in this enclave with many attractive features including an enviable private sheltered garden which wraps within the property. On arrival the reception is open and spacious and has immediate access to the well-balanced accommodation which offers bright interiors with rooms of generous proportions and fine dimensions. The main reception rooms are off the hallway. The elegant drawing room & cosy living room are very attractive features of this home. There is a well equipped kitchen for everyday use, cloak area, utility room, main bathroom & guest w.c. There are 3 bedrooms with generous proportions. This home will offer new owners a secluded area to relax and enjoy this haven of tranquility or dine al fresco in the sundrenched garden areas enjoying all day sunshine.\n\nThe location is superb, only a few minutes away from charming Foxrock village with its many amenities including bijou shops, boutiques & Cafes, Foxrock Golf Club, Leopardstown Race Course, Carrickmines Lawn Tennis Club and Westwood Fitness Club. There are many well regarded primary and secondary schools nearby. The M50, N11 with the QBC and the Luas at Carrickmines are a short distance away offering easy access to the city centre.\n\n\nEntrance Hall Wide reception hallway with attractive original features. Velux window. Door to storage press & guest w.c\n\nLiving Room A second bright and spacious reception room with steps down to the main living area, overlooking the rear garden. This room features high ceilings, electric fireplace. Storage room and access to the side.\n\nDining Room Beautiful bright and spacious room overlooking the landscaped gardens with attractive features.\n\nKitchen Fitted with an extensive array of wall and base units and an attractive counter top. Stainless steel sink unit with splashback & built in appliances. Tiled floors.\n\nUtility Room Door from kitchen to utility room with plumbing for washing machine. Door to the shed at the rear.\n\nBathroom with w.c, wash hand basin, bath. Tiled walls and floor. Door to storage press.\n\nBedroom 1 Large and spacious double bedroom with views over the private rear garden. Fitted wardrobes.\n\nBedroom 2 Large and spacious master bedroom with access to the private rear garden, feature fireplace with coal affect fire.\n\nBedroom 3/Office Bedroom or home office with window overlooking the rear courtyard. Fitted wardrobes.\n\nGarden The house is set well back from the road, the front has a cobble lock area bordered by planted beds providing off street parking. The rear garden is stunning, it enjoys the enviable south west facing orientation.
