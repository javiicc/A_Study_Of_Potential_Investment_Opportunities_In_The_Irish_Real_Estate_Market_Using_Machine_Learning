    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Four-bedroomed detached family home in secluded setting overlooking Sandycove slip, Sandycove Island and the sea beyond.\n\nOriginally a traditional country cottage, Bluebell Cottage has been extended and transformed by it's current owners over the last 30 years. It has had several additions and many improvements and now comprises a well laid out four bedroomed property extending to over 185 sq m (2000sqft). With spacious reception rooms and stunning vistas from most rooms, the feature of the property is a large sunroom located off the kitchen and livingroom which opens to the fine-sized deck with uninterrupted views of the stunning panorama over Sandycove and all it has to offer. \n\nThe property is set into the hillside, affording it much shelter from the prevailing south-westerlies and also much privacy. The steep drive leads up to parking and double garage with remote control roller door which has internal access to the house. \nBluebell Cottage comes to the market in immaculate condition throughout and with attention to the smallest details to ensure the original character of the property remains yet with the comforts of modern living to enhance.\n\nAccommodation includes a clever spacious entrance hall which straddles the property, joining the garage to the main house. The warm and inviting livingroom has a wood burning stove and opens to the impressive sunroom and it's views. The kitchen and utility room are beyond the livingroom. There is a bedroom and study (or bedroom 4) downstairs and two further bedrooms and a bathroom upstairs. Heating is oil fired. Outside, the garden have been lovingly tended to by its current owners, with mature planting all around and either decking or paving for ease of maintenance.
