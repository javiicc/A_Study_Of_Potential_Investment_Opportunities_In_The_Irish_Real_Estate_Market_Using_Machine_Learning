    Portfinn Lodge is a well established and successful trading restaurant and guest house exceptionally well located in Leenane Village overlooking Killary Fjord.\n\nThis purpose built premises has traded for over thirty years and offers excellent business potential for a new owner in one of the West of Ireland s most visited tourist destinations.\n\nThe property has a floor area of approximately 449 sq m/4,833 sq ft. It comprises of a spacious restaurant benefiting from outstanding fjord and mountain views with a well fitted kitchen, a guest lounge also with fjord and mountain views and eight en-suite guest rooms. In addition there is a self contained four bedroom staff accommodation unit. The premises are being sold with all furniture and fittings included.\n\nLeenane is situated in an area of outstanding natural beauty at the head of Killary Fjord, Ireland s only fjord, and surrounded by the Mweelrea and Maamturk mountains. The village has an international profile as the setting for the film The Field . \n\nThe area has achieved significant additional interest in recent years since the launch of the Wild Atlantic Way, the world s longest defined coastal touring route. Killary Harbour has been included in the 15 Signature Discovery Points along the 2,500 kilometre route. \n\nThe nearest town at approximately 30km is Westport, Co Mayo.
