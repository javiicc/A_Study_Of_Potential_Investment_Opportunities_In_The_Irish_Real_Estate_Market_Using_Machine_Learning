    Located in a peaceful cul de sac off Clonliffe Road, The Distillery is a modern development of apartments built in 2005. With well maintained communal gardens and a stunning roof terrace the complex is very popular with those seeking a stylish home in a terrific location close to the city centre and both Fairview and Drumcondra Villages. Number 105 is a beautifully presented top floor apartment with spectacular city views, which has been maintained to a very high standard and decorated with great style.\n\nThe accommodation comprises a hallway with wooden floor, hot press and storage closet. The open plan living area has an entire wall of glazing allowing light to flood through the room. The seating area is closest to the window with a dining area and kitchen. The kitchen is fitted with a great range of floor and wall units with fitted oven, hob and extractor fan, integrated dishwasher and a washing machine. The bedroom is a large double with a wall of glazing and great views. Both the living room and bedroom are laid in semi solid oak flooring. A large bathroom completes the accommodation. The property further benefits from a secure car parking space with mobile phone gate access.\n\nThe communal areas and gardens are meticulously maintained, and the complex boasts a spectacular communal roof garden with views of Dublin City. \n\nThe location is excellent, just a short stroll from all amenities (both social and essential) and within minutes of the city centre. There are many local amenities such as Croke Park, Fairview Park, the Botanic Gardens and Na Fianna GAA club. It is within easy reach of Mater Hospital, Temple Street Hospital, Dublin City University, St. Patrick s Teacher Training College, Trinity College, IFSC and Clontarf Seafront. The area is well served by public transport with Dublin Bus, Drumcondra Station, Clontarf Station and Connolly Station. For those travelling further afield the property is very convenient to Dublin Airport, the M50 and the M1. \n\n\nEntrance Hall 2.5m x 2m. Large hallway with oak flooring, hotpress and storage closet.\n\nLiving/ Dining Room 6.4m x 4.1m. This is a very spacious and bright room with an entire wall of glazing offering great views of the city from the Dublin Mountains to Howth. The room is laid in semi solid oak flooring.\n\nKitchen 3m x 1.5m. The kitchen forms part of the living area and is fitted with a great range of floor and wall units with dishwasher, washing machine, fitted oven, hob and extractor fan. The kitchen has a tiled floor and splashback.\n\nBedroom 3.7m x 3m. Spacious double room with large window with great city views, fitted wardrobes and semi solid oak flooring.\n\nBathroom 2.6m x 1.9m. With suite comprising w.c, wash hand basin and bath. The floor and bath surround are tiled and a mirrored wall hanging unit offers valuable storage.
