    Gleninagh, 17 Farranshone is proudly introduced to the Limerick property market by Limerick Auctioneers Rowan Fitzgerald.\r\n\r\nThis is a fantastic family home that will impress upon viewing. On entering you are welcomed by an inviting hallway with a convenient under stairs wc. Off the hallway is the living room which has a great solid fuel stove that not only adds to the character of the home but is also a back boiler to the main heating system. This is a very comfortable and relaxing space and seamlessly follows through to the bright kitchen and dining room through beautiful glass double doors.\r\nThe kitchen really is the soul of this home which is modern, well-appointed and has plenty of cupboard space, it also has a breakfast counter, separating the dining from the kitchen space. \r\n\r\nBeyond the kitchen and heading outside is a great feature, the raised deck which looks out over the property's back yard. This was a great concept for summer evenings as it effortlessly links the kitchen to the exterior. The secure, enclosed yard is paved, and at the rear of the yard is a very useful block shed which is in good condition and is equipped with power.\r\n\r\nOn the first floor, there are 3 bedrooms and the master bathroom. Two of these bedrooms easily accommodate doubles beds and the third bedroom is a single. The third bedroom also leads the way to the striking converted attic which has a wc as well as an electric shower and Velux windows to the rear. This really is a tremendous part of the house and will have many uses for its new owners.\r\n\r\nThe property has oil heating with a solid fuel stove back boiler, double glazed windows and a modern upvc front door. The home has an efficient BER of C2. Furthermore, to the front of the property is ample off-street parking.\r\n\r\nThe location is second to none with a host of amenities very close by and the city centre is only a 10 minute walk.\r\n\r\nFor more information on this great property please call Rowan Fitzgerald Auctioneers on 061 279423.
