    RE/MAX Professional Partners present to market a 3 bedroom end of terrace property located at 3 Newbridge Cottages, Killymongaun, Clifden, Co. Galway.\n\nExtending to c. 1,136 sq. ft., the property is bright and spacious, presented in turnkey condition having been excellent maintained by the current owner and is decorated to a high standard throughout. Living accommodation of the ground floor is comprised of an entrance hall, open plan kitchen/dining/living room and bathroom. Proceeding to the first floor is the landing, 3 double bedrooms and bathroom. \n\nCertified with an impressive C1 BER Certificate and heated via oil fired central heating. The property features a solid fuel fire with a stunning stone feature fireplace in the living area.\n\nExternally the property sits on an elevated site and features a garden to the rear with access via double doors in the kitchen/dining area. \n\nNewbridge Cottages are situated just off the N59 Road and within walking distance of Clifden town or a short 2 minute drive away. Local amenities include Clifden Community Playschool, Clifden Community School, Clifden District Hospital, Aldi, Lidl and Bank of Ireland along with a wide range of shops, pubs, cafes and restaurants. Serviced by Bus Eireann buses and the town can be accessed via the N59 Road from Galway City which is approx. 1 hour 15 minutes away.\n\nContact Brian Deely of RE/MAX for more information on 091 330 300.\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nNOTE: These particulars are not to be considered a formal offer.
