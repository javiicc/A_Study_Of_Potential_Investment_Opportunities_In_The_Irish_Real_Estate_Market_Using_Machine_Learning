    Power & Walsh are delighted to bring this substantial, three storey, 4 bedroom Georgian Townhouse to the market.\n\nWith overall accommodation extending to 3,600 sq. ft approx., this property was built c. 1840. The house boasts many classic Georgian features such as high ceilings throughout, original pillars in entrance hall, many with the original ornate plaster work and spacious and bright beautifully appointed rooms. \n\nOne attractive feature of this property is the benefit of two self contained apartments with separate entrance, which are generating a rental income. \n\nThis stunning property, which is in excellent condition throughout, overlooks the Denis Burke Park and River Suir with beautiful views of the mountains. There is a rear entrance from Angelsea Street.\n\nThis property needs to be viewed to be truly appreciated.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 5.08m x 1.78m. with tiled floor, antique pillars, mahogany wainscotting.\n\nStudy 4.93m x 5.08m. slate fireplace with cast iron insert, timber floors.\n\nKitchen 4.50m x 3.58m. with tiled floor and splashback, marble worktop, fitted units, insinkerator, instant hot water tap, built in; 2 ovens, grill oven, steamer oven, microwave, American fridge, courtyard off kitchen, double doors for kitchen.\n\nUtility Room\n\nLiving Room/Dining Room 6.86m x 8.53m. with mahogany parquet flooring, original marble fireplace with cast iron insert, curtains and pelmets.\n\nFIRST FLOOR RETURN\n\nWC with whb.\n\nSmall Room 1.90m x 1.98m. plumbed for use as a wet room.\n\nFamily Room 4.52m x 3.35m. with carpet, slate fireplace with tiled insert.\n\nBedroom One 5.97m x 4.11m. with carpet, velux windows x 2, dual heating, storage and oil radiators.\n\nTOP FLOOR\n\nBathroom 2.26m x 1.78m. with wc, whb, corner shower, mains shower, fully tiled walls and floor.\n\nBedroom Two 2.29m x 3.78m. double with pitch pine floor, mahogany door, south facing room with river and park views.\n\nBedroom Three 5.10m x 4.34m. with curtains, pitch pine floor, built in wardrobe, south facing room with river and park views.\n\nLobby with hotpress.\n\nBedroom Four 2.90m x 3.60m. with fitted carpet.\n\nEn Suite 1.78m x 1.30m. with wc, whb, tiled walls and floor, electric shower, extractor.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nCentrally located on the New Quay in Clonmel the property overlooks the Denis Burke Park with beautiful views of the mountains and River Suir.
