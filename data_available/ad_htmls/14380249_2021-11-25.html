    Beakstown House is a Georgian style property situated at the end of a sweeping avenue in Holycross, just 6km from Thurles. Built in 1999, in an exact replica of the original Beakstown House, this wonderful period style home has every comfort of contemporary living on some 1.4 acres of secluded grounds, lawns, gardens and mature planting. The owner's attention to detail and emphasis on high quality craftmanship is exceptional. \nBoasting 5,000 sq. Ft Beakstown House, has all the elements of a country home, with an impressive feature staircase, 2 large reception rooms, a sunroom, traditional kitchen and utility room, dining room and washroom on the ground floor.\nOn the first-floor level there are 4 large bedrooms with 4 en-suites and 2 walk in wardrobes, an impressive landing and office area. The spiralled mahogany staircase to the second floor has another bedroom and unique curved entrance to a large en-suite. \nAlso on the grounds is an adjoining 2-bed lodge with Kitchen, Dining, Living area. This would make an ideal Air B and B or spacious work area. \nBeakstown House is located on the R660 Holycross Thurles Road. It is just 2km from the picturesque village of Holycross, home to the world renowned Holycross Abbey, National School, Shop and Public Houses.
