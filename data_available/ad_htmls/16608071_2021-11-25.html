    DETACHED 4 BED HOUSE FOR SALE\nTarpey Maloney Properties have pleasure in bringing to the market this superb detached residence located in Derrylissane, on the outskirts of Menlough Village. \n\nComprised of approx. 220 sq. mt., this property consists of a front porch, main entrance hall, reception room, sitting room, open plan kitchen / dining room, utility room, downstairs W/C, 4 bedrooms up-stairs (1 of which is en-suite & 1 with shower facilities) and main bathroom. Large garage and office space adjoining the main house. This fine house sits on approx. 0.18 hectares which is a lovely mature space and features decking, a garden pond, well maintained gardens, mature hedging, tarmacked driveway etc. \n\nThe rooms in this property are very well proportioned and provide lots of storage space throughout. \n\nHeating is by way of OFCH with a gas stove in the kitchen, gas fire in the sitting room and a solid fuel open fire in the reception room. Stylish brick surround at stove in kitchen provides a feature focal point and brings definition and warmth to the room also. \n\nThis property is situated just on the outskirts of Menlough Village. Menlough has a very friendly community spirit and has great amenities to offer such as Jordan\xe2\x82\xacTM Spar Shop, St. Mary\xe2\x82\xacTM National School, pre-school facilities, the Church, community hall, public house, GAA pitch etc. \n\nSecondary schools are located nearby in Mountbellew and Castleblakeney, as well as the Agricultural College in Mountbellew. Menlough is only approx. 35 minutes drive from Galway City which makes it an excellent commuter village and approx. 20 minutes from the towns of Athenry and Ballinasloe and only 5 minutes from Monivea.\n\nViewing is strictly by appointment only. Please call Tarpey Maloney Properties on 091 764644.
