    Sherry FitzGerald takes great pleasure introducing 15 Hazelbrook to the market. Designed by RKD Architects and built by Lomac Developments circa 2006, this two bedroom second floor apartment provides generous accommodation presented in turn-key and contemporary condition. This is truly a great opportunity to acquire a modern apartment in an extremely sought-after and convenient location.\n\nResiding in an exclusive development of 54 apartments, No. 15 has a pleasant outlook to the well-maintained communal grounds below. The accommodation comprises an entrance hall with two storage rooms, an open plan kitchen/ living/dining room with floor to ceiling double-glazed windows and access to a balcony benefiting from the evening sun, two double bedrooms, the main with an ensuite, a family bathroom and storage. This apartment is incredibly luxurious with many high-quality fixtures and fittings and floor to ceiling windows in the main living space which floods the apartment with natural light. There is the added benefit of one designated underground car parking space. \n\nHazelbrook is convenient to a multitude of amenities such as local shopping in Goatstown and Kilmacud which is complimented by the facilities of Dundrum Town Centre and Stillorgan Shopping Centre. Convenient pedestrian access to the rear of the development provides easy access to the Kilmacud Luas Stop. Sporting and leisure facilities are also close by. The award-winning Dundrum Town Centre is only a short stroll away with designer shops, restaurants, and the Multiplex Cinema. Many of South Dublin s most prestigious primary and secondary schools are within easy reach and the road network in the area has been transformed giving easy access via the M50.\n\nA wonderful property in an unrivalled location viewing is advised.\n\n\nEntrance Hall Laminate wood flooring, access to hot press and walk in storage/ utility space\n\nLiving Room 8.55m x 3.40m.
