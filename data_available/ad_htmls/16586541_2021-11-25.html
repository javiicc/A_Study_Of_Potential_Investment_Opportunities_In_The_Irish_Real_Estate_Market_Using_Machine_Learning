    O'Connell Auctioneers are privileged to present this fantastic 5-bedroom detached house to the market. It is rare that a house of this stature and with the accompanying lands come to the market. \nThis property is set right back from the road and sits on grounds of c. 5.78 Acres (2.34HA) and includes beautifully mature gardens, stone chip wraparound drive and large sheds to the rear set out as dog kennels and run. The property is located just outside Mallow town on the N72 Fermoy Road with easy access to the Dublin Road N73. The accommodation extends to approximately 2,300 sq. ft. (214 sqm). \n\nAccommodation:\n\n\nEntrance Hall with parquet flooring leading to all rooms downstairs \n\nSitting Room 6.1m X 4.2m Light filled room with ceiling to floor windows and with carpet floorings, fire place and double doors leading to a covered porch area\n\nDining Room 3.5m X 3.5m open plan from sitting room area with carpet flooring looking out at the front of the house\n\nOffice 2.0m X 2.0m small office located to the rear of the house with carpet flooring \n\nKitchen 4.3m X 3.6m Fully fitted kitchen with wall and floor units on three sides and looking out to the beautiful mature rear garden\n\nLiving Room 5.1m X 3.6m Light filled room with ceiling to floor windows, parquet flooring and large fireplace\n\nUtility Room 2.7m X 1.4m\n\nWC (Off utility) 1.8m X 1.4m with WC & WHB\n\nWC (Off office) with WC & WHB\n\nMaster Bedroom 5.1m X 3.6m with carpet flooring and feature balcony and with walk in wardrobe and ensuite\n\nEnsuite 3.6m X 2.0m with WC, WHB and bath/shower\n\nBedroom 2 3.6m X 3.0m with carpet flooring overlooking the front of the house with wardrobe\n\nBedroom 3 3.0m X 2.7m with carpet flooring overlooking the front of the house with wardrobe\n\nBedroom 4 4.0m X 2.78m with carpet flooring overlooking the front of the house with wardrobe\n\nBedroom 5 3.0m X 2.8m with carpet flooring and large walk in wardrobe\n\nBathroom 3.7m X 2.0m Large bathroom with WC, WHB and shower\n\nExternally there are beautifully manicured gardens to the rear and side of the house. Behind the house there is a large shed which was previously used for dog kennels and runs. There is also the remains of the former house which could be converted subject to the relevant planning permission. The property sits on a beautiful plot of lands extending to 5.78 Acres (2.34 HA) set back in off the road giving it a countryside feel while being just two minutes from Mallow town.\n\nPlease note: Our client requires a proof of funds from a financial institution with each offer.
