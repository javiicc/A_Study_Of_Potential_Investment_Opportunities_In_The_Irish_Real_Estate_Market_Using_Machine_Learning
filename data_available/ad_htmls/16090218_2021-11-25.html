    Located just 1km from Inagh village and situated on a fantastic site extending to approximately 0.5 acres is this fantastic 5 bedroom detached family home presented for sale in excellent condition. The spacious feature entrance hallway with the open void between ground and first floor leads to an equally spacious kitchen/dining and main reception and a ground floor en-suite bedroom. The main reception features the cast iron surround solid fuel fireplace with the bay window onto the front gardens, The kitchen/dining with the built in solid oak wall and base units has also the facility of the dining area for a solid fuel or oil/gas stove with the open connecting arch to the side sun lounge. Rounding off the ground floor accommodation is the utility and ground floor WC. To the first floor are 4 double bedrooms, 1 en-suite and main bathroom. All bathrooms and en-suites are fully tiled ceiling to floor, extensive timber flooring and tiling feature throughout, solid oak fitted kitchen, cast iron surround feature fireplace, integrated appliances and the bright, spacious and well proportioned accommodation are just some of the many features worth mentioning. This would make a fantastic family home with similar houses being immediately adjacent and opposite making for ideal family living. Viewing is highly recommended and strictly by prior appointment with sole selling agents.
