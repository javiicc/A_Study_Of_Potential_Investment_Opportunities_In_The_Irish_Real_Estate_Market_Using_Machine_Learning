Upstairs the spacious accommodation continues. There are two large doubles and the third is a generous single with a large window allowing ample light throughout. Each room distinct in their own right. The family bathroom completes the first floor accommodation. To the front of the property lies the neatly landscaped front garden that offers the all-important off-street parking. \r\n\r\nThe location alone speaks volumes about this superbly appointed residence.
