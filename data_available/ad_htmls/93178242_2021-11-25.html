    Development Description

      **On Sale Now** \r\n\r\nTo make a private appointment to view our show homes call Selling Agents Sherry FitzGerald New Homes 01 667 1888 or Kelly Walsh 01 664 5500.\r\n\r\nWelcome to Archers Wood, a wonderful new development of spacious 3, 4 and 5 bed family homes in the highly sought-after village of Delgany, Co Wicklow.\r\n \r\nEnjoy the best of country, coastal and city living in this prestigious location, which is the perfect meeting point between the rolling hills of the Wicklow countryside, the beautiful coastline at Greystones and the hustle and bustle of South Dublin and Dublin City. Archers Wood combines this ideal location with a well-laid-out development that includes an abundance of green space, carefully planned to enhance its beautiful surroundings.\r\n \r\nDelgany is adjacent to the thriving coastal village of Greystones, a popular location for families as it boasts excellent retail and leisure facilities, as well as plenty of well-regarded established schools. Transport links are second to none, with regular buses, the DART service and the main M11/M50 roads only minutes away.\r\n \r\nNamed after Dr Arthur Manford Archer, a one-time resident of Bellevue House, Delgany, and the first president of Delgany Golf Club, Archers Wood is an attractive development with a wide variety of family homes. Each house is carefully laid out with modern life in mind, and packed full of family-friendly features, such as a high-quality fully fitted kitchen and ample storage space. Plus, a host of energy-efficiency features such as high levels of insulation make these houses extremely budget friendly to run.\r\n\r\n\r\nNote\r\n\r\nThese particulars are detailed for the purposes of representing the development only.
