A second double bedroom completes the accommodation, as shown on the accompanying floorplans.\n\nGarden & Grounds\nAn electric gated entrance opens onto a gravel driveway which provides ample off-street parking. Situated to the rear is a west-facing garden with mature trees adding privacy to the property. An additional benefit is a useful storage shed positioned to the rear of the house.
