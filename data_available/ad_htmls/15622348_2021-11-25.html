    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    From the moment you enter the front door, you know you are about to enter something special, as this home can only be described as stunning, captivating & absolutely breath taking. It is obvious that the highest standard of workmanship & materials have been employed throughout, together with a very attractive interior design. This property is most definitely a one off and presents a magnificent opportunity to acquire what one would describe as a little piece of heaven, in one of the most sought after areas on the South East coastline. Located only a stones throw from many beautiful beaches & just minutes from Kilmore Quay village, which offers a very attractive marina, award winning restaurants, shops, church etc. Wexford town is 20 mins approx. Rosslare Europort is a short driving distance and the new & ever improving N/M11 road network allows South Dublin to be reached in just 90 minutes while the N25 allows access to Waterford, Cork, etc., ever quicker.\n\nAccommodation is bright, spacious, simply stunning and the level of finish has to be seen to be fully appreciated! Some of the notable extras are the natural stone walls, beautiful tiling, stained glass window, beamed ceilings to name but a few. There is a sense of light and airiness throughout the entire house and is a joy to experience. While the interior is outstanding, this quality of living externally is equally as impressive once you step through the patio doors to the landscaped gardens. Expertly designed, there are a host of areas specifically designed to let you enjoy the outdoor space including raised flower beds, cobble locked / stone pathways, fire-pit, thatched gazebo, patio area, feature garden room and natural stone wall tower feature.
