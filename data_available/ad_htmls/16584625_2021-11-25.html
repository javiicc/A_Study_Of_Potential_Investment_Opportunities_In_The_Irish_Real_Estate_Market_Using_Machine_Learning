    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to present No. 44 St. John s Court to the market. A beautifully presented 3 bedroomed and 3 bathroomed terraced house in this attractive small development built in 1995 is located just off the Kilmore Road in Artane. \n\nThis lovely home measuring approximately 97 sq.m. has a much sought after south facing garden and is positioned in a quiet cul de sac setting with a nearby mature green area. \n\nOn entering there is a spacious and bright hallway with a downstairs guest w.c.. The living room and adjoining dining room run the length of the property with a lovely box bay window to the front and a window to the rear garden bringing in plenty of light throughout. The well-appointed kitchen to the rear has access to the garden and all appliances are included in the sale. The laminate flooring throughout has an attractive and easy to maintain walnut effect finish. Upstairs there are three bedrooms all with built in wardrobes, two doubles and a single, the main has a stylishly tiled ensuite with rainshower and skylight while the family bathroom to the rear completes the accommodation.\n\nOutside the front garden is walled and has a cobblelock driveway which takes two cars while the lovely south facing rear garden is mostly laid in lawn, with a selection of fruit trees and a barna shed.\n\nLocation is terrific here with easy access to local shops including Artane Castle and Northside Shopping Centres. It is approximately 1 km to the M1/M50 and 15 minutes to the airport. There are good local primary and secondary schools within walking distance while Beaumont Hospital is very close by. There is a good local bus route on the Kilmore Road giving regular access to the city centre only 6km away. All sporting activities are catered for in the vicinity of this family friendly neighbourhood. \n\nPlease email or call us in the Killester office 01 8336555 to arrange an appointment.
