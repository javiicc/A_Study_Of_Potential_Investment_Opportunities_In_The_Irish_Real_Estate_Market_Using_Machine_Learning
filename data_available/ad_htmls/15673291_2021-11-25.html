Set back off the road the property is accessed through a gated tarmac driveway with beautifully maintained gardens boasting mature plants, shrubs, and trees. The property enjoys beautifully crafted stone walls, trees and hedging as its boundary.\r\n\r\nUpon entering this beautiful property, you are struck by a sense of grandeur. The care and attention that has been afforded to this property by its current owners is very obvious. The large spacious, bright hallway with double height ceiling welcomes you into the home. To your right is the sitting room with warm wooden floor, feature red brick fireplace with a solid fuel stove and double patio doors to the garden. To the left off the hallway is the cosy living room which is currently utilised as an office. Further down the hallway to the right takes you into a sizeable kitchen/dining room with shaker style fitted kitchen providing an abundance of storage space. A central island, recessed lighting and range style gas stove are some of the bonus aspects to this space. Complementing the kitchen is a well-equipped utility room. Off the hallway to your right is a bright and airy study which features double doors overlooking the garden and a further sizeable reception room/games room which would suit a multitude of uses. Completing the ground floor is a beautifully presented shower room and the guest w.c. On the first floor off the landing are five good sized bedrooms, with three enjoying an en-suites. The large superbly presented main family bathroom completes the accommodation of this floor. Upstairs on the second floor are three fantastic storage areas which would be suited to a variety of uses. The basement features a purpose-built wine cellar, perfect for any wine enthusiasts. \r\n\r\nArdrahan is a small village in South Co. Galway. Located on the N18 Galway route, 30 minutes to Galway city and 35 minutes to Shannon Airport and well serviced by the M18 and M6 motorway with easy access at the Kiltiernan junction. All amenities are near-by with choice of primary and secondary schools, supermarkets, restaurants, sporting facilities. Kinvara is 4 miles away and the busy market town of Gort is 6miles away.\r\n\r\nKilcolgan is a busy friendly village with shops, post office, restaurants and pubs. All other amenities are available in nearby Clarinbridge and Oranmore. It is very convenient to Galway city with daily public transport connecting Kilcolgan to Galway, Limerick and Cork.
