A utility room, located off the kitchen comes complete with hand crafted cabinets, Belfast sink and downstairs WC. There is also a family room with solid pine floor finish and double doors leading to the gardens. The home office is the perfect addition for those that need to work remotely.\n\nThe first floor comprises of five bedrooms with two en-suites, walk in wardrobe and three large double bedrooms.
