    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Upon entering this lovely home, one senses great warmth and energy with superb views of both Sugar Loaf mountains and garden containing a variety of fruit trees and shrubs. The sound of the stream which cascades down along the side of the garden from the mountain, is so calming.\r\n\r\nThere are three double bedrooms, two of which are en-suite and a family bathroom. A further two rooms are in the converted attic. A large sitting room with a wood burning stove is a very comfortable spot in which to relax. The kitchen is the hub of the home with a pine ceiling, quarry tiles, a Rayburn stove and a large southern yellow pine table with built in seating which has been the source of many a soiree. \r\n\r\nSliding glass doors lead out to a patio that enjoys the afternoon sun. Beneath the kitchen there is a basement accessed from outside which houses the central heating boiler, washing machine, dryer and freezers. There is an outside lavatory and wash hand basin.\r\n\r\nWith The Glenview Hotel and Leisure Centre on the doorstep and many lovely walks and hikes, there is much to occupy those who like the outdoor lifestyle.\r\nThe house is within close proximity to the train and dart stations, the Beacon Hospital, Dundrum and Dublin Airport, also the City centre, not to mention golf clubs, riding stables, schools and churches.\r\n\r\nAll in all, an idyllic setting.\r\n\r\nAccommodation Includes:\r\nEntrance Hallway: 2.73m x 1.53mm with return 5.98m x 1.90m Double front doors, walk in hotpress, Carpeted. \r\n\r\nStairs: 2.18m x1.77m Carpeted leads to mezzanine 2.21 x 1.60m x 3.45m, Pitched pine panelling on ceiling, velux window. \r\n\r\nSitting Room: 7.54 x 3.65m Large bright sitting room with stove fireplace, sliding door to patio area, carpeted, beautiful views of the Sugar Loaf, double doors leading to kitchen.\r\n\r\nKitchen: 4.85m x 5.46m Very attractive light filled kitchen with island, Rayburn cooker, ceramic hob. Salle a Manger area has built in panelling area with custom made yellow southern pine table with built in seating, built in dresser, timber panelled ceiling, Yellow southern pine shelving, terracotta floor, sliding door to Patio area.\r\n\r\n\r\nMaster Bedroom: 2.81m x 3.57m built in wardrobes, venetian blind, carpeted. \r\n\r\nEn-Suite: 1.96m x 2.48m power shower, W.C, Bidet, WHB, vanity unity, venetian blind, obscure exterior glass, large heated towel rail. \r\n\r\nBedroom 1: 3.94m x 3.60m carpeted, venetian blind. \r\n\r\nBedroom 2: 3.0m x 3.60m carpeted, venetian blind, curtains.\r\n\r\nEn- Suite: 1.90m x 1.85m Large shower, rimless W.C, WHB, Heated radiator, vanity units, tiled floor. \r\n\r\nBedroom 4: 2.67m x 2.93m 2 velux windows, solid timber built in presses, timber panelling, carpeted. \r\n\r\nBedroom 5: 2.65m x 4.18m 2 velux windows, storage presses, recessed storage, timber panelling, carpeted\r\n\r\nMain Bathroom: 2.16m x 1.70m Bath with over head shower, W.C, WHB, velux window.\r\n\r\nBasement: 3.54m x 5.20m Large storage area plumbed with sink unit. \r\n\r\nOutside WC: 1.13m x 1.42m WHB, Terracotta tiles.\r\n\r\nServices\r\nOil Fired Central Heating\r\nSeptic Tank\r\nMains Water
