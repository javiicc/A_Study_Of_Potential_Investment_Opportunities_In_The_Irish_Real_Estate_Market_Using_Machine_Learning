    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    This is a stunning, two-storey period residence built c.1900 which had been occupied as the Glenties Parish Parochial House until recently. Situated in Glen Road, Glenties, a warm and friendly town in South-West Donegal. Located close to many amenities including numerous stunning Blue Flag beaches, an 18-hole golf course, Sliabh Liag seacliffs, and is a short distance from the popular Wild Atlantic Way route. \r\n\r\nA substantial and symmetrical building, the property features many original architectural designs including overhanging eaves with corbelled brackets, block plaster quoins, numerous leaded stained glass panels and windows plus an extensive range of ornate ceiling cornices internally. The property also contains three reception rooms, two kitchens and five spacious bedrooms. \r\n\r\nMeasuring 4000 square feet and located just off Glenties Main Street on a site measuring 0.46 acres, purchasers are given a rare opportunity to turn this property into an impressive family home or for use as a commercial venture - perhaps as a high quality guest house or restaurant.
