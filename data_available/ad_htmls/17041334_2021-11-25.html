Westmeath. \nThe property will be offered for sale in one or more lots as follows; Lot 1: Residence on c.1 Acre Lot 2: c.7 Acres Lot 3: c.1 Acre of Zoned lands Lot 4: Entire of Lot 1 & Lot 2. \n\nThis residential holding has excellent road frontage to the east and is bounded by the Riverstown river to the south and the residential estate Radharc An Mhuilinn to the north. The subject property is located in the highly sought after commuter village of Rathwire and is within close proximity to all social and essential services including schools, shops and sports facilities. There are other necessary services close by including footpaths, street lights, mains water, stormwater drainage and sewerage. \n\nLot 1 consists of traditional style farmhouse in need of repair on c.1 acre. The property is in need of total renovation however, would make an ideal residence due to its proximity to services and amenities. This two-story symmetrical farmhouse has retained much of its early character and form and is bounded by mature native trees, although in need of complete reclamation the subject property offers immense potential for the discerning purchaser. Please note: This property is currently inaccessible as the windows and doors are completely boarded. \n\nLot 3 consists of c.1 acre of lands Zoned Community, Educational and Institutional. The regeneration of lands to serve the community are considered under this zoning and would be ideally for facilities including the community centre, creches and childcare facilities, nursing homes, health/medical centres, graveyards, as well as sporting, recreational and cultural facilities.\n\nLocated in Rathwire village less than 5km to N4, 15km to Mullingar town centre and 8km to Kinnegad/M6.\n\nSors: N.
