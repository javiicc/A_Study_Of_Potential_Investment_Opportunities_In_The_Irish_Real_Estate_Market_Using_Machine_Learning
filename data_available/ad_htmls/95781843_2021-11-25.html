    Development Description

      Seamount Rise offers modern, stylish living in a breath-taking coastal setting. All homes at Seamount Rise combine modern design and high-quality finishes. Residents will benefit from a new 13-acre park with playground to be developed by Fingal County Council, adding to the abundance of scenic open spaces and parklands that Malahide already offers. Seamount Rise boasts great transport links with bus services to the airport and city centre and the added benefit of Malahide Train Station, just a 15-min walk away offering both DART and suburban commuter links. Seamount Rise offers homes for living, now and for decades to come. \r\n\r\nFor more information and to register your interest, visit www.seamountrise.com

      Location

      Seamount Rise is ideally located in the coastal town of Malahide and within walking distance to the village and DART station and just a 15-minute drive from Dublin airport. Malahide is home to numerous quality restaurants and pubs and an abundance of sporting clubs to cater for everyone at every stage of life.\r\n\r\nResidents at Seamount Rise will also benefit from living adjacent to a new 13-acre park, currently being developed by Fingal County Council with stunning views across the north Dublin coast.
