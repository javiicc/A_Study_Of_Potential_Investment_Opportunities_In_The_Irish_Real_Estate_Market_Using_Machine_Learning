    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Stanley Estate Agents are delighted to present 21 Burton House to the market.\r\n\r\nThis attractive two bedroom apartment is well presented by the current owners and comes with a designated parking space, located in this central development in the IFSC.\r\n\r\nThe apartment is situated on the 6th floor of this modern development in the heart of the vibrant Docklands with wonderful panoramic views over the IFSC. Located off Mayor Square, the apartment is situated in one of the most sought-after blocks in the popular Custom House Square development. \r\n\r\nThe location benefits from all social and essential amenities including many delightful establishments such as Marks & Spencer, Milano, Fresh, CHQ Building, Sprout & Co, The Harbourmaster & The Spencer Hotel. IFSC is served by an extensive public transport network which includes the Docklands Station for DART and inter-city trains, Dublin Bus services and Mayor Square LUAS stop meters from the building.\r\n\r\nApt. 21 is an attractive two bedroom apartment flooded with natural sunlight through the living room and both double bedrooms. This sizeable apartment further benefits from wonderful south facing views. \r\n\r\nThe apartment briefly comprises of - entrance hall with access to a storage closet and boiler room, living room with south facing balcony, kitchen, master bedroom with en-suite, one double bedroom with fitted wardrobes and a family bathroom. \r\n\r\nThe well-proportioned accommodation and overall location of this apartment make it the ideal choice for a trendy city home or an excellent residential investment.\r\n\r\nEarly viewing is essential and appointment only.
