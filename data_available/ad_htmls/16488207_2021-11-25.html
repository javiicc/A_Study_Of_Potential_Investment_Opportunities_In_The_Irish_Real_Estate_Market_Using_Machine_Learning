    Hornibrook & Associates are delighted to offer this lovely 2-bedroom semi-detached family home, with potential for extension, set in the fabulous location of Fairhill to the sales market.\n\nWithin walking distance to Cork City and on a bus route this property is ideal if location is important to you. It is close to all road links and would be perfect for someone working in either Apple or Blackpool Retail Park as well as Cork city.\n\nThe property commands fabulous views over Cork City. It is adjacent to a post office and with shops and a selection of primary and secondary schools within minutes walk, the property would be perfect for a family or an astute investor.\n\n \nACCOMMODATION comprises Entrance Hall, Sitting Room, Kitchen, 2 bedrooms, family bathroom and attic space set out as 2 separate storage rooms.\n\nACCOMMODATION:\nENTRANCE HALL:\n(11' 4" x 3' 10") \n\nThe warm parquet floored entrance hall welcomes you to this lovely property. \nThere is a radiator, centre light fitting and an opening to the floored attic with a stira stairs.\n\n\nSITTING ROOM: \n(12' x 10' 8")\n \nThis bright, spacious sitting room to the rear of the property boasts ornate French Doors to the rear garden. There is an abundance of natural light from the French doors to this room. There is a stylish stone fireplace and lovely warm solid wood floor. The room has a centre light fitting and radiator.\n\n\nKITCHEN:\n(10' 1 x 7')\n \nThis kitchen has solid wood fitted units with a modern highly polished worktop. This room leads to the patio at the rear of the house.\n\nMASTER BEDROOM:\n(13' 5"x 8')\n \nThis double bedroom is bright and spacious and boasts a large built in wardrobe and units so there is plenty of storage. It has a carpeted floor, centre light fitting, radiator and blind.\n \nBEDROOM 2:\n(9' x7'5")\n\nThis bright single room facing the front of the property overlooks the front garden. It has a carpeted floor, blind, radiator, centre light fitting and large mirrored built in wardrobe for storage.\n\n\nFAMILY BATHROOM:\n\nThis good-sized fully tiled bathroom has a bath with shower, sink and WC. \n\nATTIC:\n(18' x 17')\n\nThe floored attic is laid out as 2 bedrooms but can be used as a large storage area. It is accessed from a Stira stairs. \n\n\nEXTERIOR OF PROPERTY:\n\nThe front of the property is laid to lawn with a gated driveway for off-street parking.\n\nThe rear of the property has a lovely private garden which is laid to lawn and features a concrete patio. It is perfect for children and animals. There is a large shed here also.
