    No. 69 Maryville Road is a very fine mid-terrace family home which has been well cared for over the years. Upon entering the home a bright hallway leads to a large living room to the left. A spacious open plan kitchen/ dining room lies to the rear of the home and provides access to the rear garden. Upstairs there are three bedrooms (two double and a generous single), a family bathroom and separate wc.\nThe location of this charming home is ideal being only 200 meters away from St. Anne s Park and close to a number of amenities including Bull Island. Every conceivable sporting club like golf, tennis, football, rugby, GAA and yacht are all close by. Raheny village is a 3 minute drive while Killester and Clontarf are both only a 6 minute drive away. You will be spoilt for choice with schools, restaurants and cafes in the area.
