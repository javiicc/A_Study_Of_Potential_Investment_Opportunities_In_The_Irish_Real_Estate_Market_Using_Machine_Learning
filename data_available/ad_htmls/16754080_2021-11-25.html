    Smith and Butler Estates are delighted to present this superb two bedroom apartment which overlooks the River Liffey to the market. This stylish apartment features a welcoming entrance hallway. To the right of the hall are two bedrooms with fitted wardrobes and a fully tiled bathroom. There is a modern fully fitted kitchen with ample counter & cupboard space. The bright open plan living & dining area boasts stunning views of the River Liffey. Alexandra Quay is a secure gated development in a prime location. There are a host of amenities on your doorstep including bars, shops, cafes and restaurants as well the Bord Gais Theatre & the Aviva Stadium. The property is within walking distance of many green parks and Sandymount Strand. Furthermore, Google, Facebook, Amazon & LinkedIn all have their Irish headquarters in this district. The apartment is also fantastically serviced by DART, LUAS & Dublin Bus.
