    Nicely set back from Greenfield Road and offering a high degree of privacy can be found The Moorings. This fine home is a generous, detached double fronted five-bedroom family home and is located adjacent to Sutton Cross and the seafront. \n\nOriginally built c.1930 s it has been extensively extended and renovated over the years and now presents in walk in condition with a generous and flexible layout spanning to 196 sq.m / 2,110 sq.ft approx. Throughout, The Moorings has been decorated in neutral tones which gives a great sense of light and space, and allows any new owner to simply move in and start living.\n\nIn brief the accommodation is as follows; a welcoming entrance hall, off of which can be found the large interconnecting living and dining rooms. To the rear is the open plan kitchen / breakfast room with access to the rear garden. From here access to the utility room and shower room can be found which leads around to the study and lounge with bay window to the front. Upstairs there are five bedrooms (master en suite) and bathroom. Bedroom four benefits from sea glimpses to the front. \n\nThe location is second to none being on the doorstep of this idyllic location to include schools, shops, cafes, bars, restaurants, golf-courses, sailing clubs, football, rugby & GAA clubs, coastal harbour and cliff walks, DART station and bus routes to & from Dublin City Centre.
