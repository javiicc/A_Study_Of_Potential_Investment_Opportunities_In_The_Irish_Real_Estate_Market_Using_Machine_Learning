    Ashfield House is a beautifully presented large 4 bedroom family home in an exclusive location close to the centre of Athboy town. The property spans to approximately 246sqm of well-apportioned accommodation with a versatile floor plan.\r\n\r\nThe house is presented to the market in good condition and has been very well maintained by its current owner. The ground floor consists of a large living room with a bay window to the front, a second living/family room, guest WC which has disability access, utility room, kitchen and a separate sun room. \r\n\r\nOn the first floor there are four double bedrooms, with two ensuite, main bathroom and a hotpress. The driveway to the front provides off street parking and to the rear there is a large enclosed garden with side entrances on both sides of the house. The attic has been converted and is suitable for immediate use as a home office and extends to approximately 56sqm.\r\n\r\nAshfield house is ideally located on the Trim Road out of Athboy and it occupies a prominent position within immediate proximity to the Fair Green. Athboy Community and primary schools are just a short walk away. The town of Athboy is an hours drive to Dublin City Centre while there is also public transport providing a daily bus service which leaves Athboy multiple times per day. The active Gaeltacht area of Rathcairn is only 2 miles from Athboy with excellent schools and a Community Centre\r\n\r\nProperties like Ashfield House seldom come to the market, early viewing is recommended to avoid disappointment.
