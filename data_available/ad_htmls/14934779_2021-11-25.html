    Hunters Estate Agent is delighted to present to the market 7 Glenair Manor, a two storey five bedroom detached property finished to the highest of standards nestled in the heart of the picturesque village of Delgany. This fine family home extends to 465.2sq.m/5,007sq.ft, sitting on circa 0.78 acre site in a quiet exclusive enclave of just 9 detached homes. \r\n\r\nUpon entering the property, one is greeted by a welcoming bright and spacious hallway. From here all the principle rooms of the property are accessed. The ceilings are higher than the average throughout contributing to the feeling of light and space. To the left there is a guest w.c, a large study and a reception room complete with games rooms and feature fitted bar offering the perfect space for family meals and entertaining. The very large kitchen/breakfast room is situated to the right of the entrance hall and is complete with a Siematic fitted kitchen flanked by French doors to the patio area. There is a large island in the centre complete with seating and a breakfast bar and feature gas fired Aga. The kitchen is equipped with the finest appliances to include a built-in oven and microwave, six ring gas hob, integrated dishwasher, Samsung American style fridge freezer. Nestled off the breakfast area is a cosy sun room providing an idyllic space to unwind beside the wood burning stove. Off the kitchen is a large utility room providing ample storage that houses the dryer and separate washing machine. There is also plenty of worktop space. The ground floor is further completed by a boot room and w.c. \r\n\r\nA majestic oak staircase leads to the first floor with a vaulted double height ceiling allowing an abundance of natural light. There are five large double bedrooms complete with built-in wardrobes and quality carpets throughout. The extra large master bedroom has the added benefit of a dressing room. Off the master bedroom there is an ensuite which has a bath and a separate power shower. All the bedrooms are complimented by ensuites, one of which is a Jack and Jill style bathroom shared between two of the bedrooms. To complete the accommodation there is also a main family bathroom. \r\n\r\nSpoilt for choice for outdoor pursuits and indoor pursuits, the locality offers a wide variety of sports and leisure facilities including Wicklow County Council swimming pool and gym, Greystones Rugby Club, GAA Club and Golf Club. There is also an excellent choice of schools in the area as well as shops, cafes and restaurants. The property is serviced by superb transport links including the N11 and Greystones DART station, while the Aircoach provides a direct link to Dublin airport. Convenient local buses include the 184 and 84 to Dublin city centre. Glenair Manor is located just off Priory Road in Delgany village. Greystones town and harbour marina are approximately 0.5km from Delgany village and 2km from Greystones. Here, one can avail of the many amenities which each location has to offer including a number of award winning restaurants, retail outlets, schools and recreational amenities. There are numerous golf clubs to choose from including Delgany, Greystones, Powerscourt and the world famous Druids Glen.\r\n\r\nViewing is highly recommended.\r\n\r\nSPECIAL FEATURES\r\nDetached
