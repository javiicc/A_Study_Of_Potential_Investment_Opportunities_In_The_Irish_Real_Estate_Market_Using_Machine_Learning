    DNG are delighted to present 'Fairfield Villa', this magnificent double-fronted home combines Edwardian elegance with 21st century craftsmanship and a floor space of approx. 3391 sq. ft/ 315 sq. m, laid out over two levels. 'Fairfield Villa' is located in a quiet cul de sac off one of Rathgar's premier roads, Highfield Road.\n\nUpon entering, it is quite apparent that the standard of finish and the quality of this family home with the grand main door opening onto the reception hallway which comes with an original wooden floor, integrate ceiling coving, centre rose and a decorative arch. The hall is flanked by two impressive receptions rooms, with high ceilings, marble and slate fireplaces, original flooring and ceiling cornices. For entertaining guests, there is a generous dining room with a West facing bay window, marble fireplace, centre rose and ceiling coving. There is a guest wc, a utility room, an inner hallway which leads to a bespoke kitchen which is fitted with a tiled floor and granite worktops. This opens through to the open plan breakfast room with a vaulted ceiling and direct access to the garden. Upstairs there is a generous and light filled landing with five double bedrooms along with three ensuites and a family bathroom. \n\n'Fairfield Villa' enjoys a private gravelled driveway with off street parking for two cars to the front. There is a railed side garden and a 46 ft. (L) walled (in original brick) rear garden offering complete privacy. As it was built in c. 1904, this substantial family home has generous bay windows, with the front of the house enjoying uninterrupted South facing views over the mature and well-maintained park to the front.
