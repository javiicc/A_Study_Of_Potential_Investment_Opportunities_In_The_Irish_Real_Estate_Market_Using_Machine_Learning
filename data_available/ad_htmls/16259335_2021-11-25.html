    *** Large 4 Bedroom 2 Reception Room End Terrace Home ***\r\n \r\nProperty Description:\r\nDavid Scally of David Ross Estate Agents your local property experts are delighted and proud to present No. 50 Limekiln Green to the market for sale. Not often does a property of this specification come to the market.\r\n\r\nLocated on Limekiln Green, with nearby Tymon Park this four / five bedroom, end of terrace property is in fantastic condition having been renovated and extended. The internal accommodation is laid out over two levels boasting well-appointed rooms on each. On ground floor level the feeling of space and style is undeniable, while the first floor offers four bedrooms (two double, one single) and a family bathroom. There is also the master bedroom with large fully tiled en suite as well as a cosy balcony to enjoy a coffee in the morning sunshine. Over both levels a modern and contemporary style is consistent throughout and clear to see. With no stone left unturned to make this a wonderful family home.\r\n\r\nIt is further enhanced by a large, converted garage to the front which is fully wired and could be used as a utility room, playroom, or simply just for extra storage.\r\n\r\nThe quality continues out into the garden mature plants and lawn area. The large shed is where you find the hot tub, the perfect sanctuary to unwind after a long day. \r\n\r\nThe location of number 50 is second to none enjoying an enviable location in a very mature neighbourhood. Convenient to local shopping facilities, bus routes, schools, sports facilities etc. The property is close to Terenure and Templeogue villages, with easy access to the city centre and the M50 motorway.\r\n\r\nThis property is truly special and will suit discerning families looking for a stylish and contemporary home.\r\n\r\nViewing is highly recommended.
