    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    Located at Coolmore, Rossnowlagh this well presented five bedroom property is ideally located to avail of all that Rossnowlagh has to offer. Rossnowlagh has long been regarded as the jewel of the northwest and enjoys a balanced blend of permanent residences with holiday properties. Conveniently located close to shop, post office and the Franciscan Friary, the beach is a short stroll away. \r\nAccommodation extends to circa 2000 sq ft and provides entrance hall, living room, kitchen/dining room, utility room, three bedrooms (one ensuite) and family bathroom on ground floor level with a further two large bedrooms (both ensuite) on the upper floor. Externally, the enclosed cobble paved rear patio is complimented with a detached garage. Additional cobble paved parking area is provided to the front of the house with additional gardens to the front.\r\nThis fine property will have broad appeal and may readily suit as a permanent residence or a holiday retreat. The property may also appeal as a rental property as the area enjoys significant demand for short term holiday properties. \r\n\r\nBER Pending\r\n\r\nFor further details or to register your interest please contact Sole Agents DKP Auctioneers.
