Kilkenny.\r\nThe house has been carefully upgraded, extended and presented, for the next owner to enjoy and appreciate the property as it has been in the past.\r\nIt's a rare opportunity that a property of this style and calibre comes to the market, with a site that runs to the back of the house and measures 1 acre approx.\r\nThe original cottage no way resembles the property now. An added rear extension, incorporating all 3 bedrooms runs to the back of the original structure, with the bathroom centrally located to all of the 3 bedrooms.\r\nThe old cottage has been transformed into an open living space with a separate kitchen.\r\nGoodwins Gardens is reached by taking a right turn in Kells Village, coming from Kilkenny and just drive 2 miles out, with this property located on the left. There is a FGP For Sale Sign on the property. The location is recognised as one of the premium locations to reside, in Kilkenny, with Kells Village and its Augustinian Priory being one of the most visited tourist destinations in the County.\r\nThe village is a thriving spot with strong community involvement in tourism, sport and business. There's an award winning National School and Kilkenny City Centre is just 12 minutes' drive away.\r\nKells Village overlooks the Kings River on an elevated site, there's the local shop, pub and caf\xc3\xa9 within a short walk of each other.\r\nA low stone wall with a quirky iron railing and gates greet you as you enter the property. The front garden has a sunny aspect and although unlikely to be used as the main entrance, there's a welcome look and picture perfect appearance to it.\r\nParking for the house is to the rear and there's any amount of it!! The one acre site that's with the house runs the whole way down and is bounded by a natural hedge on the three sides. From the rear the added extension is clearly visible, with a total contrast in finish.\r\nStepping inside the cottage feels good, to the right is the old and left are the 3 bedrooms. The living room area has all the features you'd expect. The vaulted ceilings show well and increase the feeling of space. The windows have been upgraded and enlarged from original and direct access to the front is definitely a facility you'd use. The large fireplace has a solid wood burning stove fitted and generates massive heat as well as adding to the ambience of the room. The timber floors are perfect for use and aesthetically finish the room.\r\nThe kitchen area has a lovely gallery style feel to it. It's ideal for use with an ideal layout to it. There's no money to be spent as the units and appliances are in perfect condition. Again, the space is aesthetically pleasing and feels just right. The shower room has only recently been upgraded and is completed to a high specification finish.\r\nThe central access hall is a nice bright space and gives easy use to the 3 double bedrooms off it. The layout to the rooms are ideal but it's the design and use of space that really shows well. Each of the rooms are double and each offer a pristine finish to them. \r\nThe overall on Stone Cottage, Goodwins Gardens is excellent. The large site offers huge potential and the house itself in turnkey. There are some storage units and dog kennels on site, and these can be of use or removed.\r\nViewing is the only way to appreciate the property, so Ring Fran.
