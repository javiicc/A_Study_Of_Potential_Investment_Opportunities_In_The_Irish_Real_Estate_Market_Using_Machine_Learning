    C.11.5 acres of land offering potential for a small number of sites - just 5 minutes from the town of Glenties\r\n\r\nA small holding in this scenic rural area, partially populated with some dwellings. One part in particular would suit 2 or 3 residential sites. Alternatively the land which has been unused in recent years could be cultivated and reused as grazing land. Reasonably priced for a quick sale. Early viewing strongly recommended.\r\n\r\n\r\nComing into the town of Glenties from the Fintown direction, continue through the town proceeding in the direction of Killybegs on the N56, then turning left after c. 2 miles sign posted to Frosses. After approximately half a mile turn left into the L7983 continuing for approximately quarter of a mile to a bungalow on the left with stone feature on the front and a circular window by the front door. After 3 or 4 hundred metres our sign on the left denotes the beginning of the land and the boundaries can be confirmed from our site map.
