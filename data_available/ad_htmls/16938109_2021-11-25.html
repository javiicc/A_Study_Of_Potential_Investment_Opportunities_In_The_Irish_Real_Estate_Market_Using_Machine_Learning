    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Situated in a green oasis of mature trees and charming gardens is a most splendid residence. Robins Hill offers quite seclusion and privacy within 2 miles of Clifden town in the highly desirable area of Ardagh. The house is perched on top of a hill and enjoys magnificent countryside views. It is charming, interesting and very comfortable. One enters via a front porch and into a boot room/ utility room. Off this is a charming country kitchen which leads into an impressive double height dining and living room. A solid fuel stove in a stone chimney breast is a focal point of the room. The room and indeed the whole house is flooded with natural light. There is a mezzanine level where a clever office/ den is situated. The master bedroom with ensuite is also accessed via this mezzanine.\nDownstairs on the ground floor, there is a further 3 bedrooms or two bedrooms and an office subject to the owners\xe2\x82\xacTMrequirements. There is also a lovely family bathroom and a back glass porch leading into the garden beyond. The outdoors come indoors thanks to large picture window throughout.\nThe site size is large being circa 1.6 acres (0.65 hectares). Included on the plot is a fine garage, shed, small orchard and a sweet patio accessed by a winding stone path. Internet access is good and the Marconi walk and landing site of the Alcock and Brown first Atlantic flight is within walking distance of here.
