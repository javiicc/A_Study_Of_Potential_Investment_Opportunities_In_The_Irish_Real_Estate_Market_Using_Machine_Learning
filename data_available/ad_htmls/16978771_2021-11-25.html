    **Murphy Mullan** Present this lovely 3 bed end of terrace property located in this exclusive development at Carrickmines Manor. This property has a private patio area combined with its access to the communal gardens in the development this home is ideal for any discerning purchaser looking for the convenience of modern living with all the scope and outdoor space one could ask for. \r\n\r\nThis spacious and well laid property briefly comprises; entrance hall, living room, Kitchen, Guest wc, playroom and 3 bedrooms (master ensuite) and a main bathroom. Outside are front and rear garden areas.\r\nCarrickmines Manor is a stylish and sought after location, its close to all the amenities offered by the nearby villages of Foxrock and Cabinteely, between them offering an abundance of social, leisure and essential amenities, to include local shopping, restaurants and coffee shops, with a wider variety of shopping available at Carrickmines Retail Park just a stones throw away. There's lots of of sporting facilities in the vicinity including Carrickmines Lawn Tennis & Croquet Club as well as excellent transport links including the M50, N11 and the green line Luas stop at Carrickmines.
