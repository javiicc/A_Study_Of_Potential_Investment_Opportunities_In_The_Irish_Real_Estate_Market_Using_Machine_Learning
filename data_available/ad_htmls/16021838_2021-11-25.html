    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Mullery Auctioneers are delighted to present to the market for sale 'Moycullen House'. \r\n\r\nThis substantial residence is set in about 7.35 acres of mature grounds and was built circa 1890 by Lord Campbell. Moycullen House is a charming home built in the Arts & Crafts Style and set in large grounds in the hills surrounding Moycullen Village with wonderful views to Lough Corrib.\r\n\r\nApproached by a sweeping avenue, Moycullen House boasts a secluded and extremely tranquil location surrounded by mature trees and shrubbery. The house sits on an elevated position with stunning views to Lough Corrib and the surrounding countryside.\r\n\r\nThe house offers generously proportioned accommodation with many original features. The accommodation comprises an entrance hallway, living room, dining room, family room, kitchen/breakfast room, second kitchen, utility room/ laundry room, study and toilets on the ground floor. Upstairs there are five bedrooms, four of which are double and a master en-suite. There are two further family bathrooms. The attic is accessed by a small set of stairs and provides ample storage space.\r\n\r\nThe grounds are a particular feature of Moycullen House. An abundance of mature trees and shrubbery surround the house and several sun traps take advantage of the southerly rear orientation. There is a garage and conservatory situated adjacent to the house.\r\n\r\nMoycullen House enjoys a stunning location overlooking Lough Corrib. It is within minutes' drive of the bustling village of Moycullen which offers all necessary services and amenities including shops, supermarket, pubs, restaurants, school and Church. Galway city is within easy reach and will provide for all needs. Both Shannon and Dublin airports are easily accessible and Dublin City is about 2.5 hours away via the M6 Motorway.\r\n\r\nThe sale of Moycullen House represents a unique opportunity to acquire a charming country residence offering generous accommodation and large grounds in a truly wonderful setting yet within minutes of all necessary amenities and a short drive from Galway City and Lough Corrib.\r\n\r\nAccommodation\r\nEntrance Hall\r\n\r\nLiving Room\r\n\r\nDining Room\r\n\r\nFamily Room\r\n\r\nStudy\r\n\r\nWC x2\r\n\r\nKitchen / Breakfast Room\r\n\r\nStore Room\r\n\r\nKitchen\r\n\r\nUtility Room / Laundry\r\n\r\nFirst Floor\r\n\r\nLanding\r\n\r\nMaster Bedroom\r\n\r\nEn-Suite\r\n\r\nBedroom 2\r\n\r\nBedroom 3\r\n\r\nBedroom 4\r\n\r\nBedroom 5\r\n\r\nBathroom 1\r\n\r\nBathroom 2\r\n\r\nAttic\r\n\r\nStorage\r\n\r\nPlease Note:\r\nMessrs.
