    DNG Martin OConnor Estate Agents are delighted to offer for sale this unique property in the heart of Connemara with exceptional views across the open bog lands to the Maam Turk and the Twelve Pins mountain range.\r\n\r\nThe property located on an elevated site of c.0.88 Ha (2.17 Acres), enjoys a great sense of privacy due to its elevated position, overlooking the scenic bog road (Rosmuc to Cashel), popular with many artists as well as the rolling countryside views beyond. The property is partially completed and will appeal to many prospective buyers who will have the opportunity to finish it to their own personal taste. It is an excellent opportunity for those with no local links to sites in the area, to acquire a splendid property in the heart of Connemara.\r\n\r\nThis four bedroom property has the potential to become an outstanding family home/ holiday property offering well proportioned accommodation extending to 2,066 sq.ft. On the first floor, there is one further bedroom, complete with en-suite shower room, and walk-in wardrobe, as well as a storage area to the right of the landing.\r\n\r\nInternally the property is dry lined, while the majority of the internal plastering, first fixed wiring & plumbing has been completed.\r\n\r\nExternally, the ancestral cottage of the vendors is located to the rear of the property.
