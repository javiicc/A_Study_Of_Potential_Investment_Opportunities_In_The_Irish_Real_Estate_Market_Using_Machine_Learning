    DNG are proud to present to the market a wonderful property located just off the Kiltipper Road on a 0.3 acre site. Nestled on a private secluded site this detached bungalow oozes character and will be sure to stir a lot of interest among buyers. \n\nThe bright and spacious accommodation briefly comprises of an entrance hallway, living room, dining room, kitchen, sunroom, three spacious bedrooms and a main family bathroom. Outside there is a large block built shed c.565 sqft with electric roller door which is suitable for a multiple of uses . A second outbuilding to the front of the property measures c.265 sqft and could be easily converted to home office or gym. \n\nIt's rare that a property like Tilia comes to the market which offers so much potential. The landscaped gardens are well maintained with further privacy being provided with mature trees and shrubbery. The location is ideal as Tilia enjoys close proximity to The Square Shopping Centre, Tallaght Hospital, Tallaght IT, Luas, Dublin bus services and a selection of schools, pubs and restaurants.
