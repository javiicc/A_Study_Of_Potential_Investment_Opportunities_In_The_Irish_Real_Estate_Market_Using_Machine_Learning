    For sale in one or more lots:\nAnerley Lodge, 81 Wolfe Tone Street - 265,000\nThe Mews at Anerley Lodge - Price on application. Further information available upon request.\n\nAnerley Lodge, 81 Wolfe Tone Street, is an immaculate 3 bed end of terrace home which will appeal to many looking to live within the city limits. Wolfe Tone Street is stepped in history and character with many of the properties on the street built in the early 1900 s. Anerley Lodge comes to the market in excellent condition inside and out having been carefully maintained and modernised by the current owner. The property will serve to be a great home for any first time buyer or downsizer looking for a spacious home within walking distance to all Limerick city has to offer. Investors will be drawn at the opportunity of acquiring a potentially lucrative investment property previously rented for 1,750 per month. 81 Wolfe Tone Street is a short walk to Mary Immaculate College and would prove to be great property for those studying or working in the college. Anerley Lodge Mews - 81 A Wolfe Tone Street on a separate folio is also offered for sale in one or multiple lots with 81 Wolfe Tone Street. 81 A Wolfe Tone Street comprises off a partially constructed studio to the rear of 81 Wolfe Tone Street. Work required to restore this pre 63 store.\n\nConnecting O Connell Avenue with Lord Edward St, Wolfe Tone St offers a superb residential setting just outside the heart of the city-centre. Within walking distance of a wealth of amenities, the area offers immediate transport links to the wider Limerick region, Crescent Shopping Centre and University Hospital Limerick. A handful of Limericks most renowned educational institutions are set within minutes by car or on foot, namely Mary Immaculate College, The Model School, St Clements College and Laurel Hill Secondary School. Limerick Art Gallery and Peoples Park are just short 5 minutes walk, a lovely place to enjoy a coffee from one of the many local cafes in the area. Limerick (Colbert) station is a short 5 minute walk connecting you to any of Irelands train stations.\n\nInternally, 81 Wolfe Tone Street extends across two floors with spacious accommodation on each floor. The ground floor contains a spacious living room and open plan kitchen/diner at the rear of the house. There is a shower room located off the kitchen with WHB and electric shower. The first floor contains three spacious double bedrooms with velux windows allowing plenty of natural light into the first floor. The bathroom is positioned off the landing and comes complete with walk-in electric shower and full WHB facilities. \nThis property comes to the market in turn key condition and ready for immediate occupation. To appreciate the quality of location and full potential of this property, viewing is highly recommended.\n\nACCOMMODATION:Ent Hall, Living Room, Kitchen/Dining Room, 3 Double Bedrooms, 2 Bathrooms\n\nSERVICES:GFCH (Boiler upgraded recently), Double Glazed Windows, Private Rear Garden, Mains Water, On-Street Parking, 117 sqm approx.
