    Keane Mahony Smith are delighted to present this wonderful 3 bed semi-detached residence in an ever popular city location. No 71 Hazel Park is superbly located in an established development enjoying easy access to schools in Bushypark and Galway city, NUIG, UHG and Dangan Sportsground.\r\n\r\nNo 71 is a stylish chic property which presents in excellent order throughout. The current owners have maintained and upgraded the property throughout the years. There is a newly renovated bathroom, fully tiled with new sanitary ware and a new electric shower. The living room has the benefit of a solid fuel insert stove and the front and rear doors have also been upgraded. No 71 has the benefit of a downstairs room which can be used for a variety of uses; home office, games room, second tv room or small bedroom. \r\n\r\nThe accommodation of c. 935 sq. ft./ 86.9 sq. m. comprises; entrance porch, downstairs office/ playroom, cosy living room with solid fuel stove, open plan kitchen/ dining room and a rear hallway with storage on the ground floor. On the first floor, there are two double bedrooms, one single bedroom and the newly renovated family bathroom.\r\n\r\nOutside, the front garden is part tarmacadam finish and part grassed area. The property is ideally positioned at the end of the row and there is a large green space to the side of no 71. To the rear, there is a private garden space with side access, a block built storage shed/ boiler house and a patio area.\r\n\r\nDue to the location and condition of the property, it will appeal to owner occupiers seeking a mature city location close to facilities and investors given the proximity to UHG and NUIG.\r\n\r\nViewing is a must and can be arranged through Keane Mahony Smith offices.\r\n\r\nMeasurements\r\nGround Floor\r\nEntrance Porch \t1.39m x 1.10m - decorative tiling\r\nOffice/ Games room 2.25m x 4.54m - laminate flooring, multi functional space\r\nLiving room 4.98m x 3.24m - laminate flooring, solid fuel stove\r\nRear Hallway 2.02m x 3.23m - tiled flooring, under stair storage, access to rear garden\r\nKitchen/ Dining 2.24m x 5.05m - tiled floor, built in seating area in dining room.\r\nFirst Floor\r\nLanding 2.93m x 1.40m - carpet floor\r\nBedroom 1 2.36m x 2.83m - carpet flooring\r\nBedroom 2 2.35m x 4.07m - carpet flooring\r\nBedroom 3 3.00m x 3.14m - carpet flooring/ built in wardrobes\r\nBathroom 1.90m x 1.64m - fully tiled bathroom, newly renovated, electric shower
