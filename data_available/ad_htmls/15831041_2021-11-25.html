    Located within walking distance of the city centre No 4 St Anthonys Villas comes to the market offering a host of accommodation inside and out. Situated with Gardiners Hill in its backyard and St. Lukes within a short stroll this home has it all. The property abounds with the features expected from a home of this era with tremendous floor to ceiling height. Fitted with new double glazed PVC windows the current owners were mindful to keep the character of the property with the installation of sash windows to the front. It gives this home instant kerb side appeal. Painted in a sky-blue colour tone and topped with wrought iron railing and wrought iron pedestrian gate coupled with white washed wall capping this home needs to be seen to be appreciated. The property boasts external gardens front and rear and bight spacious rooms inside. All bedrooms are located at first floor level and all are double in size. The reception accommodation is home to a cosy lounge to the front, a spacious and bright dining room to the rear with galley kitchen off.\r\n\r\nThe property extends to approx. 920 sq. ft. The home has undergone continuous upgrades down through the years in keeping with modern times and gives a convenience sought by many purchasers. Public transport links abound on the door step. There is a host of shopping facilities all close by and schools both primary and second level are within walking distance. Cork city centre offers a host of social and recreational activities as well as retail and leisure.\r\n\r\n\r\nEntrance Hall 2.40m x 0.70m (7.87ft x 2.30ft)\r\nAccessed via PVC black front door complete with glazed insets and triple lock system.\r\nThe entrance hall is finished with old style tiling with the use of tones of terracotta, cream and black arranged in such a pattern as to create the effect that they were always there. Pendant carriage light. \r\n\r\n\r\nKitchen 3.35m x 2.29m (10.99ft x 7.51ft)\r\nThe kitchen of this home is galley in style with floor level units located on both sides with a finish in true red and white cork colours on the units. This room is both appealing and functional. Fitted cooker, hob and single drainer sink unit, plumbed for dishwasher. Big picture window looking out onto the rear. Tiled over work top surface and quarry style tiled floor. Door to rear garden.\r\n\r\nDining Room 3.80m x 3.13m (12.47ft x 10.27ft)\r\nA bright and airy light filled room located to the rear of the property with stained hardwood floor. An extra long window looks out onto the rear yard and garden beyond. Access to understairs storage.\r\n\r\nLounge 3.52m x 3.09m (11.55ft x 10.14ft)\r\nA lovely room located to the front of the property with sash window old in style and character but modern in design. This room is exceptionally bright. Original exposed wide beam floor stripped and stained. A cast iron fireplace sits at the centre creating a focal point to this room.\r\n\r\nBathroom 2.28m x 1.88m (7.48ft x 6.17ft)\r\nLocated via a lobby off the kitchen this shower room is fitted with three-piece white suite to include shower with electric shower, pedestal sink and wc. Fully tiled walls in neutral tones combine with an olde world terracotta tiled floor.\r\nThe lobby acts as a utility space plumbed and wired for washing machine with fitted shelving. \r\n\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 4.50m x 3.10m (14.76ft x 10.17ft)\r\nA wonderfully bright main bedroom with two sash windows looking out over the front of the property. Finished in neutral tones with carpet fitted.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 3.00m x 2.90m (9.84ft x 9.51ft)\r\nAnother fine double bedroom located to the rear and looking over the courtyard part of the rear garden. Finished with carpet.\r\n\r\nBedroom 3 3.90m x 2.20m (12.80ft x 7.22ft)\r\nDouble in size located to the rear of the property with stained wood floor. Picture window looking on to the rear lawn. \r\n\r\nLanding 4.90m x 1.40m (16.08ft x 4.59ft)\r\nThe stairs and landing form their own feature within this home. Exceptionally bright with the use of a Velux window at first floor level. Recently carpeted the twists and turns give interest as you transcend from one level to the next. Landing with double hot press fitted and fully shelved. Additional open shelved storage is available here.\r\nThe front of this property is accessed via pedestrian wrought iron gate over a pathway through an enclosed landscaped space. To the rear there is a small courtyard patio adjoining the property which leads to a lovely lawned and enclosed rear garden.
