    McGovern Estates is delighted to showcase 44 Hillside. This 4 bedroom detached family home is set on a beautifully private corner site in the highly sought after location of Hillside. It is presented in good condition throughout. The nature of the site lends itself to development potential subject to planning permission. Located on the edge of the town, the main street is just a short stroll where one can avail of an array of wonderful restaurants, shops, amenities and easy access to local Bus stops and the Dart Station. Internal viewing of this property is highly recommended.\r\n\r\nThis well presented property extends to 139m2, (1,496 sq.ft.) and is bright and spacious throughout. The accommodation briefly comprises a kitchen, dining room, living room, office / playroom, downstairs WC, 4 bedrooms with en-suite off the master bedroom and family bathroom. The property benefits from a private low maintenance rear garden which can be accessed via side passage and features a timber framed garden shed. To the front there is lawn area and off street parking available.\r\n\r\nGreystones town is within easy reach and all your needs will be catered for with a wonderful selection of bistros, boutiques, restaurants and shops, the village library, churches and a large number of highly regarded schools. Tesco & Donnybrook\r\n\r\nFair are within walking distance. A host of sport and leisure clubs abound in the area including sailing, rowing, football, rugby, tennis and GAA. The Shoreline Leisure Centre and swimming pool is on the doorstep, as are a great choice of renowned golf clubs, including Greystones, Powerscourt and Druids Glen.\r\n\r\nGreystones is situated in a stunning location between the coast and the Wicklow mountains, giving easy access to the local beaches, countryside and nearby villages of Delgany and Enniskerry. \r\nThe commuter will be delighted at the easy access to local bus stops and the DART station both within walking distance. The N11/M50 is also within easy reach making for an easy commute to the city, airport and beyond.\r\n\r\nACCOMMODATION \r\nPorch \r\nTiled floor.\r\n\r\nEntrance Hallway\r\nCarpeted, carpeted stairs to landing.\r\n\r\nLiving Room \r\nCarpeted, feature fire place with solid wood mantle, tile surround & black slate hearth inset, gas fire, feature bay window, double doors leading to dining area, under stairs storage. \r\n\r\nKitchen Room \r\nFully fitted kitchen with wall based & floor based storage cupboards, significant countertop top space with inset double stainless steel sink unit.\r\n\r\nDining Room \r\nCarpeted, door leading to kitchen area.\r\n\r\nOffice \r\nCarpeted, Tv point. \r\n\r\nDownstairs WC\r\nCarpeted, wash hand basin, WC.\r\n\r\nLanding \r\nCarpeted.\r\n\r\nBedroom 1 \r\nCarpeted, floor to ceiling built-in wardrobe & vanity unit. \r\n\r\nEn-suite\r\nWC, pedestal wash hand basin, fully tiled shower unit.\r\n\r\nBedroom 2 \r\nCarpeted, built-in wardrobe.\r\n\r\nBedroom 3 \r\nCarpeted, built-in wardrobe.\r\n\r\nBedroom 4 \r\nCarpeted, built-in wardrobe. \r\n\r\nFamily Bathroom \r\nTiled floor, part tiled walls, WC, pedestal wash hand basin with storage under, fully tiled shower unit. \r\n\r\nRear Garden\r\nThe rear garden is private and south facing.
