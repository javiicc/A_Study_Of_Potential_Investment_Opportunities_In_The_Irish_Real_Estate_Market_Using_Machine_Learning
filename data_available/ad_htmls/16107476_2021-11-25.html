    0.5 acre roadside residential site, formerly with the benefit of outline planning permission for one dwelling thereon.\n\nThe property is situated in the townland of Lyrevocane in North west Cork County, just off the N72 Mallow to Killarney road at Derrinagree, close to Derrinagree Village, Millstreet town and the Sliabh Luacra region.\n\nThe property is 54 kilometres north of Cork city, 18 kilometres west of Kanturk town and 3.4 kilometres west of Derringgree village. It is also a 25 minute drive from both Kerry Airport and the incredibly beautiful town of Killarney, internationally renowned for its hospitality and heritage. The property is also 8 kilometres north of Millstreet town, home to Green Glens Arena which played host to the 1993 Eurovision Song contest, World boxing events, Disney on Ice, Concerts, Political events, Agricultural shows as well as the annual Millstreet International horse show. \n\nLocal Amenities within Derrinagree village \nPrimary School\nR.C Church\n\nServices available adjoining the property\nPublic Water \nTelephone\nElectricity\n\nPlanning Permission\nThe property is comprised in folio 95979F and had previously the benefit of outline planning permission (now expired). Any sale will be subject to full planning permission. \n\nViewing strictly by appointment.
