    Hickey O'Donoghue are delighted to introduce to the market this 4 bedroom detached residence which extends to c. 2,915 sq ft and is situated on c. 8.5 acre site. The property has gated access leading to a sweeping tree-lined driveway and private mature gardens which have been superbly maintained and manicured. Boasting two reception rooms with a separate dining room, the accommodation in this fine property is spacious and flexible. Only 10 minutes' drive from Limerick City, this property offers all the benefits of country living while still being within a stone's throw from the city centre and all amenities such as the Crescent Shopping Centre, the University Hospital, Raheen Industrial estate, schools and sporting facilities. The property is also within easy access to the motorway (only 5km) and Shannon Airport (25km). This property is ideally situated and affords great privacy. Offering so much potential this property would be an amazing family home.
