    Development Description

      Low Density development set where the amenity rich Kilkenny City meets the lush countryside. \r\n\r\nRath Ullord homes offer a unique opportunity for all the benefits of City living within the beautiful Kilkenny County landscape.\r\n\r\nRath Ullord homes will be highly energy efficient with an 'A 'rating. They have been designed and built using the latest in leading edge sustainable and renewable technology ensuring that the owners will have minimum heating and running costs.\r\n\r\nFor interior elegance Rath Ullord homes offer a choice of high end contemporary or traditional kitchens designed and manufactured by the renowned Sinnott Kitchens of Kilkenny.\r\n\r\n\r\nThe stylish and high performing Senator Windows are designed extra-large to give maximum light and contemporary feel.\r\n\r\nHigh quality internal doors & ironmongery with matching architraves.\r\n'MyLife' sanitaryware in bathrooms for added luxury.\r\n\r\nThe front gardens to the Rath Ullord homes include a high-quality border driveway, cobble lock footpath at the front, soft landscaping including selected planting to ensure year round colour.\r\n\r\nRath Ullord homes provide State of the Art Media & Communications technology.\r\n\r\nEach Rath Ullord home is covered by the 10 year HomeBond Guarantee Scheme.
