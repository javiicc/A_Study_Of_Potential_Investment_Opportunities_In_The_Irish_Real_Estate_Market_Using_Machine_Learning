    Harkin & Associates are delighted to bring new to the Autumn Market ,This Spectacular Detached Family Home..P32 XK22\r\n\r\nLocated in this ever-increasing popular village of Courtbrack, just outside Blarney. This home boasts a balanced combination of living space, sleeping space and exceptional outdoor space.\r\n\r\nThis home has been fully redecorated professionally including new flooring. \r\n\r\nOn reaching this home you are greeted with an impressive stone-built entrance with secure cast iron gates and lighting. The extensive tarmac drive sweeps down to the front and side of this home, providing ample parking for a growing families needs.\r\n\r\nThe southerly gardens have been extensively landscaped to include a boardwalk leading to "Fiddlers Brook", at the end of the terraced gardens where nature is at its best.\r\n\r\nEntering the hallway, you are immediately impressed with the double height ceilings, allowing natural light on both levels. The sitting room boasts a bright window to the front gardens and a feature open fireplace. The kitchen and dining room are a modern affair with a full complement of appliances. The utility room has access to the back and is plumbed for washing machine and dryer, with room for a second fridge. From the dining room there are sliding doors to an extensive, paved patio area, which looks out over the mature landscaped gardens and grounds.\r\n\r\nUpstairs the main bedroom suite is a treat with not one but two substantial slide-robes and a superbly finished en-suite bathroom. \r\n\r\nAll in all, there are four other bedrooms, two downstairs and two upstairs. All serviced with full bathrooms on both floors.\r\n\r\nThe garage is every mans' dream. Downstairs it would make an ideal workshop or storage for "boys toys". Upstairs is a spectacular space with a cathedral style window overlooking the grounds, the boardwalk, "Fiddlers Brook" and the surrounding countryside and this space would make an excellent home office. It even has its own WC.\r\n\r\nPlease see virtual tour to take in all of the above and the floor plan and plot diagram for complete measurements.\r\n\r\nThis home comes highly recommended to the discerning buyer and to arrange your own private viewing please reply via daft.ie or call Robert on 087-2611588.
