      Set amongst exquisite parkland and positioned on a stunning elevated site, Knockrabo is a truly impressive collection of 1, 2 and 3 bedroom apartments and penthouses that are now available to rent. \r\n\r\nLocated in Goatstown and adjacent to Mount Anville School, Knockrabo has a host of services and amenities nearby to support any resident looking to reside in this beautiful location. There are 13 schools as well as Dundrum and Stillorgan Shopping Centres within a 5km radius. \r\n\r\nKnockrabo is fully serviced by a variety of transport options. It is a 20min walk to the N11, main Stillorgan road which has an abundance of bus services that include the 76, 70, 46A ,155 & 115. The Green Luas Line is located at Sandyford just a 20-minute walk away also.
