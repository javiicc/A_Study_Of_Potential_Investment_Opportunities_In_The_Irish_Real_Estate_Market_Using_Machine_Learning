    No. 19 Lansdowne Crescent presents a rare opportunity to acquire a high quality modern 2 bedroom end of terrace house in this highly regarded private residential enclave, conveniently positioned off Haddington Road and located within easy reach of Ballsbridge, St Stephens Green and Dublin's Silicon Docks, offering discerning purchasers a perfectly balanced accommodation in this mature tranquil setting with off street parking and generous walled rear garden.\r\n\r\nAccommodation \r\n\r\nBuilt in 1990, this modern two bedroom town house comes to the market in pristine condition, having been reconfigured and upgraded by the current owners to promote open plan living while bringing the outside in.\r\nThe light filled accommodation which stands at approximately 68 sq.m. / 731 sq.ft features a bright and spacious dual aspect living room with solid wood flooring, box bay window and feature stainless steel recessed gas fireplace as well as a floating staircase. \r\nBifolding glass doors lead to a stylish kitchen /dining room with modern fitted kitchen complete with island incorporating seating area and enjoys direct through double doors access to a wonderful walled garden completing the accommodation on the ground floor. Upstairs, a landing space divides and leads to two generous double bedrooms, one located to the front and the other to the rear. With master ensuite, both double bedrooms include large built in wardrobes with excellent storage and a well appointed family bathroom completes the accommodation. \r\n\r\nGrounds and Gardens\r\n\r\nOutside to the front of the property provides one designated off street car parking space. To the rear of the property a private east facing walled garden has been landscaped to be low maintenance and includes many choice specimen trees and shrubs which provide privacy and seclusion in a most tranquil city garden.\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\nLocation & Amenities \r\n\r\nThe property is conveniently located being only minutes from the City Centre, the Grand Canal and Ballsbridge Village. With every conceivable amenity available both social and essential including a variety of transport options on your doorstep including DART, LUAS, City Bikes and Bus make 19 Lansdowne Crescent an excellent proposition.\r\n\r\nViewing is by private appointment with John Coleman
