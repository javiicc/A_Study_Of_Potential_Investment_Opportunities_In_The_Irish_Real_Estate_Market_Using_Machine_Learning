    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    *** NEW TO THE MARKET ***\n\nThis superb builders finish 4 bed dormer bungalow with large gardens is located a short distance off the Main N55 on a beautiful large elevated site. The property consists of 4 large bedrooms, one of which is ensuite, a large family bathroom, large kitchen dining room, sitting room, sun room, utility room, toilet, hallway with large staircase, hot press and closet. The c.0.75 acre site which has lovely scenic views from the front is finished to a high standard with footpaths, kerbing, gravel driveway and roadside fencing completed.\n\nThe property is situated just 3km from Killydoon, 8km from Loch Gowna. 11km from Granard, 20km from Cavan, 34km from Longford and 125km from Dublin. It is located close to many local amenities in Killydoon village and is also close to Mullahoran National School, Mullahoran GAA Grounds and is in the catchment area for Secondary Schools located in Granard and Cavan Town. This area is well known for its excellent fishing and with so many beautiful lakes in the vicinity it is in a prime location for any fishing enthusiast.\n\nThis house would make an ideal family home or would suit someone looking for a quiet location to reside or retire.\nViewing is Highly Recommended and is Strictly By Appointment Only.
