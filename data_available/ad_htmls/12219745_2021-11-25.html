    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 1 ac
    New to the market with CCM Property Network is this charming residential site of circa 1 acre (.404 ha) with road frontage.\r\nLocated in a picturesque and peaceful location within close proximity of Inch beach and Whitegate village.\r\nThe site, which is being sold subject to planning is situated in an elevated position with wonderful countryside views.\r\nJust a short drive of Trabolgan Holiday Village and the beautiful sandy beaches of Guileen and Roches Point.\r\nThe site offers plenty of scope to create a unique family home in a private location.\r\n\r\nZoning:\r\nRural Control Zoned - The qualifying applicant would need to be from the area, have strong ties to the community and demonstrate a genuine housing need.\r\n\r\n\r\nDirections:\r\n\r\nFrom Midleton, take the second exit at Lakeview roundabout for Whitegate and continue past Saleen Village. After passing the road to Cloyne on top of Saleen hill, take the slip road to the left for Inch Beach. The the next right turn and travel for a few miles to a cross roads before Inch Beach and take a left at the cross roads. Continue for a mile or so and take a right at the "Y" . Stay on this road passing the entrance to Inch Beach on the right. One mile up the hill take a left at a white house. Drive for approx 3/4 mile to a sign on the left "cul-de-sac". Take a left and the site is on the right side inside a grey iron gate with CCM signboard erected.\r\n\r\nOR \r\n\r\nFrom Midleton, drive to Whitegate Village. Drive straight through the village, passing the sign for Irving Oil, take a left after the playground, drive for circa 1 mile and take a right at the T-junction. Travel for approx 2 miles to a cross roads and drive straight through the cross roads. Continue for a mile or so and take a right at the "Y" . Stay on this road passing the entrance to Inch Beach on the right. One mile up the hill take a left at a white house. Drive for approx 3/4 mile to a sign "cul-de-sac". Take a left and the site is on the right side inside a grey iron gate with CCM signboard erected.
