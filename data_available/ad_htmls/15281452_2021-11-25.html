    SLOPING 0.4 ACRE ROADSIDE RESIDENTIAL PLOT (SECTION 47) OVERLOOKING LOUGH FOYLE\n\nThis excellent 0.4 acre plot is located above the upper Shroove road at Carrowtrasna with wide views across Lough Foyle and the Northern Ireland coastline. Section 47 Planning Permission will probably be applicable to locate a dwelling house for locals, returning emigrants or those coming to live and work in the area. Carrowtrasna is a scenic residential area on the upper road between Greencastle and Shroove overlooking the mouth of Lough Foyle and the Magilligan coastline and is within close walking distance of Michael John's Pub, Shroove Oratory and the beautiful Big White Bay beaches.\n\nDirections: From Moville, drive circa 1.5 miles, veer left at fork in road, Drive approx 1 mile, passing through Drumaweir village and crossroads. Drive approx 2.5 miles towards Shroove, coming downhill entrance to property on left hand c 50 metres before Michael John's Pub.
