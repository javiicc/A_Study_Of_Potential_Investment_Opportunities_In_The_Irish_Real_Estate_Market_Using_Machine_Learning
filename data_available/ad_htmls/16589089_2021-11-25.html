    Sherry Fitzgerald is delighted to present to the market Lothlorien , Saggart.\r\n\r\nCountry living with Dublin on your doorstep - the best of both worlds.\r\n\r\nThis fine five bedroom detached home sits on over one acre and is ideally located on the rolling Dublin Mountains with spectacular views, not only of Dublin, but as far as the Mourne Mountains on a clear day.\r\n\r\nDouble gates open onto a meandering driveway that splits the front gardens and leads right up to the property, with ample parking.\r\n\r\nThe entrance hallway leads to four bedrooms and a family bathroom on one side, with the living room, dining room, kitchen and utility on the other, while a spacious conservatory completes the ground floor accommodation.\r\n\r\nUpstairs the landing splits the accommodation, with the large primary bedroom and ensuite on one side and another double bedroom (also ensuite) on the other. An office space completes the accommodation.\r\n\r\nLocated five minutes drive from the centre of Saggart Village and it s many pubs, cafes and restaurants, Coolmine is a very exclusive part of county Dublin, with fantastically diverse, detached homes jutting out of the hills and country lanes leading anywhere from Blessington to Kilteel and more, depending on which road you take.\r\n\r\nThis is a fantastic opportunity to finally trade the hustle and bustle for the setting you deserve. \r\n\r\nCall or email now for viewing details.
