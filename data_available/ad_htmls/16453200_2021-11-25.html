    The Thatched Cottage is a quintessentially Irish home. This property offers a unique and exclusive opportunity to live in a wonderfully presented home with uninterrupted views of Kenmare Bay and the Beara Peninsula. The property is both welcoming and spacious.\n\nThis carefully thought out and created 4-bedroom residence has many beautiful features. The property was constructed and decorated in 1998 to the highest standards with beautiful interior finishes and attention to detail. The thatched roof is both an economically sustainable material and also offers natural insulation to ensure the house is kept cool in summer and warm in the winter. \n\nThe accommodation consists of a spacious internal area of 1,603 sq. ft (148.9 sq.mts). Ground Floor: Entrance hall, open plan kitchen / dining / living area, kitchenette, utility, 2 bedrooms and a bathroom.\n\n FIRST FLOOR: 2 bedrooms and a bathroom.\n\nThe house is set on 2.915 acres (1.18 hectares) of land and is well positioned for both privacy and views with a gated entrance and meandering driveway. \nThe manicured grounds possess front and back gardens with a wide variety of plants, shrubs, hedging, trees and two man-made lily ponds. \n\nOn the sea side of the property there is a south westerly patio area with views over Kenmare Bay and the Beara Peninsula. This area is ideal as an outdoor entertaining area and allows you to relax and enjoy the beautiful surroundings in the peaceful country setting.\n\nThe detached outbuilding houses two distinct spaces with separate entrances. One area is dedicated to a sauna, shower and a water filter system and a separate area which is currently used as a tool shed.\n\nThe property is located 9km from Castlecove, 10.2 km from Sneem village and 16 km from Derrynane beach. Gleesk pier is within 1 km. \n\nLocated within the Internationally renowned Dark-Sky reserve of South Kerry which is one of only three gold tier reserves on the planet, and the only one in the Northern Hemisphere.\n\nThis is a jewel of a property located off the Ring of Kerry Road. \n\nServices: electricity, telephone.
