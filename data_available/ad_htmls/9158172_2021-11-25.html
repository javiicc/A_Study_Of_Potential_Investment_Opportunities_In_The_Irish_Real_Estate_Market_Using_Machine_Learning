      Location

      The strategic location of Borris in Ossory along the N7 makes it the perfect location for commuters seeking peaceful living but close proximity to busy surrounding towns and easy access to the capital. Travel time to Dublin by car is a mere 1 hour and 10 minutes and Ballybrophy Station, just two miles out of town, provides departures to Heuston Station.\r\n\r\nNeighbouring Portlaoise (25km) and Roscrea (11km) are booming towns with a multitude of choices for young and old; lively pubs and clubs, fine restaurants, wide-ranging shopping facilities, local services and an abundance of sports educational recreational amenities.\r\n\r\nRight on the door step of Glenall is one of Ireland's best kept secrets, the amazing Slieve Bloom Mountain Range and Environmental Park, a haven for the outdoor enthusiast. The choices are endless; from hiking, cycling and equestrian trails to picturesque picnic area and world class fishing and golfing.\r\n\r\nGlenall has it all, rural and modern living in the heart of it all.
