    Located in the very much sought after and exclusive residential area of Gaurus, 3km from Ennis Town Centre, and positioned on a site extending to approximately 0.22 hectares. The property has many features including a wrap around driveway extending to the front, side and to the rear to facilitate access to the rear garden, detached boiler house/storage room. There is a raised lawn area to the front and side and a large grass paddock area which could be used to further develop the site. Inside the property is bright, spacious and a well proportioned home boasting two receptions, kitchen, 6 bedrooms, shower room, and main bathroom. This is a rare opportunity to purchase a mature home within close proximity of Ennis town and all amenities. Viewing is highly recommended by prior appointment with sole selling agents.
