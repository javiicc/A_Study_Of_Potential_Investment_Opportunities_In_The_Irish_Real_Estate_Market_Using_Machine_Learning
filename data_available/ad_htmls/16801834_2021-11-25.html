The cottage enjoys a most picturesque and private setting and the cottage which is circa 200 years old is charming and inviting. Presented in good condition, the cottage which is circa 196 sqm in size has a large kitchen/ dining room, cosy sitting room with open fire, lounge, four bedrooms, three bathrooms, one half bathroom and a large garage and shed to the rear.
