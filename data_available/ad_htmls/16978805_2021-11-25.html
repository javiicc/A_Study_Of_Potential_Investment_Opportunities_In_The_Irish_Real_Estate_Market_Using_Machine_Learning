    No. 27 Carrigeen is a four bed semi detached home which is well presented and ready for immediate occupation. A utility and guest wc completes the downstairs accommodation. Upstairs there are four bedrooms, one of which is en suite and the main bathroom.\n\nThe front garden is part laid to lawn with mature trees/shrubbery, brick built boundary walls and a tarmac drive for off street parking. The rear garden is in lawn with a patio area and block walls defines the boundaries.\n\nThis home would make an ideal starter home or a quality investment property. Viewing is recommended.\n\n\nEntrance Hall: Timber flooring, coving and centre piece, carpet on stairs, under stairs storage.\n\nLiving Room: Timber flooring, coving and centre piece, solid fuel stove,\nfireplace with timber surround, cast iron inset with tiles, double doors leading to dining area, front aspect window.\n\nKitchen /Dining Room: Open plan kitchen/dining room with sliding door to rear patio area. \n\nDining area has timber flooring, coving and centre piece,. \n\nKitchen has tiled floor and splashback, integrated oven and hob, plumbed for dishwasher, side aspect window.\n\nUtility Room: Timber flooring, plumbed for washing machine and tumble dryer, built in storage, side access door.\n\nGuest wc: Tiled floor and splashback, wc and wash hand basin, side aspect window.\n\n\nLanding: Carpet flooring, shelved hot-press, Stira access to attic (part floored)\n\n\nBedroom 1: Timber flooring, built in wardrobes, front aspect window.\n\nEn-Suite: Tiled floor and part tiled walls, wc, wash hand basin, unit with overhead shower.\n\n\nBedroom 2: Timber flooring, front aspect window.\n\nBathroom: Fully tiled floor and walls, wc, wash hand basin, bath with electric shower unit, side aspect window.\n\n\nBedroom 3: Timber flooring, rear aspect window.\n\n\nBedroom 4: Timber flooring, rear aspect window.\n\nGarden: Tarmac driveway provides off street parking and part lawn with brick wall and mature trees/shrubs to the front.
