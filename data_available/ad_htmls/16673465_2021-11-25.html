    ***UNIQUE EXTENDED STONE COTTAGE ON LARGE SITE WITH POTENTIAL TO DEVELOPMENT/EXTEND***\n\nMURPHY MULLAN RATHGAR are delighted to bring this superb extended & unique 3-bed extended granite stone cottage with a conservatory to the market in a highly sought after location. This type of property rarely comes to the market and must be seen to be appreciated. Number 544 is an end of terrace property located on Ballymount Road Lower just off the Walkinstown roundabout and boasts a large site with off-street parking to the front and a large long rear with side access.\n\nThis property has a bright and spacious feel as you enter its doors. The property is double fronted with rooms left and right of the entrance hallway. It has been modernised and boasts a modern extended kitchen, extended bedroom and extended conservatory room. The property has the added benefit of room to extend further to the side and rear as it is located on a large corner site with a long rear garden.\n\nThe property comprises of an entrance porch/hallway, large open plan living room with stove and extra ceiling height, bedroom one is to the front and bedroom two boasts a cast iron fireplace. The third bedroom is located in the extension to the rear. There is an extended kitchen/dining room with shaker style kitchen and a family bathroom with timber panelling. Finally, there is a conservatory to the rear which leads to the large corner garden and patio area. The front garden is walled with a drive-in for parking for multiple cars. This property has the added benefit of a large side access point of approximately 5.6 meters and oozes with potential.\n\nThe location is second to none, located off the Walkinstown Roundabout with all amenities and transport facilities on its doorstep. The Red Luas line is within close proximity as are a number of bus routes including the 9, 17, 18, 27, 77A, 123, & 151. The KCR, Terenure, Harold's Cross, The Red Cow and City Centre are all within easy reach and the M50 is easily accessible. There are plenty of leisure facilities close by including the Carlisle gym and shopping facilities such as the Ashleaf Shopping Centre and SuperValu Walkinstown. There is a host of local cafes and bars close to this property.\n\nThis property is an ideal first-time purchase and awaits its lucky new owner.
