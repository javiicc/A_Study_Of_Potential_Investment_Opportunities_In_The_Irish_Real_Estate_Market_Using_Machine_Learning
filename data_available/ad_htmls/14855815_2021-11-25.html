    Located in the heart of Blackrock Village "Le Moy" is a lovely detached family home set in this much sought-after area. This home is extensive in size offering over 1,650 sq. ft of accommodation spread over two levels. The property has the benefits of one of the more coveted spaces required by all purchasers now, a home office. This home has been in the one family ownership since its construction in 1971. It has gas central heating and double glazed PVC windows and doors. \r\n\r\nA wide and welcoming reception hall brings you through the easy flow of accommodation which extends to an open plan lounge/dining room, a beautifully bright kitchen looking to the rear garden, glazed sunroom, extensive utility room, three bedrooms and two bathrooms all at ground floor level. The first floor has a third family bathroom, home office and an additional attic room suitable for a myriad of uses. The property sits perfectly on its site with drive in and plenty of parking space to the front as well as lawned landscaping. The rear garden is particularly private, is lawned and a complete sun trap. \r\n\r\nThe location of this home is second to none with the bustling village of Blackrock offering cafes as well as its popular weekly farmers market. There is a wide variety of primary and second level education facilities all close by combined with easy access to a host of sporting clubs from GAA to Rugby and Soccer to Hockey. Leisurely recreation is catered for at the 8km loop of the Blackrock greenway walking and cycling path which extends from the city to Passage west. Public transport connections are excellent with the 202 bus route outside the door and the 215 a short stroll away. Mahon Point, City Gate and access to the south city link road network are two minutes' drive from the property.\r\n\r\nViewing is highly recommended.\r\n\r\nEntrance lobby:\r\nFinished with tile and inset mosaic.
