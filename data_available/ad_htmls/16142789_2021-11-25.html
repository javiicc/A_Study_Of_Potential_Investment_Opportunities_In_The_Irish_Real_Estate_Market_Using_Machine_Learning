    A beautiful three-bedroom semi-detached home available for sale in Barrack Road, Kilkee, Co Clare. Built in c.1999, this property is superbly located just a short walk from the beach and all town centre amenities.\r\n\r\nInternally, the accommodation is deceivingly spacious and fitted to a very good standard. The main entrance is by way of entrance hall leading into a bright open plan kitchen/dining/living area with a host of features including a solid oak kitchen, Canadian solid oak flooring, front aspect bay window, solid fuel fireplace with timber surround and raised granite hearth and a sliding door leading to a south facing patio area. The sliding door and rear aspect window floods the room with natural light. A WC under the stairs completes the ground floor accommodation. At first floor level, there are three substantial bedrooms, with the master room featuring tasteful en-suite. The main bathroom completes the first-floor accommodation.\r\n\r\nExternally, a walled cobble locked driveway to the front offers ample off-street parking. A gated side entrance leads to a sun-filled south facing private rear patio area with well-defined boundary walls. It also has the benefit of being positioned at the front of the development.\r\n\r\nDue to its size and location, it would make an ideal holiday home or investment property.
