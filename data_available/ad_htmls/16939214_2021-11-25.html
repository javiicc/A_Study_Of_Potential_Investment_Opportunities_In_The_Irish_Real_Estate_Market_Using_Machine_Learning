    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    We are pleased to present to the market no. 3 Moss Cottages - a most attractive semi-detached cottage on approx. 0.1 of an acre with an abundance of potential to be moulded into the perfect home for any discerning purchaser. In need of complete refurbishment number 3 is presented as a blank canvas ready for someone to put their own stamp on it. An example of the potential for this property is clearly demonstrated by that achieved at the neighbouring cottage.\n \nThe accommodation, which spans to approximately 72 sq meters comprises of an entrance porch, a living room, two bedrooms, kitchen and bathroom. The garden to the rear is something that s not often seen this days, extremely generous in size expanding to approximately 41.7 meters in length. So the potential to expand whilst having little impact on the garden is outstanding. \n \nLocation is excellent being within walking distance to Stepaside village with many shopping and leisure facilities. Dundrum Shopping Centre s many shops, restaurants and cinemas, are just 10 minutes away as is Cornelscourt Shopping Centre and Foxrock Village s Boutiques and restaurants. The popular Step Inn for a bite to eat which are within a 10 minute walk while Johnnie Fox s one of Ireland s oldest pubs is just minutes away by car also.\n \nStepaside horse riding and golf facilities are on the door-step, while the fitness enthusiasts have many choices for walking in the locality. Transport links are well catered for, the 44 / 63 & 118 bus routes are on the Enniskerry Road. The property is also located within 5 minutes drive to 2 Luas Stops at Carrickmines and Glencairn. The M50 located at the excellent Carrickmines Park Shopping Centre, is a mere five minutes away.\n\n\nEntrance Hall Access to bedroom 1, inner hallway and living room.\n\nInner Hall\n\nLiving Room 3.30m x 4.40m. Positioned to the front, attic access, open fire.\n\nKitchen/Dining Room 6.70m x 3.10m. Overlooking the garden, access to rear, stainless steel sink\n\nBedroom 1 3.40m x 2.90m.\n\nBedroom 2 3.40m x 1.45m.\n\nBathroom 1.50m x 3.10m. with whb, wc, bath.\n\n\nDIRECTIONS:\nFrom Glenamuck Road take a left on to Enniskerry Road and pass by the Garage on the LHS and this property can be identified by For Sale board.
