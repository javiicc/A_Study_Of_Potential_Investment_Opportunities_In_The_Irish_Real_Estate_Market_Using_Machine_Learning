    Hogan Durkan are pleased to present 'Cluain Searbhog' Copay, Adare, Co Limerick to the market for sale. It is very rare that a property of this stature, in the most highly sought-after Adare becomes available and it is without doubt the property will generate both local national and international interest. Beautifully positioned on just under an acre of magnificently mature landscaped gardens, Cluain Searbhog has been tastefully designed to maximise its use of the internal spaces with bright and spacious rooms throughout and light filled south facing rooms to the rear opening onto to decking area ideal for hosting all year round, it really has to be seen to be truly appreciated. \r\n\r\nThe area of Copay is located approximately 1.4km from the centre of Adare Village past Blackabbey Road and consists mainly of large contemporary one-off housing surrounded by prime agricultural lands. The immediate area benefits from being in close proximity to the highly successful Adare GAA Club (6-minute walk). The world-famous Adare Village itself boasts a range of impressive amenities and services such as the world-renowned Adare Manor Hotel & Golf Course, Dunraven Arms Hotel, Fitzgeralds Woodlands Hotel, Adare Heritage Centre, various fine dining and chic boutiques, popular bars and supermarkets, local sports clubs and Pitch & Putt course as well as various national schools and the impressive Manor Fields to name but a few. \r\n\r\nAdare is one of both Limerick and Irelands most popular villages and appeals to those looking for the countryside living with the benefit of being just 15 minutes from Limerick City and all its associated amenities. The village of Adare is serviced by Bus Eireann Expressway Routes 13 & 14 and Bus Eireann Route 321 and is located a short distance from M20 with links to the National Road Network. The property itself is located approximately 20km from Limerick City via M20 & N21 with all its associated services and amenities and approximately 44.5km from Shannon International Airport via N18. \r\n\r\nCluain Searbhog is likely to appeal to a wide range of buyers and has something for everyone. Great care has been paid to the gardens and landscaping to both the front and rear of the property with hidden areas just waiting to be discovered while the south facing deck located off the dining room and bar area is sure to suit those who like to host all year round. The property boasts an impressive self-contained one-bedroom apartment which may also suit separate home office, granny flat or au pair apartment. High quality internal finishes compliment the six bedrooms, and the main living room is complete with gas stove. There is ample car space in the long sweeping driveway with separate garage and shed located to the side of the property for additional storage.\r\n\r\nAccommodation\r\n\r\nUpon entry, one is greeted by an entrance porch with living room located to the right and guest w/c and entrance straight ahead. Off the living room is the spacious library/office area facing to the front of the property. The bar and dining room are located off the living room and kitchen area(which has separate utility room) with access to the two sunrooms(one with wood burning stove) to the rear and to the rear gardens and decking area. There is also separate one bedroom self-contained apartment located on the ground floor. \r\n\r\nThe first floor boasts landing hallway with six bedrooms (fully carpeted) four of which are en-suite and main bathroom which boasts jacuzzi style bathtub and separate shower. Bedrooms come complete with build in wardrobes with master bedroom providing ample wardrobe space and dressing area with en-suite including jacuzzi style bathtub and separate shower.\r\n\r\nNote: Please note that no apparatus, fittings, fixtures, or services have been tested. Measurements and photographs provided for guidance purposes only. Further details and photographs available on request. \r\n\r\nDirections: From Adare GAA Grounds, head west for 350m on L1422 and the property will be located on the left handside.\r\n\r\nViewing Details: Please note that all viewings will be held in accordance with HSE Guidelines and must be adhered to at all times.
