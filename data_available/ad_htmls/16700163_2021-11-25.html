    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to bring to the market Salvador House, an extended six-bedroom detached property located along the Western Road, a prime central location within Cork City.\n\nThe accommodation in brief extends to a family room, living room, two bedrooms with ensuites and a kitchen/dining room all located on the ground floor. The first floor boasts four good sized bedrooms, a w.c, communal bathroom and a hot press.\n\nTo the front of the property there is ample parking within the spacious driveway. The back garden is low maintenance and can be accessed through the kitchen, from bedroom six and from either side of the house. The rear also boasts a detached storage shed. \n\nThe property is very well located along Western Road immediately opposite UCC and the Bons Secours Hospital and within a few minutes walk of the city centre.\n\nThis property is an ideal family home or investment opportunity. This kitchen is to the rear of the property and features a variety of kitchen appliances, a wood burning stove as well as plenty of built-in cupboards and kitchen units. The kitchen has a tilled floor and serves as a gateway to the rear garden.\n\nBedroom 5 2.76m x 2.62m.
