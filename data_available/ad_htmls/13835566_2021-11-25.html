    Are you in the market for an unique opportunity to build your dream home.\r\n\r\nLiberty Blue Estate Agents are delighted to bring to the market - Site No 3, Suaimhneas, Coolbunnia, Cheekpoint. This elevated site offers privacy and peace on the outskirts of the village of Cheekpoint.\r\n\r\nSuaimhneas translates as serenity and tranquility. It is truly a fitting name to this unique development due to its spectacular waterside views. \r\n\r\nIt presents an opportunity to have your home in what will be a very exclusive development given there is a maximum of 3 sites. \r\n\r\nThe first site is already under construction. \r\n\r\nSite No 3 has been zoned low density residential, so no local need requirement and outline planning permission has been granted for a two storey home on the site which has been validated by An Bord Pleanala. \r\n\r\nPlanning Reference - 17/761 \r\n\r\nDesigning your own home is always exciting, we believe this will be an architect's dream project to design something truly special for a very fortunate client. \r\n\r\nLocation \r\nLocated in the beautiful village of Cheekpoint, the subject site is within a 2 minute walk to the local shop and post office. Within a 5 minute walk to the local school and playground. For those who enjoy a round of golf, the multi award winning Faithlegg Hotel & Golf Course is within a 5 minute drive. It is a short 10 minute drive to Waterford University Hospital, Ardkeen stores or Passage East car ferry.
