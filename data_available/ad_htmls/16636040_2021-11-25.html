    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty

    Coastal, spacious residence on 1 acre approximately, located 1 mile from Glandore village centre and having superb views over the magnificent outer Harbour, Tralong Bay and the Galley Head lighthouse.\n\nThe house contains over 3,000 sq ft / 287 sq. mtrs approximately of living space and enjoys many features including large picture windows, spacious rooms, oil fired central heating and the spaces can easily adapt for use as a full time residence or holiday home.\n\nThe accommodation includes entrance sun room leading to hall, living / dining room, kitchen, utility, 3 bedrooms with master en suite, family bathroom and guest wc. There are 2 bedrooms and a study / office upstairs.\n\nThe large outside building contains 1,200 sq ft / 112 sq mtrs approx. of useable space and is laid out with a double garage, 2 stables, workshop and store.\n\nThe gardens contain 1 acre approximately of fertile grounds laid out primarily in lawns. To the front of the house is a south facing patio with extended, recently restored, deck area.\n\nThe property enjoys location, excellent sea views, convenience to the popular sailing village of Glandore, whilst Rosscarbery is within a 10 minute drive and offers an excellent Secondary School, beaches and acclaimed restaurants. The towns of Skibbereen and Clonakilty are both within a twenty minute drive.
