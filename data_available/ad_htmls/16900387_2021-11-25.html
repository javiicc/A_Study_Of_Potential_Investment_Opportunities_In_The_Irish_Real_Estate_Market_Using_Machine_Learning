    Vincent Walsh Auctioneers presents for sale this exquisite 5 bedroom detached dormer style bungalow standing on an elevated 1.58acre fully landscaped site, located at Tobercrossaun, Headford 1.7km to Headford town, 28.6km to Galway city, and 22.1km to Tuam. \nHeadford Town offers a wealth of top-class services including a choice of four national schools Presentation College secondary school, supermarket, shops, Church, Hotel, coffee shops access to public transport, and an array of sporting facilities such as soccer/football/rugby/etc. Angling and boating enthusiasts will enjoy the beauty of Lough Corrib/Mask Lake District. For the outdoor fanatic, Headford offers plenty of beautiful nature walks and areas for hiking.\nThe property was built in 2007/08, with great attention given to details which illustrate throughout the house with a B2 energy rating.\nThis deceptively spacious family home will not disappoint and will appeal to a wide audience, its bright and airy interior has been tastefully decorated throughout complementing the well-proportioned rooms, high-end custom cabinetry, and finishes. \nGround Floor accommodation consists of:\nA bright and spacious entrance hall with a cathedral ceiling opening to the first floor.\nLocated off the entrance hall lies a commanding sitting room with an open fireplace.\nThe family room also has an open fireplace with a solid fuel stove and additional windows to allow natural light to flood this room complementing the opulent spacious surroundings.\nA luxurious open plan kitchen/dining area, with walnut shaker-style cabinets and a center island. This opens to the adjoining dining room with a wood-sheeted cathedral ceiling and French doors leading to the decking area.\nThe utility again has shaker-style fitted cabinets with laundry facilities and a Hellothermal Geo-Thermal under-floor heating system with a 500lt buffer tank and Guest WC.\nA short stroll down the hallway leads you to three bright and airy double bedrooms, one ensuite. All with custom wardrobes providing ample storage, these rooms are meticulously decorated.\nThe main bathroom/Wet room with bathtub/shower is fully tiled.\nA beautiful custom oak staircase leads to the first floor, which is hollow-core by design, this area is bright and spacious with Velux windows.\nA further 2/3 bedrooms all with Velux windows and one with an optional fireplace, these bedrooms are designed with fitted wardrobes. The main bathroom includes an electric shower, bathtub which is fully tiled.\nA large storage/ mechanical room with easy access to all equipment for general maintenance. \nThe property has a gravel driveway with electric gates and a natural stone boundary wall that hugs beautifully landscaped gardens.\nA detached garage with roller doors and full facilities for water and ESB, including a toilet and sink and an open timber stairway to the 1st floor.\nThis property is finished to a very high standard including all the modern-day essentials, viewers will not be disappointed.\nViewing by appointment only we are available during business hours, evenings and weekends.\n\nNOTE: These particulars are not to be considered a formal offer.
