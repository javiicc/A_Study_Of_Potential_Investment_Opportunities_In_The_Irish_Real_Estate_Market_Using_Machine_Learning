    Galvin Property & Finance are delighted to present Hillcrest to the open market. A truly imposing property on one of Dublin City's most spectacular sites. Situated on Knockmaroon Hill by the boundary of the Phoenix Park this detached bungalow enjoys views right across Dublin and the South facing aspect means the mature, rolling gardens are drenched in sunshine all day. The term "blank canvas" is over used in real estate but this property is the genuine article. \r\n\r\nA long private driveway leads to a double garage along the bottom of the garden with ample parking and a double garage. A large 3 bedroom detached bungalow of 1,400sqft is positioned toward the top of the site with a large patio to the front and rear and mature, private gardens on all sides. The house is in need of modernisation but there is huge scope to extend and renovate the property (subject to PP) and create a family home with unparalleled privacy and views of the across Dublin. The site itself is over half an acre and could also have further development potential again subject to the necessary planning permission. Inside there are two large interconnecting reception rooms with a picture window to the front to take in the view and maximise the sunlight. The kitchen / breakfast room is to the rear with direct access to the patio. There are 3 bedrooms with the master bedroom en suite, the main bathroom off the entrance hall completes the accommodation.\r\n\r\nThe location is close enough to the City Centre to be convenient but also enjoys a quiet and peaceful setting. The elevated position allows for views back toward Dublin City and beyond from a position above the banks of the River Liffey and next to the boundary wall of the Phoenix Park. The Park next door offers endless amenity and is a big part of the reason the surrounding areas have become so enviable. Hillcrest is between the villages of Castleknock just 3km and the increasingly popular village of Chapelizod (1km). There are a selection of Excellent Primary and Secondary Schools nearby and an abundance of sporting and recreation facilities. Heuston Station (5km) and access to the M50 (5km) make access to Dublin's main Motorway network and Train station are both closeby with Stephens Green and the City Centre also within 10km.\r\n\r\nThe site is very private and offers an ambitious buyer a unique opportunity to the create a spectacular family home. and provides an opportunity not to be missed.
