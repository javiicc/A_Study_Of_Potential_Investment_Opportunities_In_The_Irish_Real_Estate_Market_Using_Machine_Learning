    Sherry FitzGerald are very pleased to bring to the market The Longhouse, Mulcair Drive, Annacotty. This property is a hidden gem located on the banks of the Mulcair River only minutes walk from the village of Annacotty. Designed by Jim Corbett Architects and newly built in 2011 with a B3 energy rating, this unique detached home is sure to attract plenty of attention!!\n\nThis modern home boasts many elegant features and has exceptional privacy from within, capturing natural light from several angles of the property. The large open plan living area has wide views across the large private garden and patio, towards the river. It s also boasts a very unique raised games room (mezzanine style) which hangs above the living dining space, a lovely sperate space that has a discreet entrance form the main hallway.\n\nOne enters the house through a side hallway on the left hand side of the property. The hallway is finished with modern wood flooring and has 3 double bedrooms located to the left with a main bathroom and separate office space. There is also a guest wc and utility area with access to the rear garden and back porch.\n \nThe master bedroom boasts a spacious and modern ensuite and walk in wardrobe. There is plenty of wardrobe and storage space in each bedroom.\n\nThe galley style kitchen is hidden behind the dining area. Fitted with plenty of units, a modern gloss finish kitchen, attractive countertops and integrated appliances. \n\nThe spacious and exceptionally bright dining & living area takes up the front of the property, overlooking the gardens with beautiful glass feature wall which overlooks the patio area. Kilkenny limestone floors and poured concrete walls are some of the very modern finishes that the main living area has to offer as well as the large open hearth and fireplace at the centre of this double vaulted room. A wonderful space for entertaining and relaxing.\nThis is a very unique and attractive home that will attract either first time buyers , young professionals or possibly a downsize option.\nThe lovely rural setting is actually only minutes drive from the M7 motorway and all major road networks, as well as being 5 minutes drive from The University of Limerick and Plassey Technological Park. Within walking distance to the suburbs of Annacotty and Castletroy, the location just could be better with an abundance of essential amenities all close to hand.\n\nViewing is recommended without delay and by appointment only.\n\nServices: GFCH, Under floor heating in living dining room, Mains water and sewage, Thermal dynamic heat pump, triple and double glazed windows.\nThe property is being sold fully furnished and with appliances included in the sale.\n\nAccommodation: Hall, Living/dining room, kitchen, mezzanine games room/reading room, study, 3 bedrooms, ensuite, walk in wardrobe, bathroom, utility room.\n\nBER: B3
