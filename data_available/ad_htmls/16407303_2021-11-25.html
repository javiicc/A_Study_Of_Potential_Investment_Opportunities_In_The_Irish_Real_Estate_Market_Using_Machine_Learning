    Sherry FitzGerald are delighted to present No. 49 Burnell Park Avenue to the market for sale. Upon entering it is very clear to see this home has been well cared for by the current owners and presents in pristine condition throughout. Situated in this most sought-after location in Castleknock, this 3 bedroom semi-detached home has had many upgrades in recent years to include refurbished bathroom, composite front door, Roman blinds, carpets, stove, upgraded gas boiler, thermostatic radiator valves andTegre wardrobes and under-stair storage. \n\nThe property briefly comprises an entrance hallway with bespoke, understair storage, living room with Stanley Oisin stove and an open-plan kitchen/dining room. Upstairs there are 2 double bedrooms, both with bespoke, Tegre wardrobes and recently fitted carpet, as well as a third single bedroom. A beautifully finished bathroom, fitted in 2019, completes. \n\nThe garden to rear is not overlooked, with mature trees and shrubbery providing further privacy. The garden is approx. 12 metres long, partly laid in lawn with separate patio area, providing good scope for an extension to rear (Subject to Planning Permission). A timber shed provides convenient, external storage space. The garden includes a timber-edged raised vegetable/herb bed approx. 5 sqm. A side entrance leads to the garden to front, which is partly laid in lawn, with a charming, paved pathway. A concrete driveway provides off-street parking. \n\nLocated just off the Carpenterstown Road, this property is conveniently located a 5 minute walk to the local Spar shop, Insomnia caf , medical centre, pharmacy and The Carpenter Bar & Restaurant. St. Patrick s National School is also just a 4 minute walk and there is a number of other schools located nearby (school admission policies can change and must be verified). Castleknock Village is just a 5 minute drive as well as having easy access to Blanchardstown Shopping centre and The Phoenix Park. Coolmine Train station is just a 10 minute walk approx. and there is an excellent bus service, which stops just outside Burnell Park, providing easy access to and from the city centre.\n\nThis is a fantastic opportunity to acquire a wonderful family home, in excellent condition, within the highly sought-after Burnell Park. Early viewing is advised, as is sure to impress. \n\n\nEntrance Hall 4.55 x 1.77. A long hallway with tiled flooring, accessed through a recently upgraded front door.
