    Description

      First letting of this beautifully presented two bedroom, generously proportioned apartment. No. 9 Halcam Court has been sensitively and tastefully restored. This excellent residential property is located on one of Dublin 4's most prestigious and sought-after streets. The property is fully furnished, with furniture and finishings that complement the fine character of the dwelling.\n\nAccommodation comprises of entrance hall, modern and contemporary open plan kitchen/living area with wooden floorboards. The living area boasts a large sash windows overlooking Pembroke Road.\nMaster bedroom with double wardrobe and en-suite shower, main bedroom and a second bedroom complete the accommodation. \n\nPembroke Road is perfectly located in the heart of Dublin 4 being just a short stroll from Baggot Street, Ballsbridge village and Donnybrook village, offering a bijou of shops, restaurants, stylish cafes and countless amenities. St Stephens Green and Grafton Street are within walking distance, as well as Merrion Square and the beautiful greenery of Herbert Park. Dublin's commercial centres such as Grand Canal Dock, the IFSC and South Docks IT hub are also within walking distance. Trinity College is within walking distance and UCD is easily accessible by public transport, which is offered in abundance in this area via Dublin Bus and Dart at Lansdowne Station. \n\nSPECIAL FEATURES\n-\tSecond floor, dual aspect apartment\n-\tCompletely renovated throughout\n-\tSpacious living area\n-\tAlarm and intercom\n-\tLocated within stunning period building\n-\tGas fire central heating\n-\tAmple storage throughout\n-\tExcellent Dublin floor location close to IFSC, Grand Canal Dock, St Stephens Green & Merrion Square.
