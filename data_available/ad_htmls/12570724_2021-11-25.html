    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Olivia Needham is delighted to bring to the market this charming landmark period three bedroom cottage superbly located at Belclare. The property is situated at Belclare a short walk to Gannon's shop/petrol station and is on a footpath route to Westport town and Westport Quay area. The property is also on the 450 bus route. Ideally situated for a selection of local Schools. The property is on the bus route for both Louisburgh & Westport secondary schools as well as convenient to several Nation Schools including Westport Quay, Brackloon, Scoile Padraig & Murrisk National School. The property is also on the cycle track to Westport Quay and town and Betra Beach.\r\n\r\nExternally the property sits on 0.6 acres of mature tree land gardens.
