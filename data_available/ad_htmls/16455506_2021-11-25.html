    This well presented 4 bedroom home is in excellent condition throughout and offers versatile living space, a delightful south facing rear garden and is ideally positioned in a quiet cul-de-sac of detached houses.\n\nBuilt in 2006 of timber frame construction by Ashwood Homes offering generous space both inside and out. The well apportioned accommodation with a practical layout extends to approx 164 sqm/1765 sq ft and comprises of entrance hall, sitting room, playroom/living room, kitchen/dining area, utility and guest toilet. On the first floor there are 4 large bedrooms, 2 ensuites and family bathroom.\n\nThe fully walled rear garden with mature shrubbery beds is south facing, with gates at both side entrances. The cobblelock driveway to the front has ample parking.\nLocated within an easy stroll of all village amenities and within easy access to the M50, M4 and N3. Ballivor village offers a host of excellent amenities including pre-schools, primary school, Post office, butchers, pharmacy, Centra, takeaways and even the family pet is catered for in the Veterinary clinic.\n\n\nEntrance Hall High gloss floor tiles.\n\nSitting Room Wide plank wood floor.
