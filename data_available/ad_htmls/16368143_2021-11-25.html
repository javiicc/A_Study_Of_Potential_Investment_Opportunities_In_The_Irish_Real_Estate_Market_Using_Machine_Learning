    Brian Gleeson Property are delighted to present to the market this pristine detached 4-bed property along with circa 15 acres of top-class agricultural land in the peaceful townland of Graigue in Aglish, County Waterford. \r\n\r\nThis home has been lovingly maintained by its current owners and is located in an idyllic, picturesque setting, beside the renowned Saint Declan's Way; where you can walk from Cashel to Ardmore, perfect for walking enthusiasts. \r\n\r\nThe home consists of entrance hall, living room with an open fireplace, a sunroom (offering stunning unspoilt views and an abundance of sunshine) kitchen/dining room with breakfast bar and open fire, utility room with built-in units, 4 bedrooms (master ensuite) and bathroom with a jacuzzi corner bath. There is a stira stairs giving access to a spacious floored attic providing that much needed storage space. \r\n\r\nOutside the property benefits from a vast, level, well-maintained garden offering plenty of space for kids to play, household pets to roam and even room for a pony. The garden is a haven for entertaining, or take the opportunity to relax with only the sound of running water from the beautiful River Geosh that bounds part of the land. \r\n\r\nThe circa. 15 acres of agricultural land would suit an enterprising organic farmer, horticultural, livestock or even equine interests to name a few options.\r\nThe land is currently in permanent pasture. The hedgerows are well maintained and fenced with electric fence both internally and on boundary. Traditional fields, piped for water from private well at the house.\r\n\r\nGraigue is a peaceful townland in Aglish, West Waterford that boasts lovely woodland areas where one can enjoy walks or horse riding.
