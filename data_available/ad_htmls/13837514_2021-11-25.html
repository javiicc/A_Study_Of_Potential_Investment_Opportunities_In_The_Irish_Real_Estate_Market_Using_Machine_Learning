    This is a rare opportunity to purchase a substantial 1930s house on a large attractive 0.81 acre elevated site that is very convenient to all amenities including the school. The house has a floor area of 210 msq (2,260 sqft) with bright well laid out accommodation offering endless possibilities for a family home or development. The house requires renovation and the large site offers ample room for extensions and garages if required. The elevated site also has obvious development potential. This very attractive property offers easy access to the M18 to Shannon, Limerick, Ennis and Galway. It is also just minutes from Dromoland Castle and 18 hole golf course. It would make a super family home or a very attractive development property.
