    Moran Auctioneers are delighted to bring to the Market this fine stone built detached 2 storey residence which was constructed in c. 1926 with an extension added some years ago. The property has been upgraded by the current Owners and comes to the Market in great condition throughout. The property is located in the village of Glenisland which is a area of outstanding scenic beauty with the back drop of Blackrock mountain to the side and Lough Beltra a 5 minute walk which is renowned for it's salmon and trout fishing. Located equal distance of 4 miles Castlebar and Westport and c. 55 minutes drive to Knock International Airport.
