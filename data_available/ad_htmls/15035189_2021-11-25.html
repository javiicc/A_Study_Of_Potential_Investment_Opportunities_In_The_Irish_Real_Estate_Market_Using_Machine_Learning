    KINSALE \r\nKinsale, often referred to as the Irish Riviera, is the gateway to the famous Wild Atlantic Way - a breath takingly scenic drive around the West coast of Ireland. Kinsale is also renowned for it's many and diverse eateries, catering for everybody - from fine dining (one is Michelin star) to fish and chips at the Bridge with Finbarr. Being a town built mostly on the flat, enjoying panoramic water views, with a population of less than 5,000, and a mere 20 minute drive to an airport, it is top of the list with those from abroad. It is also known for its music, everything from traditional to modern , and usually every night and for free in every pub and hotel in the town. It's Fringe Jazz festival and Gourmet festival bring in people from all over every October. It is also a yachter's paradise, with two marinas, a great club house and plenty of things to do when not sailing. The property market in Kinsale has always been keen, with prices similar to those in Dublin. and stock at the high end of the market is always in short supply. \r\n\r\nLOCATION - SCILLY\r\nScilly is a village in itself and is loaded with history - for example the iconic Spaniard Pub and now restaurant also started by the late and famous Peter Barry - also a mere 5-minute walk away and also the equally iconic Man Friday restaurant family run for two generations, a further 3 minutes from there. The famous Scilly Walk runs in front of the house. Here, one can saunter. Along the waters-edge and watch the boats sail in and enjoy the best harbour and sea views in Kinsale. This walk takes one as far as the Bulman, in Summercove, another famous pub boasting food of almost Michelin star quality. If you want to socialize this is the perfect place to be, yet this property is tucked away, hidden behind dry-stone walls and electric gates. Ensuring absolute privacy. However, this is primarily a residential area, boasting high end, private residences interspersed with the odd traditional Irish cottage. Apart from being the most sort after location in Kinsale, it is also quirky, quaint and picturesque.\r\nOLD HEAD GOLF COURSE\r\nIt is world famous. Atlantic views amazing, views from every tee. Helicopter pad. Luxurious accommodation. Wonderful restaurant and bar. Terrace looking on last tee. Recently redecorated . Great venue for a bar lunch. \r\n\r\nDESCRIPTION\r\nOne feels they are stepping onto a Cruise Ship, a 6 star Cruise Ship no less, it is so palacial, clean, sparkly, the views are panoramic and the balconies are like the deck of a ship.\r\nOceanbreeze was originally known as the long bungalow and was purchased, knocked and renamed Oceanbreeze in 2006. It is a family home and is 5000sq.ft. It is super modern and meets all the need of the modern way of life today. \r\nThe property is laid out in 2 sections over 3 floors with a lift to bring to all 3. The accommodation is well laid out, family orientated, offered in pristine condition.\r\nThe house lends itself to 2 families living there - the guest accommodation is totally self sufficient to the main house although connected. \r\nThe views from every window facing south are just amazing and to have the balconies running the width of the house on each floor is just fantastic. \r\nThe accommodation layout can be viewed on the supplied floorplan and virtual tour.\r\nWhen you arrive in the house you are greeted with the most stunning marble flooring brought all the way from Greece and even laid by Greek tradesmen. First of all, you have the porch which leads you into the entrance hall and inner hall where the lift and grand staircase is. To the right you have access to the guest part -open plan living area with kitchen/dining and French doors leading to the balcony. When you go downstairs from there you have 2 bedrooms, and 2 ensuite bathrooms complete with dressing rooms, guest WC & WHB. You can also access to the front garden from this floor.\r\nWhen you go back to the main entrance hall you have 2 big bedrooms with ensuites and dressings rooms, cloak room and the lift. Go down the stairs and you have 2 bedrooms with ensuites, security room, utility room, Guest WC & WHB and Guest accommodation suite -living area, bedroom, ensuite and dressing room, again you can access the garden from here from every room. Take the lift or stairs to the top floor \xe2\x82\xac" and you are in the main living area \xe2\x82\xac" a fantastic and impressive sitting room/dining room complete with a fireplace and French doors leading to the balcony ( it bring the outside in and vica versa). Off this room you have a state-of-the-art Kitchen/dining room, Guest WC & WHB and gallery landing.\r\n\r\nThe property lends itself to so many uses and will attract an array of buyers - it would make a really up market Guest house; the house lends itself for a family of course with the option of a home office and indeed parents coming to stay. The beauty about this house is that is fresh, bright, airy and easy to maintain, live and decorate. It's a showstopper.\r\nDue to its location, it is very attractive as you are in the hub of all the activity yet a small bit away from it. The gardens are not elaborate so easy to maintain, plenty of carparking, electric gates and the garage - ideal for cars, office, den for the kids etc. \r\nYou are only a 20 min drive to Cork Airport and a 20 min drive to the old head gold course. The yacht club is only a 10 min walk and if you fancy a jar and a bite to eat just pop across the road. \r\nState of the art home with all the perks of living in the picturesque coastal town of Kinsale
