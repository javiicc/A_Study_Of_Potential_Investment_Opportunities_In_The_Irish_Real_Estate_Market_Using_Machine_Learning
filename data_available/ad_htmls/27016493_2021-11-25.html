    Description

      Buckley Real Estate are proud to bring this homestyle two-bedroom apartment to the rental market. Available in the highly sought area of Carpenterstown, Castleknock. You are welcomed into this spacious apartment by a brief hallway with a storage cupboard to your left. Leading into the open plan living and dining area, this is fantastically treated by natural and wide open spaces finshed to a contemporary standard. The kitchen is tastefully done with a tall fridge freezer, dishwasher and washer & dryer available. The Master bedroom is bright and airy with ample storage space available. Following into the single bedroom, ideal for a home office or to use as a second bedroom. The property is located in a small private development just off Carpenterstown Road and next to Coolmine Train station with regular services to the city Center on the Maynooth line. It is close to all amenities including local transport links, schools and shops, such as the Blanchardstown Shopping Centre. It is also located within easy access to the N3 and the M50 motorway, and therefore the national road network. Castleknock has been a highly popular in Dublin for many years, so viewings are highly recommended as this apartment will surely be snapped up quickly.
