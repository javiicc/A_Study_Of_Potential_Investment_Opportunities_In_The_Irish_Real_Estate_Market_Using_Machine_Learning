    This four bedroom detached bungalow offers the opportunity to create your ideal home that can boast of size, comfort and tranquility. Conveniently located near the N20 and N21 main roads.\r\n\r\nAdare is a beautiful and charming village, with an idyllic setting on the River Maigue. The village is a designated Heritage Town and is also known as the \xe2\x82\xacPrettiest Village in Ireland\xe2\x82\xacTMcompeting annually in Ireland Tidy Towns competition and picking up numerous awards for same.\r\n \r\nAdare was originally founded in the 13th Century as its location beside the River Maigue was an ideal location for habitation, the Desmond Castle situated on the banks of the river was founded at this time, along with the Holy Trinity Abbey which is a very popular wedding venue for couples from all over Ireland. The Augustinian Priory located at the bridge in Adare was founded by the Earls of Kildare in the 14th Century and is a very popular tourist spot as visitors can still visit and walk in the Priory\xe2\x82\xacTM covered cloisters, which the monks used for prayer.\r\n\r\nAdare is renowned for its traditional Thatched cottages, originally built by the Earl of Dunraven in the early 19th century for the workers on his estate, in 1909 the main Village Hall was commissioned and constructed using an English Tudor Style of building and the Hall is in regular use by the people of Adare for creative arts, exercise and relaxation classes.\r\n\r\nAdare has a wonderful Tourist Heritage and Visitor centre, a number of exclusive top fashion boutiques, and 4 excellent National Schools - St Joseph\xe2\x82\xacTM Boys School, Our Lady\xe2\x82\xacTM Abbey Girls School, St Nicholas National School and Shauntrade National School. \r\n\r\nSpacious family home with plenty of potential!\r\n\r\nACCOMMODATION:\r\n\r\n\r\nEntrance Hallway\t1.94 x 4.85\t \r\nCarpet Flooring\r\n\r\nSitting Room\t4.25 x 6.6\t\r\nStone fireplace.
