    *** NEW TO THE MARKET ***\n\nLocated 3km from Killeshandra and 16km from Cavan town this 5 bed two storey house is an exceptional property to come to the market. Situated on a large elevated site a short distance from the main Cavan to Killeshandra Road this property would make the ideal family home with large bright rooms throughout. The property is finished to a high standard and consists of 5 double bedrooms two of which are ensuite, a spacious kitchen, sunroom, living room, sitting room, utility room, large family bathroom and hot-press. Nestled away in the beautiful countryside with its mature garden this property is ready and waiting for you to put your own stamp on it.\nViewing is Highly Recommended and Strictly By Appointment Only.
