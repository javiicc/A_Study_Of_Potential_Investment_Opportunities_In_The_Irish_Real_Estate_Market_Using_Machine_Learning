    This wonderful property is in close distance of Crookhaven and Goleen village on the Wild Atlantic Way route and also close to Barley Cove beach. The property consists of a large two storey house, which has been divided into a main house with a studio apartment, and a separate guest cottage all set in a stunning location with beautiful views to Fastnet Rock and Cape Clear Island. \r\n\r\nThe main house comprises a large open plan kitchen cum living room with an open fireplace and fully fitted kitchen units. Off the kitchen there are a large conservatory with a stove and a lounge currently used as a study and dining room. There is a bedroom and a bathroom on the ground floor whilst upstairs there are three further bedrooms, a family bathroom and a storage room. Adjacent to the main house is a two bedroom studio apartment with an open plan kitchen, dining and living room. Both, the apartment and the main house share the same entrance hall and can easily be used as one large home. There is also a separate guest cottage with a kitchen cum lounge and a separate dining room. There are three bedrooms and two bathrooms. All main living areas of all houses offer fine sea views.\r\n\r\nOutside the property offers a total of c. 1 acre which is mostly laid to lawn and partly landscaped. There is a heated swimming pool and a sauna. A number of sheds cater for outdoor storage.
