    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    Scanlon Auctioneers are delighted to bring this fine property to the market. The property is set in a spectacular elevated position and commands stunning views of the River Shannon and surrounding countryside. The property is light filled and affords beautiful views from pretty much every room. This substantial four-bedroom property boasts spacious accommodation and ample garden space to the front and rear.\r\nIdeally situated off the N69 just a few Kilometres from the picturesque towns of Glin and Tarbert. It is within 10 minutes of three primary and one secondary school. Listowel and Ballybunion with its beautiful beaches and Golf club are only 25 minutes away. Limerick city and Tralee can be reached within 50 minutes. An hour drive will get you to Shannon airport and Kerry Airport.
