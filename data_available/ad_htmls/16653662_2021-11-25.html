    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    • Overall Floor Area: 0.5 ac
    The townland of Cloondahamper is nestled between the twin villages of Lavally and Barnadearg.\r\nThis is a picturesque residential settlement and offers the perfect location for those seeking the beauty of nature, the proximity of village living and connectivity to all urban routes. Galway City is reachable in just 35 minutes via the M17/M18 Motorway, making this an eminently commutable distance for Galway City workers.\r\nThe site is located on a quite and peaceful cul de sac with no passing traffic and will provide for idyllic family living. It is just a couple of minutes drive from the village of Barnaderg. It is less than a 10 minute drive to Tuam Town with its wealth of amenities and connection to the motorway network.\r\nThe site comes with full planning permission and there are no clauses meaning it is open to all to build here. (Planning Reference 17/1464 Expires January 2023)- leaving the purchaser ample time to build a quality A Rated home to your own unique style and requirements.\r\nThere is planning permission for an elegantly designed contemporary dormer bungalow. Featuring four bedrooms in total and a beautifully appointed open plan kitchen and dining area\r\nThe design incorporates extensive use of modern styled glazing which is cleverly oriented to maximise both morning and evening sun and the garden is also south facing. Overall floor area is 203 square meters or 2,185 Square Feet.\r\nThe site is very keenly priced and the water connection is already paid. The ground is level and even with the road and is ideal for building with little fill required. Boundary walls are in place with curved walled entrance and are ready to be dressed in local natural stone. The site is secured with a new aluminium dipped farm gate and is ready for the building work to commence. \r\nThis is a wonderful opportunity and represents a bargain for the astute buyer. The site is very keenly priced and the scale of the proposed dwelling finds the perfect blend between generous family living space and affordability in terms of build cost. If you are looking for good value and want to build a modern affordable home, then this might be the opportunity you have been waiting for.
