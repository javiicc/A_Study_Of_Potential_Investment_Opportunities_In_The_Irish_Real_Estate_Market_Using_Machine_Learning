    REA Brady are delighted to present to the market this 4 bedroom detached house in a small housing development on the Hilly Road in Drumshanbo. The house is fronting onto a large common green area and is within walking distance of Drumshanbo and all its amenities including schools, childcare facilities, shops and restaurants, Drumshanbo is a vibrant town and has amenities such as the Blueway along the Shannon, Acres Lake, Lough Allen Hotel and all Lough Allen's amenities including water sports etc, Equestrian Centre, Open air pool, Jinnys Bakery, The Shed Distillery to name but a few. It also hosts the annual Joe Mooney Summer School and the An Tostal Festival. The property itself is a well laid out detached house with parking to the front, rear garden which is not overlooked at rear. The house has two living areas to the front, a downstairs wc and a kitchen and dining area with patio door at the rear and a separate utility room. There are 4 bedrooms in the property one of which is ensuite and a family bathroom.
