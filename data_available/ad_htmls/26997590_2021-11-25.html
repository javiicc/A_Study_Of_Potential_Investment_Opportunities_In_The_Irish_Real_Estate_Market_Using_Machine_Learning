    Description

      Buckley Real Estate are proud to bring this beautiful contemporary two bedroom apartment to the rental market. Impressive newly refurbished two bedroom fourth floor apartment to rent in Dublin City. This beautifully presented two bedroom apartment comes to the letting market offering spacious and contemporary living accommodation in a second to none location. The apartment has been newly refurbished for the market and is located in this modern gated complex in the Heart of Dublin's most fashionable Docklands adjacent to the bustling Grand Canal Dock. The accommodation comprises of entrance hallway, spacious open-plan sitting room & dining area, contemporary fitted kitchen, 2 double bedrooms (master ensuite) and main bathroom. This highly sought after location requires little introduction. As you leave the complex you can immediately feel the energetic atmosphere of the area, which boasts a number of amenities all within walking distance. Facebook, Google, the IFSC and Grand Canal train station are all a stone's throw away. The Bord Gais Theatre, Marker Hotel, bars, restaurants and cafes are all at your doorstep. There is also a Dublin Bike Station just outside of the complex.
