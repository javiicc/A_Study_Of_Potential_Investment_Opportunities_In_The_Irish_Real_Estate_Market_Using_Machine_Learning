    Sherry FitzGerald is proud to bring to the market this deceptively spacious period cottage filled with natural light and an abundance of character. The original part of the cottage dates back to the 1870 s and was part of the settlements that surrounded Killincarrig Castle.\nThis row of cottages is one of the best-preserved linear settlements in Co. Wicklow. The welcoming hallway leads through to the well proportioned accommodation. A character filled living room over looking the private sunny rear garden. The country kitchen and large sun room provide all the comforts of modern living with the charm of the period. There are three good size bedrooms to the front of the property and a generous family bathroom. A small courtyard to the side of the property houses a utility room and the private southerly rear garden has a chalet to the rear that measures 42.5sq.m/456sq.ft approx. A perfect home office, granny flat or home gym. The Chalet also has the ability to bring in additional income to the new owners if they so chose to rent it out. \nBlacklion is ideally located just north of Greystones town within easy walking distance of a host of restaurants, coffee shops, shopping centres, boutiques, harbour and the sea front. Sporting enthusiasts are well catered for in the area with sailing, rugby, tennis and numerous golf clubs in the locality. Not to mention an excellent nearby school network and access to N11 / M11 and M50 connection is easy, the DART and a regular bus service links the area with Bray and Dublin City Centre.\nEarly viewing is advised!\n\n\nEntrance Hall\n\nLiving Room 6.331 x 4.41m.\n\nKitchen 2.20 x 5.26m.\n\nSun Room 5.91 x 3.31m.\n\nBedroom 1 3.52 x 3.72m.\n\nBedroom 2 2.61 x 3.44m.\n\nBedroom 3 2.20 x 4.44m.\n\nBathroom 1.80 x 3.52m.
