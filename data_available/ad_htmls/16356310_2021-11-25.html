    Number 3 Merton Walk is a most impressive four bed mid-terrace property, ideally located in Mount Saint Anne s, one of Dublin 6 s most well regarded and sought-after residential developments. Located close to the Luas gate, at the rear of the development, this superb property is set amongst well maintained and manicured communal gardens and offers a potential purchaser a deceptively spacious, light filled property with a good balance of both living and bedroom space. \r\n\r\nThe accommodation extends to 158 sq.m. / 1700 sq.ft. and comprises briefly, a light filled entrance hallway with spacious shelved hot press, guest w.c., an open plan living room/dining room that opens to the fully equipped kitchen. Upstairs on the first-floor landing, a large feature window spans both landings, allowing ample natural light to flood in. A sizeable sitting room is located on this level with a glazed study area to the front. The main bedroom with fitted wardrobes and a shower room en-suite completes the accommodation at this level. On the second floor, there are three further double bedrooms, one with a shower room en-suite and a Juliet balcony. A separate shower room completes the accommodation at this level. \r\n\r\nTo the front of the property, the cobblelock driveway provides off-street car parking, along with ample visitor parking. To the rear, the garden is mainly laid out in paving and gravel with planting and shrubbery complete with a decked area that is ideal for al fresco dining. \r\n\r\nThe location of Mount Saint Anne s is unrivalled with an abundance of amenities in the vicinity. Local shopping in Milltown provides everything you need for everyday living, while nearby surrounding villages of Ranelagh, Donnybrook and Rathmines provide a wealth of shops, bars and restaurants, to name but a few. The development further benefits from private resident access to the Milltown Luas Station, allowing ease of access to the city centre and beyond.\r\n\r\n\r\nEntrance Hallway Light filled entrance hallway.
