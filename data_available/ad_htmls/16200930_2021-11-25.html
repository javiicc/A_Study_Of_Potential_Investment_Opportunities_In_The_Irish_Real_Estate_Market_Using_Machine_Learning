    Sherry FitzGerald is delighted to introduce 1 Glenart to the market. This stunning three bedroom ground floor apartment comes with a spacious and well laid out accommodation circa 980 sq.ft /91 sq.m that is flooded with natural light. With a patio that is ideal for unwinding, it also enjoys a pleasant outlook over the grounds of this prestigious development. The amenities of Blackrock village are only a short stroll away as is the Dart, Blackrock Park, QBC and Air Coach on the N11. \n\nInternally the property has been lovingly maintained by the current owners and is presented in excellent decorative order throughout along with a fully fitted kitchen space. The accommodation comprises; entrance hall with ample storage space, living / dining room with access to the spacious patio, bright kitchen, three double bedrooms, one with en-suite shower room, and a family bathroom. The large patio is paved and overlooks the well-maintained grounds of the development making it the ideal space to sit back and relax. There is designated parking with this property.\n\nLocated in a quiet and highly regarded residential development of attractive redbrick properties just off Mount Merrion Avenue, residents are within easy access of Stillorgan Village and Blackrock s beautiful coastal village offering chic boutiques and gourmet food shops together with several high street shops and Supermarkets. Some of Dublin s most renowned schools are also nearby and the property is within easy access of the DART, N11 and QBC which provide efficient access to the city centre and beyond.\n\n\nEntrance Hall 4.46m x 3.84m.
