    A very impressive three bedroom semi detached home in a lovely quiet cul de sac location with a large green area to the front. This very attractive modern home has main gas central heating, an en suite shower room, converted attic storage space, a utility room, 3 x wc's, an enclosed rear garden, ample on site parking and many other attractive features. It offers easy access to the M/N18 to Limerick, Ennis and Galway and it also offers easy access to Eastpark and Smithstown. The enclosed rear garden is ideal for children and pets and the large green amenity area to the front is also ideal for children. An ideal family home that would also be ideal for those looking to down size to a nice quiet area.
