    Helen McCormack Estate Agents are delighted to present a beautifully designed c. 120 sqm 3 bed Semi Detached Property with private rear garden at 30 Mungret Woods, Mungret, Limerick. The house is in pristine condition with a bright, warm and spacious interior including all 3 bedrooms being doubles.\n\nThis residential complex offers the perfect blend of semi-rural living; being in a leafy residential exclusive estate on the historic grounds of Mungret College. The house is a minute's walk from a very large playground on the development's grounds. The varying house types and styles epitomise a superior design in terms of overall planning and there the atmosphere and serenity that is palpable. \n\nThe property has a spacious open paved driveway to the front for private parking; flanked by tall trees. There is a large side gated entrance giving access to the private rear garden from the front as well as double French patio doors from the Dining area and, additionally, separate access from the L shaped Kitchen area. \n\nThe house is serviced by Gas Central Heating and is fully alarmed.\n\nAccommodation:\n\nEntrance Hall:\nStylish Entrance Hallway with solid oak timber flooring and feature window allowing for maximum light. \n\nGuest Wc 2.5m x 2.4m\nIn Corner position and understairs with wc and whb.\n\nSitting Room 6.08m x 3.94m\nWith solid oak flooring and featuring a beautiful large bay window and area. There is a central fireplace with an inset stove and a very attractive large built in furniture / display unit to the left of the fireplace.\n\nKitchen / Dining 6.4m x 5.7m\nOpen plan Kitchen / Dining divided by tiled and solid oak flooring. Fully fitted kitchen including breakfast bar. The area extends to an L shape offering double access to the Courtyard style patio and landscaped rear garden. \n\nFirst Floor\nMain Bedroom 4.5m x 3.6m with Ensuite Bathroom\nA double, carpeted to the front with built in wardrobes. Ensuite with wc, whb and large fitted shower.\n\nBedroom 2 4.2m x 3.5m\nAlso a double, carpeted with built in wardrobes overlooking the rear garden.\n\nBedroom 3 4.17m x 2.7m\nAlso a double with fitted wardrobes looking to the front\n\n\nMain Bathroom 2.5m x 2.5m\nwith wc, whb and featuring a bath with electric shower overhead.\n\nThis beautiful property is well located and allows for convenient access to major link roads including M20, M7 and N24.
