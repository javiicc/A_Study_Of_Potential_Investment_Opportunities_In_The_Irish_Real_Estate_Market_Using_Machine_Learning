    Elegant, three bedroom, end of terrace Regency townhouse renovated and restored in 2005 as part of the Elysium development. Dating from the 1840s, this is the essence of elegant city living, combining magnificently restored period features with modern conveniences and generous rooms. On the first floor return a dining room with walnut floor opens to a contemporary kitchen and utility room. Leading off the kitchen the curved walnut staircase climbs to the roof top office with sun terrace. On the top floor there are two large double bedrooms each with en-suites, and built in wardrobes.
