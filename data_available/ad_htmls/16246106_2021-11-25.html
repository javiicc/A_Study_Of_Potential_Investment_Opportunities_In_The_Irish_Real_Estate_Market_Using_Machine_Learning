    We are delighted to bring to the market this charming, three bedroom home which has a stunning position overlooking and adjoining the Roechrow River which flows past on its eastern boundary. Located within 4km of Killybegs fishing town in scenic south-west Donegal. Occupying a 0.6 acre plot which has a range of mature trees and is accessed from a little used rural road, this property has a delightful, tranquil setting. Accommodation comprises of large sitting room, kitchen, dining room, three bedrooms and a bathroom. A few of the traditional internal features include old-style pine doors throughout, exposed timber beam ceilings, cast-iron solid fuel stove plus a half-door at entrance.
