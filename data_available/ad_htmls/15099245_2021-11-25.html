    Description

    • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
    SALE ARRANGED ** SALE ARRANGED ** SALE ARRANGED A unique opportunity to acquire a 3 bedroom country home on an elevated site with superb views of the McGillycuddy Reeks and the surrounding countryside. Accommodation also includes a family bathroom which has a separate bath and shower, a substantial living room with a standard fireplace and a kitchen / dining area, including brand new fitted kitchen units and appliances (which have never been used). The property is in excellent condition throughout and all 3 bedrooms have new built in wardrobes.It is also a very well insulated house as the wall cavity has been pumped with granular insulation, the attic insulation has also been upgraded and it has oil fired central heating. The property is situated on a site close to 0.75 acres and which has a well defined boundary of mature trees and an composting area to the rear. There are a total of 3 well built storage sheds to the rear and side of the house, one of which houses the oil tank. The property is located on the Killarney to Waterville tourist route and is approx 6.5 miles from Killorglin and approx 10 miles from Killarney.
