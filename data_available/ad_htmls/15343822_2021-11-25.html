    New to the market, is this residential farm with stables and gallops. This property is located at Curnalee, Curraghboy which is approx. 18km from Roscommon Town & 14km to Athlone Town. There is c 36.5 acres of good quality land with 14 horse box stables with water connected, horse walker for 6 horses, lunging ring, various paddocks, tack room, 5-furlong gallop with a 2-furlong circular gallop and a number of various out-buildings, pits and sheds. This 4 bedroom house of approx. 1500 sq ft., has modern semi-solid flooring throughout the house. It comprises of an entrance hall, a living room with a solid fuel fireplace, a kitchen that is fully tiled and the dining area has a solid fuel Stanley stove with a back boiler. The kitchen is fitted with new modern NEFF appliances. There is a door from the kitchen leading to the outside patio area. Bedrooms 1, 2 & 3 are double rooms. Bedroom 4 is the master bedroom with a large walk-in wardrobe and the ensuite is tiled with an electric shower. The main bathroom is also fully tiled with an electric shower.\r\n\r\nOutside there is a fuel shed, double garage and a slate patio area of approx. 30 ft x 20 ft. There is a tarmac driveway to the front, side and rear and a garden to the front and side of the house. This house is serviced by well water and septic tank. Features include: OFCH, CCTV, parking and pVC double glazed windows and doors. This property is ideally suited to a person who is in the business of breaking and training horses or someone in the equestrian line.\r\n\r\nVIEWING IS HIGHLY RECOMMENDED. STRICTLY BY APPOINTMENT ONLY. BER: D1. For further details contact: Dalton Auctioneers
