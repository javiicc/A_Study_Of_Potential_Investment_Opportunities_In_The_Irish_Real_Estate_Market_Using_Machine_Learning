    Situated on the shores of Clowney Lake and Clonamullig Lough, a distinctive property nestled into a gently elevated site. The grounds have been meticulously landscaped and cultivated to showcase the property whilst also providing an oasis of privacy and tranquillity with unhindered views across the lakes. The generous lawns are bordered with flower beds, trees, hedging and shrubbery with a variety of annuals, perennials and fruit trees offering ever changing scenery with the seasons. \n\nComprising some 4.26 acres approx. the grounds have been arranged with form and function all interlinked by a main pathway. Along the rear of the property the generous open plan sitting and dining area benefits from the spectacular views. A set of double doors lead onto the rear patio complete with a double awning for hot summer days and for the cooler weather a heated pavilion. A study and rear hallway complete the ground floor accommodation. The master suite complete with dressing room and en-suite together with 3 double bedrooms and bathroom are located on the first floor, all generously proportioned with captivating views.
