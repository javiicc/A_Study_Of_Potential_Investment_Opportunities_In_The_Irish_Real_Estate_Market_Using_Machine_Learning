    DNG present to the market an ideal family home in the ever popular Aylesbury estate, No. 19 Pineview Rise. The home is positioned on a corner plot which offers huge potential for further development (SPP). Presented in excellent condition throughout it will appeal to all buyers keen to set up home in a mature residential setting. Features include two reception rooms, 3 bathrooms, open plan kitchen / diner and a converted attic space currently used as a 4th bedroom. The features continue outside with a large driveway offering off street parking while to the rear there is a large landscaped garden which is not overlooked and totally private.\r\n\r\nThe bright and spacious accommodation briefly comprises of a porch, entrance hallway, living room, kitchen / dining area, second reception room, and guest bathroom. On the first floor there are 3 generous sized bedrooms ensuite bathroom and a family bathroom. The attic space is also converted and is suitable for a multiple of uses. \r\n\r\nPineview is a mature and sought after estate. No.19 is ideally located overlooking a green area to the front with views of the Dublin Mountains. There is ease of access to all main road networks with the M50, N81, N7 and Belgard Road in close proximity. It is also well served by public transport with The Luas and Dublin Bus stop located nearby making the city centre easily accessible.
