    Seaspray is a turnkey 3-bedroom dormer bungalow on 0.37-acre site which is in a sheltered location on a quiet country road close to the sea. With views over the Atlantic Ocean, Achill Island and the Mullet Peninsula the residence was extensively renovated in 2021 and measures approximately 121.86 Sq. M. / 1,312 Sq.\r\nFt.\r\nOn entering the cottage you are greeted by a porch, this continues into the living room with solid wood flooring which together with a wood burning stove creates a warm and cosy ambiance. The living room leads into the kitche/dining area. The kitchen has fully fitted units with solid timber worktops, island area, belfast sink and new integrated appliances with wine fridge. There are with stunning views of the countryside and sea from all three windows in the kitchen. A door leads from the kitchen to an outdoor decking area ideal for 'Al Fresco' dining overlooking Achill Island. Completing the accommodation on the ground floor is a bathroom & large double bedroom.\r\nThe timber staircase forms part of the kitchen/dining room and leads to a further two bedrooms and bathroom on the first floor. The two large double bedrooms have breath taking sea views though two velux windows in each bedroom this completes the first-floor accommodation. The residence is being brought to the market in turnkey condition with all brand new contents included.\r\nThe property is located near the rural coastal village of Geesala at Doohoma Head on the eastern side of Blacksod Bay in North Mayo, which is part of Europe s longest coastal drive The Wild Atlantic Way, with views of Achill Island and Mullett Head, encircles the property. Doohoma is also noted for it s beautiful beach, which is never crowded or polluted, due to its isolation. It is ideal for swimming, as well as such sports as surfing and sailing. Belmullet town 23km, Ballina town 61km and Ireland West Airport Knock 100km.\r\nThis is rare opportunity to acquire a unique property and viewing is highly recommended to be truly appreciated.
