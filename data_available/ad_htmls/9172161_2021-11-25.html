    Development Description

      **On Sale Now**\r\n\r\nFor enquiries, please contact Selling Agents Sherry FitzGerald New Homes 01 667 1888.\r\n \r\nCairn, together with Selling Agents Sherry FitzGerald New Homes, are delighted to introduce Graydon, a beautiful new development of spacious family homes located in the heart of the village of Newcastle in Southwest Dublin. Ideal for growing families and commuters, Graydon offers an ideal opportunity to purchase a high-quality affordable new home in a convenient location close to Dublin City Centre, several large business parks and the N7/M50 main roads. \r\n \r\nThese spacious new homes bear all the hallmarks of a quality Cairn development, with a good selection of different house sizes and layouts, excellent energy efficiency, a high level of finish and beautiful landscaped public and private green spaces. The development also benefits from the adjoining Taobh Chnoic Park, a 2-hectare park featuring play areas, sports facilities and allotments. In addition, smaller "pocket parks" with seating and play spaces are located within the development. \r\n \r\nGraydon is ideally located off Newcastle Main Street and is within walking distance of local amenities and shops. For children, the development is perfectly set next to the new primary school. Newcastle itself is a quiet, scenic village with plenty of great local amenities and a strong sense of community - the ideal location for raising a family. The surrounding countryside offers a sense of peace and tranquillity rare for such a convenient location. \r\n \r\nGraydon's location also couldn't be better for workers and commuters. Citywest, Baldonnell and Greenogue Business Parks, home to many big employers, are just a short drive away, while the N7 main road is just 2km away. For commuters into Dublin, Hazelhatch/Celbridge train station is less than 10 minutes away, and offers fast, efficient and regular train services into Dublin. A regular bus route is also available directly from Newcastle into the city centre. \r\n \r\nFor more information and to register your interest, see www.graydon.ie.\xe2\x80\xaf \r\n\r\n\r\nNote\r\n\r\nThese particulars are detailed for the purposes of representing the development only.
