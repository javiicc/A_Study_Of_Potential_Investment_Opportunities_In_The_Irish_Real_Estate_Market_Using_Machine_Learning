    The subject property consists of a beautifully presented house, centrally located between the towns of Ballinasloe, Portumna and Loughrea in a county setting and just a short drive from the village of Killimor.\r\nConstructed in 2008, this four-bedroom home (all large doubles with two ensuites) measures approximately 264sq.m / 2,840sq.ft., and it is a perfect family home. The house is well laid out with generous open plan living areas in addition to a separate family room and sunroom. The attic has also been converted into usable space, divided into two rooms, and is accessed via a permanent stairway. This area of the house is an ideal home office and games area.\r\nThe property stands on an overall site measuring approximately 0.75 acres. The orientation of the house is such that morning sun beams through the kitchen window, with the living room and separate family room enjoying the evening sunshine. Also included on the property is a double garage. The exterior of the property is completed with professionally landscaped lawns, shrubbery and tarmac driveway accessed via electric gates.
