    Kundera is possibly one of the finest properties to come to the market in this ideal and tranquil setting in Co. Wicklow in recent times. The present owners have invested significant time and effort in extending and refurbishing the property under the watchful eye of their wonderful Architects E.M.D. The outcome is something extraordinarily special A modern luxurious residence perched among the foothills of the Wicklow mountains.\n\nThe setting is truly exceptional on Rocky valley drive where the property enjoys a wonderful private rural setting yet has ease of access to the N11 bringing the city within a 30 minute commute and the airport within 45 mins for frequent travellers.\nThe views of the garden of Irelands mountains and countryside from both the grounds and from the interior looking out are quite spectacular, with the full impact of the foothills of the big Sugarloaf experienced from all the principal rooms.\n\nThe bespoke interior exudes quality throughout from the moment you step inside. Beautifully appointed rooms with Versace Gold tiling, bespoke designer bathrooms featuring Villeroy & Boch and Hans Grohe fittings, solid timber floors, Bespoke built in units and wardrobes by Hammonds in the UK with Swarovski studded handles and a stunning main kitchen, and working kitchen designed by Siematic with Gaggenau and Miele appliances.\n\nThe cool and contemporary panelled reception hall with its Versace tiled floor and concealed storage and bathroom set the tone for what is to follow. Moving on into the property we enter the light filled lobby area with triple height ceiling and high-level picture windows, glass and stainless steel bannisters and gallery landing over. The principal rooms are light filled, well-proportioned and finished to exacting standards and all offer stunning mountain and garden views. They are all located off this space as are the large gym, sauna, and study with its meditation pool. The property provides a wonderful balance between reception and bedroom accommodation and is ideal for family living. \n\nThere is extensive use of floor to ceiling glass and large patio doors to various parts of the sun drenched patios and gardens, solid mahogany floors and a state of the art communications and music system with easy access from your phone for comfort and convenience. \n\nApproached Via electric gates the property offers a secluded mature acre of gardens. Well planted and beautifully designed to blend into the natural rock formations we find in this area. A wonderful large water feature and stunning south west facing patio area are perfect for outdoor entertaining and dining. There is also a large detached one bedroomed Annex and a hard tennis court and ample Parking.\n\nKundera is a real rarity and the magnificence of its setting cannot be overstated.\n\n \n\n\nGround Floor\n\nReception Hall bright contemporary Hall with Versace marble floor tiles and floor to ceiling window.
