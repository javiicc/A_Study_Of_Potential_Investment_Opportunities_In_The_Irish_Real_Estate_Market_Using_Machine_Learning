    Deceptively spacious two-bedroom end-of-terrace town house in a most sought after location with numerous amenities on its doorstep. Extending to c. 65 sq. m, the two-storey house offers generous open plan living space on the ground floor with a kitchen and bathroom off, the first floor enjoys two spacious double bedrooms. To the rear is a private walled patio area. The house retains many period features including fireplaces, coved ceilings, picture rails and timber floors. The city centre is a short walk away or a quick trip on the LUAS (Museum Stop). The village of Stoneybatter, with its artisan shops, delicatessens, restaurants, cafes and bars, is a short distance from the front door. The 1,750-acre Phoenix Park is less than five minutes from the house, catering for numerous sports including football, cricket and miles of running and cycling tracks. The new DIT campus at Grangegorman is less than a 10-minute walk away.
