    We at Felicity Fox are delighted to present to the market this unique top floor two bedroom duplex apartment located in the heart of Georgian Dublin. The property has been redecorated throughout recently and is presented in walk-in condition. On street resident permit parking is available and the new resident can obtain a key to Fitzwilliam Square (subject to a fee).\r\n\r\nOnce through the front door of this Georgian building the entrance hall is a grand affair with ornate plasterwork and a majestic staircase. Walk to the top floor and through the entrance door to the apartment. To the left of the hallway is the living/dining room. The room faces south east and is lit by a large window. The exceptional floor to ceiling height here gives the room an air of light and space. The room is complemented by a Godin multi fuel burning stove (to be reconnected). The kitchen is located off the living/dining room. It is a compact space and appliances are fully integrated. A cupboard in the hallway houses a washing machine and tumble dryer. Upstairs, a landing provides access to a large hot press. The master bedroom is fitted with built-in wardrobes. The second bedroom also comes fitted with built-in wardrobes and a vanity unit. The bathroom is tiled in striking black and white tiles. A thermostatic electric shower has recently been installed.\r\n \r\nFitzwilliam Place is located close to Fitzwilliam Square, Merrion Square, Grafton Street, and St Stephen\xe2\x82\xacTM Green. The area is host to a number of multinationals such as Google, Facebook and Twitter along with national bodies IBEC and the IDA. Government Buildings are a short walk away on Merrion Street. Cultural amenities include The National Gallery, The National Concert Hall and many museums. When it comes to dining out or enjoying a drink there is a range of options nearby including hostelries such as Suesey Street, Matt The Thresher and Doheny & Nesbit. The leafy banks of the Grand Canal provide the opportunity for rest and relaxation and daily strolls.
